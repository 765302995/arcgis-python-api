Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/04/2017 - 00:42 Location: Windermere Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Windermere Avenue, Warrington.  When they arrived they found that the fire involved several wheelie bins. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 111 555 or Cheshire Police on 101.
