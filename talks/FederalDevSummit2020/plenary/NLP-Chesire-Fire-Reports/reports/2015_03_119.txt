House fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 07/03/2015 - 06:22 Location: Ways Green, Winsford. Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford and one from Northwich Firefighters responded to a house fire on Ways Green in Winsford.  When they arrived four firefighters in breathing apparatus began to tackle the blaze with hose reel jets. They also confirmed that there was nobody trapped inside the property.  Crews also used a Thermal Imaging Camera to check the lloft space to ensure there were no further hot spots.  A fire investigation has now been launched to find the cause of the blaze.
