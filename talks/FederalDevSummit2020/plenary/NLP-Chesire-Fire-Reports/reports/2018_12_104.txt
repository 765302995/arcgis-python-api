 Overheated car brakes in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 07/12/2018 - 15:26 Location: Welsh Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Firefighters were called to reports of a car overheating on Welsh Road in Ellesmere Port.  On arrival crews established the overheating was coming from the brakes.  Firefighters dampened down the car using a hose reel and made sure the area was safe.
