Second motorbike fire in Chester Time of Incident: 15/02/2017 - 23:01 Location: Hough Green, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane Firefighters were called to put out a fire involving a motorbike in Hough Green. This fire is also believed to be deliberate. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
