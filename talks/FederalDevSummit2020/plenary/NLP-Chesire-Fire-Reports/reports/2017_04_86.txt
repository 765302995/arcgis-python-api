Large refuse fire on Watkin Street in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/04/2017 - 22:00 Location: Watkin Street in Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Warrington firefighters used one hose reel jet was used to extinguish a large amount of refuse.   It is believed that this fire was started deliberately.  If you can help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, or call Cheshire Police on 101.
