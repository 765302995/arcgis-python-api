Wheelie bin fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 12/03/2016 - 05:08 Location: Gresty Terrace, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Two fire engines were called to report of a wheelie bin well alight between two garages in Crewe. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  Please follow our wheelie bin safety advice.
