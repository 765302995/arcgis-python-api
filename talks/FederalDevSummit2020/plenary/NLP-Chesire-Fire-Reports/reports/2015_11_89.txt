Collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/11/2015 - 19:06 Location: Froghall Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington one from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Froghall Lane, Warrington.  When they arrived they found that the incident involved two cars.  There was woman trapped in onr of the cars.  Crews used special equipment to free the woman. She was then passed to the care of the ambulance service.
