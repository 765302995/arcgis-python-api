Car fire on M56 Time of Incident: 17/01/2015 - 12:39 Location: M56 West, J14 to J15 Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus and two hose reels were used to extinguish a car well alight on the hard shoulder of the M56. The motorway was closed while the incident was dealt with due to smoke across the carriageway.
