 Car fire in Shavington Time of Incident: 27/08/2018 - 11:59 Location: Crewe Road, Shavington Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to a vehicle fire on Crewe Road, Shavington. On arrival crews found that a car was well alight and an adjacent hedge had also set alight. Crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for 45 minutes.
