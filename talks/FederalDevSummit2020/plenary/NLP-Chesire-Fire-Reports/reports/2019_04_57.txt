 Gas cannisters leaking in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/04/2019 - 06:15 Location: Fir Tree Close, Warrington. WA4 4LU Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm, one from Penketh and one from Warrington At 6.15am firefighters were called following reports of gas cannisters leaking at a mobile kitchen unit at Stretton, Warrington. The crews, who were in attendance for around half-an-hour, isolated six cylinders and ventilated the kitchen area.
