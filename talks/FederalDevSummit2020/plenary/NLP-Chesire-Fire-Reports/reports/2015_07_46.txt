Fire at Widnes chip shop Time of Incident: 23/07/2015 - 19:33 Location: Milton Avenue, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Overheated oil caused smoke to fill a chip shop in Widnes.  A large fan was used to ventilate the premises and the oil was drained and allowed to cool. The gas and electrics were also isolated.  The crew fully inspected the frying range using a thermal imaging camera to ensure the temperature had dropped before leaving.
