Tumble dryer fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/08/2015 - 18:10 Location: Cunliffe Close in Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine from Runcorn and a fire engine from Widnes There was a tumble dryer on the ground floor of a terraced property on Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish the fire. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the property.   Tumble dryer fire safety tips
