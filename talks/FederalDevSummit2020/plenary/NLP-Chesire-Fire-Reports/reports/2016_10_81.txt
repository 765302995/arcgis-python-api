Road traffic collision in Daresbury Time of Incident: 17/10/2016 - 13:54 Location: Chester Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Warrington Two vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision on Chester Road. A car and a wagon were involved. Firefighters released one casualty, a man, from the car who was taken to hospital. No other casualties were reported.
