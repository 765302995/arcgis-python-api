Grass fire in Kettleshulme Time of Incident: 03/06/2016 - 13:11 Location: Macclesfield Road, High Peak Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A grass fire with multiple seats of fire broke out on the moors in Kettleshulme. The ground involved in fire measured 100m by 100m. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and beaters to extinguish the fire.
