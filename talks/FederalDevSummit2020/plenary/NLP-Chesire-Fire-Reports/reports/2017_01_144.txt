Motorbike on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/01/2017 - 02:37 Location: Gleadmere, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters tackled a motorbike fire on Gleadmere. A hose reel jet was used to put out the flames and police were called as it is suspected the fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about the fire is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
