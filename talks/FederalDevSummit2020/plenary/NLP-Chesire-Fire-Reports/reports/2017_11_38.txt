First floor flat fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/11/2017 - 13:55 Location: Grangeway, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one fire engine from Widnes Two fire engines from Widnes and Runcorn attended a fire involving a first floor flat on Grangeway, Runcorn. Firefighters used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel and a positive pressure ventilation unit to bring the fire under control. Crews were in attendance for one hour.
