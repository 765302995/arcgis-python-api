Balcony fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/08/2015 - 18:35 Location: Berwick Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Berwick Road, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire on a first floor balcony. They dealt with the incident and checked that the fire had not spread to the roof.  A Home Safety Assesment was also carried out at the property.
