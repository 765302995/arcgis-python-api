 Kitchen fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 14/06/2018 - 22:22 Location: Clifton Crescent, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine each from Runcorn and Frodsham Crews responded to a report of a kitchen fire on Clifton Crescent. The fire was out on arrival but had left a lot of smoke. Firefighters isolated the gas and electricity supplies and gave oxygen therapy to the property owner's dog which is now in the care of a vet.
