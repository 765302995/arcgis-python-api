 Car on fire in High Peak Time of Incident: 08/10/2019 - 13:38 Location: Macclesfield Road, Kettleshulme, High Peak Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Bollington and two from Macclesfield Shortly before 1.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on Macclesfield Road in Kettleshulme, High Peak.  On arrival the crews found the vehicle well alight at the side of the carriageway.  No-one was in or with the car.  The carriageway was temporarily closed as the crews worked at the scene.  Two firefighters each used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  They then damped the car down to make it safe.  The crews also prevented run-off water from entering the drains.  Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
