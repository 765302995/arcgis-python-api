 Bush on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/07/2018 - 23:01 Location: Minerva Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 11pm firefighters were called to reports of fire in Minerva Close in Latchford, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered a bush on fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
