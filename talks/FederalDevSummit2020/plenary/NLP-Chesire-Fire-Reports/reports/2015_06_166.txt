Unattended cooking causes Widnes fire Time of Incident: 04/06/2015 - 15:42 Location: Atterbury Close, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes A pan left cooking, unattended, on a hob caused a fire in a Widnes flat.  Firefighters had to force entry as the occupant had gone out leaving the cooker on with a pan on top.  The crew, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire and a high pressure fan to clear the property of smoke.    NEVER leave cooking unattended, please follow our safety advice - Kitchen fire safety
