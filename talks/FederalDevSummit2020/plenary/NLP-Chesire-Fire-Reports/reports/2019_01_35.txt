 Car fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 05/01/2019 - 00:39 Location: Christie Street, Widnes Attendance:  One fire engine from Penketh Crews tackled a saloon car on fire on Christie Street, which was well alight when they arrived. They used breathing apparatus and a single hose reel to extinguish the flames, and liaised with colleagues from the police as the incident is believed to have been a deliberate ignition.
