House fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/12/2015 - 17:50 Location: Marina Village, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn andone fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire at Marina Village, Runcorn.   When they arrived crews found that there was a fire in a second floor bedroom of the mid terraced property.  Crews confirmed that everyone was safe before tackling the fire. Firefighters used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to extinguish the blaze. They also isolated the gas and electricity supplies to the property.  Two smoke alarms were fitted and did alert the occupiers to the fire. A post fire Home Safety Assessment was also carried out by crews.
