 Van fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 11/03/2019 - 14:53 Location: Thomas Street, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Shortly before 3pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a van on Thomas Street in Congleton. The fire had burnt out prior to the crew's arrival. Firefighters used water to cool the vehicle's engine down. They also made the van safe by disconnecting its battery. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
