 Oven fire in Haslington Time of Incident: 28/02/2019 - 18:23 Location: Bradeley Road, Haslington Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Shortly before 6.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving an oven at a domestic property on Bradeley Road in Haslington. On arrival crews discovered a small fire in the top of the oven. Firefighters cut away the oven's wooden surround while using a thermal imaging camera to monitor temperatures. They then extinguished the fire and removed the oven from the surround. Once the fire was out firefighters used the camera to check for hotspots and make sure that the oven was safe. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
