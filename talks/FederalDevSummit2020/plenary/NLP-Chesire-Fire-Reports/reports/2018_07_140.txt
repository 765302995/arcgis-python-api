 Cat rescued from tree in Wardle Time of Incident: 14/07/2018 - 16:08 Location: Nantwich Road, Wardle Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich attended Firefighters attended to reports of a cat stuck in a tree on Nantwich Road in Wardle.  Crews used a 13.5 metre ladder to rescue the cat and bring it to safety after it had been stuck for almost seven hours.  The cat was then reunited with its relieved owners.
