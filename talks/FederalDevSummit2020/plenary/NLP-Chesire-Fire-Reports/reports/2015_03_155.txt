House fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 01/03/2015 - 08:08 Location: Lockgate West, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and two from Widnes attended A fire in a terraced property in Runcorn required firefighters to attend. The fire involved the ground floor and roof space of the bungalow and crews wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel to control and extinguish the fire. Ambulance staff also attended and dealt with one woman with burns to her hand. Updated at March 3 2015  The cause of this incident was found to be accidental.
