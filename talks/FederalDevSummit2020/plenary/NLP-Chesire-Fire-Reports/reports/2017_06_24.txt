Fire in outbuilding in Knutsford Time of Incident: 25/06/2017 - 16:29 Location: Stanley Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford, one from Altrincham and one from Northwich attended Fire crews were called to a fire in an outbuilding on Stanley Road, Knutsford. The fire was on two floors of the outbuilding and crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A thermal imaging camera was used to check the building before crews left the scene.
