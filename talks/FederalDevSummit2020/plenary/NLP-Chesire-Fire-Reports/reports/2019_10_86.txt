 Small fire in a Warrington greenhouse Time of Incident: 17/10/2019 - 20:16 Location: Capesthorne Road, Cinnamon Brow, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 8.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire within a greenhouse on Capesthorne Road in Cinnamon Brow, Warrington.  On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire.  Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.  The fire is believed to have been started accidentally via a stray firework.
