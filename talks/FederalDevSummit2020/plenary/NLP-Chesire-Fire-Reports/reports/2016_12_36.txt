Bonfire in Winsford Time of Incident: 25/12/2016 - 18:46 Location: Dee Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters and police were called to a report of an unsupervised bonfire on Dee Way. The crew used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
