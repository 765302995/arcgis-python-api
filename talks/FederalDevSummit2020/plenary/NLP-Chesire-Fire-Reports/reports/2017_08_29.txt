Outhouse fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 26/08/2017 - 17:52 Location: Granville Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and one from Winsford Fire crews were called to a fire in an outhouse in the garden of a domestic property on Granville Road, Northwich. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They were detained for 20 minutes.
