Loft fire in Rostherne Time of Incident: 28/10/2017 - 14:32 Location: Marsh Lane,Rostherne. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield. One fire engine from Sale and one fire engine from Altrincham Firefighters attended a fire in a loft involving an electrical consumer unit. They used four breathing apparatus, two hose reels and a short extension ladder to extinguish the fire. Burnt timber was cut away and a thermal imaging camera and hearth kit were used to check for any hot spots. A large fan was used to disperse the smoke.
