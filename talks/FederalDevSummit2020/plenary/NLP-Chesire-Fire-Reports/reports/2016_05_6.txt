Tree fire in Church Lawton Time of Incident: 29/05/2016 - 15:03 Location: Congleton Road South, Church Lawton. Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton Firefighters used hand held extinguishers to put out a fire involving some trees off Congleton Road South, Church Lawton.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
