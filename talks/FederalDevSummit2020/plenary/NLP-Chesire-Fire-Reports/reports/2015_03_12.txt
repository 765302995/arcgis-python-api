Microwave fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 27/03/2015 - 21:34 Location: Casson Street, Crewe. Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe Firefighters responded to a smoke alarm sounding on Casson Street, Crewe.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire in a microwave. Crews removed the appliance and used a special fan to clear smoke from the property. They also carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving. Kitchen fire safety advice
