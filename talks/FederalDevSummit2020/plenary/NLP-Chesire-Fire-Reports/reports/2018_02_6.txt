 Large fire in the open Time of Incident: 26/02/2018 - 17:21 Location: Whitchurch Road, Beeston near Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley and one fire engine from Winsford attended. Firefighters from Winsford and Tarporley used a hose reel jet to tackle a large fire in the open at Beeston near Tarporley. Crews were also assisted by a local farmer who used a tractor to help break up an extinguish the fire. The team were in attendance for approximately two hours.
