Road traffic collision in Chester Time of Incident: 01/06/2015 - 16:49 Location: Junction A483/A55 Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters were called to the scene of a road traffic collision in Chester, at the junction of A483 and A55.  A car and a van were involved.  Nobody was trapped in either vehicle but petrol was found to be leaking.  Firefighters checked the scene was safe.
