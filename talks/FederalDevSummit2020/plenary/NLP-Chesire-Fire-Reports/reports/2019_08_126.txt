 Gorse fire in High Peak Time of Incident: 08/08/2019 - 12:15 Location: Macclesfield Road, Kettleshulme, High Peak Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At 12.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on farmland in Macclesfield Road, Kettleshulme, High Peak. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a controlled gorse fire. Firefighters decided to extinguish the fire, doing so with a hose reel jet. They then damped the area down to make it safe and monitored temperatures with a hose reel jet. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour.
