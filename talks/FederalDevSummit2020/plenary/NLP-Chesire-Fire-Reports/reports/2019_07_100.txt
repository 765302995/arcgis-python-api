 Bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/07/2019 - 17:57 Location: Vulcan Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters were called to Vulcan Close, Warrington, to a bin fire. As well as the bin being set alight there were various items of discarded bedroom furniture which had been set alight deliberately. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire Police.
