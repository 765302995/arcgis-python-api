Recycling unit fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 03/07/2016 - 20:03 Location: North Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester A fire in a recycling unit in a building was tackled by firefighters. The crews, three firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used three hose reel jets to bring the fire under control. Two thermal imaging cameras were then used to check there were no further hot spots of fire and crews raked out waste from the machine. The fire is believed to have been caused by an overheated electric motor.
