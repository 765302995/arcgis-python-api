 Camper van fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 28/06/2018 - 17:07 Location: Beech Lane, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Firefighters were called to Beech Lane in Macclesfield following reports of a camper van on fire. On arrival the fire was out but petrol was leaking from the vehicle. Crews worked to stop the leak and used an Environment Agency pack to attempt to stop fuel entering the drain. Environment Agency was informed. Police attended to manage traffic.
