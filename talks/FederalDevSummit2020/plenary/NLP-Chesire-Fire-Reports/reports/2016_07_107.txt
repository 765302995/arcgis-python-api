Wheelie bins on fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 13/07/2016 - 00:05 Location: Herbert Street, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton Three wheelie bins which were well alight required firefighters to use one hose reel to extinguish them.  Firefighters believe the fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.  Always follow our wheelie bin safety advice to help prevent arson.
