 Bin fire in Handforth Time of Incident: 27/05/2019 - 14:39 Location: Wilmslow Road, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 2.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a bin being on fire on Wilmslow Road in Handforth. The crew extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. Firefighters were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
