House fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 16/02/2016 - 19:03 Location: Moran Crescent, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Bollington A television was involved in a fire in a house in Macclesfield. When crews arrived there was smoke coming from the front door of the house.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control. A covering jet was also used.    A high pressure fan was used to clear the property of smoke. The roof space of the affected property and adjoining properties were checked for fire spread using a thermal imaging camera.  An ambulance was called to check residents as a precautionary measure.
