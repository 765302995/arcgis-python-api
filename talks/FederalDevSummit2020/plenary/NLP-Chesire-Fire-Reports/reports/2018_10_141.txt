 Fire in commercial building in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/10/2018 - 10:23 Location: North Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a commercial building on North Road, Ellesmere Port, after a fire started in a printing machine. A full evacuation of the building took place prior to the fire service's arrival and crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a CO2 extinguisher to put out the fire.
