Sheep rescued from water in Lymm Time of Incident: 15/02/2017 - 09:32 Location: Lymm Dam, Church Road, Lymm Attendance: Rope rescue unit and supporting vehicle from Knutsford, fire engine from Penketh, fire engine from Warrington, fire engine from Stockton Heath A number of sheep were rescued by firefighters after they had got into difficuly in water at Lymm Dam. The Service's rope rescue unit and swift water rescue technicians used rafts to rescue the animals and bring them on to dry land. All the sheep were rescued safely and handed into the care of the farmer.
