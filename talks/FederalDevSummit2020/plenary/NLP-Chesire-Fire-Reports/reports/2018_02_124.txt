 Firefighters extinguish chimney fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 01/02/2018 - 18:51 Location: Holmes Chapel Road, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton and one from Holmes Chapel Fire Crews were called to reports of a chimney fire at a house on Holmes Chapel Road in Congleton yesterday evening. Firefighters used a high pressure hose reel and chimney rods to extinguish the fire. The incident was stood down at 9.46pm.
