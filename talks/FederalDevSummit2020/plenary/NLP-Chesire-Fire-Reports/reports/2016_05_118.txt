LPG vehicle giving off smell in Widnes Time of Incident: 10/05/2016 - 11:59 Location: Derby Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to a 4x4 vehicle which has a duel fuel conversion to LPG and is giving off a chemical smell. The crew stood by with one covering water jet. Police closed the road while the incident was dealt with. There appeared to be a small rupture in the LPG tank which had caused a leak. Advice was taken from an LPG technician. Crews used a thermal imaging camera to check the area.
