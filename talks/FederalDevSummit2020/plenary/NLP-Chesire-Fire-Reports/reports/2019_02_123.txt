 Car fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 05/02/2019 - 15:57 Location: The Close, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly before 4pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire at The Close in Northwich. On arrival the crew found the vehicle well alight on the roadway. Firefighters, including one wearing breathing apparatus, extinguished the fire with two hose reel jets. The crew were in attendance for around 40 minutes. The fire is believed to have been caused by an engine fault.
