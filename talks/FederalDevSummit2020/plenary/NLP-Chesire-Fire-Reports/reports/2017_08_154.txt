Fire involving a log burner inside a disused building in Grappenhall, Warrington Time of Incident: 02/08/2017 - 20:13 Location: Church Lane, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and Lymm Two fire engines from Warrington and Lymm were sent to reports of a fire involving a building on Church Lane in Grappenhall, Warrington. On arrival the fire crews found a small fire in the back room of a disused building that had caused a small amount of smoke within the property.  The fire involved a log burner and a flue.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  The fire crews used one hose reel and a thermal imagine camera to tackle a fire.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke.  This fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
