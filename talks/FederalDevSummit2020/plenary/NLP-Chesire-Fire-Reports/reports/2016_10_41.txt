Bin fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 24/10/2016 - 18:44 Location: Mclaren Street, Crewe. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters dealt with a bin fire on Mclaren Street, Crewe.  A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out, which was believed to have been started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire police on 101.
