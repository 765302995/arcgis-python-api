Disused building fire in Darnhall Time of Incident: 24/05/2017 - 21:31 Location: The Raven, Swanlow Lane, Darnhall, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Northwich Sheets of plywood on fire in a disused outbuilding were put out by firefighters using a hose reel jet. The crew then carried out a full inspection of the outbuilding and disused pub it was next to.
