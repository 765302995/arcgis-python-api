 Bushes fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 30/06/2019 - 15:06 Location: Bridgewater Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly after 3pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Bridgewater Avenue, Warrington. On arrival the crew found a fire involving bushes. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
