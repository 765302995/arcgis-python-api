Brazier fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 23/07/2016 - 03:42 Location: Princes Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A fire in a brazier had been left burning unattended overnight. Firefighters were called and used a hose reel jet to put out the fire behind a Princes Road property.  Always make sure braziers and barbecues are properly extinguished before leaving them.
