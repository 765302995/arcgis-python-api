 Vehicle fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 07:13 Location: Picow Farm Road, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a vehicle fire on Picow Farm Road in Runcorn.  The fire involved a transit van which was believed to be carrying drums of oil.  Firefighters, wearing one breathing apparatus, have used two hose reels to put out the fire and then dampened down the area.
