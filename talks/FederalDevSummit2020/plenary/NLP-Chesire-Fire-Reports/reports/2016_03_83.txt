Four wheelie bins on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/03/2016 - 00:58 Location: Bridge Street, Bewsey and Whitecross, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Four wheelie bins were involved in fire on Bridge Street, Bewsey. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put it out. It is suspected the fire was caused deliberately. If you saw anything, or have any information, please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
