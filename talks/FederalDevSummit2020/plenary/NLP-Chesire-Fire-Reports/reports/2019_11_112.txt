 Car on fire on Warrington car dealership forecourt Time of Incident: 08/11/2019 - 14:08 Location: Juniper Lane, Woolston, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood attended Shortly after 2pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on a car dealership forecourt in Juniper Lane, Woolston, Warrington.  On arrival the crew found the saloon car well alight.  A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet.  The vehicle was then damped down to make it safe.  A thermal imaging camera was used to monitor temperatures.  Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
