 Flooding at house in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 26/10/2019 - 09:02 Location: Knutsford Road, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Fire crews attended to flooding at a house on Knutsford Road in Alderley Edge which was affecting the electrics.  Around six inches of water had flooded the lounge area and another eight inches of water on the patio.   Firefighters isolated the electrics and have reduced the level of water as well as giving advice to the home owner.
