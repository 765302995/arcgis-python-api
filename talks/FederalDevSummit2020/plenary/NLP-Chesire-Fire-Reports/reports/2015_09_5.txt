Roof fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 29/09/2015 - 11:59 Location: Maple Avenue, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton and one from Greater Manchester Firefighters were called to a fire in the roof space and kitchen of a bungalow in Poynton.  Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels and two short extension ladders to tackle the blaze.  The gas and electricity has been isolated and all people were accounted for. The fire was contained to the loft space of the bungalow and the crews ripped away the roof for access.  It is believed that the cause of this fire was accidental.
