Fire in electrical substation in Chester Time of Incident: 25/07/2015 - 00:47 Location: Lower Bridge Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and an aerial appliance Firefighters cordoned off the area and cleared smoke after a fire at an electrical substation in Chester.  Scottish Power attended the scene and isolated the electricity. The fire was out when firefighters got access and they ventilated the building before handing the scene over to Scottish Power.
