 Fire attend to burst pipe at a house in Runcorn Time of Incident: 02/02/2018 - 00:53 Location: The Calvers, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to an address on The Calvers in Runcorn in the early hours of this morning following reports of a burst pipe and flooding at the house. The pipe was believed to have burst from under the stairs and was seeping into the rooms in the house. Crews stemmed the leak and removed the water and informed United Utilities.
