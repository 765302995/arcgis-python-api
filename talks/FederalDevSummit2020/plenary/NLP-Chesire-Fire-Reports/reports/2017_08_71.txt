Cars on fire in Adlington Time of Incident: 18/08/2017 - 02:23 Location: London Road, Adlington Attendance: one fire engine from Poynton and one from Macclesfield Three cars which were on fire next to the wall of a commercial building were extinguished by firefighters using two hose reels. The building was checked with a thermal imaging camera for heat transfer.  It is suspected that this fire may have been started deliberately. If you know anything about it then please call police on 101 quoting inc 89 of 18th August or Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.
