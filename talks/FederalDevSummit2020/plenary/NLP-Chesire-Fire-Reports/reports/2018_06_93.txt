 Smoke alarm in Chester Time of Incident: 23/06/2018 - 15:49 Location: Durham Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Firefighters from Powey Lane were called to reports that a smoke alarm had activated at a property on Durham Road, Chester. On arrival crews found that the alarm had been activated due to burnt food in a microwave. The team used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property and a 70-year-old man was treated for smoke inhalation. The team were in attendance for approximately ten minutes.
