Wheelie bin on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 24/06/2015 - 03:46 Location: Rudheath Close, Crewe Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe and Middlewich attended A wheelie bin on fire in Crewe caused two people to suffer smoke inhalation. The fire was at the rear of a property and two people got out of the property prior to the arrival of the crew. Paramedics and Cheshire Police attended. An investigation into the cause will take place.
