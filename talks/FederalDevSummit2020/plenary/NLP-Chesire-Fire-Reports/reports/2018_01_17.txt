 Boiler fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/01/2018 - 17:53 Location: Holly Bush Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm and one from Birchwood Fire crews were called to Holly Bush Lane, Warrington, after a fire started in an external boiler. Crews extinguished the fire, which was in a wood chip boiler, using a hose reel jet.
