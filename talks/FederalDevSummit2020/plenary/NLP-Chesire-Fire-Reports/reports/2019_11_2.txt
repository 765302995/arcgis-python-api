 Smoke alarm activated at conferencing centre attached to Wilmslow hotel Time of Incident: 29/11/2019 - 02:08 Location: Church Street, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Shortly after 2.10am firefighters were called to reports of a smoke alarm sounding at a conferencing centre attached to a hotel in Church Street, Wilmslow.  The crew searched the building with the aid of a thermal imaging camera and found no signs of a fire.  The alarm is believed to have been caused by steam from a shower.
