 First aid administered to a casualty in Poynton Time of Incident: 24/07/2019 - 14:34 Location: School Lane, Poynton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton. Firefighters were called to School Lane in Poynton following reports of an young male casualty who had been injured by a steel railing. Crews administered first aid then passed him over to the care of paramedics who then took him to hospital.
