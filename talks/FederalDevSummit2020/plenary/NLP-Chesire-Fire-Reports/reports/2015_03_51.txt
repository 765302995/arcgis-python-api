Motorbike on fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 21/03/2015 - 01:21 Location: Waggs Road, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines from Congleton One hose reel was used to extinguish a fire involving a motorbike in Congleton.   The motorbike was in close proximity to a garages but the crews prevented it from spreading. Police were requested to attend the scene due to the cause being suspected arson. Anyone with any information is asked to call police on 101, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
