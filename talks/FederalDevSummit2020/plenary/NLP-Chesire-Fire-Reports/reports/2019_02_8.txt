 Campfire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/02/2019 - 17:03 Location: Lees Lane, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 5pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Lees Lane in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew found a small campfire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
