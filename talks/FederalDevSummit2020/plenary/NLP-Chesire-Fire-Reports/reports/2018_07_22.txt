 Rubbish on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 27/07/2018 - 22:44 Location: Lydyett Lane, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Firefigters were called to a fire on Lydyett Lane, Barnton, Northwich.  A crate filled with cardboard was well alight and  crews used one hose reel to extinguish the fire.
