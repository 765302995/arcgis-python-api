 Van fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 03:46 Location: Kilncroft, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a van fire on Kilncroft, Runcorn. The van was well alight on arrival and crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.
