Fire in electrical unit in Capenhurst. Time of Incident: 15/01/2015 - 04:13 Location: Urenco, Capenhurst Lane, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester, two fire engines and the foam unit from Ellesmere Port, one fire engine and the control unit from Northwich and one fore engine from North Wales. Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Urenco in Capenhurst. On arrival crews confirmed the incident was a fire confined to an industrial sized electrical unit. Cheshire Fire and Rescue Service worked with the on site fire service and wearing breathing apparatus, used foam and dry powder to extinguish the blaze. Crews monitored the electrical cabinet using a thermal imaging camera and the area was ventilated. The fire is believed to have been started by an overheated circuit.
