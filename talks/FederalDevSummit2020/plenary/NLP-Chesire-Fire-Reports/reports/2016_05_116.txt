Electrical fire in traffic bollard in Widnes Time of Incident: 10/05/2016 - 18:23 Location: Earle Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Smoke was seen issuing from a traffic bollard.  Dry powder was used to put out a small fire and a thermal imaging camera used to check there were no further hot spots. Although there was smoke there was not enough to affect traffic. Once the fire was out, firefighters stayed at the scene until the electricity company arrived.
