 Grass fire at boat museum in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 24/07/2018 - 18:18 Location: South Pier Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 6.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open at the Boat Museum on South Pier Road in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered a grass fire measuring approximately 50 metres by 10 metres. Firefighters used an extended hose reel jet and beaters to extinguish the fire. They were in attendance for around an hour.
