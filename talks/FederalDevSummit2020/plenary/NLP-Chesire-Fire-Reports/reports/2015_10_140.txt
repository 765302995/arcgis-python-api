Casualty treated for smoke inhalation in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/10/2015 - 20:26 Location: Pool Lane, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath An unattended pan meant firefighters had to give first aid to a man who had suffered smoke inhalation. The fire had been put out before the crews arrived but caused damage to parts of the kitchen so gas and electricity was isolated. The occupier was admitted to hospital as a precaution.
