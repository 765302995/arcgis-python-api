 Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/01/2018 - 15:33 Location: West Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended a kitchen fire at an address on West Road, Ellesmere Port. The small fire involved a cooker, which crews removed from the premises. A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the property. One casualty was treated for smoke inhalation by paramedics.
