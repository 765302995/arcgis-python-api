 Moorland fire at Macclesfield Forest Time of Incident: 03/07/2018 - 18:58 Location: Buxton New Road, Macclesfield Forest Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield and four fire engines from Derbyshire Fire and Rescue Service Moorland fire at Macclesfield Forest At around 7pm firefighters were called to a moorland fire on Buxton New Road in Macclesfield Forest.  Crews from Macclesfield Fire Station assisted Derbyshire Fire and Rescue Service (DFRS) in fighting the grass fire, using a hose reel jet and beaters.  FRS were in command of the incident.  Firefighters from Macclesfield were in attendance for around four hours.
