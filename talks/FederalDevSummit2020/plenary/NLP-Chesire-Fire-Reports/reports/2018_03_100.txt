 Fire alarm activated at supermarket in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/03/2018 - 10:38 Location: Greenalls Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Penketh Fire alarm activated at supermarket in WarringtonAt 10.50am firefighters attended a supermarket on Greenalls Avenue in Latchford, Warrington, after a fire alarm had been activated at the store.  As a precaution, the supermarket was evacuated and firefighters discovered a small amount of smoke in the roof.  Crews investigated the roof but found no sign of a fire.
