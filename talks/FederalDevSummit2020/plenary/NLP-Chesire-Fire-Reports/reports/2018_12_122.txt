 Fire at a dry cleaner's in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/12/2018 - 17:14 Location: Gaskell Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 5.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a dry cleaner's on Gaskell Street in Warrington. The fire involving a drying machine was out on arrival. Firefighters isolated the electricity supply to the drying machine and made sure that it was safe. They also ventilated the premises. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
