House fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/10/2015 - 14:35 Location: Norleane Crescent, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes Firefighters were called to a fire on the ground floor of a semi-detached house in Runcorn.  Crews used four breathing apparatus and two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. Once the fire was out they used a high pressure fan to ventilate the property.
