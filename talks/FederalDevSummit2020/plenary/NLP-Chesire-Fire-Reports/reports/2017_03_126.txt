Hay fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 10/03/2017 - 20:39 Location: Styal Road, Wilmslow. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Styal Road, Wilmslow.  When they arrived crews found that there was some hay in a livestock feeder on fire. A water backpack was used to put it out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call 0800 555 111.
