 Van fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 29/03/2018 - 02:12 Location: Ravenfield Drive, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At 2.11am firefighters were called to a van fire on Ravenfield Drive in Widnes. On arrival the crew found the vehicle well alight and a firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to put out the flames. The hose reel jet was also used to cool off a diving cylinder in the van. Firefighters then used a thermal imaging camera to make sure that the cylinder was safe. They were at the scene for nearly an hour-and-a-half. Police also attended.
