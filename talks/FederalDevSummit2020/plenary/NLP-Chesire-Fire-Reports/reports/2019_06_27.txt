 Petrol fumes at an office in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/06/2019 - 11:41 Location: Stafford Road, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath At around 11.40am firefighters were called to a report of a strong smell of petrol in an office on Stafford Road, Latchford, Warrington. On arrival crews discovered that the smell stemmed from petrol being stored in a garage connected to the business premises. Firefighters ventilated the garage to remove the petrol fumes and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
