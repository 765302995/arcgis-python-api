Rescue of a fallen man Time of Incident: 13/09/2015 - 01:01 Location: London Road, Alderley Edge. Attendance: A fire engine from Wilmslow Crews from Wilmslow Fire Station assisted the ambulance service by gaining access to a male who had fallen behind a 7ft metal gate on London Road, Alderley Edge.  Firefighters gained access using a saw and left the casualty in the care of the ambulance service.
