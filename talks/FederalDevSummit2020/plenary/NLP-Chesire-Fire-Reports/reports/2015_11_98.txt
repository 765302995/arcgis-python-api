Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/11/2015 - 18:21 Location: Brentnall Close, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Warrington Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a wheelie bin fire on a play area off Brentnall Close, Warrington.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you saw anything that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
