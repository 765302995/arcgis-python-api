Cows rescued from a pond in Northwich Time of Incident: 03/09/2015 - 17:04 Location: Crown Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine and an animal rescue unit from Bollington and one fire engine and the rope rescue unit from Knutsford Firefighters rescued four calves stuck in a pond in Northwich.  Three of the crew wore swift water kit and safety cordons were put in place while the animals were released using a rescue path and sled to assist in reaching them.  A vet was at the scene and the calves were put in a safe place. The crews were on the scene for two and a half hours.
