Car and caravan overturned on M56 Time of Incident: 15/08/2015 - 13:35 Location: M56, junction 14 to 12 near Weaverham viaduct Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended a collision where a 4x4 and caravan had overturned on the M56.  No one was trapped and firefighters isolated the gas bottles.
