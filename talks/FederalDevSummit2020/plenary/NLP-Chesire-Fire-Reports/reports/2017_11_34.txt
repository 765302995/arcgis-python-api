Tumble dryer fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 18/11/2017 - 19:35 Location: Pooltown Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 7.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port. Two fire engines were dispatched to the address and on arrival the crews discovered the fire involved a tumble dryer. The crews used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to extinguish the fire.
