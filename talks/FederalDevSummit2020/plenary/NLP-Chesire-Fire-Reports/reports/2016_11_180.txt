Rubbish fire in building in Birchwood Time of Incident: 01/11/2016 - 15:27 Location: Strawberry close, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters were called to reports of a building fire in Warrington. On arrival, they found a small amount of rubbish in a communal area which had been on fire but was out before they arrived. They completed a full health and safety assessment of the property.  On inspection crews found smoke alarms were not fitted in the property. Luckily, this was a minor incident but, had it have been a serious fire, smoke alarms could have saved the occupants' life.  In a fire every second counts. Have a working smoke alarm and test it regularly.
