Fire in the flue of a log burner in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 14/03/2015 - 19:12 Location: Knutsford road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Cheadle and one from Wythenshawe Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish a fire inside the flue of a log burning stove at a semi-detatched property on Knutsford Road in Wilmslow. A triple extension ladder and a salvage sheet was used to bring the fire under control. A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the property.
