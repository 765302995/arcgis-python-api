 Grass fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 21:44 Location: Abbey Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield Crews attended to a grass fire, 30m by 30m, well alight on Abbey Road in Macclesfield.
