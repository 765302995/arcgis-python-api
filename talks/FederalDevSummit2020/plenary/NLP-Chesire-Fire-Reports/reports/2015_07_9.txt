Road traffic collision in Chester Time of Incident: 29/07/2015 - 11:56 Location: A540/A550 Wood Bank, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port. A road traffic collision involving a person trapped in a car in Woodbank (Two Mills) Cheshire, required firefighters to attend. The incident involved a car and a heavy good vehicle with one person trapped. The road was closed while the crew removed the roof to get the person out of the vehicle.
