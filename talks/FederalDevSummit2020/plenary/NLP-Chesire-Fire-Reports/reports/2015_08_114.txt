Disposable barbecue causes bin fire Time of Incident: 12/08/2015 - 15:01 Location: Knutsford Drive, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters have put out a small fire in a bin at Tatton Park. A hose reel jet was used to extinguish the fire, which was close to a children's play area. The fire was caused by hot ashes from a disposable barbecue.   Barbecue safety advice.
