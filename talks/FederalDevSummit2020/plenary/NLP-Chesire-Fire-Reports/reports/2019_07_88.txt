 Small fire in the open in Nantwich Time of Incident: 15/07/2019 - 18:23 Location: Kensington Court, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich At 6.25pm firefighters from Nanwich attended reports of a fire in the open on Kensington Court. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire which was being monitored by a local resident. Due to the location of the fire and the impact of the smoke on nearby properties, a decision was taken to extinguish the fire using a hose reel jet. Firefighters were in attendance at the address for approximately 15 minutes.
