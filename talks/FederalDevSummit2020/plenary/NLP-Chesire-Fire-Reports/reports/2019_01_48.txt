 Small rubbish fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 03/01/2019 - 10:52 Location: Rushgreen Road, Lymm Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 11am firefighters from Warrington were called to reports of a fire in the open on Rushgreen Road in Lymm. On arrival at the scene the team found that a small pile of rubbish had been dumped and deliberately set alight. The team used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for approximately 10 minutes.
