 Unattended fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/03/2018 - 14:06 Location: Delph Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 2.05pm firefighters were called to a fire in the open on Delph Lane in Houghton Green, Warrington. The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire that had been left unattended and got out of control. They were at the scene for around 10 minutes.
