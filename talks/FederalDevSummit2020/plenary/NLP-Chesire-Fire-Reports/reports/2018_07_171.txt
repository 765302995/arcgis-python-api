 Balcony fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 18:15 Location: South Pier Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port. One fire engine from Powey Lane. One fire engine from Chester. Firefighters attended a fire on the third floor of a business premises in South Pier Road, Ellesmere Port. Crews used an aerial ladder platform from Chester fire station and a hose reel to extinguish the small fire involving a wooden external balcony.  Crews were on scene for approximately twenty minutes.
