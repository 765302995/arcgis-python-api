Road traffic collision on M53 south at Chester Time of Incident: 09/08/2015 - 10:37 Location: M53 south at junction 12 near Hoole, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port There was a road traffic collision on the M53 south at junction 12 involving one vehicle. Fortunately nobody was trapped in the car, which was on its roof on the hard shoulder. Firefighters made the scene safe.
