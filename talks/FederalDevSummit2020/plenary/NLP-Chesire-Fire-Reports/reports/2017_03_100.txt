Road traffic collision in Sproston, Northwich Time of Incident: 15/03/2017 - 17:43 Location: Holmes Chapel Road, Sproston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel Firefighters helped to rescue a casualty following a road traffic collision involving a car and a motorcycle.  Paramedics and Police also attended this incident.  Firefighters helped paramedics to take the casualty to an air ambulance.
