 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/08/2018 - 16:01 Location: Mckinley Street, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Stockton Heath and one from Widnes Firefighters attended reports of a kitchen fire at a property on McKinley Street in Great Sankey, Warrington. On arrival the crews discovered that all persons were accounted for and that the fire was caused by food being left unattended as it was cooking. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used water to extinguish the fire and dampen down the area. Crews then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and make sure that the property was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour. For kitchen fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
