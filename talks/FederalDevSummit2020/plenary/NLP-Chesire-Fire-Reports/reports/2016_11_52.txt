Flat fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 20/11/2016 - 02:50 Location: Poolside Court, Alsager Attendance: Fire engines from Alsager, Crewe, Sandbach and Staffordshire Fire and Rescue Service A man escaped from a fire suspected to have been started deliberately at a Poolside Court flat. The fire involved the front door of the property. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire and a high pressure fan to clear smoke. A fire investigation was carried out and police were asked to attend. The man escaped prior to the arrival of the fire engines.   If you have any information about this incident, please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
