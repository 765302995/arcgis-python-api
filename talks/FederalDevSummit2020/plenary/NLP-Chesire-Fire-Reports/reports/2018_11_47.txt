 Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 22/11/2018 - 13:36 Location: Chelford Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield Dog rescued by firefighters after kitchen fireFirefighters were called to a fire in the kitchen of a semi-detached house on Chelford Road, Macclesfield. Crews forced entry to the property and used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to tackle the fire, which involved an unattended cooker.  Once the fire was out firefighters used a large high pressure fan to disperse smoke from the property.  A small puppy, which was found safe and well in its cage, was rescued and cared for by the fire crews.  For tips on avoiding a kitchen fire visit Kitchen fire safety
