 Batteries on fire outside an industrial building in Widnes Time of Incident: 11/07/2019 - 15:06 Location: Dennis Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Widnes and one from Lymm Shortly after 3pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire outside an industrial building on Dennis Road in Widnes. On arrival crews discovered a fire involving a pallet of alkaline and lithium batteries which had started to spread to a nearby industrial unit. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and removed a number of other nearby pallets. Crews also used a large fan to clear smoke from a nearby building. Firefighters were in attendance for approximately 45 minutes.
