 Chimney fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 04/02/2018 - 10:47 Location: Hale Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to a potential chimney fire in Hale Road in Widnes. Crews used a thermal imaging camera to identify the hot spot in the chimney. They then used tools to remove soot and  brick to prevent the heat build up in the chimney.
