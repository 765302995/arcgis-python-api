Car fire in Orford Time of Incident: 02/08/2017 - 16:22 Location: Collegiate Road, Orford, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters tackled a car fire on Collegiate Road. The crew, one firefighter in breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put the fire out and then made the vehicle safe. Police closed the road while the incident was dealt with.
