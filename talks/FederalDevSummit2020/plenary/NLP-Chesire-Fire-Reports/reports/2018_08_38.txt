 Tree fire in Checkley Time of Incident: 22/08/2018 - 18:01 Location: Checkley Lane, Checkley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to a tree on fire on Checkley Lane, Checkley. The fire had been caused by a lightening strike and had also set fire to 10m square of grass. Crews used two hose reel jets to put out the fire. Crews were detained for approximately 60 minutes.
