 One person rescued from a car in Chester Time of Incident: 15/04/2018 - 12:37 Location: Aldersey Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas and one from Ellesmere Port Road traffic collision in ChesterFire crews were called to a one vehicle road traffic collision on Aldersey Lane, Chester. Crews used stabilisation gear to rescue one person who was trapped in a saloon car. The casualty was then handed into the care of paramedics.
