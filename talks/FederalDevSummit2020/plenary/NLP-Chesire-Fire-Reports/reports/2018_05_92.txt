 House fire in Handforth Time of Incident: 18/05/2018 - 18:00 Location: Pickmere Road, Handforth. Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Wilmslow, one from Cheadle and one from Stockport. At approximately 6pm firefighters from Wilmslow, Cheadle and Stockport were called to reports of a house fire on Pickmere Road, Handforth. On arrival crews found that the fire involved a first floor bedroom of a maisonette property. The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. One person was treated at the scene for smoke inhalation, they were then taken to hospital as a precautionary measure. Crews remained at scene for just over one hour.
