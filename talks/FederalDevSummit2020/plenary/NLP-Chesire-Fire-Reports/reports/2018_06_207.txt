 Small fire, Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 08/06/2018 - 18:22 Location: Deans Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters responded to reports of a small rubbish fire on Deans Road, Ellesmere Port.  On arrival, crews found it involved a collection of household waste, including two exterior doors. Crews initially used hand appliances to extinguish the flames, but ended up using a single hose reel. The incident is thought to have been a deliberate ignition.
