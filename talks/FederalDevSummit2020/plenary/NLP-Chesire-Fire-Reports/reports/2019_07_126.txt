 Dryer fire at domestic property in Crewe Time of Incident: 10/07/2019 - 14:43 Location: Hargrave Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At around 2.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a dryer at a domestic property in Hargrave Avenue, Crewe. On arrival the crews discovered that the dryer was on the ground floor and everyone was accounted for. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and moved the dryer outside the property. They also administered oxygen to a casualty before handing them over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour.
