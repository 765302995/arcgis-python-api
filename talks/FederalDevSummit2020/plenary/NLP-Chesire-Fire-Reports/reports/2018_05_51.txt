 Uncontrolled fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/05/2018 - 20:39 Location: Norton Village Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Crews from Runcorn used a hose reel jet to tackle a fire on Norton Village Road. The fire had been started by a local resident, but it had become out of control. The team were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes.
