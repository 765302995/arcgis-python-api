 Fuel leak from a HGV in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/10/2019 - 07:27 Location: Winwick Road/Cromwell Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 7.30am firefighters were called to a report of a heavy goods vehicle leaking fuel at the junction of Winwick Road and Cromwell Avenue in Warrington.  On arrival the crew discovered that there was a small hole in the HGV's diesel tank.  The HGV had parked in a bus lane and a quantity of fuel had leaked onto the ground.  Firefighters used specialist paste to stem the leak.  The local authority and Highways England were informed of the incident.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
