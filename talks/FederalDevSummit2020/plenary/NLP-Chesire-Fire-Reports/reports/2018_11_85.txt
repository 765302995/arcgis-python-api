 Gas cylinder fire at a farm in Neston Time of Incident: 16/11/2018 - 16:16 Location: Liverpool Road, Neston Attendance: Three fire engine attended - one from Powey Lane, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Heswall At around 4.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a gas cylinder at a farm on Liverpool Road in Neston. On arrival crews discovered that it was a 20kg propane cylinder. A cordon was set up as firefighters worked at the scene. They used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control and cool the cylinder. Firefighters then left the remaining gas to burn off. They waited until the cylinder was empty before leaving the scene. Firefighters were in attendance for more than 45 minutes.
