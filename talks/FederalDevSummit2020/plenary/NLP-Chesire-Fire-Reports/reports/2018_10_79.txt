 False alarm in Middlewich Time of Incident: 19/10/2018 - 01:36 Location: Hightown, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich and one from Winsford Fire crews were called to a report of a fire in a ground floor flat in Hightown, Middlewich. Crews rescued one person from a first floor flat before discovering the fire was a false alarm due to a dry powder extinguisher being set off accidentally.
