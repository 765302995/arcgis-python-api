 Overheated lorry on the M56 Time of Incident: 13/08/2019 - 06:00 Location: M56, between junction 12 and 14. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn. One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port. Firefighters were called to an overheated lorry on the M56 between junction 12 and 14 westbound. Crews used a hose reel on the overheated engine to cool it down.
