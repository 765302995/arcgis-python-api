House fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 05/11/2016 - 21:38 Location: Dee Way, Winsford. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Winsford and Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Dee Way, Winsford.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire on the ground floor. Crews wearing breathing apparatus used hose reel jets to put the fire out.  An investigation has now been launched to find the cause of the blaze.
