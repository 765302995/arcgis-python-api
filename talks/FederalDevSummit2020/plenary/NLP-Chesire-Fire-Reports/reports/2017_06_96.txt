Fire at Winsford industrial property Time of Incident: 17/06/2017 - 01:53 Location: Road One, Winsford Industrial Estate Attendance: Fire engines attended from Winsford, Northwich, Holmes Chapel and Sandbach Workers at a plastics company on Winsford Industrial Estate were evacuated in the early hours of the morning as firefighters tackled a fire in a pump. Crews set up a cordon around the incident and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They were at the scene for two and three quarter hours.
