 Tree fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/07/2019 - 19:46 Location: Runcorn Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a tree fire on Runcorn Road, Runcorn. Nine conifers and the exterior of an outbuilding had been set fire accidentally after a small fire burning weeds spread. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.
