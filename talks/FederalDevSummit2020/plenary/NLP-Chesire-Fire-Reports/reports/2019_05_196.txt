 Large vehicle fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 03/05/2019 - 07:52 Location: Fiddlers Ferry Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one for Penketh attended Shortly before 8am firefighters from Lymm and Widnes were dispatched to reports of a HGV fire on Fiddlers Ferry Road.  On arrival crews discovered a wagon towing a digger which was well alight.  The team disconnected the tractor to prevent the fire from spreading. They then used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 30 minutes.
