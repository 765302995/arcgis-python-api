 Cooking fire in Chester Time of Incident: 28/09/2019 - 19:12 Location: Louise Street, Chester. CH1 4HP Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly after 7.10pm a fire crew was called to reports of a fire in a kitchen at a house on Louise Street.  The fire was out on arrival, but firefighters used a large fan to clear smoke from the house. Two women were handed to the care of the ambulance service suffering from smoke inhalation.
