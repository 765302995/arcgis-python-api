 Fire outside a residential address in Orford Time of Incident: 04/04/2019 - 00:04 Location: Cross Street, Orford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly after midnight firefighters from Warrington attended reports of a fire on Cross Street, Orford.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered that a for sale sign outside an unoccupied property had been deliberately set alight.  The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for around ten minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
