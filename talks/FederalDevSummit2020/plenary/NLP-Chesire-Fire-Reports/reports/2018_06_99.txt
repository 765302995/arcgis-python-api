 Vehicle fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 22/06/2018 - 22:09 Location: Field Lane, Wistaston. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefigters from Crewe were called to reports of a vehicle fire in Wistaston. On arrival the team found that the fire had begun in a wheelie bin and had spread to a nearby vehicle. The owner of the vehicle had used a garden hose to extinguish the car and firefighters used a hose reel jet to dampen down the scene. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
