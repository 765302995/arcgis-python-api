Overheated trailer brakes in Crewe Time of Incident: 10/05/2016 - 11:18 Location: A500, Crewe, close to Crewe Hall roundabout towards M6 Stoke Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters used a hose reel jet to cool brakes which had overheated on a trailer unit. A thermal imaging camera was then used to check there was no fire spread.
