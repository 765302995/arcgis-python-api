 Kitchen fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 04/03/2019 - 11:49 Location: Hanley Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Widnes and one from Runcorn Fire crews attended to reports of a kitchen on fire at a property on Hanley Road in Widnes.  Firefighters, wearing two breathing apparatus, used one hose reel to extinguish the fire involving cereal boxes next to the cooker.  Once the fire was out, a positive pressure ventilation fan was used to clear the smoke.  An occupier inside the property was checked over by paramedics after suffering from slight smoke inhalation.
