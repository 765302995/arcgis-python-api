 Fire involving an electrical socket at a house in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/12/2018 - 00:43 Location: Venns Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 12.45am firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a house on Venns Road in Warrington. The fire involving an electrical socket was out on arrival. Firefighters isolated the electricity supply to the house and used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property. They were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
