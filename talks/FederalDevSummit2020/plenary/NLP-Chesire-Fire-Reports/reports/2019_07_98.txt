 Car fire on M56 Time of Incident: 14/07/2019 - 19:51 Location: M56 eastbound between junction 15 to 14 Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a car fire on the M56 eastbound. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the blaze which was in the engine bay of the vehicle.
