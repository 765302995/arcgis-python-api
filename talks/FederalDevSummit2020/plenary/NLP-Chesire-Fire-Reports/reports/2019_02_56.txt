 Fire in a garden in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/02/2019 - 14:05 Location: Gerrard Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly after 2pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the back garden of a house on Gerrard Avenue, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning. Due to the size of the fire and safety concerns, the crew extinguished it with water. Firefighters were in attendance for less than 15 minutes.
