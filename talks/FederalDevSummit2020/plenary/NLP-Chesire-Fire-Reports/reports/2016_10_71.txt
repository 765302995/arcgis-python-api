Road traffic collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 19/10/2016 - 01:30 Location: Manchester Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich and one from Knutsford A car collided with a tree on Manchester Road, leaving one man trapped in the car.  The crews, working with ambulance staff, released the casualty who was then taken to hospital. Firefighters were at the scene for just over one hour.
