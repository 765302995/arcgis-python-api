Fire involving wooden trailer in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/10/2015 - 18:43 Location: Ewloe Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A wooden trailer filled with household and building waste was completely destroyed as a result of a fire in Ellesmere Port.  One hose reel was used to extinguish the blaze and a fire investigator was requested to attend the scene.
