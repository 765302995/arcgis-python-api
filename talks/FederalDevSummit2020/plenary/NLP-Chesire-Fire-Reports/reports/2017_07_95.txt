Timber on fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 09/07/2017 - 22:01 Location: Dean Row Road, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Timber was on fire in an outbuilding on Dean Row Road. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control.
