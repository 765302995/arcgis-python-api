 Grass fire in Chester Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 14:04 Location: Paddock Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich At around 2pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Paddock Road in Eccleston, Chester. On arrival the crew discovered a fire involving grass near to Eccleston Ferry. Firefighters extinguished the fire using water.
