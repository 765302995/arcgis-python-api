 Large diesel spillage in Congleton Time of Incident: 04/06/2019 - 10:45 Location: Rood Lane, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton At around 10.45am firefighters were called to a report of a spillage of petrol from a tanker on Rood Lane in Congleton. On arrival the crew discovered approximately 250 litres of diesel on the highway and that the road had been closed by the police. Firefighters used specialist paste to stop the leak and specialist mats to block off grids. However a quantity of the diesel had entered the drains. The Environment Agency was informed of this. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour. Highways England also attended to sand and grit the road.
