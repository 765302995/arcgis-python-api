 Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/05/2018 - 19:29 Location: North Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 7.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a car fire on the access road to North Road Industrial Estate in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered a saloon car well alight. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The crew were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
