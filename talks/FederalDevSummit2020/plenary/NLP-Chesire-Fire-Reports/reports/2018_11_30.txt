 Microwave fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 24/11/2018 - 17:55 Location: Harry Lawson Court, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Crews attended to a microwave on fire at a house on Harry Lawson Court in Macclesfield.  The small fire was put out by firefighters and the area was made safe.   The fire is believed to have been caused by burnt food.
