 Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 14/05/2019 - 07:06 Location: Stoneley Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly after 7am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a car on Stoneley Road in Crewe. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a saloon car's engine. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
