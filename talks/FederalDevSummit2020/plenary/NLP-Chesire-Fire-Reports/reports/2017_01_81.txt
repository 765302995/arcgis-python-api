Bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/01/2017 - 21:27 Location: Westy Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from attended from Stockton Heath and Warrington A fire believed to have been caused by a discarded cigarette broke out in a bin under a canopy outside a building in Warrington. The building was not affected although firefighters did use a thermal imaging camera to check for any remaining hot spots of fire.  Please take care when smoking. Discarded cigarettes can cause fires.
