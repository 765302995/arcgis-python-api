Car fire in Chester Time of Incident: 07/08/2016 - 15:09 Location: A55 Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester A car was on fire on the slip road of the Post House roundabout in Chester. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Police were asked to attend for traffic management as the road was blocked by the incident. Highways staff were asked to attend to clear a large oil spill from the road.
