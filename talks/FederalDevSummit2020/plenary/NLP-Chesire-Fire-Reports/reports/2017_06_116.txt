Phone mast fire in Great Sutton Time of Incident: 13/06/2017 - 00:53 Location: Sutton Way, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Sutton Way was closed as firefighters put out a fire involving a communications mast. A dry powder extinguisher was used and a thorough inspection of the mast with a thermal imaging camera was carried out to make sure there were no further hot spots that could reignite.
