 Small living room fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/04/2019 - 14:03 Location: Runcorn Road, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Warrington Fire crews attended to a small fire in the living room of an address on Runcorn Road, Runcorn.  Water was used to put out the fire which is believed to have involved paper and carpet.  A large fan was then used to clear the smoke from the property.
