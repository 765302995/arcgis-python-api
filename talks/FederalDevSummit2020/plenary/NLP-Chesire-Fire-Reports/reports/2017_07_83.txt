Rubbish fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/07/2017 - 19:02 Location: Cholmondeley Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a small fire on Cholmondeley Street, Widnes. On arrival crews found a rubbish fire which had been left unattended. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
