 Unattended fire in the open Time of Incident: 20/07/2018 - 22:55 Location: Eardswick Lane, Minshull Vernon Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe used one hose reel jet to extinguish an unattended rubbish fire off Eardswick Lane in Minshull Vernon. The team were at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.
