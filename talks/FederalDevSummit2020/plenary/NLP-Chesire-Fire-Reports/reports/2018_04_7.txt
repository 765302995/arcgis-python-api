 Fire in a takeaway in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/04/2018 - 21:08 Location: Bridge Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh, one from Warrington, one from Lymm and one from Widnes Fire in a takeaway in WarringtonFirefighters were called to a takeaway on Bridge Street, Warrington, after reports of a fire. Crews found that the takeaway was well alight on arrival and crews, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets, two main jets, a covering jet and a large fan to extinguish the fire.  Crews were in attendance for four hours. The fire is believed to have been started in a deep fat fryer.
