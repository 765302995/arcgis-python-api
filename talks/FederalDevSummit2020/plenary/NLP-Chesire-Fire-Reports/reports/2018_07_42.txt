 Two small fires in the open in Leighton Time of Incident: 26/07/2018 - 17:42 Location: Middlewich Road, Leighton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 5.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on Middlewich Road in Leighton. On arrival the crew discovered two small fires involving barbecue charcoal near Leighton Hospital and a fishing lake. Firefighters used water to put the fires out and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
