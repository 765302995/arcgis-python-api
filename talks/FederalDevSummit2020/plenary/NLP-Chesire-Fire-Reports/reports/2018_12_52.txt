 Flat fire in Neston Time of Incident: 18/12/2018 - 12:40 Location: Bridge Street, Neston Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Powey Lane and one from Heswall At around 12.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a flat on Bridge Street in Neston. On arrival crews found a small amount of paper on top of a radiator alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire and then dismantled the radiator. They used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour. The fire is believed to have been accidentally caused by smoking materials being left on top of the radiator. For fire safety tips visit Smoking / fire safety tips for smokers.
