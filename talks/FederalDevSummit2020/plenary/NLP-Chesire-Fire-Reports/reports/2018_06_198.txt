 Fire in a wooded area in Burtonwood Time of Incident: 11/06/2018 - 22:35 Location: Hall Lane, Burtonwood, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington used one hose reel jet to extinguish a fire in a wooded area off Hall Lane in Burtonwood. The crew were detained at the scene for approximately 10 minutes.
