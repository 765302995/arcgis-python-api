Bedroom fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 22/10/2015 - 07:21 Location: Ash Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich, one from Winsford and one from Chester. Firefighters were called to a fire in a bedroom in Northwich. The fire involved furniture and textiles and was extinguished. A large fan was then used to ventilate the property. One woman was taken to hospital by North West Ambulance Service and was already out of the property on the arrival of firefighters. It is believed that the fire was started accidentally.
