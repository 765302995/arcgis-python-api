Five cars set on fire in Latchford Time of Incident: 15/09/2017 - 02:20 Location: Clearwater Quays, Latchford Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Fire crews were called to a suspected arson attack involving five cars on Clearwater Quays, Latchford, Warrington. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fires. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police quoting incident number 67 of 15th September or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
