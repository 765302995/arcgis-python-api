Skip on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 14/03/2015 - 18:13 Location: The silk road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish a fire involving a skip at the rear of Arighi Bianchi & Co Ltd in Macclesfield. The skip was well alight.  One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
