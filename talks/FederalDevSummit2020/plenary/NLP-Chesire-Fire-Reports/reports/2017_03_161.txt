Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 02/03/2017 - 20:57 Location: Broad Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Deliberate ignition is suspected as the cause of a car fire on Broad Street. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
