 Grass fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 17/06/2018 - 22:37 Location: Arundel Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a grass fire on Arundel Court, Ellesmere Port. Approximately five square metres of grass was on fire and crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
