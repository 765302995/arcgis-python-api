 Fencing fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/09/2019 - 05:34 Location: Long Lane, Orford, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a school in Long Lane, Orford, Warrington after reports of a fire on the astroturf affecting fencing. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.
