 Vehicle smoking in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 21/09/2019 - 11:39 Location: Station Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Crews attended to reports of a smoke coming from a car's engine on Station Road in Wilmslow.  One hose reel was used to cool the engine believed to have been caused by the clutch overheating.    A thermal imaging camera was then used to check the battery.
