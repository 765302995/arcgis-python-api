Bedroom fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 30/03/2016 - 19:41 Location: Claremont Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Nantwich Two women were treated for slight smoke inhalation after a fire in the bedroom of a Crewe property. The fire was out when the crews arrived but it had left a lot of smoke in the house so a high pressure fan was used to ventilate and clear the smoke. Firefighters gave oxygen therapy to the two casualties who were then handed into the care of paramedics.
