Congleton chimney fire Time of Incident: 31/01/2016 - 16:48 Location: St Johns Road, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton A chimney fire in Congleton was attended by firefighters. Specialist chimney equipment was used to tackle the fire from the hearth and a full inspection of the house and loft space was carried out to make sure there were no further areas of fire.
