Road traffic collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/07/2015 - 14:39 Location: Dennis Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Two Widnes fire engines attended a road traffic collision involving two cars.  Firefighters released three casualties from the vehicles, who were handed in to the care of the ambulance.  They then made the scene safe.  A total of eight people travelling in both vehicles were taken to hospital.
