 Small kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 19/06/2019 - 18:02 Location: Harry Lawson Court, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington At around 6pm firefighters were dispatched to reports of a fire in a ground floor flat in Macclesfield.  On arrival the crew discovered a small fire in the kitchen area which had been caused by cooking.  Firefighters used a handheld appliance to extinguish the fire. They then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.  The crew were in attendance at the address for approximately 40 minutes.
