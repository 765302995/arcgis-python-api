 Large fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/11/2018 - 19:46 Location: Spring Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to a number of telegraph poles on fire in Spring Lane, Warrington. Crews used two hose reel jets to tackle the fire which was well alight and used a hose reel to damp down the area.
