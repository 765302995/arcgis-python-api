Kitchen fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 17/09/2017 - 12:23 Location: Crossfield Avenue, Lymm Attendance: Two fire engines from Birchwood and one from Irlam Firefighters were called to a kitchen fire on Crossfield Avenue, Lymm. Crews found that the fire was out on arrival of the crews which was caused by burnt cooking. Firefighters gave oxygen therapy to the occupant of the address. Crews were at the scene for just over an hour. Cooking safety tips - https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety
