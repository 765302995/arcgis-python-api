Woodland pit fire in Neston Time of Incident: 29/07/2017 - 18:16 Location: Haddon Lane, Neston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane A fire in a pit measuring 12 feet long by three feet deep was put out by firefighters. The crew had to make their way on foot into the woodland which was some distance from where they could get the fire engine to. They dug over the pit and used handheld extinguishers to put out the fire. The crew was at the scene for over an hour.
