Man rescued from farm machinery Time of Incident: 24/05/2015 - 12:05 Location: Back Lane, Smallwood. Attendance: Three fire engines and a major rescue unit were mobilised from Sandbach, Holmes Chapel and Winsford Firefighters responded to a report of a man trapped in farm machinery off Back Lane, Smallwood.  When they arrived crews found that a man had got his arm trapped in a potato harvester. Crews worked to release the casualty for nearly two hours. The man was released from the farm machinery and taken to hospital by air ambulance.
