 Bonfire in High Legh Time of Incident: 19/05/2018 - 22:56 Location: Swineyard Lane, High Legh. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington used one hose reel jet to extinguish a bonfire involving a large quantity of refuge which was well alight on Swineyard Lane, High Legh.  The cause of the fire is believed to be malicious and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
