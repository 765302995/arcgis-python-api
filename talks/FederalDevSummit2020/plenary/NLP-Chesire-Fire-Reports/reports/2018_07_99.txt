 Grass fire in Rudheath Time of Incident: 18/07/2018 - 19:34 Location: Pennys Lane, Rudheath Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters were called to a grass fire on Pennys Lane, Rudheath. The fire was approximately 40m by 20m and was well alight on arrival of the crews. They used two hose reel jets to put out the fire, which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police.
