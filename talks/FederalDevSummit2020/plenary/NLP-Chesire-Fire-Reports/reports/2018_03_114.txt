 One person rescued from a car in Widnes Time of Incident: 10/03/2018 - 17:27 Location: Fiddlers Ferry Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one from Widnes Fire crews were called to Fiddlers Ferry Road, Widnes, after a car crashed into a tree. Crews extracted one casualty from the vehicle and handed them into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for 50 minutes.
