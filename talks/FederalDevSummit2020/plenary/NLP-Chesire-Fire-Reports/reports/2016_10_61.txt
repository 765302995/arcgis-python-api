Road traffic collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 21/10/2016 - 16:12 Location: M56 east between junctions 9 and 10 Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington, Stockton Heath and Winsford Firefighters removed one man from a heavy goods vehicle following a road traffic collision and assisted paramedics with casualties. The incident involved two heavy goods vehicles and three cars. Police closed the road in both directions and the Air Ambulance attended.
