Dangerous structure in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 31/01/2015 - 15:25 Location: Marina Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A window was deemed unsafe by Police in a flat above Lloyds TSB, because the broken glass was hanging above a footpath.  Police set up a cordon on the footpath, while firefighters used a nine metre ladder to remove the broken glass and to make the window safe.
