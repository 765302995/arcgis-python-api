Unattended cooking in Congleton Time of Incident: 04/10/2015 - 15:14 Location: Woolston Avenue, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton An unattended pan caused smoke in a property resulting in firefighters being called out. There was no fire but the crew had to use a high pressure fan to clear the property of smoke.  Please never leave cooking unattended and always follow our Kitchen fire safety advice.
