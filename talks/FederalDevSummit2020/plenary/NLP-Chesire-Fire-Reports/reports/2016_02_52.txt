Road traffic collision on M6 northbound J17 to J18 Time of Incident: 18/02/2016 - 11:03 Location: M6 northbound junctions 17 to 18 Attendance: Fire engines from Sandbach, Knutsford and Crewe Emergency services have been dealing with a road traffic collision on M6 northbound between J17 and J18. Three saloon cars were involved. All vehicles have now been moved to the hard shoulder and all lanes northbound are fully open.
