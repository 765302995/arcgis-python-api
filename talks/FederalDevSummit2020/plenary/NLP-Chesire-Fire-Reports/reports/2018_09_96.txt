 Wheelie bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 10/09/2018 - 17:51 Location: Suffolk Avenue, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 5.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a wheelie bin being on fire in the open on Suffolk Avenue in Ellesmere Port.  The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which is believed to have been started by discarded smoking materials.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
