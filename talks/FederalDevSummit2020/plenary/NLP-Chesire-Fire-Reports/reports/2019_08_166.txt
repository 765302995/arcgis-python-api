 Basement flooded in Poynton Time of Incident: 01/08/2019 - 10:28 Location: London Road North, Poynton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters from Wilmslow used a water pump to remove water from the basement of a property on London Road North in Poynton.  The crew also isolated electrics at the property and were in attendance at the scene for an hour.
