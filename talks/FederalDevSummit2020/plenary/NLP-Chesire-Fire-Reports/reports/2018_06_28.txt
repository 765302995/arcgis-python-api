 RTC in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/06/2018 - 15:17 Location: A558, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane, Lymm and one from Runcorn Firefighters attended to a collision on the A558 in Warrington involving two vehicles.  Crews stabilised one of the vehicles and removed one of the doors to enable them to release the woman inside.   The woman was then handed over to the care of paramedics.
