Car fire on M56 Time of Incident: 21/03/2016 - 07:46 Location: M56 eastbound between junctions 12 and 14, near Frodsham and Halton Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A car on the hard shoulder of the M56 eastbound between junctions 14 and 12 was heavily involved in fire. Firefighters put out the fire using a hose reel jet and checked the vehicle for further heat areas using a thermal imaging camera. Police were asked to attend for traffic management.
