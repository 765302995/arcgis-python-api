 Garden fire, Crewe Time of Incident: 11/05/2019 - 01:55 Location: Heywood Green, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters turned out to a fire in a residential garden on Heywood Green, Crewe. There they found some garden furniture alight and used a hose reel to put the fire out. The property was vacant, so police were called on the basis that this was likely a deliberate ignition. They remained at the scene for a further half an hour to dampen down.
