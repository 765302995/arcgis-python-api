Road traffic collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 11/03/2016 - 17:32 Location: Hargreaves Road/Middlewich Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Two cars were involved in a road traffic collision in Middlewich Road. There was nobody trapped in either vehicle but one male casualty needed first aid from paramedics, assisted by firefighters. An ambulance was requested to take the casualty to hospital and police officers were also in attendance.
