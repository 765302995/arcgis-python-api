Cow rescued from Kermincham swimming pool Time of Incident: 27/09/2015 - 07:42 Location: Forty Acre Lane, Kermincham Attendance: The rapid response vehicle from Holmes Chapel and the animal rescue team with supporting fire engine from Knutsford The animal rescue team and firefighters were called to a report of a cow stuck in water in a swimming pool.  The crews began the rescue by pumping water out of the pool using a portable pump.  The farmer used machinery to build steps out of large hay bales as the animal rescue team got into the remaining 0.5 metres of water to help the animal back onto dry land.
