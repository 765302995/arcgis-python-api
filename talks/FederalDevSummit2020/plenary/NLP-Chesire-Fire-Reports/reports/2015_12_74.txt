Vehicle well alight in Sutton Time of Incident: 12/12/2015 - 01:34 Location: Hall Lane, Sutton Attendance: One fire engine from Sutton A vehicle was well alight on a verge on the side of the road. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  One hose reel was used to extinguish the fire.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately so if anyone has any information they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
