Man rescued after cooking left unattended in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 24/02/2015 - 04:09 Location: Oxford Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield A man who was asleep in an upstairs bedroom had to be woken by firefighters, after cooking he had left unattended caused smoke in a property, which set off a smoke alarm.  A neighbour heard the alarm sounding and dialed 999. On arrival the firefighters wore breathing apparatus and got into the property via the back door to turn the cooker off. They then went upstairs and found the man in bed.  A large fan was then used to remove smoke from the property. No one was injured in this incident. Police attended to manage traffic.
