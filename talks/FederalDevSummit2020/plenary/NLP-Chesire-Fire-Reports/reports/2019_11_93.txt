 Caravan fire at a farm in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 11/11/2019 - 08:07 Location: Newgate, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Shortly after 8am firefighters were called to a report of a caravan being on fire at a farm in Newgate, Wilmslow.  On arrival the crew discovered that the small caravan measuring approximately two metres by one metre had been completely destroyed by fire.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 562271, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
