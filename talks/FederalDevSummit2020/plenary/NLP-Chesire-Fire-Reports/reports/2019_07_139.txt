 Wood and wood chippings fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/07/2019 - 19:40 Location: Weston Link, Halton Lodge, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Runcorn and one from Widnes At around 7.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in the Weston Link area of Halton Lodge, Runcorn. On arrival crews found a large area of land containing wood and wood chippings well alight. A water supply was set up and firefighters fought the deep seated fire with a hose reel jet. Once the fire was out they damped the area down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 449605, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
