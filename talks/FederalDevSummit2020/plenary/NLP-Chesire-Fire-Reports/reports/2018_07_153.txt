 Hedge fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 12/07/2018 - 19:31 Location: Morreys Lane, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley Fire crews were called to a hedgerow and wood fire on Morreys Lane, Tarporley. Two tonnes of cut wood and 10m of hedgerow were on fire and crews used two hose reel jets and a thermal imaging camera to put out the fire.
