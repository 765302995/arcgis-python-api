Shop fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 20/03/2017 - 19:57 Location: Whitby Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters responded to a report of a fire on Whitby Road, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire in a shop. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used hose reel jets to put the fire out.  It is believed that this incident may have been started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
