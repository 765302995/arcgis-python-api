 Lorry collision in Chester Time of Incident: 19/10/2019 - 06:40 Location: Whitchurch Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester. One fire engine attended from Malpas. One fire engine attended from Winsford. Two fire engines from Malpas and Chester and a major rescue unit with support pump from Winsford attended a collision involving a lorry on Whitchuch Road, Broxton in Chester.   One casualty was trapped so firefighters used cutting equipment to remove the steering wheel enabling paramedics to release the man. Crews were in attendance for 40 minutes.
