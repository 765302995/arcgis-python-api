Van fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/02/2017 - 22:52 Location: Hazel Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A van fire in Hazel Avenue is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire which was in the engine compartment.  Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
