 Flooding at property in Great Warford Time of Incident: 26/10/2019 - 07:58 Location: Merrymans Lane, Great Warford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters attended to water entering a property on Merrymans Lane in Great Warford.  The water was beginning to flood after entering through air bricks.  Crews blocked the air bricks and began to clear the flood water.  Merryman's Lane has been closed in both directions for the safety of the fire crew.  Highways have been contacted to supply sandbags and assist with unblocking the drains.
