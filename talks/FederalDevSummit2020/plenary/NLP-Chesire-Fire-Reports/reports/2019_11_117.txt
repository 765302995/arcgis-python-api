 Vehicle trapped in flood water in Crewe Time of Incident: 07/11/2019 - 22:00 Location: Old School Lane, Hankelow, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Audlem Firefighters from Audlem attended reports of a car trapped in flood water on Old School Lane in Hankelow.  Crews attended the scene and found the vehicle stuck in water. Firefighters pushed the car out of the water and were in attendance for around 30 minutes.
