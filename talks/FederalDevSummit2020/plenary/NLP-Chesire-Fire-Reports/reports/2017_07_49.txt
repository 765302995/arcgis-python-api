Straw field on fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 18/07/2017 - 14:44 Location: Hollin Lane, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A straw field which was on fire and well alight was extinguished with two hose reels and a main water jet by firefighters. The area measured approximately 200 x 100 metres and a nearby water supply was established to feed the hoses.
