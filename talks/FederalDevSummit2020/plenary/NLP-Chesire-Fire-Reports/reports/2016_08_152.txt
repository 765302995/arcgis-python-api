Kitchen fire in Chester Time of Incident: 07/08/2016 - 05:26 Location: Lache Hall Crescent, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a kitchen fire in Lache Hall Crescent. The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A casualty was given oxygen therapy by firefighters and an ambulance was at the scene.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice.
