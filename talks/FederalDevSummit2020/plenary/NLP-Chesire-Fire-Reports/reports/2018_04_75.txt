 Chimney fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/04/2018 - 20:00 Location: Ledsham Hall Lane, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Firefighters from Powey Lane attended reports of a chimney fire at an address in Ellesmere Port.  On arrival at the scene crews first extinguished the fire via the hearth before using a thermal imaging camera to check the temperature on the chimney breast.  After extinguishing the fire, the crew advised the occupant to seek advice from a qualified professional before relighting the fire. The team were in attendance for approximately 45 minutes.
