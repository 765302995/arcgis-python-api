 Small fire in woodland in Northwich Time of Incident: 13/04/2019 - 20:43 Location: Marbury Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 8.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in woodland on Marbury Lane in Northwich. The crew used water to extinguish the small fire in the open and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
