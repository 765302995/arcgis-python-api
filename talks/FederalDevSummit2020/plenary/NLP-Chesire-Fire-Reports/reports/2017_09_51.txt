Road traffic collision on M53 J10 northbound Time of Incident: 16/09/2017 - 22:46 Location: M53 northbound, junction 10 near Cheshire Oaks Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn, Powey Lane, Ellesmere Port (2)and Chester Two vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision on the M53 close to Cheshire Oaks. One casualty was released from one of the cars by firefighters and four other casualties were treated for minor injuries at the scene by fire crews. All the casualties were then taken by ambulance to the Countess of Chester hospital. Firefighters were at the scene for an hour and a half.
