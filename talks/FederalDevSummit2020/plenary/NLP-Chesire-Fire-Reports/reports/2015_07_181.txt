Bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 03/07/2015 - 19:29 Location: Seacombe Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters extinguished a bin fire in a park in Ellesmere Port believed to have been started deliberately.  Police were requested to attend the scene. Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
