Road traffic collision on M6 junctions j20 to 19 southbound Time of Incident: 13/05/2016 - 19:07 Location: M6 junctions 20 to 19 southbound Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Warrington Two vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision on the M6 between junctions 20 and 19 southbound. Nobody was trapped in either vehicle but there were two casualties, a man and a woman, who were treated by firefighters and ambulance crews. Crews made the scene safe.
