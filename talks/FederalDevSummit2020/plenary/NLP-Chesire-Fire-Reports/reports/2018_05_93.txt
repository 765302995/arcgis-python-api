 Fire involving builders waste in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/05/2018 - 13:06 Location: Moss Lane, Moore,Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly after 1pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire behind Moore RUFC on Moss Lane in Moore. On arrival at the scene crews found that the fire involved approximately two tonnes of builders waste and wood chippings which were well alight. The team used a hose reel jet and hand tools to extinguish the fire. Crews then spent approximately 20 minutes damping down the scene. Firefighters were in attendance for approximately one hour.
