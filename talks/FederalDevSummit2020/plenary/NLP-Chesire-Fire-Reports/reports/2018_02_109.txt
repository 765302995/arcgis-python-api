 Chip pan fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/02/2018 - 19:35 Location: Hyde Close, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended a chip pan fire which was out on arrival. There was no damage to the property, but one casualty was suffering flash burns. She was given first aid by firefighters until arrival of ambulance.
