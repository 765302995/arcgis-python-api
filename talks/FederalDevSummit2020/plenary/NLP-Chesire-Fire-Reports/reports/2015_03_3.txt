Grill pan fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 30/03/2015 - 03:05 Location: Festival Way, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn A man was given oxygen due to smoke inhalation after a grill pan fire in Runcorn. The fire was out on arrival of the crews who used a large fan to clear the smoke. Smoke detectors were fitted and did activate. Safety advice for grills
