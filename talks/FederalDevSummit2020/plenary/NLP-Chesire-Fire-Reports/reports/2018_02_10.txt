 Van fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 26/02/2018 - 11:52 Location: Silk Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters attended a transit tipper van on fire in Silk Road, Macclesfield. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire which was in the rear of the van.
