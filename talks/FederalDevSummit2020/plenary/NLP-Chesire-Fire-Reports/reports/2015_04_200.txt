People evacuated following a fire at the Wagon and Horses in Eaton Time of Incident: 04/04/2015 - 01:52 Location: Wagon and Horses, Manchester road, Eaton Attendance: Three fire engines from congleton and macclesfield Three fire engines from Congleton and Macclesfield attended a fire involving a shower unit inside the Wagon and Horses on Manchester Road in Eaton. Firefighters used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel and a large fan to bring the fire under control. The premises was also evacuated.
