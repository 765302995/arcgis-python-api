Car collides with lamp post in Handforth Time of Incident: 02/03/2016 - 08:29 Location: A34 between Handforth and Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow One car was involved in a road traffic collision with a lamp post on the A34 at Handforth. Three people were in the car, with one male casualty taken to hospital by ambulance as a precaution.   Firefighters made the vehicle and scene safe as there were exposed cables from the lamp post.
