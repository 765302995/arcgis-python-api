Grass fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/07/2016 - 20:41 Location: Taylor Industrial Estate, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Arson is suspected as the cause of a grass fire in Warrington. Firefighters used a water backpack to put out the fire and are urging anyone with any information as to who may have started this fire to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
