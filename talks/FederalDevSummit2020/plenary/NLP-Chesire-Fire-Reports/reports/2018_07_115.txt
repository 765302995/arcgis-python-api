 Fire in the open in Congleton Time of Incident: 17/07/2018 - 06:28 Location: Dane Street, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters used water to extinguish a small fire on involving bushes near to the river Dane, off Dane Street, in Congleton. The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately 15 minutes. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting  IML126293; information can also be reported anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111.
