 Grass fire in Bradwall Time of Incident: 04/07/2018 - 13:12 Location: Walnut Tree Lane, Bradwall Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to Walnut Tree Lane, Bradwall, after a reported grass fire. the fire was approximately 30 square metres and had spread to trees and a hedge on the edge of the field. Crews used two hose reel jets and beaters to extinguish the fire.
