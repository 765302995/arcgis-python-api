 Tumble dryer fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 18/01/2019 - 19:45 Location: Barony Road, Nantwich Attendance:  Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters attended to a tumble dryer on fire on the ground floor of a house on Barony Road in Nantwich.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, one hose reel and a positive pressure ventilation fan was used to put out the fire and extinguish the smoke.  Crews were detained for approximately fifty minutes.
