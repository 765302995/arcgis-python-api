Fire in commercial building in Middlewich Time of Incident: 15/01/2017 - 08:40 Location: St Anns Road, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Winsford Firefighters were called to a fire involving an ironing press machine inside a commercial building and used a hose reel and a covering jet to extinguish the fire. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the building for fire spread and paramedics attended to check one casualty who was suspected of suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation.
