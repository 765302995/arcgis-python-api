Fire in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 08/06/2015 - 14:14 Location: Highlands Road, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn 100 square metres of dry scrubland which was alight needed extinguishing with a hose reel.  There were several seats of fire at this incident and fire beaters were also used by the crew to put the flames out.
