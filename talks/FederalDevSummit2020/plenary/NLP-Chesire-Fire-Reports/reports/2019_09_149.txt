 Skip fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 03/09/2019 - 03:39 Location: Caernavon Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port used a hose reel jet to extinguish the contents of a skip which had been deliberately set on fire outside an address on Caernavon Court.  The crew were detained at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.  Anyone with any information in relation to the fire is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101
