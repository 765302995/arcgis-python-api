Motorbike fire in Neston Time of Incident: 04/12/2017 - 15:57 Location: Manorial Road, Neston. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a motorbike which had been deliberately set on fire on Manorial Road, Neston. Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
