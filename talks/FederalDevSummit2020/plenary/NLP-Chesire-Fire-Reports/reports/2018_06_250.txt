 Small fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/06/2018 - 15:34 Location: Greenwich Avenue, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. One fire engine from Widnes. Firefighters were called to an address in Greenwich Avenue, Widnes following reports of a fire involving a rotavator and petrol. The occupant had been decanting petrol from the machine into a bucket when it caught light. The fire was out on arrival but had caused damage to a meter box and smoke damage to a gable end. Fire crews used a hose reel to cool down the side of the house and swill away residual petrol.
