Fire in disused building in Northwich Time of Incident: 05/02/2017 - 14:45 Location: Heath Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A hose reel jet was used by firefighters to put out a small fire in a disused building off Manchester Road. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
