Fire involving an allotment outbuilding in Rixton Time of Incident: 27/08/2017 - 09:20 Location: Chapel Lane, Rixton Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Irlam (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) There was a fire involving an allotment outbuilding. The fire spread to some trees.  Firefighters used two hose reel jets to tackle the fire. An ambulance was requested to check one casualty, who was given the all clear by paramedics.
