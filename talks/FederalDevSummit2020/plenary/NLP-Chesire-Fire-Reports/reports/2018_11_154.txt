 Vehicle fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/11/2018 - 19:43 Location: Rother Drive, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port used two hose reel jets and one breathing apparatus to extinguish two saloon cars and a transit van which were well alight on Rother Drive, Ellesmere Port. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
