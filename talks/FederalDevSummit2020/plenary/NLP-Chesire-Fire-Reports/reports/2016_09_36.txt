Bucket fire in Chester Time of Incident: 24/09/2016 - 00:00 Location: Raymond Street in Chester Attendance: Fire engines from Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property off Raymond Street in Chester.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire involving a bucket on the balcony of the property. This was out and had been caused by a discarded cigarette.  Crews carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving the scene.
