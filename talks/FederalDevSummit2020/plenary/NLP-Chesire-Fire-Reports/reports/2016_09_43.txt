Road traffic collision in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 22/09/2016 - 14:15 Location: Prestbury Road, Wilmslow Attendance: Two fire engines from Cheadle and Wilmslow Firefighters dealt with a road traffic collision involving two vehicles. One woman was reported to be trapped whilst two others were injured. Firefighters have released one woman from the vehicle before handing her into the care of the air ambulance.
