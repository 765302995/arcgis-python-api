 Chair in derelict building on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 30/05/2019 - 12:57 Location: Mirion Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Fire crews attended to a fire at a derelict building on Mirion Street in Crewe.  The fire involved a smouldering office chair and a hose reel was used to dampen it down.
