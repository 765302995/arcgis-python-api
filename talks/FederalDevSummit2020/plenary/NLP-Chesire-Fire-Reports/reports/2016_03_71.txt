Chip pan fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 17/03/2016 - 19:45 Location: Mirion Street, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Fire crews were called to a report of a house fire on Mirion Street, Crewe.  When they arrived crews were confronted with a blaze in the kitchen of the property. Main water jets and hose reels were used to put the fire out. It is believed to have been caused by a chip pan.  Smoke alarms had manage to alert the homeowners to the fire and so thankfully there were no reports of any injuries.  Please take care when cooking and follow this advice: Chip pan safety advice
