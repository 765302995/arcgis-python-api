Collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/09/2015 - 17:16 Location: Central Expressway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters gave oxygen to a passenger involved in a collision in Runcorn.  The crew responded to a reported car fire but on arrival there was no fire however there had been a collision involving a number of vehicles.   No one was trapped and the crew made the vehicles safe before leaving.
