 Small fire at a flat in Great Boughton, near Chester Time of Incident: 29/08/2019 - 19:20 Location: Vicars Cross Road, Great Boughton, near Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At 7.20pm firefighters were called to reports of a smoke alarm sounding at a flat on Vicars Cross Road in Great Boughton, Chester. On arrival the crew discovered that the alarm had been activated as a result of a small fire involving a hob in the kitchen. The fire was out on arrival and everyone was accounted for. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke that the fire had caused. They also silenced the alarm. Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
