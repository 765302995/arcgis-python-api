 Bush and tree fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 31/03/2019 - 21:52 Location: Weston Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Widnes Crews were called to a large fire involving trees and bushes along Weston Road and Sandy Lane, in Runcorn. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and a 70ml hose to put out the fire. Crews then checked the shrubbery using a thermal imaging camera. Crews were in attendance for an hour and 30 minutes.
