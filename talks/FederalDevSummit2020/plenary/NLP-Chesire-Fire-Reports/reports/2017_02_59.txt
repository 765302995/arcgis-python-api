Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 20/02/2017 - 22:31 Location: Stewart Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A car fire in Crewe is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and police were contacted.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
