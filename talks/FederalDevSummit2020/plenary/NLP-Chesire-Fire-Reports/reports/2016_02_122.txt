Bedroom fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 31/01/2016 - 11:05 Location: Landseer Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Warrington A fire in a light fitting caused light smouldering in a bedroom. Firefighters isolated the electricity supply and checked the house, including the loft space, using a thermal imaging camera to check there were no further areas of fire.
