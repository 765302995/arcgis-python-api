Road closed after wind damages a roof Time of Incident: 23/02/2017 - 12:17 Location: Edleston Road in Crewe Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe Firefighters responded to reports that tiles were blowing from a roof on Edleston Road in Crewe.  Crews set up a safety cordon and worked with Cheshire Police as they closed the road to prevent anybody from getting hurt.
