Two bins deliberately set on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 10/08/2016 - 08:04 Location: Castlefields Avenue South, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Two refuse bins which were on fire 100 metres apart required firefighters to attend and used one hose reel to extinguish them. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. Wheelie Bin - Fire Safety advice
