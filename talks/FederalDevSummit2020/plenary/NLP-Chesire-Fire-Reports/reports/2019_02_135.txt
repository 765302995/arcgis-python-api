 Caravan fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 03/02/2019 - 18:01 Location: Marston Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and one from Lymm Fire crews were called to a caravan fire on Marston Lane, Northwich. The caravan was well alight on arrival of the crews and the fire had spread to an adjoining garage. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a main jet and two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. Crews were in attendance for two hours.
