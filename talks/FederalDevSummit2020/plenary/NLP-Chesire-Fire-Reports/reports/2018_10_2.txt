 Leaves on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 30/10/2018 - 13:24 Location: Abbey Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Fire crews were called to Abbey Lane, Northwich, after a report of a fire involving a large quantity of leaves. The fire, which was a controlled burning, was causing smoke to drift into the roadway so crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish it.
