Chimney fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/07/2015 - 12:34 Location: Crewe Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters have been tackling a chimney fire in Crewe Road, Crewe. They put out the fire using specialist chimney equiment and then used a thermal imaging camera to check for further hot spots.
