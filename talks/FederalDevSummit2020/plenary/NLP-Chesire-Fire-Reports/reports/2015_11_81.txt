Three car collision in Sandbach Time of Incident: 14/11/2015 - 06:37 Location: Wheelock Bypass, Sandbach. Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to report of a collision on the Wheelock Bypass, Sandbach.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved three cars. Two men were trapped in the cars.  Firefighters used special cutting equipment to free the men who were then passed to the care of the Ambulance Service.
