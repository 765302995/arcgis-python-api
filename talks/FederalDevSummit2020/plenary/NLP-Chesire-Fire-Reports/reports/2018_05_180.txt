 Car fire in Altrincham Time of Incident: 08/05/2018 - 14:43 Location: Chester Road, Altrincham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm and two from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Car fire in AltrinchamFire crews were called to a vehicle fire on Chester Road, Altrincham. Crews found a car well alight on arrival and they used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
