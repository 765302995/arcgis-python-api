Small fire in an empty building Time of Incident: 17/09/2016 - 14:42 Location: Bewsey Farm Close, Burtonwood and Westbrook in Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Fire crews responded to a report of a building fire on Bewsey Farm Close, Burtonwood and Westbrook in Warrington.  When they arrived they found a small rubbish fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.
