Building fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 07/09/2015 - 19:17 Location: Withyfold Drive, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a building on Withyfold Drive, Macclesfield.  When they arrived crews found that there was an amount of wood on fire in the empty factory. Crews used water backpacks to put the fire out.
