 Industrial estate fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/10/2018 - 09:37 Location: Ditton Road (east), Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one from Winsford Firefighters attended to reports of a fire at an industrial estate on Ditton Road in Widnes.  Although the fire was out on arrival crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, used dry powder to make sure it was extinguished and then checked the area to ensure it was safe.
