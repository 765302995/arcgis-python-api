Motorbike accident in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/09/2015 - 21:20 Location: Broad Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath Firefighters have assisted North West Ambulance Service in responding to a motorbike collision. Stockton Heath firefighters were mobilised to the collision involving a single motorbike and assisted paramedics by removing barbed wire fencing so that they could access the casualty who had been ejected into a neighbouring field.
