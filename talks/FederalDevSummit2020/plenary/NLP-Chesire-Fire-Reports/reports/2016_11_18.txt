Rubbish fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/11/2016 - 21:24 Location: Wright Crescent, Widnes. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Widnes and Runcorn. Fire crews were called to a report of a building fire on Wright Crescent, Widnes.  When they arrived they found that the fire involved a small amount of rubbish against an empty building.  Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish the fire.
