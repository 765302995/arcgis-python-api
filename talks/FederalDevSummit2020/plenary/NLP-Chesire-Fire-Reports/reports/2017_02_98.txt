Collision in Alsager Time of Incident: 13/02/2017 - 19:47 Location: Crewe Road in Alsager. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Crewe and Sandbach Fire crews responded to a report of a collision on Crewe Road in Alsager.  When they arrived they found that the incident involved two cars and one was on its roof. Nobody was trapped and so crews made the scene safe before leaving.
