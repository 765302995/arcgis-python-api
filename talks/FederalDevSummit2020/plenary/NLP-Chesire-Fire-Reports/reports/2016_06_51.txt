Large vehicle fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 21/06/2016 - 07:33 Location: M6 junction 17 to junction 16, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and one from Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called to a large vehicle fire in Sandbach on the M6, junction 17 near to a service station. The brakes on the trailer had caught fire due to liquid oxygen. On arrival, crews used one hose reel jet to cool the brakes.
