 RTC on M6 southbound Time of Incident: 13/06/2018 - 11:21 Location: M6 South link to M56 West notificationsAttendance: Firefighters attended to a road traffic collision on the M6 southbound on the slip road to the M56 westbound.  The collision involved one heavy goods vehicle and a car, fire crews extricated one casualty out of the car and handed them into the care of paramedics.
