Flat fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 14/09/2017 - 16:40 Location: Mill Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Fire crews were called to a flat on Mill Street, Crewe, after reports of a fire. Firefighters found that a cooking fire was out on arrival and they used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. Crews were in attendance for 30 minutes. More information about kitchen safety is available here - https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety
