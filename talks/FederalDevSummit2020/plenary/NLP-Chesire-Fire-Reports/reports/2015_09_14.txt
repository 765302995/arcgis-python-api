Kitchen fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/09/2015 - 12:32 Location: Handforth Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes One man was treated by paramedics for smoke inhalation after a kitchen fire in Runcorn.  One hose reel was used by firefighters wearing breathing apparatus to extinguish the blaze.
