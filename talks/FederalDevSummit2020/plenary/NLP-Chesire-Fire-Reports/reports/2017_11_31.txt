Two vehicle collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 19/11/2017 - 17:34 Location: Lees Lane, macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow. One fire engine from Poynton. Firefighters were called to a two vehicle collision in Lees Lane, Macclesfield. One of the four casualties was trapped in a car. Crews used cutting equipment to free the casualty.
