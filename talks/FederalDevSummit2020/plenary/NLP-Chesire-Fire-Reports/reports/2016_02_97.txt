Chimney fire in Goostrey Time of Incident: 07/02/2016 - 08:33 Location: Bank View, Goostrey Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel A chimney fire in Goostrey was attended by a crew from Holmes Chapel. The fire was found in a concealed chimney void. Firefighters cut away the external wall to reach the fire and used a chimney hose to put it out.  A thermal imaging camera was then used to check there were no further hot spots.  Please follow our chimney safety advice - Chimneys
