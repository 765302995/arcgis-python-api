Conveyor belt on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 17/03/2016 - 22:00 Location: Wincham Avenue, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a fire involving a conveyor belt in an industrial building in Northwich and used a hose reel to extinguish it. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the area for hot spots.
