 Two-vehicle collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/05/2019 - 16:07 Location: Junction of Great King Street and Catherine Street, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield Shortly after 4pm firefighters were called to a report of a two-vehicle collision at the junction of Great King Street and Catherine Street in Macclesfield. On arrival crews discovered that a man was trapped in one of the vehicles. Firefighters moved the other vehicle away from his door to enable him to get out of his car. Once he had done so the casualty was handed over to the care of paramedics. The road was closed by police as emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
