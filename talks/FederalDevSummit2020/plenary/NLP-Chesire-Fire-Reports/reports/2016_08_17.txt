Collision in Tarporley Time of Incident: 27/08/2016 - 23:41 Location: High Street in Tarporley. Attendance: Fire engines from Tarporley and Chester  Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on High Street in Tarporley.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved a car that had overturned. One man had managed to get out of the car before the crews arrived. Thankfully he was not seriously hurt although he was taken to hospital for a full check up.  Crews made the scene safe before leaving.
