 Compost fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 21/06/2018 - 11:20 Location: Warren Avenue, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At 11:20am firefighters were called to reports of a garden fire at an address on Warren Avenue. On arrival at the scene crews found that the fire involve a small compost heap. The team used a rake to turn over the compost before extinguishing the fire with water.
