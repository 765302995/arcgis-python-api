House fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 19/10/2017 - 21:36 Location: Lanark Walk, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a fire in a house in Lanark Walk, Macclesfield. The fire was located to a snake tank and was out on arrival of the crews. They used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.
