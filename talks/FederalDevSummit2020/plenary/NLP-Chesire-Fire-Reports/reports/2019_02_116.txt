 Skip fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/02/2019 - 13:09 Location: Greenalls Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At approximately 1.10pm firefighters from Warrington attended reports of a fire in the open on Greenalls Avenue.  On arrival the crew found an industrial-style skip well alight.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for approximately 15 minutes.  The fire was ignited accidentally.
