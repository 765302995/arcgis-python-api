Cooker fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 21/06/2015 - 13:51 Location: Wingfield Place, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich A fire involving a cooker and the kitchen floor was attended by firefighters.  The fire was in a cooker and heat spread through to the kitchen floorboards.  Crews put the fire out and damped down the area.
