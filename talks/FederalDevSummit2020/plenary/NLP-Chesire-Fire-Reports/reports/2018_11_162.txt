 Large bonfire in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/11/2018 - 02:16 Location: Penkford Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to a large bonfire which was out of control on Penkford Lane, Warrington. The fire was approximately 10 metres from a railway line and crews used a hose reel jet and a main jet to extinguish the fire.
