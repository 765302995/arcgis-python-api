 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 18:30 Location: Clifford Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters from Widnes attended reports of a grill pan on fire at an address on Clifford Road, Warrington. the fire was out on arrival, however crews ventilated the property and used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. The team were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
