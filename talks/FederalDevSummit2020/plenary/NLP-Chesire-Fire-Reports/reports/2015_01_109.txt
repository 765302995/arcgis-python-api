House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/01/2015 - 15:56 Location: Gladstone Street, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called a report of a hose fire on Gladstone Street, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a small fire in an electrical fuse box. This was already out when they arrived. Crews carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving the scene.
