 Bonfire in Warrington park Time of Incident: 27/10/2018 - 16:56 Location: Knutsford Road, Latchford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews in Warrington were called to reports of a bonfire in a park on Knutsford Road in Latchford.  When crews attended Victoria Park they used one extended hose reel to extinguish the fire and made the area safe.
