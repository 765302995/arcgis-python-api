 Fire in the open involving rubbish in Runcorn Time of Incident: 25/04/2019 - 21:01 Location: Grangeway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 9pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in Grangeway, Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved rubbish. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire in the open and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
