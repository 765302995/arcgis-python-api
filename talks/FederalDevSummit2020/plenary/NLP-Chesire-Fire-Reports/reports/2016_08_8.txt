Road traffic collision in Middlewich Time of Incident: 29/08/2016 - 15:38 Location: Warmingham Lane, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Winsford Two cars were involved in a road traffic collision on Warmingham Lane. There were no casualties trapped in either vehicle. Firefighters made the scene safe. Two people involved in the collision were taken to hospital for a precautionary check. Police were also in attendance.
