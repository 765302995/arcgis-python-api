 Fencing on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/05/2018 - 18:47 Location: Hume Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to a fire on some fencing by a railway embankment on Hume Street, Warrington. Crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information then please call Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
