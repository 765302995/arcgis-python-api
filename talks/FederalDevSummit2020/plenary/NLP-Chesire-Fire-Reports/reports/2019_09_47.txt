 Tree fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 21/09/2019 - 15:07 Location: Jubilee Gardens, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Crews attended to a fire involving a tree on Jubilee Gardens in Nantwich.  On arrival, the fire was already out however crews used a hose reel jet to fully ensure the area was safe and checked for hot spots using the thermal imaging camera.
