House fire in Bulkeley Time of Incident: 25/12/2016 - 02:27 Location: Wrexham Road, Bulkeley Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley and one from Malpas A fire involving logs near to a wood burning stove was put out by firefighters. Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus sets used one hose reel, a safety jet and cooling jets to tackle the fire. A high pressure fan was used to ventilate the property once the fire had been extinguished. A lady suffering smoke inhalation received oxygen therapy from firefighters and was then taken to hospital as a precaution.
