Unattended cooking fills Northwich kitchen with smoke Time of Incident: 10/07/2016 - 12:31 Location: Heathcote Gardens, Rudheath, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Unattended cooking left the kitchen of a house in Rudheath full of smoke. Firefighters had to gain entry to the house as there was nobody in. They took a pan left on the hob outside and cleared the smoke using a high pressure fan.  Please, never leave cooking unattended, always follow our kitchen safety advice
