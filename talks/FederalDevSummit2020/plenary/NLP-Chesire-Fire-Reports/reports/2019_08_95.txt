 Two-vehicle collision on M56 Time of Incident: 16/08/2019 - 13:38 Location: M56 eastbound, between junctions 12 and 11 Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Runcorn, one from Widnes and one from Penketh Firefighters attended a collision involving two heavy goods vehicles on the M56 eastbound, between junctions 12 and 11. On arrival crews found a man trapped inside one of the vehicles but he was able to get himself out of it after being assessed by a doctor and was then handed over to the care of paramedics. Lane one of the motorway was closed following the collision. Firefighters checked both HGVs and made them safe. They were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.
