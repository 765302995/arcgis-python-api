Compost heap on fire in Over Alderley Time of Incident: 16/06/2017 - 11:15 Location: Hocker Lane, Over Alderley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to a compost heap which was on fire on Hocker Lane, Over Alderley. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to put out the blaze.
