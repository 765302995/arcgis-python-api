 Two people rescued from a car in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/05/2018 - 10:29 Location: Dalton Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood, one from Warrington, one from Widnes, one from Penketh, one from Lymm and the aerial ladder platform from Lymm. Two people rescued from a car in WarringtonFire crews were called to a road traffic collision on Dalton Avenue, Warrington. A car had crashed into the pond and crews had to use stabilisation equipment to assist paramedics in removing two casualties from the vehicle.
