 Fire in a garden in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 17/05/2018 - 20:43 Location: Luton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 8.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the back garden of a house on Luton Road in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a couch. Firefighters extinguished it with a hose reel jet and then damped the area down. They were in attendance for around 20 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information about the fire should call Cheshire Police on 101.
