 Fire in the open in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 17/07/2018 - 06:15 Location: Alnwick Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters used a hose reel jet to tackle a fire off Alnwick  Drive in Ellesmere Port. The fire involved a small quantity of rubbish in an alleyway near to an unused public house.  Crews were at the scene for approximately 15 minutes.
