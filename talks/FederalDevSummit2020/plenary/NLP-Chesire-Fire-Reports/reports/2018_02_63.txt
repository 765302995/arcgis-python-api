 Small fire at a car park in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/02/2018 - 20:38 Location: Harpers Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Crews attended to a small fire involving tyres on a car park at Harpers Road in Warrington last night (16 February at 8.40pm).
