 Unexploded firework in Northwich Time of Incident: 21/05/2018 - 01:30 Location: Meadow Drive, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters were called to Meadow Drive, Northwich, after a report of an unexploded firework being left near to a fire. Crews found a plastic chair had been set alight by fireworks and used a hose reel jet to extinguish it. Police were called to attend.
