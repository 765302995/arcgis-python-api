 Electrical fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 28/07/2019 - 18:39 Location: The Old Glove factory, Pepper Street, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Nantwich Shortly after 6.30pm firefighters from Nantwich were called to reports of a fire at the Old Glove Factory in Nantwich.  On arrival at the scene the crew discovered a fire in an electrical cupboard.  Firefighters used breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, they then used a thermal imaging camera to monitor the temperature.  The crews were in attendance for approximately five hours.
