Winsford children's playground fire Time of Incident: 19/11/2016 - 20:51 Location: Saxon Crossway, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters responded to a report of a fire in a children's playground on Saxon Crossway. Handheld extinguishers were used to put out the fire and damp the area down. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
