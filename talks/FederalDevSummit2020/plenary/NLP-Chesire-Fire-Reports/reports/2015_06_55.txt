Boiler room fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 20/06/2015 - 05:05 Location: Peel House Lane, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Peel House, Peel House Lane, Widnes.  When they arrived crews found a fire in a boiler room. Crews used hose reel and main covering jets to deal with the fire. A special fan was also used to clear the smoke.  One man was treated by firefighters and the ambulance service for the effects of inhaling smoke.
