 Chimney fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/03/2019 - 17:41 Location: Mill Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to a chimney fire at an address in Mill Avenue in Warrington. On arrival crews discovered the fire was out. They used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and then applied water to the wood burner to wet steam the flue. Crews used a ridge camera to monitor the flow from the flue and ensured the wood burner and chimney were safe.
