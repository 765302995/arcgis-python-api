 Tiled chimney fire in Bollington Time of Incident: 01/04/2019 - 15:18 Location: Heath Road, Bollington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Bollington and one from Macclesfield At around 3.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a tiled chimney fire on Heath Road in Bollington. The crews used chimney rods and a hose reel jet to fight the fire from inside the property. Assessing the fire from the grate and later putting water down the chimney from the roof, aided by working at height safety equipment, the crews were able to bring it under control. Firefighters also assessed the roof space, using a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. When the fire was out at around 5.25pm firefighters continued to monitor temperatures to make sure that the property was safe. They were in attendance for more than two-and-a-half hours.  For tips on avoiding a chimney fire visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chimneys.
