 House fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 20/01/2019 - 05:34 Location: Church Road, Northwich Attendance:  Six fire engines attended - two from Northwich, one from Middlewich, one from Winsford and two from Lymm Firefighters attended to reports of a fire in the roof of a house on Church Road in Northwich.  Crews, wearing four breathing apparatus, used two hose reels to help tackle the fire and bring it under control.  Once the fire was extinguished, firefighters looked out for hot spots within the loft before using cutting equipment to access the roof space and remove the loft insulation.  The fire is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault.  Everyone inside the property was accounted for.
