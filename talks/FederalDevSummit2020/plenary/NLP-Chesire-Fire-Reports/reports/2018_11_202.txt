 Trolley fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 31/10/2018 - 20:59 Location: Vernon Way, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to  a fire near the shopping centre in Vernon Way, Crewe. Cardboard pieces in a trolley were well alight. Crews used a hose reel jet to tackle the flames. They then damped down the rubbish and checked for hotspots with a thermal imaging camera.
