Person rescued from car in water in Appleton Time of Incident: 04/07/2016 - 11:20 Location: New Lane, Appleton, Warrington Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington,Altringham (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) and water rescue unit supporting fire engine from Knutsford, along with the boat from Warrington A car left the road and went into a pond leaving a lady trapped in the vehicle. Firefighters, including water rescue team members, were able to assist the lady from the car. She was checked over by paramedics. The incident was handed over to police officers.
