 Wheelie bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/09/2018 - 21:45 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port A hose reel jet was used to extinguish a wheelie bin fire at Thornton Road. Fire crews were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
