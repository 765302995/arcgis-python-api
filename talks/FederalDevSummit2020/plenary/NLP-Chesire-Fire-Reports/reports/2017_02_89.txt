Collision in Congleton Time of Incident: 15/02/2017 - 20:14 Location: Manchester Road, Congleton. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Congleton Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Manchester Road, Congleton.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved one car with a woman trapped inside. Crews released the woman with special cutting gear. She was then handed to the care of the ambulance service.
