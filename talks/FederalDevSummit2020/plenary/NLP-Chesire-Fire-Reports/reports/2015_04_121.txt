Road traffic collision on the M62 Time of Incident: 15/04/2015 - 08:38 Location: M62, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Birchwood plus vehicles from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service attended A road traffic collision involving three vehicles on the M62 required two fire engines from Cheshire to attend. Firefighters released one casualty that was trapped in a vehicle and Air Ambulance attended.
