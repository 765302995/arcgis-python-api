 Unattended bonfire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 10/05/2018 - 16:07 Location: Ambleside Close, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At around 4.05pm firefighters were called to an unattended bonfire in the open in Ambleside Close in Macclesfield. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were at the scene for around 15 minutes
