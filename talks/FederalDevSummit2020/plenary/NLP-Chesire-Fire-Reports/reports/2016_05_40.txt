Person rescued from water in Northwich Time of Incident: 24/05/2016 - 15:02 Location: Water Street, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich, the line rescue unit and supporting fire engine from Knutsford and one fire engine from Winsford A man was rescued from water at the bottom of an embankment on London Road. The line rescue swift water rescue teams attended the incident. The casualty was stabilised by the swift water team while the line rescue team set up ropes and height rescue equipment. The casualty was brought up to safety and handed into the care of paramedics. Police officers were also in attendance.
