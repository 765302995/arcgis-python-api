Tumble dryer fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/11/2016 - 07:55 Location: Richmond Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington A fire which broke out inside a tumble dryer was believed to be due to fluff within the dryer. The fire was out when crews arrived so they conducted a health and safety assessment of the property.  Tumble dryer fire safety advice
