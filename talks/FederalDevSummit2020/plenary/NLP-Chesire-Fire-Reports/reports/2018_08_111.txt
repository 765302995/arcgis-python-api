 Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 08/08/2018 - 18:11 Location: Parkgate Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield, one from Poynton and one from Congleton Fire crews were called to a kitchen fire on Parkgate Road, Macclesfield. The fire involved a pan being left on the stove and crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet, a covering jet and a large fan to extinguish the fire and clear smoke from the property. No one was injured during the incident and crews were at the scene for 50 minutes. Kitchen safety
