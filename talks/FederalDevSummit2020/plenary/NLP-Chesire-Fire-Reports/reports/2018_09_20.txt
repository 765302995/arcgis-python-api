 Serious collision in Neston Time of Incident: 24/09/2018 - 22:03 Location: Chester High Road, Neston. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - One from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane At approximately 10pm firefighters from Powey Lane and Ellesmere Port attended a serious two vehicle collision at the junction of Chester High Road and Damhead Lane. On arrival fire crews quickly assisted with releasing two casualties from the vehicles. They then made the scene safe and assisted and ambulance and police.  The team were detained for detained for approximately one hour.
