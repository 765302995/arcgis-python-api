Tumble dryer fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 10/03/2016 - 17:05 Location: Meadway,Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton and one from Cheadle (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service)  A tumble dryer was involved in a fire in a double garage.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Crews were at the scene for approximately two hours.   Please follow our tumble dryer safety advice
