Road traffic collision in Cuddington Time of Incident: 05/08/2016 - 07:26 Location: A49, Forest Road, Cuddington, Northwich Attendance: Three fire engines from Nortwich and Winsford Firefighters were called to reports of a road traffic collision involving two vehicles where one person was trapped. Crews rescued the casualty from the vehicle and handed them over into the care of awaiting paramedics.
