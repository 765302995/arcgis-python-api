 Horse rescued from brook Time of Incident: 11/11/2018 - 17:35 Location: Dutton, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm and Runcorn Horse stuck in a brookFirefighters helped rescue a horse that had become stuck in mud on a brook in Dutton. The 5 year old called Harley was stuck by his back legs.  After Harley was sedated by the vet, crews released his legs before relocating him up the embankment. Firefighters then handed Harley over to the care of his owner and the vet, who gave him the all clear.
