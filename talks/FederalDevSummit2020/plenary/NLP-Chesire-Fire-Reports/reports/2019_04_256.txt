 Large bonfire near to the M6 Time of Incident: 01/04/2019 - 09:56 Location: Old Mill Road, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach Firefighters were called to a field adjacent to the M6 after a large bonfire was causing a nuisance for motorists. Crews used a stack drag and a hose reel jet to put out the fire, which mostly consisted of trees.
