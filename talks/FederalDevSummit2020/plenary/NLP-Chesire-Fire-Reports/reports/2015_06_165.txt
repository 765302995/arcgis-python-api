Bushes on fire at Orford park in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/06/2015 - 19:33 Location: Orford Park, Alder Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish a fire involving the bushes inside the park in Orford.  The fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information as to who started this fire they should call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
