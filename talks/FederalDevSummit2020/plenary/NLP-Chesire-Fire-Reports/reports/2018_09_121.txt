 Two-vehicle fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 06/09/2018 - 01:38 Location: King Street, Middlewich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Middlewich and one from Winsford At around 1.40am firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving two vehicles on King Street in Middlewich. On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved a van and a saloon car. Firefighters extinguished the fire using water. They then damped the vehicles down to make them safe and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots on the vehicles and in a nearby property. Firefighters were in attendance for more than two hours. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
