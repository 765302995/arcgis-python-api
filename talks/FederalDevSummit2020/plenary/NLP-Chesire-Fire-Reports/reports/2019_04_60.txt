 Bonfire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 21/04/2019 - 21:07 Location: Booth Lane, Middlewich. CW10 0HE Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach Shotly before 9.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a garden fire which was spreading to neighbouring trees. The fire crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and was on scene for 20 minutes.
