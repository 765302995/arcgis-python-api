Chip pan fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 21/09/2017 - 13:11 Location: Brook Street, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton, one from Macclesfield and one from Holmes Chapel Fire crews were called to a chip pan fire on Brook Street, Congleton. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was from an unattended chip pan. One casualty was treated for smoke inhalation and taken to hospital as a precaution by paramedics. Crews then cleared smoke from the property using a large fan. Crews were at the scene for 55 minutes.  More information about chip pan safety - https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chip-pans
