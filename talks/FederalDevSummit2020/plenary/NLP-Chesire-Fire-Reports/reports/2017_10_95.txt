Microwave fire in Chester Time of Incident: 09/10/2017 - 08:42 Location: Richmond Court, Richmond Terrace, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a fire involving a microwave on Richmond Terrace, Chester. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
