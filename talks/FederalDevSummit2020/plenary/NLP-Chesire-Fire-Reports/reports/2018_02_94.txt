 House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/02/2018 - 12:03 Location: Kildonan Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Penketh House fire in WarringtonFire crews were called to a fire in house on Kildonan Road, Warrington. The fire was in the first floor of the building and crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a large fan to extinguish the fire.  One male casualty was treated by fire crews for a burn to his arm.  The fire is believed to have been caused by smoking materials.  Safety tips for smokers
