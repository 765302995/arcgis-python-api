 Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 28/02/2019 - 20:29 Location: Mill Lane, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 8.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a car being on fire on Mill Lane in Crewe. On arrival the saloon car was well alight. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They then damped the car and the surrounding area down to make them safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half. Police also attended. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 335158, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
