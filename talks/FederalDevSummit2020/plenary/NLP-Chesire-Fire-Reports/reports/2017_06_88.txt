Industrial bin on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/06/2017 - 03:45 Location: Richard Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn An industrial bin fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters used a main jet to tackle the blaze and are asking anyone with any information to contact Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 to help them stop arson attacks which can cost lives and damage property.
