Person extracted from vehicle after M6 crash Time of Incident: 21/08/2017 - 17:28 Location: M6 southbound between junction 21A and 21 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, one from Birchwood, one from Leigh and one from Lymm attended the incident Fire crews were called to a person who was trapped in a vehicle after a road traffic collision on the M6 southbound between junctions 21A and 21. One casualty who was suffering from neck pain was extracted from a vehicle by firefighters using cutting equipment. Crews were at the scene for one hour and 20 minutes. The casualty was taken by ambulance to Warrington General Hospital.
