 Bin fire at a park in Winsford Time of Incident: 19/08/2019 - 22:14 Location: Carlisle Close, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Winsford firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a bin which was well alight in a park on Carlisle Close.  Crews were at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.
