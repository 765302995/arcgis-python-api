 Fire in a bin storage area Time of Incident: 05/10/2019 - 17:31 Location: Nant Peris, Treborth Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Chester and Powey Lane At around 5.30pm firefighters were notified of a fire alarm activation at Nant Peris tower block in Blacon. On arrival crews discovered there had been a fire in a bin storage area which had been quickly extinguished by the sprinkler system prior to the arrival of firefighters. Crews removed the affected bin from the storage area and used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. The scene was then handed over to a representative from the housing trust.
