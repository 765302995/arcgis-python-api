 Collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/09/2018 - 15:44 Location: Stanney Woods Avenue, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Crews from Ellesmere Port were called to reports of a collision on Stanney Woods Avenue. Firefighters attended the scene and found the collision involved a car and a motorbike. The team provided basic first aid to the motorcyclist, although he did not require hospital treatment.
