 Chimney fire in Kelsall Time of Incident: 06/03/2019 - 20:34 Location: Chester Road, Kelsall. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington. Firefighters were called to a chimney fire at a pub in Chester Road, Kelsall. Crews used chimney rods after removing the fire from the grate. They then checked the loft space and first floor with a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.
