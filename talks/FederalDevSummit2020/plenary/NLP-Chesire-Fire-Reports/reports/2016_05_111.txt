Cooker fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 11/05/2016 - 17:55 Location: Ledward Street, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich A cooker fire caused a lot of smoke in a Winsford property. Firefighters used a high pressure fan to ventilate the house in Ledward Street. Smoke alarms were fitted and activated, alerting the householder to the fire.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice
