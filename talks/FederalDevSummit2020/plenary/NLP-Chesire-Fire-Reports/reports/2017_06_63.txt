Two females trapped in car after road traffic collision Time of Incident: 20/06/2017 - 14:31 Location: Wrenbury Road, Cholmondeley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas, one from Crewe and one from Whitchurch Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision after two females were trapped in a car on Wrenbury Road, Cholmondeley. The collision was between two cars and crews helped paramedics extract the two casualties from the cars. An air ambulance took both casualties to hospital.
