Road traffic collision in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 29/07/2017 - 12:08 Location: Highfield Crescent/Manchester Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow First aid was given by firefighters to four casualties in a road traffic collision involving four vehicles. The scene was made safe and the casualties handed into the care of the ambulance service. Cheshire Police were also at the scene.
