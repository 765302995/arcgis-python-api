 Large crop fire in Culcheth, Warrington Time of Incident: 25/06/2018 - 21:01 Location: New Hall Lane, Culcheth, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Crop fire in Warrington Fire crews were called to a crop fire on New Hall Lane, Culcheth. A large fire had broken out in a field measuring approximately 100 square metres. Crews used beaters and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
