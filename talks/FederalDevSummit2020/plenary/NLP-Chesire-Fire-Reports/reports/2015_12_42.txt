Car fire M62 west junctions 9 to 8 Time of Incident: 21/12/2015 - 16:22 Location: M62 west junctions 9 to 8 Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters used a hose reel jet to damp down the engine of a vehicle on the M62 west. The engine is believed to have overheated. Highways closed lane one of the motorway while firefighters cleared the incident.
