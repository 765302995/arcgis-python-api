 Cabin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/04/2019 - 10:09 Location: Alder Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Birchwood and one from Lymm Firefighters were called to a fire in the roof space of a cabin in Alder Lane, Warrington. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. A covering jet was also set up. One man was treated by crews for what was believed to have been minor smoke inhalation. Firefighters cut away parts of the cabin damaged by the fire. They also damped the area affected by the fire down while checking for hotspots using a thermal imaging camera. A large fan was used to disperse smoke from the cabin.
