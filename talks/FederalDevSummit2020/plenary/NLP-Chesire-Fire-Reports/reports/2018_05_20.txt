 Loft fire at house in Northwich Time of Incident: 28/05/2018 - 16:59 Location: Sandown Crescent, Northwich Attendance: Three fire engines attended from Northwich, one from Lymm and one from Winsford Firefighters attended to a house fire involving the roof of a property on Sandown Crescent in Northwich.  Crews used a ladder to gain access to the address then put out the fire using a hose reel. Firefighters also entered the property from the first floor to help extinguish the fire.   Once the blaze was out, crews dampened down the area and made sure it was safe.
