Two garages on fire in Davenham, Northwich Time of Incident: 07/05/2016 - 13:13 Location: Ashley Grange, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford There was a fire involving two single domestic garages on Ashley Grange in Davenham, Northwich. Firefighters one hose reel and one covering jet to bring the fire under control. Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus. One casualty suffered from minor burns and minor smoke inhalation. The casualty was assessed by a fast response ambulance paramedic, but was not taken to hospital.
