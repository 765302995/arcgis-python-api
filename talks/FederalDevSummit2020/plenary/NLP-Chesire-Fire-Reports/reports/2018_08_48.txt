 Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 20/08/2018 - 12:45 Location: Marshgate, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 12.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a car being on fire at Marshgate in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered a private saloon vehicle well alight on the roadway. It was close to other vehicles but the fire was not affecting them. Firefighters extinguished the fire using two hose reel jets. The fire is believed to have been started by a mechanical fault underneath the vehicle. Approximately 10 litres of diesel leaked from the car into the drainage system. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
