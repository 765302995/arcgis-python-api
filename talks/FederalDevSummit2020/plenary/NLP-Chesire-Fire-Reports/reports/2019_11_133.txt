 Fire in the open in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 05/11/2019 - 20:48 Location: Howty Close, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 8.50pm firefighters from Wilmslow were called to reports of a fire in the open on Howty Close. On arrival the crew found that a mattress had been set alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for around 15 minutes.
