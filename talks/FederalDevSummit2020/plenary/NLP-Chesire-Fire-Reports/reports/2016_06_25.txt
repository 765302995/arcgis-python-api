Kitchen fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/06/2016 - 12:01 Location: Ormond Close, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Residents were evacuated from a Widnes house while firefighters tackled a fire in the kitchen. The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was used to clear the smoke.
