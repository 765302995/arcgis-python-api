House fire in Tarvin Time of Incident: 02/07/2017 - 08:58 Location: Hockenhull Avenue, Tarvin Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a house fire on Hockenhall Avenue, Tarvin. On arrival crews found a fire in a bedroom of the property and firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The crews then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. Crews were on the scene for approximately an hour and a half.
