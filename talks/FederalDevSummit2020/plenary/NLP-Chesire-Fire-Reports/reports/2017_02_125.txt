Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/02/2017 - 09:55 Location: Longshaw Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Birchwood Emergency services were called to the scene of a road traffic collision involving two buses. There was a driver and three passengers on one bus, none of whom reported any injuries, and a driver on the second.  The driver of the second bus, a man, was trapped and was released by firefighters then handed into the care of paramedics.
