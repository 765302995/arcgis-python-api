 Fire at a farm, Chester Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 03:39 Location: Puddington Lane, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Powey Lane and Ellesmere Port Two fire engines from Powey Lane and Ellesmere Port were mobilised to a fire at a farm on Puddington Lane. Crews found a fire involving a silage stack measuring approx. 20m x 10m. Crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire, with the help of the farmer who used his machinery to pull apart and turn over damage silage. Crews remained at the scene to dampen down and were in attendance for around two hours altogether.
