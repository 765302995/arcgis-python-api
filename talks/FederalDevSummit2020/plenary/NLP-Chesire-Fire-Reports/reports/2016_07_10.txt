Kitchen fire at a property in Chester Time of Incident: 28/07/2016 - 17:18 Location: The Heywoods, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester fire station There was a fire involving a cooker inside the kitchen of a property in Chester. The cooker was well alight.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. One hose reel jet and one covering jet was used to extinguish the fire.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the kitchen.    Kitchen fire safety tips
