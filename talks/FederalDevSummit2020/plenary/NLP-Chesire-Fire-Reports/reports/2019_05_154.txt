 Motorbike on fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 11/05/2019 - 03:32 Location: Kingsway South, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters responded to reports of a small motorbike on fire near the Co-op in Kingsway South, Latchford East. They used a single hose reel to extinguish the fire. It's thought to have been a deliberate ignition, and the motorbike was completely gutted so it's not clear who it might belong to. Anyone with information should contact Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 397727.
