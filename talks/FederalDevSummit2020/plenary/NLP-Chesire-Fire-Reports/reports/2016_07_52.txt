Mattress on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/07/2016 - 23:42 Location: Maple Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn A mattress had been set on fire close to garages on Maple Avenue. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control and protect the nearby buildings and are urging anyone with any information to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101. Arson endangers lives and property.
