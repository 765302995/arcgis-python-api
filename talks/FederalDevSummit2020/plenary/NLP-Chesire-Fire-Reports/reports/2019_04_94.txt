 Ducklings rescued, Warrington Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 15:55 Location: Half Acre Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm The fire service was called following a call from a member of the public that a group of ducklings had become trapped in a  drain, with the mother duck stuck outside. The crew from Lymm arrived and removed a number of manhole covers to try to locate the ducklings and rescue them. After some time, they were able to retrieve the six ducklings from the pipework and reunite them with their mother, and they were taken to a nearby canal to be released.
