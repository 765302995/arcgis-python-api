Roof on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/07/2016 - 15:02 Location: Kenview close, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Widnes A flat roof of a semi detached bungalow was on fire. Upon arrival, firefighters found the roof constructed of a small flat roof and another roof made of tile and mixed felt. Firefighters acted quickly, using a hose reel to tackle the fire and cutting equipment to expose the affected area. Crews extinguished the fire before using a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots of fire in and around the bungalow.
