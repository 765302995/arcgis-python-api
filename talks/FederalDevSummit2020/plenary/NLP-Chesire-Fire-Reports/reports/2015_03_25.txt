Road traffic collision in Delamere Time of Incident: 25/03/2015 - 16:21 Location: Station Road, Delamere, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford A car was involved in a road traffic collision on Station Road in Delamere, leaving one man trapped.  The car had crashed into a tree.  Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to release the casualty who was treated by both the Hazardous Area Response team and the Air Ambulance team before being transferred to hospital by air ambulance.
