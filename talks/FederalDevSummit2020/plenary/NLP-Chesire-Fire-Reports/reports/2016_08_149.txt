Sheds on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/08/2016 - 11:58 Location: Gig Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Birchwood Three sheds were on fire at the back of Gig Lane in Woolston, with another two affected by the blaze. Firefighters, two in breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to tackle the fire before damping the area down.
