 Chimney fire at a pub in Bollington Time of Incident: 25/02/2018 - 14:58 Location: Bollington Road, Bollington. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one fire engine from Wilmslow attended Firefighters from Macclesfield and Wilmslow attended reports of a chimney fire at a pub in Bollington. Crews used water to extinguish the fire before using a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. The team were in attendance for approximately one hour 15 minutes.
