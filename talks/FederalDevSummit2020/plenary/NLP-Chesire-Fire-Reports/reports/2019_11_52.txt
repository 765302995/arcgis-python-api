 Two-vehicle collision in Handforth Time of Incident: 18/11/2019 - 16:03 Location: Wilmslow Road, Handforth Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Wilmslow and one from Manchester Shortly after 4pm firefighters were called to a report of two-vehicle collision on Wilmslow Road in Handforth.  On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved a van and a car and that no-one was trapped inside either vehicle.  Firefighters made the vehicles safe and were in attendance for more than 20 minutes.
