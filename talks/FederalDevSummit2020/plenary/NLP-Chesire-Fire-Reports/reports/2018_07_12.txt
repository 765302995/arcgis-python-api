 Grill pan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/07/2018 - 18:14 Location: Teal Grove, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. One fire engine from Lymm. Firefighters were called to a grill pan on fire at an address in Teal Grove, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel and two breathing apparatus to put out the flames. They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
