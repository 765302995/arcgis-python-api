 Cows rescued from canal in Burland Time of Incident: 26/07/2019 - 21:55 Location: Springe Lane, Burland Attendance: Four fire engines attended - one from Nantwich, one from Crewe and the animal rescue teams from Lymm and Bollington. Cows stuck in a canalFirefighters rescued up to 20 cows which had fallen into a canal on Springe Lane in Burland.  Working alongside the animal rescue team, crews used lighting and water rescue equipment to bring the cows to safety in a nearby field.  Two cows were also rescued after becoming stuck in mud near the canal bridge.  Crews checked to see if anymore cows needed to be rescued and informed the farmer.
