 Unattended bonfire, Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 21/10/2019 - 19:42 Location: Park Road, Ellesmere port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters responded to reports of a fire in the open on Park Road. They arrived to find a bonfire that had been left unattended, and used water backpacks to put the fire out. Colleagues at the council's street cleaning team were notified of the fire due to its location and need to clean it up properly.
