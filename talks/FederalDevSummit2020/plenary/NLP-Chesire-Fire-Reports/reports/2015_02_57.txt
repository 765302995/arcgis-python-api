Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 14/02/2015 - 22:32 Location: Deepdale, Widnes. Attendance: A fire engine from Widnes A hose reel jet was used to put out a car fire on Deepdale, Widnes.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
