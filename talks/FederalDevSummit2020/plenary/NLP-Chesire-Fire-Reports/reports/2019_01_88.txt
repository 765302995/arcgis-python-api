 One-vehicle collision in Middlewich Time of Incident: 25/01/2019 - 13:34 Location: Bostock, Middlewich Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Middlewich and one from Northwich Shortly after 1.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a one-vehicle collision near Bostock Road in Middlewich that had left the car on its roof in a ditch. On arrival crews discovered that no-one was trapped inside the vehicle. Firefighters gave the driver first aid and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
