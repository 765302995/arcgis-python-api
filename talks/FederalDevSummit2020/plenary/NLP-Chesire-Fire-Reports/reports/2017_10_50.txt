Kitchen fire in Malkins Bank Time of Incident: 19/10/2017 - 19:11 Location: Betchton Road, Malkins Bank Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar and one from Sandbach Fire crews were called to a kitchen fire on Betchton Road, Malkins Bank. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They then cleared smoke from the property using a large fan and checked the property with a thermal imaging camera. One casualty was treated by firefighters for a burn to their hand. Crews were at the scene for one and a half hours.
