Chimney fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 22/11/2016 - 18:10 Location: Chapel Street, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich A fire in a wood burning stove had spread to the area surrounding the stove. Firefighters used small gear to cut the area surrounding the stove away before using a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They then discovered a small fire in the chimney so used a chimney hose and chimney rods to tackle it. Finally, using a thermal imaging camera, they scanned the property and the neighbouring property for signs of fire.  When the weather outside is frightful, a chimney fire can seem delightful! But, to keep a chimney fire from getting out of control, sweep your chimney often to keep it clean.
