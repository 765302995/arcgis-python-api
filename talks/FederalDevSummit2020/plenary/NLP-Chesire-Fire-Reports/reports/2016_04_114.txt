Electrical box fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 09/04/2016 - 23:19 Location: Station Avenue, Frodsham. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a hose fire on Station Avenue, Frodsham.  When they arrived crews located a fire involving the electrical supply and meter box. Crews used a hose reel jet, dry powder extinguisher, thermal imaging camera and electrical gloves to tackle the fire.   Firefighters were in attendance for about 2 hours 30 minutes.
