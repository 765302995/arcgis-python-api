 Skip fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 18/05/2019 - 22:11 Location: Red Lion Lane, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a large skip fire off Red Lion Lane in Nantwich. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the flames and then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
