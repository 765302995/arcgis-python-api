 Woman trapped following collision in Marton, near Winsford Time of Incident: 27/08/2019 - 21:39 Location: Clay Lane, Marton, near Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Winsford At around 9.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a car lying on its side following a collision in Clay Lane in Marton, near Winsford. On arrival crews found that a woman was trapped inside the vehicle. The casualty was assessed by Cheshire Fire and Rescue Service's Medical Director as firefighters cut away a roof flap. This enabled firefighters to get the casualty out of the car. She was then handed over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters made the vehicle safe and were in attendance for around an hour.
