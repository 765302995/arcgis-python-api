 Fire on the side of a road in Rainow Time of Incident: 03/12/2018 - 18:07 Location: Smith Lane, Rainow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington Shortly after 6pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Smith Lane in Rainow. On arrival the crew discovered a small quantity of rubbish well alight on the side of a road. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire before damping down adjacent silage bales to make sure they were safe. The crew were in attendance for less than half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
