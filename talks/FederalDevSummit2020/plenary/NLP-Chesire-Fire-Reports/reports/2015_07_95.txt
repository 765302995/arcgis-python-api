Drain pipe on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 17/07/2015 - 18:09 Location: British Red Cross Shop, Witton Street, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich A plastic drain pipe was set on fire on Witton Street in Northwich, so firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.    This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
