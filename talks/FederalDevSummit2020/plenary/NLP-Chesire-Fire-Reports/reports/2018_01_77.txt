 Collision in Little Budworth Time of Incident: 12/01/2018 - 11:05 Location: Chester Lane, Little Budworth Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters were called to a collision involving an HGV and a car in Chester Lane, Little Budworth. Crews used cutting equipment to extricate a casualty from the car. They then handed her over to the care of paramedics. Police are managing the traffic.
