A pallet of plastic pipes well alight in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 19/05/2017 - 22:07 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port A small quantity of plastic pipes were set alight. They were located on a pallet,  near to the Stadium by some temporary traffic lights.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately so anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
