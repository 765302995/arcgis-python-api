 Small fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/08/2019 - 13:19 Location: Cherry Tree Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a fire at an address in Cherry Tree Avenue, Runcorn. Crews discovered clothes on a heater had caught fire, but were out on arrival. Crews used a large fan to disperse the smoke and checked over the occupant to ensure they were okay.   Here's a link to using heaters safely https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/heaters
