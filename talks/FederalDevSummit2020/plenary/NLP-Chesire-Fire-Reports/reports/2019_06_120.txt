 Rescue of a deer in Pott Shrigley Time of Incident: 05/06/2019 - 11:24 Location: Long Lane, Pott Shrigley, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield, one fire engine from Lymm and the animal rescue unit from Lymm. At around 11.30am firefighters were called to reports of a deer trapped in a field off Long Lane in Pott Shrigley.  On arrival the crew discovered the deer's antlers were trapped in an electrical fence wire.  The firefighters worked in partnership with a local vet and RSPCA officials to free the deer and release it back into the wild.  The crews were detained at the scene for around three hours 30 minutes.
