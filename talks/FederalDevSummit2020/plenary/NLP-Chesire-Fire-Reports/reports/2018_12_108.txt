 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/12/2018 - 03:22 Location: Padgate Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 3.20am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a wheelie bin on Padgate Lane in Warrington. The crew extinguished the fire in the open with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For advice on preventing your wheelie bins from becoming a target for arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
