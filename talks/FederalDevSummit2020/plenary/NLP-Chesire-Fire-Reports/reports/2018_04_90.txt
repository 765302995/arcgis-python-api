 Chimney fire, Northwich Time of Incident: 13/04/2018 - 21:31 Location: London Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters responded to a chimney fire at a house in London Road, Northwich. Chimney rods and a thermal imaging camera were used to deal with the incident, and a safe and well visit was conducted while crews were at the address. They were in attendance for just under an hour.  For chimney fire prevention advice visit Chimney fire safety advice
