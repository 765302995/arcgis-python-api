Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/09/2015 - 01:13 Location: Hardwick Grange, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines, one from Warrington and one from Birchwood A road traffic collision involving two heavy good vehicles in Warrington required firefighters to attend. One casualty self rescued themselves and fire crews released a person via the roof hatch of the vehicle. North West Ambulance Service attended to deal with the two people.
