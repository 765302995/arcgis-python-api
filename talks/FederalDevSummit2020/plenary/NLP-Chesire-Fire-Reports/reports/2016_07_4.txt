Small fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 29/07/2016 - 21:07 Location: Wooded area next to Rupert row, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters extinguished a small fire in a wooded area, which is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
