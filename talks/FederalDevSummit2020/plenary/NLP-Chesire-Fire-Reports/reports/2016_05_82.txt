Bathroom fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 15/05/2016 - 08:27 Location: Salisbury Close, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Alsager A bathroom extractor fan caused a small fire in an en suite bathroom. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke and a thermal imaging camera used to check there were no further hot spots that could ignite.
