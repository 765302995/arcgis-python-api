 Skip fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 10/09/2019 - 19:50 Location: Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Frodsham At around 7.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a field in Frodsham. On arrival the crew found a skip well alight. Firefighters used a main jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for less than half an hour.
