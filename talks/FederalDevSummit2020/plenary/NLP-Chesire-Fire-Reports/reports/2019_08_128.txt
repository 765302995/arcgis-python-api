 Shed fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 08/08/2019 - 01:27 Location: Edinburgh Road, Congleton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton. One fire engine from Holmes Chapel. Firefighters were called to a fire involving two sheds on Edinburgh Road, Congleton. Two firefighters in breathing apparatus used two hose reels to extinguish the fire and two propane cylinders were safely cooled. Crews were in attendance for approximately three and a half hours. Police are investigating the circumstances. Anyone with information should contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 479959.
