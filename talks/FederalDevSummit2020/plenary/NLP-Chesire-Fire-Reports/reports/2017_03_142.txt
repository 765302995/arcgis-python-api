Collision in Malpas Time of Incident: 07/03/2017 - 19:41 Location: Chester Road, Malpas. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Malpas Fire crews responded to a report of a collision on Chester Road, Malpas.  When they arrived they found that the incident involved two cars. One woman was injured so crews gave first aid before a first responder arrived to take over treatment.
