Fire involving boiler in Burwardsley Time of Incident: 16/08/2017 - 01:55 Location: The Pheasant Inn, Pennsylvania Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and one from Chester A fire involving a boiler required firefighters to cool it with water. The two storey detached house, which is believed to be on the same site as the inn, was checked with a thermal imaging camera and ventilated before crews left.
