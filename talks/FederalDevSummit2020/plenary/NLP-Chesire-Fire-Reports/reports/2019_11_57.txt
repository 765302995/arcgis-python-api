 Chimney fire in Over Peover Time of Incident: 17/11/2019 - 18:11 Location: Peover Park, Over Peover. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield. Firefighters were called to a chiment fire at an address in Peover Park, Over Peover. The fire was out on arrival and crews assessed the building with a thermal imaging camera to ensure there was no fire spread.
