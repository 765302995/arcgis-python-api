 Smoke in a domestic property in Congleton Time of Incident: 23/10/2019 - 17:28 Location: River Street, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton At around 5.30pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke being in a domestic property in River Street, Congleton.  On arrival the crew discovered that the smoke had been caused by a kettle being heated on a cooker.  Firefighters ventilated the property to remove the smoke and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
