Combine harvester fire in Chester Time of Incident: 24/08/2017 - 15:41 Location: Kinnerton Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port. Combine harvester fire in ChesterFirefighters were called to a fire involving a combine harvester on Kinnerton Road, Lower Kinnerton, Chester.  Crews tackled the blaze using a main jet. They also used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hot spots on the vehicle.  The crews were detained for one hour and 10 minutes.
