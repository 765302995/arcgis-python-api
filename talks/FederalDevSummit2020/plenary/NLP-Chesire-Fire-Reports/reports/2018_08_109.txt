 Grass fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 08/08/2018 - 20:05 Location: Appleton Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a grass fire on Appleton Drive, Ellesmere Port. The fire was approximately 3m by 3m across and crews used a fire extinguisher and beaters to put out the fire.
