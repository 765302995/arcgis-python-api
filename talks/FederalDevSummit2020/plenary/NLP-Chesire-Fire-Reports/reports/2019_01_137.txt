 Spilt building material on a road in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/01/2019 - 08:21 Location: Winwick Road, Warrington Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 8.20am firefighters were called to a report of a bag of building material having fallen off a lorry on Winwick Road near to Alban Retail Park in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that the bag had split and a small amount of its contents had spilled out onto the carriageway. The bag was resealed and removed by contractors. Firefighters then used a hose reel jet to damp down the road. The fire crew also assisted the police with traffic management. Firefighters were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
