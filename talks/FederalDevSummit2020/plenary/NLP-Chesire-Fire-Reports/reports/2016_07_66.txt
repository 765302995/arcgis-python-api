Toaster fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 19/07/2016 - 13:40 Location: Livingstone Close, Macclesfield. Attendance: Fire engines from Macclesfield and Bollington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Livingstone Close, Macclesfield.   When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving a toaster.   Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus sets tackled the fire using one hose reel. Once the fire was extinguished, fire crews used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. Cheshire fire and rescue service remained in attendance at the incident approximately 60 minutes.
