 Fire in the open, Chester Time of Incident: 17/11/2018 - 13:31 Location: Shelley Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane Crews dealt with a fire involving rubbish and a nearby fence on Shelley Road. They found that the fire had started in a garden shed, and spread into a nearby alleyway. Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the flames in the both the shed and alleyway. A home safety assessment was conducted, and they were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
