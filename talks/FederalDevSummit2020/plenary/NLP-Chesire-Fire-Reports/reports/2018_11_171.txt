 Serious collision in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 04/11/2018 - 00:13 Location: A34 Melsrose Way, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton and Wilmslow. Firefighters were called to a serious collision on Melrose Way, A34, Alderley Edge. The collision involved a minibus and a car. Crews assisted emergency services at the scene and extricated casualties.
