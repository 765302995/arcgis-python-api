 Tumble dryer fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 09/02/2019 - 09:10 Location: Norton Village, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Widnes Fire crews were called to a tumble dryer fire on Norton Village, Runcorn. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and then removed the tumble dryer from the property. Crews were at the scene for 30 minutes.
