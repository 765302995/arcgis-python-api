Wheelie bin well alight on Grasmere Avenue in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/01/2016 - 22:49 Location: Grasmere avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A wheelie bin was deliberately set alight in the open by Grasmere Avenue in Warrington, so firefighters used hand fire extinguishers to extinguish the fire.  The fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
