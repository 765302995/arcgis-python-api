Outbuilding fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/03/2016 - 16:11 Location: Wolverham Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A garden fire spread to fencing and into a shed containing gas bottles. Fortunately, bringing the fire under control using a hose reel and a main jet, the crews managed to prevent the blaze reaching the cylinders. They had to remove large amounts of timber and metal from the shed and then damped the area down.
