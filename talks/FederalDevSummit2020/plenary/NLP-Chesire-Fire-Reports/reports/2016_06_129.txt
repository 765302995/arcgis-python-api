Three car collision in Oakmere Time of Incident: 07/06/2016 - 18:09 Location: Hogshead Lane, Oakmere in Northwich Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich and Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Hogshead Lane, Oakmere in Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved three cars. There were three people who were injured.  Crews worked with the ambulance service to rescue the casualties.  They were at the scene for about one hour.
