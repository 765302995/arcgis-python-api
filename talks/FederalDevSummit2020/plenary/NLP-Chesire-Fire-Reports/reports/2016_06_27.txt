Houses evacuated after fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 25/06/2016 - 05:05 Location: Brecon Way, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford and one from Middlewich Three fire engines attended a fire at a Winsford property which caused neighbouring houses to be evacuated.  The fire was in the back yard of the house and had spread to the windows of the building, which was unoccupied. As a precaution the neighbouring houses were evacuated as crews tackled the fire using a hose reel jet. They then cleared the property of smoke and were at the scene for around fifty minutes.
