 Outbuilding fire in Nether Alderly Time of Incident: 01/06/2019 - 13:25 Location: Stubby Lane, Nether Alderly Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow, one from Macclesfield and two from Congleton Firefighters attended a fire in an outbuilding on Stubby Lane, Nether Alderly. The outbuilding contained gas cylinders for use in paintballing and was in a difficult to access woodland area. Crews used two extended main jets to extinguish the fire. They were in attendance for approximately four hours.
