 Rubbish fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 02/09/2018 - 16:25 Location: Church Street, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a rubbish fire on Church Street, Ellesmere Port. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the blaze which was well alight. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information please contact Cheshire Police.
