Youths rescued from the roof of the Old Market Place in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/05/2017 - 18:45 Location: Old Market Place, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Two youths were on the roof of the old Market Place by the Golden Square in Warrington, so firefighters used a 13.5 metre ladder to rescue them.  Police attended this incident.
