Wheelie bin on fire in Tabley Time of Incident: 07/05/2017 - 09:31 Location: Holly Grove, Tabley Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford A fire which involved a wheelie bin in a garden required firefighters to used one hose reel to extinguish it. It is believed that the fire may have started due to hot ashes from a barbecue. Please be extra careful when disposing of hot ash and never put them in a bin until fully extinguished.
