 Male trapped in car following two-vehicle collision in Daresbury Time of Incident: 09/05/2019 - 18:03 Location: Chester Road, Daresbury Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Warrington and one from Runcorn Shortly after 6pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Chester Road in Daresbury. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved two vehicles and that a male was trapped inside one of them. The road was closed by the police as emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to remove the roof of the car. They then got the casualty out of the vehicle and handed him over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour.
