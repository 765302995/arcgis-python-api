Smell of smoke from electricity box in Chester Time of Incident: 13/09/2016 - 09:15 Location: Springfields, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Firefighters were called to reports of smoke inside a domestic property. On arrival, they discovered no sign of fire, however, there was a smell of smoke coming from the electric box outside of the property, which was then found to be faulty. Crews advised the owner to contact their electricity provider and also completed a health and safety assessment of the property.
