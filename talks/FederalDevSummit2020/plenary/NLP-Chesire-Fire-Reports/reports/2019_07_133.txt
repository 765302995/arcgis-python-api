 Refuse fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 08/07/2019 - 13:18 Location: Heath Lane, Little Leigh, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 1.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Heath Lane, Little Leigh, Northwich. On arrival the crew found a quantity of refuse alight. It was a case of controlled burning that had got out of control. A water shuttle was set up to get enough water to the area and firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. The crew then worked with an on-site JCB to turn over the refuse and damp the area down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
