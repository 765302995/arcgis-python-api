 Small fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 11/03/2019 - 17:47 Location: Rossmore Road East, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to an underpass on Rossmore Road East, Ellemsere Port, after a report of an unattended fire. Crews found a small campfire involving rubbish was burning and they used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
