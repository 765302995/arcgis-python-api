 Collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 04/03/2019 - 17:10 Location: A556, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Northwich and one from Winsford Fire crews attended to a road traffic collision on the A556 near the round tower junction involving three cars.  No one involved in the collision needed to be released from their vehicles.  Firefighters assisted ambulance with the care of four casualties who were suffering from injuries believed to be minor. One man went to hospital.  The road was closed while crews made the scene safe and were in attendance for just over an hour.
