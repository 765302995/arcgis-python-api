Foal rescued from canal in Little Stanney Time of Incident: 12/03/2015 - 14:52 Location: Stanney Mill Road, Little Stanney, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and the animal rescue unit from Knutsford A foal trapped in the canal in Little Stanney was rescued by firefighters from the animal rescue unit.  The 11 hands high youngster was stuck in the water.   Firefighters were able to get the animal to the bank side so the animal rescue unit could use specialist equipment to get it out of the water.  The crews remained at the scene until a vet arrived to check the foal was fit after its ordeal.
