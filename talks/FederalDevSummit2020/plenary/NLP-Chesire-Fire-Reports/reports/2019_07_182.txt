 Dangerous structure in Chester Time of Incident: 02/07/2019 - 11:07 Location: Watergate Street, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters were called to Watergate Street in Chester following reports of wood coming loose from a shop front. The small piece of timber was approximately 60cm by 10cm and was hanging from a third story apex roof. Cordons were in place on the footpath while firefighters removed the piece of wood.
