Rubbish on fire on a roof in Runcorn Time of Incident: 13/03/2016 - 18:22 Location: Castlefields Avenue South, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes Firefighters were called to rubbish on fire on a roof. The crew used a hose reel to extinguish it. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
