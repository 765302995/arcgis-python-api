Small fire in Barthomley Time of Incident: 20/06/2017 - 16:53 Location: Rushy Lane, Barthomley Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a muck heap on fire and used two hose reels to extinguish it. The crew turned over the heap to extinguish it and used a water relay to get water to the fire.
