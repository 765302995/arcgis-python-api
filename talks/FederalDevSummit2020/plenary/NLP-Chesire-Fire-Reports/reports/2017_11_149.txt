Bonfire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 31/10/2017 - 18:48 Location: Overpool Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended reports of a small bonfire on Overpool Road in Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port. On arrival at the scene they found that the fire was close to a hedgerow and could have spread. One hose reel was used to put out the fire. If anyone has any information about how this fire may have started please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
