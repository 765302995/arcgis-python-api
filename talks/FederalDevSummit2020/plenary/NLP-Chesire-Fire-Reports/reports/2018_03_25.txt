 Fire in a playground in Winsford Time of Incident: 26/03/2018 - 19:43 Location: Furness Close, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Fire crews were called to a playground after some of its equipment was deliberately set alight on Furness Close, Winsford. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
