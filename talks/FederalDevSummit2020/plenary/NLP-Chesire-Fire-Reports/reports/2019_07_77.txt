 Small fire in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 16/07/2019 - 16:33 Location: Main Street, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly after 4.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Main Street, Runcorn. On arrival the crew found a small fire involving refuse. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
