 Flat fire in Warrington. Time of Incident: 11/11/2018 - 03:40 Location: Toll Bar road, Hulme, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Penketh. Shortly after 3.30am firefighters from Warrington and Penketh were called to reports of a building fire in Hulme. On arrival crews found the fire involved an electrical vent in a ground floor flat on Toll Bar Road. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus extinguish the fire using a hose reel jet. The team then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property. Two casualties were treated at the scene by paramedics for smoke inhalation. Crews were detained at the scene for approximately two hours 10 minutes.
