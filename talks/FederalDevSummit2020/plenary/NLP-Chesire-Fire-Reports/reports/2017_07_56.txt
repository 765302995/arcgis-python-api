Skip on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 17/07/2017 - 18:44 Location: Electricity Street, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe. A skip which was on fire in Crewe was extinguished with a hose reel by firefighters. It's believed that the fire may have been started deliberately. Call police on 101 if you know who may have started it.
