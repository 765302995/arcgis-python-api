Building fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 31/12/2016 - 11:29 Location: Station Road, Winsford Attendance: Fire engines from Winsford, Northwich and Holmes Chapel Firefighters tackled a blaze a property on Station Road. The crews used four main jets, two hose reels, and a ground monitor to bring the fire under control.   They remained at the scene for some 18 hours, damping down and monitoring the incident. The cause of the  blaze is under investigation.
