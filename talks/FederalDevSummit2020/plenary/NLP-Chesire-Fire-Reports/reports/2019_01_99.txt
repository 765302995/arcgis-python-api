 Collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 23/01/2019 - 04:10 Location: Bostock Road, Winsford. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Middlewich. One fire engine attended from Winsford. Firefighters attended a collision on Bostock Road, Winsford. On arrival crews found one car with a casualty trapped inside. Crews extricated the casualty who was then taken to hospital by paramedics.
