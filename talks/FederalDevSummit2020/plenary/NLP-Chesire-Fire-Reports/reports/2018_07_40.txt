 Allotment and garden fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/07/2018 - 19:31 Location: Arthur Avenue, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 7.30pm firefighters were called to a report of an allotment fire on Arthur Avenue in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire started in the allotment and spread through the hedge to the garden of a domestic property. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
