Vehicle fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/11/2017 - 20:24 Location: Winwick Link Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to reports of a car fire on the A49, Winwick Link Road, Warrington.  On arrival they found they found a white saloon car in a layby which was well alight  Firefighters, using breathing apparatus, used one hose reel to extinguish the fire.
