Gorse fire on Fox Hill in Frodsham Time of Incident: 12/05/2015 - 13:40 Location: Simons Lane, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters made their way on foot across Frodsham golf course to reach a bracken fire on Fox Hill.  Around 100 square metres of bracken was alight, with the crews using hand extinguishers to put out the blaze.
