 Small chemical leak, Handforth Time of Incident: 13/04/2018 - 17:22 Location: Wilmslow Way, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow, two from Macclesfield and one from Wythenshawe At 5.20pm crews attended to reports of a leak involving sodium bisulphate mixed with sodium hyperchlorite at a gym in Wilmslow Way, Handforth. An evacuation of the premises was carried out and the product removed from the site. Decontamination and ventilation then took place by fire service personnel, using two breathing apparatus and gas tight suits. Two casualties were conveyed to hospital and crews were in attendance for just under four hours.
