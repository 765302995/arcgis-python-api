Wheelie bin on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/06/2016 - 21:21 Location: Station Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a wheelie bin near to a shop which had been deliberately set on fire. The crew used one hose reel to extinguish it. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the shop.  If anyone has any information as to who started this fire they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
