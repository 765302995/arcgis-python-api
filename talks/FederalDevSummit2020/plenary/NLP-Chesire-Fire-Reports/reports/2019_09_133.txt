 Light fitting fire at leisure centre in Knutsford Time of Incident: 06/09/2019 - 09:46 Location: Westfield Drive, Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Lymm and one from Trafford At 9.45am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a light fitting at a leisure centre in Westfield Drive, Knutsford. The fire was out on arrival. Firefighters isolated the electricity supply to the light fitting and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
