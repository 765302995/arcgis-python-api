Chairs set on fire on Wilmslow school field Time of Incident: 05/08/2016 - 22:57 Location: Holly Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Chairs were on fire on a Wilmslow school playing field in what is believed to be deliberate ignition. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and are appealing for anyone with any information to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
