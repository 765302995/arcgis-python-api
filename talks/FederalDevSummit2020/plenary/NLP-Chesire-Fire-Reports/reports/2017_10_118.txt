Rubbish fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 02/10/2017 - 21:23 Location: Wilkinson Street, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a large rubbish fire on Wilkinson Street, Ellesmere Port. Crews found a large amount of refuse on fire, including a sofa, and used a hose reel jet to put it out.
