Car embedded in Crewe house Time of Incident: 11/03/2016 - 18:00 Location: Pyrus Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to deal with an incident involving a car embedded in a house. Fortunately nobody was trapped and there was no fire involved but the crew was concerned that the building was unsafe. They isolated power supplies to the property and asked for a building inspector to attend the scene. Police officers were also there.
