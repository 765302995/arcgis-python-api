 Smoke caused by toaster being left unattended in Alsager Time of Incident: 07/09/2018 - 16:34 Location: Church Road, Alsager Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich At around 4.35pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a domestic property on Church Road in Alsager. On arrival the crew discovered the alarm had been activated due to a toaster being left unattended. Firefighters used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property. For cooking safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
