 Car on fire, M53 motorway Time of Incident: 16/02/2019 - 10:59 Location: Junction 12-11 northbound, M53 motorway Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Firefighters responded to reports of a car smoking heavily on hard shoulder of the M53 northbound carriageway between junctions 12 and 11. They arrived to find the car well alight and used two hose reels and breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames. Two lanes of the motorway were shut while the firefighters carried out their work, and they remained for some time to dampen down the vehicle to ensure there would no reignition. The occupants of the vehicle were unharmed, and crews were at the scene for around 40 minutes in total.
