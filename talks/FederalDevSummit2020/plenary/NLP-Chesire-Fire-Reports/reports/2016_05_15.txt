Fire involving the engine of a coach on the M6 Time of Incident: 27/05/2016 - 15:27 Location: M6 south between junctions 20 and 19 Attendance: Fire engines from Birchwood and Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a fire in the engine compartment of a coach. The fire had been extinguished prior to the arrival of the crew who cooled the area with a hose reel.
