 Smoke coming from an address in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 15/09/2018 - 02:47 Location: Buxton Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton and Macclesfield. Firefighters attended reports of smoke coming from an address in Buxton Road in Macclesfield. Crews discovered two pans had been left on the cooker causing the smoke. Crews removed the pans and used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
