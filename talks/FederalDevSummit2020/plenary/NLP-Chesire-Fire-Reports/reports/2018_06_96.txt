 Two vehicle collision in Doddington Time of Incident: 23/06/2018 - 07:30 Location: London Road, Doddington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - One from Nantwich and one from Crewe Two vehicle collision in DoddingtonFirefighters from Nantwich and Crewe attended reports of a two vehicle collision on the A51 London Road in Doddington.  On arrival crews found that there were four people trapped in the vehicles. Crews used specialist hydraulic cutting equipment to release the casualties and one person was taken to hospital. None of the casualties are believed to have sustained any serious injuries.  The team were in attendance for approximately two hours.
