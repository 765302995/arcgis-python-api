 Collision involving two vehicles in Mobberley Time of Incident: 10/09/2019 - 20:23 Location: Town Lane, Mobberley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Shortly before 8.30pm firefighters were called to a collision in Town Lane, Mobberley. On arrival the crew discovered that there had been a collision involving two vehicles and that no-one was trapped inside either of them. A man involved in the condition was assessed by North West Ambulance Service staff. Firefighters made a vehicle safe and were in attendance for nearly an hour.
