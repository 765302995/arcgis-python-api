 Kitchen fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 10/04/2018 - 03:40 Location: Cledford Lane, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach and one fire engine from Middlewich In the early hours of this morning firefighters were called to a kitchen fire on the ground floor of a two storey house on Cledford Lane in Middlewich.  Crews used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel and a covering jet to put out the blaze.  Firefighters also used stage one ventilation to clear the premises of smoke and ensure the scene was safe.   The incident has been handed to Cheshire Police.
