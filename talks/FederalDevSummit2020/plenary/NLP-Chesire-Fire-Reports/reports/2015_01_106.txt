Kitchen fire in Neston Time of Incident: 01/01/2015 - 22:13 Location: West Vale, Neston Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Merseyside A kitchen fire in Neston was extinguished prior to the arrival of firefighters. The crew ensured the fire was fully extinguished and one of the occupants, a woman, was checked over by paramedics as a precaution.  The cause of the fire is believed to be electrical.
