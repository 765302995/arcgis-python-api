Unattended cooking causes Ellesmere Port kitchen fire Time of Incident: 22/06/2016 - 18:06 Location: Camden Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port  Cooking left unattended caused a kitchen fire at an Ellesmere Port property. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and covering jet to put out the blaze. A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke from the property.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice and never leave cooking unattended.
