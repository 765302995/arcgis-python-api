 Overheated forklift truck batteries in Crewe Time of Incident: 07/08/2018 - 04:17 Location: Weston Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 4.15am firefighters were called to a report of forklift truck batteries overheating outside the Omega Building on Weston Road in Crewe. On arrival the crew found that smoke was coming from the batteries but they were not on fire. Firefighters moved the batteries into the open air and allowed them to vent and cool while checking their temperature with a thermal imaging camera. Once they were satisfied that the batteries were safe firefighters left the scene. They were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
