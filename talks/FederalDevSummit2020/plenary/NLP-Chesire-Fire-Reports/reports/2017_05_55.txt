Smoke logging in building in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/05/2017 - 21:25 Location: Moss Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield and one from Poynton Firefighters were called to slight smoke logging in a nursing home in Macclesfield which is believed to have been caused by a smoking light fitting. All persons were evacuated from the premises and accounted for, and the crew used a thermal imaging camera to check the area.
