Lorry fire at Lymm Service Station Time of Incident: 09/05/2016 - 18:10 Location: Poplar Services, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath The tractor unit of a heavy goods vehicle carrying plant machinery was involved in fire. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was caused by overheated brakes. The crew then damped the area down.
