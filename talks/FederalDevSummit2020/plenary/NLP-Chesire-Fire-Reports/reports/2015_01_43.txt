Serious road traffic collision near Knutsford Time of Incident: 19/01/2015 - 16:00 Location: Chapel Lane, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters were at the scene of a serious road traffic collision at the junction of Chapel Lane and Chester Road at Bucklow Hill.  The collision involved two vehicles, a Fiat Qubo and a BMW coupe.  One man, the driver of the Fiat, sadly sustained fatal injuries at the scene.  .   The A556 was closed between the A50 and Bowden roundabout as emergency services dealt with the incident.   Cheshire Police are appealing to anyone who may have witnessed the collision to make contact via the non-emergency number 101, quoting incident number 515 of 19 January 2015
