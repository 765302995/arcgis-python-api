 Manure fire Time of Incident: 11/06/2018 - 19:43 Location: Chorlton Lane, Cuddington, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas Firefighters from Malpas used a hose reel jet and stack drags to extinguish a pile of horse manure which was on fire at a farm on Chorlton Lane. After extinguishing the fire the crew used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hot spots. The team were in attendance for approximately one hour.
