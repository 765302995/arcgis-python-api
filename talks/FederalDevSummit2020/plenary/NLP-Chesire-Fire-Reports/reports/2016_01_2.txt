Fire in living room in Chester Time of Incident: 29/01/2016 - 21:53 Location: Saughall Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a fire in a living room of a property which spread to upstairs. They used two hose reels and a main jet to extinguish it and everyone at the property was accounted. An investigation into the cause of this fire will take place.
