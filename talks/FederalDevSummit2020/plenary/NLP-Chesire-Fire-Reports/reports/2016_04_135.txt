Caravan fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/04/2016 - 20:36 Location: Warrington Road, Runcorn. Attendance: Fire crews from Runcorn Firefigthers were called to a report of a caravan on fire off Warrington Road, Runcorn.  When they arrived crews found the caravan was fully on fire and that the blaze had spread to a car and a children's play den. Main water and hose reel jets were used to put the fire out. Firefighters also ensured that a Propane cylinder was made safe by cooling it with water.  A fire investigation has now been launched.
