 People trapped after collision in Cuddington Time of Incident: 08/06/2019 - 10:42 Location: Forest Road, Northwich. CW8 2LD Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and one from Winsford Two people were freed from a car by firefighters following a collision on Forest Road, Cuddington. Crews attended shortly before 10:45am after reports that a car had collided with an HGV; they were at the scene for an hour.
