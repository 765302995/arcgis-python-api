Conifers and hedge on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/07/2017 - 11:10 Location: Regent Avenue, Poulton-with-Fearnhead, Warrington Attendance: Fire engines from Lymm, Penketh and Warrington Fire Stations. Conifers and hedge on fire in Warrington Fire engines from Lymm, Penketh and Warrington were sent to a property on Regent Avenue in Poulton-with-Fearnhead, where there was reports of a house fire. On arrival, fire crews found a fire involving conifers and hedges, which had spread to the outside of the property.  Firefighters used three hose reels to extinguish the fire. A thermal imaging camera was used to check for hotspots in the loft space.  Fire crews were in attendance for approximately fifty minutes.
