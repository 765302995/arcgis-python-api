 Injured man stuck on scaffolding in Widnes Time of Incident: 22/10/2019 - 10:16 Location: Tan House Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly after 10.15am firefighters were dispatched to reports of a man being trapped at height in Tan House Lane, Widnes.  On arrival the crew found that the man had fallen and injured his knee whilst working on scaffolding approximately eight foot high.  Firefighters helped to rescue the casualty.  He was brought down to the ground before being handed over to the care of paramedics.
