 Garden fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/04/2019 - 19:14 Location: Maple Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a fire in a garden on Maple Avenue, Runcorn. On arrival crews found a large amount of laminate flooring on fire and they used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
