Fire in derelict building in Crewe Time of Incident: 31/03/2015 - 21:59 Location: Lockitt Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels to extinguish a fire in a derelict building in Crewe. The building was approximately 10m x 20m and the fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Police were informed.  Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111
