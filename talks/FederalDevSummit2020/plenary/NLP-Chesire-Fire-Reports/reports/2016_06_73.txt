Flooding in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/06/2016 - 15:03 Location: Green Lane, Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port The Service has received a number of calls reporting flooding in the Ellesmere Port area. The majority of the calls were able to be dealt with over the phone.  Firefighters went to Green Lane in Great Sutton where around six properties were affected by flooding. Crews used a pump to divert water and asked for council personnel to bring sandbags to the scene.
