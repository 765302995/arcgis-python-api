 HGV overheated brakes in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/02/2019 - 17:29 Location: Birchwood Way, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Birchwood and one from Warrington Fire crews attended to reports of smoke coming from a HGV on the hard shoulder just off junction 11 of the M62, near to Birchwood Way in Warrington.  When fire arrived at the scene the smoke had stopped, however the brakes on the lorry were overheating resulting in crews having to cool them down.  The temperature of the vehicle was then monitored using a thermal imaging camera. Once the lorry was deemed safe it was able to be moved.
