 Lorry fire on M6 Time of Incident: 22/01/2019 - 03:56 Location: M6 southbound junction 16 Attendance:  One fire engine from Crewe, one from Sandbach and one from Holmes Chapel Fire crews were called to a lorry fire on the M6 southbound near to junction 16. Crews found that the rear axel of the lorry was on fire and crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fire. Crews also closed two lanes of the motorway, with assistance from Highways England, whilst they were tackling the fire.
