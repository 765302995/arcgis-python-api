 Aga cooker fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 26/05/2019 - 13:03 Location: Gore Lane, Alderley Edge Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle Fire crews attended to a fire at a house on Gore Lane in Alderley Edge.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, firefighters used one hose reel jet to put out the fire which involved an aga cooker.  Crews were in attendance for around one hour.
