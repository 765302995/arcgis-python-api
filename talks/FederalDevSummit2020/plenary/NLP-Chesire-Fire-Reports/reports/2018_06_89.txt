 Small grass fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/06/2018 - 20:02 Location: Bewsey Park Close, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh. Firefighters from Warrington used a water back pack to extinguish a 2 metre by 2 metre grass fire on Bewsey Park Close in Warrington. The team were in attendance for approximately ten minutes.
