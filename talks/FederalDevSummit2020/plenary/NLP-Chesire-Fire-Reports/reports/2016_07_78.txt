Road traffic collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 17/07/2016 - 17:01 Location: Finlow Hill Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving one saloon car where one male was reported to be trapped. Crews released one male casualty from the vehicle before handing him over into the care of paramedics.
