Fatal road traffic collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 03/01/2017 - 16:42 Location: Speke Road, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving one car with one person trapped. The crew stabilised the vehicle which was in a ditch as a precaution and assisted paramedics with first aid. Sadly, the driver of the vehicle died at the scene.  Cheshire Police have issued the following statement in relation to this collision.  "Police investigating a fatal collision in Widnes are appealing for witnesses.  At 4.35pm on Tuesday 3 January 2017, a silver Vauxhall Vectra came off the A562 Speke Road Liverpool bound carriageway, close to the junction of the Knowsley Expressway.  The driver of the vehicle, believed to be a 48-year-old man from Warrington, sadly died at the scene. A child, who was a passenger in the vehicle, was taken to Alder Hey as a precautionary measure.  Next of kin are being informed and a formal ID is due to take place today.  Anyone with any information in relation to the collision is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 566 of 3 January 2017."
