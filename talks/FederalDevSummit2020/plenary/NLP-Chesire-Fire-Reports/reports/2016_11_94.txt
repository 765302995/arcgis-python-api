Bins on fire outside Winsford house Time of Incident: 12/11/2016 - 02:28 Location: Alamein Drive, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford Recycling bins were on fire outside the front door of an Alamein Drive house. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to damp them down. It is thought the fire may have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
