Road traffic collision in Sproston Time of Incident: 08/10/2017 - 15:16 Location: Holmes Chapel Road, Sproston Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel and one from Sandbach Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision between two vehicles on Holmes Chapel Road, Sproston. Crews administered first aid to three casualties at the scene before handing them over to the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for one hour and 45 minutes.
