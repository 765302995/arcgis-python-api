 House fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 14/10/2018 - 15:29 Location: Lime Walk, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and two from Greater Manchester Firefighters were called to a fire in a terraced property on Lime Walk, Wilmslow. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a large fan to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for two hours.
