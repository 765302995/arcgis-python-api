 Hay fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 02/07/2019 - 17:58 Location: Greaves Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow, one from Lymm, one from Poynton and one from Greater Manchester Rescue Service Firefighters were called to a hay fire on Greaves Road, Wilmslow. The fire measured approximately 15m by 15m. Crews used one hose reel jet to put out the fire. They were at the scene for 25 minutes.
