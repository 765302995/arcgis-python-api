Garage fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 06/01/2017 - 21:59 Location: Park Lane, Sandbach. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Sandbach and Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Park Lane in Sandbach.  When they arrived they found that the fire involved  a garage. Crews wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels and a covering jet to deal with the incident.
