Bin on fire at school in Winsford Time of Incident: 22/04/2015 - 08:31 Location: Greenfields Primary School, Whitbys Lane, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to a bin on fire at a school in Winsford after a passer by raised the alarm. The crew used two buckets of water to extinguish the fire, which believed to have been started accidentally.
