Pan left unattended in Chester Time of Incident: 22/12/2017 - 16:25 Location: Lyon Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Powey Lane Firefighters were called to a pan which had been left unattended on Lyon Street, Chester. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a large fan to ventilate the property. The pan hadn't caught fire but had created smoke.
