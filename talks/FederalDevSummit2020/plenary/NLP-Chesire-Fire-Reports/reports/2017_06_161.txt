Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 04/06/2017 - 01:52 Location: Hough Green Road, Widnes. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters responded to a report of a fire on Hough Green Road, Widnes. When they arrived they found that the fire involved a wheelie bin that was spreading to a fence. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
