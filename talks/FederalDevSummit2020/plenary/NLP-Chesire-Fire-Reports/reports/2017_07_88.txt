Building fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 11/07/2017 - 08:11 Location: Bombardier Transportations, West Street, Crewe Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe, Nantwich, Sandbach, Middlewich, Alsager, Winsford, Congleton, Audlem plus a fire engine and the hydraulic platform from Lymm. Relief crews also attended from Northwich, Runcorn, Macclesfield, Crewe and the hydraulic platform from Chester Building fire in CreweFirefighters were called to a fire involving the roof space of a building  Eight fire engines attended the scene initially along with the hydraulic platform. Four main jets were used to extinguish the fire and to prevent the fire from spreading to other parts of the building.   The premises had been evacuated before the crews arrived and all persons were accounted for.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check the area for hot spots of fire and the crews, which included relief crews, were in attendance for approximately 11 hours and 40 minutes.  The fire is believed to have started accidentally due to an electrical fault.
