 Road traffic collision in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 16/03/2018 - 22:27 Location: Knutsford Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle (Gtr Manchester FRS) One man was trapped in a car after it collided with a building on Knutsford Road. Paramedics stabilised the casualty as firefighters carried out an extrication of the man. They then made the vehicle safe. Roads nearby were closed and a dangerous buildings inspector was called to check structural damage on the affected property.
