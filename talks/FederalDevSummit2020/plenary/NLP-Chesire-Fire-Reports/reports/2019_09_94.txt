 Tree fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 12/09/2019 - 18:14 Location: Springfield Road, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas A hose reel was used to put out a fire involving a tree on Springfield Road in Malpas.  The thermal imaging camera was then used to check the fire was fully extinguished.  Crews then made the area safe.
