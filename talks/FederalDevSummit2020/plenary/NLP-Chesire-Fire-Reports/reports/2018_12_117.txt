 Domestic smoke alarm in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/12/2018 - 22:07 Location: Sutton Way, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 10pm firefighters were called to reports of smoke in a kitchen area at a domestic property in Ellesmere Port. Crews attended the scene and discovered that the smoke had been caused by a plastic colander had melted after being left on top of a hot cooker. The team used a large fan to clear the smoke from the building and were detained at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.
