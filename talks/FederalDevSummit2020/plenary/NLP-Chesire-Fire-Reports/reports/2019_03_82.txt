 Cooking fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 12/03/2019 - 12:22 Location: Wistaston Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to a cooking fire on Wistaston Road, Crewe. The crews found a pan had been left on the cooker and was creating smoke. Crews took the fire outside and extinguished it using a dry powder fire extinguisher. Crews also cleared smoke from the property using a large fan.
