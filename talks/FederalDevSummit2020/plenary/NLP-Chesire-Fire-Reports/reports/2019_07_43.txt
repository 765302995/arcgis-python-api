 Cooking fire in Chester Time of Incident: 23/07/2019 - 21:10 Location: Penmon Close, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a report of a cooking fire on Penmon Close, Chester. Crews discovered that the fire was out on arrival and treated one casualty who was suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation. Crews used a large fan to clear smoke from the property and the casualty was handed into the care of paramedics.
