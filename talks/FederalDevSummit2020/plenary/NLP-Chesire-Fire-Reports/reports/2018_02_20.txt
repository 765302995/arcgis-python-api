 Small fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 24/02/2018 - 13:59 Location: Lower House Lane, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters responded to reports of a small fire near the McDonalds restaurant. Crews found the fire in a bush and used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames.  The fire is thought to have been a deliberate ignition, anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire police on 101, quoting incident 521 of 24 February 2018.
