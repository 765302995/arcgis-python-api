 Rescue of person in Chester Time of Incident: 04/06/2019 - 19:12 Location: Mill Lane, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Chester and Powey Lane Shortly after 7pm firefighters from Chester and Powey Lane were dispatched reports of a person trapped under a train at Bache.  Crews attended the scene and assisted medical professionals. They then used a scoop stretcher to assist with the recovery of the person, who was then passed to the care of paramedics.  Firefighters were detained at the scene for around 90 minutes.
