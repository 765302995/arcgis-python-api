 Rubbish fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 11/05/2019 - 15:44 Location: Bridge Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters tackled a rubbish fire at the side of the road in Bridge Lane, Appleton. It involved a large quantity of loose rubbish and they used a single hose reel to extinguish it. It's believed to have been the deliberate ignition of a suspected fly tipping.
