 Vehicle fire in Hatherton Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 13:42 Location: Audlem Road, Hatherton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters from Nantwich attended reports of a tractor fire at a farm on Audlem Road, Hatherton. The fire was out on arrival, although the team used one hose reel jet to cool the vehicle and a thermal imaging camera to check for any hot spots. The team were in attendance for approximately 40 minutes.
