 Peat bog on fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 17/05/2018 - 10:19 Location: Moor Lane, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a peat bog on fire on land off Moor Lane in Wilmslow. On arrival crews discovered 40 square metres of smoking peat. Firefighters used water and hand beaters to extinguish the fire.
