 Wheelie bins on fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 14/06/2018 - 21:02 Location: Dean Row Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich (providing cover at Wilmslow) Two wheelie bins had been set on fire on Dean Row Road. A quantity of plastic was also on fire, all of which were in an open storage shed. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put the fire out followed by a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.  Please follow our wheelie bin safety advice.
