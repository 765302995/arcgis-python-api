Large amount of wood on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/04/2016 - 21:32 Location: Radley Lane, Winwick, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A large amount of wood was set on fire on Radley Lane in Winwick. Firefighters tackled the blaze and police were asked to attend.  April is Arson Awareness Month. If you see anyone deliberately setting nuisance fires please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
