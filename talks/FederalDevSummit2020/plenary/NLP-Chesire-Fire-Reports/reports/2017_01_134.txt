Tiled chimney fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 02/01/2017 - 16:24 Location: Gleave Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich A coal fire in a property in Northwich was extinguished by firefighters at the hearth, whilst other crews monitored the chimney with a thermal imaging camera to check for any remaining hot spots of fire.  Clean your chimney regularly to help prevent a fire.
