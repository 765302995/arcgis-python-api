Road traffic collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/11/2015 - 11:05 Location: Thomas Jones Way, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision on Thomas Jones Way in Runcorn with one person trapped.  Fire crews removed the roof of one vehicle so that ambulance personnel were able to release the casualty and take them to hospital.
