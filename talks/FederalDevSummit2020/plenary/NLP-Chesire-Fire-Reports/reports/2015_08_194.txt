Road traffic collision on M6 Time of Incident: 02/08/2015 - 16:09 Location: M6 north junction 18 to 19 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a three vehicle road traffic collision on the M6 between junctions 18 to 19 north. The road was closed while emergency services made the scene safe, moving the cars to the hard shoulder.  Nobody was trapped in the collision.
