Rubbish fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 24/05/2015 - 16:46 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: A fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters tackled a small rubbish fire with hand held extinguishers.  The incident took place  near the Greyhound Social Club on Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port.
