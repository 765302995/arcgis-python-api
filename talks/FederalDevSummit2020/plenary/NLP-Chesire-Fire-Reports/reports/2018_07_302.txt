 Hedgerow fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 17:32 Location: A556, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 5.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on the A556 in Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a hedgerow. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for less than 10 minutes.
