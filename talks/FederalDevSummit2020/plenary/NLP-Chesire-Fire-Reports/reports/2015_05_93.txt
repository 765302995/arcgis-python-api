House fire in Chester Time of Incident: 12/05/2015 - 17:20 Location: Denbigh street, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Two fire engines from Chester were called to a fire at a property on Denbigh street in Chester. The fire involved the ground floor of the property. It was caused by an electrical heater. Firefighters used four breathing apparatus and two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.
