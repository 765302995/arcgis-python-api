Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/04/2016 - 01:47 Location: Salisbury Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Deliberate ignition was the cause of a car fire in Warrington. The fire was out when the crew arrived. They thoroughly checked the vehicle using a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no further areas of heat that could reignite.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 stating incident number 47 of 15 April.
