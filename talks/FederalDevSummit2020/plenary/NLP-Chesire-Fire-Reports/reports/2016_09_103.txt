Bedroom fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 10/09/2016 - 21:14 Location: Manor Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes A woman was rescued from a property following a fire at the property.  Firefighters gained entry to the house to rescue the woman.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  One hose reel jet was used to extinguish the fire which was in the bedroom of the property.  A large fan was then used to clear the smoke.  A fire investigation will be completed to find the cause of the fire.
