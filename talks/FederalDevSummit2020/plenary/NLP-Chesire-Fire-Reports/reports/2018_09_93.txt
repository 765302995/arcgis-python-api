 Smouldering tree in a park in Handforth Time of Incident: 11/09/2018 - 06:51 Location: Dean Road, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 6.50am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in Parsonage Park on Dean Road in Handforth. On arrival the crew discovered a tree smouldering in the park. Firefighters used a large axe and water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
