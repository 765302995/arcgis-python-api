 Chimney fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 02/11/2018 - 10:35 Location: Weston Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to reports of a chimney fire at Crewe Hall on Weston Road in Crewe.  A full evacuation took place and all floors of the building were checked for hot spots or fire spreading using a thermal imaging camera.   Crews gained access to the roof of the building and assessed from the grate of the chimney using chimney rods.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet on the roof to extinguish the fire while continuing to check the floor with the thermal imaging camera.  Crews left the scene at approximately 1.20pm.
