Car on fire on the M6 northbound Time of Incident: 28/05/2015 - 05:09 Location: M6, northbound between junctions 21A and 21. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A severe fire involved a small car on the M6 caused Police to close all the northbound side lanes of the motorway while firefighters extinguished the fire. The fire occurred between junctions 21A and 21 and the crew used a hose reel and foam to fight the fire.
