 Bin fire outside a church in Widnes Time of Incident: 15/06/2018 - 13:36 Location: Vicarage Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 1.35pm firefighters were called to a report of a bin being on fire outside St Pauls Church on Vicarage Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew found that a bin in front of the church was alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They believe that the fire was started by a discarded cigarette. The modern bin has a metal tube attached to it for discarded cigarettes. Firefighters were in attendance for around five minutes.
