 Fire involving a toaster in Widnes Time of Incident: 16/11/2019 - 17:23 Location: Brandon, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Widnes and Penketh Shortly before 5.30pm firefighters from Widnes and Penketh attended reports of a kitchen fire on Brandon. On arrival at the scene the crews found the fire had involved an electric toaster which had been extinguished by the occupant. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property. One person was treated at the scene by paramedics for smoke inhalation. Crews were in attendance at the scene for around 40 minutes.
