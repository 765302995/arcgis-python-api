Sofa on grass verge on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/05/2016 - 23:03 Location: Edgeworth Street, Bewsey and Whitecross, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A sofa on a grass verge in Edgeworth Street was well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  Arson can be dangerous and can prevent firefighters from getting to real emergencies. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
