 Three wheelie bin fires dealt with by crews in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/02/2018 - 21:16 Location: Dundalk Road, widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Fire crews were called to extinguish three wheelie bins fires reported ablaze on Dundalk Road in Widnes. Firefighters used one hose reel to put out the fire located near a community centre.
