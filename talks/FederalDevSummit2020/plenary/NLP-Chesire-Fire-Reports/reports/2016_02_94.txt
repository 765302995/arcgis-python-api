Grill pan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/02/2016 - 17:22 Location: Hillock lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters dealt with a gill pan fire, where the grill had been turned on and then left unattended, at a property on Hillock lane in Warrington. The crews isolated the gas supply from outside the property and then checked the house was clear of fire and smoke.  Kitchen fire safety
