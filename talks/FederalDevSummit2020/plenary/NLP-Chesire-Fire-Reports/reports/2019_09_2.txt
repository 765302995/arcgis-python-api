 Electrical fault in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 28/09/2019 - 23:14 Location: Moss Lane, Macclesfield. SK11 7XE Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton and one from Wilmslow Firefighters were called after reports of an electrical fault.  Crews found flood water had leaked into the electrics and isolated the sockets to make them safe.
