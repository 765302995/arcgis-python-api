 Tipper truck trapped in flood water in Cheadle Time of Incident: 29/07/2019 - 12:23 Location: A555, Cheadle. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton. One fire engine attended from Warrington. Friefighters were called to the A555 in Cheadle following reports of a tipper truck trapped in flood water.  The water us estimated to be one metre deep. Two swift water raft operatives used a raft to get to the driver to assess his welfare. Paramedics are at the scene as a precaution.  The driver remained in the cab to steer the vehicle while crews assisted with the recovery of the vehicle. Crews were in attendance until 2.30pm.
