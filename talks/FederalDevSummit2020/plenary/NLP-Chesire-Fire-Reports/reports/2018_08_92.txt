 Small fire in the open, Disley Time of Incident: 11/08/2018 - 16:29 Location: Mudhurst Lane, Disley Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Crews were called following reports of a tree on fire. They arrived to find three small seats of fire at the base of a silver birch tree. Firefighters used a hose reel and water backpack to extinguish each fire.
