Chimney fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 18/10/2016 - 19:23 Location: Brooklands Road, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines from Congleton Firefighters put out a chimney fire on Brooklands Road. The fire is believed to have involved a shared chimney void between two houses. A thermal imaging camera was used to locate the fire which was then extinguished.
