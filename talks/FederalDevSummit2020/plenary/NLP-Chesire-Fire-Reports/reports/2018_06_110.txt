 Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/06/2018 - 15:04 Location: Hallwood Park Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 3.05pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at Hallwood Park Avenue in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a small quantity of grass. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
