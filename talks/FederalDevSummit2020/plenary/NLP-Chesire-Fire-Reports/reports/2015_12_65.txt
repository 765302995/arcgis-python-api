Serious road traffic collision in Rudheath Time of Incident: 14/12/2015 - 12:35 Location: A530 Rudheath, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Middlewich Fire and rescue crews were called to a serious road traffic collision on the A530, King Street.  The collision involved three vehicles, two cars and a heavy goods vehicle. Sadly, one man, the driver of one of the cars, was confirmed dead at the scene.  The A530 King Street remains closed in both directions between Croxton Lane, Middlewich and the A556 Gadbrook roundabout. Diversions are in place via the A533.  Cheshire Police are appealing for witnesses to the collision.
