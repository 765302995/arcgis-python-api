 Building fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 26/12/2018 - 10:16 Location: Marley Road, Poynton. Attendance: Four fire engines attended - one from Poynton, one from Macclesfield and two from Greater Manchester At around 10.16am on Boxing Day firefighters from Poynton and Greater Manchester were called to reports of a fire at a community building on Marley Lane, Poynton. On arrival at the premises the team discovered a fire in the roof space of a detached building measuring approximately 30m x 15m. Additional crews from Macclesfield and Greater Manchester attended the scene. Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels to fight the fire. Crews isolated the gas and electricity supply and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots after the fire was extinguished. Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately five and a half hours.
