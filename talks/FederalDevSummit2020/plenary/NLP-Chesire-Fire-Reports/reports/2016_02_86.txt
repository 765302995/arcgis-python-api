Chimney fire in Rode Heath Time of Incident: 10/02/2016 - 19:11 Location: Sandbach Road, Rode Heath Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and one fire engine from Crewe A chimney fire was well alight, causing the roof space of a property to be full of smoke.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet and chimney rods to deal with the fire.   Chimney fire safety advice
