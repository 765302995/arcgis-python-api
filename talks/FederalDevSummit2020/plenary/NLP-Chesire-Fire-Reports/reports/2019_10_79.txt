 Van fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/10/2019 - 17:36 Location: Cullen Road, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a fire involving a van in Cullen Road, Runcorn. On arrival crews could see smoke filling the van and used breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  They then used a thermal imaging camera to monitor the heat levels. The fire is thought to be an electrical fault.
