 Vehicle fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/11/2018 - 11:32 Location: Tarporley Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters used a hose reel to cool down the engine of a car which was on fire on Tarporley Road in Warrington.  Crews then used a thermal imaging camera to check the temperature before making the area safe.
