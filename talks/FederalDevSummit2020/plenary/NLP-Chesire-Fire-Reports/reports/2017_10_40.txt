Building fire in Chester Time of Incident: 21/10/2017 - 12:27 Location: Newhall Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh, one fire engine from Powey Lane, one fire engine from Ellesmere Port On Saturday 21 October at 12:25pm firefighters were called to reports of a disused commercial building on fire on Newhall Road, Chester. One fire engine from Ellesmere Port, one fire engine from Powey Lane and one fire engine from Penketh attended the scene. Firefighters extinguished the fire involving the first floor using four breathing apparatus, two hose reel jets, two safety jets and a positive pressure ventilation fan. Firefighters were on the scene for almost three hours.
