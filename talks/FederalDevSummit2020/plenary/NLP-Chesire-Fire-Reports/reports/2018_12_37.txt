 House fire in Appleton Time of Incident: 21/12/2018 - 13:33 Location: Ash Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Lymm and one from Warrington Firefighters dealt with a fire at a house on Ash Lane in Appleton, Warrington. On arrival smoke could be seen coming from the house. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. A large high pressure fan was then used to disperse smoke. The fire is believed to have been caused by a plastic bag being placed on an oven. No-one is believed to have been in the property at the time of the fire.
