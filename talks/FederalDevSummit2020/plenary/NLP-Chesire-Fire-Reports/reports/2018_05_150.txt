 Road traffic collision on the M62 Time of Incident: 12/05/2018 - 15:34 Location: J11-12, M62 motorway Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Irlam (Manchester) Crews responded to reports of a single vehicle road traffic collision on the westbound carriageway of the M62 motorway. A blue Citroen had collided with the barrier. The 25-year-old female driver had exited the vehicle but was  given oxygen therapy by crews. Lanes 1 and 2 were closed as firefighters made the vehicle safe.
