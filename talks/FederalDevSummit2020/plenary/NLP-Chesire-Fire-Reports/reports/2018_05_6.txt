 Garden fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 29/05/2018 - 21:25 Location: Alexandra Square, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford One fire engine from Winsford attended reports of a fire on Alexandra Square. On arrival crews found that the fire involved a mattress which had been deliberately set alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire and police took away two men in relation to the incident.
