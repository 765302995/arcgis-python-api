Road traffic collision in Tremlow Time of Incident: 14/11/2016 - 16:03 Location: Macclesfield Road, Twemlow Attendance: One rapid response unit from Holmes Chapel and two fire engines from Holmes Chapel and Knutsford Two private vehicles collided in Tremlow, leaving three people injured. Firefighters worked with the paramedics on scene to release one person from one of the cars.
