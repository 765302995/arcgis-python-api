 Fire in a commercial building in Knutsford Time of Incident: 17/07/2019 - 04:52 Location: Bexton Lane, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford, one from Lymm and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a fire in a commercial building on Bexton Lane, Knutsford. The fire involved an industrial fryer in a kitchen of a two storey property. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fire. The building was then checked for hotspots using a thermal imaging camera. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
