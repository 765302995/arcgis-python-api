 Refrigeration unit fault in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/11/2019 - 05:21 Location: Lugsdale Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Penketh Firefighters were called to check a refrigeration unit after it was found to be making a hissing noise and leaking. Firefighters isolated the unit and carried out a gas check which found no dangerous gas present so an engineer was called to attend.
