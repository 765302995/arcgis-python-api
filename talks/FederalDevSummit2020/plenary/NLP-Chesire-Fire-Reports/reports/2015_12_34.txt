Kitchen fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 23/12/2015 - 10:12 Location: Hawthorn Lane, Wistaston Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Unattended cooking caused a fire in the kitchen of a Wistaston bungalow. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They gave oxygen therapy to the occupier who was suffering slight smoke inhalation and they were then handed into the care of the ambulance service.  Smoke alarms were fitted at the property.  They activated and alerted the householder to the fire.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice to avoid a fire in your home - Kitchen fire safety
