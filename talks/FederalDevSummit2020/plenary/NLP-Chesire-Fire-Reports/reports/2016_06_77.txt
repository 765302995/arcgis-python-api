Cow rescued from a fast flowing river in Byley, Northwich Time of Incident: 15/06/2016 - 17:18 Location: King Street, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Knutsford, and the rope rescue unit from Knutsford A cow was stuck in the river by King Street in Byley. A man was in the river, helping the cow by keeping its head out of the water. The water was fast flowing.  Firefighters rescued the cow, by fitting strops to the animal and then lifting it out of the water using a mechanical hoist.  The man was checked over by paramedics as he had been in the water for approximately 90 minutes.
