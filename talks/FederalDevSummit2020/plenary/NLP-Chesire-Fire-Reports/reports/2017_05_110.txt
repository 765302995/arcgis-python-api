Collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 13/05/2017 - 14:19 Location: Lunts Heath Road, Widnes. Attendance: Fire engines from Widnes and Penketh Firefighters responded to a report of a collision on Lunts Heath Road, Widnes.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two cars. There was nobody trapped but firefighters did give first aid to a woman who had been hurt in the incident. She was then taken to hospital.
