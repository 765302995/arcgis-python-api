Grill pan fire in Widnes home Time of Incident: 30/12/2015 - 15:49 Location: Mill Brow, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a residential home on Mill Brow, Widnes.  When they arrived crews found that there was a small fire in a grill pan. Hand held extinguishers were used to put the fire out.  There were no reports of any injuries.
