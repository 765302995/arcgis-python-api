 Dog stuck between two fences in Crewe Time of Incident: 02/01/2019 - 09:47 Location: Goulden Street, Crewe Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 9.45am firefighters were called to reports of a dog being stuck between two fences on Goulden Street in Crewe. On arrival the crew discovered that the dog was not physically trapped but could not get over or under the fences separating two gardens. Firefighters lifted a fence panel to get the Japanese Akita out of the space between the gardens and then left the dog in the care of its owner. The crew were in attendance for around 25 minutes.
