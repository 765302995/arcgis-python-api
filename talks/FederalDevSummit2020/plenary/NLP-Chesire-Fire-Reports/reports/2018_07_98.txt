 Hay fire in Kermincham Time of Incident: 19/07/2018 - 04:02 Location: Forty Acre Lane, Kermincham Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel, from Congleton and one from Middlewich Hay fire in KerminchamFire crews were called to a hay fire on Forty Acre Lane, Kermincham. Crews used two hose reel jets and a digger to tackle the fire which consisted of 40 small hay bales in an open barn.  Crews used a thermal imaging camera to check the hay before leaving the site.
