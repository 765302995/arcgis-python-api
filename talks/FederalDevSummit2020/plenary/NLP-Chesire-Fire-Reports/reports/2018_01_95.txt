 Small fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 09/01/2018 - 00:46 Location: The Deck, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to reports of a building fire at the Deck in Runcorn. Crews attended the scene and found the fire involved a small pile of magazines in the reception area which had been deliberately set on fire; the fire was out on arrival. Anyone with any information in relation to the fire is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 24 of 9/1/2017. Alternatively information can be reported anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on  0800 555 111.
