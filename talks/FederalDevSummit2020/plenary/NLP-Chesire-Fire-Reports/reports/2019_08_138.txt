 Tree fire near to a house in Nantwich Time of Incident: 05/08/2019 - 15:39 Location: Jackson Avenue, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Nantwich Shortly after 3.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a conifer tree close to a house in Jackson Avenue, Nantwich. The fire was out of arrival. It had caused minor damage to fascias on the house. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to damp down the areas affected by the fire and make them safe. They were in attendance for less than 10 minutes.
