Office on fire in Tattenhall Time of Incident: 03/05/2017 - 19:22 Location: Bolesworth Road, Tattenhall, Chester Attendance: Fire engines from Powey Lane, Malpas, Tarporley, Ellesmere Port and Chester aerial ladder platform with supporting fire engine Firefighters were called to a fire involving a brick built building used as an office on Bolesworth Road. Five fire engines and an aerial ladder platform attended the fire, which had spread to the roof space. Crews used a water relay and hose reel jets to tackle the blaze.
