Lorry fire on M6 between junctions 16 and 17 northbound Time of Incident: 02/11/2016 - 20:49 Location: M6 northbound between junctions 16 and 17 Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe and Sandbach The brakes of a heavy goods vehicle were on fire on the M6 between junctions 16 and 17 northbound.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A thermal imaging camera was then used to check there were no further hot spots.
