Land on fire in Rainow Time of Incident: 16/02/2016 - 00:16 Location: Macclesfield Road, Rainow, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of gorseland on fire on Macclesfield Road. An area of approximately 400m x 100m was involved in a fire which is believed to be a controlled burning but there was nobody in attendance. Crews used beaters and a hose reel jet to put out the fire due to its proximity to the road.
