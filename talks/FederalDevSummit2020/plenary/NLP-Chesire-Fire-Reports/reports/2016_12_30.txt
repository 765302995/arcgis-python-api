Light aircraft incident Time of Incident: 27/12/2016 - 12:07 Location: Swanlow Lane, Winsford. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Winsford Fire crews were called to a report that a light aircraft was in difficulty off Swanlow Lane, Winsford.  When they arrived firefighters confirmed that a plane had landed after suffering engine problems. Thankfully all on board were safe and had suffered no injuries.  There was no action required by firefighters at the scene.
