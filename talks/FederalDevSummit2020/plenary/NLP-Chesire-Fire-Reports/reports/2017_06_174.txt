Nine people rescued in a lift in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 01/06/2017 - 12:36 Location: Mill Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Nine people were rescued from a lift at Marks and Spencer's on Mill Street, Macclesfield, by firefighters. The lift had become stuck between two floors and seven adults and two children had to be rescued. Fire crews manually lowered the lift to get the people out.
