Road traffic collision in Bridgemere Time of Incident: 16/09/2016 - 17:07 Location: London Road, Bridgemere, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Audlem A casualty was trapped in a car after two vehicles collided on London Road. Firefighters released the casualty by removing a door and then handed them into the care of ambulance staff. Firefighters were at the scene for approximately 40 minutes. Police were also at the scene.
