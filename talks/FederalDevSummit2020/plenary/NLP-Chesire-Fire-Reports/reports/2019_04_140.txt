 Deliberate garden fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 15/04/2019 - 16:09 Location: Mossford Avenue, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe used one hose reel jet to extinguish a bush which had been deliberately set alight outside a residential address on Mossford Avenue.  Anyone with any information in relation to the fire is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML374984. Information can also be reported anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111.
