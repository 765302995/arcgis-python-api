Steam turbine on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 29/11/2016 - 17:34 Location: United Utilities, Mersey Valley Processing Centre, Bennetts Lane, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Widnes There was a fire involving an internal steam turbine, so fire crews used one hose reel jet, dry powder extinguishers and thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire.
