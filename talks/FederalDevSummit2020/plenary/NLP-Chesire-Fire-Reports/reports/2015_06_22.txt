Road traffic collision on the M62 Eastbound Time of Incident: 24/06/2015 - 16:27 Location: M62, between junctions 7 and 8 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Two saloon cars were involved in a road traffic collision on the M62 eastbound between junctions 7 and 8. All eastbound lanes were closed while the crews dealt with the incident. Two casualties were trapped in the incident and needed the help of firefighters to get out of vehicles.  Paramedics also attended along with the Highways Agency. A HGV also suffered minor damage in this incident.
