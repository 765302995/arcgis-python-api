Microwave fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/03/2016 - 15:09 Location: Vine Crescent, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a microwave. The fire was out on arrival but there was a lot of smoke in the property so crews used a high pressure fan to ventilate it.  Always follow our Kitchen fire safety advice
