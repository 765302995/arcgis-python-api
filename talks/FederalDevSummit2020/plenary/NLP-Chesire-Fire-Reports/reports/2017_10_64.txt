Road traffic collision in Rostherne Time of Incident: 17/10/2017 - 07:29 Location: Chester Road, Rostherne Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision between two cars on Chester Road, Rostherne. Firefighters gave one casualty first aid at the scene before handing them into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for 35 minutes.
