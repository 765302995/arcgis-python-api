Tumble dryer on fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 18/01/2016 - 13:32 Location: Cronkinson Avenue, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Crewe Firefighters were called to a tumble dryer which had been on fire but was out on arrival. A thermal imaging camera was used to check the property and the tumble dryer was removed to outside the property. Paramedics also attended.
