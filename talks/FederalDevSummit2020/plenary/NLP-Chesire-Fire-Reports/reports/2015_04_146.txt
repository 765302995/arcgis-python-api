Horse rescued from bog in Gawsworth Time of Incident: 12/04/2015 - 08:52 Location: Tidnock Lane, Gawsworth Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Bollington A horse stuck in a bog in Gawsworth was rescued by firefighters. The Animal Rescue Unit from Bollington went to the scene along with a fire engine from Macclesfield and found the 14 hand high horse firmly stuck by its forelegs.  Once the animal had been sedated by the vet the crew used farm machinery to remove the hose from the bog and back into the care of its very happy owner.
