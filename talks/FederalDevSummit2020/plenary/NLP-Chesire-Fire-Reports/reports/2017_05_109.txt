Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 13/05/2017 - 15:53 Location: Highlands Road, Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters responded to a report of a fire off Highlands Road, Runcorn.  When they arrived they were confronted with a fire involving grass and gorse. Crews used hose reel jets to put the fire out.
