Road traffic collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 11/06/2015 - 15:22 Location: Junction of Congleton Road and Church Lane, Gawsworth, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Congleton Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving three cars on Congleton Road.   Two cars were on their sides with a casualty trapped in each of them.   Crews released the man and woman from their cars and handed them into the care of paramedics.
