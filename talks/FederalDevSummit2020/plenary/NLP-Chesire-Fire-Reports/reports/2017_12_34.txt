Fire in care home in Chester Time of Incident: 20/12/2017 - 17:42 Location: Nevin Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Chester Fire crews were called to a fire in a residential care home on Nevin Road, Chester. Crews found that the fire involved an electric panel heater in a bedroom and firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a large fan to bring the fire under control. The flat had been evacuated prior to the fire crews' arrival. Crews were at the scene for 45 minutes.
