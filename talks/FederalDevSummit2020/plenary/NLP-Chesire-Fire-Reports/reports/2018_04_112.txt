 Fire at the back of a property in Frodsham Time of Incident: 10/04/2018 - 09:08 Location: Crossland Terrace, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters are currently dealing with a fire at the back of a property on Crossland Terrace in Frodsham.  Crews are using two breathing apparatus, one hose reel jet and a large fan to extinguish the fire.
