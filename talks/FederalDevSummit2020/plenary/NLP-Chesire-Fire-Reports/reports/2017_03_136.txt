Road traffic collision M6 south junction 16 Time of Incident: 09/03/2017 - 14:36 Location: M6 southbound junctions 16 to 17 Attendance: Fire engines from Middlewich, Sandbach and Winsford Three vehicles have been involved in a road traffic collision. Two heavy goods vehicles and a car were involved and one of the HGVs was on fire. Firefighters used environment packs to manage the engine oils spilt onto the roadway. Police, ambulance and highways agency have all been at the attendance.  Firefighters have now left the scene but the motorway remains closed in both directions between junctions 16 and 18.
