 Bus fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/08/2019 - 11:46 Location: High Street, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Runcorn and one from Widnes At around 11.45am firefighters from Runcorn and Widnes were dispatched to reports of a bus fire on Runcorn High Street.  On arrival the crews discovered a fire in the engine compartment of the vehicle which had spread to the back seat of the bus.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots, while continuing to use a hose reel jet to cool them down.  There were no reports of any injuries and firefighters were at the scene for around 45 minutes.
