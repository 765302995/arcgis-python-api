Building fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/12/2017 - 21:42 Location: Hough Green Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes. One fire engine from Penketh. Firefighters attended a fire at a business premises in Hough Green Road in Widnes. Crews used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel and a large fan to extinguish the fire.
