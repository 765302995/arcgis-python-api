 Lagre fire in the open in Wincham Time of Incident: 16/05/2019 - 13:58 Location: Earles Lane, Wincham, near Northwich. Attendance: Three fire engines attended from Northwich, Knutsford and Lymm Shortly before 2pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in Wincham. On arrival at the scene crews discovered that there had been a small fire which had got out of control and spread to a nearby reed bed. The fire was approximately 50 metres by 100 metres. The crews used one hose reel jet and beaters to tackle the fire and were detained at the scene for around two hours.
