Bin lorry on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/04/2017 - 07:11 Location: Cherry Tree Avenue, Lymm, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood A bin lorry which was well alight required firefighters to use a hose reel and a main water jet to extinguish it. The fire occurred in the back of the lorry and involved recycled materials and the crew used a ladder to access the fire. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the vehicle for hot spots.
