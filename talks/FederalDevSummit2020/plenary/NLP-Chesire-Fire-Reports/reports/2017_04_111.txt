Chimney fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 06/04/2017 - 08:43 Location: Birch Brook Road, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Firefighters tackled a chimney fire from the roof of a Birch Brook Road house. A seven metre ladder, roof ladder and height safety pack were used to gain access. A hose reel jet was used to tackle the fire.  Always follow our chimney safety advice.
