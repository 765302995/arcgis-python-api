 Hay on fire near a house in Congleton Time of Incident: 01/04/2018 - 23:21 Location: Loachbrook Farm Way, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach and one from Congleton Firefighters were called to a fire at a house on Loachbrook Farm Way in Congleton involving a large pile of hay on fire.  A covering jet and hose reel was used by crews to extinguish the fire.
