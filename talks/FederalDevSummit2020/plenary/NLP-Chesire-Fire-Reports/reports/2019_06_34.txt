 Large fire in the open in Minshull Vernon Time of Incident: 22/06/2019 - 22:14 Location: Eardswick Lane, Minshull Vernon Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At around 10.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Eardswick Lane in Minshull Vernon. On arrival the crews found that it was a large unattended fire involving rubbish. Firefighters extinguished the fire with two hose reel jets and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
