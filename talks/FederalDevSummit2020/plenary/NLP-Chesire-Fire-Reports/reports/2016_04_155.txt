Railway embankment on fire in Church Lawton Time of Incident: 01/04/2016 - 17:33 Location: Congleton Road North, Church Lawton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton Approximately 50 metres of railway embankment was on fire in Church Lawton. The trains on the Crewe to Stoke line were stopped while firefighters tackled the blaze using a hose reel jet.
