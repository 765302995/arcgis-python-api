Collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 27/02/2016 - 11:53 Location: A500 in Crewe. Attendance: Three fire engines from Crewe and Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on the A500 in Crewe.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved one car and a HGV. One man was trapped in his vehicle. Crews released the man who was then passed to the care of the ambulance service who took him to hospital.
