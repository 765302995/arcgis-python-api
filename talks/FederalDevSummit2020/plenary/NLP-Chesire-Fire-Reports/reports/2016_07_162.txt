Water rescue in Wildboarclough Time of Incident: 03/07/2016 - 10:36 Location: Clough House Lane, Wildboarclough, near Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and the line rescue unit and supporting fire engine from Knutsford A spectator at a motorcycle trials slipped down a rocky embankment into water. Firefighters and the line rescue unit rescued the casualty, who had fallen around seven metres, from a tributary to Cumberland Brook and handed him into the care of the air ambulance. Crews were at the scene for around one hour.
