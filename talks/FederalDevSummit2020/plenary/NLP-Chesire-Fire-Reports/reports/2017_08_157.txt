Cylinders on fire at engineering company in Winsford Time of Incident: 02/08/2017 - 16:23 Location: Deakin's Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and one from Northwich Two cylinders, one oxygen and one acetylene, part of a mobile welding kit, were on fire at a business on Deakin's Road. The premises were evacuated while the fire was put out. Firefighters, two in breathing apparatus, were committed to the building with a hose reel jet to move the cylinders to a safe area outside the building. The hose reel jet was used to cool the cylinders down.
