 Grass fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 03/07/2018 - 08:31 Location: Brooks Lane, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Firefighters were called to a grass fire in Brooks Lane, Middlewich. On arrival the fire had spread to bushes and was approximately 3metres by 6metres in size. Crews used a hose reel to put out the fire.
