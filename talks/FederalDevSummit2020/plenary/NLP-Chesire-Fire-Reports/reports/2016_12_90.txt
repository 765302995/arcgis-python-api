Horse rescued from a ditch in Nantwich Time of Incident: 14/12/2016 - 13:43 Location: London Road, Stapeley Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Knutsford which attended with the animal rescue unit. Firefighters were called to rescue a horse from a ditch and used specialist equipment to enable them to the get the 13-year-old mare out. Poppet who is a 16.2 hands high Friesian, was sedated by a vet while she was helped to safety and was none the worse for wear after her ordeal.
