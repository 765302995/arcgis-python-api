Kitchen on fire at a property in Orford, Warrington Time of Incident: 16/05/2015 - 03:18 Location: Windermere Avenue, Orford, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington There was a fire inside the kitchen of a property on Windermere Avenue in Orford, Warrington. A man was suffering from smoke inhalation.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire.  The gas and electricity were isolated.  Two large fans were used to clear the smoke from the property. Police also attended this incident.
