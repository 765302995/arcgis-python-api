Building fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 09/05/2017 - 19:21 Location: Seacombe Drive, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port Crews were called to a report of a building fire off Seacombe Drive, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived  they found a fire in an empty building. Two hose reel jets were used to tackle the fire.
