Campervan involved in fire in Delamere Time of Incident: 24/04/2016 - 17:32 Location: Delamere Forest Campsite, Station Road, Delamere Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A campervan which had been involved in a fire on a campsite was attended by firefighters. The crew cooled the vehicle with a hose reel. One person was treated by paramedics. It is believed that a leak from an LPG cylinder and regulator caused the fire.  Camping safety
