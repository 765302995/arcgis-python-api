Chip pan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/08/2015 - 19:49 Location: Greenings Court, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Birchwood A chip pan fire left a Warrington resident needing treatment for minor burns. The occupier had put the fire out but had received burns and was treated by a first responder. Firefighters used a high pressure fan to clear the property of smoke and checked the flats each side of the affected property, along with the one above, to check there had been no spread of fire.  The casualty was taken to hospital as a precaution.  Please follow our safety advice - Chip pans
