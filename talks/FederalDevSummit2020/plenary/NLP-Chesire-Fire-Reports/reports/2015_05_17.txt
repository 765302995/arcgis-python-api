Bonfire out of control in Weaverham Time of Incident: 26/05/2015 - 22:15 Location: Walnut Avenue, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A bonfire had been left unattended and got out of control in Weaverham.  Firefighters used hand held extinguishers to put out the blaze.  Please always make sure bonfires are fully out before you leave them.
