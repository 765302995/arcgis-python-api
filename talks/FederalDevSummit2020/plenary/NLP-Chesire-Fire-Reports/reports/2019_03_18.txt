 Go kart on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/03/2019 - 20:24 Location: Stewards Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish a go kart which was well alight on Stewards Avenue in Widnes.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
