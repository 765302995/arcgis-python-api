 Fire at farm in Northwich Time of Incident: 01/03/2018 - 23:19 Location: Bentleys Farm Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm and one from Warrington attended At around 11.20pm two fire engines - one from Lymm Fire Station and one from Warrington Fire Station - were called to a fire at Bentleys Farm Lane, Northwich. Crews used two hose reel jets and breathing apparatus to tackle the fire which involved a quantity of timber. Firefighters were at the scene for around two hours.
