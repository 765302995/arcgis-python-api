Smoke in Thornton-le-Moors house Time of Incident: 02/08/2017 - 21:51 Location: Poole Lane, Thornton-le-Moors, near Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Two fire engines were called to a report of smoke filling the first floor of a Poole Lane house. Two firefighters in breathing apparatus went into the property with a hose reel jet. One person was led to safety by the crew from the ground floor. A high pressure fan was used to clear the smoke. The occupier reported not suffering adverse effects from the incident and an ambulance was not required.
