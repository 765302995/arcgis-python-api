Horse stuck in a pond in Bickerton Time of Incident: 26/08/2015 - 07:57 Location: Bulkeley Hall Lane, Bickerton. Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and Knutsford and Animal Rescue Unit from Knutsford. Firefighters are currently rescuing a horse stuck in a pond in Bickerton.  Restraints were set up and a clearing established by crews in harnesses who released the horse from the water.  The teams are currently working to get the horse up the steep embankment and a farmer has provided mechanical equipment to assist with this.  A vet is also at the scene.  Update at 13:30 Fire crews have safely brought the horse up the embankment. It is now in the care of its owner and a vet.
