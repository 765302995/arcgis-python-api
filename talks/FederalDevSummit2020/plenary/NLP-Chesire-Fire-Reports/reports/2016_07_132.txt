Light on fire in an outbuilding in South Runcorn Time of Incident: 08/07/2016 - 10:30 Location: Heath Road, South Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a fire in an outbuilding behind a church in South Runcorn, in which the building's light had caught fire. After making the scene safe and clear for investigation by isolating the electrics and removing roof panels, crews conducted a thorough investigation using a thermal imaging camera and examination tools.
