Lorry cab on fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 16/02/2015 - 10:28 Location: St Michaels Way, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Winsford Firefighters have been tackling a blaze in the cab of a heavy goods vehicle.  The crew, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  The lorry's cargo was unaffected as the fire was confined to the lorry's cab.
