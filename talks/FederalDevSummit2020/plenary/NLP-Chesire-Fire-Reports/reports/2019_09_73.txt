 Kerosene leak in Wincle Time of Incident: 17/09/2019 - 16:12 Location: Top House Lane, Wincle Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Macclesfield and one from Congleton At around 4.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a kerosene tank leaking outside a domestic property in Top House Lane, Wincle. Crews used specialist paste to stem the leak. A small amount of kerosene had leaked onto the ground. Firefighters used specialist absorbent pads to remove the kerosene from the floor. The occupier arranged for the fuel provider to attend and pump the remaining kerosene out of the tank. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour.
