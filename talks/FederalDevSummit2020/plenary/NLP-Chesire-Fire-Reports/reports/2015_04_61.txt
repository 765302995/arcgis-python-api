Fire close to M6 between junctions 21 and 20 Time of Incident: 21/04/2015 - 21:07 Location: Juniper Lane, Woolston, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood A caller reported seeing smoke and flames before junction 21A of the M6.  Firefighters found a controlled burn on land next to the motorway.  They put out the fire as a precaution, using two hose reel jets.  Smoke did not affect the motorway.
