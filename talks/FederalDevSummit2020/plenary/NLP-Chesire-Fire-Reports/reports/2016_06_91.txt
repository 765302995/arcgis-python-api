Flash flooding in Nantwich Time of Incident: 14/06/2016 - 14:56 Location: Wrenbury Road, Aston, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich Firefighters were called to flash flooding which affected a number of properties. The crew used a light portable pump to remove water from the houses and police closed the road, due to the flooding. Electrics were also isolated.
