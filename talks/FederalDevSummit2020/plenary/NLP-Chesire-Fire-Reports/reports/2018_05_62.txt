 Grass fire in Neston Time of Incident: 22/05/2018 - 16:43 Location: Marshlands Road, Neston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Crews responded to reports of 15 metres of grass and scrubland well alight in trees near to Marshlands Road in Neston.  Firefighters used a hose reel to help put out the fire.
