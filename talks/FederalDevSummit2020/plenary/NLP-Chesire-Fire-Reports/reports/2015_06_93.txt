House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 14/06/2015 - 13:53 Location: Minshull New Road, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe, one from Sandbach and one from Winsford. The Hydraulic Platform was also there. Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Minshull New Road, Crewe.  When they arrived they found that there was a serious blaze in an end terrace property. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus tackled the fire with main and hose reel water jets.They also confirmed that everyone was out of the property.  Crews fought the fire, which had spread from the back bedroom all the way to the roof space, until they brought it under control just after 3pm.  A fire investigation been launched to find the cause of the blaze.
