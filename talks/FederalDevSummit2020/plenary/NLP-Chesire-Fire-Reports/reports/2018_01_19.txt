 Commercial building fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 26/01/2018 - 11:45 Location: Welsh Row, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich and one from Crewe Firefighters were called to a fire in a commercial premises on Welsh Row, Nantwich. On arrival crews discovered a fire involving a chip range and used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel jet, one dry powder extinguisher, a ladder and a roof ladder along with height safety restraints to extinguish the fire and check for its spread. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
