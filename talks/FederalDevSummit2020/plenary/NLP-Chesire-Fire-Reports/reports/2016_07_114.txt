Kitchen fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 11/07/2016 - 17:51 Location: Delamere Street, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich A chip pan caught fire in a Winsford property. The fire was out on arrival of the firefighters but had left the kitchen smoke filled. Crews gave oxygen therapy to a male casualty and used a high pressure fan to clear the smoke. An ambulance also attended.  Please take care if using a chip pan and always follow our kitchen safety advice.
