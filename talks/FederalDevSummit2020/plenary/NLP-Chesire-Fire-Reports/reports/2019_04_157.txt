 Grass fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 15:04 Location: Delph Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh One fire engine attended a grass fire on Delph Lane, Warrington. The fire was approximately 5m by 10m across and was well alight on arrival of the crew. They used a light portable pump and beaters to put out the fire which had been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident please contact Cheshire Police.
