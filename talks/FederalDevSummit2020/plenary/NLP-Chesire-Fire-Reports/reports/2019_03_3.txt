 Van fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 30/03/2019 - 04:22 Location: Rossmore Road East, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to extinguish a van fire on Rossmore Road East, Ellesmere Port. The crew, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
