Grill pan fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 13/06/2017 - 14:09 Location: Grizedale, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to a grill pan fire in a house in Widnes. When crews arrived the fire was out and all people in the house were accounted for. Crews used a large fan to clear smoke from the house before leaving the scene.  Kitchen fire safety
