 Elderly woman trapped following a collision on North Rode Time of Incident: 19/11/2018 - 13:53 Location: Buxton Road, North Rode Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Macclesfield and one from Congleton Shortly after 1.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving a car on Buxton Road in North Rode. On arrival an elderly woman was trapped inside the vehicle. Firefighters stabilised the vehicle and administered oxygen to the casualty. Once an ambulance arrived firefighters and paramedics helped the casualty get out of the vehicle. The casualty was then left in the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour.
