Car fire in Chester Time of Incident: 03/04/2017 - 13:40 Location: Frodsham Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Powey Lane A car on the first level of Tesco's multi-storey car park was on fire. There was a small fire in the engine compartment. Firefighters used dry powder to put the fire out and then damped down using a hose reel jet.
