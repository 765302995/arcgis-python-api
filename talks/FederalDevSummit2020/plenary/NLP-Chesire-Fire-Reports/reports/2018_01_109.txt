 Boiler room fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 03/01/2018 - 19:52 Location: Mallard Crescent, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton. One fire engine from Bollington. Firefighters attended fire in the boiler room of an address in Mallard Crescent, Poynton. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire.
