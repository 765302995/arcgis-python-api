Car fire in Kettleshulme Time of Incident: 06/10/2016 - 18:05 Location: Macclesfield Road, Gnathole, Kettleshulme Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a car fire on Macclesfield Road at Gnathole.  The owner had put the fire out using a dry foam extinguisher and the crew cleared oil residue using an environmental pack. Highways were asked to attend to clear oil from the road.
