 Fork lift truck fire inside industrial building in Chester Time of Incident: 27/03/2018 - 02:21 Location: Sealand Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester, one from Powey Lane and two from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a fire in an industrial building on Sealand Road, Chester. Crews found a fork lift truck had set alight in the loading area of the building. Crews, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire and a large fan to clear smoke from the property. Crews were at the scene for three hours and a half.
