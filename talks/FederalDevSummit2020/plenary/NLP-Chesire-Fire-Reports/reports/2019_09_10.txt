 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 27/09/2019 - 21:28 Location: Barnes Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh A fire crew from was called to Barnes Road after reports of a fire in the open.  When they arrived the firefighters discovered a wheelie bin had been set alight and used a water backpack to put the fire out. They were at the scene for about 20 minutes.
