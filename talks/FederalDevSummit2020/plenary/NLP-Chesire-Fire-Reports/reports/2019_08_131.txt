 Garden fire in Shavington Time of Incident: 07/08/2019 - 15:10 Location: Main Road, Shavington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly after 3pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the back garden of a domestic property on Main Road, Shavington. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning involving garden cuttings. Following a conversation with the homeowner, firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. They were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
