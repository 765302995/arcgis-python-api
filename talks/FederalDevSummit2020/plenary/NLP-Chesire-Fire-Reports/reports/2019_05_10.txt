 Fire in a wooded area in Penketh Time of Incident: 28/05/2019 - 21:29 Location: Station Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 9.30pm crews from Penketh were called to reports of a fire off Station Road.  On  arrival the team discovered a small fire in a wooded area near to the Riverside Trading Estate.  The team used a water back pack to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 413804, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
