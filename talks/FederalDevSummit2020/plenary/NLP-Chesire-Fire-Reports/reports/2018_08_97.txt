 Grass fire, Winsford Time of Incident: 10/08/2018 - 17:17 Location: Common Lane, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich Crews responded to reports of a grass fire on land near a caravan park in Winsford. They arrived to find a large area of grass measuring approximately 40 square metres was smouldering.  They used two hose reels to tackle the fire, utilising an additional water source from nearby open water. They extinguished the fire after about an hour, and remained to check the temperature of the affected area before they left.
