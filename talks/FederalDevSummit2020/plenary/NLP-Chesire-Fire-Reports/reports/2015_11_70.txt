Vehicle fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/11/2015 - 22:47 Location: Liverpool Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Fire damage was caused to a traffic light after a vehicle fire in Warrington.  The car transporter was well alight on arrival of the crew, one of whom wore breathing apparatus and two hose reels were used to extinguish the blaze.  Highways were informed due to damage to the traffic light and police were also informed to arrange for the vehicle to be removed.
