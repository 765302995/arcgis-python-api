Road traffic collision in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 17/06/2017 - 15:19 Location: A34, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Two casualties were released from a car after being involved in a collision on the A34. Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to get to the casualties who were then removed from the car on longboards. Both were then taken to hospital by ambulance. Only one vehicle was involved in the incident where crews were working for around an hour.
