 Washing machine fire in Bollington Time of Incident: 16/12/2018 - 10:19 Location: Archer Close, Bollington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, from Macclesfield and Wilmslow At around 10.20am firefighters from Macclesfield and Wilmslow were called to reports of a fire at a residential address in Bollington. On arrival firefighters discovered that a washing machine was well alight in a first floor flat. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel to extinguish the fire. A casualty suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation was given oxygen by fire service personnel.  Firefighters were in attendance for approximately an hour.
