 Fire in a garden in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/07/2018 - 14:53 Location: Ledston Close, Windmill Hill, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 2.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in Ledston Close, Windmill Hill, Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning in a garden. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
