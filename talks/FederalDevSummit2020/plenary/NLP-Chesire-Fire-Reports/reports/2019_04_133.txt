 Small refuse fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 15/04/2019 - 20:49 Location: St Michaels Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters from Widnes used one hose reel jet to extinguish a small refuse fire on a wasteland area off St Michaels Road.  The team were in attendance at the scene for around 30 minutes.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries  or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
