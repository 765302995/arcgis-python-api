Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 22/04/2017 - 00:55 Location: Dunsford, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A hose reel jet was used to put out a car fire. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information should call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
