 Rescue of a child in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/10/2019 - 14:25 Location: Victoria Park, Birchfield Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly before 2.30pm firefighters from Widnes attended reports of a child stuck in gym equipment at Victoria Park. On arrival the crews found a young boy with his arm stuck in a metal pole which was part of an outdoor gym. The crew released the boy and he was passed to the care of his parents. He did not sustain any injuries as a result of the incident.
