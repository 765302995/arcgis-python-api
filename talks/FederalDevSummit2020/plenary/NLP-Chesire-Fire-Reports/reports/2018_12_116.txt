 Small bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/12/2018 - 22:22 Location: Padgate Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington used one hose reel jet to extinguish a bin fire on Padgate Lane. The team were detained at the scene for approximately ten minutes. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
