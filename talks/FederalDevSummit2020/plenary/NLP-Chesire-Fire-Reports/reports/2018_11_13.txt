 Chemical spillage in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/11/2018 - 20:19 Location: Haddon Drive, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a chemical spillage in Haddon Drive, Widnes. A small amount of what was believed to be red diesel had spilt onto the road from a 25 litre drum. Crews used soil to contain the vapour on a spill measuring approximately 2 metre squared.
