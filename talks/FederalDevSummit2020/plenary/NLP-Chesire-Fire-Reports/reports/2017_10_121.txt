Tree fire in Chelford Time of Incident: 01/10/2017 - 16:38 Location: Mere Court, Chelford Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Fire crews were called to a tree which was on fire on Mere Court, Chelford. They used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. After it was extinguished the crews deemed that the tree was now an unsafe structure so cordoned it off and handed it over to Cheshire East Council.
