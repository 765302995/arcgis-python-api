Fire in loft of bungalow in Nantwich Time of Incident: 01/11/2016 - 14:06 Location: Delamere Road, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended Firefighters were called to extinguish a fire in the loft space of a bungalow. The crew used a hose reel to extinguish the fire and nearby properties were checked for fire spread before crews left.
