Car on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 29/06/2017 - 01:35 Location: Athey Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A car which was well alight in Macclesfield was extinguished with a hose reel. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone knows anything about it then please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
