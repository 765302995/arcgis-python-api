 Small fire in the open in Widnes Time of Incident: 16/05/2018 - 13:59 Location: Carter House Way, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 2pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open at Carter House Way in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered that the small fire involved a vehicle tyre. Firefighters put out the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
