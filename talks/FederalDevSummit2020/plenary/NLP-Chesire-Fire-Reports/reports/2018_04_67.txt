 Forklift truck fire in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 18/04/2018 - 07:30 Location: London Road, Holmes Chapel. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel and one fire engine attended from Middlewich At 7.30am firefighters  from Holmes Chapel and Middlewich attended reports of a fire involving a forklift truck at an industrial premises on London Road, Holmes Chapel. On arrival at the scene crews found that the fire had been extinguished however, after examining the vehicle with a thermal imaging camera, firefighters were concerned about the temperature of the battery. The team isolated the battery and removed it from the vehicle to allow it to cool safely; the crew were in attendance for approximately 3 hours 30 minutes.
