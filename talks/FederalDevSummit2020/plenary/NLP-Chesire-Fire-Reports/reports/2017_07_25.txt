Small fire in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/07/2017 - 22:03 Location: Maple Avenue, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A hose reel was used to extinguish a small fire on open ground. The fire, which is believed to have been started deliberately, involved rubbish. Call police on 101 if you know anything about who may have started it on Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
