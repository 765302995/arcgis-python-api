 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/11/2019 - 18:12 Location: Collin Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington used a hose reel jet to extinguish a Wheelie bin which had been deliberately set on fire on Collin Street.  The crew were detained at the address for around 10 minutes.
