Fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/06/2016 - 12:28 Location: Linnet Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A mattress was on fire in a field off Linnet Close. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately.  Arson costs lives; anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
