 Engine malfunction on a boat in Poynton Time of Incident: 30/09/2018 - 10:10 Location: Lyme Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton and one from Greater Manchester Fire crews were called to a engine malfunction on a boat on Lyme Road, Poynton. The boat was moored in the marina and crews checked the engine for signs of fire using a thermal imaging camera and then gave first aid to one casualty who was suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation.
