Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 14/07/2015 - 00:23 Location: Arderne Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Deliberate ignition is suspected in the case of a Crewe car fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and asked police to attend as it is believed arson was the cause. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting log number 30 of 14 July.
