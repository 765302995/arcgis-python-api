 Hedge fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 22/04/2019 - 10:22 Location: Andertons Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to a hedge fire on Andertons Lane, Macclesfield. A bonfire had spread accidentally to the hedge and crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
