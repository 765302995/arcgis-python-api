Lorry fire on M6 junctions 16 to 17 southbound Time of Incident: 06/04/2017 - 15:24 Location: M6 southbound between junctions 16 and 17 Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and one from Crewe Firefighters cooled the brakes of a lorry on the M6 southbound between junctions 16 and 17.  A recovery vehicle was also at the scene.
