 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 08/02/2019 - 19:07 Location: Lanark Gardens, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to a wheelie bin fire on Lanark Gardens, Widnes. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire then please call Cheshire Police on 101.
