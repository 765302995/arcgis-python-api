Road traffic collision in Grindley Brook, Whitchurch Time of Incident: 23/02/2015 - 11:43 Location: A41 Grindley Brook, Whitchurch Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and one from Shropshire FRS Fire and rescue crews have been at the scene of a road traffic collision on the A41 between Grindley Brook and Bell O The Hill.  Two vehicles were involved, a van and a saloon car.  A female casualty was trapped in the car.  Firefighters made the scene safe before removing the casualty on a spinal board and handing her into the care of the ambulance service.
