 Cooking fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 10/03/2018 - 16:42 Location: Copperfield Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton and one from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to a house on Copperfield Road, Poynton, after reports of a fire. On arrival crews found a pan of food had been left unattended which had caused a small fire. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A large fan was then used to clear smoke from the property. Cooking safety tips
