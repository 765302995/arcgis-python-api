Car fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 16/03/2016 - 05:41 Location: Bullocks Lane, Sutton, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A car was well alight on the roadway in Sutton near Macclesfield. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the blaze. The fuel tank split so crews changed the firefighting tactics and prepared to use foam, but this wasn't needed. A nearby vehicle was moved to prevent it becoming involved.  It is believed the fire was started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
