 Wood store fire in Haslington Time of Incident: 19/03/2018 - 21:20 Location: Clay Lane, Haslington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to a fire involving a wood store and conifer trees in Clay Lane, Haslington. Crews used two hose reels to extinguish the flames. They then damped down the area to ensure the fire did not reignite.
