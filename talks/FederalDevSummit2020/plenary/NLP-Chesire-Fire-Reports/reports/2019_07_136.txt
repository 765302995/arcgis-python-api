 Cooker fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 07/07/2019 - 21:24 Location: Kestrel Avenue, Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Knutsford and one from Lymm Shortly before 9.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a domestic property in Kestrel Avenue, Knutsford. On arrival the crews found that the fire involved a cooker and that everyone was accounted for. It was caused by cooking food having been left unattended. The fire was out on arrival but had left the property heavily smoke logged. The electricity supply to the property was isolated and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a large fan to clear the smoke from the address. Firefighters also administered oxygen to an occupier who was suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation. The man was handed over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for almost two hours. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
