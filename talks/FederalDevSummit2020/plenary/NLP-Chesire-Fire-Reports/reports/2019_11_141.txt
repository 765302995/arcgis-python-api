 Unattended bonfire, Widnes Time of Incident: 05/11/2019 - 18:05 Location: Parbold Court, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were notified of a bonfire that had been left unattended at Parbold Court, Widnes. They arrived to find a large fire left to burn and they used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames.
