Chimney fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 11/02/2015 - 20:46 Location: Oathills Drive, Tarporley. Attendance: A fire engine from Tarporley Fire crews used hose reel jets and special rods to tackle a chimney fire on Oathills Drive, Tarporley.  They also used a thermal imaging camera to check the loft space for further signs of fire.  Chimney fire safety advice
