 Road traffic collision on the M6 motorway Time of Incident: 16/11/2018 - 17:44 Location: J17-16 Southbound, M6 motorway . Attendance: Two appliances from Crewe and Holmes Chapel Crews responded to reports of a collision on the M6 between a lorry and two cars. Nobody was trapped or injured, but they assisted police colleagues in moving the vehicles and the occupants to the hard shoulder of the motorway so that the road could re-open. They were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
