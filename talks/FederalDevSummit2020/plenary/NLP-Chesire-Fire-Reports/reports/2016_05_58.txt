Garden bonfire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 20/05/2016 - 20:27 Location: Lindrum Avenue, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters put out a bonfire which contained a large amount of plastic as well as timber. The crew used a hose reel jet to put out the fire that was giving off a lot of smoke. They then damped the area down.
