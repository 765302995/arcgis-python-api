 Disused building fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 01/09/2018 - 18:15 Location: Palacefields, Runcorn Attendance: Four fire engine attended - one from Runcorn, one from Widnes, one pump from Chester and one hydraulic platform from Lymm. At 6:14pm firefighters from Runcorn and Widnes attended reports of a building fire on Palacefields, Runcorn. Crews attended the scene and discovered a fire in a disused community centre which was well alight. The team requested the support of a  hydraulic platform from Lymm and a pump from Chester. The team used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel and the aerial appliance to extinguish the fire. Crews were in attendance for just under two hours. The cause of the fire is currently being investigated and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 172167.
