House fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 23/07/2016 - 20:35 Location: Lower House Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Runcorn A pan left on a burning hob was the cause of a fire at a Lower Side house. There was nobody in the property so firefighters wearing breathing apparatus had to gain entry to tackle the fire and clear smoke which had built up. Houses either side of the affected property were also checked by crews to make sure there was no fire or smoke spread.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice and never leave cooking unattended.
