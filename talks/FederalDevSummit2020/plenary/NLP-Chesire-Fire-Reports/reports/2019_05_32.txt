 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/05/2019 - 02:27 Location: Matlock Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Crews attended to a vehicle fire on Matlock Close in Warrington.  Wearing one breathing apparatus, a hose reel jet was used to put out the fire. They then dampened down the vehicle and checked the temperature using the thermal imaging camera.
