 Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 10/09/2019 - 23:48 Location: Underwood Drive/Grasmere Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 11.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a car being on fire in the Underwood Drive/Grasmere Road area of Ellesmere Port  On arrival the crew found the vehicle well alight.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 509959, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
