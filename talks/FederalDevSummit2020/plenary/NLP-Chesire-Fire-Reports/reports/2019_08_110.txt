 Bin waggon fire in Chester Time of Incident: 12/08/2019 - 09:12 Location: Mill Lane, Upton, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Chester and one from Powey Lane Shortly after 9.10am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a bin waggon in Mill Lane, Upton, Chester. On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved the waggon's engine compartment. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
