 Caravan on fire at a farm in Northwich Time of Incident: 22/07/2018 - 21:14 Location: Green Lane, Wincham, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - from Northwich and Middlewich At around 9.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a caravan being on fire at a farm on Green Lane in Wincham, Northwich. On arrival crews discovered the caravan well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and then damped the area down to make it safe. They were in attendance for around an hour.
