 Building fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/03/2018 - 20:19 Location: Hough Green Road, Widnes. Attendance: Four fire engines attended from Widnes, Penketh, Warrington and Runcorn and an aerial ladder platform attended from Lymm. Building fire in WidnesFour fire engines and aerial ladder platform attended a building fire on Hough Green Road, Widnes.  The fire involved a single storey unused commercial building measuring approximately 30 metres by 30 metres. fire  On arrival at the scene the fire was well alight and crews used two hose reels, two main jets and two breathing apparatus to tackle the fire.  Crews were in attendance for 12 hours 30 minutes
