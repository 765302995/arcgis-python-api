Car on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/12/2015 - 01:23 Location: Stockham Lane, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A car was well alight on the roadway, so firefighters used one hose reel jet to deal with the fire.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately so if anyone has any information they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
