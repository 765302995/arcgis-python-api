 Chimney fire in Neston Time of Incident: 05/04/2018 - 18:53 Location: Well Lane, Neston. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane supported by an Aerial Platform from Chester. Chimney fire in NestonShortly before 7pm firefighters from Powey Lane, along with an aerial platform  from Chester, attended reports of a chimney fire on Well Lane in Ness, near Neston.  The team used specialist chimney gear, chimney rods, a hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire.  Crews were in attendance for approximately two hours and thirty minutes.
