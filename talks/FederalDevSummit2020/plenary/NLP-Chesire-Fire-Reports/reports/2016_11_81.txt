Car fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 14/11/2016 - 10:34 Location: Mobberley Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters tackled a fire in a car on Mobberley Road. The crew, one firefighter wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze. Mobberley Road was closed by police as firefighters tackled the fire. It has now reopened.
