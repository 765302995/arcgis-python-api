 Disused garage fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 30/10/2019 - 02:54 Location: Croft Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A fire involving a small domestic garage, that was attached to a property, was well alight on the arrival of firefighters. The crews used two hose reels to extinguish the fire and a cordon was set up around the disused building while the incident was dealt with.
