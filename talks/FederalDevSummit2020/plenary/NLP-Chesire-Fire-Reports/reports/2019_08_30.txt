 Motorbike on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/08/2019 - 21:23 Location: Hawleys Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Shortly after 9.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in Warrington. On arrival the crew found a motorbike well alight on a grass path leading from Hawleys Lane. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
