 Waste bins fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/08/2019 - 23:14 Location: Avondale Drive, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 11.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a bin being on fire in Avondale Drive, Widnes. On arrival the crew found a fire involving waste bins at the back of flats. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 489634, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
