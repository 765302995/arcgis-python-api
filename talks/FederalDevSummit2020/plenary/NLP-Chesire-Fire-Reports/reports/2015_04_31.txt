Fire on Runcorn Hill Time of Incident: 25/04/2015 - 19:32 Location: Highlands Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters tackled a blaze on Runcorn Hill.  Water back packs and beaters were used to put out the fire, which was unable to be reached by the appliance so the crew made their way on foot.
