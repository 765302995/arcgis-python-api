House fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 12/01/2017 - 15:27 Location: Parklands, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Crews were called to a report of a house fire on Parklands. Firefighters found a fire in the kitchen involving an electric cooker. They used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and a high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property.   Please follow our kitchen safety advice.
