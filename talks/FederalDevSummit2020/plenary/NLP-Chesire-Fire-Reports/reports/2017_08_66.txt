Car and lamppost involved in road traffic collision in Poynton Time of Incident: 20/08/2017 - 00:14 Location: London Road South, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton A road traffic collision involving one car and a lamppost with no one trapped required firefighters to attend and make the area safe. A cordon was set up around the area for safety. The road was closed in both directions due to the unsafe structure. Police and ambulance staff attended.
