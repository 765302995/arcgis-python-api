Fire in garden of Chester property Time of Incident: 03/09/2016 - 14:34 Location: Harthill Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester A fire involving rubbish is believed to have been started deliberately in the front garden of a Chester house. The fire, which had been set close to the house, was put out by firefighters using a hose reel jet.  Anyone with any information about this, or similar, incidents is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
