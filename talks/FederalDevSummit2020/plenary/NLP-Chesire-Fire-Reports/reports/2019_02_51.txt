 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 21/02/2019 - 20:00 Location: Lockett Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to Lockett Road, Widnes, after a report of a wheelie bin fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please contact Cheshire Police.
