Van fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/12/2015 - 03:48 Location: Ulverston Avenue, Poplars and Hulme, Warringon Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A fire involving a van in Warrington is believed to have been started deliberately.  One firefighters wore breathing apparatus and used one hose reel to extinguish the blaze. Police were informed.  Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
