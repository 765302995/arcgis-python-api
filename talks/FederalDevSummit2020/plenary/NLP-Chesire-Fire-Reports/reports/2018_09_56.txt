 Person trapped after a tree fell on car in Tarporley Time of Incident: 19/09/2018 - 13:31 Location: Forest Road, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and one from Winsford Fire crews were called to Forest Road, Tarporley, after a tree fell on a car, trapping a casualty. Crews worked with a professional tree surgeon to help remove the tree before crews removed the roof of the vehicle using hydraulic cutting equipment. Crews extracted the casualty from the vehicle before handing them over to the care of paramedics. The casualty has been taken to hospital via air ambulance.
