Road traffic collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/03/2017 - 18:34 Location: Welsh Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane There was a road traffic collision, involving two cars with two people trapped in one of the vehicles. Firefighters rescued the two female casualties before handing them over to paramedics.  A further two casualties were assessed at the scene by paramedics. Police attended this incident, and they temporarily closed the road.
