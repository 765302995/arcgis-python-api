Washing machine on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/03/2015 - 14:10 Location: Beech Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Wigan (Greater Manchester FRS) A washing machine was well alight in the kitchen of a Warrington property when firefighters were called.  The crews, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze.  A high pressure fan was then used to clear the property to smoke.  Firefighters gave oxygen therapy to a male casualty as a precaution.  Smoke alarms were fitted in the house and alerted the occupants to the fire.
