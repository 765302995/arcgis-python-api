 Wheelie bin fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 22/10/2019 - 00:04 Location: Linnet Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters turned out to deal with a wheelie bin on fire in Linnet Close. It was well alight when they got there, and they put it out using a water backpack. Colleagues at Cheshire Constabulary were notified due to it being a suspected deliberate ignition.  Advice on how to stop your wheelie bin being at risk of arson can be found on our website.
