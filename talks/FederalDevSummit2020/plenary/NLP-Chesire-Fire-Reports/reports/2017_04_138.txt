Car fire in Kettleshulme Time of Incident: 03/04/2017 - 10:22 Location: Macclesfield Road, Kettleshulme Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Firefighters put out a car fire on Macclesfield Road, just before Kettleshulme. Two fire engines attended to make sure there was enough water to tackle the incident as the fire was some way from a hydrant.
