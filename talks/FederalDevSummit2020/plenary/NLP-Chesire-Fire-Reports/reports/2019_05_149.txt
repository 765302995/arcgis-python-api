 Garden fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 11/05/2019 - 17:14 Location: Conway Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called by concerned neighbours to a fire in the garden of a house on Conway Avenue in Dallam. It transpired to be a controlled burning taking place, but due to large amount of flammable material involved, they put the fire out using a single hose reel. A homes safety assessment was carried out with the occupier before they left.
