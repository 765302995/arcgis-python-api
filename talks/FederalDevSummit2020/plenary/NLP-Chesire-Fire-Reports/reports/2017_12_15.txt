Fire involving a log burner in Orford Time of Incident: 25/12/2017 - 18:36 Location: Long Lane, Orford Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Birchwood A fire involving a log burner in a property on Long Lane, Orford, was extinguished by firefighters using a hose reel jet. They then checked the area around the log burner using a thermal imaging camera. Chimney fire safety
