 Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/10/2019 - 00:15 Location: Addison Square, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews attended to a car well alight on Addison Square in Widnes.  Wearing a breathing apparatus, crews used a hose reel to put out the flames and a thermal imaging camera to ensure the fire was fully extinguished.
