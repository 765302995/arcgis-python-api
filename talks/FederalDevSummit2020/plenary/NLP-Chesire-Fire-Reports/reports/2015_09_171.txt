Conifers on fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 31/08/2015 - 19:03 Location: Birchin Lane, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a fire involving conifers which are believed to have been set on fire deliberately. One hose reel was used to extinguish the fire. Police also attended. If anyone knows who may have started this fire they should call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
