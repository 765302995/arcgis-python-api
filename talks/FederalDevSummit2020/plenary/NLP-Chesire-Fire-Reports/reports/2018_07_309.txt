 Grass fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 15:21 Location: Blackshaw Drive, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 3.20pm firefighters were called to reports of a grass fire in the open on Blackshaw Drive in Warrington. The fire involving around 25 square metres of grass was out on arrival. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call police on 101.
