 Boy rescued from mud in Congleton Time of Incident: 13/07/2019 - 19:38 Location: Back Lane, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton, one from Warrington, one from Widnes, one from Lymm, one from Penketh and the rope rescue team from Lymm Fire crews were called to rescue a boy who had become stuck in mud off Back Lane, Congleton. The boy had been stuck for approximately three hours before crews managed to rescue him. Firefighters then assisted paramedics with first aid. Crews were at the scene for 45 minutes.
