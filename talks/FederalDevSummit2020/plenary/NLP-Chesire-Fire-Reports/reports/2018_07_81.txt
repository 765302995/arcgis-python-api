 Bonfire at industrial estate in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/07/2018 - 19:43 Location: Astmoor Industrial Estate, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 7.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at Astmoor Industrial Estate in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered a bonfire well alight. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
