Warehouse fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/10/2017 - 08:39 Location: Desoto Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn, one from Widnes, one from Penketh and one from Warrington Fire crews were called to a large warehouse fire on Desoto Road, Widnes. A large amount of wood was well alight in a warehouse on arrival of the crews. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a main jet to fight the fire. Crews then checked the building using a thermal imaging camera. They left the scene at 18:31.
