Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/11/2015 - 16:03 Location: Ambleside Crescent, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath A casualty was treated for smoke inhalation following a kitchen fire caused by an overheated chip pan. Firefighters were called to the scene and gave the lady first aid until the ambulance arrived. The crew, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
