Commercial garage fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 02/11/2015 - 12:47 Location: West Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters have been tackling a fire at a commercial garage. One car was in the garage at the time of the incident. The crew, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, set up a covering jet on the outside of the building which measured 10 metres by 5 metres. A hose reel jet was also used. The crews then damped the area down and checked for hot spots. Police attended to control traffic.
