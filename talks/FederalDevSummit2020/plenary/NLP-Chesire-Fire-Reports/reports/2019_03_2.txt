 House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 30/03/2019 - 05:40 Location: Small Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Penketh Firefighters were called to a fire on the ground floor of a detached house on Small Avenue, Warrington. Crews, four wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. All the people in the property were accounted for and one person was treated for the effects of smoke inhalation. A investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
