 Motorbike on fire in Helsby Time of Incident: 16/04/2018 - 16:08 Location: Low Hill, Helsby Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 4.10pm firefighters were called to reports of a motorbike being on fire on Low Hill in Helsby, Frodsham. On arrival the crew found the motorbike well alight and a firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. Firefighters were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
