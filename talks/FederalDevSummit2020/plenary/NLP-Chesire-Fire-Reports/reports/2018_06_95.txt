 Small fire in a field in Frodsham Time of Incident: 23/06/2018 - 12:09 Location: Ship Street, Frodsham. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters from Runcorn used one hose reel jet and a beaters to tackle a 4 metre by 7 metre grass fire in a field off Ship Street, Frodsham. The team were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes.
