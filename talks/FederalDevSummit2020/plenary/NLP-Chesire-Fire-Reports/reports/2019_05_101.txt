 Van fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 17/05/2019 - 21:53 Location: Palmerston Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield. Firefighters were called to a van on fire in Palmerston Street, Macclesfield. The fire was out on arrival and crews used a hose reel jet to damp down the vehicle to ensure it didn't reignite.
