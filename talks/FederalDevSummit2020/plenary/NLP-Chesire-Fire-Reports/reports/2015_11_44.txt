Chimney fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 21/11/2015 - 18:24 Location: Sandylands Park, Wistaston. Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a chimney fire on Sandylands Park, Wistaston.  When they arrived crews carried out a full check of the chimney before confirming that the fire had gone out.
