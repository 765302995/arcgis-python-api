 Car fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 23/05/2018 - 00:05 Location: Woodland Court, Alsager Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar Car fire in AlsagerDeliberate ignition is suspected in a car fire on an Alsager car park. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control and damp down the area.  Anyone with any information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
