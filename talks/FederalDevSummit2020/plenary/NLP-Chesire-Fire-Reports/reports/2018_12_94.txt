 Cellar fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 09/12/2018 - 15:49 Location: Chester Road, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Shortly before 4pm firefighters from Northwich were called to reports of a building fire on Chester Road. On arrival at the scene crews found that the was isolated to the cellar area. Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus entered the cellar where they discovered the fire involved an electrical fuse board. The team isolated the electrics and then used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately two-hours and twenty minutes.
