Fire in the open in Northwich Time of Incident: 19/10/2015 - 17:35 Location: Manchester Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A small fire was found in the open on Manchester Road. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put it out. A quantity of wooden sleepers were damaged and were damped down by the crew.  The cause of the fire is believed to have been a discarded cigarette.
