 Car fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 05/11/2018 - 08:53 Location: Cuckoo Lane, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a car fire on Cuckoo Lane, Nantwich. Crews found a car well alight in a hedgerow and the crew, one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.
