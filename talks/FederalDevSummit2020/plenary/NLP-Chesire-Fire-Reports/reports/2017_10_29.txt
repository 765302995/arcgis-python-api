Car fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 24/10/2017 - 20:23 Location: M56 West on the link road from the M53 south. Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port A saloon car had set alight on the hard shoulder of the M56 West. Fire crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The Highways Agency assisted the fire crews by closing one lane of the carriageway during the incident.
