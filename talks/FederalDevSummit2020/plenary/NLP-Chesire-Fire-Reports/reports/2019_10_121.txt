 Bin on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/10/2019 - 02:07 Location: Newhaven Road, Poplars and Hulme, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A wheelie bin which was well alight at the back of a property was extinguished with a hose reel by firefighters. It is believed that the fire was started deliberately so police officers were requested to attend.
