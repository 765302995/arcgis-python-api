Car crash in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/10/2017 - 09:02 Location: Barnfield Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Frodsham Firefighters were called to road traffic collision involving one car on Barnfield Avenue, Runcorn. Crews extracted one casualty from the vehicle who was taken to hospital by paramedics.
