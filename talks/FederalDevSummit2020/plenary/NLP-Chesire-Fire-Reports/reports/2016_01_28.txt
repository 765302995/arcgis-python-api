Fire inside a microwave oven in Frodsham Time of Incident: 22/01/2016 - 18:52 Location: Morton Avenue, Frodsham Attendance: Two fire engines - one from Frodsham and one from Ellesmere Port A fire crew from Frodsham and a fire crew from Ellesmere Port were sent to a report of a house fire on Morton Avenue in Frodsham. On arrival the fire crews found the incident to be involving a fire inside a microwave oven. Firefighters moved the microwave to the outside of the property and extinguished the fire using one hose reel. Firefighters also used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.
