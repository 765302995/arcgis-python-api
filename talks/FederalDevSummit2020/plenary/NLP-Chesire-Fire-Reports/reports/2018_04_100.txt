 Collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 12/04/2018 - 16:22 Location: Hawkshead Way, Winsford. Attendance: Fire crews from Winsford Firefighters from Winsford were called to reports that a car had collided with a bay window of a two-storey house on Hawkshead Way. The team attended the scene and found that there were no injuries and nobody was trapped in the vehicle. The team conducted initial checks on the building and a structural engineer has been called to the site.
