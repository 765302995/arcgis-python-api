 Bonfire spread in Tarporley Time of Incident: 23/10/2018 - 18:08 Location: Quarry Bank, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley Firefighters were called to Quarry Bank, Tarporley, after a bonfire spread to a nearby tree. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were at the scene for about an hour.
