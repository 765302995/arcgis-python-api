Kitchen fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 24/05/2016 - 18:12 Location: Carlton Street, Widnes. Attendance: Fire engines from Widnes  Firefighters responded to a house fire on Carlton Street, Widnes.  When they arrived they confirmed that there was a fire in the kitchen caused by a plastic tub that had been left on the cooker. Crews wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to put the fire out. A large fan was also used to clear smoke from the house.   Crews were at the property for about an hour.
