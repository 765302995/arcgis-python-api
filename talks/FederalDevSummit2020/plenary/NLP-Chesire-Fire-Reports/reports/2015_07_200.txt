Shrubbery on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 01/07/2015 - 04:53 Location: Runcorn Hill, Weston Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters had to make their way on foot with firefighting equipment to tackle a fire on Runcorn Hill.  Approximately 80 square metres of shrubbery was involved in the fire which was put out by the crew using a hose reel jet and handheld extinguishers.
