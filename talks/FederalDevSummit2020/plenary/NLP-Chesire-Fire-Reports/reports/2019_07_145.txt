 Hedgrow and fence fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/07/2019 - 12:43 Location: Camrose Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 12.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a hedgerow in Camrose Close, Runcorn. On arrival the crew found the hedgerow well alight and that a nearby fence was also involved in the fire. Firefighter used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. They fought the fire from both sides of the fence and were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
