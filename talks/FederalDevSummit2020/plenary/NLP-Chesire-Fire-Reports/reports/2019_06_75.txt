 Car on driveway on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 13/06/2019 - 20:23 Location: Beeston Drive, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford A fire involving a car on a driveway in Winsford was extinguished by firefighters using water from a hose reel. The fascia of a nearby garage was damaged by the heat from the fire so the property was checked as a precaution.
