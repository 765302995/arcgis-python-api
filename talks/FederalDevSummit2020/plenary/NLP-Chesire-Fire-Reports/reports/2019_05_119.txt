 Outbuilding fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 15/05/2019 - 01:49 Location: Grange Lane, Malpas. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas. One fire engine from Chester and Widnes. Firefighters attended an outbuilding fire in Grange Lane in Tilston, Malpas. The fire, involved a corrugated steel and wood building and a garden which were well alight.  Crews extinguished the fire using two breathing apparatus, two hose reels and a thermal image camera to extinguish the fire.
