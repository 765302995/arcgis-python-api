Paper compactor on fire in Calveley Time of Incident: 25/06/2015 - 22:56 Location: Nantwich Road, Calveley Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Tarporley A paper compactor at an industrial site in Calveley was involved in fire.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used three hose reel jets to put out the fire.  The crews removed the piece of machinery from the building and damped the area down.  They were at the scene for around thirty minutes.
