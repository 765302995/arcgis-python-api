Bedroom fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 02/02/2017 - 15:29 Location: Navigation Close, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn and Frodsham Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to put out a fire believed to be involving a mattress in an upstairs bedroom in a property in Runcorn. Crews then used a large fan to clear smoke from the building.
