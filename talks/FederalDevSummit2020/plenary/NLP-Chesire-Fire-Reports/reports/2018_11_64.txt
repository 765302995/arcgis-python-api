 Bin fire at a park in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/11/2018 - 19:29 Location: Roehampton Drive, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 7.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Hallwood Park on Roehampton Drive in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered a bin on fire. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes. The fire may have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
