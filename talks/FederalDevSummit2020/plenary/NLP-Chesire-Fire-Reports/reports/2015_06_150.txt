Grill fire at Alderley Edge bar and grill Time of Incident: 07/06/2015 - 11:15 Location: London Road, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service A fire in an electrical grill in an Alderley Edge restaurant was tackled by firefighters.  Gas and electricity was isolated and crews made the scene safe.
