 One person trapped after collision on M6 Time of Incident: 09/10/2018 - 08:55 Location: M6 South Link Road to M62 East Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, one fire engine from Birchwood, one fire engine and the Major Rescue Unit from Winsford and one fire engine and two specialist appliances from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Firefighters at the scene of a road traffic collision on the M6Fire crews were called to the M6 after a road traffic collision between two lorries and a car.  One person was trapped inside a lorry and crews removed the casualty from the vehicle before handing them over into the care of paramedics.  Two lanes were closed whilst the extraction took place.
