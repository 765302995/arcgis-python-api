Gas barbecue fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 02/10/2016 - 22:47 Location: Eaton Road, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley Reports of a gas canister on fire in the opening of a street alerted firefighters to the scene. Crews used one hose reel jet to put out the fire which was found to involve a gas barbecue. Firefighters cooled the cylinder before carrying out a health and safety assessment of the scene.
