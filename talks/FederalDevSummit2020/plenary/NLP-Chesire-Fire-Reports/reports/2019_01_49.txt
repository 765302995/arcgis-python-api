 Chimney fire in Dunham on the Hill Time of Incident: 02/01/2019 - 21:48 Location: Chester Road, Dunham on the Hill. Attendance:  One fire engine from Chester and one aerial platform from Chester At around 9.50pm firefighters Chester were called to reports of a chimney fire in Dunham on the Hill.  On arrival at the address the team discovered that the chimney flue was well alight. The team specialist chimney rods and one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews also removed a  number of bricks from the side wall to help the team access the fire.  Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately one hour 50 minutes. For more information about chimney fire safety visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/chimney-fire-safety
