Motorcycle set on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 17/04/2016 - 05:02 Location: Lavender Drive, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A motorcycle was well alight, so firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
