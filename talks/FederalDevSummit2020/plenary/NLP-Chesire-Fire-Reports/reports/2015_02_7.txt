Vehicle fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 25/02/2015 - 18:26 Location: Junctions 20 and 21 on the M6. Attendance: A fire engine from Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a vehicle fire between Junctions 20 and 21 on the M6.  When they arrived crews found  a HGV on the hard shoulder with an overheated axel.Crews used a hose reel jet to cool the axel down.
