Kitchen fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 10/01/2017 - 15:43 Location: Barhill Drive, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas Firefighters were called to a fire involving a cooker which was out on arrival. The crew ventilated the property with a large fan and the fire is believed to have started in the semi detached property due to an item being put on the cooker. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the area before the crew left. Kitchen fire safety advice
