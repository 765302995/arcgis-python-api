 Fire in commercial building in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/07/2019 - 11:43 Location: Phipps Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a fire in a commercial building on Phipps Lane, Warrington. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze. All staff were evacuated. There are no reports of any injuries. The fire was caused by an electrical fault. Crews were at the scene for an hour and 30 minutes.
