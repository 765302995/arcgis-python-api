 Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 17:35 Location: Vernon Way, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 5.35pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire in Vernon Way, Crewe. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire on the roadway involved the vehicle's engine. The road was closed as firefighters worked at the scene. They used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
