 Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 08/04/2019 - 00:48 Location: Elizabeth Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly after 12.45am firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a vehicle in Elizabeth Street, Crewe. On arrival the crew discovered that the back of a car was on fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 45 minutes. The crew notified the police of the incident. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
