Outbuilding fire in Weaverham Time of Incident: 23/12/2017 - 12:02 Location: Forster Avenue, Weaverham, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Lymm Firefighters  from Norhwich and Lymm were called to reports of a fire in an outbuilding in Weaverham.  On arrival crews found that the fire in the outbuilding was well alight and had also spread to an adjoining garage. Crews used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately 90 minutes.
