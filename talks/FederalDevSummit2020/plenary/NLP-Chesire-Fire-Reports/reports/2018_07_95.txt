 Building fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 19/07/2018 - 11:38 Location: Hinton Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Crewe and one from Sandbach. Firefighters from Crewe and Sandbach used a covering jet to extinguish a domestic building fire on Hinton Road. the team then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property. There are no reports of any injuries.
