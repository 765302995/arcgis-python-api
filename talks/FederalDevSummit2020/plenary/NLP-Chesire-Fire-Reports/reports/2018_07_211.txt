 Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 06/07/2018 - 14:49 Location: Latham Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters attended to a large grass fire at a school on Latham Avenue in Runcorn.  A beater and water were used to put out the fire. Crews then dampened down the area and checked for hot spots.
