Van fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/04/2017 - 10:58 Location: Camberwell Drive, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Penketh Crews have been tackling a fire involving cylinders and drums on the back of a van on Camberwell Drive. There were three propane cylinders, a drum of vitamin and a drum of oil. A cordon was set up as firefighters, two in breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to tackle the blaze.
