 Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/01/2018 - 23:19 Location: Finlan Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters attended a vehicle recovery compound in Finlan Road, Widnes following reports of a car well alight. The crew gained access to the site and used breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the flames.
