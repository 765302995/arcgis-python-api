Firefighters check for fire at Crewe property Time of Incident: 14/08/2015 - 12:05 Location: Ruskin Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters have attended a domestic property in Crewe following reports of burning smell coming from the plug sockets. Crews used a thermal imaging camera to make sure that the scene was safe and a home safety assessment was completed. No sign of fire was found at the property.
