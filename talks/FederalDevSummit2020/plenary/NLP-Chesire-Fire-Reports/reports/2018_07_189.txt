 Jetty fire in Moore Time of Incident: 10/07/2018 - 12:19 Location: Moore Lane, Moore, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - from Warrington and Runcorn At around 12.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a jetty on the Manchester Ship Canal near to Moore Nature Reserve on Moore Lane, Warrington. Crews used a main jet to fight the fire from the bankside. A barge later arrived to enable firefighters to deal with the incident from the waterside as well. There were lifejackets at the scene for firefighters' safety. Once the fire was extinguished crews cooled hotspots from the bankside. Firefighters standing on the barge on the water damped down three jetties to prevent reignition. They also wet a jetty from the bankside. Firefighters were in attendance for more than three hours. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information should call police on 101, give the details via General Enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
