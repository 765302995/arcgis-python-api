 Garage fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 15/04/2018 - 10:00 Location: Northern Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes, one from Runcorn, one from Warrington and one from Merseyside Fire and Rescue Service (Prescott) Garage fire in WidnesFire crews were called to a fire in a garage on Northern Lane, Widnes. On arrival crews discovered that two cars inside the garage were also affected and crews, four wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet and a large fan to extinguish the fire.  Crews were in attendance for five hours.
