 Van fire on M56 Time of Incident: 22/03/2019 - 11:27 Location: M56, eastbound between J9 and J8 Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Birchwood and one from Warrington Firefighters attended to a van on fire on the M56 eastbound carriageway between junction 9 and junction 8.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews used two hose reels to extinguish the fire.  All people were accounted for.
