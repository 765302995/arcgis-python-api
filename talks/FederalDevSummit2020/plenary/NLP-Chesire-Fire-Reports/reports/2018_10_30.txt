 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/10/2018 - 21:49 Location: Shaws Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters in Warrington were called to a fire in a kitchen on Shaws Avenue in Orford.  The fire involved an unattended pan which had a blanket placed over it.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, firefighters removed the pan from the kitchen and ensured it was fully extinguished.
