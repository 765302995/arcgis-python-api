 Lorry fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 17/05/2019 - 08:00 Location: Brookhouse Lane, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton and one from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to Brookhouse Lane, Congleton, after a report of a bin lorry which had smoke coming out the back. The crew removed the load from the bin lorry and extinguished the fire, which had been caused by a small battery.
