 Car fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/04/2018 - 17:10 Location: Thomas Jones Way, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a car fire on Thomas Jones Way, Runcorn. A Toyota Yaris was well alight on arrival of the crews and they, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A man was treated by fire crews at the scene for a burn to his hand.
