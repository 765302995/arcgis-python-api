Road traffic collision in Prestbury Time of Incident: 13/04/2016 - 23:10 Location: Bonis Hall Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Bollington One man was released from a vehicle by firefighters after a road traffic collision. Crews made access to the car to allow paramedics to assess the casualty. He was then removed from the car. The road was closed while the incident was being dealt with. Only one vehicle was involved.
