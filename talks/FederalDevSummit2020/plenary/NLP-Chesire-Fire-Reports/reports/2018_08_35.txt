 Portacabin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/08/2018 - 20:28 Location: Cantilever Bridge, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath and one from Lymm Fire crews were called to a fire in some stacked portacabins near to the Cantilever Bridge, Warrington. The upper portacabin was well alight on arrival of the crews. The crew, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were at the scene for two hours and 30 minutes.
