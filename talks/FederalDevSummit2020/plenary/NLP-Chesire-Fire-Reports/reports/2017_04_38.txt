Roof rescue in Great Budworth Time of Incident: 22/04/2017 - 14:41 Location: Cock Lane, Great Budworth, Northwich Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich and Stockton Heath, with the Knutsford line rescue unit and supporting fire engine and aerial ladder platform from Chester An aerial ladder platform was needed to rescue a woman who had fallen on the roof of a house on Cock Lane. The casualty was tended to by paramedics as crews prepared rope rescue equipment and the aerial ladder platform to carry out the rescue and bring the lady to the ground. The crews were at the scene for over an hour.
