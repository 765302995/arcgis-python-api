Small fire involving bedding in Poynton Time of Incident: 26/09/2016 - 05:23 Location: Vernon Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton Firefighters were called to a small fire involving some bedding in a sheltered housing resort. On arrival, crews discovered that a lamp had fallen onto the bed and burnt a hole into the bedding. The fire was out on arrival so crews proceeded to carry out a safety assessment.  Always switch off lamps before leaving a room. A bedside or desk lamp can start a fire if it comes into contact with fabric.
