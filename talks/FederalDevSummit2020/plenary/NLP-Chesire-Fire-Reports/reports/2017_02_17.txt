Road traffic collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 24/02/2017 - 08:09 Location: A5020 Weston, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Two heavy goods vehicles were in collision, leaving one of the drivers trapped in his cab. Firefighters released the driver and handed him into the care of paramedics. Crews have now left the scene but the A5020 remains closed for clearing.
