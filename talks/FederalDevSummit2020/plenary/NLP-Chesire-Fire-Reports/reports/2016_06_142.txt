Sheds on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 05/06/2016 - 17:29 Location: East Dudley Street, Winsford Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich, Winsford and Holmes Chapel attended Three sheds which containing cylinders were on fire in Winsford. The crews used one hose reel and two main jets to bring the fire under control. The cylinders were cooled
