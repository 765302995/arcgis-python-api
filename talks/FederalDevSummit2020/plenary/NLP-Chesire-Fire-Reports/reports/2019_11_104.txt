 Large fire in open in Middlewich Time of Incident: 09/11/2019 - 16:44 Location: Coalpit Lane, Middlewich. Attendance: A number of fire engines from Middlewich, Winsford, Northwich, Malpas, Crewe and Holmes Chapel have attended Shortly before 4.45pm firefighters were called to reports of a large fire in a field near to Coalpit Lane.  Crews attended and, wearing breathing apparatus, using hose reel jets and a main jet to tackle the fire. They also used a ground monitor to track progress.  Firefighters remained at the scene overnight and were assisted by a local farmer who has used machinery to turn over the contents of the fire.  The fire was brought under control and left for the farmer to tend to.
