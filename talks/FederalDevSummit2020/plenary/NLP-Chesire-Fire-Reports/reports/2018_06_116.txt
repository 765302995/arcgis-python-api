 Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 21/06/2018 - 23:18 Location: Broady Court, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 11.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a vehicle being on fire in a car park at Broady Court, Crewe. The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the car fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
