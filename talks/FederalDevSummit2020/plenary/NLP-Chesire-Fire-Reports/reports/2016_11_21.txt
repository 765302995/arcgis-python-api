Shed fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 26/11/2016 - 13:20 Location: Broughton Lane, Wistaston. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Broughton Lane, Wistaston.  When they arrived crews found that there was a shed on fire. A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.
