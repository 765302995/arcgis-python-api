 Smoke alarm sounding in Appleton Time of Incident: 16/10/2019 - 13:05 Location: Dingleway, Appleton, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly after 1pm firefighters were called to reports of a smoke alarm sounding at an address in Dingleway, Appleton, Warrington.  On arrival at the scene the crew found that it was a false alarm caused by cooking fumes.  Firefighters used a large fan to clear the cooking fumes from the property and were at the scene for around 15 minutes.  For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
