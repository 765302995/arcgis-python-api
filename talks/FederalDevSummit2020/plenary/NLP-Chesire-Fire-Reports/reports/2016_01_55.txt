Overheated washing machine in Chester Time of Incident: 17/01/2016 - 13:41 Location: Derwent Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a washing machine in Chester. The crew inspected the appliance and premises. There was no fire, but the washing machine had overheated. However, one female casualty had inhaled fumes from the appliance and was given oxygen therapy by firefighters. A home safety assessment was carried out.
