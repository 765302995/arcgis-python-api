Car on fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 15/12/2016 - 15:24 Location: M56 west between junctions 11 and 12 Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a car fire and used a hose reel to extinguish it. The hard shoulder and lane one of the motorway were closed while the incident was dealt with.
