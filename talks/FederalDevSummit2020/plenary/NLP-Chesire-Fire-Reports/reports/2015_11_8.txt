Sheep rescued in Astbury Time of Incident: 28/11/2015 - 10:28 Location: Oak Lane, Astbury Attendance: Two fire engines from Congleton and Alsager and the Animal Rescue Unit from Wilmslow Firefighters rescued a sheep stuck on the side of a canal in Astbury.   The animal returned to a nearby field and crews were at the scene for around 35 minutes.
