Child's finger released from hole Time of Incident: 18/02/2015 - 16:24 Location: HSBC, High Street, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters released a child who got her finger stuck in the hole of a perspex panel in a Tarporley bank.  The crew needed to remove the panel used ice to try and reduce the swelling to her finger to fully release her and, once they had released her, the casualty was taken to a local medical centre.
