 Vehicle fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/04/2018 - 01:25 Location: Stewards Avenue, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters from Widnes attended reports of a vehicle fire on Stewards Avenue. The fire was out on arrival, but the crew used one hose reel jet to cool the engine compartment of the vehicle. The fire is currently being treated as suspicious and anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
