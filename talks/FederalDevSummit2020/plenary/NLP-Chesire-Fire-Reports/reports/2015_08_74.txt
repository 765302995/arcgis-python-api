Fire involving cut wheat in a field in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/08/2015 - 15:17 Location: Old Alder Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a fire involving cut wheat in a field in Warrington and used a hose reel and fire beaters to extinguish the flames. The area affected measured approximately 30 x 10 metres.
