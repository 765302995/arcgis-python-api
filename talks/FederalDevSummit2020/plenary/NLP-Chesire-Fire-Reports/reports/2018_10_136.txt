 Fire on the M56 motorway Time of Incident: 06/10/2018 - 14:19 Location: Junction 11-10, M56 motorway Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Warrington Firefighters responded to reports of a fire on the M56 motorway between junctions 11 and 10. On arrival they found some boxes on fire on the hard shoulder. Crews used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames. One casualty suffered minor burns in the incident. They were in attendance for around 25 minutes.
