 Outbuilding building fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 29/08/2018 - 15:52 Location: Village Way, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and two fire engines from Greater Manchester. Firefighters were called to a fire in a large shed in Village Way, Wilmslow. Crews used two breathing apparatus, a hose reel and one hose reel jet to douse the flames.
