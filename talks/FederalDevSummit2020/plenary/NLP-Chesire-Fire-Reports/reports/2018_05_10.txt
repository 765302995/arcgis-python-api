 Shed fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 29/05/2018 - 18:07 Location: Millrace Drive, Wistaston. Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly after 6pm two fire engines from Crewe fire station attended a shed fire in millrace drive Wistaston. On arrival crews found a large shed was well alight. The team used two hose reels and one breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire which also spread to trees and fences. Crews were at the scene for approximately 90 minutes. There were no reports of any injuries.
