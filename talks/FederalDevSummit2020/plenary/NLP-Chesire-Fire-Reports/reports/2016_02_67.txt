Flue causes damage to Bosley caravan Time of Incident: 15/02/2016 - 20:37 Location: Stonyfold Lane, Bosley Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington and one from Macclesfield A flue caused heat damage to a static caravan.  Firefighters had to cut away parts of the structure to access the burnt areas.  They then used a thermal imaging camera to check there were no further areas of heat.
