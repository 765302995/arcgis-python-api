House fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/02/2015 - 03:35 Location: Jordangate, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Two hose reel jets and one covering jet were used to tackle a fire in an end terrace two storey property on Jordangate in Macclesfield. Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus. The gas and electric were isolated. Firefighters checked the roof space with thermal imaging cameras.  A large fan was then used to clear the smoke from the property. A fire investigation was completed.  The incident was then handed over to the police ,so that they could board up the property.
