Road traffic collision on Mill Lane in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 07/04/2017 - 20:04 Location: Mill Lane, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton and one from Bollington Two vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision on Mill Lane in Macclesfield.  Firefighters used cutting equipment to release two casualties from one  vehicle, and one casualty from the other vehicle. The casualties were then handed to the care of the paramedics.
