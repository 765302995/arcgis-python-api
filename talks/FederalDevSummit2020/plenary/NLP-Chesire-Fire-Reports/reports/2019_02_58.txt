 Unattended fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 20/02/2019 - 23:21 Location: Greenbank Lane, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters were called to a small fire in a metal container in a garden at an address in Greenbank Lane, Northwich.The fire had been left unattended so crews used a hose reel to extinguish the fire.
