 Hedge fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 23/05/2018 - 13:55 Location: Lyme Avenue, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Shortly before 2pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in Lyme Avenue, Macclesfield. Crews attended the scene and found that a small bonfire had spread to a nearby hedge. The team used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes.
