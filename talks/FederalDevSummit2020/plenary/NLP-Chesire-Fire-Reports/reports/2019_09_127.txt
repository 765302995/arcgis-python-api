 Industrial oven smoking in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 07/09/2019 - 14:23 Location: Kinsey Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a supermarket in Kinsey Road, Ellesmere Port following reports of smoke coming from an industrial oven.  The store was evacuated as a precaution.  Crews attended and checked the temperature with a thermal imaging camera. They then stripped back the oven to check continued to check the temperature to make sure there was no risk of fire.
