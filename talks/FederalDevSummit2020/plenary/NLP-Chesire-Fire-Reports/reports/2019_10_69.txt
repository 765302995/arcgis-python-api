 Overnight fire involving a caravan and a horse box in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/10/2019 - 14:26 Location: New Cut Lane, Woolston, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Warrington and one from Lymm Shortly before 2.30pm firefighters were called to a report of an overnight fire in New Cut Lane, Woolston, Warrington.  The fire involved a caravan and a horse box.  Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and cooled them down using a hose reel jet.  They were in attendance for around half-an-hour.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
