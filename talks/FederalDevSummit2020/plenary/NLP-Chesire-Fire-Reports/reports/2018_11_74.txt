 Kitchen fire, Macclesfield Time of Incident: 17/11/2018 - 18:45 Location: Alderley Walk, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Crews responded to reports of fire involving a chip pan at a house on Alderley Walk. They arrived to find that the fire was out, but that the elderly occupant had suffered minor burn injuries to her head and face. Crews administered first aid until colleagues from the ambulance service arrived.
