 Garden fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 27/08/2019 - 12:48 Location: Whitby Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port used a hose reel jet to extinguish a fire in a garden which had become out of control on Whitby Lane in the town.  The crew were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes.
