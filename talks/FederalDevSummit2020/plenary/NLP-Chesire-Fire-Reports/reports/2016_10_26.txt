Smell of burning in Knutsford Time of Incident: 26/10/2016 - 11:53 Location: Princess Street, Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich and Wilmslow A building was evacuated due reports of a smell of burning and smoke. This was believed to have been caused by the extractor fan. When firefighters arrived, they found no signs of fire but checked the ventilation system with a thermal imaging camera to make sure it was safe.
