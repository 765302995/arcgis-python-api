 Chemical leak at Lymm Truck Stop Time of Incident: 20/02/2019 - 22:21 Location: Lymm Truck Stop. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port. One fire engine attended from Lymm. One fire engine attended from Birchwood. Three fire engines, an environmental protection unit and a command support unit attended a hazardous materials incident at Lymm Motorway services to a report of a tanker that was parked up and leaking sulphuric acid. Crews used an environmental pack and chemical booms at the incident. Crews were in attendance for seven hours and thirty five minutes.
