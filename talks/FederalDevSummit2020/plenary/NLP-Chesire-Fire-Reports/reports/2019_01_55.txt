 Chimney fire in Chester Time of Incident: 01/01/2019 - 21:23 Location: Lache Park Avenue, Chester Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly before 9.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a tiled chimney fire on Lache Park Avenue in Chester. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire. Firefighters removed a register plate to expose the fire and extinguished it with water. They were in attendance for more than 40 minutes. For tips on avoiding a chimney fire visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chimneys.
