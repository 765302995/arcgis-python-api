Outbuilding building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 30/12/2015 - 03:29 Location: Old Chester Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath, one from Widnes and one from Runcorn Three fire engines were mobilised to an outbuilding fire in Walton, Warrington.  Firefighters used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel jet and a large fan to deal with a fire behind a cooker.
