Car on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 07/10/2015 - 21:01 Location: A5117 Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters tackled a fire involving a car on the A5117 at Ellesmere Port. The crew, one firefighter wearing breathing apparatus, useda hose reel jet to put out the fire. Police attended for traffic control as the car and firefighters were in the roadway, on the M56 slip road. The incident was handed over to the police once the fire was out and the vehicle made safe.
