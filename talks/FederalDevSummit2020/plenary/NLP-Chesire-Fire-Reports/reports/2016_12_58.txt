Fire outside a property on Grasmere Avenue in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/12/2016 - 18:47 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington A disused bed was on fire outside a property on Grasmere Avenue in Warrington.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.   This fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
