 Large fire in the open in Siddington Time of Incident: 07/10/2018 - 18:13 Location: Congleton Road, Siddington. Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Macclesfield, one from Northwich and one from Wilmslow Fire in SiddingtonAt around 6.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a field in Siddington. Crews attended and discovered a large fire involving silage and tyres.  The team used two hose reel jets, two breathing apparatus and one main jet to extinguish the fire. The then used 100 litres of foam on the fire.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately four hours.
