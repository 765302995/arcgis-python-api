Fire involving a pigeon coop in Winsford Time of Incident: 08/04/2017 - 23:07 Location: Queensway, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford A pigeon coop was set on fire at the rear of a property on Queensway in Winsford.  The fire was out before the fire crews arrived, but firefighters used on hose reel jet to damp down and to ensure that the fire was completely out.  A thermal imaging camera was then used to check for hotspots.  A fire investigation has been completed and police were requested to attend.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately.  If you can help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, or call Cheshire Police on 101.
