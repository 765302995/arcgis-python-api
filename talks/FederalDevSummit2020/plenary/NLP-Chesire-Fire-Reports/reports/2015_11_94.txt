Bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 10/11/2015 - 23:34 Location: Halton Brow, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A fire involving a bin at the back of a petrol station in Runcorn is believed to have been started deliberately.  One hose reel was used to extinguish the fire and anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
