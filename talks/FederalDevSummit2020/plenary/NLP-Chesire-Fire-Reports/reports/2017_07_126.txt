Fire involving a porch in Wildboarclough Time of Incident: 06/07/2017 - 18:11 Location: Midgley Lane, Wildboarclough Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield and a fire engine from Buxton in Derbyshire A fire inside a porch of a property was extinguished using a hose reel jet and a dry powder extinguisher.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. The fire was contained in the porch, it didn't spread to property.
