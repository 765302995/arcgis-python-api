Two people injured in Sandbach fire Time of Incident: 12/08/2015 - 22:19 Location: Green Street, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe, one from Sandbach and one from Middlewich.  Firefighters have attended an incident at a property in Green Lane, Sandbach. Using a breathing apparatus, crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish a fire outside the property. All people were quickly accounted for and there were two casualties. A Fire Investigation Officer is attending the scene of the incident.
