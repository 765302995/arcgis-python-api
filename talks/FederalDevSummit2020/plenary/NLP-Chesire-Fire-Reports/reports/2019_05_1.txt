Van fire on M62 Time of Incident: 30/05/2019 - 16:18 Location: M62 eastbound between junctions 9 and 10. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington. Two fire engines from Greater Manchester. Firefighters were called to a van on fire on the M62 eastbound between junctions 9 and 10. The fire followed a two vehicle collision. All occupants were out of the vehicles. Crews used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel to tackle the fire which was also affecting the road surface.
