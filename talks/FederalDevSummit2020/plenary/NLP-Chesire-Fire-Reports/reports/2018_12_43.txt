 Collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/12/2018 - 08:15 Location: Wilton Lane, Culcheth and Glazebury, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engine attended - one from Birchwood and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service At around 8.15am firefighters from Birchwood and Greater Manchester attended reports of a collision in Culcheth and Glazebury. On arrival the team found that one person was trapped in a car which had overturned on Wilton Lane. The team used specialist cutting equipment to free the casualty from the vehicle. They were then handed over to the care of paramedics. Crews were detained at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
