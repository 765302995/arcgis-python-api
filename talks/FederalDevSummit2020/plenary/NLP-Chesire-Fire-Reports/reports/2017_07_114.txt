Large quantity of wood set alight in Crewe Time of Incident: 08/07/2017 - 06:00 Location: Collins Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe A large quantity of wood (approximately three metres by three metres) was set alight in a wooded area opposite Collins Street in Crewe.  This fire was believed to have been started deliberately.  If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
