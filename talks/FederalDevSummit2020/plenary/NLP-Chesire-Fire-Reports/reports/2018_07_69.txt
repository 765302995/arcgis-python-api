 Fire in a portable toilet in Widnes Time of Incident: 23/07/2018 - 18:49 Location: Lytham Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Fire crews were called to fire in a portable toilet on Lytham Road, Widnes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish it. If you have any information about the incident then please contact Cheshire Police.
