 Man rescued by firefighters following a fall in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/04/2018 - 11:20 Location: Warwick Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield and Bollington Firefighters attended an incident on Warwick Road, Macclesfield, after a man had fallen from a tree. The major rescue unit  also proceeded to assist from winsford station.   The man was not trapped and fire crews, along with assistance from the major rescue unit in Winsford, used saws to remove branches from around the casualty before extrication and then he was transported to hospital by air ambulance.
