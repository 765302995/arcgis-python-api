Water rescue in Chester Time of Incident: 25/05/2015 - 21:34 Location: Grosvenor Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines, the aerial appliance and water rescue boat from Chester, rope rescue and supporting fire engine from Knutsford and one fire engine from Ellesmere Port Five swift water rescue technicians from the Service searched the bed of the River Dee after a man was witnessed jumping from the Grosvenor Bridge into the river.  They carried out an extensive search covering quarter of a mile of the shallow river bed.  A man was found further down river and taken to hospital with a head injury.  Police and ambulance personnel, along with the police helicopter, were working with the firefighters in the search.
