 Smoke in building in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/06/2019 - 04:08 Location: Keckwick lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Warrington Firefighters were called to signs of smoke in a building in Runcorn. On arrival everyone from the building was accounted for and the firefighters investigated with a thermal imaging camera. They used a large fan to remove the smoke from the area. The smoke is believed to have been caused by an electrical unit overheating.
