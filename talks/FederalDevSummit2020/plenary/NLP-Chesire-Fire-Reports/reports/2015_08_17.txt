Hay on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/08/2015 - 12:52 Location: Pennington Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Warrington Firefighters have extinguished a fire involving hay in a field in Warrington. Cheshire firefighters worked with a crew from Merseyside Fire and Rescue Service to extinguish the fire using beaters and water back packs.
