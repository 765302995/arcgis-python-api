 Bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 06/10/2018 - 19:56 Location: Finsbury Walk, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters responded to reports of a fire in the open in Finsbury Walk, Winsford. They located it in a bin near the park - it was smouldering and crews used a hose reel to put it out. They checked other bins in the area to make sure there weren't any more fires before leaving.
