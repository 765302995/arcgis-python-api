 Chimney fire in Moulton Time of Incident: 10/01/2018 - 21:17 Location: Main Road, Moulton Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich Shortly after 9.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a building fire on Main Road, Moulton. On arrival at the scene crews found that the fire had been caused by a blocked chimney. The crews attacked the fire from the grate using chimney rods, eventually extinguishing the fire at approximately 10.25pm
