 Storage shed fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 22/03/2018 - 03:57 Location: Holmes Chapel Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich and one from Middlewich Firefighters from Northwich and Middlewich extinguished a barn used as a storage shed which was on fire. A hose reel, a main jet and thermal imaging camera was used to put out the fire.
