Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/04/2015 - 07:03 Location: Montrose Close, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A lady was walked to safety by firefighters called to a kitchen fire in Warrington.   The fire was found to be in the kitchen as a result of a pan left on the stove. Firefighters wore breathing apparatus and used one hose reel to extinguish the fire and a large fan to clear the smoke.   Kitchen fire safety advice
