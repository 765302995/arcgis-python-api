Road traffic collision in Malpas Time of Incident: 21/05/2017 - 08:05 Location: Whitchurch Road, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and one from Whitchurch (Shropshire Fire and Rescue Service) Firefighters were called to a road  traffic collision involving one car and one person trapped. The crew handed the casualty to the care of paramedics.
