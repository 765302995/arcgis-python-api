 Two vehicles on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 18/05/2018 - 00:48 Location: Nelson Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 12.45pm firefighters were called to reports of a vehicle fire on Nelson Street in Crewe. On arrival the crew found two vehicles well alight. Firefighters used two hose reel jets and breathing apparatus to extinguish the fires before damping the area down and making the vehicles safe. They were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
