Flooding across Cheshire East Time of Incident: 13/09/2016 - 17:54 Location: Congleton, Macclesfield and Wilmslow Attendance: Nine fire engines Firefighters are attending a number of flooding incidents across Cheshire East, caused by adverse weather conditions. They have been to several incidents in Congleton, Macclesfield and Wilmslow since just before 6pm.  Please take care if driving in this weather.
