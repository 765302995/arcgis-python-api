Bathroom fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 19/09/2016 - 22:09 Location: Dutton Way, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Alsager Smoke was seen coming from the windows of a second floor flat on Dutton Way. Firefighters found a fire in the bathroom and used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control, with a covering jet in place. Two breathing apparatus were worn. A high pressure fan was used to clear the smoke.
