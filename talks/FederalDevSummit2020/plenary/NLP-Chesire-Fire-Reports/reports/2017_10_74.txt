Small fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 15/10/2017 - 13:41 Location: Woodland Avenue, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton Fire crews were called to a small fire on Woodland Avenue, Congleton. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately, if you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101.
