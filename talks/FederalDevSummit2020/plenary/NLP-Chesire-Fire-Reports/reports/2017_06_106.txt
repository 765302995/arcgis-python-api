Small fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 14/06/2017 - 17:07 Location: Grove Avenue, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow A small fire behind a Wilmslow shop is believed to have been started deliberately. The fire, which involved rubbish, was started next to a gas canister. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put the fire out and to bring the temperature of the canister down.
