 Bush fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 22/04/2019 - 20:14 Location: Beech Hall Drive, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to a fire in a bush on Beech Hall Drive, Macclesfield. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which had been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident please contact Cheshire Police.
