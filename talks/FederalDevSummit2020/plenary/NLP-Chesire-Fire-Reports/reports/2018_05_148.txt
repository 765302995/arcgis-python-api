 Kitchen fire, Alsager Time of Incident: 13/05/2018 - 03:30 Location: Woodland Court, Alsager Attendance: One fire engine from Alsager one from Crewe and one from Sandyford (Staffs) Firefighters responded to reports of a house in fire in Woodland Court, Alsager. On arrival, crews found the fire to be located in the kitchen of the property. They used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.  For kitchen safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety
