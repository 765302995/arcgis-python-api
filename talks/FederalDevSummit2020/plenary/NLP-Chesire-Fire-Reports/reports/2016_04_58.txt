Wood set on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/04/2016 - 20:21 Location: Windscale Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood A group of people were seen setting wood on fire in a Warrington park. Firefighters were called out and used water backpacks to put out the fire.  Arson is dangerous and can cost lives. If you see anyone deliberately setting fires please call Crimestoppers, anonymously,on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.  Help prevent fires in your community - be arson aware
