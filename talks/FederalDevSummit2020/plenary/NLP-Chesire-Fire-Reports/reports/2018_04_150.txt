 Chimney fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 02/04/2018 - 17:58 Location: Northwich Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Fire crews were called to a chimney fire on Northwich Road, Northwich. Crews put out the fire, which was in the hearth, and then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. They checked the area using a thermal imaging camera.
