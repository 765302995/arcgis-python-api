 Two-vehicle collision in Chester Time of Incident: 01/05/2019 - 15:50 Location: Sandy Lane, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Powey Lane and one from Winsford At around 3.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Sandy Lane in Chester. On arrival the crews discovered that the collision involved two vehicles and that a woman was trapped inside one of them. Firefighters and paramedics helped the woman get out of the vehicle. She was then left in the care of the paramedics. Firefighters made the two vehicles involved in the collision safe and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
