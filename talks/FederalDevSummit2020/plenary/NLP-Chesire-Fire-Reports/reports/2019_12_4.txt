 Chimney fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 09/12/2019 - 21:56 Location: Foxcovert Lane, Northwich Attendance: Five fire engines attended - one from Holmes Chapel, one from Middlewich, one from Knutsford, one from Northwich and one from Winsford Crews attended to a chimney fire at an address on Foxcovert Lane in Northwich.  An inspection was made of the chimney stack and roof space using the thermal imaging camera and established it was contained to the chimney.  Firefighters tackled the fire from the grate using chimney rods and a hose reel from a far.  Once it was extinguished the temperature was checked using the thermal imaging camera.
