Collision in Mere Time of Incident: 20/01/2017 - 20:04 Location: Chester Road, Mere. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Chester Road, Mere.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two light goods vehicles. One person was trapped.  Firefighters released the casualty who was then passed to the care of the ambulance service.  The road was closed while the incident was being dealt with.
