Car fire in Rode Heath Time of Incident: 20/06/2017 - 23:03 Location: Low Street, Rode Heath Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsager Firefighters put out a fire involving a car. A hose reel jet was used, with two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus. It is believed the fire was started deliberately and anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
