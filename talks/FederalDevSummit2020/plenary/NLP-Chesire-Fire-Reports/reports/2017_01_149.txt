Chimney fire in Marston Time of Incident: 31/12/2016 - 12:43 Location: Fernlea Road, Marston, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine A hose reel jet and chimney rods were used to tackle a chimney fire on Fernlea Road. The fire was tackled from ground level using the chimney rods while firefighters also attacked it from the roof with a hose reel jet.
