 Rubbish fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/07/2018 - 18:50 Location: Lyncastle Road, Appleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Shortly before 7pm firefighters from Lymm were called to reports of a small fire on Lyncastle Road in Appleton. On arrival the team found that the fire was approximately 5 metres  by 10 metres and involved a large quantity of industrial waste. The team used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately 45 minutes.
