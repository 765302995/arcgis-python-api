 Smoke alarm activated at a flat in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 20/10/2019 - 15:10 Location: Brackenwood Mews, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 3.10pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a ground floor flat in Brackenwood Mews, Wilmslow.  On arrival the crew discovered that no-one was at home.  Firefighters gained entry to the flat and removed a pan from a stove in the kitchen.  Food cooking in the pan had triggered the smoke alarm.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 15 minutes.  For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
