Horse stuck in mud in Arclid Time of Incident: 07/03/2016 - 13:20 Location: Congleton Road, Arclid Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and one from Knutsford together with the Animal Rescue Unit attended A horse that had been stuck in mud was released by firefighters but sadly had to be put to sleep by a vet, after it didn't recover from its ordeal.
