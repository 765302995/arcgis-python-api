 Road traffic collision, Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/01/2019 - 15:49 Location: Roland Avenue, Runcorn Attendance:  Two fire engines from Runcorn Two fire engines from Runcorn attended a road traffic collision involving two saloon cars. A man and a woman were transported to hospital by ambulance, and a further man was treated at the scene by paramedics. Fire service personnel were in attendance for approximately forty minutes making the scene safe.
