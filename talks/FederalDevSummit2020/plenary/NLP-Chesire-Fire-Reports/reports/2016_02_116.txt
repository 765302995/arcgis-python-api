House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/02/2016 - 00:36 Location: Greenwood Crescent, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters led a woman to safety after a fire in her house in Greenwood Crescent. The casualty was handed into the care of the ambulance service and firefighters made sure the fire was out and cleared smoke from the property. An investigation into the cause of the fire is underway. Smoke alarms were fitted and activated, alerting the householder to the fire, prompting her to call 999.
