 Animal rescue in Middlewich Time of Incident: 06/08/2018 - 19:01 Location: Booth Lane, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich and the animal rescue unit from Lymm Seagull rescued from a canalShortly after 7pm firefighters were called by the RSPCA who requested assistance with the rescue of an injured seagull on the Trent and Mersey canal in Middlewich.  Crews attended the scene and used a water sledge to reach the bird, it was then passed to the care of the RSPCA.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately 10 minutes.
