Bed involved in fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/02/2016 - 01:17 Location: Wilderspool Causeway, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called out to a fire involving a bed at a property in Latchford. Two crew members wore breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet was used to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke from the property. Smoke alarms were fitted and alerted the occupiers.
