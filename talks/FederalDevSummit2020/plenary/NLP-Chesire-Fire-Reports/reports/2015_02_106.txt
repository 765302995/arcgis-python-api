Petrol leak in Neston Time of Incident: 04/02/2015 - 14:37 Location: Fallowfield, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port The second floor of a Neston multi-storey car park was closed when petrol was seen to be leaking from a parked car.  Firefighters were called and they used absorbent sheets to contain the spillage until the car owner returned.  The incident was then handed over.
