 Smoke in a house in Penketh Time of Incident: 06/12/2018 - 12:29 Location: Oakmere Drive, Penketh Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Crews were called to reports of smoke in an address caused by a cooker on Oakmere Drive, Penketh.  Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for signs of a fire then a large fan was used to disperse the smoke and make the property safe.  The cooker was isolated and the occupier was given safety advice.  For advice on how to cook safely in your home visit our website https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/home/cooking-safety-tips
