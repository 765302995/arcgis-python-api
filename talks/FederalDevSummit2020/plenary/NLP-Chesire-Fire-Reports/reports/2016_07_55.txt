Lorry fire in Dutton Time of Incident: 20/07/2016 - 18:36 Location: Northwich Road, Dutton Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A hose reel jet has been used by firefighters to cool the engine compartment of a heavy goods vehicle. The lorry is carrying heavy plant machinery which has to be unloaded from the trailer and there is damage to the road so Highways Agency have been asked to attend. Drivers are asked to avoid the area while the incident is being dealt with.
