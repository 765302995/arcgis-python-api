 Wheelie bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 27/08/2019 - 09:51 Location: Beechfield Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port used a hose reel jet to extinguish a wheelie bin fire on Beechfield Road.  The fire is believed to have been caused by a lit cigarette being discarded in the bin.  Firefighters were at the scene for approximately 15 minutes.
