 Skip fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 03/12/2018 - 09:30 Location: Barn Road, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines attended, from Congleton and Macclesfield Skip fire in CongletonAt around 9.30am firefighters from Congleton and Macclesfield attended reports of a fire at a household waste site on Barn Road in Congleton.  On arrival crews found a skip containing electrical waste well alight.  Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and make sure that the area was safe.  Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately an hour-and-a-half.
