Refrigerator fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 05/08/2016 - 09:16 Location: Church Street, Frodsham Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and Runcorn Firefighters were called to reports of a refrigerator fire. On arrival, crews found the fire to be out so continued to investigate the premises, ensuring they were safe. The ambulance service were also in attendance and treated one female for smoke inhalation.
