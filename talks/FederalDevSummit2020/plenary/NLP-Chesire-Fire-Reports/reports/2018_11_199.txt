 Fire on the side of a road in Middlewich Time of Incident: 01/11/2018 - 11:01 Location: Bostock, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At around 11am firefighters were called to reports of a fire on the side of a road near to Davenham roundabout in Bostock, Middlewich. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire involving wood. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
