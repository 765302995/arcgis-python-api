 Fire in the open in Bollington Time of Incident: 20/11/2018 - 12:42 Location: Dunbah Lane, Bollington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters from Macclesfield were called to reports of an unattended fire in the open on Dunbah Lane in Bollington. On arrival the crew found that the landowner was present but the fire had started to spread and become out of control. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to reduce the size of fire and were in attendance for approximately half an hour.
