 Garden fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 04/07/2019 - 23:22 Location: Benbrook Grove, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton and one from Wilmslow Fire crews were called to a fire in a garden on Benbrook Grove, Wilmslow. The fire involved a quantity of rubbish and crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
