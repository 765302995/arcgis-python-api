Unattended grill pan at a flat in Crewe Time of Incident: 19/12/2017 - 18:22 Location: Blount Close, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters attended to reports of a smoke alarm going off at a flat on Bount Close in Crewe. On arrival they noticed a grill pan had been left unattended and wore breathing apparatus when entering the flat.  There were no reports of any injuries.
