Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/04/2016 - 01:18 Location: Bridge Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A car was on fire in a Warrington car park, close to other vehicles. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and asked police to attend as it is believed the fire was started deliberately.  Arson costs lives - did you see anyone setting this fire? If you have any information please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
