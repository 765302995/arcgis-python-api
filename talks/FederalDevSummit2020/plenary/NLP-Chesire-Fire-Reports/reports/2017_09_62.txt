Tumble dryer fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 13/09/2017 - 21:04 Location: Park Road, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a tumble dryer fire in a house on Park Road, Runcorn. On arrival the tumble dryer was well alight in the kitchen of the property.  Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a large fan to put out the fire and clear smoke from the property. Fire crews were at the scene for one hour and 35 minutes.
