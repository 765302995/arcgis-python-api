 Waste fires at allotment in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/10/2019 - 21:08 Location: Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 9pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port.  On arrival the crews discovered four areas of waste well alight at an allotment.  A water supply was established to get enough water to the area, which was difficult to access.  Firefighters used the water to extinguish the four seats of fire and were in attendance for around an hour.  The fires are believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the fires should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 545812, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
