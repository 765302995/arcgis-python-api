Car fire in Mow Cop Time of Incident: 05/09/2016 - 23:33 Location: Top Station Road, Mow Cop Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton A car had rolled into a hedge and was on fire in Top Station Road. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and damp down. Police were asked to attend as the car was partly blocking the road.
