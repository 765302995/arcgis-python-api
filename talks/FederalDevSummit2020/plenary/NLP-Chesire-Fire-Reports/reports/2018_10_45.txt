 Chimney fire in Disley Time of Incident: 24/10/2018 - 19:13 Location: Buxton Old Road, Disley Attendance: A hydraulic platform from Macclesfield and one fire engine from Poynton Firefighters were called to a fire involving a chimney, with reports of flames seen coming out of the pot. A thermal imaging camera was used to check the chimney and the hydraulic platform helped crews to gain access to the chimney and roof of the property. The fire was found in an extractor fan system and extinguished.
