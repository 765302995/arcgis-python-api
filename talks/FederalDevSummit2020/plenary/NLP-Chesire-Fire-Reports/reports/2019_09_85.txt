 Fire in the open, Winsford Time of Incident: 14/09/2019 - 20:05 Location: Delamere Street, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters responded to reports of a fire on land off Delamere Street. Initially they struggled to locate the fire, but when they did so they found a small fire burning away on wasteland. They used a backpack to extinguish the flames.
