 Garden fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 04/09/2019 - 18:43 Location: Arundel Close, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a fire in the garden of an address in Arundel Close, Macclesfield. The fire was a controlled burning which had got out of hand and spread to fencing. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
