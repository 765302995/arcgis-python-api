 Animal rescue in Willaston Time of Incident: 20/07/2018 - 21:15 Location: Adfalent Lane, Willaston near Neston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane, the Animal Rescue Unit from Bollington and the Rope Rescue Unit from Lymm At 9:15pm firefighters from Powey Lane, the Animal Rescue Unit from Bollington and the Rope Rescue Unit from Lymm attended reports of a horse stuck in water on Adfalent Lane in Willaston, Neston. On arrival at the scene the crews found the horse was up to its neck in water. The team worked closely with an equine veterinary nurse to remove the horse from the water to a nearby field where it was safely assessed. Fire crews were in attendance for approximately 90 minutes.
