 Outbuilding fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 01/04/2019 - 03:49 Location: Farm Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Firefighters were called to a fire in an outbuilding on Farm Road, Northwich. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a covering jet to put out the blaze. Crews were at the scene for three hours.
