 Two vehicle collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 09/12/2019 - 13:48 Location: Weston Road, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 1.50pm firefighters were called to reports of a two vehicle collision on Weston Road, Crewe.  On arrival at the scene the crew found that had been a collision between a car and a van. The van was carrying a number of LPG Cylinders.  Firefighters forced entry into the van to ensure that all cylinders had not been damaged.  The crew also made the vehicle safe.  The crew were in attendance at the incident for around one hour.
