 Chimney fire in Cuddington Time of Incident: 05/04/2018 - 20:50 Location: Hadrian Way, Cuddington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters from Northwich used specialist chimney rods to extinguish a chimney fire at a residential address in Cuddington. The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately one hour.  For more information about chimney safety visit: Chimney Fire Safety
