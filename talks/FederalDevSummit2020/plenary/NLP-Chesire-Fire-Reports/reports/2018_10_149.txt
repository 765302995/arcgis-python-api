 Two-vehicle collision in Alsager Time of Incident: 03/10/2018 - 15:52 Location: Merelake Road, Alsager Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Alsagar and one from Staffordshire At around 3.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving two vehicles on Merelake Road in Alsager. On arrival crews found that two people were in one of the vehicles that was on its side on an embankment. Firefighters stabilised the vehicle to enable the two people to safely get out of it. They did not require an ambulance. Firefighters moved the vehicle back onto the roadway and were in attendance for more than an hour-and-a-half.
