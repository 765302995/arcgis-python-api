Rubbish fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 03/01/2017 - 19:14 Location: Bradbury Road, Winsford. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a fire Bradbury Road, Winsford.  When they arrived crews found that there was a large amount of rubbish on fire. Hose reel jets were used to put the fire out.
