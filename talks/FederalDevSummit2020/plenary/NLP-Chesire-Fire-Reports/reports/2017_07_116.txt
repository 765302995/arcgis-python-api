Skip on fire in Rudheath, Northwich Time of Incident: 07/07/2017 - 20:10 Location: Scrapyard, behind Rudheath social club, off Middlewich Road, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich A skip was well alight on a scrapyard, so firefighters gained access to the yard and then used a hose reel jet and one main jet to extinguish the fire.
