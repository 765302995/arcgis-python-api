Building fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 11/03/2017 - 14:54 Location: Station Road, opposite Delamere Railway Station. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Northwich and Frodsham Firefighters responded to a report of a building fire on Station Road, opposite Delamere Railway Station.  When they arrived they found that there was some rubbish in an empty building. A hose reel jet was used to put it out.
