Horse stuck in ditch in Chester Time of Incident: 14/12/2016 - 21:38 Location: Willington Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester and animal rescue team from Bollington A horse which had got stuck in a ditch needed firefighters to help her get out.  The horse was in soft mud and was unable to get out.  A vet attended to sedate her, so that the crew could attach specialist equipment to help her out while the farmer used a digger to create a ramp in the bankside.
