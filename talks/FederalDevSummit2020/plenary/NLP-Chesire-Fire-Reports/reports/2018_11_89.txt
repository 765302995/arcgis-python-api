 Garage fire in Audlem Time of Incident: 16/11/2018 - 07:31 Location: Whitchurch Road, Audlem. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe attended. One fire engine from Nantwich attended. Firefighters were called to a single storey garage which was well alight in Whitchurch Road in Audlem. One hose reel jet, a covering jet and two breathing apparatus were used to put out the fire. They then damped down the area to prevent the fire reigniting.
