 Tractor fire in Waverton Time of Incident: 29/03/2018 - 16:52 Location: Guy Lane, Waverton, near Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one fire engine attended from Powey Lane. Firefighters from Chester and Powey Lane used two hose reel jets and one breathing apparatus to extinguish a tractor fire at a fire in Waverton, near Chester. The team were in attendance for approximately 50 minutes.
