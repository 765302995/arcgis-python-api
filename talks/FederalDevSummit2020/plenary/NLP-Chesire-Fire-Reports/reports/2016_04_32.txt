Unattended cooking fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 23/04/2016 - 12:34 Location: Samuel Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Unattended cooking caused a small fire at a house in Crewe. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, with a hose reel jet, gained entry to the property and found cooking had been left on the oven.
