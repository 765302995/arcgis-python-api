Kitchen fire in Chester Time of Incident: 28/10/2017 - 15:21 Location: Victoria Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Chester. One fire engine from Runcorn. One fire engine from Ellesmere Port. A kitchen fire in a ground floor flat was extinguished by firefighters using two breathing apparatus, one hose reel and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire. One casualty was taken to hospital via ambulance.
