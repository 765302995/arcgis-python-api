M62 car fire between J11 Birchwood and J12 Eccles Time of Incident: 22/01/2016 - 09:06 Location: M62 between J11 Birchwood and J12 Eccles Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Warrington Fire and rescue crews were called to a vehicle fire eastbound on the M62 between J11 Birchwood and J12 Eccles interchange. The crew used a hose reel to extinguish the flames and fended off the traffic with a fire engine, for the safety of the firefighters, while the incident was dealt with.  The fire is believed to have started with a fault in the engine compartment.
