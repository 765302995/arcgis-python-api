Road traffic collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 14/04/2015 - 18:29 Location: Wallerscote Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford. The door of a car was removed to release one person trapped after a collision in Northwich.  Three vehicles were involved with one car overturned on its roof and a woman trapped. The casualty was removed on a longboard and handed into the care of paramedics. Three people in total were taken to hospital.
