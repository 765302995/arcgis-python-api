 Chip pan fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 27/03/2018 - 15:48 Location: Abbey Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At 3.48pm firefighters were called to a fire at an apartment in Abbey Court in Ellesmere Port. The fire involving a chip pan was out on arrival.The crew used a large fan to clear smoke from the premises.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 25 minutes.
