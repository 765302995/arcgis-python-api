Man trapped in Chester Time of Incident: 28/04/2016 - 19:03 Location: Parkgate Road, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Parkgate Road, Chester.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two cars. There was a man trapped in one car. Special cutting equipment was used to free him. He was then passed to the care of the ambulance service and taken to hospital.
