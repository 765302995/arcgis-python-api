 Cooking fire at house in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/11/2019 - 22:08 Location: Batherton Close, Widnes. WA8 7SF Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes, one from Runcorn and one from Penketh Firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a house in Widnes just after 10pm.  Crews attended and, wearing breathing apparatus, used a large fan to clear smoke caused by a cooking fire.  One person was given first aid and left in the care of North West Ambulance Service.
