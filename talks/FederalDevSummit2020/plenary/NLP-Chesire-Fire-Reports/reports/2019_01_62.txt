 Crewe wheelie bin fire Time of Incident: 31/12/2018 - 20:36 Location: Lansdowne Road, Crewe Attendance:  One fire engine from Crewe A hose reel jet was used to put out a fire in a wheelie bin on Lansdowne Road. Firefighters then used a thermal imaging camera to check there was no further heat spread to a nearby property.  Please follow our wheelie bin safety advice.
