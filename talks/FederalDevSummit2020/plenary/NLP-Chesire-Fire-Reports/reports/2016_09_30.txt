Collision in Chester Time of Incident: 25/09/2016 - 02:10 Location: Holme Street in Tarvin. Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Firefighters responded to collision on Holme Street in Tarvin.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two vehicles. there was nobody trapped but three people were injured. Crews helped get one of the casualties onto a longboard so that they could be taken to hospital.
