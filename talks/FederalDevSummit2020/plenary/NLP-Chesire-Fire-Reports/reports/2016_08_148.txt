Man rescued from high level platform in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/08/2016 - 12:07 Location: Waterloo Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters helped to rescue a man who was taken ill on a high level platform on Waterloo Road. The crew manoeuvred the casualty onto a longboard and he was lowered to the ground by crane. He was then handed into the care of paramedics.
