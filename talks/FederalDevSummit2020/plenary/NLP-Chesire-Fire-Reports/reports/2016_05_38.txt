Garage fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/05/2016 - 17:58 Location: Windermere Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Windermere Avenue, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that there was a garage on fire. Crews used hose reel jets and a large fan to tackle the fire and stop it from spreading to the other garages next to it.  Crews were at the scene for about two hours. A fire investigation has now been launched to find the cause.
