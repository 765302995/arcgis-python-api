Grass fire on Runcorn Hill Time of Incident: 21/06/2015 - 11:39 Location: Highlands Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of a grass fire on Runcorn Hill.  The crew had to make their way to the incident on foot because of its location that meant the fire engine was unable to get close enough.  They used hand extinguishers to put the fire out.
