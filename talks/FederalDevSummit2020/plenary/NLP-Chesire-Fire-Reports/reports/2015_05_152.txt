Insulation fire in Bollington Time of Incident: 03/05/2015 - 10:03 Location: Church Street, Bollington. Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington Firefighters responded to a smoke alarm sounding at a property on Church Street, Bollington.  When they arrived they found that the fire involved an insulation jacket of a hot water cylinder in a kitchen. This had gone out and so firefighters made the scene safe and remained in attendance for approximately one hour and fifteen minutes.
