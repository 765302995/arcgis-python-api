 Three vehicle collision, M56 motorway Time of Incident: 15/02/2019 - 15:54 Location: Junction 12 westbound, M56 motorway Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and Penketh Crews responded to reports of a collision shortly before 4pm on the M56 westbound carriageway, near junction 12. They found the collision involved three vehicles and had occurred in the third lane of the carriageway. The lane was shut while crews made the vehicles safe, and then moved them onto the hard shoulder. None of the drivers or passengers in the vehicles were trapped and they were attended to by paramedics at the scene.
