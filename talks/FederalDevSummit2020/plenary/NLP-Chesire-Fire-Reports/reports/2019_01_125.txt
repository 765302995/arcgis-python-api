 Hotel cellar fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 16/01/2019 - 21:58 Location: Chester Road, Northwich Attendance:  Two fire engines attended from Northwich and one fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters attended to reports of smoke in a cellar at a hotel in Northwich.  Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, used dry powder as well as a thermal imaging camera to disperse the smoke.   The smoke is believed to have come from a coke machine regassing.
