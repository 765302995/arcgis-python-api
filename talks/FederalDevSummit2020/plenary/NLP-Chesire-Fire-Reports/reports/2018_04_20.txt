 Collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 25/04/2018 - 18:20 Location: M56 near Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one fire engine from Powey Lane At 18:20 hours on the 25th of April 2018 three fire engines form Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane attended reports of a vehicle fire on the M56 motorway eastbound between junctions 15 and 14.  Firefighters attended the scene and found the incident involved two saloon vehicles which had hit the central reservation.  Four casualties were treated at the scene by firefighters with oxygen therapy before being handed to the care of ambulance services. Crews were in attendance for approximately fifty-five minutes making the vehicles safe.
