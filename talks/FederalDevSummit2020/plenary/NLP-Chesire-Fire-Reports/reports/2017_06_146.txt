Small fire by a bridge in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/06/2017 - 18:17 Location: Cromwell avenue footpath to rear of Rutter Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters used handheld fire extinguishers to deal with a small fire by a bridge on a footpath close to Rutter Avenue.  Firefighters believe the fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
