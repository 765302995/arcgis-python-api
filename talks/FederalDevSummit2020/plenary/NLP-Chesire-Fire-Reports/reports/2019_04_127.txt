 Caravan fire in Winwick Time of Incident: 16/04/2019 - 06:45 Location: Alder Lane, Warrington. Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Warrington and two from Merseyside At around 6.45am firefighters from Warrington and Merseyside attended reports of a fire next to the railway line on Alder Lane, Winwick.  On arrival the team discovered a caravan which was well alight.  The team used two hose reels to extinguish the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  A temporary closure was put in place on the railway line while the team dealt with the fire.    Crews remained in attendance for two hours and 50 minutes.
