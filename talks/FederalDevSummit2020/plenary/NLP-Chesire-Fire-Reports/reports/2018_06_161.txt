 Shed fire in Neston Time of Incident: 17/06/2018 - 13:12 Location: Hampton Close, Neston Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port and one from Merseyside Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a shed fire on Hampton Close, Neston. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. A casualty, who was suffering from burns to his leg, was given first aid at the scene and then handed into the care of paramedics. Crews were in attendance for an hour and 20 minutes.
