 Gym fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/08/2018 - 00:26 Location: Bewsey Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington, Lymm and Penketh Firefighters attended to a fire at a gym on Bewsey Road in Warrington.  The fire was contained to the first floor and crews, wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reels and a positive pressure ventilation fan to put out the fire.  Crews then dampened down the affected area and checked for hot spots using a thermal imaging camera. Firefighters were in attendance for four hours and 30 minutes.  The fire is believed at this stage to have been caused deliberately. Anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 165750.
