 One vehicle collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 13/11/2018 - 13:50 Location: Timbrell Avenue, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly before 2pm firefighters from Crewe attended reports of a one vehicle collision on Timbrell Avenue. On arrival crews found that a car was smoking after colliding with two parked vehicles. All occupants were out of the vehicle and there were no reports of any injuries. The team cooled the engine compartment and made the vehicle safe. Crews were detained at the scene for approximately 30 minutes.
