 Poolhouse evacuated following chlorine spillage in Mollington, near Chester Time of Incident: 25/08/2019 - 14:09 Location: Parkgate Road, Mollington, near Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Powey Lane and one from Chester Shortly after 2pm firefighters were called to a report of a chlorine spillage in a poolhouse on Parkgate Road in Mollington village, near Chester. On arrival at Crabwall Manor Hotel and Spa the crews found that it was a small spillage and that the poolhouse had been evacuated. A man complaining of breathing difficulties was assessed by paramedics and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a gas monitor to check the levels of gas in the poolhouse. There were no readings of gas in the poolhouse. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
