 Small fire in Chester Time of Incident: 16/09/2019 - 23:00 Location: Whitchurch Road, Christleton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester. One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port. Firefighters were called to a fire involving a small amount of paper at a hotel in Whitchurch Road, Christleton. Crews used buckets of water to put out the fire.
