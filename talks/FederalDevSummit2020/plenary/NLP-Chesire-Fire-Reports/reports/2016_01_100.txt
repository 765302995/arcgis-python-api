Car overturned on the A54 (Buxton Road) in Congleton Time of Incident: 02/01/2016 - 12:30 Location: A54, Congleton. Attendance: Fire engines from Congleton and Macclesfield A car overturned on the A54 (Buxton Road) in Congleton, with two people trapped inside the vehicle. Fire engines from Congleton and Macclesfield were sent to the incident.  Firefighters stabilised the vehicle to release the casualties to the care of attending paramedics. Police also attended the incident.
