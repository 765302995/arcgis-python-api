 Small fire in woodland in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 20/06/2018 - 21:25 Location: Meriton Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 9.25pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in woodland on Meriton Road in Wilmslow. The crew extinguished the small fire using water and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call police on 101.
