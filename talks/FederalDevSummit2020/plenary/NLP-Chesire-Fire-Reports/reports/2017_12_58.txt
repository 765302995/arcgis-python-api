Fire in living room in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/12/2017 - 22:50 Location: Philips Lane, Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port attended A fire involving the living room of a bungalow was extinguished by firefighters using a hose reel. A large fan was also used the remove smoke from the property and a thermal imaging camera to check the area. Two casualties suffered smoke inhalation and were handed to the care of paramedics. This fire was accidental and is believed to have been caused by electrical heating.
