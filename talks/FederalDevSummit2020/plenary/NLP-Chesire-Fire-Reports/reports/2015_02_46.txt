Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/02/2015 - 12:24 Location: Seymour Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port The cause of a fire in the kitchen of an Ellesmere Port house is being investigated.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire, followed by a high pressure fan to clear the smoke.
