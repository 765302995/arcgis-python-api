Garage on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 27/08/2015 - 21:44 Location: Wharton Gardens, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to extinguish a garage which was on fire in Winsford. The crew used a hose reel to put the flames out. Police were requested to attend as it is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information they should call Crimestoppers  anonymously on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
