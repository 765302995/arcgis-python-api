 Dog in canal in Mollington Time of Incident: 13/03/2018 - 15:15 Location: Parkgate Road, Mollington Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to rescue a dog in the canal off Parkgate Road in Mollington. Crews used wading poles to locate the dog which was believed to be under water. Sadly, the dog was located deceased.
