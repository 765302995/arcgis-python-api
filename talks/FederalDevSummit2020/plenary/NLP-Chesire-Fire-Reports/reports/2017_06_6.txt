Car fire on M6 junction 20 to 19 Time of Incident: 27/06/2017 - 10:16 Location: M6 southbound between junction 20 and 19 Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel, Nortwich and Knutsford attended the incident We are attending a car fire on the M6 southbound, junction 20 to 19. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to tackle the Peugot 306 which was on fire.
