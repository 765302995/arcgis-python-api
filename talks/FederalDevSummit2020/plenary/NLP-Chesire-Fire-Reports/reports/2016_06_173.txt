Wheelie bin on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 31/05/2016 - 23:58 Location: Tanners Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A wheelie bin which is believed to have been deliberately set on fire next to a building, was extinguished with a hose reel. If anyone knows anything about this fire or who may have started it they should phone Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. Wheelie Bin - Fire Safety advice
