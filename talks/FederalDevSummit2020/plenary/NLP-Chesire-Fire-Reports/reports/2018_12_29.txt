 Wheelie bin fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 23/12/2018 - 01:41 Location: Willow Street, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton At around 1.40am firefighters were called to a report of a wheelie bin being on fire in Willow Street, Congleton. On arrival the crew found he wheelie bin well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire in the open and were in attendance for around 40 minutes. Police also attended. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For wheelie bin fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
