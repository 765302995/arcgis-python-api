 Peat land fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/08/2018 - 17:50 Location: Heath Park, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters attended to a fire involving 10sqm of peat land on Heath Park in Runcorn.  On arrival crews detected several hot spot areas and used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and cool the surrounding area.
