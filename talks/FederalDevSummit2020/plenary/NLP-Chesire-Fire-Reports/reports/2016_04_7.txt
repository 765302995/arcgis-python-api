Chip pan fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 28/04/2016 - 11:33 Location: Flatt Lane, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A chip pan fire was tackled by firefighters. They removed the pan to the garden and used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke from the property.  Traditional chip pans can cause fatal fires so please follow our chip pan safety advice.
