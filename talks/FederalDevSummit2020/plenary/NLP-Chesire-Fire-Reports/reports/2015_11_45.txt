Tree fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/11/2015 - 18:03 Location: Newhaven Road, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Newhaven Road, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved several conifer trees. A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
