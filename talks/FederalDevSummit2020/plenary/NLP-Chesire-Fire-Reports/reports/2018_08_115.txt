 Log fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/08/2018 - 10:32 Location: Park Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to land off Park Lane in Warrington following reports of a fire. Crews located the fire by a footpath involving a tree stump and a number of logs and then located another fire of a similar size. Firefighters are establishing a water source in order to fight the fires.  1.13pm Crews set up a 70mil delivery hose and high pressure hose. They are currently working to extinguish the fire which is deep seated within the tree stump.  Fire extinguished shortly after 2pm.
