Investigation of a sinking boat in Runcorn Time of Incident: 06/06/2017 - 21:29 Location: Marina Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a canal boat sinking on Marina Lane, Runcorn. Crews were asked by police to investigate whether there was anyone on the boat who needed rescuing. Fire crews searched the boat but found no one on board. They handed the incident back to the marina site manager.
