 Car on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 09/05/2018 - 12:06 Location: The slip road of the A5117 eastbound leading to the A540 in Woodbank, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port At around 12.05pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on the slip road of the A5117 eastbound leading to the A540 in Woodbank, Chester. On arrival crews found the vehicle to be well alight and temporarily closed the slip road to deal with the incident. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour and 10 minutes.
