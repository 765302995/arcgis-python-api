House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/11/2016 - 06:03 Location: Welwyn Close, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and Stockton Heath  Firefighters were called to a report of a building fire on Welwyn Close, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that a fire involving wheelie bins had spread to a garage that was attached to a semi-detached house.   Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish the fire. Crews also used a thermal image camera to check the neigbouring properties to make sure that they were safe.   An ambulance was also called to help treat a man who was suffering from the effects of inhaling smoke.   It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information that could help find those responsible they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
