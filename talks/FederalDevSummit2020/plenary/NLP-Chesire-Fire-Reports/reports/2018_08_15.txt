 Rubbish fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 27/08/2018 - 20:29 Location: Seacombe Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to Seacombe Drive, Ellesmere Port, after a report of a rubbish fire. Crews used a stack drag and a fire extinguisher to put out the fire.
