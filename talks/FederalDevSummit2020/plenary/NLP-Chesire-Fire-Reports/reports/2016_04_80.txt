Caravan well alight on Meadows Green Caravan Park in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/04/2016 - 16:39 Location: Meadows Green Caravan Park, Heath Lane, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one fire engine from Ellesmere Port A caravan was well alight and two propane cylinders were also involved in the fire. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The cylinders were then cooled and monitored by the fire crews. Firefighters left the scene at 17:42 pm.
