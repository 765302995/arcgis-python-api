 Woman trapped in Dutton Time of Incident: 09/08/2019 - 13:52 Location: Northwich Road, Dutton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Shortly before 2pm firefighters were called to a report of a woman being trapped between a car and a wall on Northwich Road in Dutton, near Northwich. The crew used specialist equipment to move the vehicle away from the wall and free the casualty. She was then handed over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
