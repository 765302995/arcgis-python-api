Car well alight on M56 by J12 westbound Time of Incident: 18/06/2016 - 13:04 Location: M56 motorway (Junction 12 westbound). Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Frodsham Two hose reel jets were used to extinguish a fire involving a car on the M56 motorway (Junction 12 westbound).  The car was well alight.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.
