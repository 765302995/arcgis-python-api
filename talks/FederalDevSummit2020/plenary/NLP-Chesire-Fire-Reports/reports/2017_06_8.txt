Garage fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/06/2017 - 01:53 Location: Kerfoot Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a car garage fire on Kerfoot Street, Warrington. On arrival crews found that the fire was external and to the rear of the building. They used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for around three hours. A fire investigation into the incident has started.
