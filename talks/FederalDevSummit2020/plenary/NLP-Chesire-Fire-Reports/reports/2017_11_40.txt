House fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 17/11/2017 - 23:01 Location: Woodland Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port were called to reports of a house fire on Woodlands Way. The fire was out on arrival but firefighters used a large fan to clear all of the smoke from the property.
