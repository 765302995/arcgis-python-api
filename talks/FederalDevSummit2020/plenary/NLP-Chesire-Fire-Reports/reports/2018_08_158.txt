 Hay bale fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 04/08/2018 - 11:46 Location: Seven Sisters Lane, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters attended to a fire on Seven Sisters Lane in Knutsford involving 30 hay bales well alight.  Crews used a stack drag and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire before dampening down the area.  They were in attendance for three areas.
