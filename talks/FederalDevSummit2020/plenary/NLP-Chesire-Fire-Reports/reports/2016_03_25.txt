Smoke filled flat in Northwich Time of Incident: 26/03/2016 - 05:46 Location: Heber Walk, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters gained entry to a flat where cooking had been left unattended on the cooker in the kitchen.  The kitchen was full of smoke.  Fire crews used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.   Kitchen fire safety advice
