Sparks escaping from a chimney in Chester Time of Incident: 02/04/2015 - 20:10 Location: Village road, Dunham on the Hill, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Smoke and sparks were escaping from a chimney.  There was a fire in the hearth, so this was extinguished.   A roof ladder was used to complete an external inspection of the roof using a thermal imaging camera.  The attics of four properties were checked for fire.
