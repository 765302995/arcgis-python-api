Man stuck in cherry picker at height in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/08/2015 - 04:04 Location: Thelwall Viaduct, Warrington Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington, Knutsford and Stockton Heath and the hydraulic platform A man was rescued from a cherry picker which was stuck at height on the Thelwall Viaduct. The crew used the hydraulic platform to get to the man and get him down from the machine.
