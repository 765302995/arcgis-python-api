Building on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 29/04/2015 - 16:40 Location: Winnington Street, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford Firefighters attended a call to a disused building on fire in Northwich. Crews used two hose reels to extinguish the fire and a large fan to remove smoke from the building. Police attended. A fire investigation into the cause will take place.  Update at 10am on April 30  This fire is suspected to have been started deliberately. It involved rubbish in the front room on the first floor of the property. The fire affected the floor and caused smoke and heat damage.  If anyone has any information as to who may have started this fire they can call Crimestopper anonymously on 0800 555 111.
