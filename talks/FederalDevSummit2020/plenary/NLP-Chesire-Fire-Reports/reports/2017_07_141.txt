Two small fires involving grass in Crewe Time of Incident: 04/07/2017 - 15:52 Location: Cranborne Road, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Two small fires which involved grass adjacent to a railway track were extinguished by firefighters using hand appliances. It is believed that the fire may have been started deliberately. If you know anything about who may have started them you should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
