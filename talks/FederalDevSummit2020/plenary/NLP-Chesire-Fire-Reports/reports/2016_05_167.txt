Lorry on fire on M6 southbound near Warrington Time of Incident: 02/05/2016 - 13:02 Location: Thelwall Viaduct, M6 southbound, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood, two from Warrington and one from Altrincham (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) A heavy goods vehicle was in collision with the central reservation barrier on the Thelwall Viaduct before catching fire. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, put out the fire, which was in the engine compartment, using two hose reel jets. One casualty had been removed from the cab before the crews arrived. Firefighters were at the scene  for approximately one hour ten minutes.
