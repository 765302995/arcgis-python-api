 Building fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/07/2018 - 14:09 Location: Brackley Street, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters from Runcorn were called to reports of a domestic building fire on Brackley Street, Runcorn. On arrival crews found a small fire involving a washing machine. Crews isolated the electricity and removed the machine from the kitchen. The team were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes.
