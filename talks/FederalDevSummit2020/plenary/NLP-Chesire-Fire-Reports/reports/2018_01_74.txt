 Bin fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 12/01/2018 - 18:19 Location: Upton Community Centre, Hough Green Road, Widnes Attendance: One appliance from Widnes Firefighters dealt with a bin fire, which was well alight in the park area. They used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames. The fire is thought to have been a deliberate ignition - anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire police on 101, quoting incident 723 of 12 January 2018
