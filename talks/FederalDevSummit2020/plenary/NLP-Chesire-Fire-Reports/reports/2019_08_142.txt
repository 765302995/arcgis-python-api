 Wheelie bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 04/08/2019 - 17:14 Location: Hillside Lane, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich. One fire engine from Winsford. Firefighters were  called to wheelie bins on fire at an address in Hillside Lane, Winsford. Crews used hose reel jets and breathing apparatus  to extinguish the flames. They also isolated the electrics and checked the attic to ensure the fire had not spread.
