Building fire in Birchwood Time of Incident: 19/11/2015 - 14:26 Location: AMEC Foster Wheeler, Faraday Street, Birchwood Attendance: Eight fire engines  Firefighters have been tackling a fire at a Birchwood company.  The crews, several firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used hose reel jets to put the fire out.  The fire involved two fume cupboards. Severe heat and smoke damage was caused to the room in which they were sited, with smoke damage in the adjoining corridor.  There was heavy smokelogging which was cleared using high pressure fans.  At the height of the incident eight fire engines were at the scene.
