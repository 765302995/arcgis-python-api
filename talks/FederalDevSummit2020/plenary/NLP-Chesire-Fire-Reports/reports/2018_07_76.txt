 Small fire at a skate park in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/07/2018 - 23:46 Location: Whitby, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 11.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire on a ramp at a skate park in Whitby, Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
