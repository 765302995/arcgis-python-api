 Dust extraction unit fire in Wardle Time of Incident: 13/09/2018 - 16:43 Location: Green Lane, Wardle. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one fire engine from Audlum Firefighters were called to a fire in a dust extraction unit at a business premises in Green Lane, Wardle. Crews used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the flames. Crews then continued to damp down the extraction unit which is approximately 5metres by 10metres in a steel cladded building.
