Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/12/2015 - 09:19 Location: Sankey Way, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a road traffic collision involving two cars. A lamp post was damaged in the incident and was lying across one of the cars so Highways were asked to attend. One of the fire engines remained at the scene until the Highways Agency arrived.
