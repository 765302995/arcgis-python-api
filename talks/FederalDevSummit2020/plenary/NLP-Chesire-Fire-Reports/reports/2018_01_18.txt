 Crash between three vehicles on M6 Time of Incident: 26/01/2018 - 11:53 Location: M6 southbound between junction 17 and 16 Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe, one from Sandbach, one from Alsager and the major rescue unit from Winsford A crash between a lorry, a van and a car on the M6 southbound was attended by fire crews. They found one person had been trapped in the collision and extracted the casualty before handing them into the care of paramedics. Two lanes were closed on the motorway whilst the rescue took place. Crews were at the scene for one hour and 15 minutes.
