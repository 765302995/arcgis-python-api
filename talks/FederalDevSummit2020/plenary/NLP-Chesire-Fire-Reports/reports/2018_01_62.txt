 Fire in wood burning stove in Chester Time of Incident: 14/01/2018 - 16:50 Location: Croughton Road, Croughton, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Powey LAne. Fire crews attended a fire in a wood burning stove in Croughton Road, Croughton. A wooden frame behind the stove had caught alight. Crews used water to put out the fire and cut away the wooden frame.
