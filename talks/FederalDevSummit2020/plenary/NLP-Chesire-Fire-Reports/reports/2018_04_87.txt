 Bin fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 14/04/2018 - 19:59 Location: Sankey Valley Park, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Crews tackled a bin fire in Sankey Valley Park, using a hand appliance to extinguish the flames. It is thought to have been a deliberate ignition, information can be passed to Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 36446.
