 House fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/09/2018 - 21:23 Location: Rose Street, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Widnes At around 9.25pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a semi-detached house in Rose Street, Widnes. On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved a bedroom and that all persons were accounted for. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews then used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property. Two causalities suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation were handed over to the care of paramedics. The fire is believed to have been started accidentally. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour and 40 minutes.
