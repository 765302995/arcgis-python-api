Garden fire in Chester Time of Incident: 10/05/2016 - 11:44 Location: Croughton Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Crews have been tackling a large garden fire. Two fire engines were needed for the amount of water that was required to put out the blaze. Approximately 50 metres x 20 metres of conifers and hedgerow were involved, but no buildings or outbuildings were affected.
