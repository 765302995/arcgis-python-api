 Three-vehicle collision in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 18/01/2019 - 08:46 Location: Styal Road, Wilmslow Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Wilmslow and one from Manchester At around 8.45am firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Styal Road in Wilmslow. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved three vehicles and than no-one was trapped inside any of them. Firefighters made one of the vehicles safe and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. The incident was also attended by police and paramedics, who tended to casualties. The road remained open while emergency services worked at the scene.
