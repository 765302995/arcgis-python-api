Circuit box fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 25/11/2015 - 18:33 Location: Charlecote Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton and one from Greater Manchester Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel jet to extinguish a fire involving an electrical circuit box.  A large fan was also used to clear the smoke and a thermal imaging camera to check for hotpots. National Grid attended the scene and the incident was handed over to them.
