Two cars on fire in High Legh Time of Incident: 16/04/2015 - 21:27 Location: West Lane, High Legh Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters extinguished a fire involving two cars on the driveway of a property in High Legh.  The crew used two hose reels, breathing apparatus and one medium expansion foamline to deal with the incident.
