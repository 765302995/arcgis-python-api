 Two-vehicle collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/10/2019 - 09:21 Location: Winwick Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 9.20am firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Winwick Road in Warrington.  On arrival the crew discovered that the collision involved two vehicles and that no-one was trapped inside either of them.  Firefighters made the vehicles safe and assisted the police in assessing the people who had been involved in the collision.  It was decided that an ambulance was not required.  Firefighters were in attendance for less than 15 minutes.
