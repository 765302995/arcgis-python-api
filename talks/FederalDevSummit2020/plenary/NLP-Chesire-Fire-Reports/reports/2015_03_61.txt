Chip pan fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/03/2015 - 02:05 Location: Frederick Street, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Widnes A woman from Widnes escaped a fire after a chip pan was left on a cooker. When firefighters arrived neighbours had got her out of the first floor flat. One hose reel was used to extinguish the fire. The woman suffered smoke inhalation and was treated by the crew before being handed to the care of Paramedics.  Chip pan safety advice
