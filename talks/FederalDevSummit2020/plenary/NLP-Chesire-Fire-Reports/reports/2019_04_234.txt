 Accidental house fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/04/2019 - 07:26 Location: Solway Grove, Runcorn. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Widnes Shortly before 7.30am firefighters from Runcorn and Widnes attended reports of a house fire on Solvay Grove.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered that a birthday card had fallen onto a gas fire causing a small fire in the living room.  The fire had been extinguished by the occupant, who was left extremely shaken as a result of the incident.  Paramedics were called to the scene and the occupant was treated for minor smoke inhalation.
