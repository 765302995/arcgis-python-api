 Motorbike fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/06/2019 - 04:47 Location: Hardy Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly after 4.45am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open involving a motorbike in Hardy Street, Warrington. On arrival the crew found the motorcycle to be well alight at the back of a domestic property. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
