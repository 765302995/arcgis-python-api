 Dog rescued from a river in Chester Time of Incident: 05/04/2019 - 10:53 Location: Ferry Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester, the rope rescue unit from Lymm and the animal rescue unit from Bollington Dog rescued from a river in Chester Firefighters were called to Ferry Lane, Chester, after a report of a sheep dog who had become stuck in a river. The border collie had become tangled in some weeds in the river and two firefighters with swift water rescue training entered the river to rescue the dog.
