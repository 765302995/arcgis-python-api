 Log burner fire in Chester Time of Incident: 11/05/2018 - 15:59 Location: Chapel House Lane South, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane At around 4pm firefighters were called to reports of a chimney fire at a domestic property on Chapel House Lane South in Chester. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire behind the closure plate of a log burner. Firefighters took the closure plate and debris outside the property and used water to extinguish the fire. They then inspected the chimney to make sure it was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour. For chimney fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chimneys.
