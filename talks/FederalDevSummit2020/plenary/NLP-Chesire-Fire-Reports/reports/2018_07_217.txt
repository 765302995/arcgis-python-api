 Domestic property fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/07/2018 - 00:01 Location: Arley Drive, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Widnes and one from Penketh At around midnight firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a domestic property in Arley Drive, Widnes. On arrival crews discovered that no-one was inside the property. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus entered the building. The fire was extinguished using water and the crews then checked for hotspots, including in the loft space, and used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property. Firefighters were in attendance for more than two hours. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information about the incident should call police on 101, quoting incident number 114800, give the details via General Enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
