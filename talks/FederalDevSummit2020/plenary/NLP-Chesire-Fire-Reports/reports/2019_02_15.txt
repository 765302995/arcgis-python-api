 Wood on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/02/2019 - 19:49 Location: Hale Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews attended to a small fire involving wood next to a caravan centre on Hale Road in Widnes.  The fire was extinguished to a manageable level using one hose reel jet before being able to burn for a further hour.
