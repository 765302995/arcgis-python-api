 Industrial bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/03/2018 - 02:02 Location: Wilderspool Causeway, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At 2am firefighters were called to a rubbish fire on Wilderspool Causeway in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered a large industrial bin well alight and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. Firefighters were at the scene for around 10 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and police have been informed about the incident. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Police on 101.
