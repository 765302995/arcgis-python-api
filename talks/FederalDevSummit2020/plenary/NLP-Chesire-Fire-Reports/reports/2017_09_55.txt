One woman trapped after crash on M6 Time of Incident: 15/09/2017 - 07:52 Location: M6 north, junction 21 to 21A Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm, one from Birchwood, one from Warrington, one from Leigh Fire crews were called to a crash between two lorries on the M6 northbound between junction 21 and 21A. One woman was trapped in the cab of a lorry and was extracted by firefighters before being handed into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for an hour and a half.
