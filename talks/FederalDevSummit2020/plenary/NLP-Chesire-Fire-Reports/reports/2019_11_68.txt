 Small fire in the open near Knutsford Time of Incident: 16/11/2019 - 11:48 Location: Old Hall Lane, Tabley, near Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters from Lymm used a hose reel jet to extinguish a number of bags of hay which were well alight in a field off Old Hall Lane in Over Tabley.
