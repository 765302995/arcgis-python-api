 Controlled burning in Willaston Time of Incident: 14/05/2018 - 18:13 Location: Wybunbury Road, Willaston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters attended to reports of a small rubbish fire in the back garden of a property on Wybunbury Road in Willaston.  This was a case of controlled burning and firefighters gave advice to the occupier.
