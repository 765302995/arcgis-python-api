 Chimney fire in Cuddington Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 19:21 Location: Manor Road, Cuddington Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich and one from Lymm Fire crews were called to a chimney fire in Manor Road, Cuddington. Crews used a 7m ladder, a roof ladder, a hose reel jet and chimney rods to extinguish the fire. They then checked the property using a thermal imaging camera.
