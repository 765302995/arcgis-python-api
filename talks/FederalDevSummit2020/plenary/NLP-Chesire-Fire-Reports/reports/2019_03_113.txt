 Outbuilding fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 06/03/2019 - 00:32 Location: Winnington Lane, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich and one from Winsford Firefighters attended a report of an outbuilding on fire on Winnington Lane, Northwich.  The fire involved a brick outbuilding attached to a domestic property. Crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, isolated the gas and electric to the property and then extinguished the fire used a hose reel jet and a covering jet.  Three properties were evacuated with the help of the crews and smoke was cleared from a neighbouring property using a large fan.  Crews were in attendance for three and a half hours and an investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
