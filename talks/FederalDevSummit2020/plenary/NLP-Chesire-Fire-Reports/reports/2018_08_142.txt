 Fire in the open in Winsford Time of Incident: 05/08/2018 - 20:14 Location: Weaver Valley Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At around 8.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Weaver Valley Road in Winsford. On arrival the crew discovered a small quantity of wood on fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
