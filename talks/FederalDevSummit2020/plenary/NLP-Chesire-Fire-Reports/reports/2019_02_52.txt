 Fire in a hotel in Runcorn Time of Incident: 21/02/2019 - 18:41 Location: Daresbury Park, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm and one from Warrington Fire crews were called to a fire in a hotel on Daresbury Park, Runcorn. The fire involved a commercial washing machine on the ground floor laundry room. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire. Crews then ventilated the property using a large fan. A full evacuation of the premises took place during the incident. There were no reports of any injuries. Firefighters were at the scene for an hour and 40 minutes.
