 Two people rescued from a house fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 19/08/2018 - 04:20 Location: Arran Drive, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn, one from Widnes and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a house fire on Arran Drive, Frodsham. Crews rescued two casualties - a mother and her baby - using a 7m ladder from a rear bedroom. Crews, four wearing breathing apparatus, then tackled the fire using two hose reel jets. The fire involved an electrical cupboard on the ground floor. The casualties were handed into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for 90 minutes.
