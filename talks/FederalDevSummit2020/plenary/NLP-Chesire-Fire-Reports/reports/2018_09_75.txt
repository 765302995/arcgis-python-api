 Cooker fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 15/09/2018 - 18:01 Location: Somerford Walk, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and Widnes. Fiefighters were called to a cooker fire in a property on Somerford Walk in Widnes. Two firefighters in breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish the fire. They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
