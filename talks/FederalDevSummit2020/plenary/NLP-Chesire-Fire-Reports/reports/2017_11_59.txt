Vehicle fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 14/11/2017 - 13:41 Location: Kinsey Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a vehicle fire which was well alight on Kinsey Road, Ellesmere Port. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
