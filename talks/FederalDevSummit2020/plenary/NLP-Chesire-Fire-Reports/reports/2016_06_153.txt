Caravan overturned on the M56 Time of Incident: 04/06/2016 - 15:23 Location: M56 between Junctions 12 and 14. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port, Widnes and Runcorn Fire crews were called to reports of  a collision on the M56 between Junctions 12 and 14.  When they arrived crews found a caravan and a car on their sides. Firefighters confirmed that there was nobody trapped before helping to make the scene safe.
