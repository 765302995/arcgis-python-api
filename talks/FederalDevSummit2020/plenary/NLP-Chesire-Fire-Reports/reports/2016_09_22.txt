Road traffic collision on the M6 North Time of Incident: 25/09/2016 - 15:14 Location: M6 North J18 to J19 Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe, Holmes Chapel, Sandbach,Alsager, Altrincham and other special appliances from Sandbach, Holmes Chapel and Winsford Firefighters released one female from a vehicle where she had become trapped following a road traffic collision. Crews used stabilisation gear to releast the woman before placing her on a stretcher to be taken to hospital. The collision involving one car and one heavy good vehicles and took place between junctions 18 and 19 on the M6 northbound.
