Industrial bin on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/10/2016 - 18:45 Location: Market Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A blaze in an industrial wheelie bin is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and are urging anyone with any information about this incident to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101. Arson damages property and costs lives.
