 Grass fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/07/2018 - 19:06 Location: Bishops Way, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 7.05pm firefighters were called to a report of a grass fire on Bishops Way in Widnes. On arrival at land commonly known as The Bongs the crew discovered that the fire involved approximately 10 square metres of grass. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
