 Refuse fire in Warrington woods Time of Incident: 25/02/2019 - 18:36 Location: Bembridge Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters attended to refuse on fire in wooded area near to Bembridge Close in Warrington.  Crews located the fire on foot carrying lighting and a backpack extinguisher, which were used to help put out the fire.
