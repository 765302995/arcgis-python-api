 Fire alarm sounding at domestic property in Peckforton Time of Incident: 10/12/2019 - 16:06 Location: Quarry Bank, Peckforton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Shortly after 4pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a domestic property in Quarry Bank, Peckforton.  On arrival no-one was at home.  Firefighters carried out a full inspection of the outside of the building using a thermal imaging camera.  They used a short extension ladder to check the first floor.  When the homeowner turned up it was established that the smoke alarm had activated due to an electrical fault.  Firefighters were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.
