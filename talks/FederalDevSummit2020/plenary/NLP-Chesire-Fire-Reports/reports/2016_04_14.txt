Fire in pipework in Birchwood Time of Incident: 26/04/2016 - 11:43 Location: Birchwood Way, Birchwood, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a fire in pipework in a sawdust extraction unit on the exterior of a premises in Birchwood. Crews used a thermal imaging camera and cooled the area.
