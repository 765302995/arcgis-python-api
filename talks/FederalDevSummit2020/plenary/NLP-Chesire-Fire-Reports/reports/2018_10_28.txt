 Flooding in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/10/2018 - 01:24 Location: Clearwater Quays, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to reports of flooding at a flat on Clearwater Quays in Warrington.  Crews established the flooding was coming from the upstairs flat and quickly managed to turn the water off.  The area was then made safe.
