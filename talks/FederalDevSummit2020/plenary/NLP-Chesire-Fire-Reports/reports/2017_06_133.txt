Motorhome on fire in Neston Time of Incident: 09/06/2017 - 11:01 Location: Hinderton Road, Neston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a large vehicle fire on Hinderton Road, Neston, involving a motorhome. Two fire engines from Powey Lane and Ellesmere Port attended the incident. Crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. The firefighters were at the scene for around one hour.
