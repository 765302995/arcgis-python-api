 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/02/2018 - 04:44 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Car on fire in WarringtonFire crews were called to a vehicle fire at Grasmere Avenue, Warrington. On arrival firefighters discovered the fire had also damaged a nearby fence and garage. They used one hose reel and breathing apparatus to put the fire out and were on scene for around one hour.
