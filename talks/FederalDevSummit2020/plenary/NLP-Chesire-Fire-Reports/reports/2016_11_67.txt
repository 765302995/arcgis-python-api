Garage fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 16/11/2016 - 16:45 Location: Chester Lane, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich and Winsford An outbuilding fire on Chester Lane, Marton, was reported. Firefighters found the fire involved a garage with one car inside. Crews used two hose reel jets to put out the fire. They removed the garage roof and damped the area down. It is believed the fire may have been started accidentally.
