Washing machine fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 10/06/2017 - 18:49 Location: Cambridge Avenue, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford There had been a fire involving a washing machine inside the kitchen for a terraced property. The fire was out when the fire crew arrived. One hose reel jet was used to ensure that the fire was completed out. A large fan was then used to clear the smoke from the property. One casualty was checked by paramedics.  Washing machine and dishwasher fire safety
