 Grass small fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/04/2018 - 21:54 Location: School Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood,one fire engine attended from Warrington and one fire engine attended from Lymm Shortly before 10pm firefighters were called to reports of a grass fire on School Lane, Birchwood. Crews from Birchwood, Warrington and Lymm attended the scene and the team used specialist to extinguish the fire. Crews were in attendance for two hours 30 minutes.
