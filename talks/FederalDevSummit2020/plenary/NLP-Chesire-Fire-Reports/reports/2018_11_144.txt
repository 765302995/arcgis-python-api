 Ongoing - Car fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 05/11/2018 - 22:05 Location: Brooks Lane, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach Update 10:45pm Firefighters from Sandbach are using one hose reel jet to extinguish a people carrier which is well alight on Brooks Lane, Middlewich.  Update - 11.45pm Crews have now extinguished the fire, the team are now attempting to stem a quantity of oil which has leaked from the vehicle into a nearby stream.  The environment agency has been informed of the incident.  Update - 8am - Crews are at the scene searching for hotspots
