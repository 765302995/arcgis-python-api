Industrial wheelie bin set alight in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 30/07/2017 - 03:20 Location: Grove Way, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilsmlow Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a fire involving an industrial wheelie bin.  A second fire was alos located close to the wheelie bin fire, so firefighters also dealt with this fire too.  It is believed that these fires may have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
