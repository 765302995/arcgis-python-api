Bin fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 06/10/2017 - 23:13 Location: Longshaw Street, Bewsey and Whitecross, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters responded to reports of a bin on fire in Longshaw Street, Bewsey and Whitecross, Warrington. A single hose reel was used to extinguish the flames. The fire is being treated as malicious, anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident 1015 of 6 October 2017.
