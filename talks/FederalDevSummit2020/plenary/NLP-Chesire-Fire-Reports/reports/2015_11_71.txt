Kitchen fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 16/11/2015 - 18:24 Location: Lower Robin Hood Lane, Frodsham Attendance: Three fire engines from Ellesmere Port and Chester Three fire engines from Ellesmere Port and Chester were sent to a house fire, where a person was reported to be inside the property.  On arrival at the property the fire crews found that there had been a fire involving a microwave, but the fire was already out.  Firefighters requested an ambulance for one casualty who was suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation. Firefighters provided oxygen therapy until the ambulance crew arrived.
