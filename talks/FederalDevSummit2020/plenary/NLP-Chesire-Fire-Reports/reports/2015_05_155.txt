Chimney fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 02/05/2015 - 19:31 Location: Newcastle Road, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton Chimney rods were used to extinguish a fire at a house in Congleton. The chimney was well alight on arrival and contained to the chimney breast.  The firefighters investigated the first floor and loft space with a thermal imaging camera to ensure the fire had not spread and used chimney rods to put it out.
