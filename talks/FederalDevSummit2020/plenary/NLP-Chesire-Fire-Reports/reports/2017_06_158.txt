Road traffic collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 04/06/2017 - 13:07 Location: Buxton New Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield and one from Chapel-en-le-Frith (Derbyshire Fire and Rescue Service) A casualty was trapped in a car which was on its side around 50 metres from the road in Macclesfield Forest. The casualty, a man, was assessed by paramedics as firefighters stabilised the car ready to release him. Specialist equipment was used to be able to get him out through the roof. He was then taken to hospital.
