 Overturned heavy goods vehicle in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/07/2018 - 07:37 Location: Cliff Lane, Grappenhall, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 7.35am firefighters were called to a report of a heavy goods vehicle having overturned on Cliff Lane in Grappenhall, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered the vehicle on its side and that no-one was trapped inside. A cordon was set up and firefighters helped to make the scene safe. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The Highways Agency were also in attendance.
