 Collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/10/2018 - 18:25 Location: Widnes Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to reports of a road traffic collision involving a car and a tractor on Widnes Road in Warrington.  Crews used hydraulic cutting equipment to remove the roof of the car and extricate the casualty who was handed into the care of paramedics.
