Road traffic collision in Middlewich Time of Incident: 19/11/2016 - 12:44 Location: Clivegreen lane, Middlewich Attendance: Fire engines from Middlewich, Northwich and Crewe Two cars were involved in a collision on Clivegreen Lane, leaving a woman trapped in one of the cars. Firefighters worked to release the casualty and handed her into the care of paramedics. The scene was then made safe. Crews were at the scene for over an hour.
