 Shed fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 24/01/2019 - 20:50 Location: Earls Way, Runcorn. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Runcorn. One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters attended a shed fire in Early Way, Runcorn which had spread to the property affecting the eves and a lean to.  It had also damaged a nearby van. Crews used two breathing apparatus, two hose reels and one covering jet to put out the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
