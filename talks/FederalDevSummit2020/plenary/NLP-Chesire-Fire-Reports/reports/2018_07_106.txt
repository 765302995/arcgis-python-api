 Bin fire at the entrance to a park in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/07/2018 - 04:25 Location: Alder Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 4.25am firefighters were called to reports of a bin being on fire on Alder Lane in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that the bin was at the entrance to a park. Firefighters extinguished the small fire with water and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
