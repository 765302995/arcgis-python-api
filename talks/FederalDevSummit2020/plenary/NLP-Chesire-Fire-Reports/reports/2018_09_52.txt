 Person trapped following a collision involving a car and a HGV in Crewe Time of Incident: 20/09/2018 - 08:19 Location: A50 junction with Mill Lane, Holmes Chapel Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Holmes Chapel and one from Sandbach At around 8.20am firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving a car and a stationary heavy goods vehicle on the A50 junction with Mill Lane in Holmes Chapel. On arrival crews discovered that someone was trapped in the saloon car. Firefighters had to cut the roof off the vehicle to free the person trapped. Once the roof had been removed using specialist cutting equipment, the casualty was extricated by firefighters and then handed over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour and 50 minutes.
