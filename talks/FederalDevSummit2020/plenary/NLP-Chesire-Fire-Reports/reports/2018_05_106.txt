 Hedge fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 17/05/2018 - 13:53 Location: Newlyn Avenue, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a hedge fire in a back garden at an address in Newlyn Avenue, Macclesfield. Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the flames.
