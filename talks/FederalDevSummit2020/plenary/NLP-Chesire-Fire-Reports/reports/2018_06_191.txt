 Refuse fire in Kermincham Time of Incident: 13/06/2018 - 22:46 Location: Cross Lane, Kermincham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel, one from Middlewich and one from Holmes Chapel Fire crews were called to a refuse fire involving tyres on Cross Lane, Kermincham. The fire was approximately 5m by 8m and the crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets and a thermal imaging camera to bring the fire under control. Crews were at the scene for one hour and 45 minutes.
