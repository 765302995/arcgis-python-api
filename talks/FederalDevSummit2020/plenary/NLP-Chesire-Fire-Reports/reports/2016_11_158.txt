House fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 04/11/2016 - 22:59 Location: Walker Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: Fire engines from Macclesfield and Bollington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Walker Street, Macclesfield.  When they arrived crews wearing breathing apparatus used hose reels and a large fan to battle the blaze. The fire is believed to have been caused by a fridge freezer. Crews also removed this from the property.  A family of four were taken to hospital to be checked over and treated for the effects of inhaling smoke.
