 One-vehicle collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 08/06/2019 - 09:41 Location: Southern Expressway, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Widnes and one from Runcorn At around 9.40am firefighters were called to a report of a one-vehicle collision near the Murdishaw island on the Southern Expressway in Runcorn. On arrival the crews discovered that a saloon car had come off the road and that an occupant, a man, was being treated by paramedics. The road was closed whilst emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters assisted paramedics by cutting trees and removing branches by the car. They also made the vehicle safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
