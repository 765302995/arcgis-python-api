Chimney fire in Goostrey Time of Incident: 07/11/2016 - 11:04 Location: Main Road, Goostrey Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called to a chimney on fire and used a hose reel and chimney rods to extinguish it. A thermal imaging camera was used to check the area for signs of fire in the roof space and a ladder to gain access to the roof. Chimney safety advice
