 Road traffic collision on M6 southbound Time of Incident: 13/09/2018 - 09:03 Location: M6 southbound J21A to J21 Attendance: Firefighters attended from Warrington 9am - Firefighters are assisting with the recovery of a vehicle and trailer which has come off the M6 southbound Junction 21A to Junction 21.  Police have closed lanes one and two of the motorway while further recovery is underway.  2pm - The motorway is now open and the vehicle and trailer has been recovered.
