 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/11/2018 - 19:14 Location: Horrocks Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. One fire engine attended from Penketh. Firefighters were called to a fire at a premises in Horrocks Lane, Warrington. Three large wheelie bins were well alight close to a building. Crews moved the bins away from the building and put out the fires. They then entered the building and checked the fire hadn't spread by using a thermal imaging camera.
