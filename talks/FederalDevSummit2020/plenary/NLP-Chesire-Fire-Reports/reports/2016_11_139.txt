Stable fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 05/11/2016 - 23:53 Location: Sandy Lane, Antrobus in Northwich. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Sandy Lane, Antrobus in Northwich.  When they arrived they found a stable on fire. Crews used main water jets and hose reels to put the fire out.   Traditional chip pans can cause fatal fires. Think safe, take extra care when cooking with chip pans or better yet - avoid using them at all!
