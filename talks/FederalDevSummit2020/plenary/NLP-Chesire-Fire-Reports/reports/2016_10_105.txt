Black smoke from access cover in Widnes Time of Incident: 13/10/2016 - 10:58 Location: Mottershead Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters attended an access cover on a footpath which had black smoke coming from it in Widnes. The road was closed while the incident was  dealt with and a cordon was set up around the area. Police were in attendance. The electricity supply company attended and the incident was handed over to them. One nearby property was evacuated as a precaution.
