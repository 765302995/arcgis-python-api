Road traffic collision in Chelford Time of Incident: 22/04/2016 - 11:46 Location: Chelford Road, Chelford Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Knutsford Two vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision in Chelford. Fortunately nobody was trapped in either vehicle. Firefighters made the scene safe. Police, paramedics and air ambulance were also in attendance.
