Fire involving a wooden crate in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/05/2015 - 09:30 Location: Chadwick Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A fire involving a wooden crate in a large disused industrial unit was attended by firefighters. The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and a large fan to remove smoke from a neighbouring building.
