 Two-vehicle collision in Somerford Booths Time of Incident: 16/10/2019 - 08:03 Location: Mill Lane, Somerford Booths Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Congleton and one from Macclesfield Shortly after 8am firefighters were called to reports of a two-vehicle collision in Mill Lane, Somerford Booths, near Congleton. On arrival crews found that someone was physically trapped in their car. Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to get the casualty out of the vehicle. They were then handed over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for approximately 40 minutes.
