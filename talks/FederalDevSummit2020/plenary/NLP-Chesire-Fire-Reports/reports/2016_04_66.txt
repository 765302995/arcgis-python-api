Car set on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/04/2016 - 20:48 Location: Wright Crescent, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A car was deliberately set on fire in Widnes.  Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was set in the engine compartment.  Police were asked to attend the incident.  This fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.  Arson can cost lives.
