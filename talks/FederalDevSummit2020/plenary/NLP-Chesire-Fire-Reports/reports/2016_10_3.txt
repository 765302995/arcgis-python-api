Car well alight on the M6 motorway in Woolston (northbound) Time of Incident: 29/10/2016 - 21:45 Location: M6 motorway, about half a mile northbound of Junction 21 (Woolston). Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Irlam (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) A car was well alight on the hard shoulder of the M6 motorway, about half a mile northbound of Junction 21 (Woolston).  All four lanes of the motorway were closed while fire crews dealt with the fire.  One hose reel jet was used to extinguish the fire. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.
