Wheelie bins on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/12/2016 - 16:54 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Wheelie bins and pagodas were on fire on Grasmere Avenue. Firefighters used hand extinguishers to put the fires, which are believed to have been started deliberately, out. Police were informed of a probable arson attack.
