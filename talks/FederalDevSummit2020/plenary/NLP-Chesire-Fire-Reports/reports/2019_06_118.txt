 Suspected blown car engine in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 05/06/2019 - 17:04 Location: A34 in Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Shortly after 5pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from a car on the A34 in Wilmslow, near the Dean Row roundabout.  On arrival the crew discovered that the saloon vehicle had a suspected blown engine. The car was not on fire. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to monitor the temperatures of the engine and the car. They also fended off traffic until police arrived. Firefighters left the scene once they were satisfied that the car was safe. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
