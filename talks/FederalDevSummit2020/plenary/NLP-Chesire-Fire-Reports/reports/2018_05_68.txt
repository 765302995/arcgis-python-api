 Man dies following fire at smoking shelter outside care home in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/05/2018 - 18:11 Location: Longbarn Lane, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Lymm and one from Warrington At 6.11pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the grounds of Heathercroft Nursing Home on Longbarn Lane, Warrington.  Crews from Warrington and Lymm were deployed to the scene and on arrival they discovered a fire in a smoking shelter area outside the home.  The team used water to extinguish the fire.  An elderly man was injured as a result of the incident. He was initially treated by firefighters at the scene and was taken to Whiston Hospital but has sadly since died.  Enquiries into the cause of the fire are ongoing.
