 Rubbish fire, Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 10/08/2018 - 19:32 Location: Berry Drive, Great Sutton Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters responded to reports of a fire in the old school grounds near the wooded area on Berry Drive, Great Sutton. They arrived to find a small fire in the open containing bits of rubbish and refuse. Once they had gained access to the site they used a back pack to put out the fire, taking around 15 minutes in all to deal with the incident
