Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 28/08/2016 - 00:37 Location: Fallowfield Court in Crewe. Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe Firefighters used a hose reel jet to tackle a car fire on Fallowfield Court in Crewe.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Constabulary on 101.
