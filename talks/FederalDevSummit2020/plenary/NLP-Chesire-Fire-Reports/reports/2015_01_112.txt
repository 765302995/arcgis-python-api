Fallen power cables in Northwich Time of Incident: 01/01/2015 - 11:40 Location: Dutton Lane, Northwich. Attendance: A fire engine from Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open off Dutton Lane in Crowton, Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that some power cables had fallen down. A cordon was set up around the incident and advice was given to local residents before the incident was handed over to the power company.
