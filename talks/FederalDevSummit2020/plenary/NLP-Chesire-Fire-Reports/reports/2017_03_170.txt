Road traffic collision in Rostherne Time of Incident: 01/03/2017 - 11:44 Location: Chester Road, Rostherne Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision on Chester Road at Rostherne. One vehicle was involved with two casualties trapped. There was no sign of fire at the incident. The crews worked to release the two men and handed the to the care of paramedics.  Firefighters worked to release the casualties and one lane was closed by police who controlled traffic.
