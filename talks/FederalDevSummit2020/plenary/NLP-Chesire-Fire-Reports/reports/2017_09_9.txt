Horse rescued in Frodsham Time of Incident: 27/09/2017 - 13:54 Location: Manley Road, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine from Frodsham and the specialist animal rescue unit from Bollington Fire crews were called to Manley Road, Frodsham, after a horse box toppled over and trapped a horse. A vet sedated the horse and then crews removed the roof off the horse box and removed the horse using strops. The horse was then placed in another horse box whilst it came round from sedation.
