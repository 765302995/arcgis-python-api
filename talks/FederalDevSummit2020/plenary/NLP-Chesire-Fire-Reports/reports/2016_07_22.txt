Small vehicle fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/07/2016 - 01:10 Location: Waverton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters put out a small vehicle fire using one hose reel jet. They then dampened down the car and the area, and made the scene safe.  This fire was believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information, contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
