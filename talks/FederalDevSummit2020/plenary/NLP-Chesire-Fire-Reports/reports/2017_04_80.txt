Thatched roof fire in Whitegate Time of Incident: 11/04/2017 - 21:26 Location: Dalefords Lane, Whitegate, Northwich Attendance: Fire engines from Winsford, Middlewich and Northwich with an aerial appliance and supporting engine from Chester Firefighters were called to a thatched roof fire in Whitegate. Crews were at the scene throughout the night, tackling the fire, removing thatch and regularly inspecting the roof for further hotspots.    Hose reel jets from the ground and from an aerial appliance were used, along with stack drags to clear the thatch.  Dalefords Lane was closed while the incident was dealt with.
