 Fire in a garden in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 19:16 Location: Armour Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 7.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a garden on Armour Avenue in Orford, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning that had got out of hand. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
