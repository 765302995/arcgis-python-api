Fire in disused buildings in Winsford Time of Incident: 20/10/2016 - 16:57 Location: off Wharton Road, Winsford Attendance: Fire engines from Winsford, Holmes Chapel, Crewe, Northwich, Knutsford and Sandbach Six fire engines were called to a blaze in several disused buildings off Wharton Road. Crews gained access to the property where they found several portable office buildings on fire. Main jets were set up to bring the blaze under control. Police officers closed Wharton Road to enable firefighters to get water from hydrants around the fire site. Firefighters, wearing respirators, brought the fire under control before proceeding to dampen down the surrounding area. A nearby building, not involved in the fire, was cooled during the incident to prevent fire breaking out.
