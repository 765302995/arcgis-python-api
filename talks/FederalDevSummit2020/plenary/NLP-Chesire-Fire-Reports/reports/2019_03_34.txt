 Fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/03/2019 - 20:16 Location: Friends Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters from Penketh used a hose reel jet to extinguish a fire involving a quantity of building materials at a construction site on Friends Lane.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
