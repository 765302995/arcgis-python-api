 Fire at a builders yard in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/07/2019 - 17:35 Location: Rudloe Court, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly after 5.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a builders yard in Warrington. On arrival firefighters discovered a well develop skip fire at the Rudloe Court site. The crew used one hose reel jet and one main jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for approximately one hour and 15 minutes.
