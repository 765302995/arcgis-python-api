 Machinery fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 21/10/2018 - 14:13 Location: St Annes Avenue, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich and one from Holmes Chapel Fire crews were called to St Anne's Avenue, Middlewich, after a report of a machinery fire. Crews found a wood burning engraving machine was on fire and had been removed from a property by the owner. Crews then used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
