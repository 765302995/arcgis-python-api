 Two-car collision in Tarvin Time of Incident: 09/08/2019 - 14:30 Location: A54 Kersall Road, Tarvin Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At 2.30pm firefighters from Macclesfield Fire Station were travelling along the A54 Kersall Road in Tarvin, near Chester, when they came across a collision involving two small cars. They administered first aid to a casualty before handing him over to the care of paramedics. The man was taken to hospital via an ambulance. The crew also made the vehicles safe and removed them from the road. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
