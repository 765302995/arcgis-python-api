 Shed fire that spread to neighbouring gardens in Prestbury Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 16:51 Location: Bollin Way, Prestbury Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Macclesfield and one from Bollington At around 4.50pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a garden in Bollin Way, Prestbury. On arrival the crew discovered a garden shed well alight. The shed contained a gas cylinder. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to fight the fire, which spread to neighbouring gardens. They cooled the gas cylinder and once the fire, which completely destroyed the shed, was extinguished firefighters damped the area down and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots to make sure that it was safe. The fire is believed to have been started accidentally. Firefighters were in attendance for around four hours.
