 Trailer on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 03/04/2018 - 13:08 Location: Nantwich Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and one from Middlewich Fire crews were called to fire in a trailer on Nantwich Road, Winsford. Crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fire.
