 Small fire in woodland in Runcorn Time of Incident: 19/09/2019 - 17:23 Location: Whitchurch Way, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly before 5.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Whitchurch Way, Runcorn. On arrival the crew found a small fire in woodland behind a community centre. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for more than 15 minutes.
