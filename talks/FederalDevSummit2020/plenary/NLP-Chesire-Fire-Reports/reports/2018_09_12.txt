 Flat fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 27/09/2018 - 00:04 Location: Pennine Court, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield, one fire engine attended from Bollington, one from Poynton, one from Lymm and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service. The hydraulic platform from Lymm also attended.  Firefighters were called to a fire in the ninth floor of a block of flats on Pennine Court, Macclesfield. The fire was in the kitchen of a property and crews, five wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. The crews then used a thermal imaging camera to check the building and a large fan to clear smoke from the property.  One casualty was treated by firefighters suffering from smoke inhalation before being handed to the care of paramedics and taken to hospital. Crews also rescued a dog from the property.  Crews were in attendance for approximately two hours.
