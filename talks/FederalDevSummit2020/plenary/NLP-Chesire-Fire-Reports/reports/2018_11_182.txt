 Wood burner fire in Great Warford Time of Incident: 03/11/2018 - 14:18 Location: Warford Hall Drive, Great Warford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow. One fire engine from Macclesfield. Firefighters attended a fire at a cottage in Warford Hall Drive, Great Warford. The fire involved a log burner inside the address causing smoke to issue from the eaves.  Crews used one jet and two breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
