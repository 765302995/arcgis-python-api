 Garden furniture fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 03/07/2018 - 10:30 Location: Rosemary Crescent, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Firefighters were called to a fire involving garden furniture at an address in Rosemary Crescent. The fire is thought to have been from sunlight shining through a paperweight and had spread to the rear door. Fire was out on arrival and crews damped down the area and checked that fire hadn't spread.
