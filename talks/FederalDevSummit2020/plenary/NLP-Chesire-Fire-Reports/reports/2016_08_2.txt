Rescue of a bird from the netting of a roof Time of Incident: 30/08/2016 - 15:11 Location: Frodsham Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one hydraulic platform from Chester Firefighters attended an incident in which they were requested by the RSPCA to rescue a bird which was trapped in the netting of a roof. Crews, using a hydraulic platform, successfully rescued the bird. Frodsham Lane was temporarily closed during the incident to protect the public as crews were working from height.
