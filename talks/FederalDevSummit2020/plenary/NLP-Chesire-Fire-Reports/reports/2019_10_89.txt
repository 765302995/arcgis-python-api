 Report of smoke in a domestic property in Hartford Time of Incident: 16/10/2019 - 17:02 Location: Chester Road, Hartford, near Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 5pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke in a domestic property on Chester Road in Hartford, near Northwich.  On arrival the crew discovered that it was in fact cooking fumes.  Food had been left unattended as it was being cooked.  Firefighters used a large fan to clear the significant amount of cooking fumes from the property and were in attendance for around 50 minutes.  For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
