 Pigeon rescue in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/07/2018 - 18:43 Location: Chesford Grange, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm along with the aerial ladder platform Firefighters were called to a pigeon trapped in a electricity pylon in Chesford Grange, Warrington. Crews used an aerial ladder platform to reach the bird which was 25-30 metres above ground after the power was isolated.
