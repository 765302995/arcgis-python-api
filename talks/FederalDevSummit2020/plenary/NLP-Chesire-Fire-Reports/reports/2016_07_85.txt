Car fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 16/07/2016 - 02:41 Location: Hawthorn Way, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield A fire involving a car is believed to have been started deliberately. The car was parked very close to a house. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control and prevent damage to the property and then damped the area down.  Police were asked to attend as the cause of the fire is suspected as arson. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
