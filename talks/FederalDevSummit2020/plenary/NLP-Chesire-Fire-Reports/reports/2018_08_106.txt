 Trailer fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 09/08/2018 - 05:21 Location: A500, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar Trailer fire in CreweFire crews were called to a trailer fire on the A500 Crewe. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was in the axle of one of the wheels on the trailer.
