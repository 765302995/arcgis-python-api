Shed fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 07/07/2015 - 19:56 Location: Shellway Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: A fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Shellway Road, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that there was a wooden shed and some tyres on fire. Crews used water backpacks and a portable pump to put the fire out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
