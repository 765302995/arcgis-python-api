Wheel of tanker on fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 27/01/2017 - 09:18 Location: M56 east between junctions 16 and 15 Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane attended A fire involving the wheel of a milk tanker which was out on the arrival, required firefighters to cool the vehicle with a hose reel. Lane one of the motorway was closed while the incident was dealt with and police and highways attended.
