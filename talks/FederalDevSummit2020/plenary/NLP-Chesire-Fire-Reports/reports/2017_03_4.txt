House fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 30/03/2017 - 10:59 Location: Langdale Road in Runcorn. Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn, Widnes and Stockton Heath plus a hydraulic platform. Firefighters responded to a report of a house fire on Langdale Road in Runcorn. When they arrived crews found that there was a fire on the first floor of a semi-detached property. Crews used hose reel jets to tackle the fire which had spread to the roof space.  There was nobody at home when the fire broke out. A fire investigation has now been launched to find the cause.
