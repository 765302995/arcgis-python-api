 Fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 07/03/2019 - 10:02 Location: Chelford Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to Chelford Road, Macclesfield, to a report of a fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish a bonfire which had been left unattended.
