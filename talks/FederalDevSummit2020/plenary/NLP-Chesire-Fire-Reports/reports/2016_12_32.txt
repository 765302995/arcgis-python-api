Scooter fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/12/2016 - 21:47 Location: Masefield Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A scooter had been set on fire in the back garden of a Masefield Avenue house. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and asked for police to attend due to the suspicious nature of the incident.
