 Fire at end terrace property in Runcorn Time of Incident: 08/05/2018 - 21:44 Location: Canon Street, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended â€“ one from Runcorn and one from Widnes Fire at end terrace property in RuncornAt around 9.45pm firefighters were called to a fire involving the front door of an end terrace property on Canon Street in Runcorn.  On arrival crews found that all persons were accounted for. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  A large high pressure fan was then used to clear the smoke from the property. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Firefighters were in attendance for nearly two hours. Police were also in attendance.  Anyone with information about the fire should call police on 101, quoting incident number 58775.
