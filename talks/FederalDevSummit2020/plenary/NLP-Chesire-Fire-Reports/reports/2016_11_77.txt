Fire on first floor of Congleton house Time of Incident: 14/11/2016 - 18:14 Location: Kensington Drive, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines from Congleton and one from Alsager An investigation is being carried out to find the cause of a fire on the first floor of a Kensington Drive house.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. One casualty, a lady, was treated by an ambulance crew for smoke inhalation. Neighbouring properties were checked to ensure there was no fire spread.
