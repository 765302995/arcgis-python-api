 Small fire in the open in High Legh Time of Incident: 19/10/2018 - 19:09 Location: Swineyard Lane, High Legh, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Just before 7.10pm firefighters were called to a small fire in the open at Swineyard Lane. Crews used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control.
