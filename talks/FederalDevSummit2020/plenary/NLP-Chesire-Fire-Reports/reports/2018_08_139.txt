 Refuse fire in a field in Crewe Time of Incident: 05/08/2018 - 21:31 Location: Mablins Lane, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 9.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a field near a primary school on Mablins Lane, Crewe. The small fire involving rubbish was out on arrival. Firefighters damped the area down with water to make sure it was safe and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
