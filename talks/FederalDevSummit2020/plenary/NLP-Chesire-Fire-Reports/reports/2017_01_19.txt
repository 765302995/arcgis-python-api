Chimney fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 25/01/2017 - 18:01 Location: Budworth Road, Aston by Budworth. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich A fire crews responded to a report of a fire at a house on Budworth Road, Aston by Budworth.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire in the chimney. Firefighters used a chimney rod and thermal image camera to deal with the incident.
