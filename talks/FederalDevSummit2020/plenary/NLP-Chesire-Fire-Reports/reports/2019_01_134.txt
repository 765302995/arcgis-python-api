 Shed fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 14/01/2019 - 22:16 Location: Moreton Road, Crewe Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - both from Crewe Crews attended to a shed on fire in the garden of a house on Moreton Road in Crewe.  Two hose reels and a covering jet were used by firefighters to extinguish the fire.  The fire is believed to have been caused by a battery charger.  Crews used a thermal imaging camera to check the fire was full put out and dampened down.
