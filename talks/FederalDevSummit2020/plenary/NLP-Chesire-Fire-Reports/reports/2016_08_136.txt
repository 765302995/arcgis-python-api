Car fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 09/08/2016 - 01:02 Location: Arkwright Road, Astmoor Industrial Estate, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A fire involving a car on Arkwright Road is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze. They then damped the area down. Police were asked to attend.
