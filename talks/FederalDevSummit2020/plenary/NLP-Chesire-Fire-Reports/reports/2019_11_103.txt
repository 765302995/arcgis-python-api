 Smoke alarm sounding in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 09/11/2019 - 20:42 Location: Carisbrook Avenue, Macclesfield. SK10 2RL Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Firefighters were called to a property on Carisbrook Avenue after reports of a fire alarm sounding.  They attended and used a thermal imaging camera and gas monitor, but there was no fire. The crews were at the scene for 50 minutes.
