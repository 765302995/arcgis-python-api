 House fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 31/12/2017 - 23:12 Location: Woodchurch Lane, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port, one from Chester and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a house fire in a living room of a property on Woodchurch Lane, Ellesmere Port. One woman was rescued from the property by fire crews before being placed into the care of paramedics and taken to hospital suffering from smoke inhalation. Sadly, as a result of the fire one dog which was inside the property died. The crews, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets and one safety jet to extinguish the fire. Crews also checked the loft and adjoining properties for smoke spread and used a large fan to clear smoke. Crews were at the scene for six hours. The cause of the fire is believed to have been accidental.
