Wheelie bin fire in Chester Time of Incident: 27/05/2017 - 19:52 Location: Cambridge Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester A hose reel jet was used by firefighters to put out a fire in a wheelie bin. Firefighters urge everyone to follow our wheelie bin safety advice.   This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
