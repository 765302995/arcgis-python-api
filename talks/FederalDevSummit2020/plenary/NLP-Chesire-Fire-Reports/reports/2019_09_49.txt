 Bedroom fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/09/2019 - 14:28 Location: York Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews attended to a fire in the bedroom of a property on York Avenue in Warrington.  Firefighters located the fire on the first floor and wearing four breathing apparatus used two hose reels to fight the flames.  A number of properties close by were checked by crews and were also found to have been affected by heat and smoke damage. The fire is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault.
