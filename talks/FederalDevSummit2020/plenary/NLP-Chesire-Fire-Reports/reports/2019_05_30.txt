 Dog rescued from canal in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/05/2019 - 06:42 Location: Upper Mersey Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters rescued a dog which had fallen 30ft down an embankment on Upper Mersey Road in Widnes.  The dog had gone into the canal and had become trapped in a lock while tangled in rope.  The swift water rescue team located the dog and removed it safely from the water.
