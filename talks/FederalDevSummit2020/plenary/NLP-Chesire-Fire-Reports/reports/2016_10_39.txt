Wheelie bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 24/10/2016 - 19:47 Location: Dart Walk, Winsford. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Winsford A hose reel jet was used to put out a wheelie bin fire on Dart Walk, Winsford.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information about who could have started these please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
