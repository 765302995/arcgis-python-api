 Reports of a kitchen fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/07/2019 - 12:05 Location: Somerville Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Widnes and Runcorn Shortly after 12pm firefighters from Runcorn and Widnes were dispatched to reports of a kitchen fire at a house on Sommerville Road.  The fire, which involved an oven, was out on arrival. However, the kitchen was heavily smoke logged.  The crews used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.  Firefighters were detained at the address for approximately 20 minutes.
