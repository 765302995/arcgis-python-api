 Skip fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 09/09/2018 - 14:48 Location: Bridges Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a skip fire on Bridges Road, Ellesmere Port. On arrival crews discovered that the fire had set fire to a portable cabin which was well alight. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police.
