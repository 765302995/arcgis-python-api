Sofa on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 18/09/2017 - 11:43 Location: Ivy Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to a sofa which was on fire in a back garden on Ivy Lane, Macclesfield. Crews used one hose reel jet to put out the fire. The fire was a suspected arson. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
