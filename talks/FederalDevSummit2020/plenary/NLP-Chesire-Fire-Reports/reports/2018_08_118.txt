 Grass fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 07/08/2018 - 21:29 Location: Macclesfield Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington, one from Congleton, one from Macclesfield and one from Derbyshire Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a grass fire on Macclesfield Road, Macclesfield. The fire was on the moor and crews used two beaters, fire extinguishers and a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
