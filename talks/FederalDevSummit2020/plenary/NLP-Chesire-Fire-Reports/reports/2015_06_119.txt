Road traffic collision in Nantwich Time of Incident: 10/06/2015 - 10:23 Location: Junction of Monks Lane and Cuckoo Lane, Acton Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich Firefighters have been at the scene of a road traffic collision in Acton, near Nantwich.  Nobody was trapped in either of the vehicles involved.  The crew made the scene safe as two casualties were assessed by the ambulance service.
