Road traffic collison in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 23/03/2016 - 19:16 Location: Melrose Way, Alderley Edge. Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow fire station and one fire engine from Cheadle fire station of Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Two fire crews attended a road traffic collision involving two cars on Melrose Way / A34 in Alderley Edge. One of the cars was on fire.  Firefighters extinguished the fire using two hose reel jets.  One firefighter wore breathing apparatus. There were two casualties, one male and one female, who were cared for by paramedics before being taken to hospital by ambulance.
