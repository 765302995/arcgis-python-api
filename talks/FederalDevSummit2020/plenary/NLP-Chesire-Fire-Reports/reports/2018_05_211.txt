 UPDATED - Fire involving a row of shops in Middlewich Time of Incident: 01/05/2018 - 19:23 Location: Wheelock Street, Middlewich Attendance: Three fire engines attended - two from Northwich and one from Winsford At around 7.20pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a row of shops on Wheelock Street in Middlewich. On arrival the crews discovered that the fire involved Farralls cleaners. Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets and a main jet to extinguish the fire. Crews then damped the area down and used two large high pressure fans to clear the smoke from the affected premises. Police were in attendance and the road was closed while the incident was being dealt with. Fire crews were in attendance for around three hours.
