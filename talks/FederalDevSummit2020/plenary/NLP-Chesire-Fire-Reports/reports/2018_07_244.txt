 House fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 04/07/2018 - 17:58 Location: Knutsford Road, Alderley Edge. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Shortly before 6pm firefighters from Wilmslow were called to reports of a house fire on Knutsford Road in Alderley Edge. The fire was out on arrival, however the team used a large fan to clear the smoke from within the building and conducted a number of checks at the address. The team were in attendance for approximately 45 minutes.The cause of the fire is believed to be unattended cooking.
