Microwave fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 10/04/2015 - 16:23 Location: Rookery Drive, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a report of a fire alarm sounding.  They found there had been a small fire in a microwave at a property in Rookery Drive.  The crew ventilated the building to clear teh smoke.
