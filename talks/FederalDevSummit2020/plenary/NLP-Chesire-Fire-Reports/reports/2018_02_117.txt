 Small kitchen fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 03/02/2018 - 20:26 Location: Cameron Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Widnes, one from Runcorn and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a fire in the kitchen of a house on Cameron Avenue, Runcorn. The fire, which had involved a small deep fat fryer, was out when firefighters arrived. Safety checks were carried out and advice was given. Crews were on scene for around an hour and a half.
