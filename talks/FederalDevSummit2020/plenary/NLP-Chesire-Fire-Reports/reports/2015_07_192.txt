Small fire in the open in Chester Time of Incident: 01/07/2015 - 23:07 Location: Grinsome Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine A small fire in the open was extinguished with two hose reels. The crew used drag tools to check the fire was out before leaving. It is thought that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information about this fire they should call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
