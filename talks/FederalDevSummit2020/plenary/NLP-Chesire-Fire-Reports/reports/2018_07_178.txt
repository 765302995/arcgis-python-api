 Bush fire in Chester Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 13:07 Location: Shelley Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters from Chester used a water back pack and beaters to extinguish a bush fire on Shelley Road, Chester. At its height the fire was approximately 40 metes by 10 metres. Crews were in attendance at the scene for approximately 90 minutes.
