 Sofas on fire in an alleway in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/05/2018 - 19:36 Location: Winwick Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 7.35pm firefighters were called to reports of two sofas being on fire in an alleyway on Winwick Road in Warrington. On arrival the crew found the sofas to be well alight and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
