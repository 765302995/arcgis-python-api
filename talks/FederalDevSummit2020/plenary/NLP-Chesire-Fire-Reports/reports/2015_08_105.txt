Vehicle fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 12/08/2015 - 23:42 Location: Third Avenue, Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and two fire engines from Crewe. Three fire engines attended a vehicle fire in Third Avenue, Sandbach. Using breathing apparatus, crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.    This fire is believed to have been started deliberatley. Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101 quoting incident number 1122 of 12 August, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
