 Burning of waste at demolition yard becomes out of control in Widnes Time of Incident: 16/01/2019 - 05:25 Location: Ditton Road West, Widnes Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly before 5.30am firefighters were called to a report of a large amount of smoke on Ditton Road West in Widnes. On arrival crews discovered a large attended fire at S Evans & Sons' demolition yard. It was a case of the burning of waste becoming out of control. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for more than an hour-and-a-half.
