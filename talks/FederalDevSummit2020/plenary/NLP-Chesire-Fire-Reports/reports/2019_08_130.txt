 Smoke at a domestic property in Sandbach Time of Incident: 07/08/2019 - 16:38 Location: Vicarage Lane, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach Shortly after 4.30pm firefighters were notified of a smoke alarm sounding at domestic property in Vicarage Lane, Sandbach. On arrival there were no signs of fire and the crew discovered that the smoke had been caused by burnt food. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property and were in attendance for around 40 minutes. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
