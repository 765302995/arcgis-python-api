Public house on fire in Neston Time of Incident: 18/01/2015 - 02:51 Location: The Brewers Arms,Park Street, Neston Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port, the aerial appliance from Chester, one fire engine from Merseyside and one from North Wales. Two hose reels and a covering jet were used by firefighters to tackle a fire in the roof space of a public house in Neston. The gas and electricity supply was isolated and once the fire was extinguished the crews stripped the roof to damp down fully. Six people had to find alternative accommodation as a result of the incident and the police closed the road while it  was dealt with.  The fire was caused by a log burner that had set the chimney alight and spread to the roof space. Wood-burning stove safety advice
