Kitchen fire Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 01/12/2017 - 22:04 Location: Sutton Way, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a kitchen fire on Sutton Way, Ellesmere Port.  While the fire was out on arrival, firefighters ventilated the property with a positive pressure ventilation fan.   An ambulance was requested for a casualty who was suffering from smoke inhalation and crews were in attendance for twenty minutes. More than half of accidental fires at home are started by cooking - often when cookers and grills are left unattended.  A few simple measures will keep you and your loved ones safe.  Visit our website for essential Kitchen Fire Safety Tips: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety
