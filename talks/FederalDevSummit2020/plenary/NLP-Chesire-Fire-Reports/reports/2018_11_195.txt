 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/11/2018 - 17:54 Location: Leigh Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters extinguished a wheelie bin well alight in an alleyway on Leigh Avenue in Widnes.   A hose reel jet was used by crews to fully extinguish the fire and make the area safe.  Follow our safety advice on how to prevent your wheelie bin being set on fire https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
