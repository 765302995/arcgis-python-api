Shipping container on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/07/2017 - 11:30 Location: Rushgreen Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to a shipping container which was on fire at a commercial premises on Rushgreen Road, Warrington. The fire involved a quantity of wood which was on fire and had spread to UVPC window frames. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
