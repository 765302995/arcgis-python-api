 Large grass fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/05/2019 - 15:24 Location: Moorfield Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Shortly before 3.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on Moorfield Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a large fire on grassland above a railway embankment. The grass fire measured approximately 20 square metres. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and beaters and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
