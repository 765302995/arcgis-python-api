 Road traffic collision on M56 Time of Incident: 12/05/2018 - 09:18 Location: Nr Junction 10, M56 Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm and one from Birchwood Firefighters attended a road traffic collision near Junction 10 of the M56 motorway eastbound carriageway. Crews found a car on its roof with two casualties trapped inside. They were released by firefighters and then passed into the care of paramedics. They were at the scene for around 45 minutes.
