Second grass fire today in Crewe Time of Incident: 17/07/2017 - 12:41 Location: Henry Street, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called for a second time to a fire involving smouldering grass in Henry Street. It's believed that the fires may have been started deliberately and police have been informed. If anyone knows who may have started them then they should call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
