Tree stump well alight in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 20/05/2017 - 16:36 Location: Hillside Drive, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port A tree stump was well alight, so firefighters used one hose reel jet to deal with the fire.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately so anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
