 Chimney fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 14/01/2019 - 20:30 Location: Whitley Avenue, Northwich Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Northwich Fireifghters were called to reports of a chimney fire at a property on Whitley Avenue in Northwich which was issuing sparks.  Crews tackled the fire from the grate inside the home using a hose reel and chimney rods.  The attic was also isolated as a precaution.
