Scrap fire in Chester Time of Incident: 21/06/2017 - 22:07 Location: Hare Lane, Great Boughton Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Firefighters were called to a fire involving scrap electrical cable on Hare Lane, Great Boughton. The fire involved an amount of scrap and PVC in a disused shed. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
