 Fire in the open in Knutsford Time of Incident: 15/05/2018 - 20:57 Location: Chester Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 8.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Chester Road in Knutsford.  On arrival the crew discovered a large amount of rubbish on fire.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put the fire out and were in attendance for less than 15 minutes.
