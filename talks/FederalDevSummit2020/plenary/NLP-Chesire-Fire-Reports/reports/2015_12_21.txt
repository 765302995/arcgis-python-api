Flooding in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/12/2015 - 14:31 Location: Massey Brook Lane, Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines from Stockton Heath and Widnes Fire crews were called to help with a flooding situation on Massey Brook Lane, Warrington.  A main had collapsed in the road and the water levels were rising effecting eight homes.  Crews attempted to divert the water and used special equipment to pump the flood away from the houses. They worked alongside other agencies until about 8pm.
