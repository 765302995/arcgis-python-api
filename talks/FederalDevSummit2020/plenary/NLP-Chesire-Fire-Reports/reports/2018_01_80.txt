 Rubbish fire in Poulton-with-Fearnhead Time of Incident: 12/01/2018 - 02:19 Location: Enfield Park Road, Poulton-with-Fearnhead Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters used two buckets of water to extinguish a small amount of rubbish which had been set alight on Enfield Park Road, Poulton-with-Fearnhead. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information please call Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
