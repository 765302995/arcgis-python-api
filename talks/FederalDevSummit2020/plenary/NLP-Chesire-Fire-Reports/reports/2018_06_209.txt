 One vehicle collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/06/2018 - 11:09 Location: Wilderspool Causeway, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a one vehicle collision on Wilderspool Causeway in Warrington. A van had been in collision with a tree and was well alight on arrival. All occupants have been accounted for. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire.
