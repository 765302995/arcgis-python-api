Road traffic collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 14/07/2016 - 18:22 Location: Ashley Way West, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Two vehicles were involved in a collision on Ashley Way West, leaving one man trapped in one of the cars. Firefighters removed the roof of the car to be able to get the man out. He was then handed into the care of the ambulance service and the crews made the scene safe.
