 Large rubbish fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 21/10/2018 - 13:58 Location: Ruskin Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe and one from Nantwich Fire crews were called to a large rubbish fire on Ruskin Road, Crewe. Crews used two hose reel jets and then checked the nearby buildings with a thermal imaging camera to check for any further fire spread. Crews were at the scene for 45 minutes.
