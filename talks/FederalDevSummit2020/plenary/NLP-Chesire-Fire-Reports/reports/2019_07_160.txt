 Small fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 05/07/2019 - 13:11 Location: Watery Lane, Astbury, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Watery Lane, Astbury, Congleton. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire and that no-one was overlooking the burning. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and were in attendance for less than an hour.
