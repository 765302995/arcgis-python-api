Kitchen fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 29/12/2015 - 12:50 Location: The Farmhouse Beefeater, Coppenhall Lane, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel jet and a dry powder extinguisher to put out an industrial grill fire in Crewe.  The gas and electrics to the kitchen area was isolated and the area ventilated to clear the smoke.  The crew removed sections of ducting and used a thermal imaging camera to check for fire spread.
