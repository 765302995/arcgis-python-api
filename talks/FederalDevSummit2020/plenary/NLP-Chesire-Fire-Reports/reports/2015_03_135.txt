Man rescued from a hospital roof Time of Incident: 04/03/2015 - 18:40 Location: Liverpool Road, Chester. Attendance: A fire engine and the hydraulic platform from Chester plus the technical rescue unit from Knutsford. Fire crews were called to assist Cheshire Police after a man had got onto the roof of the Countess of Chester Hospital.  Firefighters in the Service's hydraulic platform helped the man down.
