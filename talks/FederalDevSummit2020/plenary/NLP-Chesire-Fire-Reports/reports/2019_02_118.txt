 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/02/2019 - 19:01 Location: Frederick Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to Frederick Street, Widnes, after a report of a wheelie bin fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which had been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire then please call Cheshire Police on 101.
