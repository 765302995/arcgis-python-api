 Hedgerow fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 21/05/2019 - 15:18 Location: Kingsley Road, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Frodsham Shortly after 3.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open involving hedges on Kingsley Road in Frodsham. On arrival the crew discovered approximately six metres of hedgerow on fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started accidentally during garden maintenance.
