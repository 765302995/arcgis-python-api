 One person rescued from a car in Knutsford Time of Incident: 22/08/2018 - 19:11 Location: Holmes Chapel Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Holmes Chapel Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision involving three cars and one person trapped on Holmes Chapel Road, Knutsford. Crews rescued one casualty from a vehicle before handing them into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
