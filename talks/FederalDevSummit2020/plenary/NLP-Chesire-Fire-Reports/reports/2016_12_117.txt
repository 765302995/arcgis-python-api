Machinery on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 08/12/2016 - 05:14 Location: Berkeley Court, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and Runcorn Firefighters were called to reports of a building fire in Runcorn. On arrival, they found a fire involving dry powder inside some machinery. The fire was already out when they arrived so they used a thermal imaging camera to check for any remaining hot spots of fire.
