 Small fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 23/07/2019 - 23:55 Location: South Park Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a small fire in a park on South Park Road, Macclesfield. A small quantity of paper had been set alight. Crews used a fire extinguisher to extinguish it.
