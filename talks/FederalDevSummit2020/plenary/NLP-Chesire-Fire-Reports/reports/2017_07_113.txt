Road traffic collision by Padgate train station in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/07/2017 - 10:56 Location: Padgate Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one fire engine from Birchwood attended There was a road traffic collision involving one car, which left the main road and went down an embankment at Padgate train station.
