Motorbike on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/02/2016 - 20:44 Location: Bower Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn An abandoned motorbike was on fire on a grass verge. Firefighters put out the blaze and are urging anyone with any information about this incident to call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
