 Wheelie bin fires in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/02/2019 - 07:04 Location: Fairclough Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to two wheelie bin fires on Fairclough Street, Warrington. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fires. For wheelie bin fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
