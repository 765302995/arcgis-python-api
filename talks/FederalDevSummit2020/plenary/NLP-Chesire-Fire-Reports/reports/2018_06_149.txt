 Small fire in the open in Frodsham Time of Incident: 18/06/2018 - 20:18 Location: Vicarage Lane, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 8.20pm firefighters were called to a report of bushes being on fire on Vicarage Lane in Frodsham. On arrival the crew found a dead tree stump smouldering in the open. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and make the area safe and were in attendance for less than 15 minutes.
