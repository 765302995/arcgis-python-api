 House fire in Arley Time of Incident: 02/11/2018 - 20:59 Location: Arley Road, Arley. Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington, Lymm, Northwich, Chester and Altrincham attended, along with the hydraulic platform from Lymm. Five fire engines from Warrington, Lymm, Northwich, Chester and Altrincham attended a house fire in Arley Road, Arley, along with a hydraulic platform from Lymm. The fire was in the roof space of a two storey building of a two storey detached property. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a 13.5 metre ladder and two hose reels to put out the fire. They then used salvage sheets to protect the roof from the elements
