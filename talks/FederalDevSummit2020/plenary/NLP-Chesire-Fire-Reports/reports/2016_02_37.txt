Wheelie bin on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/02/2016 - 21:23 Location: Kinnock Park, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters used water backpacks to put out a fire in a wheelie bin. The wheelie bin had been taken into a field and set alight. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
