 Yard fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 03/08/2019 - 21:58 Location: Oxford Street, Widnes. WA8 6DE Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews used a hose reel jet to put out a fire in the back yard of a house on Oxford Street, Widnes.  The fire was reported just before 10pm and firefighters were in attendance for 15 minutes.
