Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/11/2016 - 19:54 Location: Gerrard Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A fire involving a wheelie bin is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters damped the debris down using a hose reel jet and police were asked to attend.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting police log 917 of 21 November.
