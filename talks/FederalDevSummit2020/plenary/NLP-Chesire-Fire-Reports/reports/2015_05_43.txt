Derelict building fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 23/05/2015 - 16:02 Location: Sophie's Bar, Victoria Road, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines from widnes fire station Fire crews were called to a report of a fire at the empty Sophie's Bar, Victoria Road, Widnes.  Firefighters used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel jet to deal with the fire.    It is believed that the fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that can help with the investigation please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
