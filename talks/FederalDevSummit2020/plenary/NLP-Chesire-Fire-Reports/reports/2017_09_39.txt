One person trapped after crash in Lymm Time of Incident: 20/09/2017 - 14:08 Location: Warburton Bridge Road, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood, one from Lymm and one from Irlam (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision between two cars on Warburton Bridge Road, Lymm. Crews used hydraulic cutting equipment and stabilisation equipment to release one casualty from a car which was on its side. Crews also used a hose reel jet and were at the scene for 70 minutes.
