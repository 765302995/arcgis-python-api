 Bin on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 21/04/2019 - 20:27 Location: Cranborne Road, Crewe. CW1 4LH Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters used a water back pack to put out a bin on fire on Cranbourne Road. The fire was reported at 8.27pm and the crew was on scene for almost 10 minutes.
