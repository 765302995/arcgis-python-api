Cooker on fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 23/06/2015 - 09:11 Location: Heath Road, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton Firefighters were called to a fire in a cooker which caused smoke inside the house.  The crew confirmed the cooker fire was out when they arrived and used a high pressure fan to clear smoke from the kitchen.  To avoid cooker fires please follow our safety advice - Kitchen fire safety
