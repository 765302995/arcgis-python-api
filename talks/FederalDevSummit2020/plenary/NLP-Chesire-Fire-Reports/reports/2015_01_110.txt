Chimney fire in Bickerton Time of Incident: 01/01/2015 - 15:29 Location: Bickerton Hall Road, Bickerton. Attendance: A fire engine from Malpas Firefighters were called to a report of a chimney fire on Bickerton Hall Road, Bickerton.  Crews removed hot embers from the hearth and used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.  Chimney fire safety advice
