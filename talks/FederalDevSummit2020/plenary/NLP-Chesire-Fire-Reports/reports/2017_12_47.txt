Vehicle trapped in flood water near Chester Time of Incident: 15/12/2017 - 08:58 Location: Guilden Sutton Lane, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane, one fire engine from Chester and one fire engine from Ellesmere Port attended. Firefighters attended reports of a flooding on Guilden Sutton Lane, Chester. Crews arrived at the scene and found that a car had become trapped in flood waters. The crew used safety equipment to wade through the water and push the vehicle to safety. One man was then released from the vehicle.
