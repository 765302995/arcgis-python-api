Grass fire on an embankment in Runcorn Time of Incident: 25/03/2016 - 20:35 Location: Highlands Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish a fire involving grass on an embankment.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information about this fire they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
