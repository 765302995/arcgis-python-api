 Kitchen fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/10/2018 - 18:47 Location: The Glen, Runcorn Attendance: Three fire engines attended from Runcorn, Widnes and Stockton Heath. Firefighters from Runcorn, Widnes and Stockton Heath were called to reports of a fire at a house on The Glen in Runcorn. On arrival crews found the fire involved a cooker. The team swiftly extinguished the fire and used a large fan to ventilate the property. One person was rescued from the building and crews administered first aid at the scene. The team were in attendance for approximately 50 minutes.
