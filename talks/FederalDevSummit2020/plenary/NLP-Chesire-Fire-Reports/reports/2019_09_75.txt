 Bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 17/09/2019 - 08:24 Location: Overpool Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly before 8.30am firefighters were called to a report of a bin being on fire in the open on Overpool Road, Ellesmere Port. The crew extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. Firefighters were in attendance for less than 10 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started by a discarded cigarette. Fire safety tips for smokers are available at https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/smoking-fire-safety-tips-for-smokers.
