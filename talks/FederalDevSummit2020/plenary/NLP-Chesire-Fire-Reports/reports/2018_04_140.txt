 Chimney fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 04/04/2018 - 17:00 Location: Mayfield Avenue, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to a chimney fire on Mayfield Avenue, Macclesfield. Crews used a hose reel jet and chimney rods to extinguish the fire and then checked the chimney stack using a thermal imaging camera.
