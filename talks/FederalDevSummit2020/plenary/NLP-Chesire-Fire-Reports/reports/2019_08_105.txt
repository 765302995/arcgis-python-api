 Industrial wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/08/2019 - 18:26 Location: Bridge Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish an industrial size wheelie bin at the back of a fast foot outlet on Bridge Street.  The crew were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 485047.
