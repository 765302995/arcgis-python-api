 Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 19/05/2019 - 20:23 Location: Eastgate Way, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Crews attended to a fire in a field on Eastgate Way in Runcorn.  Approximately 10 by 15 metres of grass was located on fire.  Firefighters used a fire extinguisher to put out the fire.
