 Transit van fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 07/12/2018 - 23:48 Location: Kinderton Street, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Crews attended to a transit van well alight on Kinderton Street in Middlewich.  Firefighters, wearing two breathing apparatus, used two hose reels to put out the fire before dampening down and making sure the area was safe.
