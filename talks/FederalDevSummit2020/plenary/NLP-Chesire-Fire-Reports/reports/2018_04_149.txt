 Straw and HGV fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 03/04/2018 - 06:58 Location: B5078, Barthomley Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to a fire in a HGV and its trailer which was filled with straw on the B5078, Barthomley, Crewe. Crews detached one of the trailers before it set alight from the HGV which was carrying nine tonnes of straw in total.  The fire was confined to the cab of the HGV and the first trailer and crews, wearing breathing apparatus, used three hose reel jets, a stack drag, a ladder and a farmer's digger to fight the fire.  During the incident, which lasted four and half hours, the B5078 was closed to traffic.
