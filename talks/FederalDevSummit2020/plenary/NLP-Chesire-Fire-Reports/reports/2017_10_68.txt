Fire in a Spar in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/10/2017 - 04:52 Location: Warrington Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, one from Birchwood, one from Lymm and one from Leigh (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Fire crews were called to a fire in a store room of Spar on Warrington Road, Warrington. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. A fire investigation into the cause of the fire has started. Crews were at the scene for three hours.
