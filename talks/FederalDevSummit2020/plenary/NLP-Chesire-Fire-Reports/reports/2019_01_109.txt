 Kitchen fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 20/01/2019 - 18:22 Location: Hill Street, Sandbach Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Crewe and one from Sandbach Fire crews attended to a kitchen fire at a house on Hill Street in Sandbach.  Firefighters, wearing two breathing apparatus, used one hose reel to bring the fire under control.  Once the fire was extinguished a positive pressure ventilation fan was used to clear away the smoke.  Oxygen therapy was provided to three cat which were inside the house. They were handed into the care of the RSPCA to check they were well.  Crews were in attendance for two hours.  .
