 Vehicle fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/01/2018 - 00:20 Location: Main Street, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters from Runcorn used one hose reel jet and one breathing apparatus to extinguish a vehicle fire on Main Street, Runcorn. The cause of the fire is currently being investigated and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
