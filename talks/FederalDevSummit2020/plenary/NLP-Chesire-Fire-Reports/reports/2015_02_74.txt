House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 10/02/2015 - 19:34 Location: Smallbrook Walk, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Nantwich  Fire crews were called to a report of a house fire on Smallbrook Walk, Crewe.  When they arrived they found a blaze involving the ground and first floor of a terrace property. Firefighters used hose reel jets and specialist fans to deal with the incident.  One man has been taken to hospital for a check up following the incident.  The cause of this fire is believed to have been electrical.
