Road traffic collision in Oakmere Time of Incident: 29/12/2016 - 10:24 Location: Chester Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford Firefighters attended a road traffic collision involving two vehicles in Oakmere, Northwich. Police closed the road at the junction of Abbey Lane and Chester Road. Chester Road was closed in both directions while the incident was dealt with.  One woman was trapped in one of the cars and needed the assistance of firefighters to get her out. The crew removed the roof of the car and handed the casualty to paramedics. Air Ambulance also attended along with police.
