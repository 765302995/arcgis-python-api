 Vehicle on its side on the M6 Time of Incident: 07/11/2019 - 08:29 Location: M6 southbound, between junctions 19 and 18 Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Lymm, one from Winsford and one from Trafford in Greater Manchester - as did the major rescue unit from Winsford Vehicle on its side on the M6Shortly before 8.30am firefighters were called to reports of a vehicle being on its side on the M6 southbound, between junctions 19 and 18.  On arrival the crews found that no-one was trapped inside the saloon vehicle.  Firefighters made the vehicle safe before it was moved from between lanes two and three onto the hard shoulder.  They were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
