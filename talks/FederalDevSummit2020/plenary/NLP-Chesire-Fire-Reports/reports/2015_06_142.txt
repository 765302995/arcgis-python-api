Dishwasher fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 08/06/2015 - 11:27 Location: Carlton Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford A dishwasher caused a fire in Carlton Road.  Firefighters were called to the house and removed the dishwasher from the property.  Smoke alarms weren't fitted so the crew carried out a home safety assessment before leaving the scene.
