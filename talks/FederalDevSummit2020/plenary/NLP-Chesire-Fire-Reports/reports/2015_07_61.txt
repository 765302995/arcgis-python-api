Hay fire in Tiverton Time of Incident: 21/07/2015 - 22:35 Location: Limes Lane, Tiverton, Chester Attendance: One fire engine A fire involving approximately two tonnes of hay in a field was extinguished by firefighters.  Two hose reels were used and the crew damped the area down, turning over the hay and using a thermal imaging camera to make sure the fire was fully extinguished.
