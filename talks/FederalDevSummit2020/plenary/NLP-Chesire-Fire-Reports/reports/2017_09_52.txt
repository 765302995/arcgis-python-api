Chimney fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 16/09/2017 - 22:10 Location: Limefield Avenue, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm Firefighters removed a fire after a large flame went up the chimney and there were concerns there was more debris that could catch fire. There was a small amount of combustible material in the flue which was put out using a handheld extinguisher.  Always follow our safety advice to avoid a chimney fire.
