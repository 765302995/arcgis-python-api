 Washing machine fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/09/2018 - 12:21 Location: Cromdale Way, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh At around 12.20pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from a washing machine at a domestic property on Cromdale Way in Great Sankey, Warrington.  On arrival the crew found that the washing machine was not on fire.  Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to make sure that the washing machine was safe.  They also ventilated the property.  The smoke is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault.  Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
