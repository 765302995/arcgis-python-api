 Motorbike fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/06/2019 - 17:29 Location: Palacefields Avenue, Palacefields, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 5.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in Palacefields, Runcorn. On arrival at Palacefields Avenue the crew found that a motorbike had been severely damaged by fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and make the bike safe and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
