 Tractor on fire at a farm in Middlewich Time of Incident: 06/06/2018 - 11:13 Location: Bradwall Road, Middlewich Attendance: Two fire engines attended, from Sandbach and Holmes Chapel, as well as a Rapid Response Unit from Holmes Chapel At around 11.15am firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a farm on Bradwall Road in Middlewich. On arrival the crew discovered a tractor on fire away from the farm's buildings. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The crew then damped the area down to make it safe. As a precaution, firefighters administered oxygen to a female suffering slight effects of smoke inhalation as a result of the fire. It was deemed that an ambulance was not required. Firefighters were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
