 Fire in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/03/2018 - 19:29 Location: Stockham Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 7.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire on a field on Stockham Lane in Runcorn. Crews located a fire at the back of Fleetwood Walk in Murdishaw and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. They were at the scene for just over half-an-hour.
