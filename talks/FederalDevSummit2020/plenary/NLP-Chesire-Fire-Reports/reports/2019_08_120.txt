 Car trapped in flood water, Audlem Time of Incident: 09/08/2019 - 20:55 Location: Green Lane, Audlem Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Crewe Crews were called after a car became trapped in flood water on Green Lane. The flood was in a dip in the road near an old railway bridge, and the water was around 2ft deep. The occupants of the car were a woman and her three children. Firefighters were able to push the car out of the water and into a private driveway. Before they left, they then contacted the council's highways department to request barriers to prevent further cars entering the flood water.
