 Vehicle fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 14/04/2018 - 03:45 Location: Somerville Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters tackled a car on fire in Somverville Road, Widnes. A single hose reel was used to tackle the blaze, which was well alight on arrival, and a home safety assessment was carried out. The incident is thought to have been a deliberate ignition, anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 35760.
