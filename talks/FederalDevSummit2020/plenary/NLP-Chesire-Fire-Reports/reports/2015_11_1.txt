HGV fire on M62 Time of Incident: 29/11/2015 - 00:46 Location: M62, junction 10, East Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath One hose reel was used to cool a HGV which had been on fire on the M62.  The fire was caused by an overheated rear axel and appeared to be out on arrival of the crews who used a thermal imaging to check for hotspots and cooled the area down.  Highways attended to close off lane one of the motorway while the incident was dealt with.
