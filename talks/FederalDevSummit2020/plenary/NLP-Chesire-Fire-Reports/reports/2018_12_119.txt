 Wasteland fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 04/12/2018 - 09:31 Location: Field Way, Alsager Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsager At around 9.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Field Way in Alsager. On arrival the crew found that the fire on wasteland was a case of controlled burning that was being supervised. Firefighters allowed the fire to continue and used water to wet a nearby bush as a precautionary measure. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
