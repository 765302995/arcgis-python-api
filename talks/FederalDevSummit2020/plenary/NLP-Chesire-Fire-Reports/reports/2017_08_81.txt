Dog rescued from water in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/08/2017 - 16:47 Location: Manchester Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Lymm Plumrose back on dry landA dog got stuck in the River Mersey after it went for a swim but couldn't get out because the embankment was too steep. Plumrose the 17 month old Labrador needed the help of firefighters to get out. The crews used swift water gear and a short ladder to reach the dog and get it back on dry land.
