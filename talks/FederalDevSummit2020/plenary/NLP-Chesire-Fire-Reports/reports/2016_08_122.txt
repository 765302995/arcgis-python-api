Horse rescued from mud in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/08/2016 - 15:36 Location: Alder Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and the animal rescue unit with supporting fire engine from Knutsford A horse became stuck in a ditch in sinking sand on Alder Lane. Firefighters and the Service animal rescue team were able to keep the horse calm as they utilised specialist equipment to get the animal back on to dry land.
