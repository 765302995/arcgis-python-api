 Flat fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 09/03/2019 - 19:46 Location: Percyvale Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton. One fire engine from Macclesfield attended.  Fire engines from Macclesfield and Poynton were mobilised to a flat fire on Percyvale Street, Macclesfield. On arrival crews found a fire involving a second floor flat.  As a precaution residents from the second floor were evacuated while crews used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to extinguish the fire.  One casualty suffered smoke inhalation and was taken to hospital as a precaution. Crews were on scene for just under three hours.  The fire is believed to have started from an overloaded electrical socket.
