Sheep rescued from the River Bollin in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 13/08/2016 - 09:44 Location: River Bollin, Beechwood Mews, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and the technical rescue team from Knutsford A sheep was stranded in the River Bollin, so firefighters rescued the sheep from the water and then returned it to graze in a nearby field.
