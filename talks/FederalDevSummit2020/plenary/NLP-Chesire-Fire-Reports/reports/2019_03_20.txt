 Sheep rescue in Lower Withington Time of Incident: 25/03/2019 - 14:47 Location: Dingle Bank Quarry, Holmes Chapel Road, Lower Withington Attendance: Fire engines attended from Lymm, Macclesfield, Wilmslow and Knutsford along with the Rope Rescue Unit from Lymm Sheep rescue in Lower WithingtonAt 2.41pm firefighters were called to reports of an animal rescue incident in Dingle Bank Quarry in Lower Withington.  On arrival at the scene the team found that approximately 13 sheep were stuck in a silt pond in the old quarry.  The team used inflatable floating pathway to reach the sheep and free them and bring them to safety.  Firefighters were in attendance at the scene for approximately three hours.
