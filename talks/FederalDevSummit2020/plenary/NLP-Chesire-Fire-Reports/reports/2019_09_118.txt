 Fire in children's play area in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/09/2019 - 21:24 Location: Kilford Close, Callands, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Shortly before 9.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a children's play area in Kilford Close, Callands, Warrington. On arrival the crew found a rubber tyre well alight. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
