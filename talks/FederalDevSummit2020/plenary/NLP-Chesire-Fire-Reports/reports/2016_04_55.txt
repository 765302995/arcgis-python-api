Cooking fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/04/2016 - 14:27 Location: Chaffinch Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Cooking caused a small fire at a property in Birchwood, Warrington. Firefighters used a high pressure fan to clear smoke from the house and asked for an ambulance to attend for a lady who had suffered slight smoke inhalation.  Always follow our cooking safety advice
