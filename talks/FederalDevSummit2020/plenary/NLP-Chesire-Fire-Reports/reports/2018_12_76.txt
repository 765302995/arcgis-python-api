 Chimney fire in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 13/12/2018 - 21:32 Location: West Way, Holmes Chapel. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel Shortly after 9.30pm firefighters from Holmes Chapel attended reports of a chimney fire on West Way. On arrival at the scene the team found that there was smoke coming from the chimney of a gas fire. Firefighters initially isolated the gas supply, they then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.  Crews then entered the loft space and investigated the issue where they discovered that a birds nest had fallen down the chimney. The team removed all of the hot embers and cleared the blockage, they then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately one hour ten minutes.
