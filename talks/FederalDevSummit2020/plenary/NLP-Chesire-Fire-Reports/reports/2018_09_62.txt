 Smoke-filled domestic property in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/09/2018 - 16:21 Location: Kemmel Avenue, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Lymm At around 4.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a domestic property on Kemmel Avenue in Latchford, Warrington. On arrival crews discovered that fumes from cooking had caused the alarm to go off. Firefighters used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property. They also administered oxygen therapy to the occupier before handing her over to the care of paramedics.
