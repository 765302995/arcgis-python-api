Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/07/2015 - 07:38 Location: Lingley Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Crews were called to a road traffic collision involving one car.  A lamp post was also damaged in the incident.  The car had overturned and there was one casualty in the car who was able to get out themselves.  Firefighters made the scene safe and alerted highways to the damaged lamp post.
