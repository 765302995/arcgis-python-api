Loft fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 18/02/2017 - 07:13 Location: Delamere Park Way West, Northwich. Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich and Frodsham Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire at Delamere Park Way West, Northwich.  When they arrived they confirmed that there was a fire in the loft space and everyone was out of the property.   Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet and a main cover jet to tackle the fire. A large fan and a , thermal image camera were also used.
