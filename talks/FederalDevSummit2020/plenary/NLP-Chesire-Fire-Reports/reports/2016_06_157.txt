Fern fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 03/06/2016 - 23:16 Location: Alvanley Road, Frodsham. Attendance: A fire engine from Frodsham Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Alvanley Road, Frodsham.  When they arrived crews found that there was some ferns covering an area of about 5m by 5m. Crews used water back packs and beaters to put the fire out.
