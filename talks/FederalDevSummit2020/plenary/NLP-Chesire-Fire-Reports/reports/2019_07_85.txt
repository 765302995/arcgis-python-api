 Bush fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 15/07/2019 - 21:18 Location: Trident Retail Park, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a bush fire at the rear of the cinema on Trident Retail Park. The crew were detained at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.
