Chimney fire in Norbury, Whitchurch Time of Incident: 09/08/2017 - 20:53 Location: Swanwick Green, Norbury Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas Firefighters tackled a chimney blaze at a house on Swanwick Green. They had to remove the fire from a log burner and the plate from the chimney breast to be able to put the fire out using chimney rods and a chimney hose.  Please follow our chimney safety advice.
