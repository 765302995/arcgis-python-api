 Car fire in Widnes town centre Time of Incident: 18/03/2019 - 17:20 Location: Watkinson Way, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 5.20pm firefighters from Penketh were called to reports of a vehicle fire on Watkinson Way, Widnes.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered the engine compartment of a saloon car was well alight.  One firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately half-an-hour.
