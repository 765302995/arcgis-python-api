Bonfire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 05/08/2015 - 17:37 Location: Stanneylands Road, Wilmslow. Attendance: A fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at an address on Stanneylands Road, Wilmslow.  When they arrived they found that it was a bonfire that had got out of control. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.
