Suspected fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 04/02/2015 - 10:21 Location: Grove Way, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow A Wilmslow building was evacuated when a smoke alarm activated.  Firefighters were called and found that an electrical fault in the fuse box had caused smoke logging in ducting.  The crew checked the ducting using a thermal imaging camera, with an electrician in attendance, and then handed the incident over to the utility company.
