Large fire in the open in Millington Time of Incident: 31/05/2017 - 21:02 Location: Chester Road, Millington Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford An uncontrolled burning of rubbish was extinguished by firefghters using a hose reel. The fire is believed to have been rubbish which had been fly tipped and measured approximately 10 foot by 15 foot wide.
