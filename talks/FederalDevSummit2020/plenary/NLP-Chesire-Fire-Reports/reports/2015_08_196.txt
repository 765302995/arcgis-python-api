Outbuilding fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 02/08/2015 - 03:55 Location: Stanney Lane, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port and Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Stanney Lane, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved an outbuilding. Hose reels and main water jets were used to put the fire out. Firefighters also checked the premise and area with a thermal imaging camera with no further signs of fire. They were at the scene for about four hours.
