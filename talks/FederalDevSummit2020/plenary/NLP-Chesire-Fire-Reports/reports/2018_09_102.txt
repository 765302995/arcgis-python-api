 Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/09/2018 - 14:25 Location: Burtonwood Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Birchwood Fire crews attended a road traffic collision between two cars on Burtonwood Road, Warrington. One casualty, who received superficial injuries, was treated at the scene by fire service personnel. A further casualty was treated by paramedics. The road was partially blocked whilst the emergency services dealt with the incident. Crews were at the scene for 35 minutes.
