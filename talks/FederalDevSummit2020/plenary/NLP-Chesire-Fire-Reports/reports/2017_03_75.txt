Collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/03/2017 - 18:05 Location: Central Expressway, Runcorn. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Runcorn Fire crews responded to a report of a collision on the Central Expressway, Runcorn.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two cars. Two women were trapped. Crews worked alongside paramedics to release the two women who were then handed to the care of the ambulance service.
