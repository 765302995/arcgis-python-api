 Kitchen fire in Chester Time of Incident: 06/08/2019 - 17:08 Location: Dauncey Close, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Fire crews attended to a kitchen fire on Dauncey Close in Chester.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews used a hose reel covering jet to extinguish the fire.  A large fan was used to disperse the smoke and a thermal imaging camera to monitor the area.  A woman inside the house was provided with oxygen therapy and handed to the care of paramedics.
