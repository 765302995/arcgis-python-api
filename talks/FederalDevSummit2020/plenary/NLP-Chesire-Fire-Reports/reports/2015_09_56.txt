Van set on fire on Hillside Road in Chester Time of Incident: 18/09/2015 - 23:01 Location: Hillside Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Two hose reel jets were used to extinguish a fire involving a van on the roadway on Hillside Road in Chester. The van was well alight.  Police attended this incident.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
