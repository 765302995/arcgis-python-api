 Gorse fire on Runcorn Hill Time of Incident: 06/08/2018 - 14:45 Location: Highlands Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 2.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving dry gorse at the base of a tree on Runcorn Hill on Highlands Road. On arrival the crew discovered a fire measuring approximately three metres by two metres. Due to the location of the fire, firefighters set up a water shuttle to get enough water to the area. Firefighters used an extended hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
