 Flat fire in Chester Time of Incident: 12/01/2018 - 07:58 Location: Norris Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Firefighters were called to a  fire on the ground floor of a block of flats on Norris Road, Chester. On arrival crews found that the fire was in the refuse area of the flats. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a covering jet to extinguish the fire. Crews the used two large fans to clear smoke from the property. Everyone in the building was accounted for and there are no reports of any injuries. A fire investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
