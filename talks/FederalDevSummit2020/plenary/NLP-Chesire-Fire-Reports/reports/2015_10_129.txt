Kitchen fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 08/10/2015 - 18:30 Location: Higher Downs, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one fire engine from Wilmslow One fire engine from Knutsford and one fire engine from Wilmslow went to a kitchen fire at a property on Higher Downs in Knutsford. On arrival firefighters found that the kitchen was heavily smoke logged. They quickly located a fire involving a pan on a cooker. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. They used one hose reel to extinguish the fire. Firefighters rescued two dogs that were inside the property. Then they used a large fan to clear the smoke, before checking the property for hotspots of fire.   Kitchen fire safety tips
