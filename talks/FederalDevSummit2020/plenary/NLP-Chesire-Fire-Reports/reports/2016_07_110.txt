Car on fire in Bollington Time of Incident: 12/07/2016 - 01:14 Location: Heath Road, Bollington Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington A car which was on fire in Bollington required firefighters to attend and use one hose reel to extinguish it. This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information about who may have started it they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
