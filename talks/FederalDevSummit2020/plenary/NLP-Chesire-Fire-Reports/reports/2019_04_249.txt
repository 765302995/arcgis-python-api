 Rescue of a cat in Sandbach Time of Incident: 01/04/2019 - 20:14 Location: Bold Street, Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach Firefighters from Sandbach used a 7.5 metre ladder to rescue a cat which was stuck on the roof of a three storey building on Bold Street.  Crews were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
