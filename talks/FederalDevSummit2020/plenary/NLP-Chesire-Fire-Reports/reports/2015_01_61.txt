Five vehicles involved in collision on M6 Time of Incident: 15/01/2015 - 17:20 Location: M6 SB junction 17 to 16 Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe, one from Holmes Chapel and a rope rescue unit from Holmes Chapel.  One person was released from a vehicle involved in a collision with four other vehicles on the M6. The man was handed into the care of paramedics along with four other casualties. The accident resulted in a full closure of the motorway while the incident was dealt with.
