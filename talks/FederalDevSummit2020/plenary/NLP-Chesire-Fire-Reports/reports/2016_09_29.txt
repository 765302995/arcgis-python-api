Restaurant fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/09/2016 - 07:19 Location: South Pier Road Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one fire engine from Chester Firefighters were called to a fire involving a single storey building. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used onse hose reel jet, one covering jet, one large fan and a thermal imaging camera to tackle the fire.
