 Flat fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 16/03/2018 - 02:06 Location: Swettenham Street, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Fire crews were called to a flat fire on Swettenham Street, Macclesfield. The fire was in a first floor flat and crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, tackled the fire using a hose reel jet and a large fan.  Sadly, one man died at the scene.  A fire investigation into the cause of the fire is ongoing.
