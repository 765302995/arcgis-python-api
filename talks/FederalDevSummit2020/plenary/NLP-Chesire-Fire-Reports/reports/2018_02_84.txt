 Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 12/02/2018 - 08:55 Location: Faraday Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a kitchen fire on Faraday Road, Ellesmere Port. The fire was out on arrival of the crews and they used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. Crews also gave first aid to two casualties, one for a minor burn to a hand and one for smoke inhalation.
