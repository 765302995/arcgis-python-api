Wheelie bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/04/2015 - 21:33 Location: Eaton Street in Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine from Runcorn Hose reel jets were used to put out a blaze involving four wheelie bins on Eaton Street in Runcorn.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.   Wheelie bin fire safety advice
