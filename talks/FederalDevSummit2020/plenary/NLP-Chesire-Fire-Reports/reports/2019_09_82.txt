 Car fire, Crewe Time of Incident: 15/09/2019 - 00:17 Location: Underwood Lane, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters dealt with a car on fire at an address on Underwood Lane. The car was well alight, and they used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to put it out. They also called the police due to it appearing to have been a deliberate ignition.
