Road traffic collision on the A55 in Chester Time of Incident: 26/08/2015 - 17:55 Location: A55 in Christleton, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester and one fire engine from Wrexham, North Wales Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish a car that was well alight following a road traffic collision, involving four cars, on the A55 in Christleton. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  Nobody was trapped. Police and ambulance attended. Police closed the road while the incident was dealt with. One person was taken to hospital by ambulance.
