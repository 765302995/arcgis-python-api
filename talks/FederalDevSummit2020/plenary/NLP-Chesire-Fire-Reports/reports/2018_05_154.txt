 Chimney fire, Northwich Time of Incident: 11/05/2018 - 19:38 Location: Hulse Lane, Lach Dennis, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters responded to reports of a chimney fire at a farmhouse in Lach Dennis. They tackled the fire from the grate and used a thermal imaging camera to check that it hadn't spread into the attic. A safe and well visit was also completed before crews left.  For chimney fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chimneys.
