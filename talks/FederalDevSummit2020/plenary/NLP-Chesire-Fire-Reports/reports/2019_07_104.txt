 Pallet fire in Chester Time of Incident: 13/07/2019 - 23:59 Location: Spital Walk, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters were called to Spital Walk, Chester, after a fire in a chiminea set fire to a quantity of pallets. The fire also damaged the outside of the property. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the blaze.
