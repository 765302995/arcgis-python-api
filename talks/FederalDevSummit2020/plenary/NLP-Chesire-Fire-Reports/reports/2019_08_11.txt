 Fire involving rubbish beside a busway in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/08/2019 - 20:39 Location: Brookvale Avenue North, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 8.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in Brookvale Avenue North, Runcorn. On arrival the crew found a small fire involving rubbish beside a busway. Firefighters used water to put the fire out and were in attendance for less than 10 minutes.
