Building fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 06/04/2015 - 20:05 Location: Halton Court, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Runcorn A gas cylinder and rubbish was on fire in a derelict building.   Firefighters removed the cylinder and cooled it using a hose reel jet and the fire involving rubbish was extinguished.   A thermal imaging camera was used to monitor the cylinder.
