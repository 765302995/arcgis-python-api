 Caravan fire in Chester Time of Incident: 10/09/2018 - 20:48 Location: Dodleston Lane, Pulford, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Powey Lane and one from Chester At around 8.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a caravan on Dodleston Lane in Pulford, Chester. On arrival crews discovered that a caravan on the side of a road had been completely destroyed by fire. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 45 minutes. Police were also in attendance. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
