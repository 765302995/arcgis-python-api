House fire in Great Barrow Time of Incident: 21/04/2016 - 10:22 Location: Hollowmoor Heath, Great Barrow, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port A fire on the second floor of a house was tackled by firefighters. Gas and electricity supplies were isolated as four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel and a covering jet to put out the fire, followed by a high pressure fan to clear smoke.  Nobody was trapped in the building. The fire was in the airing cupboard and, fortunately, damage was limited as the door was closed, preventing fire spread .
