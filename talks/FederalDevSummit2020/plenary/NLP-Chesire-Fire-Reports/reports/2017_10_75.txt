Fire in outbuilding in Styal Time of Incident: 15/10/2017 - 09:52 Location: Stanneylands Road, Styal Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Wythenshawe (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Fire crews were called to an outbuilding fire on Stanneylands Road, Styal, Wilmslow. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet under rapid deployment to extinguish the fire. They then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. Crews were in attendance for four hours and 30 minutes.
