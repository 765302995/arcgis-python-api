 Male trapped after quad bike overturns in Goostrey Time of Incident: 09/07/2019 - 18:53 Location: Station Road, Goostrey Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Macclesfield and one from Congleton - as did a rapid response vehicle from Holmes Chapel Shortly before 7pm firefighters were called to a report of a vehicle having overturned on a field off Station Road in Goostrey. On arrival crews discovered that a male was trapped inside the quad bike. Firefighters liaised with paramedics in getting the casualty out of the vehicle. They then handed him over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour. Paramedics also attended.
