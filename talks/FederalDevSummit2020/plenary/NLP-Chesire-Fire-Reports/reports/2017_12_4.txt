Rubbish fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/12/2017 - 19:40 Location: Davenport Row, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a quantity of rubbish which was on fire on Davenport Row, Runcorn. Crews found that a sofa and other rubbish had been deliberately set on fire and used a hose reel jet to extinguish it. If you have any information about the incident please call Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
