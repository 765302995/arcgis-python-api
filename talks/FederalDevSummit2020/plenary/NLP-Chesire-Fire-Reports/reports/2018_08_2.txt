 Small bin fire in a flat in Handforth Time of Incident: 30/08/2018 - 10:05 Location: Pickmere Road, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and two from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a fire involving a waste paper bin in a flat on Pickmere Road, Handforth. Crews used a large fan to clear smoke from the property and one casualty was assessed by paramedics for smoke inhalation. The fire was caused by discarded smoking materials. Crews were at the scene for 20 minutes.
