 Fire at a domestic property in Irlam Time of Incident: 05/12/2019 - 15:39 Location: Lords Street, Cadishead, Irlam Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Birchwood, one from Lymm and one from Trafford in Greater Manchester Shortly after 3.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a domestic property in Lords Street, Cadishead, Irlam.  On arrival crews discovered that the fire caused by candles had been put out by an occupier.  Firefighters used a large fan to clear smoke from the property and were in attendance for more than 20 minutes.  For candle safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/candles-in-the-home.
