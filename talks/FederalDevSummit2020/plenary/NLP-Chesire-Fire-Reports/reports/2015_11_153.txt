Car on fire on the hard shoulder of the M56 westbound Time of Incident: 03/11/2015 - 07:29 Location: M56, westbound near to junction 12 Attendance: One from Runcorn, one from Warrington and one from Frodsham A car on fire on the hard shoulder required firefighters to attend and use a hose one hose reel to extinguish it. No casualties were reported.
