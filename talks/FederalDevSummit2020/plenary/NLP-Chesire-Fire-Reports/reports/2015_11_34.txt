Vehicles on fire in Leighton Time of Incident: 23/11/2015 - 02:17 Location: Hesketh Croft, Leighton Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A crew was called to a fire involving two vehicles on fire on a driveway in Leighton. A minibus and a car were both on fire, which was put out by firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, using a hose reel jet. Police also attended as it is believed the fire was started deliberately. Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
