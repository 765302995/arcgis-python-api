 Shed fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 26/05/2018 - 03:55 Location: Plumley Moor Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford and one from Middlewich Fire crews attended a shed fire on the junction of Plumley Moor Road and Middlewich Road, in Knutsford. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were at the scene for 40 minutes.
