Refuse on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/09/2017 - 19:14 Location: Loushers Lane, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A small amount of rubbish had been set on fire in what is believed to have been a deliberate action. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.  Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111. Arson is a crime.
