 Hob fire in a kitchen in Chester Time of Incident: 25/04/2019 - 18:39 Location: Allington Place, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Chester and one from Powey Lane At around 6.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the kitchen of a domestic property in Allington Place, Chester. The fire involving a hob was out on arrival. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check the temperature of the hob and the surrounding areas to make sure that they were safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour. For tips on avoiding a fire in the kitchen visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
