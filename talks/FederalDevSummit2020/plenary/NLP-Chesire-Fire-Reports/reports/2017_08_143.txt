Road traffic collision involving multiple vehicles in Lymm Time of Incident: 04/08/2017 - 19:36 Location: Agden Park Lane, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm and one from Altrincham (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Firefighters were sent to a road traffic collision involving multiple vehicles in Lymm.  One female casualty was trapped, so firefighters released the casualty from the vehicle.  An ambulance and air ambulance attended.  The casualty was released and handed to the care of a doctor and paramedics.
