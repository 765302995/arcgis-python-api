 Bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/12/2018 - 18:22 Location: Whitby Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly before 6.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a bin being on fire on Whitby Road in Ellesmere Port. They extinguished the fire in the open with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
