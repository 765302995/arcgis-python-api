Man rescued from river in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/08/2015 - 03:40 Location: Kingsway South, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath, two fire engines and a boat from Warrington and one fore engine and rope rescue team from Knutsford.
