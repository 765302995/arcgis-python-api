House fire fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 11/04/2015 - 13:50 Location: Chester Road in Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Chester Road in Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived they found that there was a fire in the kitchen. Crews used hose reel jets to tackle the blaze. A special fan was used to clear the smoke.   It is believed that the cause of the fire was an electrical fault. There were no reports of any injuries.
