Van fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 29/09/2018 - 02:40 Location: A534, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach Fire crews were called to a van fire on the A534, Sandbach. Crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews also used hydraulic spreaders to open the back of the van to access a petrol can which was in the back, and remove it.
