 Fire on Poynton boat Time of Incident: 19/05/2019 - 15:33 Location: Lyme Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Firefighters attended to a fire involving a canal boat on Lyme Road in Poynton.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews used one hose reel to put out the fire and then checked the area with a thermal imaging camera.  The fire is believed to have been caused by radiated heat from an unattended oven.   Crews were in attendance for approximately one hour and fifty-five minutes.
