Fire at a property in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 16/05/2017 - 20:38 Location: Moorfield Drive in Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle Two people suffered burns following a fire inside a living room of a property on Moorfield Drive in Wilmslow.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet.  An ambulance was requested to attend.
