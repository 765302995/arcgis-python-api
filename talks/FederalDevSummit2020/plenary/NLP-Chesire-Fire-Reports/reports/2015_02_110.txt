Road traffic collision in Chelford Time of Incident: 04/02/2015 - 08:31 Location: Knutsford Road, Chelford Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters, along with other emergency services, made the scene safe after a road traffic collision on Knutsford Road.  One vehicle was involved, colliding with a fence on the railway bridge.  Nobody was trapped in the car.
