Lawnmower fire in Neston Time of Incident: 20/09/2017 - 13:03 Location: Ringway, Neston Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a lawnmower which was on fire on Ringway, Neston. The lawnmower was well alight on arrival of the crews and they used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
