 Grill pan fire at a flat in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/06/2018 - 00:39 Location: Albert Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Widnes and one from Penketh At around 12.40am firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a flat on Albert Road in Widnes.  On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved a grill pan and all persons were accounted for.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  The crew then used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property.Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.  For safety advice visit grill pan fire safety advice
