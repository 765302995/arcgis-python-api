Car vehicle fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 01/06/2017 - 20:32 Location: Chester Road, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford Fire crews were called to a car fire on the hard shoulder of the new bypass on Chester Road, Knutsford. A Mercedes C class was well alight on arrival and crews, wearing one breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Police also attended the incident to manage traffic.
