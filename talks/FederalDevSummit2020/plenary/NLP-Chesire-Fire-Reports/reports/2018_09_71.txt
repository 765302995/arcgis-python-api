 House fire in Barnton Time of Incident: 17/09/2018 - 11:40 Location: Manor Drive, Barnton Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich Fire crews were called to a house fire on Manor Drive, Barnton. The fire was in a utility room and a dishwasher was on fire. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a covering jet to bring the fire under control. Crews were at the scene for an hour and 10 minutes.
