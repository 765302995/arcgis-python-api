Quarry fire in Chester Time of Incident: 08/05/2017 - 20:05 Location: Old Coach Road, Chester. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Malpas and Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a quarry off Old Coach Road, Chester.  When they arrived crews found about 80tonnes of garden waste on fire. Hose reel jets were used to put the fire out and dampen down the area.
