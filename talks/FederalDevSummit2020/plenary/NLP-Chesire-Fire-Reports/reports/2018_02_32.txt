 Fire in a commercial building in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/02/2018 - 07:39 Location: London Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Fire crews were called to a commercial building on London Road, Warrington, after reports of a fire. Crews found a small fire in a fuse box and used a dry powder extinguisher to put it out.
