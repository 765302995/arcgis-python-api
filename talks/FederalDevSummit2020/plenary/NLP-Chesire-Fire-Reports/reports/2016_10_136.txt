Fire on children's playground in Knutsford Time of Incident: 08/10/2016 - 20:24 Location: Drury Lane, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford A fire had been started on a bench in a Knutsford children's playground. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Police also attended.   Anyone with any information about who might have started this fire is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
