 Two vehicle collision in Chelford Time of Incident: 24/09/2019 - 18:01 Location: Alderley Road, Chelford, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Wilmslow and Macclesfield Shortly after 6pm firefighters from Macclesfield and Wilmslow were called to reports of a two vehicle collision on Alderley Road, Chelford.   On arrival crews found that one person was medically trapped in their car.  Firefighters helped release the casualty from the car and they were passed to the care of paramedics.  Crews were in attendance for approximately 40 minutes.
