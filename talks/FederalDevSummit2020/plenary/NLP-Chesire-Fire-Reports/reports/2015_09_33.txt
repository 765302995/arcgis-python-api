Northwich chimney fire Time of Incident: 25/09/2015 - 13:40 Location: Penrhyn Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A fire in a chimney was attended by firefighters. The crew removed embers from the hearth and applied water. Chimney rods were also used to help put out the fire and the loft space and chimney was checked with a thermal imaging camera.
