Fire in Minshull Vernon Time of Incident: 20/03/2015 - 13:40 Location: Middlewich Road, Minshull Vernon Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Nantwich Firefighters were called to the Coach and Horses in Minshull Vernon, to a report of a fire.  The fire was out on arrival and firefighters checked the room using a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no further hot spots.  They also cut away materials around the fire incident to make sure there was no spread of fire.  Customers evacuated the building while the incident was being dealt with.
