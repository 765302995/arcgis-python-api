Car on fire in a ditch in Chester Time of Incident: 10/08/2016 - 05:44 Location: Caughall Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters were called to extinguish a car which was on fire in a ditch. Police attended at it was believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
