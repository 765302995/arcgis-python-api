Small fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 14/07/2017 - 15:41 Location: Kinderton Street, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and one from Middlewich Fire crews were called to a the Original Factory Shop, on Kinderton Street, Middlewich, after a report of smoke inside the building.  Firefighters at the scene used a thermal imaging camera to search for the fire and discovered a small fire in the premises next door. They used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
