 Three-vehicle collision in Lostock Gralam Time of Incident: 18/09/2019 - 07:49 Location: Manchester Road, Lostock Gralam, near Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Northwich and one from Lymm At around 7.45am firefighters were called to reports of a collision on Manchester Road in Lostock Gralam, near Northwich. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved three vehicles and that two people - a male and a female - were trapped inside one of them. The road was closed in both directions as emergency services worked at the scene. The casualties were assessed by paramedics. After being assessed, the female was able to get out of the vehicle by herself. Firefighters used specialist equipment to get the male out of the vehicle. They then handed him over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters also made the vehicles involved in the collision safe. They were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
