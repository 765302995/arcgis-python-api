Car fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 12/06/2016 - 04:45 Location: Kennedy Avenue, Macclesfield. Attendance: A fire engine from macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Kennedy Avenue, Macclesfield.  When they arrived they found that two wheelie bins had been set on fire and had spread to a car.  Hose reel jets were used to put the fire out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
