 Wheelie bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/07/2019 - 02:46 Location: Stokesay Court, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At 2.46am firefighters were called to reports of a fire on Stokesay Court in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered three wheelie bins which were well alight. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for around 30 minutes. For wheelie bin fire prevention tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
