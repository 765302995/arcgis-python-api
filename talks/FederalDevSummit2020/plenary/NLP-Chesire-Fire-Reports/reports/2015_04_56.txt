House fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/04/2015 - 15:08 Location: Chester Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters extinguished a fire in the lounge of a house in Ellesmere Port.  The crew wore breathing apparatus and used one hose reel to extinguish the blaze and a large fan to clear the smoke.  The smoke alarm did activate and alerted a builder working at the property who called 999. Firefighters were at the scene for just over an hour and the cause is believed to be electrical.
