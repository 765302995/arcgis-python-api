Motorbike on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 29/09/2017 - 17:24 Location: Castlefields, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A motorbike which was on fire in woodland was extinguished with water after it is believed to have been deliberately set on fire. Call police on 101 if you know anything about who may have started it or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
