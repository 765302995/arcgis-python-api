 Bin fire, Macclesfield Time of Incident: 09/06/2018 - 22:59 Location: Farwood Close, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters responded to reports of a fire in the open in Farwood Close. They arrived to find a wheelie bin on fire, which had been caused by discarded embers from a barbecue. A wooden fence had also caught fire. Crews used a hose reel to tackle the fire and, after it was extinguished, used a thermal imaging camera and stack drag as part of the dampening down process.  Advice for safe barbecue use, including disposal afterwards, can be found on our website https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/outdoor-safety/barbecue-safety
