Overheated car on the M56 Time of Incident: 19/04/2017 - 14:43 Location: M56 east between junctions 7 to 6 Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath A car which had an overheated transmission on the M56 was cooled with a hose reel. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the vehicle had cooled before crews left the scene.
