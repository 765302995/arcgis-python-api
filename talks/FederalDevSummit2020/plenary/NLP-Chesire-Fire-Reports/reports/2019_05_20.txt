 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/05/2019 - 14:21 Location: Maple Grove, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 2.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Maple Grove in Latchford, Warrington. On arrival the crew found a fire involving a wheelie bin. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour. For tips on preventing a wheelie bin fire visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
