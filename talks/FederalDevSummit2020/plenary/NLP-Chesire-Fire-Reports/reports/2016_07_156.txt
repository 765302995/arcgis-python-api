Road traffic collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 04/07/2016 - 09:44 Location: M56 West between junctions 12 and 14 Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision on the M56 which involved a coach. The crew assisted people from the coach which was down an embankment. Police and paramedics also attended.
