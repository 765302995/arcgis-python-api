 Shed fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 28/06/2019 - 16:29 Location: Barratt Road,Alsager. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe. One fire engine attended from Congleton. Firefighters were called to a shed fire in the garden of an address in Barratt Road, Alsager. The fire had spread to two further sheds and neighbouring gardens. Crews used two breathing apparatus and two hose reels to bring the fire under control. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. Crews remained in attendance for over two hours.
