Bench fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 09/04/2015 - 20:52 Location: Malt Street, Knutsford. Attendance: A fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a bench fire on Malt Street in Knutsford.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
