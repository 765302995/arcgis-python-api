 Fire in electrical transformer in Pickmere Time of Incident: 13/06/2018 - 04:57 Location: Mere Lane, Pickmere Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Fire crews were called to a fire in an electrical transformer on Mere Lane, Pickmere. Crews isolated the electrics with the help of a Scottish Power engineer and then used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
