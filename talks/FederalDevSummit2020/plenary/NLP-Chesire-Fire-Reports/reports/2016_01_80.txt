Horse stuck in horsebox in Poynton Time of Incident: 10/01/2016 - 11:55 Location: London Road North, Poynton. Attendance: Fire engines from Poynton and Knutsford Firefighters were called to a horse that had become stuck in a horse box in Poynton. The horse, named Rebel, was trapped by a bar so the Animal Rescue unit attended along with a vet and a cordon was put in place around the area while the crew released the animal.  Rebel's owner, Chloe Brookes, said: "Rebel is doing well after her ordeal. Thanks so much to the firefighters who helped release her."
