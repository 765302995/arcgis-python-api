 Compost heap fire in Newton Time of Incident: 04/08/2018 - 16:07 Location: Lees Lane, Newton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Crews attended to a large compost heap approximately 40m by 10m well alight on Lees Lane in Newton, Macclesfield.   Two hose reels were used to put out the fire and bring it under control.
