Road traffic collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 09/07/2015 - 13:13 Location: Chelford Road, Nether Alderley, Macclesfield Attendance: Three fire engines Firefighters have released two casualties involved in a road traffic collision on Chelford Road, Nether Alderley.  Two vehicles were involved, a car and a flat bed recovery vehicle.  The casualties were trapped in the car.  The crew removed the car roof to be able to release the casualties who were handed into the care of the ambulance service.
