Garage on fire at a property on Rydal Close in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 18/07/2015 - 22:37 Location: Rydal Close, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and Chester Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and Chester were sent to a fire at a property on Rydal Close in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the fire crews found the fire to be inside a garage. Firefighters used two breathing apparatus and two hose reels to extinguish the fire.
