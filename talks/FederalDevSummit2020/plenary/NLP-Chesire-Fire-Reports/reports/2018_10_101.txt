 Flat fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 12/10/2018 - 23:38 Location: Lumley Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield At around 11.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a flat on Lumley Road in Macclesfield. On arrival all persons were accounted for. A water supply was set up and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used an extended hose reel jet to fight the fire. A covering jet was also used. Once the fire was out crews dampened the affected areas down to make sure they were safe and used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property. Oxygen was administered to a male suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation. He was handed over to the care of paramedics and taken to hospital via ambulance. Firefighters were in attendance for around two-and-a-half hours.
