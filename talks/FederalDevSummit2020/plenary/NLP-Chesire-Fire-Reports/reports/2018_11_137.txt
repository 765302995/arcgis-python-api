 Bonfire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 06/11/2018 - 09:53 Location: Pooltown Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended to a bonfire smouldering on Pooltown Road in Ellesmere Port. A hose reel jet was used to extinguish the fire, which had been left overnight, before crews made sure the area was safe.
