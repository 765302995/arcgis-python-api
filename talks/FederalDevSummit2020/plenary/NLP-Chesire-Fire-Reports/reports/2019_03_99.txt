 Hay bales on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 09/03/2019 - 10:45 Location: Wervin Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood, Chester, Powey Lane, Ellesmere Port and Runcorn Hay bales on fire in ChesterFirefighters attended a fire involving a large quantity of hay bales on a field off Wervin Road, Wervin, Chester. Crews cooled surrounding plant and equipment using two jets whilst allowing the fire to burn. Crews were in attendance for ten hours at the incident. They came back the following morning to monitor small hotspots of smouldering hay and will come back later today to monitor progress.
