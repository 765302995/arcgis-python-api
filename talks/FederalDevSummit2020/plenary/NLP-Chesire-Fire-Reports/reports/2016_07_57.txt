Road traffic collision in Farndon Time of Incident: 20/07/2016 - 17:23 Location: A534/B5130 Farndon crossroads, Farndon, near Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Malpas Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision in Farndon involving three cars. Nobody was trapped in any of the vehicles. Firefighters helped paramedics with casualties and made the area safe.  Police officers controlled traffic and the air ambulance and land ambulances took casualties to hospital. The fire and rescue crews left the scene at around 19:20.
