Outbuilding fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 24/08/2016 - 01:22 Location: Thorn Road, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one from Runcorn Two fire engines from Widnes and one fire engine from Runcorn attended an outbuilding fire in Runcorn. Crews used four breathing apparatus, one main jet and two hose reel jets to put out the fire.
