 Shipping container fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/08/2018 - 20:33 Location: Station Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a fire in a shipping container on a disused timber yard on Station Road, Warrington. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet, breaking in gear and a thermal imaging camera to locate the fire and extinguish it. Crews were at the scene for one hour and 20 minutes.
