Bonfire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/12/2017 - 17:51 Location: Wharfe Lane, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port used a hose reel jet to extinguish a large pile of rubbish which had been deliberately set on fire. Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
