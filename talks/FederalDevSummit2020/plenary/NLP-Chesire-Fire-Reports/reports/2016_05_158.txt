Building fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 04/05/2016 - 13:10 Location: Malbank Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Alsager Cooking left unattended was the cause of a fire in a ground floor flat. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke from the property.  Never leave cooking unattended - follow our safety advice
