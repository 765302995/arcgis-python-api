 Log burner fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 27/10/2018 - 19:58 Location: Rookery Road, Tilston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas and Chester Crews attended to reports of a log burner on fire at a house on Rookery Road in Tilston. with smoke spreading to the neighbouring attic.  On arrival, firefighters removed the log burner from the hearth and checked both attics with a thermal imaging camera.   Firefighters deemed the temperature in the attic satisfactory and checked over the properties to make sure they were safe.
