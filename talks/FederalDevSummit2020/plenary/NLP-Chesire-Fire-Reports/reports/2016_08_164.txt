Man rescued from water in Nantwich Time of Incident: 06/08/2016 - 06:53 Location: Welsh Row, Nantwich Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington, Crewe and Chester,the water incident unit from Warrington and the rope rescue unit from Knutsford Water rescue firefighters rescued a man from water in Nantwich. Two crew members went into the River Weaver and brought the casualty safely to the riverbank. Police were also in attendance.
