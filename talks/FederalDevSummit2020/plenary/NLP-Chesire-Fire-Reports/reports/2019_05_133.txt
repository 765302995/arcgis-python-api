 Tumble dryer fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 13/05/2019 - 15:42 Location: Tilstone Close, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Northwich and one from Winsford Shortly before 3.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a house in Tilstone Close, Northwich. On arrival the crews found a tumble dryer smouldering in what was a smoke logged kitchen. They isolated the gas and electricity supplies and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. Once the fire was out firefighters cleared the smoke from the house with a large fan. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half. For tumble dryer fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/tumble-dryer-fire-safety.
