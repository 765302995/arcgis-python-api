Boiler fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 01/02/2015 - 10:28 Location: Chester Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Firefighters were called to a fire involving a boiler in Ellesmere Port. Access was gained to the building and the small fire was confined to the boiler which was isolated and stripped by the crews.
