 Bungalow fire in Henbury Time of Incident: 28/06/2018 - 15:13 Location: Chelford Road, Henbury Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield, Bollington and Wilmslow along with the Hydraulic Platform from Chester Firefighters attended to a bungalow fire on Chelford Road in Henbury.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews used a hose reel and a thermal imaging camera to help extinguish the fire in one of the bedrooms at the back of the property.  Crews then tackled the fire which had spread to the roof of the bungalow.  The people inside the bungalow were safely removed.  Firefighters were in attendance for seven hours and 30 minutes.
