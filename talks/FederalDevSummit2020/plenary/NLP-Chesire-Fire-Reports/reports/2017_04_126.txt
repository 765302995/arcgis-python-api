Conservatory fire in Elton, Chester Time of Incident: 04/04/2017 - 17:40 Location: The Paddock, Elton, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Curtains were on fire inside the conservatory of a property on The Paddock in Elton, Chester. Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish the fire. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.
