 Grill pan fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/04/2019 - 11:56 Location: Stoke Gardens, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a grill pan at an address in Stoke Gardens, Ellesmere Port. The fire was out on arrival and a woman believed to have been suffering from the effects of slight smoke inhalation was assessed at the scene. She was handed over to the care of paramedics. For grill pan fire safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/grills.
