 Shrub fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/04/2019 - 20:30 Location: Booths Hill Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Fire crews were called to Booths Hill Road, Warrington, after a report of shrub on fire. Crews used a fire extinguisher to put out the blaze which had been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire, please contact Cheshire Police.
