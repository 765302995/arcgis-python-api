Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/02/2017 - 22:14 Location: Denbigh Court, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Denbigh Court, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that there was a car on fire. They used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. if you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
