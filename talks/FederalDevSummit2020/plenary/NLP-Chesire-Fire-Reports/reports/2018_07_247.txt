 Car fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 04/07/2018 - 15:36 Location: Biddulph Road, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Fire crews were called to Biddulph Road, Congleton, after a report of a car fire. Crews found the car well alight on arrival and the crew, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
