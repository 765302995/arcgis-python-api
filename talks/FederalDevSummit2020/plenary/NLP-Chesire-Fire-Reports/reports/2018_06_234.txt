 Fire in wooded area in Halton Time of Incident: 05/06/2018 - 10:57 Location: Daresbury Firs, Halton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 10.55am firefighters were called to a report of a small fire in a wooded area at Daresbury Firs in Halton. The crew used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
