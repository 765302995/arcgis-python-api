 Car fire in Chester Time of Incident: 26/01/2019 - 20:02 Location: Hoole Road, Chester. Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Chester and Ellesmere Port used one hose reel jet and one breathing apparatus to extinguish a saloon car which was well alight on Hoole Road in Chester.  After extinguishing the fire the team checked the vehicle for any hotspots and carried out an assessment on an adjacent property to ensure there was no damage to the building.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately one hour.
