 Fire at a derelict building in Crewe Time of Incident: 09/05/2019 - 18:39 Location: Electricity Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Shortly after 6.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a derelict building in Electricity Street, Crewe. On arrival the crews discovered that it was a small fire involving a pile of branches and wood. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire in the building measuring approximately 50 metres by 50 metres. They then turned over the affected materials and damped them down to make them safe. Firefighters also removed a plastic side panel to help ventilate the building. They were in attendance for more than an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
