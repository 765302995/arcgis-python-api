Building fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/09/2016 - 23:06 Location: Harlech Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port  Firefighters were called to a building fire in Ellesmere Port with reports of people inside the building. Crews used a large fan to ventilate the building and one person was treated by the North West Ambulance Service. All persons were accounted for. The fire is believed to have been started by a heater and is believed to be accidental.
