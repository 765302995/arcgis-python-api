Skip fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 20/03/2016 - 06:10 Location: John Nicholas Crescent, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off John Nicholas Crescent, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that there was a smouldering fire in skip. Crews dealt with the incident that had not impacted on any buildings.
