 Small vehicle fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 13/08/2019 - 23:12 Location: Broughton Road, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly after 11pm firefighters from Crewe were called to reports of a car fire on Broughton Road.  On arrival at the address the team found that the fire had been extinguished by a local resident.  The crew used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots and a hose reel jet to cool the vehicle and prevent the fire from reigniting.  Firefighters were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 485302.
