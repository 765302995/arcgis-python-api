 Fire at industrial unit in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/11/2018 - 09:31 Location: Winwick Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Just after 9.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire at an industrial unit on Winwick Road in Warrington. The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the small outbuilding fire and were in attendance for more than half an hour. The fire may have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
