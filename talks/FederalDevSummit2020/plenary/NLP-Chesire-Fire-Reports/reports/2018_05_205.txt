 Small kitchen fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 02/05/2018 - 16:57 Location: Marple Crescent, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe were called to reports of a kitchen fire on Marple Crescent. On arrival at the scene the team found that the fire involved plastic which had been left on top of a cooker. The fire had been extinguished by the occupants using a dry powder extinguisher. Crews used a thermal imaging camera to ensure that the kitchen was safe. One person suffered minor smoke inhalation, he was treated by firefighters at the scene.
