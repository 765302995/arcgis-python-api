 Electrical fire at domestic property in Nantwich Time of Incident: 11/11/2019 - 18:24 Location: Jubilee Gardens, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Nantwich Shortly before 6.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a smell of burning coming from an electrical socket at a domestic property in Jubilee Gardens, Nantwich.  The fire involving a fused spur was out on arrival.  Firefighters monitored temperatures of the wall the socket was in using a thermal imaging camera.  Once they were satisfied that the property was safe they left the scene.  They were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.
