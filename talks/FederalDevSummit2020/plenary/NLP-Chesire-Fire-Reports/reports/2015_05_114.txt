Collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 09/05/2015 - 18:52 Location: Welsh Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines from Chester and Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Welsh Road, Ellesmere Port.  When they were arrived crews found that the incident involved two cars. Three people were trapped. Crews used special cutting equipment to free the three people who were then passed to the care of the ambulance service.
