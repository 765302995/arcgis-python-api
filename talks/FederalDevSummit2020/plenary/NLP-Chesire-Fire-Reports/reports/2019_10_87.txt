 Small fire in a kitchen in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/10/2019 - 13:31 Location: Hargreaves Court, Halton View, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 1.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a domestic property in Hargreaves Court, Halton View, Widnes.  On arrival the crew discovered that there had been a small fire in the kitchen caused by plastic having been left on top of a cooker.  The fire was out on arrival.  Firefighters administered oxygen to an occupant who was suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation.  They were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
