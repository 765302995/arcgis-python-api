Domestic flooding in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/12/2015 - 00:43 Location: Corbet Avenue, Warrington, WA2 8BW Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a property in Warrington where a gas boiler was leaking a large amount of water.  When entering the property crews saw that the kitchen was flooded. They ensured that the electricity had been isolated then assisted the owners to clean up. A health and safety assessment was also undertaken.
