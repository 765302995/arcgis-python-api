Cooking fumes activate fire alarm in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/12/2015 - 08:57 Location: Central Way, Bewsey and Whitecross, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a property in Warrington following a report of smoke alarms activating.  The alarm had been activated by cooking fumes from burnt food. The fire crew used a large fan to ventilate the property.
