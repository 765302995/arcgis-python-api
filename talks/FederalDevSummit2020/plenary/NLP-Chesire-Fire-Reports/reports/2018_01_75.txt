 House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 12/01/2018 - 17:03 Location: Furber Street, Crewe Attendance: Two pumps attended from Crewe. One from Nantwich, Alsager, Middlewich and Sandbach. Fire crews are currently in attendance at a house fire in Furber Street in Crewe. The road has been closed and neighbouring properties are being evacuated as a precaution.  Firefighters are using six breathing apparatus and three hose reel jets and are making a search of the property.  Update 17:58: Fire crews are using a 7metre ladder to tackle the fire.  Update 21:15: The fire has now been extinguished. Crews remain at the scene to dampen down and are monitoring hotspots within the property.
