Car set alight on a driveway in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/12/2015 - 19:51 Location: Hanley Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A car was well alight on a driveway, close to a house and an electrical sub-station.  Firefighters used two hose reel jets to tackle the fire.  The house and the electrical sub-station were not affected by the fire.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call 0800 555 111.
