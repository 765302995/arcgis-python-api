 Rescue of a person in Chester Time of Incident: 23/05/2018 - 11:04 Location: Highfield Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly after 11am firefighters from Chester were called to reports that an elderly woman was trapped  at an address on Highfield Road. Crews attended the scene and found that the woman had fallen and as was trapped in a shower cubicle. The team dismantled the cubicle and the woman was passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service. Firefighters were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes.
