Kitchen on fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 30/03/2016 - 05:57 Location: Congleton Road, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach, one from Alsager and one from Crewe attended Firefighters attended a fire involving a fridge in a kitchen of a property and used two hose reels to extinguish it. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property.
