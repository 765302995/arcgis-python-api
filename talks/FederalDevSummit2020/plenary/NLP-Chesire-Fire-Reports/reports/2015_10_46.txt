Car collides with a house Time of Incident: 21/10/2015 - 20:36 Location: Riseley Street, Macclesfield Attendance: Firefighters from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a car that had collided with a house on Riseley Street, Macclesfield.  When they arrived crews confirmed that there was an incident but there was no action from firefighters required. The incident was left in the hands of the Police.
