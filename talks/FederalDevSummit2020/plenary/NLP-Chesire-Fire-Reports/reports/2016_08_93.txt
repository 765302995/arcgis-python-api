Caravan fire in field in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 15/08/2016 - 21:50 Location: Bomish Lane, quarter of a mile from Jodrel Bank, Lower Withington, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Holmes Chapel and Bollington, and one appliance from Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called to reports of a large fire which could be seen from Jodrel Bank. On arrival, crews found a fire in a field involving a caravan, which had spread to a nearby oak tree. Crews used used one hose reel jet to put the fire out and continued to dampen the area down afterwards.
