Collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/12/2015 - 19:55 Location: Moor Lane, Widnes. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Widnes and Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Moor Lane, Widnes.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two vehicles. A small van was on its side and there was a man trapped inside.  Crews used special equipment to free the man who was then passed to the care of the ambulance service.
