 Bush on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/09/2018 - 20:23 Location: Factory Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 8.25pm firefighters were called to a report of a bush being on fire on a footpath in Widnes. On arrival at the Factory Lane footpath the crew discovered a small bush alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for more than 20 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
