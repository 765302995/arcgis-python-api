Flat fire in Chester Time of Incident: 01/06/2017 - 22:51 Location: Egerton Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a flat in Chester after a report of a fire in a kitchen. The fire, which had been caused by a pizza box being left on a hot plate, was out on arrival. Crews, from Chester and Powey Lane, used a positive pressure ventilation fan to clear the smoke from the flat. Crews were detained for about 30 minutes.
