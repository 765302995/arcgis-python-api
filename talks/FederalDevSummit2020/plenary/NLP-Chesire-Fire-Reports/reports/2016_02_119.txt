Light fitting fire in Widnes store Time of Incident: 31/01/2016 - 17:11 Location: Widnes Shopping Park, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A Widnes store was closed to the public as firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a light fitting. A thermal imaging camera was used to make sure there was no further danger of fire and the incident was handed over to the store manager.
