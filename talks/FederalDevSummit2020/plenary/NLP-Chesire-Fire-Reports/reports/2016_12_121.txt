Cylinder fire in Bostock Time of Incident: 07/12/2016 - 14:33 Location: London Road, Bostock, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford and two from Northwich Crews have been tackling a fire involving an acetylene cylinder. A cordon was set up and four fire engines had to attend to provide adequate water supplies. A ground monitor was set up to play water onto the cylinder to cool it and crews regularly monitored its temperature using a thermal imaging camera.
