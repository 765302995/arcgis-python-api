 Car fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 18/12/2018 - 21:29 Location: Ollershaw Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 9.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on a driveway on Ollershaw Lane in Northwich. On arrival the crew found a saloon car well alight near to a house. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The crew then made sure that the car and the area surrounding it were safe. No-one was injured by the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour-and-a-half. Police also attended. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 262672, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
