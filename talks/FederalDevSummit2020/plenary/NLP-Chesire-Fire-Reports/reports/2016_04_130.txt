Collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/04/2016 - 19:08 Location: Knutsford Road in Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Stockton Heath and Warrington Firefighters were called to a collision on Knutsford Road in Warrington.  When they arrived crews confirmed that there was nobody trapped. However, they did assist paramedics with caring for a casualty at the scene before they were taken to hospital.
