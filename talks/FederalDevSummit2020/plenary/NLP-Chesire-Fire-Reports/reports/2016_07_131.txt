Smouldering compost heap in Congleton Time of Incident: 08/07/2016 - 15:12 Location: Hilary Avenue, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton Firefighters were called to reports of a wood chippings on an allotment which were very hot and giving off smoke. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the chippings which were smouldering atop a compost heap.
