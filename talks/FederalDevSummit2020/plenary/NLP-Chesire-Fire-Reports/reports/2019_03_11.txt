 Chimney fire in Over Peover Time of Incident: 27/03/2019 - 19:27 Location: Stocks Lane, Over Peover. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called to a chimney fire at an address in Stocks Lane in Over Peover. On arrival the fire had already been extinguished using sand. Crews checked the first floor and attic with a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. They then emptied the log burner and opened the chimney inspection panels to check for fire spread and confirmed there had been light smoke logging in the loft space. They checked the adjacent property to ensure fire had not spread. Crews then used a ladder to monitor the chimney stack and pots with a thermal imaging camera.
