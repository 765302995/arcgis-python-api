Smoke alarm alerts occupier to kitchen fire in Chester Time of Incident: 27/03/2015 - 16:29 Location: Rowan Place, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester A fire involving a kitchen of a semi detached property in Chester required firefighters to attend and use one hose reel to extinguish it. All persons were accounted for and a large fan was used to remove smoke from the property. Smoke alarms were fitted in the property and did alert the occupier to the fire.
