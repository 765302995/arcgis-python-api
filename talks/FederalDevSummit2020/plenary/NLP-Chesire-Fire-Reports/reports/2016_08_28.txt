Single storey workshop on fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 25/08/2016 - 13:33 Location: Free Green Lane, Lower Peover, Knutsford Attendance: Four fire engines from Knutsford, Northwich, Holmes Chapel and Winsford and one appliance from Nantwich  Firefighters tackled a fire which had broken out inside a single storey workshop. On arrival, crews found the workshop to be well alight and used a main jet to put out the fire, whilst using one hose reel jet to protect a nearby diesel storage tank.  Crews were able to bring the fire under control before using two hose reel jets to dampen down the fire.
