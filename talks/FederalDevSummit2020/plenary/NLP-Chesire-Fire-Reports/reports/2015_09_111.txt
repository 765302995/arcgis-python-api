Canal boat on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 09/09/2015 - 16:08 Location: Oak Lane, Kerridge, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Knutsford A large residential canal boat on fire required firefighters to attend. The boat was extinguished with a hose reel. All persons were accounted for and the gas supply was isolated by the crew. A cordon was put in place while the incident was dealt with.
