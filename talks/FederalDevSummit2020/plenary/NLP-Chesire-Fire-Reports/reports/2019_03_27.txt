 Skip fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 24/03/2019 - 02:21 Location: Picton Avenue, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters from Runcorn used a hose reel jet to extinguish a skip fire on Picton Avenue.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately 30 minutes.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 355264.
