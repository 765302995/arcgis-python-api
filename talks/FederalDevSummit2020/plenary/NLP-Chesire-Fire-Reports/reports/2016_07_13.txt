Road traffic collision in Chester Time of Incident: 27/07/2016 - 18:10 Location: A5117, Little Stanney, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Two vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision on the A5117 on the slip road going towards the M53.  Nobody was trapped, but firefighters assessed two children who were in the rear of one of the vehicles, whilst waiting for paramedics. Fire crews then made the vehicles safe.  The incident was then handed over to Cheshire Police.
