Bonfire close to railway line in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 11/11/2016 - 19:00 Location: Peover Lane, Snelson, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a large fire close to the railway line around a mile from Chelford railway station. The fire was put out using a hose reel jet.
