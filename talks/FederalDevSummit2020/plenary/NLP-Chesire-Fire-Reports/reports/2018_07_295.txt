 Fire involving a metal container toilet block in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 20:54 Location: Oil Sites Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 8.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a metal container and grass at Stanlow Refinery on Oil Sites Road in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered a metal container toilet block well alight. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call police on 101.
