 Lorry fire on the A55 Time of Incident: 16/02/2018 - 15:00 Location: A55 westbound, between the Sainsbury's roundabout and the Posthouse roundabout Attendance: One fire engine from Chester, two from Ellesmere Port and one from North Wales Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to the A55 westbound after a lorry caught fire. On arrival crews found the lorry well alight and used two breathing apparatus and one main jet to tackle the fire. During the incident both carriageways of the A55 were shut to traffic. Crews were at the scene for an hour and a half.
