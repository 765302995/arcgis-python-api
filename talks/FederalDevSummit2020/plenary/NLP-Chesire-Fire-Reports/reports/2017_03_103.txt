Road traffic collision on M6 southbound at junction 18 Time of Incident: 15/03/2017 - 12:10 Location: M6 southbound junction 18 Holmes Chapel Attendance: Fire engines from Holmes Chapel, Sandbach, Congleton, Winsford and Crewe Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision on the M6 southbound near to junction 18 at Holmes Chapel. The collision involved six vehicles with one person trapped. Full road closure, both north and south, were put in place by police while the incident was dealt with. The Air Ambulance was also in attendance. Six people were treated by paramedics, assisted by firefighters, and one casualty was extricated from one of the vehicles and handed over to the care of paramedics.
