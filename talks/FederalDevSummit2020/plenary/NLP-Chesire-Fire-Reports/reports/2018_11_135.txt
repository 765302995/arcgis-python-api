 Small refuse fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 06/11/2018 - 15:41 Location: Esk Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At around 3.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Esk Road in Winsford. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire involving refuse. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
