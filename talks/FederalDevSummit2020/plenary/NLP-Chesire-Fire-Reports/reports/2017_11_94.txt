Fire in house in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 08/11/2017 - 07:54 Location: Holly Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews tackled a fire in a terraced house on Holly Road, Ellesmere Port. Crews, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire which was in the first floor of the property.  Police have attended the incident.
