 Building fire in Stockton Heath Time of Incident: 08/01/2018 - 18:45 Location: Victoria Road, Stockton Heath Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath, one from Lymm and one from Warrington.  At 18.45 firefighters from Stockton Heath, Lymm and Warrington were called to reports of a domestic building fire on Victoria Road, Stockton Heath. On arrival crews found the fire involved a cooker which had been left unattended. Crews used a hose reel jet, two breathing apparatus and a large fan to extinguish the fire. Two casualties were suffering from smoke inhalation and crews administered oxygen to one of the casualties. Both casualties were left in the care of North West Ambulance Service.
