Man rescued from a property fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/03/2015 - 01:31 Location: Cantilever Gardens, Station road, Warrington Attendance: Three fire engines from Warrington and Stockton Heath Three fire crews were sent to an incident where a neighbour could smell smoke coming from a neighbouring property, where one man was believed to be inside.  On arrival at the incident, one male occupant was escorted from the property by firefighters.  Firefighters then used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  A large fan was then used to clear the smoke from the property.
