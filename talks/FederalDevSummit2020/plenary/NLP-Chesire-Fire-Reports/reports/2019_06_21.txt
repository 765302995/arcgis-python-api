 Shrubbery fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 25/06/2019 - 19:37 Location: Highlands Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews were called to Highlands Road, Runcorn, after a report of a fire in a shrubbery. Crews used a fire extinguisher and a bucket of water to put out the fire.
