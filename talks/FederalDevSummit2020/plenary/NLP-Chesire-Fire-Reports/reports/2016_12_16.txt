Warrington wheelie bin fire Time of Incident: 28/12/2016 - 17:22 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A fire in a wheelie bin is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and are urging anyone with any information about this, or similar incidents, to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111. Arson is a crime that can cost lives.
