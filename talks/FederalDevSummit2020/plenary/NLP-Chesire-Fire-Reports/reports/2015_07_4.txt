Cylinder leaking on hard shoulder of M6 south near J18 Time of Incident: 30/07/2015 - 07:38 Location: M6 southbound, close to junction 18 Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Firefighters dealt with a leaking propane cylinder on the hard shoulder of the M6 near to junction 18. The crew turned off the cylinder and removed it from the site.
