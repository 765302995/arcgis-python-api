Overturned car in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/10/2016 - 17:55 Location: Kestrel Lane, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Birchwood Fire crews responded to a report of a collision on Kestrel Lane, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that there was a car that had overturned. Nobody was trapped. The driver had left the scene. Crews made sure that the vehicle was safe before leaving.
