Small fire in Walton Time of Incident: 14/05/2017 - 14:03 Location: Runcorn Road, Walton, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath, one from Runcorn and one from Penketh An electric fire radiated heat which caused a small fire in the lounge of a Runcorn Road property. A person had been reported as being inside the house but she was outside. Firefighters treated her for a slight burn to her hand. The fire was out on arrival.
