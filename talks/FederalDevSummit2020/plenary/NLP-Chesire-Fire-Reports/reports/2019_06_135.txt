 Crash in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/06/2019 - 07:53 Location: Stockport Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm and one from Stockton Heath Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision between two cars on Stockport Road, Warrington. Firefighters made the vehicles safe and then administered first aid to a casualty before the arrival of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for 40 minutes.
