 Peat bog fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 17/05/2018 - 13:23 Location: Battery Lane, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a peat bog on fire on land off Battery Lane, Wilmslow. The area affected was approximately one metre by two metres. Crews used a backpack extinguisher and water to put out the fire.
