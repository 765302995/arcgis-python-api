Collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 03/02/2016 - 19:20 Location: Waterloo Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one fire engine from Runcorn and the Major Rescue Unit from Winsford. Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Waterloo Road, Widnes.  When they arrived crews found that there was a male trapped underneath a bus. Crews used special equipment to free the casualty who was then passed to the care of the ambulance service.
