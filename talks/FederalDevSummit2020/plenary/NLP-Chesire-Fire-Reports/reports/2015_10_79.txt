Collision in Nantwich Time of Incident: 17/10/2015 - 15:48 Location: Salt Meadows, Nantwich. Attendance: A fire engine from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Salt Meadows, Nantwich.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved one car. There was nobody trapped but a man was given first aid at the scene by a First Responder before being passed to the care of the ambulance service.
