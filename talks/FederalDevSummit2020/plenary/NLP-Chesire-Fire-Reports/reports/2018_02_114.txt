 Van on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/02/2018 - 06:31 Location: Birchwood Way, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters were called to reports of a van on fire at Birchwood Way, Warrington. Crews attended and discovered a small fire in the engine compartment of a Transit van. They used one hose reel and breathing apparatus to put the fire out.
