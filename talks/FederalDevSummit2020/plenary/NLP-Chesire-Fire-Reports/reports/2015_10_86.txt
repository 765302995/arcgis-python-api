Loft fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/10/2015 - 16:21 Location: Limetree Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and a fire engine plus hydraulic platform from Stockton Heath. Two fire engines and a hydraulic platform were mobilised to a loft fire at a Warrington property.  Firefighters used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to extinguish the blaze.
