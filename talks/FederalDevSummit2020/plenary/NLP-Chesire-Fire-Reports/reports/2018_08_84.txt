 Smoke coming from a manhole in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/08/2018 - 10:30 Location: Chester Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 10.30am firefighters were called to reports of smoke coming from a manhole on Chester Road in Ellesmere Port. The crew removed the manhole cover, set up a cordon around the area as a safety precaution and liaised with the electricity provider. The electricity provider attended and the incident was handed over to them. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
