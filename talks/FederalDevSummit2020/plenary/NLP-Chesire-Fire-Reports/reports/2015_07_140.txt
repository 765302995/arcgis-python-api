Collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 11/07/2015 - 14:37 Location: Wychwood Park in Weston, Crewe. Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe and Nantwich Firefighters were called to a report of a collision near Wychwood Park in Weston, Crewe.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved three cars, one of which was on its side. There was nobody trapped but crews helped the ambulance service treat some of the casualties at the scene.
