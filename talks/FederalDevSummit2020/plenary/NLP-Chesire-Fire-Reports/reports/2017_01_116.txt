Outbuilding fire in Pott Shrigley Time of Incident: 05/01/2017 - 07:00 Location: Shrigley Road, Pott Shrigley Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield A fire in a timber outbuilding was caused by sparks from a wood burning stove. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and damped the area down.
