 Chimney fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 22/01/2018 - 18:13 Location: Middlewich Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Fire crews were called to a chimney fire on Middlewich Road, Northwich. Crews had to remove several bricks from the chimney breast to access the fire and then extinguish it.  For more information about chimney safety visit: Chimney fire safety advice
