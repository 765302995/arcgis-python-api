Dog stuck in bars of a cage in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/01/2016 - 21:19 Location: Beech House, Wilderspool Causeway, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A small dog got its head and shoulders stuck in the bars of a cage and needed the help of firefighters to get out. The rapid door opener tool was used to release the dog due to the animal suffering from distress.
