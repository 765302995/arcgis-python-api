 Fire in the open in Knutsford Time of Incident: 30/06/2019 - 15:52 Location: Tabley Road, Tabley, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly before 4pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Tabley, Knutsford. On arrival at Tabley Road the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning. Firefighters reduced the size of the fire for safety reasons and allowed the remaining fire to burn. They were in attendance for around an hour.
