Motorbike fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/11/2017 - 06:13 Location: Broome Court, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one fire engine from Widnes attended Crews from Runcorn and Widnes used one hose reel jet to extinguish a motorbike fire on Broome Court in Runcorn. The cause of the fire is currently unknown and anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
