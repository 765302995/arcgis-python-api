Fuel leak in Shavington Time of Incident: 08/11/2017 - 19:40 Location: A500, Shavington, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe. One fire engine from Nantwich. A lorry was leaking fuel from its tank onto the A500 in Shavington. The road was closed while crews used equipment including a foam line to contain the fuel and highways cleared the fuel from the carriageway.
