 Tree cuttings on fire in Woolstanwood Time of Incident: 01/06/2018 - 21:49 Location: Lodgefields Drive, Woolstanwood. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to Lodgefields Road in Woolstanwood following reports of a small amount of tree cuttings on fire. Crews used water to extinguish the fire.
