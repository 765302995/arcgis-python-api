Loft fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/12/2017 - 20:16 Location: Birchfield Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes. One fire engine from Penketh. Fire crews attended a fire in the loft of a house in Birchfield Road in Widnes. Crews used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet while extinguishing the flames. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots, and used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
