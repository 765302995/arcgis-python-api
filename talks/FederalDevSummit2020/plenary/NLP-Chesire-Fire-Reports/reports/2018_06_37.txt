 Bonfire in wooded area in a Warrington park Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 22:02 Location: Wigshaw Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a wooded area at Culcheth Linear Park on Wigshaw Lane, Warrington. The crew extinguished the bonfire using a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 10 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call police on 101.
