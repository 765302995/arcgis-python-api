Two car road traffic collision in Prestbury Time of Incident: 29/03/2016 - 19:45 Location: Bonis Hall Lane, Prestbury, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington and one from Macclesfield One casualty was trapped in a vehicle involved in a road traffic collision.  Firefighters extricated the casualty, a woman, from the car and handed her nto the care of paramedics. They made the scene safe.
