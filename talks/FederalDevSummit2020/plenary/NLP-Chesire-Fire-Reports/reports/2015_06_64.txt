Shed on fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 19/06/2015 - 11:54 Location: Blythe Avenue, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and one from Macclesfield. A shed which was in fire in the back garden of a property in Congleton was extinguished with a covering water jet and a hose reel. One woman suffered smoke inhalation and needed the help of paramedics at this incident. Power to the shed was isolated and a large fan was used to remove smoke logging.
