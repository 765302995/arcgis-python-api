 Digger fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 10/07/2018 - 14:57 Location: Cappers Lane, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines attended - from Alsagar and Crewe At around 2.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a digger on Cappers Lane in Betchton, Sandbach. On arrival crews found the digger well alight. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and a hose reel jet to cool them. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
