Industrial building fire in Elton, Chester Time of Incident: 25/10/2017 - 20:10 Location: Ash Road, Elton, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane, two from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Firefighters attended a fire at an industrial premises involving a chemical container. The container split and the chemical leaked in a building measuring twenty metres by thirty metres. They used two breathing apparatus, a hose reel, covering jet, 45 mm strike jet, two gas tight suits and soda ash. The incident was resolved by approximately 3:19am.
