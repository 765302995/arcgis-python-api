Toaster fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 09/05/2015 - 20:44 Location: Winsford-Bypass, Winsford. Attendance: Fire engines from Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Weaver Lodge on Winsford-Bypass, Winsford.  Upon arrival crews confirmed a fire involving toaster which was out on arrival. A full evacuation of the premise was completed and crews used a positive pressure ventilation fan to deal with the smoke that had filled the building.
