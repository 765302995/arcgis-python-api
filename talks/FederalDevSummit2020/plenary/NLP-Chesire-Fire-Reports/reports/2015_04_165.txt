Car fire on M6 north, junctions 21 to 21A Time of Incident: 10/04/2015 - 16:49 Location: M6 north, junction 21 to 21a Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A fire in the engine compartment of a car was put out by firefighters.  The car was on the hard shoulder of the northbound M6.  The crew used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
