 Car on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/11/2019 - 00:22 Location: Blackburne Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 12.20am firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on a domestic property's driveway in Blackburne Avenue, Widnes.  On arrival the crew found the vehicle well alight.  Two firefighters, one of whom was wearing breathing apparatus, each used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  They then damped the car down to make it safe.  Firefighters were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.  The police also attended.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 573960, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
