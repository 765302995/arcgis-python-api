Fire in the open in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 23/07/2016 - 02:07 Location: Westminster Park, George Street, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Books and aerosol cans were set on fire in an Ellesmere Port park. Firefighters used hand held extinguishers to put out the fire and asked police to attend as it is suspected the fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
