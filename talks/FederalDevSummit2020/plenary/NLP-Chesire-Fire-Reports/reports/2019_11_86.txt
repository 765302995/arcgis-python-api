 Smoke alarm in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/11/2019 - 12:08 Location: Hambleton Close, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh. Firefighters were called to smoke issuing from a ground floor flat in Hambleton Close, Widnes and a smoke alarm activating. Crews gained access to the flat and found the smoke was caused by a pan on the stove. They used a large fan to disperse the smoke. No one was in the flat at the time.
