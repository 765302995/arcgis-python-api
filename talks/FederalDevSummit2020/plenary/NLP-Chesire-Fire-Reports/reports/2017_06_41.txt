Unattended cooking fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/06/2017 - 22:51 Location: Summer Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn, one from Widnes and one from Penketh A fire caused by unattended cooking which was out on the arrival of the crews, required them to use a large fan to remove smoke from the property. One woman was escorted from the house and handed to the care of paramedics.   Cooking safety advice
