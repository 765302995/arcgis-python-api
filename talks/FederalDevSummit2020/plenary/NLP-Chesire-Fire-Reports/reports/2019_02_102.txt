 Flatbed truck fire M6 Knutsford Time of Incident: 12/02/2019 - 16:45 Location: Slip road, Knutsford Services, M6 northbound. Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel and one from Lymm Firefighters were called to a flatbed truck on fire off the slip road to Knutsford Services off the M6 northbound. The fire, which was in the engine compartment, was out on arrival. Crews used a hose reel to damp down and cool the area. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
