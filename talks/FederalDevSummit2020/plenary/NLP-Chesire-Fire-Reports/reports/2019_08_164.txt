 Woman rescued from car in flooded water, Wilmslow Time of Incident: 01/08/2019 - 18:52 Location: A555, Wilmslow Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Poynton, one from Cheadle and one from Wilmslow along with the support appliance from Warrington and the rope rescue unit from Lymm Firefighters attended to a woman trapped inside a vehicle which was deep in flooded water on the A555 in Wilmslow.  Crews used a ladder and a short extension ladder to rescue the woman.  They were in attendance for approximately forty-five minutes.
