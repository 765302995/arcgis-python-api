Chimney fire in Rode Heath. Time of Incident: 27/02/2016 - 00:37 Location: Millmead, Rode Heath. Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Millmead, Rode Heath.  When they arrived crews found that there was a blaze involving a chimney. Hand held extinguishers were used to put the fire out.
