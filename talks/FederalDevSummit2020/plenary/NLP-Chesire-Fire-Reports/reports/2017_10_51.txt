Small rubbish fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/10/2017 - 18:33 Location: Winwick Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to a small rubbish fire on Winwick Road, Warrington. Crews put out the fire, which was underneath a slide in a play park, using a fire extinguisher. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information then please call Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
