Electrical substation fire in Delamere Time of Incident: 15/03/2017 - 15:36 Location: Station Road, Delamere Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A fire was reported in an electrical substation on a camp site in Delamere. Firefighters gained access to the substation after the supply had been isolated and tackled the fire. They then damped the area down and removed the roof of the substation.
