Child with hand trapped in a bench Time of Incident: 25/03/2015 - 17:30 Location: Grange Lane, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended. Firefighters were called to assist with a child who had got their finger stuck in a park bench.  The incident took place off Grange Lane in Winsford. Crews used special cutting equipment to free the child who was then taken to the hospital to be checked over.
