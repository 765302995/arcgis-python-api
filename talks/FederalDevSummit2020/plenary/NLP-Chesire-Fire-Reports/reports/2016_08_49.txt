Microwave fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 22/08/2016 - 08:33 Location: Kingsway House Caldwell Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to reports of a smell of burning on the first floor in a building in Widnes. On arrival, they searched all floors and discovered the smell had been caused by a small microwave fire which was out when they arrived. Crews made sure the building was safe and there were no further signs of fire.
