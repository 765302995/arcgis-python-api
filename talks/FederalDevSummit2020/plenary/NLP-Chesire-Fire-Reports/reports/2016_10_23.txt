Hospital fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 26/10/2016 - 19:19 Location: Victoria Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: Fire engines from Macclesfield and Bollington Firefighters responded to a report of a fire on a ward of Macclesfield Hospital on Victoria Road, Macclesfield.  When they arrived crews wearing breathing apparatus tackled a fire on the ground floor. Other fire crews checked the first floor to ensure that the fire had not spread.  20 patients were evacuated and two members of staff were checked for the effects of inhaling smoke.  An investigation to determine the cause of the fire is now underway.
