Small rubbish fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/09/2017 - 18:18 Location: Longshaw Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a small rubbish fire on Longshaw Street, Warrington.  A small amount of rubbish had been set alight deliberately. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  If you have any information about the incident please contact Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
