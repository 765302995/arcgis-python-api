 Bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 12/04/2019 - 20:51 Location: Finsbury Walk, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Shortly after 8.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Winsford. On arrival at Finsbury Walk firefighters, who had put out another fire in the open minutes earlier a short distance away on Forest Road, discovered that the fire involved a bin. They extinguished the bin fire at Finsbury Walk with a hose reel jet. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
