Car fire in Hough, Crewe Time of Incident: 07/10/2015 - 22:57 Location: Pit Lane, Hough, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe A fire involving a single car on the driveway of a property was put out by firefighters. The crew, one firefighter wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. An investigation into the cause is being carried out.  If you saw anything suspicious or have any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
