Flat fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 30/12/2015 - 03:12 Location: Poplars Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a fire in a first floor bedroom at a property in Poplars Avenue, Warrington.  Fire crews used four breathing apparatus and two hose reel jets to extinguish the blaze and ventilated the property using a large fan.
