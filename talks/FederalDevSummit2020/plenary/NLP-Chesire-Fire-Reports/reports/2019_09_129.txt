 Hay fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/09/2019 - 10:48 Location: Upton rocks Avenue, widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to a field on fire near a garden centre near Upton Rocks Avenue in Widnes. Crews used beaters to put out the fire which had affected 20 square metres of hay. They then damped down a small pile of hay.
