 Bonfire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 16/10/2018 - 19:00 Location: Wilmslow Old Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Crews in Macclesfield attended to a large unattended bonfire well alight in a field on Wilmslow Old Road in Macclesfield.  The bonfire measured 20m by 20m and a light portable pump was used to extinguish it. Firefighters then dampened down the area to make sure it was safe.
