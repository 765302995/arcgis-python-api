 One-vehicle collision in Neston Time of Incident: 25/07/2019 - 12:05 Location: High Street, Neston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Shortly after 12 noon firefighters were called to reports of a one-vehicle collision in High Street, Neston, at the junction with Raby Road. On arrival the crew discovered that the collision involved a saloon car and that a casualty was in the care of paramedics. Firefighters made the vehicle safe and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
