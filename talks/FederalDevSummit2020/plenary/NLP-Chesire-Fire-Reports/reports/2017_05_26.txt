Fire at Crewe landfill site Time of Incident: 26/05/2017 - 20:51 Location: Maw Green Road, Crewe Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe, Sandbach and Middlewich along with the high volume pump and supporting fire engine from Congleton Four fire engines and a high volume pump attended a fire at a landfill site on Maw Green Road.  The. fire involved a large quantity of waste, believed to be around 200 tonnes. Firefighters used two hose reel jets, covering jets and one main jet to bring the blaze under control.  Once the fire was out a thermal imaging camera was used to check there were no hotspots. The crews were at the scene for three hours and fifteen minutes.
