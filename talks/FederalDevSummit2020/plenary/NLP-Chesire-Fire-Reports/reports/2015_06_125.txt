Fence fire affecting house in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 09/06/2015 - 22:07 Location: Parkfield Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester A fence fire affected the rear of a house in Ellesmere Port.  Firefighters used two hose reel jets to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately.  Police were informed and were asked to attend.
