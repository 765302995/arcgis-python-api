 Collision in Knutsford Time of Incident: 03/09/2018 - 22:44 Location: Halliwells Brow, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm, Stockton Heath and Altrincham Firefighters were called to a collision on Halliwells Brow in High Legh, Knutsford. The collision involved two saloon cars. Two casualties were removed from one of the vehicles, and a further casualty was treated as a precaution by the paramedics.
