 Rubble fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/11/2018 - 20:09 Location: Rhyl Street, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes. One fire engine from Penketh. Firefighters were called to a deep-seated fire involving rubble at a industrial premises in Rhyl Street, Widnes. Crews used a hose reel to tackle the fire and a thermal imaging camera to check the fire hadn't spread to the building. They then used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
