 Fire involving a lorry in Woodbank, near Chester Time of Incident: 11/11/2019 - 07:07 Location: A5117, Woodbank, near Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 7am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a lorry on the A5117 in Woodbank, near Chester.  On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved the vehicle's front near side brakes.  Firefighters fended off one of the road's lanes as they worked at the scene.  They extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
