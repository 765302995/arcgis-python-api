 Large bonfire in Northwich Time of Incident: 21/12/2018 - 18:09 Location: Middlewich Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 6.10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Middlewich Road in Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered a large bonfire that was not affecting nearby properties but was out of control. Located on a large field, the fire measured approximately 15 metres by 15 metres by three metres. Firefighters used a main jet and a hose reel jet to fight the fire. The driver of a modified JCD assisted firefighters in spreading out the materials on the bonfire. Once the fire was extinguished firefighters damped down the area to make sure that it was safe. They were in attendance for more than three hours.
