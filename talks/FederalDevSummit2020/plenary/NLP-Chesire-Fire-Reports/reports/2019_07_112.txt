 Bus fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 13/07/2019 - 07:18 Location: Wharton Gardens, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich Fire crews were called to a bus fire on Wharton Gardens, Winsford. Crews used two hose reel jets and a main jet to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
