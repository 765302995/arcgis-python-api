Fire in undergrowth in Chester Time of Incident: 27/08/2015 - 15:35 Location: Chapel Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley Firefighters were called to a fire in undergrowth in Beeston. The area affected was approximately 20 sqm and a water back pack was used to extinguish the fire. A thermal imaging camera was then used to check that the fire was out before the crew left.
