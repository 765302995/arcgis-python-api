 Children's log cabin well alight in Preston Brook Time of Incident: 31/08/2019 - 19:50 Location: Chester Road, Preston Brook Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Runcorn firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a children's log cabin which was well alight at a daycare centre in Preston Brook.  The cause of the fire is being treated as suspicious and anyone with any information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 501556.
