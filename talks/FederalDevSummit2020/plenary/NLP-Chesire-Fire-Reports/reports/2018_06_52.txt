 Grass verge fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 15:21 Location: Runcorn Spur Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters attended to a fire on Runcorn Spur Road in Road following reports of a grass verge well alight.  Crews extinguished the fire using a hose reel jet.  The fire is believed to have been caused by a discarded cigarette.
