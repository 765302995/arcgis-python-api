 Hedgerow fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/09/2019 - 13:40 Location: Farm Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Crews attended to a fire involving a hedgerow on Farm Lane in Macclesfield.   Two hose reels were used to extinguish the fire before crews then dampened down the area.
