Fire on Lockitt Street in Crewe. Time of Incident: 14/01/2017 - 22:25 Location: Lockitt Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish a fire in the open on Lockitt Street in Crewe. The fire involved a quantity of sheeting and copper wire.  Police also attended this incident.
