 Van fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/07/2018 - 12:51 Location: M62 Eastbound Attendance: Two fire engines attended - One from Northwich and one from Penketh. Van fire in WarringtonShortly before 1pm firefighters were called to reports of a van fire on the M62 Eastbound in Warrington. Crews attended the scene and discovered a prison van which was well alight. All of the occupants of the van were accounted for. The team used breathing apparatus and hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. Crews remain in attendance and are currently damping down. There are currently severe delays in the area as a result of the incident.
