 Electrical cable in Macclesfield field Time of Incident: 08/05/2019 - 11:10 Location: Hocker Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters attended following reports of an electrical cable falling onto a field near to Hocker Lane in Macclesfield.  The crew located the cable, which was reported to be sparking, set up a safety cordon around it and stood by with an extinguisher.  Firefighters monitored the area until the electrical supplier attended.
