 Bed fire in Widnes field Time of Incident: 08/09/2019 - 22:40 Location: Clapgate Crescent, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At 10.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in Clapgate Crescent, Widnes. On arrival the crew found a small bed well alight in the middle of a field. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for less than 10 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
