 Fire in a field in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/06/2018 - 15:39 Location: Warrington Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm Firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a field measuring approximately 10 by 50 meters on Warrington Road in Warrington.  Two beaters, a hose reel and one stack drag were used by fire crews to extinguish the fire.  The cause of the fire is believed to be controlled burning.
