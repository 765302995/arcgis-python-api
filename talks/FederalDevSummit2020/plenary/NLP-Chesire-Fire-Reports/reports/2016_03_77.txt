Kitchen fire in Chester flat Time of Incident: 16/03/2016 - 23:54 Location: Christleton Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester A fire in the kitchen of a second floor flat was attended by firefighters and ambulance.The fire was out but the occupier, a man, was suffering from smoke inhalation and was given first aid. Smoke alarms were fitted and alerted the man to the fire.  Smoke alarms can give people those extra vital minutes to get away from a fire. Make sure you have them fitted and test them regularly - Smoke alarms
