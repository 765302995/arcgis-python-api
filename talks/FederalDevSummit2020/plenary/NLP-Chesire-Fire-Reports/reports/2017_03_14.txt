Torn branches and rubbish on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 27/03/2017 - 18:59 Location: Meadow Grove, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Torn branches and rubbish were set on fire in what is believed to have been an act of arson. Firefighters used hand held extinguishers to put the fire out and asked for police to attend.  Arson is a crime. Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
