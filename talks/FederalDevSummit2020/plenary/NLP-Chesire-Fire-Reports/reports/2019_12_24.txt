 Vehicle fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 05/12/2019 - 23:31 Location: Merlin Avenue, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel Fire crews attended to a van well alight on Merlin Avenue in Knutsford.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, two hose reels were used to put out the flames.  Crews checked over the vehicle using the thermal imaging camera and dampened down the area.
