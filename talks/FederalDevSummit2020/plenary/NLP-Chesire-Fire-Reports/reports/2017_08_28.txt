Skip fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/08/2017 - 19:38 Location: Davies Close, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to a skip fire on Davies Close, Widnes. Crews use a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
