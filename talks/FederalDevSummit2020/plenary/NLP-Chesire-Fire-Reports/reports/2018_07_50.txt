 Small fire in the open in a park in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/07/2018 - 19:12 Location: Queens Park Drive, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe used water to extinguish a small pile of rubbish which had been deliberately set on fire in Queens Park, Crewe. The team were detained for approximately 20 minutes. Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
