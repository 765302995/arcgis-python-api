Fork lift truck fire in Rudheath Time of Incident: 11/02/2016 - 13:06 Location: King Street,Rudheath Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Winsford A fire involving a fork lift truck caused sprinklers to activate at Morrison's Distribution Centre in Rudheath.  Staff were evacuated from the produce building, where the fork lift truck had caught fire, and the sprinkler system activated.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and a high pressure fan to clear smoke. The fire was caused by an electrical fault on the gas-powered fork lift truck.  Crews were on site for almost three hours.
