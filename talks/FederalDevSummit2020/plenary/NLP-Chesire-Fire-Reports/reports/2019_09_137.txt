 Industrial frying range fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/09/2019 - 11:20 Location: Moorfield Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh. One fire engine attended from Widnes. Firefighters were called to a chip shop in Moorfield Road, Widnes following reports of an industrial frying range on fire. The fire was out on arrival but smoke was still coming from the roof tiles.   Crews investigated the building to check for fire spread. They found slight smouldering in the loft space and removed tiles in the area affected.  Crews wearing breathing apparatus then used a hose reel to damp down and used a thermal imaging camera to monitor the temperature.  The building, including a first floor flat, was evacuated while firefighters worked at the scene.
