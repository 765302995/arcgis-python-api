 Couch fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/08/2018 - 19:08 Location: Near to Astmoor Industrial Estate, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a small amount of refuse on fire including a couch near to Astmoor Industrial Estate in Runcorn.
