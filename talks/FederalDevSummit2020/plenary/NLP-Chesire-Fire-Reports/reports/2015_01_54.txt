Woman trapped after collision in Sandbach Time of Incident: 17/01/2015 - 12:48 Location: Church Lane, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and one from Crewe. One person was trapped and two people injured after a road traffic collision involving two cars in Sandbach. The crews released the woman who was trapped and handed her into the care of paramedics who were already treating the other two people injured. Air Ambulance attended the scene and two dogs were also released from the car.
