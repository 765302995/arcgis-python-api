 Fire in a field in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 18:51 Location: Admirals Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 6.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a field off Admirals Road in Birchwood, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved dead wood from a bonfire. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
