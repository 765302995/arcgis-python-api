Tumble dryer fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/04/2016 - 21:45 Location: St Austell Close, Runcorn Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn, Frodsham and Widnes Firefighters were called to a fire involving a tumble dryer at a property and used one hose reel to bring the fire under control. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property. All persons were accounted for at this incident. Tumble dryer fire safety advice
