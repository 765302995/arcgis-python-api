Smoke from grill pan in Neston Time of Incident: 04/10/2017 - 12:19 Location: Dawn Close Ness, Neston Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Merseyside Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to smoke in a property in Neston with persons reported. On arrival crews found smoke coming from a grill pan and  used a large fan to clear the property. All persons were accounted for. Read our cooking safety advice
