Cooker fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/06/2015 - 20:05 Location: Verve Place, Mersey Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A fire crew from Warrington was sent to a property because there was a cooker fire.  The fire was already out when the fire crew arrived.  Firefighters used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.   Kitchen fire safety
