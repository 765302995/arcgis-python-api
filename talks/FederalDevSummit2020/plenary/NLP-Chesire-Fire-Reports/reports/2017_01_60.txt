Fire in commercial building in Runcorn Time of Incident: 16/01/2017 - 00:22 Location: Astmoor Road, Runcorn Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn and Widnes attended Smoke coming from a building required firefighters to attend. On arrival the crews found a fire which was believed to involve machinery and used a hose reel to extinguish it. Panels from the machinery were removed to check for hot spots before the crews left.
