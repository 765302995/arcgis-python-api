Horse in canal in Sandbach Time of Incident: 02/02/2016 - 08:16 Location: Betchton Road, Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and the Rope Rescue Unit from Knutsford Firefighters were called to rescue a horse from a canal in Sandbach. The horse was standing in the water close to the edge of the canal bank on the arrival of the crew who used straps and a mechanical lifting machine to get the horse onto dry land.
