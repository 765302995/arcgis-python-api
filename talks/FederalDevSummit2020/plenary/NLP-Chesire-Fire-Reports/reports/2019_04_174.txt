 Grass cuttings and rubbish fire at a park in Hale Time of Incident: 13/04/2019 - 16:17 Location: Hale Park, High Street, Hale Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly after 4.15pm firefighters were called to a report of fire in the open at a park in High Street, Hale, near Widnes. On arrival at Hale Park the crew found large amounts of grass cuttings and rubbish well alight. The fire affected an area measuring approximately 100 metres by 10 metres. Firefighters used beaters to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around an hour and 20 minutes.
