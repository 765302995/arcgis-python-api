 Rubbish fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 31/03/2019 - 16:59 Location: Wellfield Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to a rubbish fire in a car park on Wellfield Road, Warrington. The fire was extinguished by firefighters using a hose reel jet. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information then please contact Cheshire Police.
