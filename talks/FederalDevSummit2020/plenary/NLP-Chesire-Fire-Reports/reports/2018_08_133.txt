 Grass fire in Hale Time of Incident: 06/08/2018 - 18:13 Location: Church Road, Hale. Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Widnes, one from Runcorn and one from Penketh. Grass fire in HaleFirefighters from Widnes, Runcorn and Penketh attended report of a grass fire on the banks of the river Mersey in Hale. On arrival crews found multiple seats of fire, totalling approximately 200 metres by 100 metres.  The team used one hose reel jet, a light portable pump and water backpacks to extinguish the fire. Crews were in attendance for approximately two hours 45 minutes.
