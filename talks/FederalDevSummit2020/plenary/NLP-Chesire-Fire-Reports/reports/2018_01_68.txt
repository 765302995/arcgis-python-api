 Vehicle fire, Northwich Time of Incident: 13/01/2018 - 21:07 Location: Saxons Lane, Northwich Attendance: One appliance from Northwich Firefighters tackled a Range Rover 4x4 car that was well alight. They used a single hose reel and breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames.  It is believed to have been a deliberate ignition, so anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire police on 101, quoting incident 835 of 13 January 2018.
