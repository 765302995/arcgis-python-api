Bonfire out of control Time of Incident: 18/02/2017 - 17:51 Location: Margaret Court, Widnes. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Margaret Court, Widnes.  When they arrived crews found that a bonfire in the back garden of a property had got out of control and had damaged a fence and the outside of the property.  Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.
