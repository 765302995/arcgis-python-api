 Fire in the open, Checkley Time of Incident: 06/07/2019 - 23:59 Location: Checkley Lane, Checkley Attendance: One fire engine from Audlem Firefighters were called following reports of a fire in a field. It turned out to be a bonfire, measuring around 5m x 5m. They used a hose reel to cool the surrounding area and ensure there was no risk of it spreading. They were in attendance for around an hour and 15 minutes.
