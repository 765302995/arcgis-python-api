Smoke in property in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/09/2016 - 07:40 Location: Moorfield Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called out due to reports of large amounts of smoke in a domestic property. On arrival, crews discovered that the smoke was caused by a pan which had been left unattended. Crews ventilated the property and carried out a health and safety assessment.  Never leave cooking unattended. Practise cooking safely in order to protect your home and your loved ones.
