Smoke in a house in Crewe Time of Incident: 13/12/2017 - 17:41 Location: Electricity Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Light smoke in a bedroom on the first floor of a property required firefighters to attend. All occupants were accounted for and a thermal imaging camera was used to check the property. It is believed an overheated lamp in a reptile tank caused the smoke and the crew damped down the area with water before leaving.
