 Light fitting burning at Chester hospital Time of Incident: 08/05/2019 - 12:45 Location: Countess of Chester Hospital, Liverpool Road Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Firefighters attended following reports of a light fitting burning at the Countess of Chester Hospital on Liverpool Road.  The fire was out on arrival.  Crews safely removed the light fitting and made sure that the fire had not spread.  The burning is believed to have been caused by an overheated ballast.
