 Rubbish fire, Lymm Time of Incident: 06/12/2019 - 18:50 Location: Maltmans Road, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm Firefighters were called to deal with a small fire in the open at a skate park. They arrived to find a quantity of rubbish had been set alight, and they used a water backpack to extinguish the flames.
