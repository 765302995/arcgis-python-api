Deliberate car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/05/2016 - 01:17 Location: Nansen Close, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A car fire was believed to have been started deliberately in Great Sankey. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 38 of 5 May.
