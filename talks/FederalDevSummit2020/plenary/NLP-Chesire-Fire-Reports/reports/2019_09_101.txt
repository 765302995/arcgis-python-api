 Bedroom fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 11/09/2019 - 14:45 Location: Kinnerley Road, Whitby, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port At 2.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a house on Kinnerley Road in Whitby, Ellesmere Port. On arrival crews found a fire in one of the bedrooms. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Once the fire was out the bedroom was damped down to make it safe and a large fan was used to clear smoke from the property. Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
