 Vehicle fire, Northwich Time of Incident: 14/04/2018 - 19:49 Location: Davenham Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters responded to reports of a saloon car on fire. It was out on arrival, but crews dampened down with a single hose reel jet. The incident is believed to have been a deliberate ignition, anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 36379.
