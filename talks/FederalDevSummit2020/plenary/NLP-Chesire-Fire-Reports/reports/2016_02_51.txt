Tumble dryer fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 18/02/2016 - 19:20 Location: Burford Close, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle(Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) A tumble dryer was the cause of a fire at a property in Wilmslow.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They then ventilated the property using a high pressure fan. All occupants were out of the house but the crews asked for an ambulance to attend as a precaution as two people were suffering slight smoke inhalation. Smoke alarms were fitted at the property and alerted the occupants to the fire.   Please follow our safety advice - tumble dryers
