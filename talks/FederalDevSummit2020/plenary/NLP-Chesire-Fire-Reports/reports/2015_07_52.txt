Road traffic collision in Frodsham Time of Incident: 23/07/2015 - 02:16 Location: A56, Chester Road/Clifton Road, Frodsham Attendance: Fire engines from Frodsham and Runcorn Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision with one man trapped. The crew used cutting equipment to get the casualty out and to the care of Paramedics. Police also attended this incident.
