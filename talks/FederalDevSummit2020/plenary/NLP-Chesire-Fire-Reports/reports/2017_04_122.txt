Grill pan fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 05/04/2017 - 00:25 Location: Swanlow Avenue, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich A casualty was treated by paramedics for smoke inhalation after a fire in a grill pan at a flat on Swanlow Avenue. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.    Please follow our grill pan safety advice.
