 Two people trapped after road traffic collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 21/02/2019 - 18:09 Location: Crowders Lane, Lach Dennis, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich and one from Northwich Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision on Crowders Lane, Lach Dennis, Northwich, where two people were trapped. The incident involved two cars and there was one casualty in each car. Crews removed the roof of both vehicles using hydraulic cutting equipment before they were handed into the care of paramedics. Fire crews were at the scene for an hour and 10 minutes.
