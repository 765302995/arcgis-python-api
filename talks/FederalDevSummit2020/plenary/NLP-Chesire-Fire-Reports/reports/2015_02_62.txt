Fire in Chester Time of Incident: 14/02/2015 - 11:41 Location: Foregate Street, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Crews were called to a report of a fire on Foregate Street, Chester.  When they arrived the found flames coming out of a manhole cover in the road. The Police closed the road while waiting for the arrival of Scottish Power. Fire crews remained at the scene to ensure it was safe while the electricity company dealt with the incident.
