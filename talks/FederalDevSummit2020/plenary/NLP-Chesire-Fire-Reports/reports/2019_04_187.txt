 Recycling wagon brakes overheat on A556 Time of Incident: 12/04/2019 - 07:32 Location: A556, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Northwich and one from Winsford Fire crews tended to the brakes of a recycling wagon that had overheated on the A556 near Northwich.  When the wagon was relocated to a lay-by firefighters continued to cool off the rear side brake with a hose reel jet until it was safe.
