 Person trapped following one vehicle collision near Nantwich Time of Incident: 04/02/2019 - 12:21 Location: A51, Reaseheath, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich At 12:21pm firefighters were dispatched to reports of a person trapped following a one vehicle collision on the A51 near to Reaseheath college.  Crews from Nantwich attended the scene and found that one person was medically trapped in the vehicle.  Police and ambulance also attended the scene and the road was closed as a precautionary measure.  The team then used specialist electronic cutting equipment to release the casualty; they were then passed to the care of paramedics.  The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately half-an-hour.
