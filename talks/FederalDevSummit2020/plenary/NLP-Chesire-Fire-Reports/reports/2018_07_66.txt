 Fire outside domestic property in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/07/2018 - 18:32 Location: Marshall Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 6.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire outside a domestic property in Marshall Avenue, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered it was a case of controlled burning. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
