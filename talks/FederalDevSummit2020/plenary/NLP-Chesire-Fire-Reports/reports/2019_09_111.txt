 Two wheelie bin fires in Widnes Time of Incident: 10/09/2019 - 00:52 Location: Avondale Drive, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews attended to two wheelie bins well alight on Avondale Drive in Widnes.  A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire which is believed to have been caused by a discarded cigarette.
