 Grass fire in Great Sutton Time of Incident: 25/07/2018 - 19:43 Location: Mill Lane, Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port used a hose reel jet and beaters to extinguish a grass fire on Mill Lane, Great Sutton. The team were detained for approximately 20 minutes. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
