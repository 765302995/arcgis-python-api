Injured swan rescued in Chester Time of Incident: 18/10/2015 - 13:40 Location: Whitchurch Road, Broxton, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and the Animal Rescue Unit and a fire engine from Knutsford An inflatable raft was used to rescue an injured swan on a lake in Chester.  Firefighters from the Animal Rescue Unit worked closely with the RSPCA to remove the animal from the lake.
