 Entangled bird rescued from lake in Knutsford Time of Incident: 13/04/2019 - 11:51 Location: Wash Lane, Allostock, Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Northwich and one from Lymm At around 11.50am firefighters were called to a report of a grebe having become entangled in the middle of a lake on Wash Lane in Allostock, Knutsford. A technical rescue specialist liaised with an RSPCA representative in attendance about the best way to rescue the bird. The specialist then retrieved the grebe using a rescue raft and water safety equipment and handed the bird over to the care of the RSPCA representative. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour.
