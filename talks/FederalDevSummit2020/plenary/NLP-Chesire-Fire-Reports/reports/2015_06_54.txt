Tent fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/06/2015 - 10:40 Location: Knutsford Road, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on the banks of the Mersey of Knutsford Road, Warrington.  When crews arrived they found that it involved a tent. Crews used hand held extinguishers to put the fire out.
