Man rescued from Nantwich flat fire Time of Incident: 11/09/2017 - 00:12 Location: Hospital Street, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Nantwich and one from Crewe One man was rescued by firefighters after a fire in the kitchen of a first floor flat. Firefighters, two in breathing apparatus, set up a covering jet while crews in breathing apparatus carried out a further search of the flat. Cordons were set up around the building while the flat was cleared of smoke and the fire confirmed to be out. The roof space was also checked using a thermal imaging camera to make sure there was no fire spread.  This fire was caused by cooking left unattended. Please always follow our kitchen safety advice.
