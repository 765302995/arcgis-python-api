Car on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 11/10/2016 - 21:27 Location: Highlands Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to damp down a car with a hose reel after it was involved in a fire. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately and police attended. If anyone knows anything about who may have started the fire they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
