 Cooking fumes activate a smoke alarm at a flat in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 07/09/2018 - 17:44 Location: Ridge View, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At around 5.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a flat in Ridge View, Macclesfield. On arrival the crew found that the alarm had been activated by cooking fumes. Firefighters used a large high pressure fan to clear the fumes from the property and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. For cooking safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
