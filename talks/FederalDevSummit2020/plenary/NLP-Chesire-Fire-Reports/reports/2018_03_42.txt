 Bin fire in Neston Time of Incident: 24/03/2018 - 22:44 Location: The Parade, Neston. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Firefighters were called to a bin fire in The Parade, Neston. The fire was out on arrival, but a second bin was discovered alight. Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the fire.
