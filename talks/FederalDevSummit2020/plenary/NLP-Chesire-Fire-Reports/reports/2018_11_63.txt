 Small fire in a garden in Crewe Time of Incident: 18/11/2018 - 21:25 Location: Derrington Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 9.25pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the garden of a house on Derrington Avenue in Crewe. The crew used water to extinguish the small fire. Firefighters were in attendance for around half an hour.
