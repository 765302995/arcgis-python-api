Car on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/08/2017 - 21:28 Location: Petersgate, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a well developed fire involving a car in Runcorn. The crew used a hose reel to extinguish the flames and damped down. It is believed that this fire may have been started deliberately. If anyone know anything about who may have started it then please call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
