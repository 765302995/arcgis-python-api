 Gas leak at Widnes Shopping Park Time of Incident: 22/09/2018 - 17:02 Location: Widnes Shopping Park, Widnes. WA87TN Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes, one from Runcorn and one from Warrington Shortly after 5pm fire crews were called to reports of a gas leak at a fast food restaurant at Widnes Shopping Park. The building was evacuated and firefighters isolated the mains gas supply. Crews were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
