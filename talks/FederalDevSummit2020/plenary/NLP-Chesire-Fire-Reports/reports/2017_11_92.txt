One person trapped in a car in Prestbury Time of Incident: 08/11/2017 - 09:58 Location: Bonis Hall Lane, Prestbury. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Fire crews were called to a car crash between three cars on Bonis Hall Lane, Prestbury. Crews extracted one female casualty from one car before handing her into the care of paramedics.
