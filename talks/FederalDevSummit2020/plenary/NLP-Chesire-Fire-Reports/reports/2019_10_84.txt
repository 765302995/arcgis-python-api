 Car on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 18/10/2019 - 04:08 Location: The Boulevard, Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 4am firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire at The Boulevard in Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port.  On arrival the crew found the vehicle well alight.  A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet.  Once the fire was out the crew handed the incident over to the police.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
