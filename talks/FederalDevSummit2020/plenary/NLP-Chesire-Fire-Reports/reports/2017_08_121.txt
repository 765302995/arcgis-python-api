Ducting fire in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 09/08/2017 - 18:45 Location: Victoria Mills, Macclesfield Road, Holmes Chapel Attendance: One fire from Holmes Chapel and one from Congleton attended Fire in ducting at a commercial premises on Macclesfield Road was tackled by firefighters. The crews, using a thermal imaging camera, worked with onsite engineers to find the fire which was causing hot spots along the ducting. Two firefighters in breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to damp the area down, and a high pressure fan to clear smoke.
