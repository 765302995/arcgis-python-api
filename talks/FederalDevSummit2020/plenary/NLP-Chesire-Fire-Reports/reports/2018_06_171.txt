 Rubbish fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 16/06/2018 - 12:54 Location: Melksham Close, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Fire crews attended to a rubbish and grass fire at the back of a property on Melksham Close in Macclesfield.  One hose reel was used to put out the fire which was started accidentally by a disregarded cigarette.
