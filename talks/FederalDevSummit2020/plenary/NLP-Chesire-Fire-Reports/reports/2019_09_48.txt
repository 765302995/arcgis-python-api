 Hay bales on fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 21/09/2019 - 14:43 Location: Massey Brook Lane, Lymm Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Penketh, one from Runcorn and one from Northwich Crews attended to a fire on a field near Massey Brook Lane in Lymm.  Firefighters used beaters and an extended hose reel jet to put out the fire.  The fire was then dampened down using a hose reel and to ensure the smoke was fully dispersed.
