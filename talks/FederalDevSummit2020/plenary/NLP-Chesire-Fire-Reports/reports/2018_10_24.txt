 Road traffic collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 27/10/2018 - 09:57 Location: Northwich Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Fire crews attended to a road traffic collision on Northwich Road in Hartford involving two cars.  Firefighters set up their equipment and removed a casualty from one of the vehicles and handed into the care of paramedics.    They then made the area and vehicle safe.
