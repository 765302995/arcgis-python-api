Small corrosive liquid leak in Widnes Time of Incident: 16/11/2016 - 19:27 Location: Gorsey Lane, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one from Warrington Firefighters were called to investigate a possible chemical leak in Gorsey Lane. The leak was contained in a building. With a covering jet in place, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus went in to try to identify the liquid, which turned out to be a small amount of cleaning fluid. The incident was handed over to the site owners.
