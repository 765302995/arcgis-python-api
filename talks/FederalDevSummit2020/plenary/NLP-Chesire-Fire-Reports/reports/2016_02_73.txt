Two cars set on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 14/02/2016 - 04:28 Location: Bradford Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish a fire involving a car that was parked outside a home on Bradford Road in Winsford.  It is believed that this car was set on fire deliberately.  There was also an attempt to set another car on fire too. Anyone with any information about these two fires, please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
