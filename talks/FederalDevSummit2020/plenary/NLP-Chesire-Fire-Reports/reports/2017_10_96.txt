Small fire in a commercial building in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/10/2017 - 05:34 Location: Bewsey Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh and one from Warrington Fire crews were called to a fire in a commercial building on Bewsey Road, Warrington. Crews found a fire in a light fitting and used a 13.5m ladder, a thermal imaging camera and a powder extinguisher to put out the fire.
