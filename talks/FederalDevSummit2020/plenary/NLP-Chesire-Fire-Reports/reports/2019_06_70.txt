 Fire in a flat in Chester Time of Incident: 16/06/2019 - 06:24 Location: Liverpool Road, Chester. Attendance: Three fire engine attended from Chester, Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane At around 6.30am firefighters from Chester, Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane were called to reports of a flat fire off Liverpool Road in Chester.  On arrival crews discovered a fire involving a light fitting in a ground floor flat.  A full evacuation of the property was undertaken.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used on hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and a thermal imaging camera to check for any hot spots.  Crews then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the building.  Firefighters were detained at the incident for 50 minutes.
