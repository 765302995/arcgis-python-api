Disused brick shed fire in Barnton Time of Incident: 22/02/2017 - 05:17 Location: Runcorn Road, Barnton, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich A blaze in a 4' x 6' brick built shed at a house on Runcorn Road was tackled by firefighters in the early hours. The crews, one firefighter wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
