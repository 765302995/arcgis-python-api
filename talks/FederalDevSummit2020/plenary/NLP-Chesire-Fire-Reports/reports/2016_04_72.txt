Fire outside shop door in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/04/2016 - 17:15 Location: Bridge Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath Newspapers outside a shop door were set on fire. Suspected to be deliberate ignition, firefighters used a water backpack to fully extinguish the fire and a thermal imaging camera to check there were no further hotspots. They gained entry to the shop and investigated to ensure there was no further fire spread.  This is suspected arson and anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
