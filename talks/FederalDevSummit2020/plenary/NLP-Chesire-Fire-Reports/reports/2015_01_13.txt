Overturned HGV on M6 southbound Time of Incident: 28/01/2015 - 11:26 Location: M6 between junctions 19 and 20, southbound Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford There was an incident involving two heavy goods vehicles and one saloon car on the M6 motorway between junctions 19 and 20.  One of the heavy goods vehicles had overturned.  Paramedics attended and dealt with two casualties, but the casualties were not trapped.  Lanes one and two of the motorway were closed by Police and Highways due to the overturned vehicle having shed its load and a fuel spillage.
