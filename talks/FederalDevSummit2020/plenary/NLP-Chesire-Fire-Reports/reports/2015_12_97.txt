Van on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 07/12/2015 - 00:46 Location: Bedford Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a van on fire, well alight, in Bedford Street. The crew, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fire. A small amount of damage was caused to an adjacent property. Police were also asked to attend.
