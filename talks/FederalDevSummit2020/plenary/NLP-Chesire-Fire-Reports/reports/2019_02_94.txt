 Small fire in a cellar in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 14/02/2019 - 12:11 Location: Prestbury Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a small fire involving an electrical box in the cellar of an address in Prestbury Road, Macclesfield. The fire was out on arrival but smoke had spread to the upper floor. The electrics and appliances were isolated by crews and a thermal imaging camera was used to check the temperature of the box.
