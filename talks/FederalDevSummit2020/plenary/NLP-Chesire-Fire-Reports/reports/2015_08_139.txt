Collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/08/2015 - 03:01 Location: Winwick Road, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Winwick Road, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that there was a car on its side. Two people were trapped inside. Firefighters cut them free and they were passed to the care of the ambulance service.
