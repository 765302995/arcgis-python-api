Road traffic collision in Gawsworth Time of Incident: 14/03/2016 - 15:36 Location: Congleton Road, Gawsworth Attendance: One fire engine from Alsager, one from Macclesfield and one from Congleton Firefighters attended a road traffic collision involving two hatchback vehicles. The crew assisted one man from one of the vehicles who was handed to the care of paramedics. Air Ambulance attended to transport the casualty to hospital. The road was closed in both directions from Gawsworth towards Manchester while emergency services were at the scene. The crew used environmental pads to absorb fluid which had leaked from both vehicles.
