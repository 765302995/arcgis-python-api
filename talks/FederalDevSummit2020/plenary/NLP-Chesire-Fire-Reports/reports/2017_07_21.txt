Fence on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 24/07/2017 - 14:06 Location: Widnes Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Fire crews were called to a fence which was on fire on land adjacent to Widnes Road, Widnes. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the which had been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
