 Bungalow fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 04/04/2018 - 16:18 Location: Old Hall Street, Malpas. Attendance: One fire engine from Chester, one from Malpas, one from Audlem and one fire engine from Whitchurch (Shropshire Fire and Rescue Service). Bungalow fire in MalpasFirefighters attended a fire at a disused bungalow on Old Hall Street, Malpas. On arrival crews found the bungalow well alight and crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used three main jets and a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control. Crews were at the scene for four hours and 30 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
