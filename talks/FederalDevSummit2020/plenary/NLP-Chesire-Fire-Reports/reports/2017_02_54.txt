Pallets on fire Winsford Time of Incident: 21/02/2017 - 14:39 Location: Grange Lane, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford A hose reel jet was used by firefighters to put out a fire involving a small quantity of wooden pallets. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101. Arson costs lives.
