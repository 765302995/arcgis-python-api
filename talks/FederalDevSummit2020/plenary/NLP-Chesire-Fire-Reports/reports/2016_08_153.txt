Shed fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/08/2016 - 22:48 Location: Brynn Street, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes A shed in between two houses was on fire in Brynn Street.The occupants of both houses had left their properties to await the arrival of firefighters. The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fire which had also spread to one of the houses. An investigation into the cause of the fire is being carried out.
