Man rescued from flat fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/07/2017 - 22:03 Location: Norland Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes, one from Warrington, one from Lymm, one from Stockton Heath, two from Penketh, one from Warrington and the aerial ladder platform from Lymm. Firefighters at the scene of a flat fire in WidnesFirefighters were called to reports of a building on fire with persons reported inside. On arrival they found a fully developed fire in a top floor flat and one man was rescued.  Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels to extinguish the fire and the remaining flats were searched for hot spots using a thermal imaging camera. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the flats.  One snake which was in the flat at the time needed rescuing and checking, but showed no signs of injury. An investigation into the cause of the fire will take place.
