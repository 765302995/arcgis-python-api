Fire in disused greyhound stadium in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/05/2015 - 10:48 Location: Greyhound Stadium, Stanney Lane, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a fire at the disused greyhound stadium.  The fire was in a stairwell in the building.
