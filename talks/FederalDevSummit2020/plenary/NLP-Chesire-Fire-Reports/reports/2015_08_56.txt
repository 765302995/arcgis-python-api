HGV fire on M6 Time of Incident: 19/08/2015 - 08:19 Location: M6, junction 18 southbound, Holmes Chapel. Attendance: Two fire engines Firefighters extinguished a fire involving a HGV tractor unit on the entry to the slip road of junction 18, M6 southbound.  The vehicles was well alight on arrival of the crews wore breathing apparatus and used two hose reels to extinguish the fire.  The slip road was closed by police while the incident was dealt with but has now re-opened.
