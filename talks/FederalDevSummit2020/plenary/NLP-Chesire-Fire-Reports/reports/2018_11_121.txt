 Small refuse fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 07/11/2018 - 20:08 Location: Howty Close, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington Firefighters from Bollington were called to reports of a fire in the open in Wilmslow. On arrival Crews found a wheelie bin containing a large amount of refuse which was well alight. The team used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to called Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. For more information about wheelie bin safety visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
