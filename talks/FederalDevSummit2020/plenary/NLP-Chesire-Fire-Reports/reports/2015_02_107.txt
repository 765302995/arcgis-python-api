Road traffic collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/02/2015 - 12:04 Location: Bridgewater Grange, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Runcorn Firefighters removed the roof of a car in order to release a woman trapped inside. The car had been involved in a collision with another vehicle. Once released the woman was handed into the care of paramedics at the scene.
