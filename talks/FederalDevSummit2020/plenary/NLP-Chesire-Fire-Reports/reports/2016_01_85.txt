Motorbike on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/01/2016 - 21:46 Location: Higham Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a motorbike on fire and used one hose reel to extinguish it. Police also attended as the fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  If anyone has any information regarding this fire they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
