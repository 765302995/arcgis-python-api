Flat fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 18/04/2015 - 15:09 Location: Waterbank Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford A flat fire in Northwich was caused by a tumble dryer.  The flat was well alight on arrival of the crews who wore breathing apparatus and two hose reels to extinguish the blaze. A thorough check of the property was then carried out using a thermal imaging camera. The crews were at the scene for approximately one hour 45 minutes.
