Small chimney fire extinguished in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 11/12/2017 - 18:34 Location: Birtles Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At 6.35pm on Monday, 11 December fire crews attended to reports of a small chimney fire on Birtles Road in Macclesfield. Firefighters extinguished the fire and there were no injuries.
