Tree fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 11/05/2017 - 19:58 Location: Bransdale Way, Macclesfield. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Bollington Fireighters were called to a report of a fire on Bransdale Way, Macclesfield.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved some conifer trees close to a shed.  Hose reel jets were used to put the fire out. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Constabulary on 101.
