 Small fire in the open in Northwich Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 19:20 Location: Earles Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 7.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Earles Lane, Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire. They used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
