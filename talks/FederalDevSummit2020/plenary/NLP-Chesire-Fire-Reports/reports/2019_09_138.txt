 Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/09/2019 - 20:52 Location: Plemston Court, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a car fire in Plemston Court, Ellesmere Port. Crews used breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the fire. It is believed to have been started deliberately. Police were in attendance.
