 Wheelie bin fire in Chester Time of Incident: 26/05/2018 - 02:09 Location: Lorne Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Fire crews were called to tackle a wheelie bin fire which had spread to the front door of a property on Lorne Street, Chester. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information then please call Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
