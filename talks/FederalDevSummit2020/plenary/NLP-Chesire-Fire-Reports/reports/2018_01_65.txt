 House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 14/01/2018 - 11:11 Location: Readesdale Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe. One fire engine from Sandbach and Nantwich. One fire engine from Lymm along with the aerial platform. Relief fire engines from Congleton, Winsford and Middlewich. House fire on Readesdale Avenue in Crewe Fire crews attended a fire at a semi-detached house in Readesdale Avenue in Crewe. Firefighters used four hose reels, a covering jet and a hydraulic platform to bring the fire under control. One casualty suffering from shock was assessed by paramedics.
