 Flat fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 03/03/2019 - 17:19 Location: Baronia Place, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a fire in a ground floor flat on Baronia Place, Nantwich. The crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets and a thermal imaging camera to put out the fire. One casualty was treated by the crew suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation and they were later taken to hospital by paramedics. Crews were at the scene for three hours and 20 minutes.
