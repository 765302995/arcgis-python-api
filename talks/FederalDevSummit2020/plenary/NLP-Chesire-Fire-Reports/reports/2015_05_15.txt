Petrol spillage in Crewe Time of Incident: 27/05/2015 - 12:19 Location: vernon way, crewe. cw12dd Attendance: No attendance data recorded Firefighters have been clearing up a petrol spillage in Tesco car park in Crewe.  The petrol had leaked through a car's petrol tank.  The crews moved the car out of the incident area and covered the affected ground in sand.  A hose reel jet was used to clear the sand-covered fuel and the owner was advised not to start the car and to arrange recovery of the vehicle.  The petrol station will remain closed until the car has been removed.
