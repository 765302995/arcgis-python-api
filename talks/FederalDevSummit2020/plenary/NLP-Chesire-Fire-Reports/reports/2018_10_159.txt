 Fire at a housing construction site in Poynton Time of Incident: 01/10/2018 - 12:11 Location: The Coppice, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton At around 12.10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at The Coppice in Poynton. On arrival at the housing construction site the crew discovered it was a case of controlled burning. Firefighters extinguished the fire using a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 25 minutes.
