 Vehicle fire at a warehouse in Crewe Time of Incident: 06/08/2019 - 11:00 Location: Weston Road, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At around 11am firefighters were called to reports of a vehicle fire at a warehouse on Weston Road, Crewe.  On arrival the crew discovered the fire involved the battery compartment of a folk lift truck which was loaded in the back of a HGV.  Firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
