Van fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 03/10/2017 - 08:37 Location: Adlington Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a van fire, which contained gas cannisters, on Adlington Road, Wilmslow. On arrival crews found the van was well alight and crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets and two ground monitor jets to fight the fire. Police set up a cordon around the incident whilst it was ongoing. One male casualty was treated at the scene for burns and handed into the care of paramedics. A fire investigation has started into the incident.
