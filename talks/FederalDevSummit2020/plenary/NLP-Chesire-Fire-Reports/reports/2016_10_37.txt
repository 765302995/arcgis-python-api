Garage fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 24/10/2016 - 22:54 Location: Cloverdale Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a garage, with people inside. On arrival, crews found the fire was out and all people were accounted for. They then moved all of the contents of the garage outside before checking for further hot spots of fire using a thermal imaging camera. Any hot spots found were dampened using a hose reel jet.
