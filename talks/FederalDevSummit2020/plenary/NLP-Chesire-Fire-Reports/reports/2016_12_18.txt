Crewe kitchen fire Time of Incident: 28/12/2016 - 14:31 Location: Moreton Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Alsager Firefighters tackled a fire in the kitchen of a semi-detached house on Moreton Road. The fire, which was put out by the crews, two wearing breathing apparatus using a hose reel jet, started after an item had been left on a cooker. A high pressure fan was also used to clear smoke from the property.  Always follow our cooking safety advice.
