 Dishwasher fire in Chester Time of Incident: 23/11/2019 - 11:35 Location: Snowdon Crescent, Lache, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly after 11.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a dishwasher at a domestic property in Snowdon Crescent, Lache, Chester.  The fire was out on arrival.  Firefighters isolated the electricity supply to the dishwasher and were in attendance for less than 15 minutes.  For dishwasher fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/washing-machine-and-dishwasher-fire-safety.
