Industrial bin fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 31/10/2017 - 23:42 Location: Prosperity Way, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Firefighters were called to reports of a fire on an industrial estate  in Middlewich. On arrival they found the fire involved an industrial bin, which was well alight.  One hose reel was used to extinguish the fire and an investigation has been launched into the cause of the fire. If anyone has any information about how this fire may have started please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
