 Small fire in the open in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 15/01/2019 - 19:34 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 7.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire on a field on Thornton Road in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire in the open. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
