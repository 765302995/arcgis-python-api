Macclesfield kitchen fire Time of Incident: 21/05/2016 - 10:29 Location: Tytherington Drive, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield A chopping board left on the hob was the cause of a kitchen fire. The occupier left the property, closing all the doors which prevented fire spread, and called firefighters. The crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A high pressure fan cleared smoke from the kitchen.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice and never leave items on the hob.
