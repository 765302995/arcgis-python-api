Gate fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/12/2015 - 01:09 Location: Sandy Moor Lane, Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Sandy Moor Lane, Runcorn.  When they arrived they found some gates to a house on fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that may help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
