Electric blanket fire in Norley Time of Incident: 04/01/2017 - 23:38 Location: Fingerpost Lane, Norley, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich An electric blanket had caught fire but was out on the arrival of firefighters. Crews used a high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property and carried out a home safety check. A man at the house was taken to hospital by ambulance as a precaution.
