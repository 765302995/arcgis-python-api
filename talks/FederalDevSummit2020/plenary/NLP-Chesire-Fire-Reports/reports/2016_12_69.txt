Cooker fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 18/12/2016 - 21:09 Location: Prestbury Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a cooker fire that was out but had left a strong smell of burning. An electric oven had completely burnt through its surrounding cupboard. The crew dismantled the cupboard, detached the wiring and removed the oven to outside the house. They then cut away all the burnt wood and took that outside. A thermal imaging camera was used to check there were no further hotspots that could ignite.
