 Shed fires in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/06/2019 - 20:38 Location: Linnet Grove, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood and one from Warrington Fire crews were called to a fire involving two sheds on Linnet Grove, Warrington. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fires. Crews were in attendance for an hour.
