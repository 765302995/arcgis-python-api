 Refuse fire at a golf club in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/07/2018 - 22:06 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 10.05pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at St Michaels Jubilee Golf Course on Dundalk Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved rubbish. Firefighters extinguished the refuse fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around an hour.
