 Bungalow fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 23/07/2019 - 11:14 Location: Cumberland Street, Macclesfield Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Macclesfield, one from Poynton and one from Wilmslow Firefighters attended to a fire at a bungalow on Cumberland Street in Macclesfield.  The fire was located in the bedroom with crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, using a hose reel to extinguish the fire.  The gas and electricity was isolated and firefighters did a thorough search of the home to ensure the fire was fully extinguished.  A fan was used to disperse of the smoke.  As a precaution crews searched the loft and properties close by to ensure the fire hadn't spread.  There are believed to be no injuries.
