 Ongoing - Animal rescue in Wrrington Time of Incident: 06/08/2019 - 13:52 Location: Grange Drive, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Warrington and Lymm, along with the animal Rescue unit from Lymm Update - 4pm  Shortly before 2pm firefighters were called to reports of a horse trapped in shallow water in Warrington.  On arrival at the scene crews discovered a 10-year-old horse, weighing approximately half a tonne, stuck in Whittle Brook.  A vet was been called to the scene and the horse was partially sedated.  The team are currently using rope rescue straps and a tractor to lift the horse and release it from the water.
