Van fire on M56 westbound Time of Incident: 01/10/2015 - 14:00 Location: M56 westbound between junctions 14 and 15, near Hapsford Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters have been tackling a van fire on the hard shoulder of the M56 westbound between junctions 14 and 15 near Hapsford. The crew, one firefighter wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the flames and then damped the vehicle down. Police closed lane one as the blaze was being tackled.
