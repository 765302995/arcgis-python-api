Railway embankment fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 23/05/2015 - 20:08 Location: Off Newcastle Road in Crewe. Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on a railway embankment in Crewe.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire of about 4msq on the embankment. They used beaters anf backpack extinguishers to put the fire out.
