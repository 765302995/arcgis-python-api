Electrical fire at restaurant in Lymm Time of Incident: 06/04/2016 - 08:19 Location: Flavours, The Cross, Warrington Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington and Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a fire at Flavours restaurant in Lymm.  Smoke was also coming from a fuse box on the ground floor of the commercial property. A large fan was being used to remove smoke from the premises.  Crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, isolated gas and electricity supplies and fought the fire. They then had to await the arrival of the electricity company to fully isolate the supply before they could completely extinguish the fire.  The fire involved the mains electrical unit in the premises and smoke damage was caused to the building.
