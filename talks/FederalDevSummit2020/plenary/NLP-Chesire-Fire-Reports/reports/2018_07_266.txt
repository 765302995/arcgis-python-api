 Electrical fault in Nantwich Time of Incident: 03/07/2018 - 00:33 Location: Talbot Street, Stepeley, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters from Nantwich were called to reports of an incident on Talbot Street in the Stapeley area of Nantwich. On arrival crews found that an electrical fault had melted within a streetlight, causing the electric to trip. The team used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots before putting a small cordon in place and alerting the local authority. The team were in attendance for approximately 45 minutes.
