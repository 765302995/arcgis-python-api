Man trapped after collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 18/04/2015 - 13:35 Location: Brook Street/The Silk Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield One man was medically trapped after a collision involving two cars in Macclesfield.  The man was released by firefighters and handed into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for approximately one hour.
