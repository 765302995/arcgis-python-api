Lamb rescued in Sutton Time of Incident: 23/05/2017 - 14:06 Location: Bollinhead Lane, Sutton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield and an animal rescue team from Bollington Firefighters and Cheshire Fire and Rescue Service's specialist animal rescue teams were called to a farm near Macclesfield after a lamb fell down a drain. The farmer used a JCB to expose the pipe and fire crews used chimney rods to coax the lamb out. Crews were at the scene for approximately two hours.
