Skip fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 23/11/2016 - 19:14 Location: Bridge Lane in Frodsham. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Frodsham Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Bridge Lane in Frodsham.  When they arrived crews found that there were two skips on fire as well as a pile of wood.  Crews used hose reel jets to put the fire out.  An investigation has now been launched to find the cause. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
