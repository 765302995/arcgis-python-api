House fire in Chester Time of Incident: 05/01/2017 - 17:04 Location: Cotton Lane, Chester. Attendance: Fire engines from Chester and Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Cotton Lane, Chester.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving some logs next to a fire. Crews wearing breathing apparatus removed the logs and used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.
