 Large refuse fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/11/2018 - 21:58 Location: Parklands View, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Firefighters from Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane attended reports of a building fire on Parklands View. On arrival at the scene crews found that there was no building fire, but there was a large amount of refuse in the area that was well alight. The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
