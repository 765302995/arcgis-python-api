Kitchen fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/06/2016 - 18:38 Location: Broughton Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe, one from Nantwich and one from Alsager Two hose reel jets and one covering jet were used to extinguish a kitchen fire. Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the property.   A thermal imaging camera was used to check for fire, to see if it had spread to the bedrooms and loft space.   Police closed the road outside the property.  Firefighters gave oxygen therapy to one male occupant.
