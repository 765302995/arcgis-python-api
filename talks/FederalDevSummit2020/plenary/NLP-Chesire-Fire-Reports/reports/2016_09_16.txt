Chimney fire in Tarvin, Chester Time of Incident: 26/09/2016 - 20:20 Location: Broomheath Lane, Tarvin, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley Firefighters dealt with a chimney fire in a bedroom of a property in Tarvin.  Firefighters extinguished the fire before cutting away the floor boards and skirting boards to expose the affected area of the bedroom.  Chimney fire safety advice
