 Container on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/06/2018 - 16:50 Location: Bewsey Farm Cclose, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. One fire engine from Widnes. Firefighters were called to a container on fire in Bewsey Farm Close, Warrington. Crews used cutting equipment and spreaders to gain access to the fire and then used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the fire.  They then damped down the contents of the container.
