 Collision in Betchton Time of Incident: 21/08/2019 - 16:09 Location: Newcastle Road, Betchton Attendance: Three fire engines attended - One from Crewe, one from Sandbach and one from Congleton Crews attended to a collision on Newcastle Road in Betchton involving a car and a motorbike.  The motorcyclist, a man believed to be in his 70's, was handed into the care of paramedics.  Fire crews made the vehicle safe.
