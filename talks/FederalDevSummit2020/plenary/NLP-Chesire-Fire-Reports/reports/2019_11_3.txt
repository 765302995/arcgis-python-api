 Two-vehicle collision in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 28/11/2019 - 15:30 Location: Buckingham Road, Wilmslow Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Wilmslow and one from Stockport in Greater Manchester At 3.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Buckingham Road in Wilmslow.  On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved two vehicles and that no-one was trapped inside either of them.  Firefighters made the vehicles safe and assessed a female casualty.  They also assisted police with traffic management.  Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
