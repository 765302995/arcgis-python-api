 Fire at scrap yard in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/11/2019 - 18:46 Location: Turf Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield At around 6.45pm crews from Macclesfield attended a fire at a car dismantling yard in Turf Lane, Macclesfield.  The fire was large, measuring 6m x 10m, and involved car tyres and other scrap items, producing a lot of black smoke.  The crews used two hose reels to tackle the blaze and a thermal imaging camera to focus on areas that needed particular attention.  They were at the scene for four hours, damping down and checking for hotspots.  An investigation into the cause of the fire is under way.  It is suspected that it was started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/ or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
