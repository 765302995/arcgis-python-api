 Unattended garden fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/11/2018 - 20:44 Location: Orford Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington used a hose reel jet to extinguish a small fire which had been left unattended in the rear yard of a property on Orford Lane, Warrington. Firefighters were in attendance at the scene for approximately 30 minutes.
