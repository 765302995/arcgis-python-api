Unattended bonfire put out in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 02/11/2015 - 16:42 Location: Tarvin Close, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters have had to put out a second unattended bonfire in Ellesmere Port. It was a large bonfire that had been set alight. The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish it.
