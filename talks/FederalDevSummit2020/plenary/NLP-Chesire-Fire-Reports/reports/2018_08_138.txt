 Overturned HGV in Lymm Time of Incident: 06/08/2018 - 10:48 Location: Cliff Lane, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 10.45am firefighters were called to a report of a heavy goods vehicle (HGV) having overturned on Cliff Lane in Lymm. On arrival the crew discovered that no-one was trapped inside the vehicle, which was carrying scrap metal. Firefighters made the vehicle safe and were in attendance for around 10 minutes. An ambulance also attended.
