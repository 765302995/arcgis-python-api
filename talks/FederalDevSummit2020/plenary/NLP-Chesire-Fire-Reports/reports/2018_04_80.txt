 House fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 15/04/2018 - 23:06 Location: London Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Firefighters were called to an electrical fire in a house on London Road, Northwich. Crews found the electrical supply was arcing, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a dry powder extinguisher, a thermal imaging camera and a large fan to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for four hours.
