Fuse box fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 04/03/2016 - 00:03 Location: Manchester Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow, two from Grater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Firefighters rescued a casualty from a fire at a property in Wilmslow. The fire was in an electrical fuse box in the utility room. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a main jet to bring the fire under the control. The female casualty was handed into the care of paramedics. Firefighters were at the scene for just under two hours.
