Casualty trapped in Macclesfield road traffic collision Time of Incident: 12/01/2015 - 11:38 Location: Junction of Buxton Road and Fence Avenue, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters attended a report of a road traffic collision resulting in one male casualty.   One vehicle was involved.   Firefighters released the casualty and handed him into the care of ambulance service.  The crews made the scene safe.
