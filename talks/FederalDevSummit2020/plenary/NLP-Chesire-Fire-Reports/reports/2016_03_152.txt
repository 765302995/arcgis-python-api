Motorbike on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/03/2016 - 20:12 Location: Access to Fairfield High School (south), Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A motorbike was on fire in an alley near to Factory Lane (at the back of Fairfield High School) so firefighters used water backpacks and buckets of water to extinguish the fire.
