Bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/09/2015 - 15:40 Location: Sutton Way, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A carelessly discarded cigarette was the cause of a bin fire in Ellesmere Port. Firefighters used water to put out the fire and urge people to follow the safety advice for smokers - Smoking / fire safety tips for smokers
