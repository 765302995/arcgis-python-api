 Building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/06/2018 - 16:55 Location: Payne Close, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood. One fire engine from Lymm. One fire engine from Warrington. One fire engine from Wigan. One fire engine from Irlam. Firefighters were called to an address in Payne Close in Warrington following reports of a fire in the roof space of a terraced house. Crews used height safety equipment, hose reels and four breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames. They then used a thermal imaging cameras to check for hotspots and checked neighouring houses. They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
