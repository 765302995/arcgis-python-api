 Fire at a fish and chips shop in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/12/2018 - 14:05 Location: Callands Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Penketh Shortly after 2pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a fish and chips shop on Callands Road in Warrington. On arrival at Callands Continental crews discovered a fire in the insulation between the ranges of a deep fat fryer. Firefighters removed all the insulation and extinguished the fire with a fire extinguisher. They were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
