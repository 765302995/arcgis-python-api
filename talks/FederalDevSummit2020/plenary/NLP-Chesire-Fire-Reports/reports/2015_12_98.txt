Outbuilding building fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/12/2015 - 22:05 Location: The Uplands, Runcorn. Attendance: Fire engines from Widnes and Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at the The Uplands, Runcorn.  When they arrived they found that there was a fire in an outbuilding of an empty property. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call 0800 555 111.
