Car fire on M56 west junctions 12 to 14 Time of Incident: 06/05/2016 - 11:57 Location: M56 west between junctions 12 and 14 Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters tackled a fire involving a hatchback that was well alight. The crew, one firefighter wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze.
