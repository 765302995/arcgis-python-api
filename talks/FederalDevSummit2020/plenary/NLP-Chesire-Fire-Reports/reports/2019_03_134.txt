 Cooker fire in Ashton Hayes Time of Incident: 01/03/2019 - 17:51 Location: Church Road, Chester. CH3 8AB Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 5.50pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at an address on Church Road. A small fire, which is believed to have been caused by conducted heat from the cooker, was out on arrival and crews removed cooker panels due to residual heat. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
