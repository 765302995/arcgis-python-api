 Small fire in the open in Crewe Time of Incident: 21/06/2019 - 23:19 Location: Davenport Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly after 11.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Davenport Avenue, Crewe. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire involving rubbish. Firefighters used water to put out the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
