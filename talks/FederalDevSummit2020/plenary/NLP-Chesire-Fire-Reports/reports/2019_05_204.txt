 Fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 30/04/2019 - 21:03 Location: Burtonwood Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly after 9pm firefighters from Warrington were dispatched to reports of a fire in a field off the M62 in Warrington.  On arrival at the scene the crew discovered a pile of refuse which is believed to have been deliberately set alight.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for around 30 minutes.
