Car collided with a tree in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 07/11/2015 - 23:58 Location: Dean Road, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters made a car safe after it collided with a tree in Wilmslow.  One hose reel and a thermal imaging camera were used as a precaution to check for signs of fire.  Police were also at the scene.
