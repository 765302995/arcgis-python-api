 Collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 20/05/2019 - 06:49 Location: Abbey Lane, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Northwich and one from Winsford Fire crews attended to a road traffic collision involving three vehicles on Abbey Lane in Northwich.  No one was trapped inside the vehicle and two people were handed into the care of paramedics.  Firefighters made the area safe.
