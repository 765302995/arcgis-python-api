Lift rescue in Handforth Time of Incident: 25/03/2015 - 23:51 Location: Chelford Road, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow A man was rescued from a lift which had got stuck on the 11th floor of a building in Handforth. Access was gained to the lift motor room in order to wind the lift down to floor level. He was then released uninjured using the lift keys.
