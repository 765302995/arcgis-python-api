 Boiler fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 09/01/2019 - 00:18 Location: Coronation Avenue, Alsager. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Alsagar Firefighters were called to a fire involving a boiler at an address in Coronation Avenue, Alsager.  Crews used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel jet one and one covering jet to put out the fire which was in the airing cupboard on the first floor.  They then used cutting away gear and a large fan to disperse the smoke.
