Road traffic collision in Chester Time of Incident: 21/04/2015 - 12:45 Location: Parkgate Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters have been at a road traffic collision on Parkgate Road.  The incident involved one vehicle.  Police, ambulance and the air ambulance attended.  The crews assisted the ambulance personnel with first aid.  One casualty, a man, was taken to hospital by ambulance.
