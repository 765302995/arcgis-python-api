 Garden fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/06/2019 - 22:28 Location: Westminster Close, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes and one from Penketh Firefighters were called to a fire in the garden of a property on Westminster Close, Widnes. A mattress had been accidentally set on fire and spread to the guttering of a house. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and dampen down the property.
