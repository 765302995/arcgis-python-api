 Fire in a children's play area in Lymm Time of Incident: 21/06/2018 - 23:47 Location: Maltmans Road, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Fire in a children's play area in LymmAt around 11.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Ridgeway Grundy Memorial Park on Maltmans Road in Lymm.  On arrival the crew discovered that the fire measured approximately two metres by two metres and involved play equipment and rubber flooring in a children's play area.  Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 25 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
