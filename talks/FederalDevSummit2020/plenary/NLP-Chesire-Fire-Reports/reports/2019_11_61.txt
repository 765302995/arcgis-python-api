 Wheelie bin set alight in a doorway in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/11/2019 - 03:34 Location: Warrington Road, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Penketh and Widnes At 3.40am firefighters from Widnes and Penketh were called to reports of a fire at a commercial building on Warrington Road.  On arrival the crews discovered that a wheelie bin had been pushed into the doorway of a bookmakers and deliberately set alight.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around one hour.  Anyone with any information in relation to the fire is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 566993 .
