Road traffic collision in in Chester Time of Incident: 06/07/2015 - 15:08 Location: Christleton Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Fire and rescue crews attended a road traffic collision on Christleton Road.  A car collided with a house leaving two casualties trapped in the car.   Firefighters released the casualties, a man and a woman, from the vehicle, suffering from shock.  They were handed into the care of the ambulance service and the crew made the scene safe.   Police officers attended the scene too.
