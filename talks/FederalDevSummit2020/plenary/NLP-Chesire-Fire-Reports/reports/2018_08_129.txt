 Kitchen fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 06/08/2018 - 19:56 Location: Swanlow Lane, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Shortly before 8pm firefighters were called to reports of a oven fire at a domestic property on Swanlow Lane in Winsford. The fire was out on arrival, but crews used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property, they also used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. The team were in attendance for approximately 45 minutes.
