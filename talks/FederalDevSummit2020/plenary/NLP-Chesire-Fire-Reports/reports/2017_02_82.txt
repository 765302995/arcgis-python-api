Road traffic incident at Tabley Time of Incident: 17/02/2017 - 09:46 Location: Tabley, M6 junction 19 Attendance: Four fire engines Fire and rescue crews dealt with an incident on the Junction 19 slip road of the M6. A lorry leaked a fluid, believed to be hydrochloric acid, onto the slip road.  Crew members in gas tight suits stemmed the leak and worked with contractors to ensure it was fully stemmed.  The vehicle and the slip road were then completely washed down by firefighters.  The tanker was removed from the scene by a recovery team, accompanied to the depot by police.
