 Grass fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 17/11/2018 - 14:39 Location: Lighthouse Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to deal with a fire that had started in some grassland off Lighthouse Road. They initially struggled to access the scene, but once they had they found the grass well alight in an area measuring around 10m x 5m. They used water backpacks and beaters to put the fire out.
