Chimney fire in Weaverham Time of Incident: 19/10/2015 - 19:38 Location: West Road, Weaverham Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Specialist chimney equipment was used to put out a fire in a Weaverham property. The crew attacked the fire from the hearth and chimney rods were also used.  Please follow our chimney safety advice to avoid a fire - Chimneys
