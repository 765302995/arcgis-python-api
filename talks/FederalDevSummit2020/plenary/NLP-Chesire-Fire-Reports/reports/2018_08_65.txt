 Small fire in the open in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/08/2018 - 16:33 Location: Brierley Terrace, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 4.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in woodland on Brierley Terrace in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire in the open. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for less than 20 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
