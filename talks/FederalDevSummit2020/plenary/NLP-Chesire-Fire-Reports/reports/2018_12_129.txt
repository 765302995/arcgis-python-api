 Fire in the open in Poynton Time of Incident: 01/12/2018 - 21:17 Location: Hazelbadge Road, Poynton Attendance: Two fire engines attended, from Poynton and Stockport Shortly after 9.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Hazelbadge Road, Poynton. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Fire crews were in attendance for nearly half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
