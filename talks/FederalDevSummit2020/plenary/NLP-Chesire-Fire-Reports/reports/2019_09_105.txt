 Fire at a scrap yard in Runcorn Time of Incident: 10/09/2019 - 21:55 Location: Percival Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly before 10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a scrap yard in Percival Lane, Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning that had been left unattended. Firefighters used a short extension ladder to gain access to the fire, which measured approximately three metres by three metres. They then extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet due to it being near to buildings. Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
