 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/04/2019 - 15:05 Location: Burtonwood Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one from Birchwood Firefighters were called to Burtonwood Road, Warrington, after a report of a vehicle fire near to the M62 slip road. On arrival crews found a saloon car had been destroyed by fire. Crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
