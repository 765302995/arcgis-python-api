 One person trapped after crash in Burleydam Time of Incident: 04/11/2018 - 20:07 Location: Whitchurch Road, Burleydam Attendance: One fire engine attended from Audlem and one from Shropshire Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision on Whitchurch Road, Burleydam. A female casualty was trapped in a car which was on its side. Crews extracted her from the car and then handed them into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for 75 minutes.
