Bin store on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/06/2017 - 20:11 Location: The Decks, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn, one from Widnes, one from Penketh, one from Stockton Heath and the aerial ladder platform from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a bin store on fire at flats in Runcorn. On arrival the crew found a movable skip on fire and used a hose reel to extinguish it. Crews checked flats above for smoke due to windows being open above the area. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the area.
