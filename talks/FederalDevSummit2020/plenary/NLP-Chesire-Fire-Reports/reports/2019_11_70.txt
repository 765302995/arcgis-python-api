 Car fire on the M62 near Warrington Time of Incident: 16/11/2019 - 03:40 Location: M62, eastbound, between junctions 9 and 10 Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 3.40am firefighters from Lymm attended reports of a car fire on the M62 near Winwick. On arrival the crew found that there have been a collision involving one vehicle which had subsequently set on fire. One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Two casualties were treated for at the scene for smoke inhalation, they were then passed to the care of paramedics.
