 Two-vehicle collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 23/11/2019 - 13:42 Location: Derby Road, Farnworth, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Penketh and one from Widnes At around 1.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Derby Road in Farnworth, Widnes.  On arrival the crews discovered that the collision involved two vehicles and that someone was trapped inside one of them.  The road was closed in both directions by the police as emergency services worked at the scene.  Firefighters administered oxygen to the casualty.  He then got out of the saloon car by himself before being taken to hospital in an ambulance.  Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
