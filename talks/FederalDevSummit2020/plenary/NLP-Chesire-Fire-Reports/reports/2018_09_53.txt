 Car fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 19/09/2018 - 22:32 Location: Orme Crescent, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters attended to a car on fire at an address on Orme Crescent in Macclesfield.
