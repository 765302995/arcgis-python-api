Road traffic collision in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 20/08/2015 - 11:12 Location: Knutsford Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Knutsford One person was trapped in a vehicle following a road traffic collision in Wilmslow. Firefighters released the woman from the vehicle, which had overturned, and made the area safe. Police and paramedics also attended.
