 Telegraph pole on fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 25/03/2019 - 13:26 Location: Chester Road, Frodsham Attendance: Two fire engines attended one from Penketh and one form Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended to reports of smoke coming from an electric telegraph pole on Chester Road in Frodsham.  On arrival the pole was found to be well alight. A cordon was set up and crews used dry powder to extinguish the fire.
