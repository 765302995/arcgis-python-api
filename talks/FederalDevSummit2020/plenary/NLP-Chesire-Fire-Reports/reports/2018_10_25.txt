 Fire at chemical plant in Widnes Time of Incident: 27/10/2018 - 08:23 Location: Dans Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh, Warrington, Runcorn and Widnes. Fire at chemical plant in WidnesFirefighters attended to a fire at a chemical plant on Dan's Road in Widnes involving an ibc container inside the premises.  Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, used one foam branch and one hose reel jet to bring the fire under control.  They then checked the area using a thermal imaging camera and made sure it was safe and the fire was fully extinguished.  Firefighters were in attendance for around three hours.
