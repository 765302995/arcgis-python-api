 Cow rescued from canal in Malkins Bank Time of Incident: 27/09/2018 - 11:46 Location: Betchton Road, Malkins Bank Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe and the animal rescue units from Lymm and Bollington Cow in the canalFirefighters from Crewe and the animal rescue units from Bollington and Lymm rescued a cow from a canal near Betchton Road in Malkins Bank.  Two water technicians secured the animal to help stop it from moving down the canal before using animal straps to remove it from the water.
