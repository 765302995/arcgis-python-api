 Garden waste fire in Mere Time of Incident: 01/08/2018 - 13:18 Location: Hulse Heath Lane, Mere. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 1.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Hulse Heath Lane in Mere. On arrival the crew discovered it was a case of uncontrolled burning of garden waste. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
