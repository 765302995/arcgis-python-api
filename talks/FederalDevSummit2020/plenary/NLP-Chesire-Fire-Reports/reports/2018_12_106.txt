 House fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 06/12/2018 - 16:07 Location: Grove Street, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn, one from Lymm, one from Warrington and one from Widnes Firefighters were called to reports of a house being well alight on Gove Street in Runcorn.  Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels to put out the fire, which is believed to have started in the loft.  A covering jet was also used and a cordon was set up  Firefighters checked neighbouring properties to see if the fire had spread.  Once the fire was extinguished crews damped the area down to make sure it was safe.
