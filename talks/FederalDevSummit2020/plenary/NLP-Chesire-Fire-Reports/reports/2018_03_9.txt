 Car fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 28/03/2018 - 22:00 Location: Arbour Close, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At 10pm firefighters were called to a car fire in Arbour Close in Northwich. On arrival the crew found smoke coming from the vehicle and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire before damping down the engine compartment. Firefighters were at the scene for around half-an-hour.
