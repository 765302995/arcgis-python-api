 Firefighters called to caravan fire in Chester Time of Incident: 29/01/2018 - 19:19 Location: Hob Lane, Chester Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At 7.19pm on Monday, 29 January 2018 two fire engines from Ellesmere Port attended a caravan fire on Hob Lane, Chester. On arrival firefighters used one hose reel and one breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire.
