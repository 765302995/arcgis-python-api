 Tumble dryer fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/06/2018 - 13:29 Location: Kershaw Street, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes. One fire engine from Warrington. Firefighters were called to an address in Kershaw Street, Widnes following reports of a fire involving a tumble dryer. Crews used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the fire. They then removed the tumble dryer from the kitchen and continued to damp down the machine to stop the fire from reigniting. A large fan was used to disperse the smoke from the kitchen.
