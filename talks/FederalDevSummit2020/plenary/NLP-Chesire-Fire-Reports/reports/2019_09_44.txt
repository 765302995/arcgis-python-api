 Woman rescued from water in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/09/2019 - 19:53 Location: Wilderspool Causeway, Warrington Attendance: Four fire engines attended - one from Warrington, one from Widnes and two from Lymm Crews attended to the river near to Wilderspool Causeway in Warrington following reports of a woman in the water.   Firefighters entered the water on two rafts and located the woman who was rescued from the water and brought back to safety.
