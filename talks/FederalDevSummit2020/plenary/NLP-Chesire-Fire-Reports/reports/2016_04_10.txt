Bedroom fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/04/2016 - 14:32 Location: Petersgate, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Frodsham A fire in the bedroom on the third floor of a property on Petersgate was tackled by firefighters. The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets and a covering jet to bring the fire under control.  Four breathing apparatus wearers then checked the loft space for fire spread and a hose reel fan was used to clear smoke from the house.Termperature checks were carried out and the area was being dampened down with hose reel jets.  An investigation to establish the cause of the fire is being carried out.
