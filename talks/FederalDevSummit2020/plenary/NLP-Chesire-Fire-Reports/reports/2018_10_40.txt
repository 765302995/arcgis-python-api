 Garden waste fire in Tarvin Time of Incident: 25/10/2018 - 21:56 Location: Hockenhull Crescent, Tarvin Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley Fire crews were called to Hockenhull Crescent, Tarvin, after a controlled burning got out of control. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which was made up of garden waste.
