 Two ducklings rescued in Middlewich Time of Incident: 27/07/2018 - 18:50 Location: Mill Lane, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane. Specialist unit from Lymm. Firefighters rescue ducklingsFirefighters were called to a two ducklings trapped in a water cooling trough at a disused power station in Mill Lane in Middlewich. Crews used a raft to enter the water and rescue the ducklings.  They were then handed over to the RSPCA.
