 Grass fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 14/08/2018 - 17:26 Location: Valley Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a grass fire on Valley Drive, Ellesmere Port. Crews used a fire extinguisher to put out the fire which had been deliberately started. If you have any information about the fire then please contact Cheshire Police.
