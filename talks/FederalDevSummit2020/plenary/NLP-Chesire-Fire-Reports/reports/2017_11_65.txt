Fire on a barge in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 13/11/2017 - 00:19 Location: Brook Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Fire crews were called to Brook Street, Macclesfield, after reports of a fire on a barge in the canal. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was located underneath the panelling. Crews were in attendance for one hour and 50 minutes.
