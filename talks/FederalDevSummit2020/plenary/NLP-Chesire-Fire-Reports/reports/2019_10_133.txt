 Man trapped in a mud bank near Northwich Time of Incident: 05/10/2019 - 15:36 Location: Hole House Lane, Little Leigh, near Northwich. Attendance: Three fire engines attended from Widnes, Northwich and Lymm Shortly after 3.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a man trapped in a mud bank on Hole House Lane, Little Leigh.  On arrival at the scene the crew found a man who was stuck approximately knee deep in a mud bank at the side of the canal.  Firefighters released the man and were detained at the scene for around 45 minutes.
