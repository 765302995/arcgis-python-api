 Shed fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 19:24 Location: Little Green, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port At around 7.25pm firefighters were called to reports of a fence being on fire in Little Green, Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port. On arrival crews discovered that it was a small fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
