Light fitting fire at Chester Museum Time of Incident: 03/04/2017 - 11:57 Location: Grosvenor Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine and the aerial appliance from Chester, one fire engine from Powey Lane and two from Ellesmere Port A light fitting is believed to have been the cause of burning at Chester Museum. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus searched the building and, finding the source, isolated the fitting and removed it from the ceiling.
