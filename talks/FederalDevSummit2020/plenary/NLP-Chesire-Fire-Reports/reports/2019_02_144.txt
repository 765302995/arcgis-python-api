 Car fire at Queens Park in Crewe Time of Incident: 01/02/2019 - 00:46 Location: Queens Park Drive, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 00.45am firefighters from Crewe were again called to reports of a fire on the car park near to the golf course at Queens Park.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered that the car that had been extinguished during a previous incident had been set on fire again.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML309676.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately one hour 30 minutes.
