 Fire in domestic property in Chester Time of Incident: 18/10/2018 - 19:06 Location: Seller Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a fire on the fourth floor of a residential property on Seller Street, Chester. The fire was in an electrical box in a lift and had caused a small amount of smoke logging. Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, isolated the electrics and extinguished the fire using a hose reel jet and a dry powder extinguisher. Crews were at the scene for 50 minutes.
