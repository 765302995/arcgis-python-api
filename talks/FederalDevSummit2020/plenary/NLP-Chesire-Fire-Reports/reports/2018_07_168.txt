 Greenhouse fire in Scholar Green Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 19:51 Location: Little Moss Lane, Scholar Green. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar. One fire engine from Congleton. Firefighters were called to a house on Little Moss Lane, Scholar Green following reports of a greenhouse, trees and refuse on fire. Crews used one hose reel jet to extinguish the flames and did a post incident home safety check. Fire crews were on scene for approximately one hour and fifteen minutes.
