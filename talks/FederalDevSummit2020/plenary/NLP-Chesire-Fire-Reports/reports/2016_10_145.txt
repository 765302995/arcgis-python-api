Shed fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 06/10/2016 - 18:53 Location: Cliffe Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe An investigation into the cause of a shed fire is being carried out by fire investigation officers. The shed was very close to the house, smoke from the fire got into the first floor of both the affected and a neighbouring house. Two hose reel jets were used by firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, to put out and damp down the fire and high pressure fans used to clear the smoke.
