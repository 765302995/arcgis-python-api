Road traffic collision on M6 southbound junctions 19 and 18 Time of Incident: 15/12/2016 - 00:28 Location: M6 southbound between junctions 19 and 18 Attendance: Two fire engines from Stockton Heath and one from Knutsford One casualty was released from a car involved in a road traffic collision on the M6 southbound between junctions 19 and 18. Firefighters had to remove a door to get the casualty out. Crews were at the scene for around an hour.
