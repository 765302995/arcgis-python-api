Car fire in Chester Time of Incident: 14/11/2016 - 18:20 Location: Wrexham Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester The road from the Posthouse roundabout to Chester Business Park was closed for firefighters to tackle a car fire. The crew, one firefighter wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze. The carriageway was then reopened.
