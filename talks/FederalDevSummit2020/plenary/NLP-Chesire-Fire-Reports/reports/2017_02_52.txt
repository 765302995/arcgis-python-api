Shed fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/02/2017 - 00:40 Location: Sandy Lane, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn Firefighters tackled a blaze in a wooden garden shed at the bottom of a Sandy Lane garden. The crews, one firefighter wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They then damped the area down. The incident was handed over to the police as it is suspected the fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
