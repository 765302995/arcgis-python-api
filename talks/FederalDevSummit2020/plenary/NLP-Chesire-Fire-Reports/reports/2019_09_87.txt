 Grass fire, Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 14/09/2019 - 18:18 Location: Valley Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called out in response to reports of a grass fire near the BMX track. Although the fire was out when they arrived, they were on hand to assist a 12-year-old girl who was suffering a medical episode. They called an ambulance and gave her oxygen until paramedics arrived to take her into their care.
