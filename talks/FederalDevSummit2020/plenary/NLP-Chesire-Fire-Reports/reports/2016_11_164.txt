Building fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 03/11/2016 - 18:06 Location: Salisbury Street, Widnes. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a building fire on Salisbury Street, Widnes.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire on the outside of the building but this had gone out. Crews used a Thermal Imaging Camera to check that the fire had not spread to the inside of the building.  This fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
