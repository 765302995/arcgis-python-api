Building fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 05/02/2017 - 13:10 Location: Peach Lane, Wistaston Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Nantwich A brick building was on fire at an hotel in Wistaston. The fire involved a tumble dryer inside the building.  Two firefighters in breathing apparatus used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. A dry powder extinguisher and a covering jet were also in use. The building was ventilated using a high pressure fan and crews left the incident after 90 minutes.
