Wheelie bin deliberately set alight in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/10/2015 - 19:08 Location: Linnet Close, Orford, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A wheelie bin was deliberately set alight behind the community centre by Linnet Close and Grasmere Avenue, so firefighters used hand fire extinguishers to deal with the fire.  This fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information about this fire they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
