Ambulance trapped under archway in Middlewich Time of Incident: 11/05/2015 - 00:47 Location: Lewin Street, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and the rapid response vehicle from Holmes Chapel An ambulance was trapped under an archway in Middlewich.  Nobody was trapped in the ambulance which had dislodged a main support beam and a structural engineer was asked to attend before the ambulance could be removed.   Lewin Street was closed to traffic and residents evacuated from nearby properties affected by the vehicle becoming stuck.  Gas to the properties was isolated but residents were allowed back in when a safety survey had been carried out.   Firefighters handed the incident over to police.
