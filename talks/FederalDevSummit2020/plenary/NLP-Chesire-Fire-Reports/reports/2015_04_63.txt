Fire involving manhole cover in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/04/2015 - 16:39 Location: Liverpool Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters dealt with a fire involving an electric manhole in Great Sankey.  A cordon was put in place around the manhole and one lane of Liverpool Road was closed outside the Sloop Inn Public House. Police and Manweb attended this incident.
