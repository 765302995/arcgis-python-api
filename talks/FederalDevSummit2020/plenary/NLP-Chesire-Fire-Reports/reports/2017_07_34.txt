Viewing platform on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/07/2017 - 19:15 Location: Risley Moss Nature Reserve, Moss Gate, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Viewing platform on fire in WarringtonA fire involving a viewing platform at a nature reserve was extinguished with a hose reel by firefighters. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately and police were requested to attend.   If anyone knows who may have started it they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
