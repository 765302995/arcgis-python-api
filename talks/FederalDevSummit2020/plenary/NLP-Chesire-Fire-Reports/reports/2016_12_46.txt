Chimney fire in Handforth Time of Incident: 22/12/2016 - 21:32 Location: Hampson Crescent, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Ladders and chimney rods were used to tackle a small fire in a chimney. Once the fire was out firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check there was no fire spread and also checked a neighbouring property.  Always follow our chimney safety advice.
