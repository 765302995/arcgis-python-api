 Tarpaulin blown from roof in Barrow Time of Incident: 27/04/2019 - 16:29 Location: Hallowmoor Heath, Barrow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended to reports of tarpaulin having blown from the roof of a property on Hallowmoor Heath in Barrow.  Crews assisted by using a seven meter ladder and height safety equipment to secure the tarpaulin back in place.
