 Small vehicle fire Time of Incident: 19/05/2018 - 07:17 Location: Hope Farm Road, Great Sutton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port attended a vehicle fire near Hope Farm Methodist Church. The fire involved a Vauxhall Zafira which was well alight. Crews used one hose reel jet and one breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. An adjacent vehicle was also damaged as a result of the incident. The cause of the fire is currently being investigated. The team were in attendance for approximately one hour 40 minutes.
