Grass on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/03/2017 - 23:42 Location: Montgomery Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to extinguish approximately 40 metres of grass which was on fire and used beaters to extinguish it. It is believed that the fire was started deliberately. If anyone knows who may have started it they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
