Horse rescued from ditch in Scholar Green Time of Incident: 18/09/2017 - 08:58 Location: Church Lane, Scholar Green Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington, one from Lymm and the specialist animal rescue unit from Lymm Horse rescued from a ditch in Cheshire Firefighters rescued a horse from a ditch on Church Lane, Scholar Green.  The horse was in a ditch approximately five feet down. A vet sedated the horse before firefighters used mechanical lifting equipment to rescue it from the ditch.  After the horse was released from the ditch it was checked by a vet.
