Bathroom fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 08/12/2015 - 06:24 Location: Nelson Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters extinguished a fire involving a shower unit in a bathroom in Crewe.   The crew wore two sets of breathing apparatus and used one hose reel and a dry powder extinguisher to put out the fire. A large fan was also used to clear the smoke and the electricity was isolated.  Extensive damage was caused to the bathroom and the crews used a thermal imaging camera to check the attic space, bathroom and bedroom for hotspots.  The fire is believed to have been caused by the electric shower unit.
