Road traffic collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 22/06/2016 - 20:15 Location: Chorleys Lane, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one from Runcorn Firefighters made the scene safe after a road traffic collision in Widnes. Two vehicles were involved. Nobody was trapped in either vehicle despite initial reports that somebody was trapped in one of the cars, and one casualty, a woman, was cared for by paramedics.
