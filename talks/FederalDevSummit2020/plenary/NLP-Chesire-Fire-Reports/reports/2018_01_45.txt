 Reported kitchen fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 20/01/2018 - 21:55 Location: Lyncroft Close, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Shortly before 10pm firefighters from Crewe were called to reports of a house fire on Lyncroft Close, Crewe. Firefighters swiftly attended the scene and found that there was no fire, but the kitchen was heavily smoke logged due to over heated cooking. The crew used an industrial fan to clear the smoke from the property.
