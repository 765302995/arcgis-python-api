Car fire on M6 southbound between junctions 21 and 20 Time of Incident: 28/09/2017 - 14:39 Location: M6 southbound between junctions 21 and 20 Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm and one from Warrington A fire in the rear wheel of a saloon car was put out by a crew using a hose reel jet. The incident was close to Lymm Services.
