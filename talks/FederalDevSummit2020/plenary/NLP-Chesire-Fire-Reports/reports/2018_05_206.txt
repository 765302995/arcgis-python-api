 Small fire at hotel in Chester Time of Incident: 02/05/2018 - 15:54 Location: Parkgate Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port and one fire engine attended from Lymm Shortly before 4pm firefighters from Lymm and Ellesmere Port were called to reports of a fire at a hotel in Chester. On arrival at the scene crews found that the hotel had been evacuated and persons were accounted for. On entering the building the team found that there was a small amount of smoke in the restaurant area which appeared to be coming from the loft. Crews conducted a search of the loft and discovered that there had been a small fire which had been extinguished.  The team then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the restaurant and were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes.
