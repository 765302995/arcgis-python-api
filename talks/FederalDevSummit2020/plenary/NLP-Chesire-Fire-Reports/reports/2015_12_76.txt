Refuse fire in an outbuilding of a derelict property in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/12/2015 - 17:15 Location: Pinners Brow, Bewsey, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A small amount of refuse was on fire inside an outbuilding of a derelict property, so firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish the fire.
