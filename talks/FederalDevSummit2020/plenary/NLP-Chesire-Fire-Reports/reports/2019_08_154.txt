 Man trapped under motorbike near Knutsford Time of Incident: 03/08/2019 - 12:13 Location: Plumley Moor Road, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and one from Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called to reports of a collision between a car and a motorbike at Plumley Moor Road.  Crews from Northwich and Holmes Chapel attended and discovered a man trapped underneath the motorbike.  They worked to free the man and handed him to the care of North West Ambulance Service.
