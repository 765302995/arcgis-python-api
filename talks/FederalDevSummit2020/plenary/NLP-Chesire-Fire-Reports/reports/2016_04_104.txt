Car fire on M62 west between junctions 10 - 9 Time of Incident: 11/04/2016 - 06:29 Location: M62 west between junctions 10 and 9 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a car on fire on the M62 westbound between junctions 10 and 9. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the small fire in the engine bay of the vehicle.
