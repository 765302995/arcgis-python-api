Tiled chimney fire in Hatherton Time of Incident: 22/11/2015 - 17:58 Location: Lodge Lane, Hatherton Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called out to a report of a chimney fire in Hatherton. The fire was out on arrival but crews used a thermal imaging camera to check there were no further hotspots. Water backpacks were then used to cool the chimney from above.
