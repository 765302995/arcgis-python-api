Road traffic collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 07/08/2015 - 13:08 Location: A500, Weston Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Crewe Crews had to remove the roof of a car to release a casualty in a road traffic collision. The released casualty, a lady, was assessed by ambulance staff and the driver of the car, a man, also received first aid from ambulance personnel. One car was involved in the incident.
