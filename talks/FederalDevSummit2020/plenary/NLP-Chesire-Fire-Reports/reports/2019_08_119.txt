 Abandoned vehicle, Runcorn Time of Incident: 09/08/2019 - 21:30 Location: Mersey Gateway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to reports of a car on fire on the Runcorn-bound slip road of the Mersey Gateway bridge. They found no sign of fire, but did come across the 4x4, which appeared to have been abandoned by the occupants. They notified the police and then moved the vehicle to a safer location.
