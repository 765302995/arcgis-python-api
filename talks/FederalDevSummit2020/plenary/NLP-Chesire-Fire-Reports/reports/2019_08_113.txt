 Two vehicle collision in Oakmere Time of Incident: 11/08/2019 - 17:24 Location: A556 Oakmere Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich and Winsford Shortly before 5.30pm firefighters from Winsford and Northwich were called to reports of a collision on the A556 in Oakmere.  On arrival the crews discovered the collision involved two cars and two casualties were trapped in the vehicles.  Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to release the casualties, they were then passed to the care of paramedics.   Enquiries in relation to the collision are ongoing and anyone with any information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 483309.
