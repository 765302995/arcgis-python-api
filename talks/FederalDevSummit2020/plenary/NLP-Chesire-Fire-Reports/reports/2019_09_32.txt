 M56 vehicle fire Time of Incident: 24/09/2019 - 10:31 Location: M56, westbound between junction 8 to 9 Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Crews attended to a vehicle fire on the M56 westbound between junction eight to nine.  The Mini was located on the hard shoulder and firefighters wearing a breathing apparatus used one hose reel to put out the fire.
