 Small fire on a barge in Runcorn Time of Incident: 24/09/2019 - 19:08 Location: Sea Lane, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a small fire on a barge on the Bridgewater canal in Runcorn.  The crew were in attendance for approximately 50 minutes.
