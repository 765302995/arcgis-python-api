Vehicle fire on the M62 Time of Incident: 11/09/2017 - 14:10 Location: M62 East, junction 11 to 12 Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and two fire engines from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a vehicle fire on the M62 East between junction 11 to 12. Crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, tackled the fire using a hose reel jet. All lanes were closed on the road while the fire crews were dealing with the incident.
