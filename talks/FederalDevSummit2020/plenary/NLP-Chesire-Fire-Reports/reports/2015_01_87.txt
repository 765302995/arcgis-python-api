Electrical fire at a premises in Wardle Time of Incident: 08/01/2015 - 19:14 Location: NWF Agriculture, Nantwich Road, Wardle Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Tarporley Firefighters used two dry powder extinguishers to deal with a fire involving an electrical switch/box. An inner cordon was established within the premises. Electricity was isolated. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. An electrician also attended this incident.
