 Motorcycle fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 24/05/2018 - 20:24 Location: Hale Gate Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Motorcycle fire in Widnes Firefighters were called to Hale Gate Road, Widnes, after a motorcycle fire.
