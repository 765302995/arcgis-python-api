 Fire in farmer's field in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/07/2019 - 11:23 Location: Winwick Lane, Croft, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Shortly before 11.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a farmer's field in Winwick Lane, Croft, Warrington. On arrival the crew found a grass fire measuring approximately 20 metres by 10 metres. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They then damped the area down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
