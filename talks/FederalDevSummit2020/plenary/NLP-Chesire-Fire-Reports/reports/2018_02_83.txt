 Fire in wooded area in Plumley Time of Incident: 12/02/2018 - 20:39 Location: Ascol Drive, Plumley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Fire crews in Northwich were called to reports of a fire in wooded area near to  Ascol Drive in Plumey last night. Firefighters used a hose reel to extinguish the fire which was believed to be pieces of wood.
