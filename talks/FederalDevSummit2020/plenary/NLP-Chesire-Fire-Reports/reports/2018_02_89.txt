 Fork lift truck on fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 09/02/2018 - 02:09 Location: Second Avenue, Poynton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington Firefighters were called to an address in Second Avenue in Poynton following reports of a fork lift truck on fire. Crews used two breathing apparatus, two hose reels and a covering jet to extinguish the flames and cool the gas cylinder. They then monitored the temperature of the cylinder with a thermal imaging camera and used a hose reel to damp down.
