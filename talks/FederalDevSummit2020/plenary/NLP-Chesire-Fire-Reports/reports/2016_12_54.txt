Boxer van on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/12/2016 - 22:04 Location: Garden Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out a fire involving one medium sized boxer van, which was well alight. After extinguishing the fire, crews used a thermal imaging camera to check for any remaining hot spots of fire on the car or on the surrounding building, which could indicate damage.  This fire was believed to have been started deliberately. Did you see anything? If so, call Crime Stoppers on 0800 555 111.
