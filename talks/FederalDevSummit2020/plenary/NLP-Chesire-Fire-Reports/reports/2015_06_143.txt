House fire in Prestbury Time of Incident: 08/06/2015 - 09:41 Location: St James Hill, Prestbury Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Firefighters extinguished a fire involving a house in Prestbury.  The fire was in the dressing room on the first floor of the three storey detatched property. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to put out the fire and a large fan to clear the smoke.  Smoke alarms were fitted and did activate.  An investigation into the cause of the fire is underway but at this stage the cause is believed to be accidental.
