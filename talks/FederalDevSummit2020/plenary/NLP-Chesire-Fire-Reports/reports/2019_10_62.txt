 Small fire involving a pickup truck's engine in Sandbach Time of Incident: 21/10/2019 - 06:55 Location: Old Mill Road, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Crewe and one from Sandbach Shortly before 7am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a pickup truck's trailer on Old Mill Road, Sandbach.  On arrival crews found the engine bay smouldering.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to cool down the engine and make it safe.  They monitored temperatures with a thermal imaging camera and were in attendance for less than 20 minutes.  The small fire is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault.
