 Small fire at a domestic property in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/10/2019 - 15:19 Location: Chapelside Close, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 3.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a domestic property in Chapelside Close, Great Sankey, Warrington.  On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire involving plastic on a gas fire.  Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
