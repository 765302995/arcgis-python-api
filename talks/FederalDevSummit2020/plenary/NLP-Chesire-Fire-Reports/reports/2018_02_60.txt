 Kitchen fire extinguished by crews in Northwich Time of Incident: 17/02/2018 - 18:13 Location: John Brunner Crescent, Northwich Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Northwich and one from Winsford Firefighters were called to a fire at a house on John Brunner Crescent in Northwich. Crews from Winsford and Northwich used four breathing apparatus, a hose reel, covering jet and a ventilation fan to extinguish the fire which was in the kitchen. No one was reported as being injured.
