 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/10/2018 - 20:28 Location: Troutbeck Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Fire crews were called to a wheelie bin fire on Troutbeck Avenue, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire then please contact Cheshire Police.
