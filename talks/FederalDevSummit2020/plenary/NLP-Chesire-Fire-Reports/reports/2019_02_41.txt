 Fire in basement of Warrington property Time of Incident: 23/02/2019 - 02:22 Location: Froghall Lane, Warrington Attendance: Five fire engines attended - one from Birchwood, one from Stockton Heath, two from Penketh and one from Lymm Fire crews attended to a guest house on Froghall Lane in Warrington following reports of a fire in the basement.  Crews, wearing six breathing apparatus, used three hose reels and one covering jet to help put out the fire. A positive pressure ventilation fan was then used to clear the smoke before crews continued to look for hot spots.  A final assessment was made of the property using a thermal imaging camera before crews left the scene.  All people inside the house were accounted for.
