Tumble dryer fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 19/12/2016 - 06:40 Location: Severn Walk, Winsford Attendance: Fire engines from Winsford and Middlewich Smoke alarms alerted the residents of a Severn Walk house to a fire in their kitchen. Firefighters evacuated them from the house and tackled the fire using a hose reel jet. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. The house was then cleared of smoke using a hose reel jet.  Smoke alarms save lives. Make sure you have fitted, working alarms in your home.
