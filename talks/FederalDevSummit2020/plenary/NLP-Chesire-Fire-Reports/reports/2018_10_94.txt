 Portaloo fire in Disley Time of Incident: 14/10/2018 - 22:05 Location: Buxton Road West, Disley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one from Greater Manchester Fire crews were called to a fire in two portaloos on a building site on Buxton Road West, Disley. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
