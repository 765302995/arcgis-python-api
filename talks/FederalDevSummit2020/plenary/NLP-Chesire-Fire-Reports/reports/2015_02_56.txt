Bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/02/2015 - 23:07 Location: Lyons Lane, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Stockton Heath Crews used a hose reel jet to put out a bin fire Lyons Lane, Warrington.   It is believed that this fire was started deliberately.
