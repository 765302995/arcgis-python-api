Kitchen on fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 21/04/2015 - 07:13 Location: Townfield Lane, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Frodsham Firefighters were called to a kitchen on fire in Frodsham. The crew stripped back the kitchen units to damp the fire down and used a large fan to remove smoke from the property before leaving. Kitchen fire safety advice
