 Fire alarm activation in Shavington Time of Incident: 20/11/2019 - 12:34 Location: Northfield Place, Shavington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly after 12.30pm firefighters attended reports that a fire alarm had been activated at a residential address in Shavington. On arrival at the scene the crew found that the alarm activation had been caused by cooking fumes. Firefighters ventilated the kitchen and conducted checks at the address. They were in attendance at the address for around 20 minutes.
