 Man's fingers trapped in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/08/2018 - 14:12 Location: Melford Court, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood and the major rescue unit with supporting appliance from Winsford Fire crews were called to rescue a man whose fingers became trapped in a brake calliper at a warehouse on Melford Court, Warrington. Crews used hydraulic cutting equipment to release the man, who had two fingers stuck. He was then handed into the care of paramedics.
