 Garden fire in Cuddington Time of Incident: 29/06/2018 - 14:51 Location: Cuddington Lane, Cuddington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At 14:51 firefighters from Northwich were called to reports of a fire in the garden of an address in Cuddington. The fire involved a small pile of peat which has ignited and caused damage to a nearby fence. The team were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes.
