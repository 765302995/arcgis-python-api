Car fire in Chester Time of Incident: 29/08/2016 - 22:46 Location: Stamford Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port A hatchback was on fire on a driveway on Stamford Road. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.  A wheelie bin was also involved in the fire and it is believed the fire was started in the bin, spreading to the car.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident log 1060 of 29 August.  Always follow our wheelie bin safety advice.
