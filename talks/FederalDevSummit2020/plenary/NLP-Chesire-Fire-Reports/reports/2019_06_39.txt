 Fire at a building site in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/06/2019 - 12:24 Location: Windmill Lane, Preston Brook, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly before 12.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a building site in Windmill Lane, Preston Brook, Runcorn. On arrival the crew found a large unattended fire involving a quantity of rubbish. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
