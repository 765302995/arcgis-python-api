 Wood fire, Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 10/10/2019 - 21:17 Location: Broadside Avenue, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One appliance from Ellesmere Port Firefighters turned out to deal with a pile of wood on fire in Broadside Avenue. It appeared to have been fence panels that had been ripped down and been set alight near the park. A wheelie bin was also on fire. They used a hose reel jet to put the fires out, and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
