 Unsafe bonfire in Widnes Time of Incident: 11/11/2019 - 22:31 Location: Cherry Sutton, Hough Green, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 10.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a bonfire located close to a house in Cherry Sutton, Hough Green, Widnes.  The small fire in the open was deemed to be unsafe so firefighters extinguished it with a hose reel jet.  They were in attendance for less than 15 minutes.
