 Fire near a disused railway track in Congleton Time of Incident: 20/02/2018 - 10:47 Location: Shellow Lane, North Rode Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters tackle reports of a fire near a disused railway track in Congleton on Shellow Lane in North Rode.
