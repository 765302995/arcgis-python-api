Refuse set alight near Holmes Chapel children's playground Time of Incident: 03/08/2015 - 04:32 Location: Middlewich Road, Holmes Chapel Attendance: One fire engine Refuse was deliberately set alight close to a children's playground. The fire, which also affected some two metres of the play area tarmac, was put out by firefighters using a hose reel jet.  Police were asked to attend due to the deliberate ignition.
