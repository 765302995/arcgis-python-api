Road traffic collision in Middlewich Time of Incident: 29/12/2016 - 12:08 Location: Pochin Way, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Winsford Firefighters were called to a collision involving two heavy goods vehicles. One man was released from a tractor unit by firefighters and Pochin Way was closed while the incident was dealt with. The crew managed a spillage of oil from the vehicles with spillage mats and the Environment Agency was requested to attend. Cheshire East Highways were also informed.
