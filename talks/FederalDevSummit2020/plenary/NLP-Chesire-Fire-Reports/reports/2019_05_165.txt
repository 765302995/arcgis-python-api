 Electrical fault in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 09/05/2019 - 16:01 Location: Morley Green Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 4pm firefighters from Wilmslow attended reports of a fire at a domestic property on Morley Green Road in the town. On arrival the crew discovered smoke coming from a light in the pantry area and that there was a small amount of charring on a wooden fascia near to the light. Firefighters isolated the electricity supply to the property. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. The crew removed the charred fascia and damped down the affected area. Firefighters were at the scene for around 45 minutes.
