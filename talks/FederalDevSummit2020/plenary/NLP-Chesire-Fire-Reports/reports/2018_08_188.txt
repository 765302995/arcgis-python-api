 Car on fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 31/07/2018 - 09:42 Location: M56 eastbound, between Junctions 7 and 6 Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Wilmslow and one from Greater Manchester At around 9.40am firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on the hard shoulder of the M56 eastbound between Junctions 7 and 6. Crews cordoned off the first lane of the motorway as they worked at the scene. All persons were accounted for and a firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for around two hours. Highways England also attended.
