Machinery fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 25/10/2015 - 23:38 Location: Queens Avenue, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish a fire involving machinery in Macclesfield.  The fire was inside a commercial building and crews were at the scene for 30 minutes.
