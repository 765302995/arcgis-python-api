Dishwasher fire in a pub Time of Incident: 28/03/2015 - 08:19 Location: Chester Road, Chester. Attendance: Fire engines from Chester and Ellesmere Port. Firefighters were called to a fire at The Oak on Chester Road, Kelsall.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved a dishwasher fire. They used special fans to clear smoke from the property. There were no reports of any injuries.
