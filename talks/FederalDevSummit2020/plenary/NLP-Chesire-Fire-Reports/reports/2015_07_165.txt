Wheelie bin fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 06/07/2015 - 00:09 Location: Park Crescent, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow A hose reel jet was used to put out a wheelie bin fire.  The fire caused heat damage to a nearby car.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
