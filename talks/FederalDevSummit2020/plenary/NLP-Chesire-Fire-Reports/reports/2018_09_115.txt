 Two-vehcle collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 07/09/2018 - 16:00 Location: Cumberland Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At around 4pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving two vehicles outside Sainsbury's supermarket in Cumberland Street, Macclesfield. On arrival the crew discovered that no-one was trapped inside either vehicle. Firefighters helped paramedics get one of the drivers out of their vehicle and into an ambulance. The crew were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
