Bushes set on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/07/2016 - 21:03 Location: Stockham Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Arson is suspected as the cause of a fire involving bushes on Stockham Close. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and are urging anyone with any information about this incident to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
