 Shed on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/11/2019 - 19:21 Location: Irwell Lane, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Runcorn and one from Widnes At around 7.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a shed being on fire in Irwell Lane, Runcorn.  On arrival crews found the outbuilding well alight.  A water shuttle was set up to get enough water to the area and firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet.  They then damped the shed down and dismantled it to make the area safe.  Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
