Small fire in the open in Wincham Time of Incident: 15/07/2015 - 22:00 Location: Wincham Lane,Wincham,Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich An unattended bonfire left to burn was put out by firefighters.  The crew used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which covered an area of around 20 square metres.
