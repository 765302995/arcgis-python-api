House fire in Chelford Time of Incident: 24/04/2016 - 00:41 Location: Robin Close, Chelford Attendance: Fire engines from Macclesfield, Knutsford, Wilmslow and Holmes Chapel Four fire engines were called to a house fire in Robin Close. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used to hose reel jets to put out the fire which was on the first floor of the property.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check there were no further areas of heat. An investigation into the cause of the fire is being carried out. Nobody was trapped in the property.
