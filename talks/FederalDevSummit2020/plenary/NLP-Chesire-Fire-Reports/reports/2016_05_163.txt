Deliberate fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/05/2016 - 17:11 Location: St Austell Close, Penketh, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A woodland fire is believed to have been started deliberately in Penketh. Firefighters used a water backpack to put out the fire in a wooded area near St Austell Close.  Arson can cost lives. If you see anyone deliberately setting fires please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
