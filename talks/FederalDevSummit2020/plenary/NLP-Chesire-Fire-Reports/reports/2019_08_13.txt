 Fire involving a slide in a Warrington park Time of Incident: 27/08/2019 - 20:16 Location: Bostock Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 8.25pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in Bostock Street, Warrington. On arrival the crew found that the fire involved a slide in a park. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for less than 15 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
