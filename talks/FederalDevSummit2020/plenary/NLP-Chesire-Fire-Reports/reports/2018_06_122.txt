 Cardboard fire in Chester Time of Incident: 21/06/2018 - 19:27 Location: Watergate Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 7.25pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Watergate Street in Chester. The fire involving a small quantity of cardboard near an electricity supply box was out on arrival. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and found that no damage was done to the electric box.
