 Smoke coming from car on the M56 in Runcorn Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 07:59 Location: M56 eastbound, between junctions 12 and 11 Attendance: Two fire engines attended - from Widnes and Runcorn At around 8am firefighters were called to reports of a car being on the hard shoulder of the M56 eastbound in Runcorn, between junctions 12 and 11. On arrival crews discovered that the stranded vehicle's engine was leaking and smoke was coming from it. Firefighters made the vehicle safe before the Highways Agency attended. Fire crews were in attendance for nearly an hour.
