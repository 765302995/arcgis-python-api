 Chimney fire in Rostherne Time of Incident: 04/02/2018 - 17:58 Location: Lady Marys Square, Rostherne. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters attended a chimney fire in Lady Marys Square in Rostherne. Embers were seen coming from the roof. Crews tackled the fire from the grate in the living room and checked the loft for any hotspots. They used a thermal imaging camera to check the temperature of the chimney as they continued to cool it with water.
