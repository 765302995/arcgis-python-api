Car well alight in Chester Time of Incident: 14/03/2015 - 04:57 Location: Pudding Lane, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley and one from Chester A 4x4 vehicle was on its side and was well alight on Pudding Lane in Chester. One firefigher wore breathing apparatus. Two hose reel jets were used to extinguish the fire.  Firefighter checked the vehicle and found that there was nobody inside the vehicle.  Police attended this incident.  Firefighters and police searched the surrounding area. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera.  A police helicopter checked the surrounding area too. This incident was handed over to the police.
