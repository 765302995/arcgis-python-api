 Small fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 11/09/2018 - 20:38 Location: Finsbury Walk, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Fire crews were called to Finsbury Walk, Winsford, to a small fire in a wood. On arrival crews found a mattress had been set alight and they used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police.
