 Commercial building fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 19/10/2019 - 08:55 Location: Edleston Road, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to a fire in a commercial building in Edleston road, Crewe. Crews used three breathing apparatus, one hose reel and a covering jet to extinguish the fire at the terraced property.  They checked for any fire spread by cutting away the floor and ceiling. They also checked neighbouring properties using a thermal imaging camera to ensure they weren't affected.  They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke and will be looking into the cause. Crews were at the scene for three and a half hours.
