Fire in the open in Winsford Time of Incident: 24/06/2017 - 16:23 Location: Bradford Road, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford The Winsford crew put out a small fire involving branches after it was deliberately set on fire near to a rangers office in Winsford. They used water to extinguish the flames. Call Police on 101 if you know anything about who may have started it.
