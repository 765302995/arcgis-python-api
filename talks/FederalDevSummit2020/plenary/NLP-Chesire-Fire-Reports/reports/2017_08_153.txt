Vehicle fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 02/08/2017 - 21:08 Location: North Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port A 4-wheel drive vehicle was on fire on the pavement of North Road. Situated next to a substation and a tree, the vehicle was well alight. Firefighters, one in breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the blaze under control and then they damped down the area. They managed to prevent the fire spreading to the nearby trees and substation. Police were asked to attend as it is believed the fire may have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting police reference 1029 of 2nd August.
