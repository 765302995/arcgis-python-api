Bathroom fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/02/2016 - 00:57 Location: Weston Road, Runcorn. Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn Firefighters responded to a fire at a property on Weston Road, Runcorn.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire in a bathroom fan but this had gone out. Firefighters removed some times and used a Thermal Imaging Camera to make sure that the fire had not spread. Extra smoke alarms were also fitted.
