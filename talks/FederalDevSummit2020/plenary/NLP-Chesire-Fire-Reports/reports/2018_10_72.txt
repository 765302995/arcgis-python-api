 Cooking fire at house in Middlewich Time of Incident: 19/10/2018 - 19:29 Location: Long Lane, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called following reports of a fire in a kitchen on Long Lane. Crews attended and discovered an oven had been left unattended. They used breathing apparatus, a hose reel and positive pressure ventilation fan to cool the oven and clear smoke. For more information on how to stay safe in your kitchen visit our advice page.
