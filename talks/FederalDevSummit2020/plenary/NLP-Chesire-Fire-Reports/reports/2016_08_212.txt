Man stuck at height in Weaverham Time of Incident: 03/08/2016 - 14:54 Location: West Road, Weaverham Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich, line rescue unit from Knutsford and hydraulic platform from Chester Firefighters rescued a man stuck at height on a hydraulic platform. Workers were repairing street lights when their hydraulic platform stuck at about eight metres high.  A fire and rescue service hydraulic platform was brought to the scene and the man rescued.
