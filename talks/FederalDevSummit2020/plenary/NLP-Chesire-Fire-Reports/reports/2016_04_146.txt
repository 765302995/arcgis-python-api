Road traffic collision on M62 east junctions 10 to 11 Time of Incident: 03/04/2016 - 06:55 Location: M62 east, junctions 10-11 Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters assisted at a road traffic collision on M62 east near Warrington. The collision involved several vehicles - a truck and three cars - and crews helped make the scene safe. No casualties were trapped in any of the vehicles.
