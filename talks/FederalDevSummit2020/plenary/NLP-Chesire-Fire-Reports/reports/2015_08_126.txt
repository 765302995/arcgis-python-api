Fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/08/2015 - 14:56 Location: Bridgewater Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A fire in the open is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters used handheld extinguishers to put out the fire.  Anyone who may have information about this deliberate fire setting is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
