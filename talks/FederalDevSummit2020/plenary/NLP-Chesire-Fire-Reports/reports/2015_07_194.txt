Fire involving tyres in disused warehouse in Runcorn Time of Incident: 01/07/2015 - 21:37 Location: Chadwick Road, Runcorn Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn, Widnes, Warrington and Stockton Heath plus the aerial platform attended Tyres inside a disused warehouse were suspected of deliberately being set on fire in Runcorn. Firefighters attended and found thick black smoke coming from the building. Six firefighters in breathing apparatus used three hose reel jets to extinguish the fire which also affected the roof of the building.
