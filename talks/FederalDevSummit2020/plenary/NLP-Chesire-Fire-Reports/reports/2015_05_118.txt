Machine fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 09/05/2015 - 06:16 Location: Queens Avenue, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters attended a commercial building fire on Queens Avenue in Macclesfield. When they arrived crews found a machine inside the building on fire.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to bring the fire under control
