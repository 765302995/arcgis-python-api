Car on fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 03/02/2016 - 09:06 Location: Clive Green Lane, Stanthorne, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich Firefighters were called to extinguish a car on fire in Middlewich. The car was at the side of the road when firefighters arrived and they used two hose reels to extinguish the flames.
