Small fire in Warrington Hospital Time of Incident: 22/08/2017 - 02:58 Location: Lovely Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and two from Penketh attended the incident Fire crews were called to a fire on a ward at Warrington Hospital on Lovely Lane, Warrington.  Six people were evacuated as a result of the fire which was out on arrival of the crews and involved a mattress. They used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.
