Small kitchen fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 16/02/2015 - 17:36 Location: Bradbury Road, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford There had been a small fire involving food inside an ovenof a property on Bradbury Road in Winsford. The fire was already out when firefighters arrived at the property.  Firefighters isolated the electricity supply, then they used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.
