House well alight in Audlem Time of Incident: 19/05/2017 - 18:58 Location: Daisy Bank Crescent, Audlem Attendance: Fire engines from Nantwich, Crewe, and Middlewich Fire crews were sent to a semi detached property on Daisy Bank Crescent in Audlem, to reports of a fire at the property.  On arrival they found the property well alight.   Firefighters used two hose reels and a large fan to extinguish the fire. Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  Fire crews found that the fire started in an electric box.  Fire crews were in attendance for approximately four hours.
