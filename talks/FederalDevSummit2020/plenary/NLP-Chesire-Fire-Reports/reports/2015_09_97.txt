Electric fire in Over Alderley Time of Incident: 12/09/2015 - 05:55 Location: School Lane, Over Alderley. Attendance: A fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on School Lane, Over Alderley.  When they arrived crews found that there had been an issue with the electric junction box. Firefighters turned off the electricity to the house. They then waited for the electricy company to arrive.
