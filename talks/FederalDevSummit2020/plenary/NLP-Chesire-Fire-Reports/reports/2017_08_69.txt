Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/08/2017 - 15:50 Location: Alder Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Penketh Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving a car and a van. Crews used cutting equipment to assist with one woman trapped in a vehicle. Alder Lane was closed in both directions while the incident was dealt with. Police and ambulance attended.
