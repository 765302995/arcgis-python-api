 Wheelie bin on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/02/2019 - 19:05 Location: Deansway, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly after 7pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Deansway in Widnes. On arrival the crew found a wheelie bin well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 10 minutes. Police also attended. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Police on 101 or give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries. For wheelie bin fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
