 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/02/2018 - 10:53 Location: Northway, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh and one Fire engine from Leigh attended Shortly before 11.00am firefighters were called to reports of a house fire on Northway in Orford, Warrington. On arrival crews found that the fire involved the kitchen area of a semi-detached property. Firefighters used two breathing apparatus and a positive pressure ventilation fan to extinguish the fire. A 70-year-old man suffered a burn to his head was taken to hospital by ambulance. Crews were in attendance for approximately one hour 15 minutes.
