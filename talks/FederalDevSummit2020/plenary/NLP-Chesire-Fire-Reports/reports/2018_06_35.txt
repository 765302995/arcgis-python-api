 Fire at disused timber yard in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 22:34 Location: B5156, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Stockton Heath and one from Lymm Fire at disused timber yard in WarringtonAt around 10.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a disused timber yard on the B5156 in Stockton Heath, Warrington.  On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved a shipping container and two mobile buildings.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to fight the fire.  The fire in the shipping container was extinguished and access was gained to a mobile building using an electric saw.  Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to put out the remaining fire.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check for hotspots.  Crews then dampened the area down to make it safe.  They cut away affected timber beams from inside a mobile building while damping down.  A hotspot was located underneath a floor. Firefighters used a saw and an axe to expose the hotspot and damped the area down to make it safe.  Firefighters were in attendance for around two hours.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
