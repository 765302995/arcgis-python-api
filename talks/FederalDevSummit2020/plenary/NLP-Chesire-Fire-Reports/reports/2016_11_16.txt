Car fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 27/11/2016 - 09:51 Location: Godscroft Lane, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine from Frodsham A car fire in Godscroft Lane is believed to have been started deliberately. The fire was in a field some distance away from the road. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and asked police to attend.  If anyone has any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
