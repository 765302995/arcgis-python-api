 Camping chair on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/03/2018 - 20:32 Location: Langley Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Firefighters were called to a fire on the balcony of a block of flats on Langley Court, Ellesmere Port. Crews found a camping chair was on fire and used a hose reel jet to put it out.
