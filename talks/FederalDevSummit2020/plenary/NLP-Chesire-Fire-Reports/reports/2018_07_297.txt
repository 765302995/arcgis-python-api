 Vehicle fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 20:32 Location: Albert Square, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 8.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a vehicle being on fire at Albert Square car park in Widnes. The crew used breathing apparatus and two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire, which is believed to have been started deliberately, and were in attendance for nearly an hour. Anyone with information about the fire should call police on 101.
