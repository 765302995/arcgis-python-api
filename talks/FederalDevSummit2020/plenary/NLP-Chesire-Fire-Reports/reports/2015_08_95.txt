Road Traffic Collision A49 Time of Incident: 14/08/2015 - 09:44 Location: A49, Whitchurch Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and one from Crewe. Firefighters went to the scene of a road traffic collision on the A49.  The road was closed in both directions between the A534 and the A41 while the incident was dealt with.  Three vehicles were involved in the collision and there were three casualties.  Two were taken to hospital by ambulance but the third casualty sadly died.
