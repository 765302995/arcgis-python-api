 One-vehicle RTC in Ridley Time of Incident: 27/04/2019 - 07:56 Location: Whitchurch Road, Ridley Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Nantwich and one from Malpas Firefighters attended to a road traffic collision on Whitchurch Road in Ridley involving one vehicle.  The road was temporarily closed while one man was safely removed from the car by fire crews.  He was then handed into the care of paramedics and taken to hospital.  Crews were at the scene for approximately one hour.
