 Garden fire in Chester Time of Incident: 25/05/2019 - 09:01 Location: Butterbache Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters attended to a fire in a garden on Butterbache Road in Chester.  The was already out when crews arrived however to ensure it was safe they dampened down the area using a hose reel.  The fire is believed to have been caused by outside lighting.
