 Cooking fumes in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/04/2019 - 10:49 Location: Ivy Road, Macclesfield. SK11 8QR Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a kitchen at Ivy Road, Macclesfield. A crew attended and used a positive pressure ventilation fan to clear fumes caused by cooking.
