 Fire at a farm in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 17/07/2018 - 20:34 Location: Chelford Road, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 8.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at a farm on Chelford Road in Alderley Edge. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning that had been left unattended. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for under an hour.
