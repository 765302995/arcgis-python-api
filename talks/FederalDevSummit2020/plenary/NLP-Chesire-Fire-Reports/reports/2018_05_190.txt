 House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/05/2018 - 12:43 Location: Payne Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to Payne Close, Warrington, after a reported fire in a house. On arrival crews found the fire was in the electrical cupboard and they used a dry powder extinguisher to put out the fire.
