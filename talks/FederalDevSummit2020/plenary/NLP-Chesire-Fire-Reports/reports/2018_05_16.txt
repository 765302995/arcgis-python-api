 Hedge fire at address in Alsager Time of Incident: 28/05/2018 - 20:11 Location: heath end road, alsager Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar and one from Crewe Crews attended to a hedge on fire in-between two houses on Heath End Road in Alsager.  One hose reel and thermal imaging camera was used by firefighters to put out the blaze which had also caused slight damage to the roof.
