 Fire in disused building in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 02/03/2019 - 17:50 Location: Parsonage Street, Macclesfield. SK11 7GY Attendance: Four fire engines attended. One from Poynton, one from Macclesfield, one from Lymm and one from Bollington Just after 5.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a disused building fire at Parsonage Street. Crews attended and discovered a large fire on the first floor of the building. They wore breathing apparatus and used two hose reel jets and a covering jet to put the fire out. They were in attendance for around two-and-a-half hours. The cause of the fire is still under investigation.
