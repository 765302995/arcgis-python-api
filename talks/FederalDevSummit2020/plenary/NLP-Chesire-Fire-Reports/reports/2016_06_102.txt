Wheelie bins on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 12/06/2016 - 04:45 Location: Kennedy Avenue, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Wheelie bins were believed to have been deliberately set on fire, causing fire damage to a nearby car. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and asked police to attend.  If you have any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
