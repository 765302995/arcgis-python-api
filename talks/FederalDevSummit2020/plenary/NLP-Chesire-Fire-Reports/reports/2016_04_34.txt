Four car collision near Nantwich Time of Incident: 23/04/2016 - 10:47 Location: A51, Willaston, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Nantwich One man had to be cut free from a car involved in a four car road traffic collision on the A51 at Willaston. Firefighters released the casualty and handed him into the care of ambulance personnel.  Police were also in attendance.
