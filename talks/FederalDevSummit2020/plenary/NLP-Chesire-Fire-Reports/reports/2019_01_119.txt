 Fire in a workshop in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 18/01/2019 - 10:05 Location: Bridges Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Shortly after 10am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a workshop at Argent Oil Terminal on Bridges Road in Ellesmere Port. The electrical fire was out on arrival. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus ventilated the workshop with a large fan. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour.
