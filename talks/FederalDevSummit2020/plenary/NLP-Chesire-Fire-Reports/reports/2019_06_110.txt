 Refuge fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/06/2019 - 23:10 Location: Windmill Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Warrington A rubbish fire on Windmill Lane in Warrington measuring 3m by 3m was reported well alight.  The refuge was burning but there was a risk of it being deemed out of control due to being in close proximity to cooking oil canisters.  One hose reel was used to extinguish the fire and the area was made safe.
