Cooker fire in Handforth Time of Incident: 30/03/2016 - 05:02 Location: Jubilee Court, Chelford Road, Handforth Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield and the hydraulic platform Firefighters were called to a pan of food which had been left on a cooker in a flat on the sixth floor of a building. One man was rescued by firefighters suffering from smoke inhalation. Paramedics attended to deal with the casualty. Kitchen fire safety advice
