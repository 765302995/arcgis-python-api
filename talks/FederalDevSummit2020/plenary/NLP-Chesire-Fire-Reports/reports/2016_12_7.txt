Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 30/12/2016 - 02:47 Location: Gordon Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A fire, believed to have started in an extractor fan, broke out in a kitchen in Warrington. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet and one covering jet to put out the fire before using a large fan to clear smoke from the property.
