 Garage fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 21/09/2019 - 14:15 Location: John Road, Lymm Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Lymm, one from Crewe and one from Birchwood Firefighters attended to reports of a garage on fire on John Road in Lymm.  Wearing one breathing apparatus, crews used one hose reel to put out the flames and as a precaution checked the fire hadn't spread to neighbouring properties.  Crews then continued to dampen down the area.
