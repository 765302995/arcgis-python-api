 Rubbish fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/06/2018 - 16:06 Location: Algernon Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a rubbish fire on Algernon Street, Warrington. Crews found that a faulty extension lead had caused a large amount of rubbish to set fire in a back garden. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.
