 Grass fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 30/06/2018 - 17:18 Location: Cunningham Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh Firefighters were notified of a fire involving areas of grass on a nearby golf course. They arrived to find three areas of a grass and an area of bushes well alight. They used a hose reel and beaters to extinguish the fires, and were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
