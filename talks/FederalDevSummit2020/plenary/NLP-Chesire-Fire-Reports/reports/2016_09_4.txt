Dishwasher fire in Disley Time of Incident: 29/09/2016 - 10:46 Location: Chantry Fold, Disley Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one appliance from Knutsford Firefighters were called to reports of a dishwasher fire in Disley. As the fire was out on arrivals crews carried out a health and safety assessment and made sure the area was safe before leaving the property.
