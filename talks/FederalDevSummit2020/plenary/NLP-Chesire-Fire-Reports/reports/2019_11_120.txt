 Flooding at a residential house in Crewe Time of Incident: 07/11/2019 - 17:11 Location: Stalbridge Road, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 5.10pm firefighters from Crewe attended reports of flooding at a residential address on Stalbridge Road. On arrival the crew found fast flowing water coming through the ceiling from the first floor. Firefighters isolated the water and the electrics at the property to make the building safe. The homeowner was then advised to call a professional plumber to deal with the leak.
