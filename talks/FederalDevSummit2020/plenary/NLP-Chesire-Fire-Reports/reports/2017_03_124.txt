Wheelie bin fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 11/03/2017 - 13:03 Location: Holford Avenue, Northwich. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Holford Avenue, Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that there was wheelie bin on fire at the rear of the property that was affecting a window. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.
