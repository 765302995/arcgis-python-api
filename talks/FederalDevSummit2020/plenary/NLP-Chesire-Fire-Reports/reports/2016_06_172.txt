Road traffic collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 01/06/2016 - 13:46 Location: M6 north between junctions 17 and 18 Attendance: Fire engines from Sandbach, Crewe, Winsford and Congleton. Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving one coach, three saloon cars and one van and a trailer with one person trapped. 70 people were evacuated from the coach onto an embankment. All three lanes of the motorway, northbound, were closed by police while the incident was being dealt with. Firefighters released one man from one vehicle, who was handed to the care of paramedics. Police, Highways England and the Air Ambulance also attended.
