 Shipping container fire at business park in Adlington Time of Incident: 12/12/2018 - 16:00 Location: Adlington Business Park, Adlington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Poynton and one from Offerton At around 4pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at the Swift unit at Adlington Business Park, Adlington. On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved two mini diggers in a shipping container. The fire was contained within the container. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Once the fire was out a mini digger was winched from the container. Firefighters then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and a hose reel jet to cool down the area affected by the fire. They left the scene once they were sure that the area was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour and 15 minutes.
