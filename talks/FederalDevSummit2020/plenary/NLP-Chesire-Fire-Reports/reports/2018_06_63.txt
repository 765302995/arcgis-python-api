 Hedge fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/06/2018 - 17:16 Location: Enfield Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a hedge fire on Enfield Road, Ellesmere Port. The fire had been caused by discarded ash and crews used two hose reel jets to put out the fire which had affected approximately two metres of hedge.
