 Fire at Brewers Fayre in Crewe Time of Incident: 22/04/2018 - 13:22 Location: Weston Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At around 1.20pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at the Brocklebank Brewers Fayre on Weston Road in Crewe. The fire involving a dishcloth was out on arrival. Crews used a large fan to clear smoke from the building. Firefighters were in attendance for more than 20 minutes.
