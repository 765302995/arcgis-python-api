Collision in Congleton Time of Incident: 07/03/2015 - 12:59 Location: Newcastle Road, Congleton. Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Newcastle Road, Congleton.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved one car and one male casualty. Crews helped ensure he got out of the care safely and into the care of the ambulance service.
