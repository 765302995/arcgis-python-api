Grass cutting machine on fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 10/04/2015 - 00:46 Location: Kellhouse Lane, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish a fire involving a grass cutting machine on the back of a trailer in Knutsford.  The machine was well alight on arrival of the crew who damped down the scene after putting out the blaze.
