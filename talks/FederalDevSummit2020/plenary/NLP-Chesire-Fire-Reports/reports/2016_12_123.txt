Coal burning fire out of control in Middlewich Time of Incident: 06/12/2016 - 15:34 Location: Newton Heath, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Winsford A coal burning fire had got out of control and was made safe by firefighters. The crew removed embers from the grate and used a high pressure fan to clear the smoke that was filling the lounge.  Always follow our home fire safety information.
