 Car fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 15:27 Location: M6 southbound, between junctions 18 and 17 Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Middlewich, one from Sandbach and one from Crewe At around 3.25pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on the M6 southbound between junctions 18 and 17. On arrival crews discovered a vehicle well alight on a works access lane. Part of the motorway was cordoned off as firefighters worked at the scene. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Fire crews were in attendance for around 35 minutes.
