Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/09/2016 - 15:45 Location: Lovely Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Warrington A road traffic collision involving two cars with one person trapped required firefighters to remove the roof of one of the vehicles, to get a man out and to the care of paramedics. Air Ambulance attended and cordons were set up and maintained by police who closed Lovely Lane while the incident was dealt with.
