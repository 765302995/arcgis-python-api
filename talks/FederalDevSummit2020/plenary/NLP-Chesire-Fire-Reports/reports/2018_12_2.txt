 House fire in Chester Time of Incident: 29/12/2018 - 13:25 Location: Burton Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and two from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a fire in the living room of a property on Burton Road, Chester. The fire involved a wicker basket containing logs and had spread to the curtains. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a large fan to put out the fire and clear smoke from the property.  The fire was started accidentally, no-one was injured and crews were detained for 55 miniutes.
