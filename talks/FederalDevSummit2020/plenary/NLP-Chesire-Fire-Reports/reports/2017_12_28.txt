Electrical fire in an outbuilding Time of Incident: 22/12/2017 - 20:02 Location: Hayhurst Avennue, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one fire engine from Holmes Chapel. Shortly after 8pm firefighters were called to reports of a tumble dryer fire in an outbuilding at an address in Middlewich. On arrival the fire was found to  have been extinguished. Firefighters removed the machine from the outbuilding and used water to damp down the scene. For more information about tumble dryer safety visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/tumble-dryer-fire-safety
