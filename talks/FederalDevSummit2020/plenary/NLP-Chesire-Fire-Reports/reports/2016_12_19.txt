Building fire in Bollington Time of Incident: 28/12/2016 - 13:05 Location: Clarence Road, Bollington Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington and one from Macclesfield Firefighters tackled a fire on the ground floor of a former cotton mill on Clarence Road. The fire involved a rubber grinding machine which firefighters, two in breathing apparatus, put out using a hose reel jet. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check for any fire spread.  After the fire, which was kept contained to the grinding machinery, was extinguished the crews damped down the area  People from apartments on the upper floors evacuated the building as a precaution while the fire was dealt with and firefighters were at the scene for around four hours.
