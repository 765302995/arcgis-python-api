 Boiler fire at Nothwich bungalow Time of Incident: 21/12/2018 - 08:38 Location: Booth Bed Lane, Northwich Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Holmes Chapel, one from Congleton and one from Northwich Firefighters attended following reports of a fire at a detached bungalow on Booth Bed Lane in Northwich. The fire involved a fired central heating boiler and all persons were accounted for. Four firefighters wearing four breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and then examined the affected area with a thermal imaging camera. A large high pressure fan was used to disperse smoke.
