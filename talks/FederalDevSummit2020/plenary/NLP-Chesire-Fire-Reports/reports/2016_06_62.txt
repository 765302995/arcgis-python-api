Chimney fire in St Andrews, Macclesfield Time of Incident: 18/06/2016 - 20:32 Location: Tudor Drive in Mottram, St Andrews, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one fire engine from Cheadle (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) There was a chimney fire at a property on Tudor Drive in Mottram, St Andrews, Macclesfield. Fire crews used two breathing apparatus, one jet and a large fan to extinguish the fire and ventilate the property.  Chimney fire safety tips
