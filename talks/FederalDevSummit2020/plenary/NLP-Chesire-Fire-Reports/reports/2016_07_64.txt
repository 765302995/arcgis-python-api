Small fire involving gate in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/07/2016 - 20:58 Location: 15 Marsh House Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a fire involving a gate which is believed to have been deliberately set on fire. The crew used a hose reel to extinguish it. Call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 if you know who may have started this fire.
