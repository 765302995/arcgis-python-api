 Small fire at a park in Macclesfield. Time of Incident: 16/11/2019 - 21:14 Location: Park Grove, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Firefighters from Poynton used a hose reel jet to extinguish a small fire which had been started deliberately at a skate park in Macclesfield.  The crew were detained at the incident for around 20 minutes.  Anyone with any information in relation to person who started the fire is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
