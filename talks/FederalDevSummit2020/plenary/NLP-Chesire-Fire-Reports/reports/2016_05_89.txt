Barbecue gas cylinder fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 14/05/2016 - 17:39 Location: Chepstow Close, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Offerton (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) The gas cylinder from a barbecue was on fire. A hose reel jet was used by firefighters to put out the fire.  To enjoy a safe barbecue always follow our safety advice
