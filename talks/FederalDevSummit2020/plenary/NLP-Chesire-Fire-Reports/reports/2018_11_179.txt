 Large bonfire fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/11/2018 - 17:51 Location: Steel Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to a bonfire which had got out of hand in Steel street, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel to diminish the size and advised the owner to make sure it didn't get bigger.
