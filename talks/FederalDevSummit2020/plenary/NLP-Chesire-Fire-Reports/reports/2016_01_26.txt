Gas leak in Crewe Time of Incident: 23/01/2016 - 02:24 Location: Broad Street, Crewe Attendance: Fire engines from Nantwich, Alsager and Crewe Fire engines from Nantwich, Alsager and Crewe were sent to a reported gas leak incident on Broad Street in Crewe. Fire crews, police and ambulance attended along with a gas engineer. Firefighters set up two covering jets as a precaution and residents were evacuated whilst the gas board investigate, to find the source and stop the leak. Fire crews were on the scene for two hours.
