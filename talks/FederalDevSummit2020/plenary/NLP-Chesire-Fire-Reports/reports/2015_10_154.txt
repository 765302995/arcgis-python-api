House fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 02/10/2015 - 19:07 Location: Drakelow Lane/Moss Lane, Byley, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Holmes Chapel Firefighters isolated the power to a boiler which was on fire in a garage in Middlewich.  The crew allowed the boiler to cool and were at the scene for around one hour.
