 Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 23/04/2019 - 20:33 Location: Watkinson Way, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly after 8.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on Watkinson Way in Widnes. On arrival the crew found a BMW well alight. Firefighters closed a lane of the carriageway as they worked at the scene. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The crew also isolated the car's battery. Once the fire was out firefighters handed the incident over to the police. Firefighters were in attendance for less than an hour.
