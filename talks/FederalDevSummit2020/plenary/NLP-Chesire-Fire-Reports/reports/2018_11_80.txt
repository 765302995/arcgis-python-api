 Fire in woodland, Warrington Time of Incident: 17/11/2018 - 00:23 Location: Nr Bembridge Close, Great Sankey Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh Firefighters were called to reports of a small fire in woodland near to Bembridge Close. Access to the location was difficult, but once on the scene they were able to quickly extinguish the flames. They notified police colleagues of the suspected deliberate ignition.
