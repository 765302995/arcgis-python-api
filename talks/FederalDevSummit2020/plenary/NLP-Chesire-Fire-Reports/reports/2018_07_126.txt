 Collision in Chelford Time of Incident: 15/07/2018 - 15:24 Location: Knutsford Road, Chelford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters attended to a road traffic collision on Knutsford Road in Chelford involving a car and a HGV.  Crews used a trifor to slowly move the car from the wall so the driver could remove herself from the vehicle. She was then handed to the care of paramedics and taken to hospital.
