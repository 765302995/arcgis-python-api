Car fire in Willington Time of Incident: 16/07/2016 - 13:24 Location: Willington Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley A fire involving a car was suspected to have been started deliberately. The car was burning in a field and a nearby hedgerow had also caught fire. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fires and then damped the area down.  Police also attended and anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
