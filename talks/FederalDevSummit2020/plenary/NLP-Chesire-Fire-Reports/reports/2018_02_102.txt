 Fire in a disused barn in Alsager Time of Incident: 06/02/2018 - 01:56 Location: Linley Lane, Alsager Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar, one from Sandbach and one from Staffordshire Fire and Rescue Service A fire in a disused barn on Linley Lane, Alsager, was put out by firefighters. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets and a thermal imaging camera to fight the fire. They were in attendance for two hours.
