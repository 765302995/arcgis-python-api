House fire in Fairfield, Warrington Time of Incident: 16/04/2016 - 20:11 Location: Hume Street, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington There was a well developed fire inside the living room of a property on Hume Street in Fairfield, Warrington . Police attended this incident and closed the road outside the property.  Firefighters extinguished the fire using one hose reel jet.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the property. The fire caused damage to the gas and electricity supply.  Gas and electricity engineers attended to isolate the damaged supply. Firefighters were on the scene for 3.5 hours.
