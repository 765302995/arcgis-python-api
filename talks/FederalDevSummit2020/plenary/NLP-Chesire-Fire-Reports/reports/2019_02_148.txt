 Small unattended bonfire in Oakhangher Time of Incident: 31/01/2019 - 19:16 Location: Holmshaw Lane, Oakhanger, near Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar Firefighters from Alsager used one hose reel jet to extinguish a small bonfire in a field off Holmshaw Lane, in Oakhanger, near Crewe.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately 30 minutes.
