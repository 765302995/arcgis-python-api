Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 29/03/2018 - 20:37 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes. Firefighters from Widnes used one hose reel jet to extinguish a wheelie bin fire on Dundalk Road, Widnes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101.
