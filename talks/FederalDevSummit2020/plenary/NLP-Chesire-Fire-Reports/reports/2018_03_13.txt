 House fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 28/03/2018 - 16:39 Location: Hesketh Drive, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm and one from Northwich Fire crews were called to a house fire in Hesketh Drive, Northwich. The fire was in the living room of the property and crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, tackled the blaze using one hose reel jet, a covering jet and a large fan.
