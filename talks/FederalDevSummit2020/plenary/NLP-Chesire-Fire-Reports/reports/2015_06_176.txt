Kitchen fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/06/2015 - 01:01 Location: Bridge View Close, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one from Runcorn Firefighters rescued a woman from the bedroom window of a house in Widnes.  The fire was in the kitchen of the property and two of the crew wore breathing apparatus and used one hose reel and a covering jet to extinguish the blaze.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke and the gas and electrics were isolated.  The woman was passed into the care of the Ambulance Service and the cause of the fire is believed to be electrical.
