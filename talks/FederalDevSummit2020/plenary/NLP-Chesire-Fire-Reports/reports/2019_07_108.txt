 Small fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/07/2019 - 20:17 Location: Rudloe Court, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Fire crews were called to a small fire in a secure compound on Rudloe Court, Warrington. Crews used a short extension ladder to gain access and an extended hose reel jet to put out the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately using aerosols. If anyone has any information please contact Cheshire Police.
