 Baling machine fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 26/02/2019 - 11:26 Location: Leslie Road, Winsford Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Winsford, one from Middlewich and one from Tarporley Firefighters attended a fire involving a baling machine in an open bay warehouse on Leslie Road in Winsford. The warehouse measured approximately 20 metres by 30 metres and contained plastics.  Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to fight the fire and isolated the electricity supply to the machine. A cover jet was also set up. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus and using a hose reel jet then removed the bales. They gained access to the material via side panels. Once the fire was under control a thermal imaging camera was used to monitor temperatures while firefighters damped down the affected areas to make them safe.  Firefighters were at the scene for around three-and-a-half hours.
