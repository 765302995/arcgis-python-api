Wheelie bins on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 28/08/2017 - 23:29 Location: Newton Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Wheelie bins were set on fire in Newton Street in what is believed to have been a deliberate incident. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and are urging anyone with any information to talk to Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
