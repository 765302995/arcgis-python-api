Sofa on fire in garden in Winsford Time of Incident: 03/05/2016 - 11:49 Location: Granville Square, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to extinguish a sofa which had been set on fire in a garden. The crew used a hose reel to extinguish the fire which had got out of control, and used water from a back pack.
