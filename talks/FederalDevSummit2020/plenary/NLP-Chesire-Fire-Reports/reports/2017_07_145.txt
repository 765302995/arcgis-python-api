Green bins on fire in Latchford, Warrington Time of Incident: 03/07/2017 - 20:47 Location: Loushers Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters used a hose reel jet and a water back pack to extinguish two small fires involving green bins on a pathway off Loushers Lane.  These fires are believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fires please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
