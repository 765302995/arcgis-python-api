 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/12/2018 - 20:54 Location: Avondale Drive, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters attended to four wheelie bins on well alight on Avondale Drive in Widnes.  A hose reel was used by crews to extinguish the fire.  For information on how to ensure your wheelie bins are kept secure please follow our safety advice https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
