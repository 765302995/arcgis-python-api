 Kitchen fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 09/06/2019 - 09:08 Location: Bradwell Grove, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Shortly after 9am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a domestic property's kitchen in Bradwell Grove, Congleton. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a microwave and everyone was accounted for. The fire was extinguished and an occupier suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation was handed over to the care of paramedics. They were taken to hospital via an ambulance as a precaution. Firefighters used a large fan to clear smoke from the property and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
