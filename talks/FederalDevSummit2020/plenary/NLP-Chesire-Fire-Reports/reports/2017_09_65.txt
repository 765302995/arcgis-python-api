Tumble dryer fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 12/09/2017 - 16:34 Location: Clydesdale, Whitby Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a tumble dryer fire on Clydesdale, Whitby, Ellesmere Port. The dryer was outside of a house on arrival of the crews and was only producing a small amount of smoke. Crews used a bucket of water to put out the fire.
