 Smoke in pumping house, Widnes Time of Incident: 05/01/2019 - 10:43 Location: Harrison Street, Widnes Attendance:  One fire engine from Widnes Crews responded to reports of smoke coming from a pumping house on Harrison Street. Crews found the smoke appeared to be caused by the electricity supply. The electrics were isolated and a positive pressure ventilation fan was used to clear the site of the smoke.
