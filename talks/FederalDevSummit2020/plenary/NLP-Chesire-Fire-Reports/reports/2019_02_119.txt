 Fuel spillage in Middlewich Time of Incident: 07/02/2019 - 09:31 Location: Webbs Lane, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At around 9.30am firefighters from Winsford were dispatched to reports of a fuel spillage on Webbs Lane, Middlewich.  Crews attended the scene and discovered that approximately 60 litres of diesel had leaked from a broken down vehicle.  The team used specialist mats to prevent further fuel from leaking into the drainage network.  They then used a hose reel jet to dilute the fuel and clear the spillage.  The environment agency have been informed of the incident.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately 40 minutes.
