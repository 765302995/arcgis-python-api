 Bush on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 17/08/2018 - 22:09 Location: Station Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Fire crews were called to a fire on Station Road, Winsford. The fire was a controlled burning which had got out of hand and had set fire to a bush. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
