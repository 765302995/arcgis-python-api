 Derelict commercial property fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/02/2018 - 06:23 Location: Hough Green Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes, Penketh and Runcorn Three fire engines attended to a fire on Hough Green Road in Widnes following reports of a commercial premises well alight. The fire was located on the ground floor and crews used one main jet, two breathing apparatus, a hose reel to extinguish the fire.
