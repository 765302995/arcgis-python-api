 Vacuum cleaner fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/01/2019 - 14:13 Location: Dennis Road, Widnes Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a fire involving a vacuum cleaner on Dennis Road in Widnes.  Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, used one extinguisher to put out the fire before removing the item and ventilating the property using a positive pressure ventilation fan.
