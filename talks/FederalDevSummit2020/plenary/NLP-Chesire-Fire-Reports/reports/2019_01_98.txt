 Tumble dryer fire in Higher Hurdsfield Time of Incident: 23/01/2019 - 19:36 Location: Cliff Lane, Higher Hurdsfield Attendance:  Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield Firefighters attended to a tumble dryer on fire at a property on Cliff Lane in Higher Hurdsfield.  On arrival, the fire had been extinguished by the owner, firefighters ensured the fire was out by using a hose reel to dampen down the tumble dryer.  One woman inside the property suffered from smoke inhalation and was given oxygen therapy.
