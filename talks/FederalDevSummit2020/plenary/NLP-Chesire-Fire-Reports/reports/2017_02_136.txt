Kitchen fire in Orford, Warrington Time of Incident: 03/02/2017 - 17:20 Location: Hastings Avenue, Orford, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Penketh & Ellesmere Port There was a kitchen fire at a mid terraced property.  Fire crews used one hose reel jet and two breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. They also used a large fan to clear the smoke. One person was given oxygen by firefighters after suffering slight smoke inhalation.
