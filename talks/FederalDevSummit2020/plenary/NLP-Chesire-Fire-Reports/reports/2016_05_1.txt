Grill fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 30/05/2016 - 04:00 Location: Dean Close, Widnes. Attendance: Fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Dean Close, Widnes.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire involving a grill but this had gone out. Crews used a fan to clear smoke from the property.
