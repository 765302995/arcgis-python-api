 Leaves and branches fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 17:17 Location: Park Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton At around 5.15pm firefighters were called to reports of leaves being on fire in the open on Park Lane in Macclesfield. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved leaves and branches near to skate ramps. They used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for less than 10 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call police on 101.
