House fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 14/05/2015 - 22:57 Location: Burnsall Drive, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one from Runcorn Firefighters extinguished a fire involving the front porch of a house in Widnes.  Two fire engines from Widnes initially went to the scene of the semi-detached house where the front porch was well alight. A third fire engine was requested for manpower and firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel jet to extinguish the blaze on the first floor, while two firefighters wearing breathing apparatuse used one hose reel and a covering jet from outside the house.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke once the fire was extinguished and the gas and electrics were isolated. The crews removed roof tiles from the porch and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. The loft was also checked and firefighters confirmed the fire had not spread into that area.  No one was injured and the house next door was evacuated while the incident was dealt with and crews were at the scene for around 90 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been caused by a discarded cigarette. Smoking / fire safety tips for smokers
