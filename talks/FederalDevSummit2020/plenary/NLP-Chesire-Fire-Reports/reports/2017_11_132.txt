Concerns for safety in Middlewich Time of Incident: 03/11/2017 - 14:30 Location: Trent & Mersey Canal, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Firefighters were called to reports of concerns for safety after a canal boat on the Trent & Mersey Canal began to lift. Crews attended the scene and gained access to the boat wearing water safety equipment. After conducting a through search of the boat it was discovered that there was nobody on board the boat. The incident has now been passed to the Canal and River Trust.
