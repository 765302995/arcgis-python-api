 Fire in the open in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 05/07/2018 - 18:40 Location: Sunbury Close, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 6.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in Sunbury Close, Wilmslow. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved tree cuttings with a mattress on top of them. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information about the incident should call police on 101, give the details via General Enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
