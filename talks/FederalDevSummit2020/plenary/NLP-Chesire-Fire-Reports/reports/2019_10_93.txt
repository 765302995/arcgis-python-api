 Small fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 16/10/2019 - 11:04 Location: Hale Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly after 11am firefighters from Widnes attended reports of a fire on Hale Road in the town. On arrival they found that a small fire had been lit by a local resident. The fire was deemed to be unsafe and a decision was taken to extinguish it using a hose reel jet. The person who started the fire was given words of advice. Firefighters were at the scene for around 20 minutes.
