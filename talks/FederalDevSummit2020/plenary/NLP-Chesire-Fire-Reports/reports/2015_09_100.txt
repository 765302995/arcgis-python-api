Small grass fire in runcorn Time of Incident: 11/09/2015 - 15:31 Location: Weston Point Expressway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to reports of a grass fire on the sliproad of the Weston Point Expressway in Runcorn. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
