 Pan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/01/2018 - 12:20 Location: Kentucky Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to reports of smoke coming from a house fire on Kentucky Close, Warrington. On arrival crews found that a pan had been left unattended and they removed the pan from the property and used a large fan to clear smoke. Cooking safety tips
