 Domestic smoke alarm in Runcorn Time of Incident: 16/07/2019 - 11:54 Location: Canal Reach, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At approximately 12pm fighters were dispatched to reports that a fire alarm had activated at a property on Canal Reach in Runcorn. On arrival firefighters discovered that the property was heavily smoke logged due to a cooker being left unattended. The occupant of the address was not present. The crew used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property and were detained for approximately 30 minutes.
