Car fire on M62 east between junctions 7 and 8 Time of Incident: 06/11/2016 - 17:52 Location: M62 eastbound between junctions 7 and 8 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Widnes, with assistance from Merseyside Fire and Rescue Service Both carriageways of the M62 near junction 7 were stopped as firefighters attended a road traffic collision. Two vehicles were involved with one car on fire.The crew, one firefighter wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. One casualty was extricated from the other car and handed into the care of the ambulance service.
