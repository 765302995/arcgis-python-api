 Electrical fault in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/01/2018 - 17:11 Location: Princes Way, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Shortly after 5pm on Sunday 21 January crews were called to reports of a small fire on Princes Way, Macclesfield. On arrival at the scene crews found that there was smoke billowing from the pavement which was believed to have been caused by an electrical fault. The team set up a cordon in the area as a precautionary measure and stayed in attendance until an electrical engineer arrived at the site.
