House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/05/2016 - 19:54 Location: Sycamore Lane, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Sycamore Lane, Warrington.   When they arrived crews found that the fire had involved a boiler which was out when they got there.  Firefighters used positive pressure ventilation to clear any smoke. There was one woman who was suffering from smoke inhalation. Crews remained with the casualty until the arrival of the ambulance.  Crews also fitted two new smoke alarms in the property as the existing one did not work and were on scene for approximately 40 minutes.
