Racing pigeon rescued in Northwich Time of Incident: 05/09/2017 - 11:24 Location: Chester Way, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford Fire crews were called to rescue a racing pigeon which had become caught in netting on Chester Way, Northwich. Crews assisted the RSPCA in rescuing the pigeon by checking the condition of a flat roof.
