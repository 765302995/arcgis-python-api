 Rubbish on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/05/2018 - 19:52 Location: Cunningham Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 7.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Cunnigham Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew found rubbish on fire and used water to put it out. Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
