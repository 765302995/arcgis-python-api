Person trapped in overturned car in Marthall Time of Incident: 25/06/2017 - 15:04 Location: Chelford Road, Marthall Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford and one from Wilmslow Overturned car in MarthallFire crews were called to a road traffic collision on Chelford Road, Marthall, after reports that a person was trapped. Two fire engines - one from Wilmslow and one from Knutsford - attended the incident and found one vehicle was overturned with a person trapped inside.  Firefighters assisted North West Ambulance crews to extricate the casualty, who was left in the care of paramedics.  Crews were at the scene for approximately one hour and fifteen minutes.
