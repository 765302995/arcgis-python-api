 Person trapped following collision near Crewe Time of Incident: 29/03/2018 - 10:37 Location: A500, near Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe attended reports of a collision involving a HGV and a transit van on the A500 near Crewe. On arrival crews used specialist electronic cutting equipment to release on casualty who was trapped in the transit van.  The man has since been passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service
