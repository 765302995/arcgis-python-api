House fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 13/11/2016 - 19:54 Location: Congleton Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield A fire in the upstairs floor of a property in Macclesfield required firefighters to attend and used a covering jet and a hose reel to extinguish it. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property. Police also attended.
