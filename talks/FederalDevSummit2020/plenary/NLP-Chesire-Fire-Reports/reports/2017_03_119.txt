Bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 11/03/2017 - 20:29 Location: Ways Green in Winsford Attendance: A fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters tackled a commercial wheelie bin fire on Ways Green in Winsford with a hose reel jet.  It is believed that the fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
