 Fire in the open in Frodsham Time of Incident: 29/06/2018 - 08:44 Location: Bellemonte Road, Frodsham. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At approximately 8.45am firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a field off Bellemonte Road in Frodsham. The fire involved approximately 20 square metres of peat/ grassland which was well alight. The team used one main jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately one hour 45 minutes.
