 One vehicle collision in Dutton Time of Incident: 22/08/2019 - 06:44 Location: Barkers Hollow Road, Dutton Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Runcorn and Lymm At around 6.45am firefighters from Runcorn and Lymm were called to reports of a collision on Barkers Hollow Road, Dutton.  On arrival the team found the incident involved one vehicle which was on its side. The driver of the car was trapped in the vehicle.  Firefighters stabilised the car, they then used specialist cutting equipment to release the driver. He was then passed to the care of paramedics.   The crews were in attendance for one hour and ten minutes.
