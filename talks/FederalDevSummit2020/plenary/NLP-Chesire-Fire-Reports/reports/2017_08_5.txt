Fire at rear of Rudheath properties Time of Incident: 30/08/2017 - 16:24 Location: Malpas Road, Rudheath, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich .Firefighters have been tackling a fire at the back of some properties on Malpas Road. They used two hose reel jets to bring the fire, which did not involve any nearby properties, under control. The fire was caused by uncontrolled burning of garden refuse.
