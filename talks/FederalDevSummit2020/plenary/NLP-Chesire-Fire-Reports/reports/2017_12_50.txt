Collision on the M56 eastbound Time of Incident: 14/12/2017 - 21:48 Location: M56 Eastbound near junction 11 Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Whilst in attendance at a collision on the M56 westbound crews became aware of a collision on the eastbound carriageway. Firefighters attended and found the collision involved two saloon cars. There were no people trapped and the crew assisted by making the vehicle safe.
