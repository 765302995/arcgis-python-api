Hay baler fire in Chester Time of Incident: 09/07/2015 - 19:34 Location: Aldford Road, Huntington, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port. Firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a hay baler at Aldford Road, Huntington, Chester.  Firefighters used two hose reels to put the blaze out. Beaters were also utilised to prevent fire spread. Crews were engaged at scene approximately an hour and a half.
