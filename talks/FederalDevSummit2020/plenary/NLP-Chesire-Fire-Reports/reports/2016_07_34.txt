Car and tractor in collision in High Legh Time of Incident: 23/07/2016 - 11:43 Location: Warrington Road, High Legh Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington, Stockton Heath and Winsford A car and tractor were involved in a road traffic collision in Warrington Road. Nobody was trapped but there were two casualties from the car who were handed into the care of ambulance staff.  Firefighters made the scene safe and police closed nearby roads while the incident was dealt with.
