Large vehicle on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 19/04/2016 - 15:50 Location: Bridges Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port, one from Chester and one from Frodsham Firefighters were called to a large mechanical machine on fire which had spread to a pile of peat. Hose reel jets and foam were used to damp down the surrounding area and extinguish it.  Four fire engines attended for water supplies.
