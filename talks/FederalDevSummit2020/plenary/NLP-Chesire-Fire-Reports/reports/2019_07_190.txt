 Hedgerow and fence fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 30/06/2019 - 17:15 Location: Hawthorn Road, Little Sutton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At 5.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open at the back of a domestic property on Hawthorn Road in Little Sutton, Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew found a hedgerow and a fence panel well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They also checked if the fire had spread to a garage and a shed near to the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 442939, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
