Collision involving HGV on M6 Time of Incident: 30/01/2015 - 03:07 Location: J18 M6 south Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Holmes Chapel A HGV which had jackknifed on the M6 was made safe by firefighters.  Initially there was concern the vehicle was leaking diesel but fortunately this was not the case and the crews ensured it was safe before leaving.
