 Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 03/11/2018 - 17:01 Location: Pembroke Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters were called to a kitchen fire in Pembroke Road, Macclesfield. Crews used to breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the fire. They then used a large fan to disperse the flames. One casualty was given first aid at the scene and handed over to paramedics.
