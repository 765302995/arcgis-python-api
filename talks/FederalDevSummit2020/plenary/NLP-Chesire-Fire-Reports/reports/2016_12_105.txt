Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 11/12/2016 - 01:31 Location: Birchfield Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes It is believed that a car fire on Birchfield Road was started deliberately. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and asked police to attend.  If you have any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
