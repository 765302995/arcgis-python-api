 Bathroom fire in Moston Time of Incident: 31/12/2017 - 20:34 Location: Mill Lane, Moston Attendance: One fire from Sandbach and one from Middlewich Firefighters were called to a fire in a bathroom of a house on Mill Lane, Moston. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a large fan to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for one hour. The fire was accidental.
