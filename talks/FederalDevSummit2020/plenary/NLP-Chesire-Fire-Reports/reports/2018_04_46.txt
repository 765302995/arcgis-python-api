 Fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 21/04/2018 - 03:01 Location: Downside, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes, Penketh and Runcorn Firefighters attended to a garden fire and a flat heavily smoke logged on Downside in Widnes. The fire involved a quantity of refuse against the building and three people were led to safety by firefighters.  Crews used breathing apparatus and ventilated the property and also administered oxygen therapy to one person.
