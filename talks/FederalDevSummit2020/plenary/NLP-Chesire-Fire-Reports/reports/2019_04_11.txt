 Wheelie bin fire in widnes Time of Incident: 27/04/2019 - 16:45 Location: Bancroft Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Penketh and one from Runcorn Firefighters attended to a wheelie bin on fire inside a garage on Bancroft Road in Widnes.  Crews used one hose reel to extinguish the fire and made the area safe.  They remained at the scene for forty minutes.  To ensure your wheelie bin is kept safe and secure follow our simple tips https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
