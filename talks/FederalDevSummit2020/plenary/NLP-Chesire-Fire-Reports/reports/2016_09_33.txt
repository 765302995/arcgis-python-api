House fire in Chester Time of Incident: 24/09/2016 - 19:57 Location: Rake Lane, Little Stanney in Chester. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire off Rake Lane, Little Stanney in Chester.  When they arrived crews were confronted with a fire in the living room of the property. They used two breathing apparatus, one covering jet and one hose reel jet to deal with the incident.  A Home Safety Assessment was also carried out.   Crews were at the scene for about one hour and fifteen minutes.
