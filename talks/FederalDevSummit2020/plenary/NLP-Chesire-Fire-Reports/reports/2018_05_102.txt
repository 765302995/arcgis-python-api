 Fire in a manhole in a field in Handforth Time of Incident: 17/05/2018 - 20:31 Location: Knowle Park, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 8.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a field in Knowle Park, Handforth. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved tree debris in a manhole. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots in the manhole. Firefighters were at the scene for around 45 minutes. Police were also in attendance.
