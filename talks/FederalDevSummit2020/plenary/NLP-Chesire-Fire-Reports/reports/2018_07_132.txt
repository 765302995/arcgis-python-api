 Fire in field in Tarporley Time of Incident: 14/07/2018 - 20:44 Location: Nantwich Road, Tarporley. CW69HN Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich and one from Chester Fire crews were called to reports of a fire in a field near to an electrical substation. The fire appeared to have been extinguished on arrival, but firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to make sure the fire was out.
