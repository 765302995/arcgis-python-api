House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 17/05/2017 - 02:26 Location: Selworthy Drive, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Nantwich Three fire engines were called to a fire involving a two storey semi detached domestic property with persons reported. Firefighters used two hose reels and one covering jet to fight the fire. Everyone in the property was accounted for and paramedics attended.
