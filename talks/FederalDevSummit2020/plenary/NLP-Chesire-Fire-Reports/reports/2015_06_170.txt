Truck on fire in garage in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/06/2015 - 18:56 Location: Hawthorne Avenue, Woolston, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters extinguished a fire involving a pick up truck inside a garage in Warrington.  The crew wore breathing apparatus and used one hose reel to put out the fire and the truck was removed from the garage. A large fan was then used to clear the smoke from the building.
