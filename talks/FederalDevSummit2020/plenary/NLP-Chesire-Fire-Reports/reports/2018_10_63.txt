 Kitchen fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 21/10/2018 - 09:37 Location: Church Lane, Wistaston Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to a fire in a kitchen of a two storey house on Church Lane, Wistaston. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet, a covering jet and a large fan to extinguish the fire and remove smoke from the property. Crews were at the scene for 50 minutes. The fire is believed to have been caused by a faulty toaster.
