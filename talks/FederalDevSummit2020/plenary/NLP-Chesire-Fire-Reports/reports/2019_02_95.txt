 Fire at a public house in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 14/02/2019 - 00:08 Location: Wolverham Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after midnight firefighters from Ellesmere Port were called to reports of a fire at a public house on Wolverham Road.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a fire in a first floor living room.  Four firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  They then used a large fan to clear all of the smoke from the property and a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  The team were in attendance at the address for approximately three hours.
