House fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 18/06/2017 - 09:56 Location: Carrwood Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle Firefighters were called to a detached house on fire and on arrival found the ground floor involved. The crews used one hose reel to extinguish the flames and a large fan to remove some from the property. There was no one in the property at the time and smoke alarms were fitted and neighbours apparently raised the alarm.
