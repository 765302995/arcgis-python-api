Shed and bushes on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 26/05/2015 - 21:42 Location: Malpas Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich Bushes were set on fire which then spread to the shed of a property in Northwich.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.  Police also attended as it is believed the fire was started deliberately.  If you saw anyone lighting this fire, or have any information about this incident, please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
