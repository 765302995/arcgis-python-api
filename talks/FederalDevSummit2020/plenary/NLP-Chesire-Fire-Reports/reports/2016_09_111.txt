Tumble dryer fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 08/09/2016 - 13:46 Location: Moss bank, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford Firefighters were called to a fire involving a tumble dryer in a domestic property in Winsford. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet backed by one safety jet to put out the fire. They then carried out a full home safety assessment on the property.   There are various ways in which tumble dryer fires can start but the good news is there's a number of things you can do to prevent yours from going up in flames.
