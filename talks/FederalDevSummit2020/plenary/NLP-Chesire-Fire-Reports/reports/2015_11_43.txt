Collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 21/11/2015 - 18:55 Location: Bradford Road, Winsford. Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Bradford Road in Winsford.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved one car. A man was trapped in the vehicle.  Crews used special cutting equipment to free him. He was then passed to the care of the ambulance service.
