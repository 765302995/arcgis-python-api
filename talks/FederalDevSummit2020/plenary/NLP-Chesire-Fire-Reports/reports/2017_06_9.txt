Turkey coop on fire in Willaston Time of Incident: 27/06/2017 - 00:24 Location: Mill Lane, Willaston Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port, one from Powey Lane and one from Merseyside Fire and Rescue Service (Bromborough). Turkey coop on fire in Willaston Fire crews were called to a farm where a turkey coop was on fire on Mill Lane, Willaston. On arrival crews found a single storey coop, approximately 50m by 10m, well alight. There were no birds inside the coop at the time of the fire.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used three main jets to put out the fire.  It is believed that this fire may have been started deliberately. If anyone knows who may have started it then please call Cheshire Police on 101 of Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
