 Pigeon loft fire in Handforth Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 02:22 Location: Spath Lane, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a pigeon loft well alight in a garden on Spath Lane in Handforth.  Crews, wearing one breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets and one 45 millimetre jet to help put out the blaze.  A thermal imaging camera was used to look at the impact the fire had caused to neighbouring properties after windows cracked and a shed had also caught fire.    A number of pigeons are believed to have died in the fire.  The cause of the fire is unknown and is under investigation.
