 One-vehicle collision in Disley Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 05:39 Location: Buxton Road, Disley Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Poynton and one from Stockport Shortly after 5.30am firefighters were called to reports of a one-vehicle collision on Buxton Road in Disley. The two female occupants were out of the car when crews arrived. A trauma technician assessed one of the casualties at the scene before the crews handed them over to the care of paramedics. The casualties were taken to hospital via ambulance. Firefighters left the incident in the hands of the police and were in attendance for around an hour.
