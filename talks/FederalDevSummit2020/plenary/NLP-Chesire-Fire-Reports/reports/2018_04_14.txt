 Industrial paper shredder fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/04/2018 - 21:07 Location: Pickerings Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Widnes and one from Runcorn At around 9.05pm firefighters were called to a fire at Grundy Skip Hire on Pickerings Road in Widnes. On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved an industrial paper shredder. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire, which is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault on the machinery. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
