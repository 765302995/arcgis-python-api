 Smoke-filled flat in Northwich Time of Incident: 16/06/2019 - 19:14 Location: Heber Walk, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 7.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke-filled flat in Heber Walk, Northwich. The crew gained entry and found that no-one was inside the flat and that the smoke had been caused by cooking food having been left unattended. Firefighters ventilated the property and were in attendance for more than 45 minutes. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
