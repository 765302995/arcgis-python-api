 One person trapped in a car in Northwich Time of Incident: 22/10/2018 - 09:56 Location: Wallerscote Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision between two vehicles on Wallerscote Road, Northwich. One casualty was trapped in a car and crews helped released the casualty from the vehicle and hand them into the care of paramedics.
