 Fire in a cellar at a commercial premises in Chester Time of Incident: 10/04/2019 - 23:17 Location: Liverpool Road, Upton, Chester Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Chester, one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port At around 11.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at commercial premises on Liverpool Road in Upton, Chester. On arrival at the scene, the crews discovered that the fire involved an electricity box in the cellar and that everyone was accounted for. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet and a dry powder extinguisher to put out the fire. A large fan was used to clear smoke from the premises. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
