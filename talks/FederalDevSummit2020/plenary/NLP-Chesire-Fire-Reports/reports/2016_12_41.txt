Kitchen fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 23/12/2016 - 19:13 Location: Westminster Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish a fire inside the kitchen of a property on Westminster Street in Crewe.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the property.  The fire was caused by an unattended pan that had been left on the stove.
