Car fire on M6 Time of Incident: 18/01/2015 - 12:43 Location: J18, M6 north. Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel, one from Knutsford and one from Middlewich Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus and one hose reel was used to extinguish a car on fire on the hardshoulder of the M6. Police and the Highways Agency put a rolling road block in place while the incident was dealt with.
