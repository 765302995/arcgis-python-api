 Fire at a building site in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/02/2019 - 19:36 Location: Manchester Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Penketh Shortly after 7.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on Manchester Road in Warrington. On arrival crews found a large amount of timber well alight at a building site by Padgate Lane and Marsh Street. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which did not spread to a building nearby, and were in attendance for around 45 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started accidentally as a result of sparks from cutting steel.
