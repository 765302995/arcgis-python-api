Combine harvester on fire in High Legh Time of Incident: 13/09/2015 - 18:26 Location: Moss Lane, High Legh Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Stockton Heath A combine harvester was well alight in the middle of a corn field. A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire and then to cool the machinery. Because of the remote location of the incident a water shuttle had to be set up by firefighters. Crews were at the scene for around two hours.
