Smoke alarm alerts neighbour to kitchen fire in Chester Time of Incident: 15/05/2017 - 18:43 Location: Penmon Close, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Powey Lane A man was escorted from a property in Chester by firefighters, after a smoke alarm sounding alerted neighbours to fire. The small fire was out on the arrival of crews who gained access and removed the man from the property. The fire occurred in the kitchen which was smoke logged.
