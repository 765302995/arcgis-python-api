 Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/07/2018 - 23:02 Location: Dunsford, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to a car fire on Dunsford, Widnes. Crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which was well alight on arrival of the firefighters. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire then please call Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
