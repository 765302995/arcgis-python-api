 Small kitchen fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 26/12/2018 - 21:53 Location: Main Street, Frodsham. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Widnes. At approximately 9.50pm firefighters from Widnes and Runcorn were dispatched to reports of a kitchen fire in Frodsham. On arrival at the scene the team discovered a small fire involving the oven. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish the fire and then isolated the gas supply to the property. Crews were on scene for approximately one hour.
