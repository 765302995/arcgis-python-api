Tree fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/05/2017 - 21:08 Location: Brownhill Drive, Padgate, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A water backpack and beaters were used by firefighters to put out a tree fire.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately so anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
