 Domestic building fire in Chester Time of Incident: 19/07/2018 - 12:20 Location: Nicholas Street Mews, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane along with an aerial platform from Crewe Firefighters from Powey Lane and Crewe attended reports of a fire at an address on Nicholas Street Mews, Chester. On arrival at the scene crews found that an unattended pan had overheated and caused the smoke alarm to activate. The crews removed the pan from the property and used a large fan to clear the smoke. The team were detained at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.
