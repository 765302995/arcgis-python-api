 Wheelie bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 04/03/2018 - 02:22 Location: Stephenson Walk, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Firefighters from Middlewich attended reports of a small fire in Winsford. On arrival the crew found that the fire involved two wheelie bins which had been deliberately set on fire. Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
