Unattended cooker in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/07/2016 - 22:09 Location: Croft Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to an unattended cooker which had been on fire but out on their arrival. The crew used a large fan to remove smoke from the property before leaving. Please take extra care when cooking and do not leave unattended.  Kitchen fire safety advice
