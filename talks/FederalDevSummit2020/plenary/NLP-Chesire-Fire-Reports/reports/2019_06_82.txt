 Person trapped after road traffic collision in Wilkesley Time of Incident: 13/06/2019 - 09:13 Location: Wilkesley Lane, Wilkesley Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Winsford Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision between a car and a lorry on Wilkesley Lane, Wilkesley, where one person was trapped. A woman who was trapped in a car was assisted from the vehicle by firefighters. She was then handed into the care of paramedics with suspected minor injuries.
