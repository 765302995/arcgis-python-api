 Man stuck in tree, Widnes Time of Incident: 02/11/2019 - 13:28 Location: Bechers, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes The Fire Service was called after a man got stuck 20ft high in a tree, trying to rescue a cat. The man was slipping due to the tree being wet, so the crew used a short extension ladder to bring both him and the cat to safety.
