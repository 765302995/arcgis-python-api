Tyre on fire on wasteland in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 27/08/2015 - 20:33 Location: Wasteland near to Hooton station by Hooten Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port One hose reel jet was used to extinguish a fire involving a tyre on wasteland near to Hooten Station in Ellesmere Port. Firefighters had to force entry to get onto the site.
