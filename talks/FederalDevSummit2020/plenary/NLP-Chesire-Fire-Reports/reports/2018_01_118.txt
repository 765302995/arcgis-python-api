 Electrical fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 01/01/2018 - 12:57 Location: Grove Park, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm, one from Wilmslow and one from Altrincham (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Fire crews were called to an electrical fire in the living room of a detached house on Grove Park, Knutsford. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a large fan to put out the fire. Crews were at the scene for two hours.
