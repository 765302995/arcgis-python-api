Road traffic collision on M62 motorway near to J9 Winwick Time of Incident: 14/03/2017 - 17:28 Location: M62 motorway (eastbound) near to J9 in Croft Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Birchwood There was a road traffic collision, involving numerous vehicles, in the third lane of the M62 motorway (eastbound) near to Junction 9 in Winwick, Warrington. One vehicle had over turned. One casualty was transported to hospital by ambulance.  Air ambulance also attended.  Police temporarily closed the road in both directions.  The incident was left with Police and Highways Agency.
