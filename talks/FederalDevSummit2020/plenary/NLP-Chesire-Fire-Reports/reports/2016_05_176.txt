Bathroom fire in Chester Time of Incident: 01/05/2016 - 11:07 Location: Poole Lane, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one fire engine from Chester Two fire crews from Ellesmere Port and one fire crew from Chester were sent to a fire involving a first floor bathroom inside a property on Poole Lane, Thornton le Moors, Chester. Firefighters used two hose reels, one covering jet and a large fan to fight the fire. Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus. The fire crews were at the incident for approximately four hours and 30 minutes. A fire investigation will be completed to find the cause of the fire.
