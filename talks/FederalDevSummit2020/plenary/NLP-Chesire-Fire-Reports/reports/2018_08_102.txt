 Small grass fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 09/08/2018 - 16:55 Location: Shellway Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 4.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a grass fire on Shellway Road in Ellesmere Port. The crew extinguished the small fire with water and were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
