 Religious building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/10/2018 - 09:55 Location: Hob Hey Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood, as did one from Leigh At around 9.55am firefighters were called to reports of a fire at the Culcheth Christian Fellowship building on Hob Hey Lane in Warrington.  On arrival crews discovered smoke logging in the building and signs of fire.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus were deployed with a hose reel jet for further investigation.  Crews discovered that the fire was caused by an electrical fault on a switchboard.  An electrical company was called to isolate the electricity supply to the building.  Firefighters used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the building and were in attendance for around two hours and 20 minutes.
