 Collision in Chester Time of Incident: 25/11/2018 - 23:11 Location: Marsh Lane, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port. Firefighters were called to a collision in Marsh Lane, Chester. A car had left the road and ended up in a drainage ditch. Two people were stranded on top of the car and were rescued by firefighters. There aren't believed to be any injuries.
