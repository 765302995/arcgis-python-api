 House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/08/2018 - 17:48 Location: Archers Green Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a house fire on Archers Green Road, Warrington. The fire was in a bedroom on the first floor of the property and crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.  Crews were at the scene for three hours. An investigation into the cause of the fire is ongoing.
