Car fire in Kelsall Time of Incident: 09/10/2016 - 01:53 Location: Chester Road, Kelsall Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester A car fire on Chester Road is believed to have been started deliberately. Two hose reel jets were used to bring the fire, which also affected the outside of the house, under control.   Anyone one with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
