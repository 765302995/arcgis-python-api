 Empty flat fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 18/03/2019 - 08:56 Location: Wheelock Street, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich. One fire engine attended from Wisnford. Firefighters were called to an empty first floor flat above an empty shop in Wheelock Street in Middlewich following reports of a fire. Crews gained entry to the flat and used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel to put our the fire which involved paper.
