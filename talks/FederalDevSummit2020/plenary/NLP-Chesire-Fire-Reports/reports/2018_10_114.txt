 Cooker fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/10/2018 - 15:04 Location: Leonard Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to reports of smoke coming from an address in Leonard Street, Warrington. Crews discovered a cooker left unattended causing smoke. A man was treated for slight smoke inhalation and an ambulance was called as a precaution. Crews used a large fan to disperse the smoke.  Follow a few simple measures to prevent a fire while cooking to keep you and your loved ones safe https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/home/cooking-safety-tips
