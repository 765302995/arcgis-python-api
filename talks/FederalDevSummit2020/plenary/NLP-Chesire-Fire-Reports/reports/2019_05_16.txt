 Boy stuck on top of a climbing boulder in Widnes park Time of Incident: 28/05/2019 - 13:30 Location: Birchfield Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 1.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a boy being stuck at the top of a climbing boulder at Victoria Park on Birchfield Road in Widnes. The crew used a ladder to enable the boy to get down from the boulder. Firefighters were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
