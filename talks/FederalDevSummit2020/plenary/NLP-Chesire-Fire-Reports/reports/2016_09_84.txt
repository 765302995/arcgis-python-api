Outbuilding fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/09/2016 - 08:40 Location: Manchester Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one fire engine from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a fire in an outbuilding with reports of a person inside. When they arrived on the scene, they found all persons were accounted for. Crews used one hose reel jet to put out the fire. The occupant of the building was handed into the care of paramedics.
