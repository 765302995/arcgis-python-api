 Unattended garden fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 01/05/2019 - 07:51 Location: Castlefields, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Runcorn firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish a small unattended fire in the rear garden of a residential property.  The team were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
