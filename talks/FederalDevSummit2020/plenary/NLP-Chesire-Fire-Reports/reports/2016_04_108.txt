Rubbish on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/04/2016 - 20:03 Location: Newhaven Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters put out a fire in the open using a hose reel jet. The fire, in a corn field, was believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about this, or similar fires, please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.  April is Arson Awareness Month #StopArson
