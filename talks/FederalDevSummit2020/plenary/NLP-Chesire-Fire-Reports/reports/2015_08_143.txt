Fence fire in Neston Time of Incident: 08/08/2015 - 14:16 Location: Quarry Road, Neston. Attendance: A fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Quarry Road, Neston.  When they arrived crews found that there was an amount of rubbish on fire next to a fence. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.
