 Smoke at a flat in Chester Time of Incident: 14/08/2019 - 12:00 Location: Norris Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Chester and one from Powey Lane At 12 noon firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a flat on Norris Road in Chester. On arrival crews discovered that the alarm had been activated as a result of toast being burnt. Firefighters ventilated the flat to clear the smoke and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
