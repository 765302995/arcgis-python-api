 Rubbish fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 18/09/2019 - 22:32 Location: Shurlach Lane, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich. Firefighters were called to a large amount of rubbish on fire on land off Shurlach Lane, Northwich. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
