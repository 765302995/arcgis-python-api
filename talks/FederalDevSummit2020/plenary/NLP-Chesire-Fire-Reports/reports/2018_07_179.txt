 Small fire in Duddon Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 12:16 Location: Tarporley Road, Duddon. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley At 12:16 firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a construction site on Tarporley Road, Duddon. On arrival crews found a small a small fire which had been left unattended. The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes.
