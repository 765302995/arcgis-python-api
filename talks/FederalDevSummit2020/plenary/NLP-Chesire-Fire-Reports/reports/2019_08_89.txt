 Kerosene leak at a farm Time of Incident: 18/08/2019 - 18:33 Location: Crooked Yard Road, Macclesfield Forest Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Shortly after 6.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a spillage of kerosene at a farm on Crooked Yard Road, Macclesfield Forest. On arrival the crews discovered that the kerosene had leaked from a tank onto the ground. The leak was from a pump. Firefighters isolated the pump. They also liaised with the Environment Agency and used specialist pads to prevent the kerosene from contaminating nearby water courses and going into drains. Firefighters were in attendance for around two hours.
