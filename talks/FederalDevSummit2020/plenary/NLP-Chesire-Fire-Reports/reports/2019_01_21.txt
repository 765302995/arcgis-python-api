 Waste bin fire in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 07/01/2019 - 11:23 Location: Sandiford Road, Holmes Chapel Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters attended to reports of a waste bin on fire in a kitchen on Sandiford Road in Holmes Chapel.  The fire, believed to have been caused by smoking materials, was put out by a positive pressure ventilation fan.  One man was handed to the care of paramedics.
