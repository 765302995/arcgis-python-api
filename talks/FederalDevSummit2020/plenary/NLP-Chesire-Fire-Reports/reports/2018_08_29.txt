 House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/08/2018 - 07:14 Location: Stoneley Road, Crewe Attendance: Four fire engines attended from Alsager, Crewe, Sandbach and Winsford At 07:14 am on Saturday 25 four fire engines from Alsager, Crewe, Sandbach and Winsford were called to reports of a fire at a detached property on Stoneley Road, Crewe.  Crews attended the scene and found that the fire was well alight.  Firefighters used four breathing apparatus, two hose reels and a positive pressure ventilation fan to extinguish the fire.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and an investigation has been launched by Cheshire Police.  Crews were in attendance for four hours.
