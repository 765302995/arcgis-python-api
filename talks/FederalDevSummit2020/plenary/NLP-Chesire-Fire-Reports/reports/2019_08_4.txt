 Two-vehicle collision in Knutsford Time of Incident: 29/08/2019 - 13:01 Location: Ashley Road, Mere, Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Lymm and one from Knutsford At around 1pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Ashley Road, Mere, Knutsford. On arrival the crews discovered that the collision involved two vehicles and there was no-one trapped inside either of them. The occupants were assessed by paramedics. Firefighters fended off traffic as emergency services worked at the scene. They then handed the incident over to the police. Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
