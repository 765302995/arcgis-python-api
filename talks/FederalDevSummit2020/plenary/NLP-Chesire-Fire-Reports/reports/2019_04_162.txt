 Skip fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 08:14 Location: Weaver Valley Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Fire crews were called to a rubbish fire near to Weaver Valley Road, Winsford. On arrival crews found an industrial skip on fire and they used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
