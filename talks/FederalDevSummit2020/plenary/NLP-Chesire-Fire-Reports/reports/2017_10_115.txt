Empty tanker on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/10/2017 - 10:21 Location: Lousers Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a fire involving an empty HGV tanker on Lousers Lane, Warrington. Crews used two hose reel jets and foam to put out the fire. They then checked the tanker using two breathing apparatus and a thermal imaging camera.
