 House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/12/2018 - 13:05 Location: Reynolds Street, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Warrington and one from Birchwood Shortly after 1pm firefighters from Warrington and Birchwood attended a fire at a house on Reynolds Street in Warrington. On arrival the crews discovered a fire on the ground floor of a mid-terraced property. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They then used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property. Firefighters provided first aid to a casualty who had suffered singeing to the hair and face. The casualty was then handed over to the care of paramedics. Crews also provided oxygen and treatment to two domestic cats.  Firefighters were in attendance for around three hours.
