 Underground electrical fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 24/11/2019 - 16:48 Location: Samuel Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly before 5pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire on Samuel Street, Crewe.  Firefighters from Crewe were dispatched to the scene and on arrival they found that the incident involved an underground electrical cable.  The crew evacuated a small number of nearby properties as a precautionary measure and a small cordon was also put in place.  The team then used a thermal imaging camera to monitor the temperature of the pavement while they awaited the arrival of Scottish Power.  Firefighters were detained at the scene for around two-and-a-half-hours
