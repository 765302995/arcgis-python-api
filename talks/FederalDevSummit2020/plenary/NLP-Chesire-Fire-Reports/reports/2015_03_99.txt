Chimney fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 10/03/2015 - 22:27 Location: Crewe Road, Wistaston Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Chimney rods and a thermal imaging camera were used by firefighters dealing with a chimney fire in Wistaston. A ladder was used to gain entry to the chimney pots and the loft space temperature was monitored using a thermal imaging camera to ensure the joists had not been affected. Chimney safety advice
