 Collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/10/2018 - 06:51 Location: Westmere Drive, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Crews attended to a road traffic collision on Westmere Drive in Crewe involving two cars with one its roof.  No one was trapped inside the vehicles but were handed three people into the care of paramedics with injuries believed to be minor.
