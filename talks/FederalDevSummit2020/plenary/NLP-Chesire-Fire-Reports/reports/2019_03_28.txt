 Hedge fire in Greenbank, Northwich Time of Incident: 24/03/2019 - 00:05 Location: Marshall Lane, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly after midnight firefighters from Northwich were called to reports of a fire on Marshall Lane, Greenbank.  On arrival at the scene the team found one large hedge row of 20 foot high Connifers which were well alight.  The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for approximately half-an-hour.
