 Fire at a park in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/10/2019 - 06:24 Location: Cresswell Close, Callands, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 6.30am firefighters were informed of a fire at a park in the Cresswell Close area of Callands, Warrington.  On arrival the crew discovered a quantity of wood on fire under children's play equipment.  Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
