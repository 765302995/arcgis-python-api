Fire in engine compartment of small crane in Northwich between the A556 and A49 Time of Incident: 04/08/2016 - 16:29 Location: Chester Road, Oakmere, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to reports of a fire which had broken out in the engine compartment of a small crane. Crews isolated the battery inside the crane and put out the fire using a hose reel jet.
