 Man trapped following collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 19/10/2018 - 13:41 Location: M6 northbound between junctions 16 and 17 Attendance: Three fire engines attended - from Alsager, Sandbach and Crewe At around 1.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision on the M6 northbound between junctions 16 and 17. On arrival crews discovered that a car had collided with the central reservation and that one person was trapped inside the vehicle. Firefighters set up extraction equipment while paramedics assessed the trapped man. The casualty was then removed from the car and handed over to the care of the North West Ambulance Service.
