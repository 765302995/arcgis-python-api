Oven fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/04/2015 - 13:50 Location: Fallowfield Grove, Poulton-with-Fearnhead, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in an oven at a house in Poulton-with-Fearnhead.  The fire was out on arrival but smoke was cleared from the property.
