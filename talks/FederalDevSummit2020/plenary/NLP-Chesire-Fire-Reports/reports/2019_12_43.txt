 Fire involving a mobile unit in Poynton Time of Incident: 02/12/2019 - 17:22 Location: Chester Road,Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton At around 5.20pm firefighters from Poynton were called to reports of a fire near to the railway line on Chester Road in Poynton.  On arrival at the scene the crews found a mobile building unit which was well alight.  The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Firefighters then used a hose reel jet to dampen down the scene and a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  The crew were in attendance at the scene for around one hour 40 minutes.
