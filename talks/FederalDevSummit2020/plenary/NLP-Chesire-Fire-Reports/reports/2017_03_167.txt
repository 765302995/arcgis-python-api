Lorry fire on M53 south between J7 and 8 Time of Incident: 01/03/2017 - 18:32 Location: M53 southbound between junctions 7 and 8, near Ellesmere Port Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port, Chester and Powey Lane A lorry was on fire on the M53 southbound near Ellesmere Port.   Firefighters, three wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reels to tackle the blaze. A thermal imaging camera was then used to monitor temperature and the area was damped down. Crews were at the scene for an hour and a half.
