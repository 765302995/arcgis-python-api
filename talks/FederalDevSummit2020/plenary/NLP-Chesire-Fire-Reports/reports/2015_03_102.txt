Chlorine leak in Winsford Time of Incident: 10/03/2015 - 14:06 Location: Lifestyle Centre, The Drumber, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford A leak of chlorine in the swimming pool area at the Lifestyle Centre in Winsford required firefighters to attend. Crews assisted Police and Paramedics at the incident and the building was ventilated.
