 Small fire in the basement area of a department store in Chester Time of Incident: 14/12/2018 - 05:21 Location: Eastgate Street, Chester. Attendance: Five fire engines attended - one from Chester, one from Powey Lane, two from Ellesmere Port and one from Deeside. At 5.21 firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a department store on Eastgate Street, Chester.  On arrival at the scene the team found that there was a small fire in the basement area involving a washing machine.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, use one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Crews were in attendance one hour.
