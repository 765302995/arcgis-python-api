Shed fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/02/2015 - 14:32 Location: Snowdon Close, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A man suffered serious burns to his hands after a fire in a shed in Warrington. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish the blaze involving an electrical heater. North West Ambulance Service attended to treat the man for his injuries.
