 Tumble dryer fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 16/06/2018 - 13:50 Location: Dereham Way, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and Warrington Fire crews were called to reports of a tumble dryer well alight at a property on Dereham Way in Runcorn.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, firefighters tackled the fire with one hose reel and a positive ventilation fan until the fire was extinguished.
