 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/07/2019 - 20:10 Location: Barbauld Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At 8.10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Barbauld Street, Warrington. On arrival the crew found a wheelie bin well alight. Firefighters put out the fire with water and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. For wheelie bin fire prevention tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
