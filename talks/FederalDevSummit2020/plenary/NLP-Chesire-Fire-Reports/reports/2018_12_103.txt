 Refuse fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/12/2018 - 16:07 Location: Woodford Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Crews attended to three lose piles of refuse well alight on wasteland on Woodford Close in Warrington.  A hose reel was used by firefighters to extinguish the fire before they made sure the area was safe.
