Road traffic collision on the A500 Crewe Time of Incident: 05/12/2016 - 16:41 Location: A500, Barthomley, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving six cars and a heavy goods vehicle on the A500. The crews worked with paramedics and carried out a full extrication to release a woman trapped in one of the vehicles. She was handed into the care of the ambulance service. Firefighters were at the scene for around an hour and a half.
