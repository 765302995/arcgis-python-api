House fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 10/06/2017 - 16:19 Location: Chapel Road, Alderley Edge Attendance: Two fire engines from Wilmslow and Macclesfield There was a fire involving insulating material in the loft space of a property.  Firefighters used one hose reel and covering jets to extinguish the fire. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  One female casualty was assessed by firefighters.  Crews then checked the loft space of the property for hotspots, and they also checked the neighbouring property too.
