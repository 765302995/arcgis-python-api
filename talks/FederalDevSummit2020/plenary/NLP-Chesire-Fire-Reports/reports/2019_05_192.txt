 Large unattended bonfire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 03/05/2019 - 21:12 Location: Longbenton Way, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 9.10pm firefighters from Runcorn were called to reports of a fire off Longbenton Way.  On arrival the crew discovered  a large unattended bonfire approximately 5 metres by 5 metres which involved loose refuse.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for approximately one hour.
