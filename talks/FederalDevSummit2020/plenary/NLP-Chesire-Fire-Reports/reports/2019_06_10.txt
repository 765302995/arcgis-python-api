 Grill pan fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 27/06/2019 - 23:51 Location: Bollinbarn Drive, Macclesfield Attendance: Three fire engines attended, two from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Shortly before 12 midnight firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a domestic property on Bollinbarn Drive, Macclesfield. The fire that triggered the alarm was out on arrival. It was caused by food cooking under a grill. Firefighters used a large fan to clear smoke from the property and were in attendance for around 45 minutes. For grill pan fire safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/grills.
