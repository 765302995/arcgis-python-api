Two people rescued from height Time of Incident: 06/08/2015 - 05:52 Location: Whitby Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one rope rescue unit with a supporting fire engine from Knutsford  Firefighters have rescued two people from the roof of flats in Whitby Road, Ellesmere Port. Crews used a seven metre ladder to rescue the people, who were passed into the care of paramedics.
