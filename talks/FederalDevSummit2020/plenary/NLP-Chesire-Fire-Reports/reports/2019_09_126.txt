 Ground floor fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 07/09/2019 - 16:36 Location: Valley Drive, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended reports of a fire at an address on Valley Drive, Great Sutton.   The fire was on the ground floor of a semi-detached property and firefighters used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to extinguish the fire.  They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke. One casualty was suffering slight smoke inhalation and a further casualty required a precautionary check by paramedics.   Crews remained at scene just over two hours.
