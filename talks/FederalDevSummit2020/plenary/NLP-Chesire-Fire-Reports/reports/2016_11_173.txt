Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/11/2016 - 20:15 Location: Dickson Street, Widnes. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews responded to a report of a fire next to a building on Dickson Street, Widnes.  When they arrived crews found that there was a wheelie bin on fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.
