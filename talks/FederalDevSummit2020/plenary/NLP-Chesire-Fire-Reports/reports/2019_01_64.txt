 Fire in utility room in Warrington Time of Incident: 31/12/2018 - 18:44 Location: Woodlands Drive, Warrington Attendance:  One fire engine from Lymm and one from Warrington A report of a house fire was attended by firefighters from Lymm and Warrington. The crews found a fire in a utility room at the property. Two firefighters using breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was then used to ventilate the building. They were at the scene for just over an hour and a half. The cause of the fire is being investigated.
