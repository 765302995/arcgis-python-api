 Car fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 12/04/2019 - 22:03 Location: Park Street, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Shortly after 10pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in Park Street, Congleton. On arrival the crew found a car well alight. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. The crew then damped the car down to make it safe and were in attendance for around an hour. The police also attended. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 372685, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
