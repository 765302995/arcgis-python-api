Kitchen fire Macclesfield Time of Incident: 04/05/2016 - 13:10 Location: High Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Congleton Firefighters tackled a fire in the kitchen of a house on High Street. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, put out the fire using a hose reel jet. They assessed  the rest of the property, including the roof space, to make sure there was no fire spread and damped down and removed damaged items from the kitchen.  One casualty, a woman, was given oxygen by the crews and gas and electricity supplies have been isolated.
