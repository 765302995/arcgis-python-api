 Grill pan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/02/2018 - 17:27 Location: Runnymede, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Lymm Fire crews were called to a fire in a house, caused by a grill pan on Runnymede, Warrington. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews were in attendance for thirty minutes.
