Smoke from trailer wheels on M6 Time of Incident: 19/06/2017 - 17:42 Location: M6 north, between junctions 16 and 17 Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Sandbach Smoke from the trailer of a car transporter required firefighters to damp down and cool the brakes with a hose reel. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the vehicle before crews left the incident.
