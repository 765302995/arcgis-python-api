Wheelie bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 11/09/2017 - 22:25 Location: Bradbury Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford A wheelie bin was destroyed by fire on Bradbury Road. A high pressure hose was used to put out the blaze, which is thought to have been started deliberately.  If you have any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.  Always follow our wheelie bin safety advice
