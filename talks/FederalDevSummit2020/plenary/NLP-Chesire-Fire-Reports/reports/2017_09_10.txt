Dog rescued after getting head stuck in a gate in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 27/09/2017 - 11:14 Location: Dean Row Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Fire crews were called to rescue a dog which had its head caught in between a gate and a stone wall on Dean Row Road, Wilmslow. Crews used a saw to cut a section out of the wooden gate to release the dog, Phoebe, who then celebrated by having a run around the garden.
