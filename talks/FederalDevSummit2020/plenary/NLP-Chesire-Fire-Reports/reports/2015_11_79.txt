Tractor on fire in Chelford Time of Incident: 14/11/2015 - 11:30 Location: Knutsford Road, Chelford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Holmes Chapel Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish a fire involving a tractor.  One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.  Police closed the road while fire crews dealt with the incident.
