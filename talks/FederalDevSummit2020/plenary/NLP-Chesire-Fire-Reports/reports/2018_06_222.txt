 Conifer on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/06/2018 - 17:32 Location: Christie Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters attended to a conifer on fire affecting a fence at the back of a property on Christie Street in Widnes.  Crews used one hose reel to extinguish the fire and made sure the area was safe.
