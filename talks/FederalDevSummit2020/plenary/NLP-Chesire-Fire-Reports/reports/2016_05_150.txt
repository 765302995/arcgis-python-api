Skip on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/05/2016 - 22:00 Location: Davy Road, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes Firefighters were called to a skip on fire and used a hose reel to extinguish it. The skip was well alight and in close proximity to a building so the crew cooled the exterior of the building with water and inspected it with a thermal imaging camera before leaving.
