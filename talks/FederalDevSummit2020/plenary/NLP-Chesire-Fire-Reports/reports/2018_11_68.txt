 Smoke at block of flats in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/11/2018 - 16:38 Location: Water Street, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Just after 4pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a block of flats on Water Street in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered smoke caused by cooking food having been left unattended at one of the flats. Firefighters ventilated the smoke and were in attendance for around 40 minutes. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
