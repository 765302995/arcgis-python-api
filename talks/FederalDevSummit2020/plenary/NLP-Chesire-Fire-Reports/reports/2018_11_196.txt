 Small fire at a new housing estate building site in Goostrey Time of Incident: 01/11/2018 - 16:54 Location: Blackberry Gardens, Goostrey Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 4.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Blackberry Gardens in Goostrey. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire at a new housing estate building site. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
