Barn well alight in Malpas Time of Incident: 06/06/2015 - 19:57 Location: Edge Green Lane in Malpas Attendance: A fire engine from Malpas and a fire engine from Chester A fire engine from Malpas and a fire engine from Chester were mobilised to a farm on Edge Green Lane in Malpas where there was a barn on fire. The fire involved five tonnes of straw and 20 tonnes of logs. The barn was well alight. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to bring the fire under control.
