Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 04/06/2016 - 22:45 Location: Grizedale, Widnes. Attendance: A fire engine from Widnes Firefigters used a hose reel jet to put out a car fire at Grizedale, Widnes.  This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Constabulary on 101.
