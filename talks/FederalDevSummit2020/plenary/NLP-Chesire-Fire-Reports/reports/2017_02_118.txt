Small fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 08/02/2017 - 17:57 Location: Pinewood Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford A handheld extinguisher was used by firefighters to put out a small fire on Pinewood Road. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111, or Cheshire Police on 101.
