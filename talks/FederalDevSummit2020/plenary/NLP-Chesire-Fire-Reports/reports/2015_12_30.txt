Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/12/2015 - 03:20 Location: Tavlin Avenue/Hawleys Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington One crew was called to a report of a car on fire in Warrington. The vehicle was well alight and police closed the road as firefighters tackled the blaze using a hose reel jet, with one crew member in breathing apparatus.  They then damped the area down.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
