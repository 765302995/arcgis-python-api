Refuse lorry brakes on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 19/11/2015 - 09:42 Location: Watkinson Way, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A fire and rescue crew was called to a report of a refuse lorry on fire. There had been a fire involving the brakes. Firefighters cooled the brakes using a hose reel and monitored the area with a thermal imaging camera.
