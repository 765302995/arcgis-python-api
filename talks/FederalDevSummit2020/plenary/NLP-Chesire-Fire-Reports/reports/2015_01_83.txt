Dangerous roof at a Tesco store in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/01/2015 - 12:18 Location: Winwick Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath The high winds caused damage to the roof of the Tesco Store on Winwick Road making it dangerous, so firefighters used the hydraulic platform to make the roof safe by removing about eight foot of the guttering.
