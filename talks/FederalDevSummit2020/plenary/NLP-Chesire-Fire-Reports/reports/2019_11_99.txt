 Woman faints in Poynton Time of Incident: 10/11/2019 - 11:42 Location: Chester Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton At around 11.10am firefighters were called to a report of a woman having fainted on Chester Road in Poynton,  The crew administered first aid to the woman before she went to hospital to be assessed.  Firefighters were in attendance for less than five minutes.
