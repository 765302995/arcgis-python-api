 Rubbish fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 11/05/2019 - 14:32 Location: Birchwood Way, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters turned out to deal with a fire in the open at the Bennett Recreation Ground. They found a pile of rubbish in the open that had deliberately been set on fire, and used a single hose reel to put it out. Police were also in attendance, as there were concerns that the group responsible were still in the area and may set further fires.
