 Collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/12/2018 - 10:50 Location: Manor Park Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Runcorn and one from Widnes At around 10.45am firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Manor Park Avenue in Runcorn. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved two cars and a mobile catering unit. The gas supply to the mobile catering unit was isolated and the road was closed as emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters made the vehicles safe, police dealt with traffic and paramedics tended to two male casualties. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
