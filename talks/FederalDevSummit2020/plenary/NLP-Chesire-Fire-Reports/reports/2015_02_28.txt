Fire at Winsford packaging company Time of Incident: 19/02/2015 - 10:01 Location: Jiffy Packaging, Road Four, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich Fire and rescue crews have been tackling a fire in a piece of machinery at a packaging company.  The fire is believed to have started in a macerator machine and firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, investigated using a hose reel jet.   The sprinkler system in the building activated and a full evacuation of the property took place.   Crews inspected the ducting using height safety equipment and a thermal imaging camera.  Personnel were allowed back into the premises and production is expected to restart later this afternoon.
