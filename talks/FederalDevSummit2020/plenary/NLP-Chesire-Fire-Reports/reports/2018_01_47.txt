 Skip fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/01/2018 - 00:59 Location: Davy Road, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At approximately 1am firefighters from Runcorn were called to reports of a small fire in Davy Road in Runcorn. On arrival at the scene the crew found that the fire involved a large skip containing paper based products. The crew gained access to the site using a ladder before using a hose reel jet and a main jet to extinguish the fire.
