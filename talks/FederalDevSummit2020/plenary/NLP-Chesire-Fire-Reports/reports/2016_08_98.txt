Horse on its side in field in Congleton Time of Incident: 14/08/2016 - 00:49 Location: Congleton Edge Road, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and the Animal Rescue Unit from Knutsford Firefighters were called to assist a horse which was on its side in a field. The crew assisted a vet with the elderly horse which sadly had to be put to sleep.
