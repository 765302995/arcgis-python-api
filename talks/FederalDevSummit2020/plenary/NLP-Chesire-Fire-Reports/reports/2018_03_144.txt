 Fallen tree in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 03/03/2018 - 09:53 Location: Brocklehurst Avenue, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters from Macclesfield were called to reports of a dangerous structure on Brocklehurst Avenue, Macclesfield. On arrival at the scene the team found that a 25ft tree had fallen and was precariously resting on another tree. The crew closed the road as a precautionary measure and a  nearby resident was evacuated. Attendance of a professional tree surgeon was requested by the local authority.
