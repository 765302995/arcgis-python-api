 Wheelie bin fires in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/07/2019 - 22:33 Location: Victoria Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to Victoria Road, Ellesmere Port, after several wheelie bins were set alight. Seven wheelie bins had been deliberately set alight and crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fires. Crews then checked the bins for any hotspots using a thermal imaging camera. If anyone has any information about the incident please contact Cheshire Police.
