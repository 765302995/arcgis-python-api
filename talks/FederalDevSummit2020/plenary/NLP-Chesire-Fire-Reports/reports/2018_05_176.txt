 Broken down vehicle made safe in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/05/2018 - 19:28 Location: M6 northbound to M62 westbound link road in Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 7.25pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on the hard shoulder of the M6 northbound to M62 westbound link road in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that there was no fire but they used a hose reel jet to cool the broken down vehicle's engine as a precaution. Firefighters made the vehicle safe and provided a fend off until the highways agency arrived. The fire crew were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
