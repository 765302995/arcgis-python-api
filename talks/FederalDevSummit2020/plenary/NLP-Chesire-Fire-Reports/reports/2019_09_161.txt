 Fire in a flat in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/09/2019 - 09:21 Location: Norland Street, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended from At around 9.20am firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a flat on Norland Road, Widnes.  On arrival the team found the fire was contained to a third floor flat.  Firefighters evacuated the building as a precautionary measure.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, then entered the flat used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  They then used a hose reel jet to dampen down.  There were no reports of any injuries.
