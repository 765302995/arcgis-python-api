Caravan fire in Chester Time of Incident: 02/01/2015 - 03:55 Location: Church Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester A caravan was well alight on arrival of firefighters who wore breathing apparatus and used to hose reels to extinguish the blaze. A gas cylinder was also at the scene and a thermal imaging camera was used to monitor the temperaturewhich was fortunately cool. An investigation into the cause of the fire is ongoing.
