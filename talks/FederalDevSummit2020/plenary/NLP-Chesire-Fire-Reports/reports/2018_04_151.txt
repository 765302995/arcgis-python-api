 Burst water pipe at rest home in Poynton Time of Incident: 02/04/2018 - 05:57 Location: Dickens Lane, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Crews were called to a water pipe having burst at a rest home on Dickens Lane in Poynton.  The ground floor and lift were covered in water after the pipe burst in the hallway. Firefighters isolated the electrics affected by the water and assisted with salvaging the water.
