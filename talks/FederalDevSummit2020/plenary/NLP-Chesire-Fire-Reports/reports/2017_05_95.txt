Fire in oven in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 15/05/2017 - 23:21 Location: North Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to an industrial oven on fire in North Road, Ellesmere Port. The crews worked alongside an engineer on site and used a hose reel to reach the seat of the fire and extinguish it. A thermal imaging camera to check the area for hot spots of fire before leaving.
