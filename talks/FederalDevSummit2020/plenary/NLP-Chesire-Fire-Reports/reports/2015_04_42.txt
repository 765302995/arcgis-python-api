Fire in the bedroom of a flat in Chester Time of Incident: 24/04/2015 - 19:37 Location: Richmond Terrace, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters used two hose reel jets to tackle a bedroom fire on the first floor of a flat on Richmond Terrace in Chester. Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus. A thermal imaging camera was used to check for hotpots.   A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the property.  A fire invetsigation will be completed to determine the cause of the fire.  Police attended this incident.
