 Fire at the side of a domestic garage in Widnes Time of Incident: 23/09/2018 - 19:54 Location: Ditchfield Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Widnes and one from Penketh At around 7.55pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a domestic garage on Ditchfield Road in Widnes. On arrival crews discovered that it was a small fire at the side of the garage. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
