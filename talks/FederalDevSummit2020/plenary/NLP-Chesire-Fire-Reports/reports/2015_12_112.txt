Chimney fire in Mobberley Time of Incident: 03/12/2015 - 16:31 Location: Small Lane, Mobberley Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters extinguished a chimney which was on fire in Mobberley. The small fire was in the chimney breast and debris from the hearth was removed and a hose reel used to put the flames out. Chimney safety advice
