 Small waste fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 19/06/2019 - 14:21 Location: New Road, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton At around 2.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on New Road, Congleton. On arrival the crew found a fire involving a small quantity of waste at the entrance to a field. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. They also turned the waste over and damped it down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for less than 20 minutes.
