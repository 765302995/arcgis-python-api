Kitchen fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 10/04/2015 - 16:59 Location: Mersey Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Runcorn Firefighters have been tackling a blaze in the kitchen of a terraced house in Widnes.  The crew, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  Please follow our kitchen safety advice to avoid a fire in your home - Kitchen fire safety
