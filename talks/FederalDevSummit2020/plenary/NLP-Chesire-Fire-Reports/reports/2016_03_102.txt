Road traffic collision in Congleton Time of Incident: 12/03/2016 - 11:14 Location: A34, Astbury, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton, one from Alsager and the rapid response vehicle from Holmes Chapel One man was trapped in a two car collision in Congleton. Firefighters stabilised the vehicle and used specialist equipment to release the casualty who was handed into the care of the ambulance service and taken to hospital. Crews made the scene safe. Police were in attendance for traffic management.
