Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/06/2017 - 23:10 Location: Longshaw Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Arson is suspected to be the cause of a wheelie bin fire in Longshaw Street. Firefighters used a water backpack to contain the blaze and are appealing for anyone with any information about this incident to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
