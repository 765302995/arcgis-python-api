 Three-car collision in Sandbach Time of Incident: 12/04/2018 - 17:23 Location: Smithfield Lane, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Sandbach and one from Crewe At around 5.20pm firefighters were called to a collision on Smithfield Lane in Sandbach.  On arrival the crews found that the incident involved three cars.  Firefighters made the scene safe and assisted with a female casualty.  Crews were in attendance for around 30 minutes  Police and paramedics were also in attendance.
