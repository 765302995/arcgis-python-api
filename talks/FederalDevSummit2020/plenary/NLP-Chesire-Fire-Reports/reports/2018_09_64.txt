 Pigeon rescued from netting in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 17/09/2018 - 20:11 Location: Cromwell Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to rescue a pigeon which had become stuck in netting near to a social club on Cromwell Road in Ellesmere Port.  Firefighters liaised with the RSPCA by using a seven metre ladder to reach the pigeon 25 feet high.  Once safely brought down the pigeon was handed to the care of the RSPCA.
