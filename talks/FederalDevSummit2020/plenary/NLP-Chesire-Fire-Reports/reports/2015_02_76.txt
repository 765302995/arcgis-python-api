Two people released after collision on M6 Time of Incident: 10/02/2015 - 13:28 Location: M6 north junction 17 to 18 Attendance: Fire engines from Sandbach, Holmes Chapel, Middlewich and Crewe  Two people were trapped after a collision on the M6 involving one car. The saloon car was on its side and this was the vehicle the casualties were trapped in.  Crews stabilised the vehicle and were then able to release them.  The casualties were handed into the care of paramedics and the scene made safe.
