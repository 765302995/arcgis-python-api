 Chip pan fire in Chester Time of Incident: 09/10/2018 - 18:30 Location: Stamford Road, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - One from Chester and one from Powey Lane. Shortly after 6.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a residential property in Chester. Crews from Chester and Powey Lane swiftly attended the scene and on arrival they discovered a chip pan fire which was extinguished using a fire blanket. The team conducted fire safety checks at the address and were in attendance for approximately one hour. For further information about chip pan safety visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chip-pans
