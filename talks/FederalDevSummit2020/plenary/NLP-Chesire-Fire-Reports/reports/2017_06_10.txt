Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/06/2017 - 23:31 Location: Dover Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a car which was on fire on Dover Court, Ellesmere Port. The car was well alight on arrival and crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The incident is a suspected arson. If you have any information about the incident please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
