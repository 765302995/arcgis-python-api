One person trapped after a collision in Broxton Time of Incident: 13/11/2017 - 07:35 Location: Old Coach Road, Broxton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas and one from Chester Road traffic collision in BroxtonFirefighters rescued a female casualty who had been trapped in a car after a single car road traffic collision on Old Coach Road, Broxton. Crews released the casualty from the vehicle and she was taken to hospital via air ambulance.
