 Flooding in Nantwich Time of Incident: 26/10/2019 - 11:13 Location: Shrewbridge Road, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Sandbach Fire crews rescued a number of people from properties which had flooded in the Shrewbridge Road area of Nantwich.  This included two children and an adult from one address as well as two children, two adults and a dog from another.  Around 15 properties were checked by firefighters from the swift water rescue team for flooding.  The road was also closed.
