Roof blown off a row of Runcorn houses Time of Incident: 23/02/2017 - 10:19 Location: Handforth Lane, Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters have been called to a report that a roof has blown off a row of terrace houses in Runcorn.  On arrival crews discovered that part of the flat roof on a row of three storey properties was hanging over the road. This was affecting a number of properties.  Crews have set up a cordon around the incident and are working to make the scene safe.  A number of people were evacuated while the incident was dealt with.  Please avoid the area is you can.
