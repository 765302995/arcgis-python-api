Kitchen fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 04/09/2017 - 05:15 Location: Royal Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a kitchen fire on Royal Avenue, Widnes. Firefighters found that a fridge had caught fire but was out on arrival of the crews.  They used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.  Information about kitchen safety advice
