 Shed fire in Astbury Time of Incident: 01/04/2019 - 23:38 Location: Watery Lane, Astbury. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton and one fire engine from Alsager Shortly after 11.30pm firefighters from Congleton attended reports of a fire in an outbuilding on Watery Lane, Astbury.  On arrival the team discovered a wooden shed which was well alight.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire; they also used a hose reel jet to check for any hotspots.  The fire is believed to have damaged a nearby electricity cable, so Electricity North West have been requested at the scene.  The fire is believed to have been started accidently.   Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately 40 minutes.
