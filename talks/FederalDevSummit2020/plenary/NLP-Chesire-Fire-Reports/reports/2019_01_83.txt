 Electrical fault at a commercial building in Northwich Time of Incident: 26/01/2019 - 13:11 Location: Leicester Street, Northwich. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly after 1pm firefighters were called to reports that a fire alarm had activated at New Look on Leicester Street in Northwich town centre.  On arrival at the scene the team found that the store had been evacuated and all persons were accounted for.  Firefighters isolated the electrics in the store and the conducted a detailed search of the premises and discovered that the alarm activation had been caused by a fault transmitter on a light fitting.  Crews were detailed at the scene for approximately 40 minutes.
