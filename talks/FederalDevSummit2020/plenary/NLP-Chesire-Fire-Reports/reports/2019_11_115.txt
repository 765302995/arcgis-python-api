 One vehicle collision near Northwich Time of Incident: 08/11/2019 - 00:49 Location: King Street, Byley, near Northwich. Attendance: Three fire engines attended from Middlewich, Northwich and Winsford Shortly before 1am  firefighters from Northwich, Middlewich and Winsford attended a one vehicle collision on Kings Street in Byley.   On arrival the team found one person trapped in the van. The crew helped paramedics remove door from the van, they then  used a long board to help extricate one casualty.  Firefighters remained in attendance for approximately one hour and twenty five minutes.
