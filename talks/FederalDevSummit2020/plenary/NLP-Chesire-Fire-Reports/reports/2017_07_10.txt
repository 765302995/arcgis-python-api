Van fire M6 north near junction 19 Time of Incident: 27/07/2017 - 21:38 Location: Slip road to Knutsford service station, junction 19 northbound Attendance: Fire engines from Knutsford, Lymm and Stockton Heath Two hose reel jets were used by firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, to put out a fire in a van which was well alight on Knutsford service station sliproad. Specialist cutting equipment was used so the crew could get access to the interior of the van. They were at the scene for just under an hour.
