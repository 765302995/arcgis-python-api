 Large bonfire in Over Alderley Time of Incident: 23/08/2019 - 20:58 Location: Wrigley Lane, Over Alderley, near Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At around 9pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in Wrigley Lane, Over Alderley, near Macclesfield. On arrival the crew found a large attended bonfire containing garden waste well alight. The fire measured approximately 10 metres by five metres. Firefighters reduced the size of the fire with a hose reel jet and damped down the surrounding area to make it safe.. They then allowed the remaining fire to burn. Firefighters were in attendance for nearly an hour.
