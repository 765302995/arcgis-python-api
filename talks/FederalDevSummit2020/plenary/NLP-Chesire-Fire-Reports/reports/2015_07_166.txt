Fire in disused Runcorn pub Time of Incident: 05/07/2015 - 19:52 Location: Leaside, Halton Brook, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a fire in the Burma Star, a disused pub on Leaside.  The fire, which is believed to have been started deliberately, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.   Anyone with any information about this, or similar, incidents is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
