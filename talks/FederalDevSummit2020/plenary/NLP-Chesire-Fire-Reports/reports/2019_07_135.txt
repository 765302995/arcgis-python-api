 Fence fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 08/07/2019 - 05:20 Location: Park Lane, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton At around 5.20am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Park Lane, Congleton. On arrival the crew found a small section of fencing alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
