 House fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 03/06/2019 - 05:01 Location: Lacey Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes, one fire engine from Runcorn and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a fire in a first floor bedroom in a house on Lacey Street, Widnes. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used a main jet and a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Two casualties were taken to hospital for checks. Fire crews also evacuated adjacent properties before checking for fire spread. Crews were at the scene for two hours. The fire is believed to have been started accidentally by a candle.
