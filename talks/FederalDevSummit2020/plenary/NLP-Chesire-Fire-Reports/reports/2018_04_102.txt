 Straw on fire on the A500 in Crewe Time of Incident: 11/04/2018 - 18:12 Location: A500, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar Firefighters were called to a large pile of straw reported smouldering after it had fallen off a trailer on the A500 in Crewe opposite the services entrance.  Crews used one hose reel jet and a snap drag to extinguish the fire.
