Car on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/08/2015 - 17:10 Location: Winstanley Close, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A car was on fire on the driveway of a property on Winstanley Close, Great Sankey, Warrington. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.
