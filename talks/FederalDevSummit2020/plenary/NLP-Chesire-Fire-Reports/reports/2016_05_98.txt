Unattended chip pan causes fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/05/2016 - 00:30 Location: Station Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington An unattended chip fan caused a kitchen fire leading to a casualty needing treatment for smoke inhalation. The fire was out but had caused a lot of smoke in the property. Firefighters treated the man for smoke inhalation and used a high pressure fan to clear smoke.  Never leave cooking unattended. Always follow our kitchen safety advice.
