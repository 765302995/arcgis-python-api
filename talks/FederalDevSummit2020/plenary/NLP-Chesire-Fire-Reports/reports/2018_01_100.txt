 Road traffic collision in Altrincham Time of Incident: 07/01/2018 - 14:33 Location: Back Lane, Altrincham Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and two from Greater Manchester Fire Service Cheshire firefighters assisted Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service after a road traffic collision on Back Lane, Ashley, Altrincham. The crash involved two vehicles and two casualties were taken to hospital. Fire crews used hydraulic spreaders and cutters to gain access to the vehicles.
