 BBQ fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/07/2018 - 04:56 Location: Edgewater Place, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to a fire in a garden at Edgewater Place just before 5am. The fire had spread to a table and a fence and firefighters used a hose reel to put the fire out. You can find more information about how to use BBQs safely here - BBQ safety
