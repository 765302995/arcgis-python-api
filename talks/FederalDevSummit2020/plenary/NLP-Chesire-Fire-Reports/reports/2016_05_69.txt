Kitchen fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 17/05/2016 - 16:15 Location: Sutton Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton Firefighters were called to a kitchen on fire in the first floor of a detached property. One hose reel was used to extinguish the fire.  There were no working smoke alarms in the property. The crew carried out a home safety assessment. Ambulance staff also attended.  Always have working smoke alarms fitted. Kitchen fire safety advice
