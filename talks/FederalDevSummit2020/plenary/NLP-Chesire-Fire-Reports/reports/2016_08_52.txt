Kitchen fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 21/08/2016 - 11:08 Location: Talbot Way, Stapeley Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to reports of a kitchen fire but discovered that the fire was out on arrival. They then checked for any remaining heat spots of fire and carried out a full health and safety assessment of the property.  Over half of house fires start in the kitchen. A few simple tips for taking care whilst cooking could keep you and your loved ones safe.
