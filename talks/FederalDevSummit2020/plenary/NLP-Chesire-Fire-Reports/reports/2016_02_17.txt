Fire in a Winsford property Time of Incident: 25/02/2016 - 23:50 Location: Crossfield Avenue, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford and one from Middlewich Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire in Crossfield Avenue. The fire involved the main electrics which were isolated. A dry powder extinguisher was used to put out the fire. Everyone in the property was accounted for and the electricity board was asked to attend.
