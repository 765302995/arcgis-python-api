 Rescue of person in Poynton Time of Incident: 14/07/2018 - 15:42 Location: Poynton. Attendance: 1 fire engine from Macclesfield, one fire engine from Wilmslow and the major rescue unit from Winsford attended. Fire crews from Macclesfield and Wilmslow and the major rescue unit from Winsford were called to Poynton after reports of a man trapped under a tractor.  Firefighters freed the man using specialist equipment and he was taken to hospital by ambulance for further treatment.
