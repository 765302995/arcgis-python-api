Outbuilding fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/09/2015 - 21:21 Location: Badger Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Alsager One man was treated for smoke inhalation after a fire in a brick outbuilding in Crewe.  The fire involved a small amount of refuse and the crews used one hose reel to extinguish it.   Police and paramedics also attended the scene.
