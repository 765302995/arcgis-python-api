 Fire at a house in Neston Time of Incident: 22/11/2019 - 19:28 Location: Churchill Court, Neston Attendance: Four fire engines attended, one from Powey Lane, one from Ellesmere Port, one from Deeside in North Wales and one from Heswall in Merseyside At around 7.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a house in Churchill Court, Neston.  Two firefighters each wearing breathing apparatus gained entry to the house and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire involving a pan of food that had been left unattended as it was cooking.  Firefighters were in attendance for nearly an hour-and-a-half.  For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
