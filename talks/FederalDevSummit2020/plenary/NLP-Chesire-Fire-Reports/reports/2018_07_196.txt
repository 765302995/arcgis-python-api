 Grill pan fire in Neston Time of Incident: 09/07/2018 - 20:06 Location: Churchill Court, Neston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane At 8.05pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a flat in Churchill Court, Neston. The fire involving a grill pan was out on arrival. Firefighters used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. For grill pan fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/grills.
