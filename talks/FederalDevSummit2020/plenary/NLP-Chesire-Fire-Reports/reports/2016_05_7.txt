Barn fire in Chester Time of Incident: 29/05/2016 - 11:44 Location: Birds Lane, Tattenhall, Chester Attendance: Fire engines from Tarporley and Chester attended Firefighters responded to a report of an outbuilding fire on  Birds Lane, Tattenhall, Chester.   When they arrived they found the fire involved a barn and a tractor. Crews used two hose reels, one main jet and one breathing apparatus to extinguish the blaze.  Crews were in attendance for more than three hours
