Road traffic collision in Knutsford Time of Incident: 05/03/2017 - 11:35 Location: Northwich Road, Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Knutsford and one from Altrincham (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Three cars were involved in a road traffic collision on Northwich Road. Firefighters removed a door from one of the cars to enable them to release two casualties who were then handed into the care of the ambulance service. They then made the scene safe.
