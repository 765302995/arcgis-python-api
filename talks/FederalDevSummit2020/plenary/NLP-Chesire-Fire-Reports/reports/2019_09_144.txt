 Garden fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 03/09/2019 - 18:14 Location: Clare Place, Winsford. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to a fire in a garden at an address in Clare Place, Winsford which had spread to fencing and a wall of the house.  The fire was out on arrival and firefighters damped down the affected area using a hose reel. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
