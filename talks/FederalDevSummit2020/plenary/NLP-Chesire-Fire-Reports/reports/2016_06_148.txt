Fence fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 04/06/2016 - 20:58 Location: Davenham Crescent, Crewe. Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Davenham Crescent, Crewe.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving a fence panel. Crews used a hose reel to put it out.  This fire was believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Constabulary on 101.
