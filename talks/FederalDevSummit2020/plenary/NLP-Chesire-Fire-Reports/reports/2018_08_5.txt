 Man rescued in Weston Time of Incident: 29/08/2018 - 19:54 Location: Sandford Crescent, Weston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe and the rope rescue unit from Lymm Firefighters were called to rescue a man who had become stuck on a cherry picker on Sandford Crescent, Weston. The cherry picker's battery had run flat leaving the man 6m in the air. Crews used a 7m ladder to rescue the man and they were at the scene for 30 minutes.
