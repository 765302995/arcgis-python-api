Skip set on fire on a driveway in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/07/2015 - 20:49 Location: Mersey Walk, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from warrington A skip on a driveway on Mersey Walk in Latchford was set on fire. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.   The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this or similar incidents is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
