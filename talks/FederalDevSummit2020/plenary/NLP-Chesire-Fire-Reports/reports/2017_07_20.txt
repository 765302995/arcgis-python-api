Industrial tumble dryer fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 24/07/2017 - 14:41 Location: Hurdsfield Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield and one from Congleton Fire crews were called to an industrial tumble dryer which was on fire on Hurdsfield Road, Macclesfield. Crews used a dry powder extinguisher to put out the fire.
