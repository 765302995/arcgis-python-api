 Small fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 21:01 Location: Fairfield Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to deal with a fire near a bin next to the funfair on Fairfield Road. It was a barbecue that had been left against a bin and was not properly out. They used a water backpack to extinguish any remaining flames and embers.
