Cars deliberately set on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 22/05/2015 - 00:05 Location: Brooklyn Street/Nantwich Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Two cars which are suspected to have been deliberately set on fire in Crewe were extinguished by firefighters overnight. The fire spread from one of the vehicles, which was totally destroyed in the fire, and caused damage to the front of the second vehicle. Both vehicles were parked on the roadway and the fire did not involve property. Police attended. If anyone has any information as to how this fire started they should call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
