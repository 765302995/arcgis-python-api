 Car fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 08/04/2018 - 23:20 Location: Mobberley Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford At around 11.20pm firefighters were called to a car fire within the compound of an auto body repairs building on Mobberley Road in Knutsford. On arrival the crew discovered a vehicle alight. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The crew them dampened the area down. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Police have been notified of the incident.
