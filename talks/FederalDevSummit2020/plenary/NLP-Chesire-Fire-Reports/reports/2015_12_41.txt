Garage fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/12/2015 - 17:02 Location: Dudley Walk, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a fire in an empty garage on Dudley Walk, Macclesfield.  An investigation has been launched to find the cause of the blaze.
