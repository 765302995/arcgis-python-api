Small amount of smoke in a building in Tarporley Time of Incident: 19/01/2017 - 14:56 Location: The Heath Centre, Park Road, Tarporley Attendance: Two fire engines attended Firefighters were called to a small amount of smoke in a building in Tarporley. A full evacuation took place and a thermal imaging camera was used to check the area by firefighters deployed onto the roof. The crew removed part of the roofing for inspection purposes and found no signs of heat or smoke.  It is believed that the smoke was caused following work carried out on a building.
