 Collision on West Road in Congleton Time of Incident: 05/05/2019 - 18:58 Location: West Road, Congleton. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Congleton and Macclesfield At around 7pm firefighters from Congleton and Macclesfield attended reports of a collision on West Road, Congleton.  On arrival the team found there had been a collision between two cars and one person was trapped.  The crew used specialist cutting equipment to release the casualty and they were then passed to the care of paramedics.  The team were in attendance at the scene for around one hour.
