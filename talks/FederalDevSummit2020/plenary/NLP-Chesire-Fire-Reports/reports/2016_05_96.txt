Single vehicle road traffic collision in Antrobus Time of Incident: 14/05/2016 - 10:24 Location: Northwich Road, Antrobus Attendance: Two fire engines from Stockton Heath One car was involved in a road traffic incident in Antrobus. A casualty was trapped in the vehicle and was extricated by firefighters, under the supervision of ambulance personnel. Northwich Road was closed while the emergency services dealt with the incident. Crews were at the scene for approximately one hour.
