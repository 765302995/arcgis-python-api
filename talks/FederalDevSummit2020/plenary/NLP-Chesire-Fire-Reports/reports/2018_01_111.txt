 Wheelie bin fires in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 03/01/2018 - 18:52 Location: Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port. Firefighters were called to two wheelie bins on fire in Bunbury Green in Ellesmere Port. The bins were pushed against a fallen tree. Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the fire.
