Tree and bushes on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 28/05/2016 - 23:53 Location: Pinewood Road, Winsford. Attendance: Fire engines from Winsford and Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Pinewood Road, Winsford.  When they arrived crews found a large number of felled trees and bushes on fire. A second fire engine was called to help deal with the blaze.  Hose reels and covering jets were used to bring the incident under control.
