 Wheelie bin fire in wooded area in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/04/2019 - 21:25 Location: Chadwick Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Shortly before 9.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Chadwick Lane in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered a wheelie bin well alight in a wooded area. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 368156, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For advice on preventing your wheelie bins from being targeted by arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
