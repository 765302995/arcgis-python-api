 Reports of a fire in a bedroom in Northwich Time of Incident: 18/07/2019 - 00:00 Location: Wade Crescent, Barnton, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly after midnight firefighter from Northwich were called to reports of a fire in a first floor bedroom at a house in Barnton. On arrival at the address the crews discovered that a television had malfunctioned and set on fire, resulting in the owned taking evasive action and throwing the TV out of the window. Thankfully, as a result of the owners actions, they were able to prevent the fire from taking hold.
