 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/05/2018 - 08:56 Location: Avery Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington, one from Birchwood and one from Lymm Fire crews were called to a kitchen fire on Avery Close, Warrington. On arrival, all people in the house were accounted for and crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a large fan to put out the fire.
