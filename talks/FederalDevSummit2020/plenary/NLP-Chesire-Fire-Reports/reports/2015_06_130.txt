Kitchen fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 09/06/2015 - 17:51 Location: The Circuit, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle One woman was assessed by paramedics for smoke inhalation after a kitchen fire in Alderley Edge.  Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish the fire and a large fan to clear the smoke. They were at the scene for just over an hour.
