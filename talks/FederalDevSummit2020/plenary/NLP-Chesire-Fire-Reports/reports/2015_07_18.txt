Large branch overhanging road in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 27/07/2015 - 10:13 Location: Palmerston Street/Water Street, Bollington, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine and the hydraulic platform from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a large branch which was unsafe and overhanging Palmerston Street/Water Street, Bollington. The crew closed the road off while the hydraulic platform was used to removed two branches that were overhanging the road.
