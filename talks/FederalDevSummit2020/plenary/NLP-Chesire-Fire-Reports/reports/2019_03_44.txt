 Cottage fire at a farm in Wildboarclough Time of Incident: 21/03/2019 - 13:31 Location: Heild End, Wildboarclough Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Macclesfield and one from Buxton At around 1.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a farm in Heild End, Wildboarclough. On arrival crews discovered a small fire in the loft space of a detached cottage and that no-one was inside the building. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters then cut away charred roof timbers and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and make sure that the cottage was safe. They were in attendance for around two-and-a-half hours.
