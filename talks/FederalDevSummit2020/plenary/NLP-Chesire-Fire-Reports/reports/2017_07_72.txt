Small rubbish fire in Appleton Time of Incident: 14/07/2017 - 21:20 Location: Farnham Close, Appleton Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath Fire crews were called to a small amount of rubbish and a bicycle which was on fire on Farnham Close, Appleton. The fire was started deliberately and crews used a hose reel jet to put out the flames.  If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
