 Large grass fire on hill in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/07/2018 - 08:15 Location: Runcorn Hill, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 8.15am firefighters were called to a report of a large grass fire on Runcorn Hill. The crew extinguished the fire using two main jets. Firefighters then damped the area down to make sure it was safe. They were in attendance for around two hours.
