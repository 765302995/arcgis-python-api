 Waste fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 27/03/2019 - 21:06 Location: Warrington Road, Lymm. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath. One fire engine attended from Lymm. Firefighters were called to a fire involving waste at an address in Warrington Road, Lymm. They used a water back pack and a stack drag to extinguish the fire.
