Building fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 23/09/2015 - 02:42 Location: Oxford Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Two fire engines from Crewe were called to a fire at the back of a building on Oxford Street. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke which had got into the building.
