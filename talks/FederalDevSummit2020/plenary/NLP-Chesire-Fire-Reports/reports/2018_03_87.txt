 Boiler fire in Prestbury Time of Incident: 17/03/2018 - 06:26 Location: Withinlee Road, Prestbury Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a boiler on fire in the cellar of a Withinlee Road house. They isolated the boiler and two firefighters in breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke from the building.
