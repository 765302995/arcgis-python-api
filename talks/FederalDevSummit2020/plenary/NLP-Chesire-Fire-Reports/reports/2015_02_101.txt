Road traffic collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 05/02/2015 - 02:50 Location: M6 between junctions 16 and 15 south Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to assist Staffordshire Fire and Rescue following a road traffic collision involving two heavy goods vehicles, with one person trapped. The crews worked to release the man from his vehicle. Paramedics also attended.
