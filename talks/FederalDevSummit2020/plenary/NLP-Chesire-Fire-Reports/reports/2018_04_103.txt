 Wheelie bin fires in Crewe Time of Incident: 11/04/2018 - 15:22 Location: Hungerford Road, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to four wheelie bins on fire in an alleyway off Hungerford Road in Crewe. Crews used two hose reels to extinguish the flames. Police have been informed.
