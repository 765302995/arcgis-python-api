 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 28/12/2018 - 18:23 Location: Ann Street West, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to Ann Street West, Widnes, to a report of a bin fire. Two wheelie bins had been set alight deliberately and crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. If you have any information about the fire please contact Cheshire Police.
