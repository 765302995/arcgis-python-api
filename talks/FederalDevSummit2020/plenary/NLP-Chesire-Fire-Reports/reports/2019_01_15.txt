 Wheelie bin fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 09/01/2019 - 00:22 Location: Sandon Street, Crewe. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters attended a wheelie bin fire in Sandon Street in Crewe. The bin was next to a door of a charity shop causing the shop to be smoke logged, but the fire did not spread to the premises. They used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and a large fan to disperse the smoke.
