 Two cars on fire in Wettenhall Time of Incident: 26/08/2019 - 00:05 Location: Long Lane, Wettenhall Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Winsford and one from Tarporley Shortly after 12 midnight firefighters were called to reports of two cars being on fire on Long Lane in Wettenhall. On arrival crews found both vehicles well alight. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to fight the fire. Foam was also used. Once the fires were out the cars were damped down to make them safe. The fires are believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fires should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 496372, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
