 Cooker fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/04/2019 - 15:09 Location: Runcorn Road, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Warrington Firefighters attended to a fire at a house on Runcorn Road in Runcorn.  The fire, believed to have been caused by magazines left on a cooker, was extinguished using water. A large fan was used to disperse the smoke.  A man was handed into the care of paramedics and taken to Warrington Hospital as a precaution.
