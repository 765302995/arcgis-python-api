Grass fire on Runcorn Hill Time of Incident: 20/04/2016 - 15:22 Location: Runcorn Hill, Weston Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes Arson is suspected as the cause of a grass fire on Runcorn Hill. A hose reel jet and water backpacks were used by firefighters to put out the blaze which covered an area of approximately 300 metres by 100 metres.  Anyone with any information about this, or similar, incidents is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
