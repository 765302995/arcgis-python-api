 House fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 01/11/2018 - 13:20 Location: Ellis Lane, Frodsham Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Widnes At around 1.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a house on Ellis Lane in Frodsham. The fire, which is believed to have been caused by the electricity supply to the property, was out on arrival. The crews made sure that the house was safe and used a thermal imaging camera to check for signs that the fire had spread to neighbouring properties. There were no signs of this. The incident left approximately 20 houses without electricity. Once they were satisfied that the area was safe firefighters left the incident in the hands of an electrical engineer. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
