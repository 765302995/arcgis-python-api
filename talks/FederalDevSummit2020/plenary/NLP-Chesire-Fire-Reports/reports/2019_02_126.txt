 Kitchen fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 04/02/2019 - 23:55 Location: Saxon Crossway, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended - both from Winsford Firefighters attended to a kitchen fire at a first floor flat on Saxon Crossway in Winsford.  Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, used one hose reel and one covering to jet to extinguish the flames.  Fire crews also administered oxygen therapy to one person suffering from smoke inhalation. Three others also received treatment from paramedics for smoke inhalation but weren't taken to hospital.  The fire is believed at this stage to have been caused by a chip pan.  Firefighters were in attendance for around one hour and 15 minutes.
