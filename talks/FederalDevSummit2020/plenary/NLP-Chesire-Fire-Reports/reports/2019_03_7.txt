 Kitchen fire in Chester Time of Incident: 29/03/2019 - 01:18 Location: Rowan Place, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and two from Ellesmere Port A kitchen on fire in a top floor flat required firefighters to attend and use a hose reel to extinguish flames. The fire is believed to have started due to cooking and spread to materials nearby. A large fan was used by the crew to remove smoke from the flat and paramedics attended after the occupant suffered slight smoke inhalation.  For kitchen safety advice please visit: www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety
