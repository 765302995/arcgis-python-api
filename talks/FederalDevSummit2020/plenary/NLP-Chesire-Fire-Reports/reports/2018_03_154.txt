 Wheelie bin fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 01/03/2018 - 16:58 Location: Broughton Lane, Wistaston. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters attended a fire involving a wheelie bin which had spread to the side of the house in Broughton Lane, Wistaston. Ash from the log burner had caused accidental ignition of the wheelie bin. Crews put out the fire and damped down.
