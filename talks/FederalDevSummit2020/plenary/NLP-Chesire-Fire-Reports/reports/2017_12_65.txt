One person trapped following collision in Congleton Time of Incident: 12/12/2017 - 10:21 Location: Park Street, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and one from Macclesfield. Firefighters attended a three vehicle collision at the junction of Park Street and Moor Street in Congleton. On arrival at the scene crews found that the collision involved two cars and a large goods vehicle. One person was trapped in their vehicle as a result of the collision. Firefighters used electronic cutting equipment to extract the person from the vehicle, they were then passed to North West Ambulance service.
