Small fire on the side of the canal in Ellesmere port Time of Incident: 08/04/2017 - 16:07 Location: Oil Sites Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters used a water backpack to extinguish a small fire on a canal bank.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately.  If you can help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, or call Cheshire Police on 101.
