 House fire in Bollington Time of Incident: 05/04/2018 - 01:07 Location: Palmerston Street, Bollington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington and one from Macclesfield A fire which started in a wheelie bin on Palmerston Street, Bollington, spread to a house and caused a kitchen fire. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Crews were at the scene for three hours.
