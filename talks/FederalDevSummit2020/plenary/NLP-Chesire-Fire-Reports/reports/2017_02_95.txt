House fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 14/02/2017 - 20:56 Location: Knutsford Road, Alderley Edge. Attendance: Fire engines from Wilmslow, Knutsford and Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Knutsford Road, Alderley Edge.  When they arrived crews found that the house was filled with smoke. This was coming from a fire on the ground floor. Firefighters used four breathing apparatus and a positive pressure ventilation unit to deal with the incident. One man, suffering smoke inhalation, was handed over to the care of awaiting ambulance personnel. Crews were engaged at the scene for over two hours.
