 Tent on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 17/05/2018 - 05:49 Location: Crewe Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Fire crews attended to a tent well ablaze in the grounds of a lifestyle centre on Crewe Street in Crewe.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet and one rake to put out the fire and remained at the scene to dampen down the area.
