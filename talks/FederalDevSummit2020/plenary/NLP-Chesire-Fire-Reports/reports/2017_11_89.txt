One person rescued from a car on the A500 Time of Incident: 09/11/2017 - 08:34 Location: A500, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe and one from Nantwich One female casualty was removed from a vehicle after a road traffic collision on the A500, near Shavington. The crash involved two cars and the female was handed into the care of paramedics.
