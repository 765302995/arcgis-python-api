 Horse stuck in a ditch rescued in Bulkeley Time of Incident: 01/04/2019 - 15:29 Location: Wrexham Road, Bulkeley Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Crewe and one from Lymm - as did the rope rescue unit from Lymm Shortly before 3.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a horse being stuck in a ditch at a farm on Wrexham Road in Bulkeley. On arrival crews discovered that it was a deep ditch and an equine vet was at the scene. Firefighters assisted the vet in putting sheets around the horse to keep it warm. A plan of action was then devised, which resulted in firefighters lifting the horse from the ditch with the aid of slings. They then handed the horse over to the care of the vet. The rope rescue unit from Lymm assisted with the rescue. Firefighters were in attendance for more than two hours.
