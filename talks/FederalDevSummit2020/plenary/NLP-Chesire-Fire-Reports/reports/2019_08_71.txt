 Food left unattended at flats in Chester Time of Incident: 21/08/2019 - 01:27 Location: Treborth Road, Chester Attendance: Fire engines from Chester, Powey Lane and Ellesmere Port attended A fire alarm sounding at a block of flats in Chester alerted firefighters to a pan of food which had been left unattended on a cooker. The fire was out on arrival and crews gave oxygen therapy to one man who was suffering smoke inhalation. Paramedics attended and an ambulance took the casualty to hospital. Crews were in attendance for approximately one hour.
