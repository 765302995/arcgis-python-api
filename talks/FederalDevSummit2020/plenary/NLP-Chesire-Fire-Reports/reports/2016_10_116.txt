Wheelie bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 10/10/2016 - 21:36 Location: Woodchurch Lane, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port A wheelie bin fire had spread to some fencing in the opening of a street in Ellesmere Port. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.  This fire was believed to have been deliberate. Do you have any information? Contact Crime Stoppers on 0800 555 111
