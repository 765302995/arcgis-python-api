Collision in Knutsford Time of Incident: 11/06/2016 - 18:15 Location: Princess Street, Knutsford. Attendance: A fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters attended a report of a collision on Princess Street, Knutsford.  When they arrived they found that a car had collided with a wall. Thankfully nobody was seriously hurt. Crews made the scene safe before leaving.
