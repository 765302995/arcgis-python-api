 Conservatory fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 17/09/2018 - 11:58 Location: Allendale, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Penketh Firefighters were called to a conservatory fire on Allendale, Runcorn. The fire was well alight on arrival and crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire. One man was taken to hospital for a check up after suffering from slight smoke inhalation. Crews were in attendance for two hours.
