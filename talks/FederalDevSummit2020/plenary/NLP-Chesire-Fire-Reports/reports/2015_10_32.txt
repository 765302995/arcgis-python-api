Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 24/10/2015 - 23:13 Location: Nantwich Road, Crewe. Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a car fire on Nantwich Road, Crewe.   It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
