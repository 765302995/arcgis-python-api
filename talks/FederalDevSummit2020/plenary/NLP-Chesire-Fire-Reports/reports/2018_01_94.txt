 Collision on the M56 eastbound Time of Incident: 09/01/2018 - 08:30 Location: M56 eastbound Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one fire engine from Lymm Firefighter at the scene of a collision on M56Firefighters from Warrington and Lymm attended reports of a one vehicle collision on the M56 eastbound near to junction 10. On arrival at the scene crews found that one person was medically trapped in the vehicle.  Crews used electronic cutting equipment to release the casualty from the vehicle and they were passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service.
