 Grass fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 26/03/2018 - 12:38 Location: Cledford Lane, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Firefighters attended a large grass fire on land off Cledford Lane in Middlewich. Crews used a water backpack and beaters to extinguish the fire. The fire is believed to be deliberate and police have been informed. Anyone with information should call police on 101.
