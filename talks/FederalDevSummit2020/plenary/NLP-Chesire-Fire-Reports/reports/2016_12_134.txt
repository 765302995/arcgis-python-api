Road traffic collision on M56 Stoak Time of Incident: 05/12/2016 - 07:45 Location: M53/M56 at Stoak Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester. Four cars were involved in a road traffic collision in lanes two and three of the M56 at Stoak, where it merges with the M53. One woman was trapped in one of the cars and was released by firefighters. She was handed into the care of the ambulance service and firefighters made the scene safe.
