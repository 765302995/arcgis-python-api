Flooding in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 14/06/2016 - 15:20 Location: Coliseum Way, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Flash flooding which affected a number of premises in Coliseum Way, Ellesmere Port required firefighters to attend. The affected premises were evacuated and the crews made door to door checks on neighbouring shops to isolate electrics.
