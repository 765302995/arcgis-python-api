Road traffic collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 08/06/2016 - 22:59 Location: Weston Point Expressway, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn One car was involved in a collision with a street sign on Weston Point Expressway. A man was trapped in the car and was treated by firefighters until the ambulance arrived. The crews then assisted ambulance staff to release the casualty from the car and made the vehicle safe.
