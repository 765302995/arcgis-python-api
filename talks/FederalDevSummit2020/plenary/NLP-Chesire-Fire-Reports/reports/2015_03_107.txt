Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 08/03/2015 - 14:12 Location: Brookhouse Close, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Congleton One man was taken to hospital suffering from smoke inhalation after a small fire in the kitchen of a property in Macclesfield. One hose reel was used to extinguish the smouldering fire and a large fan was used to clear the smoke. Kitchen fire safety advice
