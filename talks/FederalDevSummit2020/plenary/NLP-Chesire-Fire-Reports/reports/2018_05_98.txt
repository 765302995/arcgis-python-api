 Persons reported fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/05/2018 - 00:10 Location: Worsborough Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Four fire engines attended - two from Penketh, one from Warrington and one from Widnes At around 12.10am firefighters were called to a persons reported fire at a domestic property in Worsborough Avenue in Great Sankey, Warrington. On arrival crews discovered that smoke alarms were sounding and all persons were accounted for. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. A covering jet was also set up. Once the fire was out crews used a large high pressure fan to remove the smoke from the property. A man suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation was passed over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for around two hours.
