 Small bonfire in woodland at a Warrington park Time of Incident: 06/08/2018 - 14:55 Location: Somerset Way, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 2.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in woodland at Woolston District Park on Somerset Way in Warrington.  On arrival the crew discovered a small bonfire unattended.  Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via General Enquiries (opens in new window) or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
