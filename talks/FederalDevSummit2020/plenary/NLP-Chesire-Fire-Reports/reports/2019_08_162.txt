 Collision in Rainow Time of Incident: 01/08/2019 - 20:40 Location: Macclesfield Road, Rainow Attendance: Two fire engines attended one from Macclesfield and one from Poynton Firefighters attended to a collision on Macclesfield Road in Rainow involving one car and a horse box.  The vehicles were made safe and one man along with a 10-year-old child were handed into the care of paramedics.  Another man and a woman were treated by fire crews.  A horse sadly died at the scene.  Crews assisted in clearing the roadway and making the vehicles safe.
