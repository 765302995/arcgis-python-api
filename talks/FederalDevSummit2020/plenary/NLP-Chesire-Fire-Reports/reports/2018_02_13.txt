 Chimney fire in Audlem Time of Incident: 25/02/2018 - 18:29 Location: Daisy Bank Crescent, Audlem Attendance: One fire engine from Audlem Firefighters attended a chimney fire at a residential property on Bank Crescent in Audlem. Crews attended the scene and used specialist chimney rods and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire. The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately two hours.
