Airing cupboard fire in Stockton Heath Time of Incident: 10/10/2016 - 18:25 Location: Belvoir Road, Stockton Heath, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and Stockton Heath Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a fire in an airing cupboard in a domestic property in Stockton Heath. Once the fire was out, they used a large fan to ventilate the smoke from the property. They also isolated the electrics whilst they dealt with the fire.
