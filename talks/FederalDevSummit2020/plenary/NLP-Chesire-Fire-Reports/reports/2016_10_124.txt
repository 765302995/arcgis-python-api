Settee on fire on open ground in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/10/2016 - 19:25 Location: Oleary Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A settee which was alight on open ground was extinguished with a hose reel. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone knows anything about it then they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
