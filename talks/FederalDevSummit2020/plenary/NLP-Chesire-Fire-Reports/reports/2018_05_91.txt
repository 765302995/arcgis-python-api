 Small bonfire in Crewe park Time of Incident: 18/05/2018 - 22:48 Location: Queens Park Gardens,Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe used one hose reel jet to extinguish a small bonfire near to the golf course in Queens Park, Crewe. The fire involved a pile of rubbish and is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML68144. Information can also be passed anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111.
