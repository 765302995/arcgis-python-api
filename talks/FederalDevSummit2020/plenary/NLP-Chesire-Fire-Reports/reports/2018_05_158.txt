 Man rescued from water in Thelwall Time of Incident: 11/05/2018 - 10:16 Location: Warrington Road, Thelwall Attendance: Fire engines from Lymm (1), Warrington (1), Penketh (1), Widnes (1) and the rope rescue unit from Lymm Firefighters, police and ambulance crews are in Warrington Road in Thelwall, rescuing a man who has fallen into the water from the bridge. Firefighters recovered him from the water on a longboard before he was stabilised and assessed by paramedics. He was then taken to hospital by air ambulance.
