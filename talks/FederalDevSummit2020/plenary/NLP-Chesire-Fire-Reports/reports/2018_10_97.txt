 One person trapped in car in Cuddington Time of Incident: 14/10/2018 - 11:37 Location: Norley Road, Cuddington Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley and one from Northwich Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision on Norley Road, Cuddington, between two cars. Crews assisted one female casualty from a vehicle who had been trapped. Crews then handed her over into the care of paramedics.
