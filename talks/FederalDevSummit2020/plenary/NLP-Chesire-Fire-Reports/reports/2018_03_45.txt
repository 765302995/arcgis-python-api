 Cat rescue in Shavington Time of Incident: 24/03/2018 - 15:53 Location: Main Road, Shavington, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Cat rescue in ShavingtonFirefighters assisted the RSPCA in rescuing a cat which had been stuck about 20ft up a tree since 4pm the previous day. Crews used a 7 metre ladder to rescue the cat in Main Road, Shavington.
