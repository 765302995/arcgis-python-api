 Dishwasher fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/10/2018 - 19:27 Location: Gresford Close, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Wigan Fire crews were called to a fire at a house on Gresford Close in Warrington involving a dishwasher in the kitchen.  Firefighters, wearing two breathing apparatus, used one hose reel, a positive pressure ventilation fan as well as a hearth hit and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire.   Crews were in attendance for approximately fifty minutes.
