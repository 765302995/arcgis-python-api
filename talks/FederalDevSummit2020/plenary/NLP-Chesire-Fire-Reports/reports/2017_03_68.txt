Horse in mud in High Legh Time of Incident: 21/03/2017 - 11:02 Location: Wrenshot Lane, High Legh Attendance: Two vehicles from Knutsford, one fire engine from Birchwood and one from Stockton Heath A horse which became stuck in mud in a field and was on its side needed the help of firefighters to get out. The horse, Murphy, was sedated by a vet and the crew used specialist animal rescue equipment to get the horse back on to firm ground and to its feet.  Sadly, despite the best efforts of the crews and the vet, Murphy did not survive.
