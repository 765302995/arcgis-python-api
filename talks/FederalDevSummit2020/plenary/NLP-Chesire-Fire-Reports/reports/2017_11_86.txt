Electrical substation fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/11/2017 - 15:22 Location: Waterloo Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes One crew from Widnes attended an electrical substation fire on Waterloo Road, Widnes. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and a dry powder extinguisher to control the fire, whilst engineers from Scottish power isolated the substation. The fire has since been extinguished and the site has been passed to Scottish Power.
