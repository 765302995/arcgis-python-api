 Commercial building fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 13/07/2019 - 06:37 Location: Halton Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn, one from Widnes and one from Penketh Firefighters were called to a fire in a disused commercial building on Halton Road, Runcorn. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  A fire investigation is ongoing. Crews remain at the scene to dampen down any hotspots.
