 Large fire at a house in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/04/2018 - 22:16 Location: Burtonwood Road, Warrington Attendance: Six fire engines attended - two from Penketh, one from Warrington, one from Lymm, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Wigan - plus a hydraulic platform from Lymm House fire WarringtonAt around 10.15pm firefighters were called to a persons reported fire at a property on Burtonwood Road in Warrington.  On arrival crews discovered a large detached house which was well alight.  All persons were out of the building on arrival.  Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels and two main water jets to fight the fire, which had spread to the roof.  A covering jet was also used to damp down a garage.  Due to the size of the blaze further fire engines were deployed to the scene and a hydraulic platform also attended.  Damage caused to a house in WarringtonParamedics treated three people at the scene and gas and electrics at the property were isolated.  Firefighters continued to work through the night using a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots of fire on the collapsed building.  They left the scene at around 11.45am.  An investigation into the cause of the fire has been conducted. It is believed to have been started accidentally by smoking-related materials.
