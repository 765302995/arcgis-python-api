Conservatory fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 29/01/2017 - 12:03 Location: Bradwall Close, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Firefighters were called to a fire involving a conservatory in Ellesmere Port. On arrival, crews discovered the fire was well developed and was in danger of spreading to other parts of the house. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to tackle the fire, and to prevent it from spreading any further.  One man was taken to hospital to be treated for smoke inhalation and minor burns. The fire was accidental and is believed to have started due to an electric heater.
