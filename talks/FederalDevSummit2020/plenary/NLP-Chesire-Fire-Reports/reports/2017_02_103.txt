Fire in Chester nursing home Time of Incident: 12/02/2017 - 19:10 Location: Lightfoot Street, Chester Attendance: Fire engines from Chester, Powey Lane and Ellesmere Port All residents and staff from a Lightfoot Street nursing home were evacuated after a fire was reported in one of the bedrooms. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus put out the fire using a hose reel jet. A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke from the building. One casualty was taken to hospital suffering from smoke inhalation.
