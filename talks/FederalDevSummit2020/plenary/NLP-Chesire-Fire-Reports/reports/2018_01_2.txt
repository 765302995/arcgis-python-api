 One person trapped after crash in Kelsall Time of Incident: 30/01/2018 - 16:16 Location: Kelsall Bypass, Kelsall Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one fire engine from Winsford. Firefighters from Winsford and Chester attended a two vehicle collision on the A54 Kelsall Bypass. One woman was medically trapped in one of the cars so the team used electronic cutting to release the casualty and she was passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service. Crews were in attendance at the scene for approximately 30 minutes.
