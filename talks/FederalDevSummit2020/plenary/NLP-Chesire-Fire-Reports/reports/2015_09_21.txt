Grass and hedgerow on fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 27/09/2015 - 15:19 Location: Eaton Lane, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley Approximately 10 square metres of grassland and hedgerow was involved in fire. A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire and police were asked to attend to manage traffic while the crew tackled the incident.
