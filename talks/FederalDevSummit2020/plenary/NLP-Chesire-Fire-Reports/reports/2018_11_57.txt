 Bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 19/11/2018 - 19:56 Location: Truro Close, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Runcorn firefighters used water to extinguish a bin which had been deliberately set alight in a Wooded area of Truro Close. The team were detained at the scene for approximately 25 minutes.   Anyone with any information in relation to the fire is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
