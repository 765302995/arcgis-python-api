 Wheelie bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 18/07/2018 - 02:47 Location: Sandringham Gardens, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 2.50am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open near a house's garden wall at Sandringham Gardens in Ellesmere Port. The crew discovered that the fire involved a wheelie bin. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 10 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. For wheelie bin fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
