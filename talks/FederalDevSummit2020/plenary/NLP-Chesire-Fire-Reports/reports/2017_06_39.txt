Large detached property on fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 23/06/2017 - 04:33 Location: Audley Road, Alsager Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe, one from Alsager and one from Staffordshire Fire and Rescue Service A fire in the roof space of a large disused property required firefighters to attend and use a main water jet to extinguish the flames. Crews stripped tiles from the roof to access the fire and fully extinguish it.
