 Garden leaves on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 04/01/2019 - 15:40 Location: Cow Lane, Northwich Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Northwich and one from Winsford Fire crews extinguished garden leaves which had been set alight on Cow Lane in Northwich.  Firefighters used a hose reel to put out the fire and then dampened down the area to ensure it was safe.
