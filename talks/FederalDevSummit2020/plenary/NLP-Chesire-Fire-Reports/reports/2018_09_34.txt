 Small fire in the open in Northwich Time of Incident: 23/09/2018 - 14:53 Location: Greenbank Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 2.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Greenbank Lane in Northwich. The crew extinguished the small fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
