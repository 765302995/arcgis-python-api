Car well alight after it was set on fire deliberately in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 14/02/2016 - 02:09 Location: Green Lawns Drive, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A  car was well alight, after it was set on fire deliberately. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to deal with the fire.  One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.  This incident was handed over to Cheshire Police.
