 Skip fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 08/05/2019 - 19:50 Location: Middlewich Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 7.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Middlewich Road in Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a skip. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
