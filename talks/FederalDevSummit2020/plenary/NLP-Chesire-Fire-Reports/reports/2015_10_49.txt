House fire in Chester Time of Incident: 21/10/2015 - 18:26 Location: Tarvin Road, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Tarvin Road, Chester.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved a grill pan. There was nobody home at the time of the fire. Firefightrers tackled the blaze with main water jets and hose reel jets. A large fan was also used to clear the smoke.  The property did have smoke alarms that activated. Crews carried out a Home Safety Assessment with the householder before leaving the scene.  Kitchen fire safety
