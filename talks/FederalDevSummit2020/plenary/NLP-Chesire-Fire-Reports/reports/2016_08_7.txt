Garden fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 29/08/2016 - 19:17 Location: Stamford Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A small fire in the garden of a Stamford Road house was put out by firefighters. A water backpack was used by the crew to put out the wood fire as smoke and ash was drifting across the street.
