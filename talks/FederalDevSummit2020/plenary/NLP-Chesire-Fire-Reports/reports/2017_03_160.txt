Bedroom fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/03/2017 - 01:57 Location: Mayfield Road, Grappenhall and Thelwall, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Stockton Heath Firefighters tackled a fire in a bedroom of a Mayfield Road house. A hose reel jet was used by the crews, one firefighter wearing breathing apparatus, to tackle the blaze. A high pressure fan was also used. An investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
