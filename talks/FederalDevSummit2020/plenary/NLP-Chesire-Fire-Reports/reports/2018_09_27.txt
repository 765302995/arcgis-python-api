 Fire at Daresbury Laboratory in Runcorn Time of Incident: 24/09/2018 - 01:05 Location: Keckwick Lane, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Warrington At around 1.05am firefighters were called to a report of an electrical fire at Daresbury Laboratory on Keckwick Lane in Daresbury, Runcorn. On arrival crews discovered that the fire inside a control room had been extinguished by on-site personal. A corridor was smoke logged and an on-site electrician isolated the electrics. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a large high pressure fan to clear the smoke. Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
