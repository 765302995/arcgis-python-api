 Woman rescued after being trapped underneath bus Time of Incident: 12/12/2018 - 21:53 Location: Kilncroft, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn, one from Widnes and the major rescue unit and supporting pump from Winsford Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision between a bus and a pedestrian on Kilncroft, Runcorn. The casualty was trapped underneath the bus and firefighters used high pressure airbags to lift the bus off the woman. They were then handed into the care of paramedics and taken to Aintree Hospital with serious injuries. Crews were at the scene for 50 minutes.
