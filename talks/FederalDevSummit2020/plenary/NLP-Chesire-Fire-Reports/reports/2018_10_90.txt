 Bonfire extinguished in Runcorn Time of Incident: 15/10/2018 - 19:11 Location: Halton Brook Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Crews in Runcorn attended to a bonfire which was deemed out of control in Halton Brook Avenue, Runcorn.  Firefighters used a high pressure hose reel to put out the fire and then made the area safe.
