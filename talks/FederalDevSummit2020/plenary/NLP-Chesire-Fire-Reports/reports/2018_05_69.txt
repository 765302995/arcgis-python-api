 Large shed on fire in Neston Time of Incident: 21/05/2018 - 17:04 Location: Burton Road, Neston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 5.05pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at an outbuilding on Burton Road in Neston. On arrival the crew discovered that a large shed measuring five metres by four metres was well alight and that the fire had affected a fence by the shed. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The crew then damped the area down and turned over the burnt wood to make sure the area was safe. Firefighters were at the scene for more than an hour.
