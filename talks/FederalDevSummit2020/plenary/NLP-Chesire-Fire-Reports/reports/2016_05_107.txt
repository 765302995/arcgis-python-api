Bushes on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/05/2016 - 19:27 Location: Hawleys Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a small fire involving bushes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111. Arson costs lives.
