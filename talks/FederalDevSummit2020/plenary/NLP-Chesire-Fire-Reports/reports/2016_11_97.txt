Cylinder on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/11/2016 - 16:13 Location: Newton Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a small propane cylinder on fire which they allowed to vent naturally. Water from a hose reel was used to cool the cylinder  and a cordon was set up for safety. Police also attended for traffic control.
