 Small fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 21:47 Location: College Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a small fire in the open at College Close, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was started deliberately. If you have any information then please contact Cheshire Police.
