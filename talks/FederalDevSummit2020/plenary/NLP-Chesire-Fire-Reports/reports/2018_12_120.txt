 Bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 03/12/2018 - 22:53 Location: Appleton Village, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 10.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open near to shops in Appleton Village in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire involving a bin. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
