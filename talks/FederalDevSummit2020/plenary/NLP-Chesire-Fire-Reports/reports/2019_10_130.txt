 Car submerged in flood water in Hartford Time of Incident: 06/10/2019 - 10:38 Location: Littledales Lane, Hartford, Northwich. Attendance: Three fire engines attended from Northwich, Winsford and Warrington. Shortly after 1.30am firefighters were dispatched to a reports of a car submerged in floodwater on Littledales Lane, Hartford. On arrival the crews found the car was approximately 3 foot deep in water under the railway bridge. Firefighters rescued to the two occupants from the car and a recovery crew were called to recover the vehicle. There were no injuries as a result of the incident and firefighters were detained for around 90 minutes. Littledales Lane has now been closed as a result of the flooding.
