Unattended cooking caused smoke in property in Crewe Time of Incident: 08/02/2016 - 14:06 Location: Frank Webb Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a property which was full of smoke due to unattended cooking. The crew gained entry to the property and removed two dogs to safety. All persons were accounted for at this incident. Kitchen fire safety advice
