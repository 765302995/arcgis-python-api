HGV with overheated axle on M6 Time of Incident: 23/05/2017 - 12:56 Location: M6 North junction 18 Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel Fire crews were called to the M6 Northbound at junction 18's roundabout above the motorway to a HGV which had black smoke coming out of it. Firefighters found that an axle had overheated and used one hose reel jet to cool it before the HGV was recovered by a recovery vehicle.
