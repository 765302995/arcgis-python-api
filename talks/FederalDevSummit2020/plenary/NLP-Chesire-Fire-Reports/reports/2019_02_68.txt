 Small fire in the open in Crewe Time of Incident: 18/02/2019 - 16:41 Location: Laburnum Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly after 4.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in woodland on Laburnum Avenue in Crewe. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire in the open and were in attendance for nearly 40 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
