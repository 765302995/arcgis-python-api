 Wheelie bin fires in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/03/2019 - 02:38 Location: Fairclough Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a number of wheelie bin fires on Fairclough Street, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fires which were on Fairclough and Jackson Street. One of the fires also set fire to some fencing. The fires were started deliberately. If you have any information about them then please contact Cheshire Police on 101.
