Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 30/03/2016 - 18:36 Location: Chester Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters attended a road traffic collision involving an overturned vehicle. One casualty, a woman, was treated at the scene by firefighters and ambulance personnel before being airlifted to hospital. The crews made the scene safe and were at the incident for just under an hour.
