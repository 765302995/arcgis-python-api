Fire involving refuse lorry in Crewe Time of Incident: 19/03/2015 - 14:51 Location: Remer Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a refuse lorry.  There was a fire in the gearbox area and a hose reel jet was used to put it out.  The crew dammed up entries to the drains to stop diesel oil contamination.
