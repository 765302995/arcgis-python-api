 Pile of leaves on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/10/2018 - 16:23 Location: Green Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 4.25pm firefighters were called to a report of a pile of leaves being on fire on a tennis court on Green Lane in Warrington. They put out the fire using a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than 10 minutes.
