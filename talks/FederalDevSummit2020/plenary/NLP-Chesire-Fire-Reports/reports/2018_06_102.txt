 Road traffic collision Congleton Time of Incident: 22/06/2018 - 21:07 Location: Buxton Road, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Congleton Update - Firefighters from Congleton attended a road traffic collision on the A54, Buxton Road, Congleton. On arrival crews found that a car had come off the carriageway and landed on an embankment at the side of the canal. The team assisted by releasing one casualty and removing the vehicle from the side of the canal. Sadly the driver of the car died at the scene. Firefighters were in attendance for approximately one hour.
