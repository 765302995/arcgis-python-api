 Serious collision in Arclid Time of Incident: 08/10/2018 - 02:22 Location: Congleton Road, Arclid. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Sandbach and Holmes Chapel At 2.22am firefighters from Sandbach and Holmes Chapel attended reports of a collision on Congleton Road in Arclid. The team attended and discovered the incident involved two vehicles. Crews assisted by making the vehicles safe. Four casualties were passed to the care of paramedics at the scene. Firefighters were in attendance at the scene for approximately one hour 45 minutes.
