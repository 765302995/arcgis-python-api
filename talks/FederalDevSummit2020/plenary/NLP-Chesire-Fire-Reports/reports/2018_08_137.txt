 Fire in the open at a farm in Chester Time of Incident: 06/08/2018 - 14:29 Location: Harthill Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas Fire in the open at a farm in ChesterAt around 2.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open at a farm on Harthill Lane in Chester.  On arrival the crew discovered a farm waste heap measuring approximately four metres by three metres by three metres well alight.  Firefighters used two hose reel jets to fight the midden fire.  They also used mechanical means to dig out the fire heap and damp the area down to make it safe.  As damping down took place a thermal imaging camera was used to check for hotspots.  Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
