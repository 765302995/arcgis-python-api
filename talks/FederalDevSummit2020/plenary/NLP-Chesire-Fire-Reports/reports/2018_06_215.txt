 Bedroom fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 07/06/2018 - 21:09 Location: Briarfield Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a fire in the bedroom of a mid-terraced house in Briarfield Road, Ellesmere Port. Crews used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to extinguish the flames and then used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
