 Two small fires in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 10/09/2019 - 15:51 Location: Grangeway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly before 4pm firefighters were called to a report of two fires in the open in Grangeway, Runcorn. They used water to put out the small fires and were in attendance for around 10 minutes. The fires are believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fires should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
