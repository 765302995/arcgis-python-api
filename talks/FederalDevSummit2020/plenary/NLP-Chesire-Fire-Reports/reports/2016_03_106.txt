Substation fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 12/03/2016 - 02:42 Location: Charter Way, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield An electrical substation was involved in fire.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a dry powder extinguisher to put out the small fire. Electrical engineers had isolated the external supply before the crews could get inside to deal with the fire. Firefighters were at the scene for approximately two hours.
