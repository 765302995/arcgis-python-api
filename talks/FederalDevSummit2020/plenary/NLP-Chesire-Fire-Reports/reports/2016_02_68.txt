Cars on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/02/2016 - 18:32 Location: Rushgreen Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath, one from Manchester and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Several cars were involved in fire at a property in Warrington. The vehicles were in a compound and located close to a portable office building.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, created a fire break and used hose reel jets to tackle the blaze. The fire was brought under control and crews continued to damp down. They were at the scene for just over one hour.
