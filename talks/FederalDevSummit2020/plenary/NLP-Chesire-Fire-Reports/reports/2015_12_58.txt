Report of electrical cable overheating in Crewe Time of Incident: 15/12/2015 - 22:40 Location: Camm Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to a domestic property in Crewe due to concerns about an electrical cable overheating.  Fire crews used a thermal imaging camera to investigate and isolated the electricity supply. The incident was reported to Scottish Power.
