House fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/03/2016 - 01:15 Location: New School Lane, Childer Thornton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port One person was taken to hospital after a bedroom fire at a house in New School Lane. The first floor of the house was well alight. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a covering jet to bring the fire under control.  A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke as crews continued to damp down the affected area.  The casualty, a man, was taken to hospital by ambulance. The cause of the fire is under investigation.   Please, always make sure you have smoke alarms fitted and working - Smoke alarms
