Shed fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 29/09/2015 - 17:35 Location: Bedford Rise, Winsford. Attendance: A fire engine from Winsford Firefighters responded to a fire on Bedford Rise, Winsford.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving conifers that had spred to a shed. Hose reel jets were used to put the fire out.  A Home Safety Assesment was also carried out before crews left the scene.
