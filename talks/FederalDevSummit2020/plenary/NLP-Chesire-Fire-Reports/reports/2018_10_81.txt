 Bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/10/2018 - 20:07 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to a fire in a large industrial bin on Grasmere Avenue, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information then please contact Cheshire Police.
