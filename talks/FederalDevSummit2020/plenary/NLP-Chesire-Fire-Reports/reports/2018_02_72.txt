 Two cars on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 15/02/2018 - 00:17 Location: Grizedale, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to two cars which were on fire on Grizedale, Widnes. Both vehicles were well alight on arrival of the crews and they, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fires. These fires were started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 with any information. Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
