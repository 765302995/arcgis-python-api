 Wood on fire in Winsford field Time of Incident: 17/09/2018 - 19:34 Location: Finsbury Walk, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters attended to a small amount of wood well alight on a field on Finsbury Walk in Winsford.  Crews used one hose reel to put out the fire and made the area safe.
