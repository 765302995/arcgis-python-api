House fire in Chester Time of Incident: 01/11/2016 - 22:14 Location: Daniell Way, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Three fire engines were sent to a house fire in Daniell Way, Great Boughton. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, went into the house, using two hose reel jets to tackle the blaze. Gas and electricity supplies were isolated and crews used a high pressure fan to clear the smoke. There was one casualty reported, a lady, suffering from slight smoke inhalation. She was treated at the scene by firefighters. An investigation into the cause of the fire is being carried out.
