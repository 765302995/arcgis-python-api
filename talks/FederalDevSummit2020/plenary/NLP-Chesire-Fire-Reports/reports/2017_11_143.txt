Wheelie bin fires in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/11/2017 - 20:24 Location: Bechers, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to deal with two wheelie bin fires on Bechers, Widnes. The bins were well alight on arrival and crews used a fire extinguisher to put out the fire. The fire was started deliberately and anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.  Advice on how to stop wheelie bin fires
