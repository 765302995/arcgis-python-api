 Large uncontrolled rubbish fire in Rixton Time of Incident: 05/02/2018 - 17:55 Location: Manchester Road, Rixton, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood and one from Lymm Fire crews were called to a large uncontrolled rubbish fire on Manchester Road, Rixton, Warrington. Crews, wearing respirators, used a hose reel jet and turning over equipment to put out the fire.
