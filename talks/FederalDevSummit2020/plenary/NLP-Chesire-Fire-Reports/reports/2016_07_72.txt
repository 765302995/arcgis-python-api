Car fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 18/07/2016 - 18:13 Location: A34, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow An electrical fault was the cause of a fire involving a car on the A34 near Handforth industrial estate. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control and then continued to cool the car.
