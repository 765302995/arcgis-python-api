 House fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 15/03/2018 - 21:16 Location: Ordsall Close, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe and one from Sandbach Fire crews were called to a house fire on Ordsall Close, Sandbach. The fire was on the ground floor of the property and crews, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets, a covering jet and a large fan to bring the fire under control. One person in the property self evacuated prior to the fire service's arrival and was treated for the effects of smoke inhalation. A further four people from neighbouring properties were also treated for smoke inhalation. Crews were at the scene for approximately two hours and forty five minutes.
