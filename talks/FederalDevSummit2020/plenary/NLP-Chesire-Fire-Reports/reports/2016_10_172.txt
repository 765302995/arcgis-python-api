Fryer on fire in kitchen in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 02/10/2016 - 09:41 Location: London Road, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one appliance from Poynton Firefighters were called to reports of a fryer which was on fire in the rear kitchen of a property. Crews sectioned off a part of the building and made the shop stable. They then inspected the building for any further signs of fire.
