 Smoke logged house in Bollington Time of Incident: 01/01/2019 - 15:16 Location: Cedarway, Bollington Attendance:  Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield At around 3.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a house being full of smoke in Cedarway, Bollington. On arrival crews discovered that the smoke was caused by a small fire involving a toaster in the kitchen. The fire was out on arrival. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a large high pressure fan to clear the smoke from the house. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
