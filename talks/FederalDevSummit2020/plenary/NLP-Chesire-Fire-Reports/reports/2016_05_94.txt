Tumble dryer fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 14/05/2016 - 11:49 Location: Criccieth Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A tumble dryer was involved in a fire in an Ellesmere Port flat. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and gas supplies were isolated. Crews were at the scene for around an hour.
