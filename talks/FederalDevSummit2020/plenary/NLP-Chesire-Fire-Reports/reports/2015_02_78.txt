Road traffic collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 09/02/2015 - 17:06 Location: High Street, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich A road traffic collision involving three cars left one woman trapped in one of the cars.  Both lanes of High Street had to be closed as emergency services dealt with the incident.  Firefighters cut the woman free and handed her into the care of the ambulance service.
