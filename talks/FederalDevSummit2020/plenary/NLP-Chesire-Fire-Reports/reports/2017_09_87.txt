Flat fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 06/09/2017 - 12:00 Location: Naylor Court, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Middlewich Fire crews were called to a flat fire on Naylor Court, Northwich. Crews located the fire in the kitchen of the property and found a small amount of clothes which had accidentally been set alight by smoking materials.
