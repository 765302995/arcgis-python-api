Warrington flat fire Time of Incident: 12/02/2017 - 04:24 Location: Boston Boulevard, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: Fire engines from Penketh, Warrington and Stockton Heath A fire in a first floor flat on Boston Boulevard was caused by unattended cooking. Firefighters gave oxygen therapy to a woman from the flat and dealt with the incident.  Please follow our cooking safety advice and never leave cooking unattended.
