Fire reported at an address in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/11/2017 - 19:13 Location: Barrow Hall lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one from Warrington At 7.13pm on the Friday, 3 November two fire engines from Penketh and one fire engine from Warrington attended a fire on Barrow Hall Lane, Great Sankey, Warrington involving a kitchen of a domestic property. Crews used a positive pressure ventilation fan to extinguish the fire. One man was taken to hospital for a precautionary check suffering from smoke inhalation. Crews were in attendance for forty minutes.
