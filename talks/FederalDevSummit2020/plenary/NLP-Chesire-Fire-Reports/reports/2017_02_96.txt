Wood burning stove on fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 14/02/2017 - 12:09 Location: Springwood Avenue, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a wood burning stove on Springwood Avenue. The crew used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and a high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property. The roof space was also confirmed to be clear of fire and smoke.
