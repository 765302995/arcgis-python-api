 Hot tub fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 28/05/2018 - 11:04 Location: Marple Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters attended to a hot tub fire at an address on Marple Road in Northwich.  When crews arrived the hot tub, which was at the side of the house, had been extinguished by residents.  Firefighters then dampened down the area and made sure it was safe.
