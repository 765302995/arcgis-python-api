 One-vehicle collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 29/04/2019 - 13:06 Location: Dover Drive, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Shortly after 1pm firefighters from Northwich and Winsford were mobilised to reports of a road traffic collision on Dover Drive in Winsford.  On arrival crews found that a car had collided with a bollard and a woman was trapped in the car.  Firefighters used electronic cutting equipment to access the casualty. They then used a long board to release her from the vehicle.  The casualty was then passed to the care of paramedics  Crews were at the scene for approximately 30 minutes.
