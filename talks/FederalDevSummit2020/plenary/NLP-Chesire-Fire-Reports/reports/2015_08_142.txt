Collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 08/08/2015 - 16:16 Location: Eastbound carriageway of the M56 at Junction 14. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port and Runcorn Firefighters responded to a report of a collision on the eastbound carriageway of the M56.  When they arrived crews found that there was a man trapped in his vehicle. Crews freed him and he was passed to the care of the ambulance service.
