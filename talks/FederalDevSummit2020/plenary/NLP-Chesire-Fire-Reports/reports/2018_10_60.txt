 Garage fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/10/2018 - 23:22 Location: Brownhill Drive, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Fire crews were called to a garage fire on Brownhill Drive, Warrington. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for 50 minutes.
