Bailing machine and straw fire in Meadowbank in Winsford Time of Incident: 17/08/2016 - 16:42 Location: Meadowbank Grove, Meadowbank, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one fire engine from Northwich A fire involving a bailing machine in a field had spread to approximately 100 square metres of straw and several bales of hay. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to tackle the fire.
