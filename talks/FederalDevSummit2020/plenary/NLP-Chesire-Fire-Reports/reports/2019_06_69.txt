 Man falls into river in Congleton Time of Incident: 16/06/2019 - 14:27 Location: Fern Crescent, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton At around 2.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a man having fallen into the River Dane in the Fern Crescent area of Congleton. The crew worked with paramedics to rescue to man with the aid of a longboard. Firefighters then helped paramedics to get him into an air ambulance. The man was taken to hospital via the ambulance. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
