Fire in attic of Hartford property Time of Incident: 14/05/2016 - 16:48 Location: Abbey Lane, Hartford, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Holmes Chapel There was a fire in the attic of a Hartford house. The fire involved the boiler. Crews isolated gas and electricity to the property and put out the fire using a hose reel jet, with two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus.
