 Car fire in Chester Time of Incident: 17/05/2019 - 18:06 Location: Wrexham Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester. One fire engine attended from Powey Lane. Firefighters were called to a car on fire in Wrexham Road, Chester. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames. The fire is believed to have been accidental due to a fault with the ignition.
