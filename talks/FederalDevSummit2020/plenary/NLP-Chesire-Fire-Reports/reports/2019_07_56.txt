 Two fires in the open in Crewe Time of Incident: 20/07/2019 - 18:44 Location: Middlewich Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At around 6.45pm firefighters were called to reports of hedgerow fires on Middlewich Road in Crewe. On arrival the crews found two fires in the open approximately 500 metres apart. Firefighters extinguished both fires with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
