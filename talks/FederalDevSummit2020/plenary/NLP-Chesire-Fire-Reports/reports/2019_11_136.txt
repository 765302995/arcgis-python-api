 Fire in the open, Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/11/2019 - 20:03 Location: Oldfield Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to deal with a fire in an alleyway at the back of houses on Oldfield Street, Ellesmere Port. The fire, which involved some rubbish, was well alight and spreading to nearby gardens. When they arrived, the firefighters found it was affecting some nearby trees too, and they use a hose reel to tackle it. It continued to spread to some fence panel, but they brought it under control and managed to extinguish it. They stayed a while longer to damp down and ensure there was no chance of reignition.
