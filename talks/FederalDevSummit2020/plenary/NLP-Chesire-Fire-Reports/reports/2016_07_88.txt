Kitchen fire in Neston Time of Incident: 15/07/2016 - 16:03 Location: New Heyes, Neston Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a kitchen on fire in a mid town house and used a hose reel jet to extinguish it. All persons were accounted for and a thermal imaging camera was used to check the area before crews left. Kitchen fire safety advice
