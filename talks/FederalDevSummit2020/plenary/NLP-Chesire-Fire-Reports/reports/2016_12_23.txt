Kitchen fire in Widnes chip shop Time of Incident: 28/12/2016 - 11:31 Location: Cronton Lane, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of fire in ducting in the kitchen of a chip shop. Three fire engines attended and the crews used two breathing apparatus, dry powder extinguishers, hose reel jets, covering jets, thermal image cameras and a high pressure fan to deal with the incident. Firefighters also provided casualty care for one casualty while they waited for an ambulance to arrive.
