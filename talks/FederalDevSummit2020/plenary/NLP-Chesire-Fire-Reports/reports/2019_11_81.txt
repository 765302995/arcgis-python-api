 Smoke logged flat in Chester Time of Incident: 13/11/2019 - 19:57 Location: Francis Court, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Chester and one from Powey Lane Shortly before 8pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a flat in Francis Court, Chester.  On arrival crews found the flat smoke logged as a result of food being burnt.  No-one was unaccounted for.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to clear the smoke from the flat and were in attendance for nearly half-an-hour.  For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
