 Van fire in Anderton Time of Incident: 06/11/2019 - 00:33 Location: Marbury Road, Anderton, near Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Shortly after 12.30am firefighters from Northwich were dispatched to reports of a vehicle fire on the car park near to the Anderton Boat lift. On arrival at the scene the crews discovered a van which was well alight. One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 558088.
