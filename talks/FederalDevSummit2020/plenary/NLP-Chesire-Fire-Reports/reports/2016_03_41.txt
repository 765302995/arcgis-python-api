Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 23/03/2016 - 02:27 Location: Lime Street, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Arson is suspected in the case of a car fire in Lime Street. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to bring the blaze under control.  April is Arson Awareness Month but be vigilant at all times - if you know anything about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
