Chimney fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 13/11/2017 - 19:32 Location: Parr Green Lane, Malpas. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas Crews from Malpas were called to reports of a chimney fire at a residential address. On arrival the fire was well alight and flames could be seen coming from the top of the top of the chimney. Firefighters used a 7-metre ladder, breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to tackle the fire.
