House fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 24/02/2016 - 21:33 Location: Maple Road, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Firefighters led a woman to safety from a fire in the kitchen of a house in Maple Road.  The fire was put out and the casualty was taken to hospital as a precaution. Smoke alarms were fitted and worked to alert the householder to the fire.
