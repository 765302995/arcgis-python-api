 Rescue of a person in Congleton Time of Incident: 10/11/2018 - 12:39 Location: Buxton Road, Congleton. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Congleton and one from Macclesfield Shortly after 12.30pm firefighters from Congleton and Macclesfield were called to assist paramedics at the scene of a collision on Buxton Road. Crews attended the scene and found that a car had gone over an embankment and dropped 25 feet into a stream. Nobody was trapped into the vehicle, although the driver of the car was in need of medical assistance. The team used a longboard to rescue the casualty and he was passed to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately one hour.
