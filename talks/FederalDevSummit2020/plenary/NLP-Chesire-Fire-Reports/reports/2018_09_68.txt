 Tyres on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/09/2018 - 17:39 Location: Earle Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to reports of tyres well alight on Earle Road in Widnes.  Crews attended to the incident, near a gym, and used a hose reel to put out the fire.
