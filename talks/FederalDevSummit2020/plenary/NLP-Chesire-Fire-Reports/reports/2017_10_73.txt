Bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 15/10/2017 - 17:37 Location: Albert Square, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Fire crews were called to three large industrial bins on fire on Albert Square, Widnes. The bins were well alight on arrival and crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about it then please call Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
