Roof blown off a row of some garages Time of Incident: 23/02/2017 - 16:06 Location: Wellington Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report that a roof had come loose on a row of garages off Wellington Road, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived they worked alongside partner agencies to make the scene as safe as possible.
