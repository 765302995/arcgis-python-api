 Small fire in woodland in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/05/2018 - 16:55 Location: Old Hall Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 4.55pm firefighters were called to a small fire in woodland on Old Hall Road in Warrington. They used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire in the open and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
