 Chimney fire in Coddington Time of Incident: 09/02/2019 - 15:54 Location: Beachin Lane, Coddington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Fire crews were called to a chimney fire on Beachin Lane, Coddington. Crews used a 13.5m ladder, working at height equipment and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
