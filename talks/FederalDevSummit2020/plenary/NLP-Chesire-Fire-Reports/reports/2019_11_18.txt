 Fire on a playing field in Bruche Time of Incident: 24/11/2019 - 19:17 Location: Larkfield Avenue, Bruche, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Warrington and Lymm. At around 7.20pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a park on Larkfield Avenue in Bruche.  On arrival the crews discovered the fire involved a wooded walkway which was well alight.  Firefighters used an extended hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  The crews were detained at the park for approximately 30 minutes.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 573047.
