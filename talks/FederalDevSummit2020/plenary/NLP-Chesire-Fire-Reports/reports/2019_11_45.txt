 One-vehicle collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 20/11/2019 - 21:10 Location: Stoneley Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Crews attended to a road traffic collision involving a car that is believed to have collided with a fence.  The car was not on fire however a hose reel was used to cool the engine and it was then made safe.  No one was trapped inside the car.
