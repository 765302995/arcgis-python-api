One car involved in Plumley road traffic collision Time of Incident: 17/02/2016 - 10:51 Location: Manchester Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a report of a road traffic collision where a car had collided with a wall. There was one casualty in the car and firefighters liaised with paramedics about her removal. They were able to assist her from the vehicle without having to use cutting equipment and then made the scene safe.
