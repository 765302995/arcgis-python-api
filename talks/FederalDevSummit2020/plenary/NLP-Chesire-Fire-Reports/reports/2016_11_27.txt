Bathroom fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 25/11/2016 - 15:58 Location: Ivy Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A fire involving an electric shower was put out by firefighters. The electricity supply was isolated as the crew, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A thermal imaging camera was then used to check there were no further hot spots of fire either in the bathroom or any fire spread through to the roof space and a high pressure fan ventilated the house.
