Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/05/2015 - 10:59 Location: Scott Street, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters attended a fire on Scott Street, Fairfield and Howley, Warrington.  TWhen they arrived they found that the fire involved the ground floor kitchen of the property. Firefighters used four breathing apparatus, two hose reels and a positive pressure ventilation fan to bring the fire under control.
