Chimney fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 02/04/2016 - 14:43 Location: Bond Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield The top of a Macclesfield property chimney was on fire. Firefighters used water to put out the fire and checked the loft area using a thermal imaging camera.  Always follow our chimney safety advice.
