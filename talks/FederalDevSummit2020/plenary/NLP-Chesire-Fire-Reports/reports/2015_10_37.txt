Fire near a medical centre in Winsford Time of Incident: 24/10/2015 - 00:45 Location: Dene Drive, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Dene Drive in Winsford.  When they arrived they found that a rubbish fire close to a medical centre had caused heat damage to the exterior of the building. The fire was extinguished using a hose reel jet.  Crews removed cladding from the exterior of the building to check for fire spread. They also gained access to the building to check for further damage inside a full inspection took place using thermal imaging camera and there was no fire inside the property. Crews were in attendance for approximately 1 hour and 20 minutes  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
