Grassland fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/03/2015 - 16:16 Location: Castle Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A second grassland fire was tackled by Runcorn firefighters.  Approximately 15 metres of grass and embankment was involved in the blaze which was put out by the crew using a main jet.   To avoid fires in the open please follow our safety advice - Countryside safety
