 HGV collides with a railway bridge in Widnes Time of Incident: 19/08/2018 - 20:24 Location: Victoria Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes attended At around 10.25pm firefighters were called to a report of a heavy goods vehicle having collided with a railway bridge as it drove under it on Victoria Road in Widnes. The road was closed by police as a crew from Widnes Fire Station cleared debris from the bridge and from the top of the HGV. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes. Network Rail also attended.
