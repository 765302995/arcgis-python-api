Kitchen fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 04/10/2015 - 17:49 Location: Bancroft Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one from Runcorn Crews were called out to a report of a fire in the kitchen of a property in Widnes in which the call stated there were people reported in the building. The alarm had been raised because of overheated food in a microwave which had caused smoke in the property. Firefighters used a high pressure fan to clear the smoke.  To avoid cooking fires please follow our Kitchen fire safety advice.
