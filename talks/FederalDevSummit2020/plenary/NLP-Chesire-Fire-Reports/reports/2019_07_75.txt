 Boy stuck in a bike in Chester Time of Incident: 16/07/2019 - 19:32 Location: Cheyney Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters were called to a boy who had got his foot stuck in his bike on Cheyney Road, Chester. Crews managed to release his foot which was stuck in the bike pedal. The boy was taken to hospital by his parents as a precaution.
