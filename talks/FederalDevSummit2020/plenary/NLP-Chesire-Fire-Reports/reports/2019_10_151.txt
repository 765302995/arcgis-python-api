 Falling fascia in Chester Time of Incident: 01/10/2019 - 14:41 Location: Foregate Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester. Firefighters were called to reports of wooden fascia having fallen off a bank in Foregate Street, Chester. Crews put a temporary cordon round the affected area until a more permanent one was put in place by the council. The aerial ladder platform was in use so that a firefighter could remove fascia board to make it safe. The council building inspector will then assess the structure.
