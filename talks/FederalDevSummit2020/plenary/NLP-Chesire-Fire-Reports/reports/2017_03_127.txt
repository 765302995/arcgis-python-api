Cooker fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/03/2017 - 20:35 Location: Mill Lane, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Mill Lane, Warrington.  When they arrived they found that the incident had been caused by a pan that had been left on the hob. There was no fire but the property was filled with smoke. Crews used a large fan to clear this.
