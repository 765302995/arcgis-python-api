Microwave fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/01/2017 - 16:15 Location: Westminster Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters performed a health and safety assessment of a property after a fire in the microwave caused the kitchen to fill with smoke. The fire did not affect the property and the smoke was allowed to ventilate naturally.  Please take when care when cooking.
