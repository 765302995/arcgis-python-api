Flat fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 25/01/2015 - 01:37 Location: Rainow Way, Wilmslow. Attendance: A fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a report of a flat fire on Rainow Way in Wilmslow.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved a quad bike outside a ground floor flat. Two people had got out of the flat via a window. A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out.  An investigation has now been launched to find the cause of the fire.
