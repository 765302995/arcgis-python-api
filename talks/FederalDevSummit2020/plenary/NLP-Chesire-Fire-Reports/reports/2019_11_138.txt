 Nuisance bonfire, Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/11/2019 - 19:29 Location: Wheatlands, Halton Brook, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters turned out to reports of a fire that had deliberately been started and left near the bridge in Wheatlands, Halton Brook. It had been abandoned and left to burn, with firefighters using a single hose reel to put it out.
