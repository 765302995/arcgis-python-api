 Digger fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 08/01/2019 - 22:48 Location: Viking Way, Congleton. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters were called to a digger on fire on land off Viking Way, Congleton. Crews used breathing apparatus and two hose reels to put out the fire. They the damped down the hotspots. The fire is believed to have been the result of an electrical fault.
