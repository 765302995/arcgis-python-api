Chip pan fire in Cuddington Time of Incident: 06/03/2016 - 17:58 Location: Manor Road, Cuddington, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford, one from Northwich and one from Middlewich Firefighters were called to a chip pan on fire with persons reported in the property. One hose reel was used to extinguish the fire and a large fan removed smoke from the property. One person was assessed by paramedics.
