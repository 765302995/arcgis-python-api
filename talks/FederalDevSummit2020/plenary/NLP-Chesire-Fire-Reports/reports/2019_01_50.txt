 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/01/2019 - 21:39 Location: Cross Street, Widnes. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters from Widnes used a hose reel jet  to put out a fire in a wheelie bin on Cross Street. Crews were detained at the scene for approximately ten minutes.  Please follow our wheelie bin safety advice.
