Small fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/11/2015 - 16:13 Location: Westbrook Centre, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Warrington Firefighters used a water back to put out a small fire at the Westbrook Centre, Warrington.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
