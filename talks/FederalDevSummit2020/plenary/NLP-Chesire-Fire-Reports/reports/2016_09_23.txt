Washing machine smoking in kitchen in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/09/2016 - 14:48 Location: Radway Green, Great Sutton Street Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to reports of a washing machine smoking inside a kitchen in a domestic property. Crews removed the washing machine from the property to investigate the fire, which was believed to have been called by an electrical fault, before carrying out a health and safety assessment.
