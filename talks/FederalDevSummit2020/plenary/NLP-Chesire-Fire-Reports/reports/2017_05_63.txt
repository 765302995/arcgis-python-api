Very tall tree set alight in Crewe Time of Incident: 20/05/2017 - 20:25 Location: Near to the old Lodgefield School site, by Sharnbrook Drive, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe A large tree was well alight. The tree was very tall, and it was difficult for firefighters to access it because of the location away from the roadway.  Firefighters used two extra long hose reels to extinguish the fire.   The fire is believed to have been started deliberately so anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
