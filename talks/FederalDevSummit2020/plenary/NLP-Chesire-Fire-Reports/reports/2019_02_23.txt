 Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 24/02/2019 - 22:53 Location: Haslington, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly before 11pm firefighters were called to a report of a car being on fire in Haslington, Crewe. On arrival the crew found a hatchback well alight in a field. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which completely destroyed the car. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 331790, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
