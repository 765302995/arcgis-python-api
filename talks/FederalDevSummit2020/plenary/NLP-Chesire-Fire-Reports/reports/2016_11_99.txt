Road traffic collision in Knutsford Time of Incident: 11/11/2016 - 11:22 Location: Chester Road, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving a heavy good vehicle and a saloon car with one woman trapped. The crew extricated the casualty from the vehicle and handed the woman to the care of paramedics.
