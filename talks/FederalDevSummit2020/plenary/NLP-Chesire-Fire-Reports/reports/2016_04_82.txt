Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/04/2016 - 22:20 Location: Farrell Street in Fairfield and Howley, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Two fire crews from Warrington fire extinguished a fire inside the kitchen of a property on Farrell Street in Fairfield and Howley, Warrington. A plastic recycling tub had been put onto a cooker top which set on fire. Firefighters used one hose reel jet.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. Police attended this incident.
