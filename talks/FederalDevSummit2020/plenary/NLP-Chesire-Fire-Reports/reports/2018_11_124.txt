 Two-vehicle collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/11/2018 - 09:25 Location: London Road/Shepcroft Lane, Stretton, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Lymm Two-vehicle collision in WarringtonAt around 9.25am firefighters were called to reports of a collision involving two vehicles on London Road/Shepcroft Lane in Stretton, Warrington.  The road was closed by the police in both directions as emergency services worked at the scene.  Firefighters extricated a casualty from one of the vehicles and handed them over to the care of paramedics.  Fire crews also made the vehicles safe.  They were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
