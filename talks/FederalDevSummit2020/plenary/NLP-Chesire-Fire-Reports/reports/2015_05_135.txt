Road traffic collision on M6 northbound junctions 18 to 19 Time of Incident: 05/05/2015 - 16:24 Location: M6 northbound junctions 18 to 19 Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich, one from Winsford and one from Knutsford A road traffic collision on the M6 was made safe by firefighters.  Nobody was trapped in the vehicle, a van,  but police and the highways agency closed two lanes as firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, dealt with a leak in a LPG tank on the van.
