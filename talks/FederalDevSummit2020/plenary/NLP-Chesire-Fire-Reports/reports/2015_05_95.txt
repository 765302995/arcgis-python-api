Extractor fan causes takeaway fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 12/05/2015 - 14:03 Location: Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port An overheated motor in an extractor fan caused a small fire at an Ellesmere Port takeaway.  The fire was found in the roof void and a hose reel jet was used to put it out.  A thermal imaging camera was then used to check there were no further hotspots of fire.
