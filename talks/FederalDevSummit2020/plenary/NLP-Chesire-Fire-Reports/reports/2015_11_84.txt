Cooker fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 13/11/2015 - 14:09 Location: Cardenbrook Grove, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Greater Manchester FRS An electric hob was accidentally turned on by a dog, causing a kitchen fire as there were items on top of the hob. Firefighters were alerted to the incident and gained access to the property. They found a fire in the kitchen and used a hose reel jet to put it out. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke from the house. Two dogs were rescued by firefighters.  Please see our kitchen safety information to avoid fires.
