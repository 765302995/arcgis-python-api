 Crews extinguish building fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 29/01/2018 - 20:58 Location: Byley Way, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Winsford At 8.58pm on Monday, 29 January two fire engines from Winsford attended a building fire on Byley Way in Winsford. On arrival crews found the fire involved a large quantity of rubbish between two garages which caused slight damage to one garage. One hose reel and one thermal imaging camera were used to extinguish the fire.
