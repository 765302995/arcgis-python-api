Road traffic collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 28/11/2016 - 11:16 Location: M6 southbound junctions 18 and 17 Attendance: Fire engines from Holmes Chapel, Sandbach, Middlewich and Winsford Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision on the southbound carriageway of the M6, between junctions 18 and 17. The road was closed while  emergency services worked at the scene. The collision involved two heavy goods vehicles and a saloon car with one person trapped. The crews assisted paramedics with one casualty at the scene.
