Fire in industrial premises in Crewe Time of Incident: 22/06/2017 - 05:06 Location: Gresty Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe, Nantwich, Alsager, Middlewich, Audlem and the aerial appliance and supporting fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a fire in a dust unit at Morning Foods Ltd on Gresty Road, Crewe. The dust unit was well alight on arrival of the fire crews and was on the ground, first and second floors of the four-storey building. Crews, six wearing breathing apparatus, used three hose reel jets, a covering jet and a thermal imaging camera to tackle the fire. The first pump left the scene after just under two and a half hours with the last pump leaving after just under seven hours.
