Bonfire in Millington Time of Incident: 26/10/2016 - 18:51 Location: Chester Road in Millington. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Knutsford Firefighters responded to reports of a fire near the Cheshire Lounge off Chester Road in Millington.  When they arrived they found that there was two large bonfires that had been left to burn. Crews used hose reel jets to put the fires out.
