Wheelie bins on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/07/2016 - 22:01 Location: Plumtree Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Two wheelie bins were set on fire in Bewsey and Whitecross. Firefighters used a hosereel jet in what is suspected to be an arson attempt.  Always follow our wheelie bin safety advice.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
