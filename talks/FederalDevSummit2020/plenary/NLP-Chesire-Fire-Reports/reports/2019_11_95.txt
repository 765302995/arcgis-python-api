 Fire at a house in Chester Time of Incident: 11/11/2019 - 02:00 Location: Boleyn Close, Blacon, near Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Chester and one from Powey Lane At 2am firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a house in Boleyn Close, Blacon, near Chester.  The fire involving an electric razor was out on arrival.  Two occupants had inhaled smoke caused by the fire.  Firefighters administered oxygen to them before handing the casualties over to the care of paramedics.  They were taken to hospital as a precaution.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
