 Chimney fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 01/04/2018 - 16:30 Location: Doric Avenue, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Crews were called to a chimney fire on Doric Avenue in Frodsham caused by wood burning on a stove.  Firefighters removed the wood burning stove from the grate and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and monitored the drop of the chimney's temperature.
