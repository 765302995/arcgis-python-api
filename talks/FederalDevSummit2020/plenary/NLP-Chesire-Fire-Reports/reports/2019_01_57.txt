 Smell of burning at a nursing home in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/01/2019 - 20:56 Location: Weaste Lane, Warrington Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Penketh and one from Trafford At around 8.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a possible fire at a nursing home on Weaste Lane in Warrington. On arrival they discovered a slight smell of burning on the second floor. The building was evacuated and firefighters carried out a full inspection of the building, using a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. They could find no signs of fire. Firefighters advised the nursing home to arrange for an electrician to attend and inspect the building's electrics as soon as possible. Firefighters were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.
