Pallet fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 14/05/2017 - 01:06 Location: grove way, wilmslow. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a report of a building fire on Grove Way in Wilmslow.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved some wheelie bins and pallets. Crews used hose reel jets to tackle the blaze that did cause some damage to a building.  An investigation has now been launched to find the cause of the fire.
