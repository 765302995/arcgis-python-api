 Large bonfire in Chester Time of Incident: 19/01/2019 - 12:33 Location: Irons Lane, Chester Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Fire crews attended to a large bonfire well alight on Irons Lane in Chester.  Firefighters used two hose reel jets to put out the fire before dampening down the area and making sure it was safe.
