Fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 27/11/2016 - 20:49 Location: Dee Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters tackled a deliberate fire in the open in Dee Way. The fire involved wheelie bins and mattresses and was put out using a hose reel jet. Police also attended the scene.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 847 of 27 November.
