 Chimney fire in Stanthorne Time of Incident: 09/11/2018 - 20:02 Location: Clive Green Lane, Stanthorne Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Middlewich Shortly after 8pm firefighters from Middlewich attended reports of a chimney fire at a residential address on Clive Green Lane in Stanthorne. On arrival at the scene crews found that the fire was well alight. The team used specialist chimney rods and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately 90 minutes.
