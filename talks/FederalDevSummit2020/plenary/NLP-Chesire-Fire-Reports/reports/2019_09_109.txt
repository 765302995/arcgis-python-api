 Owl stuck in a chimney in Knutsford Time of Incident: 10/09/2019 - 18:05 Location: Leycester Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm SOwl after it was rescuedhortly after 6pm firefighters were called to a report of an owl being stuck in a chimney on Leycester Road, Knutsford.  They used a ladder to get onto the roof of the domestic property and chimney rods to help free the owl.  Once the owl was rescued by firefighters it was handed over to the care of the RSPCA.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
