 Bonfire extinguished by crews in Widnes Time of Incident: 21/03/2018 - 22:11 Location: Baguley Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Crews in Widnes were called to extinguish a bonfire which had been set alight in the back garden of a property on Baguley Avenue. Firefighters used one hose reel to put it out.
