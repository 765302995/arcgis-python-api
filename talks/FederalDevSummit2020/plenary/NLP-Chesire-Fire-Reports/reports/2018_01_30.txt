 Multiple wheelie bin fires in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/01/2018 - 03:44 Location: Charlton Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Wheelie bin fireAt 3.44am firefighters were called to reports of a small fire on Charlton Street, Warrington. On arrival at the scene crews found that two wheelie bins had been set on fire. The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fires.  While leaving the scene the team were alerted to a third wheelie bin fire, this time to the rear of Powell Street, again the team used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  All of the fires are believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 75 of 24 January 2018.
