Dishwasher fire in Chester Time of Incident: 07/06/2016 - 19:17 Location: York Dive, Chester. Attendance: Fire engines from Chester and Frodsham Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on York Dive, Chester.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved a dishwasher. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used hose reel jets to put the fire out. A large fan was also used to clear the smoke.
