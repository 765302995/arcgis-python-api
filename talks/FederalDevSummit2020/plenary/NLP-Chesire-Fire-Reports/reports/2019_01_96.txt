 Stove fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 24/01/2019 - 01:53 Location: Princeway, Frodsham Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Frodsham and one from Runcorn Firefighters attended to reports of a tea towel being left on a stove on Princeway in Frodsham.  Crews used a positive pressure ventilation fan to disperse the smoke.
