 Fire in field in Crewe Time of Incident: 07/03/2018 - 12:48 Location: Vicarage Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe attended At around 1.10pm firefighters were called to a fire in a field on Vicarage Road in Crewe. The crew discovered an unattended green waste fire. They used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames.
