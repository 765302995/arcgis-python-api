Derelict building fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/04/2016 - 21:42 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters put out a small fire in a derelict stadium in Ellesmere Port. A hose reel jet was used to extinguish the flames.  Anyone with any information about this or previous incidents at Ellesmere Port Greyhound Stadium is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 1022 of 13 April.
