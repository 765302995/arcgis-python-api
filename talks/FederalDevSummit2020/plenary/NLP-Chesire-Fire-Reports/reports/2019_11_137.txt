 Fire in playground, Lymm Time of Incident: 05/11/2019 - 19:44 Location: Whitbarrow Road, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm The fire service was called after a fire was started at a children's playground in Whitbarrow Road, Lymm. The report came in as a large fire thought to have been started by youths, but when they arrived they found a small, unattended fire in the playground which they extinguished using a single hose reel.
