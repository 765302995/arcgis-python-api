Cat rescued from tree in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 28/10/2015 - 13:50 Location: Queens Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow An RSPCA officer called us out to rescue a cat from a tree. The cat, Ossie, had been stuck 10 metres up the tree for 48 hours. Firefighters used a 13.5 metre ladder to climb up, coax Ossie over to them and then bring him back to the ground to his grateful owner.
