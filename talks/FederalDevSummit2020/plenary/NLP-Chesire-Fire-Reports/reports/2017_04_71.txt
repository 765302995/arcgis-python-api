Tree fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 18/04/2017 - 18:21 Location: Ledward Street, Winsford. Attendance: Afire engine attended from Winsford Fire crews responded to a report of a fire off Ledward Street, Winsford.  When they arrived crews found a fire involving rubbish piled against a tree. A hose reel was used to deal with the fire.
