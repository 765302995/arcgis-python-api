chimney fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 17/06/2015 - 15:21 Location: The Underway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes Ladders, chimney rods and a hose reel jet were used to put out a fire in a chimney.  Firefighters removed the remaining embers and made sure there were no further hotspots of fire.
