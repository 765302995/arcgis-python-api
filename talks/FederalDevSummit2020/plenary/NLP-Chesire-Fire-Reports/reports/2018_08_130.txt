 Fire in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 06/08/2018 - 19:41 Location: Union Street, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At 19:40 firefighters from Runcorn were called to called to reports of a fire in a fire in a wooded area in Union Street, Runcorn. Crews attended the scene and discovered sofa which was well alight. The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 146790.
