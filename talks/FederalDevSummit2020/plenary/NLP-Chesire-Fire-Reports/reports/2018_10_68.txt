 Rescue of people from River Mersey Time of Incident: 20/10/2018 - 11:47 Location: Mersey View Road, Widnes. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Widnes, Warrington, Runcorn, Penketh and Lymm as well as a water incident unit from Warrington and a rope rescue unit and aerial ladder platform from Lymm. At 11:45am firefighters were called to help rescue three people who were stranded in the River Mersey at Widnes. Crews worked with other agencies, including RNLI, to rescue the people who were left in the care of North West Ambulance Service. Firefighters were on scene for around four hours.
