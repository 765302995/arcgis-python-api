Basement kitchen fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 29/01/2017 - 21:41 Location: Robin Hood Lane, Frodsham Attendance: Two fire engines from Powey Lane and Frodsham Firefighters were called to a house in Frodsham following a small fire in the basement kitchen. Crews used a thermal imaging camera to check the surrounding areas behind panelling for any signs of fire having spread.
