 Dangerous structure, Chester Time of Incident: 11/08/2018 - 17:45 Location: Foregate Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane Firefighters responded to reports of a detached flagpole dangling from the roof of the McDonalds restaurant in the city centre. Crews established a working platform while police colleagues managed traffic and the passing public. They used their height safety gear to get to work on making the flagpole safe, removing it from the roof and lowering it to the ground.
