 Wheelie bin fire in the open in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/05/2018 - 00:57 Location: Deepdale, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 12.55am firefighters were called to a wheelie bin fire in the open in Deepdale, Widnes. On arrival the crew found the wheelie bin to be well alight and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They were in attendance for less than 10 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information about the fire should call police on 101.
