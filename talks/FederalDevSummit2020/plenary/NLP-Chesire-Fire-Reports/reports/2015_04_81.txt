Building and woodland fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/04/2015 - 19:19 Location: Bewsey Farm Close, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Two small fires in a derelict building and in woodland close by were extinguished by firefighters.  The building fire was put out with one hose reel and the woodland fire was around 200m from the building and handheld extinguishers were used to put it out. Crews were at the scene for approximately 15 minutes.
