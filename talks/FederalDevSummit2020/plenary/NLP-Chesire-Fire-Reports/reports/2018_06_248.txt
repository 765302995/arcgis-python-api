 Mattress fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/06/2018 - 21:15 Location: Hale Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 9.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a mattress being on fire up against a fence in Hale Street, Orford, Warrington. On arrival the crew found the mattress to be alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
