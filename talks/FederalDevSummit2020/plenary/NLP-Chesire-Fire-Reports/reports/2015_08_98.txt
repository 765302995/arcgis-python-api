Garden fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 13/08/2015 - 23:54 Location: The Crescent, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford. Firefighters were called to a property in Northwich following reports of an unattended bonfire getting out of control.  Hand held extinguishers were used to put out the fire, which was close to a neighbour's fence and a home safety check was completed with the property owner.
