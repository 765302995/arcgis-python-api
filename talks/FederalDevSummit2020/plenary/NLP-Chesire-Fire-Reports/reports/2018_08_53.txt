 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/08/2018 - 19:14 Location: Melford Court, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 7.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a wheelie bin being on fire in Melford Court, Woolston, Warrington. On arrival the crew found the wheelie bin well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and a rake to extinguish the fire in the open and were in attendance for around 15 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For advice on how to prevent your wheelie bins from being targeted by arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
