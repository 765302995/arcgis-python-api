House fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 13/12/2016 - 20:06 Location: Tidnock Avenue, Congleton. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters responded to a report of a fire at a property on Tidnock Avenue, Congleton.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire in the kitchen but this was now out. Firefighters checked that the fire was completely out and used hand held extinguishers to dampen some areas that were still hot.  The man who lived at the house was also treated for the effects of inhaling smoke.
