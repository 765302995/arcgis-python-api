 Water rescue in Northwich Time of Incident: 07/09/2019 - 03:05 Location: Manchester Road, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters from Northwich were called to Wade Brook, just off Manchester Road, Northwich following reports of a man in the water.   A passerby had managed to throw him a life ring but was unable to pull him from the water. Crews rescued the man using throw lines and hauled him out of the water.  Crews were detained for approximately fifteen minutes.
