Small fire on window ledge in Chester Time of Incident: 14/07/2016 - 09:27 Location: Stubbs Place, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters were called to a small fire in a living room window ledge in Chester. The fire was caused by a concave mirror which was left on a window ledge in the living room, which caught the sun rays and set fire to some paper on the window ledge. Firefighters proceeded to make the scene safe.  Please do not keep mirrors next to a window, because they can concentrate the sun's rays, causing a near-by objects (such as curtains, clothing, paper or furniture) to set fire.
