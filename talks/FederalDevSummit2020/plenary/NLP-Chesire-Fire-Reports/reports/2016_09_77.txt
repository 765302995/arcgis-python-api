Tumble dryer fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/09/2016 - 17:34 Location: Vulcan Close, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath  A tumble dryer fire caused smoke logging in a Vulcan Close property. Firefighters confirmed the fire was out and cleared the smoke. One casualty, a lady, was checked over by the ambulance service.
