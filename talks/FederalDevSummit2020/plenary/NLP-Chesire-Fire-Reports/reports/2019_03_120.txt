 One-vehicle-collision on M6 Time of Incident: 04/03/2019 - 15:40 Location: M6, Northbound, Junction 17- Junction 18 Attendance: Four fire engines attended - two from Crewe, one from Sandbach and one from Middlewich One-vehicle-collision on M6Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision on the M6 Northbound between junction 17-18.  Lanes one and two of the motorway were closed while crews extricated two women from inside the vehicle.  One other woman was able to get out of the vehicle without assistance from fire crews.  Crews then made up their equipment after being at the scene for around 50 minutes.
