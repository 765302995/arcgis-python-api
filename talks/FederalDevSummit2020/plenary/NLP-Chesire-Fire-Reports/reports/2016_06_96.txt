Road traffic collision in Congleton Time of Incident: 13/06/2016 - 17:58 Location: Macclesfield Road, Congleton. Attendance: Four fire engines from Holmes Chapel, Congleton and Macclesfield There was a road traffic collision involving two vehicles on Macclesfield Road in Congleton.  Two casualties were trapped. Firefighters released the casualties from the vehicles. Paramedics attended this incident.
