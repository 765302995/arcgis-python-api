Bonfire set alight in Winsford Time of Incident: 30/10/2017 - 15:32 Location: Glebe Green Drive, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Fire crews were called to a bonfire having been set alight on a field near to Glebe Green Drive in Winsford. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.
