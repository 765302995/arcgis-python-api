Road traffic collision in Wistaston Time of Incident: 05/03/2017 - 12:43 Location: Wistaston Road, Wistaston, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Two cars were involved in a collision on Wistaston Road. Firefighters gave trauma care to a man who was trapped in one of the cars. Crews removed the roof of the vehicle to remove the casualty.
