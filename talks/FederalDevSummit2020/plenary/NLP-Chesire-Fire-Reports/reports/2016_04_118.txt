Oven fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/04/2016 - 19:58 Location: Derek Avenue in Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Derek Avenue in Warrington.  When they arrived crews confirmed that there had been a fire in the oven but this had gone out. A special fan was used to clear the smoke from the property.  One man was treated for the effects of inhaling smoke.  https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety
