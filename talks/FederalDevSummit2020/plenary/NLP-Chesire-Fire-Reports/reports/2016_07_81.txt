Deliberate small fire in Neston Time of Incident: 16/07/2016 - 23:19 Location: Church Lane, Neston Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A fire in a field was believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters had to make their way to the incident on foot where they put out the fire, involving a hay bale, using handheld extinguishers.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
