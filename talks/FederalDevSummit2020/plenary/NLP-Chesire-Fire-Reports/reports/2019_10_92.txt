 Reports of a motorcycle fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/10/2019 - 12:01 Location: Liverpool Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around noon firefighters were called to reports of a vehicle fire on Liverpool Road in Warrington. On arrival at the scene the crew discovered that there were no signs of a fire. However, a motorcycle had overheated. Firefighters removed the battery from the bike and made it safe. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. Firefighters were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
