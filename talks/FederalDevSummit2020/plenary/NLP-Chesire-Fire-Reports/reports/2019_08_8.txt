 Large rubbish fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/08/2019 - 08:46 Location: Alder Lane, Burtonwood Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to land near a pub in Alder Lane, Burtonwood, Warrington, following reports of a fire. On arrival the crew discovered a large amount of rubbish, measuring approximately four metres by four metres, on fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They then raked out the debris while damping the rubbish down to make it safe.
