 Road traffic collision in Chester Time of Incident: 17/04/2019 - 16:10 Location: Guy Lane, Brown Heath Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly after 2pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision on Guy Lane, Brown Heath.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered that the incident involved one car which had collided with a tree.  The team used electronic cutting equipment to release the driver who was trapped in the car; they were then taken to Aintree Hospital via Air Ambulance   A passenger from the car had got out of the vehicle prior to the arrival of firefighters; they were also taken to hospital.  The team were detained at the scene for around one hour.
