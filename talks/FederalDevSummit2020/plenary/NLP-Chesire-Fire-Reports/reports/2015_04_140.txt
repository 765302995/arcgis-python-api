Chimney fire in Calveley Time of Incident: 13/04/2015 - 11:12 Location: School Lane, Calveley Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley and one from Crewe Firefighters extinguished a chimney fire at a school in Calveley.  The crews used a seven metre ladder, height safety equipment, chimney rods and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the blaze. The premises was then ventilated. Chimney safety advice
