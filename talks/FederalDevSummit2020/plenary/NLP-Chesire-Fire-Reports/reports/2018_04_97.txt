 Cars on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 12/04/2018 - 22:49 Location: Warkworth Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port At around 10.50pm firefighters were called to reports of a car fire at Warkworth Court in Ellesmere Port. On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved two vehicles. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. They then damped the area down and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots at close-by properties. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour and 15 minutes. Police were also in attendance.
