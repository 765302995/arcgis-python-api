Kitchen fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 16/10/2016 - 07:06 Location: Allerton Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a kitchen on fire in a mid terraced house. The fire was on the ground floor and crews used one hose reel and a covering jet to extinguish the fire. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property. Paramedics attended to deal with three people due to smoke inhalation. Kitchen fire safety advice
