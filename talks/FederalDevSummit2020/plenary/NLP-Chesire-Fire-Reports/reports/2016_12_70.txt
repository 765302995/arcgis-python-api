Refuse on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 18/12/2016 - 21:05 Location: Coronation Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A small amount of rubbish had been set on fire in Coronation Street. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put it out.  Arson is a crime. If you see anyone deliberately setting fires please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
