 Burning of waste, Warrington Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 12:06 Location: Orford Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Crews were called following reports of thick black smoke coming from near a property on Orford Avenue. They arrived to find a controlled burning of waste being carried out. Due to the smoke levels being created, they extinguished the flames using a single hose reel.
