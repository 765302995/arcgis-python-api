 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/07/2019 - 13:40 Location: Eastford Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a car fire on Eastford Road, Warrington. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus and the crew used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information please contact Cheshire Police.
