Road traffic collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 22/12/2017 - 13:06 Location: St Georges Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich Fire crews were called to a person trapped after a road traffic collision on St Georges Road, Winsford. The collision involved two vehicles with one casualty. Crews used stabilisation equipment and removed the front and rear doors using cutting equipment. The casualty was then removed and handed into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for approximately 55 minutes.
