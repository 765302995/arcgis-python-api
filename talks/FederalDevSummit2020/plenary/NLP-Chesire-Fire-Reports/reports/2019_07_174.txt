 House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/07/2019 - 09:41 Location: Prescott Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington, one from Birchwood and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a house on Prescott Street, Warrington, after a report of a fire. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to fight the fire. Smoke was then cleared from the property using a large fan. Everyone who was in the house was accounted for and no injuries have been reported. An investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
