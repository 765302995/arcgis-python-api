Deliberate car fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/11/2015 - 01:06 Location: Avalon Court, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A car fire, well alight on arrival of the crew was extinguished using one hose reel.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and police attended the scene.  Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
