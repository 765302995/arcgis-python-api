House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/06/2015 - 00:12 Location: Northway, Orford, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a fire at a house in Orford.  The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.   A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke from the house which had become smoke logged.  Nobody was trapped in the incident.  The fire was caused by a cooker being left on and unattended.  Please don't let this happen to you - follow our kitchen safety advice - Kitchen fire safety
