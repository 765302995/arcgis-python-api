 Multi-vehicle collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 19/09/2019 - 17:27 Location: M56 eastbound, between junctions 10 and nine Attendance: Four fire engines attended, one from Warrington, one from Lymm, one from Penketh and one from Birchwood Shortly before 5.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a multi-vehicle collision on the M56 eastbound, between junctions 10 and nine. The motorway was closed as emergency services worked at the scene. On arrival firefighters discovered that the collision involved saloon cars and a heavy goods vehicle. Paramedics were treating casualties and no-one was trapped inside any of the vehicles. There were no hazardous materials involved in the incident. Firefighters made the vehicles involved in the collision safe and were in attendance for around two hours. They left the incident in the hands of the police.
