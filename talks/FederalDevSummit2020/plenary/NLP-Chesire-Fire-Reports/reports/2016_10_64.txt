Fire involving refuse under a railway bridge in Widnes Time of Incident: 20/10/2016 - 17:48 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes There was a small fire under a railway bridge near to Dundalk Road, so firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish the fire.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information about this incident, they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
