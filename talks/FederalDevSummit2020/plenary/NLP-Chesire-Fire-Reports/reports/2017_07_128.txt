Large rubbish fire in Gawsworth Time of Incident: 06/07/2017 - 11:57 Location: Congleton Road, Gawsworth Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield, one from Holmes Chapel and one from Runcorn Fire crews were called to tackle a large rubbish fire on Congleton Road, Gawsworth. Crews, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire. Crews were at the scene for approximately three hours. The Environment Agency were also in attendance at the incident.
