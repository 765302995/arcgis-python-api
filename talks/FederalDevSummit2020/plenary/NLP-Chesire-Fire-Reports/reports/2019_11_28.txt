 Car on fire in Elton Time of Incident: 23/11/2019 - 13:01 Location: A5117 in Elton, near Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 1pm firefighters were called to a report of a car being on fire on the A5117 in Elton, near Chester.  On arrival the crew found a saloon car well alight where the road meets School Lane.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus each used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Once the fire was out they damped the vehicle down to make it safe.  The crew were in attendance for more than 45 minutes.
