 Collision in Dunkirk, Chester Time of Incident: 04/01/2018 - 08:19 Location: A5117, Dunkirk, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane. One fire engine from Ellesmere Port. Firefighters attended a collision on the A5117 following reports of a casualty trapped in a vehicle. Crews extracted the casualty from the vehicle using cutting equipment and handed her to paramedics.
