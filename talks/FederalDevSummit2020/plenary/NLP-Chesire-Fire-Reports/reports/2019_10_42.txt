 Kitchen fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 24/10/2019 - 16:08 Location: Princess Street, Winsford. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Winsford and Northwich Shortly after 4pm firefighters from Winsford and Northwich were called to reports of a house fire on Princess Street, Wharton.  On arrival crews found the fire was contained to an electric cooker.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Crews then isolated the electrics and  removed the cooker from the property.  Fire crews were in attendance at the address for approximately 45 minutes.
