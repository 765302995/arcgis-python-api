Wheelie bin fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 20/09/2016 - 20:23 Location: Bridge Street in Congleton. Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Bridge Street in Congleton.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two wheelie bins.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
