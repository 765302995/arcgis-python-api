 Female trapped following a collision in Chester Time of Incident: 10/01/2019 - 13:03 Location: Hartford Way, Chester Attendance:  Two fire engines attended, one from Chester and one from Winsford Shortly after 1pm firefighters were called to a report of a two-vehicle collision on Hartford Way in Chester. On arrival crews found that a female was trapped inside a vehicle. Firefighters stabilised the vehicle and removed one of it's doors to get the casualty out of it. After freeing the casualty from the vehicle they handed her over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour. Police also attended, to control traffic.
