 Campervan fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 25/07/2019 - 14:51 Location: London Road North, Poynton Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Poynton and one from Stockport At around 2.50pm firefighters were called to reports of a campervan being on fire near to Brookside Garden Centre on London Road North in Poynton. On arrival crews found the campervan well alight in the roadway. Police closed the road in both directions and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus each used a hose reel jet to fight the fire. Once the fire was extinguished they damped the campervan down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour-and-a-half.
