 Skip fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 02/05/2019 - 18:31 Location: Grangeway, Runcorn. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Runcorn and Widnes At around 6.30pm firefighters from Runcorn and Widnes were called to reports of a fire at Grangeway Youth and Community Centre.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a commercial skip which was well alight.  Crews used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They also used a ladder to access the roof of a nearby building to check for any damage.  Firefighters were in attendance for approximately 45 minutes.
