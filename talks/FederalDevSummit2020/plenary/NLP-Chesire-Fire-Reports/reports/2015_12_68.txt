Car on fire on M56 east junctions 15 to 14 Time of Incident: 14/12/2015 - 08:40 Location: M56 east junctions 15 to 14 Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Firefighters extinguished a car on fire on the hard shoulder of the M56. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to tackle the blaze. The hard shoulder and lane one were affected by the incident.  Firefighters left the scene and handed the incident over to the Highways Agency at 9.20am.
