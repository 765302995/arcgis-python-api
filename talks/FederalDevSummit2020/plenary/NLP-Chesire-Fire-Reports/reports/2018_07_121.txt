 Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 15/07/2018 - 23:09 Location: Stour Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews attended to a car well alight on Stour Court in Ellesmere Port.  The fire involved the car bonnet deliberately set on fire. Crews, wearing one breathing apparatus, used a hose reel to ensure the fire was extinguished.
