 Fence and outbuilding fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 28/11/2018 - 10:50 Location: Woodvale Road, Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Lymm and one from Altrincham At around 10.50am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Woodvale Road in Knutsford. On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved a wooden fence which had spread to a small wooden outbuilding. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Once the fire was out they damped the affected areas down and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes. The fire is believed to have been caused by ashes being disposed off in a wheelie bin. People are reminded to never put hot items in a wheelie bin, for example smoking materials, hot embers from coal/wood and hot ash.
