Car well alight on a car park on Grasmere Avenue in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/07/2015 - 10:03 Location: Grasmere avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A vehicle was deliberately set alight on a car park on Grasmere Avenue. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.  This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
