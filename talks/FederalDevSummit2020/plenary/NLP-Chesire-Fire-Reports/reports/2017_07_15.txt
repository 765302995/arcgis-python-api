Sheep rescued from canal in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/07/2017 - 15:03 Location: Barnfield Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn, the animal rescue unit from Bollington and the rope rescue unit from Lymm Sheep rescue Bridgewater CanalFire crews were called to reports of a sheep which was stuck in a canal on Barnfield Avenue, Runcorn. Firefighters launched the rescue raft to get the sheep out of the Bridgewater canal. The RSPCA also attended the incident. Crews were at the scene for approximately one hour.
