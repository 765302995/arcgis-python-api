Chip pan fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/08/2016 - 07:40 Location: The Beehive, Halebank Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a fire involving a chip pan in a kitchen at a commercial property. Electrics were isolated and the fire was extinguished with a hose reel jet. The property was ventilated using a large fan. Chip pan safety advice
