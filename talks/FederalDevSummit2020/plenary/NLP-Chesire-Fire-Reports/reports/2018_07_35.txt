 Bin fires at a playground in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/07/2018 - 00:28 Location: Insall Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 12.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a playground on Insall Road in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered two seats of fire at the playground. One of the fires involved a blue playground bin and the other one involved a blue recycling bin. Firefighters extinguished the fires using an extended hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. The fires are believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fires should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
