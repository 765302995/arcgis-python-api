 Small living room fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 29/04/2019 - 16:07 Location: Paragon Close, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Penketh and one from Widnes Shortly after 4pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a semi-detached house in Paragon Close, Widnes.  The small fire in the living room was out on arrival.  Firefighters used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.  They also administered oxygen to an occupier who was suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation before handing the man over to the care of paramedics.  Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour.
