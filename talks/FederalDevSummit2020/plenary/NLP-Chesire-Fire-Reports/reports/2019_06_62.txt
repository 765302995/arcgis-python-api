 Toaster fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/06/2019 - 13:55 Location: Europa Boulevard, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Northwich and one from Birchwood Fire crews attended to reports of smoke at a property on Europa Boulevard in Warrington involving a toaster.  Although the fire was out on arrival, firefighters used a blanket to put over the toaster and ensure it was fully extinguished.  A large fan was then used to disperse the smoke and make the area safe.
