 Static caravan fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 17/12/2018 - 15:48 Location: Middlewood Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton and one attended from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a fire in a static caravan on Middlewood Road, Poynton. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. Two casualties suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation were taken to hospital by paramedics as a precaution.
