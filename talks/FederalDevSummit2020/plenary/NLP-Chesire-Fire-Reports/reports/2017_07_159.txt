Dishwasher fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 30/06/2017 - 22:25 Location: Cronton Lane Mews, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a house in Widnes after a dishwasher set alight. The incident, which was on Cronton Lane Mews, Widnes, was attended by fire engines from Widnes and Penketh. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet and a large fan to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the house for 25 minutes. For more information about dishwasher and washing machine safety please visit - https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/washing-machine-and-dishwasher-fire-safety
