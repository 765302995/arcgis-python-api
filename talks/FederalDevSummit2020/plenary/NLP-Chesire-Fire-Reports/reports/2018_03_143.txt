 First floor flat fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 03/03/2018 - 10:56 Location: Dickson Close, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes and one fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters from Widnes and Runcorn attended reports of a fire at a residential property in Widnes. On arrival at the scene the crews found that the fire involved a first floor flat. Firefighters used one hose reel jet and four breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. There were no reports of any injuries at the scene and the team were in attendance for approximately one hour 30 minutes. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 293 of 3 March 2018. Information can also be passed anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
