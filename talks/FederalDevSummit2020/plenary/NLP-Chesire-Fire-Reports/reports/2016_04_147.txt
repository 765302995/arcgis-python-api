Kitchen fire in Chester Time of Incident: 03/04/2016 - 02:06 Location: Maple Grove, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Firefighters led three people to safety after a kitchen fire had been reported at a house in Chester. Crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and cleared smoke from the property. Smoke alarms were fitted and activated, alerting the residents to the fire. Three casualties, one woman, a girl and a boy, were treated for smoke inhalation at the scene by ambulance personnel.  Smoke alarms save lives. Make sure your home has fitted and working smoke alarms.
