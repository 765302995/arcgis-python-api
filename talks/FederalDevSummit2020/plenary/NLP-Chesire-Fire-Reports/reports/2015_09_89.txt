Car fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 12/09/2015 - 19:22 Location: M56 Westbound between Junctions 10 and 11. Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington and Runcorn Firefighters responded to a car fire between Junctions 10 and 11 on the M56.  Crews used two hose reels and two breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. The driver of the vehicle suffered burns to his hands and received treatment from ambulance crews.
