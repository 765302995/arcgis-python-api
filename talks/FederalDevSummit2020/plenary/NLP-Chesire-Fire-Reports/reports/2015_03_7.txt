Fire in at an old racing track Time of Incident: 28/03/2015 - 18:40 Location: Thornton road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to deal with a blaze at the old dog track off Thornton Road in Ellesmere Port.  The fire involved several plastic seats. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.   It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
