 Smoke in kitchen in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 09/11/2019 - 16:42 Location: Kinsey Road, Ellesmere Port. CH65 9JR Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly before 4.45pm fire crews responded to reports of smoke in the kitchen of a business premises on Kinsey Road.  They used a thermal imaging camera to check for heat sources, but there was no fire. Advice was passed on to staff at the business.
