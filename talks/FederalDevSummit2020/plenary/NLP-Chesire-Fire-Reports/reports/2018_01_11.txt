 HGV fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 29/01/2018 - 05:36 Location: Newcastle Road, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich and two from Crewe Fire crews were called to a fire involving a HGV on Newcastle Road, Nantwich. An LED exterior light was on fire. Crews removed the faulty light fitting from the vehicle. During the incident one lane of the road was closed. The road was reopened by 06:36.
