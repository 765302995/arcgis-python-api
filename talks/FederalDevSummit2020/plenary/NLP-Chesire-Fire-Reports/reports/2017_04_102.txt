Electric heater fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 07/04/2017 - 15:31 Location: Bond Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A small electric heater was removed from a house in Bond Street after it was involved in a fire. A hose reel jet was then used to put out the fire and a high pressure fan used to clear smoke from the property. A lady from the house was taken to hospital suffering slight effects of smoke inhalation.
