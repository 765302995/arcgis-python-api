 Fire in the open in Congleton Time of Incident: 30/06/2019 - 16:08 Location: Mill Green, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Shortly after 4pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire near to a wooded area in Mill Green, Congleton. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire affecting a bush. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for less than an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
