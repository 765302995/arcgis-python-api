Tarmac truck blaze in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/09/2015 - 20:29 Location: Skyline Drive, Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Skyline Drive, Warrington.  When they arrived they found a tarmac truck on fire. Crews used two hose reel jets to put the fire out.
