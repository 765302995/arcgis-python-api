 Fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/01/2019 - 21:28 Location: Golborne Road, Warrington. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 9.30pm firefighters from Warrington were called to reports of a fire in the open on Golborne Road.  On arrival at the address the team discovered a bonfire which was out of control in the rear garden of a residential property.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and the team were detained at the scene for approximately one hour 15 minutes.
