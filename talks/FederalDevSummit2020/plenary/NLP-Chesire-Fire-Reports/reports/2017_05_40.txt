Small fire in the open in Weaverham Time of Incident: 24/05/2017 - 20:06 Location: Northwich Road, Weaverham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich A handheld extinguisher was used to put out a small fire in woods. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
