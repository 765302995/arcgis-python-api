Horse trapped in a fallen stable Time of Incident: 23/02/2017 - 12:20 Location: Cottons Lane, Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters responded to a report of a stable that had collapsed trapping a horse.  The incident took place on Cottons Lane, Sandbach. Fire crews worked alongside a vet to release the horse and get it to a place of safety.
