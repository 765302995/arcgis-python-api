 Woodland fire in Oakmere Time of Incident: 01/09/2018 - 07:46 Location: Gallowsclough Lane, Oakmere. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters from Northwich used water to extinguish a small deep seated fire in a woodland area near Gallowsclough Lane in Oakmere.
