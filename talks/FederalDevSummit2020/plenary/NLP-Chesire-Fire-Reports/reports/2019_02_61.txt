 Bin fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 19/02/2019 - 19:36 Location: Pickford Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters attended to a small bin on fire outside a bus station on Pickford Street in Macclesfield.
