Road traffic collision on Weston Point Expressway in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/12/2015 - 18:13 Location: Weston Point Expressway in Weston, Runcorn. Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn, Widnes and Frodsham Fire engines from Runcorn, Widnes and Frodsham fire stations were sent to the scene of a road traffic collision on Weston Point Expressway in Weston, Runcorn. Fire crews were on scene for approximately fifty minutes. Firefighters released one casualty from a car who was then taken to hospital by ambulance.
