 Car fire at Sandbach Services Time of Incident: 18/02/2019 - 12:00 Location: M6 Junction 17 to Junction 16, Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach. Car fire at Sandbach ServicesFirefighters were called to a car on fire in the car park of Sandbach Services. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to tackle the fire which was well alight.
