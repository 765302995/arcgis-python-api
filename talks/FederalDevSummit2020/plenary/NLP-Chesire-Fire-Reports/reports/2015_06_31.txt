Road traffic collision in Rushton Time of Incident: 23/06/2015 - 16:31 Location: Oulton Mill Lane, Rushton, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford One vehicle was involved in a road traffic collision, ending up on its roof and trapping a casualty inside.  Firefighters set up a cordon so they could work safely to release the lady.  Police and ambulance were also at the scene.
