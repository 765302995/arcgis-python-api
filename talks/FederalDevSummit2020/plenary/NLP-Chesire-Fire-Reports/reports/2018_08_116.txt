 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/08/2018 - 05:38 Location: Cyril Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a wheelie bin fire on Cyril Street, Warrington. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which involved three wheelie bins. If you have any information about the incident please contact Cheshire Police.
