 Small car fire in Chester Time of Incident: 29/04/2019 - 15:22 Location: Parkgate Road, Ledsham, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane At around 3.20pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on Parkgate Road in Ledsham, Chester.  On arrival the crew discovered that a small electrical fire had been put out with handheld fire extinguishers.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to damp the car down to make it safe.  They also used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.  Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour-and-a-half.
