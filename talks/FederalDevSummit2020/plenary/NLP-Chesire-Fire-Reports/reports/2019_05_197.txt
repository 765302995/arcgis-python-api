 Garage fire in Kerridge Time of Incident: 03/05/2019 - 07:45 Location: Higher Lane, Kerridge. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield and Greater Manchester. Garage fire in KerridgeFirefighters from Macclesfield and Greater Manchester attended reports of an outbuilding fire in Kerridge.  On arrival at the address crews discovered a garage, approximately 5 metres by 5 metres, which was well alight.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  They then used a hose reel jet to dampen down and prevent the fire from reigniting.
