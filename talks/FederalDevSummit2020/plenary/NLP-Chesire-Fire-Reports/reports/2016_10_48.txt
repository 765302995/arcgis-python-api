Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 23/10/2016 - 14:01 Location: Cornbrook Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A fire in the kitchen of a terraced property required firefighters to use two hose reels and one main jet to extinguish the flames. A large fan was then used to ventilate the property before crews left.
