 Tyres on fire in Astbury Time of Incident: 10/05/2018 - 19:18 Location: Watery Lane, Astbury,Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Congleton A large quantity of tyres was on fire in Watery Lane. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze. They were at the scene for around two hours.
