 Kitchen in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 15/04/2019 - 23:54 Location: Kennedy Avenue, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Macclesfield Shortly before 12am firefighters from Macclesfield were dispatched to reports of a kitchen fire at a residential address on Kennedy Avenue.  On arrival at the address the team discovered the fire involved a chip pan.   Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The team then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.  Two casualties were treated at the scene by North West Ambulance Service.  Crews were detained approximately one hour.    For further information about chip pan safety visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chip-pans .
