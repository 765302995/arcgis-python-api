 Bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/10/2018 - 17:48 Location: Roehampton Drive, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a fire in a bin on a children's play area on Roehampton Drive, Runcorn. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please contact Cheshire Police.
