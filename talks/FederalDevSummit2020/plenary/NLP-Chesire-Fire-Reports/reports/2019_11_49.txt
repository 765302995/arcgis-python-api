 One vehicle collision in Tarporley Time of Incident: 19/11/2019 - 20:21 Location: A54 Badcocks Lane, Tarporley. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley. One fire engine attended from Malpas. One fire engine Attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a one vehicle collision on the A54 Badcocks Lane, Tarporley. The car had left the carriageway and entered a field.  Paramedics and the fire service doctor treated one casualty at the scene.  Firefighters also assisted with the casualty and used a thermal imaging camera to check the surround area to ensure there were no further casualties.  Crews were there for approximately 30 minutes.
