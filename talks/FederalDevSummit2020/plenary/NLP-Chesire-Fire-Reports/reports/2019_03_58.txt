 Collision in Tarporley Time of Incident: 18/03/2019 - 11:36 Location: Beech Lane, Tarporley. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters attended a one vehicle collision in Beech Lane, Tarporley. A car had collided with a house. A man was treated for injuries believed to be minor by paramedics and was taken to hospital as a precaution. Crews inspected the damage and made the vehicle safer.
