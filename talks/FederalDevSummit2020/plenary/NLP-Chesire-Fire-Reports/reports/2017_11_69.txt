Casualty trapped after road traffic collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 12/11/2017 - 14:49 Location: Runcorn Road, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich and one from Lymm Road traffic collision in NorthwichFire crews were called to a road traffic collision with one person trapped on Runcorn Road, Northwich. One casualty was extracted by firefighters from a car and taken to hospital via air ambulance. Crews were at the scene for an hour and a half.
