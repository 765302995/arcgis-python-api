 Woman rescued from a car after becoming trapped in Chester Time of Incident: 08/04/2019 - 18:30 Location: Hapsford Interchange, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane and Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to help a woman who had become trapped in her vehicle on the Hapsford Interchange, Chester. The boot of the woman's car, a VW Caddy, had become damaged and would no longer open. Because the woman was a wheelchair user, her exit from the vehicle was via the boot. Crews used hydraulic cutting equipment to open the boot so the woman could get out. She was uninjured.
