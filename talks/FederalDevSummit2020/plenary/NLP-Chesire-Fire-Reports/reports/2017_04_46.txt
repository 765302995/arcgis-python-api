HGV fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 21/04/2017 - 17:52 Location: Cloister Way, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Cloister Way, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found a HGV on fire. Two hose reels, a ground monitor and a thermal imaging camera were used to deal with the incident.  Crews were on scene for 2 hours and 50 minutes. A fire investigation has been launched to find the cause.
