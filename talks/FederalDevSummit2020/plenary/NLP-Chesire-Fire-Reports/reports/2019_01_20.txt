 Garage fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/01/2019 - 17:40 Location: Withington Avenue, Warrington Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters attended to a garage fire on Withington Avenue in Warrington.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, one hose reel was used to put out the fire.  The main door of the garage was opened in order for it to be ventilated. Firefighters then removed the plasterboard to check the fire had not spread to a utility room next door.  A positive pressure ventilation fan was also in use to ensure smoke was cleared.  Crews were in attendance for one hour.
