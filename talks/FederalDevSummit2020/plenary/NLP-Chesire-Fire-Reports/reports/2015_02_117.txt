Car fire on M62 east, junctions 10-11 Time of Incident: 03/02/2015 - 11:17 Location: M62 east, junctions 10-11 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, one from Birchwood and one from Greater Manchester FRS A fire involving a saloon car on the M62 eastbound, between junctions 10 and 11, was tackled by firefighters.  The crew, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.
