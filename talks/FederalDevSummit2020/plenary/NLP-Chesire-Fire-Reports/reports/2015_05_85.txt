Car well alight in Lymm, Warrington Time of Incident: 13/05/2015 - 19:57 Location: Dane Bank Road East, Lymm, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath A saloon car was well alight on a roadway in Lymm, so firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.
