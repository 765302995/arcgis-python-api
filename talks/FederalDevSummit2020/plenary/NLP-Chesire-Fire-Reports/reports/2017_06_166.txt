Building fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 03/06/2017 - 12:32 Location: Greystone Park, Crewe. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Crewe, Nantwich and Alsager Firefighters were called to a report of a building fire of Greystone Park, Crewe.  When they arrived crews found that several fires had been set in an empty building. Hose reel jets were used to fight the fire.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that may help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire police on 101.
