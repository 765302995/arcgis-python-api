 Deliberate rubbish fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 12/07/2018 - 16:54 Location: Railway Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters from Congleton used one hose reel jet to extinguish a sofa and a small quantity of tyres which had been deliberately set on fire on Railway Street. The team were detained at the scene for approximately ten minutes.
