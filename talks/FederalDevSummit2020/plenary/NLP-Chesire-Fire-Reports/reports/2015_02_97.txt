Road traffic collision on M6 Time of Incident: 05/02/2015 - 17:30 Location: M6 south J17 to J16 Attendance: One fire engine and the rope rescue unit from Holmes Chapel and one fire engine from Congleton. The motorway was closed while emergency services cleared a road traffic collision involving three cars. No one was trapped and firefighters assisted in fending off traffic. Police closed the southbound carriageway while the incident was dealt with.
