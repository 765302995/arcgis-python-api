Fatal flat fire in Chester Time of Incident: 24/12/2017 - 20:40 Location: Clover Place, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane At 8:40pm on Sunday 24 December firefighters were called to reports of a fire at Clover Place, Chester. Crews from Chester, Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane attended the scene and found that the fire involved a flat which was well alight. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus entered flat, which was well alight, and a man was found inside the flat. Sadly, despite the best effort of the emergency crew, they were unable to save the man and he was pronounced dead at the scene. Crews then used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The death is no being treated as suspicious and a joint police and fire investigation into the cause of the fire is currently underway and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 711 of 24/12/2017.
