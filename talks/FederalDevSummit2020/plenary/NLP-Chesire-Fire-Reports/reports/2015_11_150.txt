Fire in woods in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/11/2015 - 20:09 Location: Southworth Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood A large fire which had been set on fire in woods required firefighters to attend. The fire was a controlled burning of rubbish which had been started in between trees on farm land.  The crew, who were alerted to the fire by a member of the public, used beaters to extinguish the fire and stack drag tools to remove the rubbish from the fire.
