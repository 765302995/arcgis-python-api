Fire involving a disused building in Middlewich Time of Incident: 05/08/2017 - 13:39 Location: Newton Heath, Middlewich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich and Holmes Chapel There was a fire involving a boarded up building. The fire was located on the first floor. Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus. The fire crews used one hose reel and one covering jet to extinguish the fire. A full search of the building was completed by firefighters, and they found that there was nobody inside the building. This incident has now been handed over to Cheshire Police.
