Combine harvester on fire in field in Gawsworth, Macclesfield Time of Incident: 16/08/2016 - 12:48 Location: Crabtree Moss Farm Marton Lane Gawsworth Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton, one fire engine from Macclesfield and one fire engine from Alsager Firefighters were called to reports of a large vehicle fire. On arrival, crews discovered a combined harvester was on fire in a field so used two hose reel jets and one covering jet to tackle the fire.
