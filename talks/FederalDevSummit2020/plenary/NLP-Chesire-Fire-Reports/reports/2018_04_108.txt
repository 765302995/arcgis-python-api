 Alarm box fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 10/04/2018 - 21:49 Location: Handforth Road, Wilmslow Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Wilmslow and one from Stockport At around 9.50pm firefighters were called at a property on Handforth Road in Wilmslow after reports of flames being seen coming from an alarm box. On arrival the fire was out and the crew isolated the power and used a thermal imaging camera to check the area. They were at the scene for more than half-an-hour.
