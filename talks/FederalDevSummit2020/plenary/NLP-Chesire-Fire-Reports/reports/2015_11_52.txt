Dangerous structure in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 20/11/2015 - 14:28 Location: Percyvale Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine and the aerial appliance from Macclesfield Firefighters were called out to a report of roof tiles falling from a three storey building in the high wind and landing on a car below. The crew carried out a full inspection of the roof's ridge tiles from the hydraulic platform and found them to be unsafe. The incident was handed over to the property owners who are arranging for an emergency roofer to fix the issue.
