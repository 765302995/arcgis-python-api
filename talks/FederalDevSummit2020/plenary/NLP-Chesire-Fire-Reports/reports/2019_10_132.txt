 Teenager stuck in a swing in Northwich Time of Incident: 05/10/2019 - 16:41 Location: Greenbank Park, Alfred Street, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters from Northwich released a teenage girl who was stuck in a swing at Greenbank Park. The crew were in attendance for around 15 minutes.  The teenager was uninjured as a result of the incident.
