 Small woodland fire in Woolston Time of Incident: 17/04/2019 - 18:03 Location: Woolston Park, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters from Birchwood used one hose reel jet to extinguish a small fire in a woodland area at Wooston Park.  The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.
