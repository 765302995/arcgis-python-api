Cow rescued from mud near Northwich Time of Incident: 11/06/2015 - 10:12 Location: Fishpool Road, Delamere, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and the animal rescue unit from Bollington A cow which had been stuck in mud for 24 hours was rescued by firefighters.   A crew from Winsford along with the Service's animal rescue unit based at Bollington were able to get the animal out of the mud with the aid of the owner's tractor.   A vet was asked to attend to check on the cow's welfare.
