 House fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 29/05/2018 - 01:58 Location: Eversley, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes, Stockton Heath, Lymm and Runcorn with two from Penketh House fire in WidnesFirefighters attended to a house on fire on Eversley in Widnes which had spread to a neighbouring property.  Cheshire Police attended to put a cordon in place and residents in the properties were evacuated as a precaution.  Crews used an aerial appliance in the bedroom of the house to see through the roof.  Firefighters wearing four breathing apparatus used a short extension ladder and a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and checked for hot spots.
