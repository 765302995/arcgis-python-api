 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/09/2018 - 16:42 Location: Cinnamon Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a car fire in Cinnamon Lane, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel to put out the fire and then a hose reel jet to cool the vehicle down. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check the temperature. The owner was organising recovery of the vehicle.
