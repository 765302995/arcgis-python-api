 Wheelie bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 12/02/2019 - 22:05 Location: Finsbury Walk, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to a fire involving a wheelie bin which was well alight on land off Finsbury Walk in Winsford. Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the flames.  Prevent wheelie bin fires by following these tips: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
