 Small fire at a house in Crewe Time of Incident: 29/05/2018 - 18:12 Location: Frank Webb Avenue, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich At 6.11pm a resident on Frank Webb Avenue reported that a smoke alarm had been activated at a neighbouring property. The resident forced entry the address and found that the occupants were out. They also discovered a small fire in the living room, which is believed to have been caused by a candle which had been left unattended. Crews from Nantwich attended the scene and used a large fan to clear all of the smoke from the property, the also completed a home safety assessment once the occupant returned home to the property. The team were in attendance for approximately 40 minutes. For information on how to use candles safely visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/candles-in-the-home
