 Fire involving five wheelie bins in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/07/2018 - 04:12 Location: Orford Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 4.15am firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on Orford Avenue in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered five wheelie bins on fire. The fire spread to the gate at the back of a domestic property. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and then damped the area down to make it safe. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour. Police were also in attendance. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. For wheelie bin fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
