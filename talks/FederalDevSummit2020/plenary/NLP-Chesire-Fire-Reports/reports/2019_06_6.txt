 Ducklings rescued in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 28/06/2019 - 13:12 Location: Delamere Drive, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a canal off Delamere Drive, Macclesfield following reports of ducklings trapped in a tunnel. Crews liaised with an RSPCA officer and, using a net, rescued the ducklings.
