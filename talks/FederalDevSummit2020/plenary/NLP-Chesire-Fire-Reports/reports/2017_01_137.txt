Fire in engine of vehicle Time of Incident: 02/01/2017 - 10:53 Location: Congleton Road, North Scholar Green Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton A small fire which broke out in the engine compartment of a vehicle required firefighters to attend. Although the fire was out on arrival, crews used a hose reel jet and a covering jet to investigate and dampen down. The crews proceeded to make the vehicle safe, and the fire is believed to have been due to an electrical fault.
