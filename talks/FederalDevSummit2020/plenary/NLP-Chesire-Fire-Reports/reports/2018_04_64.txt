 Firefighters rescue man in water in Middlewich Time of Incident: 18/04/2018 - 18:39 Location: Kinderton Street, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich as well as the rope rescue unit and support appliance from Lymm Firefighters attended to reports of a man in the canal near to Kinderton Street in Middlewich.  When crews arrived they found the man in the water and used water safety equipment and a short extension ladder to rescue him.  He was then taken into the care of paramedics.
