 HGV fire on M56 Time of Incident: 18/03/2019 - 07:13 Location: M56 eastbound between J12-J11 Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one fire engine attended from Penketh Fire crews attended to a fire involving a HGV on the M56 eastbound between junction 12 to junction 11.  The fire was located in the wheel arch of the trailer and crews used a hose reel to put it out.  Crews then remained at the scene until the wheel arch had cooled down.
