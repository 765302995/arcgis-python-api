 Unattended bonfire in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/11/2019 - 20:16 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish an unattended bonfire, around 5 metres by 5 metres,  on a field off Dundalk Road. The crew were detained at the scene for around 30 minutes.
