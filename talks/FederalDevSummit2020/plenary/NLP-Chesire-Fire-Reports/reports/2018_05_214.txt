 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/05/2018 - 09:32 Location: Banks Crescent, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters from Lymm were called to reports of a kitchen fire on Banks Crescent in Warrington. On arrival at the scene the team found that the fire had involved a grill pan which had been extinguished by the occupants. The crew used an industrial fan to clear the smoke from the property and were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes.
