 Car collides with building in Barrow Time of Incident: 28/01/2019 - 13:28 Location: Barnhouse Lane, Barrow Attendance:  One duty fire officer attended Firefighters attended to a residential home following reports of a car colliding into a building.  Crews did an assessment of the area and set up a cordon until the area was deemed safe.  The incident was then handed over to a structural engineer.
