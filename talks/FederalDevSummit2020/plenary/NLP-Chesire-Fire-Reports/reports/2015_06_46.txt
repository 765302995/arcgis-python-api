Boat on fire in Farndon garden Time of Incident: 21/06/2015 - 13:03 Location: Greenway, Farndon, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and one from Wrexham (NW Fire and Rescue Service) A boat was involved in a fire in the garden of a Farndon property.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze.  The house that the boat was parked near to was checked thoroughly to ensure there was no fire spread.  An investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
