Van collides with house in Knutsford Time of Incident: 13/04/2016 - 06:06 Location: Manchester Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford The driver of a van which collided with a house was treated by ambulance personnel. There was nobody in the property and firefighters isolated gas and electricity supplies before asking for a structural engineer to attend. They then handed the incident over to the police.
