 Bedroom fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/10/2019 - 01:40 Location: Libson Close, Warrington Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Warrington, one from Birchwood and one from Lymm At 1.40am firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property in Libson Close, Warrington. On arrival the crews discovered the fire involved a first floor bedroom and that no-one was unaccounted for. Two firefighters entered the property and the fire was extinguished with a hose reel jet. A large fan was then used to clear smoke from the property. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour. The fire is being treated as suspicious and police have arrested a 16-year-old boy on suspicion of arson with intent to endanger life.
