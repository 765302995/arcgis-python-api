 Kitchen fire, Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 06/07/2019 - 15:35 Location: Clayhill Green, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to tackle a kitchen on fire in Little Sutton. It was in a mid-terraced house and was well alight on arrival. Four firefighters used breathing apparatus and two hose reels to extinguish the flames, and a large fan to ventilate the property. No-one was injured, and the crews were at the scene for around 45 minutes.
