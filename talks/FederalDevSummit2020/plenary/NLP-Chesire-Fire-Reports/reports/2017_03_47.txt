Two car collision in Grappenhall Time of Incident: 25/03/2017 - 10:11 Location: Thelwall New Road/Mottram Close, Grappenhall Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh The scene was made safe by firefighters following a two car collision. There was nobody trapped in either vehicle but an ambulance was called as a precaution as both drivers were suffering shock.
