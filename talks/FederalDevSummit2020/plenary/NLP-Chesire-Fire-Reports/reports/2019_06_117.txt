 Garage fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/06/2019 - 15:29 Location: Tomlinson Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Warrington and Penketh At around 3.30pm firefighters from Warrington and Penketh were deployed to reports of a building fire on Tomlinson Avenue.  One arrival the team discovered that a garage containing a motorbike was well alight.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.   One casualty was suffering from minor smoke inhalation, they were provided with oxygen therapy at the scene.  Crews were detained at the scene for around one hour 45 minutes.
