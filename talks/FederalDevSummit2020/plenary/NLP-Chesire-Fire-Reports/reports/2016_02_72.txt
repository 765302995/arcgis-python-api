Car fire in Penketh Time of Incident: 14/02/2016 - 19:02 Location: Warrington Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington One car and an industrial bin were heavily involved in fire on Warrington Road. A hose reel jet was used to put out the blaze which is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
