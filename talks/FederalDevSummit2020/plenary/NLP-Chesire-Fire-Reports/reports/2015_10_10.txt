Road traffic collision on the M6 between junctions 18 and 19 Time of Incident: 27/10/2015 - 14:20 Location: M6 between junctions 18 and 19, northbound Attendance: Fire engines from Sandbach and Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving an overturned car and a lorry. No one was trapped in this incident but the crew assisted with the care of four casualties. Police and paramedics also attended
