 Smoking car engine fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/05/2019 - 15:03 Location: Widnes Shopping Park, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to a vehicle emitting smoke in a car park at a shopping centre in Widnes. The crew used fire extinguishers while gaining access to the engine compartment. Firefighters then used a thermal imaging camera to monitor the temperature and continued using a powder fire extinguisher to bring the temperature down.
