 Bin fire, Lymm Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 19:03 Location: The Crescent, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm Firefighters were called to deal with a fire in a public waste bin in The Crescent. It had been caused by a discarded dispoasable barbecue, and they used a water backpack to extinguish the flames.
