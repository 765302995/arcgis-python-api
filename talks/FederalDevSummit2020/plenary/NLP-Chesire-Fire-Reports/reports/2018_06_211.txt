 Road traffic collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 08/06/2018 - 07:30 Location: Ollershaw Lane, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich, one fire engine from Winsford and the major rescue unit from Winsford attended Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision, which was outside a garage, with people trapped. On arrival the crews found the incident involved a coach and a van. The firefighters used cutting equipment and spreading tool to remove one man from a vehicle. Paramedics attended to deal with the casualty. Crews were in attendance for around one hour.
