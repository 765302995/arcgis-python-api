 Kitchen fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 05/01/2019 - 10:56 Location: Alder Lane, Orford, Warrington Attendance:  One fire engine from Warrington, one from Birchwood and one from Penketh Three fire engines attended following reports of a fire in a kitchen with someone still inside the building. The fire, which had been caused by burnt food, was out on arrival, however fire crews administered first aid to a casualty suffering the effects of smoke inhalation. They remained in attendance for around half an hour.
