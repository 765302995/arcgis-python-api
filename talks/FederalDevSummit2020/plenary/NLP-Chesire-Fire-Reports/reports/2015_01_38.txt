Washing machine fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/01/2015 - 19:12 Location: Newquay Close, Runcorn. Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn and Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Newquay Close, Runcorn.  When they arrived crews found that the fire had been caused by a washing machine. A hose reel jet was used to put the blaze out. Crews also used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.  Firefighters also carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving.   Washing machine - safety advice
