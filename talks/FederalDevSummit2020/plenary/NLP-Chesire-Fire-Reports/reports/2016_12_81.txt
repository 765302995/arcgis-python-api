Flat fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/12/2016 - 01:30 Location: Golding Drive, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Alsager Firefighters were called to a report of a small fire in a flat in Crewe. The fire was out when they arrived but there was a casualty, a lady, who had suffered slight burns. She was given first aid by firefighters and an ambulance was called.  There were working smoke alarms in the flat which alerted the resident to the fire.
