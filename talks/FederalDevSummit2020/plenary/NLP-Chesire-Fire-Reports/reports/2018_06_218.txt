 Cooking fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/06/2018 - 14:43 Location: Langley Beck, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a house fire on Langley Beck, Widnes. On arrival crews found cooking that had been left unattended on a hob which was causing smoke to build up. Crews used a large fan to clear smoke from the property and removed the pan.
