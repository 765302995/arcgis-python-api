House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 11/07/2015 - 21:07 Location: Sandhurst Avenue, Wistaston. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to a house fire on Sandhurst Avenue, Wistaston.  When they arrived crews found that the fire was in a first floor bedroom. Crews used breathing apparatus, a hose reel jet and a covering jet to deal with the incident.   The home owners were treated by the ambulance service after suffering the effects of smoke inhalation.
