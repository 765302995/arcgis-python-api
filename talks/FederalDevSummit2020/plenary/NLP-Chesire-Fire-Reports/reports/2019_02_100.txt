 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/02/2019 - 21:30 Location: Greymist Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. One fire engine attended from Lymm. Firefighters attended reports of a fire in a kitchen at an address in Greymist Avenue, Warrington. The fire involved unattended cooking. Crews used two breathing apparatus, hose reel jet and covering jet to extinguish the fire.  Firefighters were there approximately 60 minutes.
