House fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/03/2015 - 10:25 Location: Whinfell Grove, Runcorn Attendance: Three fire engines Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus extinguished a fire in the airing cupboard of a house in Runcorn.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check for hotspots and the crews stripped back the damage to confirm the fire was out. The house was naturally ventilated initially and a large fan was also used to clear the smoke.  The cause of the fire is believed to be electrical.
