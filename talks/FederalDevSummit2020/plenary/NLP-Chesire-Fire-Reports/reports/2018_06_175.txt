 Small fire in wooded area in Northwich Time of Incident: 15/06/2018 - 22:27 Location: Station Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley Fire crews attended to a small fire in wooded area in Delamere on Station Road in Northwich.  Firefighters used water to put out the fire and dampened down the area until it was fully extinguished.
