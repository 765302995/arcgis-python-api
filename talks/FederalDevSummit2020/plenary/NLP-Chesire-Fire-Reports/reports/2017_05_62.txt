Man rescued from a boat in Poynton Time of Incident: 20/05/2017 - 20:42 Location: Nelson Pit, Lyme Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow, Knutsford, Macclesfield and one from Offerton (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service), and the boat A man was hanging off a canal boat, so firefighters used a rescue boat to rescue the man. Firefighters then assessed the casualty before he was then handed over to Police.
