Wheelie bins on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 28/04/2016 - 21:29 Location: Dene Drive, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford Firefighters were called to two commercial wheelie bins on fire in a compound near to a commercial building and used a hose reel to extinguish the flames. This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone knows anything about this fire they should call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
