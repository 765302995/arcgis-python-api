False alarm at a Wilmslow restaurant Time of Incident: 14/10/2016 - 18:23 Location: Wilmslow Road, Alderley Edge. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Piccollinos on Wilmslow Road, Alderley Edge.  When they arrived crews checked the property with a thermal imaging camera. They found that the incident was a false alarm caused by a faulty air conditioning unit.  The restaurant was evacuated while the investigation was underway.
