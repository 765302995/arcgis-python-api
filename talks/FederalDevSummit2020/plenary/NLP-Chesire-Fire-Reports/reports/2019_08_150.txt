 Overheated washing machine in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 03/08/2019 - 15:57 Location: Tytherington Drive, Macclesfield. SK10 2JG Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Crews attended a house on Tytherington Lane after reports that a washing machine was overheating.  They isolated the machine and removed it from the house.
