 Man rescued from floodwater in Crewe Time of Incident: 13/06/2019 - 11:09 Location: Clay Lane, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to Clay Lane, Crewe, after a person became stuck in their car after driving into floodwater. The man, who had ignored a road closure sign, had driven into floodwater and become trapped in his car. Firefighters waded into the water to rescue the man.
