Computer equipment on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 05/06/2016 - 16:41 Location: Harthill Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas Firefighters were called to an amount of computer equipment which is believed to have been deliberately set on fire on a road. The crew extinguished the fire and police were in attendance. If anyone knows anything about this incident they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
