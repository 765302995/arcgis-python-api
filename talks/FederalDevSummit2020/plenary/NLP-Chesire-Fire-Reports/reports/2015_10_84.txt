Collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/10/2015 - 18:49 Location: Stanney Lane in Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Stanney Lane in Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two cars. One casualty was helped from the car and passed to the care of the ambulance service.
