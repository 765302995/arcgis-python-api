 Cooker fire, Sandbach Time of Incident: 13/01/2018 - 14:22 Location: Union Street, Sandbach Attendance: One appliance from Crewe and one from Sandbach Fire crews from Crewe and Sandbach attended the incident involving a pan of food that had been left unattended on the cooker.  Two breathing apparatus and one hosereel were used to extinguish the flames. One casualty was suffering with smoke inhalation and they were passed into the care of paramedics.  Firefighters remained at the scene for an hour, during which a home safety check was carried out.
