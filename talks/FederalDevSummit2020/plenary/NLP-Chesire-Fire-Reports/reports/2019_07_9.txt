 Collision in Tattenhall Time of Incident: 27/07/2019 - 21:25 Location: Bolesworth Road, Tattenhall Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Chester, one from Malpas and one from Warrington Firefighters attended to a collision involving two vehicles on Bolesworth Road in Tattenhall.  One woman was safely removed from the vehicle and handed into the care of paramedics.  Crews then made the scene safe.  The road was temporarily closed in both directions.
