 Three vehicles on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/01/2018 - 01:04 Location: Brandon, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to an arson attack on Brandon, Widnes, where three cars were set alight. Firefighters used two hose reel jets, a covering jet and a thermal imaging camera to put out the fires. Crews also used hydraulic cutting equipment to gain access to the vehicles. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 33, of 26/01/18. Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
