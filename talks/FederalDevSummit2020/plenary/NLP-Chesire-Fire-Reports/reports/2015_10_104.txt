Fire in concrete bunker in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/10/2015 - 15:56 Location: Poplars Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington One hose reel was used to extinguish a fire in a concrete bunker in Warrington.  The bunker was around 3mx3m and crews used a short extension ladder to gain access to the area.  Police were requested to attend the scene due to the cause being suspected as deliberate.  Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101 or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
