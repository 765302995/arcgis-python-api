 Collision involving a van and a motorcycle in Stockport Time of Incident: 15/01/2019 - 17:50 Location: Macclesfield Road, Hazel Grove, Stockport Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Poynton and one from Offerton At around 5.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Macclesfield Road in Hazel Grove, Stockport. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved a transit van and a motorcycle. The motorcyclist was trapped under the van. Paramedics assessed the casualty and firefighters put privacy screens in place. They then used specialist cutting equipment to extricate the casualty and handed him over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters also used a hose reel jet at the scene and were in attendance for around two hours.
