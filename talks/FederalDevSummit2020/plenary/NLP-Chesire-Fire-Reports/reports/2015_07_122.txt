Light fitting fire at a Crewe gym Time of Incident: 14/07/2015 - 09:21 Location: Bannatynes Health Club, Flour Mill Way, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe An electrical fault in a light fitting caused a small fire at a Crewe gym. Firefighters isolated the electricity supply and requested an electrician to attend.  The crew removed the affected light fitting and handed the incident over to gym management.
