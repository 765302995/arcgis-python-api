Road traffic collision in Chelford Time of Incident: 17/06/2015 - 23:19 Location: Congleton Road, Chelford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford A van collided with a building in Congleton Road.  Fortunately nobody was trapped in either the van or the building.  Firefighters put a cordon in place and made the scene safe.  The incident was then handed over to the police.
