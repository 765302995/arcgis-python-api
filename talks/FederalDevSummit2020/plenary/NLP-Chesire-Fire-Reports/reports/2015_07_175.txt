Small deliberate fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 04/07/2015 - 16:22 Location: Harden Park, Alderley Edge. Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Greater Manchester Firefighters damped down a small fire started deliberately inside a building in Alderley Edge.  The fire was out on arrival. Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
