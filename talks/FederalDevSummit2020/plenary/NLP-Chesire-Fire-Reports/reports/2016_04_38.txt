Bin fire at some Macclesfield flats Time of Incident: 22/04/2016 - 19:17 Location: Carisbrook Avenue, Macclesfield. Attendance: Fire engines from Macclesfield and Bollington  Firefighters responded to a report of an alarm sounding at some flats off Carisbrook Avenue, Macclesfield.  When they arrived they found that there was a fire in the communal bin area of the property. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They also used special fans to clear smoke from the flats.
