Garage on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 08/03/2016 - 23:28 Location: Whirley Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a fire involving a double garage made of brick and wood. One hose reel and a covering jet was used to extinguish the flames. The road was closed in both directions by police while the incident was dealt with.
