 Vehicle fire in Gawsworth Time of Incident: 23/06/2019 - 11:03 Location: Cowbrook Lane, Gawsworth Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield and one from Congleton Fire crews were called to a fire involving an agricultural vehicle in a field on Cowbrook Lane, Gawsworth. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used an extended hose reel jet to put out fire which was in a tractor loader. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
