 Car on fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 08/05/2018 - 17:34 Location: Forge Fields, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Sandbach and one from Crewe At around 5.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire down the side of an embankment at Forge Fields in Sandbach. On arrival the crews discovered a saloon car well alight. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. A hose reel jet was then used to damp down the area and firefighters then isolated the car's battery to make the vehicle safe. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Fire crews were in attendance for more than an hour. Police were also in attendance. Anyone with information about the fire should call police on 101.
