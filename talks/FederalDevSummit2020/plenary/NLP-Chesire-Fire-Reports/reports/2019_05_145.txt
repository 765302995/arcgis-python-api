 TV on fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 12/05/2019 - 00:26 Location: Amelia Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called following reports of a small fire outside a house in Amelia Street, Orford. It involved a TV that appeared to have been set alight on the path, around a metre from the house and half a metre from the car. A single hose reel was used to extinguish the flames. The occupier was advised to contact police to report the deliberate ignition.
