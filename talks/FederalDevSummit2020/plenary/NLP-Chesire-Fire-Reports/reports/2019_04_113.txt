 Garden fire in Handforth Time of Incident: 18/04/2019 - 15:43 Location: Wilmslow Road, Handforth. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Wilmslow firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a small garden fire involving a tree and a fence panel in a garden in Handforth.  The team also provided oxygen therapy to one casualty, they did not require hospital treatment.
