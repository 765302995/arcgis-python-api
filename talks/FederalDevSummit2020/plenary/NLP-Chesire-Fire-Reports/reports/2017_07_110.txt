Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 08/07/2017 - 15:47 Location: Enfield Road in Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engine from Powey Lane and Chester Two fire engines from Powey Lane and Chester attended Enfield Road in Ellesmere Port to a kitchen fire. On arrival fire crews found a fire involving a pan on a hob.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. They used one hose reel to extinguish the fire.
