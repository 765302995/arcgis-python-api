Boy rescued after fall Time of Incident: 11/07/2015 - 12:53 Location: Victoria Avenue, Crewe. Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to help rescue a boy who had fallen about 6ft into a BMX bowl.  The incident took place off Victoria Avenue, Crewe. Fire crews helped rescue the boy who is believed to have suffered leg injuries and passed him to the care of the ambulance service.
