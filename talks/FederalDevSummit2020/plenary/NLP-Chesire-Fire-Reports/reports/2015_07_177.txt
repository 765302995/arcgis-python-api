Furniture in garden on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 04/07/2015 - 01:48 Location: Wenlock Close, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Crews extinguished a fire involving a small amount of furniture in the back garden of a property in Macclesfield.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with information is urged to call police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
