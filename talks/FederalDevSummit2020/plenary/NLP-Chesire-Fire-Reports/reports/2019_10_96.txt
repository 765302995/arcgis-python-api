 Road traffic collision, Childer Thornton Time of Incident: 15/10/2019 - 17:38 Location: Welsh Road, Childer Thornton Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Fire crews attended the scene of a road traffic collision at the junction of Heath Lane and Welsh Road. It involved three vehicles, though no-one was trapped. They made the scene safe and gave oxygen to one of the men involved in the collision as they waited for paramedics to arrive. Crews were in attendance for around 30 minutes.
