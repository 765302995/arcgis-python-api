Motorbike on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/07/2015 - 02:33 Location: Virginia Gardens, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A motorbike was involved in a fire in Virginia Gardens. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze, which is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
