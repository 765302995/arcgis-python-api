 Back garden fire affecting carriageway in Cuddington Time of Incident: 25/08/2019 - 12:38 Location: Meadow Close, Cuddington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly after 12.30pm firefighters were informed of a fire in the back garden of a domestic property in Meadow Close, Cuddington. It was a case of controlled burning, the smoke from which was affecting a carriageway on the A49. As such firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
