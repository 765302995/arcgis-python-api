 House fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/10/2019 - 13:18 Location: Conway Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Three fire engines attended, two from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane At around 1.15pm firefighters from Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane were called to reports of a house fire in Conway Court, Ellesmere Port.  On arrival crews found that there was a fire in a first floor bedroom which had begun to spread to the ground floor of the property.  Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  The crews also isolated the gas and electricity supplies to the property and used a large fan to clear smoke from the address.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 545425.
