 Car fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 23/09/2018 - 23:14 Location: Dorchester Way, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At around 11.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on Dorchester Way in Macclesfield. On arrival the crew discovered a saloon car well alight on the roadway. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault. They were in attendance for around an hour.
