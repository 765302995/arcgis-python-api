 Fire in the open at a leisure park in Crewe Time of Incident: 17/07/2018 - 21:55 Location: Dunwoody Way, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 9.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at Phoenix Leisure Park on Dunwoody Way, Crewe. On arrival the crew discovered an unauthorised campfire. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
