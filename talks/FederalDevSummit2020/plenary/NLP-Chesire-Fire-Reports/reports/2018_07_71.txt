 Barn fire in Poole, Nantwich Time of Incident: 23/07/2018 - 16:25 Location: Wettonhall Road, Poole Attendance: Two fire engines from Nantwich and one from Crewe Firefighters tackled a fire involving two bay wooden barns on Wettonhall Road in Poole. The barns were approximately 15m squared and contained 30 tonnes of manure. Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, fought the blaze with two hose reel jets and one covering jet. All the livestock were removed.
