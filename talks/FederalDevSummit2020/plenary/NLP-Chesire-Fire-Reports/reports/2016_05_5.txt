Planter fire in fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 29/05/2016 - 18:08 Location: Victoria Street, Crewe. Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Victoria Street, Crewe.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a planter on fire but this had gone out. Crews use a hose reel jet to damp down the area.
