Smoke filled flat in Runcorn Time of Incident: 11/09/2017 - 18:29 Location: Hanover Court, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A room in a flat was left smoke filled after a slow cooker was left on an electric hob. Firefighters gained entry to the property and cleared the smoke after giving oxygen therapy to the occupier.  Please always follow our kitchen safety advice.
