 Man stuck on scaffolding in Nantwich Time of Incident: 01/09/2019 - 02:57 Location: Marsh Lane, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich along with a rope rescue unit from Lymm Shortly before 3am firefighters were called to reports that a man was stuck on scaffolding in Nantwich.  On arrival at the scene the crews found that a man had climbed up scaffolding outside a house on Marsh Lane and fallen asleep.  Two firefighters used a ladder and height safety equipment to reach the man and bring him to safety.  Firefighters were in attendance for around one hour.
