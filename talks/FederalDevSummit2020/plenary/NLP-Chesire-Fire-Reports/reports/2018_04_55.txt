 House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/04/2018 - 08:57 Location: School Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, one from Penketh and one from Birchwood House fire in WarringtonFirefighters were called to a fire with persons reported inside. On arrival crews found a semi-detached house with the ground floor alight. Fortunately, everyone was accounted for and the crews isolated the gas and electricity and tackled the fire using a hose reel jet.  A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke from the affected and neighbouring properties. An investigation into the cause of the fire is being carried out.
