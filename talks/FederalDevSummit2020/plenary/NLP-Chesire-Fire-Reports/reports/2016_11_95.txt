Car fire in Neston Time of Incident: 11/11/2016 - 20:36 Location: North Parade, Parkgate, Neston Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters used a hose reel jet to damp down the engine compartment of a car parked on a driveway on North Parade, Parkgate. There had been a small fire in the compartment.
