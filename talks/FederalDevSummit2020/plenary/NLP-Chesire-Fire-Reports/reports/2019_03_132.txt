 Vehicle fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 02/03/2019 - 01:25 Location: Schubert Close, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Just before 1am firefighters were called to reports of a car on fire at Schubert Close. Crews found a vehicle well alight on arrival and, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They were at the scene for around one hour.  The cause of the fire is under investigation.
