 Sign on fire in Disley Railway Station Time of Incident: 15/08/2018 - 06:20 Location: Buxton Road West, Disley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Fire crews were called to Disley Railway Station, on Buxton Road West, after a sign was set on fire on a platform. Crews used a hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to put out the fire. The fire was started deliberately and the incident has been passed to the British Transport Police.
