 Tree fire in Doddleston Time of Incident: 30/05/2018 - 11:09 Location: Church Croft, Doddleston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Firefighters from Chester used one hose reel jet to extinguish a tree stump which was on fire on Church Croft in Doddleston.  The team were in attendance for approximately 25 minutes.
