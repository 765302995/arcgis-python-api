House fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 17/10/2017 - 19:49 Location: Portman Place, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford Fire crews were called to a fire in a house on Portman Place, Winsford. On arrival crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, found a fire involving a blanket in the property and they used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews then cleared smoke from the property using a large fan.  The fire was believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information please call Cheshire Police on 101 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
