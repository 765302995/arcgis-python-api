Van fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 17/11/2017 - 15:34 Location: Alderley Road, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters have been called to a van fire on Alderley Road, Wilmslow. Crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, are currently tackling the blaze using a hose reel jet to fight the fire. Alderley Road has been shut to traffic whilst this incident is ongoing.  Update at 5pm: The fire is extinguished and the van has been recovered. The road is now open.
