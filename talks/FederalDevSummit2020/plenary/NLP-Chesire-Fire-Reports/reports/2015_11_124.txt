Rubbish fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/11/2015 - 16:23 Location: Dundalk Road, WidneS Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to a fire at the rear of a property on Dundalk Road in Widnes.  Upon arrival crews found a small quanity of rubbish alight and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
