 Horse rescue in Wettenhall Time of Incident: 16/08/2018 - 15:50 Location: Long Lane, Wettenhall Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and the rope rescue team from Lymm Firefighters rescued a heavily pregnant horse which had fallen in a river on Long Lane, Wettenhall. Crews entered the water to assess the horse, along with a vet, and then lifted the horse to the safety of the embankment.  Crews were at the scene for three hours.
