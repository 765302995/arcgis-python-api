 Flat fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 13/03/2018 - 21:31 Location: Green Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield, one from Poynton and one from Bollington Fire crews were called to a flat fire on Green Street, Macclesfield. On arrival crews found the flat was heavily smoke logged and crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a large fan to tackle the fire and clear smoke from the property. The fire had been started by unattended cooking. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
