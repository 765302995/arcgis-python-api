Road traffic collision on M56 west between junctions 14 and 15 Time of Incident: 15/05/2015 - 12:41 Location: M56 west between junctions 14 and 15 Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and two from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving a camper van.  One man was trapped and crews stabilised the vehicle so that they could remove the casualty safely. Lanes two and three were closed by police while the incident was being dealt with.
