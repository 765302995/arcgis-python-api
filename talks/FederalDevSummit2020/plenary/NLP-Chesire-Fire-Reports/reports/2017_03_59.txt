Building fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 22/03/2017 - 16:41 Location: Princess Street, Knutsford. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Northwich and Knutsford Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at The Old Sessions House in Knutsford.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire in a cooking range but this was out. Firefighters checked that there was no further risk of fire before leaving.
