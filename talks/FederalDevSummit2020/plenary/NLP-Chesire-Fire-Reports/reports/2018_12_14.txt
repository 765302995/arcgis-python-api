 Tumble dryer fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 26/12/2018 - 11:13 Location: Grange Close, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At 11:12 on 26th December 2018 two fire engines from Crewe attended reports of a tumble dryer on fire in an outbuilding at a residential address on Grange Close. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish the fire. Crews were on scene for 15 minutes.
