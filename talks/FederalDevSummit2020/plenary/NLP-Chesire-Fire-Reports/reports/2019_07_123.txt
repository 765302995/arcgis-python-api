 Compressor on fire at business in Chester Time of Incident: 10/07/2019 - 19:57 Location: Overleigh Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester. One fire engine attended from Powey Lane. Firefighters attended a dental surgery in Overleigh Road, Chester following reports of heavy black smoke coming from a compressor for a dental suction pipe. On arrival crews discovered the compressor, which was housed in a wooden box, was well alight. Crews used one hose reel to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for 30 minutes.
