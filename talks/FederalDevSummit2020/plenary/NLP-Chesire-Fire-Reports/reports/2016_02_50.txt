Motorbike on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 19/02/2016 - 02:56 Location: Denbigh Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Deliberate ignition is suspected as the cause of a motorbike fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
