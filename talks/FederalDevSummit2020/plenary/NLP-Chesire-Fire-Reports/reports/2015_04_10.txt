Smoke caused by cooking Time of Incident: 28/04/2015 - 19:20 Location: Walter Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Birchwood A large fan was used to clear smoke caused by cooking at a property in Warrington. An ambulance was requested for one of the occupants as a precaution for smoke inhalation. The crews were at the scene for around 15 minutes.
