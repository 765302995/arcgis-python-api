Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 30/08/2016 - 03:36 Location: Blackledge Close, Poulton-with-Fearnhead Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Firefighters tackled a car fire on Blackledge Close in Poulton-with-Fearnhead. The crew, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze. Police were also in attendance.
