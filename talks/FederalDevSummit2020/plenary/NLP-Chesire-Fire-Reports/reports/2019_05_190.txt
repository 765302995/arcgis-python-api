 Playground fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 04/05/2019 - 16:00 Location: Ludlow Close, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At 4pm firefighters from Winsford attended reports of a fire at a children's play area on Ludlow Close.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered that play equipment had been deliberately set on fire.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for around 30 minutes.  Anyone with any information in relation to the fire is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
