Unattended cooking causes smoke in Warrington property Time of Incident: 17/06/2016 - 00:43 Location: Quebec Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A man was treated for smoke inhalation after cooking had been left unattended. Firefighters could find no sign of fire and they treated the casualty.  Never leave cooking unattended, always follow our kitchen safety advice.
