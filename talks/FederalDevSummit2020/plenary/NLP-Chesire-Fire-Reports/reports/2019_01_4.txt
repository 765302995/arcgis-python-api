 Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 11/01/2019 - 20:04 Location: The Underway, Runcorn. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a grass fire off The Underway in Runcorn. The fire involved approximately 10 metres of grass. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately.
