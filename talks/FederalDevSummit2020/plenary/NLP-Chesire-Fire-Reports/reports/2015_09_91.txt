Grass fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 12/09/2015 - 17:07 Location: Gerrard Avenue, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters dealt with a grass fire off Gerrard Avenue, Ellesmere Port.   It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
