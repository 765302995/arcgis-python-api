 Damaged gas pipe fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 25/04/2018 - 13:46 Location: Heron Close, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Wilmslow Damaged gas pipe fire in KnutsfordFirefighters attended to a damaged gas pipe well alight on Heron Close in Knutsford.  Crews made the area safe and used a hose reel and a covering jet to extinguish the fire.
