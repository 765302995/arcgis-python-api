Car fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 27/10/2015 - 11:01 Location: Warren Court, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine from Frodsham Firefighters were called to a report of a car fire in Frodsham. The saloon car was well alight as crew, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to tackle the blaze. They then damped the area down.  The fire is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault in the engine compartment.
