 Small fire in a building in Crewe Time of Incident: 10/02/2018 - 21:50 Location: Heath Street,Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters attended a fire in a premises in Heath Street in Crewe. A number of tea towels had set on fire. Crews used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the fire. They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
