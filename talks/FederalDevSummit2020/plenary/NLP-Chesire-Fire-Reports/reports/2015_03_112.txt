Skip fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 07/03/2015 - 22:12 Location: Distaff Road, Poynton. Attendance: A fire engine from Poynton A hose reel jet was used to put out a skip fire on Distaff Road, Poynton.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
