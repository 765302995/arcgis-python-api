Road traffic collsion on M6 between junctions 17 and 16 southbound Time of Incident: 09/10/2015 - 12:43 Location: M6 junction 17 to 16 southbound near Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach (rapid response vehicle) Firefighters were called to a report of a road traffic collision involving a car and a heavy goods vehicle. Fortunately nobody was trapped in either vehicle. One fire engine was at the scene to fend off traffic from the incident.
