Fire involving a Christmas decoration in Lymm, Warrington Time of Incident: 13/12/2015 - 05:13 Location: Willow Close in Lymm, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and Stockton heath Firefighters from Warrington and Stockton heath fire station were sent to a property on Willow Close in Lymm, where there was a report of a fire involving Christmas decorations.  On arrival at the property the fire crews found that there had been a fire involving a Christmas decoration, bit the fire was already out.  One person suffered slight burns to their hand and suffered slight smoke inhalation.  Christmas fire safety tips
