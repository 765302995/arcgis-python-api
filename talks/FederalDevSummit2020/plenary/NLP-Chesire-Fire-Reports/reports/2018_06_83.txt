 Jetty on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/06/2018 - 15:17 Location: Lapwing Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn. One fire engine from Ellesmere Port. Firefighters were called to a nature reserve in Lapwing Lane, Warrington. A jetty on the Manchester Ship Canal was well alight. Crews used a main jet to put our the fire and then damped the jetty down to prevent ignition.
