 Farm machinery on fire in Cholmondeley Time of Incident: 03/07/2019 - 19:24 Location: Higginsfield Road, Cholmondeley Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Nantwich and one fire engine from Tarporley Firefighters attended to farm machinery well alight near to Higginsfield Road, Cholmondeley  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews used to hose reels to put out the fire.  Firefighters then dampened down the area until the fire was fully extinguished.   The machinery was also monitored using a thermal imaging camera.   Crews were in attendance for 90 minutes.
