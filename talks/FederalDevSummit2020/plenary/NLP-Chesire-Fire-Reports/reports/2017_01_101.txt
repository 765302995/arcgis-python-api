Grill fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/01/2017 - 22:46 Location: St Peters Way, Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Warrington Fire crews responded to a report of a building fire on St Peters Way, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a grill pan on fire but this had gone out. Crews used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.
