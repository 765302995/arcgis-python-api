Two tractors well alight inside a barn in Hankelow Time of Incident: 15/06/2015 - 20:20 Location: Mill Lane, Hankelow Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich, one from Audlem and one from Crewe Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets and one covering jet to extinguish a fire involving two tractors inside a barn (approx. 20 metres by 20 metres) at a farm in Hankelow. The tractors were well alight and the crews used two hose reel jets and one covering jet to extinguish the blaze.
