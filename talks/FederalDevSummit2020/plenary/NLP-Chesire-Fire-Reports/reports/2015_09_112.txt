Road traffic collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 09/09/2015 - 15:13 Location: Crewe Green Road, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Alsager Firefighters released a man from a vehicle following a road traffic collision in Crewe. The casualty was handed to the care of paramedics. Police also attended and the road was closed in both directions while the incident was dealt with.
