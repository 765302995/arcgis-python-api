 Small fire near to canal in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 18/04/2018 - 18:34 Location: Cromwell Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Crews were called to extinguish reports of a small fire near to a canal and woodland on Cromwell Road in Ellesmere Port.  Firefighters used water to put out the fire.
