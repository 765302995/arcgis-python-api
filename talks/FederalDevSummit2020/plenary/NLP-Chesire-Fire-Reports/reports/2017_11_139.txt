Industrial bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/11/2017 - 19:40 Location: Thelwall Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to reports of a fire a commercial premises on Thelwall Lane, Warrington. On arrival they found an industrial bin was well alight. One hose reel jet was used to extinguish the fire and an investigation has been launched into the cause of the fire. If anyone has any information about how this fire may have started please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
