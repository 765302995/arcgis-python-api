Fire at Hartford restaurant Time of Incident: 13/07/2016 - 11:40 Location: Chester Road, Hartford, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford and one from Middlewich Firefighters were called to a fire involving the roof space and ducting at a Hartford restaurant. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to tackle the flames in an extension of the property. Height safety equipment was used so the crews could get on to the roof to remove tiles. Gas and electricity supplies have been isolated while the incident was tackled.  Police attended to manage traffic.
