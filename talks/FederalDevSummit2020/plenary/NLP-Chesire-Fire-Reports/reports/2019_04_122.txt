 Bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 16/04/2019 - 21:26 Location: Amelia Close, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Fire crews were called to a bin fire on Amelia Close, Widnes. Crews used a fire extinguisher to extinguish the fire which had been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police.
