Building fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 05/06/2015 - 13:36 Location: Brunswick Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to ensure a building fire in Macclesfield was fully extinguished.  The fire was out on arrival of the crew who cut away the fire damaged area to check it had not spread.
