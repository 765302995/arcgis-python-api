 Mattress fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 04/07/2019 - 02:38 Location: Hatton Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters attended to a fire on Hatton Street in Macclesfield involving a bed and mattress.  The fire, located at the back of a property, was extinguished using a hose reel.
