Fire in the loft space of a property in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/09/2015 - 17:23 Location: Foxley Heath, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one from Runcorn There was a fire in the loft space of a detached property on Foxley Heath in Widnes. Smoke could be seen coming from the roof and the first floor of the property when the fire crews arrived.  Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  Two hose reel jets and one covering jet was used to tackle the fire.  An inner cordon was established around the property. The road was closed.  Police were requested to attend for traffic control.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check for hotspots of fire.  A fire investigation will be completed to find out the cause of the fire.
