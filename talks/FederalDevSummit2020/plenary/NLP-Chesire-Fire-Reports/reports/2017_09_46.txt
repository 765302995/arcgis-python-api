Fire in light fitting in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/09/2017 - 00:10 Location: Ikea, Europa Boulevard, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a fire in a light fitting in at Ikea on Europa Boulevard, Warrington. Crews used a scissors lift and a dry powder fire extinguisher to put out the fire.
