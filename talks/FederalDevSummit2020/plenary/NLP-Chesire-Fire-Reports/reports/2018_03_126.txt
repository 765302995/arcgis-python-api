 Small fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 07/03/2018 - 17:33 Location: Gleneagles Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended a small fire behind a shop in Gleneagles Road, Great Sutton.  Crews used water to put the fire out. Police attended due to two young people throwing aerosol cans into the fire.
