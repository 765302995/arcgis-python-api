 Fire involving a telegraph pole in Alsager Time of Incident: 01/04/2019 - 20:46 Location: Close Lane, Alsager. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar At around 8.45pm firefighters from Alsager attended reports of a fire in the open on Close Lane.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered the fire involved a telegraph pole and bushes.  Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire and a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  An engineer was called to the site to check the safety of the telegraph pole.  Crews were detained at the scene for around 90 minutes.
