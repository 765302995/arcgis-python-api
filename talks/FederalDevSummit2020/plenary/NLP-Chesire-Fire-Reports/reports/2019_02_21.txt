 Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 25/02/2019 - 13:22 Location: Weston Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly after 1.20pm firefighters were called to reports of fire in the open on Weston Road in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small grass fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
