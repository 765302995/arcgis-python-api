 Canal boat submerged in Lymm Time of Incident: 02/08/2019 - 18:34 Location: Burford Lane, Warrington. WA1 39JN Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm, one from Birchwood and two from Greater Manchester Fire crews were called to reports of a submerged barge near to Burford Lane, Lymm.  On arrival they used an inflatable sled to assess the boat.  There was no-one on board and the incident was handed over to police and the canal trust.
