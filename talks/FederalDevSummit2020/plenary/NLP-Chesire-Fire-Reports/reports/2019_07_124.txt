 Bottle fridge fire at a pub in Crewe Time of Incident: 10/07/2019 - 19:03 Location: Brookhouse Drive, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe. Firefighters were called to a pub in Brookhouse Drive, Crewe following reports of a fire involving a bottle fridge. The fire was out on arrival and firefighters used a large fan to disperse the smoke. They then cut away a panel to inspect for any fire spread and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. The pub had to be evacuated during the incident.
