Road traffic collision on the M6 between junctions 16 and 17 Time of Incident: 07/03/2016 - 08:47 Location: M6 between junctions 16 and 17 north Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Alsager Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision on the M6 involving one heavy good vehicle with one person trapped. The hard shoulder and lane one was closed while the incident is dealt with. Air Ambulance also attended for one casualty.
