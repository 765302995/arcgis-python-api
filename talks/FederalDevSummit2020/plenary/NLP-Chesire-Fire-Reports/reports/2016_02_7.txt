Chimney fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 27/02/2016 - 13:30 Location: Highfield Road, Congleton. Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton Firefighters attended a report of a chimney fire on Highfield Road, Congleton.  The fire involved a log burner. Crews used handheld extinguishers and chimney rods to deal with the incident.
