Potpourri fire in garden lounge in Wistaston Time of Incident: 05/08/2016 - 09:44 Location: Crewe Road, Wistaston Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a garden lounge. On arrival, they found the fire to be out but the lounge remained filled with smoke. Crews ventilated the property and ensured the area was safe. The fire is believed to have been caused by potpourri which was left to burn overnight.  Never leave a candle unattended - doing so could cause a fire.
