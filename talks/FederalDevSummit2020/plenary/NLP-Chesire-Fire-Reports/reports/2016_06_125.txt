Garage and outbuilding on fire in Neston Time of Incident: 08/06/2016 - 14:40 Location: Bendee Road, Neston Attendance: Fire engines from Chester and Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a fire involving a garage and outbuilding. The buildings contained cylinders which were removed. Two hose reels were used to extinguish the fire along with a main water jet.
