 Smoke alarm activated in Congleton Time of Incident: 15/09/2019 - 11:42 Location: Havannah Street, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Congleton and one from Crewe At around 11.40am firefighters were notified of a smoke alarm sounding at a flat in Havannah Street, Congleton. On arrival crews discovered that the alarm had been activated by cooking fumes. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the fumes from the flat and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
