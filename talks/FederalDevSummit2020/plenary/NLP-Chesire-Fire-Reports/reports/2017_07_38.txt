Barn containing hay bales on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 20/07/2017 - 11:59 Location: Lea Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a large fire in a barn which contained hay bales on Lea Lane, Chester. Crews used two main jets and a hose reel jet to tackle the fire which involved approximately 50 tonnes of hay. After extinguishing the fire the crews checked the scene using a thermal imaging camera.  Crews were at the scene for approximately four hours.
