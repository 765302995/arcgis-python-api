Hearth fire in Norley Time of Incident: 28/01/2016 - 15:48 Location: Moss Lane, Norley, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters attended a report of a house fire in Norley. The fire was in the hearth of a fireplace and was causing smoke logging. The crew cleared embers from the hearth and used a high pressure fan to clear the property of smoke.
