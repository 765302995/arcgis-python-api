Small fire in flat in Crewe Time of Incident: 26/05/2016 - 12:42 Location: Middlewich Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe An oven glove left on a hob caused a small fire in a flat. The fire had burnt out but had left the kitchen full of smoke. Firefighters used a high pressure fan to clear it.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice and never leave items on the hob.
