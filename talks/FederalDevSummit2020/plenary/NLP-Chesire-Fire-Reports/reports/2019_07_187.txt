 Skip fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 30/06/2019 - 22:42 Location: Cross Lane, Croft, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Shortly before 10.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Cross Lane, Croft, Warrington. On arrival the crew found a large skip well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which is believed to have been started accidentally via a discarded barbecue. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour. For barbecue safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/outdoor-safety/barbecue-safety.
