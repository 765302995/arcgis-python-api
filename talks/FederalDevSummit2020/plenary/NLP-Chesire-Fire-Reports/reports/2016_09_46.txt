Fire in an empty building Time of Incident: 21/09/2016 - 18:21 Location: Mirion Street in Crewe Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe A hose reel jet was used to put out a fire in an empty building off Mirion Street in Crewe.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
