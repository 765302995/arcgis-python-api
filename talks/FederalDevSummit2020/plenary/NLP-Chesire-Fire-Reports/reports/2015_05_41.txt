Bonfire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 23/05/2015 - 23:27 Location: Blagg Avenue, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Blagg Avenue, Nantwich.  When they arrived crews found that there was an unsupervised fire in the open. Firefighters extinguished the fire using a hose reel jet and carried out a Home Safety Assessment at the property.
