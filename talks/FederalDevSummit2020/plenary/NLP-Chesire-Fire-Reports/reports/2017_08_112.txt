Fuel spilt on motorway services, Tabley Time of Incident: 10/08/2017 - 12:53 Location: Northbound, M6 motorway services, Northwich Road, Tabley Attendance: One fire engine attended Ruptured fuel tank on LGV causes fuel spill on motorway slip road Firefighters were called to an incident involving a large goods vehicle with a ruptured fuel tank. Approximately 250 litres of fluid had leaked onto the road.  Cordons were set up around the vehicle and the slip road closed. The crew used specialist equipment to soak up the liquid and the Environment Agency was informed.  Firefighters left the scene just before 15:40.
