 Small fire in Aston by Budworth Time of Incident: 02/03/2019 - 17:52 Location: Cann Lane, Aston by Budworth. CW9 6LX Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to reports of a small fire at a farm on Cann Lane. Crews attended just after 5.52pm and used a hose reel jet to put out an unattended fire.
