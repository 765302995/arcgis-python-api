 Smoke alarm activated in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/07/2019 - 13:04 Location: Regent Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly after 1pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a domestic property on Regent Road in Widnes.  On arrival the crew discovered that burnt food in a microwave had activated the alarm.  Firefighters cleared smoke from the property and were in attendance for more than 20 minutes.
