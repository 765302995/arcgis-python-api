 Two small grass fires in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/07/2018 - 18:13 Location: Valley Drive, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port used a hose reel jet and beaters to tackle a grass fire in Ellesmere Port. There were believed to be two seats of fire, both of which were approximately 20 metres squared. Crewe were in attendance for approximately one hour.
