Toaster fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 29/09/2015 - 17:18 Location: Percival Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a home on Percival Road, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived they found that there had been a fire involving a toaster but this had gone out. Crews used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.
