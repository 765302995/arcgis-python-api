Skip fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 25/06/2016 - 04:59 Location: Footway from Moorside to Church Walk, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters tackled a fire in a skip in Knutsford.  It is believed the fire was started deliberately and police were asked to attend.  Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
