 Chip pan fire in Chester Time of Incident: 26/07/2019 - 12:39 Location: St Oswalds, Chester. Attendance: Four fire engines attended; one from Chester, two from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane; along with an aerial platform from Chester. At 12.40pm firefighters were dispatched to reports of a kitchen fire in a sixth floor flat on St Oswalds, Chester.  On arrival the crews discovered that had been a chip pan fire which had been extinguished.  Firefighters then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.  Crews were in attendance at the scene for around one hour.
