 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/03/2019 - 13:41 Location: Winwick Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly after 1.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Lightcliffe Skoda's car park on Winwick Road in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire involving a car's engine. Firefighters extinguished the fire using a hose reel jet and made the vehicle safe. They were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
