 Car fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 07/06/2019 - 13:01 Location: M6 northbound, between junctions 16 and 17 Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Alsager and one from Crewe Shortly after 1pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a car on the M6 northbound, between junctions 16 and 17. On arrival crews found the saloon vehicle's engine compartment well alight in lane one of the smart motorway. Lanes one and two were closed as firefighters worked at the scene. They used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters then damped the engine compartment down to make it safe.  They used a thermal imaging camera to monitor its temperature and once the crews were sure that the vehicle was safe they handed the incident over to the police. Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
