 Rubbish fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 27/08/2018 - 19:57 Location: Stanney Mill Lane, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a rubbish fire on Stanney Mill Lane, Ellesmere Port. Crews used a high pressure hose reel jet to put out the fire.
