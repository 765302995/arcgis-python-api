 Heater fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/05/2019 - 11:14 Location: Imperial Mews, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 11.15am firefighters from Ellesmere Port were called to reports of a kitchen fire on Imperial Mews.  On arrival at the address two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, entered the building and found the fire involved a heater.  The team removed the heater from the building and were detained at the scene for around 20 minutes.
