Chimney fire in Peckforton Time of Incident: 15/03/2015 - 11:00 Location: Peckforton Hall Lane, Peckforton Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley A chimney fire was attended by firefighers.  Chimney rods were used to access the fire internally, then firefighters got on to the roof to remove a broken chimney pot.  A hose reel jet and chimney rods were then used to put out the fire from the outside.  To avoid a chimney fire please follow our safety advice - Chimneys
