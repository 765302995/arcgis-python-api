 Tumble dryer fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 28/01/2019 - 17:01 Location: Whetstone Hey, Ellesmere Port. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 5pm firefighters from Ellesmere Port were called to reports of a tumble dryer fire in at Whetstone Hey.  Crews swiftly attended and the fire was already out on arrival at the scene. The team isolated the gas and electrics at the property and conducted investigation work at the scene.  The team were detained for approximately 20 minutes.
