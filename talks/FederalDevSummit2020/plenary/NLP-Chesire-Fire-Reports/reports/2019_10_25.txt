 Bedroom fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/10/2019 - 20:34 Location: Riverside Close, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Penketh Firefighters attended to reports of a fire in the bedroom of a property on Riverside Close in Warrington.  Crews, wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to extinguish the flames.  A large fan was then used to disperse the smoke and firefighters checked for hot spots.  One woman inside the property was treated by paramedics for smoke inhalation.  Fire crews remained on scene for just over two hours.
