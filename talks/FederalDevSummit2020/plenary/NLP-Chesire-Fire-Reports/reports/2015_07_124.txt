Stolen car on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 13/07/2015 - 18:47 Location: Leinster Gardens in Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A stolen car was well alight on the roadway in Leinster Gardens in Runcorn.  The car was completely destroyed by fire.  Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.   This fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
