House fire in Chester Time of Incident: 24/06/2016 - 23:12 Location: Shrewsbury Way, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port The first floor of a house was involved in fire at a property in Chester. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to bring the blaze under control. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke. One casualty was given first aid by a crew member. The cause of the fire is under investigation.  Always make sure you have a working smoke alarm.
