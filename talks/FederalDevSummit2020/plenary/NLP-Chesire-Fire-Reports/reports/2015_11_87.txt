Flat fire in Great Sutton Time of Incident: 12/11/2015 - 08:27 Location: Singleton Road, Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and two plus the aerial appliance from Chester A man was rescued by firefighters from a fire in a third floor flat on Singleton Road. The casualty was rescued by the crews using a 13.5m ladder.  Four fire engines and the aerial appliance attended the scene as firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, tackled the blaze. Electricity to the outside of the property was isolated, along with gas supplies, as the fire was being dealt with.   A fire investigation is now underway. Police and ambulance were also in attendance.
