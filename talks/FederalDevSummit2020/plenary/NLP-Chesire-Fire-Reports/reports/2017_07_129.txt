Fire involving large vehicle in Crewe Time of Incident: 05/07/2017 - 22:42 Location: A500 Barthomley, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Alsager Three fire engines were called to a fire involving a large vehicle on the A500 and used two hose reels to extinguish it. The engines were needed to provide water for the incident and to cool the vehicle.
