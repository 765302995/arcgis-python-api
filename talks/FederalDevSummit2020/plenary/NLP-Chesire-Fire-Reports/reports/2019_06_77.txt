 Broken down vehicle checked on M56 Time of Incident: 13/06/2019 - 18:01 Location: M56 east between junctions 12 and 11 Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn, one from Widnes and one from Winsford A thermal imaging camera was used to check a large broken down vehicle on the M56. There was no signs of fire so firefighters handed the vehicle over to the Highways Agency.
