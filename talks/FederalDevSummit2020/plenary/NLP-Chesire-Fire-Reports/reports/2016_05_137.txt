Grill pan fire in Scholar Green Time of Incident: 08/05/2016 - 00:29 Location: Little Moss Lane, Scholar Green Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and one from Kidsgrove (Staffordshire Fire and Rescue Service) Smoke alarms alerted the occupant of a Scholar Green property to a fire in the kitchen. The fire involved a grill pan and, although it was out, it left the premises smokelogged. Firefighters ventilated the property and carried out a home safety assessment.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice and never leave cooking unattended.
