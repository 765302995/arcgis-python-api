 Fire involving a propane gas cylinder in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/03/2019 - 15:55 Location: Thomasons bridge Lane, Walton, Warrington. Attendance: Three fire engines attended Warrington, Lymm and Penketh. Shortly before 4pm firefighters from Lymm, Penketh and Warrington were called to reports of a fire in the open on Thomasons Bridge Lane, Walton.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered that a cylinder containing approximately 50 litres of propane gas was well alight.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and cool the cylinder. They also monitored the temperature of the cylinder with a thermal imaging camera.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately 25 minutes.
