House fire in Eaton Time of Incident: 15/04/2016 - 17:24 Location: School Lane, Eaton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton, one from Macclesfield and one from Alsager One hose reel jet and one covering jet were used to extinguish a fire inside the living room of a property on School Lane in Eaton.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the property.  One female casualty was treated by paramedics and was then taken to hospital by ambulance.  Police attended this incident.
