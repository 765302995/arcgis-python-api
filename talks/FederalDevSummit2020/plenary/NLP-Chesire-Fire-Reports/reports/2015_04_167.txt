Road traffic collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 10/04/2015 - 13:55 Location: Ditton Road (west), Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes A road traffic collision involving a heavy goods vehicle and a car resulted in a woman being trapped in the car.  The casualty was suffering from neck pain so needed extricating from the vehicle by firefighters.  The car was stabilised as the crew removed the vehicle's roof to be able to get her out and hand her into the care of paramedics.  They made the scene safe as the casualty was taken to hospital by ambulance.
