Chimney fire in Prestbury Time of Incident: 01/12/2017 - 22:26 Location: Withinlee Road, Prestbury Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a chimney fire on Withinlee Road, Prestbury.  Crews extinguished the fire using a water backpack.  Regular inspection and cleaning of chimney flues will help prevent fires within chimneys. Here are some top tips for safer chimneys:  Keep chimneys and flues clean and well maintained  Make sure embers are properly put out before you go to bed  Always use a fire guard to protect against flying sparks from hot embers
