 Van fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/02/2019 - 20:36 Location: Percival Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to Percival Lane, Runcorn, after a report of a van fire. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which was well alight on arrival. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire then please call Cheshire Police on 101.
