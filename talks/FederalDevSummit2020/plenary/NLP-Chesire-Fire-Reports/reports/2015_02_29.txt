Small fire at a property in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 18/02/2015 - 17:29 Location: Durham Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A female casualty was given first aid by firefighters following a small fire at a property on Durham Court in Ellesmere Port. The fire was out when the fire crew arrived.  An ambulance was requested.  Police attended this incident.
