 Decking on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 14/06/2018 - 13:05 Location: Witton Street, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich. One fire engine from Winsford. Firefighters were called to decking on fire at the rear of a pub in Witton Street, Northwich. Crews used a hose reel and gained access to underneath the decking to get better access to the fire and to allow for the use of the thermal imaging camera in the affected area.
