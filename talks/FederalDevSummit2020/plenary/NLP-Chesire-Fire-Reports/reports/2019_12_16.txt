 One vehicle collision in Birchwood Time of Incident: 08/12/2019 - 19:31 Location: Heather Close, Warrington. wa37nx Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Birchwood and Warrington At around 7.30pm firefighters from Birchwood and Warrington were called to reports of a one vehicle  collision on Heather Close.  On arrival the crew found one casualty was medically trapped in the vehicle.   Crews used specialist cutting equipment to release the casualty, he was then passed to the car of paramedics.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
