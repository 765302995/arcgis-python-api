 Man rescued from a cliff face in Frodsham Time of Incident: 01/12/2018 - 14:36 Location: Old Chester Road, Frodsham Attendance: Four fire engines attended - from Powey Lane, Chester, Ellesmere Port and a support pump from Lymm Shortly after 2.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a man needing to be rescued on Helsby Hill on Old Chester Road, Frodsham. On arrival crews found him in need of medical attention on the side of a cliff face. Firefighters put in place working at height safety measures. Paramedics assessed the casualty and firefighters liaised with them to formulate a plan to rescue him. Firefighters took him to the top of the hill and once he was on level land they handed him over to the care of paramedics. Fire crews were in attendance for around two-and-a-half hours
