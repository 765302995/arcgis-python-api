 Garden shed fire in Haslington Time of Incident: 02/04/2019 - 01:34 Location: Crewe Road, Haslington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At 01:33am firefighters from Crewe were mobilised to a fire involving a garden shed in Haslington.  On arrival at the scene the team found the shed was well alight and the fire had spread to a garden fence. The exterior of a nearby residential property had also been damaged.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. They also used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  Crews were in attendance at the address for approximately two and a half hours.
