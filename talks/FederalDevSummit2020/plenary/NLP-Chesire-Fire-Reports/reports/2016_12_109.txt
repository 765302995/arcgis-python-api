Smoke-filled kitchen in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 10/12/2016 - 13:08 Location: Earls Gardens, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a smoke-filled kitchen on Earls Gardens. They found a plastic carton had been left on the hob which had caught fire, causing the smoke. A high pressure fan was used to ventilate the kitchen. Smoke alarms were fitted at the house and alerted the occupier to the incident.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice.
