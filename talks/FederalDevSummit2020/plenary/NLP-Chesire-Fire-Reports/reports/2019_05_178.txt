 Fire in a playground in Crewe Time of Incident: 06/05/2019 - 16:18 Location: Sherborne Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to a fire in a playground area on Sherborne Road, Crewe. Crews found two separate, small fires had been set on a grass verge and crews used a hose reel jet to put out both fires. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please contact Cheshire Police.
