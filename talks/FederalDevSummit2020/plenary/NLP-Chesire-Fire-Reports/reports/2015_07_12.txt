Digger fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/07/2015 - 20:01 Location: Omega Boulevard, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a digger on Omega Boulevard, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found a small fire in the seating area. A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out.
