Car on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/01/2015 - 15:55 Location: Manley Gardens, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A car involved in fire meant that the A57 near Manley Gardens had to be closed as firefighters tackled the blaze.  A hose reel jet, with one crew member in breathing apparatus, was used to put out the fire.  One female casualty and child who had been in the car were tended to by ambulance personnel.
