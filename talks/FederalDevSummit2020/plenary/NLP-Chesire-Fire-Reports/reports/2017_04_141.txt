Dishwasher fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 02/04/2017 - 21:22 Location: Sandgate Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield An electricity fault is believed to have caused a dishwasher fire at a property on Sandgate Road. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check the property and removed the appliance from the house.
