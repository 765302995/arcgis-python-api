Chimney fire in Caldecott, near Chester Time of Incident: 06/02/2017 - 17:42 Location: Flennens Brook Road, Caldecott, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas Firefighters tackled a chimney fire at a house in Flennens Brook Road. The crew removed the fire from the hearth and cooled the grate and chimney area down. Chimney rods were then used to clear embers from inside the chimney. Brickwork was removed to gain access to the interior of the chimney and debris removed.  Smoke alarms were fitted at the house and worked to alert the occupants to the fire.  Always follow our chimney safety advice.
