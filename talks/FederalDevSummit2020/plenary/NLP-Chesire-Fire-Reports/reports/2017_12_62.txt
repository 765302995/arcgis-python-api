Vehicle fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/12/2017 - 23:30 Location: Camelot Way, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters from Runcorn used a hose reel jet to tackle a vehicle fire on Camelot Way, Runcorn. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
