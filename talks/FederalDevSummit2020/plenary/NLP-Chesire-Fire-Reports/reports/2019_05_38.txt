 200 tyres on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/05/2019 - 14:44 Location: Desoto Road, Widnes Attendance: Seven fire engines attended - one from Widnes, one from Penketh, one from Lymm, one from Sandbach, one from Winsford, one from Warrington, one from Runcorn and the aerial appliance Firefighters attended to reports of black smoke seen near to Desoto Road in Widnes.  The fire, involving a large skip full of up to 200 tyres, was extinguished using three main jets and a hose reel jet.  Crews monitored the fire throughout the night by using a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots and dampened down the area using one main jet.
