 Garden fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/05/2019 - 22:55 Location: Rostherne Avenue, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews attended to a large amount of rubbish on fire in the garden of a property on Rostherne Avenue in Ellesmere Port.  One hose reel jet was used to extinguish the fire and a brick outhouse as a precaution.  Firefighters then checked neighbouring gardens using the thermal imaging camera to ensure it hadn't spread further.
