Fire in domestic property in Chester Time of Incident: 17/11/2017 - 02:17 Location: Liverpool Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a domestic property on Liverpool Road, Chester, after a fire in a hall. The fire was out on arrival but one man was treated by fire crews and then taken to hospital by paramedics suffering from smoke inhalation. Crews were at the scene for 40 minutes.
