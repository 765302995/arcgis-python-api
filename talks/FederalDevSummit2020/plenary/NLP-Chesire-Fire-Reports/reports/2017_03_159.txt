Road traffic collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 03/03/2017 - 06:24 Location: Middlewich Road, Bradfield Green Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Three cars were involved in a road traffic collision, leaving a man trapped in one of the vehicles. Firefighters released the casualty, handing him into the care of the ambulance service, and made the scene safe. Middlewich Road was closed while emergency services worked at the scene.
