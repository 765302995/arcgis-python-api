 Deliberate bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/05/2019 - 22:30 Location: Dallam Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Warrington Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish an industrial size bin which was well alight on Dallam Lane.  The team were detained for approximately 25 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and is thought to be linked to two previous bin fires on Winwick Road and Sankey Street.  Anyone with information about any of the fires should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101 quoting IML 392486, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111
