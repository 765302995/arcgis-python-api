 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 24/04/2018 - 04:56 Location: Deepdale, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters from Widnes used a hose reel jet to tackle a wheelie bin fire on Deepdale, Widnes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
