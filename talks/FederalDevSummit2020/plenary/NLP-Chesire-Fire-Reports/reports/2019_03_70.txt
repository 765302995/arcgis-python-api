 Smoke logged kitchen in Handforth Time of Incident: 15/03/2019 - 21:21 Location: Knowle Park, Handforth Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Wilmslow and one from Stockport in Greater Manchester Shortly before 9.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a domestic property in Knowle Park, Handforth. On arrival crews discovered that the mid-terraced property's kitchen was smoke logged as a result of cooking food having been left unattended. Firefighters cleared the smoke from the property and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and make sure that the kitchen was safe. They also assessed the occupier to make sure that she was not suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation. Firefighters were in attendance for less than an hour. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
