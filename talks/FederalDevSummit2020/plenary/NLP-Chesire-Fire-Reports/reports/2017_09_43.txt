Swan rescued in Whatcroft Time of Incident: 18/09/2017 - 17:53 Location: Old Lane, Whatcroft, Davenham Attendance: Animal rescue team and supporting fire engine from Lymm and one fire engine from Northwich The animal rescue team rescued a swan that was tangled in netting 40 metres out on a lake. They used a rescue raft to reach the swan, untangled it and brought it ashore. The RSPCA was asked to attend as the swan had also swallowed a hook and line.
