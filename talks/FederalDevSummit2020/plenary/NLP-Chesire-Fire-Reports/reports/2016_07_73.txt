Fridge freezer fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 18/07/2016 - 10:58 Location: Chiltern Avenue, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle a fire involving a fridge freezer. Smoke was cleared from the property using a high pressure fan and the fridge freezer was taken outside. Two casualties, both female, were assessed by paramedics at the scene.
