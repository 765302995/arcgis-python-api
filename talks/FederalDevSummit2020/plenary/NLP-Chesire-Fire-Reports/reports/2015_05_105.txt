Car on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 10/05/2015 - 23:59 Location: Lilac Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A car was well alight on the driveway of a house in Widnes when firefighters were called.  Police also attended the incident where the crew, one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fire.
