Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/04/2017 - 00:55 Location: Statham Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Warrington. Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Statham Avenue, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that there was a wheelie bin on fire in  a commercial compound. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you can help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
