Chimney fire near Chester Time of Incident: 10/05/2016 - 18:47 Location: Fiddlers Lane, Saughall, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters were called to a chimney fire in Saughall. They used water to put out the fire and checked both inside and outside the property with a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no hot spots.  Please follow our chimney safety advice
