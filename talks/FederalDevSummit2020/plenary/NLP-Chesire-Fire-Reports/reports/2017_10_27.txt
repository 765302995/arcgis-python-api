Wheelie bin fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 25/10/2017 - 00:59 Location: Naylor Court, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters were called to a wheelie bin fire on Naylor Court, Northwich. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The bin was close to a building and caused superficial damage to the building. The fire is believed to have been an arson and anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101 or call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
