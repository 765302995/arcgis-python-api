Tractor on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 12/05/2015 - 10:12 Location: Astle Lane, Chelford Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a tractor on fire in Macclesfield. Two high pressure hoses were used to extinguish the flames. No buildings were damaged by this fire which is believed to have started due to an electrical fault.
