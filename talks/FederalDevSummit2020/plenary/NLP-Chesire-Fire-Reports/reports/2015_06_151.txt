Cooking fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 07/06/2015 - 09:27 Location: Rathbone Park, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire involving cooking.  When they arrived they couldn't find any sign of fire but there was a lot of smoke in the property.  A high pressure fan was used to clear the smoke and ventilate the house and an ambulance was called as a precaution for the lady from the house who was suffering slight smoke inhalation.  She was assessed and monitored by firefighters until the ambulance arrived.
