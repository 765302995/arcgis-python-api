Flat fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/07/2015 - 23:14 Location: Palmyra Square North, Warrington. Attendance: Three fire engines A block of flats was evacuated after a fire broke out in Warrington.  The fire was in a property on the fourth floor of the building and firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels to extinguish the blaze which had been caused by a tumble dryer.  A large fan was used to clear smoke from the property. No one was injured.Tumble dryer fire safety advice
