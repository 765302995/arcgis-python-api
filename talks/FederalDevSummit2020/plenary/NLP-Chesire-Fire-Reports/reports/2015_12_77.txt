Kitchen fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 11/12/2015 - 16:05 Location: Trafford Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow  Firefighters were called to a report of a cooker on fire in Wilmslow. The fire was out on arrival and crews ventilated the property using a large fan. There were no smoke detectors in the property so a health and safety assessment was completed and advice given to the occupants.
