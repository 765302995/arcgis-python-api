 Kitchen fire in Neston Time of Incident: 03/10/2018 - 01:28 Location: West Vale, Neston. Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Powey Lane, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Heswall Shortly before 1.30am firefighters from Ellesmere Port, Powey Lane and Heswall attended reports of a domestic building fire in Neston. Crews attended the scene and found a fire in the kitchen area of a flat. Two occupants had evacuated the flat prior to the arrival of the fire service. Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, while the team used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property. Crews also isolated the electrics to the property. One person was treated at the scene for smoke inhalation, they were then passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service, Firefighters were in attendance at the scene for approximately 90 minutes.
