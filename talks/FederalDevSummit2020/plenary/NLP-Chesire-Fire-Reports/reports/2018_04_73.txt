 Dangerous gable end on property in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 17/04/2018 - 10:38 Location: Kings Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 10.40am firefighters were called to reports of an unsafe gable end on a two-storey property on Kings Road in Wilmslow.  On arrival the crews found the unsafe structure swaying in the wind.  For safety firefighters set up a cordon around the area and an adjoining property was evacuated.  A building inspector from Cheshire East Council attended, assessed the structure and arranged for a scaffolding firm to remove the gable end to a safe height
