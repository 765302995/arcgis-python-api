 Outbuilding fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 20/04/2019 - 02:23 Location: St Michael's Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Penketh and Widnes Fire crews were called to reports of a fire at a nursing home on St Michael's Road. On arrival they found it was in fact a smoking shelter at the rear of the property that had caught alight. They used a single hose reel to put the fire out, while the incident had also caused damage to a van parked nearby. They were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
