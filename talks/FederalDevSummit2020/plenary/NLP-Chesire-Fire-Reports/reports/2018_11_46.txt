 Two-car collision in Oakhanger Time of Incident: 22/11/2018 - 17:07 Location: Butterton Lane, Oakhanger Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Shortly after 5pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving two cars on Butterton Lane in Oakhanger. On arrival crews discovered that a female was trapped in one of the two saloon cars. The police and paramedics were in attendance and the road was closed as emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to remove the roof of the car so they could gain access to the casualty. They then removed the casualty from the vehicle on a longboard and handed her over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
