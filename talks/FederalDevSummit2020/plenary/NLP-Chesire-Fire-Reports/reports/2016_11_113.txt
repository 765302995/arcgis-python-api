Tumble dryer fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 08/11/2016 - 13:40 Location: Chelford Road, Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Knutsford and Macclesfield Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to put out a fire involving a tumble dryer in an outbuilding.
