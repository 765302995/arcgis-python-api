Burning out of control in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/04/2016 - 00:45 Location: In the open next to M6 junctions 21-21A Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called out to what was thought to be a vehicle fire on the M6 but turned out to be uncontrolled burning next to the motorway. A hose reel jet was used to bring the fire under control.
