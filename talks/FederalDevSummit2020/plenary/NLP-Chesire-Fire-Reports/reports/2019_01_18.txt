 Wheelie bin fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 08/01/2019 - 23:07 Location: St Clair street, Crewe. Attendance:  Two fire engines attended from Crewe and one from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a fire at a flat in  St Clair Street, Crewe. A wheelie bin was well alight next to the door to the flat. The fire spread to the door.  One casualty was rescued from the property and given first aid, and then taken to Leighton Hospital.  Firefighters used four breathing apparatus, one hose reel and positive pressure ventilation to fight the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.
