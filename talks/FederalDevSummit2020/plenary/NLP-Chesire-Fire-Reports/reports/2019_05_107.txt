 Large fire in the open Widnes Time of Incident: 16/05/2019 - 16:54 Location: Lighthouse Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Large fire in the open WidnesAt around 5pm firefighters from Widnes attended reports of a fire in the open on Lighthouse Road. On arrival crews discovered a large fire which was spreading along the beach near Hale Head Lighthouse.  The fire was emitting a large plume of smoke across the River Mersey. Firefighters used beaters and hand appliances to extinguish the fire involving large amounts of wood and scrub.  The fire stretched approximately 100 metres down the beach.
