 Car fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 07/06/2019 - 08:14 Location: Epsom Avenue, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 8.15am firefighters were called to reports of fire on Epsom Avenue, Wilmslow.  On arrival the team found a car which had been deliberately set alight on open ground.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 30 minutes.  Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML421752.
