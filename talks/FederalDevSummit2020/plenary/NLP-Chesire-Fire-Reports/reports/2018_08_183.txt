 Fire in the open at a farm in Minshull Vernon Time of Incident: 01/08/2018 - 06:38 Location: Eardswick Lane, Minshull Vernon Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 6.40am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at a farm on Eardswick Lane in Minshull Vernon. On arrival the crew discovered that the small fire involved tyres and other refuse. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
