BBQ fire in Neston Time of Incident: 04/07/2015 - 12:00 Location: Winstanley Road, Neston Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters cooled a gas cylinder attached to a barbecue that had been involved in a fire in Neston.  The fire was out on arrival of the crew who then cooled it and checked for hot spots. Please take care when barbecuing during the warm weather. Barbecue safety advice
