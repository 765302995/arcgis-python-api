 Two vans on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 05/12/2019 - 04:18 Location: Bond Street, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield Crews attended an industrial estate in Bond Street, Macclesfield, following a report of two vans being on fire.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet and a main jet to extinguish the fire, which did not spread to any commercial buildings in the industrial estate.  Crews then damped the vans down to make them safe.  A commercial building near to the vans was reported as having sustained heat damage as a result of the fire.  The building and other industrial premises were inspected with thermal imaging cameras to make sure that they were safe.  The road was closed while crews dealt with the fire.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 581011, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
