 Fire in the open involving a large quantity of rubbish in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/04/2019 - 05:39 Location: Alder Lane, Winwick, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly after 5.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Alder Lane in Winwick, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a large quantity of rubbish. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to fight the fire. They set up a water shuttle to get enough water to the area. The crew dragged away debris to expose the deep seated fire and enable them to extinguish it. Firefighters were in attendance for around two hours.
