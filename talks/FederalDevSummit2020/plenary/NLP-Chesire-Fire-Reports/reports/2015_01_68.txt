Rubbish fire spread to outhouses in Crewe Time of Incident: 13/01/2015 - 23:02 Location: Nantwich Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe A takeaway in Crewe suffered damage from a fire after a controlled burning of rubbish and cardboard, in the yard behind, got out of control. It affected guttering and timbers of two outhouses nearby. Electricity supply was also damaged and needed to attention of the service provider.  Burning rubbish / bonfires and the law
