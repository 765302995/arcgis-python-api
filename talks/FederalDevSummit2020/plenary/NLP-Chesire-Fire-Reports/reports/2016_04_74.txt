Suspected petrol leaking from cans in Congleton Time of Incident: 17/04/2016 - 15:29 Location: Boundary Lane, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and one from Sandbach Firefighters were called to a house in Congleton where it was suspected that petrol and oil were leaking from containers in a shed. Two firefighters wearing gas tight suits got into the shed and found that the leak was water but there were several containers containing oil and petrol that needed making safe.  There was no danger to the surrounding area but a company has been called in to remove the containers.
