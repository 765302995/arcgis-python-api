 Two-vehicle collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 22/06/2019 - 17:57 Location: Westminster Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Shortly before 6pm firefighers were called to reports of a collision on Westminster Road in Macclesfield. On arrival the crew found that the collision involved two small vehicles, one of which had overturned. No-one was trapped in either vehicle but the driver of the overturned vehicle had sustained what are believed to be minor injuries. They were assessed at the scene by paramedics. Police closed the road whilst emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters made the two vehicles safe and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
