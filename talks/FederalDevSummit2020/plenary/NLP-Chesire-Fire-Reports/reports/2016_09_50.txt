Road traffic collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 20/09/2016 - 20:55 Location: Fowey Close, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving a car and a 13-year-old boy from Macclesfield. The crews helped paramedics to release the casualty.  Police issued the following statement:  At 8.49pm on Tuesday, 20 September emergency services were called to an address in Fowey Close, Macclesfield following the report of a road traffic collision.  The incident involved an unattended car and a 13-year-old boy from Macclesfield. No one else was involved in the incident.  Paramedics attended the scene but the boy had sustained injuries which later proved to be fatal.  Investigations are ongoing to establish the exact cause of the accident.
