Vehicle battery fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 26/08/2016 - 13:21 Location: Northwich Road, Tabley Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich Firefighters were called to reports of an electrical car battery fire. On arrived, they found the fire was out but there had been a spillage from the battery and water tank. Crews made the spillage safe before checking the whole of the vehicle with a thermal imaging camera to check for any remaining heat spots of fire.
