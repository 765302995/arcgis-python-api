 Chimney fire in Bollington Time of Incident: 26/02/2018 - 11:47 Location: Bollington Road, Bollington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington Firefighters attended a fire involving a chimney at a pub in Bollington Road, Bollington. The fire was smouldering in the chimney situated in the attic. Crews cut away the surrounding chimney breast to expose the seat of the fire and used a water backpack to extinguish the falling embers. They then cut away masonry from the chimney breast and removed soot and embers.
