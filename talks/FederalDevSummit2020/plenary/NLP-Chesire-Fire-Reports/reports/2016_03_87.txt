Road traffic collision in Sandbach Time of Incident: 14/03/2016 - 13:51 Location: Park Lane/Crewe Road, Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and one from Alsager Firefighters were called to deal with a road traffic collision involving two saloon cars. The road was closed at the junction of Crewe Road and Park Lane in both directions while the incident is dealt with. One woman was removed from one of the vehicles by firefighters. Paramedics and police attended. One man and one woman in the other vehicle were treated by paramedics.
