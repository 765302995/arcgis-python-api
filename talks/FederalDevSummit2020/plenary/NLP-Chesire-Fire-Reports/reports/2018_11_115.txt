 One-vehicle collision in Knutsford Time of Incident: 08/11/2018 - 22:30 Location: Swineyard Lane, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 10.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving one vehicle on Swineyard Lane in Knutsford. On arrival the crew discovered that a casualty was being treated by paramedics. Firefighters assisted paramedics in getting the man into an ambulance. He was then taken to Salford Royal Hospital. Firefighters also made the vehicle safe and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
