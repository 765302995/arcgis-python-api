Bedroom fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/04/2015 - 23:22 Location: Penshaw Court, Runcorn Attendance: Fire engines from Widnes and Runcorn Firefighters were called to a bedroom on fire in Runcorn and used one hose reel to extinguish the flames. The bedroom was on the first floor. One female was taken to hospital suffering from smoke inhalation.  The fire is believed to have been started accidentally.
