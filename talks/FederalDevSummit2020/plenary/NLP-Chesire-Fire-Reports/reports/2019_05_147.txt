 Fire in the open, Styal Time of Incident: 11/05/2019 - 19:13 Location: Styal Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called following reports of a small fire near Styal Prison. They found a fire involving a homeless campsite on the prison boundary and used a hose reel jet, ladder and a water backpack to extinguish the flames. The incident is thought to have been a deliberate ignition, and the matter was handed over to prison staff and the police.
