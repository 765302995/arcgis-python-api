 Rubbish fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 29/03/2019 - 23:40 Location: Willow Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a rubbish fire on Willow Close, Runcorn. The fire was inside a garage and crews, four wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
