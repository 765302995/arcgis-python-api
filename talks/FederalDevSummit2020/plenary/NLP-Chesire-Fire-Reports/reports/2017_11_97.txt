Fire in a diesel generator in Northwich Time of Incident: 07/11/2017 - 22:12 Location: Winnington Avenue, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich Fire crews were called to a fire in a generator on a building site on Walker Road, Northwich. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets, a main jet and a covering jet to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
