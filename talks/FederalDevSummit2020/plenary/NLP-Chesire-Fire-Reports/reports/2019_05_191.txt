 Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/05/2019 - 13:29 Location: Oliver Lane, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port At approximately 1.30pm firefighters from Ellesmere Port were dispatched to reports of a house fire in Great Sutton.  On arrival crews discovered a fire in the kitchen of a two storey property.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.Crews then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.  Firefighters were in attendance at the address for approximately 30 minutes.
