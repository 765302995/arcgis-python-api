 Two vehicle collision in Sandbach Time of Incident: 21/07/2018 - 19:11 Location: Moston Road, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Shortly after 7pm firefighters from Crewe attended a two vehicle collision on Moston Road in Sandbach.  On arrival crews made both vehicles safe using one thermal imaging camera, they also provided first aid to one casualty who was then past to the care of North West Ambulance Service.  The team were in attendance for approximately 90 minutes.
