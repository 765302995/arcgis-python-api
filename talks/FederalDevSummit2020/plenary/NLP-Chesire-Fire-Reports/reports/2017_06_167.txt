Bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 02/06/2017 - 21:40 Location: Roehampton Drive, Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Runcorn A hose reel jet was used to put out a bin fire on Roehampton Drive, Runcorn.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. if you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
