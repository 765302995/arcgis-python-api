Washing machine on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 23/12/2015 - 09:27 Location: Weaver Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Fire and rescue crews were called to a report of a fire involving a washing machine in the garage of an Ellesmere Port property. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  Electricity was isolated and a home safety assessment was carried out once the incident had been tackled.
