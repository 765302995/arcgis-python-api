Car fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 02/01/2017 - 05:14 Location: Stone Barn Lane in Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Runcorn A hose reel jet was used to put out a car fire on Stone Barn Lane in Runcorn.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you can help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
