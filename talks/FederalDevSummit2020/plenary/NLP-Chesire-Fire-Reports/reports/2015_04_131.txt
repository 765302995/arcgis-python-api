Chimney fire in Macclesfield. Time of Incident: 13/04/2015 - 20:56 Location: Clough House Lane, Wildboarclough. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters used small hand tools to dig out a fire in the grate of a chimney in Wildboarclough. A thermal imaging camera was used to inspect all sides of the chimney breast and loft space to ensure the fire had not spread. Chimney safety advice
