 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/06/2019 - 00:35 Location: Houghton Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to a wheelie bin fire on Houghton Street, Widnes. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze. The fire was started deliberately. Anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire Police.
