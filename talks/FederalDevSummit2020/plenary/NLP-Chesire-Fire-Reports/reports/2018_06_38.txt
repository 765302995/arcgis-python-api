 Two car collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 21:19 Location: Poplars Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Birchwood At around 9.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving two cars on Poplars Avenue in Warrington. On arrival crews discovered that one of the vehicles had overturned but no-one was trapped inside. Firefighters made the scene safe and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
