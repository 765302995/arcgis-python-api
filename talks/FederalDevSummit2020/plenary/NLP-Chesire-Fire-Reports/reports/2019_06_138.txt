 Rubbish fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/06/2019 - 21:04 Location: Gorsey Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a rubbish fire on Gorsey Lane, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information then please contact Cheshire Police.
