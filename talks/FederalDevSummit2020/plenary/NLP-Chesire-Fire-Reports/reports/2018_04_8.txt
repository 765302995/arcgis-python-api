 Fire in a car wash in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/04/2018 - 20:36 Location: Church Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Fire crews were called to Church Street, Warrington, after reports of a fire at a car wash. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which was in an industrial jet. Crews then checked the car wash with a thermal imaging camera.
