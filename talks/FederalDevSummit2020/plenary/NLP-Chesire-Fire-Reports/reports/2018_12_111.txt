 Car stranded in waterway in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/12/2018 - 14:25 Location: Park Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one attended from Birchwood. The water incident unit and a supporting fire engine attended from Warrington and the rope rescue unit attended from Lymm. Firefighters were called to reports of a car being stranded in a ford waterway in Park Lane, Higher Walton, Warrington, following heavy rainfall. Crews rescued the motorist and the vehicle was then recovered by a local tractor.
