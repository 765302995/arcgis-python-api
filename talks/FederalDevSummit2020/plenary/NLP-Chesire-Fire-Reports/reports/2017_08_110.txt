Shed fire in Church Lawton Time of Incident: 11/08/2017 - 11:51 Location: Woodgate Avenue, Church Lawton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and one from Sandbach A fire in a shed was tackled by firefighters on Woodgate Avenue. The fire had spread to a second shed. Firefighters, two in breathing apparatus, used a main jet and a hose reel jet to bring the blaze under control.
