 Tree fire in Crewe park Time of Incident: 28/05/2018 - 17:16 Location: Pebble Brook Park, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Crews attended to reports of a fire in a tree near to the entrance of Pebble Brook Park in Crewe.  On arrival, firefighters found the fire to involve a tree trunk and used water to put it out.
