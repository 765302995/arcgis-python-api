 Smoke from burnt food in Nantwich Time of Incident: 10/12/2018 - 22:17 Location: St Lawrence Court, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters attended to reports of a smoke alarm sounding due to burnt food at a property at St Lawrence Court in Nantwich.  A fan was used to clear the smoke inside the property until it was deemed safe.
