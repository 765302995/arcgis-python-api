 Highways maintenance van fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 25/06/2019 - 11:03 Location: Cheshire Avenue, Lostock Gralam, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Northwich and one from Lymm Shortly after 11am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a transit van that was resurfacing Cheshire Avenue in Lostock Gralam, Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that a small bitumen drum at the back of the highways maintenance vehicle had overheated. Firefighters removed the gas cylinders in the van and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and cool down the drum, They were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
