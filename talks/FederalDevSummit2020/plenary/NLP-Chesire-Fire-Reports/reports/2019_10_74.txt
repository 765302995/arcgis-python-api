 Chip shop fire in Chester Time of Incident: 19/10/2019 - 09:10 Location: Weston Grove, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester. One fire engine attended from Powey Lane. Firefighters were called to a chip shop in Weston Grove, Chester following reports of a fire.  On arrival crews discovered fat in the chip pan fat disposal unit was on fire. They used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. They then used a dry powder extinguisher to ensure it didn't reignite.  A large fan was used to disperse the smoke. Crews were in attendance for around an hours.
