Overheated electrics in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 01/12/2016 - 23:15 Location: Hawthorn Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Heat and smouldering from an electrical cupboard in a house in Ellesmere Port meant all occupants needed to be evacuated. Firefighters at the scene then isolated the electricity before making the property safe.
