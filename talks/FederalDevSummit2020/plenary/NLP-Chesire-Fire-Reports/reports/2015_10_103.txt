Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/10/2015 - 16:38 Location: Rossmore Road West, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine A kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port was caused by items being left on top of a cooker.  The small fire was out on arrival of the crew and had started after the cooker had accidentally been switched on. The firefighters carried out a home safety assessment before leaving.
