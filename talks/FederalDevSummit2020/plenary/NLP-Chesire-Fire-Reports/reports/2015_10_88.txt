Child's hand freed from escalator Time of Incident: 16/10/2015 - 14:48 Location: Wharf Street, Fairfield and Howley, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington were called to an incident involving a child who had trapped their hand in an escalator.  The fire crew used small tools to free the child's hand and were on scene for fifteen minutes. The child was then passed into the care of paramedics.
