Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 20/03/2015 - 20:17 Location: Grosvenor Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A car fire in Crewe is believed to have been started deliberately. One hose reel jet was used to extinguish the blaze and police were requested to attend the scene.  Anyone with any information about the incident is urged to contact police on 101, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
