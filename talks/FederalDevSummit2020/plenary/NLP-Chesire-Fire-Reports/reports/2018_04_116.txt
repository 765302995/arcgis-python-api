 Refuse fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/04/2018 - 21:24 Location: Westbrook Way, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At 9.22pm firefighters were called to fire in a wooded area on Westrook Way in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved loose refuse. Firefighters used water to extinguish the flames and were at the scene for around 10 minutes. It is believed that the fire was started deliberately. Police have been notified of the incident.
