 Fire in a field in Worleston Time of Incident: 21/06/2019 - 21:25 Location: Main Road, Worleston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich At 9.25pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a field near to Rookery Hall Hotel and Spa on Main Road, Worleston. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning that had got out of control and was posing a threat to nearby trees. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
