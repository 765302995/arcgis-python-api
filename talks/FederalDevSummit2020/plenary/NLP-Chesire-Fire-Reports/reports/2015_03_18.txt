Building fire in Davenham Time of Incident: 26/03/2015 - 10:43 Location: Firthfields, Davenham, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a smell of burning at Davenham Methodist Church Hall.  The crews isolated the electrical supply and used a thermal imaging camera to check for areas of excessive heat.  A faulty light fitting was found to have caused the burning smell.
