 Fire in Handforth Railway Station's ticket office Time of Incident: 22/06/2019 - 08:43 Location: Station Road, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 8.45am firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Handforth Railway Station. On arrival at Station Road in Handforth the crew discovered that it was a fire involving a printer in the ticket office. The fire, which is believed to have been started accidentally, was out on arrival. Firefighters used a large fan to remove smoke from the ticket office and were in attendance for less than 20 minutes.
