Car well alight in sandstone barn in Willington Time of Incident: 16/05/2017 - 10:55 Location: Willington Lane, Willington, Tarporley Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich, Winsford, Powey Lane, Congleton and Ellesmere Port. Fire crews were called to a car on fire inside a sandstone barn in Willington. Five fire engines and a high volume pump attended the incident with crews wearing breathing apparatus using a hose reel jet to tackle the blaze. The electricity board were asked to attend to disconnect supplies.  They extinguished the fire around 12:15 and monitored the area with thermal imaging cameras before leaving the scene.
