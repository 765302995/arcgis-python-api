 Chimney fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 08/01/2018 - 14:21 Location: Milton Rough, Acton Bridge, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich. One fire engine from Lymm. Firefighters were  called to an address in Acton Bridge following reports of a chimney fire. Crews used chimney rods to tackle the fire from the ground floor. They used the hydraulic platform and a thermal imaging camera to ensure the fire hadn't spread. Police closed the road while firefighters worked at the scene.
