 Bin fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 15/11/2019 - 08:29 Location: Main Street, Frodsham. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Frodsham At around 8.30am firefighters from Frodsham were called to reports of a small fire on Main Street.  Crews attended the scene and found small bin fire.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for around 15 minutes.
