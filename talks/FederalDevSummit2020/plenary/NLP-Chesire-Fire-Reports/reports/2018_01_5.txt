 One person trapped following collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 30/01/2018 - 10:53 Location: Rivacre Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Shortly before 11am firefighters were called to reports of a one vehicle collision on Rivacre Road in Ellesmere Port. On arrival at the scene crews found that a hatchback car had overturned.  A woman was medically trapped in the vehicle so crews stabilised the car and used electronic cutting equipment to release her and she was taken to hospital.
