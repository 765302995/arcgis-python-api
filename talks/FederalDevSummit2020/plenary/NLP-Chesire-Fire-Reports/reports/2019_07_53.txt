 Grass small fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 21/07/2019 - 13:04 Location: Middlewich Road, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a grass fire on land off Middlewich Road in Nantwich. Crews used a  hose reel and beaters to extinguish the grass fire which involved three seats of fire. They then damped down the area to ensure the fire didn't reignite.
