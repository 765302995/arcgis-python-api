 Rescue of person in Handforth Time of Incident: 16/06/2019 - 00:09 Location: Dean Road, Handforth. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after midnight firefighters were dispatched to reports of a man trapped on a fence on Dean Road, Handforth.  On arrival the crew found a 20-year-old man whose leg was impaled on an iron fence railing.  Firefighters used electronic cutting equipment to remove the railing and free the casualty.  The man was then passed to the care of paramedics.  The crew were in attendance at the incident for approximately one hour.
