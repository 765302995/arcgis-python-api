Road traffic collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 23/07/2016 - 00:33 Location: Junction of Elm Drive and Remer Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Alsager One car collided with a house leaving the driver trapped in the vehicle. Firefighters released the casualty who was handed into the care of the ambulance service and taken to hospital. A structural engineer was asked to attend to assess the damage to the house.
