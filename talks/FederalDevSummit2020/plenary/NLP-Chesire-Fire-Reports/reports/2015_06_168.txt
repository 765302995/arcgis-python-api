Hedge fire which spread to garage and shed in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 03/06/2015 - 21:09 Location: Gleneagles Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A fire involving a conifer hedge in Ellesmere Port spread to a nearby garage and shed. The fire, which is thought to have been started deliberately, needed firefighters to use three hose reels to get the flames under control. The area was checked with a thermal imaging camera before the crews left. If anyone has any information as to who started this fire they should call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
