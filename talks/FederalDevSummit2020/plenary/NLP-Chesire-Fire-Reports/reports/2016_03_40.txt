Power cable fallen on car in Tattenhall Time of Incident: 23/03/2016 - 09:45 Location: A41 Chester Road, Tattenhall, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester A power line from a telegraph pole fell onto a car causing injuries to a casualty. Firefighters gave first aid to the casualty as they were waiting for the ambulance to arrive, made the scene safe and requested attendance of the electricity board.
