Fire involving peat in Northwich Time of Incident: 05/07/2017 - 10:53 Location: Delamere Forest, Ashton Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich Firefighters were called a small fire involving 10 square metres of peat which was approximately 250 metres into Delamere Forest. Two fire engines were required for water shuttle and crews used a hose reel to damp the fire down.
