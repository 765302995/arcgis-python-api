Vehicle fire and collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 14/12/2017 - 21:18 Location: M56 Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm and two fire engines from Warrington. Firefighters attended reports of a collision on the M56 westbound carriageway near to junction 11. On arrival crews found that the collision involved five saloon cars, one of which was well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire before making all of the vehicles safe.
