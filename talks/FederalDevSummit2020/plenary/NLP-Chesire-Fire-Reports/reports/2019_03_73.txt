 Motorbike fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 14/03/2019 - 22:06 Location: Liverpool Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to a motorbike fire on Liverpool Road, Widnes. The bike was well alight on arrival of the crews and they used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire then please contact Cheshire Police.
