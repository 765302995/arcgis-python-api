 Van fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 30/03/2019 - 00:51 Location: Lime Avenue, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters attended a van fire on Lime Avenue, Northwich. On arrival the van was well alight. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information please contact Cheshire Police.
