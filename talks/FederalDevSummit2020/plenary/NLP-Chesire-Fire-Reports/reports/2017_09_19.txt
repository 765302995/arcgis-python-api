Fire in an industrial unit in Knutsford Time of Incident: 25/09/2017 - 14:24 Location: Haig Road, Parkgate Industrial Estate, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Lymm Fire crews have been at the scene of a fire at Albis Ltd on Haig Road, Parkgate Industrial Estate, Knutsford. The fire involved an extractor fan on the ground floor of the manufacturing plant. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, tackled the blaze using a hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera. The sprinkler system also activated. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke. An investigation into the cause of the fire is being carried out.  All the people on site were evacuated as the fire was being fought, and allowed back just before 3.30pm.
