Kitchen fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 22/11/2017 - 08:20 Location: Ella Grove, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford, one from Lymm and one from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a kitchen fire in a house on Ella Grove, Knutsford. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, put out the fire and used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. One casualty was taken to hospital by paramedics for a precautionary check. Crews were at the scene for two and a half hours.
