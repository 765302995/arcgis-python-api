 Fire involving rubbish in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/07/2019 - 20:47 Location: Halton Lodge Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 8.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Halton Lodge Avenue, Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a quantity of rubbish. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 15 minutes. Like the other two fires in Runcorn on Sunday night, the fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 449619, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
