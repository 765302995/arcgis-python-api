 Skips fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/11/2018 - 20:15 Location: Clay Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 8.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving skips on Clay Lane in Burtonwood, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered two large concrete containers containing rubbish well alight. They implemented a temporary road closure due to smoke and access issues. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and then damp the affected materials down to make sure they were safe. The crew were in attendance for around an hour.
