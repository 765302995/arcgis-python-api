Sofa fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 24/05/2015 - 19:52 Location: Victoria Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: A fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were caled to a report of a fire on Victoria Road, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived they found a disgarded sofa on fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out. They also carried out a Home Safety Assessment at the property next to the incident.
