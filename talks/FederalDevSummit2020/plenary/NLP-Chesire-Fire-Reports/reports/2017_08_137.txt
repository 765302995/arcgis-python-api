Small fire in woodland in Great Sankey, Warrington Time of Incident: 05/08/2017 - 19:07 Location: Whittle Avenue, Great Sankey, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh There was a small fire in woodland involving branches and refuse, so firefighters used one hose reel jet to deal with the fire.  It is believed that the fire may have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
