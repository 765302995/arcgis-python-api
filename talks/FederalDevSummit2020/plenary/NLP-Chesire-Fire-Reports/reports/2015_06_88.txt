Derelict hut on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 15/06/2015 - 19:39 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port fire station attended a fire involving a derelict hut on Thornton Road in Ellesmere Port. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel to extinguish the fire.
