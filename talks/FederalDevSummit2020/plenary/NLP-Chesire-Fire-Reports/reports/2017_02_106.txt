Lorry fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/02/2017 - 14:09 Location: Moorfield Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Penketh The engine compartment of a lorry containing diesel was on fire on Moorfield Road. Firefighters disconnected the battery and used a hose reel jet to cool the engine and outer compartment. A dry powder extinguisher was used to put the fire out.
