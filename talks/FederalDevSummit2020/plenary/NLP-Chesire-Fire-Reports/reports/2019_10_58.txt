 Fire at skate park, Lymm Time of Incident: 21/10/2019 - 19:29 Location: Ridgeway-Grundy Memorial Park, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm Firefighters dealt with a small fire at the skate park at the Ridgeway-Grundy Memorial Park. It involved plastic garden furniture, and took them around 10 minutes to extinguish with a water backpack. Colleagues at Cheshire Constabulary were notified of the incident as it related to anti-social behaviour at the park.
