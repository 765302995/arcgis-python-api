 Unattended bonfire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 01/01/2019 - 21:41 Location: Sutton Lane, Middlewich Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Middlewich At around 9.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Sutton Lane in Middlewich. On arrival the crew discovered that it was an unattended bonfire. Firefighters put out the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
