Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 23/02/2017 - 23:00 Location: Iona Crescent, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh A car on a driveway was on fire in what is believed to have been a deliberate incident. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put the fire out.  Anyone with any information should call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
