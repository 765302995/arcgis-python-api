Compost heap smouldering in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 13/10/2017 - 07:59 Location: Priory Park, Priory Lane,Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters attended a smouldering compost heap at the back of a car park at the rugby club. They used a hose reel to damp down and raked the compost.
