 Two vehicle collision in Cranage Time of Incident: 22/02/2018 - 15:37 Location: Middlewich Road, Cranage Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters from Runcorn attended a collision involving a car and a van at the junction of the of the A50, Knutsford Road and Middlewich Road in Cranage. The crew made the vehicles safe while five casualties were extracted from the vehicle by paramedics. All of the casualties were passed to North West Ambulance Service.
