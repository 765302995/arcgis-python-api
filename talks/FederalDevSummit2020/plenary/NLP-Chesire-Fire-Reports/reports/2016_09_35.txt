Collision in Nantwich Time of Incident: 24/09/2016 - 13:43 Location: Dingle Lane, Bridgemere in Nantwich Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe Firefighters responded to a report of a collision on Dingle Lane, Bridgemere in Nantwich.  When they arrived crews found that there was a car on its roof. Nobody was trapped and so crews made the scene safe before leaving.
