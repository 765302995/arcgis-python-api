Wheelie bin fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 03/07/2016 - 00:37 Location: Colshaw Drive, Wilmslow. Attendance: A fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Colshaw Drive, Wilmslow.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved a wheelie bin. A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out.
