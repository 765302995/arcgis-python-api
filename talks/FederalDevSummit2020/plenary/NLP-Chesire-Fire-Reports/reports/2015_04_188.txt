Hay fire in Chester Time of Incident: 07/04/2015 - 14:01 Location: Little Stanney Lane, Stoak, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a barn on fire at a farm in Stoak. As they were en route to the incident the farmer called to say the fire was out.  However, the crews wanted to ensure there was no danger of reignition so checked the barn using a thermal imaging camera.
