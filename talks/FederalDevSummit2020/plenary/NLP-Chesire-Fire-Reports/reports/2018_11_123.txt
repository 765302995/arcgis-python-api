 Vehicle fire in Warrington. Time of Incident: 07/11/2018 - 18:23 Location: milner street, warrington. wa51ad Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. Firefighters from Warrington extinguished two cars which had been deliberately set alight on Milner Street. The team were detained at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.  Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
