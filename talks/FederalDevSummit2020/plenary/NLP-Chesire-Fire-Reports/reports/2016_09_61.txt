Wheelie bin fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 19/09/2016 - 20:56 Location: Market Street in Congleton. Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Market Street in Congleton.  When they arrived the crew found that there was a wheelie bin on fire. A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
