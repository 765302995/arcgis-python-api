Small fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/02/2015 - 02:38 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington One hose reel was used to extinguish a small fire in Warrington. The fire was in a brazier and was not out of control but with the consent of the owner the crew ensured it was fully extinguished by using the hose reel.
