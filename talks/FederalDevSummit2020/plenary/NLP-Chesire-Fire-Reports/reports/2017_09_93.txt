Unattended rubbish fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 04/09/2017 - 16:20 Location: Factory Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh Fire crews were called to a fire in an allotment on Factory Lane, Widnes. The fire was burning rubbish but had been left unattended so crews used a fire extinguisher to put out the fire.
