Hay fire in Chester Time of Incident: 11/07/2015 - 14:42 Location: Burwardsley Road, Chester. Attendance: Four fire engines from Ellesmere Port, Chester, Tarpoley and Malpas Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Burwardsley Road, Chester.  When they arrived crews were confronted with a blaze involving about 800 tons of hay. Firefighters used four main jets to bring the fire under control. Firefighting continued throughout the night and into the morning. At about 8am this morning a final hotspot was dealt with. The cause of this blaze is believed to be spontaneous combustion.
