Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/04/2017 - 15:29 Location: Henshall Avenue, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Penketh Crews are tackling a kitchen fire at a house in Latchford which resulted in a casualty suffering from smoke inhalation. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, initially tackled the well-established fire in the kitchen using a hose reel jet.  The fire broke through to the first floor of the semi-detached house and breathing apparatus wearers were increased to four, with more hose reels being deployed. Firefighters isolated gas and electricity supplies.  Crews used a thermal imaging camera to check there were no further hot spots. The casualty, a man, was taken to hospital by ambulance.
