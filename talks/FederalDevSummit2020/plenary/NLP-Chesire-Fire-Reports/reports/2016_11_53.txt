Casualty rescued by firefighters from Crewe flat fire Time of Incident: 20/11/2016 - 01:45 Location: West Street, Crewe Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe, Nantwich and Alsager Firefighters rescued a man from a flat on West Street in Crewe after a fire was discovered in the kitchen. The crews, four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, took the casualty from the flat and handed him into the care of paramedics. A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out. The fire is believed to have involved an electric cooker. Smoke alarms were fitted in the flat and alerted the man to the fire.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice to help prevent a fire in the home.
