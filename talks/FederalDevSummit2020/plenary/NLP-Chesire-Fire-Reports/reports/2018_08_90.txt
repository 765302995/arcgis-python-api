 Wheelie bin fire, Runcorn Time of Incident: 11/08/2018 - 18:21 Location: Handforth Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters tackled a blaze involving a wheelie bin and a mattress in the open at the bottom of Handforth Lane. They used a single hose reel to extinguish the fire, which is thought to have been a deliberate ignition.
