 Two-vehicle-collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 24/05/2019 - 02:04 Location: Water Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington Fire crews attended to a road traffic collision involving a car and a minibus on Water Street in Macclesfield.  Crews cleared oil which had leaked from one of the vehicles, used specialist tools to isolate the battery and scanned the area with a thermal imaging camera.  No one was trapped inside the vehicles.
