Fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 16/10/2015 - 17:14 Location: Riverside in Northwich. Attendance: A fiee engine from Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Riverside in Northwich.  When they arrived they found that the fire involved a sofa and a tree stump. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out a fire.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
