 Fire in Delamere Forest in Northwich Time of Incident: 07/08/2018 - 08:36 Location: Station Road, Northwich Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Tarporley At around 8.35am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Delamere Forest on Station Road, Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered a fire involving approximately eight square metres of undergrowth and deep seated peat near the railway line on the Grufalow Walk and Delamere Forest's Visitor Centre. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. They were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
