Cooker fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/08/2017 - 13:50 Location: Hargrave Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a cooker on fire in a property in Crewe. On arrival they found a plastic container which had been left on a hob.  The crew wore breathing apparatus and used a hose reel to extinguish the flames. A large fan was also used to removed smoke from the property.  Cooking safety
