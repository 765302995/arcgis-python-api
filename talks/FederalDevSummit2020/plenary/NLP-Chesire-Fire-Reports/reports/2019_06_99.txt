 Fire in a garden in Runcorn Time of Incident: 09/06/2019 - 16:26 Location: Rosemary Avenue, Beechwood, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly before 4.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the back garden of a domestic property in Rosemary Avenue, Beechwood, Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning that had got out of control. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control and were in attendance for less than 20 minutes.
