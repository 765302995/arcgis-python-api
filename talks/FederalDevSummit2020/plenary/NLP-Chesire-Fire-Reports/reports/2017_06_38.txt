Fire in industrial building in Handforth Time of Incident: 23/06/2017 - 10:58 Location: Lower Meadow Road, Handforth. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Fire crews were called to a fire in an industrial building on Lower Meadow Road, Handforth. The fire, which was at Didsbury Engineering Company, was in a light fitting approximately 50ft in the air. Crews used a high pressure hose reel to put out the fire. The building was evacuated during the incident and all persons accounted for.
