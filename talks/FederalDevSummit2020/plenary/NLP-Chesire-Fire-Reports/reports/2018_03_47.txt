 Trapped bird in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 24/03/2018 - 13:14 Location: Gawsworth Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton Firefighters assisted the RSPCA following reports of a bird perched on an aerial being trapped by wire. Crews used a 7 metre ladder and a roof ladder to rescue the bird from the aerial which was on a property in Gawsworth Road, Macclesfield
