Fire in disused youth club in Crewe Time of Incident: 15/07/2017 - 19:48 Location: Greystone Park, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe and one from Sandbach Firefighters were called to a fire in a disused youth club building on Greystone Park, Crewe. Fire crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish multiple seats of fire in the building.  The incident is a suspected arson attack. If you have any information about the incident please call Cheshire Police on 101 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.  Arson prevention
