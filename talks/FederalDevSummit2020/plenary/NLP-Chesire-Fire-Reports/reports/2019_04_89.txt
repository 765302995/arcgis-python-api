 Fire in woodland, Warrington Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 19:37 Location: Longshaw Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh Firefighters were called following reports of trees being on fire around the back of St Mark's Church. They arrived to find a number of trees and shrubs, which appeared to have been delibereately set alight. They used a single hose reel to extinguish the fire, and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
