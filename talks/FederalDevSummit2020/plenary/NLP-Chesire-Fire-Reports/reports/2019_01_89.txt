 Horse's foot stuck in a metal gate at a farm in Lower Withington Time of Incident: 25/01/2019 - 12:05 Location: Salters Lane, Lower Withington Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Macclesfield and one from Bollington - as did the animal rescue unit from Bollington Shortly after 12 noon firefighters were called to a report of a horse's foot being stuck in a metal gate at a farm on Salters Lane in Lower Withington. On arrival an animal rescue team carried out an assessment. The horse was sedated and monitored using a heart detector. Specialist cutting equipment was used to free the horse's foot from the gate. The horse was then handed over to the care of a vet and its owner. Firefighters were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
