Road traffic collision with one car on fire on the M62 Time of Incident: 08/02/2015 - 15:55 Location: M62 west between junctions 8-7 Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A car was well alight on the M62 after it had been involved in a road traffic collision. Firefighters from Merseyside dealt with the incident. Cheshire fire crews assisted the Highways Agency in clearing the carriageway.
