Garden fire out of control in Congleton Time of Incident: 14/05/2016 - 15:50 Location: Manor Park Road, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines from Congleton and one from Macclesfield A garden fire got out of control and spread to a 12 metre high tree and chicken coop. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire on the ground and turned over debris. They used a ladder and height safety equipment and a hose reel jet to tackle the tree fire.  Moorland Treescapes worked with us to make the tree safe.
