Fire in second floor flat in Chester Time of Incident: 02/08/2015 - 17:10 Location: Foregate Street, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Smoke was seen coming through the window of a second floor flat and firefighters were called. The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, gained entry and found a fire caused by cooking left unattended. The fire was put out using a hose reel jet.  Please, never leave cooking unattended.  Follow our safety advice - Kitchen fire safety
