 Pallets on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 17/01/2019 - 19:54 Location: Market Centre, Crewe Attendance:  Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to a fire at the back of commercial buildings in Crewe. On arrival crews found pallets well alight and in close proximity to the buildings. Two hose reels were used to extinguish the flames and firefighters checked the nearby buildings for spread of fire. Police were called to this incident as it is believed that the fire may have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information they should call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 297269, or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
