 Cooking left unattended in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/06/2019 - 04:24 Location: Norland Street, Widnes. WA8 3TU Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh A smoke alarm sounded after cooking had been left unattended at a house on Norland Street. Firefighters attended and used a positive pressure ventilation fan to clear smoke. You can find more information on kitchen safety here.
