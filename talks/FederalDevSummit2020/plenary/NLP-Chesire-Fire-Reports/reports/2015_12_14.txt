Road traffic collision on the M6 just before Thelwall Viaduct (northbound) Time of Incident: 27/12/2015 - 17:29 Location: M6 motorway (Northbound between J21 and J21A) just before the Thelwall Viaduct Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington There was a road traffic collision involving two cars in lane four of the M6 motorway (Northbound between J21 and J21A) just before the Thelwall Viaduct.  Nobody was trapped inside a vehicle.  One vehicle was smoking and there was petrol on the motorway.  The motorway was totally closed in both directions while fire crews made the scene safe.
