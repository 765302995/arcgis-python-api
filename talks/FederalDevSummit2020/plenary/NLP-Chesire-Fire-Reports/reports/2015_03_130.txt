Chimney fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 05/03/2015 - 18:16 Location: Holmes Chapel Road, Knutsford Attendance: A fire engine from Northwich Firefighters were called to deal with a house fire on Holmes Chapel Road in Knutsford.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved a chimney stack.  Hose reel water jets and chimney rods were used to tackle the fire.
