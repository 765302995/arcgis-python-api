 Caravan fire extinguished by firefighters in Crewe Time of Incident: 12/03/2018 - 20:52 Location: Eardswick Lane, Minshull in Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Two fire engines from Crewe attended a caravan which was reported on fire on Eardswick Lane in Mishull.
