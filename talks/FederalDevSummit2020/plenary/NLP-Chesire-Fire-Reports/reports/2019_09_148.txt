 One person trapped following a collision near Chester Time of Incident: 03/09/2019 - 13:02 Location: Church Road, Saughall. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Powey Lane and Chester Shortly after 1pm firefighters from Chester and Powey Lane were dispatched to reports of a collision on Church Road in Saughall.  On arrival the crews found there was one person trapped following a one vehicle collision.  Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to release the casualty, who was ten passed to the care of paramedics.  Fire crews were in attendance for approximately thirty minutes.
