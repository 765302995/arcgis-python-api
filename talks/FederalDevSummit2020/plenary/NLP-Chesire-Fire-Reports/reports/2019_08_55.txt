 Car fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 22/08/2019 - 21:56 Location: Kingsley Walk, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford attended Shortly before 10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a vehicle in Kingsley Walk, Winsford. On arrival the crew found that the fire involved a hatchback car. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 50 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 493228, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
