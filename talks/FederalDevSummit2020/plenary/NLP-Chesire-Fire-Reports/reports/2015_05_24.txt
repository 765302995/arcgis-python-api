Skip fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/05/2015 - 15:31 Location: Garth Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A skip at the household waste recycling centre was involved in a fire.  Two hose reel jets were used to put out the fire and flood the skip as it was possible that there was a gas bottle at the bottom of it.
