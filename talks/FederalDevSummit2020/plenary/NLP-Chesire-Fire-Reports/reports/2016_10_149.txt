Road traffic collision on Chester Road in Knutsford Time of Incident: 06/10/2016 - 11:12 Location: Chester Road, Mere Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford A road traffic collision on Chester Road in Knutsford required the attendance of firefighters. However, on arrival, crews found that no casualties were in fact trapped. They handed one woman into the care of paramedics.
