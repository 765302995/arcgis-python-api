 Wheelie bin fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 13/10/2018 - 21:14 Location: Somerton Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At around 9.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a back garden on Somerton Road in Macclesfield. The crew discovered that the fire, which was out on arrival, involved a wheelie bin. The fire had completely destroyed the bin. Firefighers used a hose reel jet to dampen down the bin and make sure that it was safe. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been caused by hot ash from a barbecue having been put inside the wheelie bin.
