 Fire at an unused hotel in Widnes Time of Incident: 15/11/2019 - 20:20 Location: Page Lane, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Widnes and Penketh At 10.20pm firefighters from Widnes and Penketh were dispatched to reports of a fire at an unused hotel on Page Lane.  On arrival crews discovered a fire in a first floor room.  Four firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  The crews were in attendance at the premises for around 15 minutes.
