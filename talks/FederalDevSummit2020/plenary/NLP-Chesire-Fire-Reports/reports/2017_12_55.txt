Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/12/2017 - 15:19 Location: Brookland Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Penketh A fire in the kitchen of a mid terraced property required firefighters to attend and use a hose reel to extinguish it. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property. Paramedics also attended and one person suffered from smoke inhalation.
