Shed fire in Chester Time of Incident: 06/03/2015 - 19:27 Location: School Lane,Elton, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on School Lane in Chester.  When they arrived crews found that the shed was on fire in the garden. Crews used hose reel jets and covering jets to deal with the incident, which also affected the garden fence.  A fire investigation has been launched to find the cause of the blaze but at this stage it is believed that it may well have been started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
