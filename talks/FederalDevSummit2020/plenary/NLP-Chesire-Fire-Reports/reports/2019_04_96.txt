 Rubbish fire, Wybunbury Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 15:06 Location: Sally Clarkes Lane, Wybunbury Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich Firefighters tackling a fire in WybunburyFirefighters were called out following reports of thick black smoke coming from a field near Wybunbury. They located the fire near a farm on Sally Clarkes Lane, which was carrying out a controlled burning of wood and plastic across an area measuring around 10m x 10m.  Firefighters used two hose reels to put the fire out, making use of a nearby stream as an open water supply. They were in attendance for just over an hour.
