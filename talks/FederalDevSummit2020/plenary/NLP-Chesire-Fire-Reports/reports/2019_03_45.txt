 One-vehicle collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 21/03/2019 - 08:43 Location: Mill Lane, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At around 8.45am firefighters were called to a report of a one-vehicle collision on Mill Lane in Winsford. On arrival the crew discovered that the incident involved a saloon car. The road was closed as emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters removed the driver's door and helped paramedics to get a casualty out of the vehicle. They then made the car safe and left the casualty in the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
