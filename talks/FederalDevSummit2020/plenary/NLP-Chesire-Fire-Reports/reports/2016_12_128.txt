Deliberate fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/12/2016 - 18:42 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters tackled a fire started deliberately on Grasmere Avenue. The fire was in the park area and crews had to get to the scene on foot, using hand extinguishers to tackle the blaze. They are urging anyone with any information as to who may have started the fire to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
