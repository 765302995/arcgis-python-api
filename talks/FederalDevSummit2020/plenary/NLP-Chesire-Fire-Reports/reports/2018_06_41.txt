 Grass fire on a footpath in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 19:56 Location: Runcorn Spur Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 7.55pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire on a footpath leading to the Asda footbridge on Runcorn Spur Road in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved approximately four metres by 10 metres of grassland. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. It is believed to have been started deliberately and is one of a number of fires in the area that day. Anyone with information about the fire or other fires in the area should call police on 101.
