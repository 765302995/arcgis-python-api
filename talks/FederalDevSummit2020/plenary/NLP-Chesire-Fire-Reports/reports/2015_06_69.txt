Building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/06/2015 - 17:06 Location: Delenty Drive, Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington and Birchwood Firefighters were called to a report of a building fire on Delenty Drive, Warrington.  When they arrived they found smoke coming out of a first floor window of the empty building.   Crews used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
