 Small fire in Minshull Vernon Time of Incident: 04/06/2019 - 05:16 Location: Eardswick Lane, Minshull Vernon. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to an unattended fire at an address in Eardswick Lane, Minshull Vernon. Crews used hose reel to extinguish the fire due to the amount of rubbish on fire.
