Two people trapped after collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 14/08/2015 - 18:13 Location: A5117 at junction with Poole Hall Lane Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Two people were released from their cars and taken to hospital after a collision in Ellesmere Port.  Four cars were involved in the collision with a man trapped in one vehicle and a woman trapped in another.  Crews were at the scene for around one hour 15 minutes.
