Road traffic collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 08/08/2016 - 19:07 Location: Castlefields Avenue North, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Two cars and a motorcycle were involved in a road traffic collision, and a person was reported to be trapped.  On arrival at the incident, the fire crew found that nobody was trapped.  Police attended the incident and closed the road in both directions. The motorcycle rider received treatment from paramedics.  Firefighters made the scene safe.
