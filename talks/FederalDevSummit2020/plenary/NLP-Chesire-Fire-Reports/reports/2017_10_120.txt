Shed fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/10/2017 - 20:15 Location: Rushmore Drive, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a fire involving a garden shed on Rushmore Drive, Widnes. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire which was started by an unattended tumble dryer. Crews were at the scene for 40 minutes.
