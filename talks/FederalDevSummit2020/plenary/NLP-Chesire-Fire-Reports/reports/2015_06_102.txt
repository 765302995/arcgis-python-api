Summerhouse on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/06/2015 - 04:31 Location: Delph Lane, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines A fire involving a summer house in a garden in Runcorn was extinguished with a hose reel. A large fan was also used to deal with this fire. It is believed that the cause of this fire could have been electrical.
