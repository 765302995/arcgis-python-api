Log burner on fire in Langley Time of Incident: 04/02/2016 - 22:38 Location: Langley Hall Close, Langley Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a small fire involving a log burner in a living room. A thermal imaging camera was used at this incident to check that the fire hadn't spread and a large fan removed smoke from the property. Chimney safety advice
