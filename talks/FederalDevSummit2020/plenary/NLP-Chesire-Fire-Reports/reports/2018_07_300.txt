 Fire outside a pub in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 18:06 Location: Gorsey Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Birchwood At around 6.05pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving fencing and a garden at Orford Hotel on Gorsey Lane in Fairfield and Howley, Warrington. On arrival crews discovered a small fire at the rear of the pub. The majority of the fire had been extinguished by the pub's staff prior to fire crews' arrival. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to fight the fire and cut away ivy at the rear of the pub. The building had no fire or heat damage. Only fencing and the ivy was damaged. Once the fire was extinguished firefighters used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke. They were in attendance for around two hours.
