 Boat fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 30/05/2019 - 14:48 Location: Bradford Road, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford. One fire engine attended from Northwich. One Fire engine attended from Chester. Firefighters were called to a fire involving a boat on Winsford Flashes off Bradford Road. Crews used a boat to reach the affected boat which was smouldering. The crews used a bucket to douse the boat. Police have been informed in order to locate the owner.
