Cooker fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 21/02/2016 - 17:38 Location: Fulshaw Park, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a report of a cooker fire at a property in Wilmslow. The fire was out but had left smoke in the property, which was cleared by the crew using a high pressure fan.  Please follow our safety advice - Kitchen fire safety
