 Two women trapped following collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/06/2018 - 14:14 Location: Clifton Interchange of the M56 in Runcorn Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Penketh, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Winsford At around 2.10pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision on the Clifton Interchange of the M56 in Runcorn. On arrival crews discovered that the collision at the roundabout involved a heavy goods vehicle and a saloon car. Two women were trapped in the car. The road was closed while emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters and later paramedics assessed the casualties before they self-extricated themselves from the car. They were both taken to Whiston Hospital via ambulance. Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes. Police were also in attendance.
