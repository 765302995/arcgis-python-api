Tumble dryer fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/09/2017 - 00:15 Location: bebington road, ellesmere port. ch664rx Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port A fire in a tumble dryer was tackled by firefighters at a property on Bebington Road. The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used hose reel and covering jets to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was then used to ventilate the property and gas and electricity supplies were isolated. Firefighters damped down and stripped out the kitchen to make sure there were no further hotspots.  Always follow our tumble dryer safety advice.
