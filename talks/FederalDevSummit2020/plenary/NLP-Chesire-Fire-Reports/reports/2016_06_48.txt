Kitchen fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 22/06/2016 - 10:57 Location: Crabmill Drive, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and one from Crewe A fridge and freezer in the kitchen of a Sandbach property was involved in fire. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke.
