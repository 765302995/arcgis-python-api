Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 07/09/2015 - 23:38 Location: Groby Road, Warmingham Road, crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe One hose reel was used to extinguish a car fire believed to have been started deliberately in Crewe.  Police were informed and attended the scene.  Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101 or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
