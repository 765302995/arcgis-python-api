Room on fire on 10th floor in Handforth Time of Incident: 24/04/2016 - 17:51 Location: Stanley Park Grange, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and fire engine from Greater Manchester attended A fire involving a bin room on the 10th floor of a high rise building was extinguished with a hose reel. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property.
