Two car collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/12/2015 - 19:37 Location: Daresbury Expressway, Warrington. Attendance: Three fire engines from Runcorn and Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on the Daresbury Expressway, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two cars which had collided head on. Two people were trapped. Firefighters cut the roofs off both cars to free a man and a woman.  Both casualties were then handed to the care of the ambulance service. A HART team and a BASICS team were also in attendance and assisted at the incident.
