Horse rescued from ditch in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 28/09/2016 - 11:02 Location: Knutsford Road, Chorley, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and the animal rescue unit and supporting fire engine from Bollington A horse was stuck in a ditch in a field on Knutsford Road in Chorley. A vet was called to sedate the animal whose hind legs were stuck. Firefighters and the animal rescue team were then able to release the horse.
