Fire involving air conditioning ducting in Chester Time of Incident: 22/06/2015 - 12:09 Location: Leen Lane, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines and the aerial appliance from Chester Firefighters have been at the scene of a fire involving air conditioning ducting on the roof of Rosies nightclub on Leen Lane.  Crews called for the aerial appliance to attend and the ducting and surrounding area, including the floor below, was thoroughly checked with a thermal imaging camera.  Ventilation ducting was cut away to make sure there was no spread of fire.  The fire was caused by the use of a gas blow torch next to the ducting.
