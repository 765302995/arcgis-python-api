Guttering blown on a school roof Time of Incident: 23/02/2017 - 11:50 Location: Kingshead Close, Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Stockton Heath Fire crews attended a school on Kingshead Close, Runcorn after wind had blown some guttering on the roof.  Firefighters used ladders to get onto the roof to remove the guttering and ensure that the area was safe.
