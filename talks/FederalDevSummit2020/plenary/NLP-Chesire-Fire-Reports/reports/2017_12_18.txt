Rubbish fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 25/12/2017 - 07:18 Location: Thrush Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Fire crews were called to Thrush Way, Winsford after reports of a rubbish fire. Crews found a small quantity of rubbish well alight on arrival and used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
