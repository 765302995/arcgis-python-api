 Building fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 06/11/2018 - 14:26 Location: Mirion Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters attended to a quantity of refuse well alight within a derelict building on Mirion Street in Crewe.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet and a safety jet to extinguish the fire before damping down the area to make sure it was safe.
