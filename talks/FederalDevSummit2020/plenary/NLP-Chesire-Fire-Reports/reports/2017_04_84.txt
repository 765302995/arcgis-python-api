Wheelie bins on fire by Liverpool Road in Great Sankey Time of Incident: 09/04/2017 - 04:05 Location: Liverpool Road, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Wheelie bins had been set on fire down an alley off Liverpool Road in Warrington, so firefighters used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately.  If you can help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, or call Cheshire Police on 101.
