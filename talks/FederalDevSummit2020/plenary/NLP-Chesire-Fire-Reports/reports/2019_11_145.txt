 Small bonfire in Winsford Time of Incident: 04/11/2019 - 18:21 Location: Bradbury Road, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a small bonfire near to the railway on Bradbury Road in Winsford.  Crews were in attendance at the scene for round 15 minutes.
