 Kitchen fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 29/08/2018 - 16:39 Location: Glastonbury Close, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn. One fire engine from Widnes. Firefighters were called to kitchen fire at an address in Glastonbury Close, Runcorn. Crews used two breathing apparatus and hose reel jets to extinguish the flames. Everyone was accounted for and the occupant was checked over by paramedics as a precaution. Firefighters then used a large fan to disperse the smoke and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. They also cut away the burnt kitchen worktop to dampen down.
