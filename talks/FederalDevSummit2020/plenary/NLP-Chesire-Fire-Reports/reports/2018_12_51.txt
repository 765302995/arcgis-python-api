 Unsupervised fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/12/2018 - 18:06 Location: Johnsons Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Shortly after 6pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving rubbish on Johnsons Lane in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire in a yard at an industrial premises that was not being supervised. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
