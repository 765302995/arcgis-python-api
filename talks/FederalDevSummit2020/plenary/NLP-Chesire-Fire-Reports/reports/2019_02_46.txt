 Bonfire in Warrington garden Time of Incident: 22/02/2019 - 17:52 Location: Sandalwood Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters attended to a large bonfire in a garden on Sandalwood Close in Warrington.  After giving advice to the occupants, crews used a hose reel to extinguish the fire before dampening down the area.  Crews left the scene when the area was deemed safe.
