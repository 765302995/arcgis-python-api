Road traffic collision on Middlewich Road in Winsford Time of Incident: 14/04/2016 - 18:17 Location: Middlewich Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters gave first aid to a female casualty following a road traffic collision involving two vehicles on Middlewich Road in Winsford.  Firefighters also made the vehicles safe. Police closed the road.  Paramedics attended.
