 Car engine fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 04/06/2019 - 09:05 Location: A54, Wincle, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Shortly after 9am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a car on the A54 in Wincle, Macclesfield. The fire involving the vehicle's engine was out on arrival. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to cool down the engine and make it safe. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
