 Skip fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 05/11/2018 - 17:35 Location: Wilding Street, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe used a hose reel jet to extinguish a skip which had been deliberately set on fire on Wilding Street. Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
