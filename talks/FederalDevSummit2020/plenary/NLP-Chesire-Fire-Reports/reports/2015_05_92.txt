House fire in Neston Time of Incident: 12/05/2015 - 18:26 Location: Olive Drive, Neston Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters used two hose reel jets and one covering jet to tackle a fire at a semi detached  property on Olive Drive in Neston. The fire had started in the shed and spread to the house. Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  The gas and electricity were isolated and two large fans were used to clear the smoke. The loft space of the property and the adjoining property were checked for fire. Police attended and a fire investigation will be completed to find the cause of the fire.
