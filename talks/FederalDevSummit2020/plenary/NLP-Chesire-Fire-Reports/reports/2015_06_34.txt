Rubbish fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 22/06/2015 - 19:51 Location: Mirion Street, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a building on Mirion Street, Crewe.  When they arrived crews found a small amount of rubbish on fire in an empty youth club. A hose reel jet was used to put it out.
