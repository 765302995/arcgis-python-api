Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 13/10/2015 - 17:45 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes One hose reel was used to extinguish a fire involving a wheelie bin in Widnes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and police where also requested to attend the scene.  Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
