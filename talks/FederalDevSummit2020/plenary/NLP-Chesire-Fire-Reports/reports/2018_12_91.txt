 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/12/2018 - 12:18 Location: Mersey Street, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Birchwood At around 12.20pm firefighters were called to reports of a kitchen fire on Mersey Street, Warrington. Crews from Warrington and Birchwood attended the scene and discovered an oven fire in a flat which had been extinguished by the occupants. The team used a large fan to clear the smoke from the flat and were detained at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.
