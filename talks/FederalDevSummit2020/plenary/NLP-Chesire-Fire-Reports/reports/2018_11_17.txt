 Bin wagon fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/11/2018 - 12:15 Location: Telford Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane At 12:15 firefighters from Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane were called to reports of a fire at an industrial building in Ellesmere Port. On arrival at the scene the team found that there was a small fire in the rear of an industrial bin wagon inside a building on Telford Road.  Crews initially removed the wagon from the building, they then used water to extinguish the fire. The team then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the building. Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
