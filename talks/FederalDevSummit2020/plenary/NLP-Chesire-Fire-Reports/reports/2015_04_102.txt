Large grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 17/04/2015 - 13:23 Location: The Underway, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Crews used beaters to extinguish a large grass fire in Runcorn.  Police were requested to attend the scene due to it being the latest in a number of incidents recently.  Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101, or alteratively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
