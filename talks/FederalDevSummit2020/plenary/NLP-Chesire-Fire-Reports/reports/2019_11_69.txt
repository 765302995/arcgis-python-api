 Animal rescue in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 16/11/2019 - 08:37 Location: Poplar Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield along with an animal rescue team from Bollington Shortly after 8.30am firefighters from Macclesfield and Bollington attended reports of an animal rescue on South Park, Macclesfield. Crews attended the scene and on arrival they found that a heron with a broken wing was trapped in a pond. The RSPCA water rescue unit were called to the scene and the crews worked together to reach the heron and bring it back to the shore. The heron has now been passed to the RSPCA for treatment. The crews were detained at the scene for around one hour 20 minutes.
