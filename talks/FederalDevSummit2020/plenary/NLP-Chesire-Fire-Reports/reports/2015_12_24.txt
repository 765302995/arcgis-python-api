House fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/12/2015 - 13:23 Location: John Street, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on John Street, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire and so they used a hose reel jet and a large fan to deal with the incident. There was no reports of any injuries.
