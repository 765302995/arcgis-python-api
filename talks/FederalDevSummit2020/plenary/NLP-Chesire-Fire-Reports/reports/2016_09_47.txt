Barge stuck in mud Time of Incident: 21/09/2016 - 17:15 Location: Stocks Hill in Winsford. Attendance: A fire engine from Winsford and the rescue unit from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a report of a barge that was stuck in some mud near Stocks Hill in Winsford.  Firefighters went into the water and managed to get the barge out of the mud. There were no reports of any injuries.
