 Living room fire in Marton Time of Incident: 05/03/2019 - 14:10 Location: Congleton Road, Marton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow. One fire engine from Macclesfield. Fire crews were called to a fire at an address in Congleton Road in Marton. The fire was in the living room of a detached property. Crews used a hose reel and two breathing apparatus to tackle the fire. They then damped down the area and used a large fan to disperse smoke.
