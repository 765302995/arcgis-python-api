Kitchen fire in Wrenbury Time of Incident: 14/04/2016 - 13:18 Location: Station Road, Wrenbury Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters tackled a kitchen fire in a mobile home. A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire, with two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus.  Smoke alarms were fitted in the property which alerted the occupier to the incident.  Smoke alarms save lives - make sure you have them fitted and working.
