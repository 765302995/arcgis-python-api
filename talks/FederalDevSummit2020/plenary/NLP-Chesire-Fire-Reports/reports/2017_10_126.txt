Road traffic collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 01/10/2017 - 12:57 Location: M56 Westbound junction 11 to 12 Attendance: One fire engine from Frodsham, one from Lymm, one from Runcorn and one from Warrington Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision on the M56 Westbound between junction 11 and 12. Fire crews cut one female casualty out of a car and handed her into the care of the paramedics.
