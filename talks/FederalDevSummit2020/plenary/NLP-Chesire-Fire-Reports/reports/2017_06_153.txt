Small fire at school in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/06/2017 - 11:56 Location: Cardinal Newman Catholic High School, Bridgewater Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a small fire in a school. The school was fully evacuated and the fire was confirmed on the ground floor of the premises. One hose reel was used to extinguish the small fire and a thermal imaging camera was used to check the area. A large fan was also used to removed smoke from the premises. Please visit the school website for further information.
