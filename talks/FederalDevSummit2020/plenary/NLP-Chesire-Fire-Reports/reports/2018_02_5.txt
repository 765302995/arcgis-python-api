 Electrical fault in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/02/2018 - 20:09 Location: Regent Street, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 8pm firefighters were called to reports of a building fire in Ellesmere Port after a resident noticed a burning smell coming from their loft. On arrival at the scene the team found that the smell had been caused by a faulty light fitting. The crew isolated the fitting and the resident was advised to contact a qualified electrician.
