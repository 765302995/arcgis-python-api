Van on its side in Gawsworth. Time of Incident: 26/12/2015 - 12:30 Location: Gawsworth Road, Gawsworth. Attendance: Fire engines from Congleton and Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Gawsworth Road, Gawsworth.  When they arrived they found that there was a van on its side with a man trapped.  Crews used special cutting gear to free him. He was then passed to the care of the asmbulance service.
