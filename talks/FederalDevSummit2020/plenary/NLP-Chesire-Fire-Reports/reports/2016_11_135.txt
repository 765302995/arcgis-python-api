Two car collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 06/11/2016 - 04:05 Location: Edleston Road, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engine from Crewe and one from Alsager. Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Edleston Road, Crewe.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two cars. Two people were trapped. Firefighters using specialist equipment rescues the two people who were then taken to hospital by ambulance.
