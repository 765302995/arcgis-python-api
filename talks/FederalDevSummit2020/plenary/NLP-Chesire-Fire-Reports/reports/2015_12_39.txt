Fire at Frodsham hotel Time of Incident: 22/12/2015 - 11:48 Location: Forest Hills Hotel, Bellemonte Road, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to the Forest Hills Hotel after a smell of burning was identified in the men's changing rooms. There had been a small fire in a light fitting which was out on arrival of the crew. The hotel had been evacuated as a precaution and firefighters ventilated the property.
