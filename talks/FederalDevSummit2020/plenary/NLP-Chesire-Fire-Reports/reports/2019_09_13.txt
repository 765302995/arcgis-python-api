 Collision in Neston Time of Incident: 27/09/2019 - 18:43 Location: Leighton Road, Neston. CH64 3SQ Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Firefighters were called to a two-car collision in Neston just before 6.45pm.  The crew, which was at the scene for around 30 minutes, assessed the cars and made them safe.
