Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/04/2015 - 10:56 Location: Raglan Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters tackled a kitchen fire in a bungalow in Raglan Road.  The crew, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  The property was then cleared of smoke using a high pressure fan.
