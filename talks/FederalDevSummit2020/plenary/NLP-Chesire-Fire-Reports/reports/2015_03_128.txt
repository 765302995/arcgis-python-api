Small rubbish fire in Preston Brook Time of Incident: 06/03/2015 - 09:21 Location: Chester Road, Preston Brook, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of a small fire on the side of the railway track at Preston Brook.   They found a fire involving a small quantity of rubbish and used a hose reel jet to extinguish it.
