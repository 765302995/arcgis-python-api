Building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/06/2016 - 04:17 Location: Grammar School Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, two from Stockton Heath, one from Birchwood and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Five fire engines attended a fire at a building on School Road in Latchford. The fire was on the ground floor of the property. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used hose reel and covering jets to tackle the blaze. A high pressure fan was also used to clear smoke. The cause of the fire is believed to have been electrical.
