 Bin fire, Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 06/10/2018 - 21:33 Location: Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were notified of some bins that had been set on fire at Bunbury Green Park. They arrived to find one bin on fire and used a hose reel to put out the flames. Police were notified due to suspicion of deliberate ignition.
