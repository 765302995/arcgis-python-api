 Large grass small fire on Spike Island, Widnes Time of Incident: 20/05/2019 - 17:30 Location: Upper Mersey Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At 5.30pm firefighters from Widnes were dispatched to reports of a grass fire on Spike Island.  On arrival the team discovered the fire was affecting approximately 50 square metres of marshland.  Firefighters used a water pump and main jet to soak the affected area. They also used beaters to extinguish the fire and a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.  Crews were detained at the scene for around one hour.
