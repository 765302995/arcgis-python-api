 Car fire on M6 Time of Incident: 27/07/2019 - 07:45 Location: M6,Junction 18 to Junction 19 Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Crewe and one from Sandbach Crews attended to a car fire on the M6 between junction 18 to junction 19.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews used two hose reels to extinguish the fire.  Highways attended to manage traffic control.  Crews were detained at the incident for approximately one hour.
