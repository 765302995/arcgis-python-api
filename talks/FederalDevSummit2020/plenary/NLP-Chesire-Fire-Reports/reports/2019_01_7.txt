 Chimney fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 11/01/2019 - 03:18 Location: St Johns Avenue, Knutsford. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a chimney fire at an address in St Johns Avenue, Knutsford. On arrival crews assessed the chimney with a thermal imaging camera and exposed the chimney flue above the log burner to investigate the heat source. They then removed embers and debris from the flue to remove the heat source.
