 Grass fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 19/06/2018 - 20:05 Location: Vauxhall Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to reports of a small amount of grass on fire on fields near to Vauxhall Way in Winsford.  A hose reel was used to ensure it was fully extinguished.
