 House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/01/2018 - 06:16 Location: Cliftonville Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. One fire engine attended from Lymm. Firefighters attended a fire involving a consumer unit in a house in Cliftonville Road, Woolston. The crews used breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the fire. They used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
