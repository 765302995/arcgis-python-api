 Chimney fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 11/04/2019 - 22:50 Location: Wistaston Road, Crewe Attendance: Three fire engines attended - two from Crewe and one from Nantwich Fire crews attended to a chimney fire at a property on Wistaston Road in Crewe.  Firefighters inspected the attic and removed debris from the chimney. A ladder was set up to help put out the fire from the chimney pot using a chimney hose.  A road closure was put in place as a precaution while firefighters dealt with the fire.
