 Collision in Scholar Green Time of Incident: 25/04/2018 - 17:18 Location: Congleton Road North, Scholar Green. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters from Congleton attended reports of a collision on Congleton Road North in Scholar Green. On arrival at the scene the crew found the collision involved three cars. Thankfully, nobody was trapped in any of the vehicles, so the team worked with Police to make the cars safe. Crews were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
