 Fire in the open in Middlewich Time of Incident: 26/04/2018 - 21:07 Location: St Annes Avenue, Middlewich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Middlewich and one from Holmes Chapel At around 9.05pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on St Annes Avenue in Middlewich. On arrival crews found the fire to be intentional burning of debris. Fiurefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
