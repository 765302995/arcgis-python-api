Heavy goods vehicle well alight in Ellesmere Port. Time of Incident: 14/03/2015 - 10:53 Location: Bridges Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A heavy goods vehicle was on fire on Bridges Road, Ellesmere Port. The vehicle was well alight.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
