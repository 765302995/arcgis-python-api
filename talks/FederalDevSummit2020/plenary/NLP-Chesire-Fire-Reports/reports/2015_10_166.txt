Skip fire at Alban Retail Park Time of Incident: 01/10/2015 - 07:07 Location: Hawleys Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to reports of black smoke coming from behind a retail unit at Alban Retail Park. Upon arrival, crews found a skip well alight and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
