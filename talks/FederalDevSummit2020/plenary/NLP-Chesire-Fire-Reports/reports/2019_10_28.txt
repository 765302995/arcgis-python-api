 Man rescued from River Weaver Time of Incident: 26/10/2019 - 14:20 Location: River Weaver, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Fire crews were called to reports of a man in difficulty in the River Weaver in Nantwich.  When firefighters arrived he was seen clinging to a branch by the riverside requiring an immediate rescue.  A throw line was used to bring him to safety and he was handed into the care of paramedics.
