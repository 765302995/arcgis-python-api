Rubbish on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/03/2016 - 15:03 Location: Enfield Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a large amount of rubbish on fire which is suspected to have been started deliberately. Police also attended. If anyone has any information about this fire they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
