 Small fire in the open in Chester Time of Incident: 15/07/2019 - 16:52 Location: Canadian Avenue, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 4.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Canadian Avenue, Chester. On arrival the crew found a small unattended bonfire. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
