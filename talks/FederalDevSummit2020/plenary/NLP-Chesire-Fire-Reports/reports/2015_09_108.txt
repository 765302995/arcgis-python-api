Grill pan fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/09/2015 - 21:14 Location: Frederick Street, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters attended an incident in Widnes where fumes from an unattended grill pan had activated a smoke alarm.    Firefighters used a positive pressure ventilation fan to clear the fumes and provided fire safety advice to the occupier.  Safety advice for grills
