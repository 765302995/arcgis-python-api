 Chimney fire in Shavington Time of Incident: 13/06/2019 - 15:06 Location: Weston Lane, Shavington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe and one from Nantwich Fire crews were called to a chimney fire on Weston Lane, Shavington. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which was caused by a blockage in an open coal fire. The house was then cleared of smoke using a large fan. Crews were at the scene for 30 minutes.
