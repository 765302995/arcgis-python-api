Small fire inside a microwave in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/01/2015 - 14:21 Location: Troutbeck Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington There was a small fire inside a microwave. The fire was out when the fire crew arrived.  Firefighters gave first aid to one male casualty, and an ambulance was requested to attend.  The fire crew then conducted a home safety assessment at the property.
