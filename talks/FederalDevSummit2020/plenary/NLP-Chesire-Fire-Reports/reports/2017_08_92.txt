Fire involving rubbish in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 14/08/2017 - 01:46 Location: Chelford Road, Henbury Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a fire involving rubbish near the rear of a property which resulted in smoke entering a premises. The crews from Macclesfield extinguished the fire with a hose reel. They also used stack drags to move the rubbish and extinguish it fully.
