 Industrial building fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 05/07/2018 - 11:03 Location: Deakins Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and one fire engine attended from Winsford Shortly after 11am firefighters were called to reports of a fire at an industrial building on Deakins Road in Winsford.  The fire involved an acetylene cylinder.  A full evacuation of the site took place and a 100-metre cordon was set up as a precautionary measure.  Crews initially used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire and cool the cylinder.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus then used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Once the fire was out and the cylinder was cooled the crews left the site.  Firefighters were in attendance for approximately three hours and 45 minutes.
