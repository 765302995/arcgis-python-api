 Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/08/2018 - 21:29 Location: Stonehills Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 9.30pm firefighters were called to a report of fire in the open on Stonehills Lane in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that the grass fire measured approximately three metres by 10 metres. They used water and beaters to extinguish the fire, which is believed to have been caused by smoking materials. Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
