Unattended bonfire in Burtonwood Time of Incident: 28/12/2017 - 16:55 Location: Burtonwood Road, Burtonwood Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Penketh An large unattended bonfire on Burtonwood Road, Burtonwood, was extinguished by firefighters. Crews used a mechanical digger and a main jet to put out the fire. Crews were at the scene for two and a half hours.
