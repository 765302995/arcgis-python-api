 Small fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/04/2019 - 21:19 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Warrington firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a small refuse fire at the rear of a residential address on Grasmere Avenue.  The team were detained at the scene for around ten minutes.
