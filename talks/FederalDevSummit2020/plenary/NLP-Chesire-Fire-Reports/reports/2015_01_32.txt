Chimney fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 22/01/2015 - 18:09 Location: Saltersley Lane, Wilmslow. Attendance: A fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a report of a chimney fire on Saltersley Lane, Wilmslow.  They used hose reeel jets and special rods to tackle the fire.   https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-fire-safety-advice/chimneys
