M53 road traffic collision near Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 24/09/2015 - 11:32 Location: M53 south between junctions 9 and 10 Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Fire and rescue crews have been at the scene of a road traffic collision on the M53 between junctions 9 and 10. Firefighters stabilised and removed the roof of one of the two cars involved to release a casualty, a woman, who was trapped. The air ambulance landed at the scene and the casualty was handed into the care of paramedics before being airlifted to hospital.
