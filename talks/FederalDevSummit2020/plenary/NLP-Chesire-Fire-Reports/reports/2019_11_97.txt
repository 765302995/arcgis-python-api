 Van fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/11/2019 - 21:38 Location: Warrington Road, Risley, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Birchwood and one from Warrington Shortly after 9.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a van being on fire on Warrington Road in Risley, Warrington.  On arrival crews discovered that it was a box van.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet.  Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
