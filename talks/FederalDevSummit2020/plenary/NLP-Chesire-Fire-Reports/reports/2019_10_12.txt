 Fire involving chemical containers in Chester Time of Incident: 29/10/2019 - 13:20 Location: Guilden Sutton Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford, one from Powey Lane, one from Widnes and the environmental protection unit attended Firefighters were called to a fire involving chemical containers within a compound at a commercial building where one man suffered from burns. Firefighters used a hose reel to put the fire out and to damp the area down. They held the chemicals within the compound using safety sheets and specialist equipment and water run off was managed. The crew gave medical assistance to the man until paramedics attended. He was then taken to hospital by ambulance. A fire investigation into the cause will take place.
