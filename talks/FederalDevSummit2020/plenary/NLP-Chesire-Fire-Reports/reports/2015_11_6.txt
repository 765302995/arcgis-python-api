Car collided with railings in Widnes Time of Incident: 28/11/2015 - 14:11 Location: Sinclair Avenue, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters removed the roof of a car to free the casualty inside after a car collided with railings in Widnes.  The crews worked closely with other agencies in attendance and handed the casualty into the care of paramedics. They were at the scene for 45 minutes.
