Deep fat fryer fire at a shop in Penketh, Warrington Time of Incident: 25/09/2017 - 19:27 Location: Warrington Road, Penketh, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Penketh Firefighters were sent to reports of a small fire involving a deep fat fryer at a shop in Penketh.  On arrival firefighters found that the fire was already out. They completed a full inspection of the kitchen and the flue.  They used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
