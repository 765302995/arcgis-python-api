Two cars well alight in Glazebrook in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/04/2017 - 02:48 Location: Bank Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Two hose reel jets were used to extinguish two car fires on Bank Street in Glazebrook, Warrington. The cars were well alight.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus to fight the fires.  It is believed that these fires were started deliberately.  If you can help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, or call Cheshire Police on 101.
