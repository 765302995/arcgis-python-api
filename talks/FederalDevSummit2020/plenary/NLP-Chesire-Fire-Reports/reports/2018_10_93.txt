 Possible gas leak in Poynton Time of Incident: 15/10/2018 - 10:19 Location: Park Lane, Poynton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Shortly after 10.15am firefighters were called to reports of a possible gas leak at a residential property in Poynton. Crews attended the scene and on arrival the occupant had left the property. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus then entered the address and isolated the gas. The team then used a large fan to ventilate the property. The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
