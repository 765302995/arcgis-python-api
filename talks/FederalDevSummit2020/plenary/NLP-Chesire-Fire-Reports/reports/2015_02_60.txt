Chimney fire in Prestbury Time of Incident: 14/02/2015 - 18:24 Location: Macclesfield Road, Prestbury. Attendance: A fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a chimney fire on Macclesfield Road, Prestbury.  When they arrived crews found that the fire was in a wood burner. They used water back packs to put the fire out.
