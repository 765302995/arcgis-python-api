Wheelie bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 21/10/2015 - 19:46 Location: Warrington Road, Runcorn. Attendance: Fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Warrington Road, Runcorn.  When they arrived crews found that there was a wheelie bin fire close to a building. Hose reel jets were used to put the fire out.   It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.   Please follow our wheelie bin safety advice - Wheelie Bin - Fire Safety
