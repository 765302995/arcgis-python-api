Tree on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 09/09/2015 - 12:35 Location: Arbour Crescent, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine A fire involving conifer trees in a garden was extinguished by firefighters using a hose reel. One man was treated for smoke inhalation by the crew. It is believed that the fire was caused accidentally by a gas weed burner which was being used near the conifers.  Please take care during the drier months when using gas weed burners.
