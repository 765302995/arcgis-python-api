Fire at a disused building in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/08/2016 - 23:06 Location: Greenway Road in Runcorn. Attendance: Five fire engines and one hydraulic platform Five fire engines and one hydraulic platform were sent to a fire at a disused building on Greenway Road in Runcorn. Fire crews used three breathing apparatus, one hose reel, one 45mm main jet and thermal imaging camera. Police attended this incident. It is believed that this fire was not started deliberately.
