Refuse fire at the rear of a property in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/10/2016 - 15:05 Location: Clegge Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath A quantity of refuse that was well alight. The refuse was located against a rear wall of the extension of a property. Fire crews used one hose reel to bring the fire under control.  Firefighters removed the facia on the extension of the property, to check that the fire had not spread.  Police were requested to attend this incident.  This fire was believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
