Horse stuck in ditch in Frodsham Time of Incident: 09/09/2015 - 16:22 Location: Lower Rake Lane, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine from Frodsham and the animal rescue unit from Bollington. A horse which was stuck down a ditch in Frodsham required firefighters to assist in getting it out. The crew constructed a step from an embankment to the waters edge to get to the animal and also dug away part of the embankment. The vet was in attendance along with the owner.
