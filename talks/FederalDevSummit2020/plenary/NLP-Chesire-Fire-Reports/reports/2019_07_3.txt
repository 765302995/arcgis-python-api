 Horse rescue in Malpas Time of Incident: 29/07/2019 - 10:08 Location: Malpas Road, Malpas. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port along with the rope rescue unit from Lymm and the animal rescue unit from Bollington. Horse trapped in MalpasFirefighters were called to a horse trapped in a wooden bridge on Malpas Road, Tilston, Malpas. Its back legs had fallen through the bridge causing the animal to be trapped.  The road was closed in both directions while the crews liaised with a vet to work out the best course of action.   Crews then used a telehandler to rescue the horse, named Bubbles, and handed it over to the vet to be assessed.
