 Firefighters called to rescue of woman in the River Dee Time of Incident: 31/03/2018 - 04:03 Location: River Dee, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane and one from Chester. The rope rescue unit from Lymm and the hydraulic platform vehicle from Chester Firefighters were called in the early hours of the morning to reports of a woman in the River Dee, Chester.  Crews from Powey Lane and Chester as well as the rope rescue unit from Lymm along with the water rescue unit and the hydraulic platform vehicle from Chester attended to the incident.  The woman was rescued by officers from Chester local policing unit before fire arrived at the scene. Fire crews assisted police and ambulance with administrating first aid before she was taken to the Countess of Chester Hospital suffering from hypothermia.
