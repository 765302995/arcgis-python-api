Road traffic collision on Wheelock bypass near Sandbach Time of Incident: 18/11/2015 - 11:57 Location: Junction of Clay Lane and Wheelock bypass near Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Nantwich Firefighters released a female casualty from a car following a road traffic collision. Two vehicles were involved in the incident. Firefighters made the scene safe and the casualty was taken into the care of paramedics.
