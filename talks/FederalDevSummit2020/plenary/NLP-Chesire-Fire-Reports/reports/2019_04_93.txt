 Fire in shrubs, Widnes Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 16:32 Location: Hale Gate Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to tackle a fire in large area of shrubs off Hale Gate Road. They used beaters to put out the fire, which appears to have been caused by a discarded cigarette. They were in attendance for around 25 minutes.
