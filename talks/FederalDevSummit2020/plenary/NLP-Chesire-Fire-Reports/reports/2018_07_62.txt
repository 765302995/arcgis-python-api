 Fire involving a large amount of rubbish at a park in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/07/2018 - 19:47 Location: Wimborne Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 7.45pm firefighters were called to a report of fire at a park in Wimborne Close in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered a large amount of rubbish alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire using a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
