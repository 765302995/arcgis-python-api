Road traffic collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 28/03/2017 - 05:57 Location: A500, Shavington, Crewe. Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe, Nantwich and Alsager attended. A road traffic collision involving a heavy goods vehicle and a light goods vehicle required firefighters to attend. One man was trapped in the light good vehicle and the crew used cutting equipment to get the casualty out and to the care of paramedics.
