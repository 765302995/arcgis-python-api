 Caravan fire in Chester Time of Incident: 17/06/2018 - 02:40 Location: A41, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas and Chester Wearing two breathing apparatus, fire crews extinguished a caravan well alight along the A41 in Broxton, Chester using two hose reels and a thermal imaging camera.  Crews were in attendance for 35 minutes.
