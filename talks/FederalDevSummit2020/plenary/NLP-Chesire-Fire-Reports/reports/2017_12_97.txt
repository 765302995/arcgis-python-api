Cat rescued from engine in Crewe Time of Incident: 30/11/2017 - 16:54 Location: Weston Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to help remove a cat which had become stuck in the engine bay of a van on Weston Road, Crewe. Crews managed to release the cat before they handed the cat over to the care of the RSPCA.
