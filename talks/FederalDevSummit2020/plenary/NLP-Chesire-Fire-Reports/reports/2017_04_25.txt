Grill pan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/04/2017 - 08:41 Location: Chapel Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Penketh A grill pan fire caused a lot of smoke in a property on Chapel Lane. A hose reel jet was used to make sure the fire was out and a high pressure fan used to clear smoke from the house.
