 Road flooded in Frodsham Time of Incident: 19/07/2019 - 17:08 Location: Hollow Lane, Kingsley, Frodsham Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Runcorn, one from Widnes and one from Penketh Shortly after 7pm firefighters were called to a report of flooding in Hollow Lane, Kingsley, Frodsham. On arrival crews found that a road was covered in water. The water covered an area measuring approximately 75 metres by 40 metres and was up to three feet deep. Various homes were affected by the water. Firefighters closed Hollow Lane and slowed down traffic on Kingsley Road. They also isolated the utility supply to an affected home. Firefighters used a hand pump to transfer flood water to a nearby drain. Once the road was cleared they handed the incident over to a utilities company. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour and 45 minutes.
