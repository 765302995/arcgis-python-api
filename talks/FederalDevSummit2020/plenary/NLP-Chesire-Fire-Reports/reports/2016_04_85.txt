Fence and shed on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 15/04/2016 - 13:30 Location: Eccleston Avenue, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A crew was called to a report of a shed and a fence on fire in Eccleston Avenue. The firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They then dampened the area down and were at the scene for about an hour.
