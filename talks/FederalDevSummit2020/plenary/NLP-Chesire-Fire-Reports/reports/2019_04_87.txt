 Fire in woodland, Warrington Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 20:32 Location: Radley Common, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to deal with a fire in woodland at Radley Common. They found an area of trees measuring around 10m x 2m alight, and used beaters and a water backpack to put the fire out. It is thought to have been a deliberate ignition, anyone with information is asked to call police on 101, quoting IML 378942.
