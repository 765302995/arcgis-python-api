Wheelie bins on fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 09/11/2015 - 01:49 Location: Park Crescent, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Two wheelie bins that were well alight were extinguished with two hose reels. A third wheelie bin was also damaged. Police attended this incident as it is believed that the fire was started deliberately. Wheelie Bin - Fire Safety advice
