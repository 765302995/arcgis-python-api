A wheelie bin fire spread to a kitchen of a property in Widnes Time of Incident: 19/06/2016 - 05:50 Location: Hale Road in Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Widnes firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish a fire involving a wheelie bin at the rear of a property on Hale Road in Widnes. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. The fire spread to the kitchen of the property, which was full of smoke. Firefighters extinguished the fire and then used a large fan clear the smoke from the property. Police were requested to attend this incident.
