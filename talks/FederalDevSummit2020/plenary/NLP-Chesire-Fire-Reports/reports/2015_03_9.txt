House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/03/2015 - 09:22 Location: Padgate Lane, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Padgate Lane, Warrington.   When they arrived crews confirmed that there was nobody in the property before tackling it with hose reel jets.  Specialist fans were also used to clear the smoke.   A fire investigation has been carried out and it is believed that the blaze, which was at the rear of the property was caused accidentally by a candle.  Candle safety advice
