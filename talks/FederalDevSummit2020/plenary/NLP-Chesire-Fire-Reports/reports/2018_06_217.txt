 Small fire in a school in Chester Time of Incident: 07/06/2018 - 15:38 Location: Wealstone Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a school on Wealstone Lane, Chester, after reports of a fire. Crews discovered a small fire in the toilet of the school and used a hose reel jet and a large fan to put out the fire.
