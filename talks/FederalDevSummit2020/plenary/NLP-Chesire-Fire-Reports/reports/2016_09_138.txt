Runcorn home fire Time of Incident: 02/09/2016 - 04:02 Location: Brookvale Avenue North, Runcorn. Attendance: Fire engines from Widnes, Runcorn and Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a sheltered housing home on Brookvale Avenue North, Runcorn.  When they arrived crews confirmed that there was a fire in one of the flats on the third floor of the building. Crews confirmed that everyone was out of the property before tackling the fire.  Firefighters used a ladder to get to the flat and a hose reel jet to deal with the fire.  Crews were able to put the fire out and continued to dampen down the area.  Residents are now being allowed back into areas of the property which have not been affected by the fire.
