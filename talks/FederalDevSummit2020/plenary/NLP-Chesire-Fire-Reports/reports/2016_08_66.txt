Fire inside the garage of a property in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 18/08/2016 - 13:34 Location: Ledsham Hall Lane in Ledsham, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port There was a fire involving a jet wash appliance,  inside a garage of a property on Ledsham Hall Lane in Ledsham. The fire was out when the fire crews arrived at the property.  Firefighters helped paramedics with one male casualty. The casualty was then taken to hospital for a precautionary check.
