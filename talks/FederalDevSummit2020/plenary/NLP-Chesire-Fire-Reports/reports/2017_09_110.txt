Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/09/2017 - 07:30 Location: Warrington Road, Glazebury, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood A car was well alight in the middle of Warrington Road in Glazebury. The incident was coned off as firefighters tackled the blaze and used a thermal imaging camera to check there were no further hot spots.  The cause of the fire is believed to have been an electrical fault.  Birchwood firefighter tackles Warrington Road car fire
