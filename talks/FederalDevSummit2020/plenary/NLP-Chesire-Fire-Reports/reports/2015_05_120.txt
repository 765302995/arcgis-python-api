Car on its side in Tabley Time of Incident: 09/05/2015 - 01:44 Location: Sudlow Lane, Tabley. Attendance: A fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Sudlow Lane, Tabley.  When they arrived crews found that there was one car on its side. Nobody was trapped and so crews ensured the scene was safe before leaving.
