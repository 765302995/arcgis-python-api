Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 11/06/2015 - 14:07 Location: Chester Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A grass fire on the canalside had to be reached by firefighters on foot because of its distance from the road.  The crew put out the fire using a water backpack.  There have been several similar small fires over the last few days so firefighters asked the police to attend as it is believed they are being started deliberately.  If people see anyone acting suspiciously please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
