Fire in machinery in Northwich Time of Incident: 12/05/2017 - 08:10 Location: Tata Chemicals Europe Limited, Works Lane, Northwich Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich, Knutsford, Winsford and Middlewich Firefighters were called to a fire involving machinery. Two hose reel were in use and the premises was evacuated while the incident was dealt with. The fire was on the third floor within an industrial building which is constructed over five floors. The fire was contained to an extraction system and crews worked with on-site maintenance staff to deal with the incident. Fire engines left the scene 13:24 although a crew planed to check the area again before the incident was closed.
