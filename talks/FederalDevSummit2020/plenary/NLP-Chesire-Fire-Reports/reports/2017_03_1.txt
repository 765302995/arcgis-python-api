House fire in Chester Time of Incident: 30/03/2017 - 20:14 Location: Circular Drive, Chester. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Chester and Powey Lane. Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Circular Drive, Chester.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire in a bedroom caused by a hairdryer that had been left on. This had gone out so crews carried out a Home Fire Safety Check before leaving.
