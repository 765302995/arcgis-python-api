Fire at Winsford recycling plant Time of Incident: 08/09/2015 - 16:57 Location: Barlow Drive, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich Firefighters attended a fire at a recycling plant on Barlow Drive. The fire involved a plastic and tin baler and was confined to the machine. The electricity supply to the machine was isolated as firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no further hot spots.
