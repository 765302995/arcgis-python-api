House fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/12/2016 - 17:38 Location: Crown Avenue in Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Widnes Fire crews responded to a report of a fire in a house on Crown Avenue in Widnes.  When they arrived firefighters were confronted with a developing fire in the bedroom of a mid-terraced property. They also checked the neighbouring properties to ensure that they were safe.  Firefighters used one hose reel, two breathing apparatus and a large fan to fight the fire.
