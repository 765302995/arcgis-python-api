 Flooding in basement of pub in Winsford Time of Incident: 27/10/2019 - 11:27 Location: Red Lion, Wharton Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to a pub in Winsford with a flooded basement. The water had risen to one meter high and was affecting electrics so firefighters isolated the power and advice was given to owners who used a pump to get the water out.
