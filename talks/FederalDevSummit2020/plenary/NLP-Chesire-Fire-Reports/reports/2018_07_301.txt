 Wasteground fire on Helsby Hill in Frodsham Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 17:37 Location: Helsby Hill, Frodsham Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Lymm At around 5.35pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire on Helsby Hill in Frodsham. On arrival crews discovered around 30 metres by 40 metres of wasteground well alight. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire that involved deep seated dry heather. They then checked for hotspots to make sure that the area was safe. Firefighters, who set up a water shuttle to ensure that they had enough water to fight the fire, were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
