Offices on fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 01/11/2016 - 00:02 Location: Countess Avenue, Wilmslow Attendance: Fire engines from Wilmslow and Cheadle A fire in the offices of a commercial building required firefighters to attend and use one hose reel to extinguish it. A large fan was also used to removed smoke from the area. This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information they should call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
