Trees on fire in Weaverham Time of Incident: 24/05/2016 - 11:36 Location: Northwich Road, Weaverham, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A small fire spread to trees in Weaverham, prompting firefighters to be called. The fire was out on arrival but firefighters at the scene used one hose reel jet to dampen the remaining heat.
