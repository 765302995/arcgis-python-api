 One-vehicle collision in Middlewich Time of Incident: 10/11/2019 - 08:09 Location: Warmingham Lane, Moston, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich, as did the rope rescue unit from Sandbach Shortly after 8am firefighters were called to a report of a one-vehicle collision in Warmingham Lane, Moston, Middlewich.  On arrival they found the vehicle on its roof in a ditch.  No-one was trapped inside the vehicle.  Firefighters administered first aid to a casualty before handing her over to the care of paramedics.  Firefighters also made the vehicle safe and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
