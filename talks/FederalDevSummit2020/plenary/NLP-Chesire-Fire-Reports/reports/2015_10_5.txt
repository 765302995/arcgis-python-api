Wheelie bin set on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 28/10/2015 - 22:23 Location: Woodfield Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to reports of a wheelie bin on fire in Ellesmere Port.  Upon arrival crews found an industrial wheelie bin well alight and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. This fire is believed to have been started deliberatley.
