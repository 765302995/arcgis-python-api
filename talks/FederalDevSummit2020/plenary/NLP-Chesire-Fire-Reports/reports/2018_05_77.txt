 Small fire in a field in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/05/2018 - 22:06 Location: Lumber Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Fire crews were called to a small fire in a farmers field on Lumber Lane, Warrington. The fire, measuring approximately 10 square metres, was caused by embers from a nearby controlled burn and crews used a hose reel jet to put it out.
