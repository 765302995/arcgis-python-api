 Lorry fire on M6 Time of Incident: 02/01/2018 - 12:02 Location: M6 Southbound between junction 21 and 20 Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm, one from Birchwood and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a lorry fire on the M6 Southbound between junction 21 and 20. Crews used a hose reel jet to cool the brakes of the lorry which had overheated. One lane was closed whilst firefighters dealt with the incident.
