 Fire in a field in Mottram St Andrew Time of Incident: 06/08/2018 - 17:56 Location: Wilmslow Road, Mottram St Andrew. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Shortly before 6pm firefighters from Wilmslow were called to reports of a fire in a field on Wilmslow Road in Mottram St Andrew. The team attended the scene and used water backpacks and beaters to tackle the blaze. The team were in attendance for approximately one hour. The cause of the fire is believed to be accidental.
