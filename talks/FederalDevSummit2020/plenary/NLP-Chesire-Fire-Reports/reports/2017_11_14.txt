House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/11/2017 - 19:17 Location: Wheelman Road, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe,one from Middlewich and one from Winsford Firefighters were called to reports of a house on fire on Wheelman Road, Crewe.  One person was rescued from the first floor window using a seven metre ladder. Crews used four breathing apparatus, a thermal image camera, three hose reels and a positive pressure ventilation fan to extinguish the fire.  Crews remained on the scene for three hours, and investigations are ongoing.
