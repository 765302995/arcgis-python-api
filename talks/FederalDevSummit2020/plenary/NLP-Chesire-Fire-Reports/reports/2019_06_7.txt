 Shed fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 28/06/2019 - 13:00 Location: Hayes Crescent, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews attended to a shed alight at an address on Hayes Crescent, Frodsham.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, firefighters used two hose reels to tackle the fire.  Crews also checked the loft space.
