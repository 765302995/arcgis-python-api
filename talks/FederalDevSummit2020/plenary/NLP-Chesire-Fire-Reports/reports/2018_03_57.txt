 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/03/2018 - 03:25 Location: Broadbent Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 3.25am firefighters were called to a car fire on Broadbent Avenue in Warrington. The crew found a car on the road well alight and a firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. Firefighters then damped the area down and were at the scene for more than an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Police were informed of the incident and attended the scene.
