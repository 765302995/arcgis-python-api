Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/02/2016 - 08:12 Location: Delph Lane, Winwick, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington One casualty was trapped in a road traffic collision on Delph Lane. Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to rescue the casualty, a lady. Police and ambulance were also at the incident. Two cars were involved in the collision.
