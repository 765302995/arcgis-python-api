 Small fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/11/2019 - 19:11 Location: Liverpool Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 7.10pm firefighters were called to reports of a small fire on Liverpool Road, Warrington.  The fire was out on arrival, so firefighters used a hose reel jet to dampen down and prevent it from reigniting.  The crew were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
