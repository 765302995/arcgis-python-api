 Dryer fire at a domestic property in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/11/2018 - 16:46 Location: Foster Street, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Penketh and one from Widnes At around 4.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a dryer at a domestic property on Foster Street in Widnes. On arrival the crews discovered that the occupants had evacuated the property and that the fire was out. The fire had caused light smoke logging at the address. Firefighters isolated the electricity supply to the appliance, ventilated the property and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots to make sure that the affected area was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.
