Flat fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/02/2015 - 19:35 Location: Ulverston Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a report of a flat fire on Ulverston Avenue, Warrington.  When they arrived they confirmed that there was a blaze in a first floor flat. Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus rescued one man from the flat. He was then handed to the care of the ambulance service while crews tackled the fire.  Main water jets and hose reels were used to bring the fire under control.  It is believed that the fire may have started due to discarded smoking materials.   https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/smoking-fire-safety-tips-for-smokers
