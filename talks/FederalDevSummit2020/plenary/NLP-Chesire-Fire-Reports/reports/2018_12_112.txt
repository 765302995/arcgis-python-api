 Fridge fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/12/2018 - 13:34 Location: Cunliffe Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to reports of a fridge being on fire at an address in Cunliffe Close, Runcorn. The crew used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet in putting out the fire. They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke. A female casualty was suffering from smoke inhalation and given oxygen. An ambulance was requested as a precaution.
