 Water leak on a road in Middlewich Time of Incident: 06/09/2018 - 00:27 Location: Kinderton Street, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich At around 12.30am firefighters were called to reports of water leaking from a road that had travelled into the cellar of the Boars Head Hotel on Kinderton Street in Middlewich. The crew diverted the water away from the building before leaving the incident in the hands of United Utilities. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
