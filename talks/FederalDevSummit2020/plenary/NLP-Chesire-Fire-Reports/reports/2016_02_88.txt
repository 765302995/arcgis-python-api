Fire at social club in Whitby, Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 10/02/2016 - 08:30 Location: Dunkirk Lane, Whitby, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Deeside (North Wales Fire and Rescue Service) Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Deeside were called to a fire at a sports and social club on Dunkirk Lane in Whitby. The fire was in the kitchen of the building. Firefighters, three wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel and one main jet to put out the fire.  Firefighters were at the scene for just over one hour. An investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
