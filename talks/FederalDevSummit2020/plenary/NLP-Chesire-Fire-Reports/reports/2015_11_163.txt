Speed camera on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 02/11/2015 - 01:59 Location: Chester Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters extinguished a fire involving a speed camera that had been set alight deliberately.  A tyre had been used to start the blaze and the crew used a hose reel jet to put it out.  Police were requested to attend the scene and anyone with any information is urged to call them on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
