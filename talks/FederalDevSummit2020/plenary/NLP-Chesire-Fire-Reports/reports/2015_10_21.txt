One person trapped after collision on M6 Time of Incident: 26/10/2015 - 07:22 Location: M6 south, junction 19 to juntion 18 Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford, one from Holmes Chapel and one from Greater Manchester Firefighters cut a man free from a car involved in a collision on the M6.  The incident involved two saloon cars and lanes two and three of the motorway were closed while the firefighters stabilised the vehicles and removed the roof of the car to rescue the casualty.  The crews were at the scene for around 70 minutes.
