Derelict building fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 09/04/2016 - 14:20 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a report of a fire involving the old Greyhound Social Club in Ellesmere Port.  When they that they arrived crews found that the incident involved a large quantity of refuse outside the building. There was also a second fire within the derelict club.
