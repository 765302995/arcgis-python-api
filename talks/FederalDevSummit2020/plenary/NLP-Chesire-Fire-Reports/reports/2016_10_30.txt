Bin fire in Chester Time of Incident: 25/10/2016 - 20:09 Location: Graham Road, Chester. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Graham Road, Chester.  When they arrived they found a bin on fire. Crews extinguished the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone has information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
