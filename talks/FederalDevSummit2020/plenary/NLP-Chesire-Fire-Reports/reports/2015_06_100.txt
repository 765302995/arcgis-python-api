Cylinder leaking outside Widnes Asda Time of Incident: 12/06/2015 - 14:57 Location: Widnes Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes The Service received a call to say a cylinder was releasing gas outside Asda in Widnes.  The crew cordoned off the area around the 68 kilo CO2 cylinder and made the scene safe.  The cylinder supplier was also called to attend.
