Shed fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 23/10/2017 - 22:43 Location: Cledford Lane, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich, Sandbach and Holmes Chapel At 22:42 hours on 23 October one fire engine from Sandbach, Middlewich and Holmes Chapel attended a fire involving a large shed which spread into a neighbouring garage. Firefighters used one hose reel, four breathing apparatus and a positive pressure ventilation fan to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for approximately three hours.
