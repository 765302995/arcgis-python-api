 Three-vehicle collision on the M6 southbound between J19 and J20 Time of Incident: 17/04/2018 - 10:01 Location: M6 southbound between junctions 19 and 20 Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Birchwood At around 10am firefighters were called to reports of a three-vehicle collision on the M6 southbound between junctions 19 and 20.  On arrival crews discovered that a man was trapped in a vehicle.  The Highways Agency closed off lanes one and two of the motorway plus the hard shoulder while the incident was being dealt with.  Fire crews removed the trapped man from the vehicle using a longboard and handed him over to the care of paramedics.  He was then taken to hospital via an ambulance and the road was reopened.  Police were also in attendance.  Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
