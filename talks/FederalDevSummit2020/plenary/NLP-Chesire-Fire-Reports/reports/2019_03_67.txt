 Car fire in Hankelow Time of Incident: 16/03/2019 - 12:21 Location: Hall Lane, Hankelow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Audlem Shortly after 12.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a car being on fire in Hankleow. On arrival at Hankelow Hall on Hall Lane the crew found a saloon car well alight. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet.
