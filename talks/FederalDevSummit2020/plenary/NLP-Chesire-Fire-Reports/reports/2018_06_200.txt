 Grass fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 11/06/2018 - 18:43 Location: Ludlow Close, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters from Winsford used a water back pack to extinguish a small grass fire in the grounds of Over Hall Community Primary School.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
