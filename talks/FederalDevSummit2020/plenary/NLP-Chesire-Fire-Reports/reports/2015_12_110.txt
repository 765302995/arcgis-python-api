Fire on a boat in Bollington Time of Incident: 04/12/2015 - 08:23 Location: Grimshaw Lane, Bollington Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on a boat in the marina in Bollington. The small fire involved a diesel heating unit on a 60 foot barge, which was out on arrival. Crews used a thermal imaging camera to ensure it was fully out and there were no further hotspots. They isolated the diesel supply to the heating unit.
