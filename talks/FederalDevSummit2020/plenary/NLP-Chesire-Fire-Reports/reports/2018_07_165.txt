 Grass fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 21:05 Location: Queensbury Way, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to a wooded area off Queensbury Way in Widnes following reports of a fire. Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the fire which covered approximately four square metres of land. The fire is thought to have been started deliberately and police have been informed.
