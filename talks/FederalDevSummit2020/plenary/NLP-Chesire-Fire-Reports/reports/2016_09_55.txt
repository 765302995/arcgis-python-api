Bungalow roof fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/09/2016 - 16:13 Location: Wren Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Frodsham Firefighters were called to reports of a garage fire. On arrival, they discovered the fire had spread to the space roof of a bungalow so the crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to put out the fire. Finally, crews dampened down before checking the roof with a thermal imagine camera to ensure no further spots of heat remained.
