 Car fire on M53 Time of Incident: 27/07/2019 - 08:08 Location: M53, northbound between junction 8 and junction 7 Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended to a car on fire on the M53 northbound between junction 8 and junction 7.  Crews, wearing one breathing apparatus, extinguished the fire using a hose reel.  There was no one trapped inside the vehicle.  Highways closed the northbound carriageway as a precaution.
