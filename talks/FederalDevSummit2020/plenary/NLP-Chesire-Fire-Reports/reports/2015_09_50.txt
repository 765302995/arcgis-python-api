Road traffic collision in Calveley Time of Incident: 19/09/2015 - 17:26 Location: Nantwich Road, Calveley Attendance: Two fire engines from Nantwich and Crewe Two fire engines from Nantwich and Crewe were sent to a road traffic collision, where a person was reported to be trapped, on Nantwich Road in Calveley. On arrival at the incident, the fire crews found the incident involved the collision of a car with a lamppost. The car had overturned. One casualty had already been released from the vehicle before the fire crews arrived. The casualty was helped by paramedics.
