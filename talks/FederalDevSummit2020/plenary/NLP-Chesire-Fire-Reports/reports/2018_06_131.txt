 Small fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 20/06/2018 - 15:01 Location: Crabmill Lane, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to Crabmill Lane, Sandbach, after a report of a small grass fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The fire was a controlled burning which had been left unattended.
