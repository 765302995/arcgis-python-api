Bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 31/10/2017 - 18:30 Location: Queensbury Way, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to reports of a bin fire on a retail park off Queensbury Way, Widnes. On arrival they found that the fire was well alight and used one hose reel was used to extinguish the fire. An investigation has been launched into the cause of the fire. If anyone has any information about how this fire may have started please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
