 Female trapped following a collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 13/01/2019 - 09:38 Location: M6 northbound, between junctions 19 and 20 Attendance:  Four fire engines attended - one from Lymm, one from Tarporley, one from Birchwood and one from Altrincham At around 9.40am firefighters were called to reports of a collision on the M6 northbound between junctions 19 and 20. On arrival crews found that a female was trapped in a vehicle. Firefighters and the police cordoned off lanes one and two and the hard shoulder as the emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters also helped paramedics to get the casualty out of the vehicle after her condition had been assessed. The casualty was then left in the care of the North West Ambulance Service. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
