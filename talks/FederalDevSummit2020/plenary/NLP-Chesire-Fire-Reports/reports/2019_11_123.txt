 Two-vehicle collision in Eccleston, near Chester Time of Incident: 07/11/2019 - 12:40 Location: Wrexham Road, Eccleston, near Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Chester and one from Powey Lane At 12.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a two-vehicle collision on Wrexham Road in Eccleston, near Chester.  On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved a light goods vehicle and a saloon car.  The road was closed in both directions as emergency services worked at the scene.  Firefighters made the car safe and it's two occupants were assessed by paramedics.  Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
