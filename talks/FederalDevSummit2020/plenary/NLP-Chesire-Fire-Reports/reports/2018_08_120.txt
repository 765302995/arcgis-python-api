 Bin fires in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/08/2018 - 20:59 Location: Roehampton Drive, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews were called to two separate bin fires in Hallwood Park, off Roehampton Drive, Runcorn. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish both fires which were started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police.
