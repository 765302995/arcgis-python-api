 House fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 15/02/2019 - 22:52 Location: Kemberton Drive, Widnes Attendance: Three fire engines from Widnes, Warrington and Penketh Firefighters were called to reports of a house fire in Kemberton Drive, Widnes. On arrival crews found the fire to be quite severe, and they used four breathing apparatus and two hose reels to tackle it. Once the fire was out, a positive pressure ventilation fan was used to clear the property of smoke, and crews carried out an inspection of the property to check for further hotspots. Crews continued to monitor the house throughout the night checking for hotspots to prevent reignition.  All occupants were accounted for and a fire investigation is being carried out as it is a suspected arson attack.
