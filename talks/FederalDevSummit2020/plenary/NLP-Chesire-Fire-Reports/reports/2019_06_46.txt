 Small fire in the open in Congleton Time of Incident: 21/06/2019 - 21:12 Location: High Street, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Shortly before 9.15am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in High Street, Congleton. The crew extinguished the small fire with a hose reel jet. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
