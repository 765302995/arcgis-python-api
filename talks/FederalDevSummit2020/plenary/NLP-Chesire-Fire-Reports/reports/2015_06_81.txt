Tree fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 16/06/2015 - 20:53 Location: Overdale Lane, Oakmere in Northwich. Attendance: A fire engine from Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Overdale Lane in Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that it involved a pine tree. They used hose reel jets to put the fire out.
