 Flat fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/03/2019 - 17:46 Location: Albert Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh, one fire engine from Warrington and one from Widnes Firefighters were called to a fire in a top floor flat on Albert Road, Widnes. Crews, six wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a covering jet to extinguish the fire. Everyone in the flats evacuated safely. One person was treated by paramedics for smoke inhalation. Crews then used a large fan to clear smoke from two floors of the property before checking the site with a thermal imaging camera. An investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
