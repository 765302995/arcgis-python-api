Grass fire in Delamere Forest Time of Incident: 08/08/2015 - 14:07 Location: Delamere Forest, Station Road, Northwich. Attendance: Fire engines from Tarporley and Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Delamere Forest in Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that there was an area of about 40msq that was on fire. Hose reel jets were used to put the fire out.
