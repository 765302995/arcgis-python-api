Extractor unit on fire at Appleton business Time of Incident: 11/04/2016 - 14:57 Location: Swineyard Lane, Appleton, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath An extractor unit was involved in a fire which could have developed further had there not been a sprinkler system fitted which contained the fire. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which caused smoke logging to a building measuring 200 metres by 80 metres. An investigation into the cause of the fire will be carried out.  Business safety and sprinkler systems
