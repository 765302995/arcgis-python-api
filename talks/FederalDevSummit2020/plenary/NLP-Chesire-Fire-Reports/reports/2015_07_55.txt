Four cows stuck in a ditch in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/07/2015 - 16:10 Location: Stoke Pickton Village, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port, and the animal rescue unit from Bollington attended Firefighters were called to four cows stuck in a ditch in Ellesmere Port. The Animal Rescue Unit attended and the crews dug the bankside away to help with the release of the cows.  They built a ramp in order to guide the animals out of the water.
