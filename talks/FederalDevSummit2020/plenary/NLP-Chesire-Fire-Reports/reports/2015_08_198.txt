Fire at a Warrington theme park Time of Incident: 01/08/2015 - 17:46 Location: Gullivers World Theme Park, Shackleton Close in Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Fire crews were called to a report of a fire in a building at Gullivers World Theme Park.  When they arrived they found that the fire involved a television on the first floor of a two storey building. Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish the fire.
