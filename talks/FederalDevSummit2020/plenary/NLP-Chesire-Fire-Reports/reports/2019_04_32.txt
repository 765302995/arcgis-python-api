 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/04/2019 - 15:55 Location: Farcroft Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Firefighters were called to a kitchen fire on Farcroft Close, Warrington. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.
