 Fire at derelict building, Widnes Time of Incident: 05/07/2019 - 17:20 Location: Coronation Drive, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to reports of a fire at an abandoned care home. They arrived to find a small fire near the exterior of the building, and used a hand appliance to put out the flames.  They remained for some time checking for spread within the fascia of the building, including cutting into the roof space. They didn't find anything further, but called the police due to the suspected deliberate ignition.  Anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 447596.
