 Chimney fire in Siddington Time of Incident: 01/11/2018 - 18:55 Location: Congleton Road, Siddington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters extinguished a chimney fire at a three story detached property on Congleton Road in Siddington.  Crews tackled the fire from the grate with water and made sure the area was safe.  Follow our tips on how to avoid a chimney fire https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/chimney-fire-safety
