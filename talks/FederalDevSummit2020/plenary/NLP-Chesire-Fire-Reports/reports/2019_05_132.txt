 Hedges fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 13/05/2019 - 19:03 Location: Ivy Road in Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Shortly after 7pm firefighters were called to reports of hedges being on fire on Ivy Road in Macclesfield. On arrival the crew found that the fire involved around three metres of conifers. Firefighters extinguished the fire in the open with a hose reel jet. They then damped the area affected by the fire down to make it safe and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 400087, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
