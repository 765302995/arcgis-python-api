Fire involving Iceland freezer in Northwich Time of Incident: 10/04/2015 - 01:08 Location: Iceland, Witton Street, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich An electrical fault on a freezer caused a fire in Iceland in Northwich.  Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus to enter the premises due to the ground floor being full of smoke. Hose reel and covering jets were used to extinguish the fire before the crews used a large fan to clear the smoke.
