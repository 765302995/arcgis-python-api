Substance discarded on wheelie bins in Crewe Time of Incident: 28/10/2015 - 23:59 Location: Badger Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Nantwich Firefighters from Crewe and Nantwich were mobilised to reports of an unknown substance discarded in the open.  Firefighters investigated using gas tight suits, protective gloves, one hose reel jet and two breathing apparatus and handed over a substance to the police that had been discarded on wheelie bins. The substance was thought to be a paint thinner product.  Firefighters were on scene for approximately five hours.  Police are now investigating. Anyone with information can contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 995 of 28 October 2015. Alternatively information can be given anonymously by contacting Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
