Skip fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/01/2016 - 18:06 Location: Warrington Road, Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and stack drag to tackle a skip fire at a business off Warrington Road in Runcorn. They then turned over the rubbish in the skip and damped it all down. On-site personnel used a digger to empty the skip.
