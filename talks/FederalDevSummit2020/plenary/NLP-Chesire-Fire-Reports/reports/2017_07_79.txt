Bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 13/07/2017 - 21:18 Location: Meadow Row, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a wheelie bin which had been set on fire on Meadow Row, Runcorn. The bin was well alight on arrival and crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The fire was started deliberately. If anyone has information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
