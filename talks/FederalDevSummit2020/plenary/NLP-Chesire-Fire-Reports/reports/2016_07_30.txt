Smoke issuing from Winnington building Time of Incident: 24/07/2016 - 11:06 Location: Winnington Avenue, Winnington, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Knutsford Smoke was seen issuing from a building on Winnington Avenue. Firefighters gained access to using and a high pressure hose reel was used as a covering jet. The electricity supply was isolated as smoke cleared from the building.
