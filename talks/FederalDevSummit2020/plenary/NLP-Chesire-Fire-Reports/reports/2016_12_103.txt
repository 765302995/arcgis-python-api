Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/12/2016 - 19:18 Location: Viscount Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A wheelie bin fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and are urging anyone with any information about this, or similar, incidents to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 714 of 11 December.
