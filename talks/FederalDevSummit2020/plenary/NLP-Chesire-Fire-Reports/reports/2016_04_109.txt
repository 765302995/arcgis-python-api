Deliberate fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 10/04/2016 - 19:57 Location: Wades Lane, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford A small fire had been set deliberately under a tree in Wades Lane. Firefighters used a water backpack to put it out.  Anyone with any information about this, or similar fires, please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
