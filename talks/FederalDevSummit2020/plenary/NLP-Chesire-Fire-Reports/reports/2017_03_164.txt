Road traffic collision in Neston Time of Incident: 01/03/2017 - 21:48 Location: Hooton Road, Willaston, Neston Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and two from Ellesmere Port An overturned vehicle left four casualties who were given first aid by firefighters until the ambulance arrived. The crew made the scene safe and the casualties were handed into the care of ambulance personnel.
