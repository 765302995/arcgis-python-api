 Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 02/08/2018 - 19:02 Location: Park Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Grass fire in RuncornFire crews were called to a grass fire on Park Road, Runcorn. Crews found a fire measuring approximately 50m squared and used beaters and a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  Police also attended the incident.
