 Child stuck up a tree in Widnes Time of Incident: 19/06/2019 - 17:13 Location: Hough Green Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to Hough Green Road, Widnes, after a report of a child stuck up a tree. Fire crews used a short extension ladder to rescue the child who was stuck approximately 8ft up a tree. He was unharmed.
