 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/07/2019 - 22:06 Location: Newhaven Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to a wheelie bin fire on Newhaven Road, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire then please contact Cheshire Police.
