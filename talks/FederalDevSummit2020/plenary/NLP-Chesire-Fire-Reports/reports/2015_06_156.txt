Car well alight in Sandbach Time of Incident: 05/06/2015 - 23:32 Location: Third Avenue, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A car was well alight, so firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.  Police attended this incident.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about the incident is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
