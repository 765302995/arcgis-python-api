 Building fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 22/06/2018 - 22:13 Location: Arley Drive, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - One from Widnes and one from Penketh. Changing rooms on fireFirefighters from Widnes and Penketh were called to reports of a building fire on Arley Drive, Widnes. On arrival crews found that the fire was in the changing rooms next to the playing fields.  The team used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.  The cause of the fire is currently under investigation and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
