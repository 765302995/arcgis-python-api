Car fire on A51 Tarporley bypass Time of Incident: 04/05/2016 - 13:44 Location: A51 Tarporley bypass Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley Firefighters tackled a car fire on the A51 Tarporley bypass. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze. Traffic control was managed by the police.  Drivers are advised to avoid the area for the time being.
