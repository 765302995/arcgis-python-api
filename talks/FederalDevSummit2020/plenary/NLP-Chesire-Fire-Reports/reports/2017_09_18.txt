Road traffic collision in Winwick Time of Incident: 25/09/2017 - 15:27 Location: Newton Road, Winwick, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh and one from Warrington A road traffic collision involving a car and a heavy goods vehicle left a casualty trapped in the car. Newton Road was closed while firefighters isolated the car battery and prepared the vehicle ready to release the man as soon as the ambulance arrived. The car door was removed using specialist cutting equipment to enable him to be carefully removed from the car and handed into the care of paramedics.
