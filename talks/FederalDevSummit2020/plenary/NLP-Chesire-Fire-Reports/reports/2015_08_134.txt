Fencing and grass on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/08/2015 - 17:49 Location: Weir Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Firefighters had to tackle a fencing and grass fire using specialist wildfire equipment due to the location of the incident. An extended hose reel jet was set up to get water from an open water source. They were at the incident for around 40 minutes.
