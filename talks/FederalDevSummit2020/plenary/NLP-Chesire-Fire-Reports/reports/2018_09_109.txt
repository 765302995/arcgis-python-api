 Refuse fire at flats in Crewe Time of Incident: 07/09/2018 - 20:56 Location: Grosvenor Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At around 9pm firefighters were called to a report of a bin chute being on fire at flats on Grosvenor Street in Crewe. On arrival crews discovered a small fire involving rubbish. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
