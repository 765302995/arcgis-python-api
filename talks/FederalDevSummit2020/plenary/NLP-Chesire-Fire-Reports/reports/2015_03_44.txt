Bedroom fire in Chester Time of Incident: 21/03/2015 - 19:28 Location: Prenton Place, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester and one form Ellesmere Port Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels to extinguish a fire in the bedroom of a mid terraced house in Chester. A large fan was used to clear the smoke and an investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
