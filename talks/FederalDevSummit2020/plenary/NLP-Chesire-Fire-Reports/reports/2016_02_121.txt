House fire in Prestbury Time of Incident: 31/01/2016 - 13:54 Location: Park House Lane, Prestbury Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Fire and rescue crews were called to a report of a house fire in Park House Lane. The fire was out on arrival but one casualty needed medical treatment. Firefighters completed a home fire safety assessment before leaving and were at the property for around 20 minutes.
