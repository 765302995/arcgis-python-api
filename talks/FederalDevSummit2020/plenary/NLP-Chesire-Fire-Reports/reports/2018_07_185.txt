 Straw fire at a farm in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/07/2018 - 14:31 Location: Tan House Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine is in attendance from Penketh Firefighters fought a fire in the open at a farm on Tan House Lane in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered a large amount of straw on fire. They used two hose reel jets and a mechanical digger to fight the fire. Update at 3.15pm  Slow progress is being made with the fire. Crews have set up a water shuttle from a nearby hydrant to get enough water to the area. They are expected to be detained for another hour.  Update at 4.30pm  The fire has been extinguished and crews have left the scene.
