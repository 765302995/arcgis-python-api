 Butane cylinder on fire in Delamere Park Time of Incident: 14/06/2019 - 12:06 Location: The Burrows, Delamere Park, near Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly after midday firefighters were called to reports of a butane cylinder on fire outside an address on Delamere Park.  The fire was out on arrival. However, the crew used water to cool the cylinder and a thermal imaging camera to ensure it had returned to a safe temperature.  Firefighters were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
