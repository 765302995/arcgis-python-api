Car and trailer fire on M6 north junction 21 Time of Incident: 07/08/2016 - 13:35 Location: M6 northbound junction 21 Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood, one from Knutsford and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service A car and trailer were involved in a fire on the hard shoulder of the M6 just after J21 northbound. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.
