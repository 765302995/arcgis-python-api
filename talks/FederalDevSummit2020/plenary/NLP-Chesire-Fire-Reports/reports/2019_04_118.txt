 Small fire in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 17/04/2019 - 17:45 Location: Heath Road, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At 5.45pm firefighters were dispatched to reports of a fire in the grounds of Runcorn Town Hall.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a small bonfire which had been started deliberately.  Firefighters used on hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for around 30 minutes.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries  or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
