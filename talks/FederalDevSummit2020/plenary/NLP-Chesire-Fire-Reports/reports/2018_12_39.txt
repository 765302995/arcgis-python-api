 Smoking fuse box at a flat in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/12/2018 - 00:20 Location: Rectory Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Lymm and one from Warrington At around 12.20am firefighters were called to a report of an electrical fire at a flat on Rectory Lane in Warrington. On arrival crews discovered a small amount of smoke coming from an internal fuse box. Firefighters isolated the electricity supply to the flat and cut away part of the electrical box to expose cables. They then applied a small amount of water before monitoring the fuse box's temperature with a thermal imaging camera. Firefighters arranged for an electrical engineer to visit the property and only left the premises once they were sure that the fuse box was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
