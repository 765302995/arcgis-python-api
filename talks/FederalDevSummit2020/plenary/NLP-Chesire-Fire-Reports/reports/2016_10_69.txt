Road traffic collision in Reaseheath Time of Incident: 19/10/2016 - 07:32 Location: Wettenhall Road, Reaseheath Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Wettenhall Road was closed as firefighters extricated a casualty from his car after a road traffic collision. One car was involved in the incident. The casualty, a man, was released from the car on a long board and taken to hospital by ambulance.
