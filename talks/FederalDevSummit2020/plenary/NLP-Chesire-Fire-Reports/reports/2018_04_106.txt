 Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 11/04/2018 - 00:48 Location: Queens Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At 12.47am firefighters were called to a car fire on Queens Road in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew found the car well alight and a firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. The fire is being treated as suspicious and police were in attendance. Anyone with information about the fire should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
