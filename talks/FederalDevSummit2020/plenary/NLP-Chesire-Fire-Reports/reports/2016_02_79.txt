Kitchen fire in the flat above the Puss in Boots pub in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 12/02/2016 - 22:24 Location: Puss in Boots Public House, Buxton Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Two fire crews from Macclesfield were sent to flat above a pub, where there was a fire inside the kitchen of the flat.  Fire crews used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel covering jet and a large fan to tackle a fire in kitchen.   Kitchen fire safety tips
