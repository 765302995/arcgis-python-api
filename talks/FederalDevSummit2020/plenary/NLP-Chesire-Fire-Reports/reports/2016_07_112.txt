Rubbish and pallets on fire near Chester Time of Incident: 11/07/2016 - 18:48 Location: A55 Post House roundabout, Marlston-cum-Lache, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester It is believed a fire involving rubbish and pallets was started deliberately in Marlston-cum-Lache. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
