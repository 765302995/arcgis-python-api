Fire in property in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/05/2017 - 00:00 Location: Boston Boulevard, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines, one from Warrington and one from Penketh A fire in a three storey property required firefighters to attend and use one hose reel to extinguish it. One person suffered from slight smoke inhalation and a large fan was used to remove smoke from the property.
