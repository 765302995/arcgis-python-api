Road traffic collision on M6 Time of Incident: 23/08/2017 - 13:10 Location: M6 northbound next to the entrance of Knutsford services between junction 18 and 19 Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel, one from Middlewich and one from Lymm Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision on the M6 northbound next to the entrance of Knutsford services between junction 18 and 19.  The collision was between three LGVs and a horse box. Crews removed one casualty with leg injuries from a LGV and assisted paramedics.  An Air Ambulance, Police and the Highways Agency all in attended the incident.
