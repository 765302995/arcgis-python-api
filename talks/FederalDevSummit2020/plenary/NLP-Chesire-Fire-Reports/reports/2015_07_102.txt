Road traffic collision on the M6 northbound Time of Incident: 16/07/2015 - 23:24 Location: M6 junctions 17 to 18, northbound Attendance: Fire engines from Sandbach, Winsford and Holmes Chapel. A serious road traffic collision involving three heavy good vehicles and one light good vehicle required firefighters to attend.&nbsp; Five casualties were involved in this incident and one was released from a vehicle by crews. The crews helped contain a spillage of milk from one of the vehicles.  Updated at 12 noon on 17th July 2015  Sadly one person died at this incident.
