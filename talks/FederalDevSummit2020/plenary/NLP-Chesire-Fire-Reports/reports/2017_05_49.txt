Felled trees on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/05/2017 - 12:38 Location: Manchester Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a forest off Manchester Road, Warrington, where a large number of felled trees had been set alight in a forest. Crews were unsure whether it was a controlled burning which had been left unattended or arson. They have put out the fire.
