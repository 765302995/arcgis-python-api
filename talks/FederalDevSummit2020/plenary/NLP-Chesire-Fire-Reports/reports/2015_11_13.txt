People carrier on fire on M62 Time of Incident: 27/11/2015 - 18:22 Location: M62 East Junction 7 to 8 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Birchwood One firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish a people carrier well alight on the motorway.  Police closed all three lanes while the incident was dealt with due to the smoke.  The fire was caused by an electrical fault.
