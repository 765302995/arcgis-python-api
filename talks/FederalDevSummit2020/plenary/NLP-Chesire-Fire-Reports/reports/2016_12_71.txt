Grill pan fire in Chester Time of Incident: 18/12/2016 - 17:13 Location: Treborth Road, Chester Attendance: Fire engines from Chester and Ellesmere Port along with the hydraulic platform and supporting fire engine from Chester A grill pan was the cause of a small fire in a Treborth Road flat.  Firefighters, two in breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.   Please follow our kitchen safety advice.
