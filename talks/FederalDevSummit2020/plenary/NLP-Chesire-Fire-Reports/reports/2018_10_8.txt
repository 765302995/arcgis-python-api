 Overheated articulated lorry brakes on the M62 Time of Incident: 29/10/2018 - 16:41 Location: M62 westbound, between junctions 12 and 11 Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Birchwood and one from Salford At around 4.20pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving an articulated lorry between junctions 12 and 11 of the M62 westbound. On arrival crews discovered that the breaks on the lorry's trailer had overheated. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and a hose reel jet to cool the brakes. They were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half. Highways England also attended.
