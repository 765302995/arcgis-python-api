Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/01/2017 - 19:38 Location: Grosvenor Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh A car fire on Grosvenor Close is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control. They then damped the area down. Police officers also attended the scene.   Anyone with information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
