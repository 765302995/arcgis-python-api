Kitchen fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 03/06/2016 - 03:07 Location: Chester Road, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Winsford A grill pan was the cause of a fire in the kitchen of a Middlewich flat. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, took a hose reel jet to put out the fire and remove the grill pan from the property. A high pressure fan was then used to clear the smoke.  Always follow our safety advice to prevent kitchen fires.
