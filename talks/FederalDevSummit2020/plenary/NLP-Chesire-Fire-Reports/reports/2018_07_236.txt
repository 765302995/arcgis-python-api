 Car fire in Willaston Time of Incident: 04/07/2018 - 22:34 Location: Green Lane, Willaston. Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters from Nantwich extinguished a car which was well alight on Green Lane in Willaston. After extinguishing the fire the team used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. Crews were at the scene for approximately 30 minutes.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
