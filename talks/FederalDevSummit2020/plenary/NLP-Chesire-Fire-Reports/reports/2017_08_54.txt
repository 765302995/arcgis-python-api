Play area fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 21/08/2017 - 22:05 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a fire in a children's play area on Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Please call Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 921 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 if you have any information about the incident.
