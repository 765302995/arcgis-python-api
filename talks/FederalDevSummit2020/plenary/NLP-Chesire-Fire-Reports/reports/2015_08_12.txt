Motorcyclist rescued from barbed wire fence in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 27/08/2015 - 21:15 Location: Buxton New Road, Macclesfield Forest, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A motorcyclist who was trapped in barbed wire fencing was release by firefighters who used bolt croppers. The road was closed by Cheshire Police while the incident was dealt with.
