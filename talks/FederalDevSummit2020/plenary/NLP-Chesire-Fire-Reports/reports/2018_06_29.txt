 Grass fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 27/06/2018 - 13:46 Location: Canterbury Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 1.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a grass fire on Canterbury Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
