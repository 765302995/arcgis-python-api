Van fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 03/12/2017 - 15:11 Location: Manchester Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters attended a van fire in Manchester Road, Macclesfield. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. They then checked for hotspots with a thermal imaging camera.
