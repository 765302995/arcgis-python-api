Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/06/2017 - 18:20 Location: Jackson Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington, and one from Stockton Heath. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus and one hose reel jet was used to extinguish a kitchen fire on Jackson Avenue in Fearnhead, Warrington.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke. An ambulance was requested to attend.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check for hotspots of fire.    Kitchen fire safety tips
