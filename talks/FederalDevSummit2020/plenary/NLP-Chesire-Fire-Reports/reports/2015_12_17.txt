Serious collision on the M62 Time of Incident: 27/12/2015 - 04:09 Location: Junctions 8 to 9 on the M62 Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington, Widnes and Winsford. Firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving a car and a lorry on the M62.  When the crews arrived they found that the car had gone under the lorry. Crews worked alongside the police and external contractors to remove the lorry and get to the car.  Firefighters remained at the scene for more than four hours.
