 Large skip fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/02/2018 - 12:29 Location: Kingsland Grange, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a large skip fire in Kingsland Grange in Warrington. Crews used one hose reel to damp down the waste and pulled it apart to prevent further ignition. They used a thermal imaging camera to ensure there were no hotspots in the skip.
