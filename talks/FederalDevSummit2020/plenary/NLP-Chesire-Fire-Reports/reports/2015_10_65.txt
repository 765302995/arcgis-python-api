Cooker fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 18/10/2015 - 21:32 Location: Badger Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe One person was assisted from a property by firefighters after a fire involving an oven in Crewe.  The fire was out on arrival of the Service and a large fan was used to clear the smoke. Smoke alarms were fitted at the property.
