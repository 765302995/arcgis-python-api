Loft on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 13/08/2017 - 07:12 Location: Navigation Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich A fire in the loft of a property was extinguished with a hose reel by firefighters. The area was cut away to allow crews to access the fire which was in an end terraced property. Firefighters checked the next door property before leaving.
