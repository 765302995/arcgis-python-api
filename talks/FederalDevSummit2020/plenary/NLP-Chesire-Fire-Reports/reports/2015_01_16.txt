Cooker fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 26/01/2015 - 21:05 Location: Danefield Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Unattended cooking caused a small fire in a cooker in Northwich.  The fire was out when the firefighters arrived but there was slight smoke logging to the house so the crew used a high pressure fan to clear it.  The occupants had evacuated the house after calling the Service.  Please don't leave cooking unattended - follow our safety advice: Kitchen fire safety
