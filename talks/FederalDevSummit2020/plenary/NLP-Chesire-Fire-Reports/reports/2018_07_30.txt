 Grass fire in Lostock Gralam Time of Incident: 27/07/2018 - 14:20 Location: Station Road, Lostock Gralam Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and one fire engine attended from Northwich. At 14:20 firefighters from Northwich and Winsford were called to reports of a fire near Locstock Gralam train station. Crews attended the scene and found that a railway embankment, approximately 40 metres by 100 metres, was well alight. The team are currently using two main jets, a hose reel jet and beaters to tackle the fire.  Update at 17:30 - Crews have dampened down the area and made sure the fire is fully extinguished and is safe.
