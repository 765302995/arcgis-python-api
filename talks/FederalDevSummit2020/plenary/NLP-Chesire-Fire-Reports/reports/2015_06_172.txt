Chimney fire in Haughton Time of Incident: 03/06/2015 - 09:02 Location: Ferret Oak Lane, Haughton Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley A chimney fire at a bungalow in Haughton was attended by firefighters.  The fire was put out using a water backpack extinguisher and chimney rods.  The loft space was then checked with a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no hotspots of fire.
