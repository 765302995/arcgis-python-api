 Bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 30/05/2019 - 15:53 Location: Newnham Drive, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a bin fire in Newnham Drive, Ellesmere Port, which had spread to a fence. Crews used a hose reel to put out the fire.
