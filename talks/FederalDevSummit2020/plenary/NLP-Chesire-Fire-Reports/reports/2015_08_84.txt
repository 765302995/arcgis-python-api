Hay bale on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 16/08/2015 - 08:22 Location: Bottoms Lane, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters damped down a hay bale that was smouldering from fire in Chester.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
