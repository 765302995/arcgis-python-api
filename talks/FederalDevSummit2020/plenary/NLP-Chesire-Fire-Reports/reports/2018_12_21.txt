 Small vehicle fire in Chester Time of Incident: 25/12/2018 - 11:58 Location: Mannings Lane South, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly before midday firefighters from Chester attended reports of a car fire on Mannings Lane South. On arrival the team discovered a small fire in the engine compartment of a car which had been caused by an electrical fault. The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, they then used a hose reel jet to check for any hotspots. Crews were detained at the scene for approximately 25 minutes.
