 Pan left unattended in Winsford Time of Incident: 23/11/2018 - 23:38 Location: Hambleton Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to reports of a pan left unattended on a cooking hob at an address on Hambleton Way in Winsford.  Although it hadn't caused a fire there was a lot of smoke in the property which had to be cleared and made safe.  Follow our tips on how to ensure you cook safely https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/home/cooking-safety-tips
