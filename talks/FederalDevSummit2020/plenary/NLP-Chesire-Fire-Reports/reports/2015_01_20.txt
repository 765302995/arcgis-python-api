Building on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/01/2015 - 03:19 Location: Terrace Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a derelict building on fire in Widnes and used two hose reels to extinguish the 4 metre by 5 metre building. Ceiling tiles were also removed for ventilation and a thermal imaging camera then used to check the area before the crews left.
