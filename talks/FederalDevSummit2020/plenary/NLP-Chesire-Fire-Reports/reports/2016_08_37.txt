Sofa fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 24/08/2016 - 01:16 Location: Sycamore Road, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn Firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving rubbish at the back of some flats. On arrival, crews found a fire involving two sofas and some rubbish at the back of the flats. They used one hose reel jet to put out the fire before checking the building with a thermal imaging camera to ensure there were no remaining spots of heat.
