 Small rubbish fire in Willaston Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 18:44 Location: Tricketts Lane, Willaston. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called to land on Tricketts Lane in Willaston following reports of a fire. On arrival crews discovered a small amount of rubbish on fire. They used a hose reel to extinguish the fire and then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
