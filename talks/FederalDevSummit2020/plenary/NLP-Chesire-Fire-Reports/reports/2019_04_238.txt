 Car fire in Prestbury Time of Incident: 03/04/2019 - 20:50 Location: Butley Lanes, Prestbury Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington Shortly before 9pm firefighters from Bollington were called to reports of a car fire on Butley Lanes, Prestbury.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a white Mercedes in a field at the side of the road which was well alight.  One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus, used one main jet to extinguish the fire. They also used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately one hour.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
