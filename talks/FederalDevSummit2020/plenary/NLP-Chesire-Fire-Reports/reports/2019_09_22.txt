 Sheep in Daresbury canal Time of Incident: 25/09/2019 - 19:27 Location: Red Brow Lane, Daresbury Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Lymm and one from Warrington Sheep in Daresbury canalFire crews attended to reports of a sheep in the canal on Red Brow Lane.  After a plan was made two animal rescue technicians entered the water to rescue the sheep.  The sheep was then carried back to the field.
