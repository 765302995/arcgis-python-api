House fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 24/12/2015 - 17:46 Location: Green Lane, Northwich. Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Green Lane, Northwich.  When they arrived they found that there had been a fire involving a cooker hood but this had gone out. Smoke alarms had not worked because they had no batteries in them.  Crews carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving.
