 Lorry fire on M6 Time of Incident: 16/03/2018 - 11:34 Location: M6 northbound between junctions 18 and 19 Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich, one from Sandbach and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a lorry fire on the M6 northbound between junctions 18 and 19. Crews used a dry powder extinguisher, a hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to put out the fire which was in the brakes of the lorry.
