 Silo in Knutsford Time of Incident: 15/09/2018 - 13:48 Location: A50, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood, Sale and Macclesfield One fire engine from Sale, Birchwood and Macclesfield and an aerial appliance from Macclesfield responded to a silo fire at a farm off the A50 at Mere, Knutsford. The fire involved a grain dryer and eight tonnes of grain in the silo.  Crews assisted the farmer in emptying the contents of the silo and used two hose reels to extinguish the fire.
