 Metal ship container fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 10/12/2018 - 18:27 Location: New Bridge Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Crews attended to a metal ship container well alight on an open field near to New Bridge Road in Ellesmere Port.  Crews cut away the partition wall then wearing one breathing apparatus crews used two hose reels to extinguish the fire.  The area was then fully dampened down and made safe.
