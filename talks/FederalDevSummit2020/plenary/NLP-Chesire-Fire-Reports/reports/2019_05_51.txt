 Wooden hut fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 24/05/2019 - 08:05 Location: Castlefields Avenue South, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews attended to a wooden hut well alight in woods near to Castlefields Avenue South in Runcorn.  Firefighters set up a low pressure hose to put out the fire.  The area was then dampened down, while a thermal imaging camera was used to check for hot spots.
