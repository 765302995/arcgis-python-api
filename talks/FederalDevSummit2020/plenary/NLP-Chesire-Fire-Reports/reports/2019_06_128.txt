 Skip fire at industrial estate in Widnes Time of Incident: 04/06/2019 - 10:24 Location: St Michaels Industrial Estate, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly before 10.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at St Michaels Industrial Estate in Widnes. On arrival the crew found a fire involving a skip and a large quantity of debris. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. On-site personnel then opened up surrounding containers so that firefighters could check if the fire had spread to them. They checked for hot spots and were satisfied that the fire had not spread to any of the containers. Firefighters made sure that the area affected by the fire was safe and were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
