 House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 19/12/2018 - 15:07 Location: Frank Webb Avenue, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to a smoke-logged house in Frank Webb Avenue in Crewe. Crews used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Two dogs were rescued from the address. Firefighters used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
