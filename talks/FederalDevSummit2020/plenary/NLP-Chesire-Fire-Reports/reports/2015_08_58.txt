Smoke caused by chip pan in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/08/2015 - 02:02 Location: Quebec Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A man was treated by paramedics for smoke inhalation caused by a chip pan left on the hob in Warrington.  Firefighters ventilated the property which was full of smoke when they arrived. Chip pan safety advice
