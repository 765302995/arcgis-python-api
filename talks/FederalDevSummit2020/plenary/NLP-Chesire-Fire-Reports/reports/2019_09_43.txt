 Man trapped following collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 22/09/2019 - 15:07 Location: Ditchfield Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters from Runcorn released a man who was physically trapped in his car following a collision on Ditchfield Road, Widnes.  The man is not believed to have sustained any injuries.   Firefighters were in attendance at the scene for around 15 minutes.
