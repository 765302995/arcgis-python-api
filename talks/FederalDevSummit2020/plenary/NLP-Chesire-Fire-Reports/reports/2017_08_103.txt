Road traffic collision in Over Alderley Time of Incident: 12/08/2017 - 17:02 Location: Macclesfield Road, Over Alderley Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Wilmslow A road traffic collision with one woman trapped and needing the assistance of firefighters to get out was attended by a crews from Macclesfield and Wilmslow. The firefighters used cutting equipment to remove the roof of the car and get the casualty out. Air Ambulance and paramedics attended the look after the casualty.
