Flat fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/05/2017 - 18:29 Location: Arpley Street, Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Warrington and Stockton Heath Firefighters responded to a report of a flat fire on Arpley Street, Warrington.  When they arrived crews used a hose reel jet and a large fan to deal with the fire. There was nobody at home at the time of the incident.
