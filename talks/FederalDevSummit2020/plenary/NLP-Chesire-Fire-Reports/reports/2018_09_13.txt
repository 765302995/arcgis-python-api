 Motorbike fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/09/2018 - 21:14 Location: Wolverton Drive, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a motorbike fire on Wolverton Drive, Runcorn. Crews used a fire extinguisher and buckets of water to extinguish the fire. The motorbike was severely damaged.
