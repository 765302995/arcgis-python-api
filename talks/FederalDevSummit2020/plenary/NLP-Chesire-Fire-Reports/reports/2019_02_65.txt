 Small bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/02/2019 - 19:38 Location: Palmyra Square North, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a small bin fire on Palmyra Square North, Warrington. They used a fire extinguisher to put out the fire which had been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident please contact Cheshire Police.
