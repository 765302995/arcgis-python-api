Dangerous structure in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/04/2016 - 17:27 Location: Winifred Street, Fairfield and Howley, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Birchwood Firefighters were called to assist with a gable end of a property which had fallen away onto a pathway. The area was cordoned off and crews awaited the arrival of a structural engineer.
