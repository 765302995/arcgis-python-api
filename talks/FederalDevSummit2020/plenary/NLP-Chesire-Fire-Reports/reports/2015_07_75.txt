Fire in childrens play area in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 20/07/2015 - 16:26 Location: Luton Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port One hose reel was used to extinguish a fire involving a slide and matress in Ellesmere Port.  The fire was well alight on arrival of the crew and is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
