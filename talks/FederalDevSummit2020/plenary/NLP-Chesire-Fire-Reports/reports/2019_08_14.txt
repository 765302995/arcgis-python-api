 Fire involving a quantity of rubbish in Crewe Time of Incident: 27/08/2019 - 16:52 Location: Timbrell Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engines attended from Crewe Shortly before 5pm firefighters from Crewe were called to reports of a fire in Timbrell Avenue in the town.  On arrival the crew found a fire involving a fire involving a quantity of rubbish at the back of a garage.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
