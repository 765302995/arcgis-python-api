 Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 03/01/2018 - 09:48 Location: Croft Drive, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield. One fire engine from Bollington. One fire engine from Wilmslow. Firefighters attended a kitchen fire at an address in Croft Drive in Macclesfield. Crews used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the flames. A large fan was used to disperse the flames.
