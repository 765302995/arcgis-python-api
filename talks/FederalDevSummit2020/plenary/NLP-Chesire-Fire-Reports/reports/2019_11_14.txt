 Fire involving a fuse box outside a house in Lower Withington Time of Incident: 25/11/2019 - 14:02 Location: Lapwing Lane, Lower Withington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Wilmslow and one from Macclesfield At around 2pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a fuse box outside a house in Lapwing Lane, Lower Withington.  On arrival crews discovered it was a small fire.  The electricity supply to the property was isolated and a cordon was set up for safety reasons.  The fire was put out using two dry powder extinguishers.  A covering jet was also set up and a thermal imaging camera was used to monitor temperatures.  Once the fire was out the house was fully inspected by firefighters.  The fire had damaged the external fuse box but had not effected the house.  Firefighters requested that the electricity supplier attended the scene.  Once the supplier had arrived the incident was handed over to them.  Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
