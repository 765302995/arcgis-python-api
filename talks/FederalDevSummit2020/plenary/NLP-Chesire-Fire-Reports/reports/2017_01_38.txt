Dishwasher fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/01/2017 - 18:55 Location: Bunbury Drive, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn A fire in a dishwasher filled the kitchen of a Bunbury Drive house with smoke. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and a high pressure fan to clear the smoke. Everyone from the property was accounted for. Crews were at the scene for one hour and ten minutes.
