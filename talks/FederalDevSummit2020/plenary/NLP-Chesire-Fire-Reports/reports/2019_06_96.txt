 Man stuck in mud on river embankment in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/06/2019 - 19:59 Location: Wilderspool Causeway, Warrington Attendance: Four fire engines attended - one from Warrington, one from Stockton Heath, one from Penketh and one from Widnes - as did the water incident unit from Warrington and a hydraulic platform and a rope rescue unit from Lymm At around 8pm firefighters were called to reports of a man being stuck in mud on a River Mersey embankment at Wilderspool Causeway, Warrington. On arrival the crews assessed the casualty and his surroundings and formulated a plan of action. A fire service boat was launched and acted as a safety boat for firefighters. The man was placed on a longboard and carried up the bankside. He was then handed over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
