Kitchen fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 18/04/2016 - 21:43 Location: Runcorn Road, Northwich Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich and Winsford attended Firefighters were called to a fire involving a kitchen and used two hose reels to extinguish it. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property. The occupier was out of the property on the arrival of the crews and paramedics attended. Kitchen fire safety advice
