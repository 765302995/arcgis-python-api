Bus fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/11/2017 - 06:20 Location: Legh Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one fire engine from Penketh Firefighters attended a fire involving a single decker bus. The fire was extinguished by crews using two hose reels and one breathing apparatus. The road was closed by police and fire crews dealt with the incident.
