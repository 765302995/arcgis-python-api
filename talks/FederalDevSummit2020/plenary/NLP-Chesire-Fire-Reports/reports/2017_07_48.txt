Grass and garden waste on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 18/07/2017 - 15:20 Location: Hangmans Lane, Lostock, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and two vehicles from Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called to a small fire involving grass and garden rubbish adjacent to a brine pumping station. The building was inspected and not involved and the crew used two hose reels to extinguish the fire and damp down.
