Road traffic collision in Sandbach Time of Incident: 05/07/2016 - 13:45 Location: The Hill, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe, one from Sandbach and one from Alsager Two cars were involved in a road traffic collision in Sandbach. Two casualties were trapped in  the vehicles and released by firefighters. Air and land ambulances were at the incident, with one casualty being taken to hospital by air and the other by land.  Firefighters made the scene safe.
