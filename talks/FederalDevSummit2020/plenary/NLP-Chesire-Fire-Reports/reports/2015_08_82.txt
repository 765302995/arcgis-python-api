Cooking fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/08/2015 - 16:57 Location: Alton Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe A pan left unattended caused a large amount of smoke in a flat in Crewe.  Firefighters ventilated the property before leaving.
