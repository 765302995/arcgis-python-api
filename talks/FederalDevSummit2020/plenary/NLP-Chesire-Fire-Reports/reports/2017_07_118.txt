Small fire left unattended in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/07/2017 - 12:54 Location: Knutsford Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a small camp fire which had been left unattended in the woods on Knutsford Road, Warrington. Crews used a fire extinguisher to put out the fire.
