Woman rescued from house fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 26/07/2015 - 02:17 Location: Wilton Close, Northwich Attendance: Three fire engines A woman was rescued from the roof of a house on fire in Northwich.  Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus one used a hose reel and covering jet to extinguish the fire which involved a dishwasher.   Two people were checked over by the Ambulance Service and crews were at the scene for about 90 minutes. Washing machine and dishwasher fire safety advice
