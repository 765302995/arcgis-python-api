Fire in disused building in Crewe Time of Incident: 26/09/2017 - 16:00 Location: Greystone Park, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe A fire involving a disused building in Crewe was extinguished with two hose reels and a covering water jet. The building measured 7m x 30m and was of concrete construction and the fire involved approximately 10% of the building. An inner cordon around the fire was set up for safety.
