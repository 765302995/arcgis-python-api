 Small fire at a children's playground in Winsford Time of Incident: 23/11/2019 - 19:30 Location: Denbigh Drive, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At 7.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a children's playground in Denbigh Drive, Winsford.  On arrival the crew found a small fire involving rubber tiling.  Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
