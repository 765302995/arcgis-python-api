Car on fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 08/12/2015 - 21:07 Location: A51, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a car which was well alight and used a hose reel to extinguish it. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately so if anyone has any information they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
