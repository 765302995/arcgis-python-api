 Fuse box fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/01/2019 - 09:21 Location: Latchford Street, Warrington Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Lymm Firefighters extinguished a fuse box on fire at a property on Latchford Street in Warrington.  Although the fire was out on arrival, dry powder was used by crews to cool any hotspots.  A thermal imaging camera was also used to check if the fire had spread to the ceiling.
