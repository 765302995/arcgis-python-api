Wheelie bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 02/01/2017 - 18:28 Location: Bradbury Road, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out wheelie bins which were on fire in a bus stop in Winsford.  This fire was believed to have been started deliberately. Do you have any information? Report it by calling Crimestoppers, which can be done anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
