 HGV tyre on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/06/2018 - 21:28 Location: Bridges Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane Crews attended to a report of a HGV tyre on fire on Bridges Road in Ellesmere Port.  On arrival crews found one discarded tyre well alight and used a hose reel to extinguish the fire.
