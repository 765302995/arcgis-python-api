Collision in Tiverton Time of Incident: 25/02/2015 - 21:48 Location: Whitchurch Road, Tiverton. Attendance: Fire engines from Tarporley and Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Whitchurch Road, Tiverton.  When they arrived crews found that there was a car on its roof. Nobody was trapped although two people were being treated by paramedics. The road was closed in both directions while the incident was ongoing.
