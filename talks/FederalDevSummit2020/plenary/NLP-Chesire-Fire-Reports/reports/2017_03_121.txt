Building fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 11/03/2017 - 18:25 Location: The Drumber, Winsford. Attendance: Three fire engines from Winsford, Northwich and Middlewich Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Wyvern House, The Drumber, Winsford.  When they arrived crews confirmed that the fire involved a storage compound, external to the main building. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to deal with the incident. They were at the scene for about an hour.  An investigation has now been launched to find the cause of the fire. It is however believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
