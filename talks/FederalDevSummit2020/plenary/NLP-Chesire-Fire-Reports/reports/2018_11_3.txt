 Bus fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/11/2018 - 10:54 Location: Admirals Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a bus on fire in Admirals Road, Birchwood. The fire was in the engine compartment and was affecting the rear seats.  All passengers were accounted for and the bus company arranged for a replacement vehicle and recovery of the affected vehicle.  Crews used a hose reel to put out the fire. They then damped down the vehicle and stripped down the bus to ensure the fire was out.
