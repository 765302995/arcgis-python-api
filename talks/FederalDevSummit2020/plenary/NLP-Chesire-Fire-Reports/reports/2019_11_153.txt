 Van on fire, Chester Time of Incident: 03/11/2019 - 00:54 Location: Kipling Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters were called to deal with a  van on fire in Kipling Road. They arrived to find it well alight and used two hose reel to extinguish the flames, remaining at the scene for some time afterwards to damp down and perform a final check with a thermal imaging camera.  Colleagues at Cheshire Police were notified due to it being a suspected deliberate ignition. Anyone with information is asked to call 101, quoting IML 555586.
