 Moped on fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 10/02/2019 - 10:44 Location: Grosvenor Avenue, Alsager Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 10.40am firefighters were called to a report of a moped being on fire in Grosvenor Avenue in Alsager.  On arrival the crew found the moped well alight. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire, which left the moped completely destroyed.  The crew were in attendance for around half-an-hour.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 318234, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
