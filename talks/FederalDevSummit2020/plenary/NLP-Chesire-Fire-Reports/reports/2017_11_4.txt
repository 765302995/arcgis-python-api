Cooker fire in Chester Time of Incident: 28/11/2017 - 19:12 Location: Clover Place, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a house on Clover Place, Chester, after a cooker fire. The fire was out on arrival of the crews. They used a large fan to clear smoke from the property and removed the cooker from the property. Cooking safety tips
