 Large skip fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 26/06/2019 - 14:47 Location: Leslie Road, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to a large skip fire at a recycling centre at Woodford Park Industrial Estate on Leslie Road in Winsford. The skip was well alight on arrival. Crews established a water supply and the site was evacuated as firefighters worked at the scene. Firefighters worked with on-site personnel to remove items from the skip and put them in an empty skip nearby using a mechanical digger. Two main jets and a hose reel jet were used to extinguish the fire. Once the fire was out firefighters damped the skip down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around two hours.
