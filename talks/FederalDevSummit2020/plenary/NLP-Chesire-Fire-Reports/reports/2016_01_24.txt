Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 23/01/2016 - 10:48 Location: Bebington Road in Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port fire station were sent to a kitchen fire at a property on Bebington Road in Ellesmere Port. Firefighters used breathing apparatus, one hose reel jet and a large fan to deal with the fire.  Kitchen fire safety
