Road traffic collision in Altrincham Time of Incident: 13/01/2017 - 18:19 Location: Ashley Road, Altrincham. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one from Altrincham (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Firefighters dealt with a diesel spillage following a road traffic collision on Ashley Road in Altrincham. Police closed the road in both directions.
