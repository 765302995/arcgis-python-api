 Smoke coming from a house in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/06/2018 - 17:21 Location: Willis Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 5.20pm firefighters were called to reports of smoke coming from a house and smoke alarms sounding in Willis Street, Warrington. On arrival the crew found that the smoke was caused by fumes from cooking. Firefighters used a large high pressure fan to clear the smoke from the property and were in attendance for around five minutes.
