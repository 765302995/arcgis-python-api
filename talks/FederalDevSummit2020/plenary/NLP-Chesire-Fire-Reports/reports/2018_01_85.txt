 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/01/2018 - 02:09 Location: Gregory Close, Great Sankey Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one fire engine from Penketh. Firefighters from Warrington attended reports of a building fire in Great Sankey, Warrington. The fire was out on arrival, however crews used a large fan to clear the smoke from the premises before conducting a home fire safety assessment. The fire is believed to have been caused by an unattended cooker.
