 Electric heater fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/12/2018 - 10:06 Location: Quebec Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines - one attended from Warrington and one from Birchwood Firefighters attended to reports of a fire at a ground floor flat on Quebec Road in Warrington.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews used a hose reel to put out the fire before dampening down the area.   One person inside the flat suffered from smoke inhalation and was handed into the care of paramedics.  The fire is believed to have been caused by an electrical heater in the bedroom.
