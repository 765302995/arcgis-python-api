Building fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 16/01/2016 - 14:21 Location: Heath Road South, Runcorn. Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn, Widnes, Frodsham, Chester and Stockton Heath, along with a hydraulic platform from Stockton Heath. Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a commercial building on Heath Road South, Runcorn. When they arrived crews confirmed that there was a fire. They used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control.
