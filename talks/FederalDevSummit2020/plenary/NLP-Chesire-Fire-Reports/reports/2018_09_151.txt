 Kitchen fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 31/08/2018 - 20:12 Location: Hayes Crescent, Frodsham. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly after 8pm firefighters from Widnes attended reports of a kitchen fire in Frodsham. Crews attended the scene and found the fire had been caused by a grill pan which was out on arrival. The team used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property and conducted a health and safety assessment at the property. The team were in attendance for approximately 40 minutes.
