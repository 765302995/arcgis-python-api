 Tree fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/05/2019 - 14:24 Location: Birchall Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At 2.30pm firefighters from Birchwood attended reports of a tree fire on Birchall Street, Warrington. In arrival the team discovered that there had been a fire involving a conifer tree. The fire was out on arrival. However, the team used one hose reel jet to dampen down and prevent the fire from reigniting. The team also used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. Firefighters were in attendance at the scene for around 30 minutes. The fire is believed to have been caused by a discarded cigarette.
