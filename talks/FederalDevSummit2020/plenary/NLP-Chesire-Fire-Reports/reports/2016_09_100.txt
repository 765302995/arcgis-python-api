Car fire on M62 near junction 8 Time of Incident: 11/09/2016 - 10:01 Location: Junction 8 westbound M62 Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath A car was on fire on the hard shoulder just after junction 8 west. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, put out the fire using a hose reel jet and fended off traffic around the affected vehicle.
