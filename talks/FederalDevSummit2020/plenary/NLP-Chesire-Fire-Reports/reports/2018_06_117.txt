 Fire at car wash in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/06/2018 - 23:05 Location: Tanners Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Penketh At around 11.05pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at Proshine hand car wash on Tanners Lane in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that a couch and an industrial recycling bin was on fire and the flames had caused damage to a converted shipping container used as an office. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The fire had spread to insulation and wooden lining of the structure. Firefighters removed burnt material and damped the area down to make it safe. They were in attendance for around 40 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
