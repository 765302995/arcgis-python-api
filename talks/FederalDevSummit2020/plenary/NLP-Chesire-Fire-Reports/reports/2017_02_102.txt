Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/02/2017 - 19:30 Location: Forrest Way, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A hatchback car was heavily involved in fire on Forrest Way. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and police were asked to attend.
