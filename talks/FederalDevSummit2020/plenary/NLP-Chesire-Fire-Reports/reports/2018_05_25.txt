 Pallets on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/05/2018 - 06:53 Location: Percival Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Widnes Fire crews were called to Percival Lane, Runcorn, after reports of a large number of pallets were on fire. Crews are currently at the scene and fighting the blaze, involving around 50 pallets, using two hose reel jets and two main jets. One casualty has been treated at the scene suffering from burns. A fire investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
