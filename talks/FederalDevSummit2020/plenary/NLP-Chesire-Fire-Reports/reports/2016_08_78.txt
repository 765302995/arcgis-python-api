Vehicle engine fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 17/08/2016 - 15:23 Location: Queens Park Gardens, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich A fire broke out in the engine compartment of a car in the driveway of a property. Firefighters called to the scene used one hose reel jet to put out the fire before dampening down any remaining hot spots of fire. Crews then did a final check of the vehicle with a thermal imaging camera and carried out a health and safety assessment of the area.
