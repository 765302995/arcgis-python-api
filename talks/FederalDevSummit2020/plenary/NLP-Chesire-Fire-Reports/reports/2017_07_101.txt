Overhead cable down in Frodsham Time of Incident: 09/07/2017 - 12:48 Location: Waterloo Lane, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn An overhead cable had come down onto the road and set a patch of grass alight. Firefighters put out the fire and set up a cordon around the collapsed cable. Waterloo Lane was closed in both directions. The electricity company attended the incident and isolated the cable. The road blocks remained in place until the damage was fixed.
