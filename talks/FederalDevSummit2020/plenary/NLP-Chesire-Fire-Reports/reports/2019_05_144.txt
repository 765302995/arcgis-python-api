 Chip pan fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 12/05/2019 - 11:40 Location: Aldersley Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At 11.40am firefighters were called to a report of a chip pan fire at a domestic property on Aldersley Road in Crewe. On arrival the crews discovered that the chip pan had been taken into the garden by the occupiers. Firefighters used a fire blanket to put out the fire. They then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. Firefighters were in attendance for less than half-an-hour. For chip pan fire safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chip-pans.
