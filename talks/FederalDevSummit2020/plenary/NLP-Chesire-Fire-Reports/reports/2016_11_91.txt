Fire in property in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/11/2016 - 10:54 Location: Briarwood Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Stockton Heath A fire in the ground floor of a terraced house required firefighters to use one hose reel to extinguish the flames. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property. One man suffered smoke inhalation and was treated by paramedics.
