Cooker fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/02/2015 - 13:14 Location: Tarvin Close, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A fire in a first floor flat was extinguished by firefighters wearing breathing apparatus. The fire involved a cooker that had been left unattended and a large fan was used to clear the smoke. Kitchen fire safety advice
