Carbon dioxide leak in Nantwich Time of Incident: 15/08/2015 - 03:28 Location: Welsh Row, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Crewe. Firefighters were called to a report of a cylinder leaking carbon dioxide in Nantwich.  Two of the crew wore breathing apparatus to investigate. The CO2 had been isolated and the detector showed clear. An engineer was requested to attend the scene before the firefighters left.
