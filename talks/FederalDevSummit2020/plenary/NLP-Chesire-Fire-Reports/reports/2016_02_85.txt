Road traffic collision in Capenhurst Time of Incident: 11/02/2016 - 07:45 Location: Capenhurst Lane, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of somebody trapped after a road traffic collision on Capenhurst Lane. When they arrived at the incident they found nobody trapped and made the scene safe.  Police officers and ambulance personnel were also at the scene.
