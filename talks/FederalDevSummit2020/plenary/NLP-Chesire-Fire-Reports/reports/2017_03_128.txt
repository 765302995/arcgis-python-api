Tumble dryer fire Time of Incident: 10/03/2017 - 19:57 Location: Trinity Street in Chester. Attendance: A fire engine and hydraulic platform from Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at the Crown Plaza Hotel on Trinity Street in Chester.  When they arrived they found that the fire involved a tumble dryer in a loading bay. Fire crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out. The hotel building was not effected.  One man was treated with oxygen after inhaling smoke. Crews were  at scene approximately thirty minutes.
