Casualty trapped in Sutton road traffic collision Time of Incident: 20/01/2015 - 12:01 Location: Walker Lane, Sutton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and one from Wilmslow A woman was freed by firefighters from a road traffic collision in Sutton. One vehicle was involved in the collision in Walker Lane.  The casualty was handed into the care of paramedics and the air ambulance attended.
