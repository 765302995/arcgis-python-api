Fire in the open at Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 27/03/2017 - 20:18 Location: Dock Yard Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters tackled a fire involving several bales of recyclable plastic on Dock Yard Road. Two main jets were used to tackle the blaze, with covering jets protecting unaffected bales. A fork lift truck was also used to remove bales not involved in the fire. Crews were in attendance for approximately three hours.
