 Large vehicle fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/12/2018 - 21:01 Location: Oil Sites Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port At around 9pm on Boxing Day firefighters from Ellesmere Port attended  reports of a large vehicle fire on Oil Sites Road. On arrival at the scene the team discovered a HGV tractor unit and trailer unit which were well alight. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel jets to extinguish the fire, they then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  Crews were on scene for one hour and 40 minutes.
