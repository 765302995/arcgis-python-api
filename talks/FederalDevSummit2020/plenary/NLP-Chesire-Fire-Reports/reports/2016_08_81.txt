Wheelie bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/08/2016 - 21:49 Location: Eccleston Avenue, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A fire had broken out inside a wheelie bin containing rubbish and aerosols. Crews called to the scene used one hose reel jet to put out the fire.  Please take care when disposing of aerosols. Always ensure the can is empty before you put it in the bin, and check the contents of the can and label to see if there is specific advice for disposing of it. Full or partially full aerosol cans can be taken to hazardous waste collection site.
