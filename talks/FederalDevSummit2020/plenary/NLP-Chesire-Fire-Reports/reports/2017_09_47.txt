Kitchen fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/09/2017 - 21:54 Location: Dean Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh and one from Widnes Fire crews were called to a flat fire on Dean Street, Widnes. On arrival crews found one casualty suffering from smoke inhalation which had been caused by burnt food. Crews gave oxygen therapy until paramedics arrived. Crews were at the scene for one hour and 15 minutes. Cooking safety information - https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety
