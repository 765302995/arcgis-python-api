 Large fire in the open in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 12/10/2019 - 19:23 Location: Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly before 7.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port.  On arrival the crew discovered that it was a large bonfire involving household waste and wheelie bins.  Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.  Police also attended.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.  For advice on how to prevent your wheelie bins from being used by arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
