 Reports of a kitchen fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 15/06/2019 - 17:11 Location: Dutch Close, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Shortly after 5pm firefighters from Winsford were called to reports of a kitchen fire in an unattended flat on Dutch Close.  On arrival the crew forced and discovered that an iron had been left on top of an electrical cooker hob causing smoke in the flat.  Firefighters isolated the electricity supply to the cooker and used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.  The crew were detained at the scene for approximately two hours 20 minutes.
