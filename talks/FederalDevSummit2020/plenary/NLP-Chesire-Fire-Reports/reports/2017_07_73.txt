Car fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 14/07/2017 - 20:34 Location: Junction 19 M6 south Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm, one from Holmes Chapel and one from Knutsford Fire crews were called to a car which was on fire on the M6 southbound at junction 19. Crews put out the fire using a dry powder fire extinguisher and a hose reel jet. Crews monitored the fire using a thermal imaging camera.
