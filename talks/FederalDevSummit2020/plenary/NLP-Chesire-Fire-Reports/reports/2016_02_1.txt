Car collides with a tree Time of Incident: 28/02/2016 - 05:19 Location: Long Shoot Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: Fire engines from Holmes Chapel and Congleton Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Long Shoot Road, Macclesfield.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved a car that had collided with a tree. A woman was trapped in the car. Crews cut her free and she was passed to the care of the ambulance service.
