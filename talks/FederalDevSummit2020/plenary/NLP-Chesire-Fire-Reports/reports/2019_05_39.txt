 RTC in Whitley Time of Incident: 25/05/2019 - 12:11 Location: Tarporley Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Fire crews attended to a road traffic collision on Tarporley Road in Whitley involving a motorbike and a tractor.  Although no one was trapped, firefighters assisted ambulance with two casualties and made the area safe.
