 Fire on a driveway in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/06/2019 - 10:22 Location: Poolside Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly before 10.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire on a domestic property's driveway on Poolside Road in Runcorn. On arrival the crew found two wheelie bins well alight. The fire had caused minor damage to a vehicle on the driveway. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. For advice on how to prevent a wheelie bin fire visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
