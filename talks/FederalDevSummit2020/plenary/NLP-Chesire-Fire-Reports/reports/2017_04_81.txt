Bus on fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 11/04/2017 - 10:22 Location: M6 south, between junctions 17 and 16 Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Sandbach Firefighters were called to a double decker bus on fire on the M6. Three hose reels were used fighting the fire. Thick black smoke was coming from the vehicle and the carriageway was closed while the incident was dealt with. There were no casualties at this incident.
