Car on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/10/2015 - 01:21 Location: Castle Rise, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A car which was well alight on the arrival of firefighters was extinguished using a hose reel. Police attended this incident as it was suspected to have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information about this fire they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
