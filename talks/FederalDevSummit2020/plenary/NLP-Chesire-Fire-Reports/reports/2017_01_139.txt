Furniture fire in Winsford. Time of Incident: 01/01/2017 - 21:39 Location: Wharton Gardens, Winsford. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Winsford A hose reel jet was used to put out a fire involving some furniture on Wharton Gardens, Winsford.  This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you can help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
