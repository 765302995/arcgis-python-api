 Bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 15:13 Location: Lanark Gardens, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to a small bin fire in Lanark Gardens, Widnes. The crew used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which had been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please contact Cheshire Police.
