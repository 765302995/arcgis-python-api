Diesel spillage in Knutsford Time of Incident: 17/08/2017 - 16:06 Location: Chester Road, Plumley, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Lymm Firefighters were called to a spillage of diesel on the road from a lorry, near to the Smoker Pub heading towards Northwich. Firefighters assisted with the removal of the diesel tank from the highway after it became detached from the vehicle. Approximately 100 litres of fuel was believed to have spilt onto the roadway. Police closed the nearside of the A56 while the incident was dealt with. Highways were requested to attend.
