Road traffic collision in Warrington - M62 Time of Incident: 01/12/2016 - 06:57 Location: M62 between junctions 9 and 8 Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Warrington Seven vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision on the M62, including a van carrying acetylene. Crews checked the cylinder for damage and monitored it with a thermal imaging camera. The supplier was called to come and recover the cylinder and the van was left in lane two until they arrived.  The remaining vehicles were removed from the carriageway by the Highways Agency. Firefighters then moved the van to the hard shoulder and handed the incident over to police officers.  Nobody was trapped in any of the vehicles but there were two casualties who were treated by the ambulance service.
