 Rescue of person stuck in a lift in Crewe Time of Incident: 13/06/2019 - 08:41 Location: Crewe Green Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to the Contemporary Art Building, on Crewe Green Road, Crewe, after a report of a person stuck in a lift. Crews managed to release the lift which was stuck between the first and ground floor of the building and rescue one woman.
