 Smoke caused by cooking in Crewe house Time of Incident: 27/04/2019 - 20:20 Location: Heywood Green, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters attended to reports of an alarm sounding at an address on Heywood Green in Crewe.  Crews established the alarm was sounding due to smoke caused by food being left in a pan.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the property.  One man was handed into the care of paramedics and taken to hospital.
