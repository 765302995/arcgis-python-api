Car fire in Birchwood Time of Incident: 07/06/2016 - 09:12 Location: Harcourt Close, Birchwood, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood. Firefighters were called to an incident involving a parked car on fire in a car park in Harcourt Close, Birchwood in Warrington. On arrival, firefighters found the car to be well alight and use one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. It is believed this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
