 Fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/05/2018 - 19:48 Location: Insall Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 7.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open opposite Padgate Library on Insall Road in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that the small fire involved bushes. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for less than 10 minutes.
