 Collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/01/2019 - 22:50 Location: Liverpool Road, Warrington Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Penketh Firefighters attended to a road traffic collision on Liverpool Road in Warrington.  Crews set up their equipment to help release a woman trapped inside a vehicle.  The woman was then handed into the care of paramedics.  Firefighters then made sure the vehicle was safe.
