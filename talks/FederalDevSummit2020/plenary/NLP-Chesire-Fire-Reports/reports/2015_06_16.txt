Small fire in Winsford park Time of Incident: 26/06/2015 - 01:52 Location: Minster Close, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Hand extinguishers were used to put out a small fire in a park in Winsford.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this or similar incidents is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
