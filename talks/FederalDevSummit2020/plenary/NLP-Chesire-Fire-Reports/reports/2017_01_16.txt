Roof terrace on fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 26/01/2017 - 15:07 Location: High Street, Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe one from Sandbach and one from Alsager A fire involving decking on a roof terrace was extinguished by firefighters using a hose reel. Police were asked to attend as it was suspected that this fire may have been started deliberately. If anyone knows anything about this fire they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
