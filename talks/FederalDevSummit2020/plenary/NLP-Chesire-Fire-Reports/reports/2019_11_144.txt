 Reports of a vehicle fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 04/11/2019 - 20:11 Location: M56 Eastbound near Helsby Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Runcorn and Powey Lane Shortly after 8pm firefighters were called to reports of a car fire on the M56 near Helsby.  On arrival the crews that the electrical compartment of the engine was overheating causing a large amount of white smoke.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to cool the engine and made the vehicle safe.  Crews were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
