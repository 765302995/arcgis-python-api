 Grass fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 08/08/2018 - 13:33 Location: Cotswold Way, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and one fire engine from Northwich Grass fire in WinsfordFirefighters were called to a fire near the bike track off Cotswold Way, Winsford. Crews used two hose reels jets and beaters to extinguish the flames then damped down the area to make sure there were no hotspots.
