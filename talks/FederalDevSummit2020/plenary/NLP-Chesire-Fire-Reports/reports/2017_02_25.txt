Car destroyed by fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/02/2017 - 18:06 Location: The Uplands, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn A car was well alight, so firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  The was car was completely destroyed by the fire.   It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information about who may have started the fire please call Crimstoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
