Tumble dryer fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/01/2016 - 17:25 Location: Padgate Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Crews were called to a report of a fire involving a tumble dryer. There had been a small fire in the appliance which was out when firefighters arrived.  There was smokelogging to the property so a high pressure fan was used to remove the smoke. Smoke alarms were fitted and alerted the occupants.
