Garden fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 14/10/2017 - 12:46 Location: Woodford Lane, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to a house on Woodford Lane, Winsford following reports of a fire in a garden. One fire engine from Winsford attended and crews used a backpack extinguisher to put out a small fire. A woman suffered burns as a result of the fire and was taken to Leighton Hospital by North West Ambulance Service.
