Outbuilding fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 11/03/2017 - 22:28 Location: Manchester Road in Lymm Attendance: Fire engines attended from Warrington and Birchwood Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a building off Manchester Road in Lymm.  When they arrived they found that a outbuilding was on fire. Crews used hose reel jets to put the fire out.
