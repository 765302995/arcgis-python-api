Flat fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/09/2017 - 00:51 Location: St Katherines Way, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington, one from Penketh and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a fire in a ground floor flat on St Katherine's Way, Warrington. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They then cleared smoke from the property using a large fan. No one was injured during the incident. Crews were in attendance for one hour and 45 minutes.
