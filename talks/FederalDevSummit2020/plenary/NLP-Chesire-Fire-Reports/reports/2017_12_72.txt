Vehicle fire in Tabley Time of Incident: 06/12/2017 - 11:36 Location: Knutsford Service Station, Northwich Road, Tabley. Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel and one fire engine from Winsford attended Firefighters from Holmes Chapel and Winsford attended a reported vehicle fire at Knutsford Service station. Firefighters arrived and found that the brakes had overheated, so the crew used a hose reel jet to cool the vehicle.
