Bin fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 13/06/2017 - 19:58 Location: Off Alton Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters used a water backpack to put out a fire in a bin. It's believed the fire was started deliberately. Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 839 of 13 June.
