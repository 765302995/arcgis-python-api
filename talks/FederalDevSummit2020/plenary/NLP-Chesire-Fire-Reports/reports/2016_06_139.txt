Fire in the open in Malpas Time of Incident: 05/06/2016 - 22:19 Location: Beeches Close, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and one from Whitchurch (Shropshire Fire and Rescue Service) Firefighters put out a fire in Beeches Close, Malpas. It was a bonfire which had got out of control. Crews put out the fire and then turned over and damped down the debris.
