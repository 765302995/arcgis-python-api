 Cooking food left unattended in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/05/2018 - 20:38 Location: Auckery Avenue, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 8.40pm firefighters attended a house in Auckery Avenue in Ellesmere Port after its smoke alarm sounded. On arrival the crew discovered a large amount of cooking fumes after a pan was left unattended. Firefighters cleared the smoke with a large high pressure pan and were in attendance for around 50 minutes. For cooking safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
