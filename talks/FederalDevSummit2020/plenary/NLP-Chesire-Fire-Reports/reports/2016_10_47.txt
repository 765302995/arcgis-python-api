Chimney fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 23/10/2016 - 17:49 Location: Hurdsfield Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A fire in a chimney required firefighters to use specialist tools to remove a blockage from inside the chimney and extinguish the fire. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the area. Chimney safety advice
