Cylinder involved in fire in Henbury Time of Incident: 29/07/2016 - 13:27 Location: Fanshawe Lane, Henbury Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Congleton A propane cylinder in an outbuilding was involved in fire. The 3.9kg cylinder was cooled using a covering jet. A thermal imaging camera was then used to check it was no longer retaining heat. The cylinder was then contained within a dam of water.
