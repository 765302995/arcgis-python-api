 Reports of a dangerous structure in Chester Time of Incident: 09/12/2019 - 07:29 Location: Grosvenor Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly before 7.30am firefighters from Chester were called to reports of a dangerous structure on Grosvenor Road.  On arrival the crew found that a large sheet of tarpaulin was stuck in a tree and at risk of blowing into a vehicles on a nearby road.  The crew used a ladder and extendable poles to reach the tarpaulin and remove it from the tree. Firefighters were detained for around 50minutes.
