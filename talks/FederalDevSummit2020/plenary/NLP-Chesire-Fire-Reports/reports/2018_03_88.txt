 Smoke coming from Northwich house gas fire Time of Incident: 16/03/2018 - 23:00 Location: Registry Close, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Occupants of a Registry Close house saw smoke issuing from a gas fire. Firefighters were called and, removing the fireplace, they found that the timbers in the timber-framed property were burning. Water from a hose reel jet was applied to the burning wood and the loft space was also checked for any fire spread. Firefighters cut away plaster around the affected area to make sure the fire was completely out. Gas engineers were also called to attend.
