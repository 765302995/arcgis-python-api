Overturned car in a field in Stockton Heath, Warrington Time of Incident: 03/04/2015 - 08:05 Location: Chester road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington There was an overturned saloon car in a field by Hunts Lane/Chester Road in Stockton Heath.  Nobody was trapped. The occupants were with the police when fire crews arrived. Firefighters made the car safe.
