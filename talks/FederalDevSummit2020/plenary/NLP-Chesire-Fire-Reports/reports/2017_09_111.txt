Wheelie bins on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/09/2017 - 03:36 Location: Bruche Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm and one from Warrington Two wheelie bins and fence panels were on fire at a property on Bruches Avenue in Poulton-with-Fearnhead. A hose reel jet was used to put out the flames.
