 Cooker fire in Chester Time of Incident: 23/12/2018 - 02:29 Location: Bouverie Street, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Chester and one from Powey Lane At around 2.30am firefighters were called to a report of a cooker fire at a house in Bouverie Street, Chester. The fire was out on arrival. Firefighters made sure the cooker was safe and ventilated the property. They were in attendance for nearly half-an-hour. For kitchen fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
