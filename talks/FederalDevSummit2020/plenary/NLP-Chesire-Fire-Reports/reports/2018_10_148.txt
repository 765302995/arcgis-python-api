 Small fire in woodland in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/10/2018 - 17:08 Location: Winwick Park Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 5.10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in woodland on Winwick Park Avenue in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
