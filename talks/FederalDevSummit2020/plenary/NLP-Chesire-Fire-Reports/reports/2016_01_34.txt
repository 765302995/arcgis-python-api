Kitchen fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 21/01/2016 - 20:02 Location: Prescott Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Fire Station and one from Cheadle Fire Station One fire engine from Wilmslow Fire Station and one from Cheadle Fire Station were sent to a property on Prescott Road in Wilmslow where there was a report of a kitchen fire.  On arrival the fire crews found a fire in the kitchen of a terraced property. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire .  Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus. A large fan was also used to clear the smoke from the property. A thermal image camera was used to see if the fire has spread. Fire crews were at the scene for approximately one hour.
