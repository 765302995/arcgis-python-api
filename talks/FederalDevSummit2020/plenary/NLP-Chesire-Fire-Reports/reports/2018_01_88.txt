 Vehicle fire in Winwick Time of Incident: 10/01/2018 - 18:13 Location: Winwick Link Road, Winwick Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath At 6.13pm firefighters from Stockton Heath attended reports of a vehicle fire on the A49 Winwick Link Road. On arrival at the scene crews found that the fire involved the engine of a Vauxhall Corsa which had overheated.
