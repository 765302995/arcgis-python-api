 Small fire in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 08/09/2019 - 17:24 Location: Gosforth Court, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly before 5.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Gosforth Court, Runcorn. On arrival the crew found a small quantity of rubbish on fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
