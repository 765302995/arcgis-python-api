 Conservatory fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 24/10/2019 - 23:43 Location: Heath Road, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Crewe and one from Sandbach Fire crews attended to reports of a conservatory fire on Heath Road in Sandbach.  A hose reel was used to put out the flames and a large fan dispersed the smoke.  The fire is believed to have been caused accidently and involved a faulty battery.    One man suffered burn injuries and smoke inhalation and was treated by paramedics and taken to hospital.
