Rubbish fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 30/11/2017 - 20:47 Location: Blackbrook Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a small refuse fire on Blackbrook Avenue, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about this incident please call Cheshire Police on 101.
