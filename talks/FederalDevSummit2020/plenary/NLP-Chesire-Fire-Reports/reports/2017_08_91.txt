Car well alight in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 14/08/2017 - 14:43 Location: New Port Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A car which was well alight on a road in Ellesmere Port was extinguished with two hose reels. Police were in attendance as the fire is thought to have been started deliberately. Call police on 101 of Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 if you know anything about who may have started it.
