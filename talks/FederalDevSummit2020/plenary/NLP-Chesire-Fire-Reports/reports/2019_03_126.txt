 Small fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 02/03/2019 - 17:58 Location: Brecon Way, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Just before 6pm firefighters were called following reports of a fire at a playground on Brecon Way. Crews attended and used a hose reel jet to put out a fire underneath a climbing frame. They also used a thermal imaging camera to make sure the fire was out.
