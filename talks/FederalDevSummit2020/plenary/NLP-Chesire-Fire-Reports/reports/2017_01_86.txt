Tiled chimney fire in Wybunbury Time of Incident: 11/01/2017 - 16:55 Location: Gorsey Bank Crescent, Wybunbury Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a chimney fire in Wybunbury. Crews used a thermal imaging camera to check for remaining spots of fire before clearing debris and ash away from the loft space.
