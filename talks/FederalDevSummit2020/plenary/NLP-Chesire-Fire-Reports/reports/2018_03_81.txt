 Factory fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 19/03/2018 - 13:24 Location: Aston Lane South, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Widnes At around 1.25pm firefighters were called to a fire at the YKK Fasteners (UK) Ltd factory in Whitehouse Industrial Estate on Ashton Lane South in Runcorn. Crews discovered a smouldering rubber fan belt. They extinguished the smouldering fire and ventilated the area. Firefighters were at the scene for less than half-an-hour.
