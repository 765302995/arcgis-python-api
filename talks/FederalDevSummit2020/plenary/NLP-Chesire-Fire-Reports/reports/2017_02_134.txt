Commercial wheelie bin on fire on Cheshire Business Park in Northwich Time of Incident: 03/02/2017 - 19:15 Location: Cheshire Business Park, Cheshire Avenue, Lostock Gralam, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford and Northwich A commercial wheelie bin was on fire in an open wooden sided external store. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
