 Small fire in Willaston Time of Incident: 13/07/2019 - 21:27 Location: Tricketts Lane, Willaston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to a fire in a wooden structure off Tricketts Lane, Willaston. The structure was completely destroyed by fire and the firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames.
