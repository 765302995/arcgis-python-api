 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 27/02/2018 - 02:13 Location: Eversley, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters from Penketh used a hose reel jet to extinguish a wheelie bin fire on Eversley. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
