 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/03/2019 - 17:14 Location: Horrocks Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 5.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a wheelie bin being on fire on Horrocks Lane in Warrington. Firefighters extinguished the fire in the open using a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 15 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For wheelie bin fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
