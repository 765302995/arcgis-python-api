 Van fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 10/04/2018 - 18:52 Location: M56, near Junction 11 Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Lymm At 6.52pm firefighters were called to a van fire on the hard shoulder of the M56 by Junction 11 (the Daresbury roundabout). On arrival crews discovered the engine of a commercial van had overheated. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to cool the engine and were at the scene for around 20 minutes.
