 Car fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 27/08/2018 - 13:16 Location: M6 southbound between junction 18 and 17 Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford, one from Congleton and one from Crewe Fire crews were called to a car fire on the M6 southbound. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. The motorway was closed whilst firefighters fought the fire. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
