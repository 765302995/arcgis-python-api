 Washing machine on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 06/04/2019 - 00:55 Location: Wrexham Road, Chester. CH4 9DG Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Powey Lane. Firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out a fire in an outbuilding on Wrexham Road.  The fire was in a large washing machine and the crews also isolated the gas supply to the building to make sure it was safe. They were on scene for an hour.
