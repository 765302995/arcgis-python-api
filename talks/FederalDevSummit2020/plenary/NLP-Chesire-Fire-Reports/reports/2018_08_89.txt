 Road traffic collision, Northwich Time of Incident: 12/08/2018 - 05:25 Location: Chester Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford Crews responded to reports of a single vehicle road traffic collision on Chester Road in Northwich. Crews worked with fellow emergency services to make the vehicle safe however, sadly, the male driver was confirmed deceased at the scene.
