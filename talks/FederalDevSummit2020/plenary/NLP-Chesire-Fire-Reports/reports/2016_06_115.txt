Flooding in Warrington property Time of Incident: 10/06/2016 - 03:22 Location: Knutsford Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath Firefighters assisted at a flooding incident that was affecting the electrics of a Warrington property. The crew isolated the electricity supply and found the source of the flooding. The householder was then advised to contact an electrician. The flooding was caused by weather conditions.
