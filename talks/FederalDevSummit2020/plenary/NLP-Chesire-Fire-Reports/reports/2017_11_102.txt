Bedroom fire in Handforth Time of Incident: 06/11/2017 - 20:25 Location: Woodlands Road, Handforth. Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow. One fire engine from Bollington. One fire engine from Cheadle. Firefighters attended a fire in a bedroom on Woodlands Road in Handforth. Crews used one hose reel, two breathing apparatus and a large fan to extinguish the fire. One casualty was assessed for minor smoke inhalation.
