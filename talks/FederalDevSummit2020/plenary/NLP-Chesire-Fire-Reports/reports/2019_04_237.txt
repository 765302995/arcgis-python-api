 House fire in Great Sankey Time of Incident: 03/04/2019 - 21:16 Location: Ross Close, Great Sankey Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Warrington and Penketh At around 9.15pm firefighters from Penketh and Warrington were dispatched to reports of a house fire on Ross Close in Great Sankey.  On arrival the team discovered a fire in a ground floor bedroom. All occupants of the address were accounted for and had nod sustained any injuries.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used one house reel jet to extinguish the fire. They then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
