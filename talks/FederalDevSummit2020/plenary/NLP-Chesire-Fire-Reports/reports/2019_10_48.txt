 Car fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 23/10/2019 - 00:03 Location: Knutsford Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Shortly after midnight firefighters were called to a report of a car being on fire on Knutsford Road in Wilmslow.  On arrival the crew found the saloon car to be completely destroyed by the fire.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  They then damped the car down and used a thermal imaging camera to monitor its temperature.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 545894, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
