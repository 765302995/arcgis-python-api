 Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/06/2018 - 21:22 Location: Grass fire in Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Widnes Firefighters from Runcorn and Widnes used one hose reel jet to extinguish a grass fire at Runcorn Hill Nature Reserve. The team were in attendance for approximately one hour.
