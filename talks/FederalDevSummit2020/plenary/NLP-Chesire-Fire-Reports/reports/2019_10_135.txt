 Large tree fallen onto a building in Congleton. Time of Incident: 05/10/2019 - 07:04 Location: Daneside Close, Congleton. Attendance: Station Manager McCourt attended Shortly after 7am Station Manager McCourt attended reports of a dangerous structure on Daneside Close, Congleton. On arrival at the address the he found that a large tree had fallen onto a residential apartment block. A cordon has been put in place as a precautionary measure and an engineer has been called to the scene.
