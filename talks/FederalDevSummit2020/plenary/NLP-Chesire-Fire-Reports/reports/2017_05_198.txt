Rubbish on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/05/2017 - 04:35 Location: Barbauld Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Refuse on fire at the rear of a commercial property spread to windowsills of the building. Firefighters, two in breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control and were at the scene for just over two hours.
