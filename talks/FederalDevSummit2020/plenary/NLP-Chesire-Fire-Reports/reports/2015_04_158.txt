Domestic building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/04/2015 - 20:46 Location: Dudley Street, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a property off Dudley Street, Warrington.  When they arrived crews wearing breathing appratus used hose reel jets and special fans to deal with the blaze.  There were no reports of any injuries but a full fire investigation will now been launched to find the cause of the incident.
