Fire at Daresbury leisure centre Time of Incident: 06/08/2017 - 23:29 Location: De Vere, Daresbury Park, Daresbury Attendance: Four fire engines from Runcorn, Warrington, Stockton Heath, Runcorn and Lymm Overheated towels burnt through to a vertical service duct at a Daresbury leisure centre. Firefighters, two in breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke. The building was evacuated while the incident was dealt with.
