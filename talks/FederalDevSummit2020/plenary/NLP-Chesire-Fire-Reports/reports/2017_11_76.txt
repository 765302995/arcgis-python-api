Pan fire, Little Neston Time of Incident: 10/11/2017 - 17:42 Location: Talbot Avenue, Little Neston, Neston Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Crews were called to reports of a small fire in the kitchen area. On arrival, they found there had been a small pan fire, which had caused slight damage to the property. The occupant was suffering form smoke inhalation and an ambulance was requested.
