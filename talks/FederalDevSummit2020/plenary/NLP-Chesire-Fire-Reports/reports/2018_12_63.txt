 Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/12/2018 - 10:36 Location: Buttermere Avenue, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Shortly after 10.30am firefighters from Ellesmere Port and Chester were called to reports of a house fire on Buttermere Avenue, Ellesmere Port. On arrival crews were faced with a well developed fire in the kitchen. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The crews then used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property. A thermal imaging camera was then used to check for hotspots and make sure that the house was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for approximately three hours.
