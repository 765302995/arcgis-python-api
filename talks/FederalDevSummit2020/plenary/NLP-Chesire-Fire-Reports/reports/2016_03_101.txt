Three vehicle road traffic collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 12/03/2016 - 12:23 Location: Chelford Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Two cars and a lorry were involved in a road traffic collision in Macclesfield which also damaged a house.  Firefighters made the vehicles and the boundary wall of the house safe as seven people from the vehicles were treated by ambulance personnel for minor injuries.
