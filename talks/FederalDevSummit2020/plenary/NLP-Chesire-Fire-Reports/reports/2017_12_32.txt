Road traffic collision on Weston Point Expressway, Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/12/2017 - 06:53 Location: Weston Point Expressway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one fire engine from Widnes At 6.53am this morning (Friday, 22 December) fire crews from Runcorn and Widnes attended a road traffic collision on the Weston Point Expressway in Runcorn. Firefighters provided casualty care and oxygen therapy to the person trapped in the vehicle before extracting them. Crews were on the scene for 45 minutes.
