Man rescued from car stuck in water in Lymm Time of Incident: 28/12/2015 - 10:27 Location: Whitbarrow Road, Lymm Attendance: Two fire engines and the rescue boat from Warrington and one fire engine from Stockton Heath. A man was rescued from a car stuck in flood water in Lymm.  Crews used a raft to rescue the man and handed him into the care of paramedics to be checked over. Firefighters were at the scene for 30 minutes.
