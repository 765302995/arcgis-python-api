 Vehicle fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 03/02/2018 - 21:37 Location: Broadway, Barnton, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters were called to a vehicle fire near to an alleyway at Broadway, Barnton. Crews discovered a Vauxhall Vectra alight and used one hose reel, breathing apparatus and a thermal imaging camera to put the fire out.
