 Jetty fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/07/2018 - 23:27 Location: Moore Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one from Warrington Fire crews were called to Moore Lane, Warrington, to a fire involving a jetty. Crews used a light portable pump and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which was approximately 20m.
