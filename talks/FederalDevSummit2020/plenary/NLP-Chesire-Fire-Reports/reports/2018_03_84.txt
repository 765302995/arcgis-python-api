 Three car collision in Disley Time of Incident: 17/03/2018 - 22:42 Location: Buxton Road, Disley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton and one from New Mills (Derbyshire Fire and Rescue Service Three cars were involved in a road traffic collision in Disley. One casualty was trapped in one of the cars. Firefighters stabilised the cars and the casualty was released from one of them. The crews then made the scene safe. Police and paramedics were also in attendance.
