 One person rescued from a car in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/04/2018 - 00:27 Location: Hightown, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to an overturned vehicle on Hightown, Crewe. Crews removed the door of the vehicle using cutting equipment and rescued one casualty who was trapped. They were then handed into the care of paramedics.
