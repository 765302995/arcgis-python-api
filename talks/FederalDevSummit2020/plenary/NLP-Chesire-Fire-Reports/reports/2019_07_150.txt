 Roof collapse, Northwich Time of Incident: 06/07/2019 - 18:08 Location: Astbury Drive, Northwich Attendance: Three fire engines from Northwich, Winsford and Lymm and the Major Rescue Unit Three fire crews and officers from the Major Rescue Unit responded to reports of a concrete shed roof collapsing on a 50-year-old man in Astbury Drive, Barnton.  The man was trapped but largely unharmed by the incident, and crews worked with the Major Rescue Unit to safely release him. He was passed into the care of paramedics and taken to hospital with minor injuries.
