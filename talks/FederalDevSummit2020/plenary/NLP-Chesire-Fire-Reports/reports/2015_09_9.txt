Car fire on the M62 Time of Incident: 28/09/2015 - 20:59 Location: Between Junctions 10 and 11 on the M62. Attendance: A fire engine from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a report of a car fire between Junctions 10 and 11 on the M62.  When they arrived crews found that there was a hatchback car on fire. Two hose reel jets were used to put the fire out.
