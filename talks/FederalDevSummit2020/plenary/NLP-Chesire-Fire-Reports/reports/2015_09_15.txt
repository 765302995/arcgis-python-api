Building fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 28/09/2015 - 09:24 Location: Waters Green, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine and the aerial appliance from Macclesfield and one fire engine from Congleton Two fire engines and the aerial appliance have been tackling a fire in a three-storey building in Waters Green. The fire was on the third floor and the building was evacuated as firefighters tackled the incident. The crews gained access to the third floor where they used a hose reel jet and a main jet to put out the fire. An investigation into the cause is being carried out by a fire investigation officer.
