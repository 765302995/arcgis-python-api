 Chimney fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 17/11/2019 - 14:44 Location: Mereworth Drive, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich. Firefighters were called to a chimney fire at an address in Mereworth Drive, Northwich. Crews tackled the small fire from the grate using chimney rods, and a hose reel jet. They then used a thermal imaging camera and checked the attic to ensure it hadn't spread. They then used a chimney hose on the debris within the chimney.
