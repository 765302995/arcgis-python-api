Chimney fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 05/12/2015 - 17:19 Location: Harefield Drive, Wilmslow. Attendance: A fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a house on Harefield Drive, Wilmslow.  When they arrived crews found that the fire was in a chimney. Crews used water to put the fire out.
