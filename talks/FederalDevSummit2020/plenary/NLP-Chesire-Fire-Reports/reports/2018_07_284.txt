 Large field fire near to a railway embankment in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 10:56 Location: Slutchers Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines are in attendance - one from Warrington and one from Lymm - as well as an hydraulic platform from Lymm At around 10.55am firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a field near to a railway embankment on Slutchers Lane in Warrington. Crews initially used water and beaters to fight the large grass fire. Due to its close proximity to the railway track, the track was closed while firefighters deal with the incident. Firefighters proceeded to tackle the flames with water, including from a hose reel jet. An aerial platform was used to fight the fire from above. A water shuttle was put in place to get water to the area and firefighters worked alongside Network Rail staff.  UPDATE AT 2.15PM:  The West Coast Main Line remained closed. Firefighters were expected to remain at the scene for another hour.  UPDATE AT 2.30PM:  Firefighters made good progress with the water shuttle and hydraulic platform.   UPDATE at 4:30PM:  The fire was extinguished and the track reopened.
