 Kitchen fire at Congleton flat Time of Incident: 26/08/2019 - 17:51 Location: Buxton Road, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters attended to a kitchen fire at a flat on Buxton Road in Congleton.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews used a hose reel to put out the fire which started from the oven.  A large fan was then used to disperse the smoke.
