Cow rescued from canal near Winsford Time of Incident: 29/06/2016 - 17:39 Location: Clive Back Lane, Winsford Attendance: Fire engines from Holmes Chapel and Knutsford along with the animal rescue unit from Knutsford Firefghters were called to reports of a cow stuck in a canal. Crews used an inflatable raft to coax the cow from the water and to the safety of a nearby field. They were detained at the scene for one hour and thirty five minutes.
