 Fuel leak in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/07/2019 - 10:56 Location: Winwick Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Fire crews were called to a fuel leak on Winwick Road, Warrington. A petrol tank of a wagon had leaked and crews used sand to absorb the spillage and then used dammit paste to plug the leak.
