Large timber fire in Langley Time of Incident: 22/10/2016 - 06:18 Location: Jaran Sutton, Main Road, Langley, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield There was a fire involving wood (approximately five metres by five metres) on the old Scraggs industrial estate.  One hose reel  was used to extinguish the fire.
