 RTC in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 27/10/2018 - 07:35 Location: Lynton Lane, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Fire crews attended to a road traffic collision involving a car and a pedestrian on Lynton Lane in Alderley Edge.  Firefighters stabilised the car before releasing one man from the vehicle and handing him into the care of paramedics.  Crews were in attendance for 45 minutes.
