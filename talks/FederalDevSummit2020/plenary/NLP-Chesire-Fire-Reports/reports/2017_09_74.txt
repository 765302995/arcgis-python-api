Caravan fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 10/09/2017 - 07:26 Location: Woodford Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton, one from Stockport and one from Cheadle (both Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) A fire involving a caravan on the driveway of a Woodford Road property also damaged windows and fascia boards on the house. Firefighters, three wearing breathing apparatus, used three hose reel jets to bring the fire under control. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke and a thermal imaging camera used to check there were no further hot spots that could reignite. One person, a female, was led to safety from the property by firefighters.
