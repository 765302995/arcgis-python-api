 Small fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/08/2018 - 23:21 Location: Brook Lane, Rixton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At approximately 11.20pm firefighters from Birchwood were called to reports of a fire near to the M6 Thelwall Viaduct. Crews attended the scene and following a search of the are the team located a drum of cable wire which had been deliberately set on fire on Brook Lane, Rixton. The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in detained for approximately one hour.
