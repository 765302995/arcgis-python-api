 Two cars on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 04/02/2018 - 06:55 Location: Edleston Road/Broady Court, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to two vehicles on fire at Edleston Road/Broady Court in Crewe. Crews used one hose reel to put the fire out and were on scene for around one hour 20 minutes.
