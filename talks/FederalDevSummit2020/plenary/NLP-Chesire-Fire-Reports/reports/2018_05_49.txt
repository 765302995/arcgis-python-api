 Van fire in Chester Time of Incident: 23/05/2018 - 21:35 Location: Liverpool Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Firefighters from Powey Lane were called to reports of a vehicle fire on Liverpool Road, Chester. On arrival they found that the fire was in a storage container in the rear of the van. The team used specialist cutting equipment to access the fire and one hose reel jet to extinguish the blaze. The team were in attendance for approximately 45 minutes.
