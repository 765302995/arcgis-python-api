Horse rescued from water in Chester Time of Incident: 12/10/2016 - 18:11 Location: Parkgate Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and the animal rescue unit from Knutsford Firefighters pulled a horse out of water after it became stuck. The animal, who was four years old and called Issabelle, had fallen into a water-filled ditch.  The large animal rescue unit worked with Green Watch Chester using ropes and lines to help the horse onto dry land.
