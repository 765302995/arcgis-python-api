Garage and building fire in Chester Time of Incident: 13/07/2015 - 05:03 Location: St Thomas Pathway, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester, two from Ellesmere Port, one from Frodsham and one from Tarporley Firefighters extinguished a fire involving industrial garages in Chester which then spread to a commercial property.  The initial fire was well alight on arrival and involved a row of four timber garages containing propane gas cylinders. Two main jets were used to bring the fire under control and a house opposite had to be evacuated due to the heat damaging the window and a street light.  Access was then gained to the commercial property which joined onto the garages to check for fire spread and a small fire was located in the joists of the first floor. Six firefighters wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire.  The cause of the fire is believed to be accidental.  Video Video - garage and building fire in Chester
