Caravan on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/11/2016 - 19:43 Location: Grizedale, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters tackled a caravan fire which also involved a gas cylinder. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to bring the blaze under control. The crews, who were at the scene for just under an hour, then cooled the lpg cylinder until it was brought down to a safe temperature.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 816 of 17 November.
