Road traffic collision in Latchford, Warrington Time of Incident: 04/07/2016 - 18:13 Location: Common Lane, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Stockton Heath There was a road traffic collision involving two cars outside Warrington Town Football Club in Latchford.  One female casualty was trapped inside one of the vehicles.  Firefighters and paramedics released the casualty from the vehicle. The casualty was then handed to the care of an ambulance crew. Firefighters then made the area safe.
