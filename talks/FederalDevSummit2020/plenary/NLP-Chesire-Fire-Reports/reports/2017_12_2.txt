Chimney fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 29/12/2017 - 21:08 Location: Congleton Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield attended. Crews from Macclesfield were called to reports of a chimney fire on Congleton Road in Macclesfield. On arrival at the scene the crew first exposed the chimney breast using cutting equipment. The then used a thermal imaging camera and water back pack to extinguish the fire.
