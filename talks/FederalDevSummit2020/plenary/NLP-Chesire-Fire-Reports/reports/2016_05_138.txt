Deliberate fire in the open in Northwich Time of Incident: 07/05/2016 - 19:01 Location: Moss Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Deliberate ignition is suspected as the cause of a fire in the open on Moss Farm. Firefighters used beaters, water backpacks and a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  Anyone with any information about this, or similar incidents, is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
