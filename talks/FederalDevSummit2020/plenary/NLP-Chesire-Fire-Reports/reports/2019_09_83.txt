 Wheelie bin fire, Runcorn Time of Incident: 14/09/2019 - 21:26 Location: Castle Rise, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called when a fire broke out in a grassy area of Castle Rise. It was a wheelie bin that was well alight, along with some garden waste that had also caught fire. They used a single hose reel to put out the flames.
