Road traffic collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 15/11/2016 - 19:56 Location: Buxton New Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield One car was involved in a road traffic collision leaving a woman trapped in the vehicle. Paramedics treated one casualty while firefighters stabilised the car and used hydraulic cutting equipment to remove the roof of the car to release, on a long board, the second casualty. The car had left the road and was in a field close to the Cat and Fiddle pub.
