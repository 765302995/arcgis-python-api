Gates on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 13/05/2016 - 02:36 Location: Lower House Lane, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Wooden gates attached to a house on Lower House Lane were involved in a fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and damped down the area. Three wheelie bins were also involved in the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.  Always follow our wheelie bin safety advice.
