Cooker fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/12/2015 - 09:03 Location: Stewart Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Sandbach Firefighters were called to a property in Crewe following reports of an active smoke alarm and a strange smell.  Fire crews forced entry into a property where they found a fire caused by a plastic item left on the hob. The fire was extinguished using breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet.
