 Car fire on M6, Lymm Time of Incident: 29/11/2018 - 12:06 Location: M6 Lymm. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington attended. One fire engine from Birchwood. One from Altrincham. Car fire on M6, LymmFirefighters were called to a car on fire between Lymm and the slip road for the M56. The vehicle, which was on the hard shoulder was well alight.  Crews used breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the flames.
