 Shop fire in Chester Time of Incident: 31/03/2019 - 17:13 Location: Frodsham Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a shop on Frodsham Street, Chester, after a report of a fire on the outside of the building. Crews found a small fire in a wood panel which had been started by a discarded cigarette. Crews used a hose reel jet a large fan to tackle the fire. Crews were at the scene for an hour and 30 minutes.
