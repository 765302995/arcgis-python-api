Forklift truck fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 11/09/2016 - 14:06 Location: Radnor Park Industrial Estate, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton One casualty sustained injuries after a fork lift truck caught fire on Radnor Park Industrial Estate. Firefighters gave first aid to the casualty and brought the fire under control using a hose reel jet with one firefighter wearing breathing apparatus. The area was damped down and the casualty handed into the care of paramedics.
