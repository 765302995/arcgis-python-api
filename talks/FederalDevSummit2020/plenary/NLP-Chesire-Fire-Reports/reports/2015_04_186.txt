Bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/04/2015 - 18:36 Location: Oakwood Gate, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a bin fire off Oakwood Gate in Warrington. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
