 Car collides with a tree in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/09/2018 - 08:28 Location: Daresbury Expressway, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Widnes At around 8.25am firefighters were called to a report of a car having collided with a tree on the Daresbury Expressway in Warrington. On arrival crews discovered that no-one was trapped in the vehicle. Firefighters made the car safe and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
