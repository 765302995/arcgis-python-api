 Fire near the M56 in Frodsham Time of Incident: 09/10/2018 - 17:40 Location: Hatley Lane, Frodsham. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters from Runcorn were called to reports of a smoke billowing over the M56 near Frodsham. Crews attended the scene and discovered a fire in a field at the side of the carriageway. The team were assisted by the owner of the land who used a digger to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance at the scene for approximately 40 minutes.
