 Boiler fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 20/06/2019 - 07:07 Location: Bickerton Road, Malpas. Attendance: Two fire attended from Chester and Whitchurch Shortly after 7am firefighters were dispatched to reports of afire at a residential property in Malpas.  On arrival the crews discovered a fire approximately one metre by one metre which involved an oil fuelled boiler located outside the house.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose jet to extinguish the fire.  The crews were in attendance for around one hour.
