Two vans on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 08/02/2015 - 21:07 Location: City Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Two vans which had set on fire in Chester required firefighters to use water from two hose reels to extinguish it. The two vans were next to each other on a car park and crews pushed a third van out of the way, to avoid it being involved in the fire. It is believed that this fire started accidentally.
