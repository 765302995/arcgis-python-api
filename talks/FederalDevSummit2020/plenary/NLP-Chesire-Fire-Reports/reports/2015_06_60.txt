Building fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 19/06/2015 - 17:49 Location: Langford Road, Northwich. Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich and Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a property on fire off Langford Road, Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving a mattress. Crews dealt with the fire, which was started accidentally, and carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving. There were no reports of any injuries.
