 Fire in industrial building in Runcorn Time of Incident: 15/04/2018 - 11:37 Location: Barlow Way, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm and one from Northwich Fire crews were called to a fire in a waste compactor at a industrial site on Barlow Way, Runcorn. The fire involved approximately ten thousand tonnes of waste and was extinguished using a deluge system on site before arrival of the fire crews. Crews checked the site using a thermal imaging camera as a precaution.
