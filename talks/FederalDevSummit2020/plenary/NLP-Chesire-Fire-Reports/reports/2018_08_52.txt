 Man trapped following a collision in Nantwich Time of Incident: 19/08/2018 - 19:22 Location: Middlewich Road, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Nantwich and one from Crewe At around 7.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision at Sainsbury's supermarket on Middlewich Road in Nantwich. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved one vehicle and that a man was trapped inside it. Firefighters removed the driver's door to create space and then removed the casualty from the vehicle. They handed him over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
