Kitchen fire in Littleton Time of Incident: 03/10/2017 - 14:51 Location: Stamford Court, Littleton Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and Northwich A tumble dryer was the cause of a kitchen fire at a property on Stamford Court. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, put out the fire and removed the dryer outside. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke.  Please follow our tumble dryer safety advice.
