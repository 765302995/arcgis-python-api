 Bedroom fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 26/04/2018 - 17:43 Location: Wyche Avenue, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Nantwich and one from Crewe At around 5.40pm firefighters were called to a house fire in Wynche Avenue, Nantwich.  On arrival the crews discovered that the fire involved an upstairs bedroom, which was well alight, and that all persons were accounted for.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a covering jet to put out the fire and a large high pressure van to clear the smoke from the property.  The crews then damped down affected areas and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.  A woman suffering from smoke inhalation was handed over to the care of North West Ambulance Service paramedics.  Fire crews were in attendance for more than four-and-a-half hours.
