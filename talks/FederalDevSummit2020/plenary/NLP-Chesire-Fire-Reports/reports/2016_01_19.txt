Fire in bathroom in Alsager Time of Incident: 24/01/2016 - 11:07 Location: Audley Road, Alsager Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach, Alsager and Crewe attended Firefighters were called to a small fire involving a fan in a bathroom which was out on their arrival. They cut away plaster to assess the incident and used a thermal imaging camera to check that the fire was out. The crew gave first aid to one person suffering from smoke inhalation. Paramedics also attended.
