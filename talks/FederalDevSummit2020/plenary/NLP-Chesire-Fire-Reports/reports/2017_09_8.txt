Shed on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 27/09/2017 - 19:25 Location: Finsbury Walk, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to a shed on fire in Winsford and used a hose reel to extinguish it. The wooden shed measured seven foot by five foot and firefighters were in attendance for approximately 45 minutes. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone knows anything about how this fire started then they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
