Smoke and fumes in premises in Tabley Time of Incident: 06/07/2016 - 01:23 Location: Northwich Road, Tabley Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Greater Manchester Smoke and fumes caused by an oil leak in machinery in a commercial premises in Tabley required firefighters to attend. The crew used a hose reel and an environment protection pack to prevent the oil from entering drains.
