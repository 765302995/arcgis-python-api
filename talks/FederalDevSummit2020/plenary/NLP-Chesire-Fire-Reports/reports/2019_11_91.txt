 Tiled chimney fire in Pott Shrigley Time of Incident: 11/11/2019 - 21:46 Location: Shrigley Road, Pott Shrigley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington At around 9.45pm firefighters were called to reports of a tiled chimney fire at a domestic property on Shrigley Road, Pott Shrigley.  On arrival the crew discovered that the incident involved a fire grate and that an occupier was suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation.  She was handed over to the care of paramedics.  Firefighters assessed the fire grate, aided by a thermal imaging camera.  They found no signs of burning and left the scene once they were satisfied that the property was safe.  Firefighters were in attendance for nearly an hour.
