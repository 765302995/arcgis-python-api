 Horse box LPG cylinder leaking in Broxton Time of Incident: 13/06/2019 - 17:07 Location: Bolesworth Hill Road, Broxton, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and one from Chester Firefighters were called to what was believed to be a horse box on fire in Broxton. On arrival they found the vehicle was not involved in a fire but that an LPG cylinder under the vehicle was leaking and gassing off. The cylinder was checked and allowed to cool and the power to the vehicle was isolated while the incident was dealt with.
