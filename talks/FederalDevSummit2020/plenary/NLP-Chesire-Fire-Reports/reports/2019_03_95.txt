 Collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 09/03/2019 - 23:27 Location: Old Park Road, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to a one vehicle collision in Old Park Road, Crewe. Two casualties were medically trapped in the vehicle. Crews stabilised the vehicle and removed the roof to enable the casualties to be removed and handed into the care of paramedics.
