 Garage fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/07/2018 - 09:26 Location: Trefoil Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood and one fire engine attended from Warrington At 9.26am firefighters from Birchwood and Warrington were mobilised to a report of a building fire on Trefoil Close in Warrington. On arrival crews found a fire involving two garages. Firefighters used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the fire. They were at the scene for approximately two hours and 45 minutes.
