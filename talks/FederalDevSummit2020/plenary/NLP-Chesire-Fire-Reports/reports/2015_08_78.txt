Building fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/08/2015 - 04:47 Location: Halebank Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one from Merseyside Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels to extinguish a fire involving an electrical junction box in a store room in Widnes.  The fire caused a lot of smoke on the first floor of the building and the crew used a large fan to ventilate the area.
