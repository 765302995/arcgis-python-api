 Shed fire in Chester Time of Incident: 25/05/2019 - 01:19 Location: Fairford Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Chester and one form Penketh Firefighters attended to a large shed well alight on Fairford Road in Chester.  Two hose reels were used to put out the fire and a thermal imaging camera was used to check the temperature.   Crews were at the scene for two hours.
