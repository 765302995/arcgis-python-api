Fire in electrical substation in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 03/07/2015 - 01:55 Location: Oil Sites Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters tackled a fire believed to have been started intentionally in an electrical substation.  Engineers from the electricity suppliers made sure there was no power into the substation and the crew used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze before handing the incident over.
