Shed on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/08/2017 - 13:35 Location: Gainsborough Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a shed on fire and used a hose reel to extinguish it. The fire also involved a fence. The crew also used a thermal imaging camera at the incident to check the fire was out before leaving. It is believed that the fire started accidentally due to an unsupervised controlled burning.
