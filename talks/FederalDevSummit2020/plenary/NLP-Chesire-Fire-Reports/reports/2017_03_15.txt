Fire in Warrington field Time of Incident: 27/03/2017 - 18:46 Location: Westbrook Crescent, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A fire in a field off Westbrook Crescent is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters used a water backpack and beaters to put out the fire and are urging anyone with any information about who may have started it to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
