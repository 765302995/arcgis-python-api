Road traffic collision on M6 north junctions 18 to 19 Time of Incident: 25/03/2017 - 00:27 Location: M6 north between junctions 18 and 19 Attendance: Fire engines and specialist vehicles from Holmes Chapel, Sandbach, Knutsford and Winsford A woman was rescued from an overturned car on the M6 northbound carriageway between junctions 18 and 19. Firefighters rescued the casualty from the car and handed her into the care of the ambulance crew.
