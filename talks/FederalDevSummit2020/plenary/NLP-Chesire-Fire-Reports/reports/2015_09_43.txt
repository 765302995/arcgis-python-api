Road traffic collision on M56 west between junctions 10 and 11 Time of Incident: 22/09/2015 - 07:58 Location: M56 west between junctions 10 and 11 Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Warrington Two cars and a heavy goods vehicle were involved in a road traffic collision on the M56 west near Runcorn. A female casualty was assisted from a car by paramedics and taken to hospital as firefighters made the scene safe.
