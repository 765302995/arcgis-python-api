Chimney on fire in Over Peover Time of Incident: 26/01/2017 - 16:24 Location: Stocks Lane, Over Peover Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a heavily smoke logged ground floor of a property and two people with smoke inhalation. The crew used a large fan to remove the smoke from the property. The smoke came from a chimney which was on fire and the crew used specialist equipment to extinguish the flames.
