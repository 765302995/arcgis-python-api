Swan rescued from frozen pond Time of Incident: 04/02/2015 - 10:54 Location: Peckforton Hall Lane, Peckforton. Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas, one from Knutsford and the Animal Rescue Unit from Knutsford Firefighters rescued a swan stuck in a frozen pond in Peckforton. The animal was approximately 7 metres out into the pond and was rescued by the Animal Rescue Team after ensuring all members of the public were well clear.   The swan was brought back to dry land and then taken by the RSPCA officer to Stapeley Grange RSPCA Centre in Nantwich.
