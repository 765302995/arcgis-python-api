House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/04/2016 - 17:01 Location: Troutbeck Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath  Three fire engines were called to a report of a house fire in Warrington. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and a high pressure fan to clear smoke. One casualty was treated by crews for slight smoke inhalation, and was handed into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for around an hour.
