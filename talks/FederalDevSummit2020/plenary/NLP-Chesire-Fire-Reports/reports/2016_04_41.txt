Motorbike on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 22/04/2016 - 03:36 Location: York Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a motorbike which was well alight, believed to be near houses. One hose reel was used to extinguish the flames. No properties were affected by this fire.
