 Transit van fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 19/08/2019 - 01:36 Location: Moorditch Lane, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly after 1.30am firefighters were called to reports of a van being on fire in Moorditch Lane, Frodsham. On arrival the crew found a transit van well alight. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Specialist cutting equipment was used to gain access to fire in the back of the van. Once the fire was out the van was damped down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 489688, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
