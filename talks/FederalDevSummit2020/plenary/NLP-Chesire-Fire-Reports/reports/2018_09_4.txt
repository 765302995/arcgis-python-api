 Fire at a cemetery in Widnes Time of Incident: 28/09/2018 - 11:52 Location: Birchfield Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 11.50am firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Widnes Cemetery and Crematorium on Birchfield Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of intentional burning that was not being controlled. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
