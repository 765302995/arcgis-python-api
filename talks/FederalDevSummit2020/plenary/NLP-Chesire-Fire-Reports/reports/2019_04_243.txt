 Bedroom fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 02/04/2019 - 14:15 Location: Cholmondeley Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Firefighters were called to a fire in the bedroom of a house on Cholmondeley Road in Ellesmere Port.  Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, used one hose reel to put out the fire. Neighbouring properties were then checked as a safety precaution to see if the fire had spread.  One man was checked over by paramedics after suffering from smoke inhalation.
