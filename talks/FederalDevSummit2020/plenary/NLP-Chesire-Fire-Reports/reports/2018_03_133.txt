 Rescue of a man up scaffolding in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/03/2018 - 21:31 Location: Sutton Way, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port. One fire engine from Powey Lane. Firefighters were called o Sutton Way in Ellesmere Port to assist police and paramedics in lowering a man from scaffolding. Crews used line rescue equipment, one nine metre ladder and oxygen therapy. The man was then taken to hospital.
