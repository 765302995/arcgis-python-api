 Tumble dryer fire at Knutsford residential home Time of Incident: 18/09/2019 - 10:06 Location: Royce Court, Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Lymm and one from Northwich Fire crews tackled a fire at a residential premises on Royce Court in Knutsford.  Firefighters located the fire in the laundry room and wearing two breathing apparatus used a hose reel to extinguish the blaze.  The fire is believed to have involved a tumble dryer.  A large fan was used to disperse the smoke and a thermal imaging camera checked the fire hadn't spread and was fully put out.
