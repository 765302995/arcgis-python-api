Garage fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/12/2016 - 13:31 Location: Regency Park, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Two fire engines attended a call to a fire involving a double garage on Regency Park. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used hose reel jets and a covering jet to tackle the blaze. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check there were no hot spots. The debris was then damped down. Firefighters were at the scene for around an hour and a half.
