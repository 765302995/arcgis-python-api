Tumble dryer fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 25/06/2016 - 17:19 Location: Buxton Old Road, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton A tumble dryer was on fire at a Congleton property. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. One casualty, a man, was given oxygen therapy and handed into the care of the ambulance service. Smoke detectors were fitted and activated, alerting the householder to the incident.
