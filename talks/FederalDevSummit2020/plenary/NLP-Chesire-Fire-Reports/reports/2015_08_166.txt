Person trapped by vehicle in Northwich Time of Incident: 05/08/2015 - 12:38 Location: Cheese Hill Lane, Northwich Attendance: Three fire engines from Knutsford, Northwich and Wilmslow  Firefighters have rescued someone who had been trapped by a car. Crews from Knutsford, Northwich and Wilmslow used a winch to remove the car from the person who was then taken into the care of paramedics.
