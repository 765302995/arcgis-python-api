 Farmland fire in Chester Time of Incident: 22/05/2018 - 11:12 Location: Station Lane, Mickle Trafford, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 11.10am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a field on Station Road in Mickle Trafford, Chester. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved farmland measuring roughly 30 metres by 30 metres. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and beaters to extinguish the fire. They then damped the area down to make it safe. The crew were in attendance for more than an hour. It is believed the fire may have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Police on 101.
