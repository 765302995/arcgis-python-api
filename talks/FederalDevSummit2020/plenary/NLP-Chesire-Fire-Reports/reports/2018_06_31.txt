 Large grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/06/2018 - 11:38 Location: Stonehills Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 11.35am firefighters were called to a report of a fire near a school field on Stonehills Lane in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered it was a large grass fire. Firefighters used beaters and water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 35 minutes.
