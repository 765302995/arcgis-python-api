 Fire in an outbuilding at a commercial building in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/12/2018 - 19:26 Location: Lovely Lane, Warrington Attendance: Three fire engines attended - two from Warrington and one from Penketh Shortly after 7.30pm on Christmas Eve firefighters from Warrington and Penketh were dispatched to reports of a fire at a commercial building on Lovely Lane. On arrival the team discovered a fire in an outbuilding which was well alight. Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to tackle the fire. The teal also removed the roof tiles from the building to reveal any hotspots. After extinguishing the fire the team used a large fan to clear the smoke from the building. Crews were detained at the scene for approximately one hour 50 minutes. The cause of the fire remains under investigation and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
