 Kitchen fire, Winsford Time of Incident: 04/01/2019 - 20:48 Location: Chiltern Way, Winsford Attendance:  One fire engine from Winsford and one from Middlewich Crews from Winsford and Middlewich attended following reports of a fire in the kitchen at a house on Chiltern Way. It involved a chip pan and was out on arrival, however, four casualties were suffering from possible smoke inhalation and three attended hospital for precautionary checks.  Firefighters ventilated the property using positive pressure ventilation fans and were in attendance for just over an hour.  For information and advice about using chip pans safely, visit our website https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chip-pans
