 Mattress fire in the open in Crewe Time of Incident: 12/05/2019 - 18:05 Location: Electricity Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Shortly after 6pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Electricity Street in Crewe. On arrival crews found a foam mattress alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 399131, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
