 Smoke alarm activated at domestic property in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/10/2019 - 17:42 Location: Laburnum Grove, Grange, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 5.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at an occupied domestic property in Laburnum Grove, Grange, Runcorn.  On arrival the crew found the occupier in the front garden.  They were believed to be suffering from the effects of inhaling cooking fumes that had triggered the smoke alarm.  They were assessed by paramedics and firefighters used a large fan to clear the fumes from the property.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.  For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
