Diesel spillage in Lymm Time of Incident: 21/03/2017 - 10:37 Location: M56 east, Lymm junction Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to an incident where diesel had leaked from a heavy good vehicle following a road traffic collision with a barrier. The crew used specialist mats to contain and soak up the spillage. Highways agency was informed and the crew awaited the arrival of the recovery truck before leaving.
