 Domestic garage fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/06/2019 - 20:42 Location: Chiltern Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Warrington and one from Penketh Shortly before 8.45pm firefighters were called to reports of a domestic garage being on fire on Chiltern Road in Warrington. On arrival the crews found a garage containing a large amount of refuse well alight. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The crews then made sure that the fire had not spread to other garages nearby. Firefighters removed the burnt material from the garage and were in attendance for around an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
