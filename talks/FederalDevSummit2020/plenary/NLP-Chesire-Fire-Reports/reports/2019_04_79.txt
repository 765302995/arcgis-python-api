 Grill fire in Pulford Time of Incident: 20/04/2019 - 15:02 Location: Wrexham Road, Pulford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Powey Lane Two fire engines were sent to Wrexham Road, Pulford, after a report of a grill fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Crews were at the scene for 20 minutes.
