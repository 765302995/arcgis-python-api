 Sheep rescued in Audlem Time of Incident: 26/10/2019 - 15:16 Location: Moss Hall Lane, Audlem Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters rescued up to 17 sheep which were stuck in flood water in Moss Hall Lane in Audlem.  Animal rescue and the swift water team used farmers machinery and two rafts to bring the sheep to safety.  Sadly it is believed around 20 sheep had already died before crews arrived at the scene.
