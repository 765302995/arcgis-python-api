Water rescue in Northwich Time of Incident: 12/03/2016 - 17:16 Location: A556 Leftwich, Northwich Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich, Winsford, Warrington with rescue boat and the line rescue unit and supporting fire engine from Knutsford Swift water firefighters rescued a female from water next to the A556 near Leftwich. After the casualty had been taken from the water the crews gave her medical care until the ambulance service arrived at the scene. Police were also at the incident to manage traffic.
