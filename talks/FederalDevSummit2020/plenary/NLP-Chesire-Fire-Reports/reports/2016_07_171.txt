Car on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/07/2016 - 04:28 Location: Marsden Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a saloon car which was well alight and extinguished it with a hose reel. Police were requested to attend as it is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone knows who may have started it they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
