 Roof fire in Norley Time of Incident: 18/05/2019 - 19:41 Location: Fingerpost Lane, Norley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich. One fire engine from Winsford. Firefighters were called to a roof fire at an address in fingerpost Lane,  Norley. All occupants were accounted for. Crews used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel to put out the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. and cut away the affected area.
