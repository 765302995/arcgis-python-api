 Grass cuttings and fence fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 22/04/2019 - 17:11 Location: Darnhall School Lane, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford One fire engine was sent to Darnhall School Lane, Winsford, after a report of grass cuttings and a fence on fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
