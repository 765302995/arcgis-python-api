 Fire at Northwich flat Time of Incident: 25/10/2019 - 02:31 Location: Church Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Northwich and one from Lymm Firefighters attended to a flat on Church Road in Northwich.  Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, used a hose reel to put out the fire and a large fan to disperse the smoke.  People inside the property were all accounted for.  As a precaution people inside the flats were advised to evacuate the property and one woman suffered smoke inhalation and was treated by paramedics.  The fire is believed to have been caused accidentally by a burnt fuse board.  Crews remained in attendance for four hours.
