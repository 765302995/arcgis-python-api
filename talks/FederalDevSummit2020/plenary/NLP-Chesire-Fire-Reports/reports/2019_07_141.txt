 Tree fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 07/07/2019 - 15:36 Location: Riverside, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly after 3.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a tree in Riverside, Northwich. On arrival the crew found the 12ft tree smouldering. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
