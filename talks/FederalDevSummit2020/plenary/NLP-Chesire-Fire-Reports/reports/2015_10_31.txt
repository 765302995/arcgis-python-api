Car fire in Neston Time of Incident: 25/10/2015 - 01:40 Location: Change Lane, Neston. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Change Lane, Neston.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved a car on a driveway. A further three cars had been damaged by heat.  Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels and a covering jet  to put the fire out.   A home safety check was also carried out.
