 Tree fire in Stockport Time of Incident: 03/07/2019 - 18:59 Location: Buxton Road West, Stockport Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Fire crews attended to a fallen tree which was alight on Buxton Road West in Stockport.   Two hose reels were used by firefighters to extinguish the blaze.  A small cordon was put in place as a precaution.  The incident was then handed to Cheshire East Highways.
