Fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 30/12/2016 - 20:18 Location: Dee Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Rubbish was, it is believed deliberately, set on fire in Dee Way. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and are appealing for anyone with any information to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 to help stop this criminal activity and to help keep the community safe.
