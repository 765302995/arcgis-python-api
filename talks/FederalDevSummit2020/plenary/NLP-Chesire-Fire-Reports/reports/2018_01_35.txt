 Kitchen fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 24/01/2018 - 15:43 Location: Wrenbury Drive, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich. One fire engine from Winsford. Firefighters attended a kitchen fire at house in Norley Place, off Wrenbury Drive, Northwich. The kitchen was well alight, and firefighters used four breathing apparatus and two hose reel jets.  Crews also used a large fan to disperse the smoke and forced entry to an adjoining property due to it also suffering smoke logging. There was one casualty suffering the effects of smoke inhalation.
