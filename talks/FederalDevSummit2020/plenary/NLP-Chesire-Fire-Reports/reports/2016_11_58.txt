Washing machine fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 19/11/2016 - 10:10 Location: Caesars Close, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn A fire involving a washing machine caused smoke logging to the kitchen of a Runcorn property. Firefighters isolated the power supply and used a high pressure fan to clear smoke. A hose reel jet was used to damp the area down.
