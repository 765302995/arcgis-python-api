Car wash fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 03/07/2016 - 00:58 Location: Lugsdale Road, Widnes. Attendance: Four fire engines from Widnes, Runcorn and Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Tescos on Lugsdale Road, Widnes.  When they arrived they found that there was a fire involving the car wash. Crews made sure that the petrol was isolated before tackling the blaze with hose reel jets.  Crews were detained for 2 hours at the incident.
