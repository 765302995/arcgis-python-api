Bin fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 02/11/2017 - 21:14 Location: Cliff Road, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish a bin fire at Wilmslow Parish Hall, Cliff Road, Winslow. If anyone has any information about who may have started this fire please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
