 Fire in the open in Minshull Vernon that spread to fence panels Time of Incident: 24/08/2019 - 12:44 Location: Nantwich Road, Minshull Vernon Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 12.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Nantwich Road in Minshull Vernon. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning that had spread to nearby fence panels. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 25 minutes.
