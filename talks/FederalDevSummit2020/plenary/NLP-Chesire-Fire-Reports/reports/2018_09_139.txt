 Fire on grass verge of the M6 Time of Incident: 02/09/2018 - 15:05 Location: M6 southbound junction 20 to 19 Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Serivce Fire crews were called to a small fire on a grass verge on the M6 southbound. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
