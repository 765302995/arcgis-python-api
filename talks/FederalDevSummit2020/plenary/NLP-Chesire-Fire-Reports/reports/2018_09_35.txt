 House fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 23/09/2018 - 07:03 Location: Booth Lane, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Sandbach attended Firefighters were called to reports of a house fire on Booth Lane, Middlwich. The open coal fire was out on arrival. Crews used  a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property. Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
