Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 09/05/2015 - 03:27 Location: Raby Court, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port Fire crews attended a report of a flat fire on Raby Court, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire in the kitchen but this had gone out. Firefighters carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving the scene.
