 Small shed fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 18/04/2018 - 19:48 Location: Sunart Close, Wistaston Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Fire crews attended to a shed ablaze in the garden of an address on Sunart Close in Wistaston, crewe.  Firefighters used one hose reel and one thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire.
