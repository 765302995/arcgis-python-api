 Small fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 14:02 Location: Thorneyholme Drive, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters were called to a small fire in woodlands on Thorneyholme Drive, Knutsford. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which had been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire, please contact Cheshire Police.
