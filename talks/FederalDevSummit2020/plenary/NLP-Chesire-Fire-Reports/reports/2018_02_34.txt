 Bonfire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 21/02/2018 - 20:45 Location: Faraday Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port One fire engine was sent to Faraday Road, Ellesmere Port, to tackle a bonfire which had begun to encroach onto other properties. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and stop it spreading.
