 Small fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 10/01/2018 - 19:46 Location: Betchers, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters from Widnes attended reports of a small fire on Betchers. On arrival at the scene the crew found that the fire involved a large pile of rubbish which had been deliberately set on fire. Crews used one extended hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
