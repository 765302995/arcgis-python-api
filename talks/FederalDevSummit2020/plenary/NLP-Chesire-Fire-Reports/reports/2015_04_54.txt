Shed and fence fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 22/04/2015 - 16:55 Location: Dunwoody Way, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe A shed and fence were totally destroyed by what is believed to have been a deliberate fire in Crewe.  Two hose reels were used to extinguish the blaze and firefighters were on the scene for just over half and hour.  Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101, or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
