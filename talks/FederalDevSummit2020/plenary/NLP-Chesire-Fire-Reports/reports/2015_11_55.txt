M6 Road Traffic Collision Junction 18-19 Time of Incident: 20/11/2015 - 12:07 Location: M6 North, J18-19 Attendance: Fire engines from Sandbach, Knutsford and Winsford Firefighters responded to reports of several vehicles involved with a road traffic collision on the M6 North between junctions 18 and 19.  On arrival fire crews quickly released one casualty who was passed into the care of paramedics. Firefighters also assisted in making the scene safe.  Please note that shortly after this incident, there was a second road traffic collision between junctions 16 and 17.  All closures as a result of this incident have now been lifted.
