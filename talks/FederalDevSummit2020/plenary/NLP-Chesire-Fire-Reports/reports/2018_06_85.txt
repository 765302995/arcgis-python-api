 Pan of food on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 24/06/2018 - 12:25 Location: Enfield Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended a fire involving a pan of food at a house in Enfield Road, Ellesmere Port. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel put out the fire which had started after a pan of food had been left on the cooker. A large fan was used to disperse the smoke.
