One vehicle collision near Winnington swing bridge Time of Incident: 21/11/2017 - 10:27 Location: Runcorn Road, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one fire engine from Frodsham attended. Firefighters attended at the scene of a one vehicle collision at the junction of Winnington Lane and Runcorn Road in Northwich. On arrival at the scene a white saloon car had come off the road and overturned near to Winnington swing bridge. A 74-year-old man was released from the vehicle and passed to North West Ambulance service.
