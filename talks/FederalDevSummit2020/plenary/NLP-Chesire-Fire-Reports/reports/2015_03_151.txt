Tree on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/03/2015 - 20:29 Location: Warrington Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath A fire which involved a tree in Warrington required firefighters to use a water back pack to extinguish it. It is believed that the fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information then please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
