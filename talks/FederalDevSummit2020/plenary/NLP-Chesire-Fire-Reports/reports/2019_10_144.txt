 Collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 02/10/2019 - 22:51 Location: M56 eastbound, at Junction 12's exit slip road Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 10.50pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision on the M56 eastbound. On arrival the crew found a car on its side on Junction 12's exit slip road. The driver had got out of the car by himself. Firefighters made the vehicle safe and were in attendance for around 15 minutes. Paramedics and Highways England also attended.
