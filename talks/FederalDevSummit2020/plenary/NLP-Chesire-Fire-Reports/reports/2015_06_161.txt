Horse rescued from trailer in Allostock Time of Incident: 05/06/2015 - 12:21 Location: Middlewich Road, Allostock, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine and the animal rescue unit from Knutsford Jack, a three year old horse, was rescued from a trailer after becoming stuck over the restraining chest bar.   The crews used specialist cutting equipment to remove the bar after Jack was sedated by a vet and the horse was led to safety.
