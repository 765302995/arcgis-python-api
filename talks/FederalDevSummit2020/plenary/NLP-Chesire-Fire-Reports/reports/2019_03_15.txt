 Industrial building fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/03/2019 - 16:24 Location: Barlow Way, Runcorn. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Runcorn and Penketh At 4.24pm firefighters were called to reports of an industrial building fire on Barlow Way, Runcorn.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a small fire involving the cladding of a two-storey building.  Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire, they then removed some of the cladding to ensure the fire had not spread. The team also used a thermal imaging camera to monitor the temperature.  Crews were in attendance at the scene for approximately 30 minutes.
