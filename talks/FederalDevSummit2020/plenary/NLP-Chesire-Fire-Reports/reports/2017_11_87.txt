Jacknifed lorry on the M6 Time of Incident: 09/11/2017 - 12:06 Location: M6 Southbound between junction 20 and 19. Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood. One fire engine from Stockton Heath. Jacknifed lorry on the M6 south between J19-J20 The M6 was closed in both directions following a collision involving a HGV.  The lorry hit the central reservation on the southbound carriageway between junctions 20, Lymm interchange, and 19, Knutsford.  A quantity of fuel split onto the road and as a result the carriageway was closed in both directions. There were no reports of any injuries.  Firefighters were at the scene assisting to contain the fuel leak, and now Highways are working to clear the road.  Local diversions are in place and motorists are advised to avoid the area.
