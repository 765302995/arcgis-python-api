Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/08/2016 - 20:40 Location: Longbarn Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington One person was trapped in a two car collision on Longbarn Lane. Firefighters removed the passenger door to release the casualty, a lady, and handed her into the care of paramedics. A second casualty, a man, was also tended to by the ambulance service. Crews made the scene safe.
