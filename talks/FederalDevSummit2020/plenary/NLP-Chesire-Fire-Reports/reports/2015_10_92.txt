Finger trapped in swing at Stanney Park Time of Incident: 15/10/2015 - 12:34 Location: Stanney Lane, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to assist a female who had trapped her finger in a swing at Stanney Park, Ellesmere Port.  The fire crew used bolt cutters to free the lady who sustained only minor injuries.
