Shed fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/08/2015 - 22:39 Location: St Peters Way, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Warrington. The fire in St Peter's Way, Warrington, was extinguished using two hose reel jets and a home safety assessment was also carried out at the property.  This fire is believed to have been started deliberatley. Police were informed and requested to attend the scene.  Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101 quoting incident number 1082 of 12 August, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
