 Trailer fire in Neston Time of Incident: 10/01/2019 - 17:10 Location: Chester High Road, Neston. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Powey Lane and two from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a fire involving an articulated lorry trailer on Chester High Road, Ness, Neston. The lorry was carrying frozen food.  Crews used three breathing apparatus and two hose reels jets to extinguish the fire and continued to damp down the trailer using two hose reels and two breathing apparatus.  Approximately 44 tonnes of smouldering food products were removed from the trailer using a digger, which they continued to damp down. The vehicle was then towed away from the scene.
