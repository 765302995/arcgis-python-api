Strong smell of burning in a commercial building in Chester Time of Incident: 18/11/2017 - 14:33 Location: Vicars Lane, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters from Chester were called to reports of a strong smell of burning at a commercial building in Vicars Cross, Chester. On arrival the crew discovered that the source of the smell was a 'laser machine', which was then isolated by firefighters.
