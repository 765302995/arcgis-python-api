Small fire at bike track in Winsford Time of Incident: 09/08/2016 - 21:02 Location: Woodford Lane West, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to a small fire containing paper which is believed to have been deliberately lit at a bike track in Winsford. The crew used a hose reel to extinguish the fire. If anyone knows who may have started this fire they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
