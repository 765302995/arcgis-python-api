 One vehicle collision in Chester Time of Incident: 20/12/2018 - 00:16 Location: A55, Huntington, Chester. Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Chester and two from Ellesmere Port. Shortly after 00.15am firefighters from Chester and Ellesmere Port were called to reports of a one vehicle collision on the A55 in Huntington. Firefighters attended the scene and found that one man was trapped in a van which had overturned on the carriageway. The team used specialist cutting equipment to release the casualty and he was passed to the care of paramedics. Crews were detained at the scene for approximately one hour ten minutes.
