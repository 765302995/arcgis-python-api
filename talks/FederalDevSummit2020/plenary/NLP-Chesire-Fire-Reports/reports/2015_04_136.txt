Chimney fire in Chester Time of Incident: 13/04/2015 - 17:58 Location: Long Lane, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine and the aerial appliance from Chester The aerial appliance was used so firefighters could fully inspect the chimney breast of a property in Chester.  There was a small fire inside and the crew removed the chimney pot and used chimney rods to extinguish it. Chimney safety advice
