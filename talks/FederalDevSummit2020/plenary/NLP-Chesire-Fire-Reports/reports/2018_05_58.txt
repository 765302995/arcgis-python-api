 Rescue of person in Nantwich Time of Incident: 23/05/2018 - 10:27 Location: Brown Avenue, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich At approximately 10.30am firefighters from Nantwich were contacted by North West Ambulance Service who requested assistance with an incident involving an elderly lady who had fallen and was trapped in a shower. The team attended the scene and dismantled the cubicle, she was then passed to the care of the ambulance team. Crews were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
