Five cows in the canal in Norbury, Whitchurch Time of Incident: 18/08/2016 - 11:20 Location: Quoisley Lock in Norbury, Whitchurch Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and the rope rescue unit A herd of 50 cows made it to a canal tow path near Quoisley Lock in Norbury.  Five cows had fallen into the canal.  Three cows managed to get out of the canal, but the other two cows were stuck in the water.  Firefighters rescued the cows from the canal.
