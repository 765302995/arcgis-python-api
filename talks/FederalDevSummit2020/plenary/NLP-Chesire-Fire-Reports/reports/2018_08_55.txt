 Small fire in a garden in Knutsford Time of Incident: 19/08/2018 - 15:19 Location: Southfields, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 3.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a domestic property's garden on Southfields, Knutsford. On arrival the crew discovered a small amount of garden rubbish alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
