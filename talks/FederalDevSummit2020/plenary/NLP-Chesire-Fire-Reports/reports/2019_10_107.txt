 Fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/10/2019 - 18:04 Location: Manchester Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly after 6pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Manchester Road in Warrington.  On arrival the crew discovered it was a case of controlled burning.  Due to the location of the fire, the crew extinguished it with a hose reel jet.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
