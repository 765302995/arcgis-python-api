 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/04/2019 - 22:47 Location: Hale Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 10.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a wheelie bin in the open on Hale Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered the wheelie bin well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For advice on preventing your wheelie bins from being targeted by arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
