 Motorbike fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 11/05/2018 - 08:17 Location: Stanthorne Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich A motorbike fire caused damage to the back door of a Stanthorne Avenue property. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and a thermal imaging camera to check there were no further hotspots. An investigation into the cause of the fire is being carried out.
