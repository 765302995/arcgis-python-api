Outbuilding fire in Antrobus Time of Incident: 10/05/2016 - 13:28 Location: Caldwells Gate Lane, Antrobus Attendance: Two fire engines Firefighters tackled a fire in an outbuilding at a property in Antrobus with hose reels. The outbuilding, which measured approximately 10 metres by 5 metres contained building products. The roof of the barn collapsed and an inner cordon was set up around the area.
