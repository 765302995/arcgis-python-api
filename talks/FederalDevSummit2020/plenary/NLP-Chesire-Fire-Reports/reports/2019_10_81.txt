 Skip fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 18/10/2019 - 11:01 Location: Mobberley Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 11am firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Mobberley Road in Knutsford.  On arrival the crew found that the fire involved rubbish in a skip.  Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
