 Fire involving a telephone kiosk in Chester Time of Incident: 23/09/2019 - 08:39 Location: Saighton Lane, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 8.40am firefighters from Chester were called to reports of a fire involving a telephone kiosk on Saighton Lane.  On arrival at the scene the crew found that the phone within the kiosk was smouldering.  The crew used dry powder to suppress the smouldering and a telephone engineer was called to the site.  A temporary cordon was set up around the kiosk was set up as a precautionary measure.  Firefighters were detained at the scene for around 30 minutes.
