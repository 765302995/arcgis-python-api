Road traffic collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 20/02/2015 - 16:54 Location: A41/A550 Childer Thornton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Fire and rescue crews have been dealing with a road traffic collision on the A41/A550 at Childer Thornton.  The incident involved two vehicles, with a female casualty trapped in one of them.  Police stopped traffic as firefighters released the casualty by removing the side door of the car with specialist cutting equipment.  She was then handed into the care of the ambulance service and taken to hospital as the crews made the area safe.
