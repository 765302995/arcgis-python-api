 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/07/2018 - 15:22 Location: Laburnum Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. One fire engine from Birchwood. Firefighters were called to an address in Laburnum Avenue in Woolston following reports of a car on fire in a garage. Crews used four breathing apparatus and two hose reels to extinguish the fire which had spread to a neghbouring shed and fence. The fire is believed to have been due to an electrical fault.
