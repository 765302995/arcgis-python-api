Road traffic collision in Chester Time of Incident: 18/02/2016 - 09:33 Location: Blacon Avenue, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Fire and rescue crews have been at the scene of a road traffic collision in Blacon, involving a car which collided with a house.  Two casualties, a man and a woman were treated by fire crews and ambulance staff.  Both casualties were taken to hospital by ambulance.  Blacon Avenue was closed while emergency services dealt with the incident. Structural engineers were asked to attend as the front of the property is unstable.
