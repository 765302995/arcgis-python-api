 Kitchen fire in Cranage Time of Incident: 09/12/2019 - 08:44 Location: Byley Lane, Cranage. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Middlewich and Northwich At approximately 8.45am firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a house on Byley Lane, Cranage.  On arrival at the address the crews found a large fire in the kitchen.  Four firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets and a covering jet to extinguish the fire, before using a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.  Crews also isolated the gas and electricity at the address.  One person was treated paramedics at the scene, he has since gone to hospital as a precautionary measure.  Firefighters were in attendance for around two hours.
