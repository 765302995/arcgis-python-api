 Fire in the open in Winsford Time of Incident: 07/08/2018 - 08:29 Location: Over Fair Close, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At around 8.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving an aerosol can the open in Over Fair Close in Winsford. The fire was out on arrival. The crew used water to make sure the affected area was safe and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
