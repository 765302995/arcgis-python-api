 Boiler fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 14/05/2018 - 05:18 Location: Burlea Close, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At 5.18am firefighters from Crewe were called to reports of a house fire at an address on Burlea Close. On arrival at the scene crews found that the fire involve a boiler.  Crews used two breathing apparatus and one covering jet to extinguish the fire; they then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property. The team were in attendance for approximately one hour.
