 Fire at Pizza Hut in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/09/2018 - 17:16 Location: Trident Retail and Leisure Park in Halton Lea, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Widnes At around 5.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at Pizza Hut on the Trident Retail and Leisure Park in Halton Lea, Runcorn. On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved electrics in a large refrigeration unit and that all persons were accounted for. The gas and electricity supply to the building was isolated and adjacent buildings were evacuated as firefighters worked at the scene. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews also used a covering jet. Firefighters searched the adjacent cinema to check if fire or smoke from Pizza Hut had spread. Once the fire was out crews used two large high pressure fans to clear the smoke. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
