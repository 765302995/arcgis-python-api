Fire on Orford Park in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/04/2017 - 23:35 Location: Orford Park, Alder Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Warrington firefighters used a water backpack to extinguish a fire on Orford Park.  Police also attended this incident.  If anyone knows anything about how this fire may have started they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
