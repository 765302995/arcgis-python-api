 BBQ fire in Willington Time of Incident: 14/07/2018 - 19:26 Location: John Street, Willington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Winsford Firefighters were called to a BBQ fire at John Street. Crews used a hose reel to put the fire out and to cool a propane cylinder. You can find more information about how to use BBQs safely here - BBQ safety advice
