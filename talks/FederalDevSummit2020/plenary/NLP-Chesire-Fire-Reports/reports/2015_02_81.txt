Large vehicle on fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 09/02/2015 - 06:01 Location: M6 between junctions 18 and 17 southbound Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe, Sandbach, Holmes Chapel and Middlewich Firefighters were called to attend to a large vehicle which was well alight on fire in the M6.  Police closed the motorway while the fire was dealt with using two hose reels.
