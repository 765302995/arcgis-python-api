Stolen car involved in a road traffic collision in Culcheth, Warrington Time of Incident: 10/07/2016 - 02:32 Location: Holcroft Lane, Culcheth / Glazebury Attendance: One fire engine from Leigh A stolen car was involved in a road traffic collision on Holcroft Lane in Culcheth.  No other cars were involved, but the stolen car flipped over and hit a tree and a lamp post. Police attended this incident.  Fire crew made the vehicle safe.
