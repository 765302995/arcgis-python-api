Deliberate motorbike fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/08/2015 - 20:49 Location: Bexhill Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters extinguished a fire involving a motorbike which is believed to have been started deliberately.  Police were informed and requested to attend the scene.  Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101 quoting incident number 1049 of 11 August, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
