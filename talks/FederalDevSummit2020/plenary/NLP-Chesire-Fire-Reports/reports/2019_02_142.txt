 Smoke logged domestic property in Chester Time of Incident: 02/02/2019 - 16:12 Location: Chapel Close, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Shortly before 4.15pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke at a domestic property in Chapel Close, Chester. On arrival crews discovered a smoke alarm was sounding and that the smoke was caused by a fire involving food being cooked that had been left unattended. The fire was out on arrival and the property was heavily smoke logged. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
