Cooking incident in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/01/2017 - 19:36 Location: Church Street, Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Warrington and Stockton Heath Firefighters responded to a report of a fire at a three storey building on Church Street, Warrington.  Residents were evacuated while crews investigated the incident which turned out to be caused by smoke from cooking that had been left unattended.
