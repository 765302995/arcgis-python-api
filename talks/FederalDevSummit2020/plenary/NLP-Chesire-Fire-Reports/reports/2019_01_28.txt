 Large rubbish fire, Marthall Time of Incident: 06/01/2019 - 04:15 Location: Chelford Road, Marthall Attendance:  Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters attended following concerns reported by police colleagues of rubble from a demolished building smouldering. They arrived to find a large rubbish fire measuring approximately 10m x 10m and 3m high. They used a hose reel to extinguish it due to environmental concerns and were in attendance for around two hours.
