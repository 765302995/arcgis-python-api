 Rubbish fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 21/04/2018 - 18:14 Location: Woodhatch Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters attended to a large amount of rubbish being burnt at the back of a property on Woodhatch Road in Runcorn. A hose reel was used by crews to extinguish the fire.
