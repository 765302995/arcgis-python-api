Road traffic collision near Chester Time of Incident: 02/04/2017 - 23:44 Location: B5132/A5117 junction at Elton Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters made the scene safe after a car was involved in a road traffic collision on the B5132/A5117 junction at Elton. One casualty, a woman, was taken to hospital. The car had hit a lamp post and turned over.
