 Car fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 22/10/2018 - 20:20 Location: Beeston Drive, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters from Winsford attended reports of a car fire on Beeston Drive. On arrival crews found that a Ford C-Max was well alight. One firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately one hour 20 minutes.
