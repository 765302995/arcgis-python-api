 Kitchen fire in Cranage Time of Incident: 05/04/2019 - 13:35 Location: Byley Lane, Cranage Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich and one fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to a kitchen fire on Byley Lane, Cranage. On arrival crews found a small amount of smoke in the property which had come from an unattended pan on the hob. Crews ventilated the property and removed the pan.
