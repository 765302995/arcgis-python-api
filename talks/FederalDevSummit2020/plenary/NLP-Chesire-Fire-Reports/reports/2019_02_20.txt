 Campfire in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/02/2019 - 14:16 Location: Redwood Drive, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 2.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Redwood Drive in Crewe. On arrival the crew found that it was a small campfire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for less than 15 minutes.
