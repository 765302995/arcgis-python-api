Road traffic collision in Cuddington Time of Incident: 30/07/2015 - 14:58 Location: Norley Road, Cuddington, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Frodsham One car was involved in a road traffic collision, leaving one casualty needing assistance from the emergency services. The car was on its side in the roadway and firefighters assisted the female casualty through the tailgate of the vehicle before handing her into the care of paramedics. They then made the scene safe.
