Wheelie bin blaze in Congleton Time of Incident: 08/03/2015 - 05:38 Location: West Street, Congleton. Attendance: A fire engine from from Congleton Firefighters were called to a report of a fire outside the Dental Centre on West Street, Congleton.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved three wheelie bins that had been pushed up against the building. Crews used hose reel jets to deal with the blaze.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
