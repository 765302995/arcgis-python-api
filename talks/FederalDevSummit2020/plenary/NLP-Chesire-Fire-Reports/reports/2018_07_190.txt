 Fire in a garden in Knutsford Time of Incident: 10/07/2018 - 12:15 Location: Chelford Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 12.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a garden on Chelford Road in Knutsford. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a hedgerow and garden waste. They used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which was a case of controlled burning that had been left unattended. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
