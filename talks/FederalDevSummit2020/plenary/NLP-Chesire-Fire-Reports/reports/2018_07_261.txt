 Compost heap fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/07/2018 - 09:59 Location: Smithy Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a large fire involving a compost heap, trees and adjacent fencing in Smithy Lane, Warrington. Crews extinguished the flames and were there approximately 50 minutes.
