 Fire in the open in Kingsley Time of Incident: 07/08/2018 - 14:00 Location: Norley Road, Kingsley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one fire engine attended from Widnes At 2pm firefighters from Runcorn and Widnes attended a large field fire near to Wharton's Lake Garage in Kingsley. The team found that the fire was approximately 300 metres square and was well alight. The team used hose reel jets to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately one hour 15 minutes.
