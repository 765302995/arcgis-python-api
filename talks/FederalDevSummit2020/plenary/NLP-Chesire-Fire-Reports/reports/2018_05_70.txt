 Two-vehicle collision in Chester Time of Incident: 21/05/2018 - 16:17 Location: Station Lane, Mickle Trafford, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Powey Lane and one from Chester At around 4.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision involving two vehicles on Station Lane in Mickle Trafford, Chester. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved two saloon cars. One of the cars had rolled into a field. The three people inside had got out and were assessed by paramedics. One person was trapped inside the other car. Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to remove the roof and get her out of the vehicle and then handed her over to the care of paramedics. In total there were five casualties. Casualties were taken to hospital, some by air ambulance. Firefighters were in attendance for around two hours.
