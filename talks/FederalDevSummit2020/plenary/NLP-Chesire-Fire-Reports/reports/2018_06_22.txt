 Small grass fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 27/06/2018 - 21:18 Location: Bradford Road, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters from Winsford used a handheld appliance to tackle a small grass fire on the banks of the river Weaver in Winsford.
