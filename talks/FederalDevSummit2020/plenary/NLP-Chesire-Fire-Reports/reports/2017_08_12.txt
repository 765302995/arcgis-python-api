Flat fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 29/08/2017 - 06:31 Location: Halton Lodge Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Runcorn A lava lamp left on top of a cooker is believed to have been the cause of a flat fire on Halton Lodge Avenue. The fire was out but left heavy smoke logging in the property which was cleared by firefighters using a high pressure fan.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice and never leave items on top of cookers.
