 Fire in a loft in Somerford Time of Incident: 09/06/2019 - 14:50 Location: Holmes Chapel Road, Somerford Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Congleton and one from Sandbach At 2.50pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from the loft of a domestic property on Holmes Chapel Road in Somerford. A fire believed to involve a cable leading to a solar panel isolation switch was out on arrival. The electrics were isolated and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus utilised a dry powder extinguisher as a precaution. They also used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots in the loft space and make sure that the property was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour.
