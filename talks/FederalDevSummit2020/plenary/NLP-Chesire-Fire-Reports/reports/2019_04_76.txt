 Horse rescued in Winwick Time of Incident: 21/04/2019 - 06:33 Location: Alder Root Lane, Winwick Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and the rope rescue team from Lymm Fire crews were called to help rescue a horse which had become stuck in a gate on Alder Root Lane, Winwick. The horses back legs had become stuck over the gate and fire crews dismantled the gate to release the horse.
