 Flat fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 27/06/2019 - 15:35 Location: Wagg Street, Congleton Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Congleton, one from Sandbach and one from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a flat on Wagg Street, Congleton, following reports of a fire. On arrival no-one was in the flat. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel to extinguish the fire. Firefighters then used a large fan to disperse the smoke. They isolated the electricity supply and checked other flats to ensure that the fire had not spread. They discovered that the fire and smoke had been contained to the one flat. Firefighters removed affected items from the flat and checked for hotspots using a thermal imaging camera. The cause of the fire is being investigating.
