Van and lorry in collision on A500 at Barthomley Time of Incident: 07/11/2016 - 05:42 Location: A500 Barthomley, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe, one from Sandbach and one from Winsford Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving a lorry and a van on the A500. Crews released one casualty from one of the vehicles and handed them into the care of paramedics. They then made the scene safe and ensured no fluids from the vehicles entered the drainage system.
