Building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/10/2017 - 18:20 Location: Marsh House Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one fire engine attended from Birchwood On the 20 October 2017 at 6:20pm one fire engine from Warrington fire station and one fire engine from Birchwood fire station attended a building fire on Marsh House Lane, Warrington.  The fire involved a range in a building and crews used two breathing aparatus, one hose reel and a positive pressure ventilation fan to extinguish the fire.  The fire service were in attendance for one hour and thirty five minutes.
