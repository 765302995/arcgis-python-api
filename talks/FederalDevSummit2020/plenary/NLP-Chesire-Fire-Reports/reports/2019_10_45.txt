 Garden shed fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 24/10/2019 - 02:36 Location: Chestnut Avenue, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield attended Shortly after 2.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving an outbuilding in Chestnut Avenue, Macclesfield.  The fire involving a small empty shed in the back garden of a domestic property was extinguished prior to fire crews arriving.  Firefighters damped the shed down to make it safe and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.  They were in attendance for around 45 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 546716, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
