 Serious collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/10/2018 - 13:17 Location: Southern Expressway, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn. One fire engine from Widnes. Firefighters were called to a serious collision on the Southern Expressway in Runcorn. The collision involved a casualty trapped in a vehicle.  Crews worked to free the casualty while paramedics treated the casualty.
