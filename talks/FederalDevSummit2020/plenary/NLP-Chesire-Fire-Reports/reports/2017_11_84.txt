Mattress on fire at park in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 09/11/2017 - 19:28 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to reports of a mattress on fire in a park on Thornton Road in Ellesmere Port. A hand appliance was used to extinguish the fire.
