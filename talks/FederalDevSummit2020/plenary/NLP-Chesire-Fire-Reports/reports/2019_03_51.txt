 Car fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 19/03/2019 - 19:15 Location: Macclesfield Road, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters attended to reports of a car on fire in a layby on Macclesfield Road in Alderley Edge.  Wearing one breathing apparatus, one hose reel was used to put out the fire which had also spread to nearby hedges and trees.   A thermal imaging camera was also used.
