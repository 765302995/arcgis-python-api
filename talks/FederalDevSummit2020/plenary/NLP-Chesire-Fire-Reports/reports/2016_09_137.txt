Garage fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 02/09/2016 - 04:04 Location: Dark Ark lane, Frodsham Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Firefighters were called to a fire involving an outbuilding at a domestic property on Dark Ark Lane, Manley in Frodsham. On arrival, crews found one detached garage was well alight and so they used two hose reel jets to tackle the fire.
