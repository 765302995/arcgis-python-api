 Park bench and picnic table fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 22/07/2018 - 19:54 Location: Boddington playing fields, Cliff Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 7.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at Boddington playing fields on Cliff Road in Wilmslow. On arrival the crew discovered a park bench and picnic table smouldering. The fire, which was out on arrival, was caused by a barbecue. Firefighters used water to make the area safe and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
