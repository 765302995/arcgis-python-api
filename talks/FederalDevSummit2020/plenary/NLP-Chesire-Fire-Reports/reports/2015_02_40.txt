Collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 16/02/2015 - 23:43 Location: Runcorn Road in Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a collision in Runcorn Road in Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved a car and a lorry. One man was trapped in the car. Crews used specialist equipment to cut the man free. He was then handed to the care of the ambulance service.
