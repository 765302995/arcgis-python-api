Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 14/07/2017 - 13:36 Location: Queens Avenue, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Poynton Firefighters in breathing apparatus tackled a kitchen fire at a house on Queens Avenue. The crew used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control. The gas and electricity supplies was isolated as they tackled the fire and damping down was carried out. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke.  Kitchen safety advice
