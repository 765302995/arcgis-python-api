 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 20/04/2019 - 09:24 Location: Frank Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Fire crews were called to a wheelie bin fire on Frank Street, Widnes. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which had also spread to a fence panel. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any informaiton please contact Cheshire Police.
