 Wheelie bin fire in Davenham Time of Incident: 19/05/2018 - 13:40 Location: Hartford Road, Davenham, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters from Northwich used one hose reel jet to extinguish a wheelie bin fire on Hartford Road, Davenham. Crews were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes. For more information about wheelie bin safety visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
