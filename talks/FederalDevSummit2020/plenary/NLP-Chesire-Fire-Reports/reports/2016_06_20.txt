Caravan destroyed by fire at Stretton Time of Incident: 25/06/2016 - 22:04 Location: Tarporley Road, Stretton, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service A caravan was completely destroyed in what is believed to have been a deliberate fire at Stretton. Crews tackled the blaze but were unable to save the caravan. Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
