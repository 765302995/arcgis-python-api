 Bedroom fire in Chester Time of Incident: 05/03/2018 - 19:25 Location: Lorne Street, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Chester. One fire engine from Powey Lane. Firefighters were called to a fire in a bedroom in a house on Lorne Street, Chester. Crews used one hose reel, one covering jet and two breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check to for hotspots and a large fan to disperse the smoke.
