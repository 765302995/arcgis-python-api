 Small refuse fire in Daresbury Time of Incident: 10/07/2018 - 09:04 Location: Red Brow Lane, Daresbury Attendance: One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath At around 9am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Red Brow Lane in Daresbury. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a small quantity of refuse. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around five minutes.
