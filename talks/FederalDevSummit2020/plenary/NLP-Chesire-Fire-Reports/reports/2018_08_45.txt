 Collision near Northwich Time of Incident: 21/08/2018 - 07:25 Location: Marston Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly before 7.30am firefighters from Northwich were called to reports of a two-vehicle collision on Marston Lane, Marston. The incident involved a car and a van and occurred near to Four Lanes Garage. The team made the vehicles safe and administered first aid to two casualties in the car until the arrival of paramedics.
