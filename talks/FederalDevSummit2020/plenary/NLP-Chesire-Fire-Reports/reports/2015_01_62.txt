Outhouse on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/01/2015 - 09:20 Location: Grappenhall Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath Firefighters tackled a fire in a single storey brick outhouse.  Fortunately the house was not affected.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.  Crews then stripped away the roof and damped the area down.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check there were no further hot spots.  The fire is believed to have been caused by discarded ashes.
