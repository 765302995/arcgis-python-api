Garage fire at a Knutsford pub Time of Incident: 11/06/2016 - 02:33 Location: Brook Street, Knutsford. Attendance: Fire engines from Knutsford and Altrincham Firefighers were called to a report of a fire at the Legh Arms in Knutsford.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving a garage. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jets to bring the fire under control.
