Road traffic collision in in Chester Time of Incident: 07/05/2015 - 06:31 Location: A55/A41 Great Boughton, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Two vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision near Great Boughton.  The collision involved a van and a heavy goods vehicle.  A man was trapped in the van in the passenger seat and released by firefighters using specialist cutting equipment.  He was taken to hospital by the ambulance service.
