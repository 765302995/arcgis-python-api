Hedgerow and bale on fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 09/08/2015 - 18:25 Location: Dark Lane, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas Two electrical cables had come down on scrubland causing a fire. Around 50 yards of hedgerow and a bale were involved in the fire. Firefighters used a high pressure hose reel jet to tackle the b laze.  A fire break was created using a tractor to dig out a channel and the cable supply was isolated by the utility company. The crew then checked the incident area using a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no further hot spots.
