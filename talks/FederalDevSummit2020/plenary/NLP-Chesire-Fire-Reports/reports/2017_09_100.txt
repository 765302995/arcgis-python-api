Hay fire in Chorley Time of Incident: 03/09/2017 - 02:33 Location: Fire Tree Lane, Chorley Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich A small fire had spread from a brazier to hay that was nearby (approx. 10m by 5m of loose hay). The fire was out when the crew arrived, but firefighters used a hose reel to ensure the fire was completed out, and they used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
