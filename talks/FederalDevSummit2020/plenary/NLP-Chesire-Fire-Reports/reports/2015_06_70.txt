Small fire in toilets in Ellesmere Port supermarket Time of Incident: 18/06/2015 - 16:22 Location: Morrisions Supermarket, Chester Road, Whitby Attendance: Two fire engines A small fire in the toilets of Morrisons Supermarket required the store to be evacuated. The fire was out on the arrival of crews who dealt with the smoke before leaving. This fire is believed to have been started deliberately and Police attended.
