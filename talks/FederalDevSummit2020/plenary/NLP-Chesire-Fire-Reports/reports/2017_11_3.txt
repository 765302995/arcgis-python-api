House fire in Doddington Time of Incident: 28/11/2017 - 22:55 Location: London Road, Doddington Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe and one from Nantwich Fire crews were called to a house fire on London Road, Doddington. On arrival crews found a fire in the living room of the property and four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property and a thermal imaging camera to check the property.  Crews left the property at 4:40am.
