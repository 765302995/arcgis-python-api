 Domestic building fire in Stapeley Time of Incident: 01/03/2018 - 16:40 Location: Pear Tree Field, Stapeley. Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Crewe attended. Firefighters attended a fire involving a gas mains box of a pub on Pear Tree Field, Stapeley. They used a hose reel jet, a covering jet, a large fan and two breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire.  Crews were in attendance for two hours and 30 minutes.
