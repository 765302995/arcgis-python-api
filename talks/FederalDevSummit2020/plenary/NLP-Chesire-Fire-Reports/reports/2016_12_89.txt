Collision in Chester Time of Incident: 14/12/2016 - 17:49 Location: Marston-cum-Lache, Chester. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Chester Fire crews responded to a report of a collision on the A55 Marston-cum-Lache, Chester.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two cars. There was nobody trapped so firefighters ensured that the scene was safe before leaving.
