 Shetland pony rescued in Mobberley Time of Incident: 12/01/2018 - 14:24 Location: Ostlers Lane, Mobberley Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow, one from Lymm and the animal rescue unit from Lymm Fire crews were called to help rescue a Shetland pony which was trapped in a metal saddle rack by its left rear leg on Ostlers Lane, Mobberley. Crews used hydraulic cutting equipment to release the pony.
