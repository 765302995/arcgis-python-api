Smell of burning in Macclesfield restaurant Time of Incident: 29/02/2016 - 23:03 Location: Pizza Express Market Place, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a smell of burning in the basement of a restaurant on Market Place. The crews investigated the premises using a thermal imaging camera but could find no heat source. The occupiers were advised to call an electrician.
