 One-vehicle collision in Nantwich Time of Incident: 11/03/2019 - 08:59 Location: B5074, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich, as did a co-responder vehicle from Nantwich Shortly before 9am firefighters were called to a report of a one-vehicle collision on the B5074 in Worleston, Nantwich. On arrival the crew found the vehicle, a saloon car, on its side. No-one was trapped inside the car but the vehicle was leaking fuel. Firefighters isolated the vehicle's battery and stopped the leak. They also soaked up fuel from the road using an environmental grab pack. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour. Police arranged for the car to be recovered, and for Highways England to clean up the road.
