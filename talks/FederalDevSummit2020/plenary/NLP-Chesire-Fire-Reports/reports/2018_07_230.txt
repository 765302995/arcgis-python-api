 Grass fire in Cuddington Time of Incident: 05/07/2018 - 11:03 Location: Kennel Lane, Northwich. Attendance: Four fire engines attended - one from Tarporley, one from Lymm, one from Chester and one from Runcorn Shortly after 11am firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a field off Kennel Lane in Cuddington. On arrival crews found approximately 200 square metres of trees and grass well alight. Firefighters used two hose reel jets and beaters to extinguish the fire.
