 Trees on fire at a park in Widnes Time of Incident: 13/04/2019 - 18:17 Location: Crow Wood Park, Bancroft Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Shortly after 6.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Crow Wood Park on Bancroft Road, Widnes. On arrival the crew found a small number of trees on fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 373394, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
