Road traffic collision on A556 at Plumley Time of Incident: 13/07/2016 - 16:39 Location: A556 Chester Road, Plumley Attendance: Fire engines from Knutsford, Northwich and Winsford Fire and rescue crews were at the scene of a serious road traffic collision involving a heavy goods vehicle  and trailer and a cyclist. Firefighters extricated one casualty, who was handed into the care of medical personnel.
