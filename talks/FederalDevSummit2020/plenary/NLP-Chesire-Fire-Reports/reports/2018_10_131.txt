 Road traffic collision, Ledsham Time of Incident: 06/10/2018 - 19:19 Location: Parkgate Road, Ledsham Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Crews responded to reports of a road traffic collision involving two cars on Parkgate Road in Ledsham. They extricated one casualty who was trapped and helped to treat four further casualties, before passing them all into the care of paramedics. Both vehicles were made safe to help traffic management and crews were at scene for slightly over one hour.
