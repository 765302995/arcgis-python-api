 Small fire in the open in Malpas Time of Incident: 09/05/2019 - 15:41 Location: Hall Lane, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas At around 3.40pm firefighters from Malpas were dispatched to reports of a fire in the open on Hall Lane, Malpas. On arrival the crew discovered a fire involving wood and garden waste. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They also used a rake to spread the waste and sprayed water over it to prevent it from reigniting.
