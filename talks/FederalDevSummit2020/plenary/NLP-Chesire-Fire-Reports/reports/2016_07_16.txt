Fluorescent dye coming out of land drain in the River Dee Time of Incident: 27/07/2016 - 13:15 Location: Sandy Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester A green liquid was reported coming out of a land drain that feeds into the River Dee. A fire and rescue crew were called to the scene to work out what the liquid was.  The water safety authority also attended and were able to determine the nature of the liquid. It was found to be a harmless fluorescent dye, which is used to check the flow of drain water.
