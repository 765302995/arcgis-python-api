 Fire in the open that spread to a fence and shed in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/03/2019 - 15:18 Location: Warrington Avenue/Deeside Close, Whitby, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 3.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on Warrington Avenue in Whitby, Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered that controlled burning had spread to a fence and shed at an address in Deeside Close. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for more than an hour.
