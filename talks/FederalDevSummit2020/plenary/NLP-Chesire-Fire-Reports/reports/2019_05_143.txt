 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/05/2019 - 12:32 Location: Cliff Lane, Grappenhall, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Lymm and one from Warrington Shortly after 12.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being of fire on Cliff Lane in Grappenhall, Warrington. On arrival they found the vehicle well alight off the main roadway. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The vehicle was then damped down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
