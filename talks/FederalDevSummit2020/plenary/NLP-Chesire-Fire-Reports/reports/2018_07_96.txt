 Cow rescued froma canal near Nantwich Time of Incident: 19/07/2018 - 11:36 Location: Coole Lane, Coole Pilate Attendance: Four fire crews attended, one fire engine from Lymm, one fire engine from Whitchurch, the animal rescue unit from Bollington and the Rope rescue unit from Lymm. Cow in a canalFirefighters from Lymm, Whitchurch and Bollington attended the Shropshire Union Canal in Coole Pilate following reports that a cow had fallen into the canal.  The team worked with the farmer at the scene and used a rope rescue kit to retrieve the cow from the water. The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately two hours 30 minutes.
