Car on fire in a field in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/11/2015 - 21:41 Location: Squires Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine fron Widnes A vehicle which was on fire in a field was extinguished with a hose reel jet. Police were requested to attend as this fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information as to who may have started this fire they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers  anonymously on 0800 555 111.
