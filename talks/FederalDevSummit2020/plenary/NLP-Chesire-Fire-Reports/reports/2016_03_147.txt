Small fire in a flat in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/03/2016 - 13:36 Location: Killingworth Lane, Birchwood, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine Firefighters were called to a small fire involving paper in a flat. The fire was out on the arrival of the crew who used a large fan to remove smoke from the property. A fire investigation into the cause of this fire is currently underway.
