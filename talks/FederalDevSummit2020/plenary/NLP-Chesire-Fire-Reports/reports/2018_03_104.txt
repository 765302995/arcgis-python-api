 Car fire on the M53 Time of Incident: 13/03/2018 - 19:49 Location: M53, south between junction 10 and 11 Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Fire crews were called to a vehicle fire on the M53 southbound between junction 10 and 11. Crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the car which was well alight on arrival of the crews. Crews were in attendance for 40 minutes.
