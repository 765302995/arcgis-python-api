 Overheated chip pan in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 01/10/2018 - 14:09 Location: Nicholson Avenue, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At around 2.10pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a domestic property in Nicholson Avenue, Macclesfield. On arrival the crew discovered that the alarm had been activated by smoke caused by an overheated chip pan. The chip pan had already been removed from the property when firefighters arrived. Firefighters ventilated the property to remove the light smoke logging and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. For chip pan fire safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chip-pans.
