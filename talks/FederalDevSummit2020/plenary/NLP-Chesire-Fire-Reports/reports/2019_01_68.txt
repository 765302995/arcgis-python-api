 House fire in Chester Time of Incident: 30/01/2019 - 09:07 Location: Meynell Place, Chester Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Chester and one from Powey Lane Shortly after 9am firefighters from Chester and Powey Lane attended reports of a fire at a residential address on Meynall Place.  On arrival at the scene the crews discovered that the fire involved an electrical heater.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  They then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.  Crews were detained at the scene for approximately an hour.
