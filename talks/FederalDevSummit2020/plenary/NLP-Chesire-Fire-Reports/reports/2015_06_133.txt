Fire in workshop in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 09/06/2015 - 01:24 Location: Indigo Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and two from Chester Fire engulfed a workshop in Ellesmere Port and was well alight when firefighters arrived.  The crews used three main jets and a hose reel to get the fire under control and extinguish it. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the area. The tin roofed building had a double decker bus inside which was also affected by the fire and a structure at the back of the building was also involved. A fire investigation into the cause of this fire will take place.
