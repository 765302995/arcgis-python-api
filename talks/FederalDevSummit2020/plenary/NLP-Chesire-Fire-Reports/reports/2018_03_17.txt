 Horsebox fire in Chester Time of Incident: 27/03/2018 - 16:17 Location: Broxton Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Crewe and one from Ellesmere Port At around 4.15pm firefighters were called to a fire involving a horsebox on Broxton Road in Chester. On arrival they discovered that the fire involved an overheated wheel and that a horse had been taken out of the horsebox. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames and firefighters were at the scene for around 35 minutes. The horse is not believed to have been injured by the fire.
