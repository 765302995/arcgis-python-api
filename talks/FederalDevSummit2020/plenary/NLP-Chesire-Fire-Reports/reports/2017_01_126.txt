Fire in cab of articulated lorry in Tarporley Time of Incident: 03/01/2017 - 10:06 Location: Nantwich Road, Tarporley Attendance: Two fire engines from Nantwich and Crewe Firefighters were called to a fire involving the cab of an articulated lorry, in which on site staff had used dry powder to supress the fire. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to put out the fire. Firefighters then dampened the cab down and continued to monitor the temperature until they could be sure the scene was safe.
