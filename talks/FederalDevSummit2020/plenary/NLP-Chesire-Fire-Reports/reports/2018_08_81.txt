 Calf rescued in Scholar Green Time of Incident: 15/08/2018 - 01:50 Location: Station Road, Scholar Green Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar, the rope rescue unit from Lymm and the animal rescue unit from Bollington Calf during rescueFire crews were called to a report of a calf stuck in the canal at Station Road, Scholar Green. Crews rescued a 90kg calf using animal slings. They also assisted the farmer to ensure the animal arrived safely back at the farm.  Crews were detained at scene for approximately one hour and twenty minutes.
