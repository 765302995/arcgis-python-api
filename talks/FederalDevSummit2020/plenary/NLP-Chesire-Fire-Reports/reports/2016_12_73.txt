Two vehicle collision in Wistaston Time of Incident: 18/12/2016 - 13:13 Location: Nantwich Road, Wistaston, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Two cars were involved in a collision on Nantwich Road. An outer cordon was set up by police as firefighters and paramedics assessed the condition of a lady in one of the cars and she was then able to be released and handed into the care of the ambulance. The driver of the second car, a man, was also taken to hospital as a precaution.
