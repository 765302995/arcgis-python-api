 Person trapped following collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/09/2019 - 17:55 Location: A56, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Widnes Fire crews from Runcorn and Widnes were called to a collision on the A56 at Runcorn after reports that a woman was trapped.  Firefighters released the woman and handed her to the care of the ambulance service. Crews were in attendance for just over an hour.
