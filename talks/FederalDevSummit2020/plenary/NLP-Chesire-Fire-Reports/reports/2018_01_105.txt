 Small fire at a shop in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/01/2018 - 11:38 Location: Albert Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one fire engine from Penketh   At 11.38am on Saturday, 6 January fire crews were called to reports of a small fire at a shop on Albert Road in Widnes. One fire engine from Widnes and one from Penketh attended and quickly established the fire involved a cupboard in the property. Two breathing apparatus, one dry powder extinguisher and a covering jet were used to put the fire out. Crews were at the scene for 50 minutes.
