 Van fire in Bosley Time of Incident: 06/04/2018 - 08:02 Location: Leek Road, Bosley. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Van fire in BosleyFirefighters were called to a van well alight in Leek Road, Bosley. Crews used two hose reels and breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. They then damped down the vehicle to prevent hotspots re-igniting.
