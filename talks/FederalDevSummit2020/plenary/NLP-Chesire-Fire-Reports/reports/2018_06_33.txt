 Light fitting fire at a sports shop in Crewe Time of Incident: 27/06/2018 - 10:21 Location: Grand Junction Way, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Crewe and one from Sandbach At around 10.20am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a light fitting at Sports Direct on Grand Junction Way in Crewe. A full evacuation was carried out and fire crews investigated using a thermal imaging camera. The electrics were isolated and firefighters dismantled the light fitting and made sure the area was safe. The fire is believed to have been caused by an over headed reducer in the fitting. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
