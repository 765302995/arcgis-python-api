Wilmslow car fire Time of Incident: 27/03/2017 - 17:51 Location: A538, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton The front brakes of a car were on fire on the A538 in Wilmslow. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to cool the brakes down and a thermal imaging camera to make sure the affected area was fully cooled.
