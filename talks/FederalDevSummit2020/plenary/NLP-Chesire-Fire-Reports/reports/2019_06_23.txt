 Fire in a derelict building in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/06/2019 - 18:26 Location: Hough Green Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to a small fire in a derelict building on Hough Green Road, Widnes. A small fire had been set near to a derelict  building which had set fire to some curtains. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
