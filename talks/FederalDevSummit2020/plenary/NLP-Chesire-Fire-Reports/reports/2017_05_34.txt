Domestic building fire in Lymm, Warrington Time of Incident: 26/05/2017 - 09:18 Location: Oldfield Road, Lymm, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Penketh Firefighters were called to a house on Oldfield Road, in Lymm, Warrington, after a fire started in a bedroom. Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to put out the fire which was on the ground floor of the property.
