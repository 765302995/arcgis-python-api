 Person trapped after crash in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 03/02/2019 - 22:46 Location: Rivacre Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving one vehicle on Rivacre Road, Ellesmere Port. Crews used hydraulic cutting equipment to extract one male casualty from the car before he was handed into the care of paramedics.
