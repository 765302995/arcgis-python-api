Road traffic collision in Hapsford Time of Incident: 14/11/2017 - 19:00 Location: A5117, Hapsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn A fire engine from Runcorn came across a road traffic collision involving one car which had come off the roadway on the A5117, Hapsford. Crews administered first aid to one casualty until the arrival of paramedics.
