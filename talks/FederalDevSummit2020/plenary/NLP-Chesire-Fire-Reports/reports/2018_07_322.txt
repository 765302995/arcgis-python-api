 Grass fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 30/06/2018 - 15:36 Location: James Close, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane Crews tackled a fire in the garden of a house that had been started by the spontaneous combustion of wet grass. This had spread to the nearby conifers and hedge. A single hose reel was used to extinguish the flames and a positive pressure ventilation fan was used to clear smoke form the property next door.
