Overturned car in stream in Congleton Time of Incident: 20/10/2017 - 09:03 Location: Chelford Road, Congleton. Attendance: Specialist water rescue team from Lymm and supporting engine from Warrington Fire crews were called to an overturned car in a stream on Chelford Road, Congleton. The swift water rescue boat was launched so crews could check whether anyone was in the car. No one was found and the incident was handed to the police.
