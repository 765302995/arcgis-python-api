 Fire at a terraced house in Crewe Time of Incident: 19/11/2018 - 14:22 Location: Stalbridge Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At around 2.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a terraced house on Stalbridge Road in Crewe. On arrival crews discovered that all persons were accounted for and that the fire involved a small rubbish bin in a first floor bedroom that had been converted into a home office. The electricity supply to the room was isolated and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. A covering jet was also used. Firefighters then dug out materials that had been damaged by the fire and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. They also used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property.
