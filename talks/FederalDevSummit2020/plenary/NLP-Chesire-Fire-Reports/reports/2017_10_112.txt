Road traffic collision in Willaston Time of Incident: 04/10/2017 - 08:17 Location: Junction of Damhead Lane and Neston Road, Willaston Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Chester Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision between three vehicles at the junction of Damhead Lane and Neston Road, Willaston. Firefighters administered first aid at the scene to two casualties. They were in attendance at the scene for 30 minutes.
