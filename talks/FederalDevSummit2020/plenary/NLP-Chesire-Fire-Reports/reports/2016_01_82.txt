Log burner fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 08/01/2016 - 19:10 Location: Heath Lane, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: A fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Heath Lane, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire in a log burner but this had gone out.  Firefighters used a special fan to clear the smoke and carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving.
