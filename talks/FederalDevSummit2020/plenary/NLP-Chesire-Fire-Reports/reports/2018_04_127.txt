 Chimney fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 06/04/2018 - 10:01 Location: Birch Grove, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a chimney fire at an address in Birch Grove, Knutsford. Crews used chimney rods to put out the fire before using a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots in the chimney stack.
