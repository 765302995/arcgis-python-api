 Trailer on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/04/2019 - 21:46 Location: Old Alder Lane, Burtonwood, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 9.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a trailer being on fire on Old Alder Lane in Burtonwood, Warrington. On arrival the crew found the vehicle well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
