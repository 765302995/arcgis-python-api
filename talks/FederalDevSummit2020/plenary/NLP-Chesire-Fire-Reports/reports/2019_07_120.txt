 Play equipment fire at a park in Runcorn Time of Incident: 11/07/2019 - 02:56 Location: Palacefields Park, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Play equipment on fire in Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Palacefields Park, near to Runcorn Ambulance Station.  On arrival the crew found playground equipment well alight.  Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.  The fire, which left the playground equipment completely destroyed, is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 452595, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
