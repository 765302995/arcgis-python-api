 Small fire at a park in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/04/2019 - 20:42 Location: Woolston Park, Woolston, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Shortly before 8.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at Woolston Park in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for less than 10 minutes.
