Combine harvester fire in Tilston Time of Incident: 03/08/2016 - 12:41 Location: Field near Grange Lane and Scar Lane, Tilston, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and one from Shropshire Fire and Rescue Service A combine harvester was on fire in a field in Tilston, near Malpas. Firefighters used two hose reel jets with foam to tackle the blaze.
