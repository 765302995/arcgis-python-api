Chimney on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 21/04/2016 - 21:56 Location: Crewe Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a chimney on fire and used chimney rods to extinguish it. A ladder was also used to carry out an inspection of the chimney pot and a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots. Chimney safety advice
