Small bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 02/01/2017 - 19:53 Location: Crook lane, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters used one hose reel jet to put out a fire involving a small bin in Winsford.   This fire was believed to have been started deliberately. Do you have any information? Report it by calling Crimestoppers, which can be done anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
