Car fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/05/2015 - 07:54 Location: Central Expressway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters rescued a man from a burning car in Runcorn.  When the crew arrived they found a casualty inside a car which was on fire.  Firefighters gave the casualty first aid until the ambulance arrived.  When he was in the care of paramedics the crew made the vehicle safe and cleared the area.  The air ambulance also attended.
