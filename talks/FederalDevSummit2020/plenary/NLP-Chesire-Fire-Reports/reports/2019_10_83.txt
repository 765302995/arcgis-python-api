 One-vehicle collision in Backford, near Chester Time of Incident: 18/10/2019 - 06:59 Location: Dunkirk Way, Backford, near Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Shortly before 7am firefighters were called to reports of a one-vehicle collision in Dunkirk Way, Backford, near Chester.  Crews removed undergrowth to enable the driver to get out of the vehicle.  He is not believed to have sustained any significant injuries as a result of the incident.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
