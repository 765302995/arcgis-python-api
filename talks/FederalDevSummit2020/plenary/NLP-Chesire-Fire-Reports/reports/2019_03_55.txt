 Accidental gas leak in Handforth Time of Incident: 18/03/2019 - 17:51 Location: Richmond Avenue, Handforth. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Shortly before 6pm firefighters were called to reports of a gas leak at a residential property in Handforth.  Firefighters swiftly attended the scene and on arrival they discovered that a gas cooker had been left on accidentally.  The team ventilated the building and used a gas monitor to monitor gas levels within the address.  One casualty was provided with oxygen therapy at the scene as a precautionary measure; she was then passed to the care of paramedics   The team were detained at the address for approximately 50 minutes.
