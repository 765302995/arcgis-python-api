Dishwasher fire in Willaston near Nantwich Time of Incident: 04/08/2016 - 13:54 Location: Balmoral Place, Willaston Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe A fire in a dishwasher caused smoke logging to a Balmoral Place property. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet at the incident and took the dishwasher outside. They then ventilated the property and checked there were no hotspots of fire.
