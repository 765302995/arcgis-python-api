 Motorbike fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 20/04/2019 - 17:24 Location: Stanneylands Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Fire crews were called to Stanneylands Road, Wilmslow, after a report of a motorbike fire. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
