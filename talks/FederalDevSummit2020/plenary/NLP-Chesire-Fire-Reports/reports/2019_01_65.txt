 Large refuse fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 31/12/2018 - 18:23 Location: Milton Road, Widnes Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Widnes A large amount of refuse is believed to have been set alight deliberately behind houses on Milton Road. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and ask that anyone with any information about this incident call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
