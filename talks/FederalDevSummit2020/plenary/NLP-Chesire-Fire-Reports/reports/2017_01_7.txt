Overturned vehicle on the M56 Time of Incident: 29/01/2017 - 07:33 Location: m56 East J11 to J10 Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn, Warrington and Stockton Heath Firefighters attended a road traffic collision involving one overturned vehicle. As there were no signs of fire, and no one was trapped, firefighters assisted by making the scene safe.
