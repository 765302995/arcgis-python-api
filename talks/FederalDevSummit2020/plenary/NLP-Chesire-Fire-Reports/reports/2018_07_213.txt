 Person trapped following a collision involing two cars and a tractor in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 06/07/2018 - 08:23 Location: Marsh Lane, Holmes Chapel Attendance: Three fire engines are in attendance - from Holmes Chapel, Sandbach and Congleton Firefighters at the scene of a road traffic collision in Holmes ChapelFirefighters dealt with a collision involving two cars and a tractor at a farm on Marsh Lane, Holmes Chapel.  One of the vehicles collided with a single-storey, brick-built outbuilding.  One person was trapped and extracted by firefighters before being handed over to the care of paramedics. Fire crews closed the A54 road while they worked at the scene and a cordon was set up.  A further casualty was also taken to hospital via ambulance.
