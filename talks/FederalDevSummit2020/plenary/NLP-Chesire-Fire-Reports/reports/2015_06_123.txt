Car on fire on M56 Time of Incident: 09/06/2015 - 22:24 Location: M56 junctions 9-10 westbound Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, one from Knutsford and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service A car on the hard shoulder of the M56 between junctions 9 and 10 westbound was on fire.   Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.
