 Two people rescued from their cars after M62 crash Time of Incident: 07/05/2018 - 17:41 Location: M62 eastbound between junction 10 and 11 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, one from Birchwood and one from Lymm Road traffic collision on M62Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision between two cars on the M62 eastbound. Firefighters used cutting equipment to remove one casualty from their vehicle and helped another casualty out of their car. Both carriageways were closed during the incident and one casualty was taken to hospital via air ambulance.  The other casualty was taken to hospital by paramedics. Crews were in attendance for an hour and 40 minutes.
