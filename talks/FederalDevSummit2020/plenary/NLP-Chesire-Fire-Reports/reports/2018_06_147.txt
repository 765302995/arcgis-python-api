 Wheelie bin fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 19/06/2018 - 01:30 Location: London Road South, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton At around 1.30am firefighters were called to a report of a wheelie bin being on fire on a pavement on London Road South in Poynton. The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 10 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call police on 101.
