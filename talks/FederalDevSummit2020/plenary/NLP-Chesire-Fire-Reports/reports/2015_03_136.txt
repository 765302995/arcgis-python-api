Chimney fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/03/2015 - 18:32 Location: Weston Road, Runcorn. Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn and Widnes Firefighrers were called to a report of a house fire on Weston Road in Runcorn.  When they arrived they found that there was a blaze in the chimney. Crews tackled the fire from both the roof and the grate with hose reels and specialist tools.  Chimney safety advice
