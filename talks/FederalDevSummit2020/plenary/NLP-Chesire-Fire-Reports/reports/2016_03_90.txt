Road traffic collision in Chester Time of Incident: 14/03/2016 - 05:48 Location: Junction with Parkgate Road and Long Lane, Saughall, chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Winsford Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving a heavy goods vehicle and a car which was on fire. Two hose reels were used to extinguish the fire. Paramedics attended and police closed the road while the incident was dealt with.
