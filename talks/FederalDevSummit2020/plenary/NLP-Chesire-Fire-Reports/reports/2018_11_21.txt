 Chimney fire in Bunbury Time of Incident: 25/11/2018 - 20:38 Location: Orchard Close, Bunbury. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley Firefighters were called to a chimney fire at an address in Orchard Close, Bunbury. Crews used chimney rods to remove hot ashes from the flue. They then checked for hotspots on the first floor and the loft space with a thermal imaging camera.
