Skip fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 01/11/2017 - 00:44 Location: Laburnum Grove, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish a skip fire on Laburnum Grove on Runcorn. An investigation has been launched into the cause of the fire. If anyone has any information about how this fire may have started please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
