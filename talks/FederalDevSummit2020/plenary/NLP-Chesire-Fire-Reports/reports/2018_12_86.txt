 MIddlewich flat fire Time of Incident: 11/12/2018 - 03:58 Location: Hightown, Middlewich Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Winsford, one from Middlewich and one from Holmes Chapel Crews attended to reports of a fire located in flats in Hightown, Middlewich.  The fire involved a door on the third floor and four people were evacuated from the flats as a safety precaution.  Firefighters, wearing two breathing apparatus, used one hose reel to put out the blaze and a fan to clear the smoke.  A thermal imaging camera was then used to check for any signs the fire had spread to the loft.  Everyone inside the flats were accounted for.
