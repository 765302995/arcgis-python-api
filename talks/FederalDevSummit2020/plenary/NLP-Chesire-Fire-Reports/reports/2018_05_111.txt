 Wheelie bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 17/05/2018 - 00:59 Location: Grangemoor, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a wheelie bin well alight on Grangemoor in Runcorn which had caused minor damage to a garage.  Crews used one hose reel to put out the bin fire which had burned a hole in the garage door.  Firefighters then made the area safe and were in attendance for around one hour and ten minutes.
