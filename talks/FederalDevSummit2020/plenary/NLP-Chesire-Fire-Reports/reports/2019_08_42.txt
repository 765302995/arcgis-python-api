 Two-car collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 24/08/2019 - 12:03 Location: Middlewich Road and Seaton Street junction, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Winsford and one from Middlewich Shortly after 12 noon firefighters were called to a report of a two-vehicle collision on Middlewich Road in Winsford. On arrival crews discovered that the collision at the junction with Seaton Street involved two cars and that no-one was trapped inside them. Firefighters administered first aid before paramedics arrived. The crews then handed the incident over to the police. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
