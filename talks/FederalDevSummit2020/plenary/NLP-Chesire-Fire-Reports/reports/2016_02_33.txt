Wilmslow log burner fire Time of Incident: 22/02/2016 - 10:10 Location: Grove Avenue, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters tackled a fire in a log burner on Grove Avenue. The fire was in the flue area which was difficult for rod access to reach the full height of the chimney. Crews came up with a solution by creating an access point in the loft area so they could get to the fire, using small tools, and put it out.   Please follow our safety advice - Chimneys
