Deliberate fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/04/2015 - 20:33 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes. Handheld extinguishers were used to put out a fire in the open in Widnes.  The fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with information is urged to call police on 101, or alternatively contact Crimes toppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
