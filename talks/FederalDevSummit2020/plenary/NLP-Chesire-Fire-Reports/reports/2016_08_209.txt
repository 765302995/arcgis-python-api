Car collided with house in Davenham Time of Incident: 03/08/2016 - 17:41 Location: Mere Bank, Davenham Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford Firefighters assisted a lady whose car had collided with a house in Mere Bank. She had been able to get out of the car before firefighters arrived at the scene. The crews assessed the damage to the property, which was considerable. They set up a cordon around the scene and asked for a building inspector to attend. An ambulance was also called as a precaution.
