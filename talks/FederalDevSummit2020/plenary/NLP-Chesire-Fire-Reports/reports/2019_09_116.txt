 Small fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/09/2019 - 22:44 Location: Hilden Road, Padgate, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 10.45pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire on a footpath on Hilden Road in Padgate, Warrington. The crew extinguished the small fire with a hose reel jet. Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
