Road traffic collision in Bostock Time of Incident: 02/04/2017 - 08:36 Location: Bostock Road, Bostock, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford A car collided with a wooden telegraph pole, ending up on its roof and leaving the pole in a precarious position. Firefighters released a casualty from the car and moved him to a safe place. The crews then made the scene safe and waited with the ambulance service to assist with the casualty until he was transferred to hospital.
