 Woodland fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 14:30 Location: Glazebrook Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes. At 14.30pm firefighters from Widnes were called to reports of a fire in a woodland area off Glazebrook Lane in Warrington. Crews attended the scene and discovered a woodland area, approximately 30 metres square, which was well alight. The team used two hose reel jets to tackle the fire.
