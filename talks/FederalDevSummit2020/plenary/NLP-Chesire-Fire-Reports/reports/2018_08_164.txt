 Stable barn fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 03/08/2018 - 23:54 Location: Sandymoor Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn, Widnes and Penketh Firefighters attended to a stable barn well alight on Sandymoor Lane in Runcorn.  One hose reel was used by crews to extinguish the fire before ensuring the barn was cooled down and made safe.
