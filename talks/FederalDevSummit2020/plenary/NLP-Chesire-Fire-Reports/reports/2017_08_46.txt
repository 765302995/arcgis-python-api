Small fire in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/08/2017 - 20:43 Location: Budworth Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a mattress which was on fire on Budworth Close, Runcorn. The fire had been started deliberately and crews used a water back pack to put out the fire. If you have any information about the fire please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
