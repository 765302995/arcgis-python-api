 Grass fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 19:58 Location: Mount Pleasant Drive, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At around 7.55pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire on Mount Pleasant Drive in Winsford. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved approximately 10 metres by five metres of grass near a skate park. They extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
