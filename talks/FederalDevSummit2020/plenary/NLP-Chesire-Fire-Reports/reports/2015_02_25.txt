Chimney extractor fire in Mobberley Time of Incident: 20/02/2015 - 15:43 Location: Town Lane, Mobberley Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters have been dealing with a fire involving a chimney extractor at a pub in Mobberley.  Using a thermal imaging camera, the cew found the fire inside an external extractor fan.  They removed the top of the fan and put the fire out using a hose reel jet.
