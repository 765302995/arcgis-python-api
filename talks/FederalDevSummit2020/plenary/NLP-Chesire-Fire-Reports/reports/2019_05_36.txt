 Wood and pallets on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 25/05/2019 - 19:18 Location: Grangeway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended to a small amount of wood and pallets on fire on Grangeway in Runcorn.  A hose reel jet and hose reel were used to put out the fire.
