 Hedgerow fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 29/10/2018 - 15:56 Location: Oxford Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 3.55pm firefighters were called to a report of hedge being on fire on Oxford Road in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered approximately five metres of a conifer hedgerow well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 45 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
