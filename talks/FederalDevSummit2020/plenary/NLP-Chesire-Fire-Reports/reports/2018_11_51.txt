 HGV Fire in Woolston Time of Incident: 21/11/2018 - 17:19 Location: Manchester Road, Woolston, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 5.20pm firefighters from Warrington were called to reports of a HGV fire on Manchester Road, Woolston. On arrival at the scene crews found that the nearside brakes of the vehicle had overheated. The team use one hose reel jet to cool the brakes and then arranged recovery of the vehicle. Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately 30 minutes.
