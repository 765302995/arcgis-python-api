Caravans on fire at caravan park in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/01/2016 - 12:04 Location: Warburton Bridge Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, one from Birchwood and one from Stockton Heath. One fire engine from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service also attended. Firefighters tackled a fire involving seven caravans at a site in Warrington.  Fire crews used four hose reel jets to deal with the incident. Five caravans were destroyed by the fire and a further two caravans suffered heat damage.  Firefighters removed several cylinders and used a thermal imaging camera to make sure they are safe. A safety cordon was put in place while the incident was dealt with.  The fire is believed to have been started accidentally.
