Motorcycle in Ledsham road traffic collision Time of Incident: 17/06/2016 - 06:40 Location: Badgersrake Lane, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A road traffic collision involving a motorbike left the rider needing medical treatment. Paramedics treated the casualty while firefighters cooled the motorbike using a hose reel jet. Police closed the road while the incident was being dealt with.
