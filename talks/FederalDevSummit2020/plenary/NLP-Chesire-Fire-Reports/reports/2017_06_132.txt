Wooden wendy house fire in Winwick, Warrington Time of Incident: 09/06/2017 - 19:16 Location: Maple Road in Winwick Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and Stockton Heath Two fire engines from Warrington and Stockton Heath attended a fire involving a wooden wendy house in the garden of a property on Maple Road in Winwick. Firefighters used one hose reel to bring the fire under control.
