 Road traffic collision in Chester Time of Incident: 16/06/2018 - 17:26 Location: Long Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane and Chester Firefighters attended to a road traffic collision on Long Lane in Chester involving two vehicles.  Crews helped to remove a casualty from one of the vehicles using a ked and a spine board.  The casualty was then handed into the care of paramedics and taken to hospital.
