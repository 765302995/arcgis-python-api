 Fire in the open in Northwich Time of Incident: 29/06/2018 - 16:23 Location: Knutsford Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 4.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Knutsford Road in Antrobus, Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning close to a field full of barley. Due to safety concerns, firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet.
