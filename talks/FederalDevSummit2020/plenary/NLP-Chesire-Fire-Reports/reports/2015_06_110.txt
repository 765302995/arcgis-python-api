Kitchen fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 11/06/2015 - 17:24 Location: Rope Bank Avenue, Wistaston Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish a kitchen fire in Wistaston.  Smoke alarms were fitted in the two storey semi detached property and did activate. Crews were at the scene for around 40 minutes.
