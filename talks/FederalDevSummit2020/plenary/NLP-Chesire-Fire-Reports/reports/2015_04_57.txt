Fire involving railway embankment in Northwich Time of Incident: 22/04/2015 - 13:32 Location: Chester Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A fire involving grass on a railway embankment was dealt with by firefighters. A caution for reduced speed was put in place for trains and Network Rail were also in attendance. The crews used hand beaters and water to put the smouldering grass out which was situated on the Manchester to Crewe line.
