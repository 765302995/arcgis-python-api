Cat rescued from Jubilee Bridge Time of Incident: 05/05/2015 - 13:16 Location: Jubilee Bridge, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were requested by the RSPCA to assist in the rescue of a cat which was stuck on the ledge of the Jubilee Bridge.  The cat had been stranded for over 24 hours and was rescued by the crew and left in the care of RSPCA.   The crew nicknamed the cat Lucky after its ordeal.
