Hazardous chemical leakage in Widnes Time of Incident: 20/07/2016 - 11:55 Location: Moss Bank Road and Brown Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Runcorn Firefighters cordoned off the road between Moss Bank Road and Brown Street due to a hazardous chemical which spilled out across the area. Crews secured the area and the incident was passed over to Halton Borough Council.
