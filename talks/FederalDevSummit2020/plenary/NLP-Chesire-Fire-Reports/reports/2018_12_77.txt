 Boiler fire in Birchwood Time of Incident: 13/12/2018 - 00:17 Location: Colebrook Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood and one from Warrington Boiler fire in Birchwood caused by an electrical socketFirefighters were called to a fire involving a central heating boiler in a garage of a domestic property on Colebrook Close, Birchwood. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, extinguished the fire using a hose reel jet.  The fire was started by an overloaded electrical extension cable.  Crews were at the scene for an hour.
