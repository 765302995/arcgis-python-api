 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 31/05/2019 - 21:24 Location: Barnes Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to Barnes Road, Widnes, after a report of a wheelie bin fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident please contact Cheshire Police.
