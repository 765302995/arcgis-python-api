Tumble dryer fire in Chester Time of Incident: 20/03/2015 - 13:33 Location: Arnhem Way, Huntington, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester A small fire involving a tumble dryer was investigated by firefighters.  The crew put out the fire using a handheld extinguisher and checked the area with a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no further hot spots of fire.  The fire involved clothing and a memory foam pillow.  Please see our tumble dryer safety information - Tumble dryer fire safety
