 Chimney fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 19/02/2019 - 10:02 Location: Blythe Avenue, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton and one from Sandbach Fire crews were called to a chimney fire on Blythe Avenue, Congleton. Crews used chimney rods, a hose reel jet and the hydraulic platform from Macclesfield to tackle the fire. One casualty was oxygen by the crew after suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation before being handed into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for two hours 25 minutes.
