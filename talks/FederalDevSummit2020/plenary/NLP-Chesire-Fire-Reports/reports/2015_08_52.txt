Four people trapped inside a car following a road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/08/2015 - 18:51 Location: Junction of Lythgoes Lane and Winwick Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington There was a road traffic collision involving a car and a van at the junction of Lythgoes Lane and Winwick Road in Warrington. Four people were trapped in the car. Firefighters stabilised the vehicle and then removed the roof and two sides of the car, so that they could rescue the four people from the car. The casualties were then handed to the care of paramedics.
