Van on fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 23/11/2015 - 04:55 Location: Dragons Lane, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach Deliberate ignition was the cause of a fire involving a van in Sandbach. The transit van was well alight and was put out by firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, using two hose reel jets. Police were asked to attend. Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
