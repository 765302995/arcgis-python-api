Park bin on fire in Latchford, Warrington Time of Incident: 08/04/2017 - 18:11 Location: Westy Park, Bridgewater Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters used a water backpack to extinguish a fire involving a bin by a children's play area on West Park in Latchford.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately.  If you can help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, or call Cheshire Police on 101.
