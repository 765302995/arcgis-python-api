Road traffic collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 19/07/2015 - 09:55 Location: Berwick Road West, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A road traffic collision involving two vehicles and three people with one person trapped required firefighters to attend. The crew released one casualty from a vehicle and handed them to the care of paramedics. Air Ambulance also attended.
