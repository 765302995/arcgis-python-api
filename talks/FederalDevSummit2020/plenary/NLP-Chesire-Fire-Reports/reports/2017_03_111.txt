Electrical fire at Crewe business Time of Incident: 13/03/2017 - 10:38 Location: Quakers Coppice, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Crews were called to a fire involving burning cables at a business premises in Quakers Coppice. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus were at readiness with dry powder extinguishers, a hose reel and a main jet. The electrical supplies were isolated which stopped the cables from smoking.  One man was handed into the care of the ambulance service for treatment for a suspected electric shock.  The company's maintenance crew lifted floor tiles around the affected area under the supervision of the firefighters and the incident then left with site maintenance.
