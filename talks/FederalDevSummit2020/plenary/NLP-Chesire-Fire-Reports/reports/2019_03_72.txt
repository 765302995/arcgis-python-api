 Cooker fire in Crewe flat Time of Incident: 15/03/2019 - 11:01 Location: Stalbridge Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters attended a ground floor flat following reports of a fire involving a cooker.   Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  A covering jet was also set up and once the fire was out a large fan was used to disperse the smoke.   A woman was taken to hospital suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation.  For tips on avoiding a kitchen fire visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
