Motorbike on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 15/02/2017 - 22:40 Location: Saddlery Way, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester A motorbike fire on the footpath between the railway line and Saddlery Way is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters tackled the fire and handed the incident over to the police.  If anyone has any information about this fire please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
