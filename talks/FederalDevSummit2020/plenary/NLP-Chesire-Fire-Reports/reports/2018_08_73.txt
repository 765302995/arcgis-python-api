 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/08/2018 - 18:28 Location: Westbrook Crescent, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. One fire engine from Penketh. Firefighters were called to a car on fire which was well alight on a driveway at an address in Westbrook Crescent in Warrington. The fire had spread to the guttering of the house. Firefighters used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. Police are investigating.
