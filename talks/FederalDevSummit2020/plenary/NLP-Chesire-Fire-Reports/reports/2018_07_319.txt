 Grass fire, Runcorn Time of Incident: 30/06/2018 - 20:25 Location: Stockham Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Crews were called to deal with a grass fire on Stockham Lane. They had to cut through a padlocked barrier to gain access along the lane and then found an area of grassland measuring approx 25 sq m well alight. They used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames.
