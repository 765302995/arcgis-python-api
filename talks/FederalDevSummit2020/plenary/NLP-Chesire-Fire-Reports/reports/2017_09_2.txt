Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/09/2017 - 21:42 Location: Higher Lane, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service's Altrincham station A road traffic collision involving two vehicles required firefighters to attend and use cutting equipment to release one woman from a vehicle after she became trapped. Paramedics also attended to look after casualties along with police to deal with road closures.
