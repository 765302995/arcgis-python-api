 Fire in a commercial building in Nantwich Time of Incident: 29/08/2018 - 07:54 Location: Station Road, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Fire crews were called to a fire in a light fitting in Morrison's on Station Road, Nantwich. The store was evacuated whilst crews dealt with the fire. They used two carbon dioxide fire extinguishers to put out the fire and then checked the area with a thermal imaging camera. They were at the scene for 20 minutes.
