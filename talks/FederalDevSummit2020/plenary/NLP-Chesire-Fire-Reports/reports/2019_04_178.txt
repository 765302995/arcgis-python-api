 Sofa on fire in a back garden in Crewe Time of Incident: 12/04/2019 - 22:01 Location: Richard Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the back garden of a domestic property in Richard Street, Crewe. On arrival the crew found a sofa alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for more than 10 minutes.
