 Chip fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/02/2018 - 02:02 Location: Iveagh Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Crews in Runcorn attended to reports of a chip fire at a flat on Iveagh Close in Runcorn. Firefighters used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel to put out the fire.
