Electrical substation fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 04/04/2015 - 14:02 Location: Chester road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Tarporley Firefighters dealt with a fire in an Electrical substation in Northwich. This was fire in an electrical pole.  The surrounding area was checked with a thermal imaging camera. Scottish Power were requested to attend.
