Silage and tyre fire in Astbury Time of Incident: 07/09/2017 - 13:59 Location: Watery Lane, Astbury Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton Fire crews were called to a silage and tyre fire on Watery Lane, Astbury. On arrival crews found a small quantity of silage and a number of tyres on fire and they used a hose reel jet to extinguish it.
