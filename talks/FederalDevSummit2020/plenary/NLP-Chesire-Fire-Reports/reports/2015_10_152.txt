Car fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 02/10/2015 - 21:59 Location: Congleton Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Wilmslow Two hose reel jets were used to extinguish a car fire well alight in Macclesfield.  Two of the crew wore breathing apparatus and were at the incident for around 45 minutes.
