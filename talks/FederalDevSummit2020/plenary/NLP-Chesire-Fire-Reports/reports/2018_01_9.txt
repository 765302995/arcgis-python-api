 Chimney fire at a public house in Mobberley Time of Incident: 29/01/2018 - 18:40 Location: Church Lane, Mobberley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one fire engine from Lymm At 6.39pm on Monday, 29 January two fire engines from Wilmslow and Lymm fire stations attended to a fire involving a chimney on Church Lane in Mobberley. People were evacuated from the building while firefighters put out the fire using one hose reel, one thermal imaging camera, small gear and a positive pressure ventilation unit to cut away at the chimney. Fire crews were in attendance for approximately one hour and thirty minutes.
