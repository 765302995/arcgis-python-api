Saloon car fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 02/09/2016 - 07:18 Location: Bridgewater Grange, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A fire broke out in the engine compartment of a saloon car and firefighters were called to the scene. Crews used one hose reel jet to put out the fire and handed one man, who was suffering from smoke inhalation, into the care of paramedics.
