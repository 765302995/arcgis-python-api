 Manure on fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 28/07/2018 - 04:14 Location: Beech Road, Tarporley. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to a large mound of horse manure on fire off Beech Road, Tarporley. The heap, which was approximately 12 metres by 8 metres and 5 metres deep, was well alight. Crews used two hose reels to extinguish the flames and then turned over the manure to ensure it didn't reignite.
