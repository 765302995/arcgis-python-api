Flat fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/12/2017 - 07:47 Location: Boydell Avenue, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm and one from Warrington. Latchford flat fireShortly before 8.00am firefighters from Warrington and Lymm attended a flat fire in Latchford.  On arrival they found that the first floor flat was well alight and used one hose reel jet, two breathing apparatus to bring the fire under control.  Crews were at the scene for nearly three hours and one person was treated for smoke inhalation. The fire is believed to have been caused by an electrical heater.  Heater safety advice
