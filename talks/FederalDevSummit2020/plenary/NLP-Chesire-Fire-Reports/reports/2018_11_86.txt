 Waste lorry fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/11/2018 - 14:05 Location: Macclesfield Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Holmes Chapel and one from Congleton Shortly after 2pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a large vehicle on Macclesfield Road in Crewe. The fire involving the engine compartment of a lorry carrying garden waste was out on arrival. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to cool the waste lorry's engine compartment and a thermal imaging camera to make sure it was safe. They were in attendance for more than an hour.
