Bedroom fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/06/2017 - 12:52 Location: Ashberry Drive, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath, one from Warrington and one from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a persons reported fire in a bedroom. On arrival everyone was out of the property and the fire was out so the crews damped down the fire with a hand appliance before leaving.
