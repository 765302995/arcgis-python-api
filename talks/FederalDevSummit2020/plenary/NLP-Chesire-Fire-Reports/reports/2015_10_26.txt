Camper van on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 25/10/2015 - 17:49 Location: Kingsley Runcorn Road, Little Leigh, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich and one from Warrington Firefighters extinguished a fire involving a camper van with gas canisters inside in Northwich.  Two fire engines initially attended but explosions could be heard from the gas canisters and a third fire engine was requested to assist with water. One hose reel was used to fight the fire and the crews were at the scene for around 80 minutes.
