Dog down a manhole in Congleton Time of Incident: 18/06/2015 - 14:22 Location: Brunswick Street, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton A dog that had fallen down a drain and got stuck was rescued by firefighters. The Staffordshire Bull Terrier dog, called Bear, was walking with its owner on wasteground when he went accidentally fell down an uncovered manhole. When the crew arrived they found the seven-year-old dog two metres down and used a ladder to rescue him.
