 Rubbish fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 25/03/2018 - 19:31 Location: Manor Lane, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters attended a fire in the open off Manor Lane, Davenham, Northwich. On arrival crews found the fire to be involving rubbish which was out of control and had spread to farm machinery and hay bales. Crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.
