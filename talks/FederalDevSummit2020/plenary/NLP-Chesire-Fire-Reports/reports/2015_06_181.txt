Grill pan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 31/05/2015 - 17:56 Location: Tenby Close, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a grill pan at a house in Burtonwood and Westbrook.  The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and a high pressure fan to clear the smoke.
