 Lorry fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/10/2018 - 01:13 Location: Warrington Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes and one fire engine from Penketh Fire crews were called to a HGV which was on fire on Warrington Road, Widnes. On arrival crews found the cab of the lorry well alight and crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the blaze. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire Police.
