 Car fire on M6 Time of Incident: 27/04/2018 - 15:49 Location: M6 southbound between junction 17 and 16 Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Middlewich Fire crews were called to tackle a car fire on the M6 Southbound. On arrival crews found the car well alight and they used two hose reel jets, two dry powder extinguishers and cutting equipment to tackle the fire. Part of the motorway was closed whilst the incident was ongoing.
