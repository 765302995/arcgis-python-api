 Smoke at a flat in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/05/2019 - 19:59 Location: Thornhills, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 8pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a flat in Thornhills, Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered that the alarm had been activated by cooking fumes. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property and were in attendance for less than 10 minutes. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
