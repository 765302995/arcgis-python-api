 Burst water main in Frodsham Time of Incident: 05/09/2019 - 11:23 Location: Kingsley Road, Frodsham. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Burst water main in FrodshamFirefighters were called to the flooding of two properties in Kingsley Road, Frodsham after a water main burst.  Water has flooded the basement and affected the electrics. Crews isolated the electrics and are using a pump to try and remove the water.  United Utilities attended to isolate the leek.  Crews were at the scene for approximately three hours and 40 minutes.
