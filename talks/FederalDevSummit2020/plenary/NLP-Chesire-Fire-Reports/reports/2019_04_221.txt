 Rubbish fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 06/04/2019 - 21:21 Location: Holmshaw Lane, Oakhanger. CW1 5XF Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsager Rubbish at the side of the road near to Holmshaw Lane was set alight at around 9.20pm.  Firefighers from Alsager used two hose reel jets to put the fire out. They were at the scene for about 30 minutes.
