 Industrial skip fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 27/11/2018 - 09:16 Location: Gresty Road, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Shortly after 9.15am firefighters from Crewe were called to reports of a fire at the rear of an address on Gresty Road.  On arrival at the scene, the team found that two industrial skips containing paper and general waste were well alight.  Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fires. They then emptied the skips and continued to cool the contents with a hose reel jet.  The team then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  The cause of the fire is believed to be accidental.  Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
