Bungalow flooded with one foot of water in Knutsford Time of Incident: 14/06/2016 - 18:31 Location: Yewlands Drive, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford A bungalow was flooded with approximately one foot of water, so firefighters used a light portable pump to remove the water from the property. The water entered the property via a sloping driveway.
