Sheds on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/05/2017 - 22:38 Location: Lumber Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Newton-le-Willows (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Two sheds at the back of a house on Lumber Lane were on fire. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to bring the blaze under control and were at the scene for around one hour.
