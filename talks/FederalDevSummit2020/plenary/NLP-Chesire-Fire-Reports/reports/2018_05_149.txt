 Lift rescue, Wilmslow Time of Incident: 12/05/2018 - 20:00 Location: Station Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters responded to reports of people stuck in a lift at a residential premises in Station Road, Wilmslow. Two men had been trapped inside for around two hours and the lift engineer was unavailable. Crews were able to release the men from the lift.
