Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/11/2017 - 01:54 Location: Hall Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a wheelie bin which was on fire on Hall Street, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Please call Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
