 Two cars on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/04/2018 - 01:49 Location: Dunsford, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Two cars on fire in WidnesFire crews were called to Dunsford, Widnes, after reports that two cars were on fire. Crews found both cars were well alight on arrival and they used two hose reel jets to put out the fires.  The incident is being treated as arson and anyone with information should call Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
