 Vehicle fire on M56 Time of Incident: 22/05/2019 - 00:43 Location: M56,Westbound J10-J11 Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Lymm Firefighters attended to a vehicle fire on the hard shoulder of the M56 westbound between junction 10 and junction 11.  One hose reel was used to put out the fire which is believed to have been caused by brakes overheating.
