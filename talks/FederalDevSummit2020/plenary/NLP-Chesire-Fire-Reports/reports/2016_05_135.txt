Bonfire causing smoke on M6 southbound at J17 to 16 Time of Incident: 08/05/2016 - 15:14 Location: M6 southbound between junctions 17 and 16 Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Holmes Chapel Controlled burning on private land caused smoke to blow across the motorway near Sandbach. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
