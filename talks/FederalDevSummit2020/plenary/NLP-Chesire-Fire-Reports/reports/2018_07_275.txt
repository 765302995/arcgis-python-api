 Grass fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 17:49 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Widnes used a hose one hose reel jet to extinguish a grass fire off Dundalk Road in Widnes. At its height the fire involved approximately 100 metres by 3 metres of grassland. The team were in attendance for approximately one hour 45 minutes.
