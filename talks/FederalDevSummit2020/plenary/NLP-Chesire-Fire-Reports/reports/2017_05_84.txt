Grill Pan fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 17/05/2017 - 20:04 Location: Fallowfield, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Runcorn Smoke was issuing from a property, so firefighters gained entry to the property and found a fire involving an unattended grill pan. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish a fire.  Kitchen fire safety
