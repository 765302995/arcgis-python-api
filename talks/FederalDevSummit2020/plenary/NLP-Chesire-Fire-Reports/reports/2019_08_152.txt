 BBQ cylinder fire in Chester Time of Incident: 03/08/2019 - 13:27 Location: Sherrington Lane, Chester. CH3 9JU Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas Shortly before 1.30pm firefighters were called to a BBQ cylinder fire at Sherrington Lane.  The crew moved the BBQ away from the house and cooled the cylinder and surrounding area. They were on scene for an hour.
