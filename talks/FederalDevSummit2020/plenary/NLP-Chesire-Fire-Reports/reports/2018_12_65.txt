 Vehicle fire in Neston Time of Incident: 15/12/2018 - 23:29 Location: Woodlands Road, Neston. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a vehicle fire on Woodlands Road, Neston. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident please contact Cheshire Police.
