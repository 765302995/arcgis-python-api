 Two wheelie bin fires in Runcorn Time of Incident: 11/04/2019 - 21:13 Location: Sanders Hey Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews extinguished two wheelie bin fires on Sanders Hey Close in Runcorn.  A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire.  Ensure your wheelie bin is kept safe by following our safety tips https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
