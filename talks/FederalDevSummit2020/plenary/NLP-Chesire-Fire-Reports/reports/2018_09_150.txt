 Vehicle fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 01/09/2018 - 01:28 Location: Kilncroft, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters from Runcorn used one hose reel jet and one breathing apparatus to extinguish a van which had been deliberately set on fire on Kilncroft, Runcorn. The team were in attendance for approximately 50 minutes. Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML171538. Information can also be passed anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111
