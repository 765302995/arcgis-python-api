Fire in park building in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/10/2015 - 19:12 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Two fire engines were called to a report of a building on fire in a Warrington park. A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire and police were asked to attend as it is believed the fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
