 Small bush fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 16/05/2019 - 18:59 Location: Maltmans Road, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters were called to a bush fire on Maltmans Road, Lymm. Approximately 10 square metres of bush was on fire and crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
