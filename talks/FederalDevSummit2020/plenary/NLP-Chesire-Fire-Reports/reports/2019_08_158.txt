 Fire in the open in Tarporley Time of Incident: 02/08/2019 - 19:11 Location: Brook Road, Tarporley. CW6 9HE Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Tarporley Shortly before 7.10pm firefighters attended Brook Road after reports of a fire in the open.  The fire was out on arrival, but a man who was burning wood received minor burns. He was initially treated by firefighters before being handed to the care of North West Ambulance Service.
