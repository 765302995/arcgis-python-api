 One person assisted from a car in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/09/2018 - 12:15 Location: Wilderspool Causeway, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Widnes Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision on Widlerspool Causeway, Warrington. Crews assisted one person from a car after a crash between a HGV and a car. The woman was uninjured.
