Small vehicle fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 20/06/2017 - 14:09 Location: Tarporley Road, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to a car on fire in the car park of the Fox and Barrel pub, on Tarporley Road, Tarporley. Crews used one hose reel jet to put out the fire.
