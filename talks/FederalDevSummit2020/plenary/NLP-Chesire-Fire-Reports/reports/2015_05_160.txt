Car fire on M6, junction 20 Time of Incident: 01/05/2015 - 16:09 Location: M6 junction 20 southbound Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Birchwood Firefighters have been at the scene of a car on fire on the hard shoulder of the M6 near junction 20.  A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire in the engine compartment.
