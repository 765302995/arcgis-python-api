 Fuse box fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/12/2018 - 17:14 Location: Booths Hill Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Lymm and one from Penketh Firefighters attended to an address on Booths Hill Road in Lymm following reports of a fire involving a fuse box.  On arrival, crews discovered the fire was already out when they arrived but had a hose reel and dry powder extinguisher on standby.   A thermal imaging camera was used to inspect the fuse box and firefighters notified an electrical company.  Crews were in attendance for around an hour.
