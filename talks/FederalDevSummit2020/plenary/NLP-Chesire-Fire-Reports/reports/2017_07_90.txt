Excavator on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 10/07/2017 - 20:35 Location: Middlewich Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and one from Winsford Fire crews were called to a JCB excavator which was on fire in a scrap yard on Middlewich Road, Northwich. The JCB was well alight on arrival and firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
