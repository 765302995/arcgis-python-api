 Overheated HGV brakes in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 27/12/2018 - 11:13 Location: Middlewich Road, Holmes Chapel Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Holmes Chapel and one from Middlewich At around 11.10am firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from a tyre on a heavy goods vehicle on Middlewich Road in Holmes Chapel, at the M6's Junction 18 roundabout. On arrival crews discovered that the smoke was caused by overheated brakes on the HGV's trailer. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to cool the brakes. They also opened the trailer and inspected it with a thermal imaging camera to make sure that the fire had not spread.. Firefighters were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
