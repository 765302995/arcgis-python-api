Grass and gorse fire in Disley Time of Incident: 25/03/2017 - 16:56 Location: Unnamed road close to Higher Lane, Disley Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton A large area of grass and gorse was on fire on the hillside above Cliff Clough. Firefighters used beaters to bring the fire, which covered an area of some 100m x 50m under control.
