Sofa on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 30/12/2016 - 17:50 Location: Bradbury Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford A sofa is believed to have been deliberately set on fire on Bradbury Road. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and are urging anyone with any information to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101. Arson is a crime and can kill.
