Chimney fire in Neston Time of Incident: 23/04/2016 - 18:43 Location: Mill Lane, Neston Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a chimney fire in Neston. Although the fire was out on arrival there were high temperatures in the flue liner. This was monitored and the crew took regular temperature checks using a thermal imaging camera until it had cooled.
