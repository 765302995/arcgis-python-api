Car fire affecting property in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/07/2016 - 17:15 Location: Trefoil Close, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Birchwood and Warrington A car fire also affected property in Trefoil Close. The car was on a driveway and the fire affected the nearby property. Firefighters used two hose reels and one main jet to bring the blaze under control. A fire investigation is underway to determine what caused the blaze.
