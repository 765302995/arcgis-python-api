Car deliberately set on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 03/12/2016 - 00:44 Location: Lacey Court, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes A car was deliberately set on fire. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Police attended this incident.   Anyone with any information about this is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
