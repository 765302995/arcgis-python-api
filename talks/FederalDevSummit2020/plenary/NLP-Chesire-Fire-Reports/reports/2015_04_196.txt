Derelict building fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 05/04/2015 - 17:11 Location: Black Lane, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Fire crews responded to a report of a fire in a derelict building on Back lane in Macclesfield.  When they arrived they found a small fire involving wood and tiles. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
