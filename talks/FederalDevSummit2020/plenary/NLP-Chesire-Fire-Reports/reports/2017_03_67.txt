Chip pan fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 21/03/2017 - 14:37 Location: College Fields, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh and one from Runcorn A chip pan on fire in a flat required firefighters to attend and use a hose reel to extinguish it. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property. One woman was given oxygen therapy by firefighters prior to the arrival of paramedics.
