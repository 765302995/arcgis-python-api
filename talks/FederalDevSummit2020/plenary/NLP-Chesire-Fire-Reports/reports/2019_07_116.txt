 Skip fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 11/07/2019 - 20:05 Location: Middlewich Road, Rudheath, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly after 8pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Middlewich Road in Rudheath, Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire in a skip within a compound. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than an hour.
