 Kitchen fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 16/10/2019 - 06:35 Location: Swanlow Lane, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At around 6.30am firefighters from Winsford were called to reports of a kitchen fire on Swanlow Lane. On arrival the crew found that the fire had involved a toaster which had been extinguished by the occupant.   Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property and the occupant was given oxygen therapy to treat potential smoke inhalation. He was subsequently taken to hospital as a precautionary measure.
