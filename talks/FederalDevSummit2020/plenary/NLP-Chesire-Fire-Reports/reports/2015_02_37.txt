Car fire in Chester Time of Incident: 17/02/2015 - 16:58 Location: A55 near Marlston-Cum-Lache in Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Firefighters were called to deal with a car fire on the A55 near Marlston-Cum-Lache in Chester.  Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.  The road was closed while firefighters dealt with the incident.
