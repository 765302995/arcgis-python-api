Building fire in Moulton Time of Incident: 17/10/2017 - 15:16 Location: Jack Lane, Moulton Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Middlewich Fire crews were called to a fire in an outbuilding on Jack Lane, Moulton. The building was well alight on arrival of the crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fire.
