 Grass fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/03/2018 - 14:11 Location: Minshull New Road, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to a grass fire on an embankment off Minshull Road, Crewe. The area affected of grass  was 40 metres by 30 metres. Crews used two beaters and a water backpack . They then damped down the area to prevent further ignition.
