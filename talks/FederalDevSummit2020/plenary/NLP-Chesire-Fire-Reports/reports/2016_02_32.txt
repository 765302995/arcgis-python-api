Unattended chip pan causes Crewe kitchen fire Time of Incident: 22/02/2016 - 15:52 Location: Market Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A chip pan left heating unattended on a cooker caused a fire at a property in Crewe. The fire caused a lot of smoke resulting in a casualty being given oxygen therapy by firefighters. She was then taken to hospital by ambulance. Smoke alarms were fitted in the property. They activated and alerted the householder to the fire.  Please follow our safety advice and never leave cooking unattended - Chip pans
