 Fire involving a large quantity of rubbish at a farm in Frodsham Time of Incident: 24/07/2018 - 19:41 Location: Marsh Lane, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Frodsham At around 7.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at a farm on Marsh Lane in Frodsham. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a large quantity of rubbish. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire, which measured aprroximately10 square metres. The site's owner assisted the crew with dragging out the rubbish using plant machinery. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
