 Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 30/10/2018 - 00:24 Location: Southway, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to Southway, Widnes, after a report of a car fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish a fire which was in the engine compartment of a car. The fire was started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire then please call Cheshire Police.
