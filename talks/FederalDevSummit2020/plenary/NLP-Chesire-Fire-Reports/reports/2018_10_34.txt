 Collsion in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/10/2018 - 17:07 Location: Cliff Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters attended to a road traffic collision on Cliff Lane in Warrington involving three vehicles.  Crews stabilised one car and removed the roof to safely extricate a woman who was handed into the care of paramedics.  The road was closed in both directions while emergency services were at the scene dealing with the incident.  Crews were in attendance for 90 minutes.
