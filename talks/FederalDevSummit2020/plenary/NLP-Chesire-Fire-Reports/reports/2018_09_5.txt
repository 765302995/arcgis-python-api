 House fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 28/09/2018 - 09:15 Location: Moss Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and one from Winsford Fire crews were called to a house fire on Moss Road, Northwich. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel, a covering jet and two large fans to put the fire.  Crews were in attendance for approximately two hours and 10 minutes.
