Car fire on M6, junction 19 Time of Incident: 25/08/2017 - 14:23 Location: A556 junction with the M6 (junction 19) Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm Fire crews were called to a car fire on the A556 junction with the M6 (junction 19). Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the car. Crews were detained for 20 minutes.
