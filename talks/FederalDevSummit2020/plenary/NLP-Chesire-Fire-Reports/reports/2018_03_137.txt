 Bonfire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 05/03/2018 - 15:05 Location: Parkgate Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to Parkgate Road, Macclesfield, to reports of a fire. Crews found an unattended bonfire at the rear of a property and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
