Two vehicle collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 29/06/2019 - 05:04 Location: New Road, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to a collision involving a car and a van in New Road, Winsford. On arrival the car was well alight. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. No one was trapped in the car. Two casualties were assessed by paramedics.
