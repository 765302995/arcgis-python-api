Pallets on fire on a field at Bridgewater High School in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/08/2015 - 20:32 Location: Bridgewater High School (lower school) Hall Drive, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath Stockton Heath firefighters used hand fire exinguishers to extinguish a fire involving pallets on the school field at Bridgewater High School (lower school) on Hall Drive in Warrington. Police also attended this incident.  This fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
