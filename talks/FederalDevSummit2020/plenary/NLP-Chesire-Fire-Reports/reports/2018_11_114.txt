 Hedge fire in a back garden in Northwich Time of Incident: 08/11/2018 - 22:57 Location: Ollershaw Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly before 11pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a hedge in a back garden on Ollershaw Lane in Northwich. The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around an hour.
