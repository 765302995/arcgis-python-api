Van on fire on M6 northbound at J18 Time of Incident: 10/07/2016 - 13:45 Location: M6 northbound, junction 18 near Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel, one from Knutsford and one from Middlewich A van being towed was on fire on the M6 northbound near to junction 18 near Knutsford. The van was uncoupled from the tow truck and firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fire. They then damped the area down. Police officers attended to manage traffic.
