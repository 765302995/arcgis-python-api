 HGV fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 19/09/2018 - 03:10 Location: Cliff Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a HGV which had caught fire at Lymm services. Fire crews wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to put out the fire. They then checked the vehicle using a thermal imaging camera. The fire had been caused by overheated brakes which then set fire to wood and electrical items which were in the trailer.  Crews were at the scene for an hour and 20 minutes.
