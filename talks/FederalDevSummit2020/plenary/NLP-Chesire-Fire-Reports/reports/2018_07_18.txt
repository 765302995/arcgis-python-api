 Collision on M6 between junction 18 and 19 Time of Incident: 28/07/2018 - 08:38 Location: M6 northbound between junctions 18 and 19. Attendance: Four fire engines attended. Two from Crewe, one from Alsager and one from Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called to a collision involving two vehicle on the M6 Northbound between junctions 18 and 19. Two casualties were extracted from two separate vehicles. Three casualties were taken to hospital.
