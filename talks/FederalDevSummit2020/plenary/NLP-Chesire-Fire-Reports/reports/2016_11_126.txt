Rubbish on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 06/11/2016 - 19:37 Location: Regent Street, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A large amount of rubbish, including a mattress, had been set on fire on Regent Street. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and are appealing to anyone with any information as to who may have started the blaze to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
