 House fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 25/12/2018 - 17:24 Location: Percy Street, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Northwich and one from Winsford. Shortly before 5.30pm on Christmas Day firefighters from Northwich and Winsford were dispatched to reports of a house fire on Percy Street, Northwich. On arrival the team discovered a fire involving a shower unit in a downstairs bathroom. Two firefighters, wearing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The team also isolated the gas, electric and water at the property. Firefighters were detained at the address for approximately one hour.
