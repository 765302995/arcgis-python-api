Collision involving car and HGV on M6 Time of Incident: 28/01/2015 - 22:49 Location: M6 north, J20 Attendance: Two fire engines from Stockton Heath and one from Warrington A car and a HGV were involved in a collision on the M6. Fortunately no one was trapped and the car was on the hard shoulder on arrival of the crews. Firefighters assisted the Highways Agency in fending off the traffic before leaving.
