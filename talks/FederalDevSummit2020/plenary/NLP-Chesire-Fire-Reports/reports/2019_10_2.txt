 Road traffic collision in Nether Alderley Time of Incident: 30/10/2019 - 06:48 Location: Chelford Road, Nether Alderley Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Macclesfield A road traffic collision involving a car in a hedge and one casualty trapped required firefighters to attend. The road was closed in both directions while firefighters stabilised the vehicle and released the casualty from the car. Paramedics and police attended.
