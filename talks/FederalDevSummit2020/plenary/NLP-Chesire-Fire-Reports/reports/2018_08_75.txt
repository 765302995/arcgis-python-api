 Aerial hanging off roof in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 15/08/2018 - 14:10 Location: Everest Close, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to reports of a metal aerial hanging off a roof in Everest Close, Ellesmere Port. Crews used a 7 metre ladder to assist them in removing the aerial.
