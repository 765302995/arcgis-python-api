 Collision in Wimboldsley Time of Incident: 15/07/2018 - 00:25 Location: Nantwich Road, Wimboldsley. CW100LW Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich, one from Winsford and one from Crewe, as well as the major rescue unit. Fire crews were called to a serious collision just after 00:20 at Nantwich Road. As a result of the collision a person was trapped in a car and firefighters worked with the other emergency services to free the casualty who was taken to hospital for further treatment. Crews were on scene for one hour 40 minutes.
