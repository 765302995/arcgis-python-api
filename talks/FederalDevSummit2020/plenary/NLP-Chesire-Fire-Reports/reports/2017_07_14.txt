Laundry room fire in Haslington Time of Incident: 26/07/2017 - 20:50 Location: Clifford Grove, Haslington Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters tackled a fire in a laundry room at a house on Clifford Grove. The crew, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet with a covering jet in place to put out the fire.
