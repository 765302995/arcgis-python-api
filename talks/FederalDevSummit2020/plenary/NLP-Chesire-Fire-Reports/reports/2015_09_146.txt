Building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/09/2015 - 18:40 Location: Shepperton Close, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington, one from Stockton Heath and an aerial appliance from Chester A fire in a detached house in Warrington was located in the bedroom and extinguished.  On arrival crews found smoke issuing from the property and more resources were requested.  Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel and covering jet to extinguish the blaze. A short extension ladder was used to access the loft and a thermal imaging camera to check for fire spread.  No casualties were involved and the crews were at the scene for around two hours. The fire is believed to have been caused by a tortoise heat lamp. Smoke alarms were fitted and did activate as a result of the fire.
