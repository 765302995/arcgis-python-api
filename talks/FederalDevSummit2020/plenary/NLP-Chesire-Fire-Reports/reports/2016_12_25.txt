Bus axle fire on M6 southbound J18 to 17 Time of Incident: 28/12/2016 - 04:12 Location: M6 southbound junctions 18 to 17 Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and one from Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called to a report of a bus axle on fire on the M6. 50 passengers were evacuated from the bus as firefighters cooled the axle using a hose reel jet. Police closed the slip road at junction 17 while the incident was dealt with.
