 Small fire at a school in Runcorn Time of Incident: 15/07/2019 - 12:52 Location: Wharford Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Widnes Fire crews were called to a small fire at a school on Wharford Lane, Runcorn. The fire involved an electrical sander in a design and technology classroom. Staff and pupils were successfully evacuated prior to fire service arrival. Crews then used CO2 extinguishers to put out the fire, which was accidental. Crews were at the scene for 30 minutes and staff and pupils have returned to the classrooms.
