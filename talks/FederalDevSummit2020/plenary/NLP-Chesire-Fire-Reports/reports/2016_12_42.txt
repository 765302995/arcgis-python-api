Road traffic collision in Byley Time of Incident: 23/12/2016 - 11:56 Location: Moss Lane, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving two cars with two people needing rescuing. The crews removed the roofs of both the vehicles to get one person out of each car and one person self-rescued themselves from one of the cars. Police closed the road in both directions while the incident was dealt with. Air Ambulance and paramedics also attended.
