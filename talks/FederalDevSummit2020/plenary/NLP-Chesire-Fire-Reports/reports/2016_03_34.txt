Detatched garage on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 25/03/2016 - 01:01 Location: Church Road, Barnton, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich A detached garage was well alight, so fire crews used two main jets to extinguish the fire.  Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus. One fire crew remained at scene for 6 hours 30 minutes damping down to ensure there were no further hot spots. A fire investigation will be completed to find out the cause of the fire.
