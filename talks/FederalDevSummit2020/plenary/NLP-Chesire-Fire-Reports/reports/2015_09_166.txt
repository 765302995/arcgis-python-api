Kitchen fire in Moston Time of Incident: 01/09/2015 - 18:18 Location: Warmingham Lane, Moston Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Middlewich A jacket left on a cooker caused a fire in a kitchen in Moston.  The fire was out on the arrival of the firefighters who gave the occupier a home safety assessment before leaving.  Please don't leave anything on you hob.
