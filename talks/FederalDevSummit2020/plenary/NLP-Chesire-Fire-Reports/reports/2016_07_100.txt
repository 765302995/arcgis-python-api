Wheelie bins on fire in a walkway in Runcorn Time of Incident: 14/07/2016 - 04:24 Location: Halton Brook Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Seven wheelie bins were on fire in the walkway that crosses the express way on Halton Brook Avenue, in Runcorn. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
