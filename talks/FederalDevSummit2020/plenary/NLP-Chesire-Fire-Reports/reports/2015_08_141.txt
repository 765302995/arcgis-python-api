Garage fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 08/08/2015 - 18:08 Location: Overton Close, Congleton. Attendance: Fire engines from Congleton and Macclesfield Firefighters responded to a report of a fire on Overton Close, Congleton.  When they arrived crews were confronted with a blaze involving a detached garage. Hose reel jets were used to deal with the fire.
