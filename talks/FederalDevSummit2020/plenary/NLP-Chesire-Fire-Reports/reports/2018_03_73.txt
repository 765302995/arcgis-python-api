 Firefighters extract casualty from vehicle in M6 collision Time of Incident: 21/03/2018 - 09:39 Location: M6, junction 21A to junction 21 Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington, Lymm, Birchwood and Leigh (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Servcice) attended. Road traffic collision on M6Firefighters extricated a passenger who was trapped in their vehicle following a collision involving a number of vehicles on the M6 south between junction 21A to junction 21.
