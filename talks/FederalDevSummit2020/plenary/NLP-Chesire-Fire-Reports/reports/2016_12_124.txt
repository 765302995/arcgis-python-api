Road traffic collision in Knutsford Time of Incident: 06/12/2016 - 15:11 Location: A556 Chester Road, Bucklow Hill, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford and one from Altrincham (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) The A556 Chester Road in Bucklow Hill was closed by police following a road traffic collision involving three vehicles.  One female casualty was trapped inside one of the vehicles.  Fire crews released the casualty from the vehicle. The casualty was handed to the care of paramedics.  A full road closure was put in place on A556, Chester Road, Bucklow Hill, between the Mere Golf Club and the Bowden Interchange.
