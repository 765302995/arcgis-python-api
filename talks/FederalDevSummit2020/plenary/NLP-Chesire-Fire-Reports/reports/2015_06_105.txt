Cat rescued from a tree in Crewe Time of Incident: 11/06/2015 - 19:47 Location: Queens Drive, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A call from the RSPCA asking for a cat to be rescued from a tree required firefighters to attend and help. The crew used a ladder and height safety equipment to get to the cat and removed branches from the tree to aid the rescue. The owners of the cat attended and were very grateful to have their cat safety back on the ground and gave a donation to the Firefighters Charity as a thank you.
