 Skip fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 01/09/2018 - 19:57 Location: Great King Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters from Macclesfield used one hose reel jet to extinguish a skip fire on Great King Street. After extinguishing the fire the team used a thermal imaging cameral to check for any hotspots. Crews were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes.
