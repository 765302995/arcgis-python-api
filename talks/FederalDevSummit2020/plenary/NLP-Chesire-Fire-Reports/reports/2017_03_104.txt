Car on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 15/03/2017 - 01:49 Location: Brandon, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A car which was well alight required firefighters to use a hose reel to extinguish it. Police also attended as it is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information around who may have started this fire they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
