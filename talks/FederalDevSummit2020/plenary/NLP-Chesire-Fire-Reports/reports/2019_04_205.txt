 Deliberate fire in Widnes and crew attacked Time of Incident: 08/04/2019 - 21:14 Location: Lanark Gardens, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to Lanark Gardens, Widnes, after a report of a fire. On arrival crews found a large amount of wood chippings were well alight and crews used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.  Whilst firefighters were fighting the fire, which had been started deliberately, they were attacked by a group of young people firing fireworks at them.  None of the crew were injured.  Police attended the incident. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number IML 369055.
