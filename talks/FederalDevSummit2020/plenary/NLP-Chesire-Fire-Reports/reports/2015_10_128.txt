Fire in a wooded area in Anderton, Northwich Time of Incident: 08/10/2015 - 20:24 Location: Hough Lane, Anderton, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters used hand fire extinguishers to deal with a small fire in a wooded area in Anderton.  This fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information about this fire they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
