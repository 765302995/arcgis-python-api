 Refuge fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 01/04/2019 - 20:18 Location: Bowland Rise, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters from Winsford used one hose reel jet to extinguish a large amount of refuge which had been deliberately set alight against a garden fence on Bowland Rise.  The team were detained at the scene for around 15 minutes.
