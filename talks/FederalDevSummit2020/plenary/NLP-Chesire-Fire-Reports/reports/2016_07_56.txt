Road traffic collision on M6 near Crewe Time of Incident: 20/07/2016 - 18:11 Location: M6 southbound, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters helped Highways Agency remove a car involved in a road traffic collision on the M6 near Crewe. A car and a lorry were involved in the collision, leaving the car stranded in lane three and the lorry on the hard shoulder. The fire engine was used to fend off oncoming traffic as Highways Agency towed the car to the hard shoulder. Nobody was trapped in either of the vehicles.
