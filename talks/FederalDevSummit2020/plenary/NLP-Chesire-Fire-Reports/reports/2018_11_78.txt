 Outbuilding fire, Chester Time of Incident: 17/11/2018 - 12:44 Location: Virginia Drive, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters turned out to reports of a fire in an outbuilding on Virginia Drive. On arrival they found that the fire, which had involved the facia and roof timber of a garage, had already been put out by neighbour using fire extinguishers.  However, they remained at the scene and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots, and they dampened down the building to prevent reignition. A home safety assessment was also conducted, and they were at the scene for around an hour.
