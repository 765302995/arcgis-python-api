Van on fire in Stockton Heath Time of Incident: 17/04/2017 - 22:40 Location: Hawthorne Grove, Stockton Heath, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath A van which was well alight on a roadway required firefighters to attend and use two hose reels to extinguish it. Two cylinders which were unaffected by the fire were removed from the vehicle.
