 Wheelie bin fire, Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/10/2018 - 21:19 Location: Gleaner Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Crews attended to two wheelie bins on fire on Gleaner Close in Runcorn.  The fire was out on arrival but crews ensured it was fully extinguished by using a hose reel and dampening down the area.  Want to know how to prevent a wheelie bin fire?
