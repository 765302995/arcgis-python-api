Tumble dryer fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 07/11/2015 - 14:27 Location: Boundary Street, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Knutford Firefighters removed a tumble dryer from a property after a fire in Northwich.  The fire was out on arrival of the crews who then carried out a home safety assessment. Tumble dryer fire safety advice
