Fire in public house in High Legh Time of Incident: 13/04/2017 - 10:17 Location: Bears Paw Inn, Warrington Road, High Legh Attendance: One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath Firefighters have been tackling a fire at a pub on Warrington Road. The crews used two hose reel jets to bring the fire under control. A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke from the building. There were no casualties.  An investigation into the cause of this fire will be carried out.
