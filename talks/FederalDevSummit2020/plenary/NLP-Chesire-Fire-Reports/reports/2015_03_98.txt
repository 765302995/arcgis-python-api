Overheated car engine on the car park at Alsager train station Time of Incident: 11/03/2015 - 20:26 Location: Audley Road, Alsager Train Station, Alsager Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a car on fire at Alsager Train Station. On arrival they found no fire, although the vehicle had overheated, so the crew disconnected the car battery as a precautionary measure.
