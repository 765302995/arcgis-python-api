Sheep rescued from canal in Chester Time of Incident: 25/07/2015 - 18:23 Location: Shropshire Union Canal off Liverpool Road, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines and the boat from Chester and one fire engine from Knutsford. Firefighters rescued a sheep stuck in a canal in Chester.  The crews used a hose inflation kit, belt lines and strops to rescue the animal which was then released into a nearby field. They were at the scene for about one hour 15 minutes.
