 Corn field fire in Arclid Time of Incident: 06/07/2018 - 16:27 Location: Congleton Road, Arclid Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton and one engine from Crewe Firefighter using a beater to put out the fireFire crews were called to a large fire in a corn field on Congleton Road, Arclid. The fire was approximately 200 square metres and crews used two hose reel jets and two beaters to extinguish the flames.  Crews are currently dampening down hotspots in the field.
