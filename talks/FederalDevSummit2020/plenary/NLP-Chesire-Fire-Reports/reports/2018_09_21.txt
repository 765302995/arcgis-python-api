 Wheelie bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 24/09/2018 - 21:18 Location: Bunbury Green, Warwick Court, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters extinguished a wheelie bin which had been deliberately set alight on Bunbury Green in Ellesmere Port. The team were detained at the scene for approximately 30 minutes. For more information about how to prevent wheelie bin fires visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
