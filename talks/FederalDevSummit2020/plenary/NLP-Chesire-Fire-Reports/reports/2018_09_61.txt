 Shed fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/09/2018 - 17:11 Location: Halton Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Widnes Firefighters attended to a garden shed well alight at an address on Halton Road in Runcorn.  Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, tackled the blaze with one hose reel to bring the fire under control.  They were at the scene for approximately 90 minutes.
