Road traffic collision in Knutsford Time of Incident: 04/03/2017 - 15:52 Location: Holmes Chapel Road, Knutsford. Attendance: Two fire engines from Middlewich and Holmes Chapel and one rope rescue unit from Holmes Chapel There was a road traffic collision involving two vehicles.  One car was on its roof with a casualty trapped inside. And there were five people in the other vehicle.  The six casualties were assessed by paramedics - three adults and three children.  Police are carrying out an investigation.
