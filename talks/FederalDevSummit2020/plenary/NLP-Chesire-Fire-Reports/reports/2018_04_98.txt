 Garden fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/04/2018 - 22:31 Location: Goldfinch Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 10.30pm firefighters were called to reports of sparks and high flames from controlled burning on Goldfinch Lane, Birchwood, Warrington. The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire in the back garden of a property. Firefighters were at the scene for less than 15 minutes.
