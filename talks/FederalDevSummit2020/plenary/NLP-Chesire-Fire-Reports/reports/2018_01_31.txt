 Motorbike fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 25/01/2018 - 00:10 Location: Farm Close, Weaverham, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters from Northwich used one hose reel jet to extinguish a motorbike which had been deliberately set on fire on Farm Close, Weaverham. Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident 20 of 25 January 2018.
