Refuse fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 19/05/2017 - 22:53 Location: Copperfield Road, Poynton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton A small quantity of refuse was well alight, located next to a skip.  Firefighters used one hose reel to deal with the fire.   The fire is believed to have been started deliberately so anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
