 Collision in Nantwich Time of Incident: 11/12/2018 - 17:33 Location: A51, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich. One fire engine attended from Crewe. Firefighters were called to a collision on the A51 in Nantwich. Crews removed the roof of a vehicle to release one casualty who was trapped in car.  He was then left with paramedics who also assessed a second casualty.
