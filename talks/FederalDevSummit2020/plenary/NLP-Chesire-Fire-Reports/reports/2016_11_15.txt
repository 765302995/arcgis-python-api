Train fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 27/11/2016 - 12:00 Location: Adams Hill, Knutsford Attendance: Fire engines from Knutsford, Wilmslow, Northwich and Altrincham (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) The line was stopped in both directions at Knutsford railway station after a fire broke out underneath a train.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet and a main jet to put out the fire and were at the scene for around an hour and a half.
