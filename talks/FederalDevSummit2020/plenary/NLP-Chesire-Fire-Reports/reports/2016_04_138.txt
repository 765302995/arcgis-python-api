Man trapped under gate in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/04/2016 - 12:58 Location: Langford, Hale, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A man was trapped by his leg under a heavy gate. Firefighters were called to the incident and used specialist cutting gear to release the casualty. He was handed into the care of paramedics as the crew made the scene safe.
