 Collision between a bus and a skip wagon in Sandbach Time of Incident: 26/11/2018 - 07:10 Location: Middlewich Road, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe, as did a response vehicle from Sandbach At around 7.10am firefighters were called to reports a collision involving a bus and a skip wagon outside the McDonald's restaurant on Middlewich Road in Sandbach. The road was closed by the police as emergency services worked at the scene. The drivers of both vehicles were assessed by paramedics and firefighters put a covering jet in place and assisted the police in clearing the road. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
