Cooker fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/06/2015 - 10:41 Location: Densham Avenue, Orford, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in an oven at a house in Orford.  The crew, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and ventilated the property.   Stay safe in the kitchen.  See our safety advice - Kitchen fire safety
