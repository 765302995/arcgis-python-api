 Skip fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/02/2019 - 22:32 Location: Arley Drive, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Frodsham Shortly after 10.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a skip being on fire on Arley Drive in Widnes. On arrival the crew found the small skip well alight. Firefighters put out the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
