 Wheelie bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 20/01/2019 - 05:30 Location: Bradbury Road, Winsford Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Winsford Fire crews attended to a wheelie bin fire in the back garden of a property on Bradbury Road in Winsford.  One hose reel was used to put out the fire and make the area safe.  For information on how to keep your wheelie bins safe please visit our website https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
