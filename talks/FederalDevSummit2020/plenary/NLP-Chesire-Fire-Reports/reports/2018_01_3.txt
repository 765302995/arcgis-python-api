 Chimney fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 30/01/2018 - 15:01 Location: Cronton Lane, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one from Ellesmere Port. Firefighters from Penketh and Ellesmere Port were called to reports of a chimney fire at a restaurant on Cronton Lane, Widnes. Crews attended the scene and used a nine meter ladder to examine the fire before using water to extinguish the fire.
