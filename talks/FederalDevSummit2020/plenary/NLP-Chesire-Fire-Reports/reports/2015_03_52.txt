Carbon monoxide alarm in Nantwich Time of Incident: 20/03/2015 - 22:40 Location: Manor Road North, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Nantwich Five people were taken to hospital to be checked over after a carbon monoxide alarm operated at their home in Nantwich.   An ambulance was requested to attend the scene due to symptoms reported by the occupiers and firefighters ventilated the property and isolated the gas and electricity.  National grid was also contacted to attend.
