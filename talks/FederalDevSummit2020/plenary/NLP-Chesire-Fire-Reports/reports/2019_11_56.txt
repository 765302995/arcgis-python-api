 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/11/2019 - 20:18 Location: Lockerbie Close, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a small fire in wooded area off Lockerbie Close, Warrington. Crews located the fire which involved a wheelie bin. Crews used water to extinguish the flames.
