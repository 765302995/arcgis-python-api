 Two small fires in Frodsham Time of Incident: 20/04/2019 - 11:04 Location: Simons Lane, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to Simons Lane, Frodsham, after a report of a fire. Crews located two small fires near to the Sandstone trail and used a fire extinguisher to put them out.
