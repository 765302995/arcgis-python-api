Vehicle on fire in Haslington Time of Incident: 27/02/2017 - 10:17 Location: A534 Haslington, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to a heavy goods vehicle on fire and used two hose reels and one covering jet to extinguish the fire. They used cutting equipment to gain access to the rear of the vehicle which is believed to contain a large quantity of cans of fish. An inner cordon was set up at this incident and a  thermal imaging camera was also used to check the area.
