 Small fire in Mottram St Andrew Time of Incident: 07/03/2018 - 23:08 Location: Mill Lane, Mottram St Andrew Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a building site in Mill Lane in Mottram St Andrew. The fire, which involved a  large amount of tree cuttings and debris, was extinguished with a hose reel.
