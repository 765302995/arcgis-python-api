 Person trapped after crash in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/02/2018 - 15:36 Location: Church Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Birchwood Road traffic collision in WarringtonFire crews were called to a one vehicle road traffic collision on Church Street, Warrington. A car was on its side and a casualty was trapped. Crews used hydraulic cutting equipment to extract the casualty from the vehicle, who was then handed into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
