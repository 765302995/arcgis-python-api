Trees on fire outside property in Chester Time of Incident: 02/10/2016 - 22:09 Location: Whitchurch Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a fire involving trees outside a property in Chester. The fire was believed to have been caused by discarded smoking materials.  Always take care when throwing away smoking materials. Being careless can cause a serious fire.
