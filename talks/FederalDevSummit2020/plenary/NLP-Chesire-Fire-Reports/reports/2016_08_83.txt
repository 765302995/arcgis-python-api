Cooker fire within kitchen of property in Ellesmere Port. Time of Incident: 16/08/2016 - 19:11 Location: Princes Road in Ellesmere port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port attended an incident on Princes Road in Ellesmere port. The incident was a fire involving a cooker within kitchen of domestic property. Firefighters used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel jet to bring the fire under control. The premises was left to ventilate naturally.  Stay safe in your kitchen - read our kitchen fire safety tips
