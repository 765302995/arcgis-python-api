House fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 10/02/2016 - 12:20 Location: Darnhall School Lane, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford An unattended candle is believed to have caused a fire at a house in Winsford. The fire was in a bedroom.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the blaze. A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke from the property. Everyone was evacuated from the house while the incident was dealt with and gas and electricity supplies were isolated.  Please follow our candle safety advice - Candles in the home
