Rubbish fire which set light to hedge in Crewe Time of Incident: 18/07/2017 - 21:13 Location: Christleton Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A large fire involving the intentional burning of rubbish which had got out of control and spread to bushes was extinguished by firefighters using a hose reel. The fire was close to a railway but the smoke did not affect the line.  Fires such as this can quickly get out of control. Our advice is never to set light to anything, especially near property, trees or hedges.
