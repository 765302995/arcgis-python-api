 Rubbish fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 11/03/2019 - 18:31 Location: Percival Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews were called to Percival Lane, Runcorn, after a report of an uncontrolled rubbish burn. Crews found a quantity of wood burning in a commercial premises and they used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
