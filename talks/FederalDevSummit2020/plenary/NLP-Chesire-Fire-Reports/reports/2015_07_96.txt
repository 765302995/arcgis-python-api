Fire on central reservation in Chester Time of Incident: 17/07/2015 - 16:38 Location: A55, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A fire on a central reservation in Chester was extinguished with a hose reel. The fire involved grass and and one lane of the A55 was closed while the fire was dealt with. It is believed that the fire may have been started due to a discarded cigarette.
