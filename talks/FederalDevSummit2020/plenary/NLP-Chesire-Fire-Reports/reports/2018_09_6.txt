 Collision on the M53 near Chester Time of Incident: 27/09/2018 - 22:23 Location: M53 Southbound near Chester Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Chester, one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port. Shortly before 10.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a multi vehicle collision on the M53 near Chester. on arrival at the scene crews found that the collision involved three saloon cars and all of the occupants had self extricated. The team made the vehicles safe and assisted with traffic management at the scene. One casualty was passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service.
