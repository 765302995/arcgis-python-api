Car fire in Little Bollington Time of Incident: 04/06/2017 - 20:19 Location: Lymm Road, Little Bollington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford Car fire in Little BollingtonA car was on fire in a bus stop on Lymm Road. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put the fire out. Police also attended and closed the road because of smoke travelling across the road.
