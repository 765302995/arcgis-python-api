 Detached garage fire in Mickle Trafford, near Chester Time of Incident: 08/11/2019 - 12:08 Location: Nickolson Close, Mickle Trafford, near Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Shortly after 12 noon firefighters were called to a report of a fire in Nickolson Close, Mickle Trafford, near Chester.  On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved a wheelie bin and a double detached garage near to a domestic property.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire, which did not spread to the inside of the garage.  Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check that the fire had not spread to the inside of the garage.  The fire is believed to have been started accidentally as a result of ashes from a log burner having been placed in a wheelie bin.  Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.  For advice on preventing fires involving wheelie bins or hot ash visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
