Rubbish fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 14/09/2017 - 11:10 Location: Cumber Lane, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Fire crews were called to a rubbish fire on Cumber Lane, Wilmslow. On arrival they found a rubbish burning which had been left unattended. The used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
