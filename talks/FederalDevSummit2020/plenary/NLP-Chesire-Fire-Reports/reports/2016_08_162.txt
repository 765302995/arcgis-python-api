Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/08/2016 - 11:35 Location: Winwick Lane, Croft, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Leigh (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) One person was trapped after a road traffic collision in Croft. Three cars were involved. Firefighters released the casualty from the car and handed him into the care of the ambulance service. He was taken to hospital by land ambulance. The crews made the scene safe.
