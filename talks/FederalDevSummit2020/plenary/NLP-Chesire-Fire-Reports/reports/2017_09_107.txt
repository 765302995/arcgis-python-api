Fire inside a disused building in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/09/2017 - 19:50 Location: Page Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one from Widnes There was a fire inside a first floor room of a disused building. Smoke was coming from a first floor of the building. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire.  Police were requested to attend, because it is believed that the fire was started deliberately.  If you have any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
