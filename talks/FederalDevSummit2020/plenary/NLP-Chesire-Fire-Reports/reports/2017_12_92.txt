House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/12/2017 - 18:18 Location: Chepstow Close, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to a house on fire in Burtonwood and Westbrook, Warrington.  The fire involved the loft of the house and crews extinguished the fire using a hosereel and two breathing apparatus. They were in attendance for an hour and thirty five minutes.
