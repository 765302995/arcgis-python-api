 Teenager stuck in swing in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/11/2019 - 19:57 Location: Rowland Close, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 8pm a fire crew was called to Rowland Close after a teenage girl had become trapped in a child's swing.  Firefighters used hand tools to release the girl and were in attendance for 25 minutes.
