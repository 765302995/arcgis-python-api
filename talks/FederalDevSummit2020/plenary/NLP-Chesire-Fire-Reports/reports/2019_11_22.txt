 Fire alarm activation in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 24/11/2019 - 15:44 Location: Cannon Street, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Powey Lane and Ellesmere Port At around 3.45pm firefighters were called to reports that a fire alarm had activated at a residential address on Cannon Street, Ellesmere Port.  Firefighters attended the address and found that the activation was the result of a washing machine fire which was out on arrival.  The crew removed the washing machine from the property and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
