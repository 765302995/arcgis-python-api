House fire in Bewsey, Warrington Time of Incident: 07/05/2016 - 15:09 Location: Grafton Street, Bewsey, Warrington Attendance: Three fire engines from Warrington, Stockton heath and Penketh Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish a fire inside a property on Grafton Street in Warrington. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the property.  One casualty was treated by ambulance personnel for smoke inhalation. Police also attended this incident.
