Shed well alight in Chester Time of Incident: 17/07/2015 - 18:49 Location: Maple Grove, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester A shed was well alight in the back garden of a property on Maple Grove in Chester. Firefighters used two hose reels and one covering jet to extinguish the fire. Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check for hotspots.
