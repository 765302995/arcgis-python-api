 Car on fire at the Sandbach services of the M6 Time of Incident: 06/06/2018 - 09:31 Location: Sandbach services between junctions 16 and 17 of the M6 Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach At around 9.30am firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire at the Sandbach services between junctions 16 and 17 of the M6. On arrival the crew found a saloon vehicle well alight in a car park. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
