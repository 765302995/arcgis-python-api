Fire outside building in Boughton Time of Incident: 17/05/2017 - 12:56 Location: Boughton, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester A disused fridge freezer at the rear of a takeaway restaurant was on fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and are appealing for anyone with any information to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 as it is believed the fire was started deliberately.
