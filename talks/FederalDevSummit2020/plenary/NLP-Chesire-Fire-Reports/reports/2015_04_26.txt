Fire on railway embankment in Crewe Time of Incident: 26/04/2015 - 19:10 Location: Rochester Crescent, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Sandbach. A fire on a railway embankment required firefighters to attend and extinguish it. The fire involved 50 metres of grass which was well alight on the side of the track and two hose reels were used to put it out. A thermal imaging camera was then used to check the area before crews left.
