Deliberate fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 04/07/2015 - 00:07 Location: Poplar Close, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford Firefighters extinguished a small fire involving a fence and bushes outside a Winsford property.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101 or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
