 Commercial building fire in Chester Time of Incident: 13/02/2019 - 23:59 Location: A51 Boughton, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Chester and Powey Lane. Shortly before midnight firefighters from Chester and Powey Lane were dispatched to reports of a commercial building fire on the A51 in Boughton following multiple calls from the public.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a fire in the open at the rear of a commercial building.  While the fire was being monitored by the occupier of the building, crews were concerned that it was likely to spread so a decision was taken to extinguish it.  The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for around 20 minutes.
