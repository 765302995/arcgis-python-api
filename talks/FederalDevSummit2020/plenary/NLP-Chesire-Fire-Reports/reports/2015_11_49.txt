Garage fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/11/2015 - 13:16 Location: Horseshoe Drive, Macclesfield. Attendance: Fire engines from Macclesfield, Bollington and Poynton Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Horseshoe Drive, Macclesfield.   When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving a two-storey garage that was attached to a house.   Firefighters used two hose reel jets, four breathing apparatus, a main jet and large fan to deal with the blaze.  They remained on scene for two hours and forty five minutes.
