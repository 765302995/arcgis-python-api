Car on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 11/07/2016 - 22:36 Location: West Park, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a hatchback car on fire and used two hose reels to extinguish it. This fire is believed to have been started deliberately and police were called.  If anyone has any information about who may have started it they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
