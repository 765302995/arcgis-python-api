Bush and house fire in Chester Time of Incident: 25/08/2017 - 14:00 Location: Hough Green, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Fire crews were called to a large bush fire, which had spread to a house, on Hough Green, Chester. Approximately 20m of conifer bushes were well alight on arrival of the crews which spread to the gable end of a detached house. Crews used two hose reel jets to fight the fire. Cheshire Police also attended the incident. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident please call Cheshire Police on 101 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
