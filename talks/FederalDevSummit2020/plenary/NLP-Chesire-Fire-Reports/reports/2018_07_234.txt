 Small fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 05/07/2018 - 07:03 Location: Danefield Road, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich. Firefighters from Northwich used water to extinguish a tree stump which was on fire under near a footbridge on Danefield Road. After extinguishing the fire the team used cutting equipment to remove the remaining tree stump to prevent the fire reigniting. Crews were at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
