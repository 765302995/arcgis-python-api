 Electrical fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/09/2019 - 14:41 Location: Holmfield Avenue, Runcorn. WA7 5UR Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to Holmfield Avenue after reports of a fire in a house.  They found an electrical fault had caused a small fire, which was out on arrival, and isolated the electrics to make the house safe. The crew also discovered a gas leak and isolated the supply. They were at the house for just under an hour.
