 Wood fire in the grounds of a high school in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/05/2019 - 13:29 Location: Grangeway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 1.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the grounds of St Chad's Catholic and Church of England High School on Grangeway, Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire in the open involved a quantity of wood. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which measured approximately eight square metres. They then cooled the area affected by the fire down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 398937, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
