Wheelie bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 21/02/2015 - 01:14 Location: Palace Fields Avenue, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn One hose reel jet was used to extinguish a wheelie bin fire, where the wheelie bin was well alight.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
