Casualty trapped in car on Buxton New Road Time of Incident: 06/08/2015 - 12:11 Location: Buxton New Road, Rainow Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield and one from Bollington. Firefighters have attended a road traffic collision on Buxton New Road in Rainow involving one saloon car.  One casualty was trapped in the vehicle and released by firefighters using hydraulic cutting gear before being transported to hospital by air ambulance.  Firefighters were at the scene for approximately one hour and ten minutes.
