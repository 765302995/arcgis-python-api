Car in collision with lorry on M56 Time of Incident: 29/08/2015 - 07:13 Location: M56 Eastbound near junction 14 Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended a collision on the M56 involving a car and lorry between junction 12 and 14.  No one was trapped and the Ambulance Service attended for one casualty. Police were also at the scene and the crew were there for around 45 minutes.
