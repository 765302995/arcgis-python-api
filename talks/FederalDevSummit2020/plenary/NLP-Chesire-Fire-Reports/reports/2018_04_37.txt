 Garage fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 22/04/2018 - 09:44 Location: London Road, Northwich Attendance: Three fire engines attended - two from Northwich and one from Winsford At around 9.45pm firefighters were called to reports of an outbuilding on fire on London Road in Davenham, Northwich. On arrival crews found a garage well alight. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. A main jet was also used to fight the fire. Crews then damped down the area and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. A man suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation was handed over to the care of paramedics. Fire crews were in attendance almost four hours.
