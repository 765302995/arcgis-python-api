 Crash in Gawsworth Time of Incident: 27/04/2018 - 11:08 Location: Woodhouse Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Road traffic collision in GawsworthFire crews were called to a road traffic collision on Woodhouse Lane, Gawsworth. A landrover which had struck an embankment was stabilised using stabilisation equipment by crews. One casualty was taken to hospital by paramedics.
