 Fire in the open, Warrington Time of Incident: 06/07/2019 - 12:08 Location: Lapwing Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh Firefighters were called to deal with a fire at the Moore Nature Reserve. They found it involved a jetty, which was smouldering. They used a hose reel to put it out, and a thermal imaging camera to ensure there were no remaining hotspots. They were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
