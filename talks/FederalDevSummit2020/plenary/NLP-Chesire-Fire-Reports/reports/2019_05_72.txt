 Small fire involving street furniture in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/05/2019 - 03:13 Location: Victoria Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At around 3.15am firefighters were dispatched to reports of a small fire involving street furniture in the grounds of Macclesfield Hospital.   Crews arrived at the scene and found a small fire involving a bollard which was in close proximity to a number of oxygen tanks.  Firefighters used dry powder to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for around 20 minutes.
