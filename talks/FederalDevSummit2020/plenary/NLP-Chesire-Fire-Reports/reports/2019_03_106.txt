 Parrot rescue in Crewe Time of Incident: 07/03/2019 - 12:54 Location: Wistaston Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to Wistaston Road, Crewe, to rescue a parrot which had escaped from a property. The parrot was at the top of a tree and crews, under the instruction of experts from the RSPCA, used a hose reel jet to spray the parrot with water to try and persuade it to come down. The parrot came down after around 10 minutes and was reunited with its owner. The parrot was checked over and was fine.
