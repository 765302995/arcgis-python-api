 Van fire in Haslington Time of Incident: 17/12/2018 - 18:42 Location: Kingsley Road, Haslington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly before 6.45pm firefighters were called to reports of a van being on fire on Kingsley Road in Haslington. On arrival the crew discovered a small van well alight. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters then damped the engine compartment down to make sure that it was safe. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
