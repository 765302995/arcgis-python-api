 Wheat fire in High Legh Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 13:12 Location: A50, High Leigh Attendance: Five fire engines attended - one from Lymm, one from Warrington, one from Birchwood, one from Runcorn and one from Wilmslow. Wheat fire in High LeghShortly after 1pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a field near to the A50 in High Legh.  Five crews attended the scene and found approximately 50 square metres of wheat which was well alight. The team used four main jets, one hose reel jet and beaters to extinguish the fire.  the team were in attendance at the scene for approximately three hours.
