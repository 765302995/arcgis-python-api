Garage fire in Helsby Time of Incident: 06/11/2016 - 11:01 Location: Meliden Grove, Frodsham Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Firefighters attended a fire involving a garage at a house on Meliden Grove. The crews, six wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels to bring the blaze under control. They then removed debris from the garage and asked for an electrical engineer to attend to isolate mains power as the electricity supply had been damaged.  One man was taken to hospital as a precaution. Crews were at the scene for just over four and a half hours.
