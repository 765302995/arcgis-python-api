 House fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 06/04/2018 - 18:23 Location: Bankes Lane, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one fire engine attended from Widnes At 6:22pm firefighters from Runcorn and Widnes attended a kitchen fire on Bankes Lane, Runcorn, involving a toaster. The fire was out on arrival, but the property was heavily smoke logged, so crews used a large fan to clear all of the smoke. Crews were in attendance for 30 minutes.
