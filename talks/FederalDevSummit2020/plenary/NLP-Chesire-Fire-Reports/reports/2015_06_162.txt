Refuse fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 05/06/2015 - 02:31 Location: Brecon Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Police were requested to attend the scene of a fire in Winsford due to firefighters suspecting the cause to be deliberate.  The fire involved a large amount of rubbish in the front garden of a property and one hose reel was used to extinguish the blaze.  Anyone with any information about the incident is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
