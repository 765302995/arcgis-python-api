 Skip fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 15/04/2018 - 00:38 Location: Chatsworth Drive, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Crews tackled a skip that was alight near a residential address in Chatsworth Drive. A single hose reel jet was used to extinguish the flames, and it's believed to have been a deliberate ignition. Anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire |Police on m101, quoting IML 36643.
