 Possible house fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/09/2018 - 07:29 Location: Avery Close, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At approximately 7.30am firefighters from Warrington were called to reports of a possible house fire Avery Close. The caller reported that there was a hazy smoke in a number of rooms at the address. Crews attended the scene and found that the smoke had been caused by an electrical fault in the loft area. The team isolated the electric at the property and used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. An electrician has also been called to attend the property. Firefighters were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
