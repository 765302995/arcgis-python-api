 Rubbish fire, Middlewich Time of Incident: 16/02/2019 - 22:13 Location: Fairacre Drive, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich Crews were called to a small rubbish fire in a residential street in Middlewich. They found the fire in a hedgerow and used a water backpack to extinguish the flames. They were there for around 10 minutes.
