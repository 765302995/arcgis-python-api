Storage garage on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/08/2016 - 14:05 Location: Poplars Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A garage used for storage was on fire on Poplars Avenue. The garage was well alight.Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze. There were other garages nearby which were checked for smoke and fire spread.  It is believed the fire was started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
