 Shed load in Saltney Time of Incident: 07/11/2019 - 13:46 Location: River Lane, Chester Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Chester and Powey Lane At around 1.45pm firefighters were called to reports that a lorry had shed its load on River Lane, Saltney.  Crews attended the scene and on arrival they found that a number of gallon drums containing cooking oil had fallen onto the road.  As a result approximately 140 litres of cooking oil had spilt onto the road.  Firefighters used specialist equipment to soak up the oil and prevent the oil from entering the drains, the also cleared all of the damaged containers from the roadway.  Crews were in attendance at the scene for around 40 minutes.
