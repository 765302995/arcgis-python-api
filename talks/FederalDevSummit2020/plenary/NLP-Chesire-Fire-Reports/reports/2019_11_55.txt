 Bedroom fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/11/2019 - 22:07 Location: Kirkham Close, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh. One fire engine from Warrington. Firefighters were called to a bedroom fire at an address in Kirkham Close, Warrington. The occupier extinguished the fire using a dry powder extinguisher.  Crews used a large fan to disperse the smoke and a thermal imaging camera to check there were no hot spots.  One casualty suffering from smoke inhalation was given oxygen.
