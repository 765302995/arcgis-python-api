 Field fire in Over Peover Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 11:13 Location: Grotto Lane, Over Peover. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Fire in a field in Over Peover At 11:13 firefighters from Wilmslow were called to reports of a fire in a field in Over Peover. On arrival crews discovered a two grass fires approximately 120m x 10m.  The team used hose reel jets and beaters to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes.
