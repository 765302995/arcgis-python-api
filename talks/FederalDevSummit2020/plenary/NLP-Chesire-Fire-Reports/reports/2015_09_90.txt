Building fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 12/09/2015 - 18:16 Location: Mill Lane, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port  Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at an empty building on Mill Lane, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found a fire close to the entrance of the property which they extinguished using two breathing apparatus, a hose reel and  main water jet.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke. No casualties were reported and crews were in attendance for about 60 minutes.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
