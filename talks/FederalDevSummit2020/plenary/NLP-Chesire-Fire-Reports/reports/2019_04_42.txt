 Van fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 23/04/2019 - 04:34 Location: Donagh Close, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a van fire on Donagh Close, Macclesfield. On arrival the van was well alight and firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information please contact Cheshire Police.
