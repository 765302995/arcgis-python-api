Arson in Winsford Time of Incident: 24/10/2016 - 18:34 Location: Dee Way, Winsford. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Dee Way, Winsford.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fence panel on fire and another fire that involved a sofa.  Hose reel jets were used to put the fires out.  It is believed that these fires were started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
