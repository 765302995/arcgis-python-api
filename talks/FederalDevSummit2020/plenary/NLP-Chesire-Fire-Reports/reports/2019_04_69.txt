 Carpets on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 21/04/2019 - 15:45 Location: Kingsley Walk, Winsford. CW7 2HE Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open at Kinglsey Walk. They attended, found a number of carpets on fire and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
