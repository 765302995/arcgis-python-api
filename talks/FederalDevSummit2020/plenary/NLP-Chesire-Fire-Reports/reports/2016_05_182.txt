Large rubbish fire in Wardle Time of Incident: 30/04/2016 - 16:12 Location: Nantwich Road, Wardle. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Tarporley. Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at the empty Jolly Tar Inn on Nantwich Road, Wardle.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving a large amount of demolition waste outside the property.  Crews used main water jets to put the fire out and damp down the surrounding area.
