Car well alight in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 23/01/2016 - 03:26 Location: Wentworth Avenue, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A car was well alight, so firefighters used one hose reel to deal with the fire. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
