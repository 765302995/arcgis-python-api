 Chimney fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 25/01/2018 - 18:28 Location: Chester Lane, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and the aerial appliance from Chester Firefighters were called to a chimney fire on Chester Lane, Winsford. Crews used chimney rods, a hose reel jet and the aerial appliance to extinguish the fire. The road was briefly shut while crews tackled the fire. Chimney fire safety
