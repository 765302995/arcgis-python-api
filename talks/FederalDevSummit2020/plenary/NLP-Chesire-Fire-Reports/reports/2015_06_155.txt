Small fire on an astroturf pitch at Sutton High School in Ellesmere port Time of Incident: 06/06/2015 - 10:47 Location: Sutton High School, Mill Lane in Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters used hand extinguishers to deal with a fire in the middle of the astroturf pitch on Mill Lane in Ellesmere Port.  This fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about the incident is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
