 Congleton flat fire caused by unattended cooking Time of Incident: 08/12/2018 - 16:20 Location: Brierly Road, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Fire crews attended to an address on Brierly Road in Congleton following reports of a fire in the home.  On arrival firefighters, wearing two breathing apparatus, used a hose reel to extinguish the fire.  Crews established the fire had started from food being left unattended on a cooker.  A fan was used to disperse smoke and make sure the house was safe.  Cooking safety tips can be found on our website.
