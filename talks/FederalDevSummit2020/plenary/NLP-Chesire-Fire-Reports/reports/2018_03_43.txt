 Chimney fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 24/03/2018 - 19:41 Location: Littler Lane, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to a chimney fire in Littler Lane, Winsford. Flames could be seen coming out of the chimney. Crews used chimney rods and a hose reel to tackle the fire. They then checked for hotspots to ensure the fire did not reignite.
