 Fires on a field in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 22:33 Location: Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 10.30pm firefighters were called to a report of fires on a field in Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered multiple seats of fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fires and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. The fires are believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
