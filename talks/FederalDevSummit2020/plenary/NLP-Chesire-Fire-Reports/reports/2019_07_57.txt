 Collision in Chester Time of Incident: 19/07/2019 - 22:47 Location: Chowley Oak Lane, Tattenhall, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas At around 10.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision in Tattenhall, Chester. On arrival at Chowley Oak Lane the crew made a vehicle safe. Everyone was accounted for and there were no signs of fire in the vehicle. Firefighters checked the vehicle for hot spots using a thermal imaging camera and were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
