 Dishwasher fire in Chester Time of Incident: 02/11/2018 - 09:30 Location: Stamford Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane and Ellesmere Port Fire crews attended to a dishwasher alight at an address on Stamford Road in Chester.  Firefighters, wearing two breathing apparatus, used a positive pressure ventilation fan to put the fire and dampened down the area.
