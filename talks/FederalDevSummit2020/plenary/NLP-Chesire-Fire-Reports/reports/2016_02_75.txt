Car fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 13/02/2016 - 20:09 Location: Brookledge Lane, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton There was a small fire inside the engine compartment of a car, so firefighters used a water back pack to cool the engine. The engine was then checked with a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots of fire.
