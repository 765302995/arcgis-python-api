 Car fire in Chester Time of Incident: 05/02/2018 - 22:26 Location: Waverley Terrace, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Fire crews were called to a car fire on Waverley Terrace, Chester. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which had been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident please call Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
