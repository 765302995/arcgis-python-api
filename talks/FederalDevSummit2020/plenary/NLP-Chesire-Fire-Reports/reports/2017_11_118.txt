Bedroom fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 04/11/2017 - 20:58 Location: Wheelman Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At 9pm on Saturday, 4 November two fire engines from crewe attended a fire on wheelman road involving a bedroom at a domestic property. Firefighters used one hose reel, positive pressure ventilation fan and thermal image camera to extinguish the fire. One casualty was checked over by ambulance crews at the scene due to suffering from smoke inhalation. Firefighters were in attendance for one hour.
