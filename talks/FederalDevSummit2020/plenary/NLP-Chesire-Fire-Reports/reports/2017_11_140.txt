Skip fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/11/2017 - 19:17 Location: Bechers, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish a skip fire on Betchers, Widnes. An investigation has been launched into the cause of the fire. If anyone has any information about how this fire may have started please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
