Bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/12/2017 - 22:20 Location: Fairclough Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters attended a bin fire on Fairclough Avenue, Warrington.  On arrival crews discovered two wheelie bins on fire, which had also spread to the gate and rear fence panel.  Firefighters extinguished the fire using one hose reel jet.  The incident has been passed over to police for investigation.
