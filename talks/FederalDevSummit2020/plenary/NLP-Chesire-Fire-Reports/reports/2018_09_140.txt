 Fire in scrubland in Middlewich Time of Incident: 02/09/2018 - 10:21 Location: Road Beta, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Fire crews were called to a fire in scrubland on Road Beta, Middlewich. The fire measured approximately 10m by 5m and was well alight. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
