 Fire in open small fire in Peover Time of Incident: 19/05/2018 - 21:48 Location: A50, Peover. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford Shortly before 10pm firefighters from Knutsford and Lymm attended a fire on the A50 at over Peover, near Knutsford.  On arrival crews found a large bonfire which was encroaching on a nearby hedgerow. After conducting an assessment of the scene and decision was taken to extinguish the fire.  Crews used two main jets to extinguish the fire and were on scene for one hour and 10 minutes.
