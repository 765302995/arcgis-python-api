House fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 01/01/2017 - 16:49 Location: Crescent Road, Congleton. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Congleton and Alsager Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Crescent Road, Congleton. When they arrived crews confirmed that there was a fire in the bedroom of the semi-detached property. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus tackled the fire with hose reel jets and a large fan.  The neighbouring property was also checked to make sure that the fire had not spread.  Two people were treated for the effects in inhaling smoke.  It is believed that this fire was stated by a set of hair straighteners that had caused a smoldering fire.  Electrical fire safety advice
