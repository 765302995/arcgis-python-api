 Grass fire in country park in Nether Aderley Time of Incident: 21/06/2018 - 21:27 Location: Bradford Lane, Nether Alderley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 9.25pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at Wizard Country Park on Bradford Lane in Nether Alderley. On arrival the crew discovered that the grass fire measured approximately 20 square metres. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and then dampened the area down to make it safe. They were in attendance for around 35 minutes.
