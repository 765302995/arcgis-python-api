Fire in Winsford playground Time of Incident: 06/05/2015 - 22:12 Location: Brecon Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford A hose reel jet was used to put out a fire involving playground apparatus.  Firefighters believe the fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information regarding this, or similar, incidents, please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
