 Man suffers facial injury in fall in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/11/2019 - 09:18 Location: Widnes Road, Penketh, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Shortly after 9.15am firefighters were called to a report of an elderly man having fallen from a pavement and sustained a facial injury on Widnes Road in Penketh, Warrington.  The crew administered first aid to the man before handing him over to the care of paramedics.  He was taken to hospital via an ambulance.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
