Chip pan fire at Alban's Retail Park in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/09/2015 - 08:51 Location: Hawleys Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters have attended a chip pan fire at a commercial building on Alban Retail Park, Warrington. Crews used two breathing apparatus, two hose reels, two dry powder extinguishers, one covering jet and positive pressure ventialtion equipment to deal with the incident.  Firefighters also inspected the roof to ensure that the fire had not spread and were on scene for around 50 minutes.  Chip pan safety advice
