 Trailer fire in Birchwood Time of Incident: 20/10/2018 - 06:41 Location: Leacroft Road, Warrington. Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighers were called to an HGV trailer fire at Leacroft Road, Birchwood at around 6.40am. The fire was out on arrival, but crews used a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots and then used a hose reel jet to cool overheated brakes.
