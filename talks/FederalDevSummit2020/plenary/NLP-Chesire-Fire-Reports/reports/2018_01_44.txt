 Cellar fire in Chester Time of Incident: 21/01/2018 - 03:53 Location: Westminster Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester, one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port. Shortly before 1am on Sunday 21 January 2018 firefighters were called to reports of a house fire on Westminster Road, Chester. Crews from Chester, Powey Lane and Ellesmere Port attended the scene and on arrival it was discovered that the fire  was in a cavity wall in the cellar of the house. Two firefighters, wearing two breathing apparatus, used a thermal imaging camera to locate the fire and cutting equipment to isolate the fire. Crews remained on scene for approximately two and a half hours.
