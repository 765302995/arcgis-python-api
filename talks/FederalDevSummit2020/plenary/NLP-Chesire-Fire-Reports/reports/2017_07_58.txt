Grill pan on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/07/2017 - 18:27 Location: Warren Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, one from Lymm and one from Penketh Firefighters were called to a grill pan in a cooker on fire and used a hose reel to extinguish it. The cooker was removed to outside the property and all persons were accounted for. Grill pan safety advice.
