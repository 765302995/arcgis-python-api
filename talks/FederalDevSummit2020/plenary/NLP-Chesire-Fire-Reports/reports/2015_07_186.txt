Motorbike on fire on the M6 southbound Time of Incident: 03/07/2015 - 13:47 Location: M6 southbound between junctions 17 and 16. Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and one from Holmes Chapel A motorbike on fire on the hardshoulder of the M6 was extinguished by firefighters using hose reel. Three lanes of the motorway were stopped due to heavy smoke logging. One man suffered from burns at this incident and was treated by the crew prior to the arrival of paramedics. Highways Agency also attended.
