 Hay bales on fire in Hough Time of Incident: 21/08/2019 - 16:26 Location: Newcastle Road, Hough Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Crewe, one from Nantwich and one from Audlem Firefighters attended to a number of hay bales on fire in a shed on Newcastle Road in Hough.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews used an extended hose reel jet to put out the fire and then dampened down the area.  All hay bales were removed from the shed and a thermal imaging camera was used to check for hot spots.
