Outbuilding fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 21/08/2016 - 17:14 Location: Alder Avenue, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and two from Warrington and Runcorn Firefighters were alerted to an outbuilding fire on Alder Avenue in Widnes. Fire crews, wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to put out the fire before damping the area down afterwards.  View a video of the fire
