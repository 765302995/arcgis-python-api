Collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 28/08/2016 - 12:33 Location: Junction 21 and 22 on the M6. Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington, Birchwood and Stockton Heath Firefighters responded to a collision between Junction 21 and 22 on the M6.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved a heavy goods vehicle and a car.  One woman was trapped.  Firefighters freed the casualty who was then passed to the care of the ambulance service.
