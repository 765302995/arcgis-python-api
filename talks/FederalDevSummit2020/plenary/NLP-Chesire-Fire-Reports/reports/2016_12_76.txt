House fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 17/12/2016 - 13:51 Location: Chaucer Grove, Sandbach. Attendance: Three fire engines from Sandbach, Crewe and Alsager fire stations Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Chaucer Grove, Sandbach.  When they arrived they confirmed that there was a blaze on the first floor of a semi-detached property. Crews wearing breathing apparatus used hose reel and main water jets to tackle the fire. Thermal imaging cameras and large fans were also used during the incident.  The neighbouring properties were all checked to ensure that there was no fire spread.   Crews were detained for two hours.
