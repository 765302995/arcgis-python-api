 Wheat field fire in Cronton Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 14:54 Location: Hall Lane, Cronton, Widnes Attendance: A fire engine from Widnes attended Firefighters are currently dealing with a fire in a wheat field on Hall Lane in Cronton, Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved half a dozen hotspots in the field, which previously contained grass. An area measuring approximately 14 metres by three metres was smouldering with hotspots. Firefighters are currently using beaters to extinguish the fire.  UPDATE: 5.15pm:  Small pockets of wheat and grass now extinguished.
