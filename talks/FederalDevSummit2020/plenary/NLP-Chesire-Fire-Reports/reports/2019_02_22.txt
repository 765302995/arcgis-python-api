 One-vehicle collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/02/2019 - 08:34 Location: Barthomley Road/Butterton Lane, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Shortly after 8.30am firefighters were called to reports of a one-vehicle collision on the junction of Barthomley Road and Butterton Lane in Crewe. On arrival crews found a saloon car on its side and that a female was trapped inside it. She did not appear to be injured. Firefighters made the vehicle safe and helped the casualty to get out of the car. They then handed her over to the care of paramedics to be assessed. Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
