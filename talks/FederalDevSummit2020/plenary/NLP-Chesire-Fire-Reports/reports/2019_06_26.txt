 Smoke alarm triggered at a flat in Chester Time of Incident: 25/06/2019 - 12:00 Location: Clover Lane, Lache, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At 12 noon firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm having being activated at a flat on Clover Lane in Lache, Chester. On arrival the crew discovered that the alarm had been triggered as a result of cooking food having been left unattended. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the cooking fumes from the premises and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
