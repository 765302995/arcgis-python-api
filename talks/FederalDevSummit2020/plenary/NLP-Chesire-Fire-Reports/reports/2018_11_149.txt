 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/11/2018 - 20:59 Location: Sandy Brow Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters from Birchwood used two hose reel jets and two breathing apparatus to extinguish a car fire on Sandy Brow Lane, Warrington. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with any details is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 233039. Information can also be passed anonymously via Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
