Industrial bin on fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 19/10/2015 - 19:03 Location: Tatton Street, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters were called to report of a bin on fire in a car park in Knutsford. The crew put out the fire in an industrial skip, which is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
