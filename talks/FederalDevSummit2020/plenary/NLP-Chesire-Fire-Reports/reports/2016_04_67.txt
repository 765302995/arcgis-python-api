Casualty rescue in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 18/04/2016 - 19:48 Location: The Silk Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine and the hydraulic platform from Macclesfield Firefighters assisted police and ambulance in rescuing a casualty from an embankment.  The aerial platform was used in the rescue and police closed the road while it was in use.
