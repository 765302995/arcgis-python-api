Car on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/11/2019 - 12:54 Location: Lyncastle Way, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm Firefighters dealt with a car fire at an industrial estate in Appleton Thorn, Warrington. The fire was out on the arrival of firefighters who are currently using a thermal imaging camera to check the vehicle and a hose reel to damp down any hot spots.
