 Fire in engine compartment of a car in Chester Time of Incident: 13/05/2019 - 11:41 Location: Woodlands Avenue, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 11.40am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a car on Woodlands Avenue, Chester. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire in the engine compartment. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
