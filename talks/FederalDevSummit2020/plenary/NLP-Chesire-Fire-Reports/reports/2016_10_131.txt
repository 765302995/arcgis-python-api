Road traffic collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 09/10/2016 - 13:40 Location: M6, near to junction 21 Attendance: Fire engines from Birchwood, Stockton Heath, Knutsford and Altrincham attended Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision which required two people needing rescuing from a vehicle. The crews used cutting equipment to get the pair out and onto long boards and to the care of paramedics.
