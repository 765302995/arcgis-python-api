 Bedroom fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 21/04/2018 - 12:50 Location: Bradfield Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Fire crews were alerted to a bedroom fire on Bradfield Road, Crewe.  Firefighters used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel, positive pressure ventilation unit and one covering jet to extinguish the fire.
