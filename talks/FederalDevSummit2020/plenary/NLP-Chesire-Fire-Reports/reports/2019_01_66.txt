 Smokelogging in Nantwich Time of Incident: 31/12/2018 - 17:12 Location: Hastings Road, Nantwich Attendance:  One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Crewe Unattended cooking left a Nantwich kitchen smokelogged. Firefighters had initially been called to a report of a kitchen fire. On arrival, found no fire but a large amount of smoke which they cleared using a high pressure fan.  Never leave cooking unattended. Please follow our kitchen safety advice.
