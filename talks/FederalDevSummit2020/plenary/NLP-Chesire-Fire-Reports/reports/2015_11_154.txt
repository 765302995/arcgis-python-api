Small rubbish fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/11/2015 - 02:32 Location: Mersey Street, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a domestic property on fire with persons reported. On arrival a small rubbish fire was found in a bin compound with no casualties. The fire was extinguished with a hose reel.
