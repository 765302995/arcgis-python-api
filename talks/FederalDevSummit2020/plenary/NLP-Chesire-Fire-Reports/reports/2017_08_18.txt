Tractor fire in Buerton Time of Incident: 28/08/2017 - 14:00 Location: Hankins Heys Lane, Buerton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich A tractor was heavily involved n fire in a Buerton farmyard. Firefighters, two in breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control. They then cooled the tractor and surrounding area down using a hose reel. A thermal imaging camera was used to make sure there were no hot spots around the farmyard.
