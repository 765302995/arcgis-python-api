 Fire at a park in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/06/2018 - 18:56 Location: Lysander Drive, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 7pm firefighters attended reports of a fire at a park on Lysander Drive in Warrington. The fire involved a bin which had been deliberately set alight. The team used a water back pack to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes. Anyone with any information in relation to the fire is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
