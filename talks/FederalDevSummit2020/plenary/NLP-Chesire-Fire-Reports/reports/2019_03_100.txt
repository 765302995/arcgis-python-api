 Man rescued from ditch in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/03/2019 - 21:06 Location: Winmarleigh Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath Firefighters went to the assistance of a man who had fallen over a three foot wall into a ditch and couldn't get out in Winmarleigh Street, Warrington. Crews used a scoop to rescue the man from the enclosed space and handed him over to paramedics.
