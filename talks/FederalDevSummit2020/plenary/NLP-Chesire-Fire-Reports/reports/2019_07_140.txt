 Fire in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/07/2019 - 19:05 Location: Whitchurch Way, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly after 7pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in Whitchurch Way, Runcorn. On arrival the crew found approximately two square metres of wood alight under a footbridge at a busway. Firefighters extinguished the fire, which was near traffic lights, with water and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
