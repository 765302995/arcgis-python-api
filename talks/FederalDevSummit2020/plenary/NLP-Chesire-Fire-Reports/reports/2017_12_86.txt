Collision on M6 Time of Incident: 02/12/2017 - 08:02 Location: Junction 20, M6, Southbound . Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one fire engine from Lymm attended. Firefighters attended a collision at junction 20 of the M6 southbound.  A HGV and a car were involved in the collision, and as a result diesel was leaking from the HGV.  Crews stopped the fuel leak from the HGV tank and the vehicles were recovered.
