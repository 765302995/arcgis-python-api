 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/08/2019 - 15:25 Location: Molyneux Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. Firefighters were called to a fire in a kitchen at an address in Molyneux Avenue, Warrington. An unattended pan on a cooker had caught fire but was out on arrival. Crews used a large fan to disperse smoke. Here's a link to information about kitchen safety https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety
