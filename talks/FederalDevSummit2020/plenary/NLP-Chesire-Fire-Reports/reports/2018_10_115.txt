 Garage fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 10/10/2018 - 12:23 Location: Barnton Way, Sandbach. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Sandbach and one from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe and Sandbach attended reports of a fire at a residential address in Sandbach. On arrival at the scene crews discovered a fire in a converted garage along with a small fire on the driveway. The team used electronic cutting equipment to remove the garage door, the then used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance at the scene for approximately two hours.
