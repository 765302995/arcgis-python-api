Building fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 16/01/2016 - 15:27 Location: Crewe Road, Nantwich. Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe and from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at an empty building on Crewe Road, Nantwich.  When they arrived they were confronted with a well developed fire on the first floor.  Crews used hose reels and main water jets along with special fans to bring the blaze under control. They remained at the scene for more than two hours.  A fire investigation is ongoing but at this stage it is believed that it was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
