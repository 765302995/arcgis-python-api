 Small fire at a park in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/11/2019 - 20:31 Location: Wellfield Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Warrington firefighters used a water backpack to put out a small fire involving a quantity of rubbish on a park on Wellfield Street.  The crew were in attendance at the incident for around 15 minutes.
