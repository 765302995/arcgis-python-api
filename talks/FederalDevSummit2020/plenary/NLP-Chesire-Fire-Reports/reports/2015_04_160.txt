Collision in Middlewich Time of Incident: 10/04/2015 - 19:52 Location: Bostock Road, Middlewich. Attendance: A fire engine from Middlewich Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Bostock Road, Middlewich.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved one car. A man had managed to get out of the car and was being treated by paramedics. Firefighters helped make the area safe before leaving.
