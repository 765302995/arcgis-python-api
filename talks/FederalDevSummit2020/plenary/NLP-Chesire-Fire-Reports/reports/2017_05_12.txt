Shed and fence fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/05/2017 - 22:38 Location: Weston Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Widnes A fire in a large wooden garden shed had spread to fencing. Firefighters used two main and two hose reel jets to bring the fire under control.  They were at the scene for approximately one hour forty five minutes.
