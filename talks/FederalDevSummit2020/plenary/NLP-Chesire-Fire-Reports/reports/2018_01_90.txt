 Person trapped after crash in Stockton Heath Time of Incident: 10/01/2018 - 14:34 Location: Gaskell Street, Stockton Heath Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm, one from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision on Gaskell Street, Stockton Heath. The crash involved one vehicle and crews released on casualty from the car before they were handed into the care of paramedics.
