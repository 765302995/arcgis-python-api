Derelict building fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 12/04/2016 - 00:49 Location: Rochester Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port, one from Chester and the aerial appliance from Chester Four appliances were called to a building fire in Ellesmere Port. This was a fire on the first floor and roof space of a derelict building. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the blaze and a high pressure fan to clear smoke. They were at the scene for approximately three hours.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
