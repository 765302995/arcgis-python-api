Two car collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/12/2016 - 11:35 Location: Laburnum Avenue/Manchester Road, Woolston, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engine from Warrington Fire engines attended a two car collision on Laburnum Avenue. Nobody was trapped in the incident and the crews made the scene safe. A child who was in one of the cars was taken to hospital for a precautionary check.
