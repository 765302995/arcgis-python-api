 Two vehicle collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 10/11/2018 - 07:45 Location: Buxton Old Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters from Macclesfield attended reports of a vehicle collision near to the Stanley Arms on Buxton Old Road. On arrival crews found that the collision involved two vehicles and the driver of one vehicle was medically trapped. The team used specialist cutting equipment to release the casualty and they were passed to the care of paramedics. Crews were detained at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
