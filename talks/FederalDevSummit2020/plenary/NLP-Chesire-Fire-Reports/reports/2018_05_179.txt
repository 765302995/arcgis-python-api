 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/05/2018 - 17:02 Location: Manchester Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 5pm firefighters were called to a reports of a car being on fire on Manchester Road in Warrington. On arrival the crew found a fire in the engine compartment of a saloon car. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which is believed to be a case of accidental ignition due to an electrical fault. The crew were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
