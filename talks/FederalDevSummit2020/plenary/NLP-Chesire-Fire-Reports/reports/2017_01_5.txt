Deep fat fryer fire in kitchen in Widnes Time of Incident: 29/01/2017 - 12:20 Location: Lower House Lane, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and Runcorn Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet and a dry powder fire extinguisher to put out a fire involving a deep fat fryer in a kitchen in a commercial building.  More than half of accidental fires in the home are started by cooking. You can protect your home and family by always following cooking safety guidelines.
