Road traffic collision in Clotton Time of Incident: 06/08/2016 - 21:55 Location: High Street, Clotton Attendance: Fire engines from Chester, Ellesmere Port and Winsford One casualty was rescued from a car after it was in collision with a tractor. Firefighters had to remove the roof of the car to remove the lady who was then handed into the care of the ambulance service.
