 Derelict building fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 29/05/2019 - 19:31 Location: New Road, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Winsford and one from Middlewich Fire crews attended to a fire involving a derelict building on New Road in Winsford.  Firefighers, wearing two breathing apparatus, brought the fire under control with one hose reel.  A thermal imaging camera was then used to ensure no one was inside the building and the fire was fully extinguished.  They were at the scene for approximately one hour and twenty five minutes.
