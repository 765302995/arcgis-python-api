Shed on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/02/2015 - 17:11 Location: Allotments on Factory Lane / Derby Road, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters used two hose reel jets to tackle a shed fire that was well alight within an allotment area. Firefighters set up an inner cordon. Environmental health were informed.  Police also attended this incident.
