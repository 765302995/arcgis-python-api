Small fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/05/2017 - 19:01 Location: Padgate Lane in Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Padgate Lane in Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved some trees. A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out.  This fire was believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
