Building fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 03/05/2015 - 15:59 Location: Darwin Street, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a domestic building on Darwin Street, Northwich.   The fire was located in the communal area of the property. Crews used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to bring the fire under control. Crews were at the scene for about three hours
