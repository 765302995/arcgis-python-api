 Garage fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 29/07/2018 - 23:54 Location: Grangemoor, Runcorn. Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the garage of an address in Grangemoor, Runcorn. Crews fought the fire which had spread to a second garage using two breathing apparatus and one hose reel. Fire and police investigations are ongoing.
