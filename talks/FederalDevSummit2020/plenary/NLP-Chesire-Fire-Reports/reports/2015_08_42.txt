Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 22/08/2015 - 00:51 Location: Percyvale Sreet in Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Two fire engines from Macclesfield attended a cooker fire in the kitchen of a flat located on the ground floor of a building on Percyvale Sreet in Macclesfield.  Firefighters used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel jet, one covering jet and a positive pressure ventilation fan to bring the fire under control.  Smoke alarms were fitted in the property and raised the alarm to the occupier.  Kitchen fire safety tips
