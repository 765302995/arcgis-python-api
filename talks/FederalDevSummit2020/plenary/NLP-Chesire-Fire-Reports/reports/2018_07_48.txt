 Small fire in the open in Barnton Time of Incident: 25/07/2018 - 21:55 Location: Chestnut Grove, Barnton, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters from Northwich used a water back pack to extinguish a small fire in the open Chestnut Grove in Barnton. The team were at the scene for approximately 15 minutes.
