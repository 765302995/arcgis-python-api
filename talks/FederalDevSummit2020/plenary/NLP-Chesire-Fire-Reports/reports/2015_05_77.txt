Fatal road traffic collision in Sandbach Time of Incident: 16/05/2015 - 14:10 Location: Booth Lane, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Sandbach Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Sandbach were called to Booth Lane in Sandbach to a road traffic collision involving two cars and a tanker, where a person was trapped in a vehicle. Firefighters released a casualty. Unfortunately this was a fatal incident.
