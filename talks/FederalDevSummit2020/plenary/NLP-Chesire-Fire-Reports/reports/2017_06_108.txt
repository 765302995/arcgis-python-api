Unattended cooking fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 14/06/2017 - 12:58 Location: The Uplands, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Widnes Ben with dogFire crews were called to a house fire in Runcorn after cooking was left unattended.  Two fire engines, from Runcorn and Widnes, attended the fire which was started after a pan was left unattended on a cooker.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and also rescued a dog, Missy, from the property. A large fan was then used to clear smoke from the kitchen.  Crews were in attendance for approximately 45 minutes.  Kitchen fire safety
