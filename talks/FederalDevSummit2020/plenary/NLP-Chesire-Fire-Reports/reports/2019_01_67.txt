 Plant machine loader fire in Mouldsworth Time of Incident: 30/01/2019 - 11:54 Location: Gonger Lane, Mouldsworth Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Crewe and one from Northwich Shortly before 12 noon firefighters were dispatched to reports of a vehicle fire at a farm in Mouldsworth.  On arrival at the scene crews discovered a plant machine loader was well alight.  They were concerned that the fire was likely to spread as it was close to other plant equipment.  A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Crews then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.  Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately an hour-and-a-half.
