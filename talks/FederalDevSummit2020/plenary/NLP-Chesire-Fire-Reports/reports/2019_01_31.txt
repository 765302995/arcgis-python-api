 Large wood fire, nr Brereton Time of Incident: 05/01/2019 - 12:57 Location: Brereton Lane, Sproston Attendance:  One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Winsford Firefighters turned out following reports of a building on fire close to the M6 motorway. On arrival it transpired to be large bonfire involving wood and a wooden trailer.  Given the amount of black smoke and its proximity to the motorway, crews helped the farmer to manage the bonfire better by removing the trailer and spreading out the remaining remnants. They used breathing apparatus and a hose reel and were at the scene for around an hour and a half.
