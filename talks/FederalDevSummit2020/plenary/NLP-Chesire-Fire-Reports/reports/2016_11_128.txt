Unattended bonfire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 06/11/2016 - 16:32 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A crew was called out to a bonfire that had been relit and left unattended on playing fields near Thornton Road. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
