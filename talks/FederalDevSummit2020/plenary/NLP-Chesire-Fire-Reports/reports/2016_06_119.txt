Flooding in Poynton Time of Incident: 08/06/2016 - 19:30 Location: London Road North, Poynton. Attendance: A fire engine from Poynton Firefighters responded to reports of flooding at the Bulls Head Hotel on London Road North, Poynton.  When they arrived crews confirmed that the cellar was filled with water. Crews used a Light Portable Pump to help clear the water.
