 Vehicle fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 03/12/2019 - 04:03 Location: Parkgate Way, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters from Runcorn used a hose reel jet to extinguish a motorbike which was well alight on Parkgate Way.  The crew were detained at the incident for around 30 minutes.   The cause of the fire is being investigated and anyone with any information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 579416.
