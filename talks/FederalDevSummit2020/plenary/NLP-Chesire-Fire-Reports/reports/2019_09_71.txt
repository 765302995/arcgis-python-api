 Bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/09/2019 - 19:31 Location: Near Oakwood Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 7.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open near to Oakwood Avenue in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a bin. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for around five minutes.
