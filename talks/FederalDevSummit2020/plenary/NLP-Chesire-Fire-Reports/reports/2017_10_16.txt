Barn fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 27/10/2017 - 03:52 Location: Linley Lane, Alsager Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar, one from Sandbach and one from Sandyford (Staffordshire Fire and Rescue Service) A barn fire, measuring 10m by 10m was tackled by firefighters on Linley Lane, Alsager. The crews used two hose reel jets, a stack drag and a thermal imaging camera to put out the fire. Crews were at the scene for two hours and 40 minutes.
