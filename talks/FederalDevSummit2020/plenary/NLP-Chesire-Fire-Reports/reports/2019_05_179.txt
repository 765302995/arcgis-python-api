 Outbuilding fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/05/2019 - 14:00 Location: Deansway, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to an outbuilding fire on Deansway, Widnes. Crews found a small amount of rubbish had been set on fire and they used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
