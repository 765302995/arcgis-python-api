Road traffic collision in Prestbury Time of Incident: 18/05/2015 - 14:46 Location: Bonis Hall Lane, Prestbury Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a car on its side and a woman trapped in Prestbury. The vehicle was stabilised on arrival of the crew and the casualty was assessed by Paramedics before being removed from the vehicle.
