 Building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/12/2018 - 22:51 Location: Withycombe Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Penketh and one fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a building fire on Withycombe Road, Warrington. On arrival crews discovered a large amount of smoke inside a care home building. Crews found that a pan of milk had been left and had caused the smoke. They removed the pan from the property and cleared smoke from the building. Everyone was accounted for and evacuated before the arrival of crews and there were no injuries. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
