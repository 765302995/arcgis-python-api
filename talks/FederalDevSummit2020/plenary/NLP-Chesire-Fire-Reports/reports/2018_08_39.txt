 Dog rescued from canal Warrington Time of Incident: 22/08/2018 - 15:01 Location: Cadishead Way, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from an Lymm Firefighters were called to the Manchester Ship Canal near Cadishead Way in Warrington following reports of a dog in the water. The rope rescue unit assessed the access to the canal and the boat was mobilised to assist.  The rope rescue unit rescued the dog form the water.
