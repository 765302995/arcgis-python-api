Mattress on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 17/04/2017 - 21:14 Location: Southern Expressway, Hallwood Park, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a fire in an underpassage which involved a mattress. The fire was extinguished with a hose reel. Police were requested to attend and it is suspected that the fire was started deliberately. If anyone knows who may have started the fire they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
