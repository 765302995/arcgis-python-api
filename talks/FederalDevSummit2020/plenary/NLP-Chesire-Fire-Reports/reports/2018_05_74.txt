 Wooden lodge on fire in Eaton Time of Incident: 21/05/2018 - 04:33 Location: Back Lane, Eaton Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Congleton, two from Macclesfield and one from Northwich Wooden lodge on fire in EatonFire crews were called to a wooden lodge which was on fire on Back Lane, Eaton. On arrival crews found a well developed fire involving a single storey wooden lodge.  Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, tackled the fire using four main jets. The incident is ongoing.  Crews were at the scene for nine hours.
