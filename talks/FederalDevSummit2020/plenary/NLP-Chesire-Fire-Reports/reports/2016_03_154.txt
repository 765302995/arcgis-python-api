Casualty rescue in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/03/2016 - 14:41 Location: Everite Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to assist with the rescue of a casualty who was trapped under a large bale of paper, weighing around one tonne. The crews assisted paramedics with first aid to the man and also shored up surrounding paper bales to keep the area safe. The casualty was taken to hospital by land ambulance.
