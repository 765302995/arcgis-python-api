 Fire on school playing fields in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/07/2018 - 22:23 Location: Birchfield Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 10.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Birchfield Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire was on the playing fields at Wade Deacon High School. Firefighters used water and beaters to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
