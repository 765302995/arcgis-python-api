 Refuse fire in a garden in Chester Time of Incident: 05/07/2018 - 20:10 Location: Boughton, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 8.05pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a garden in Boughton, Chester. On arrival the crew discovered refuse in the back garden of a domestic property well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which caused damage to guttering and a rear window of the property. They were in attendance for nearly half-an-hour.
