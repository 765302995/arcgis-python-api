 Rubbish fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/04/2018 - 00:09 Location: Slutchers Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one fire engine from Penketh attended Rubbish fire in Warrington Shortly after midnight firefighters from Warrington and Penketh were called to reports of a fire on Slutchers Lane in Warrington.  On arrival crews found that the fire involved a large quantity of rubbish which was well alight. The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
