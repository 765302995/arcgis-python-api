 Tumble dryer fire in Bollington Time of Incident: 11/09/2019 - 17:20 Location: Irwell Rise, Bollington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington At 5.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a tumble dryer at a domestic property in Irwell Rise, Bollington. The fire was out on arrival. Firefighters removed the tumble dryer from the address and were in attendance for around five minutes. For tumble dryer fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/tumble-dryer-fire-safety.
