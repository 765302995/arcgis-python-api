 Garage fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 16/11/2018 - 10:50 Location: Malpas Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes and one from Runcorn At around 10:50am firefighters from Widnes and Runcorn were called to reports of a fire at house on Malpas Road, Runcorn. Crews attended the scene and found the fire involved a garage adjoined to a domestic property. Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The team then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. Firefighters were in attendance at the scene for approximately one hour ten minutes.
