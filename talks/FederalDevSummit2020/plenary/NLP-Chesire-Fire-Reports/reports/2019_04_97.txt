 Car fire, Wilmslow Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 14:44 Location: Manchester Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to reports of a car on fire on a roundabout in Manchester Road. They used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames, and continued to monitor the vehicle for hotspots for a further 10 minutes to prevent reignition.
