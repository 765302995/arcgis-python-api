Wheelie bins on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 01/01/2017 - 02:21 Location: Southway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A fire involving two commercial wheelie bins was tackled by firefighters. Hose reel jets were used to put out the blaze and police were informed as it is believed the fire was started deliberately.  If anyone has any information about this fire please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
