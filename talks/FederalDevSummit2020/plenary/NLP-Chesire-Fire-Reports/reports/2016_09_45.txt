Wheelie bin fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 21/09/2016 - 19:56 Location: Stonehouse Green in Congleton Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton Firefighters responded to a report of a fire behind Argos on Stonehouse Green in Congleton.  When they arrived crews found that there was two commercial wheelie bins on fire. A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out.  This fire was believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
