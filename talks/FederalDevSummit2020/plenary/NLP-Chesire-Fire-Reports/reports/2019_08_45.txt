 One-vehicle collision in Davenham Time of Incident: 24/08/2019 - 07:29 Location: Hill Top Grange, Davenham, near Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 7.30am firefighters were called to a report of a one-vehicle collision in Hill Top Grange, Davenham, near Northwich. On arrival the crew found the saloon car on its side. No-one was trapped inside. Firefighters made the car safe and a man involved in the collision was assessed by paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
