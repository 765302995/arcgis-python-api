 Fire in the open in Blacon Time of Incident: 27/09/2018 - 20:30 Location: Western Avenue, Blacon, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - One from Chester, one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Chester and Ellesmere Port used one hose reel jet to extinguish a fire involving a quantity of waste at the rear of a property on Western Avenue, Blacon. The team were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
