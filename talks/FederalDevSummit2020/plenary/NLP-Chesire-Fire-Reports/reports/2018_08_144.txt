 Large grass fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 05/08/2018 - 17:37 Location: Shipbrook Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Northwich and one from Winsford At around 5.35pm firefighters were called to reports of a large grass fire on Shipbrook Road in Rudheath, Northwich. On arrival crews discovered a fire measuring approximately 100 square metres. Firefighters used an extended hose reel jet and numerous beaters to extinguish the fire. They were in attendance for around two hours.
