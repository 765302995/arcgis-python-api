 Man trapped after two-car collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/04/2018 - 17:48 Location: Glazebrook Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 5.45pm firefighters were called to a collision involving two cars on Glazebrook Lane in Rixton-with-Glazebrook, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that an 18-year-old man was trapped inside one of the vehicles. Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to release the casualty and a longboard to remove him from the car. The casualty was then passed to the care of the North West Ambulance Service. Paramedics took him to hospital in an ambulance. Fire crews made the scene safe and were in attendance for more than an hour.
