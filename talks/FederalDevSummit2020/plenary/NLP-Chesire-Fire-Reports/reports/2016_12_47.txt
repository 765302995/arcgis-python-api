Chimney fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 22/12/2016 - 21:24 Location: Hollins Hill, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley Firefighters used chimney rods to put out a fire in the chimney of an Utkinton house. They had to remove the fire from the hearth before using the rods. They then checked the loft space with a thermal imaging camera to make sure there had been no fire spread.  Please follow our chimney safety advice.
