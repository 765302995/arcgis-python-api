 Chimney fire, Aston Time of Incident: 13/04/2018 - 18:53 Location: Wrenbury Road, Aston Attendance: One fire engine from Audlem Crews tackled a tiled chimney fire at a house in Aston, near Wrenbury. They used chimney rods and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire and the occupier was advised on chimney maintenance. Firefighters were in attendance for just over an hour.
