 Log and bush fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 29/06/2018 - 16:57 Location: Warrington Road, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood attended At around 4.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a field on Warrington Road in Lymm. On arrival the crew discovered a large log and the surrounding bush well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and two beaters to extinguish the fire.
