 Rubbish fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/02/2018 - 04:22 Location: Sharp Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. One fire engine from Penketh. Firefighters were called to a rubbish fire in Sharp Street, Warrington. The fire had spread to an outbuilding. Crews used two hose reels and a covering jet to extinguish the fire.
