 Fire in a detached garage in Crewe Time of Incident: 10/06/2019 - 11:50 Location: James Atkinson Way, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At 11.50am firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a boiler in a detached garage at a domestic property in James Atkinson Way, Crewe. The fire was out on arrival. Firefighters isolated the gas, electricity and water supplies to the garage and an investigation is being carried out to establish the cause of the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour-and-a-half.
