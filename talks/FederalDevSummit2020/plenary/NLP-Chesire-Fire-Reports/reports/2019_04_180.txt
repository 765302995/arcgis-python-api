 Fire in the open in Winsford Time of Incident: 12/04/2019 - 20:50 Location: Forest Road, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Winsford and one from Northwich At around 8.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Forest Road in Winsford. On arrival crews discovered a fire involving a large amount of conifers and shrubbery on a grassed area. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which is believed to have been started deliberately using an accelerant. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 372649, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
