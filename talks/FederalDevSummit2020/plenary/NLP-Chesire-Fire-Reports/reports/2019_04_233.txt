 Casualty trapped following two-vehicle collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/04/2019 - 09:09 Location: Manor Park Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 9am firefighters were called to reports of a two-vehicle collision on Manor Park Drive in Ellesmere Port. On arrival crews discovered that someone was trapped inside one of the vehicles. Firefighters opened one of the vehicle's doors using hydrologic cutting equipment to enable them to get the casualty out of it. They then handed the casualty over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
