Kitchen fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/07/2016 - 20:19 Location: Grangeway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes Fire crews were sent to a flat in Runcorn where there was a fire inside the kitchen of the property. Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  Firefighters rescued a man from a property.  The casualty was suffering from smoke inhalation. He was handed to the care of paramedics. Two hose reel jets and one covering jet was used to extinguish the fire. A large fan was used to clear the smoke. A fire investigation will be completed to find the cause of the fire.
