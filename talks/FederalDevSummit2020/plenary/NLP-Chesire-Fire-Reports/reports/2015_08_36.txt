Road traffic collision involving one vehicle in Rainow Time of Incident: 23/08/2015 - 04:22 Location: Rainow road, Rainow Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Two fire crews from Macclesfield attended a road traffic collision involving one vehicle with one casualty trapped inside a vehicle.  Firefighters conducted a complex extraction of the casualty with the help of paramedics.  Fire crews used cutting equipment, spreaders, cutters, rams, a dewalt saw, glass punch and protection equipment to release the casualty.  The casualty was then handed to the care of the paramedics.  One hose reel and hand tools were also used.
