 Decking fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 21:24 Location: Riverside Grove, Wistaston. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to an address in Riverside Grove, Wistaston following reports of garden decking on fire. On arrival the fire was out and crews used a hose reel to damp down the area. They then cut away the affected area and used a thermal imaging camera to check for further hot spots.
