 Two-vehicle collision in Nantwich Time of Incident: 19/06/2019 - 09:21 Location: A500, Willaston, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Crewe and one from Nantwich At around 9.20am firefighters were called to a report of a collision on the A500 in Willaston, Nantwich, near the Cheerbrook roundabout. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved a car and a heavy goods vehicle and that no-one was trapped inside a vehicle. Oil had leaked from one of the vehicles onto the road. Firefighters used specialist absorbent mats to soak up the oil and were in attendance for less than 10 minutes.
