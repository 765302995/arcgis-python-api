Small tumble dryer fire Time of Incident: 30/09/2015 - 21:26 Location: Bagnall Close, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a small fire involving a tumble dryer in Warrington. The fire was out on arrival however firefighters completed a health and safety asessment of the property and ensured the scene was safe. Tumble dryer fire safety advice
