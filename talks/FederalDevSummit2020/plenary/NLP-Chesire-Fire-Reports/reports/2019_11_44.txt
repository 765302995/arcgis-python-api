 Fire at a bungalow in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/11/2019 - 23:40 Location: King James Court, Runcorn Attendance: Three fire engines attended - two from Runcorn and one from Widnes Fire crews attended to reports of a fire at a bungalow on King James Court in Runcorn.  Firefighters, wearing two breathing apparatus, used one hose reel to extinguish the flames. A covering jet was also in place.  A large fan was used to disperse the smoke.  Everyone was accounted for with one person treated by paramedics for smoke inhalation.  Crews were in attendance for one hour and forty minutes.
