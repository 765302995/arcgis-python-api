 Collision in Poynton Time of Incident: 06/11/2018 - 21:47 Location: Chester Road, Poynton Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Poynton and one from Stockport At around 9.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving a car on Chester Road in Poynton. Crews made the saloon vehicle safe and assisted paramedics in tending to the three casualties. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
