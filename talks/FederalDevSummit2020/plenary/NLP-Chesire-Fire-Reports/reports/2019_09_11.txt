 Oven fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/09/2019 - 21:02 Location: Skipton Close, Runcorn. WA7 2SG Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters used a large fan to clear smoke from a kitchen at a house in Runcorn following a small fire in an oven.  The fire was out on arrival and the crew were at the house for around 25 minutes.  For further information on cooking safety please click here.
