Communications pole overhanging road in Over Peover Time of Incident: 25/02/2016 - 09:57 Location: Well Bank Lane, Over Peover Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a communications pole which had been damaged and left overhanging a road. The crew assisted police with traffic management while waiting for the communications company to attend. The road was closed while the incident was dealt with.
