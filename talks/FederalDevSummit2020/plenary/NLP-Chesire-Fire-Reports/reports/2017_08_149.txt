Moped set alight on a field in Frodsham Time of Incident: 03/08/2017 - 17:23 Location: Ship Street, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called out to a fire involving a moped on a playing field by Ship Street in Frodsham.   It is believed the fire may have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
