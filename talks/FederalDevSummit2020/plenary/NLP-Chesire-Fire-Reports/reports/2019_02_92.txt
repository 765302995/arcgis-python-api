 Mattress fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 14/02/2019 - 18:43 Location: Luton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 6.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a mattress being on fire in the open on Luton Road in Ellesmere Port. The fire was out on arrival. Firefighters dampened the area down to make it safe and were in attendance for nearly half-an-hour.
