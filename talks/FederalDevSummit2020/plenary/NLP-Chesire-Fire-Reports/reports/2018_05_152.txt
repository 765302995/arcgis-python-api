 Wheelie bin fire, Crewe Time of Incident: 12/05/2018 - 02:10 Location: Victoria Centre, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Crews responded to reports of two wheelie bins being set alight near the Victoria Centre in Crewe. They used a hose reel to extinguish the flames and police attended as well.  Advice on how to avoid your wheelie bin becoming a target can be found at https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety/how-to-prevent-a-wheelie-bin-fire
