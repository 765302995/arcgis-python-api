Cooking fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 04/10/2016 - 17:12 Location: Naylor Court, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Unattended cooking left a lady needing oxygen therapy at an incident in Northwich. A pan left unattended had been on fire and caused smoke logging to the Naylor Court property. Firefighters used a high pressure fan to clear the smoke and gave oxygen to the casualty until the ambulance arrived. The lady was taken to hospital as a precaution.
