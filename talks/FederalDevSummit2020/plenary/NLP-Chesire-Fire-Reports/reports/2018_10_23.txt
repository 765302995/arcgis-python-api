 Unattended cooking pan, Wilmslow Time of Incident: 27/10/2018 - 15:12 Location: Holly Road North, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Fire crews attended to a pan left unattended at a flat on Holly Road North in Wilmslow.  Firefighters removed the pan from the cooker and placed it outside while using a positive pressure ventilation fan to clear the smoke from the flat.  Follow our safety advice to ensure you cook safely https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/home/cooking-safety-tips
