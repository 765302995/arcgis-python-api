 Peat smouldering on Runcorn Hill Time of Incident: 28/07/2018 - 10:40 Location: Runcorn hill, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a small area of peat smouldering on Runcorn Hill. Crews used a hose reel to put and a stack drag to ensure it didn't reignite.
