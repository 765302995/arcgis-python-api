Motorbike fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 21/09/2017 - 23:32 Location: Ship Street, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine from Frodsham A hose reel jet was used to put out a motorbike that was heavily involved in fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and police were informed. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
