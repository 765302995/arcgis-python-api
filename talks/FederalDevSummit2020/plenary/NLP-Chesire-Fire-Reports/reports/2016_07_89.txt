Road traffic collision on the A54 Time of Incident: 15/07/2016 - 13:44 Location: Chester Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester There was a road traffic collision involving two vehicles on the A54 near Ashton Lane, where one woman was trapped inside her vehicle. Firefighters released the woman from the vehicle before handing her over into the care of paramedics. Crews then proceeded to make the scene safe.
