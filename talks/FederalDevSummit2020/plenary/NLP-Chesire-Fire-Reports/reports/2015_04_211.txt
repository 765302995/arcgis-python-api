Cooker fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/04/2015 - 21:13 Location: Terrace Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire after smoke detectors were heard.  Crews had to make their own entry into the house where they found there had been a small fire in a cooker.  The firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet, followed by a high pressure fan to clear the smoke.
