 Serious collision near Chester Time of Incident: 30/09/2019 - 19:01 Location: Welsh Road, Woodbank, Chester. Attendance: Four fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port, Chester and Powey Lane. Shortly after 7pm firefighters were dispatched to reports of a serous collision on Welsh Road in Woodbank. On arrival at the scene crews found that the incident involved three vehicles and two casualties were trapped in two of the cars. Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to release the casualties and they were passed to the care of paramedics. Crews were detained at the scene for around one hour 20  minutes.
