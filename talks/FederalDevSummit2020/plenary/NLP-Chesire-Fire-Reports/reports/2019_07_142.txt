 Small fencing fire at a school in Winsford Time of Incident: 07/07/2019 - 15:28 Location: Gladstone Street, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Shortly before 3.30pm firefighters were called to a report of fire within the grounds of St Chad's Church of England Primary and Nursery School in Gladstone Street, Winsford. On arrival the crew found a small amount of fencing on fire at the back of the school. Firefighters extinguished the fire, which involved wood, with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
