 Rubbish on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/06/2018 - 13:10 Location: Forshaw Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to an address in Forshaw Street in Warrington following reports of rubbish being burnt at the rear of the property causing lots of black smoke. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and advice was given to the owner.
