Wood chippings on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 09/05/2017 - 22:10 Location: Wood Lane, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley A large pile of wood chippings which were on fire in a compound was extinguished with two hose reel jets by firefighters. The pile involved around 2,000 tonnes of wood chip and crews checked the pile with a thermal imaging camera. A mechanical digger was also used to remove the chippings to enable damping down before.
