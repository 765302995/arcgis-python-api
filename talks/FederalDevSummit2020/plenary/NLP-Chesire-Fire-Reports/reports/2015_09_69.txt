Chimney fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/09/2015 - 21:25 Location: Aldersey Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters have attended a chimney fire involving a coal log burner in Aldersey Road, Crewe. Crews dealt with the incident using small tools and a thermal imaging camera. They also completed a health and safety assesment at the property and were in attendance for approximately 40 minutes.
