M6 Road Traffic Collision Junction 16-17 Time of Incident: 20/11/2015 - 12:28 Location: M6 Northbound Junction 16-17 Attendance: One fire engine from Alsager, one from Congleton, one from Knutsford and two from Crewe. Firefighters were mobilised to a road traffic collision involving four saloon vehicles on the M6 Northbound between junctions 16 and 17.  Crews released one female casualty who was medically trapped. The casualty was taken to hospital by air ambulance.  Please note that shortly before this incident, there was a road traffic collision between junctions 18 and 19.  All closures as a result of this incident have now been lifted.
