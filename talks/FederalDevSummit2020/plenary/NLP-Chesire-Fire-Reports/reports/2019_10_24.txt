 Fire at a flat in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/10/2019 - 21:38 Location: Wordsworth Avenue, Widnes Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Widnes, one from Runcorn and one from Penketh Crews attended to a fire at a flat on Wordsworth Avenue in Widnes.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews used one hose reel jet to put out the fire.  A large fan was used to disperse the smoke.    The fire is believed to have involved two seats.  Everyone was accounted for with no reports of any injuries.  Crews were in attendance for approximately one hour and thirty minutes.
