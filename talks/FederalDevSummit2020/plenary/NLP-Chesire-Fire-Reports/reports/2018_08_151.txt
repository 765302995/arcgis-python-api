 Barbecue and hedgerow fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 05/08/2018 - 13:32 Location: Beech View Road, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 1.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a hedgerow fire on Beech View Road in Frodsham. On arrival the crew discovered that a fire caused by a barbecue outside a domestic property had spread to the hedgerow and that neighbours had extinguished it using a domestic hose. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to damp the area down to make sure it was safe. They also used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
