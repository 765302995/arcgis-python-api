Gas cylinder on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/07/2017 - 15:50 Location: Bramshill Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Fire crews were called to a gas cylinder fire which had spread to a fence on Bramshill Close, Warrington. Crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
