 Car leaking oil on M56 Time of Incident: 23/07/2019 - 13:56 Location: M56, westbound between junction 15 to junction 16. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Crews attended to reports of a vehicle leaking oil on the M56.  The car was monitored by firefighters and cooled down before being safely recovered.
