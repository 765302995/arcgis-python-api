Car on fire at a car compound in Crewe Time of Incident: 29/10/2016 - 01:06 Location: Delamere Court, St Marys Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe A car was on fire inside a car compound, so firefighters used one hose reel jet to tackle the fire. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.  Police also attended this incident.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
