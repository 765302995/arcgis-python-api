 Kitchen fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 27/07/2018 - 13:00 Location: st johns road, congleton. cw122bd Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Congleton and one from Macclesfield. At 1pm firefighters from Congleton and Macclesfield were called to reports of a fire at a house on St Johns Road in Congleton. Crews attended the scene and discovered a kitchen fire. The team extinguished the fire and then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property. A 69-year-old man was treated at the scene for smoke inhalation.
