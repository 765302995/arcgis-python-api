Collision in Tarporley Time of Incident: 07/03/2017 - 18:27 Location: A49/A51 roundabout in Tarporley. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Tarporley Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on the A49/A51 Roundabout in Tarporley.  When they arrived they found that the collision involved a car and a horse box. Nobody was trapped and so crews assisted by making the scene safe. There was a horse in the lorry but this was uninjured in the incident.
