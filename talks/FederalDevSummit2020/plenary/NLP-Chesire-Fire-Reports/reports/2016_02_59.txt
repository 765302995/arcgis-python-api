Wheelie bins on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/02/2016 - 01:04 Location: Lilford Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Wheelie bins were on fire outside two flats. The fire slightly affected the building. Firefighters tackled the blaze and an investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
