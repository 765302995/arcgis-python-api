Building fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 06/04/2016 - 16:52 Location: Chester Way, Northwich Attendance: Three fire engines from Northwich, Winsford and Knutsford Fire Stations Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Chester Road in Northwich.   The incident involved a large quantity of rope within a warehouse approximately 100m x 20m. Firefighters used two breathing apparatus, a hose reel, a covering jet and a positive large fan to deal with the incident.  Crews were engaged at scene approximately one and a half hours.
