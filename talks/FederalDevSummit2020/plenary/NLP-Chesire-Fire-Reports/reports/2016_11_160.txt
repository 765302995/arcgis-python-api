Building fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/11/2016 - 19:46 Location: Eagles Way, Runcorn. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Runcorn, one from Widnes and one from Stockton Heath along with the hydraulic platform. Firefighters were called to a report of a building fire on Eagles Way, Runcorn.  When they arrived crews confirmed that there was a blaze in an empty property. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used main water and hose reel jets to tackle the blaze.  Thermal Imaging Cameras were also used to check for hot spots to make sure that the fire was completely out.  A fire investigation has now been launched to find the cause of the fire.
