Road traffic collision on Buxton New Road in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 10/10/2015 - 22:37 Location: Buxton New Road in Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield and Bollington A fire crew from Macclesfield and a fire crew from Bollington were sent to a road traffic collision involving one car, which had overturned on Buxton New Road in Macclesfield. One male casualty was physically trapped inside the vehicle. Police closed the road.  The fire crews stabilised the vehicle before safely releasing one casualty from the car.  The casualty was then handed to the care of paramedics.
