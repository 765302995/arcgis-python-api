Tree fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/09/2017 - 03:43 Location: Wistaston Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of smoke seen coming from a building on Wistaston Road. On inspection the incident was found to be leaves on fire at the base of a nearby tree. A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire.
