 Skip fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 08/04/2019 - 00:33 Location: Abbey Close, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 12.30am firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a skip in Abbey Close, Widnes. On arrival the crew found the skip well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire in the open and notified the police of the incident. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
