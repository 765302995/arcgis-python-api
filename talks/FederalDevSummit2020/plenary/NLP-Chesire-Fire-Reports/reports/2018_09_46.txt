 Flooding in Weston Time of Incident: 20/09/2018 - 22:00 Location: Main Road, Weston. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefigthers were called to a flooding in Weston. Three foot of water was encroaching on a house in Main Road,Weston. Crews began using a small pump to remove the water from the front of the house and the drains. They then used a main pump and another pump to help them remove the water.
