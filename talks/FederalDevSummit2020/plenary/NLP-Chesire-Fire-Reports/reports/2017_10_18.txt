Fire in garden which spread to house in Winsford Time of Incident: 27/10/2017 - 00:02 Location: Bradbury Road, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich A large fire in a rear garden which spread to a ground floor extension of a house was extinguished by firefighters using a hose reel. No one was in the property at the time and crews gained access to the property to extinguish the flames. Police also attended. Crews remained in attendance for just over two hours. A fire investigation into the cause is taking place.
