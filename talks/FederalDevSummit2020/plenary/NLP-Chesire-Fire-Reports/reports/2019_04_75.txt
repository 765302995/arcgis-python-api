 Car fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 21/04/2019 - 07:29 Location: M56 west, junction 8 to 9 Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm, one from Warrington and one from Birchwood Fire crews were called to a car fire on the hardshoulder of the M56 westbound between junction 8 and 9. Crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, used hydraulic cutting equipment and a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Crews were at the scene for two hours.
