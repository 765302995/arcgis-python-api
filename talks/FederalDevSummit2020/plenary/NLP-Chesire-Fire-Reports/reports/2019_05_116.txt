 Fire in a garden in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/05/2019 - 16:24 Location: Vincent Close, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 4.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a garden in Vincent Close, Great Sankey, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered bushes, trees and fences at the back of a house well alight. The fire spread to the garden next door and other surrounding properties. Two firefighters each fought the fire with a hose reel jet. Once the fire was out they damped the areas affected by it down to make them safe. The fire caused damage to external soffits on one of the houses.
