 Fire in a garden in Nether Alderley Time of Incident: 11/04/2018 - 06:49 Location: Welsh Row, Nether Alderley, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At 6.47am firefighters were called to reports of a fire on Welsh Row in Nether Alderley, Macclesfield. On arrival the crew found a small quantity of rubbish on fire in the back garden of a house. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire. They were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
