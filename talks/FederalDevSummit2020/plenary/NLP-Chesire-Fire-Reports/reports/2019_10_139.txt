 Three-vehicle collision in Chester Time of Incident: 04/10/2019 - 13:03 Location: Junction of Long Lane and Parkgate Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Powey Lane and one from Chester Shortly after 1pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision on the junction of Long Lane and Parkgate Road in Chester. On arrival the crews discovered the collision involved three vehicles and that someone was trapped inside one of them. She was assessed by paramedics before firefighters got her out of the vehicle. The casualty was then handed over to the care of the paramedics. A three-way road closure was put in place by the police as emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
