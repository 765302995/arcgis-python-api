 One-vehicle collision in Tarporley Time of Incident: 21/03/2019 - 15:27 Location: A49/A51 roundabout in Tarporley Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Chester and one from Winsford Shortly before 3.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a one-vehicle collision on the A49/A51 roundabout in Tarporley. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved a Ford Fiesta and a telephone pole. The car was on its roof and a man was physically trapped inside it. Firefighters got the casualty out of the car and handed him over to the care of paramedics. He was airlifted to hospital. Paramedics also assessed two other people who were in the car when it collided with the telegraph pole - a male and a female. One of them was also taken to hospital. The A51 was closed as emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
