Three car collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 08/05/2015 - 20:30 Location: M6 between junctions 20 and 21. Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on the M6 between junctions 20 and 21.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved three cars. Nobody was trapped but crews helped the ambulance service in checking over casualties.
