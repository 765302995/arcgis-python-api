Road traffic collision in Neston Time of Incident: 18/12/2016 - 13:48 Location: Chester High Road, Neston Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Deeside (North Wales Fire and Rescue Service) Two cars were in a collision on Chester High Road. A lady was trapped in one of the cars and was released by firefighters, then handed into the care of the ambulance service. They then made the scene safe and left the incident after about forty minutes.
