 Fire involving wheelie bins inside a hut in Warrington Time of Incident: 30/07/2018 - 15:59 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - from Penketh and Birchwood Wheelie bin firesAt around 4pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire inside a hut on a field on Grasmere Avenue in Warrington.On arrival crews discovered that the small fire involved wheelie bins inside the metal hut.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire  Police on 101, give the details via general enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
