 Serious collision on the A41 in Chester Time of Incident: 11/10/2018 - 23:44 Location: Whitchurch Road, Hatton Heath Attendance: Three fire engines attended from Chester, Malpas and Winsford At 11.44pm three fire engines and the major rescue unit attended reports of a serious collision on the A41 in Hatton Heath. Crews attended and discovered there had been a collision between a car and a HGV and one person was trapped in the car. The team used specialist cutting equipment to release the casualty from the car and he was passed to the care of North West ambulance Service. The man has been taken to Walton Hospital. Fire crews were in attendance for approximately one hour.
