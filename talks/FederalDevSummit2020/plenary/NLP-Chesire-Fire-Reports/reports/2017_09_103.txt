Fire in a cornfield in Northwich Time of Incident: 02/09/2017 - 17:03 Location: Manchester Road, Lostck Gralam, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters used handheld fire extinguishers and beaters to extinguish a fire in a cornfield. Police attended this incident.   It is believed that the fire was started deliberately.  If you have any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
