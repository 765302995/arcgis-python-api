 Overheated brakes on M6 Time of Incident: 05/03/2019 - 05:11 Location: M6, Southbound, Junction 19 - Junction 20 Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Stockton Heath and one from Lymm Firefighters attended to brakes overheating on a trailer on the M6 southbound between junction 19 and junction 20.  Crews used one hose reel and were in attendance for around 30 minutes.
