Road traffic collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 01/03/2017 - 11:02 Location: High Street, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters, police and ambulance are dealing with a road traffic collision at Over Square, with a full road closure in place. One car was involved, nobody was trapped but the driver was taken to hospital as a precaution. Firefighters made the scene safe.
