Road traffic collision in Chester Time of Incident: 22/07/2015 - 13:49 Location: Roundabout on the A54/A51, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Firefighters attended a road traffic collision on the A54 involving three vehicles, with one on its roof, and 12 casualties. Crews assisted paramedics with the casualties and two Air Ambulances attended.  The road was closed while the incident was dealt with.
