Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/07/2016 - 19:23 Location: Holly Bush Lane, Rixton-by-Glazebrook, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Birchwood A lorry and a car were involved in a road traffic collision in Rixton-with-Glazebrook. The lorry had overturned in a field. Nobody was trapped in either vehicle. Firefighters made the scene safe and handed one casualty into the care of paramedics. Police were also at the incident.
