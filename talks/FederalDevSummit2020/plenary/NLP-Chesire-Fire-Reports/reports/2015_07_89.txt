Sofa set on fire in the woods in Bewsey, Warrington Time of Incident: 18/07/2015 - 13:31 Location: Wellfield Street, Bewsey, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood A sofa was set on fire in the woods, close to bushes, near to Wellfield Street in Bewsey.  Firefighters used water back packs and buckets of water to extinguish the fire.  This fire was started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
