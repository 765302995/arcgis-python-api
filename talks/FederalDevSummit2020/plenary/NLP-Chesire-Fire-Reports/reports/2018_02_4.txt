 Electrical fault in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 26/02/2018 - 21:01 Location: Coare Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters from Macclesfield attended reports of an electrical fire at a residential property on Coare Street. On arrival at the scene the team found that the fire was confined to the fuse box of the property. The crew isolated the power to the property and the homeowner was advised to contact a qualified electrician.
