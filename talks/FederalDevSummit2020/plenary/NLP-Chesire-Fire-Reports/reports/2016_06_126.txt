Jetty on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/06/2016 - 07:16 Location: Moore Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A fire on a wooden jetty was spotted by the captain of a ship travelling on the Manchester Ship Canal. The captain alerted firefighters who attended the incident. The crews had to gain access to the site by cutting a padlock on a gate. They then put out the fire using a hose reel jet.
