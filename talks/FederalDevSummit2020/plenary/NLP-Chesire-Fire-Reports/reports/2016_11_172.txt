Small fire in Macclesfield Forest Time of Incident: 01/11/2016 - 20:36 Location: Standing Stone Road, Macclesfield Forest. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in Macclesfield Forest.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved an amount of timber. Crews used hose reel jets to put the fire out.  This blaze is believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
