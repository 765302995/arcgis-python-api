Fatal collision in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 17/03/2017 - 18:34 Location: London Road, Holmes Chapel Attendance: One fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel Firefighters helped paramedics and police with an elderly man who was involved in a collision with a vehicle on London Road in Holmes Chapel.   The vehicle failed to stop. The man was taken to hospital by ambulance in a very serious condition.  Sadly this is now a fatal incident.     If anyone has any information about this incident,  please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
