Road traffic collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 20/12/2016 - 16:52 Location: M6 North J21 to J21A Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving three vehicles, however, all people were accounted for. Casualties were taken into the care of paramedics and firefighters proceeded to make the scene safe. Ambulance and Police also attended.
