Building evacuated due to smell of burning in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/08/2016 - 15:50 Location: Lister Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one fire engine from Frodsham Firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a light in an industrial estate in Runcorn. An evacuation had taken place in a two storey office building following reports of a smell of burning on the second floor. Crews isolated the electrics and performed a full investigation of the surrounding area using a thermal imaging camera to check for heat spots.
