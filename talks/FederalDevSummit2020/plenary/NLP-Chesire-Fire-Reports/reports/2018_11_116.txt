 Hedgerow fire in Neston Time of Incident: 08/11/2018 - 21:31 Location: Wood Lane, Neston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane At around 9.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a hedgerow on Wood Lane in Neston. On arrival the crew discovered a large hedgerow well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguished the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and make sure the area was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
