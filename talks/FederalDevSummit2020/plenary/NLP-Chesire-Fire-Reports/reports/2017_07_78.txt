Flat on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 14/07/2017 - 05:20 Location: Margaret Court, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes, one from Penketh and one from Warrington Firefighters were called to a fire in a ground floor flat on Margaret Court, Widnes. On arrival crews found a female casualty suffering from burns outside the property. Crews gave her first aid until the paramedics arrived and tackled the fire, with two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, using a hose reel jet. The female casualty has been taken to hospital.  An investigation into the cause of this fire will take place.
