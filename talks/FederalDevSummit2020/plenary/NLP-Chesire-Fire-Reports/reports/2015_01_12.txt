Road traffic collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 28/01/2015 - 17:36 Location: Congleton Road, Gawsworth, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Congleton Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving five cars, one of which was on its side in Macclesfield. No one was trapped and paramedics were treating one person for minor injuries. The crews assisted paramedics in making the scene safe before leaving.  Cheshire Fire and Rescue Service is urging people to take extra care on the roads during the cold weather conditions with snow and ice likely to impact on the roads.
