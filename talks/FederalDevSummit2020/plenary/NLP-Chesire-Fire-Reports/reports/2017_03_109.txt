Small fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/03/2017 - 21:58 Location: Tennyson Drive, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a fire in an alley off Tennyson Drive, Warrington.  The fire, which is believed to have been started deliberately, was in an empty outbuilding. If anyone has any information that may help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
