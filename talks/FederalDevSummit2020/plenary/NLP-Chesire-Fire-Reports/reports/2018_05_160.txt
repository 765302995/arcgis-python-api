 Kitchen fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 11/05/2018 - 08:07 Location: Wolverton Drive, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes Smoke alarms alerted a householder to a small kitchen fire. Firefighters attended and checked the area to make sure the fire was completely out. They then ventilated the building using a high pressure fan.
