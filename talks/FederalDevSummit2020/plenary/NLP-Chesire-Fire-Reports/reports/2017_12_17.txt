Man treated for smoke inhalation in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/12/2017 - 13:33 Location: Somerville Crescent, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane A man was treated for smoke inhalation by firefighters after a fire on Somerville Crescent, Ellesmere Port. The fire was out on arrival of the crews and they treated the man before being handing over to the care of paramedics.
