Road traffic collision on Woolley Avenue in Poynton Time of Incident: 22/10/2016 - 23:27 Location: Woolley Avenue, Poynton. Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton and one from Offerton (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) There was a road traffic collision on Woolley Avenue in Poynton. The incident involved three vehicles.  Firefighters helped to release one casualty from one of the vehicles, before making the scene safe.  Three ambulances attended for three casualties.
