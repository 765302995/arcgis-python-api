 Wheelie bin fire put out by crews in Runcorn Time of Incident: 14/02/2018 - 05:36 Location: Rawdon close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire Crews extinguished a wheelie bin which was reported ablaze on fields near to Rawdon Close in Runcorn in the early hours of this morning. Firefighters used water to ensure the fire was out.
