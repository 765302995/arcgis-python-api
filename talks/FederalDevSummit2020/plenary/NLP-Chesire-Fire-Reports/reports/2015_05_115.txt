Small fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 09/05/2015 - 18:33 Location: Woodchurch Lane, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: A fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Woodchurch Lane, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that there was some astro turf on fire in some bushes. Crews used a back pack extinguisher to put the fire out.
