Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/04/2015 - 20:02 Location: Junction 8 slip road, M62, Earrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A car well alight on the exit slip road of junction 8 of the M62 was extinguished by firefighters wearing breathing apparatus. They used one hose reel to put out the blaze and left the incident in the hands of police.
