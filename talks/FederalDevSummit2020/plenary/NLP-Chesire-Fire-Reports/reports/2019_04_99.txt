 Campfire left unattended, Macclesfield Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 13:37 Location: Craig Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called following reports of a fire in a field. Upon arrival they found an unattended campfire that had been left, so used a water backpack to put out the fire to avoidit spreading further.
