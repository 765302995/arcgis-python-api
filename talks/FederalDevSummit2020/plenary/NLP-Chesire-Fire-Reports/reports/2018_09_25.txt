 House fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 24/09/2018 - 08:28 Location: Dunsford, Widnes. Attendance: Four fire engines attended, one from Penketh, one from Lymm, one from Runcorn and one from Warrington. Shortly before 8.30am firefighters were called to reports of a fire at an address on Dunsford in Widnes.  Crews from Runcorn, Lymm, Penketh and Warrington attended the scene and discovered a fire in the roof space of the property.  Six firefighters wearing breathing apparatus entered the roof space via a neighbouring property. The team used two hose reel jets and one main jet to extinguish the fire.  One person was treated at the scene by North West Ambulance Service.  The team were in attendance for approximately one hour.
