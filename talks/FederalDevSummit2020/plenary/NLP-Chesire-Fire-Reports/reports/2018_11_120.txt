 Winsford car fire Time of Incident: 08/11/2018 - 01:08 Location: Bradford Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford A car was reported well alight on Bradford Road. Firefighters, one in breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and then damped the area down.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and Cheshire Police also attended the incident. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111
