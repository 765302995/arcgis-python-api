HGV fire on M62 Time of Incident: 30/05/2017 - 15:31 Location: M62 west junction 10 to 9 Attendance: Fire engines from Penketh and Stockton Heath A heavy goods vehicle was on fire on the M62 westbound between junctions 10 and 9 near Warrington. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to fight the fire.
