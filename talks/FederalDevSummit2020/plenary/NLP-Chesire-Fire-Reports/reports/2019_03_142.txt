 Tumble dryer fire in Chester Time of Incident: 28/02/2019 - 17:52 Location: Greenwood Avenue, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly after 5.50pm firefighter were called to reports of a fire at a domestic property on Greenwood Avenue in Chester. The small fire involving a tumble dryer was out on arrival. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and make sure that the tumble dryer was safe. They were in attendance for around 15 minutes. For tumble dryer fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/tumble-dryer-fire-safety.
