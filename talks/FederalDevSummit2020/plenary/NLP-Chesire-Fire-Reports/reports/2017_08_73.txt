Car on fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 17/08/2017 - 17:12 Location: Westbound, M56 between junctions 15 and 16 Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a car well alight on the M56 and used a hose reel to extinguish it. All lanes of the motorway westbound were closed while the incident was dealt with.
