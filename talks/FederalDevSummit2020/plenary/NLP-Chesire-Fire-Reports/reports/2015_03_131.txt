Two people cut free after collision in Little Bollington Time of Incident: 05/03/2015 - 17:47 Location: Bowden Roundabout, Little Bollington Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and two from Greater Manchester Two people had to be cut free from two vehicles involved in a collision in Little Bollington. One person was trapped in a pick-up truck and another person was trapped in a car. A third casualty had released themselves by the time the crews arrived.  Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to release the two people trapped and handed them into the care of paramedics.
