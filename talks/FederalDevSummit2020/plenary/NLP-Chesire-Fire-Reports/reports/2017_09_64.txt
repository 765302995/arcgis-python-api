House fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 13/09/2017 - 04:27 Location: Knutsford Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow, one from Poynton, the aerial appliance and a supporting fire engine from Macclesfield and two fire engines from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service (Cheadle and Wythenshawe) Firefighters were called to a house fire on Knutsford Road, Wilmslow. On arrival crews found a two storey detached house which was well alight on all floors.  Crews used two hose reel jets, one main jet and the aerial ladder platform to extinguish the fire. No one was in the property at the time of the fire. A fire investigation to determine what started the fire is underway although the cause of the fire is not suspicious.  Crews were at the scene for 8 hours and 30 minutes.
