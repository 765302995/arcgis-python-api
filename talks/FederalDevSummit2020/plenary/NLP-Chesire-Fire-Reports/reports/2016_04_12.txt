Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/04/2016 - 20:11 Location: Stewards Avenue, Widnes. Attendance: A fire engine from Widnes Firefighters used water backpacks to put out a wheelie bin fire off Stewards Avenue, Widnes.  This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
