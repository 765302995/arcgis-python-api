Rubbish on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 13/11/2016 - 16:22 Location: Grove Street, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a large amount of rubbish on fire in a rear garden of a terraced house. The fire was close to property but not affecting it. The crew used a hose reel to extinguish the flames and damp down the area.
