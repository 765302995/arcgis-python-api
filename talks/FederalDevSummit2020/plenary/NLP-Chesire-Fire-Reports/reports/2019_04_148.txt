 Bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 20:42 Location: Lanark Gardens, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Fire crews were called to a deliberate fire involving a wheelie bin and pallets on Lanark Gardens, Widnes. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which had been set in a basketball court. Cheshire Police also attended the incident.
