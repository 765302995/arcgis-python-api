 Flat fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/02/2018 - 23:16 Location: Bewsey Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Penketh and one from Warrington Fire crews were called to a flat fire on Bewsey Road, Warrington. The fire was in a business on the ground floor and crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Crews then cleared smoke from the property using a large fan. One casualty was taken to hospital by paramedics suffering from smoke inhalation. Police were also in attendance. Crews were at the scene for four hours and 15 minutes.
