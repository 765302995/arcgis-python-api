Wheelie bin fire against house in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/05/2017 - 03:13 Location: Cartwright Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters tackled a fire involving a wheelie bin which was against a house wall. A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information about this or similar incidents is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101. Arson costs lives.
