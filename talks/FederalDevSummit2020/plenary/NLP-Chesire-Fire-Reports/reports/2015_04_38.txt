Out of control burning in Astbury Time of Incident: 25/04/2015 - 11:19 Location: Watery Lane, Astbury Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton A controlled bonfire became out of control after petrol was thrown on it.   One man suffered burns and the ambulance and air ambulance was called to the scene.  Firefighters used a high pressure hose to put out the fire which was in an open field.
