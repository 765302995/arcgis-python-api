Greenhouse on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 21/02/2015 - 04:46 Location: Middlewich Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich One hose reel jet was used to tackle a fire involving a polytunnel greenhouse at the rear of a property on Middlewich Road in Winsford, where the greenhouse was well alight.  The the owner sustained slight burns, so was was given fire aid by firefighters until an ambulance arrived.
