 Car fire in Chester Time of Incident: 30/04/2019 - 22:54 Location: Sealand Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly before 1pm firefighters from Chester were dispatched to reports of a vehicle fire on a car park off Sealand Road.   On arrival at the scene crews found that the vehicle was well alight.  One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. A thermal imaging camera when then used to check for any hotspots.  Crews were detained at the scene for approximately 30 minutes.
