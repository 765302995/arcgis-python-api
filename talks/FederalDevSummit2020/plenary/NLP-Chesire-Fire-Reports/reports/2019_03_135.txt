 Overheated fuse box in Runcorn Time of Incident: 01/03/2019 - 12:37 Location: Cunliffe Close, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Widnes Shortly after 12.30pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke at a domestic property in Cunliffe Close, Runcorn. On arrival there was a slight smell of burning. Crews isolated the electricity supply to the property and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. Firefighters discovered that the burning smell was coming from an overheated fuse box. They instructed the occupiers to leave the electrics off until an electrician arrived. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
