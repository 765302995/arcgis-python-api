Deliberate fires in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/04/2015 - 20:17 Location: Highlands Road/Horses Hill, Runcorn. Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn Firefighters used extended hose reels to put out a series of deliberate fires in Runcorn.  Initially one fire engine attended Highlands Road and dealt with a number of small fires. While in attendance at this incident the crew requested a second fire engine to attend Horses Hill where there were further fires.  Anyone with any information about who is responsible for the fires is urged to contact police on 101, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
