Domestic building on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 19/03/2017 - 12:08 Location: Braemar Avenue, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines, one from Northwich and one from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a domestic building on fire in Northwich and used a hose reel to extinguish it.  One man and a woman were treated by paramedics. A large fan was used to remove smoke from the property.
