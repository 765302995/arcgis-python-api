 One vehicle collision in Congleton Time of Incident: 02/12/2018 - 09:42 Location: Buxton Road, Congleton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton At approximately 9.40am on Sunday 2 December firefighters were called to reports of a one vehicle collision on Buxton Road, Congleton. On arrival at the scene crews found that a car had collided with a house. The driver of the vehicle was out of the car on arrival and they are not believed to have sustained serious injuries. The team assisted with making the vehicle safe and liaised with Police officers and paramedics at the scene. Crews were detained for approximately 45 minutes.
