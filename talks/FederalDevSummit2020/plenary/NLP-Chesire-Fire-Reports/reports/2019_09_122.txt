 Controlled burn in Northwich Time of Incident: 08/09/2019 - 02:52 Location: Tarporley Road, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters were called to a controlled burning on land off Tarporley Road, Northwich, which appeared to have got out of hand.  Crews used a hose reel to get the fire under control and then made the assessment that the fire was safe to continue to burn as long as it was monitored by the occupant.
