 Fire in the open in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/11/2018 - 17:20 Location: Hale Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 5.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a small fire in the open on Hale Road in Widnes. The crew extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
