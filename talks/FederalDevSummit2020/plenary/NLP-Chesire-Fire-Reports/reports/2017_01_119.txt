Wheelie bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 04/01/2017 - 21:34 Location: Wharton Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a fire involving a wheelie bin. Police also attended.  Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
