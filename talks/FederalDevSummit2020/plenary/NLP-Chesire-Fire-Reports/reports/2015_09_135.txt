Grill pan fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 05/09/2015 - 19:20 Location: Willow Green, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A kitchen was full of smoke after a grill pan fire in Northwich.  Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus removed the grill to the garden and extinguished the fire before using a large fan to clear the smoke from the kitchen. Kitchen fire safety advice
