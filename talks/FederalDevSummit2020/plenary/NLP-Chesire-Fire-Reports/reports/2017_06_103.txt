Trees on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/06/2017 - 17:21 Location: Pasture Lane, Poulton with Fearnhead, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters used a water backpack to put out a fire that had been started in some trees. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
