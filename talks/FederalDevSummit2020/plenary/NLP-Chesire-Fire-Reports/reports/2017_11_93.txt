Bird hide on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/11/2017 - 09:28 Location: Forrest Way, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Penketh Firefighters were called to a fire in a wooden bird hide on Forrest Way, Warrington. The hide measured 4m by 2m and the fire was extinguished by crews using a hose reel jet.
