 Two-vehicle collision in Frodsham Time of Incident: 24/02/2019 - 12:27 Location: Chester Road, Frodsham Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Widnes Shortly before 12.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Chester Road in Sutton, Frodsham. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved two vehicles and no-one was trapped inside either of them. Casualties were assessed by paramedics at the scene. Firefighters made the vehicles safe and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
