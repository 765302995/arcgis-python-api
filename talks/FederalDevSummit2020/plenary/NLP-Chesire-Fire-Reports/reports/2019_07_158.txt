 Tree fire, Wilmslow Time of Incident: 05/07/2019 - 20:30 Location: Handforth Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Crews were called to deal with a fire in a tree stump near The Wilmslow Academy. On arrival they found the fire had already been extinguished by members of the public using buckets of water, but firefighters used a water backpack and thermal imaging camera to make sure it was completely out. They were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
