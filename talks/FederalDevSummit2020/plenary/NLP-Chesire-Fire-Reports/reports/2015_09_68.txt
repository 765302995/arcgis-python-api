Small rubbish fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 16/09/2015 - 22:35 Location: Whitbys Lane, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters have dealt with a small bonfire on land to the rear of Beckenham Grove, Winsford. Crews extinguished a large quantity of refuse using one hose reel jet.
