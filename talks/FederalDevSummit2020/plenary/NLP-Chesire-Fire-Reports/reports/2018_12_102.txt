 Bedroom fire at house in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 07/12/2018 - 18:05 Location: Beeston Close, Holmes Chapel Attendance: Two fire engines attended -one from Middlewich and one from Crewe Firefighters attended to a fire at a detached house on Beeston Close in Holmes Chapel.  Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, used one hose reel to put out the blaze which was located in a bedroom in the attic space.  The cause of the fire is believed to be an electrical heater which had been left turned on.  Firefighters then used a fan to clear away the smoke and make sure the area was safe.  They were in attendance for around three hours.
