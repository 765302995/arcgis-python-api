Road traffic collision in Sandbach Time of Incident: 21/06/2015 - 19:44 Location: Salt Line Way, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Sandbach Firefighters attended a report of a road traffic collision with a car on fire.  A car had gone into a hedge and collided with a tanker parked in yard.  The occupants of the vehicle had left the scene.  Firefighters confirmed with the tanker owner that there were no hazardous materials on the vehicle.  The crew made the scene safe and asked for police to attend the incident.
