 Collision involving two cars in Northwich Time of Incident: 02/02/2019 - 20:14 Location: Weaverham Road in Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 8.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving two vehicles on Weaverham Road in Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that the collision involved two cars and no-one was trapped inside either vehicle. Firefighters made one of the vehicles safe and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. Police also attended.
