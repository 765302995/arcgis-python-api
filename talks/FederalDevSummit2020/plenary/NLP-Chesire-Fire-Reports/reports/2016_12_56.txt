Smoke in kitchen in Sandbach Time of Incident: 21/12/2016 - 16:37 Location: Park Lane, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Smoke in a kitchen required firefighters to use a hose reel to extinguish the source. All persons were accounted for and out of the property on the arrival of the crew. Paramedics attended and police closed the road while the incident was dealt with.
