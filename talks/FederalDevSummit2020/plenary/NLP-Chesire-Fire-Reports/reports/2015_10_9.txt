Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/10/2015 - 12:48 Location: Crab Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Firefighters were called to reports of a car fire at the Padgate Campus of the University of Chester in Warrington.  Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish a fire located in the engine compartment of the vehicle.
