Tyre fire in Adlington, Macclesfield Time of Incident: 09/05/2017 - 00:12 Location: Street Lane, Adlington Macclesfield. Attendance: Fire engines from Poynton and Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Street Lane in Adlington, Macclesfield. When they arrived crews found a large amount of tyres on fire.  This had spread to some trees. Firefighters battled the fire with hose reel jets. Some roads around the area were closed.  Crews left the scene at 10am but will check the scene periodically. All roads around the site are now open.
