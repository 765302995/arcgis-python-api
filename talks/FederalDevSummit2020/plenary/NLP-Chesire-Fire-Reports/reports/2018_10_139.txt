 Van fire in Chester Time of Incident: 05/10/2018 - 10:45 Location: Christleton Road, Boughton, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley and one from Widnes Fire crews were called to Christleton Road, Chester, after a report of a van fire. On arrival crews found the van was well alight and crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Whilst crews fought the fire the road was temporarily closed to traffic.
