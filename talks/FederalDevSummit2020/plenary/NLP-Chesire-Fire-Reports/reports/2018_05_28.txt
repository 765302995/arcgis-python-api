 Fence on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 27/05/2018 - 15:23 Location: Birkdale Gardens, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Fire crews were called to Birkdale Gardens, Winsford, after a fence panel caught fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the panel and a small rubbish fire.
