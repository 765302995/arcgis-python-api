 Wooden pallets on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/04/2019 - 21:47 Location: Lanark Gardens, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Crews attended to a wooden pallet on fire at a park near Lanark Gardens in Widnes.  Water was used to extinguish the fire and before crews searched the rest of the park to ensure there were no further fires nearby.
