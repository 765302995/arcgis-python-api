Road traffic collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 14/06/2016 - 21:58 Location: Southern Expressway and A56 Brookvale, Runcorn, runcorn. Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn and Frodsham Firefighters released one woman from a car after it was involved in a road traffic collision.
