 Chimney fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 28/02/2019 - 20:23 Location: Wesley Avenue in Alsager Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar Shortly after 8.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a chimney being on fire at a domestic property on Wesley Avenue in Alsager. The small fire involving a flue was out on arrival. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check temperatures and make sure that the chimney and the property in general were safe. They were in attendance for around 10 minutes. For tips on avoiding a chimney fire visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chimneys.
