 Fence fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/04/2019 - 15:46 Location: Robinson Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a fence fire on Robinson Road, Ellesmere Port. On arrival the fence was well alight and crews used a hose  reel jet to put out the fire before cutting away a small section of the fence using a reciprocating saw.
