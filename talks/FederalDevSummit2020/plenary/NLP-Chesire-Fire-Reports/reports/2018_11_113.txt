 Vehicle fire near Crewe Time of Incident: 09/11/2018 - 12:11 Location: A500, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly after 12pm firefighters were called to reports of a vehicle fire on the A500, at junction 16 of the M6, near Crewe. Firefighters from Crewe attended the scene and discovered a van which was well alight on the Staffordshire side of the border. The team, assisted by firefighters from Staffordshire Fire and Rescue Service, used two breathing apparatus, a hose reel jet and dry powder to extinguish the fire. Crews were detained at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
