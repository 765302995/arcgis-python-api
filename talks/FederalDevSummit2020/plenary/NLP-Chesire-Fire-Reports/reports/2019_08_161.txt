 Grill pan fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/08/2019 - 20:48 Location: Weates Close, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Penketh and one from Widnes Crews attended to a grill pan fire at a pub on Weates Close in Widnes.  The fire was extinguished using a hose reel and crews monitored the area using a thermal imaging camera.  Crews were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.  Read our advice on how to use grill pans safely https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/grills
