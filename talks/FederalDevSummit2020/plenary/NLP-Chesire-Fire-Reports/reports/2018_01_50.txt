 Junction box on fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 19/01/2018 - 14:06 Location: Moss Lane, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich and the aerial platform from Lymm Fire crews were called to a fire in a junction box on a telegraph pole on Moss Lane, Sandbach. Crews closed the road for around two hours during this incident whilst an electrician turned off the supply. Crews then used the aerial ladder platform and a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was well alight on arrival.
