 Vehicle fire on M56 westbound Time of Incident: 21/09/2019 - 13:53 Location: M56, westbound between junction 12 to junction 14 Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Fire crews attended to a vehicle on fire on the westbound carriageway of the M56 between junction 12 to junction 14.  One hose reel was used to extinguish the flames and to dampen down the vehicle.
