Shed fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/04/2015 - 10:18 Location: Adlington Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish a shed fire caused by a discarded cigarette.
