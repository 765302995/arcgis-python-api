 Loose refuse fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 14/02/2019 - 20:36 Location: Godscroft Lane, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly after 8.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on Godscroft Lane in Frodsham. On arrival the crew discovered approximately 10 square metres of rubbish well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
