Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 16/08/2016 - 00:02 Location: Macclesfield Road, Nether Alderley Attendance: Two fire engines from Wimslow and Macclesfield and one appliance from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to reports of a fire in Nether Alderley near Macclesfield. Upon arrival, crews found a fire within the kitchen of a property. Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, put out the fire using a hose reel jet and a covering jet before using a pressurised van to ventilate the property.
