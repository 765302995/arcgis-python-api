Trees on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 20/07/2016 - 21:16 Location: Woodchurch Lane, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Trees were set on fire in a suspected deliberate arson incident. Firefighters used beaters and water back packs to put out the fire.  Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101. Arson endangers lives, property and wildlife.
