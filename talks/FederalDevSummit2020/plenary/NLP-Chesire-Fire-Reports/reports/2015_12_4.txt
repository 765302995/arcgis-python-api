Lead flashing removed from roof in Nantwich Time of Incident: 30/12/2015 - 10:17 Location: Millstone Lane, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to Millstone Lane in Nantwich following reports of a dangerous structure.  The fire crew used a 7 metre ladder to access the roof of a property and remove a large piece of lead flashing which was hanging over the pavement.
