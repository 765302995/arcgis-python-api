 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 21:41 Location: Scott Avenue, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to a fire in Scott Avenue in Widnes. On arrival crews found that a wheelie bin and a number of trees were on fire. Crews used a hose reel to put out the fire, which is believed to have been started deliberately.
