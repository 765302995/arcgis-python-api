Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/01/2017 - 10:48 Location: Wedgwood Drive, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Birchwood. A fire in a kitchen required firefighters to attend and use a hose reel to extinguish it. All persons were accounted for at this incident and a large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property along with a thermal imaging camera to check that the fire was out before the crew left.
