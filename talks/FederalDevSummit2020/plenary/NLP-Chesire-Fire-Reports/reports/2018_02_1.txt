Bedroom fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 27/02/2018 - 15:12 Location: Warrington Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh. One fire engine from Widnes. Firefighters attended a fire in a bedroom at an address in Warrington Road in Widnes. Crews used a hose reel and two breathing apparatus to put out the fire. They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
