Car fire in Stretton Time of Incident: 07/12/2016 - 11:36 Location: Tarporley Road, Stretton, near Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath A car was on fire at the roundabout on Tarporley Road in Stretton.  Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze. The M56 slip road was closed for a while as the crew dealt with the fire.
