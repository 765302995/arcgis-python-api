 Two-vehicle collision in Middlewich Time of Incident: 25/04/2018 - 07:22 Location: Clivegreen Lane, Middlewich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Middlewich and one from Winsford At around 7.20am firefighters were called to a collision involving two vehicles on Clivegreen Lane in Stanthorne, Middlewich. On arrival crews discovered than no-one was trapped in the vehicles. Firefighters made the scene safe and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
