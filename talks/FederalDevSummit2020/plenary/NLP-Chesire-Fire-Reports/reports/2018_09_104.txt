 Smoke caused by unattended toaster in Alsager Time of Incident: 08/09/2018 - 16:34 Location: Church Road, Alsager Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich At around 4.35pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a domestic property on Church Road in Alsager.  On arrival the crew discovered that the alarm had been activated by a toaster that had been left unattended.  Firefighters used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
