 Reports of a building fire at a shop in Sandbach Time of Incident: 23/05/2018 - 20:48 Location: Penda Way, Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach Firefighters from Sandbach were called to reports of a possible fire at newsagents on Penda Way. Crews attended the scene and found that a full evacuation of the store had taken place. The team used a thermal imaging camera to examine the scene and found that a food chiller had over heated. Firefighter isolated the chiller and removed it from the premises. The team were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
