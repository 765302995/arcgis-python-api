Car well alight on M56 by junction 12 Time of Incident: 30/08/2017 - 20:45 Location: M56 east, junction 12 to 11 Attendance: One fire engine from Frodsham and one from Powey Lane A car was well alight, so firefighters used a hose reel jet to deal with the fire. Police attended this incident and closed lane one of the motorway so that firefighters could extinguish the fire.
