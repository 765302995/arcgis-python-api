Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/12/2017 - 01:00 Location: North Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a car on fire in North Avenue in Warrington which had spread to the front door of a house. Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the fires. Police have been informed and a joint investigation is underway.
