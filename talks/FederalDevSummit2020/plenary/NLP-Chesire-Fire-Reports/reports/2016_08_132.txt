Boat stuck on banking in Marina in Winsford Time of Incident: 09/08/2016 - 13:19 Location: Wharton Park Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters removed a boat from the banking of Winsford Marina. The boat had become stuck on the banking, with two people inside. With help from a barge, crews managed to remove the boat from the banking.
