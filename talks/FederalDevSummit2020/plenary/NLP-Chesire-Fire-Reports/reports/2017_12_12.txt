Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/12/2017 - 20:00 Location: Cunningham Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Fire crews were called to a wheelie bin fire on a playing field on Cunningham Road, Widnes. Crews used a fire extinguisher to put out the blaze. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident please call Cheshire Police on 101 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
