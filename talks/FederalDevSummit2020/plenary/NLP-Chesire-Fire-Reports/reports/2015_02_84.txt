Vehicle on fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 08/02/2015 - 20:12 Location: Wimboldsley, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich A car which had deliberately been set on fire in Wimboldsley required firefighters to attend and use water from one hose reel to put it out.  If anyone has any information about this arson fire they should call Cheshire police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
