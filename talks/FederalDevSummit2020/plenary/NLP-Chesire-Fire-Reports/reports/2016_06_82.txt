Woman and a dog rescued from a riverbank in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/06/2016 - 20:23 Location: Howley Lane, Warrington. Attendance: Three fire engines from Stockton Heath and Warrington A dog was stuck on a riverbank near to Howley Lane in Warrington.  People were trying to rescue the dog.  A woman became trapped in mud on the riverbank.  Firefighters rescued the woman, who was then handed to the care of paramedics.  The dog was rescued and handed back to its owner.
