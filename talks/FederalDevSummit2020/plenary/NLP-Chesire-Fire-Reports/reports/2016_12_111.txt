Casualty trapped after road traffic collision in Bostock Time of Incident: 09/12/2016 - 18:50 Location: Brick Kiln Lane,Bostock, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich  One person was trapped in a car after a road traffic collision involving two vehicles. Firefighters removed the roof of the car and then released the casualty, a man, from the vehicle. He was handed into the care of the ambulance service. The second vehicle was a van.  Brick Kiln Lane was closed while the emergency services dealt with the incident.
