Tumble dryer fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/01/2017 - 11:11 Location: Hollins Lane, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to an incident at Hollins Park Hospital in Warrington.  When they got there they found that there had been a small fire involving some fluff in a tumble dryer but this was out when they arrived.
