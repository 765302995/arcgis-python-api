Fire in Ellesmere Port house electrical box Time of Incident: 14/12/2015 - 10:49 Location: Rugby Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port An electrical box was the cause of a small fire at an Ellesmere Port property. A small amount of cable on the internal supply had been affected. Firefighters checked the property with a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no further hotspots.
