Cooker fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/10/2015 - 13:03 Location: Livingstone Close, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a fire involving a cooker. A high pressure fan was then used to clear the property of smoke. The crew gave first aid to a man who had sustained an injury to his foot. The fire is believed to have been caused by a faulty cooker.  To avoid kitchen fires please follow our Kitchen fire safety advice.
