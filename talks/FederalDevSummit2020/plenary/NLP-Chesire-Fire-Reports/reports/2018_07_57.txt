 Back garden fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/07/2018 - 14:17 Location: Timbrell Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters dealt with a rubbish fire in the back garden of a property on Timbrell Avenue in Crewe.  A hose reel was used to put out the fire and a thermal imaging camera helped to see if the fire had spread further.
