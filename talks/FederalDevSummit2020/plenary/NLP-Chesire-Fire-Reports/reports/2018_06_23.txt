 Barbecue fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 27/06/2018 - 21:06 Location: Lindfield Estate North, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford Firefighters from Knutsford attended reports of a barbecue fire in Wilmslow. On arrival crews found that the fire had been extinguished by the homeowner. The team used one hose reel jet to cool the barbecue and were in attendance for approximately ten minutes. For more information about barbecue safety visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/outdoor-safety/barbecue-safety
