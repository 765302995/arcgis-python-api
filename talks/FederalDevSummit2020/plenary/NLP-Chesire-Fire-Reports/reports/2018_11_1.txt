Fire in woodland in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 29/11/2018 - 13:26 Location: New Bridge Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a fire in a wooded area off New Bridge Road, Ellesmere Port. Crews made their way on foot  and used water backpacks and beaters to extinguish multiple seats of fire.
