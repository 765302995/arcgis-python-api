 Small fire in Bostock Time of Incident: 22/10/2018 - 16:11 Location: Bostock Road, Bostock, near Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Firefighters from Middlewich used a hose reel jet to extinguish a small fire in a woodland area at the side of the A533, Bostock Road in Bostock. The team were detained at the scene for approximately 15 minutes.
