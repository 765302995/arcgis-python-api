 Three vehicle collision in Winwick Time of Incident: 19/03/2019 - 06:50 Location: Newton Road, Warrington. Attendance: Three fire engines attended - two from Warrington and one from Penketh Shortly before 7am firefighters from Warrington and Penketh attended a report of a three vehicle collision on Newton Road, Winwick.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered that two casualties were trapped in their cars and there was one walking wounding  Firefighters used specialist electronic cutting equipment to release the two trapped casualties.  All three casualties have since been passed to the care of paramedics and taken to Aintree Hospital.  Fire crews were in attendance for approximately one hour and twenty minutes.
