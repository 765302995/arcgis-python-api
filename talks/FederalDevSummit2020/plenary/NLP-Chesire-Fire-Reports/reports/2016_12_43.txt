Man rescued from Macclesfield roof Time of Incident: 23/12/2016 - 02:57 Location: New Hall Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Knutsford to support the rope rescue team Firefighters used a seven metre ladder to rescue a man on the roof of a New Hall Street house. The crews were able to bring him safely down to ground level.
