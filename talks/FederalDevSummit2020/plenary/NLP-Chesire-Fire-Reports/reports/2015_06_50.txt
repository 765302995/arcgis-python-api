Collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 21/06/2015 - 00:16 Location: Station Road, Acton Bridge, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich Firefighters responded to a report of a collision on Station Road, Northwich.  When they arrived they found that a car had crashed into a vacant end terraced property. One man was trapped in the car. Crews cut him free and passed him to the care of the ambulance service who took him to hospital.
