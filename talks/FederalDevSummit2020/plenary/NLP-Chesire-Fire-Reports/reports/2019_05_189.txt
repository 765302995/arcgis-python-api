 Fire in the open in Beeston Time of Incident: 04/05/2019 - 17:56 Location: Chapel Lane, Beeston, Near Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly before 6pm firefighters from Chester attended reports of a fire in a woodland area on Chapel Lane, Beeston.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a fire approximately 4 metres by 5 metres which had become deep seated in the peat ground.  The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They then used shovels to turn over the peat and cooled the soil with water.  The team were detained for approximately one hour.
