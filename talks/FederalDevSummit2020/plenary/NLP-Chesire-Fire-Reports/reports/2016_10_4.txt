Refuse and tree fire on Weaver Valley Road in Winsford Time of Incident: 29/10/2016 - 15:16 Location: Weaver Valley Road, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford A quantity of refuse and a tree was set on fire deliberately, so firefighters attended and extinguished the fire.  This fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
