 Two vehicle collision near Northwich Time of Incident: 15/11/2019 - 14:53 Location: A49 Warrington Road, Acton Bridge near Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich and Lymm Shortly before 3pm firefighters from Northwich and Lymm responded to reports of a road traffic collision on the A49, Warrington Road, in Acton Bridge.  On arrival at the scene crews found that the incident involved involving two cars and two people were medically trapped in their vehicles.  Firefighters used specialist equipment to release the casualties and they were then passed to the care of paramedics.  crews were in attendance at the incident for over 50 minutes.
