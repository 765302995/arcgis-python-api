 Carbon dioxide leak in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/06/2018 - 04:39 Location: Europa Boulevard in Burton and Westbrook, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and two fire engines from Penketh Crews attended to reports of six carbon dioxide cylinders leaking at IKEA on Europa Boulevard in Burton and Westbrook, Warrington.  A full evacuation of the store took place as a precaution before firefighters wearing two breathing apparatus stemmed the leak and made sure it was safe.  The room was left open to allow for ventilation.  Ambulance were also in attendance.
