 Kitchen fire in Chester Time of Incident: 19/07/2019 - 19:25 Location: Bouverie Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly before 7.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a kitchen fire at a domestic property in Bouverie Street, Chester. The fire was out on arrival. Firefighters isolated the electricity supply to the address and used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. They also used a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots and make sure that the kitchen was safe. The fire is believed to have been caused by an overheated extraction unit. Firefighters were in attendance for less than an hour.
