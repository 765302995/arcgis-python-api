Car fire on M6 northbound near Warrington Time of Incident: 14/05/2017 - 13:45 Location: M6 northbound junctions 21A to 22 Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington, Birchwood and Leigh (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) A saloon car was heavily involved in fire on the hard shoulder of the M6 northbound between junctions 21A and 22. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and crews made the scene safe, remaining in attendance at the incident approximately one hour.
