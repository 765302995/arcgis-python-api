 Industrial bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/05/2019 - 22:22 Location: Winwick Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 10.20pm Warrington firefighters were called to reports of a fire at the rear of a supermarket on Winwick Road.  On arrival the team discovered an industrial size bin which was well alight.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111
