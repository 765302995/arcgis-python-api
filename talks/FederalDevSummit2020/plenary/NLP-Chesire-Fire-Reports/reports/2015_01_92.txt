Portable building fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/01/2015 - 12:42 Location: Waterloo Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters have been tackling a fire in a portable cabin at Catalyst Trade Park.  The blaze involved an electric heater.  The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, turned off the power and used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  A high pressure fan was then used to clear the smoke.
