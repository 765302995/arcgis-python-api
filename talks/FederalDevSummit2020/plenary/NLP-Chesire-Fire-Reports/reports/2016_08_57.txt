Car on fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 19/08/2016 - 11:42 Location: Toft Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one fire engine from Northwich One car caught fire following a minor road traffic collision. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out and dampened down the car. Toft Road was closed during the incident but has now been cleared and is open as usual.
