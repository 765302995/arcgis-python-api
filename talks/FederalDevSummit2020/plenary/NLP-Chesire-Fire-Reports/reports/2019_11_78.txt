 Fire involving log burner in Congleton Time of Incident: 14/11/2019 - 21:02 Location: The Mount, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines attended -one from Congleton and one from Holmes Chapel Fire crews attended to a fire involving a log burner at a house on The Mount in Congleton.  Firefighters extinguished the fire using water and removed hot embers.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check the chimney which the log burner was attached to and the loft space in case the fire had spread.   They found no hot spots and the area was deemed safe.
