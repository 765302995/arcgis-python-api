Car fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 15/10/2016 - 13:22 Location: M56 between Junctions 12 and 14. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn, one fire engine from Widnes and one from Ellesmere port. Firefighters responded to a report of a car fire on the M56 between Junctions 12 and 14.  When they arrived crews found that there was a saloon care ablaze on the hard shoulder. Firefighters used one breathing apparatus and one hose reel to extinguish the fire.
