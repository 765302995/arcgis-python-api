Cooker fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/09/2016 - 12:13 Location: Bray Close, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke and one person was assessed by paramedics for possible smoke inhalation. Hard wired smoke alarms alerted the occupant to the fire.  Kitchens are a hot spot for accidental fires, most of which could have been avoided. Avoid causing damage to your home by always cooking safely.
