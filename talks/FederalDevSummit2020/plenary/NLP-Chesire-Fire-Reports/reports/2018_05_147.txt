 Smoke logged house in Widnes Time of Incident: 13/05/2018 - 08:50 Location: Barrows Green Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 8.50am firefighters were called to a smoke logged house in Barrows Green Lane, Widnes. They used a large high pressure fan to remove the smoke, which was caused by burnt toast, and were at the scene for around 20 minutes.
