 Motorbilke fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/05/2019 - 20:14 Location: Bridgewater Canal tow path, near to Halton Road, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Runcorn firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish a motorbike which had been deliberately set alight on the tow path next to the Bridgewater Canal.  The team were in attendance for approximately 45 minutes.  Anyone with any information in relation to the fire is asked to call Crimestppers on 0800 555 111.
