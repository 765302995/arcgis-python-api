 Chimney fire in Baddiley Time of Incident: 24/01/2018 - 22:59 Location: Baddiley Close, Baddiley Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At 22.59 firefighters from Crewe were called to reports of a chimney fire in Baddiley. On arrival at the scene the team initial used a thermal imaging camera to locate the source of the fire before using chimney rods to attack it.  The crew then exposed the chimney breast on the first floor of the property before using a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
