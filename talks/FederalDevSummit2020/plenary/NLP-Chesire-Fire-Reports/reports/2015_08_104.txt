Smoke in Winsford home Time of Incident: 13/08/2015 - 07:10 Location: Ullswater Avenue, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters have attended a house in Ullswater Avenue following reports of smoke coming from under the stairs near to an electricity box.  Firefighters ensured the scene was safe and completed a home safety assessment. The property's electricity was isolated and the electricity supplier has been alerted.
