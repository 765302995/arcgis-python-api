 Car on fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 14/01/2018 - 03:12 Location: Milton Road, Widnes Attendance: One appliance from Widnes Firefighters tackled a blaze in a small hatchback, which was well alight. They used a single hosereel to extinguish the flames, and then requested police attendance as it is thought to have been a deliberate ignition. Anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire police on 101, quoting incident 122 of 14 January 2018,
