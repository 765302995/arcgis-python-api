 Burnt food causing smoke in Crewe Time of Incident: 07/04/2019 - 01:33 Location: Stalbridge Road, Crewe. CW2 7LS Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to reports of a fire in a house on Stalbridge Road, Crewe, just after 1.30am.  Smoke at the house was caused by burnt cooking and firefighters ventilated the property.
