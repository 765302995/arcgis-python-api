Road traffic collision on M56 at junction 14 Time of Incident: 24/02/2017 - 06:18 Location: M56 east junctions 14 to 12 Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters assisted the driver of a heavy goods vehicle to safety after a collision involving the one vehicle.
