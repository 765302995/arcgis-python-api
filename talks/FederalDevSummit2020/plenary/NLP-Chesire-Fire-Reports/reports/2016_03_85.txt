Tree on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 14/03/2016 - 21:38 Location: Quarry Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters extinguished a tree that was on fire with water after it was believed to have been set on fire deliberately. Police were informed. If anyone has any information about this incident they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
