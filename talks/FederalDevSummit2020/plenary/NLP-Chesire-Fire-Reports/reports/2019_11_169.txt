 Small fire involving roofing materials in Sandbach Time of Incident: 31/10/2019 - 18:16 Location: Zan Drive, Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach Firefighters were called to a small but deep seated unattended fire involving roofing materials and general waste and extinguished it with a hose reel. The firefighters also checked a nearby garage and a shipping container for damage before leaving.
