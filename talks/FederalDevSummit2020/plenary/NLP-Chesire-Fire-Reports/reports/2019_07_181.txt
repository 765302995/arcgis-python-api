 Car fire in Mere Time of Incident: 02/07/2019 - 15:24 Location: Chester Road, Mere. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters were called to a car fire in Chester Road, Mere. A Toyota Aventis had a burning clutch which had caught fire. The AA put out the fire before crews arrived, Crews damped the car down using hose reels to ensure the fire didn't re-ignite.
