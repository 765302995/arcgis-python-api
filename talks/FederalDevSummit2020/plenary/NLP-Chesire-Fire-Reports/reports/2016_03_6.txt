Horse stuck in a stable in Handforth Time of Incident: 30/03/2016 - 08:43 Location: Sagars Road, Handforth Attendance: The specialist Animal Rescue Unit from Knutsford and an accompanying fire engine attended along with one fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to rescue a horse which had got stuck in a stable and was upside down. Whisky Mac was put back on his feet with the use of farm machinery and the assistance of the crews, who used specialist straps to secure and lift him. A vet attended to sedate the 30-year-old horse while the rescue took place and cared for Whisky Mac throughout his ordeal.
