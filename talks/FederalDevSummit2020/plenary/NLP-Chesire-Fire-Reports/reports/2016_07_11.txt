Deliberate fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 28/07/2016 - 14:07 Location: Greystone Park, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe A small fire in the open is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters were called after black smoke was seen issuing from behind the old youth centre near Greystone Park. They found a small fire involving bedding, which they put out.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 480 of 28 July.
