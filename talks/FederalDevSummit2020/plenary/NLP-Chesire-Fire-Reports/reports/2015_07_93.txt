Bedroom fire at Halton General Hospital Time of Incident: 17/07/2015 - 19:02 Location: Halton General Hospital, Hospital way, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one from Runcorn Two fire engines from Widnes and one from runcorn were sent to fire in a bedroom on a secure hospital unit at Halton General Hospital. Fire fighters used two hose reels, four breathing apparatus and a large fan to fight the fire.  The fire was believed to have been started deliberately.
