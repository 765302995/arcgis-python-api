 Wheelie bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/04/2019 - 21:03 Location: Okell Street, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 9pm firefighters were called to a report of a bin being on fire in Okell Street, Runcorn. On arrival the crew found a wheelie bin well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. For wheelie bin fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
