Trees on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 29/08/2017 - 14:35 Location: Scott Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Handheld extinguishers were used by firefighters to put out a small fire in a wooded area behind Scott Avenue. It is believed the fire was started deliberately and anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
