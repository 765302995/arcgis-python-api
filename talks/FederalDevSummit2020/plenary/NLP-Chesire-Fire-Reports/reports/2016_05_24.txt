Chimney fire in Goostrey Time of Incident: 25/05/2016 - 17:06 Location: New Platt Lane, Goostrey. Attendance: A fire engine from Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on New Platt Lane, Goostrey.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire in the chimney. Crews used hose reel jets and chimney rods to put the fire out.  Chimney fire safety advice
