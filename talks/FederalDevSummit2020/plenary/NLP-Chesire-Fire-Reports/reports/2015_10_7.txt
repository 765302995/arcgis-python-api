Road traffic collision on the M6 (J19) northbound Time of Incident: 28/10/2015 - 18:21 Location: M6,Junction 19 northbound Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath A car was involved in a road traffic collision on the M6 motorway. The car left the roadway and came to rest on its roof down the embankment. One male driver was examined by paramedics and was then helped from the vehicle by paramedics and firefighters.
