Building fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 27/09/2015 - 19:07 Location: Chester Way service road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich and one from Winsford Three fire engines were called to a report of a fire on the ground floor of a building on Chester Way service road. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a water jet to tackle the blaze in the 80m x 50m concrete cladded building. The crews then thoroughly checked the building to make sure there were no further area of heat and the incident handed over to the police. Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
