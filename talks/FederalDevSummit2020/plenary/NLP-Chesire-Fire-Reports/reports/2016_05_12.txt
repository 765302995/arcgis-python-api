House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/05/2016 - 14:56 Location: St Mawgan Court, Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines from Birchwood and Warrignton Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire at St Mawgan Court, Warrington.  When they arrived they found a small fire in the bedroom of the property. Crews used hose reel jets and special fans to deal with the incident. They also carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving.
