Domestic electrical box on fire in Mouldsworth Time of Incident: 05/01/2016 - 09:03 Location: Smithy Lane, Mouldsworth, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Fire in an electrical box inside a farmhouse in Mouldsworth caused heavy smokelogging. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets and dry powder to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was used to clear the smoke. A fire engine remained at the scene to further cool the box once the energy company isolated the power.  All people were accounted for at the incident.
