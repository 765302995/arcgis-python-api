 Two-car collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/06/2019 - 17:07 Location: A5117 in Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 5pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision on the A5117 in Ellesmere Port, between Cheshire Oaks Designer Oulet and the Strawberry Roundabout. On arrival the crews discovered that the collision involved two saloon cars and that no-one was trapped in either vehicle. Firefighters assisted the police in making the cars safe and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
