Lorry fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 07/08/2015 - 06:23 Location: Lapwing Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Crews tackled a fire involving a lorry well alight on parkland.  Firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used three hose reel jets to put out the fire.  Three cylinders and a refrigeration unit were on the lorry and were cooled and monitored using a hose reel jet and thermal imaging camera.  One man received burns and was treated by firefighters pending the arrival of the ambulance service.
