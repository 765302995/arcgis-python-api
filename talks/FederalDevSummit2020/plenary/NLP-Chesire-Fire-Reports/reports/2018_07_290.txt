 Conservatory fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 00:10 Location: Whetstone Hey, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Firefighters were called to a conservatory well alight at a house on Whetstone Hey in Ellesmere Port.  Crews, wearing six breathing apparatus, used two hose reels and a positive pressure ventilation fan to extinguish the fire.  Firefighters were in attendance for over two hours.
