 Outbuilding fire, Northwich Time of Incident: 10/06/2018 - 13:09 Location: King Street, Northwich Attendance: Five fire engines (Lymm, Winsford, Holmes Chapel, and two from Crewe) Firefighters responded to reports of a building on fire on King Street, near Northwich. Five fire engines form Lymm, Winsford, Crewe (two) and Holmes chapel attended to tackle the blaze, which involved a building  measuring around 15m x 5m. Crews used four main jets and one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for five hours. Some local road closures were put in place during the incident but they have now all reopened.
