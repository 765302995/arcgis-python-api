Van fire on the M62 Time of Incident: 18/12/2017 - 14:59 Location: m62 junction 7 to junction 8, warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, one from Penketh and one from Widnes Fire crews were called to a van fire on the M62 between junction 7 and 8. Fire crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was in the engine bay of the vehicle. They then checked the rest of the vehicle using a thermal imaging camera.
