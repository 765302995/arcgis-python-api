Road traffic collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 02/10/2015 - 14:41 Location: Southern Expressway, Hallwood Park, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines Firefighters and other emergency services have been at the scene of a road traffic collision on Southern Expressway. The incident involved a car and a heavy goods vehicle and a man was trapped in the car. Firefighters removed the vehicle roof to release the casualty who was handed into the care of paramedics.  Police closed the Widnes bound carriageway on the Southern Expressway near Hallwood Road flyover while emergency services dealt with the incident.
