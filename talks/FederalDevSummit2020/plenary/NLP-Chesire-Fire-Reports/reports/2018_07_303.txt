 Bushes fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 17:24 Location: Bowland Rise, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At around 5.25pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open at Bowland Rise in Winsford. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved bushes. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
