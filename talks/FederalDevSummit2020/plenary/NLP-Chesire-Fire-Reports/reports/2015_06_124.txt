Cars on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/06/2015 - 22:14 Location: Lovely Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Two cars were involved in fire, with one car well alight and one heat damaged with a small fire.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fires.  Police were asked to attend as it is believed the fires were started deliberately.  The crew was at the scene for approximately one hour.
