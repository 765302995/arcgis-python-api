Domestic building fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/07/2016 - 15:48 Location: Woodfield road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Firefighters were called to a domestic building fire in Ellesmere Port. Firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire, which had originated from the cooker which had caught fire. One casualty, suffering from smoke inhalation, was handed into the care of the paramedics.  Kitchen fire safety
