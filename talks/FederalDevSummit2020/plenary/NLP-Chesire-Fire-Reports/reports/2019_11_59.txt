 Two vehicle collision in Chester Time of Incident: 17/11/2019 - 12:45 Location: Tarvin Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester. One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port. Firefighters were called to a two vehicle collision on Tarvin Road, Guilden Sutton, Christleton, Chester. Crews extricated one casualty  .  The made the scene safe and remained in attendance approximately 45 minutes.
