 Outbuilding fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 23/05/2019 - 12:52 Location: Well Lane, Northwich Attendance: Six fire engines attended - one from Lymm, one from Warrington, one from Stockton Heath, one from Sandbach, one from Crewe and one from Birchwood Firefighters have been called to a fire in an outbuilding on Well Lane, Northwich. The crew, one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets are currently fighting the fire which is a building approximately 10m by 8m.  UPDATE 5.30pm  Crews are continuing to damp down the affected area using a hose reel jet. They are also using a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.  UPDATE 8am - Crews have been continuing to check for hotspots using the thermal imaging camera.  Update 10am - After working throughout the night firefirghters declared the fire safe this morning.
