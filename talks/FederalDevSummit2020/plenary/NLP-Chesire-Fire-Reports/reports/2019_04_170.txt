 Fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/04/2019 - 20:33 Location: Farnworth Road, Penketh, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 8.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in the grounds of Whitemoss Nursery and Garden Centre on Farnworth Road in Penketh, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning that had been left unattended. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for nearly half-an-hour.
