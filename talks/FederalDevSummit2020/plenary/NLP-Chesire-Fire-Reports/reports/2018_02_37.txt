 Washing machine fire in Shavington Time of Incident: 21/02/2018 - 17:36 Location: Rope Lane, Shavington Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to a washing machine fire in a house on Rope Lane, Shavington. Crews removed the machine from the property and extinguished the fire using a hose reel jet. They then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.
