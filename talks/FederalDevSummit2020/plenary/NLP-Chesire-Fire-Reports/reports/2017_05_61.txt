Fire at the back of a block of flats in Northwich Time of Incident: 20/05/2017 - 23:10 Location: Victoria Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters used a hose reel to extinguish a fire at the back of a block of flats.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately so anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
