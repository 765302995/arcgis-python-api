 Camper van fire in Chester Time of Incident: 25/07/2019 - 18:18 Location: Bolesworth Hill Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Malpas and one from Chester Crews attended to a fire involving a camper van and a car well alight on Bolesworth Hill Road in Chester.  Firefighters, wearing two breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  Crews then checked for hot spots and dampened down.    People were evacuated from the area as a precaution and two people were treated by medic on site for shock.
