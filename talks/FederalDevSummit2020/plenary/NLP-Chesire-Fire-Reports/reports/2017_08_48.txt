Small fire in the open in Crewe Time of Incident: 22/08/2017 - 17:53 Location: Sherborne Road, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to a small fire in the open on Sherborne Road, Crewe. A mattress and a small amount of wood had been deliberately set alight. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. If you have any information about the incident please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
