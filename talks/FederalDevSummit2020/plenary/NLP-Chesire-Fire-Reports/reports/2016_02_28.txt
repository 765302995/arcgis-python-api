Car fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 23/02/2016 - 17:47 Location: Macclesfield Road, Wilmslow. Attendance: Fire engines from Wilmslow and Stockport (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service). Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Macclesfield Road, Wilmslow.  When they arrived crews found that there was a blaze involving a car. Crews used two hose reels and foam jets to put the fire out.
