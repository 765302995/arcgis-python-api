Chimney fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 12/12/2017 - 21:55 Location: Crewe Road, Wistaston Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe attended a chimney fire in Wistaston. On arrival at the scene the crew initial used water to extinguish the fire before using a thermal imaging camera and chimney hose to inspect and clear the chimney.
