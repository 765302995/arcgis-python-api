 Bathroom fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 31/03/2018 - 16:05 Location: Swan Street, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton and one from Macclesfield Bathroom fire in CongletonFirefighters in Congleton and Macclesfield were called to a bathroom fire at a property on Swan Street in Congleton.  Crews used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel, one thermal imaging camera and one positive pressure ventilation fan to extinguish the fire which is believed to have been caused by an electrical dehumidifier.
