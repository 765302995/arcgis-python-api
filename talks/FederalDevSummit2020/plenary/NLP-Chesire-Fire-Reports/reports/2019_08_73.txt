 Two-car collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 20/08/2019 - 19:29 Location: Fairfield Road/Standford Drive, Northwich Attendance: One fore engine attended from Northwich Shortly before 7.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision in the Fairfield Road/Standford Drive area of Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that the collision involved two cars and that no-one was trapped inside either of them. Firefighters made the vehicles safe and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
