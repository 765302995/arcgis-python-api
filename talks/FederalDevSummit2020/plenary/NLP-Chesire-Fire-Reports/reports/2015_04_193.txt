Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 06/04/2015 - 18:39 Location: Abbots Mews, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A small fire was found in the kitchen of a property in Ellesmere Port.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a covering jet to put the fire out, followed by a high pressure fan to clear the area of smoke.  Smoke alarms were fitted in the property and everyone was accounted for.
