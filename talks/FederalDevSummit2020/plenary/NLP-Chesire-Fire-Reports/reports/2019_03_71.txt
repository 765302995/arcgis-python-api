 Small fire in a playground in Widnes Time of Incident: 15/03/2019 - 20:30 Location: Upton Rocks Park, Lanark Gardens, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 8.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a playground in Lanark Gardens, Widnes. On arrival at Upton Rocks Park the crew discovered that it was a small fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. Police also attended.
