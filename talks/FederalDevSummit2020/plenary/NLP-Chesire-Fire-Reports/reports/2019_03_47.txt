 Fire in the open in Winsford Time of Incident: 20/03/2019 - 19:16 Location: Clay Lane, Marton Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Winsford and Holmes Chapel At 7.15pm firefighters from Winsford and Holmes Chapel were dispatched to reports of a fire on Clay Lane, Marton.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a large uncontrolled fire involving timber and more than 100 tyres.   Firefighters used one 45 millimetre hose with hand controlled branch to extinguish the fire. In total it is estimated that they used 15,000 litres of water in total.    The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately three hours.
