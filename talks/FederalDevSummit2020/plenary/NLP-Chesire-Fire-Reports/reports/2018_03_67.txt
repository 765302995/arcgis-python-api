 Bin fires at a playground in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/03/2018 - 08:51 Location: Samuel Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Bin fires at a playground in WarringtonAt around 8.50am firefighters were called to a fire in a playground on Samuel Street in Warrington.  Crews discovered that the fire involved several small bins.They used water to extinguish the flames and were at the scene for around 20 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Police have been informed of the incident.
