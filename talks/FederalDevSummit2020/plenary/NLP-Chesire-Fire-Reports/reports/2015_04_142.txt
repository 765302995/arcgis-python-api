Man trapped in collision in Ashley Time of Incident: 12/04/2015 - 16:09 Location: Mobberley Road, Ashley Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Greater Manchester One man was medically trapped after a road traffic collision in Ashley.  Police, paramedics and the Air Ambulance attended the scene and the man was released by the Ambulance Service and taken to hospital. Firefighters made the scene safe.
