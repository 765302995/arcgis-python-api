 Female trapped following a one-vehicle collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/01/2019 - 18:06 Location: Chester Road, Stockton Heath, Warrington Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Lymm Shortly after 6pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision on Chester Road in Stockton Heath, Warrington. On arrival the crews discovered that the collision involved a saloon car which was on its side in a hedgerow. A female was trapped inside the car. Firefighters got the casualty out of the vehicle and handed her over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for around 25 minutes.
