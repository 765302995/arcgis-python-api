 Car in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 23/10/2018 - 16:59 Location: Welsh Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to Welsh Road at its junction with Ledsham Hall Lane, Ellesmere Port following reports of a car well alight near the traffic lights. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to tackle the fire, as well as a foam extinguisher. They then damped down the vehicle to ensure it didn't reignite.
