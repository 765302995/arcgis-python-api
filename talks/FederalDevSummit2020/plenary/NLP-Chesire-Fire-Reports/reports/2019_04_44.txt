 Commercial building fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/04/2019 - 01:13 Location: Castlefields Avenue East, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn, one from Widnes and one from Penketh Three fire engines were called to a fire in a commercial building on Castlefields Avenue East, Runcorn. The fire was in a single storey building approximately 10m by 20m. The firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets and a covering jet to extinguish the fire. The crew then checked the site using a thermal imaging camera. Crews were at the scene for two hours and 30 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please contact Cheshire Police quoting incident number IML 382004.
