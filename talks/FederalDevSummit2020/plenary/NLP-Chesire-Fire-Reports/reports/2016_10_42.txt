Bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/10/2016 - 18:38 Location: Brentnall Close, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Warrington A hose reel jet was used to put out a bin fire on Brentnall Close, Warrington.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
