 Large bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/05/2019 - 21:46 Location: Sankey Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington used one hose reel jet to extinguish an industrial size bin which is believed to have been deliberately set alight on Sankey Street.  The crew were detained at the scene for around 25 minutes.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101 quoting IML 392486, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111
