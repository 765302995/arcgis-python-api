 Garden fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 15/04/2019 - 13:23 Location: Chester Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port used two hose reel jets to extinguish a garden fence and hedge which were well alight outside an address on Chester Road.   The team then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  Firefighters were detained at the address for approximately one hour 30 minutes.
