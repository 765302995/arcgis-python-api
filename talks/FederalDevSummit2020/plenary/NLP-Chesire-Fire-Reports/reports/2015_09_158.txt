Collision on M6 Time of Incident: 02/09/2015 - 17:42 Location: M6 southbound junction 19 to 18 Attendance: The rapid response unit from Holmes Chapel and one fire engine from Knutsford, one from Greater Manchester and one from Northwich Part of the M6 was closed while a collision involving two vehicles was dealt with.  The incident happened on the southbound carriageway between junction 19 and 18. No one was trapped and the crews assisted with casualty care and clearing the roadway.  Air Ambulance also attended the scene.
