Large vehicle fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 21/07/2016 - 21:08 Location: Buxton Road, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines from Congleton Firefighters used one hose reel jet to cool a fire on the brakes of a large vehicle, which had spread to the back wheel. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any remaining heat spots of fire before making the scene safe.
