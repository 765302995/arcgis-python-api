 Food left in microwave, Wrenbury Heath Time of Incident: 21/01/2019 - 19:07 Location: Heath Lane, Wrenbury Heath Attendance:  Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters attended to reports of a fire at a property on Heath Lane, Wrenbury Heath.  The fire was out when firefighters arrived however the home was full of smoke.  Crews opened a window to disperse the smoke.  The fire is believed to have been caused by food being left in a microwave.
