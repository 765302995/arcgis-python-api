 Man rescued from a roof in Crewe Time of Incident: 08/05/2018 - 14:05 Location: Peel Square, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to rescue a man from a roof on Peel Square, Crewe. The man had been doing repairs on the roof when someone stole his ladder. Crews used a 7m ladder to get his down. If you have any information about the theft then please call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 58327, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
