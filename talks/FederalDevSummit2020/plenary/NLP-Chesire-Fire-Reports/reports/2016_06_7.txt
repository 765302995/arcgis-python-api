Loose refuse fire under Central Expressway, Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/06/2016 - 21:24 Location: Central expressway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes Firefighters were called to tackle loose refuse which was on fire under the central expressway in Halton Lodge, Runcorn. Crews used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire and used a thermal imaging camera to check the area for heat spots.
