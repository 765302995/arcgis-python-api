Cat up a tree in Chester Time of Incident: 26/06/2017 - 12:44 Location: St Werburghs and St Columbas Catholic Primary School, Lightfoot Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Cat rescued from a treeA cat which was approximately 20 foot up a tree at a school, needed the help of firefighters to get down. The RSPCA were also in attendance and the crew used a ladder to get the cat down and to safety.
