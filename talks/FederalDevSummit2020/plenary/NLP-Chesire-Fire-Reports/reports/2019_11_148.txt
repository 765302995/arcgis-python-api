 Vehicle fire in Great Sutton Time of Incident: 04/11/2019 - 00:42 Location: Capenhurst Lane, Great Sutton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port. Crews were called to a vehicle on fire in a field off Capenhurst Lane, Great Sutton. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. They then used a hose reel to damp down the car.
