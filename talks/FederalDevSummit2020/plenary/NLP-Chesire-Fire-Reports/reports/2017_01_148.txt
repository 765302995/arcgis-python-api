Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 31/12/2016 - 21:53 Location: Seacombe Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A car was reported on fire on Seacombe Drive. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to bring the fire under control. Fortunately there was no damage to nearby properties. The cause of the blaze is being investigated.
