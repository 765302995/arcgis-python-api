 Gas oven fire at a church in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 28/05/2019 - 13:18 Location: Whitby Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Shortly after 1.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Trinity Methodist Church on Whitby Road in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crews discovered a fire involving a gas oven in the kitchen. The gas supply was isolated and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. A large fan was then used to clear smoke from the building. Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
