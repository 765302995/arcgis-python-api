 Oven fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/11/2018 - 14:09 Location: Foxley Heath, Widnes Attendance: Three fire engines attended, from Widnes, Runcorn and Penketh Just before 2.10pm firefighters were called to reports of an oven fire at a detached domestic property at Foxley Heath in Widnes. On arrival crews discovered that the property was heavily smoke logged after a turned on cooker had been left unattended and that all persons were accounted for. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a large high pressure fan to clear the smoke and were in attendance for nearly an hour. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
