 Recycling bins on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 29/10/2019 - 21:22 Location: Sandy Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A fire involving two large recycling containers, which was well alight on the arrival of firefighters, were extinguished with a hose reel jet. Police were called at the fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone knows anything about this fire they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
