 Vehicle fire on A57 in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/02/2019 - 06:39 Location: A57, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Fire crews attended to a car well alight on the A57 in Woolston, Warrington.  A full road closure was put in place while firefighters, wearing one breathing apparatus, used two hose reels to extinguish the fire.
