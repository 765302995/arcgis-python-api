Road traffic collision on M6 between Crewe and Sandbach Time of Incident: 07/08/2016 - 06:23 Location: M6 northbound between junctions 16 and 17. Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe, Sandbach, Middlewich and Alsager A van collided with bridge on the M6 northbound, between Crewe and Sandbach. There was one casualty who was tended to by firefighters and ambulance staff. The crews helped police in clearing the motorway of debris and the contents of the van. All three lanes were closed while the incident was dealt with. Firefighters were at the scene for around one hour.
