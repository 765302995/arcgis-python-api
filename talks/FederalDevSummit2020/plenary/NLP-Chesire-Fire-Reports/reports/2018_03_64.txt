 Rubbish fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 22/03/2018 - 14:13 Location: Milton Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Rubbish fire in WidnesAt around 2.13pm firefighters were called to a fire in the open on Milton Road in Widnes.  On arrival the crew found a large quantity of rubbish well alight and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames.  They were at the scene for around 20 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
