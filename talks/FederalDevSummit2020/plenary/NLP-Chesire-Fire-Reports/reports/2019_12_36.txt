 Accidental bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/12/2019 - 12:53 Location: Sankey Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington used a hose reel jet to extinguish a bin fire on  Sankey Street.  The cause of the fire is believed to be accidental and the crew were detained at the scene for around 10 minutes.
