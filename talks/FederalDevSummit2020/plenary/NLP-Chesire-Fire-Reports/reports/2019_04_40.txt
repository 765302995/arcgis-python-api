 Fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/04/2019 - 19:17 Location: Radley Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 7.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Radley Lane in Warrington. The crew used water to put out the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
