Car on fire on road in Widnes Time of Incident: 20/04/2015 - 09:59 Location: Tynwald Crescent, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to a car on fire on a road in Widnes and used a hose reel to extinguish it. The fire involved the engine of the vehicle and is believed to have started by an electrical fault.
