House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 14/06/2015 - 15:09 Location: Lime Tree Avenue, Crewe. Attendance: Fire engines from Holmes Chapel, Audlem, Congleton and Middlewich. Firefighters responded to a report of a house fire on Lime Tree Avenue, Crewe.  When they arrived they were confronted with a significant blaze. They used main and hose reel water jets to bring the blaze under control. Special fans were also used to clear smoke from the property.  There were five people in the house at the time of the fire. One teenage boy had suffered serious burns in the incident. He was taken to hospital by air ambulance.  An investigation to find the cause of the blaze has now been launched.
