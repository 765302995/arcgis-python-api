 Fire in the open in Northwich Time of Incident: 12/05/2019 - 17:00 Location: School Lane, Moulton, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At 5pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on School Lane in Moulton, Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved wood and grass. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
