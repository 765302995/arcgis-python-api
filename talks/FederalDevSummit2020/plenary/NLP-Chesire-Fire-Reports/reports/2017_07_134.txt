Van fire on M56/M53 Time of Incident: 05/07/2017 - 15:12 Location: Slip road on to M53 to M56, junction 15 Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Crewe attended the incident. Firefighters were called to a van fire on the slip road on junction 15 between the M56 and M53. Crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, tackled the fire, which was well alight on arrival with two hose reel jets.
