Chester river rescue Time of Incident: 25/05/2016 - 14:43 Location: Grosvenor Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine and the aerial appliance from Chester, and two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters have been assisting police and ambulance personnel to recover a casualty from the river. The Grosvenor Bridge was closed while the emergency services were dealing with the incident. The fire and rescue crews left the scene just before 3.30pm.
