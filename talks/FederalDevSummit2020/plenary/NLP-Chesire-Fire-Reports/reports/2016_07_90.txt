One car involved in road traffic collision in Congleton Time of Incident: 15/07/2016 - 11:44 Location: Holmes Chapel Road, Somerford, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and one from Middlewich One vehicle was involved in a road traffic collision on Holmes Chapel Road in Somerford, Congleton. Firefighters handed one casualty over to the care of paramedics and proceeded to make the scene safe.
