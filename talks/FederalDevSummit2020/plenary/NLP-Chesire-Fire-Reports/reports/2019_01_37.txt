 Wheelie bin fire, Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/01/2019 - 17:26 Location: Windmill Hill Avenue North, Runcorn Attendance:  One fire engine from Runcorn Crews responded to reports of a wheelie bin on fire in the subway. They used a water backpack to extinguish the flames and then contacted colleagues at the police due to the suspected deliberate ignition.  Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire police on 101, quoting IML 286474.
