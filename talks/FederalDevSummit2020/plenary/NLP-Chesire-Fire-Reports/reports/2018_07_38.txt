 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/07/2018 - 20:06 Location: Clegge Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 8.05pm firefighters were called to a report of a wheelie bin being on fire by commercial premises on Clegge Street in Warrington. The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters then checked that the fire had not spread to the commercial premises. A thermal imagining camera confirmed that there were no hotspots at the building. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour. For wheelie bin fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
