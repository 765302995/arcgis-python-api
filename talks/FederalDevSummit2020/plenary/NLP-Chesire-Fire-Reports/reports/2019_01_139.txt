 Small bonfire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 13/01/2019 - 13:33 Location: Fernwood, Runcorn Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly after 1.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in Fernwood, Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small bonfire. Firefighters extinguished the fire using water and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
