 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/03/2018 - 18:55 Location: Grace Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Penketh At 6.55pm firefighters were called to a kitchen fire involving a cooker at a domestic building in Grace Avenue, Orford, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet and two breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames. They were at the scene for just under half-an-hour. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
