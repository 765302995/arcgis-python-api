Person trapped in road traffic collision in Tarporley Time of Incident: 02/04/2015 - 16:28 Location: Royal Lane, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford, one from Northwich and one from Chester attended Firefighters were called to assist a man who was trapped in a road traffic collision in Tarporley. Two cars were involved in the incident and crews removed the roof of one of the cars to get the man out. Paramedics and the Air Ambulance attended.
