Chimney fire in Mow Cop Time of Incident: 16/01/2016 - 20:11 Location: West Street, Mow Cop. Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on West Street, Mow Cop.  When they arrived crews found that there was a chimney fire. Crews put the fire out and checked to make sure it had not spread to other areas of the house. They also carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving.
