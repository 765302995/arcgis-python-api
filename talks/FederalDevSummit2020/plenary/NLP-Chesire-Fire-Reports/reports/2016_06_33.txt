Gazebo fire in garden of Warrington house Time of Incident: 24/06/2016 - 02:23 Location: Calgarth Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Deliberate ignition is suspected as the cause of a gazebo fire in the front garden of a Warrington house. Firefighters put out the fire and urged anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
