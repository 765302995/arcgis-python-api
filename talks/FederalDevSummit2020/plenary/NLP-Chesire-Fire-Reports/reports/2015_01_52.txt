Explosion at house in Elton Time of Incident: 18/01/2015 - 06:06 Location: School Lane, Elton, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A gas canister exploded at a house in Elton forcing the front windows of the property out. The occupier's and neighbours were evacuated and a small fire located in the front room. On investigation the cause of the explosion and fire was a propane gas canister which had been near an open fire place.
