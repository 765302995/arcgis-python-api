Chimney fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 01/04/2015 - 21:41 Location: Ledsham Hall Lane, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A chimney fire was reported in Ellesmere Port.  Firefighters arrived to find no obvious spread of fire from the chimney and carried out a full investigation using a thermal imaging camera to check there were no hot spots of fire.
