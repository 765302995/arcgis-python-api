 Electric cooker fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/02/2019 - 15:24 Location: Fernhurst, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn. One fire engine from Widnes. Firefighters were called to a fire involving an electric cooker at an address in Fernhurst, Runcorn. Crews used a hose reel and two breathing apparatus. Once the fire was out, they used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
