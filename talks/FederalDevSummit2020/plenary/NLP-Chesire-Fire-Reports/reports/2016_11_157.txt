House fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 05/11/2016 - 06:28 Location: Wingfield Place, Winsford. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Winsford and Northwich Firefighters responded to a report of a house fire on Wingfield Place, Winsford.  Fire crews wearing breathing apparatus tackled the blaze with hose reel jets and large fans. They also carried out a full search of the building to make sure that there was nobody inside. The property was empty.  A full fire investigation has now begun to fine the possible cause of the blaze.
