Unattended burning in Sandbach Time of Incident: 21/03/2015 - 14:47 Location: Station Road/Hill Street, Elworth,Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine Firefighters used one hose reel, a stack drag and a short extension ladder to extinguish a large amount of paper and card on fire in Sandbach. The contents were inside a metal container on a builder's yard and it was put out by the crew.
