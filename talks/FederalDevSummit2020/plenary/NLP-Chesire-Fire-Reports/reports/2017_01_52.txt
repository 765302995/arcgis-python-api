Lorry fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 17/01/2017 - 08:28 Location: A50/A534 Arclid, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines from Holmes Chapel and Sandbach Firefighters used one hose reel jet to cool an articulated lorry after the axle was on fire. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for any remaining hot spots of fire on the vehicle and in the trailer, in which a load of boxed furniture was being carried. However, the fire had not spread this far and the lorry was declared safe.
