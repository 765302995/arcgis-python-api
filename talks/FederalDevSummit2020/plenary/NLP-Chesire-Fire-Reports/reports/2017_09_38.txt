Kitchen fire in Swettenham Time of Incident: 20/09/2017 - 16:12 Location: Trap Road, Swettenham Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and one from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to a kitchen fire on Trap Road, Swettenham. On arrival crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, found a tablecloth on fire which had been left on an aga. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
