Fire in loft space in Woolstanwood, Crewe Time of Incident: 06/06/2017 - 08:45 Location: Lodgefields Drive, Woolstanwood, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe A fire in a loft which was out on the arrival of firefighters needed inspecting with a thermal imaging camera. One man suffered from smoke inhalation and was given oxygen by the crew. Paramedics also attended.
