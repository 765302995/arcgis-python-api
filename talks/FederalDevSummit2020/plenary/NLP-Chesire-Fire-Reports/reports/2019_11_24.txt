 Wheelie bins on fire in a park in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/11/2019 - 03:51 Location: Thorntondale Drive, Whittle Hall, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 3.50am firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a park in Thorntondale Drive, Whittle Hall, Great Sankey, Warrington.  On arrival the crew found multiple wheelie bins alight.  Firefighters extinguished the large fire with an extended hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.  For advice on how to prevent wheelie bin fires visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
