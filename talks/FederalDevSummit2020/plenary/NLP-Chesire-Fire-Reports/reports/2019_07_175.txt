 Outbuilding fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 03/07/2019 - 08:49 Location: Park Road, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe and one from Nantwich Fire crews were called to a fire in an outbuilding on Park Road, Nantwich. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They then cleared smoke from the building using a large fan and checked it for hotspots using a thermal imaging camera. Crews were at the scene for half an hour.
