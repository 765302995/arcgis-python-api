Disused caravan fire in Chester Time of Incident: 01/09/2017 - 08:49 Location: Chester Road, Bridge Trafford, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters tackled a fire involving a disused caravan on Chester Road in Bridge Trafford. A hose reel jet was used bring the blaze under control and the crews removed items from inside the caravan to so they could check for hot spots with a thermal imaging camera. They were at the scene for around forty minutes.
