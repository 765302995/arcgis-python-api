Tractor fire in Chester Time of Incident: 24/10/2015 - 21:58 Location: Coalpit Lane, Chester. Attendance: Fire engines from Chester Firefighters were called to deal with a fire involving a tractor on Coalpit Lane, Chester.   Crews used two hose reel jets to tackle the fire.   It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
