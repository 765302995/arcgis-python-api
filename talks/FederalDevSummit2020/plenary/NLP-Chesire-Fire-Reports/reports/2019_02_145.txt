 Bin fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 31/01/2019 - 22:37 Location: West Street, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe used a hose reel yet to extinguish a bin fire at the rear of an address on West Street, Crewe.  The cause of the fire is believed to be accidental.  Firefighters were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes.
