Collision in Frodsham Time of Incident: 22/12/2015 - 17:27 Location: Chester Road in Frodsham. Attendance: One fire engine from Frodsham and one from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Chester Road in Frodham.  When they arrived crews found that there was nobody trapped. However, one man required first aid. Crews treated the man before passing him to the care of the ambulance service.
