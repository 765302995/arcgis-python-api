 Industrial building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/09/2018 - 07:34 Location: Baronet Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 7.35am firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from machinery at the Perstorp UK Ltd chemical company on Baronet Road, Warrington. The fire that is believed to have been caused by friction from a jammed conveyor belt in a sacking machine was out on arrival. The machine was smouldering when the crew arrived. Firefighters isolated the electricity supply to the conveyor belt and made sure that the machine was safe. They were in attendance for around 25 minutes.
