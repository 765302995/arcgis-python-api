 Fire in the open in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 23/05/2018 - 19:44 Location: Shellway Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port were called to reports of a fire involving a small amount of rubbish on Shellway Road. Crews attended the scene and found that the fire had been extinguished by personnel at the site. The team assisted with damping down the scene and were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
