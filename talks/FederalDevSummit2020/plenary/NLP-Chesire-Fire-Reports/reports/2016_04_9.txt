Bushes on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 27/04/2016 - 16:39 Location: Whitby Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving bushes close to Ellesmere Port Hospital. A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire and nearby properties were checked with a thermal imaging camera to make sure there was no heat spread.
