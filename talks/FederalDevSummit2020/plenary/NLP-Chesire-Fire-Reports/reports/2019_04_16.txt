 Smell of smoke at Nantwich property Time of Incident: 27/04/2019 - 13:35 Location: Cowfields, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters attended to reports of the smell of smoke coming from a communal staircase at a property on Cowfields in Nantwich.  On arrival, crews investigated by using the thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots.  They established there was no fire at the property and it was deemed safe.
