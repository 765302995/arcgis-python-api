 Tractor fire in Baddington Time of Incident: 30/05/2018 - 10:00 Location: Coole Lane, Baddington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Nantwich and one from Crewe. At 10am firefighters from Crewe and Nantwich were called to reports of a vehicle fire on Coole Lane, Baddington. Crews attended the scene and found a tractor well alight. The team used two hose reel jets and two breathing apparatus to tackle the fire. Crews also used specialist mats to stop leaking oil from entering nearby water courses. The team were in attendance for approximately one hour.
