Forest fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 03/06/2017 - 17:19 Location: Willington Road, Delamere in Northwich. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Winsford, Northwich and Chester Fire crews were called to a report of a forest fire off Willington Road, Delamere in Northwich.  When they arrived crews found a smouldering fire covering an area of about 20msq.  Firefighters used water backpacks and beaters to put the fire out.
