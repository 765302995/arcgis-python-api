 Domestic building fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 23/09/2018 - 16:52 Location: St Marys Road, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Nantwich and one from Crewe At around 4.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a two-storey domestic property on St Marys Road in Nantwich. On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved a log burner on the ground floor. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. They then used a thermal imaging camera and a ladder to check for hotspots to make sure that the property was safe. Firefighters checked the loft space. There was no smoke logging in the property and firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
