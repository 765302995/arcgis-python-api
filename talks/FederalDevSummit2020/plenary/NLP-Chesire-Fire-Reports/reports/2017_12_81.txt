Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/12/2017 - 09:38 Location: Wilkinson Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington. One fire engine from Birchwood. Firefighters attended a wheelie bin which was well alight in Wilkinson Street in Orford. Fire crews used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which was close to a house.
