Car fire on M6 Time of Incident: 21/01/2015 - 02:35 Location: J18 M6 North Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel,one from Middlewich and one from Knutsford A car was well alight on the hardshoulder of the M6 on arrival of the crews. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish the blaze and the hardshoulder and lane one of the carriageway was fended off by the Service, Police and Highways.
