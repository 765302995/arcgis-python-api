 Garage fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 10/10/2019 - 12:18 Location: Long Lane, Orford, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. One fire engine attended from Penketh. Firefighters were called to a fire involving a garage on land at a school in Long Lane, Warrington.  Crews used on hose reel and two breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames. They then checked two adjoining garages to check if the fire had spread but  they were unaffected.  Crews then damped down the affected area and emptied the contents of the garage. Fire investigators will be investigating the circumstances.
