 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 15/04/2019 - 20:38 Location: Lanark Gardens, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 8.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Lanark Gardens in Widnes.  On arrival the crew discovered a wheelie bin which was well alight.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For advice on preventing your wheelie bins from being targeted by arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
