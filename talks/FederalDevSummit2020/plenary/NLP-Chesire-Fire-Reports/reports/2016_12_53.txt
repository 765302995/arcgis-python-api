Road traffic collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 22/12/2016 - 08:36 Location: Chester Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving one saloon car which had collided with a wall and a tree. One male casualty was able to get out of the car and was handed into the care of paramedics. Firefighters proceeded to make the scene safe.
