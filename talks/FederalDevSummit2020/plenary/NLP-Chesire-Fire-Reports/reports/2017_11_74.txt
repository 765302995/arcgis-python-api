Fire involving a patio heater in Welsh Row, Nantwich Time of Incident: 10/11/2017 - 19:30 Location: Welsh Row, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich Firefighters dealt with a small fire involving a patio heater in the courtyard of a licensed premises. The plastic shrouding around the heater had caught alight but was out on arrival. Crews remained to cool the cylinder and then removed it to a safe place.
