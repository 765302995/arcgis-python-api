 Car fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 18/09/2019 - 21:07 Location: Sherbourne Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a car on fire in Sherbourne Road in Macclesfield. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information should contact police on 101 quoting IML 516751.Alternatively information can be given anonymously by contacting Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
