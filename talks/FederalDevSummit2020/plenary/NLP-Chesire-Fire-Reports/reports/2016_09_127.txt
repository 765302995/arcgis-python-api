Fire in Warrington flat Time of Incident: 05/09/2016 - 06:06 Location: Greenings Court, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington, two from Stockton Heath and one from Widnes An investigation into the cause of a fire in a Greenings Court flat. The affected flat was on the second floor of a four-storey block. The building was evacuated as crews tackled the blaze.  Firefighters, six wearing breathing apparatus, brought the fire under control using hose reel jets. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke and adjoining flats were checked for signs of fire spread. The cause of the fire is now the subject of a joint police and fire investigation.  Everyone from the building was accounted for. Firefighters are still at the scene.
