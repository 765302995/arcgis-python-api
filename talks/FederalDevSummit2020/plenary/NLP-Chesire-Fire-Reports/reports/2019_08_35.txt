 Car on fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 25/08/2019 - 11:05 Location: M6 southbound, between junctions 19 and 18 Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Lymm, one from Holmes Chapel and one from Trafford Shortly after 11am firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on the M6 southbound, between junctions 19 and 18. On arrival the crews found the vehicle well alight on the hard shoulder. The southbound motorway was temporarily closed by the police whilst firefighters dealt with the incident. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The car was then damped down to make it safe and able to be moved off the motorway by a recovery vehicle. Firefighters were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.
