 Cardboard compactor on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/05/2018 - 09:55 Location: Cockhedge Way, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood, one from Lymm and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a fire in a cardboard compactor in a commercial building on Cockhedge Way, Warrington. Crews used hydraulic cutting equipment and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
