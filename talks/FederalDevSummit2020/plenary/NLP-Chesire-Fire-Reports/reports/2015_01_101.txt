Flat fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 02/01/2015 - 23:25 Location: Alderley Walk, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a flat fire on Alderley Walk in Macclesfield.  Crews wearing breathing apparatus turned the gas and electricity off before fighting the fire with a hose reel jet.  A special fan was also used to clear smoke from the property.  One woman who was living in the flat was taken to hospital after the incident.  The fire is believed to have been started accidentally by discarded smoking materials.
