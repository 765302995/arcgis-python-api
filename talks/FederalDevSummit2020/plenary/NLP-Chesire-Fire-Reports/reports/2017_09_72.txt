Road traffic collision in south Widnes Time of Incident: 10/09/2017 - 13:24 Location: Moor Lane, south Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A road traffic collision on Moor Lane left one woman medically trapped in one of the three cars involved. Paramedics gave oxygen therapy to the casualty while emergency services waited for an ambulance. Firefighters then helped the casualty from the car to the ambulance and made the scene safe.
