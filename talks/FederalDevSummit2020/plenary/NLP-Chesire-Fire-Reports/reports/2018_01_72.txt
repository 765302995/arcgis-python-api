 Car fire, Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 12/01/2018 - 20:16 Location: Rivacre Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One appliance from Ellesmere Port A car is thought to have been deliberately ignited outside an address in Rivacre Road. The fire as in the engine compartment, and crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames. Police attended the scene, and anyone with information is asked to call 101 quoting incident 829 of 12 January 2018.
