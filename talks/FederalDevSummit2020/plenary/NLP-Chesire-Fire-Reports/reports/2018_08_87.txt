 Fire on field in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/08/2018 - 09:49 Location: Manchester Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a fire on a field off Manchester Road in Warrington. The fire which was 10 metres by 10 metres was issuing smoke across Manchester Road, so crews used a main jet to extinguish the fire.
