 Wheelie bin fire that spread to a fence in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 11/09/2019 - 03:02 Location: Caernarvon Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 3am firefighters were called to reports of a wheelie bin being on fire in Caernarvon Court, Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire had spread to a fence. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for more than 20 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For advice on preventing your wheelie bins from being targeted by arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
