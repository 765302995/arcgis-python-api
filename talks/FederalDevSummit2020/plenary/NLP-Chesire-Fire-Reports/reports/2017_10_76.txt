Bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 15/10/2017 - 01:56 Location: Ways Green, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford Crews were called to reports of a fire at the back of a building on Ways Green, Winsford. Firefighters attended and discovered bins on fire outside the premises. Two hose reel jets were used to extinguish the fire and the building was inspected to determine if further damage had been caused.
