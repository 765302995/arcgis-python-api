Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 09/02/2017 - 20:09 Location: Car park between Tipkinder Park and Queen's Park Drive, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters believe a fire was started deliberately in a car on a car park near to Queen's Park Drive. The crew used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting police log 737 of 9th February 2017.
