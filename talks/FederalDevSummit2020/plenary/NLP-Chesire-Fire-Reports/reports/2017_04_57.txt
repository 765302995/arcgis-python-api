Computer fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 20/04/2017 - 16:38 Location: Victoria Road, Northwich. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Northwich Firefighters were called to as report of a fire on Victoria Road, Northwich.  When they arrived they found that there had been a fire involving a computer. The fire had gone out but a person in the flat had inhaled smoke and so required medical attention.
