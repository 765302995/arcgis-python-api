 Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 18:13 Location: Runcorn Spur Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 6.10pm firefighters were called to reports of grass being on fire on a footpath leading to the Shopping City in Runcorn. On arrival at Runcorn Spur Road in Halton Lodge the crew discovered that the fire involved approximately two metres by eight metres of grass. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which is believed to have been started by the sun's rays on what was a hot day. The crew were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
