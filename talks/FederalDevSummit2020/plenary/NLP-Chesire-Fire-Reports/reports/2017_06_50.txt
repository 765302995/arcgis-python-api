Small rubbish fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/06/2017 - 01:45 Location: Alnwick Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a small rubbish fire on Alnwick Drive, Ellesmere Port. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire which was well alight on arrival.
