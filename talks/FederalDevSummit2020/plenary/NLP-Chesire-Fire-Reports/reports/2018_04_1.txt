House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/04/2018 - 06:11 Location: Warrington Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington ad two from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a fire in a house on Warrington Road, Warrington. On arrival crews found the fire was in the roof space of the property and crews used a hose reel jet and a dry powder extinguisher to put out the fire.
