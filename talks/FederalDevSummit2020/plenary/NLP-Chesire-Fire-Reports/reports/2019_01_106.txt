 House fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 21/01/2019 - 09:02 Location: Peover Lane, Congleton Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Congleton and one from Macclesfield Firefighters attended to a fire at a property on Peover Lane in Congleton.  Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, used dry powder to extinguish an oil rang cooker which was alight.  A positive pressure ventilation fan was then used to disperse the smoke.  Crews were in attendance for just under one and a half hours.
