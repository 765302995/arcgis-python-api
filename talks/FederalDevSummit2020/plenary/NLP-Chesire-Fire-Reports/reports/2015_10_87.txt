Fire at Bridgewater High School, Warrington Time of Incident: 16/10/2015 - 16:15 Location: Broomfields Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington were called to a fire in a store room at Bridgewater High School, Warrington.  Fire crews used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which was in a store room. A high pressure fan was then used to ventilate the room.
