 Bin fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 29/03/2018 - 00:31 Location: Beeston Street, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At 0.29am firefighters were called to a fire in the open at Beeston Street in Northwich. On arrival the crew found a bin on fire and used a hose reel jet to put it out. Firefighters were at the scene for less than 10 minutes.
