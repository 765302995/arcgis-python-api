House fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 24/10/2015 - 09:08 Location: Alan Street, Northwich. Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich and Knutsford Firefighers were called to a report of a house fire on Alan Street, Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire in the kitchen. Firefighters used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel jet to put the fire out.  To avoid a fire like this please follow Kitchen fire safety
