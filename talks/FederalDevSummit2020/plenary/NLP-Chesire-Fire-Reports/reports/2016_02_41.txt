Macclesfield road traffic collision Time of Incident: 20/02/2016 - 17:41 Location: Wilmslow Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Wilmslow One car was involved in a collision with a telegraph pole. A casualty was tended to by firefighters who made the scene safe and fended off traffic until the police and ambulance service arrived.
