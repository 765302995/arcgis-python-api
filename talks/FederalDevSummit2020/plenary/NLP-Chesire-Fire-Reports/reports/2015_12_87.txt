Electrical fire in a property in Congleton Time of Incident: 09/12/2015 - 06:18 Location: Newcastle Road, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel and Congleton A fire in an electrical consumer unit in a property in Nantwich required firefighters to attend. The crews used a hose reel and a dry powder extinguisher to put it out along with a covering water jet. A large fan was then used to remove smoke from the property.
