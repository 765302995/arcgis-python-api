 Motorbike fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 17/04/2018 - 20:56 Location: Kingshead Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At 8.55pm firefighters were called to a reports of a motorbike being on fire in Kingshead Close in Runcorn. On arrival the crew found the motorbike well alight and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. The motorbike was completely destroyed by the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call police on 101.
