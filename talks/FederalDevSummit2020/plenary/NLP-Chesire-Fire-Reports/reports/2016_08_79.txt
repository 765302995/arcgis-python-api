Straw on fire in a field in Sandbach Time of Incident: 17/08/2016 - 13:19 Location: Newcastle Road, Betchton, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach one from Alsager, and two from Crewe Cropped straw which was on fire in a field required firefighters to use water from hose reels to extinguish it. The area measured approximately 4,000 metres square and a fire break in the field was created to help prevent the fire from spreading.
