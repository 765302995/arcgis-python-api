 Collision on A50 at Cranage Time of Incident: 22/09/2018 - 20:52 Location: Knutsford Road, Cranage. cw48hj Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich and a rapid response rescue unit from Holmes Chapel attended Shortly after 8.50pm firefighters were called to a collision involving a car and a tractor on the A50 at Cranage. One person was trapped in the car and was extricated by fire crews in attendance.
