 Garden fire in Stockton Heath Time of Incident: 28/05/2019 - 21:24 Location: Ackers Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath At around 9.30pm firefighters from Stockton Heath were called to reports of a garden fire on Ackers Road.  On arrival crews discovered a bush, approximately 2 metres by 2 metres, was well alight.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for around 15 minutes.
