 Bonfire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 28/09/2018 - 21:46 Location: Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a large bonfire on Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port. Crews used a hose reel jet and a stack drag to put out the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with information is asked to contact Cheshire Police.
