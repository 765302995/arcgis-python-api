 Small farm fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/04/2018 - 17:30 Location: Shorwell Close, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Small fire in WarringtonFirefighters from Penketh were called to reports of a fire in a field near Shorwell Close, Warrington. On arrival crews found that the fire was approximately 10m x 10m and had been started by a farmer who was burning a quantity of logs, wood and hay.  After consulting with the farmer a decision was taken to extinguish the fire as the smoke was affecting a nearby road.The team were detained at the scene for approximately two hours.
