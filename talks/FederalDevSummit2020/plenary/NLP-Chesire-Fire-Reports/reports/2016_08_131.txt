Smell of burning in basement in Winsford Time of Incident: 09/08/2016 - 16:45 Location: The Verdin Exchange, High Street, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford Firefighters were called to a smell of burning in a basement in Winsford. The commercial property was evacuated and the smell is believed to have  come from a light fitting. A thermal imaging camera was used to check the area.
