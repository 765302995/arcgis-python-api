Road traffic collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 28/11/2016 - 15:33 Location: A556, Rudheath, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford Three vehicles were involved in a collision on the A556 at Rudheath. Nobody was trapped in any of the vehicles but there were two casualties needing medical treatment from the ambulance service. Firefighters closed the road just past Robert's bakery whilst the incident was dealt with, before making the scene safe.
