 Motorbike on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/03/2018 - 16:05 Location: Brindley Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a motorbike well alight in a subway in Brindley Road in Runcorn. Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the flames and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. Police have been notified of the incident.
