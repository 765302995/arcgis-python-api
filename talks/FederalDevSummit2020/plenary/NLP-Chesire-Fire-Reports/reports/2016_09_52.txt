Cooking fire in Bollington Time of Incident: 20/09/2016 - 20:24 Location: Church Street in Bollington. Attendance: A fire engine from Bollington Firefighters responded to an alarm sounding at a property on Church Street in Bollington.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire involving some food. This was out but there was a lot of smoke in the property.  Crews used a special fan to clear the smoke.
