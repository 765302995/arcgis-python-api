Dumped cannabis plants well alight in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/02/2017 - 05:48 Location: Sutton Weaver, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Frodsham A number of cannabis plants (5 metres by 5 metres) had been dumped and set on fire under a swing bridge in Sutton Weaver, Runcorn.  The cannabis plants were well alight.  Firefighters extinguished the fire and then handed the incident over to Cheshire Police.
