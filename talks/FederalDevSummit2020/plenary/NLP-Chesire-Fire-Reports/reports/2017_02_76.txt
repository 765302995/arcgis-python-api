Camper van fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 18/02/2017 - 17:01 Location: West Road, Congleton. Attendance: Fire engine from Congleton Firefighters responded to a report of a fire involving a camper van on West Road, Congleton.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving a gas cylinder. Hose reel jets were used to put the fire out.
