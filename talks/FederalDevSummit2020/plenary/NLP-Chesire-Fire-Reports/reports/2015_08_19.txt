Shed fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/08/2015 - 22:52 Location: Locker Avenue, Orford, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Stockton Heath and two fire engines from Warrington   Fire fighters have attended a shed on Locker Avenue in Orford, Warrington, which also caused damage to the upper and lower floors of a building. Crews used two breathing apparatus and two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire and were at the scene for seventy minutes.
