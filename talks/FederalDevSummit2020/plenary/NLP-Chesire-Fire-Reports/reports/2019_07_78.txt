 Car on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 16/07/2019 - 16:29 Location: Church Lane, Backford, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane At around 4.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on Church Lane in Backford, Chester. On arrival the crew found a saloon car well alight. Part of Church Lane was cordoned off as the emergency services dealt with the incident. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes. Police also attended.
