 Man trapped in Chester Time of Incident: 07/02/2018 - 06:35 Location: Station Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester, one from Powey Lane and the major rescue unit from Winsford Firefighters were called to Chester Railway Station after reports that someone was trapped underneath a train. Crews helped to rescue a male casualty before he was handed into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for 30 minutes.
