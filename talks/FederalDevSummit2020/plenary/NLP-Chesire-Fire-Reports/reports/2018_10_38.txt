 Bungalow fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/10/2018 - 23:06 Location: Bardsley Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a fire in the front room of a bungalow on Bardsley Avenue, Warrington. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a large fan to extinguish the fire. One casualty who was suffering from smoke inhalation was taken to hospital by paramedics. Crews were at the scene for one hour and 20 minutes.
