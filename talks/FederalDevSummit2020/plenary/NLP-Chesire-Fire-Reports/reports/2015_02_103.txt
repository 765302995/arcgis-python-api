Chimney on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 04/02/2015 - 22:25 Location: Newlands Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a chimney on fire in Macclesfield and used a roof ladder to get onto the roof.
