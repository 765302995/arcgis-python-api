Small fire in Handforth Time of Incident: 04/10/2016 - 08:03 Location: Caldy Road, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to an electrical fire in a home in Handforth. After cutting away the floor and chiselling some concrete the crew found an exposed electrical cable, which they believe to have been the source of the fire. The crew made the scene safe and left the occupier some advice.
