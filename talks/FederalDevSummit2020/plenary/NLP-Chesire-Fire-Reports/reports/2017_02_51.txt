Fire in empty Chester shop Time of Incident: 22/02/2017 - 00:44 Location: Bridge Street, Chester Attendance: Fire engines from Chester, Ellesmere Port, Powey Lane, Tarporley along with the aerial platform from Chester and the high volume pump from Congleton A fire is believed to have been started deliberately in an empty shop on Bridge Street. The blaze involved a disposable barbecue and started on the first floor of the property. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and a high volume fan to clear the smoke. Crews were at the scene for around two hours.
