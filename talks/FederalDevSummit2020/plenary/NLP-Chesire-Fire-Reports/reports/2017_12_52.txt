Two vehicle collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 14/12/2017 - 19:05 Location: M56 Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn, one from Stockton Heath and one from Warrington. Firefighters attended reports of a collision on the M56 westbound carriageway between junctions 9 and 10. Crews arrived at the scene and found that the collision involved a HGV and a saloon car. There were no reports of any injuries and firefighters assisted by making the vehicle safe.
