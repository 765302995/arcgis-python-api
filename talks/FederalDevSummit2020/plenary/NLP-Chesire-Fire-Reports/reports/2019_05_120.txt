 Vehicle fires in Chester Time of Incident: 14/05/2019 - 22:49 Location: Plemstall Lane, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester. Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere port. Firefighters attended a fire involving seven vehicles on land off Plumstall Lane in Mickle Trafford, Chester. The fire involved six caravans and one van was extinguished using one ground monitor and two hose reel jets. Crews were in attendance for approximately one hour and 30 minutes.
