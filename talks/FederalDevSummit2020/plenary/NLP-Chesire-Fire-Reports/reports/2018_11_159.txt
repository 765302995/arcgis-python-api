 Garden Fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/11/2018 - 17:33 Location: Kingsway South, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters from Penketh used a hose reel jet to extinguish a garden fire on Kingwasy South. While the fire was attended, crews extinguished the fire as there was a risk that it may spread to a nearby property.
