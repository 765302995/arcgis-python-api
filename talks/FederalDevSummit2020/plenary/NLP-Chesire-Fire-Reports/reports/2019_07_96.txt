 Petrol spillage in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/07/2019 - 05:33 Location: Skyline Drive, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to a petrol spillage on Skyline Drive, Warrington. A fuel tank had spilt in a car and was leaking petrol. Crews used absorbent material to mop up the fuel and sealed the leak using dammit paste.
