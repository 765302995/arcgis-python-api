 Industrial tumble dryer fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 01/02/2018 - 14:47 Location: Peach Lane, Wistaston Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to a fire in a hotel on Peach Lane, Wistaston. On arrival crews found a fire in an industrial tumble dryer. The crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet and a large fan to tackle the fire. Crews left the scene after 45 minutes.
