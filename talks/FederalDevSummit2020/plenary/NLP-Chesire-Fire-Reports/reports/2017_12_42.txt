Collision in Adderley Time of Incident: 16/12/2017 - 09:20 Location: Green Lane, Adderley Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe. One fire engine from Audlem Firefighters attended a collision involving a van which was on its side in Green Lane, Adderley. Crews freed the casualty through the windscreen and handed her over to the care of paramedics.
