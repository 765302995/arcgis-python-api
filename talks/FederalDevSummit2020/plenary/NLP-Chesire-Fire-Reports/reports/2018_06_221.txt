 Kitchen fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 06/06/2018 - 19:17 Location: Randle Bennett Close, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach, Crewe and Middlewich Firefighters attended to a kitchen fire at an address on Randle Bennett Close, Sandbach.  Crews used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel as well as a positive pressure ventilation fan to put out the fire involving a bag of rubbish.  All people inside the property were accounted for.
