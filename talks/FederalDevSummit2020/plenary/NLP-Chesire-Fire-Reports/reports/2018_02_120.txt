 Woman trapped in car following collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/02/2018 - 12:25 Location: Winwick Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters removed a trapped woman from a car following a collision at Winwick Road, Warrington. The woman was handed over to the care of North West Ambulance Service along with a second casualty. Fire crews were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes.
