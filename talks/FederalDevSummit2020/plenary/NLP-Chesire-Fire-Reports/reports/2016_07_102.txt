Road traffic collision in Delamere Time of Incident: 13/07/2016 - 19:34 Location: Abbey Lane, Delamere, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Tarporley Two cars were involved in a road traffic collision which left two casualties trapped in the vehicles.  Firefighters released both casualties and handed them into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for one hour and fifteen minutes.
