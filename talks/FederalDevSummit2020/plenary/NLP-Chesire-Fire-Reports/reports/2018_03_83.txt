 Fire alarm in Mobberley Time of Incident: 18/03/2018 - 05:33 Location: Edenfield Road, Mobberley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford Firefighters responded to a fire alarm at a residential home in Mobberley. Staff had evacuated residents from the building while firefighters checked the area for fire, including checking the loft space with a thermal imaging camera and isolating the electrics. A blown light bulb was found to have set off the alarm.
