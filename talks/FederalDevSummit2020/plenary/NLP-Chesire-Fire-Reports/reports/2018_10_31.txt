 Chimney fire in Faddiley Time of Incident: 26/10/2018 - 21:10 Location: Willbank Lane, Faddiley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters attended to a chimney fire at a house on Willbank Lane in Faddiley.  Crews assessed the fire using a thermal imaging camera before using a seven metre ladder, a roof ladder and height safety equipment to reach the chimney. The fire was then extinguished using a hose reel jet.  Firefighters were at the scene for two hours.  Have you viewed our tips to help avoid a chimney fire?
