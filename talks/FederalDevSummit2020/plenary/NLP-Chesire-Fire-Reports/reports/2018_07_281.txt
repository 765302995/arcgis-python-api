 Roof felt fire at an Italian restaurant in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 13:43 Location: Wilmslow Road, Alderley Edge Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Wilmslow and one from Macclesfield At around 1.40pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from an extractor fan and an alarm sounding at the Piccolino Italian restaurant on Wilmslow Road in Alderley Edge. On arrival crews discovered a fire involving roof felt and that no smoke was entering the premises. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
