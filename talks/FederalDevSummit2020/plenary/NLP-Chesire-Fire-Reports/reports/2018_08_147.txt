 Woodland fire in Neston Time of Incident: 05/08/2018 - 16:54 Location: Haddon Lane, Neston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane At around 4.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a woodland fire on Haddon Lane in Neston. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire was deep seated and under growth. They extinguished the smouldering fire using a hose reel and a pump. Firefighters, who pumped water from a nearby water source, then discovered other seats of fire. They put out all the seats of fire and damped down the area to make sure it was safe before leaving the scene. Firefighters were in attendance for more than three-and-a-half hours.
