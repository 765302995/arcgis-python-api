Kitchen fire in Bollington Time of Incident: 09/08/2015 - 12:03 Location: High Street, Bollington Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington and one from Macclesfield A kitchen fire in an end terraced cottage was tackled by firefighters. The crew, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire followed by a high pressure fan to clear the property of smoke.  The fire involved a toaster located in a kitchen unit and the owner of the property was alerted to the fire by his dog.  To avoid kitchen fires please follow our safety advice - Kitchen fire safety
