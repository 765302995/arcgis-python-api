 Skip fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 15/05/2019 - 19:14 Location: Ince Marshes, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a skip fire near Ince marshes, Ellesmere Port. Crews used cutters to remove the padlock so they could gain access to the skip. They then used a hose reel to extinguish the fire.
