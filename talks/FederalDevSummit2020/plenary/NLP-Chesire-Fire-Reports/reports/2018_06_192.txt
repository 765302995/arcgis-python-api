 Fire in disused social club in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/06/2018 - 19:36 Location: Bank Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Firefighters attended at Bank Street, Glazebrook, Warrington, after a report of a fire involving disused social club. The crews used a high pressure lance and small tools to enter the property and extinguish the fire. The crew were at the scene for two hours.
