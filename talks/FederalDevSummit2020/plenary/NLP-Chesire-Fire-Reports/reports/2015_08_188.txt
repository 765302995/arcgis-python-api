Large goods vehicle in Runcorn road traffic collision Time of Incident: 02/08/2015 - 23:52 Location: Sutton Causeway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Frodsham Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving a large goods vehicle. Fortunately nobody was trapped in the incident. Crews made the scene safe and sealed drains as a precaution to make sure diesel couldn't escape into the system.
