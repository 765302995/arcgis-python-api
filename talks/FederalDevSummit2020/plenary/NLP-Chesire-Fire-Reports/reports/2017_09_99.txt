Road traffic collision in Poynton Time of Incident: 03/09/2017 - 11:42 Location: Park Lane, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton and one from Offerton Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision on Park Lane, Poynton. The crash was between one moving vehicle and three stationary vehicles. Fire crews made the scene safe and assisted paramedics with two casualties. Crews were detained for approximately 20 minutes.
