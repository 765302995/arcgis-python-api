Fire in back garden in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/09/2015 - 21:47 Location: Wellswood Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters extinguished a large fire in a back garden that had got out of control in Ellesmere Port.  The crew used one hose reel and a stack drag to put out the blaze.
