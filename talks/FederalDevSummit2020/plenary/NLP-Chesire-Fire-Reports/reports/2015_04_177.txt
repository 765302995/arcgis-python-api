Large vehicle on fire at Crewe farm Time of Incident: 09/04/2015 - 14:02 Location: Middlewich Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called out to a farm in Crewe, to a report of a heavy goods vehicle on fire.  The crew, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put the fire out and cool hot spots on the vehicle.  The fire was caused by a leak of hydraulic fluid.
