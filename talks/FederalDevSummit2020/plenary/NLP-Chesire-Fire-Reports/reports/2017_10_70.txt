Garden fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 15/10/2017 - 20:05 Location: Nicholson Avenue, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to a fire in a garden on Nicholson Avenue, Macclesfield. Crews found a quantity of wood and roofing felt well alight on arrival and they used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
