 Pigeon rescued from netting in Neston Time of Incident: 26/05/2019 - 09:58 Location: Brook Street, Neston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Crews used a 13.5m ladder to help rescue a pigeon which had become stuck in netting on a bridge on Brook Street in Runcorn.  The pigeon was then handed into the care of the RSPCA.
