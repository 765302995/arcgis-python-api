 Log burner fire in Calveley Time of Incident: 19/10/2018 - 16:24 Location: Station Road, Calveley Attendance: One fire engine and a target response vehicle attended from Nantwich At around 4.25pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a domestic property on Station Road in Calveley. The fire involving a log burner was extinguished prior to firefighters' arrival. There was slight smoke logging in the property. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots to make sure that the property, including the loft space, was safe. They also removed burnt debris from the log burner. For safety tips relating to log burners visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wood-burning-stoves and https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chimneys.
