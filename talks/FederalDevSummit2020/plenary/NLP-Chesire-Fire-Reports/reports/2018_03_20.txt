 HGV fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 27/03/2018 - 14:01 Location: Winsford Trading Estate Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and one from Winsford A fire in a HGV on Winsford Trading Estate was extinguished by firefighters. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was caused by a faulty battery.
