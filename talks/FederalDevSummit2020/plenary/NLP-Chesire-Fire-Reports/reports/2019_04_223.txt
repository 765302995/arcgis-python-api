 Tree fire in Neston Time of Incident: 06/04/2019 - 10:36 Location: Romney Close, Neston. CH64 9RJ Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port attended Just after 10.30am fire crews were called to reports of trees on fire in the garden of a house on Romney Close.  They used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.
