 Three-vehicle collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/02/2019 - 19:06 Location: Midland Way, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Penketh Shortly after 7pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving three vehicles on Midland Way in Warrington. On arrival crews discovered that no-one was trapped in any of the vehicles and there was no sign of any smoke or fire. Firefighters made the vehicles safe and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
