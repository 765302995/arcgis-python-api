 Small fire in the open in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 21/11/2019 - 21:46 Location: Sutton Way, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called following reports of two youths throwing things onto a fire in the open in Sutton Way, Ellesmere Port.  The crew found the small fire in the open and used a hose reel jet to put it out.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/ or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
