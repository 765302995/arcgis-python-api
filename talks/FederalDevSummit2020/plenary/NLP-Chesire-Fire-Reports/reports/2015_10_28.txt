Kitchen fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 25/10/2015 - 12:07 Location: Wood Acres Court, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters used a large fan to clear smoke caused by a pan being left on a hob in Wilmslow.
