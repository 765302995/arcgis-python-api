Sofa fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 22/04/2016 - 21:31 Location: Station Road, Winsford. Attendance: Fire engines from Winsford, Middlewich and Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Station Road, Winsford.  When they arrived crews found that there was a sofa on fire outside the property against the wall. The householders had all evacuated the property before firefighters arrived.  A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out. It had caused heat damage to the building and cracked a window. There was no further damage to the inside of the property.  This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or the Police on 101.
