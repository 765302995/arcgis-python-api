 Gazebo fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/11/2018 - 13:44 Location: Blackburne Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood and Warrington Gazebo fire in WarringtonFire crew were called to a gazebo well alight on Blackburne Close in Poulton-with-Fernhead.   Firefighters, wearing two breathing apparatus, used two hose reels to put out the fire which had also spread to boundary fencing.  The area was then made safe and crews were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
