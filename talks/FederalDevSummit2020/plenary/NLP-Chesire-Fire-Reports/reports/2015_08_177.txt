Car fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 04/08/2015 - 04:06 Location: Stoneyfold Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A saloon car was well alight on the roadway. The burning vehicle was blocking the road so police were also asked to attend as firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, put out the blaze using a hose reel jet. The car, which is believed to have been set alight deliberately, was totally destroyed in the fire.  If you have any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
