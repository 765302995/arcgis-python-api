 Fork lift truck fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/05/2019 - 21:36 Location: Leacroft Road, Warrington Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Lymm Fire crews attended to reports of a fork lift truck alight on an industrial estate on Leacroft Road in Warrington.   The fire had already been extinguished before crews arrived however it was still smoldering.  Crews then used cutting equipment to dismantle the truck and made the area safe.
