 Bin store fire in Chester Time of Incident: 03/11/2018 - 12:45 Location: Trafford Street, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port. Firefighters were called to a building in Trafford Street Chester following reports of a fire in a bin store. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames, then used a large fan to disperse the smoke from the stairwell.
