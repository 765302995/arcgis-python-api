 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/03/2018 - 23:56 Location: St Margaret's Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At 11.55pm firefighters were called to a car fire on St Margaret's Avenue in Warrington. On arrival the crew found a vehicle well alight and a firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. Firefighters were at the scene for around 50 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and police were in attendance. Anyone with any information about the fire should call Cheshire Police on 101.
