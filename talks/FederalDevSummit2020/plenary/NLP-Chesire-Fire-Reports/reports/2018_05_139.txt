 Fire outside a school in Winsford Time of Incident: 13/05/2018 - 19:41 Location: Montgomery Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At around 7.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire outside Oaklands School in Montgomery Way, Winsford. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a hedgerow. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and then damped the area down. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
