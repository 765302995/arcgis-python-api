Road traffic collision on Swanley Lane, Nantwich Time of Incident: 29/10/2015 - 18:23 Location: Swanley Lane, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one fire engine from Crewe Firefighters helped paramedics to treat a male casualty who had been involved in a road traffic collision. Nobody was trapped in the collision. The collision involved a van and a tractor. The male casualty was then transported to hospital by ambulance.
