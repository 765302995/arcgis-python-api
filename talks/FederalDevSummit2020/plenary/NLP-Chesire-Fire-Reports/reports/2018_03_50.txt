 Wheelie bins on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 24/03/2018 - 00:19 Location: Oxford Street, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters attended reports of wheelie bins on fire in an alleyway in Oxford Street, Widnes. The fire which had spread to fencing and trees was extinguished using a hose reel. Police have been informed.
