House fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 25/08/2015 - 23:45 Location: Grange Lane, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford and one from Middlewich A fire in the groud floor of a property in Winsford was extinguished by firefighters wearing breathing apparatus.  One hose reel and a covering jet were used by the crews as well as a large fan to clear the smoke.  All people were accounted for and a joint investigation into the cause of the fire is underway with police.
