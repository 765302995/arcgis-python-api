 Two-vehicle collision in Chester Time of Incident: 01/10/2018 - 09:16 Location: Plough Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 9.15am firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving two vehicles on Plough Lane in Chester. On arrival the crew discovered that the collision involved two saloon cars. Firefighters made the vehicles safe and one casualty was treated by paramedics. The crew were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
