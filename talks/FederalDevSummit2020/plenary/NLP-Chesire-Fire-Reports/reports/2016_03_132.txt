Bin wagon battery compartment on fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 08/03/2016 - 05:28 Location: Civic Hall car park, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton The battery compartment of a bin wagon was involved in fire. Firefighters used a dry powder extinguisher to put it out. There was a small amount of battery fluid which was cleared up using sand.
