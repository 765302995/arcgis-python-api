 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/09/2019 - 23:11 Location: Hopwood Street, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Penketh Firefighters attended to a wheelie bin fire on Hopwood Street in Warrington.  The bin was extinguished using a hose reel.  Crews then checked the fire hadn't spread to the property nearby before making the area safe.
