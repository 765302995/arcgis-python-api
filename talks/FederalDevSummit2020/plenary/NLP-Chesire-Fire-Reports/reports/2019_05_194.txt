 Small fire in a park in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 03/05/2019 - 19:14 Location: Barlow Road, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters from Wilmslow attended reports of a fire in Lacey Green Park.  On arrival the crew discovered a small fire involving paper and magazines which is believed to have been started deliberately.  The team used water to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for approximately 15 minutes.  Anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
