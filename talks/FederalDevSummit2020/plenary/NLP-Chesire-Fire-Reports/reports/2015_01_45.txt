Road traffic collision in Astbury Time of Incident: 19/01/2015 - 08:11 Location: The Village, Astbury Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton, the rapid response vehicle and a fire engine from Holmes Chapel A road traffic collision involving two cars in Astbury left one man trapped.  Fire and rescue crews removed the roof of the car and assisted ambulance personnel to remove the casualty.  They then made the scene safe.
