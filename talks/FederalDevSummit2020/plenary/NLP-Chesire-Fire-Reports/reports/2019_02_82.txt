 Fire near a warehouse, Runcorn Time of Incident: 15/02/2019 - 17:33 Location: Blackheath Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters tackled a blaze involving a large pile of rubbish and grass material near a warehouse distribution centre. There was thought to have been around 70 tonnes of hedge cuttings amongst it, and it measured around 50 square metres in size. Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the flames.
