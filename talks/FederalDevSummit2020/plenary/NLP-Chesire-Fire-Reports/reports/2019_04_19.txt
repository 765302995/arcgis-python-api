 Chimney fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 26/04/2019 - 19:01 Location: Byrons Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Firefighters attended to a chimney fire at a property on Byrons Lane in Macclesfield.  One hose reel was used to extinguish the fire in the chimney grate and a large fan was used to clear away the smoke.  Fire crews were at the scene for 30 minutes.
