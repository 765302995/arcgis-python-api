House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/07/2015 - 20:31 Location: Grange Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Grange Avenue, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire involving a t-towel but this had gone out.  Firefighters carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving.
