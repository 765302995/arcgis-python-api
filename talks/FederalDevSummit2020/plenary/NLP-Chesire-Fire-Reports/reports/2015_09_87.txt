House fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 12/09/2015 - 22:38 Location: Welles Street, Sandbach. Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe and Sandbach Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Welles Street, Sandbach.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire but it was out when they arrived. Crews used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.
