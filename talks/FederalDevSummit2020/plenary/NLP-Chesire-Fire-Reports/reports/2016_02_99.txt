Man trapped on the M6 Time of Incident: 06/02/2016 - 05:38 Location: M6 between Junctions 17 and 18. Attendance: Fire engine from Crewe and Holmes Chapel Firefighters responded to a report of a collision on the M6 between Junctions 17 and 18.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved a car and a lorry. One man was trapped. Crews used special cutting gear to free him. He was then passed to the care of the ambulance service.  Fire service personnel were on scene for approximately forty minutes.
