Shed fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/12/2016 - 19:21 Location: Longshaw Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters tackled a fire involving a brick built shed on Longshaw Street. A hose reel jet was used to tackle the blaze which is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
