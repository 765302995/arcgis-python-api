 HGV collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/10/2018 - 18:14 Location: Southern Expressway, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn. One fire engine from Widnes. Firefighters were called to a collision involving a HGV in the Southern Expressway. The driver of the HGV was trapped in the vehicle which had overturned. Crews extricated the man and handed him to the care of paramedics. They then made the vehicle safe.
