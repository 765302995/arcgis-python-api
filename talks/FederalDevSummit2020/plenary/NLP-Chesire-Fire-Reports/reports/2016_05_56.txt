Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 21/05/2016 - 00:49 Location: Cameron Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A car was involved in fire on Cameron Road. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to tackle the blaze. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 42 of 21 May.
