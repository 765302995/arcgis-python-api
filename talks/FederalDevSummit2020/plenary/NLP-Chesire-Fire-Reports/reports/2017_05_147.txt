Large muck heap fire in Over Alderley Time of Incident: 08/05/2017 - 14:48 Location: Findlow Hill Lane, Over Alderley Attendance: One pump from Macclesfield and one from Wilmslow A large straw muck heap which was well alight at a farm was being tackled by firefighters. Machinery was used to turn over the heap to help firefighters tackle it. The heap of straw and muck was approximately 10 metres by 15 metres and four metres high and was well alight.
