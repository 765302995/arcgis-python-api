 Wheelie bin fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 11/11/2019 - 00:46 Location: Alton Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 12.45am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Alton Street, Crewe.  On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a wheelie bin.  Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for more than 20 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.  For advice on preventing wheelie bin fires visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
