Dog rescue in Chester Time of Incident: 23/09/2016 - 19:29 Location: Lower Bridge Street Attendance: Two fire engines and the boat from Chester along with the rope rescue unit from Knutsford. Firefighters were called to a report of a dog that was stranded in the River Dee.  Crews entered the water wearing safety equipment and rescued the dog from the riverbank on a raft.
