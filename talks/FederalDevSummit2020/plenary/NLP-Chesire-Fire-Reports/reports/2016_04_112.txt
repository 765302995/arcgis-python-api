Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/04/2016 - 11:53 Location: Holcroft Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood A car was heavily involved in fire in a field at a Warrington farm. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They were at the scene for just under half an hour.
