Road traffic collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 07/09/2017 - 12:18 Location: Nantwich Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to road traffic collision between a car and a heavy goods vehicle. The crews released one woman casualty from a vehicle and handed her to the care of paramedics. Air ambulance also attended. The road was closed at the junction with Manor Way and Broughton Lane.
