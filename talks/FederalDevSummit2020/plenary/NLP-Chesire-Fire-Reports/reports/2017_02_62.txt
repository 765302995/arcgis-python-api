Road traffic collision in Minshull Vernon Time of Incident: 20/02/2017 - 17:45 Location: Queens Crescent, Minshull Vernon Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Two fire crews from Crewe were sent to a road traffic collision involving two vehicles on Queens Crescent in Minshull Vernon.  On arrival at the incident, fire crews found that there was nobody was trapped. Firefighters made the vehicles safe. Police closed the road in both directions.  One casualty with minor injuries was helped by paramedics.
