 Fires at a derelict building in Crewe Time of Incident: 12/05/2019 - 15:36 Location: Electricity Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Shortly after 3.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a derelict building in Electricity Street, Crewe. On arrival the crews discovered that there were two seats of fire in the building, which measures approximately five metres by 20 metres. Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fires. They then dragged out affected materials and dampened the areas affected by the fires down to make them safe. Firefighters also used a thermal imaging camera to monitor temperatures. They were in attendance for around an hour. The police also attended. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 399025, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
