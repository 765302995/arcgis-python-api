 Small fire in open in Widnes Time of Incident: 14/07/2018 - 16:22 Location: Warrington Road, Widnes. Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to a small fire at Warrington Road, Widnes.  On arrival they found a pallet alight at the side of the road. Crews used one hose reel to extinguish the fire.
