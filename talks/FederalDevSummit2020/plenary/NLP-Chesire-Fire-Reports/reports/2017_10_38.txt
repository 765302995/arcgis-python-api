Bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/10/2017 - 14:58 Location: Cromwell Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a bin fire on Cromwell Road, Ellesmere Port. On arrival crews found a bin well alight and they used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
