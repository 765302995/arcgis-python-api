Shed on fire in Shavington Time of Incident: 07/01/2016 - 16:13 Location: Stock Lane, Shavington Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to a shed on fire in Shavington. One main water jet and a hose reel were used to extinguish the flames. An LPG cylinder was involved and was cooled with water from a ground monitor.  A cordon was put in place by police and neighboring properties were evacuated while the incident was dealt with.
