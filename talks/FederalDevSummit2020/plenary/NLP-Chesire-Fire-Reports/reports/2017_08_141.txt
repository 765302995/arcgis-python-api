Road traffic collision on the M6 by Knutsford Time of Incident: 05/08/2017 - 08:24 Location: M6 by Knutsford Attendance: Four fire engines from Knutsford, Lymm, Birchwood, and Altrincham (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) There was a  road traffic collision on the M6 motorway near to Knutsford involving a car and a van.  One casualty was trapped inside a vehicle.  Firefighters  released the casualty, who was then handed to the care of paramedics.  Police, an ambulance and an air ambulance attended this incident.
