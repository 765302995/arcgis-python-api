 Garages on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/03/2018 - 20:47 Location: Vulcan Close, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood. One fire engine from Warrington. Firefighters attended a fire involving two garages which were well alight in Vulcan Close in Warrington. Crews used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the flames. Crews then began damping down the garages to prevent further issues. Police were informed. Anyone with information should contact Cheshire Police on 101.
