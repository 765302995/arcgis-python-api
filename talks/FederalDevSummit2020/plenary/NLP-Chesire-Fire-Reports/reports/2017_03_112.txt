Fire in ground floor living room in Runcorn Time of Incident: 13/03/2017 - 07:49 Location: Picton Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn and Penketh Firefighters were called to a fire involving the living room of a semi-detached house on Picton Avenue. Two covering jets and a hose reel were used by firefighters in breathing apparatus to bring the fire under control. Police also attended and Picton Avenue was closed while the incident was dealt with. Crews checked the roof space of the property for fire spread and nearby properties were evacuated.  The crews were at the scene for just under two and a half hours. An investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
