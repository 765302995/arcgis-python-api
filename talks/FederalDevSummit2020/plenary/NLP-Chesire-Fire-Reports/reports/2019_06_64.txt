 Collision involving three vehicles in Sandbach Time of Incident: 16/06/2019 - 23:50 Location: Crewe Road in Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Crewe and one from Sandbach Shortly before 12 midnight firefighters were called to reports of a collision on Crewe Road in Sandbach. On arrival the crews discovered that the collision involved a hatchback car and two stationary vehicles, one of which had come to rest against a house. No-one was trapped inside any of the vehicles. Two casualties were assessed at the scene by paramedics. The road was closed as emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters assessed the building for damage and passed their findings onto a local authority building inspector. The fire crews were in attendance for less than an hour. Police also attended.
