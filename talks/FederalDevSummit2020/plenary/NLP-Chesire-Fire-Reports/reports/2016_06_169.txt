Tumble dryer fire Time of Incident: 01/06/2016 - 20:24 Location: Buxton Road, Disley. Attendance: Fire engines from Poynton and Greater Manchester Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Buxton Road, Disley.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving a dishwasher. Crews wearing breathing apparatus used hose reel jets and a large fan to deal with the incident.  Tumble dryer fire safety
