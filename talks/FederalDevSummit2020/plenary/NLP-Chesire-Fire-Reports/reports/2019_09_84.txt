 Tumble dryer fire, Nantwich Time of Incident: 14/09/2019 - 21:14 Location: Barony Court, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich Firefighters were called to tackle a tumble dryer fire in an apartment. When they arrived there was no fire, but lots of smoke issuing form the tumble dryer, which had smokelogged the communal area of the building. They used a large fan to clear the smoke, isolated the electrics, and removed the tumble dryer from the building. They checked for any other signs of fire using a thermal imaging camera, and used a hose reel to cool the affected area.
