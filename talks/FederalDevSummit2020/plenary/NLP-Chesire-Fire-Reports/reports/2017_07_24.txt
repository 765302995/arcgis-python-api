Caravan on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/07/2017 - 00:17 Location: Chapel Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Penketh Two hose reels were used to extinguish a caravan which was on fire. It's believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you know anything then call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
