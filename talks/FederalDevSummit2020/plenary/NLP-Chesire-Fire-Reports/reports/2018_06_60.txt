 Scooter fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 00:02 Location: Wellington Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to a scooter fire on Wellington Road, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information please call Cheshire Police on 101.
