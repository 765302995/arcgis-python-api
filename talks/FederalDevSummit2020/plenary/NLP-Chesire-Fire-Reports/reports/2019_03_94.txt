 Water rescue in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/03/2019 - 05:14 Location: Knutsford Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington, Chester, Widnes, Penketh and Stockton Heath. Firefighters were called to reports of a man in the river behind the retail park in Knutsford Road, Warrington. Crews made a search of the area with police and located a man on the riverbank. He was rescued and handed to paramedics.
