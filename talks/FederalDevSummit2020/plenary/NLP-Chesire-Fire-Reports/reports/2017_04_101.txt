Caravan fire in Penketh Time of Incident: 07/04/2017 - 16:49 Location: St Alban Road, Penketh, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Penketh Firefighters were called to a report of a caravan on fire on St Alban Road. They found a small fire in the kitchen area, involving clothing. The fire was out and the crews cleared smoke using a high pressure fan.
