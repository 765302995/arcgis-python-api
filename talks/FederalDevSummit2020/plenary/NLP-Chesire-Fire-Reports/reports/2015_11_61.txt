Shop canopy damaged Time of Incident: 18/11/2015 - 21:57 Location: Langdale Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to reports of a dangerous structure in Runcorn.  Upon arrival, crews found that a canopy had partially come away from a hairdressers posing a potential danger to the public. Firefighters removed the canopy and ensured that the building was safe before leaving the scene.
