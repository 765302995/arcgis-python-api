Cooker fire in Chester Time of Incident: 08/10/2015 - 14:07 Location: parkgate road, chester. Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Chester Fat on a cooker caused a fire in the kitchen of a house on Parkgate Road. Firefighters were called and used a fire blanket to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was then used to clear the property of smoke.  To avoid a fire like this please follow Kitchen fire safety
