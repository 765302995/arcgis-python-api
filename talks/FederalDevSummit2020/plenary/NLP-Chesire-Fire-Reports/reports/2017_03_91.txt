Fire involving a wood burning heater in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/03/2017 - 17:10 Location: Poole hall Industrial Estate, Poole Hall Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Three fire engines from Ellesmere Port, Chester and also Bromborough (Merseyside Fire and Rescue Service) Firefighters used one hose reel jet and one covering jet to extinguish a fire involving a wood burning heater in an industrial unit on Poole Hall Industrial Estate. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. A thermal imaging camera was also used.
