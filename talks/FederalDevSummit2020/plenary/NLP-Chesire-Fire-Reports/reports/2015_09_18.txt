Skip on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/09/2015 - 22:40 Location: New Cut Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood One hose reel jet was used by firefighters to put out a blaze in a skip. The crew believe the fire was started deliberately and asked police to attend. Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
