Kitchen on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 08/06/2016 - 16:42 Location: Clifton Drive, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich, one from Winsford and one from Widnes Firefighters were called to a kitchen on fire and used one hose reel and a covering jet to extinguish it.
