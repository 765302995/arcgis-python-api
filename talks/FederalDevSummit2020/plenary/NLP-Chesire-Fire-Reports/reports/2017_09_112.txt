Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/09/2017 - 03:17 Location: Hough Green Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A fire involving a car and fencing panels was put out by firefighters using a hose reel jet. A second car was also involved in the fire.  It is believed the fire was started deliberately. If you have any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
