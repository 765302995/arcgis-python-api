 Fire involving a coaster at a flat in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/07/2018 - 12:55 Location: Dingleway, Appleton, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 12.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a flat on Dingleway in Appleton, Warrington. The fire involving a coaster was out on arrival. Firefighters used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the property and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
