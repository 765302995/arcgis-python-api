 Fire at a farm in Aston by Budworth Time of Incident: 27/08/2019 - 20:18 Location: Budworth Road, Aston by Budworth, near Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Knutsford and one from Lymm Shortly after 8.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a farm on Budworth Road in Aston by Budworth, near Knutsford. On arrival crews found a large quantity of combustible materials well alight in the open. Firefighters set up a water shuttle to get enough water to the area. A firefighter used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The area affected by the fire was then damped down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for nearly an hour-and-a-half.
