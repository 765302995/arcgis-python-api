 Rescue of a person at height in Warrngton Time of Incident: 07/08/2018 - 12:58 Location: Glazebrook Lane, Glazebrook, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Shortly before 1pm firefighters from Lymm were called to reports of an elderly man stuck on the roof of a property on Glazebrook Lane. On arrival crews found that the man had become trapped on the roof of a single storey building while undertaking repairs. The team used a short extension ladder to rescue the man, he was then passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service.
