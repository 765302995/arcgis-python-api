 Skip on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 08/03/2018 - 19:35 Location: Cheshire Oaks, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A skip on fire at Cheshire Oaks was extinguished with water by firefighters from Ellesmere Port. A thermal imaging camera was then used to check the area before the crew left.
