Flooding in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/09/2019 - 14:00 Location: Densham Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Widnes - as did a water incident unit from Warrington Fire crews were called to Densham Avenue following flooding in the area.  Flood water was at knee level and firefighters assessed the situation and checked properties. They then used rafts to rescue a small number of people from their homes.  There were reports of people walking through and playing in the water. Crews urged adults and children to stay out of the water as there could have been hidden dangers such as open drains, damaged road surfaces and submerged debris that could cause serious injury.
