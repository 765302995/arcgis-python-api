 Domestic building fire in Chester Time of Incident: 06/08/2018 - 21:44 Location: Sycamore Drive, Chester. Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Chester Shortly before 10pm firefighters from Chester and Tarporley attended reports of a fire at an address on Sycamore Drive in Chester.  On arrival at the scene crews discovered a fire in a first floor bedroom.  The team used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel covering jet to extinguish the fire.  The crews were detained at the scene for approximately fifty-five minutes.
