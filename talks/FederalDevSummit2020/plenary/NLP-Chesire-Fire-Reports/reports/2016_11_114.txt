Owl rescued from disused property in Runcorn Time of Incident: 08/11/2016 - 11:15 Location: Greenway Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Firefighters were called to rescue a Tawny Owl after it apparently fell down a chimney and got stuck in a disused property. The RSPCA were also in attendance to look after the owl once it was rescued.
