Car on fire on driveway in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/01/2017 - 02:19 Location: Reynolds Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a car on fire on a driveway which was well alight. The crew used a hose reel to extinguish the fire and police were requested to attend due to the fire being believed to have been started deliberately.
