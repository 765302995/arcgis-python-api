 Two-car collision in Little Stanney Time of Incident: 25/11/2019 - 16:51 Location: A5117 in Little Stanney, near Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port At around 4.50pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision on the A5117 in Little Stanney, near Ellesmere Port.  On arrival crews discovered the collision involved two saloon cars and that no-one was trapped inside either of them.  Firefighters made the vehicles safe and handed a casualty, a woman, over to the care of paramedics.  The incident was then handed over to the police.  Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
