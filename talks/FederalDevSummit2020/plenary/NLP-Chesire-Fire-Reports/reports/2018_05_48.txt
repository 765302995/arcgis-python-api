 Outbuilding fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 24/05/2018 - 00:07 Location: Coleridge Close, Sandbach. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Sandbach and one from Crewe Shortly after midnight, two fire engines from Sandbach and Crewe alongside the rapid response unit from Sandbach attended a fire on Coleridge Close.Crews attended the scene and found a shed outside a residential property was well alight. The fire had also damaged a nearby property. Firefighters used 3 hose reels to extinguish the fire and was in attendance for 90 minutes.
