 Small fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 18/04/2019 - 22:26 Location: Ashton Road, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley Fireefighters were called to a large unattended bonfire on land off Ashton road, Northwich. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
