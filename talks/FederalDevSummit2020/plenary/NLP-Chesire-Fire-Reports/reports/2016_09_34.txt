Girl rescued from mud Time of Incident: 24/09/2016 - 14:23 Location: Willington Road, Duddon, Chester Attendance: The water incident unit from Chester, along with the rope rescue unit and support engine from Knutsford. Fire crews were called to a report of a teenage girl who had got stick in mud off Willington Road, Duddon, Chester.  Firefighters rescued the girl who was then passed to the care of the ambulance service.
