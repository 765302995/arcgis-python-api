 Persons trapped following collision in Woolston Time of Incident: 22/06/2018 - 18:29 Location: Manchester Road, Woolston, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Lymm and one from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington and Lymm attended reports of a road traffic collision on Manchester Road, Woolston. On arrival crews found the incident involved one van and one car, with one casualty trapped in the van.  Crews released the casualty from the van and he, along with a second casualty, was passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service.  Crews were at scene for approximately 35 minutes
