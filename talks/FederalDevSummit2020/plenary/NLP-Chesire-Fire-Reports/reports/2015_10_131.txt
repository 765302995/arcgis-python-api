Two cars and outbuilding involved in Smallwood fire Time of Incident: 08/10/2015 - 14:44 Location: Newcastle Road,Smallwood, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and one from Crewe Two cars and an outbuilding were involved in a fire at a property in Smallwood. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze. The electricity supply was isolated. The house and the outbuilding suffered external heat and smoke damage. It is believed that this fire was started accidentally.
