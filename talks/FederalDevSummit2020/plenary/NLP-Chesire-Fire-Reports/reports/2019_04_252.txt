 Fire involving a motorbike in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/04/2019 - 19:03 Location: Factory Lane, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters from Widnes used a water backpack to extinguish a motorbike which had been deliberately set alight in a field off Factory Lane. Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 362932. information can also be reported anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111.
