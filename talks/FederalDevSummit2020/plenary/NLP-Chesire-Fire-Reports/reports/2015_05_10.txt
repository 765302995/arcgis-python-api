Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/05/2015 - 12:11 Location: Old Hall Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath An electrical fault in a fridge is believed to have been the cause of a fire in a kitchen in Warrington. Firefighters attended and used a hose reel to bring the fire under control.  A thermal imaging camera was also used to check that the fire hadn't spread before the crews left.
