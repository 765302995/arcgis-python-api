Chimney fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 16/02/2017 - 18:33 Location: Brookside Avenue, Poynton. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Poynton Firefighters were called to a house fire on Brookside Avenue, Poynton.  When they arrived crews found that there was a chimney fire. They extinguished the fire from the hearth using special equipment.
