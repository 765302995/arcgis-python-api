 Van fire in Chester Time of Incident: 02/01/2019 - 16:55 Location: Saighton Lane, Chester. Attendance:  Two fire engines attended, one from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Shortly before 5pm firefighters from Chester were dispatched to reports of a vehicle fire on Saighton Lane. On arrival at the scene the team discovered a van which was well alight. A further fire engine from Ellesmere Port was requested at the scene to assist with water supply. The team then used two hose reel jets and one breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. Crews were at scene just over one hour.
