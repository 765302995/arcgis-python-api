Flat fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 14/01/2017 - 10:09 Location: Edleston Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Crewe, and one from Alsager Two fire engines from Crewe and one fire engine from Alsager were sent to a fire involving a flat. The fire crews used four breathing apparatus, two hose reel jets and a large fan  to deal with the fire.  Firefighters requested an ambulance for one casualty for precautionary checks. Crews were detained for approximately three hours. It is believed that the fire was accidental and started due to an electrical item.
