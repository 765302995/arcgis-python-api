 Firefighters called to a house fire in Saighton, Chester Time of Incident: 03/01/2018 - 16:37 Location: Chester Lane, Saighton in Chester Attendance: Fire Crews from Chester Fire Crews in Chester were called to reports of a house fire on Chester Lane in Saighton in Chester. Firefighters used to one hose reel jet and two breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire which was believed to have been in the living room.
