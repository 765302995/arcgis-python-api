 Car fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 26/05/2018 - 12:43 Location: M56, westbound junction 12 to 14 Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes, one from Runcorn and one from Ellesmere Port Fire crews attended a car fire on the M56 westbound. The car's brakes had overheated and crews used a hose reel jet to cool the brakes down before giving the car an escort to Chester Services.
