Bin storage building fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 27/02/2017 - 17:05 Location: Siding Court, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Penketh A blaze involving bins in a storage building was tackled by firefighters. Crews used two hose reel jets to bring the fire under control and a thermal imaging camera to monitor the termperature. An investigation into the cause of the fire is being carried out.
