 Smoke coming from a microwave in Runcorn Time of Incident: 11/09/2019 - 15:15 Location: Worcester Court, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Runcorn and one from Penketh At 3.15pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from a microwave at a domestic property in Worcester Court, Runcorn. They made sure that the microwave was safe and cleared the smoke using a large fan. Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
