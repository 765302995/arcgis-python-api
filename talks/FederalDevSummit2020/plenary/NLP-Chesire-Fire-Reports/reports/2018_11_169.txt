 Shrub land on fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 04/11/2018 - 05:57 Location: Chelford Road, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford Firefighters were called to land off Chelford Road, Knutsford following reports of shrub land on fire. Crews used two hose reel to extinguish the fire.
