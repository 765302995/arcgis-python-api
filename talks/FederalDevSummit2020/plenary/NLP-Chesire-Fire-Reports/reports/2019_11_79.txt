 Small fire in the open in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 14/11/2019 - 07:39 Location: Great King Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At 7.40am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Great King Street, Macclesfield.  On arrival the crew found a small fire within the grounds of a church.  Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for more than 20 minutes.
