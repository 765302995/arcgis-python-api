Garage fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 05/11/2016 - 19:33 Location: Butler Way in Nantwich. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Crewe and Nantwich Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Butler Way in Nantwich.  When they arrived crews found that a fire in a wooden bin storage had spread to a garage. Hose reel jets were used to bring the blaze under control.
