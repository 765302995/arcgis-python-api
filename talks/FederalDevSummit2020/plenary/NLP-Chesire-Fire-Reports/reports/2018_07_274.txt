 Garden fire in Little Leigh Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 18:02 Location: Church Road, Little Leigh Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly after 6pm firefighters from Northwich attended reports of a garden fire in Little Leigh near Northwich. On arrival crews found a small fire at the base of a tree which was close to a gas storage tank. The team swiftly extinguished the fire and were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
