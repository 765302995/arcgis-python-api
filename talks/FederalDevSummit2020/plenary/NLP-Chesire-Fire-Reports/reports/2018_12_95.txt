 Report of leaking boiler in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 09/12/2018 - 02:33 Location: Barracks Square, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters were called to reports of a boiler leaking at a property on Barracks Square in Macclesfield.  Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, used one hose reel to assess the boiler and detection of carbon monoxide.  Following an inspection the boiler was found not to be leaking and a gas supplier was informed as a precaution.
