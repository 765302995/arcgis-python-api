 Wheelie bin smouldering near to a house in Northwich Time of Incident: 04/01/2019 - 00:02 Location: Church Road, Northwich Attendance:  Two fire engines attended from Northwich Shortly after midnight firefighters were called to a report of a wheelie bin being on fire near to a house on Church Road in Northwich.  On arrival the crew found the bin smouldering.  Firefighters damped down the wheelie bin with a hose reel jet to make it safe and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
