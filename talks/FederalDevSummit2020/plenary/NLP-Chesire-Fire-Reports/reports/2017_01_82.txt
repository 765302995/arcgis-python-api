Fuse box fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 11/01/2017 - 21:02 Location: Chapel Lane, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a building in Wilmslow after occupants reported a smell of burning in the lounge. Crews investigated the premises using a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots of fire and discovered the fuse box had been on fire but was now out. The electrics were then isolated.
