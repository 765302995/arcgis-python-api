 Hedgerow fire at a golf course in Chester Time of Incident: 13/04/2019 - 18:20 Location: Westminster Park, Hough Green, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At 6.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at a golf course in Westminster Park, Hough Green, Chester. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire involving a hedgerow. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for nearly half-an-hour.
