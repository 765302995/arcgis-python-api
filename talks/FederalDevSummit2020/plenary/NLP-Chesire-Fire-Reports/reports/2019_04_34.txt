 Small roadside fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/04/2019 - 09:16 Location: A57, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 9.15am firefighters were called to a report of a fire on the A57 in Great Sankey, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire by the roadside. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for more than 20 minutes.
