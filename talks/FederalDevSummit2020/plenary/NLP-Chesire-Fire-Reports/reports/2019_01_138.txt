 Large skip fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 13/01/2019 - 20:08 Location: Middlewich Road, Northwich Attendance:  Two fire engines attended from Northwich At around 10.10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Middlewich Road in Northwich. On arrival crews discovered that it was a large skip fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire at AAA Skip Hire. A water relay was set up to ensure that there was enough water to fight the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
