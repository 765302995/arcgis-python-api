Road traffic collision off the M56 Time of Incident: 09/11/2016 - 10:19 Location: M56 East J10 to J9 Attendance: Two fire engines from Stockton Heath and Warrington and one from Birchwood After leaving the motorway, one vehicle came to rest on an embankment on all four wheels. Firefighters liaised with paramedics and released one man from the vehicle using an extraction device. Crews then placed the man on a long board before paramedics took him to hospital.
