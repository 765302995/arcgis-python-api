 Rubbish fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 21/06/2018 - 23:01 Location: Rothesay Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 11pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at the back of a domestic property in Rothesay Close in Runcorn. On arrival the crew found that the fire involved rubbish. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
