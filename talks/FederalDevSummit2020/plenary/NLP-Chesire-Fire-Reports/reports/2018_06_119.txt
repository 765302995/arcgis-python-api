 Grass fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 21/06/2018 - 22:57 Location: Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 11pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at Bunbury Green in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew found a small pile of cut grass alight. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 25 minutes.
