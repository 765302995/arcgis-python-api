 Fuel leakage from a car in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/10/2019 - 19:28 Location: Warwick Court. Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At 7.28pm firefighters were called to a car which had been tipped on its side in Warwick Court, Ellesmere Port.  Approximately 25 litres of fuel was leaking from the vehicle. Crews tipped the car back onto its wheel and used a hose reel to disperse the fuel.
