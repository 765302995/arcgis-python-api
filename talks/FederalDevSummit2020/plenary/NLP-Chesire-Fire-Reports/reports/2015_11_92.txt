Hay fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 11/11/2015 - 07:08 Location: Grassy Lane, Frodsham. Attendance: One fire engine from Frodsham Firefighters extinguished a fire involving a bale of hay next to a burnt out car in Frodsham.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with any information should call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
