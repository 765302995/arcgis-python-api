Warrington wheelie bin fire Time of Incident: 21/07/2015 - 00:02 Location: Attlee Avenue, Culcheth, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood A wheelie bin was heavily involved in fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze which is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
