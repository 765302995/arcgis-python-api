 Two-vehicle collision in Henbury Time of Incident: 03/01/2019 - 13:42 Location: A537, Chelford Road, Henbury Attendance:  Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield At around 1.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision on the A537 Chelford Road in Henbury. Police, firefighters and paramedics attended the scene and found that there had been a collision between a car and a bus. The A537 was closed in both directions between Monks Heat and Broken Cross as emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to free a woman who was trapped in the car. The casualty was then handed over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
