 Three vehicle collision near Chester Time of Incident: 15/11/2019 - 17:52 Location: Platts Lane, Hatton Heath, near Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Chester and Ellesmere Port Shortly before 5pm firefighters were dispatched to reports of a multi-vehicle collision on the A41 near Chester. On arrival at the scene crews found the incident involved two saloon cars and a HGV. Initial indications suggested that two people were medically trapped in their vehicles. Although, thankfully, after initial treatment from paramedics they were able to get out of their cars themselves. Firefighters then made the vehicles safe and were detained at the scene for around 50 minutes.
