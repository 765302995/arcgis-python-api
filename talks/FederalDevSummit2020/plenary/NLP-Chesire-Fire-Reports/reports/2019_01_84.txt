 One vehicle collision in Wardle Time of Incident: 26/01/2019 - 03:11 Location: Nantwich Road, Wardle. Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Nantwich and one from Tarporley At around 3.10am firefighters from Nantwich and Tarporley were dispatched to reports of a car on fire following a one vehicle collision on the A51, Nantwich Road, in Wardle.  Crews swiftly attended the scene and discovered that the fire was already out on arrival and there was nobody trapped in the vehicle.  The team used and thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. They also stemmed an oil leak and made the vehicle safe.  Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately 40 minutes.
