Road traffic collision on the M6 nothbound J18 to J19 Time of Incident: 18/02/2016 - 10:45 Location: M6 between Junctions 18 and 19. Attendance: A fire engine from Middlewich Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on the M6 between Junctions 18 and 19.  When the crews arrived they found that the incident involved eight vehicles. Firefighters confirmed that there was no one trapped and no vehicles on fire.  They assisted the Highways Agency with making the scene safe.
