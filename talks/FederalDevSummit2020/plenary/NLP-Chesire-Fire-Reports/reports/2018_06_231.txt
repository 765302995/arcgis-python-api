 Cooking left unattended in Acton Time of Incident: 05/06/2018 - 14:23 Location: Chester Road, Acton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich At around 2.20pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke alarms sounding at a property on Chester Road in Acton.  On arrival the crew found that no-one was inside.  Firefighters forced entry and discovered that cooking food had been left attended.  They turned off the hob and used a large high pressure fan to clear the smoke from the property.  Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
