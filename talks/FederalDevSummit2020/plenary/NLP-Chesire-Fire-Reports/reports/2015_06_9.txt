Large conifers on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 27/06/2015 - 18:43 Location: Firdale Rad, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich One hose reel jet was used to extinguish a fire involving eight conifer trees (approximately 40 foot high). The conifer trees were well alight.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this or similar incidents is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
