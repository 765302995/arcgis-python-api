 4x4 fire in Chester Time of Incident: 28/07/2018 - 11:28 Location: Wrexham Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended a 4x4 well alight on Wrexham Road, Chester. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
