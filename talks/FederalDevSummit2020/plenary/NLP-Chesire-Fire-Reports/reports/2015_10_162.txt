Sheep rescue in Crewe Time of Incident: 01/10/2015 - 17:56 Location: Rope Lane, Crewe. Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe along with the Technical Rescue Unit from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a report of an animal in difficulties on Rope Lane, Crewe.   When they arrived they found that a sheep had become stuck in mud. Firefighters used a rope at a safe distance to successfully pull the sheep from the mud.  Crews were at the scene for about an hour.
