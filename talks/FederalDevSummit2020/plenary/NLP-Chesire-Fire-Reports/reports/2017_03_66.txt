Brakes overheating on heavy goods vehicle on the M56 Time of Incident: 21/03/2017 - 15:12 Location: M56 east between junctions 10 to 9 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath A heavy goods vehicle needed its brakes cooling with a hose reel after a fire occurred. A thermal imaging camera was used to check the vehicle before crews left. Lane one was closed while the incident was dealt with and police and highways attended.
