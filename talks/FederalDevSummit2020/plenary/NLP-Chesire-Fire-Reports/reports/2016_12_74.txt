Bedroom fire in Penketh Time of Incident: 18/12/2016 - 08:14 Location: Druridge Drive, Penketh, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A fire in the bedroom of a Druridge Drive house was tackled by firefighters. Gas and electricity supplies were isolated. The crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze. A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke. An ambulance was called to care for a man who was suffering from smoke inhalation and shock.  The fire is believed to have been caused by electric heaters.
