Macclesfield grass fire Time of Incident: 21/06/2015 - 18:19 Location: Western Drive, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield An area of grassland measuring 20 metres by 10 metres was involved in a fire believed to have been started deliberately.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet and beaters to put out the blaze.  Police were asked to attend.
