 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/08/2019 - 14:59 Location: Bridge Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington used a hose reel jet to extinguish a wheelie bin fire on Bridge Street in the town.  The crew were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.  For advice on how preventing wheelie bin fires visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
