Serious collision in Culcheth Time of Incident: 04/11/2015 - 07:27 Location: Holcroft Lane, Culcheth, Warrington. Attendance: Three fire engines and a major rescue unit Firefighters were called to the scene of a collision involving a car and a bus in Culcheth.  The crews worked closely with paramedics to release the driver of the bus who was treated at the scene prior to being air lifted to hospital.  The road was closed while the incident was dealt with and motorists advised to avoid the area. Firefighters left the scene at 9.55am.
