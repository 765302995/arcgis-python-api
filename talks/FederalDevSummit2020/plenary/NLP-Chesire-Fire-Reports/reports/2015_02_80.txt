Chimney fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 09/02/2015 - 13:53 Location: Church Street, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas Firefighters have been dealing with a chimney on fire at a Malpas house.  The crew used a high pressure hose and chimney rods to clear the fire in the chimney breast and checked the roof void and other fireplaces.
