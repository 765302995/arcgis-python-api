 Wheelie bin and couch fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/08/2018 - 04:31 Location: Forster Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters attended to a property on Forster Street in Warrington following reports of a wheelie bin and a couch on fire.  On arrival crews established around six wheelie bins had been set alight as well as the couch and used a hose reel to put it out.
