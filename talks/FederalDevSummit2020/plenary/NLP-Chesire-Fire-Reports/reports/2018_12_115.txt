 Van fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/12/2018 - 00:23 Location: Somerville Road, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Widnes and Runcorn At approximately 00.20am firefighters from Widnes and Runcorn were dispatched to reports of a large van fire outside a house on Somerville Road. On arrival crews found that a large van was well alight and one casualty was suffering from smoke inhalation.  One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and paramedics attended to assist the casualty. After extinguishing the fire the team conducted a home safety assessment and fitted a replacement smoke alarm. Crews were in attendance for approximately one hour and twenty minutes.
