Hay bale fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/05/2015 - 14:41 Location: Fiddlers Ferry, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a field close to Fiddlers Ferry, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that there was a hay bale on fire. Crews used backpack extinguishers to put the fire out.
