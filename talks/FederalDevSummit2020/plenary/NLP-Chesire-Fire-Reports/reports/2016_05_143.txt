Kitchen fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 07/05/2016 - 00:00 Location: Styal Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilsmlow and one from Cheadle Wilmslow and Cheadle firefighters were sent to a kitchen fire, but when they arrived at the property the fire was already out.  Firefighters gave Oxygen therapy to a woman.   The kitchen was full of smoke so a large fan was used to clear the smoke.  Kitchen fire safety tips.
