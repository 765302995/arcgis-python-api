Smoke logged electrical substation in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/01/2015 - 21:41 Location: Glebeland, Culcheth, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Greater Manchester Crews requested an electrical engineer and used a thermal imaging camera to monitor the temperature of a substation in Warrington.  No visible signs of fire were located and the premises was naturally ventilated to clear the smoke. The incident was handed over to the electrical engineer who isolated the power supply.
