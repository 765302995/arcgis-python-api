 Chimney fire in Chester Time of Incident: 14/02/2019 - 19:42 Location: Chester Road, Dunham-on-the-Hill, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly before 7.45pm firefighters were called to reports of a chimney fire at a domestic property on Chester Road, Dunham-on-the-Hill, Chester. The fire involved a wood burning stove.  Firefighters extinguished the chimney fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than 15 minutes. For tips on avoiding a chimney fire visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chimneys.
