House fire in Haslington Time of Incident: 06/05/2015 - 22:53 Location: Pool Meadows Road, Haslington Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Clothes left drying on a heater caused a small fire at a Haslington house.  The fire was out when firefighters arrived but the crew had to clear smoke from the property using a high pressure fan.  Please follow our safety advice to avoid fires in the home - Heaters
