 Van fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 20/09/2018 - 10:55 Location: Badgersrake Lane, Little Sutton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane At around 10.55am firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a van on Badgersrake Lane in Little Sutton, Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered a Ford Connect van well alight. Firefighters, including one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. They were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
