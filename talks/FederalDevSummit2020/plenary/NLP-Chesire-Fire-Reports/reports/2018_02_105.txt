 Fire in a field in Chester Time of Incident: 05/02/2018 - 19:17 Location: Picton Gorse Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Fire crews were called to a fire in a field off Picton Gorse Lane, Chester. A deceased cow had been set alight and crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The crews then called the RSPCA.
