 Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/07/2018 - 15:01 Location: Runcorn Hill, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a grass fire well alight on Runcorn Hill in Runcorn measuring 30 square metres.   A hose reel was used to put out the fire which is believed to have been started accidentally.
