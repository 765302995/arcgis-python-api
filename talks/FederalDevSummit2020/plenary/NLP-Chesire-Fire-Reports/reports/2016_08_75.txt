Hay bailing machine and hay on fire in Pott Shrigley Time of Incident: 17/08/2016 - 17:27 Location: Shrigley Road, Pott Shrigley, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield fire station and one from Bollington There was a fire involving a hay bailing machine and a large bale of hay. Fire crews used two breathing apparatus and two hose reels to extinguish the fire.
