Collision involving car and bus in Tiverton Time of Incident: 23/11/2015 - 19:46 Location: Whitchurch Road, Tiverton, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley and one fire engine and major rescue unit from Winsford. Firefighers stabilised a car and bus involved in a collision in Tiverton.  One woman self rescued with assistance from the Ambulance Service and three people off the bus were given precautionary checks from paramedics.
