 Kitchen fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 06/10/2018 - 09:44 Location: Admirals Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Birchwood Crews were called to reports of smoke coming from a flat on Admirals Road in Birchwood. They arrived to find a fire had started in the kitchen. They used two breathing apparatus and a single hose reel to extinguish the flames. No-one was injured, and a positive pressure ventilation fan was used to clear the smoke from the flat.
