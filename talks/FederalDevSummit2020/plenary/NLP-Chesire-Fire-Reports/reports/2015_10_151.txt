Man trapped after collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/10/2015 - 07:53 Location: Statham Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Birchwood One man was trapped in a van after it collided with a bus in Warrington.  Three people were also injured on the bus and the crews gave initial first aid to them until paramedics arrived.  Firefighters released the man who was handed into the care of paramedics.
