 Tree fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 24/02/2018 - 14:38 Location: Off Shackleton Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters responded to reports of a tree on fire at an address off Shackleton Close in Bewsey, Warrington. It appears to have been caused by a controlled burning that spread to the adjacent tree, approximately 10m high.  Crews used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames and used cutting tools to dampen down some smaller areas of the tree. The occupiers were advised to have the tree professionally felled.
