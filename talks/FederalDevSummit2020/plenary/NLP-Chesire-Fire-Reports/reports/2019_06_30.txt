 Boat fire in Wardle Time of Incident: 23/06/2019 - 22:29 Location: Nantwich Road, Wardle Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a boat fire on a marina on Nantwich Road, Wardle. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. The fire is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault and most of the boat was destroyed. Crews were at the scene for an hour and 20 minutes. Boat safety advice
