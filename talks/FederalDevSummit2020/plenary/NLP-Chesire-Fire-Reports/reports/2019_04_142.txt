 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/04/2019 - 15:22 Location: Sharp Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 3.20pm firefighters from Birchwood were called to reports of a vehicle fire on Sharp Street, Warrington.  On arrival the team discovered a hybrid car which was well alight.  One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to bring the fire under control. A second firefighter then used another hose reel jet to help to extinguish the fire.  One person was treated at the scene for smoke inhalation and light burns.  The team were in attendance for around one hour.
