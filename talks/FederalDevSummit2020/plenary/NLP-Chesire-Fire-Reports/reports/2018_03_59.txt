 Fire at landfill site in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/03/2018 - 22:36 Location: Silver Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 10.35pm firefighters were called to a fire at a landfill site on Silver Lane in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered two piles of cables on fire. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. The crew were at the scene for around 25 minutes.
