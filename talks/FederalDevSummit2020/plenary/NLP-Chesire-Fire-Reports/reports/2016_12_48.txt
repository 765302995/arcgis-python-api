Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/12/2016 - 21:21 Location: Troutbeck Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A hose reel jet was used to put out a wheelie bin fire in an open area off Troutbeck Avenue. Firefighters believe the fire was started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.  Always follow our wheelie bin safety advice.
