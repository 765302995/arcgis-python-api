Chip pan fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 30/04/2016 - 18:00 Location: Nutfield Avenue, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Sandbach Firefighters were called to a report of a flat fire on Nutfield Avenue, Crewe.  When they arrived they found that there had been a fire involving a chip pan but this had gone out. The flat was fitted with smoke alarms that had raised the alarm. There were no reports of any injuries at this incident.
