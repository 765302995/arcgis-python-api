 Small fire involving loose cable and a shopping trolley in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/11/2019 - 07:44 Location: Stanley Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 7.45am firefghters were called to a report of a fire in Stanley Street, Warrington.  On arrival the crew found a small amount of loose cable on fire in a shopping trolley at a telephone exchange.  Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 559049, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
