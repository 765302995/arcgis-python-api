 Fridge fire at supermarket in Middlewich Time of Incident: 24/07/2019 - 19:02 Location: Newton Bank, Middlewich. Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel. One fire engine attended from Middlewich. One fire engine attended from Winsford. Firefighters were called to a supermarket in Newton Bank in Middlewich following reports of a freezer on fire on the shop floor. The fire was extinguished using four breathing apparatus, one covering jet and two hose reel jets. Crews then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots and used two large fans to clear the smoke. Fire crews were in attendance for approximately one hour and 50 minutes.
