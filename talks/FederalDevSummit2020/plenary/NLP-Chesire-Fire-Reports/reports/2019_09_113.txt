 Two vehicle collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 09/09/2019 - 21:30 Location: Badger Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Crews attended to a two vehicle collision on Badger Avenue in Crewe.  On arrival, crews found no one was trapped in their vehicle.  One woman and one man were treated at the scene by paramedics with injuries believed to be minor.   The vehicles and the area was made safe by firefighters.
