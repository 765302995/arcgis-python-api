Car on fire, Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 07/10/2017 - 08:37 Location: Chelford Road, Holmes Chapel Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called to reports of a car on fire on Chelford Road, near Holmes Chapel. They arrived to find smoke billowing from the engine so used a single hose reel to make the vehicle safe. They remained at the scene for a further 15 minutes to monitor the engine's temperature.
