 Digger fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/12/2018 - 20:19 Location: Manchester Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Lymm and one from Warrington Firefighters extinguished a trailer carrying a digger well alight on Manchester Road in Warrington.  Wearing one breathing apparatus, crews used a hose reel to bring the fire under control and made the area safe.
