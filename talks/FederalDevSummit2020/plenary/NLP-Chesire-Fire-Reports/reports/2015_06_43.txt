Stack of building panels and skip on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/06/2015 - 15:31 Location: Charon Way, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, one from Widnes, one from Stockton Heath A skip and building panels were involved in a fire in Westbrook.  There were multiple seats of fire which were put out by firefighters, three wearing breathing apparatus, using a main jet and a hose reel jet.  Crews were at the incident for approximately four hours and the fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
