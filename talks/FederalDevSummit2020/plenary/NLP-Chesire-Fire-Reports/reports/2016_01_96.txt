Flat fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 03/01/2016 - 16:53 Location: Croft Court, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester One man was rescued by firefighters and treated for smoke inhalation after a flat fire in Ellesmere Port.  The fire was caused by a pan on a cooker which had been left unattended and two of the crew wore breathing apparatus and used a hose reel to extinguish the blaze which was confined to the pan but had caused a lot of smoke to fill the property.  The casualty was taken to hospital and the crews were on the scene for around an hour. Kitchen fire safety advice
