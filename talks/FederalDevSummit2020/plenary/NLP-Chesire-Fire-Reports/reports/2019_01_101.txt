 Motorcyclist trapped underneath lorry in Woolston Time of Incident: 22/01/2019 - 05:54 Location: Kingsland Grange, Warrington Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Lymm and one from Birchwood and the major rescue unit and support pump from Winsford Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision between a lorry and a motorcyclist on Kingsland Grange, Woolston. The motorcyclist had become trapped underneath the lorry and crews helped to extract the casualty from underneath the lorry before handing them into the care of paramedics.  Crews were at the scene for 30 minutes.
