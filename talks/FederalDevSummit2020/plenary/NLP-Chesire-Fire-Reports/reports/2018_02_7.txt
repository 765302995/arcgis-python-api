 Car fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/02/2018 - 15:41 Location: Northwich Road, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a  three vehicle collision at the entrance to an industrial estate in Northwich Road, Preston Brook. One of the cars appeared to be smoking and crews used a hose reel to damp it down.
