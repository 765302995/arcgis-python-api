 Collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 18/07/2018 - 11:21 Location: M6 Southbound between junctions 19 and 18 Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Lymm and one from Greater Manchester At approximately 11:20 firefighters from Lymm and Greater Manchester were called to reports of a collision on the M6 southbound carriageway near Knutsford.  Crews used electronic cutting to release one casualty who was mechanically trapped. The also released a further three casualties who were medically trapped in their vehicles. All of the casualties have since been passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately 50 minutes.
