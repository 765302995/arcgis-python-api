 Fire extinguished at a flat in Chester Time of Incident: 17/02/2018 - 04:04 Location: Victoria Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Powey Lane Firefighters in Chester were called to reports of a fire at a flat on Victoria Road in the early hours of the morning. Crews used breathing apparatus and one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which is believed to have been caused by a cooking pan left unattended on the stove. A full evacuation of the premises was put in place as a precaution.
