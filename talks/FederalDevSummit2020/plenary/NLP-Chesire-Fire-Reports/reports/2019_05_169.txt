 Tumble dryer fire at Hartford Golf Club Time of Incident: 09/05/2019 - 07:16 Location: Wallerscote Road, Weaverham, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 7.15am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the boiler room at Hartford Golf Club on Wallerscote Road in Weaverham, Northwich. The small fire involving a tumble dryer was out on arrival. Firefighters isolated the gas and electricity supplies and used a large fan to clear smoke from the premises. They were in attendance for around 45 minutes. For tumble dryer fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/tumble-dryer-fire-safety.
