 Suspected carbon monoxide leak in Poynton Time of Incident: 28/04/2019 - 20:43 Location: Barnaby Road, Poynton. Attendance: Four fire engines attended - one from Poynton, two from Stockport and one from Offerton At around 8.45pm firefighters from Poynton, Stockport and Offerton attended reports of a suspected carbon monoxide leak at a house on Barnaby Road.  On arrival at the scene two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used a carbon monoxide detector to search the address.   Following a thorough search of the address, along with neighbouring properties, the team were unable to find any leaks.  Five casualties were treated at the scene by paramedics as a precautionary measure.   The team were in attendance for approximately one hour.
