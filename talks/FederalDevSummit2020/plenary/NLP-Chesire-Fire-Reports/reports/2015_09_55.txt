Car well alight in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/09/2015 - 23:34 Location: Tudor Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Two hose reel jets were used to extinguish a fire involving a car on Tudor Road in Runcorn.  The car was well alight. Police attended this incident.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
