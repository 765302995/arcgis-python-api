Van engine on fire on M62 near Warrington Time of Incident: 12/03/2016 - 10:10 Location: M62 east at Westbrook, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington The engine of a van was completely destroyed in a fire on the sliproad of the M62 east between junctions 8 and 7. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put it out.
