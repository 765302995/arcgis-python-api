Woman trapped in a car Time of Incident: 28/08/2016 - 15:08 Location: Near the Swan Inn, Macclesfield Road, Kettleshulme. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one fire engine from Whaley Bridge (Derbyshire Fire and Rescue Service) Firefighters were called to a report of a collision near to the Swan Inn, Macclesfield Road, Kettleshulme.  When they arrived crews found that the collision involved one car. A woman was trapped inside. Firefighters rescued the woman who was then transferred to hospital by Air Ambulance.
