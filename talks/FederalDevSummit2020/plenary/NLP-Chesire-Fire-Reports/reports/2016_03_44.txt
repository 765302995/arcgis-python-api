Chimney fire in Winterley Time of Incident: 22/03/2016 - 19:31 Location: Pool Lane, Winterley Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Embers were seen coming from the top of a chimney and firefighters were called to the scene. A wood burning stove was involved and there had been a small fire in the chimney. Crews checked the loft space with a thermal imaging camera to check there were no hot spots in that area.  Always follow our Chimneys safety advice.
