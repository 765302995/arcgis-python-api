 Van fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/04/2018 - 00:57 Location: Old Liverpool Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Van fire in WarringtonFire crews were called to Old Liverpool Road, Warrington, after reports of a van fire. Crews found a removals van well alight and the crew, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
