 Peat bog fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 03/06/2018 - 13:31 Location: Cumber Lane/Moor Lane, Wilmslow Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Wilmslow and one from Ellesmere Port At around 1.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a peat bog on Cumber Lane/Moor Lane in Wilmslow. On arrival the crews discovered that the fire measured approximately 20 metres by five metres. Firefighters used a main jet, water back packs and beaters to extinguish the fire. They also used shovels and spades to dig out the affected area. The crews then damped the area down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around two hours.
