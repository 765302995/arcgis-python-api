Fire underneath a car in Altrincham Time of Incident: 29/09/2016 - 09:58 Location: Lymm Road, Little Bollington, Altrincham Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath A fire broke out underneath a car on Lymm Road and firefighters were called to the scene. Crews put on breathing apparatus and used one hose reel jet to put out the fire.
