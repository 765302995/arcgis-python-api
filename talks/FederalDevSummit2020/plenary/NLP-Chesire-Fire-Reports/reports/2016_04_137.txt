House fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 05/04/2016 - 18:15 Location: Wardle Mews, Middlewich. Attendance: A fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Wardle Mews, Middlewich.  When they arrived they found that there had been a fire involving a cooker but this had gone out. The property was fitted with smoke detectors that helped raise the alarm.  Crews carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving.
