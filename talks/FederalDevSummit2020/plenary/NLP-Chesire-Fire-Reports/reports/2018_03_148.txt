 Commercial building fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 02/03/2018 - 17:01 Location: Middlewich Road, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich and one fire engine attended from Winsford Shortly after 5pm firefighters from Northwich and Winsford attended reports of a commercial building fire on Middlewich Road, Northwich. On arrival at the scene the crews found that the fire involved a large amount of waste material which was close to the exterior wall of a commercial building. The team used two hose reels and thermal image camera to extinguish the fire and remained at scene for one hour and fifty five minutes.
