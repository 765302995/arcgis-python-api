Bonfire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 31/10/2017 - 23:32 Location: Colshaw Drive, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish a small bonfire which had been left unattended on the Green, off Colshaw Drive, Wilmslow. If anyone has any information about who may have started this fire please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
