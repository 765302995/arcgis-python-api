 Bungalow fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 21/07/2019 - 20:02 Location: Carleton Road, Poynton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton. One fire engine from Macclesfield, One fire engine from Wilmslow. One fire engine from Offerton. Firefighters attended a fire in a bungalow on Carleton Road, Poynton. The fire involved wheelie bins which spread to underneath a study area of the bungalow.  Crews used one covering jet, two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to extinguish the fire. Three casualties were treated for smoke inhalation by firefighters. Crews were on scene for approximately five and a half hours.
