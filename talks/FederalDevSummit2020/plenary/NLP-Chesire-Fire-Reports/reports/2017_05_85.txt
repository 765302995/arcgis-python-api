Caravan well alight in Bewsey, Warrington Time of Incident: 17/05/2017 - 18:57 Location: Summerfield Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath A caravan was well alight, so firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Police attended this incident.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately so anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
