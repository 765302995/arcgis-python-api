 Vehicle fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 13/07/2018 - 10:39 Location: M6, Junction 17 Northbound Attendance: Three fire engines attended - One from Crewe, one from Sandbach and one from Middlewich At 10:40am Firefighters from Crewe, Middlewich and Sandbach attended reports of a vehicle fire on the M6 northbound near to junction 17. On arrival crews found one car which was well alight. The team used two hose reel jets and two breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. They were in attendance for approximately one hour.
