 Chimney fire, Winsford Time of Incident: 13/01/2018 - 13:59 Location: Chester Road, Winsford Attendance: One appliance from Winsford Firefighters were called to deal with a chimney fire at a house in Winsford. Attempts were initially made to extinguish the flames using a steam method but, after proving unsuccessful, chimney rods were used to put out the fire.  Advice on chimney fire safety can be found at our website.
