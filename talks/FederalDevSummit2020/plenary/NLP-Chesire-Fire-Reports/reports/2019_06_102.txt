 Tumble dryer fire at an eatery in Crewe Time of Incident: 09/06/2019 - 12:41 Location: Weston Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Shortly after 12.40pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from a tumble dryer at the Rockery Wood eatery on Weston Road in Crewe. On arrival crews discovered a small fire contained within the tumble dryer on the first floor of the building. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The tumble dryer was removed from the building. Firefighters used a large fan to clear smoke from the Rockery Wood and were in attendance for nearly an hour. For tumble dryer fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/tumble-dryer-fire-safety.
