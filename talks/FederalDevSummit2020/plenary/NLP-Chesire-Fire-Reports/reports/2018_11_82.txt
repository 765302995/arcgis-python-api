 Small fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 16/11/2018 - 19:10 Location: Lanark Gardens, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh Firefighters dealt with a small fire in the open at Upton Rocks park. They used a single hose reel to extinguish the fire in the playground, and notified police colleagues due to the suspected deliberate ignition.
