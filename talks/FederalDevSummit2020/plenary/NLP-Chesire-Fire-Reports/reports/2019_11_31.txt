 Fire at a domestic property on a farm in Audlem Time of Incident: 22/11/2019 - 21:22 Location: Bagley Lane, Audlem Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Audlem and one from Nantwich At around 9.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a domestic property on a farm in Bagley Lane, Audlem.  On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved a beam above a fireplace.  Two firefighters each wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Once the fire was out firefighters exposed areas around the beam and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.  They also cleared smoke from the property with a large fan.  Firefighters were in attendance for nearly three hours.
