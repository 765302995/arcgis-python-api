Campervan on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/07/2016 - 16:20 Location: Moorside, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A campervan containing cylinders was on fire in Latchford. Two LPG cylinders were taken out of the vehicle as firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to tackle the fire. Two butane gas cylinders were also on board but not involved in the fire and were cooled by the crew using a hose reel jet. The vehicle battery was isolated.
