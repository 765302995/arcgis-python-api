 Rubbish fire in Chester Time of Incident: 22/03/2018 - 23:16 Location: Willow Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 11.15pm firefighters were called to a rubbish fire on Willow Road in Chester. The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the small quantity of rubbish that was alight in the open. They were at the scene for around 10 minutes.
