House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/12/2015 - 20:59 Location: Harrison Close, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a house on Harrison Close, Warrington.  When they arrived they found that there had been a fire in the kitchen involving some oven gloves. Crews carried out a Home Safety Assesment before leaving.
