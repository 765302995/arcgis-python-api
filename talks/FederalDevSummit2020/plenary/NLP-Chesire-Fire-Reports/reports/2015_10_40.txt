Smoke coming from cooker in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/10/2015 - 03:50 Location: Callands Road, Burtonwood and Westbrook, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine fron Warrington A cooker which had been left on a low heat caused smoke in a building in Warrington. Automatic smoke alarms raised the alarm and firefighters gained access to a kitchen to turn the cooker off. Smoke was then removed from the building using a large fan.
