Bathroom fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 10/06/2017 - 17:02 Location: Wesley Place, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and two from Crewe There was a fire in the bathroom of a ground floor flat  Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus. Gas and electric were isolated.  The fire crews use two hose reels and a large fan to bring the fire under control. Police and ambulance attended.  Two casualties were assessed  by paramedics.
