Deliberate skip fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/02/2015 - 23:00 Location: Toll Bar Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish a small fire in a wooden skip.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and the police were contacted as a result. Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
