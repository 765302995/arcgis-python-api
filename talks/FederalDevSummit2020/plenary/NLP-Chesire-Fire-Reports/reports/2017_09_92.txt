Small fire in Leighton Hospital laundry room Time of Incident: 04/09/2017 - 18:07 Location: Leighton Hospital, Middlewich Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe A small smouldering fire in a laundry room was believed to have been caused by a build up of cotton and fluff. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
