Derelict building fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/09/2015 - 17:27 Location: Chester Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes One hose reel was used to extinguish a small fire in a derelict building in Runcorn.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
