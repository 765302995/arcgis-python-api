 Small vehicle fire on M56 slip road Time of Incident: 21/10/2019 - 16:30 Location: M56,westbound, junction 11 Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn, one fire engine attended from Widnes and one fire engine attended from Warrington. Firefighters were called to a fire involving a car on the slip road of the M56 west at junction 11. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to tackle the fire. They then damped down the vehicle to ensure it didn't reignite.
