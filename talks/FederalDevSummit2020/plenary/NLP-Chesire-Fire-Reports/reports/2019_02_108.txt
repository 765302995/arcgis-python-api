 Collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 10/02/2019 - 13:15 Location: Rossmore Road West, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port At around 1.15pm firefighters were called to reports of car having collided with an electrical junction box on Rossmore Road West in Ellesmere Port. On arrival crews discovered that no-one was trapped inside the saloon car. A cordon was set up and the junction box was deemed to be safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour. Police and paramedics also attended.
