Shop fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 03/01/2017 - 19:35 Location: Age UK, Chester Road in Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port, Chester and Runcorn plus Chester's Hydraulic Platform. Firefighters responded to a report of a fire on Chester Road in Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving the ground floor of an Age UK shop. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used hose reels and main water jets to tackle the fire. The road was closed during the incident.  An investigation will now be launched to find the cause of the blaze.
