Van well alight on Town Lane in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/04/2017 - 23:59 Location: Town Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes A van was well alight on Town Lane in Widnes.  One firefighter wore breathing apparatus. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately.  If you can help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, or call Cheshire Police on 101.
