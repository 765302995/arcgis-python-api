 Fire involving rubbish and grass in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 19:53 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 7.55pm firefighters were called to a report of rubbish being on fire on a footpath on Dundalk Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered a small refuse and grass fire. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour
