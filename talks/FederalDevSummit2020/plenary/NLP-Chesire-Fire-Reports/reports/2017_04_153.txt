Deep fat fryer fire Time of Incident: 01/04/2017 - 15:54 Location: Hulme Square, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Hulme Square, Macclesfield.   Firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a dry powder extinguisher to fight the fire.  A positive pressure ventilation fan was also used to clear smoke.
