 Vehicle fire in Wimboldsley Time of Incident: 02/08/2018 - 14:58 Location: Nantwich Road, Wimboldsley. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and one from Middlewich Vehicle fire in WimboldsleyFirefighters from Winsford and Middlewich extinguished a vehicle fire on the A530, Nantwich Road, in Wimboldsley. The team were detained at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.
