 Road traffic collision on the A534 in Sandbach Time of Incident: 01/05/2018 - 09:32 Location: A534, Sandbach. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe and one fire engine from Sandbach. Shortly after 9.30am firefighters from Crewe and Sandbach attended a road traffic collision on the A534 in Sandbach. The collision involved one small commercial vehicle which was on its side and there was one person trapped in the vehicle. The team released the casualty and they were handed over to the care of paramedics. Crews were in attendance for approximately twenty minutes.
