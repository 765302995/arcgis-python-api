Cars on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/03/2017 - 22:27 Location: Rawdon Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Two cars were on fire in a Rawdon Close garage. The fire also spread to the garage itself. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets. Once the fire was out the area was damped down. Police were informed as it is suspected the fire was started deliberately.
