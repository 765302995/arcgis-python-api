 Person trapped in Middlewich Time of Incident: 02/10/2018 - 18:53 Location: Prosperity Way, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Shortly before 7pm firefighters were called to reports of a person trapped in the grounds of a substation on Prosperity Way, Middlewich.  Crews attended the scene and found that a 15-year-old girl had climbed over an external fence and fallen.  The team used bolt cutters to open the gate and release the casualty; she was then passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service.  Firefighters were in attendance for approximately 50 minutes.
