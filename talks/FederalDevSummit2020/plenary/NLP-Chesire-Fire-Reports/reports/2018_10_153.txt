 Commercial building fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 02/10/2018 - 11:14 Location: Dene Drive, Winsford. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Winsford and one from Northwich At 11.15am firefighters from Winsford and Northwich were called to reports of an electrical fire at Winsford Cross Shopping Centre. Crews attended the scene and found that there had been a small fire involving an electrical light fitting. The fire had been extinguished by staff using a C02 extinguisher. The team isolated the electric and a small number of shoppers were evacuated as a precautionary measure. Firefighters then removed the ceiling to check to see if the fire had spread. Crews were in attendance at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
