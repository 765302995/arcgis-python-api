Building fire in Alpraham Time of Incident: 29/01/2017 - 01:00 Location: Hilbre Bank, Alpraham. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Crewe and Nantwich Firefighters responded to a report of a building fire on Hilbre Bank, Alpraham.  When they arrived crews found that expansion foam around a boiler was melting. Crews removed the foam and checked that there was no chance of a further fire before leaving.
