 Propane cylinders fire in Ridley Time of Incident: 08/04/2018 - 20:58 Location: Wrexham Road, Ridley Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Tarporley and one from Malpas At around 9pm firefighters were called to a fire in the open on Wrexham Road in Ridley. On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved three propane cylinders. An additional three propane cylinders and an oil-fired boiler were affected by the heat from the fire. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a thermal imaging camera and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and cool down the propane cylinders. They were in attendance for approximately three hours.
