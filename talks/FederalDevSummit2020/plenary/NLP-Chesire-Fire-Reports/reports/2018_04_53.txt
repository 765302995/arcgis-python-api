 Recycling lorry on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 20/04/2018 - 09:44 Location: Ring Road, Great Boughton, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Recycling lorry on fire in ChesterFirefighters tackled a fire involving a recycling lorry in Great Boughton. Ring Road was closed while crews dealt with the fire. They used two hose reels to put out the blaze, with two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus.  One fire engine will remain at the scene until the vehicle is recovered.
