Smoke from boiler in Chester Time of Incident: 24/06/2017 - 18:09 Location: Hockenhall Hall, Hockenhull Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Tarporley Smoke from a boiler required firefighters to attend. The crew wore breathing apparatus and used a large fan to remove smoke from the ground floor outbuilding. The boiler was isolated and hot ash was removed from the grate and chimney.
