 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/05/2018 - 05:13 Location: Henshall Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters attended to a wheelie bin on fire which also affected a fence panel on a garden fence. A hose reel was used to extinguish the fire which was located on Henshall Avenue in Warrington.
