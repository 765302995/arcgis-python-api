Pan of oil on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 13/07/2016 - 23:32 Location: Cardigan Close, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A pan of oil caught fire in a kitchen in Macclesfield, setting fire to the cooker hood. Firefighters headed to the scene as the occupiers of the house waited safely outside. Crews used a positive pressure ventilator to clear the kitchen of smoke and proceeded to make the scene safe.
