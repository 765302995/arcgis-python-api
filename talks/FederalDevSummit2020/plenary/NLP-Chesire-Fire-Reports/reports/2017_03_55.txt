Rescue from embankment in Handforth Time of Incident: 24/03/2017 - 13:56 Location: Viewlands Drive, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and the rope rescue unit from Knutsford A lady had fallen down an embankment and was rescued by emergency services.  Wilmslow firefighters and the rope rescue unit from Knutsford used rope rescue equipment and a longboard to bring the lady, who had sustained arm injuries, up to the surface and into the care of paramedics.
