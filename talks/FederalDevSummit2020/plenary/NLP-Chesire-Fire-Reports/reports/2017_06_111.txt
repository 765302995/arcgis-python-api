Smouldering fire in substation in Nantwich Time of Incident: 14/06/2017 - 09:11 Location: Pall Mall, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich A small fire involving an electrical substation required firefighters to attend. A cordon was set up around the area and a burnt windowsill, which was smouldering, was cut back. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the area and Scottish Power were requested to attend.
