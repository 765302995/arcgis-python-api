 Vehicle fire on the M6 near Warrington Time of Incident: 22/06/2018 - 19:44 Location: M6 Northbound junction 21 to 21a Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington attended reports of a vehicle fire on the M6 northbound between junctions 21 and 21a. On arrival crews found that the vehicle was not on fire, but the engine had blown. The team made the vehicle safe and used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. The team were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes.
