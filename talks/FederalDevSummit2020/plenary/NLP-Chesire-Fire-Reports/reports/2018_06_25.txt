 Grass fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 27/06/2018 - 17:56 Location: M56 Eastbound Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Shortly before 6pm firefighters from Powey Lane attended reports of a fire on the eastbound carriageway of the M56 near Frodsham. On arrival crews found that a grass verge at the side of the road was well alight. The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and then spent  20 minutes damping down to ensure the fire did not reignite. the team were in  attendance for approximately one hour.
