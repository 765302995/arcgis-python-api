 Cooking left unattended in Crewe Time of Incident: 05/01/2018 - 00:25 Location: West Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Nantwich Firefighters had to force entry into a flat on West Street after the occupant had left cooking unattended. There was a woman in the flat. Firefighters and paramedics treated her for smoke inhalation as a precaution. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to put out the small fire, and a high pressure fan to clear smoke.  Kitchen fire safety advice
