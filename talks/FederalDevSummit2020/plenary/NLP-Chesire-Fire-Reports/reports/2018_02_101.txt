 Washing machine on fire in Clutton Time of Incident: 06/02/2018 - 14:24 Location: Township Close, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas and one from Chester Firefighters were called to a fire in a washing machine on Township Close, Clutton, Chester. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was confined to the washing machine. The property was then checked using a thermal imaging camera and a large fan was used to clear smoke from the property. An ambulance was called to carry out a precautionary check on one casualty. Crews were at the scene for one hour and 20 minutes. Washing machine fire safety
