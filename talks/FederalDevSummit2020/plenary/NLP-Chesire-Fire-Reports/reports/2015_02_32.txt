Chimney fire in Chester Time of Incident: 18/02/2015 - 02:52 Location: King Street, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port plus the hydraulic platform. Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on King Street in Chester. When they arrived they found the property full of smoke and believed the source was the log burner.  Crews wearing breathing apparatus removed the back of the fireplace to access the flames. Hose reel jets were then used to tackle the blaze.
