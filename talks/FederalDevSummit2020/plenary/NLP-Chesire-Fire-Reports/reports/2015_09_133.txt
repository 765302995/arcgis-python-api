Gas leak in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/09/2015 - 17:14 Location: Norris Street, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a gas leak at a property in Warrington.  On arrival there was deemed no fire risk at the premises but the crew assisted with first aid to one woman at the scene until paramedics arrived and the incident was handed over to National Grid.
