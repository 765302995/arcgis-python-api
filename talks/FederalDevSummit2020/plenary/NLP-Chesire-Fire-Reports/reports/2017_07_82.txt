Kitchen fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/07/2017 - 19:43 Location: Festival Way, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a kitchen fire on Festival Way, Runcorn. On arrival crews found a small fire behind the back of a cooker and firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Crews then used a large fan to ventilate the property.
