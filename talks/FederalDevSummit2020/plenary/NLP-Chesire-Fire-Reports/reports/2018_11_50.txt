 Flooding at a block of flats in Knutsford Time of Incident: 21/11/2018 - 18:52 Location: Hollow Lane, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Shortly before 7pm firefighters were called to reports of flooding at a block of flats in Knutsford. Crews from Lymm attended the scene and on arrival they found that the flooding was affecting two flats and a car park area. The team isolated the electrics at the property and made the area safe. United Utilities were also called to the scene to assist with the leak. Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately two hours.
