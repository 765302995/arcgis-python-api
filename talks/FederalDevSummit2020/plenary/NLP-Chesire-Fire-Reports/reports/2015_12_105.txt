Flooding in Farndon Time of Incident: 04/12/2015 - 13:00 Location: Townfield Lane, Farndon Attendance: Water rescue boat from Chester, water rescue boat from Warrington, fire engines from Chester, Warrington and Northwich. Cheshire Fire and Rescue Service are currently in Farndon where the River Dee has flooded. Fire service personnel are using water rescue boats to visit properties in the flood area and offer reassurance and assistance where needed.  We are working closely with other agencies, including North Wales Fire and Rescue Service, the Environment Agency, Natural Resource Wales, Cheshire West & Chester Council, Cheshire Constabulary and North Wales Police to manage flood risks and keep residents safe and well.
