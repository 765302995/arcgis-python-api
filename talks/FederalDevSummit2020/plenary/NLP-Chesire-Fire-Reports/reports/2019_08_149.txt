 Unattended cooking fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/08/2019 - 21:27 Location: Sheffield Close, Warrington. WA5 1XP Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Unattended cooking caused significant smoke in a house at Sheffield Close, Warrington.  Firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and then used a large fan to ventilate the property.
