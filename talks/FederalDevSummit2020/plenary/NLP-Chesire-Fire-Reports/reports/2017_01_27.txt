Small bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/02/2017 - 01:07 Location: Morgan Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters used one hose reel jet to put out a small fire in a large industrial bin.  This fire was believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information, please call Crime Stoppers on 0800 555 111.
