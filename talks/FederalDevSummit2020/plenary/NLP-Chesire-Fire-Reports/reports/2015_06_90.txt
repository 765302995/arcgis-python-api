Collision in Knutsford Time of Incident: 15/06/2015 - 15:54 Location: Mobberley Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters provided first aid to a woman involved in a collision on the junction outside Knutsford Fire Station.  The collision involved a lady on a mobility scooter and a car and trailer. The crew provided casualty care before leaving the woman in the hands of paramedics.
