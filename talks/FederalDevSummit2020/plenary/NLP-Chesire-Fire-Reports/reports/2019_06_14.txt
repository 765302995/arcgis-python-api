 Fire in a garden in Widnes Time of Incident: 27/06/2019 - 20:20 Location: Gutticar Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At 8.20pm firefghters were called to a report of a fire in the front garden of a residential property on Gutticar Road, Widnes. On arrival the crew found that it was a small fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
