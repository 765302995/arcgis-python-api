Fuse box fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 02/01/2017 - 14:24 Location: Hawthorn Lane, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one appliance from Winsford A fire involving an electrical fuse box took place in the basement of a domestic property on Hawthorne Lane, Wilmslow. Firefighters used four breathing apparatus and one hose reel jet to bring the fire under control.
