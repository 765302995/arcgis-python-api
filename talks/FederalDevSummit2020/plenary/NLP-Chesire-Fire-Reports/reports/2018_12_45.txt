 Man trapped under train in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 19/12/2018 - 17:23 Location: Hooton Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Five fire engines attended - two from Ellesmere Port, one from Chester, one from Powey Lane and one from Winsford. Shortly before 5.30pm firefighters were dispatched to reports of an incident at Hooton train station in Ellesmere Port. Crews attended the scene and found that a man had become trapped under a train. The team used specialist equipment to release the casualty. He was then passed to the care of the HART team from North West Ambulance Service to release the casualty. Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately one hour.
