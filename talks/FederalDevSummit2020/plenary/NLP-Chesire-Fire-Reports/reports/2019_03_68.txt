 Two-car collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/03/2019 - 08:59 Location: Earle Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At around 9am firefighters were called to reports of a collision in Earle Street in Crewe. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved two cars. Firefighters helped a casualty get out of one of the cars before handing them over to the care of paramedics. Police also attended the incident. The fire crews made the two vehicles safe.
