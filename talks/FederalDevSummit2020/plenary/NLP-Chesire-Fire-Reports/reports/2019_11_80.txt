 Fire involving a van on a transporter in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/11/2019 - 23:18 Location: Kingsland Grange, Woolston, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Birchwood and one from Lymm At around 11.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a van on a transporter in Kingsland Grange, Woolston, Warrington.  On arrival crews found them well alight.  A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet.  The abandoned van and the transporter were then damped down to make them safe.  No-one was injured as a result of the fire.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.  It is believed that the fire may have been started deliberately.  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 564403, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
