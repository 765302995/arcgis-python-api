 Smoked-logged flat in Crewe Time of Incident: 10/07/2018 - 12:07 Location: Worsdell Close, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At around 12.05pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a cooker at a flat in Worsdell Close, Crewe. The fire, which was caused by a bag of clothing being left on an electric hob, was out on arrival. The flat was heavily smoke logged. Firefighters used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the flat and were in attendance for around an hour.
