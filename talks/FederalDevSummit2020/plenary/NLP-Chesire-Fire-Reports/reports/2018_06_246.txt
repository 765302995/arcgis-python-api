 Fire at a farm in Tarporley Time of Incident: 03/06/2018 - 06:48 Location: Peckforton Hall Lane, Tarporley Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Tarporley and one from Nantwich At around 6.50am firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a farm on Peckforton Hall Lane in Tarporley. On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved two wooden sheds that were approximately five metres by three metres. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. They then turned over the burnt wood and damped the area down. A large quantity of birds died as a result of the fire. The cause of the fire is not believed to be suspicious. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour and forty five minutes.
