car collided with a lamp post in Malpas Time of Incident: 01/11/2015 - 04:58 Location: A41 at Hampton, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and one fire engine from Whitchurch (Shropshire Fire and Rescue Service) One fire engine from Malpas and one fire engine from Whitchurch (Shropshire Fire and Rescue Service) were sent to an incident involving a road traffic collision on the A41 at Hampton, Malpas. A car had collided with a lamp post.  On arrival the fire crews found that there was nobody trapped and there was no fire. The fire crews made the vehicle safe. A thermal imaging camera was used to check for fire.
