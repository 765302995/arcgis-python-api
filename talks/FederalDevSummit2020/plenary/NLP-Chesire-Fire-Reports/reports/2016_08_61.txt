Fire at Mid Cheshire College in Winsford Time of Incident: 18/08/2016 - 19:59 Location: Mid Cheshire College, High Street, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford and Northwich Two industrial bins were on fire at Mid Cheshire College. The fire spread to a building. Fire crews used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to extinguish the fire. A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the building.
