 Tumble dryer fire fire in Willaston Time of Incident: 18/02/2019 - 10:02 Location: Victoria Mill Drive, Willaston. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe. One fire engine attended from Nantwich. Firefighters were called to a tumble dryer fire in a garage at an address in Victoria Mill Drive in Willaston. Crews used two breathing apparatus, one covering jet and one hose reel to fight the fire. They checked the fire and smoke hadn't spread to the adjoining property and continued to turn over debris and damp down to ensure the fire didn't reignite. A woman was assessed by paramedics.
