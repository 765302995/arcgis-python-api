 Light aircraft crash in Winsford Time of Incident: 14/03/2018 - 12:21 Location: Vauxhall Way, Winsford Attendance: Three fire engines attended - two from Winsford and one from Northwich At 12.18pm firefighters were called to reports of a light aircraft coming down in a field near Vauxhall Way in Winsford.  Crews discovered that the solitary occupant - the pilot - had exited the rotary wing aircraft.  The aircraft was not on fire.  The pilot was assessed at the scene by North West Air Ambulance paramedics and is believed to have only suffered minor injuries. They did not go to hospital.  Crews made the aircraft safe and were at the scene for an hour-and-a-half.
