 Rubbish fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/03/2018 - 11:27 Location: Pitts Heath Lane, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to the intentional burning of refuse which had got out of hand in a field off Pitts Heath Lane, Runcorn. Crews used a hose reel to put out the fire.
