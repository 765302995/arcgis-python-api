 House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/04/2018 - 20:59 Location: Marshall Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and Penketh Fire crews attended to an address well alight on Marshall Avenue in Warrington.  Firefighters used one hose reel, two breathing apparatus and a positive pressure ventilation fan to put out the fire.  The fire is believed to have been caused by an oven glove left in the oven.
