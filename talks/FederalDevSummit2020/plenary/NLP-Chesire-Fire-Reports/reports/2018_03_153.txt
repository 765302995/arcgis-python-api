 Chimney fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 01/03/2018 - 21:54 Location: Longview Avenue, Alsager Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar At around 9.55pm a fire engine from Alsager Fire Station attended a chimney fire at Longview Avenue, Alsager. Crews used a ladder, a thermal image camera and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  They were at the scene for almost an hour. There are no reports of any injuries sustained as a result of the fire.  For information about chimney safety visit Chimney fire safety advice
