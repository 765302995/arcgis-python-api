 Bonfire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 31/05/2019 - 18:15 Location: Overpool Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a bonfire which was out of control on Overpool Road, Ellesmere Port. The fire was approximately 3m by 3m and had started to burn the outside of an outbuilding. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.
