 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 20/05/2019 - 19:03 Location: Ditchfield Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly after midnight firefighters were called to a report of a wheelie bin being on fire near to a house on Ditchfield Road in Widnes  On arrival the crew found the bin well alight  Firefighters used water back packs to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
