Chip pan fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 16/01/2017 - 19:15 Location: Ruskin Road, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines from Congleton Firefighters were called to a report of a chip pan fire at a house on Ruskin Road. A lady was given oxygen therapy for smoke inhalation. The property was  ventilated and two people were taken to hospital as a precaution.  Always follow our chip pan safety advice.
