Light fitting fire in Handforth Time of Incident: 09/11/2016 - 08:15 Location: Redesmere Road, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) A fire involving an electrical light fitting broke out on the ground floor of a building in Handforth. The fire was out when crews arrived but they stayed on scene to monitor the light fitting with a thermal imaging camera, checking for any remaining hot spots of fire.
