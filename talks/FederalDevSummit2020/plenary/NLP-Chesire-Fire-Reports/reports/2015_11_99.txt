Unattended cooking fills Runcorn flat with smoke Time of Incident: 10/11/2015 - 13:28 Location: Camelot Way, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a flat and rescued a dog. Cooking had been left unattended. The crews had to gain entry where they found the black and white terrier in the smoke-filled flat. The dog was taken out and left with a neighbour and the smoke cleared using a high pressure fan.  Please, stay safe, keep your pets safe - never leave cooking unattended - Kitchen fire safety
