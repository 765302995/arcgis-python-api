Fire at a Scrap yard in Northwich Time of Incident: 06/11/2017 - 10:28 Location: Chapel Street, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a scrapyard on Chapel Street, Wincham, which involved a oil storage tank. One crew from Northwich  attended the scene and used foam to extinguish the fire. Once the fire had been extinguished the crew then used one hose reel jet to cool the tank.
