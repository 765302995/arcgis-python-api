House fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/11/2016 - 20:45 Location: Silverne Drive, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fsire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews responded to a report of a house fire on Silverne Drive, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire in the bathroom of the property. Crews used hose reel jets to put the fire out.
