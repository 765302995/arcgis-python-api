 Collision in Lower Whitley Time of Incident: 11/11/2018 - 04:54 Location: A49, Tarporley Road, Lower Whitley, near Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Lymm and Birchwood Shortly before 5am firefighters from Lymm and Birchwood attended reports of a one vehicle collision on the A49, Tarporley Road in Lower Whitley. Crews attended and assisted with making the vehicle and scene safe. One person was treated by paramedics. The team were detained at the scene for approximately 30 minutes.
