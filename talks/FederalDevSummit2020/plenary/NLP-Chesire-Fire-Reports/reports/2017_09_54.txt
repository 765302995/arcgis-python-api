Chimney fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/09/2017 - 21:05 Location: Medway Road, Culcheth and Glazebury, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood A chimney fire was tackled by firefighters at a property on Medway Road. The crew put out the fire, removed the stove and swept the flue liner. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check outside on the chimney stack to monitor temperature and make sure there was no fire spread.
