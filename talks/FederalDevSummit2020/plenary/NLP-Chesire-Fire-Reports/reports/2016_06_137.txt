Commercial building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/06/2016 - 11:58 Location: Liverpool Road, Warrington Attendance: Three fire engines from Warrington, Birchwood and Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a fire in an office block in an industrial building on Liverpool Road, in Bewsey and Whitecross in Warrington. Smoke was believed to have been coming from an extractor fan unit on the ground floor. Crews liaised with the onsite engineer to remove the fan before checking the area for hot spots using a thermal imaging camera. The incident was then handed over to the onsite engineers.
