Pigeon loft fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/10/2016 - 20:46 Location: Wilmslow Crescent, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath  A wooden pigeon loft was on fire at a house on Wilmslow Crescent. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to bring the blaze under control. Crews then damped down the area. The loft measured approximately eight metres by four metres. Fortunately the house was not affected by the fire which is believed to have been started accidentally.
