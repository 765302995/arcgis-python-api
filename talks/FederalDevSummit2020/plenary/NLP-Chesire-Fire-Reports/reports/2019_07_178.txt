 Car fire on M56 Time of Incident: 02/07/2019 - 17:41 Location: M56 west between junction 11 and 12 Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a car fire on the M56 west. The fire was in the engine of a BMW estate car and crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews also closed one lane of traffic for safety reasons whilst they were tackling the blaze.
