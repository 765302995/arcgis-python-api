Smoke filled kitchen in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/09/2015 - 18:01 Location: Stewards Avenue, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one fire engine from Runcorn Two fire engines from Widnes and one fire engine from Runcorn were sent to a house fire, where a person was reported to be trapped inside the property, on Stewards Avenue in Widnes.   On arrival at the property, the fire crews found that nobody was trapped and their was an unattended pan left on the hob in the kitchen.  The property was full of smoke. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus to enter the property.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke.   Kitchen fire safety tips
