Fuse box fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 22/01/2015 - 18:46 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at the Ditton Community Centre.  The centre was evacuated while crews wearing breathing apparatus investigated the incident. They confirmed that there had been a fire in an electrical fuse box but this was already out.  A large fan was used to clear smoke from the premises.
