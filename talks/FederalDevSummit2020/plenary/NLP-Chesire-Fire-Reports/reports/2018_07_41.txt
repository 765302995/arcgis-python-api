 Fire in the garden of a manor house in Frodsham Time of Incident: 26/07/2018 - 18:34 Location: Manley Road, Frodsham Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Powey Lane At around 6.35pm firefighters were called to reports of fire in the rear garden of a manor house on Manley Road in Frodsham. On arrival crews discovered a fire measuring approximately 10 square metres. The accidental fire involved grass, bushes and fences and did not affect the manor house. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and then damped the area down to make sure it was safe. They were in attendance for around an hour.
