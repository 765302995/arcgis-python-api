 Loose roof in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/02/2019 - 14:41 Location: Halton Lea, runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a business premises in Halton Lea following reports of metal roofing becoming loose. Firefighters used a 13.5metre ladder and lowered two pieces of roofing to safety.
