Disused building fire in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 29/05/2017 - 15:49 Location: Manor Lane, Holmes Chapel Attendance: One fire engine each from Holmes Chapel, Middlewich and Congleton A small fire in a disused building on Manor Lane was tackled by firefighters. The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, searched the building to make sure there were no casualties and used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  It is believed the fire was started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
