Fire on Brickfield Park in Orford, Warrington Time of Incident: 29/08/2017 - 19:10 Location: Orford Lane, Orford, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Warrington firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a fire involving a suitcase in a wooded area on Brickfield Park.  It is believed the fire was started deliberately and anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
