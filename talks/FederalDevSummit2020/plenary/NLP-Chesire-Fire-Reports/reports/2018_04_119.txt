 Chimney fire in Mobberley Time of Incident: 08/04/2018 - 10:04 Location: Breach House Lane, Mobberley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 10.05am firefighters were called to a chimney fire at a house on Breach House Lane in Mobberley after its smoke alarms had gone off. On arrival the crew discovered that a bird's nest had fallen down the chimney and was on fire. Firefighters used water to extinguish the flames. They were in attendance for around 15 minutes. For advice on avoiding a chimney fire visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chimneys
