Road traffic collision in Sandbach Time of Incident: 09/04/2015 - 11:35 Location: Park Lane, Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and the rope rescue unit from Sandbach Firefighters assisted police in clearing the road after a road traffic collision in Sandbach.  The Service was orginally called to the incident involving a van and two cars due to the possibility of people being trapped but everyone was out of the vehicles.
