 Small fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/03/2018 - 23:21 Location: Barnes Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Fire crews were called to Barnes Road, Widnes, after reports of a fire in a field. Crews located the fire in Crow Wood Park and used a fire extinguisher to extinguish it. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information please call Cheshire Police on 101.
