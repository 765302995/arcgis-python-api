Chimney fire in Mobberley Time of Incident: 22/04/2016 - 09:01 Location: Pepper Street, Mobberley, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford A chimney fire in Pepper Street was tackled by firefighters. Chimney rods and a hose reel jet were used to put out the fire.  Always follow our chimney safety advice.
