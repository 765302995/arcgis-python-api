 House fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 03/05/2019 - 02:20 Location: Green Villa Park, Wilmslow. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Wilmslow and Greater Manchester At 2.20am firefighters from Wilmslow and Greater Manchester were called to reports of a fire at a residential property on Green Villa Park.   On arrival at the scene the crews discovered a small carpet fire in a bedroom at the property.  All occupants were accounted for and there were no reports of any injuries.  Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.  The fire is believed to have been caused by a candle on a bedside table.  Crews were detained at the scene for around 40 minutes.  For more information about candle safety visit - https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/candles-in-the-home .
