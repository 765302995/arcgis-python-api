Chimney fire well alight in Nantwich Time of Incident: 23/02/2017 - 19:27 Location: Shrewbridge Road, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters used a hose reel jet and chimney rods to extinguish a chimney fire, which was well alight, inside a mid terraced property.  A thermal imaging camera was then used to check for hotspots of fire in the loft space.  View our chimney fire safety tips
