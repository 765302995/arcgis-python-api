 Persons reported fire at a house in Poynton Time of Incident: 21/06/2018 - 20:53 Location: Lyme Road, Poynton Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Poynton, one from Bollington and one from New Mills in Derbyshire At around 8.55pm firefighters were called to a persons reported fire in a two-storey detached house on Lyme Road in Poynton. On arrival crews discovered that all persons were accounted for and that the fire involved a wood burning stove on the ground floor and smoke was logging to the first floor. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. A covering jet was also used. Crews then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots, exposing the chimney breast and assessing the first floor and the roof space, to make sure that the property was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
