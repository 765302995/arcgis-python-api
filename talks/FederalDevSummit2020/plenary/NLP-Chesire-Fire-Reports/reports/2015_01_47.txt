Road traffic collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/01/2015 - 18:04 Location: Tan house lane, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Runcorn Two people were cut free from two cars involved in a collision in Widnes. Crews removed the roof of one of the cars using cutting equipment in order to rescue the woman trapped inside. She was handed into the care of paramedics and the firefighters then removed the door of the second car in order for the man to be released. He was also handed into the care of paramedics and the crews made the scene safe before leaving.
