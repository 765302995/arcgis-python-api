Collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 20/10/2015 - 20:31 Location: Westbound carriageway of the M56. Between Junction 12 and 14. Attendance: Fire engines from Winsford and Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on the westbound carriageway of the M56.  When they arrived crews found one person trapped in a car and a further two needed treatment for injuries.  Crews used cutting equipment to free the casualty who was passed to the care of the ambulance service.
