Fire involving car on the M62 Time of Incident: 26/11/2015 - 21:39 Location: M62, between junctions 9 and 8, westbound Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Widnes A small fire involving a car was extinguished by firefighters on the hard shoulder of the M62. The crew used a hose reel and a thermal imaging camera at this incident. Lane one of the carriageway was closed while the incident was dealt with. The fire is believed to have been started due to an electrical problem.
