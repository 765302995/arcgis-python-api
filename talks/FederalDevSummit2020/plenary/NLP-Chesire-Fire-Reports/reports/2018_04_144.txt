 Fire in a disused building in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/04/2018 - 00:02 Location: Slutchers Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one fire engine attended from Penketh. Shortly after midnight two fire engines from Warrington and Penketh attended a fire in an unused building on Slutchers Lane in Warrington town centre. The fire involved a small unused brick building, measuring approximately two metres by three metres. The team used one hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to tackle the fire and remained in attendance for approximately one hour.
