 Smoke from candle in Nantwich Time of Incident: 29/11/2018 - 05:39 Location: King Place, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called to reports of a fire at an address on King Place in Nantwich.  Although there was no fire when crews arrived, there was a lot of smoke which needed to be cleared.  The smoke was caused by a melted candle on a storage unit.  Follow our advice on how to use candles safely https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/candles-in-the-home
