 Vehicle fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 28/06/2018 - 04:29 Location: Tetton Lane, Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach Firefighters from used two hose reel jets to extinguish a vehicle fire on Tetton Lane in Sandbach. The team were in attendance for approximately one hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
