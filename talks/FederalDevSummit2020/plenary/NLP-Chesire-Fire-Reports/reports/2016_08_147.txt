Swan rescue in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/08/2016 - 12:52 Location: Appleton Reservoir Park, Park Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines and the boat from Warrington The RSPCA asked firefighters to attend to help rescue a swan entangled with fishing line and a hook in its beak. The Service's swift water technicians went into the water to help bring the swan back to the side of the reservoir to have the line and hook removed.
