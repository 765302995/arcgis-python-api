 Quad bike fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 17/09/2019 - 18:02 Location: New Road in Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Shortly after 6pm firefighters were called to a report of a quad bike being on fire on New Road in Winsford. The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire in the open which caused extensive damage to the small quad bike. Firefighters then damped the quad bike down to make it safe. They were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.
