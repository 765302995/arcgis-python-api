 Broken window in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/03/2019 - 21:01 Location: Hope Farm Precinct, Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 9am firefighters were called to reports of a dangerous structure at Hope Farm Precinct in Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered that a window at a flat had been smashed. Broken glass was on the path outside the flat. Firefighters cordoned off the path and made the window and the glass safe. They were in attendance for less than 50 minutes.
