 Grass fire in Bowden Time of Incident: 15/07/2019 - 21:54 Location: A556, Bowden Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At approximately 10pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open near the A556 Bowden roundabout. On arrival the crew discovered a fire in the open measuring approximately 20 metres by 10 metres. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance at the scene for around one hour 45 minutes.
