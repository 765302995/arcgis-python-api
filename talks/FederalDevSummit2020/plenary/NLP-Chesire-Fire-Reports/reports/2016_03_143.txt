Shed well alight in Congleton Time of Incident: 04/03/2016 - 22:11 Location: Harvey Road, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines from Holmes Chapel and Congleton A metal shed  was well alight, so firefighters used two hose reels to extinguish the fire.  Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus. A large fan was used to clear the smoke as the fire crews dealt with the fire.
