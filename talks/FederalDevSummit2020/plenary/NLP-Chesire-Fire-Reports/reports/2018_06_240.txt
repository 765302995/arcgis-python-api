 Fire underneath Jubilee Bridge in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/06/2018 - 18:23 Location: Jubilee Bridge, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Runcorn, one from Warrington, the aerial ladder platform from Lymm and a fire boat attended Fire underneath Jubilee Bridge in WidnesCrews attended to reports of a fire on the westbound side of the Silver Jubilee Road Bridge in Runcorn after smoke was reported coming from underneath.  A boat was used to get to the fire before firefighters used an aerial ladder platform to reach the fire. One handheld jet and two tanks of water used to put out the fire
