Pallet fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/02/2016 - 20:06 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port.   When they arrived crews found that the fire involved pallets stacked up by an outbuilding. Firefighters used two hose reels to extinguish the fire and inspected the inside of the building which was heavily smoke logged with a Thermal Imaging Camera.
