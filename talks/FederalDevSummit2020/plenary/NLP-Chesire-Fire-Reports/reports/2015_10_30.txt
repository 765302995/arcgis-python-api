Pan left on hob in Nothwich Time of Incident: 25/10/2015 - 11:44 Location: Delamere Close, Barnton, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish a fire in the kitchen of a property in Northwich.  The fire was caused by an unattended pan left on the hob. The crews cleared the smoke from the property and were at the scene for just over 30 minutes. Kitchen fire safety advice
