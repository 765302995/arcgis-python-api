Tumble dryer fire in Wincle Time of Incident: 02/09/2016 - 02:12 Location: Barlow hill, Wincle Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters had to dismantle a tumble dryer to put out a fire inside of the dryer. Firefighters called to the scene first used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel jet and a covering jet to put out the tumble dryer fire which broken out in a single storey outbuilding. Crews isolated the electricity and removed the tumble dryer from the building before dismantling it to fully tackle the fire.
