 Fire in a Runcorn garden spreads to roadside trees Time of Incident: 16/06/2019 - 17:33 Location: Boston Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 5.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the back garden of a domestic property having spread to roadside trees. On arrival at the property in Boston Avenue, Runcorn, the crew discovered that it was a case of a supervised fire which had got out of control. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
