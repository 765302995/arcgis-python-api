 Lorry fire on M62 Time of Incident: 08/02/2018 - 13:32 Location: M62 junction 11 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Lorry fire on M62Fire crews were called to a lorry fire on the M62 near junction 11. Crews used a hose reel jet to fight the fire.  They were at the scene for approximately half an hour.
