 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/11/2018 - 19:25 Location: Afton, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 7.25pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Afton, Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered a wheelie bin well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes. For advice on preventing your wheelie bins from becoming a target for arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
