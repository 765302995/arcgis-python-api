 Fire at a cafe in Macclesfield indoor market Time of Incident: 18/08/2019 - 13:09 Location: Castle Street Mall, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Shortly after 1pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a café at the Grosvenor Centre Indoor Market in the Castle Street Mall, Macclesfield. The fire in the main electrical unit was out on arrival and the indoor market was evacuated. Firefighters cut away materials affected by the fire to prevent it from spreading. They left the market once they were satisfied that the café was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
