Van fire on M6 north junctions 18 to 19 Time of Incident: 05/10/2016 - 14:56 Location: M6 northbound between junctions 18 and 19, near Holmes Chapel Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich Firefighters have been tackling a fire in the engine compartment of a van. The incident is on the M6 northbound between junctions 18 and 19.  Traffic has built up in the area because of this incident, so drivers are advised to avoid this route for the time being.
