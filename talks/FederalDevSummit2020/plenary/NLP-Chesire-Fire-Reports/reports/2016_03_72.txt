Chimney fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 17/03/2016 - 18:34 Location: Mount Pleasant Road, Scholar Green. Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton Firefighters responded to a report of a chimney fire off Mount Pleasant Road, Scholar Green.  Crews used a hand held appliance to put the fire out. They also used a Thermal Imaging Camera to check that the fire had not spread to the loft space.
