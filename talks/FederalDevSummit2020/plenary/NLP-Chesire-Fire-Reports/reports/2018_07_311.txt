 Grass fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 14:52 Location: Loushers Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 2.50pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at Loushers Lane playing fields in Latchford, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered approximately 50 metres by 10 metres of grass alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and two beaters to extinguish the fire. They then damped the area down and checked for hotspots to make sure it was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for nearly an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call police on 101.
