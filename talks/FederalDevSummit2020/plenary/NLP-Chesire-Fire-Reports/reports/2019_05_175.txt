 Large skip fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 07/05/2019 - 16:45 Location: Park Road, Tarporley. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford. One fire engine from Northwich. Firefighters were called to a large skip fire at Oulton Park in Park Road, Tarporley. Crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. A gas cylinder was located and removed from the area as a precaution.
