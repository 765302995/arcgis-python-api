Woodpile well alight in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 15/08/2017 - 21:02 Location: Prestbury Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Woodpile well alight in WilmslowA woodpile which was well alight in a field required firefighters to attend on foot and use a main jet to extinguish it. This was a large fire and water was pumped from a nearby pond to fight it.
