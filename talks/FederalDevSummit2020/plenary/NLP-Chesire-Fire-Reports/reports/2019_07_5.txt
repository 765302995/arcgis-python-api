 Basement flooded at a house in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 28/07/2019 - 18:12 Location: Daveylands, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow. At around 6.15pm firefighters from Wilmslow were called to reports of a fire at a property in Wilmslow.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered flooding in the basement of a residential property on Daveylands which was affecting the electrics.  Crews used an electric pump to clear the flooding and were detained at the address for approximately 30 minutes.
