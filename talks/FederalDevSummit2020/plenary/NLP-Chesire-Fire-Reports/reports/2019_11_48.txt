 Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 19/11/2019 - 21:23 Location: Hinton Road, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to a fire involving a car in Hinton Road, Crewe. Crews used breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They also used a thermal imaging camera to check the temperature had decreased to a safe level.
