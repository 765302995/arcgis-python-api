 Skip fire in the open in Widnes Time of Incident: 16/08/2019 - 03:25 Location: Dennis Road, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Widnes and Lymm At  around 3.30am firefighters from Widnes and Lymm were called to reports of fire on Dennis Road.          On arrival the crew discovered a skip fire, which was well alight and had spread to surrounding rubbish.  Firefighters used two hose reel jets and one main jet to extinguish the fire and the crews were in attendance for around two hours.  The cause of the fire is believed to be accidental.
