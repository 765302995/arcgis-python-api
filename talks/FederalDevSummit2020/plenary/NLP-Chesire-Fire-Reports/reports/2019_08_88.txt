 Conifer trees on fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 18/08/2019 - 20:39 Location: Crewe Road, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach At around 8.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on Crewe Road in Sandbach. On arrival the crew found conifer trees on fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 489562, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
