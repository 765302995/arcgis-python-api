 Boy stuck in tree in Burwardsley Time of Incident: 19/09/2018 - 11:57 Location: School Lane, Burwardsley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley Fire crews were called to rescue a boy who trapped his leg between two branches on School Lane, Burwardsley. Crews used hydraulic cutting equipment to remove a branch and release the boy. He was then checked over by paramedics.
