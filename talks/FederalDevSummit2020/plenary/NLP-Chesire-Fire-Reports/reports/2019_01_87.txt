 Garage fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/01/2019 - 19:09 Location: Crow Wood Lane, Widnes. Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Widnes and one from Penketh At around 7.10pm firefighters from Widnes and Penketh were dispatched to reports of a fire in an outbuilding at the rear of Crow Wood Lane in Widnes.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a quantity of waste well alight in a domestic garage.  Crews used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.
