 Garage fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 13/10/2018 - 19:07 Location: Balmoral Avenue, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Northwich and one from Lymm At around 7.05pm firefighters were called to reports of a garage being on fire on Balmoral Avenue in Northwich. On arrival crews found the outbuilding well alight. Firefighters, including one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. They then emptied the garage to make sure that the fire had not spread and that the outbuilding was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around two hours. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
