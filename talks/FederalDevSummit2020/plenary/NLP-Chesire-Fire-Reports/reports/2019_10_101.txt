 Log burner fire in Lower Peover Time of Incident: 13/10/2019 - 19:43 Location: Mill Lane, Lower Peover. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich. One fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel. Firefighters were called to a log burner fire in Mill Lane, Lower Peover which was causing smoke logging on the ground floor. Crews emptied the log burner using a hearth kit and then used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
