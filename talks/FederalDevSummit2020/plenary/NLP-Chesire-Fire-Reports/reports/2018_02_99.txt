 Fire in a cellar in Bunbury Time of Incident: 06/02/2018 - 21:27 Location: Vicarage Lane, Bunbury Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley and one from Nantwich Fire crews were called to a fire in the cellar of a property on Vicarage Lane, Bunbury. The fire was through a wooden beam and is believed to have been caused by an open fire on the ground floor. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet and a fire extinguisher to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for two hours.
