Fence post and wheelie bin on fire in Winnington Time of Incident: 16/05/2016 - 06:22 Location: Winnington Lane, Winnington, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A wheelie bin had been pushed up against a fence post and set on fire. Firefighters used a water back pack to put out the fire.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111. Arson costs lives.
