 Wheelie bins fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/03/2018 - 19:12 Location: Bechers playing fields, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 7.20pm firefighters were called to reports of a small fire at Bechers in Widnes.  The crew found several wheelie bins alight at a playground.  They used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. Police have been informed of the incident.
