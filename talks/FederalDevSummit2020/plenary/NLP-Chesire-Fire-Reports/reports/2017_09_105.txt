Three fires on a skate park in Crewe Time of Incident: 01/09/2017 - 20:57 Location: Skate park by St Georges Playing Field in Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe There were three separate small fires on a skate park, so firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fires.  These fires were started deliberately.  If you have any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
