House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/02/2015 - 05:11 Location: Old Liverpool Road, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Warrington and Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a report of a woman shouting for help at a property on Old Liverpool Road, Warrington.   When they arrived crews found that Police had helped the woman from the property. They then set about tackling the fire with hose reel jets. A fire investigation is now underway to find the cause of the fire.
