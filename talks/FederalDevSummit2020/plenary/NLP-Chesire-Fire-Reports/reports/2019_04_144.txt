 Garden fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 15/04/2019 - 14:31 Location: Gingerbread Lane, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Shortly after 2.30pm firefighters from Nantwich were dispatched to reports of a garden fire at an address on Gingerbread Lane.  On arrival it was found that the fire had spread to a detached garage.  Firefighter used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire, they then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately 40 minutes.  The cause of the fire is believed to be accidental.
