Road traffic collision on M53 at Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 06/04/2017 - 08:09 Location: M53 junction 8, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port, one from Powey Lane, one from Chester and one from Merseyside Fire and Rescue Service Firefighters released a casualty from a van which had overturned on the M53 near to junction 8. Specialist cutting equipment was used to rescue the man from the van and handed him over to the ambulance service.
