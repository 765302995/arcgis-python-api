House fire in Churton Time of Incident: 03/08/2015 - 07:51 Location: Chester Road, Churton by Aldford, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester and one from Wrexham (North Wales FRS) A fire in the airing cupboard of a Churton by Aldford house was tackled by firefighters. Fortunately nobody was trapped in the fire which was put out by the crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, using a hose reel jet.  Gas and electricity supplies were isolated and an ambulance was called for a casualty suffering smoke inhalation. A thermal imaging camera was used to make sure there was no spread of fire.
