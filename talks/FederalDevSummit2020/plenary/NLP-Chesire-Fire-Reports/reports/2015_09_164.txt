Sofa on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 01/09/2015 - 23:17 Location: West Avenue, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters extinguished a fire involving a sofa which was well alight on a street in Crewe.  The blaze caused slight heat damage to a car opposite as well as the fascia of a house. Police were requested to attend the scene due to the fire being suspected arson and an investigation is underway.  Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
