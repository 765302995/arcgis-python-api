 Bin fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 30/04/2019 - 18:50 Location: Old Hall Road, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters from Northwich used one hose reel jet to extinguish a bin which had been deliberately set alight on Old Hall Road in Leftwich.  The team were in attendance at the scene for around 15 minutes.  Anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
