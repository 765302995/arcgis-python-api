Road traffic collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 09/11/2016 - 10:09 Location: Congleton Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield and Wilmslow One vehicle turned on its side after leaving the road leaving a lady trapped in the car. Firefighters stabilised the vehicle before assisting paramedics in releasing the female, who was handed into the care of the ambulance service.
