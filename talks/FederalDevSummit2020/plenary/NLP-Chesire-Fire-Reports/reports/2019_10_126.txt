 Kitchen fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 08/10/2019 - 05:07 Location: Daisy Bank, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm. One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath. Firefighters were called to a kitchen fire at an address in Daisy Bank, Lymm. All occupants were accounted for but were treated for smoke inhalation.    Crews used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the fire and a large fan to disperse smoke.
