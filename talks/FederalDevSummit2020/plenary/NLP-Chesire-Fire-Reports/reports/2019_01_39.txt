 Fire in a cellar in Congleton Time of Incident: 04/01/2019 - 16:31 Location: Buxton Road, Congleton Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Congleton and one from Macclesfield Firefighters attended to reports of a fire in a cellar on Buxton Road in Congleton.  Crews used a thermal imaging camera to find the smoke was coming from a pellet machine.  They then ventilated the area by opening the barrel and used a hand appliance to put out a fire.   An electrician has been called to the property.
