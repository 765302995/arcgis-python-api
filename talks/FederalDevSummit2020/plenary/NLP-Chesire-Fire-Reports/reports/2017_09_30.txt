A number of wheelie bins on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/09/2017 - 00:00 Location: Delamere Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Deliberate ignition is believed to be the cause of several wheelie bins on fire on Delamere Street.
