 Conifers smoldering in Cranage Time of Incident: 25/05/2019 - 18:30 Location: Byley Lane, Cranage Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Fire crews attended to around 15ft of conifers smoldering on Byley Lane in Cranage.  One hose reel jet was used to extinguish the fire and the checked the area for hot spots.
