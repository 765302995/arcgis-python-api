House fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 13/10/2017 - 13:37 Location: Bowyer Avenue, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Nantwich Fire crews were called to a fire on the first floor of a domestic property on Bowyer Avenue, Nantwich. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet and a large fan to extinguish the fire. The crews then used a thermal imaging camera to check the property. All the people inside the house had evacuated by the time fire crews arrived. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
