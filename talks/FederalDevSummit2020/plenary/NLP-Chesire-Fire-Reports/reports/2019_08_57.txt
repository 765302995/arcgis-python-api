 Scrambler bike fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/08/2019 - 19:47 Location: New Bridge Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 7.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a scrambler bike being on fire on New Bridge Road in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew found the bike well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and then left the incident in the hands of the police in attendance. Firefighters were in attendance for more than 20 minutes. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 493089, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
