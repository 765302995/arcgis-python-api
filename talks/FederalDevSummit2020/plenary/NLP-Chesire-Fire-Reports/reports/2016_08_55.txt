Two sheds well alight in Northwich Time of Incident: 20/08/2016 - 11:34 Location: Northwich Road, Weaverham, Northwich Attendance: Three fire engines from Stockton Heath, Northwich and Winsford fire stations Two sheds were well alight.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. Two hose reel jets were used to extinguish the fire.  One casualty was suffering from smoke inhalation and was handed in to the care of the paramedics.
