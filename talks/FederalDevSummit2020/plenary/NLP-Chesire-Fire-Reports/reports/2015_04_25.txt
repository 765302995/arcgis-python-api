Wheelie bin on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 26/04/2015 - 19:44 Location: Next to H Oldfield and Sons Ltd, macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A wheelie bin which was against a building in Macclesfield required firefighters to attend and extinguish it with a hose reel. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the building. Wheelie Bin - Fire Safety advice
