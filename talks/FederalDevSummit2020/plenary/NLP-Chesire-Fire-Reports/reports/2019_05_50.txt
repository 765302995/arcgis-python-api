 Bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 24/05/2019 - 14:10 Location: Gooseberry Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters attended to a small bin fire on Gooseberry Lane in Runcorn.  One hose reel jet was used to put out the fire which is believed to have been caused by a discarded cigarette.
