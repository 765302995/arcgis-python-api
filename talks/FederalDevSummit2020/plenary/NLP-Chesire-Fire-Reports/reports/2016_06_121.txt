Dangerous structure in Middlewich Time of Incident: 08/06/2016 - 18:14 Location: Lewin Street, Middlewich. Attendance: Fire engines from Middlewich and hydraulic platforms from Stockton Heath and Macclesfield. Firefighters were called to a report of a chimney stack that had become unsafe on Lewin Street, Middlewich.  When they arrived crews confirmed that the structure was a danger. A cordon was set up and firefighters set about making the structure safe.  They did this with the use of a hydraulic platform.
