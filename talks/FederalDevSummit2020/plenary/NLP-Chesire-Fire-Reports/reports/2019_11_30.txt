 Smell of electrical burning at domestic property in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/11/2019 - 00:55 Location: London Road, Stockton Heath, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Shortly before 1am firefighters were called to a report of a smell of electrical burning in a boiler cupboard at a domestic property in London Road, Stockton Heath, Warrington.  On arrival crews discovered that wires burning within an immersion heater were causing the smell.  Firefighters isolated the heater and were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.
