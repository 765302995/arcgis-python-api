 Pallets and fence panels fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/09/2019 - 11:47 Location: Arley Road, Appleton, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 11.45am firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on Arley Road, Appleton, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved pallets and fence panel that had spread to bushes. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. They then damped the area affected by the fire down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.
