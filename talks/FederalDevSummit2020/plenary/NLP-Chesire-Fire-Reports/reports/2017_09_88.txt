Wheelie bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 06/09/2017 - 00:59 Location: Grangemoor, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A wheelie bin was on fire close to a garage and street lighting. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to bring the blaze under control. The garage was not affected but there was some damage to the street light.  Police also attended and the fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.  Please follow our wheelie bin safety advice.
