 Bonfire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/10/2018 - 18:00 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a bonfire which had been left unattended on Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port. Crews used a fire extinguisher and beaters to put out the fire.
