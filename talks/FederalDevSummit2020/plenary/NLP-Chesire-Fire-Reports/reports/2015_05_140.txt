Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/05/2015 - 22:13 Location: Seacombe Drive, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port One hose reel was used by firefighters to extinguish a fire in a car in Ellesmere Port. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire, which was in the footwell of the car. Police were requested to attend the scene.
