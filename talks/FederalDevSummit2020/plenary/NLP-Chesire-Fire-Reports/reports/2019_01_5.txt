 Barge on fire in Stoak village Time of Incident: 11/01/2019 - 16:52 Location: Little Stanney Lane, Stoak Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port At around 4.50pm firefighters were called to reports of a large amount of smoke on Little Stanney Lane in the village of Stoak, near Chester. On arrival crews discovered a barge in the Shropshire Union Canal well alight and that no-one was on-board. Firefighters overcame access problems so that they could extinguish the fire. Firefighters then put out the fire with a main jet. They also used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. The fire caused extensive damage to the barge.
