Motorbike destroyed by fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 07/06/2015 - 03:18 Location: Ways Green, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Winsford firefighters were sent to Ways Green in Winsford where there was a fire involving a motorbike. The motorbike was completely destroyed by fire and there was heat damage to a window of a nearby house and damage to a rear light of a vehicle. Firefighters used one hose reel jet.
