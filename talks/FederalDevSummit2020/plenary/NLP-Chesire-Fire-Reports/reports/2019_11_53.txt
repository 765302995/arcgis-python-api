 Person stuck in mud in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 18/11/2019 - 11:47 Location: Broad Walk, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to woman stuck knee-deep in mud on a path near Broad Walk, Wilmslow. Crews used salvage sheets and ladders to get to the woman and free her from the mud. They then handed her over to paramedics to be assessed.
