 Flooding in cellar, Bollington Time of Incident: 01/08/2019 - 20:01 Location: Princess Street, Bollington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Bollington and one from Macclesfield Crews attended to flooding in a cellar on Princess Street in Bollington.  The electrics were isolated and a pump was set up to reduce the flood water.  Crews then visited houses nearby and found five were affected and needed support.  Highways and the council were notified.
