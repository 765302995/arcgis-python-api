 Fire in a back garden in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/08/2018 - 05:36 Location: Wardour Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 5.35am firefighters were called to a report of a wheelie bin being on fire in a back garden on Wardour Street, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered a small amount of rubbish on fire. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For advice on how to prevent your wheelie bins from being targeted by arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
