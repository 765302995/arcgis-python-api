 Shed fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 14/12/2018 - 18:44 Location: Laburnum Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Fire crews were called to a fire in a shed on the allotments on Laburnum Road, Macclesfield. The fire had spread the three sheds and crews used two main jets and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  The fire is believed to have been started by a wood burner in one of the sheds. Crews were at the scene for three hours.
