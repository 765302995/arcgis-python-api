 Large fire in Marthall Time of Incident: 04/11/2018 - 00:05 Location: Chelford Road, Marthall. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a large fire on land off Chelford Road, Marthall. Crews used a digger to smother the fire with soil, then used a hose reel to damp down.
