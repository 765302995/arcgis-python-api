 Smoke-logged flat in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 11/04/2019 - 20:16 Location: Moss Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: Three fire engines attended - Two from Macclesfield and one from Poynton Firefighters attended to reports of an alarm sounding inside a flat on Moss Lane in Macclesfield.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, and carrying one hose reel jet and one safety jet firefighters entered the flat. A man was rescued and taken to hospital as a precaution. Crews discovered smoke was coming from unattended cooking on the hob and used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
