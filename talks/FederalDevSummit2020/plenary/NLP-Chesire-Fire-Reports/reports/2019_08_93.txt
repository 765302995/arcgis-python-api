 Fire at a domestic property in Crewe Time of Incident: 17/08/2019 - 01:39 Location: Drayton Crescent, Crewe Attendance: Three fire engines attended, two from Crewe and one from Sandbach Shortly after 1.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a domestic property in Drayton Crescent, Crewe. The fire was out on arrival. Firefighters used a large fan to clear smoke from the property and were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
