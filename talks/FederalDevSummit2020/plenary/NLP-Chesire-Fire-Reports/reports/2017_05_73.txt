Small fire by Sankey Valley Park in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/05/2017 - 17:00 Location: Longshaw Road by Sankey Valley Park, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Warrington firefighters used hand held fire extinguishers to deal with a small fire, located near to a burnt out caravan at the bottom of Longshaw Road by Sankey Valley Park.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately so anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
