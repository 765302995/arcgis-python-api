Flat fire in Neston Time of Incident: 07/02/2015 - 23:11 Location: High Street, Neston Attendance: Three fire engines, Two from Ellesmere Port and one from Merseyside Seven people and two dogs were rescued from a fire involving two flats in Neston.  The fire was in the access hallway of the three storey flats and firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels to extinguish the blaze. Four people were led to safety from one flat and three from the adjoining flat.   Police and a fire investigator attended the scene and the incident is being investigated as arson.  Anyone with any information is asked to call police on 101 quoting incident number 916 of 7 February, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111
