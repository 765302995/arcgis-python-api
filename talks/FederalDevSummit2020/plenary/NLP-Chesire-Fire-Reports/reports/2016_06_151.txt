Flooding in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/06/2016 - 18:21 Location: Dorset Gardens on Charlton Close in Runcorn. Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn and Frodsham Fire Stations Firefighters responded to a report of flooding at Dorset Gardens on Charlton Close in Runcorn.  When they arrived crews found that heavy rainfall had caused a false ceiling at the property to collapse. Water had affected light fittings throughout the three storey building.   Firefighters isolated the water and electrics at the property and assisted police, ambulance service and Halton Borough Council with the evacuation of 41 residents to another property.  Firefighters remained in attendance at the incident approximately five hours.
