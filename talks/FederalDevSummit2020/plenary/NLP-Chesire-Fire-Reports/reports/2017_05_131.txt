Bedroom fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/05/2017 - 05:11 Location: Festival Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Two fire engines were called to a bedroom on fire in Warrington. On arrival the crews found the fire to be out and four casualties suffering smoke inhalation. The fire was in a back bedroom of a semi detached property and the casualties were given oxygen therapy by crews and paramedics also attended. Firefighters damped down the area before leaving
