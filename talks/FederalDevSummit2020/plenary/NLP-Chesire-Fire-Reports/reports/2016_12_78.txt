Small fire in Chester Time of Incident: 16/12/2016 - 22:03 Location: Church Street, Chester. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters were called to deal with a small fire in an alleyway off Church Street, Chester.  Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
