 Large bonfire, Warrington Time of Incident: 05/11/2019 - 18:47 Location: Vulcan Close, Fearnhead, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Firefighters were called out to deal with a large bonfire that had got out of control on a grass verge in Vulcan Close. They swiftly extinguished it using a single hose reel.
