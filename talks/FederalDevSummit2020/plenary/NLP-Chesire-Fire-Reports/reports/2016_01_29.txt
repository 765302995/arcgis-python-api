Car well alight in Malpas Time of Incident: 22/01/2016 - 18:38 Location: Dog Lane, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas A car was well alight, off road, in Malpas.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  Two hose reel jets were used to extinguish the fire. The vehicle was then checked with a thermal imaging camera, to ensure that the fire was completely out.
