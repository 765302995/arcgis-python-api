Car on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 22/03/2017 - 06:49 Location: A54, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford A car which was well alight on the roadway, outside Winsford Grange Nursing Home, required firefighters to attend and use two hose reel to extinguish it. The road was closed while the incident was dealt with. Police attended for traffic management.
