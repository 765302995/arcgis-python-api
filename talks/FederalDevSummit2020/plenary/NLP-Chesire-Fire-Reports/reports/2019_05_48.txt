 Shed fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 24/05/2019 - 16:02 Location: Barnabas Avenue, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach Firefighters attended a fire involving a metal shed in the rear garden of an address in Barnabus Avenue in Crewe. The fire spread to two fence panels. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
