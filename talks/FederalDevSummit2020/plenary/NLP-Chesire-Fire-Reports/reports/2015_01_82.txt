Chimney fire caused by a log burner in Malpas Time of Incident: 10/01/2015 - 14:33 Location: Fishers Lane, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas There was a chimney fire, caused by a log burner, at a property on Fishers Lane in Malpas.  Firefighters removed the log burner and cooled the embers, then they checked the roof space with a thermal imaging camera.
