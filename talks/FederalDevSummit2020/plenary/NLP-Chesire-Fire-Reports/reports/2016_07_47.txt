Pallets on fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 21/07/2016 - 19:44 Location: Charlcote Crescent, Wistaston, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Arson is suspected as the cause of a fire involving pallets in a field. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and are urging anyone with any information about this incident to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
