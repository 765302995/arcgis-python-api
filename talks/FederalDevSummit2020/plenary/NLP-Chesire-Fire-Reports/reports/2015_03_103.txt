Small fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 10/03/2015 - 05:31 Location: Mill Lane, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Knutsford A fire in a light fitting was attended by firefighters.  A  covering jet was laid as firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire.  A high pressure fan was then used to clear the building of smoke.
