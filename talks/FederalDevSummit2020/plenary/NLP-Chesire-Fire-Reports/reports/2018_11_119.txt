 Road traffic collision near Chester Time of Incident: 08/11/2018 - 05:42 Location: Whitchurch Road, Milton Green, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Two cars were involved in a collision in Milton Green. Fortunately nobody was trapped in either of the vehicles and occupants of the cars were assessed by ambulance staff at the scene. Firefighters made the vehicles safe. The A41 was closed in both directions while the incident was being dealt with.
