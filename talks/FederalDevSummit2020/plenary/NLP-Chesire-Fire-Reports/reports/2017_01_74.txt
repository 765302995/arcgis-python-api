Chimney fire in Winterley Time of Incident: 12/01/2017 - 23:09 Location: Bowkers Croft, Winterley Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A bird's nest was the cause of a chimney fire in Winterley near Crewe. Firefighters used a hearth kit and thermal imaging camera to clear the fire and make sure there were no further hotspots.  Always follow our chimney safety advice.
