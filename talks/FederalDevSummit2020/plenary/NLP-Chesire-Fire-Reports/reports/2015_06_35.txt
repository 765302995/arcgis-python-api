Conifer fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/06/2015 - 18:13 Location: Burley Lane, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called t a report of a fire off Burley Lane, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that a bonfire had got out of control and spread to some conifer trees. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
