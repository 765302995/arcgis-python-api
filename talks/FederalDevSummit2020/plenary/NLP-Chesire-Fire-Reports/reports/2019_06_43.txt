 Smoke in a flat in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/06/2019 - 02:13 Location: Princes Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 2.15am firefighters were called to reports of a smoke alarm sounding at a flat on Princes Road in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered that smoke from food being cooked in an oven had activated the alarm. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke from the flat and were in attendance for less than an hour. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
