Fatal collision in Chester Time of Incident: 24/05/2015 - 21:41 Location: Tarporley Road, Chester. Attendance: Fire engines from Tarporley and Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Tarporley Road, Chester.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved one car. Sadly, they confirmed that this was a fatal incident. Crews assisted the other emergency services in securing the scene.
