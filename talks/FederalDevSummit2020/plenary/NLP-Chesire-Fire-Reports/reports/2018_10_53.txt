 Vehicle fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 22/10/2018 - 23:04 Location: Overton Heath Road, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas Firefighters from Malpas attended reports of a vehicle fire outside a property on Overton Heath Road. On arrival the team found that a transit van was well alight, there was also a risk that the fire may spread to another nearby vehicle. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire, they then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately two hours.
