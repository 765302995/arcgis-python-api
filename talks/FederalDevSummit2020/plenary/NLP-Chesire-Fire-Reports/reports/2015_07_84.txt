Bin on fire inside the kitchen of a property in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 18/07/2015 - 22:11 Location: Blackden Walk in Wilmslow Attendance: Two fire engines from Stockport and Cheadle Two fire crews were sent to a kitchen fire at a property on Blackden Walk in Wilmslow. On arrival the fire crews found a fire in a waste bin in a ground floor kitchen. The fire was out on arrival, but crews helped one casualty who was suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation.
