 Fire in a fridge in Northwich Time of Incident: 06/04/2019 - 10:16 Location: London Road, Northwich. WA16 9NW Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel and one from Northwich attended Firefighters used breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to put out a fire in a fridge.  They also used a positive pressure ventilation fan to clear smoke following the fire. The crews were at the scene for about an hour.
