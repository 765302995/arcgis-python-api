Dangerous chimney stack in Middlewich Time of Incident: 01/07/2017 - 15:06 Location: Lewin Street, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel and the aerial appliance from Lymm Fire crews were called to secure a dangerous chimney stack on Lewin Street, Middlewich. The aerial appliance from Lymm attended the incident alongside the engine from Holmes Chapel and set up a cordon around the structure before making the structure safe.
