Bedroom fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/01/2015 - 08:19 Location: Haley Road North, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish a fire in the bedroom of a house in Warrington. The gas and electrics were isolated and a fire investigator was requested to attend the scene. The cause of the fire was deemed to be as a result of discarded smoking equipment.
