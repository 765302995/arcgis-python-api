 Quad bike fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/02/2019 - 15:03 Location: Shepcroft Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm attended. Firefighters were called to an outbuilding in Shepcroft Lane in Warrington following reports of a quad bike on fire. The quad bike had been removed from the barn prior to the arrival of firefighters. Crews used a hose reel to put out the fire.
