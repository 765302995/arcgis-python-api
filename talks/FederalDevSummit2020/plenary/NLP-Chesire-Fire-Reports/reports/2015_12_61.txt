Refuse fire in Gawsworth Time of Incident: 15/12/2015 - 07:32 Location: Gawsworth Road, Gawsworth Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters used two hose reels to extinguish a refuse fire in Gawsworth.  The fire started as controlled burning but spread. The crews turned over the rubbish and damped down the scene.
