 Two-car collision on the M62 Time of Incident: 06/12/2019 - 14:35 Location: M62 eastbound, between junctions nine and 10 Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Warrington and one from Birchwood Shortly after 2.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision on the M62 eastbound, between junctions nine and 10.  On arrival crews discovered the collision involved two saloon cars and that no-one was trapped inside either of them.  Firefighters administered oxygen to a casualty before handing him over to the care of paramedics.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.  Highways England also attended.
