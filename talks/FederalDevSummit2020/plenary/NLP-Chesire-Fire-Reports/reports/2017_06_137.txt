Road traffic collision near Malpas Time of Incident: 08/06/2017 - 16:30 Location: Shocklach Hall Lane, Shocklach Oviatt, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas, one from Chester and two from Shropshire Fire and Rescue Service One person was reported to be trapped after a car was involved in a single vehicle road traffic collision. They managed to get themselves out of the vehicle before emergency services arrived. Firefighters made the scene safe.
