 Commercial building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/12/2019 - 21:46 Location: Warrington Road, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Birchwood and Warrington Firefighters from Warrington and Birchwood used to breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to extinguish a small fire in a room at commercial building in Warrington.  The crew also used a thermal imaging camera to monitor the temperature of the room before ventilating the area.  The crews were in attendance for around one hour 15 minutes.
