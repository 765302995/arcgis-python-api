Chimney fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 28/04/2016 - 20:10 Location: Cumber Drive, Wilmslow. Attendance: A fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters responded to a report of a house fire on Cumber Drive, Wilmslow.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire in the chimney. Special rods were used to put the fire out.
