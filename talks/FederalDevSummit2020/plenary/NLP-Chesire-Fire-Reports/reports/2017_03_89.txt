Van fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 16/03/2017 - 22:40 Location: Old Quay Street, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines A van was set on fire in what was believed to be a deliberate ignition. There were canisters in the back of the vehicle which were found to be empty. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Police were asked to attend.
