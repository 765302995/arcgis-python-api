 Skip fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 26/10/2018 - 23:05 Location: Wharton Gardens, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and one from Northwich Crews were called to a large skip on fire in a garden on Wharton Gardens in Winsford.  On arrival, firefighters saw the fire had also spread to two fence panals and used two hose reels to bring it under control.  Crews then dampened down the area and made sure it was safe.
