 Industrial building fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/06/2018 - 08:37 Location: Ditton Road, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines, one from Penketh and one from Runcorn Industrial building fire in Widnes  Firefighters from Runcorn and Penketh attended reports of a fire in Ditton Road, Widnes. The fire involved a corrugated metal shed, approximately 15 metres by 15 metres, which was well alight.  The team used two hose reel jets, one main jet  and one breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire.  Crews were in attendance for approximately three hours.
