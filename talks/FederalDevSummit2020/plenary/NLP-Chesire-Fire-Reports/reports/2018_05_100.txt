 Outbuilding building fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 17/05/2018 - 22:30 Location: Alexandra Road, Middlewich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Middlewich and one from Sandbach At around 10.30pm firefighters were called to reports of fire involving an outbuilding on Alexandra Road in Middlewich. On arrival the crews discovered several fence panels well alight adjacent to lock-up garages. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and opened all adjacent garages to make sure that the fire had not spread. The fire is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
