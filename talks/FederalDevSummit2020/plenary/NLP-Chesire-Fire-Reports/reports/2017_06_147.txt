Fire in bedroom in Chester Time of Incident: 06/06/2017 - 15:52 Location: Norman Way, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Chester Firefighters were called to a fire in a first floor bedroom. All persons were accounted for and the crew extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. Roof spaces of neighbouring properties were checked for fire spread. The electric was isolated at the property.
