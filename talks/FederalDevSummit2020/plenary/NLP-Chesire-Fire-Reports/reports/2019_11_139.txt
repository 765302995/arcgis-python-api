 Unattended bonfire, Warrington Time of Incident: 05/11/2019 - 19:01 Location: Summerfield Avenue, Dallam, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh Firefighters responded to reports of a bonfire that had been left unattended in Summerfield Avenue in Dallam. Although it was a fairly small fire, it needed dealing with so they used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames.
