Small fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 30/04/2016 - 18:06 Location: Chester Road, Northwich. Attendance: A fire engine from Northwich. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a rubbish fire off Chester Road, Northwich.  This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
