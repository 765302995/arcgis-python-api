Two car collision in Neston Time of Incident: 16/03/2016 - 18:13 Location: High Road in Neston Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on High Road in Neston.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved a people carrier and another car. One person was trapped in their vehicle and a further five people required first aid.  Crews rescued the trapped casualty who was then handed to the care of the ambulance service.  The road was closed while the incident was dealt with.
