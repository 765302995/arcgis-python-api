Building fire in Chester Time of Incident: 26/08/2016 - 15:27 Location: Upton Lane, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester A fire broke out in utility room of a detached property. Firefighters were able to bring the fire under control using four breathing apparatus, two hose reel jets and one large fan to ventilate the property. They then reduced the breathing apparatus to two before using a hose reel jet to dampen down the property. Crews then carried out a check of the property using a thermal  imaging camera to check for any remaining hot spots of fire. An ambulance was requested for one man who was suffering the effects of smoke inhalation.
