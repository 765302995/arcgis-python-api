Man trapped in car after collision in Nantwich Time of Incident: 08/03/2015 - 07:54 Location: London Road, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Crewe Firefighters released a man who was medically trapped in a car on its roof in Nantwich. The road was closed while the man was released by the crews and handed into the car of paramedics from the Air Ambulance Service.  Two other people had released themselves from the car prior to the arrival of the fire and rescue service.
