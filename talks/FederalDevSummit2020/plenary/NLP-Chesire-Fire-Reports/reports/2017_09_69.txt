HGV brakes on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/09/2017 - 14:11 Location: Old Chester Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm and one from Penketh HGV brakes on fire in WarringtonFire crews were called to a HGV fire by the Walton Arms pub, on Old Chester Road, Warrington. Fire crews found that the HGV's brakes were on fire on arrival and crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out. They then checked the brakes using a thermal imaging camera.
