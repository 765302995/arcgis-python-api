 Industrial bin fire in Padgate Time of Incident: 28/09/2019 - 02:43 Location: Station Road, Padgate, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood A hose reel jet was used by firefighters to put out a fire in a large industrial bin on Station Road.  The Birchwood crew was called at 2.40am and stayed at the scene for 20 minutes.
