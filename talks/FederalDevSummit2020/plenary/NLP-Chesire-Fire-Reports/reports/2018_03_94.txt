 Kitchen fire in Bollington Time of Incident: 16/03/2018 - 05:00 Location: Henshall Road, Bollington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington and one from Poynton Firefighters were called to a house on Henshall Road, Bollington, after reports of a kitchen fire. On arrival crews found a small kitchen fire which they extinguished using two breathing apparatus, a hose reel jet,  a covering jet and a large fan. Crews were at the scene for one hour.
