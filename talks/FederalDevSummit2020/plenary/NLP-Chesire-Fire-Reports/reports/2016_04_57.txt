Fire in the open in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 19/04/2016 - 20:23 Location: Twinnies Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow A fire in a pit spread to bushes in the garden of a Wilmslow property. Two hose reel jets were used by firefighters to put out the blaze. They then damped the area down and used a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no further areas of heat.
