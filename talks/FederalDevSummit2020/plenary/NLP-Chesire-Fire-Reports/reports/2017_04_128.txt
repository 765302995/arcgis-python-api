Flat fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/04/2017 - 07:09 Location: Bruche Heath Gardens,Poulton-with-Fearnhead, Warrington Attendance: Fire engines from Birchwood, Warrington and Stockton Heath A casualty was treated for smoke inhalation after a fire in flat on Bruche Heath Gardens. The fire was in a store room with the casualty in an adjacent flat. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The casualty, a man, was treated by ambulance staff.
