 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/04/2019 - 18:27 Location: Hood Lane North, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Shortly before 6.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Hood Lane North in Warrington.  On arrival the crew found a wheelie bin well alight.  Firefighters used a water back pack to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.  For advice on preventing your wheelie bins from becoming a target for arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
