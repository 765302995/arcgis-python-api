 Log burner fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 08/02/2018 - 14:40 Location: West Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and one from Winsford Firefighters were called to a house on West Road, Northwich, after a log burner caught fire. On arrival crews found that the logs adjacent to the log burner were ablaze and the crew, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews then cleared the property of smoke using a large fan.
