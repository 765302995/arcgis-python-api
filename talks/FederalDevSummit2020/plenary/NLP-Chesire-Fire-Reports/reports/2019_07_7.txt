 Collision on M56 Time of Incident: 27/07/2019 - 23:44 Location: M56,eastbound between junction 7 to junction 6 Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm and three fire engines attended from Greater Manchester Firefighters attended to a two-vehicle collision on the M56 eastbound between junction 7 to junction 6.  Crews removed the door of one vehicle to safely extricate one man from the vehicle who was then handed into the care of paramedics.  Highways attended to close lanes one and two.  Crews were in attendance for around 50 minutes. .
