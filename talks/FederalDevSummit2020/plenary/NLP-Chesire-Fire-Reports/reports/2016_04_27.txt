Bin fire in Widnes which affected property Time of Incident: 24/04/2016 - 14:37 Location: St Michaels View, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to an external bin on fire which involved mattresses and a property. The crew used one hose reel to extinguish the flames and cut away roof space and removed tiles at a property to check for fire spread.
