 Chip pan fire in Disley Time of Incident: 10/07/2019 - 20:18 Location: Buxton Road West, Disley. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Firefighters were called to a chip pan fire at a flat in Buxton Road West, Disley. On arrival crews used breathing apparatus and a hose reel . They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke. No fire damage was caused and all occupants were accounted for. Crews were in attendance for 45 minutes. Find out more about cooking safely by going to https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chip-pans
