Flooding in Wildboarclough Time of Incident: 21/11/2016 - 18:32 Location: Clough Road, Wildboarclough Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A fire engine from Macclesfield attended a report of flooding at a house on Clough Road. The crew used a light portable pump to reduce the water from a culvert outside the property to prevent it from flooding into the house.
