 Overheated cable in house's cellar in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 20/08/2019 - 18:54 Location: Chester Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Bollington, one from Wilmslow and one from Poynton Shortly before 7pm firefighters were called to a report of light smoke logging and the smell of burning in the cellar of a four-storey house on Chester Road in Macclesfield. On arrival crews discovered that everyone was accounted for. The electricity supply was isolated and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus investigated the property. The electricity supplier was requested to attend after it was found that a cable had overheated and suffered charring. Firefighters used a large fan to clear smoke from the house and were in attendance for around an hour.
