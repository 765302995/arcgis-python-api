 Small fire in woodland in Winsford Time of Incident: 16/11/2018 - 16:56 Location: Grange Lane, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Shortly after 6.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in woodland on Grange Lane in Winsford. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire involving wood. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.
