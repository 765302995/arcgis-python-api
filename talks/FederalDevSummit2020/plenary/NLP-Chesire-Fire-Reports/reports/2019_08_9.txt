 Smoke at a house in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/08/2019 - 23:57 Location: Birchall Street, Croft, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 12 midnight firefighters were called to reports of a smoke alarm sounding at a house in Birchall Street, Croft, Warrington. On arrival the crew found that the smoke which had activated the alarm was caused by food being burnt. Firefighters isolated the gas supply and used a large fan to clear the smoke from the house. They also assessed a man who was inside the house. He showed no signs of injury or smoke inhalation. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
