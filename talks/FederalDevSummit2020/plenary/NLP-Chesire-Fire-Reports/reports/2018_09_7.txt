 Wheelie bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 27/09/2018 - 21:47 Location: Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters used water to extinguish two wheelie bins which had been deliberately set alight on Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port.  The team were at the scene for approximately 20 minutes. Anyone with any information in relation to the fire is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 196674. Information can also be passed anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111. View our safety advice on how to prevent a wheelie bin fire. https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
