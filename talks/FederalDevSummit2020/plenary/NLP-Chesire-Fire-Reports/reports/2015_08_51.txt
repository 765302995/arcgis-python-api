Fire at recycling plant in Widnes Time of Incident: 19/08/2015 - 21:11 Location: Ditton Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and one from Runcorn. Firefighters have tackled a fire affecting recycling waste in Widnes.  A digger was used to remove unaffected materials and firefighters then used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. Fire crews contained the small smouldering fire using a covering jet and a thermal imaging camera was used to monitor the affected refuse pile and make sure that the scene was safe.
