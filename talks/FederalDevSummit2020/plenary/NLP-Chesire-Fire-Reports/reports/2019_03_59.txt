 Flooding in Knutsford Time of Incident: 18/03/2019 - 09:53 Location: Burleyhurst Lane, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford Firefighters were called to reports of flooding at two addresses on Burleyhurst Lane in Knutsford.   Firefighters attended the scene and discovered that the flooding had been caused by a blocked culvert under one of the properties.  The team used a pump to drain the flooding into a nearby brook and then cleared the blockage in the culvert.
