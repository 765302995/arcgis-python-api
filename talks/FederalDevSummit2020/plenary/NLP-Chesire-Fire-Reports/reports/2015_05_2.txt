Rubbish in a kitchen set on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 29/05/2015 - 10:35 Location: Queens Park Gardens, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Rubbish which had been put near a toaster set on fire and required firefighters to attend and use a hose reel. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property. Smoke alarms sounded and alerted the owner to the fire.
