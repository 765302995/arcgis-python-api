 Small vehicle fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 05/05/2019 - 16:07 Location: Gorstage Lane, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly after 4pm firefighters from Northwich were dispatched to reports of a vehicle fire in Weaverham.  On arrival the crew discovered one saloon car which was well alight.  One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus, used foam to extinguish the fire.  The crew were detained at the scene for around one hour.
