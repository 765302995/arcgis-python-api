Chimney fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 09/05/2015 - 20:59 Location: Green Lane, Poynton. Attendance: A fire engine from Poynton Firefighters were called to deal with a chimney fire on Green Lane, Poynton.  Crews tackled the fire with chimney rods from the grate. They also checked the roof space to make sure that the fire had not spread.  Chimney fire safety advice
