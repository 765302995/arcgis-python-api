Fire at a Runcorn plant Time of Incident: 09/04/2017 - 06:20 Location: British Gypsum Isover Limited, Northwich Road, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn, one from Widnes, one from Stockton Heath, one from Birchwood and one from Winsford At 06:20 am fire crews were sent to British Gypsum Isover Limited on Northwich Road in Runcorn, to deal with a fire within the plant.  On arrival the fire crews found that there was a fire involving ducting and insulation.  Onsite staff have isolated the area. And plant crew are inspecting the cyclone area of the plant.   Firefighters used hose reel and main water jets to tackle the fire. They also worked alongside site staff to ensure that a silo was successfully cooled.  Update 5pm   Crews remain at the site damping down and taking temperature checks to ensure that the scene remains safe.
