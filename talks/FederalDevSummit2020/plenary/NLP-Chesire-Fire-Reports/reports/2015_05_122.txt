Collision in Neston Time of Incident: 08/05/2015 - 17:44 Location: Chester High Road, Neston. Attendance: A fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Chester High Road, Neston.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved one car. A man had managed to get out of the vehicle. Crews helped paramedics treat the man who was then taken to hospital.
