Small fire in loading bay in Alsager Time of Incident: 21/06/2017 - 12:21 Location: Asda, Lawton Road, Alsager Attendance: Three fire engines attended Firefighters were call to slight smoke logging in a supermarket loading bay. A full evacuation of the premises was completed by staff from the supermarket before crews arrived. A small fire involving a power supply unit was found and the crew wearing breathing apparatus used an extinguisher to put the fire out. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the area and the crews were detained for just over three hours.
