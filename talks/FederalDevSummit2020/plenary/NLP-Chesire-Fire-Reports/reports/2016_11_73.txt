Overturned lorry on the M6 Time of Incident: 15/11/2016 - 01:21 Location: Junctions 18 and 19 on the Northbound carriageway of the M6. Attendance: Two rapid response units from Sandbach and Holmes Chapel, one major rescue unit from Winsford, and five fire engines from Knutsford, Alsager, Middlewich, Sandbach and Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a collision between Junctions 18 and 19 on the Northbound carriageway of the M6.  When crews arrived they found that a goods lorry had overturned. The driver was trapped. Firefighters released the man who was then handed to the care of the ambulance service.
