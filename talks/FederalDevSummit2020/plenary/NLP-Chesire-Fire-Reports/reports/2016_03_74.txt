Fire in Middlewich flat Time of Incident: 17/03/2016 - 12:33 Location: Wheelock Street, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Winsford Unattended cooking caused a fire in a ground floor flat. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Electricity supplies to the property were isolated and a high pressure fan used to clear smoke.  Never leave cooking unattended - follow our kitchen safety advice
