At height rescue of a man in Crewe Time of Incident: 30/06/2017 - 18:28 Location: West Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe and the rope rescue unit from Lymm Firefighters were called to a block of flats in Crewe after a man became stuck in a window after trying to gain entry through it after being locked out. The man was stuck on the first floor of the building and crews used a short extension ladder to rescue him. An ambulance also attended the incident as a precaution. Crews were at the scene of the incident for one and half hours.
