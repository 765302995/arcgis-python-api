Shed fire spread to two properties in Bewsey, Warrington Time of Incident: 25/03/2016 - 17:36 Location: Southworth Avenue, Bewsey, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Two hose reel jets were used to extinguish a shed fire which had also spread to two terraced properties. The fire affected the solar panels on the two properties.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke from within the properties.
