House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/02/2015 - 12:48 Location: Richmond Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines from Stockton Heath and Warrington Fire crews were called to deal with a house fire on Richmond Avenue, Warrington.  When they arrived they found that the blaze involved a cooker. Firefighters in Breathing Apparatus used hose reel jets to tackle the fire. A special fan was also used to clear smoke from the property.  Crews also carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving the scene.  https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety
