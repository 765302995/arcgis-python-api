 Fire in an unused building in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/06/2019 - 17:05 Location: Mirion Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines in attendance from Crewe At around 5pm two fire engines from Crewe were dispatched to reports of a fire on Mirion Street.  On arrival at the scene the crews discovered a fire inside a former youth club.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used a short extension ladder to access the roof of the building, they then used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Crews then checked the roof of the building using a thermal imaging camera and cutting away part of the roof.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 438052.
