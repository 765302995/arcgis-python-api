 Two people rescued from a car on M6 Time of Incident: 30/10/2018 - 14:10 Location: M6 southbound junction 19 Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm, one from Northwich and two fire engines from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Road traffic collision on M6Fire crews were called to a crash between a car and a HGV on the M6 southbound near to junction 19. Two casualties were helped from a car by fire crews and then handed into the care of paramedics.
