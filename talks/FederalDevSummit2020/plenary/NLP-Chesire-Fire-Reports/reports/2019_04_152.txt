 Tree fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 17:35 Location: Warrington Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Fire crews were called to a fire in a tree on Warrington Road, Widnes. Crews used a beater to put out the fire. The fire had been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire please contact Cheshire Police.
