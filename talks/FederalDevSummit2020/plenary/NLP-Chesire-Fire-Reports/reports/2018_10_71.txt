 Gas cylinder on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 20/10/2018 - 04:13 Location: Tryfan Way, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Just before 4.15am crews were called to Tryfan way following reports of a gas cylinder on fire. Firefighters attended and discovered a canister on fire in a metal wheelbarrow. They used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and cool the canister.
