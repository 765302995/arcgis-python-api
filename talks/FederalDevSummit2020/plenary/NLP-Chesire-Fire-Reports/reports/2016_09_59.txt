Chalet on fire in Sound near Nantwich Time of Incident: 20/09/2016 - 03:35 Location: Sound Lane, Sound near Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe, one from Nantwich and one from Alsager A chalet was on fire in Sound Lane. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a covering jet to put out the blaze. Smoke alarms were fitted in the building and they alerted the occupier to the fire.
