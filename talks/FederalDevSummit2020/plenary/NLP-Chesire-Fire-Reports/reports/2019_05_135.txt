 Logs on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/05/2019 - 21:08 Location: Upper Mersey Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly after 9pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Spike Island, Upper Mersey Road, Widnes. On arrival the crew found a number of logs on fire. Firefighters extinguished the fires with water and were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
