Water in electrical substation in Chester Time of Incident: 26/11/2015 - 20:01 Location: Queen Street, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters used a pump to drain water from an electrical substation in Chester.  Scottish Power attended the scene and advised the area was safe for the crew to enter to remove the water.  The incident was then handed over to Scottish Power to try and identify the source of the water leak.
