 Tent fire in Ashton Time of Incident: 24/06/2018 - 05:17 Location: Kelsall Road, Ashton, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester attended Firefighters from Chester attended reports of a fire in a tent on Kelsall Road in Ashton. On arrival crews  found that a man had sustained serious burns as a result of the fire. The team provided first aid at the scene and the casualty was then taken to Whiston Hospital by North West Ambulance Service.
