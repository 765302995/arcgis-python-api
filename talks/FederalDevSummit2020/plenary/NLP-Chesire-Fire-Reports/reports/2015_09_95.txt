Electric box fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/09/2015 - 08:42 Location: Gregory Close, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Gregory Close, Warrington.  When they arrived they found that there had been a fire in an electrical box. This had been put out by the homeowner.  Crews turned off the electricity and carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving.
