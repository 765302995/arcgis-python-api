 Tree stump on fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 21/05/2018 - 20:58 Location: Mottram Road, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 8.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a tree stump being on fire in the open on Mottram Road in Alderley Edge. The crew used water to extinguish the fire, which is believed to have been started by discarded smoking materials. Firefighters were at the scene for around 15 minutes.
