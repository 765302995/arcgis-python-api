 Fire involving a lorry and a trailer in Chester Time of Incident: 16/05/2018 - 12:28 Location: Ince Lane, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port At around 12.30pm firefighters were called to a report of large vehicle being on fire at the Gowry Landfill Site on Ince Lane in Chester. On arrival the crews discovered that the fire involved a lorry and a trailer. A cab on the trailer was well alight and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. The crews then damped the area down. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
