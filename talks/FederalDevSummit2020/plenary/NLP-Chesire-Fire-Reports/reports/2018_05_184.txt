 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/05/2018 - 23:41 Location: Ellen Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to Ellen Street, Warrington, after a report of a wheelie bin fire. Crews found the fire was located in an alleyway and used a hose reel jet to extinguish it. This was believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about this incident please call Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
