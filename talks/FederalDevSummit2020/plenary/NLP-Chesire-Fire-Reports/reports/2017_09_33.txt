Road traffic collision on the A550 Time of Incident: 21/09/2017 - 17:19 Location: A550 Welsh Road Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Chester Fire crews were called to a one vehicle road traffic collision on the A550 Welsh Road. The vehicle was well alight on arrival and crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fire. One casualty was taken by paramedics to hospital. Crews were at the scene for 50 minutes.
