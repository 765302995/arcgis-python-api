Tumble dryer fire in Chester Time of Incident: 16/05/2017 - 10:51 Location: Heath Close, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Powey Lane Firefighters were called to a tumble dryer fire on Heath Close, Chester, at 10:51 this morning. One crew from Chester and one from Powey Lane attended the incident and used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
