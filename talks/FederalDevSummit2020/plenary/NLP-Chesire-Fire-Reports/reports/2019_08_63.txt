 Fire in a flat in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/08/2019 - 00:34 Location: Perrins Road, Warrington. Attendance: Four fire engines attended - two from Warrington, one from Penketh and one from Newton-le-Willows Shortly after 12.30am firefighters from Warrington, Penketh and Newton-le-Willows were dispatched to reports of a domestic building fire on Perrins Road,  On arrival the crews discovered a fire in a ground floor flat.  Four firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, entered the building and used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and police officers have arrested a 15-year old boy on suspicion of arson with intent to endanger life.  Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML492404.  Crews remained in attendance for just over two hours and 45 minutes.
