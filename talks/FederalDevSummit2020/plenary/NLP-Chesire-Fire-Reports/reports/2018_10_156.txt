 Washing machine fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/10/2018 - 19:08 Location: Ottawa Gardens, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a washing machine well alight in the kitchen of an address on Ottawa Gardens in Warrington.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews used a hose reel and a positive pressure ventilation fan to extinguish the fire.  Everyone inside the address was accounted for.
