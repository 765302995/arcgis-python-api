Overheated brakes on tanker in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/06/2016 - 07:23 Location: M6 south, between junctions 21 and 20 Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Warrington Firefighters used a hose reel to cool brakes on a tanker after they overheated. The vehicle was located on the hard shoulder and Highways Agency attended.
