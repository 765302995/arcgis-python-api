 Reported car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/11/2018 - 12:37 Location: Chester Road, Higher Walton, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath Firefighters from Stockton Heath attended reports of a car fire in Highter Walton. On arrival at the scene there was smoke coming from the bonnet of a saloon car but there was no sign of any fire. The team used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hot spots, they then moved the vehicle to a safe location at the side of the road. Crews also assisted with clean up work at the site, removing a quantity of oil which had leaked from the vehicle. The team were detained at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
