 Skip fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/04/2018 - 14:09 Location: Reynolds Street, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Lymm At around 2.09pm firefighters were called to reports of a skip being on fire in Reynolds Street, Latchford, Warrington.  On arrival the crews found the skip to be well alight.  Firefighters used a main jet and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  They were at the scene for around 20 minutes.
