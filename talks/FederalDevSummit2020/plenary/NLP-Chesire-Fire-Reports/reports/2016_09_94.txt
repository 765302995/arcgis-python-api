Car fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 13/09/2016 - 03:23 Location: Palmer Road, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach A car fire is believed to have been started deliberately near garages on Palmer Road. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to bring the fire under control.  Anyone with any information regarding this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 77 of 13 September.
