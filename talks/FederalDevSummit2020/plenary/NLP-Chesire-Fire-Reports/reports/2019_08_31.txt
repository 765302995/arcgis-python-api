 Bins on fire near a park in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/08/2019 - 18:56 Location: Bewsey Park, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 7pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at the back of Bewsey Park in Warrington. On arrival the crew found two bins on fire. Firefighters extinguished the fires with water and were in attendance for around 15 minutes. The fires are believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fires should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101 or give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries.
