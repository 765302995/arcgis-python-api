 Fire in an outbuilding in Widnes Time of Incident: 13/11/2018 - 12:47 Location: Cronton Lane, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Widnes and Penketh. At 12:45 Firefighters from Widnes and Penketh attended reports of a building fire on Cronton Lane. On arrival crews discovered a refuse fire in a brick outbuilding. The team extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. Crews were in attendance for approximately 35 minutes.
