 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 19/11/2018 - 17:17 Location: Deansway, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters  from Widnes used a hose reel jet to tackle a wheelie bin which had been deliberately set on fire on Deansway. The team were detained at the scene for approximately 20 minutes. Anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For more information about wheelie bin safety visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety .
