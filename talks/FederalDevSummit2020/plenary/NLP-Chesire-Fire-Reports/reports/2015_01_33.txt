Fire in log burner in Tarporley Time of Incident: 21/01/2015 - 21:42 Location: Quarry Bank, Tarporley Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford A fire in the flue of a log burner resulted in firefighters dismantling the burner.  They also checked the room above and loft space to make sure there was no fire spread.   Please follow our safety advice for Wood-burning stoves
