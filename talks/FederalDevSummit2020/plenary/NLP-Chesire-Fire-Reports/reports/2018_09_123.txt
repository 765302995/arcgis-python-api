 Chimney fire at a farm in Burleydam Time of Incident: 05/09/2018 - 20:52 Location: Lodmore Lane, Burleydam Attendance: One fire engine attended from Audlem At around 8.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a chimney fire at a farm on Lodmore Lane in Burleydam. On arrival the crew discovered a hearth smouldering. Firefighters removed debris and damped the area down to make it safe. They used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and removed a bird's nest that was affected by the fire using chimney rods. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour. For tips on avoiding a chimney fire visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chimneys.
