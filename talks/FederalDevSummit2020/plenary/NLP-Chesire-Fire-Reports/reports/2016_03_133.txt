Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 07/03/2016 - 16:48 Location: Stokesay Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Arson is suspected in the case of a car fire in Croft Court. The car was against a garage wall and the fire damaged guttering on the garage. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze. Police officers were asked to attend due to the suspicious nature of the incident.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
