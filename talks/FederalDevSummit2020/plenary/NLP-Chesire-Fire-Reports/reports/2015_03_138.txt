Collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 04/03/2015 - 17:59 Location: Northwich Road, Northwich. Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich and Widnes Fire crews were called to a report of a collision on Northwich Road, Dutton, Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved one car. A man was trapped inside. Crews cut him free and passed him to the care of the ambulance service.
