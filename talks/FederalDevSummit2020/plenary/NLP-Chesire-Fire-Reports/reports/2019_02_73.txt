 Large rubbish fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 17/02/2019 - 21:10 Location: West Street, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe attended. Firefighters were called to a fire in a demolished garage in West Street, Crewe. A large amount of rubbish had been set on fire. Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the flames and then continued to damp down and turn over the rubbish to ensure it didn't reignite.
