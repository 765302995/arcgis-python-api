 Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 27/03/2019 - 03:13 Location: Millfield Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to Millfield Road, Widnes, after a report of a car fire. Crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information then please contact Cheshire Police.
