 Rubbish fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 23/03/2018 - 21:16 Location: Chilington Avenue, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to a rubbish fire in Chilington Avenue, Widnes. The rubbish which was well alight was approximately 5 square metres in size. Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the flames and informed police.
