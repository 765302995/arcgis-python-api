Chimney fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 21/01/2016 - 16:50 Location: Nantwich Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters tackled a chimney fire at a property on Nantwich Road. Once the fire was out a full investigation of the building was carried out due to heavy smoke logging in the loft space and roof void.  Please follow our chimney safety advice - Chimneys
