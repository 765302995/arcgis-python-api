Fire on a railway embankment Time of Incident: 07/04/2015 - 19:02 Location: Hale road, widnes. Attendance: A fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to a fire on a railway embankment off Hale Road in Hale Road in Widnes.  The trains had to be stopped while crews tackled the blaze that involved an area of grass. They used beaters and water backpacks to put the fire out.
