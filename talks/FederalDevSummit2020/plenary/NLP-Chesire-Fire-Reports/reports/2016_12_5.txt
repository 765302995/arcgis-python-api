Chimney fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 30/12/2016 - 11:35 Location: Lowcross Lane, Malpas Attendance: Two fire engines from Malpas and Chester Firefighters were called to a house in Malpas due to reports of a strong smell of burning from the chimney. When firefighters arrived, they discovered the top of the chimney was very hot so they removed a plate from the chimney fire to reveal a smouldering nest inside. Crews removed the nest from the premises and dampened it.
