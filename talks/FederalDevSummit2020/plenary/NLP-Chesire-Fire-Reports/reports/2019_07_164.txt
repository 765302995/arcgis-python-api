 Fire in a back garden in Congleton Time of Incident: 04/07/2019 - 15:54 Location: Fern Crescent, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Shortly before 4pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the back garden of a domestic property in Fern Crescent, Congleton. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a sofa. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than 20 minutes.
