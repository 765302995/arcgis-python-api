 Car trapped in flood water in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/10/2019 - 11:32 Location: Stockport road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh and one from Birchwood along with the water incident unit from Warrington and the Rope Rescue Unit from Lymm A car that was trapped in flood water, with two people trapped inside the vehicle, was attended by firefighters. On arrival firefighters rescued the two people from the car and got the vehicle out of the water. Police attended and closed the road.
