Cooker on fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 06/03/2017 - 13:55 Location: Chapel Lane, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Wythenshawe Firefighters were called to a cooker on fire and used a hose reel to extinguish it. The fire was in a first floor flat and the crew used a large fan to remove smoke from the property.
