 Reports of an industrial fire in Radway Green Time of Incident: 29/04/2019 - 10:49 Location: Radway Green Road, Radway Green Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Alsagar and Crewe Shortly before 11am firefighters from Alsager and Crewe were dispatched to reports of a fire at an industrial unit in Radway Green.  On arrival at the scene crews found that all staff working at the premises had been safely evacuated.  Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to search the building for any signs of fire.  While searching the premises firefighters discovered that there had been a small explosion in the boiler room.  Firefighters conducted safety checks and isolated gas and electricity supplies to the room.  A specialist engineer was called to the site.  Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour.
