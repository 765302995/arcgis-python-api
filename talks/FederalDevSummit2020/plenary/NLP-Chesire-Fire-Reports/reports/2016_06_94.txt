Kitchen fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 13/06/2016 - 19:50 Location: Lynton Lane, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Fire crews were sent to deal with a fire in a kitchen of a flat in Alderley Edge. On arrival at the property, fire crews found that there had been a fire inside an oven, but the fire was already out.  Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke from the flat.
