Fire in compressor room in Widnes Time of Incident: 11/11/2015 - 06:56 Location: Desoto Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes, one from Runcorn and the aerial appliance and supporting fire engine from Stockton Heath One person suffered minor smoke inhalation after a fire in the compressor room of a building in Widnes.  The building was 30m x 30m and two of the crew wore breathing apparatus and used a hose reel and covering jet to extinguish the fire. A large fan was also used to clear the smoke.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check the building for hot spots before the incident was handed over. The aerial appliance was not used.  An Ambulance was requested due to one person suffering from slight smoke inhalation and a fire investigator was also requested to attend the scene.
