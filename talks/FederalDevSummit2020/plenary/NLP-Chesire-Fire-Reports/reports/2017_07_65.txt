Large amount of plastic on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 16/07/2017 - 21:38 Location: Cholmondeley Road, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Runcorn, one from Widnes and one from Frodsham Fire crews were called to a large rubbish fire on wasteland off Cholmondeley Road, Runcorn. The fire involved 100m by 100m of plastic ducting. Crews used three main jets and two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire which was giving off large plumes of smoke. Crews were at the scene for approximately one hour 20 minutes.
