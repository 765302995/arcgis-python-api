 Flat fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/08/2018 - 14:29 Location: Wilderspool Causeway, Warrington Attendance: Four fire engines attended - one from Birchwood, one from Lymm, one from Penketh and one from Widnes At around 2.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a flat fire on Wilderspool Causeway in Warrington.  On arrival crews found a ground floor flat well alight and that all persons were accounted for.  The fire also affected a first floor flat.  Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  A covering jet was also used.  Gas and electricity supplies were isolated and once the fire was out crews searched the upper floors for signs of fire.  Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and a large high pressure fan to disperse smoke.  One woman was treated by paramedics for smoke inhalation.  Firefighters were in attendance for more than three hours.
