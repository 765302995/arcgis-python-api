Serious collision in Tarporley Time of Incident: 27/05/2016 - 21:38 Location: Forest Road, Tarporley. Attendance: Fire engines from Tarporley, Northwich and Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Forest Road, Tarporley.  When they arrived they confirmed that this was an incident involving a car and a motorcycle. No action was required by the attending crews and so they helped by making the scene safe.
