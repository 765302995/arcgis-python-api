Tumble dryer fire in Chester Time of Incident: 24/02/2017 - 01:43 Location: Dicksons Drive, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Powey Lane A tumble dryer is believed to have been the cause of a house fire in Dicksons Drive. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reels and a covering jet to bring the blaze under control. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke and a thermal imaging camera checked for any further hot spots. The area was then damped down and the adjoining property checked for fire spread.  Crews were on scene for around one hour and forty-five minutes.
