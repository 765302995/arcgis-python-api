Woman rescued from Warrington house fire Time of Incident: 29/12/2016 - 08:45 Location: Telford Close, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a house fire on Telford Close in Warrington, where a person was reported to be trapped inside the property.  On arrival the fire crew found a woman on the first floor of a three storey property who needed rescuing.  The crew rescued the woman using a ladder at a window. The crew used a hose reel to extinguish the fire and a large fan to remove smoke from the property.
