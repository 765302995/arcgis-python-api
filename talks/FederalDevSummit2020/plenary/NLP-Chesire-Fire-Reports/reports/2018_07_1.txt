Fire in a garden in Widnes Time of Incident: 30/07/2018 - 16:06 Location: Lower House Lane, Wdnes Attendance: Onere engine attended from Widnes At around 4.05pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a garden on Lower House Lane, Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered it was a case of controlled burning of building waste. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to dampen down the flames and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
