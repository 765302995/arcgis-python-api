Wheelie bin on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 11/07/2016 - 01:27 Location: Ripon Row, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Deliberate ignition is suspected as the cause of a fire in a wheelie bin in Runcorn. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and are urging people to follow our wheelie bin safety advice.  Anyone with any information about this, or similar, incidents is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 11.
