 Vehicle fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 23/02/2018 - 19:38 Location: Honister Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One engine from Warrington Firefighters responded to reports of a blue BMW saloon car well alight on the driveway of a house. A single hose reel and breathing apparatus were used to extinguish the flames. They remained at the scene for a further hour to make the vehicle safe and dampen down.   The incident is thought to be have been a deliberate ignition. Anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire police on 101, quoting incident 850 of 23 February 2018.
