Bin compound fire Time of Incident: 03/07/2016 - 02:00 Location: Kingsway, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Kingsway, Widnes.   When they arrived crews found that the fire involved an outside bin compound with three commercial wheelie bins on fire. Crews used three hose reels and one breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire.  Crews were in attendance for one hour at the incident.
