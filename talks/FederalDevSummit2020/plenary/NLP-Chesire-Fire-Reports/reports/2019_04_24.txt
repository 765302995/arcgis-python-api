 Motorcycle fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/04/2019 - 20:39 Location: Navigation Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 8.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a motorcycle being on fire in Navigation Street, Warrington. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 384542, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
