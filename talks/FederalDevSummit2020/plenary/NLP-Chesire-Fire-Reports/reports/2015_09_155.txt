Fire at derelict building in Alsager Time of Incident: 02/09/2015 - 22:09 Location: Linley Lane, Alsager Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe, one from Sandbach, one from Alsager and a fire engine and aerial appliance from Macclesfield. Firefighters were called to a fire involving a derelict building at Tollgate Farm, Linley Lane, Alsager. Four fire engines and a hydraulic platform attended the incident where crews used a seven metre ladder, two breathing apparatus and covering jet to bring the blaze under control. Firefighters were at the scene for almost three and a half hours.   This fire is suspected to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
