 Two children rescued at height in Winsford Time of Incident: 20/02/2018 - 15:32 Location: Basford Way, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters from Northwich were called to reports that two people were trapped on top of a container on Basford Way, Winsford. Crews attended the scene and located two 12-year-old boys on top of a metal shipping container. The team used a specialist seven meter ladder to rescue the boys.
