 Collision in Neston Time of Incident: 08/03/2019 - 17:57 Location: Lees Lane, Neston. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane. One fire engine from Ellesmere Port. Firefighters attended a one vehicle collision on Lees Lane, Little Neston.  One casualty was released from the vehicle by fire service and was treated by paramedics.  Crews remained in attendance for approximately 60 minutes.
