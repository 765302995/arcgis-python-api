Fire at Moss Farm Cricket Club near Northwich Time of Incident: 05/09/2016 - 01:30 Location: Firdale Road, Winnington, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich A fire in a building at Moss Farm Cricket Club was tackled by firefighters. Crews used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control and then removed machinery from the affected building. They were at the scene for just over three and a half hours.
