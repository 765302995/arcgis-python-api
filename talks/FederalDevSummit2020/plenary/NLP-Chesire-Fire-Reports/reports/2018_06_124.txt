 Kitchen fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 21/06/2018 - 14:53 Location: Smith Grove, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Crewe and one from Nantwich. Shortly before 3pm freighters were called to reports of a kitchen fire in Crewe. On arrival crews found that the fire had been caused by a hoverboard.  The team used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel yet to extinguish the fire before using a large fan to clear all of the smoke from the property.  A 72-year-old woman was treated by paramedics at the scene as a precautionary measure.  Crews were in attendance at the scene for approximately one hour.
