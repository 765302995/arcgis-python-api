 Wheelie bin on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 30/11/2019 - 17:31 Location: Meadow Row, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Crews attended to a wheelie bin on fire on Meadow Row in Runcorn.  A hose reel was used to extinguish the fire and make the area safe.
