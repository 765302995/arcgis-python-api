 Bin fire in a play area in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/04/2019 - 19:52 Location: Callands Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 8pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a play area on Callands Road in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a bin. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for less than 15 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 368117, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
