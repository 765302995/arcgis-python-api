Hotel fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 23/02/2017 - 00:02 Location: Kinderton Street, Middlewich. Attendance: Fire engines from Middlewich, Winsford and Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at The Boars Head Hotel on Kinderton Street, Middlewich.  When they arrived they found that there was a fire in the bedroom on the first floor. Crews ensured that everyone was out of the hotel before tackling the fire with hose reel jets and large ventilation fans.   One casualty was treated for smoke inhalation and crews were on scene for around three hours.
