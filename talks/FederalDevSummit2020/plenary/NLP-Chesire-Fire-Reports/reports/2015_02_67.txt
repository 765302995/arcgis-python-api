Fire in commercial building in Chester Time of Incident: 12/02/2015 - 16:12 Location: Jupiter Drive, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester and the hydraulic platform A fire involving a filtering system on the outside of a building required firefighters to attend and use two hose reels and a thermal imaging camera. The premises was evacuated while the incident was dealt with. The hydraulic platform was used to inspect the filtration system.
