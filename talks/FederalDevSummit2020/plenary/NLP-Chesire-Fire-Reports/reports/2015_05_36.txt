House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/05/2015 - 07:15 Location: Old Liverpool Road, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a hose fire on Old Liverpool Road, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found a fire in the kitchen.  Firefighters used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.    The premise was fitted with smoke alarms which alerted the occupants to the fire. An ambulance was requested by fire crews for one occupant sufering the affects of smoke inhalation.
