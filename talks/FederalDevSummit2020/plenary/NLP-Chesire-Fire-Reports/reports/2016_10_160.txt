Fire in machinery in Winsford Time of Incident: 04/10/2016 - 19:30 Location: Road Four, Winsford Industrial Estate, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters attended a call to a fire alarm sounding at Jiffy Packaging Co Ltd and on arrival found a fire out on arrival. The fire had involved ducting and the crew used a thermal imaging camera to check the area and a hose reel to cool down overheated machinery. The shutters were opened at the premises for ventilation and electrics were isolated. The crew were on scene for approximately one and a half hours.
