 Pub fire in Chester Time of Incident: 21/03/2018 - 13:06 Location: High Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine and the aerial appliance from Chester, two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one fire engine from Wrexham (North Wales Fire and Rescue Service) attended Firefighters dealt with a fire at a pub on High Street, Farndon in Chester.  When crews attended they found a heavily smoke-logged building.  The first floor fire was out before firefighters arrived however crews had to carry out an extensive search of the building to locate the fire.   Two breathing apparatus and one hose reel jet was used to put out the fire and an investigation is now underway to establish the cause.
