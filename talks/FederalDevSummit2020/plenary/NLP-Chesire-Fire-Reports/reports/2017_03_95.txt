Van on fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 16/03/2017 - 06:38 Location: M6 between junctions 20 and 21 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, one from Stockton Heath and one from Greater Manchester (Altrincham). A van which was well alight on a slip road on the M6 saw firefighters use two hose reels to extinguish the flames.
