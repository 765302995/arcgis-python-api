Smoke alarms sounding at high-rise building in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/12/2019 - 16:21 Location: Kingsway South, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: Six fire engines attended - one from Warrington, one from Birchwood, one from Stockton Heath, one from Lymm and two from Penketh - as did an aerial ladder platform from Lymm At around 4.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a high-rise building in Kingsway South, Latchford, Warrington.  On arrival crews found alarms sounding on two floors.  Firefighters inspected the floors and the rooms within them using thermal imaging cameras.  Crews later inspected all the lower floors as a precaution.  They found no signs of fire.  The alarms are believed to have been activated by smoking materials in an communal area.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
