 Skip fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/07/2019 - 19:27 Location: Rudloe Court, Poulton-with-Fearnhead Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Fire crews were called to a skip fire on Rudloe Court, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire Police.
