Small fire involving rubbish and wheelie bins in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/07/2017 - 00:08 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters used a hose reel to extinguish a small fire involving rubbish and wheelie bins. It's believed that this fire was started deliberately so if anyone know anything about who may have started it they should call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
