Serious collision in Middlewich Time of Incident: 08/04/2016 - 17:42 Location: Croxton Lane in Middlewich. Attendance: Fire engines from Middlewich and Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Croxton Lane in Middlewich.  When they arrived they found that there had been a serious collision involving two cars.   Crews worked alongside the other emergency services to deal with three casualties and make the scene safe.  Sadly, it has been confirmed that the man driving one of the cars involved in this incident died at the scene. The other casualties were not seriously hurt.  Police are appealing for witnesses of the incident. Please call 101 if you can help. Please quote reference 736 of April 8.
