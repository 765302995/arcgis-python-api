 Smoke-logged plant room in Leighton Time of Incident: 15/09/2018 - 11:38 Location: Middlewich Road, Leighton. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe and one from Birchwood Firefighters were called to Leighton Hospital after an alarm was activated in a plant room. Crews discovered the room was smoke-logged and used a thermal imaging camera to establish the source. They found an air compressor which was causing the smoke and isolated the electrics.
