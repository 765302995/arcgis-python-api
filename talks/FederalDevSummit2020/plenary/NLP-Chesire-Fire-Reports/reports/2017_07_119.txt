Motorbike fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/07/2017 - 03:15 Location: Cunliffe Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a motorbike fire on Cunliffe Close, Runcorn. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Police also attended the incident which is suspected to be arson. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.  Arson prevention
