Van on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/02/2016 - 19:11 Location: Chiltern Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington The engine bay of a commercial van was involved in a fire outside a property on Chiltern Road. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze. Police were also informed of the incident.
