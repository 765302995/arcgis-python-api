 Horse stuck in septic tank in Neston Time of Incident: 12/12/2018 - 07:17 Location: Benty Heath Lane, Neston. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port. One fire engine from Lymm and the technical rescue unit from Lymm. Horse RescueFirefighters attended reports of a horse trapped in Benty Heath Lane, Neston. The horse was trapped in a septic tank which was approximately two metres by two metres. Crews used a pump to drain the water from the tank.  A vet then sedated the horse so that firefighters could safely rescue the horse.
