 Small fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 17/06/2018 - 20:15 Location: Grange Lane, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Fire crews were called to Grange Lane, Winsford after a report of a fire. Crews found a small wood fire which had been left unattended and they used a fire extinguisher to put it out.
