 Vehicle fire on the M62 Time of Incident: 28/11/2018 - 16:03 Location: M62 eastbound between junctions 10 and 11 Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Wigan Shortly after 4pm firefighters were called to reports of a vehicle fire on the M62 eastbound between junctions 10 and 11. On arrival crews discovered that the vehicle had pulled over to a safe location and that the fire involved its engine. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and cool the engine. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The Highways Agency also attended to manage traffic.
