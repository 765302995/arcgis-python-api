Fire in the open in Handforth Time of Incident: 29/04/2019 - 16:55 Location: Finsbury Way, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Shortly before 5pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open involving rubbish in Handforth.  On arrival at Finsbury Way the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning that was unsupervised.  Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet.  They were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
