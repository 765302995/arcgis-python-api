 Chimney fire in Disley Time of Incident: 03/11/2018 - 21:30 Location: Bentside Road, Disley. Attendance: One engine from Poynton Firefighters were called to a fire involving a chimney at an address in Bentside Road, Disley. Crews used a hose reel to tackle the fire from the hearth. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots in the roof space and chimney.
