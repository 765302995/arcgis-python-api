 Cavity wall fire in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 15/09/2018 - 12:46 Location: Bromley Drive, Holmes Chapel. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel and Sandbach Firefighters were called to an address in Bromley Drive, Holmes Chapel after an attempt to remove a wasps nest with a blow torch caused smoke to come from the cavity wall. Crews discovered small seats of fire in wall cavity. They used a hose reel to damp down and a thermal image camera and builders flexible camera to check for hotspots.
