 Bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/03/2018 - 09:15 Location: Heath Lane, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port At around 9.15am firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a house on Heath Lane in Ellesmere Port. The bin fire was out on arrival and the crews used a large fan to clear the smoke.
