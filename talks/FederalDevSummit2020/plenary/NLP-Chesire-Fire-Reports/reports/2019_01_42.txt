 Cat rescued from tree in Over Alderley Time of Incident: 04/01/2019 - 12:10 Location: Hocker Lane, Over Alderley Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters rescued a cat which was found stuck in a tree on Hocker Lane in Over Alderley.  Crews initially used a 12 metre ladder to reach the cat before placing a sheet close to the ground for it to jump safely onto. The cat then jumped down and went on its way!   The RSPCA were also in attendance.
