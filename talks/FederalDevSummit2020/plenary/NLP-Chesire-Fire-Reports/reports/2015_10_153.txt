Chimney fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 02/10/2015 - 21:32 Location: Middlewich Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots after a chimney fire in Winsford.  The also inspected the loft space to ensure the fire had not spread and removed the remaining embers from the hearth. Chimney safety advice
