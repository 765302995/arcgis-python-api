 Kitchen fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 13/04/2018 - 19:41 Location: Halebank Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters responded to reports of a kitchen fire. On arrival, they found the property had become smokelogged due to the iron being left on the hob. A positive pressure ventilation fan was used to clear the smoke from the house.
