 Fire at derelict building in Runcorn Time of Incident: 16/09/2019 - 15:08 Location: Wivern Place, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters attended to a derelict building on Wivern Place in Runcorn that was reported on fire.  On arrival, they discovered the area was of controlled burning and a hose reel jet was used to put it out.
