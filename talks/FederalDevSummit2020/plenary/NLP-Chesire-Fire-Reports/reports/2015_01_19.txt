Tractor fire in Cholmondeley Time of Incident: 26/01/2015 - 12:01 Location: Croxton Green, Cholmondeley. Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Crewe One hose reel was used to extinguish a fire involving a tractor that was well alight on arrival of the crews. The firefighters cooled the vehicle before inspecting to ensure the fire was fully extinguished.
