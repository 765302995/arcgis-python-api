Boiler on fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 15/05/2017 - 16:59 Location: Meadow Lane, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and one from Chester Firefighters were called to a boiler which was well alight. The crews isolated electricity and oil to the boiler and extinguished the fire. There was no damage to the interior of the property and two people suffered smoke inhalation, which paramedics dealt with.
