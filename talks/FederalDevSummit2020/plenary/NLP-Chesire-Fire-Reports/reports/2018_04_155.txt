 Petrol tanker spillage in Marton Time of Incident: 31/03/2018 - 23:18 Location: Congleton Road, Marton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters attended to reports of a petrol tanker having spilt diesel on Congleton Road in Marton.  Officers assisted with traffic control while crews try to utilise dammit paste to stop the leak. An environmental bag was also used to absorb some of the spillage from the fuel tank.  The Highways Agency cleared up the road and the tanker was safely removed with no petrol believed to have spilt into the grids.
