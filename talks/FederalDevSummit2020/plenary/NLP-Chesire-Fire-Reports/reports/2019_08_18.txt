 Tractor fire in Waverton Time of Incident: 27/08/2019 - 13:50 Location: Guy Lane, Waverton Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Chester and one from Powey Lane Shortly before 2pm firefighters were called to reports of a tractor fire in Guy Lane, Waverton.  On arrival the crews discovered a fire involving the engine compartment of the vehicle.  A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  The engine compartment was then damped down to make it safe.  Firefighters were at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
