 Flood water in Warrington school Time of Incident: 19/07/2019 - 17:20 Location: Pepper Street, Lymm, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 5.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a school in Pepper Street in Lymm, Warrington, being affected by flood water. On arrival at Ravenbank Community Primary School the crew found that part of the school was flooded and that the electricity supply to the building had been isolated. Firefighters used a pump to remove the flood water from the school and were in attendance for around an hour.
