 Van fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 20/11/2018 - 03:41 Location: Burns Road, Congleton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Shortly after 3.40am firefighters from Congleton were called to reports of a vehicle fire on Burns Close. On arrival at the scene the team found a van which was well alight. Crews used two breathing apparatus and two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 246212. Information can also be passed anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111.
