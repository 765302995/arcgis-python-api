 Outbuilding fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 27/04/2018 - 15:14 Location: Pooltown Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a fire in an outbuilding on Pooltown Road, Ellesmere Port. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which had also spread to a fence.
