Shop fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 07/11/2017 - 15:07 Location: Hibel Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to reports of fire at Tesco store on Hibel Road, Macclesfield. Two crews attended the store and discovered that the fire involved a computer unit within an electrical room. Crews used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and removed the computer from the building.
