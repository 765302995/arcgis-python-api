 Commercial building fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 22/02/2018 - 15:43 Location: Nantwich road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Two fire engines from Crewe attended a commercial building fire on Nantwich Road, Crewe. The crew used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which involved an extractor fan. The team were in attendance for approximately 45 minutes.
