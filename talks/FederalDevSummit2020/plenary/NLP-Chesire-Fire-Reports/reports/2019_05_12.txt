 Rubbish small fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 28/05/2019 - 21:10 Location: Cranford Drive, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford Shortly after 9pm firefighters from Knutsford were dispatched to reports of a rubbish fire on Cranford Drive.  The fire was out on arrival. However, the team used one hose reel jet to dampen down and a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  Crews were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes.
