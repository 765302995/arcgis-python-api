 Shed fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 15/05/2018 - 00:51 Location: Wharton Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and Northwich Firefighters attended to a fire involving a shed and fence which had spread to a house on Wharton Road in Winsford.    A hose reel and a covering jet were used to put out the blaze.  Everyone inside the properties were accounted for.   Fire crews were in attendance for just over two and a half hours.
