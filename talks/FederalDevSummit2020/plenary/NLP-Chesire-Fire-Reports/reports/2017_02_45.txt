Recycling bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/02/2017 - 01:04 Location: West Lane in Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one fire engine from Widnes. Firefighters were called to a report of a fire near McDonalds on West Lane in Runcorn.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire in a recycling cage.  Crews used hose reel jets to put the fire out. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information about who may have started the fire please call Crimstoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
