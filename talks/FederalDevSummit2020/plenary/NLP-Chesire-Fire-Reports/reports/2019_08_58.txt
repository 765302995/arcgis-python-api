 Small fire in a garage in Haslington Time of Incident: 22/08/2019 - 18:00 Location: Oakland Avenue, Haslington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At 6pm firefighters were called to a report of a small fire in a domestic garage in Oakland Avenue, Haslington. The fire was out on arrival. Firefighters checked temperatures inside the garage using a thermal imaging camera to make sure it was safe and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
