Collision in Knutsford Time of Incident: 20/09/2016 - 20:52 Location: Pepper Street in Mobberley. Attendance: A fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters responded to a report of a collision on Pepper Street in Mobberley.  When they arrived crews found that a car had gone off the road into a garden. There was nobody trapped and so crews assisted Cheshire Police with making the scene safe.
