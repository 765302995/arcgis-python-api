Grass fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 22/03/2015 - 18:27 Location: Forest Hills Golf Course, Bellemonte Road, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine from Frodsham Firefighters had to make their way on foot to a report of a fire on a Frodsham golf course.  They used hand extinguishers to put out the fire which was near the seventh hole on the course.   Please follow our outdoor safety advice to prevent grassland fires - Countryside safety
