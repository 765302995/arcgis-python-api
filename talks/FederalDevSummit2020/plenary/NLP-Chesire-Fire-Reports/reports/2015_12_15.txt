One sheep rescued from water in Northwich Time of Incident: 27/12/2015 - 12:08 Location: Eaton Lane, Davenham, Northwich Attendance: Fire crews from Northwich and Knutsford Fire crews from Northwich and Knutsford were sent to an incident where one sheep was stranded within approximately 12 inches of water by Eaton Lane, Davenham, Northwich. Swiftwater rescue crews used a rescue sled to rescue the sheep.
