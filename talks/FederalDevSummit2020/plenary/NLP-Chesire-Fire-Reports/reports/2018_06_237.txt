 Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/06/2018 - 21:00 Location: Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Crews were called to reports of a kitchen fire at a ground floor flat on Sutton Way in Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port.  The fire, believed to involve a motorbike battery charging on the cooker, was put out by crews wearing two breathing apparatus and using a hose reel.    All people were accounted for.
