Northwich skip fire Time of Incident: 19/06/2015 - 15:37 Location: Middlewich Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford Three large industrial skips were involved in fire which was also affecting wooden pallets.  Hose reel jets were used to put out the fire, and then firefighters dug out the skip to make sure there were no further areas of heat that could reignite.
