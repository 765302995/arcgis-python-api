One person trapped following two vehicle collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/11/2017 - 18:38 Location: Broad Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from St Helens (Merseyside Fire and Rescue Service) attended Road traffic collision in WarringtonFirefighters from Warrington responded to reports of a two vehicle collision on Broad Lane, Warrington.  On arrival the crews used electronic cutting gear to take the roof off one of the vehicles and release one casualty who was trapped inside.  The casualty was then passed to the ambulance service.
