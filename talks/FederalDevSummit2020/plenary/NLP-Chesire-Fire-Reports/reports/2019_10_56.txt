 Rubbish fire, Runcorn Time of Incident: 21/10/2019 - 22:47 Location: Parkers Court, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to tackle a fire on wasteland near Parkers Court. It turned out to be a small bonfire, measuring around 3 square metres and containing mostly household rubbish. They used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames, and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
