Outbuilding fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 06/08/2015 - 18:52 Location: Station Road, Winsford. Attendance: Fire engines from Winsford and Sandbach Firefighters were called to a report if a fire off Station Road in Winsford.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving outbuildings. Crews tackled the incident with main and hose reel jets.  An investigation has now been launched to find the cause of the fire.
