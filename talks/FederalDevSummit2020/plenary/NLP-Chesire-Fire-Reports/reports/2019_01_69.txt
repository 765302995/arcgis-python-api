 Industrial bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/01/2019 - 19:45 Location: Foundry Street, Warrington. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington used one hose reel jet to extinguish an industrial bin fire on Foundry Street.  Crews were detained at the scene for approximately 15 minutes.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
