M6 south road traffic collision junctions 19 to 18 Time of Incident: 19/11/2016 - 11:34 Location: M6 southbound between junctions 19 and 18 Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters made the scene safe after two vehicles were in collision on the M6 southbound. The vehicles involved were a van and a light goods vehicle. Nobody was trapped in the vehicles.
