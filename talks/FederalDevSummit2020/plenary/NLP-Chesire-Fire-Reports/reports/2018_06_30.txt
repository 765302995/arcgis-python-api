 Fire in the open in Mottram St Andrew Time of Incident: 27/06/2018 - 11:38 Location: Wilmslow Road, Mottram St Andrew Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 11.40am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Wilmslow Road in Mottram St Andrew. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a bonfire that had got out of control. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and then damped the area down to make it safe. They were in attendance for around an hour.
