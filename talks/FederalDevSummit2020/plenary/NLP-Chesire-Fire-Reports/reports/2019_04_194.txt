 Chimney fire in Little Bollington Time of Incident: 11/04/2019 - 18:47 Location: Park View, Little Bollington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Fire crews attended to reports of a chimney fire at an address on Park View in Little Bollington.  On arrival, the fire was already extinguished but crews conducted a full search of the chimney breast and loft space using a thermal imaging camera to check it was safe.
