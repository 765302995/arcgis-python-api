 Small unattended fire in Anderton Time of Incident: 30/09/2019 - 19:56 Location: Daisy Bank Lane, Anderton, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters from Northwich used a hose reel jet to extinguish a small unattended fire at a business premises on Daisy Bank Lane, Anderton. The crew were detained at the scene for around 15 minutes.
