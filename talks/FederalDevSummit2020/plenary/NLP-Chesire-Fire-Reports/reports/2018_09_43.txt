 Collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/09/2018 - 07:45 Location: Robin Lane, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield and Congleton. Crews attended a two vehicle collision at the junction of Robin Lane and London Road, Macclesfield.One person was reported as being medically trapped and crews  Firefighters quickly assisted with one casualty who was medically trapped. Fire crews remained at scene for approximately twenty minutes assisting ambulance and making the scene safe.
