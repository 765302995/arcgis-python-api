 Tyre fire in a field in Congleton Time of Incident: 22/03/2018 - 19:49 Location: School Lane, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton At 7.48pm firefighters were called to a tyre fire in a field on School Lane in Congleton. On arrival the crew found that the fire involved several tyres and they used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. Firefighters were at the scene for around 15 minutes. It is believed that the fire was started deliberately. Police have been informed about the incident.
