 Disused building fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 06/07/2018 - 14:37 Location: Handley Hill, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and one from Northwich Firefighters were called to a fire at a disused building on Handley Hill in Winsford after smoke was seen coming from an old primary school site. On arrival, crews located the fire in one of the classrooms and used four breathing apparatus, a large fan and two hose reel to put out the fire. Firefighters believe the fire has been started deliberately by youths forcing entry to the school by setting fire to debris inside. Anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101.
