Fire in the Vale Royal Abbey pub Time of Incident: 06/04/2016 - 20:50 Location: Vale Royal Abbey Arms in Oakmere. Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich and Tarporley Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property at the Vale Royal Abbey Arms in Oakmere.  .  When crews arrived they found that there was a blaze in the basement. Crews used hose reel jets and special fans to deal with the incident. Electricity was also turned off.  A fire investigation is underway.
