 Outbuilding building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/06/2018 - 18:52 Location: Cantilever Gardens, Station Road, Latchford, Warrington. Attendance: Three fire engines attended - One from Penketh, one from Stockton Heath and one from Widnes Two shipping containers on fire in WarringtonFirefighters from Penketh, Stockton Heath and Widnes attended reports of an outbuilding fire at Cantilever Gardens on Station Road, Warrington.  On arrival the team found the incident fire involved two shipping containers which were well alight. The team used two hose reel jets, two breathing apparatus, water shuttling and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire.  Crews were in attendance at scene for approximately one hour and 40 minutes.
