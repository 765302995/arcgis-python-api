Rubbish on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/07/2017 - 18:50 Location: Mersey Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to a fire involving rubbish in a wooded area and extinguished it with water. It's believed that the fire may have been started deliberately. Call police on 101 if you know who may have started it.
