 Cooker fire in Goostrey Time of Incident: 12/06/2019 - 11:34 Location: New Platt Lane, Goostrey Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Shortly after 11.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the kitchen of a domestic property in New Platt  Lane, Goostrey. The fire involving a cooker hood was out on arrival and everyone was accounted for. The occupier was alerted to the fire by smoke alarms sounding. The electricity supply to the cooker was isolated and firefighters damped it down to make it safe. They also cut away part of the kitchen and used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes. For kitchen fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
