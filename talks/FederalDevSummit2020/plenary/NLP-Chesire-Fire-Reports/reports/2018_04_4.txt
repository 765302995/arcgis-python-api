 Bedroom fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 28/04/2018 - 09:34 Location: Wyndham Close, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford, one from Northwich and one from Middlewich Fire crews were called to a fire in a bedroom in a domestic property on Wyndham Close, Northwich. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a covering jet and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. One casualty was handed over to the care of paramedics suffering from burns. A fire investigation into the cause of the incident is underway.
