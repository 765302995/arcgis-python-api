Van fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 02/10/2017 - 23:44 Location: New Warrington Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Fire crews were called to a van fire on New Warrington Road, Northwich. On arrival crews found the van was well alight and used two hose reel jets to put out the fire. Police also attended this incident and arrested a male on suspicion of arson.
