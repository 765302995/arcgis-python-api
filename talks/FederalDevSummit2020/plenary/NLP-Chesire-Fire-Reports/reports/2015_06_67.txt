Collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 18/06/2015 - 17:54 Location: The Dingle, Crewe. Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on The Dingle, Crewe.  When they arrived crews found that there was a woman trapped in her car. Crews cut her free and passed her to the care of the ambulance service.
