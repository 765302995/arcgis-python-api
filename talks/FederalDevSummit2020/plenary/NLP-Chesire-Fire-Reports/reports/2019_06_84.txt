 Smoke coming from a car in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/06/2019 - 14:47 Location: Knutsford Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 2.45pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from a car on Knutsford Road in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that the smoke was caused by overheated brakes. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to cool down the brakes and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
