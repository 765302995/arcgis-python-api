Commercial building fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 24/12/2017 - 02:17 Location: Webbs Lane, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Winsford Firefighters from Middlewich and Winsford attended reports of commercial building fire on Webbs Lane, Middlewich. On arrival crews found that the fire involved a vehicle on fire in a car park. Crews used two hose reel jets, one breathing apparatus and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately 70 minutes.
