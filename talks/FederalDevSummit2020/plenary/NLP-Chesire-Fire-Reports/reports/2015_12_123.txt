Road traffic collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/12/2015 - 08:31 Location: Fiddlers Ferry Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters attended a road traffic collision which left one lady trapped in a vehicle. One vehicle was involved which was on its side. Crews stabilised the vehicle and made preparations to release the casualty once she had been assessed by medical personnel.  The casualty was assessed by advanced paramedics who found that she was able to be assisted from the vehicle by firefighters.   Fiddlers Ferry Road remains closed by police.
