One person rescued from a car on the M56 Time of Incident: 27/12/2017 - 01:17 Location: M56 Westbound between junction 8 and 9 Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm, one from Stockton Heath and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision involving one vehicle on the M56 Westbound between junction 8 and 9. Fire crews removed one casualty from the vehicle by removing the roof before handing them into the care of paramedics. Crews were in attendance for 90 minutes.
