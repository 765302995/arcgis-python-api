 Cat stuck in tree in Great Sutton Time of Incident: 17/09/2018 - 19:16 Location: Hargrave Drive, Great Sutton in Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to reports of a cat stuck in a tree on Hargrave Drive in Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port.  Firefighters assisted the RSPCA by climbing a short extension ladder to reach the cat which was 20ft high and a hose reel jet.   Crews safely brought the cat down and reunited it with its owner.
