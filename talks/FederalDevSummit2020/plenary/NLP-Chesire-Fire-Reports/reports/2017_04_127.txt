Fractured pipe in Disley Time of Incident: 04/04/2017 - 17:03 Location: Waterside, Disley. Attendance: Fire engines from Poynton and Greater Manchester Firefighters responded to reports of an explosion at Disley Paper Mill, Waterside.  When they arrived they found that there had been a fracture on one of the steam pipes that had been mistaken for an explosion. there were no injuries and no action required by crews.
