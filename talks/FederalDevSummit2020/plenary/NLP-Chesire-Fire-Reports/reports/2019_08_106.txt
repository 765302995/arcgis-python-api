 Unattended cooking fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 13/08/2019 - 10:55 Location: Moss Road, Congleton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton. One fire engine from Macclesfield. Firefighters were called to a house in Moss Road in Congleton after a pan was left unattended on a cooker causing smoke. Crews used a large fan to disperse the smoke. A woman was checked over by paramedics as a precaution.
