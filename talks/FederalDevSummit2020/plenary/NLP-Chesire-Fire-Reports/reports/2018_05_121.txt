 Fire involving a boiler at a house in Winsford Time of Incident: 15/05/2018 - 19:12 Location: Stocks Hill, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Winsford At 7.12pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a boiler at a house on Stocks Hill in Winsford. On arrival the crews discovered that the occupant had extinguished the ground floor fire using their own hose reel jet. Firefighters turned off the electricity supply to the boiler and used a dry powder extinguisher to make the area safe. A man was treated by paramedics for smoke inhalation before the casualty was taken to Leighton Hospital. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
