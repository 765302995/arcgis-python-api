Kitchen fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 11/10/2017 - 07:04 Location: Lord Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Nantwich Patch the labrador at a neighbours house after being rescued by firefighters Firefighters were called to a fire in a kitchen. The crews used one hose reel to extinguish the fire before using a large fan to clear the smoke.  All occupants were accounted for, one dog was rescued from the premises by firefighters.  The dog that was rescued was a Labrador named Patch. Patch can been seen pictured at a neighbours house.
