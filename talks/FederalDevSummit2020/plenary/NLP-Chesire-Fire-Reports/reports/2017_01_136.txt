House fire in Congleton. Time of Incident: 02/01/2017 - 13:11 Location: Belgrave Avenue, Congleton. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Congleton, Macclesfield and Holmes Chapel. Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Belgrave Avenue, Congleton.  When they arrived they found a blaze in a two storey end terraced house  and there was a man still inside. Crews wearing breathing apparatus rescued the man who has suffered serious burns.   Firefighters then used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel, a thermal image camera and positive pressure ventilation to deal with the fire.  The injured man was taken to hospital.  An investigation has been launched to find the cause of the fire.
