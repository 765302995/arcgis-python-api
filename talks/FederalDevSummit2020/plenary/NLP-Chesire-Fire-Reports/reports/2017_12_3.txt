Smoke inside a property in Great Sankey Time of Incident: 29/12/2017 - 10:14 Location: Liverpool Road, Great Sankey Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a hairdressers on Liverpool Road, Great Sankey, after reports of smoke inside the property. Crews investigated and found the fire, involving a fuse, was out on arrival. They then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property which had been evacuated prior to the arrival of the fire service.
