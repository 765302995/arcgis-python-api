Dog rescued from water in Chester Time of Incident: 22/07/2015 - 12:16 Location: River Lane, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester, the water rescue team and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a Husky clinging to a riverbank, 20 foot down an embankment. The water rescue team from Chester was sent to assist in the rescue, due to the location of the dog, who was later reunited with its owner.
