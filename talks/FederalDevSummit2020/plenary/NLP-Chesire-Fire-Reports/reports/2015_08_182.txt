Collapsed structure in Rostherne Time of Incident: 03/08/2015 - 17:24 Location: Yarwood Heath Lane, Rostherne, nr Altrincham Attendance: Specialist appliance and supporting fire engine from Winsford, the rope rescue unit and support fire engine from Knutsford and fire engines from Wythenshawe and Altrincham (GMFRS) Firefighters from Cheshire and Greater Manchester were called to a report of a wall collapsing on a scissor lift on a construction site. Two site workers had suffered injuries and the crews assisted ambulance paramedics with trauma care.  The casualties were taken to hospital.
