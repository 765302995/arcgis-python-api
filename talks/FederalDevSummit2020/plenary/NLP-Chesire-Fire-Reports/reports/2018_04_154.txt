 Ceiling collapses at block of apartments in Middlewich Time of Incident: 01/04/2018 - 15:04 Location: East Road, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich and one from Winsford Firefighters in Middlewich were called to reports of a ceiling having collapsed at a block of apartments on East Road.  Crews cordoned off the affected rooms after a tap was believed to have been left running in a bathroom on the top floor causing the ceiling to fall in. Nobody was reported trapped in the building at the time.  Firefighters stopped the water leak and the incident was handed over to building contractors and an electrician.
