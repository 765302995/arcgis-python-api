 Fire at Pulford hotel Time of Incident: 30/05/2019 - 08:26 Location: Wrexham Road, Pulford Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Chester, one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port 9am  Firefighters are currently at a hotel in Pulford on Wrexham Road following a fire in the cellar.  The electrics have been isolated and everyone inside the hotel has been accounted for and evacuated as a safety precaution.  Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, are using a hose reel covering jet to bring the fire under control.  11am  The fire has now been extinguished.  Crews were in attendance for around one hour and 35 minutes.
