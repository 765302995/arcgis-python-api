Motorbike fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 13/11/2017 - 18:13 Location: Coroners Lane, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh One crew from Penketh used a hose reel jet to extinguish a motorbike fire on Coroners Lane, Widnes. The cause of the fire is currently being investigated and anyone with any  information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting incident 689 of 13 November 2017. Information can also be passed anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111.
