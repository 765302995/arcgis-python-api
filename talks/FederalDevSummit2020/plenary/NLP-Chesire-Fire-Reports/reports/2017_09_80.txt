Motorbike destroyed in Runcorn fire Time of Incident: 07/09/2017 - 20:00 Location: Saltash Close, Brookvale, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A water backpack was used by firefighters to put out a fire involving a motorbike in woodland. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
