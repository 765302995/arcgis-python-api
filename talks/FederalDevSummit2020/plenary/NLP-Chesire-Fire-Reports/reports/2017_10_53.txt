Overheated HGV in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 19/10/2017 - 09:15 Location: Buxton Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a lorry which had smoke coming from its brakes. A hose reel and a thermal imaging camera were used to cool the brakes down and make sure they there were no further traces of heat.
