Small grass fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 06/07/2017 - 12:39 Location: Cross Lane, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Faulty electricity pylon in CongletonFire crews were called to a small grass fire on Cross Lane, Congleton, which was started by a faulty electricity pylon. Crews used a powder fire extinguisher to put out the fire whilst they awaited the arrival of the electricity board at the scene. Crews then put out a small fire which had started in the electricity pylon with a hose reel jet.
