 Wheelie bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 15/08/2019 - 18:02 Location: Hadleigh Grove, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Runcorn firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a wheelie bin fire on Hadleigh Grove.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101. Information can also be reported anonymously via Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.  For more information about how to prevent wheelie bin fires visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety/how-to-prevent-a-wheelie-bin-fire
