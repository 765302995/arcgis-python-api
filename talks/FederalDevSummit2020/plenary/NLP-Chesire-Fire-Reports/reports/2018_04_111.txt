 False alarm at Warrington shopping centre Time of Incident: 10/04/2018 - 15:01 Location: Golden Square Shopping Centre, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Runcorn At 2.56pm firefighters were called to reports of a suspected fire at Golden Square Shopping Centre in Warrington town centre. On arrival crews discovered that it was a false alarm. Contractors had accidentally set off sprinklers at the former British Homes Stores unit, which caused a fire alarm to go off.
