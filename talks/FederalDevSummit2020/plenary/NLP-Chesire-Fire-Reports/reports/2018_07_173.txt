 Grass fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 15:27 Location: Simons Lane, Frodsham. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly before 3.30pm firefighters from Runcorn attended reports of a fire in a field near to Frodsham Golf Club. Officers attended and found that approximately 40metres by 20 metres of grass was well alight and had also spread to a nearby hedgerow. The team used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire and were in attendance at the scene for approximately one hour. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
