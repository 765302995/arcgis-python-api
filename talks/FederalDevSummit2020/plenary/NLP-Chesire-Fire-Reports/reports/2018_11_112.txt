 Van fire in Plumley Time of Incident: 09/11/2018 - 17:03 Location: Chester Road, Plumley. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich along with a support unit from Lymm Shortly after 5pm firefighters from Northwich and Lymm were called to reports of a vehicle fire on the A556 in Plumley. On arrival at the scene crews found that a van was well alight on the eastbound carriageway outside the Smoker Inn. Police attended the scene and diverted traffic via the filter road while firefighters used a hose reel jet and breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
