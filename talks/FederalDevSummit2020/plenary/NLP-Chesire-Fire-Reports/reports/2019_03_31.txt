 Chimney fire in Chester Time of Incident: 23/03/2019 - 16:57 Location: Lodge Lane, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Chester and Powey Lane Shortly before 5pm firefighters from Powey Lane and Chester attended reports of a chimney fire at an address on Lodge Lane.  On arrival at the scene the team found a fire in the upper chamber of the chimney which was well alight.  Crews used an aerial platform to access the fire from above they then used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately one hour 30 minutes.  For more information about chimney safety visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/chimneys
