House fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/08/2016 - 00:26 Location: Caernarvon Close, Runcorn Attendance: Fire engines from Widnes and Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Caernarvon Close, Runcorn.  When they arrived crews found a fire in the kitchen. A hose reel jet and a large fan was used to deal with the incident.   A woman was treated at the scene with oxygen for slight smoke inhalation but did not require any further treatment. Crews were in attendance for 50 minutes at the incident.
