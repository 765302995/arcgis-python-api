 Overturned lorry in Nantwich Time of Incident: 09/07/2019 - 12:48 Location: Wrexham Road, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Overturned lorry in NantwichFirefighters attended after a lorry had overturned on Wrexham Road, Nantwich.   An oil leak was found on the vehicle, which the crew stemmed. Firefighters used paste and absorbent mats to clear the oil from the road.  A man was taken to Leighton Hospital as a precaution and the lorry was recovered.
