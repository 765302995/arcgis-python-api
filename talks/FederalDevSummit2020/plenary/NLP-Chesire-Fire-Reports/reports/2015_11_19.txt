Flat fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/11/2015 - 18:11 Location: The Knoll, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn and Frodsham A fire in a third floor flat in Runcorn was caused by a chip pan.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus and used a fire blanket, one hose reel and a covering jet to extinguish the blaze.  A large fan was then used to ventilate the flat and crews were at the scene for around an hour. Chip pan safety advice
