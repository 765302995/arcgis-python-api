 Small kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/03/2018 - 16:14 Location: St Stephens Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters attended a fire involving a small appliance left on the hob at an address in St Stephen's Avenue in Orford. Crews removed the appliance from the kitchen ad used water to extinguish the flames. Crews then cleared the smoke from the kitchen.
