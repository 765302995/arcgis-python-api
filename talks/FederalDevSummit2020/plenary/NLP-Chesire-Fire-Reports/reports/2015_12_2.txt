Kitchen fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 30/12/2015 - 13:34 Location: Lacey Court, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters have attended a fire in Widnes caused by a grill pan which had been left unattended.  Fire crews rescued one casualty and used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They also ventilated the property using a high powered fan. Safety advice for grills
