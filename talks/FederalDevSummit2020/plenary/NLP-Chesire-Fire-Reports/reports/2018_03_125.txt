 Collision in Betchton Time of Incident: 07/03/2018 - 20:56 Location: Love Lane, Betchton. Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach. One fire engine from Alsager. Firefighters from Alsager and Sandbach along with the rapid response rescue unit were called to Love Lane in Betchton following reports of a car on its side with the driver trapped. On arrival firefighters quickly dealt with the incident by using stabilising and cutting equipment to release the casualty. They folded back the roof using the cutting equipment in order to free the woman and give first aid. Police and paramedics were in attendance.
