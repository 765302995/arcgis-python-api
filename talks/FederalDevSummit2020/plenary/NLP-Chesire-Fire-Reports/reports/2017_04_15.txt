Casualty rescued from house fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/04/2017 - 12:04 Location: Grangeway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn A casualty was rescued from a  fire at a Grangeway house. Cooking had been left unattended and firefighters had to gain entry to the house after the alarm was raised. They escorted a man from the property and gave him first aid as a precaution while they waited for the ambulance to arrive. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke from the property.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice and never leave cooking unattended.
