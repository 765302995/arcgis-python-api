Disused building fire at Great Sutton school Time of Incident: 26/05/2017 - 21:53 Location: Seacombe Drive, Great Sutton Attendance: Two fire engines each from Ellesmere Port and Chester, one from Frodsham and one from Runcorn A disused school outbuilding was on fire on Seacombe Drive. Firefighters, three in breathing apparatus, used three main jets to bring the fire under control and then used a high pressure fan to clear the smoke and ventilate the building, which measured 20 meters by 40 metres. A thermal imaging camera was then used to make sure there were no further hotspots.
