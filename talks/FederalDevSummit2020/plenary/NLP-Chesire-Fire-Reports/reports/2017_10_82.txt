Pigeon rescued in Chester Time of Incident: 13/10/2017 - 10:32 Location: Eastgate Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine and the hydraulic platform from Chester Fire crews were called to a release a pigeon which had become entangled in netting on Eastgate Street, Chester. Crews used a hydraulic platform to reach the bird which was tangled in netting on the facia of a three storey building. They then untangled the pigeon.
