Flat fire in Warrington with 5 people needing rescuing Time of Incident: 24/08/2015 - 05:46 Location: Froghall Lane, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington, two from Stockton Heath and the aerial platform Five adults needed rescuing from the second floor window of a block of flats after a fire broke out in the ground floor flat. Approximately 20 people were in the flats at the time and all self rescued apart from the five, who were brought down from a window ledge by firefighters using a ladder. It is believed that the cause of the fire was due to an electrical fault.  Two tarantulas sadly died as a result of this fire. It is believed that the fire started in the underheating mat in the spider tank.  Electrical fire safety advice
