 Kitchen fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 21/04/2019 - 14:51 Location: Foxendale Close, Northwich. CW8 4GZ Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Just before 3pm firefighters from Northwich attended a kitchen fire at a house on Foxendale Close. Wearing breathing apparatus, they used a hose reel jet to put out the fire - and then used a positive pressure ventilaton fan to clear smoke. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
