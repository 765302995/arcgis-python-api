Chip pan fire in Chester Time of Incident: 22/11/2016 - 17:30 Location: Frodsham Street, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a chip pan fire on a second floor flat. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, managed to bring the fire under control before using a large fan to clear smoke from the property. One ambulance was requested for one casualty suffering smoke inhalation.
