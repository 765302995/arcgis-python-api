Fire involving mobile home in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 02/02/2015 - 03:28 Location: Thornton Road/Cambridge Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port  Two main jets and a hose reel were used to extinguish a fire involving a mobile home in Ellesmere Port. The structure was well alight on arrival of the crews and was approximately 15m x 6m. Firefighters damped down the scene.   This fire is being treated as arson. If anyone has any information they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
