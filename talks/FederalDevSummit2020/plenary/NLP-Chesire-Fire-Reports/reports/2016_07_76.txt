Bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/07/2016 - 19:57 Location: Eccleston Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood A black bin was on fire at the side of a driveway of a property in Warrington. Firefighters extinguished the fire by damping down with a hose reel jet. The fire is believed to have been caused by a disposable barbecue left inside the bin.
