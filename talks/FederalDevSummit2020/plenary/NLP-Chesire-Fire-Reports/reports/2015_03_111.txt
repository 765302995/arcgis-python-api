Building fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 08/03/2015 - 04:32 Location: West Street, Congleton. Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton Firefighters were called to a fire on West Street, Congleton.  When they arrived crews found that a fire in the open had spread to a building. Crews used a hose reel jet and a large fan to put the fire out.  A fire investigation has been launched to find the cause of the blaze.
