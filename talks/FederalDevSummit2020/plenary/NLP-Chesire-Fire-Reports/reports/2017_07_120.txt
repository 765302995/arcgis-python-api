Garage fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 06/07/2017 - 21:00 Location: Morley Green Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one fire engine from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service (Wythenshawe) Garage fire in WilmslowFire crews were called to a garden garage which was on fire on Morley Green Road, Wilmslow. The garage was well alight on arrival and crews used two hose reel jets and a main jet to put out the fire. The firefighters then checked the garage with a thermal imaging camera.
