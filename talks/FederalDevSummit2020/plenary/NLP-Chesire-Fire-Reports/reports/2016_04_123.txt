Collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 08/04/2016 - 17:06 Location: Derby Road, Widnes. Attendance: Fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a collision off Derby Road, Widnes.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two cars. Nobody was trapped and so crews made safe the scene and a lamp post that had been damaged in the incident. The Ambulance Service attended to several casualties at the scene.
