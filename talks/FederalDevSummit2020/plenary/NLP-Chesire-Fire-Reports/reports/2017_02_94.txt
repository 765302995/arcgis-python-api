Shed and static caravan on fire in Wirswall Time of Incident: 14/02/2017 - 22:04 Location: Wirswall Road, Wirswall, near Wrenbury Attendance: Fire engines from Malpas, Audlem and Whitchurch (Shropshire Fire and Rescue Service) A garage and a static caravan were heavily involved in fire on Wirswall Road. The garage measured fifteen metres by fifteen metres. Firefighters tackled the blaze using five hose reel jets. A thermal imaging camera was then used to check there was no further fire spread. Crews were engaged at the scene for approximately seven and a hour hours.
