 Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 05/04/2019 - 16:33 Location: Chelford Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters attended to a kitchen fire at a house on Chelford Road in Macclesfield.  The fire, involving the hobs on the cooker, was extinguished using a hose reel.  The occupier of the house was outside of the home at the time and isn't believed to have suffered any injuries.
