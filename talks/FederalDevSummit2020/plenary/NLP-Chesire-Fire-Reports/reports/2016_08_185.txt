Car collides with garage in Nantwich Time of Incident: 04/08/2016 - 10:39 Location: Meeanee Drive, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine A car crashed into a prefabricated garage on Meeanee Drive. Nobody was trapped in the car but structural damage was caused to the free standing garage. Firefighters cordoned off the area and requested a structural engineer attend to assess the damage to the building..
