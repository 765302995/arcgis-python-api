Fence fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/04/2015 - 20:03 Location: Armstrong Close, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a house on Armstrong Close in Warrington.  When they arrived they found that a fire involving a barbecue had spread to a fence. Crews used hand held appliances to put the fire out.
