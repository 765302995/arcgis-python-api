 Flat bed truck fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 24/11/2018 - 04:05 Location: Caernarvon Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a flat bed truck well alight on Caernarvon Court in Ellesmere Port.  Wearing one breathing apparatus, crews used a hose reel to extinguish the fire and make the area safe.
