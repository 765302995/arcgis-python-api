 Peat fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/08/2018 - 11:15 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to Grasmere Avenue, Warrington, after a report of a peat fire. The fire involved approximately 3sq m of peat and affected the roots of a tree. Crews dug out a large amount of peat and used fire extinguishers to put out the fire.
