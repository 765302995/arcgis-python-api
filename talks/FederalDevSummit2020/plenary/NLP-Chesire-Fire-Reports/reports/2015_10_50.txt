Road Traffic Collision, High Street, Congleton Time of Incident: 21/10/2015 - 14:41 Location: High Street, Congleton Attendance: A rapid response vehicle from Holmes Chapel and a fire engine from Macclesfield. Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving one vehicle which had crashed into Congleton Town Hall.  Fire crews released one male and one female casualty from the car who were both trapped.
