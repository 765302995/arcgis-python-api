Chimney fire in Cuddington Time of Incident: 20/10/2015 - 13:55 Location: Forest Road, Cuddington Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters tackled a chimney fire in a house in Cuddington. The fire was in a log burner and crews tackled the fire from the grate and roof level. A seven metre ladder was used, along with height safety equipment, chimney rods and hoses.
