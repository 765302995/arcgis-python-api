Building fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 19/06/2015 - 17:43 Location: Woodchurch Lane, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a building fire on Woodchurch Lane, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived they found that ther was a fire involving an empty community centre. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
