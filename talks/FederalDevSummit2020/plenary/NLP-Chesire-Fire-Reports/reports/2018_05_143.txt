 Litter bin fire outside a shop in Northwich Time of Incident: 13/05/2018 - 15:55 Location: Town Square, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 4.55pm firefighters were called to reports of a litter bin being on fire outside a shop in Town Square, Northwich. They extinguished the small refuse fire using water and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
