 Smoke-logged loft in Wistaston Time of Incident: 09/01/2019 - 11:26 Location: Rope Bank Avenue, Wistaston. Attendance:  Two fire engines attended from Crewe. Firefighters were called to an address in Rope Bank Avenue in Wistaston following reports of smoke in the loft. Crews spotted charring to the joist after cutting away a piece of the ceiling. They checked for hotspots with the thermal imaging camera and confirmed there was no fire spread. The charring is believed to have had occurred due to work being carried out in that area.
