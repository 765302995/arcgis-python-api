House fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/11/2017 - 15:08 Location: Warrington Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh, one from Widnes and one from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a house fire on Warrington Road, Widnes. The fire was on the first floor of the property in a bedroom. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two covering jets, a hose reel jet and two large fans to extinguish the fire. One person was treated for smoke inhalation by paramedics. Crews were at the scene for an hour and ten minutes.
