 Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 11/03/2019 - 00:56 Location: Fleet Street, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly before 1am firefighters were called to a report of a car being on fire on a driveway in Fleet Street, Ellesmere Port. The vehicle was alight on arrival. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 343739, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
