Chimney fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 25/05/2016 - 21:21 Location: Birtles Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines and the aerial platform Firefighters were called to a chimney on fire and used chimney rods and a hose reel to extinguish the fire. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property.  Chimney safety advice
