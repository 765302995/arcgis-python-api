 Car on fire on the A500 Time of Incident: 03/08/2019 - 13:40 Location: A500, Barthomley. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At 1.40pm Crewe firefighters were called to a car on fire on the A500 at Barthomley.  Police closed the road in both directions while the fire crew, wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put the fire out.  They stayed at the scene to damp down and were in attendance for an hour.
