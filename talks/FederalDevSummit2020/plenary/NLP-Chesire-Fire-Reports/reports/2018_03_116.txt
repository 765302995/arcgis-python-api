 Fire in a milking parlour in Calveley Time of Incident: 10/03/2018 - 01:00 Location: Calveley Hall Lane, Calveley Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe, one fire engine from Nantwich and one from Tarporley Fire crews were called to a fire in a milking parlour on Calveley Hall Lane, Calveley. Crews, four wearing breathing apparatus, used three hose reel jets, hand tools and a thermal imaging camera to tackle the fire which was well developed on arrival of the firefighters.  Crews spent two hours putting out the fire.  No animals were in the parlour at the time.
