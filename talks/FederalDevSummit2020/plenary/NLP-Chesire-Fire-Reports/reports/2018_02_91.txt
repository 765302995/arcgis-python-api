 Chimney fire in Basford Time of Incident: 08/02/2018 - 17:55 Location: Weston Lane, Basford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters attended a fire in the chimney above a wood burning stove at an address in Weston Lane, in Basford. Crews disassembled the flue and used a hose reel to extinguish the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to ensure there were no hotspots in the upper floors.
