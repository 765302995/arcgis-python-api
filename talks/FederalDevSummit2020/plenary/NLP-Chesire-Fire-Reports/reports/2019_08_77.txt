 Bat rescue in Davenham Time of Incident: 20/08/2019 - 12:41 Location: River Weaver in Davenham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood along with the animal rescue units from Bollington and Lymm. The bat after it had been rescuedAt 12.40pm firefighters were dispatched to reports of a bird being trapped in a tree near to the Blue Bridge on the River Weaver in Davenham.  On arrival they discovered it was actually a bat which was trapped in fishing line.  Firefighters released the bat and it was passed to the care of a local vet.
