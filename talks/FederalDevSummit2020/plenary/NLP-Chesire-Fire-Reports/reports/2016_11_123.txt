Wheelie bins fire spreads to garage in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/11/2016 - 23:06 Location: Ferryside, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Stockton Heath and two from Warrington Fire in wheelie bins spread to two outside garages on Ferryside in Grappenhall and Thelwall. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels to put out the blaze. They also used a thermal imaging camera to search for any further hot spots and were at the scene for three hours and thirty minutes. Residents nearby were evacuated while the incident was being dealt with and an investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
