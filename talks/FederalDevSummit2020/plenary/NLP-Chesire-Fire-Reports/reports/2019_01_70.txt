 Log burner fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/01/2019 - 11:42 Location: Kerfoot Street, Warrington. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Penketh. One fire engine from Warrington attended. Firefighters were called to a log burner fire at a business premises in Kerfoot Street in Warrington. Crews used a hose reel to put out the fire.
