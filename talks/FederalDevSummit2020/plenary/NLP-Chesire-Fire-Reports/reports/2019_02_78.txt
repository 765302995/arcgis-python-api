 Motorbike fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 16/02/2019 - 19:15 Location: Shackleton Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Crews were called to deal with a motorbike on fire in a hotel car park. They used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames and notified colleagues at Cheshire Police as it was a suspected deliberate ignition. They were at the scene for around 20minutes.
