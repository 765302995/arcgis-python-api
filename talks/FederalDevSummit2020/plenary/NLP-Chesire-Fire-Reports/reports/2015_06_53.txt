Shed fires in Chester Time of Incident: 20/06/2015 - 11:30 Location: Elm Square, Chester. Attendance: Fire engines from Chester Firefighters were called to a fire on Elm Square, Chester.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two sheds. Crews used hose reel jets to put the fire out.  An investigation has been launched to find the cause of the fire. At this stage it is believed that the fires were started deliberately. If anyone has any information that could help with this investigation they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
