 Grass fire in High Peak Time of Incident: 08/05/2018 - 09:12 Location: Higher Lane, High Peak. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington and one from Congleton Grass fire in High PeakFire crews were called to a grass and gorse fire on Higher Lane, High Peak. The gorse fire was approximately 5m by 5m and crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
