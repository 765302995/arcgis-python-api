Industrial boiler backfiring at Leighton Hospital Time of Incident: 09/10/2015 - 23:41 Location: Leighton Hospital, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Nantwich A fire alarm automatically activated within the boiler room at Leighton Hospital in Crewe, so firefighters were sent to the hospital.  On arrival they found that an industrial boiler in the boiler room was backfiring so the fire crew had to isolate three boilers. They also had to stop the electricity to the three boilers, before they handed the incident over to an electrical contractor.
