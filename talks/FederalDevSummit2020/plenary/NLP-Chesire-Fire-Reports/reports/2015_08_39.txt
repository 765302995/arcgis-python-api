Property damaged by lightening in Nantwich Time of Incident: 22/08/2015 - 20:23 Location: Prince Edward Street in Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Crewe One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Crewe attended a report of a house fire on Prince Edward Street in Nantwich. The house had been damaged by lightening which had struck a telegraph pole that was outside the house. Fire crews checked the property using a thermal imaging camera and also ventilated the property due to slight smoke logging.
