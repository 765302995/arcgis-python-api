Man helped after fire caused smoke in a home in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/03/2015 - 04:38 Location: Kentmere Place, Warrington Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington and Stockton Heath A man had to be helped from a property in Warrington after a small fire caused lots of smoke. The man was at the front door when crews arrived and was escorted out of his home, which was heavily smoke logged. The smoke alarm did sound and woke neighbours who raised the alarm. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property. The man was given first aid by the firefighters and Paramedics attended to take him to hospital. The fire is believed to have been started accidentally and involved clothes and a gas fire. Smoke alarm information and advice
