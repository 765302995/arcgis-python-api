Building fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 07/03/2015 - 20:38 Location: Swanlow Lane, Winsford. Attendance: Fire engines from Winsford, Northwich, Middlewich and Chester plus the hydraulic platform. Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a derelict property on Swanlow Lane, Winsford.  When they arrived crews were confronted with a well developed blaze on the ground floor that had spread to the roof space. Crews battled the fire through the night with main water jets and hose reels.   A full fire investigation has now been launched to find the cause of the blaze, although at this stage it is believed it was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
