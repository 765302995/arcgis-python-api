Kitchen fire in Chester Time of Incident: 27/05/2015 - 18:52 Location: Glan Aber Park, Chester. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port and Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Glan Aber Park, Chester.   The fire involved a microwave oven in the kitchen, which caused slight damage to kitchen cupboards. Firefighters used one hose reel jet, two breathing apparatus and a positive pressure ventilation fan to extinguish the fire.  Kitchen fire safety advice
