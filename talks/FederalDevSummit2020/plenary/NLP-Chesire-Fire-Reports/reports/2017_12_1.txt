Skip fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 30/12/2017 - 04:53 Location: Wistaston Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish a skip fire outside a property on Wistaston Road, Crewe. The cause of the fire  is currently under investigation and anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 55511.
