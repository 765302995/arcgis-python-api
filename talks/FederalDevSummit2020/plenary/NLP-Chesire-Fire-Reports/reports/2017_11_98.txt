Vehicle fire near Widnes Time of Incident: 07/11/2017 - 16:20 Location: Watkinson Way, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to reports of a small vehicle fire on Watkinson Way on the outskirts of Widnes. One crew from Penketh attended the scene and found that there was smoke coming from the bonnet of a blue Vauxhall Astra. Firefighters isolated the battery to make the vehicle safe.
