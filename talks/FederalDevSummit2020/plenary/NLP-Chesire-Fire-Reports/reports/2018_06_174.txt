 Small peat fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 16/06/2018 - 08:13 Location: Station Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters attended to reports of a small peat fire in a forest on Station Road, Delamere in Northwich.  Crews dug a trench around the affected area and drenched the peat with a hose reel jet. They then dampened down the area to ensure the fire was fully extinguished.
