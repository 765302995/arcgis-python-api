 Bonfire left unattended in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/11/2019 - 23:32 Location: Arbury Lane, Warrington. WA2 0RJ Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Lymm Firefighters were called to a bonfire which had been left unattended.  They used a hose reel jet to put the fire out and stayed at the scene to damp down. Crews were at the incident for around 50 minutes.
