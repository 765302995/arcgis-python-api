 Brazier fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/03/2018 - 19:57 Location: Knutsford Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At 7.56pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire on Knutsford Road in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered a brazier well alight down the side of a shop and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. They were at the scene for 10 minutes.
