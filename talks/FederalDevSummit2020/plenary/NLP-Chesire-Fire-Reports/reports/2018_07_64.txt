 Domestic garage fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 24/07/2018 - 19:35 Location: Balmoral Avenue, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich At around 7.35pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a domestic garage on Balmoral Avenue in Northwich. On arrival crews found smoke coming from the garage, which was part of a row of garages. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to fight the fire while the crew removed items from the garage. The fire affected a garage next to the one that contained the fire. Once the fire was extinguished firefighters made sure the area was safe. They were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
