Lorry collided with street light in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/07/2015 - 09:01 Location: Folly Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters created a cordon and kept the scene safe after a lorry collided with a street light. The light was left in an unstable condition so the crew stayed on standby at the scene until the highways department arrived.
