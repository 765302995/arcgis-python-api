Smoke from electicity box in Northwich Time of Incident: 06/01/2017 - 08:53 Location: High street, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich and Winsford Firefighters were called to reports of a building fire in Northwich. After arriving at the scene, they saw a full evacuation had taken place due to a strong smell of smoke. Crews investigated the building for signs of fire and, after finding none, they discovered that the smoke had come from a faulty electrical box on a security door.
