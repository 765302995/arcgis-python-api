Building fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 09/08/2015 - 01:56 Location: Griffiths Road, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a premises on Griffiths Road, Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved lagging around steam pipe. Firefighters used short extension ladder, ceiling hook, two hose reel jets.
