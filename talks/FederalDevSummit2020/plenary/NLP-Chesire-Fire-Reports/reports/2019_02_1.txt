Kitchen fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 27/02/2019 - 16:58 Location: Fulbeck Way, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a kitchen fire at an address in Fulbeck Way, Widnes. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire. The crew then removed a damaged cupboard and oven from the property and isolated the electricity and gas supply.
