Double garage on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/03/2017 - 23:05 Location: St Asaph Drive, Warrington Attendance: Three fire engines from Warrington, Penketh and Stockton Heath A double garage between two houses was heavily involved in fire. Firefighters, four in breathing apparatus, used two hose reels and a main jet to bring the blaze under control. A high pressure fan was also used. The two adjoining houses, which were unoccupied at the time of the fire, were thoroughly inspected for fire and smoke spread and the residents from another nearby property were evacuated as a precaution.
