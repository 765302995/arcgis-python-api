 Small fire at a toy store in Chester Time of Incident: 13/04/2019 - 21:59 Location: Foregate Street, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Chester and one from Powey Lane At around 10pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a toy store on Foregate Street in Chester. On arrival at The Entertainer store crews discovered a fire exit door smouldering. Firefighters removed the door and extinguished the small fire with water. They then used a large fan to clear smoke from the store. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour.
