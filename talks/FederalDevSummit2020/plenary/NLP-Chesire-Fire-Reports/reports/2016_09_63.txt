Bin fire at Morrisons in Congleton Time of Incident: 19/09/2016 - 19:15 Location: Morrisons on Victoria Street in Congleton Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton. Firefighters used hose reel jets to put out a fire involving two commercial wheelie bins.  The incident took place at Morrisons on Victoria Street in Congleton.  An investigation has been launched to find the cause of the blaze. If you have any information that may help please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
