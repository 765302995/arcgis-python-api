 Fire in a garden in Winsford Time of Incident: 10/07/2018 - 10:40 Location: Llandovery Close, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich attended At around 10.40am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a garden in Llandovery Close, Winsford. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of intentional burning that was uncontrolled. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for more than 20 minutes.
