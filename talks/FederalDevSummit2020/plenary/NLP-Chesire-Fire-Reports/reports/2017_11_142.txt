Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/11/2017 - 16:44 Location: Slater Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one from Birchwood On Thursday, 2 November at 4:45pm a fire engine from Birchwood and a fire engine from Penketh were called to reports of a house fire on Slater Street in Latchford in Warrington. Fire fighters wearing breathing aparatus extinguished a fire in the kitchen using a hose reel and a covering jet.   Fire crews then used a positive pressure ventilation fan to clear the smoke from the property. Fire service are still at the incident.
