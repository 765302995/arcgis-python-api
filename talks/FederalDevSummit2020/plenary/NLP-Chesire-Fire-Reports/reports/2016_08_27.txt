Smoke in a domestic property in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/08/2016 - 14:00 Location: Mill Lane, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a domestic property as there were reports of smoke coming out from under the stairs. On arrival, crews found that the smoke was coming from the main electrical supply and so they carried out a full inspection of the property. They then gained access to the property's slate roof and carried out an inspection using a thermal imaging camera which allowed them to check for any hot spots of fire.
