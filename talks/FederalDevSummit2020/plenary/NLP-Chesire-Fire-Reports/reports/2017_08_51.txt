Outbuilding fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 22/08/2017 - 14:45 Location: Runcorn Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and one from Lymm Fire crews were called to a fire in an outbuilding at the back of the Beech Tree Inn, on Runcorn Road, Northwich. Crews used one hose reel jet to put out the fire. Crews were at the scene for approximately 15 minutes.
