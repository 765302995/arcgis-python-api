 Car fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 15/08/2019 - 11:20 Location: Windsor Grove, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At 11.20am firefighters from Runcorn were called to reports of a car fire on Windsor Grove.  On arrival the crew discovered a fire in the back of the car.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and isolated the battery in the vehicle.  The crew were detained at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.
