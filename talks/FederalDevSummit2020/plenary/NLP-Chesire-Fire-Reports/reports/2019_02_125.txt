 Tumble dryer fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/02/2019 - 10:09 Location: Station Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Firefighters were called to reports of a fire at an address on Station Road in Ellesmere Port.  The fire involved a tumble dryer and was extinguished using a hose reel jet.
