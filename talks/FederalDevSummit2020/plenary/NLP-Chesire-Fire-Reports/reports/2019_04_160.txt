 Hotel fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 09:59 Location: Chester Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Lymm Firefighters were called to a fire in a hotel on Chester Road, Runcorn. The hotel was successfully evacuated and no one was injured. The fire was in a transformer in a fuse box. Crews used a hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to put out the fire. Crews were in attendance for two hours and 5 minutes.
