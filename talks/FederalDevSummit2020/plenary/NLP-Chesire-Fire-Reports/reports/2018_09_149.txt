 Vehicle fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 01/09/2018 - 05:42 Location: Wilmslow Drive, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port used one hose reel jet and one breathing apparatus to extinguish a Vauxhall Corsa which was well alight on Wilmslow Drive, Great Sutton. Crews were in attendance for approximately 40 minutes. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 171619. Information can also be passed anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111.
