Washing machine on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 08/06/2017 - 21:02 Location: Minshull New Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engines from Crewe and Nantwich attended Two fire engines were called to a fire involving a washing machine in the laundry room of a nursing home. The premises was evacuated and firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  Washing machine and dishwasher fire safety
