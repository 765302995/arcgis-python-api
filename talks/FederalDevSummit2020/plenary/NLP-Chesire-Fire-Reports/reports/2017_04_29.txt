Chip pan fire in Weaverham, Northwich Time of Incident: 24/04/2017 - 17:34 Location: Poplar Road, Weaverham, Northwich Attendance: Three fire engines from Northwich and Winsford There was smoke issuing from a property, so fire crews entered the property and found a chip pan on fire.  The chip pan had been left unattended.  The fire crews extinguished the fire.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  Police also attended this incident.  Chip pan fire safety tips
