Road traffic collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 13/12/2016 - 15:03 Location: M6 North J18 TO J19 Attendance: Four fire engines from Middlewich, Congleton, Knutsford and Sandbach A road traffic collision involving one vehicle on the M6 had left one woman trapped inside the vehicle. Firefighters released the woman from the vehicle, before handing her into the care of paramedics for assessment.
