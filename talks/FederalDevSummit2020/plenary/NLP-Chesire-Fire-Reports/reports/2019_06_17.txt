 Horsebox fire in Scholar Green Time of Incident: 26/06/2019 - 16:44 Location: Townsend Lane, Scholar Green Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Congleton and one from Alsager At around 4.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a horsebox being on fire at a farm in Townsend Lane, Scholar Green. On arrival crews found the diesel-powered horsebox well alight. Two propane gas cylinders were removed from the horsebox. They were not involved in the fire. Batteries were isolated and a water shuttle was set up to get enough water to the area. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus each used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Once the fire was out the contents of the horsebox were removed, it was damped down and a thermal imaging camera was used to check for hotspots. Firefighters were in attendance for around two hours.
