 Electrical fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/12/2018 - 10:20 Location: Regent Street, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a fire on land off Regent Street in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire was caused by an electrical fault which was affecting the power supply to nearby houses. The electrical supply company attended and isolated the power. Firefighters were in attendance for around four hours - until they were sure that the fire was out and the area was safe.
