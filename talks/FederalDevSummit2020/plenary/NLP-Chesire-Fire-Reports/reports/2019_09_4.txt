 Vehicle fire on M62 Time of Incident: 28/09/2019 - 18:35 Location: M62 eastbound at J11 Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood and one from Warrington Firefighters were called to damp down after a van caught fire on the M62 near to junction 11.  After damping down the crews made the vehicle safe and were at the scene for 45 minutes.
