 Vehicle fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/04/2019 - 02:30 Location: Gorsewood Road, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At approximately 2.30am firefighter from Runcorn were called to reports of a vehicle fire on Gorsewood Road.  On arrival the team discovered one van which was well alight.  One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus, used an extended hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately one hour.  The fire is believed to have been stated deliberately and anyone with information should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
