Wooden shed completely destroyed by fire in Rixton Time of Incident: 18/08/2016 - 14:06 Location: Brook Lane in Rixton, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A wooden shed was well alight on Brook Lane in Rixton, Warrington.  Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  The wooden shed (approximately five metres by eight metres) was completely destroyed by the fire.
