 Cooking fumes in Warrington flat Time of Incident: 23/02/2019 - 01:57 Location: Bexhill Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Warrington, one from Lymm and one from Penketh Firefighters attended to reports of smoke coming from a property on Bexhill Avenue in Warrington.  Crews forced entry to the property to find the smoke was coming from food cooking and used a positive pressure ventilation fan to disperse the fumes.  One person inside the flat was handed to the care of paramedics and taken to hospital suffering from smoke inhalation.  Crews were in attendance for approximately 90 minutes.
