 Cigarette bin fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 17/05/2019 - 15:12 Location: Market Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly after 5.10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire near a pedestrian crossing on Market Street in Crewe. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a cigarette bin mounted on a small pole. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for less than 20 minutes.
