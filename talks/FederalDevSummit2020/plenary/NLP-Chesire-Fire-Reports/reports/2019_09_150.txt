 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/09/2019 - 21:43 Location: Poplars Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a wheelie bin fire on Poplars Avenue, Warrington.  The crew were detained at the scene for around 15 minutes.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to call Cheshire police on 101, quoting IML 503366. Information can also be passed anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111.  For more information about how to prevent wheelie bin fires visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety/how-to-prevent-a-wheelie-bin-fire
