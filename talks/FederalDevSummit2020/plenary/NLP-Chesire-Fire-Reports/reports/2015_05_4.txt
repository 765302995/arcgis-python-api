Fire involving wood in Winsford Time of Incident: 28/05/2015 - 23:44 Location: Whitbys Lane, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to a small quantity of wood which had deliberately set on fire in Winsford. The fire involved an old wooden electricity post which had been decommissioned and no electricity present and wood. Police also attended this incident. If anyone knows how this fire started they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
