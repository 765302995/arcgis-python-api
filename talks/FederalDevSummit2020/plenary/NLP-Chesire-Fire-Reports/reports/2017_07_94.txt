Wheelie bins on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 10/07/2017 - 00:54 Location: Cherry Sutton, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Two wheelie bins were well alight on Cherry Sutton. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and police were asked to attend as it is believed the wheelie bins were set on fire deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 40 of 10 July.  Please follow our wheelie bin safety advice.
