Grass fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/04/2015 - 20:05 Location: Farrell Street, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Warrington Firefighters tackled a grass fire off Farrell Street, Warrington.   The crew used water back packs and beaters to put out the fire, which covered an area of about 30 meters by 20 meters.
