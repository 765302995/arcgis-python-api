M56 west road traffic collision between j12 and 14 Time of Incident: 21/03/2016 - 07:57 Location: M56 westbound between junctions 12 and 14 Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Four vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision on the M56 westbound between junctions 12 and 14. Nobody was trapped in the vehicles. Firefighters removed all of the vehicles to the hard shoulder.
