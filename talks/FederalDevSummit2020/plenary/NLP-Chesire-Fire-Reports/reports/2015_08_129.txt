Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/08/2015 - 02:41 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A wheelie bin was on fire on a children's park in Warrington. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this or similar incidents is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.  Please follow our wheelie bin safety advice - Wheelie Bin - Fire Safety
