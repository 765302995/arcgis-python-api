Domestic garage on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 19/09/2015 - 05:09 Location: Franklyn Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Crewe firefighters were sent to an incident involving a fire inside a domestic garage on Franklyn Avenue, Crewe. On arrival at the property the fire crews found that the garage was well alight.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. Three hose reel jets were used to fight the fire. It is believed that the cause of this fire was an electrical fault.
