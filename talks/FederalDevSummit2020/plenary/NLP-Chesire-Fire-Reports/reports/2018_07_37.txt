 Collision involving a car and a scooter in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/07/2018 - 20:18 Location: Chester Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended - from Powey Lane and Chester At around 8.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision on Chester Road in Ellesmere Port. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved a car and a scooter. Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to remove the scooter's handle bar. A male casualty was then able to be handed over to the care of paramedics and taken to hospital. Firefighters were at the scene for around half-an-hour. Police were also in attendance.
