Combine harvester fire in Sound Time of Incident: 25/08/2017 - 17:41 Location: Sound Lane, Sound Attendance: Two fire engine from Crewe Fire crews were called to a combine harvester which was on fire on Sound Lane, Sound. Crews used two hose reel jets to put out the fire and a thermal imaging camera to check for any hot spots. Crews were detained at the scene for one and half hours.
