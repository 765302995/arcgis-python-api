 Car fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 19/09/2019 - 00:30 Location: Chester Road, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters were called to a car on fire on Chester Road, Knutsford near junction 19 of the M6. Crews used breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the fire.
