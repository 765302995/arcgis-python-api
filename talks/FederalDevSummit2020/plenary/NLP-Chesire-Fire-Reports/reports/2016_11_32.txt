Wheelie bin fire spread to house in Knutsford Time of Incident: 24/11/2016 - 02:27 Location: Mobberley Road, Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended Firefighters were called to extinguish a wheelie bin which was on fire and had spread to the rear of a nearby house. A gas barbecue and cylinder were also involved and the cylinder was cooled with water. The crew used one hose reel to extinguish the flames and a large fan to ventilate the property due to heavy smoke logging. Paramedics attended and checked occupants. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check that the fire was out before leaving. Wheelie Bin - Fire Safety advice
