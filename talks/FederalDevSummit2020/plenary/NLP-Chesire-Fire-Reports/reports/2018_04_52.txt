 Hazardous materials in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 20/04/2018 - 10:43 Location: Cumberland Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington and one fIre engine from Wilmslow Firefighters from Wilmslow and Bollington were called to reports that a plastic drum containing an unknown substance had been found abandoned near Cumberland Street, Macclesfield.  The crews attended the scene and found that the drum contained an unknown clear liquid. After liaising with Hazardous Materials and Environmental Protection Officers (HMEPO), a decision was taken to wrap the drum with polythene bags.  After securing the drum, a small cordon was set up around the drum as a precautionary measure. The incident has now been passed to Cheshire East Borough council who will arrange for the container to be collected.
