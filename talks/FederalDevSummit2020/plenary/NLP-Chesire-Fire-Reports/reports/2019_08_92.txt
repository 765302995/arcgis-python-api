 Two people trapped following collision in Woodbank Time of Incident: 18/08/2019 - 09:40 Location: A5117 at the junction with the A540 in Woodbank, near Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Powey Lane and one from Chester Shortly after 9.30am firefighters were called to a report of a two-vehicle collision on the A5117 at the junction with the A540 in Woodbank, near Chester. On arrival the crews found that two people were trapped. Firefighters got the man and the woman out of the vehicle they were trapped in before handing them over to the care of paramedics. They were taken to hospital via ambulance. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
