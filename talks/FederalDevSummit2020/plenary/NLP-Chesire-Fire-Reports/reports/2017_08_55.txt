Fire in bungalow in Runcorn Time of Incident: 21/08/2017 - 17:57 Location: Woodridge, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a fire in a bungalow on Woodridge, Runcorn. The fire was in a fuse box and was out on the arrival of the fire crews. They used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.
