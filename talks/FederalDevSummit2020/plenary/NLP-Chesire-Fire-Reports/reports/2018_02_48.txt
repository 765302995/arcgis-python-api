 Outbuilding building fire in Chester Time of Incident: 19/02/2018 - 02:12 Location: Stratford Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one fire engione attended from Powey Lane At 2.12am firefighters from Chester were called to reports of a building fire on Stratford Road, Chester. On arrival at the scene the fire was found to involve a shed which was well alight. Crews used a hose reel jet and breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire.
