 Hedge fire in Elton Time of Incident: 02/09/2018 - 01:32 Location: Willow Grove, Elton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane At approximately 1.30am firefighters from Powey Lane were called to reports of a fire in the open in Elton. Crews attended the scene and found that a 10 metre row of hedge was well alight. The team used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire and were in attendance at the scene for approximately 30 minutes. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 172518. Information can also be passed anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111.
