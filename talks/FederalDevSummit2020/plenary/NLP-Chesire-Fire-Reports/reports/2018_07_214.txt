 Scrub and peat land fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 06/07/2018 - 05:14 Location: Nortonwood Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 5.15am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Nortonwood Lane in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved approximately 60 square metres of scrub and peat land. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and beaters to extinguish the fire. They then turned over the peat and damped down the area to make sure it was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for more than two hours.
