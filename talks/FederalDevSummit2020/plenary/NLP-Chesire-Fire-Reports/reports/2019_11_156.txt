 Road traffic collision, M56 motorway Time of Incident: 02/11/2019 - 19:44 Location: M56 eastbound carriageway, nr Junction 12. Attendance: Three fire engines from Lymm, Widnes and Frodsham Crews were called following a road traffic collision on the M56 eastbound carriageway near Junction 12. It involved one saloon car which had rolled onto lanes 1 and 2, and was smoking.  The occupants had managed to get out of the vehicle and were being tended to by firefighters. The fire engines were used to fend traffic off onto the shoulder.  A 13-year-old boy was passed into the care of the paramedics when they arrived, who was taken to hospital as a precautionary check for a possible neck injury.
