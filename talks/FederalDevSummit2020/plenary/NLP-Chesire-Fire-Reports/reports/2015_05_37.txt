Collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 24/05/2015 - 07:05 Location: Congleton Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: Fire engines from Macclesfield and Congleton Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Congleton Road, Macclesfield.  When they arrived they found that there was a man injured in his car. Crews rescued the man from the vehicle and he was handed to the care of the ambulance service.
