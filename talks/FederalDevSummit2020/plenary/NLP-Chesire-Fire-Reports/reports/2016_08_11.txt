Truck fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 29/08/2016 - 00:20 Location: Brookdale Park in Crewe. Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Brookdale Park in Crewe.  When they arrived crews found that there was a pick up truck on fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call Crimstoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Constabulary on 101.
