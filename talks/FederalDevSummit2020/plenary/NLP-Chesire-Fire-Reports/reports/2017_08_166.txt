Unattended pan on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/08/2017 - 14:57 Location: Manx Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Penketh Firefghters were called to a fire involving an unattended pan which was out on arrival. The crew used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property. An ambulance was requested for one casualty suffering from smoke inhalation.
