 Fatal collision involving a car and a tractor in Barthomley near Crewe Time of Incident: 03/01/2019 - 21:56 Location: A500 in Barthomley, near Crewe Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Crewe and one from Alsager Shortly before 10pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision on the A500 in Barthomley near Crewe. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved a car and a tractor.  Police closed the road in both directions as emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters assisted in removing a woman from the car. They also made the vehicle safe.  Sadly the woman died at the scene.  Police are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision to call 101, quoting IML 285839, give the details via general enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
