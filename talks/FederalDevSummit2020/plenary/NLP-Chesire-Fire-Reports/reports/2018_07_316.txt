 Deliberate ignition, Crewe Time of Incident: 30/06/2018 - 23:04 Location: Groby Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to deal with a fire close to a car sales yard. They arrived to find three bonfires that had spread to a nearby hedgerow, measuring around 5m x 5m. They used two hose reels to extinguish the flames and they remained at the scene to dampen down.  It's thought to have been a deliberate ignition, so anyone with any information as to how the fires started is asked to contact police.
