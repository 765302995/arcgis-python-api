Fire in roof space of bungalow in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 24/05/2016 - 23:01 Location: Hillside Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port and Chester Firefighters were called to a fire in a roof space of a bungalow. Two hose reels were used to extinguish the fire. A thermal imaging camera was also used at this incident.
