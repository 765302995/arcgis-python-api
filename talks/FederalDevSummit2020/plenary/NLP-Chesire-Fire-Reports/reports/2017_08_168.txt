Caravan fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 31/07/2017 - 22:11 Location: Dee Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and one from Northwich A caravan was heavily involved in fire next to single storey garages on Dee Way. Firefighters, two in breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to tackle the blaze and then dampened down the area. Police were asked to attend as it is believed the fire was started deliberately.
