Outhouse on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 27/01/2017 - 03:50 Location: Wharton Gardens, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich Firefighters were called to an outhouse on fire which spread to a ground floor of a two storey end terraced property. The crew used one hose reel to extinguish the fire and a thermal imaging camera to check that the fire was fully out. Crews were in attendance for several hours checking the area for hot spots.
