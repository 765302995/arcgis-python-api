 Strong smell at garage in Chester Time of Incident: 30/10/2019 - 00:45 Location: Parkgate Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters were called to reports of a strong smell at a fuel station in Chester. Firefighters used a detector and found no signs of flammable substances present. The crew put a cordon in place while the incident was dealt with and ventilated a shop area. An ambulance attended and paramedics checked a member of staff.
