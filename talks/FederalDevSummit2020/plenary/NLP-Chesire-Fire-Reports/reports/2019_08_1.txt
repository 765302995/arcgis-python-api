Fire involving an extractor hood in Disley Time of Incident: 29/08/2019 - 20:47 Location: Cloughside, Disley Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Poynton and one from Stockport in Greater Manchester At around 8.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving an extractor fan at a domestic property in Cloughside, Disley. On arrival the crews found that the fire involving an extractor hood had been caused by cooking. The fire was out on arrival. Firefighters isolated the electrics. They also checked the temperatures of the extractor hood and the surrounding area using a thermal imaging camera to make sure that they were safe. Firefighters were in attendance for less than an hour.
