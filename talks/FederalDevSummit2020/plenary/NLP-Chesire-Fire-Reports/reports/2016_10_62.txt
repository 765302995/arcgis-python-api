Kitchen fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 21/10/2016 - 14:46 Location: Claughton Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Flames spread to a kitchen after a fire involving refuse began at the rear of the property. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fire before damping down the area. They then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.  This fire was believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111
