Car set alight in Neston Time of Incident: 01/01/2015 - 02:59 Location: The Birches, Neston. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A car was well alight, so firefighters used two hose reel jets to tackle the fire.  Police attended this incident.  This fire is thought to have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
