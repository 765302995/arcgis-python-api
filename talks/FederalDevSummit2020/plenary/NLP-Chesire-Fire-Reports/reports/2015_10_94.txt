Road traffic collision on A534 Sandbach Time of Incident: 15/10/2015 - 06:32 Location: Old Mill Road, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters have attended a road traffic collision involving two saloon cars on the A534 in Sandbach.  Crews have released a male casualty from one of the vehicles who is now in the care of paramedics.  The A534 is closed in both directions from Old Mill Road to Crewe Road roundabout. Please avoid the area if possible.  Updated at 11:15 This road has now re-opened.
