 Fire involving fly-tipped rubbish in Frodsham Time of Incident: 15/05/2018 - 23:54 Location: The Ridgeway, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Frodsham At around 11.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at the side of a road at The Ridgeway in Frodsham. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved around 40 square metres of fly-tipped rubbish. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were at the scene for around half-an-hour.
