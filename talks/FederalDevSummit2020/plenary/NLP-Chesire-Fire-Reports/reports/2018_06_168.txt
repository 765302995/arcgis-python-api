 Grill pan fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/06/2018 - 15:11 Location: Wilderhope Close, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters attended to a grill pan fire at a property on Wilderhope Close in Crewe.  The fire, caused by heating of fat, was contained by the occupier before firefighters arrived the home.   Crews however made sure the fire was fully extinguished by using a dry power extinguisher and checking for hotspots.  The oven was removed from the kitchen as a precaution.
