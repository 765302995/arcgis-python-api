Large animal rescue in Bollington Time of Incident: 03/05/2015 - 13:17 Location: Clarke Lane, Bollington. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield, One fire engine from Bollington and the animal rescue unit from Bollington Fire crews responded to a report of a horse trapped in a trailer on Clarke Lane, Bollington.   The horse was sedated by a vet before crews released the horse by removing the rear of the box trailer.  Crews were at the scene for about 2 hours 45 minutes.
