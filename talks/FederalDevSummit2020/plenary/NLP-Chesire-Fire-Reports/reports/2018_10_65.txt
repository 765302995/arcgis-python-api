 Cellar fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 20/10/2018 - 17:33 Location: West Street, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Nantwich attended Firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a cellar on West Street shortly after 5.30pm. Crews used positive pressure ventilation equipment to disperse fumes from a discarded cigarette and gave advice to the occupier.
