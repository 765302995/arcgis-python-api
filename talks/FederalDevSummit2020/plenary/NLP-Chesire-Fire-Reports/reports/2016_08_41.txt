Bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/08/2016 - 20:27 Location: Newburgh Close in Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters used a hose reel jet to tackle a bin fire on Newburgh Close in Runcorn.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
