Kitchen fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 06/09/2016 - 15:44 Location: Broughton Lane, Wistaston Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and two fire engines from Nantwich and Alsager Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to put out a fire in the kitchen of a domestic property. One woman was taken to hospital by ambulances crews whilst crews ventilated the property with a large fan.
