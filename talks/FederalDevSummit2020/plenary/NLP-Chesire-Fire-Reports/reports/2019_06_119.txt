 Two-car collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 05/06/2019 - 16:07 Location: Junction of the A539 Wilmslow Road and Greendale Lane, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Macclesfield and one from Wilmslow Shortly after 4pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision on the junction of the A539 Wilmslow Road and Greendale Lane in Macclesfield. On arrival the crews discovered the collision involved two cars and that no-one was trapped inside them. Firefighters made the vehicles safe before handing the incident over to the police. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour.
