 Heath land fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/02/2019 - 11:47 Location: Weston Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters attended heath land that was well alight off Weston Road in Runcorn. Approximately 20 metres of heath land was affected. Firefighters used a hose reel to put out the fire.
