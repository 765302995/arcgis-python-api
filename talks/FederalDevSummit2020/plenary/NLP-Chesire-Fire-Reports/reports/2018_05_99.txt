 Fire at a motor repairs garage in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 17/05/2018 - 22:35 Location: Eccups Lane, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around firefighters were called to a report of a fire at C Mountney & Sons Garage on Eccups Lane in Wilmslow. On arrival the crew discovered a quantity of pellets well alight in the grounds of the motor repairs garage. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and a rake to extinguish the fire. The fire is believed to have been started accidentally. Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
