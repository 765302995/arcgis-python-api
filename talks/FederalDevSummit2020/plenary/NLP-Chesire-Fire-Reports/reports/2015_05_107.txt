Cooker fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 10/05/2015 - 13:49 Location: Fitz Crescent, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton A grill pan on fire caused one person to be taken to hospital suffering slight smoke inhalation and a burn to her arm.  The small fire was out when the crew arrived but the householder, a lady, was taken to hospital as a precaution.
