 Smoke coming from lamp post in Widnes Time of Incident: 21/11/2019 - 12:25 Location: Wilmere Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to reports of smoke coming from a street lamp post in Wilmere Lane, Widnes.  The crew extinguished the fire using dry powder.  The local authority were informed so that the lamp post could be isolated.
