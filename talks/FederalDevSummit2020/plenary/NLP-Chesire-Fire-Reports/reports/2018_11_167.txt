 Garden fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 04/11/2018 - 16:53 Location: West Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Crews attended to a fire in a garden of a house on West Street in Crewe.  Firefighters used one hose reel to dampen down the surrounding areas as well reduce the size of the fire and allow it to continue as controlled burning.
