Man rescued from water in Poynton Time of Incident: 20/03/2016 - 14:46 Location: Green Lane, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton and one from Knutsford A man was rescued from water after he fell 10 feet from the roadway on his mobility scooter. Firefighters and ambulance personnel were able to rescue the casualty using a long board using height safety equipment. Police officers were also in attendance and closed the road during the rescue operation.
