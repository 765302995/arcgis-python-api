Caravan on fire in Elton Time of Incident: 14/03/2017 - 15:42 Location: A5117, Elton Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters suspect deliberate ignition as the cause of a caravan fire on the A5117 near to Cryers Lane. The crew used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control and asked for police to attend.
