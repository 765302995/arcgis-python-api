Road traffic collision in Cholmondeley Time of Incident: 27/10/2017 - 16:31 Location: A49, Cholmondeley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas and one fire engine from Tarporley. Firefighters attended a collision involving two cars on the A49 at Cholmondeley, Malpas. Two casualties were trapped in the cars and had to extricated by firefighters. The casualties were then taken to hospital by ambulance.
