Cooking fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 23/04/2017 - 18:56 Location: Parkfield Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Unattended cooking caused heavy smoke in a Parkfield Road house. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the small fire on the cooker. A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke from the property. Crews checked the loft space to make sure there was no fire spread.
