 Building fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 03/05/2018 - 10:00 Location: Parkgate, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Fire in KnutsfordShortly after 10am firefighters were called to reports of smoke coming from the roof of a property on Parkgate in Knutsford.  Firefighters from Wilmslow attended the scene and used a thermal imaging camera to examine the structure. The team discovered that the smoke was coming from a small fire on a fascia board which had been extinguished  The cause of the fire is believed to have been accidental as the caller had been burning off wood paint. The team were in attendance for approximately 25 minutes.
