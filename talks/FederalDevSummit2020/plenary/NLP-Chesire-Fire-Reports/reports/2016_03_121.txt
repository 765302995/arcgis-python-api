Cellar flooded in Sandbach Time of Incident: 10/03/2016 - 09:08 Location: Congleton Road, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach Firefighters attended the flooding of a large cellar in a property in Sandbach. The crew used a pump to remove around 1,800 litres of water from the area which is believed to have come from nearby ground. United Utilities have been informed due to electrics being affected.
