Outbuildings on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 13/11/2016 - 16:55 Location: Wistaston Road, Willaston Attendance: One fire engine from Alsager and two from Crewe Firefighters were called to two outbuildings on fire and used two hose reels to extinguish the fire. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the area before crews left.
