Caravan on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 25/08/2017 - 00:02 Location: Bradbury Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich Firefighters were called to extinguish a caravan which is believed to have been deliberately set on fire in Winsford. The crew used two hose reels to put the flames out. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the area and police were requested to attend. If anyone knows anything about this fire and who may have started it then call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
