Car fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 04/02/2015 - 00:54 Location: Warren Avenue, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A car fire in Northwich is believed to be the result of arson. The fire was out on arrival of the crew who used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.  Police were also in attendance and anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101, or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
