Small fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 21/07/2015 - 14:16 Location: Bridges Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters damped down the remains of a compost fire. The fire had been put out by works personnel but the crew was called in to ensure there was no chance of reignition.  A hose reel jet was used to damp down and a thermal imaging camera to check there were no hot spots.
