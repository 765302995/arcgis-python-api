 Small fire in open in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/03/2019 - 18:49 Location: Wigshaw Lane, Culcheth. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Shortly before 7pm firefighters were called to a small fire in the open off Wigshaw Lane. Crews use a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
