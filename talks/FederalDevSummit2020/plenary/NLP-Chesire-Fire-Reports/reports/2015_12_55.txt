Road traffic collision in Frodsham Time of Incident: 16/12/2015 - 16:22 Location: Manley Road, Frodsham Attendance: Fire engines from Frodsham, Runcorn and Widnes attended Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving two vehicles with two people trapped. The junction of Manley Road and New Pale Road was closed while the incident is dealt with.  Updated at 5.20pm  One woman was freed from a vehicle by the crew who used cutting equipment. A second woman also needed the assistance of the crews to get out of one of the vehicles. Paramedics attended along with police.
