Tumble dryer fire in Rudheath Time of Incident: 04/08/2016 - 09:09 Location: Cottage Close, Rudheath, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A tumble dryer caught fire at a house in Cottage Close. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control. The tumble dryer was then taken outside and the house ventilated to clear smoke.
