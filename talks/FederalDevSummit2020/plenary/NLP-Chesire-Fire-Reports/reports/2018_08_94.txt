 Road traffic collision, Widnes Time of Incident: 10/08/2018 - 22:52 Location: South Lane, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Penketh Crews responded to reports of a road traffic collision with people trapped on South Lane in Widnes. The collision involved two saloon cars, and two passengers were medically trapped as a result of the collision.  Fire service personnel helped to free them from the vehicles and pass them to the care of paramedics at the scene. In all, four people were conveyed to hospital as part of the incident, with crews at the scene for around 50 minutes.
