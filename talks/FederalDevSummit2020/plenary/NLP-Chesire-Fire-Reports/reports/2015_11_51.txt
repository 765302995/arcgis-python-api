Motorcycle deliberately set alight in Crewe Time of Incident: 21/11/2015 - 03:54 Location: Fallowfield Court, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine in Crewe A motorcycle was well alight so firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
