 Fire in wooded area in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/06/2018 - 19:22 Location: Queensbury Way, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes attended At around 7.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a wooded area on Queensbury Way in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire and used water to put it out. Firefighters were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
