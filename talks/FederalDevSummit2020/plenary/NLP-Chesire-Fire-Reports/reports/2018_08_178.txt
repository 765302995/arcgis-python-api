 Kitten rescued from storm drain in Middlewich Time of Incident: 01/08/2018 - 20:18 Location: Pochin Way, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich Kitten rescued from storm drain in MiddlewichFirefighters were called out by the RSPCA to rescue a kitten stuck in a storm drain. The crew was able to get access to the drain and rescue the kitten who was handed into the care of the RSPCA officer.
