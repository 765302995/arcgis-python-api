 Fire involving an electrical transformer in Northwich Time of Incident: 23/07/2019 - 16:27 Location: Hough Lane, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich and Runcorn At 4.30pm firefighters attended reports of a fire involving a telegraph pole in Northwich.  Crews attended the scene and fond that an electrical transformer on the pole was smoking.  A temporary road closure was put in place and the electricity was isolated.  Firefighters then used a hose reel jet to cool the transformer.  The incident was then passed to engineers from Scottish Power.
