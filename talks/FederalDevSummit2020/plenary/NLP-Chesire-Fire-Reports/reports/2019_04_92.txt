 Car fire, Crewe Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 17:00 Location: Broad Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to tackle a car fire in a car park at the rear of a charity shop on Broad Street, Crewe. It was well alight when they arrived, and they used a single hose reel and breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames, as well as a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots on the vehicle while damping down took place.. Police were called due to it being a suspected deliberate ignition. Anyone with information about the incident is asked to call 101, quoting IML 378689.
