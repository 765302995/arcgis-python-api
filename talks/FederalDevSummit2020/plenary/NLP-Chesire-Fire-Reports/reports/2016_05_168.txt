Chimney fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 02/05/2016 - 11:23 Location: Giantswood Lane, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton Firefighters tackled a chimney fire in Giantswood Lane. The crew had to tackle the blaze from both the hearth area and the roof, using a ladder and fall arrest harness. The fire was tackled from above using a hose reel jet and from the ground floor using chimney rods and a hose reel. The fire area was checked using a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no further hot spots.  Please always follow our chimney safety advice.
