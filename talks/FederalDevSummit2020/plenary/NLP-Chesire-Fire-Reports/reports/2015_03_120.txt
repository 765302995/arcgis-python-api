Collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/03/2015 - 05:15 Location: Kingsway South, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Kingsway South, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved one vehicle that had collided with a building. There was a casualty trapped inside. Crews used special equipment to free them and they were passed to paramedics.
