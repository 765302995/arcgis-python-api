Fire at a recycling plant Time of Incident: 06/06/2016 - 17:10 Location: Ditton Road in Widnes Attendance: Fire engines from Widnes and Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Ditton Road in Widnes.  When they arrived crews found that there was an amount of material smouldering in the warehouse. Staff from the plant used machinery to remove the material. This was then dampened down by crews using main water jets. It was then put in a skip.
