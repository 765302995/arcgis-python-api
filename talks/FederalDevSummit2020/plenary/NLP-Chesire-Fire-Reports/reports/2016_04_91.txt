Car and motorbike in serious collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/04/2016 - 08:24 Location: Glazebrook Lane, Rixton-with-Glazebrook, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and two from Salford (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Firefighters have been at a serious road traffic collision involving a car and a motorbike. One man was trapped underneath the car. Firefighters used cutting gear and a long board to release the casualty as he was given trauma care by a firefighter and paramedic. The casualty was taken to hospital by air ambulance.  People are asked to avoid the area as the road is expected to remain closed for the time being.  Motorbike safety information
