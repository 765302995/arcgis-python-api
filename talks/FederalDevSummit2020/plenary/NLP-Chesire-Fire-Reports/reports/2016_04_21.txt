Road traffic collision on the M62 west between junctions 8 and 7 Time of Incident: 25/04/2016 - 12:19 Location: M62 west, between juntions 8 and 7 Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving vehicles with one person trapped. The crew got a man out of his vehicle and handed him to the care of paramedics.
