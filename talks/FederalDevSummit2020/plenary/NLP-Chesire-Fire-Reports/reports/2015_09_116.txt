Building fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 08/09/2015 - 17:55 Location: Mill Lane, Frodsham. Attendance: Fire engines from Widnes, Runcorn and Frodsham Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a building on Mill Lane, Frodsham.          When they arrived they found that the incident involved a fork lift truck inside the building. Firefighters used one main jet, one hose reel jet and breathing apparatus to bring the fire under control. Adjacent buildings were also checked for any fire spread and crews were in attendance for just over one hour.
