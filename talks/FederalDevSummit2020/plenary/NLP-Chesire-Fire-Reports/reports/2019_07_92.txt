 Hedge fire in Stapeley Time of Incident: 15/07/2019 - 14:39 Location: Wybunbury Lane, Stapeley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a hedge fire on Wynbunbury Lane, Stapeley. The fire was caused by overhead power lines arching and crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The electricity company were called to attend.
