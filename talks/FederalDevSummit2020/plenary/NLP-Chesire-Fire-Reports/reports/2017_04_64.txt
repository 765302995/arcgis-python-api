Tractor on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 19/04/2017 - 15:36 Location: Belmont Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Penketh Crews were called to a tractor which is well alight. No other vehicle was involved and the road was closed while the incident was dealt with by the crew who used a hose reel.  Diesel and oil was spilt onto the roadway and the Environment Agency were contacted.
