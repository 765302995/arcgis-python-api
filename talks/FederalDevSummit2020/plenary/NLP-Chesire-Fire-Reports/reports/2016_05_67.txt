Oil boiler fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 17/05/2016 - 21:12 Location: Peover Lane, Snelson Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Knutsford An oil-fired boiler was involved in fire at a property in Snelson. The fire was out on arrival. Firefighters isolated the fuel supply and exposed and monitored the area around the boiler.  When the owner of property discovered the fire they closed the door which prevented fire spread to the rest of the house.  After it was confirmed that there were no further hotspots, the crews handed the incident over to the occupier.
