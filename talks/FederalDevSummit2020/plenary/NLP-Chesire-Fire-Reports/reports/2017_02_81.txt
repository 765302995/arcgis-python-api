Congleton chimney fire Time of Incident: 17/02/2017 - 15:29 Location: Weathercock Lane, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton Firefighters tackled a chimney fire on Weathercock Lane. Smoke was coming through the chimney and around the chimney breast. Crews began by attacking the fire from the grate and then set up chimney rods but there seemed to be a blockage part way up the chimney. They set up height safety equipment to tackle the fire from the roof.  Always follow our chimney safety advice.
