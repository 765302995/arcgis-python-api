 Small rubbish fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 22/01/2018 - 19:29 Location: Audley Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to extinguish a small rubbish fire in a garage on Audley Street, Crewe. Crews used a fire extinguisher to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident please call Cheshire Police on 101 or call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
