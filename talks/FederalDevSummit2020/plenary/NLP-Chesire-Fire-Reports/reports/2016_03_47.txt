Wooden outhouse on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/03/2016 - 09:24 Location: Seathwaite Close, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn A fire in a wooden outhouse attached to a Runcorn property was tackled by firefighters.  The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the blaze under control. They then used a short extension ladder to get into the loft space to check there was no further fire spread.  A building control officer was asked to attend to check whether there was any structural damage to the house. An investigation into the cause of the fire is taking place.
