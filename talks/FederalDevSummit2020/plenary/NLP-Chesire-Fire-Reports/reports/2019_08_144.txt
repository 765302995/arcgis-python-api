 Smoke caused by unattended cooking in Crewe Time of Incident: 04/08/2019 - 05:41 Location: Badger Avenue, Crewe. CW1 3LJ Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe and one from Middlewich Smoke alarms alerted a person to smoke caused by unattended cooking at a house on Badger Avenue.  Firefighters from Crewe and Middlewich attended and, though the fire was out on arrival, used a thermal imaging camera to check for potential issues.  One person was handed to the care of North West Ambulance Service suffering from smoke inhalation.  Click here for more details on how to stay safe in the kitchen.
