Fire in the open in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 09/05/2016 - 21:29 Location: Beech Hall Drive, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A fire in the open is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters used water backpacks and beaters to put out the fire.  Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
