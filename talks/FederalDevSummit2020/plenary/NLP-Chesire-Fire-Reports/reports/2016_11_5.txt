Building evacuated in Adlington Time of Incident: 29/11/2016 - 07:23 Location: Second Avenue, Adlington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Smoke in a building caused alarms to activate, leading to a full evacuation of the premises. Crews found no source of fire, however, they did discover that the alarm has been caused by electrical fan machinery so they proceeded to check for hot spots of fire using a thermal imaging camera.
