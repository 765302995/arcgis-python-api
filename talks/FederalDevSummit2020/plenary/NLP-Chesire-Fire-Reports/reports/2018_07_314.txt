 Garden fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 03:40 Location: Lingley Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh Firefighters responded to reports of a fire in the garden of a house in Lingley Road, Warrington. On arrival they found the fire involved a fence, decking and garden furniture, and crews used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames.  It's thought the fire may have been started accidentally by a discarded cigarette, so a home safety assessment was carried out with the occupiers before leaving.
