Postbox on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 08/11/2015 - 20:42 Location: School Road, Princess Street, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford A postbox which was on fire in Winsford was put out with a dry powder extinguisher. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. Please call Cheshire Police on 101 if you know who may have started this fire or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
