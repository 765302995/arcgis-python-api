 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 19/05/2019 - 17:38 Location: Hale Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews attended to a wheelie bin on fire down an alleyway on Hale Road in Widnes.
