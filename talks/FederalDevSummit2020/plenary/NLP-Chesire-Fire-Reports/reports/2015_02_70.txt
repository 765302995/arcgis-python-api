Chimney fire in Warmingham Time of Incident: 11/02/2015 - 18:56 Location: School Lane, Warmingham. Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a chimney fire at the Bears Paw Innon School Lane in Warmingham.  When they arrived crews used a thermal imaging camera to check the chimney for signs of a problem. They confirmed that all was okay with the fire and there was no risk to the premises.
