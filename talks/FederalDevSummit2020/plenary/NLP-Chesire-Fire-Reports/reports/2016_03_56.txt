Warrington chimney fire Time of Incident: 20/03/2016 - 19:35 Location: Fearnhead Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and the aerial platform Crews attended a chimney fire in Poulton-with-Fearnhead. The aerial platform was also called as firefighters were working at height as well as tackling the fire from the hearth using chimney rods and a hose reel jet. A thermal imaging camera was used to check there were no areas of heat in the loft space. Crews were at the scene for just over an hour.  Always follow our Chimneys safety advice.
