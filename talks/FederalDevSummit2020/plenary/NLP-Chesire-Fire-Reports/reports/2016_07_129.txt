Child slipped over a cliff onto a ledge in Runcorn Time of Incident: 08/07/2016 - 19:48 Location: Off Highlands Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and the rope rescue unit and support engine from Knutsford A child slipped over a cliff onto a ledge and was stuck on the ledge, so firefighters rescued the child from the ledge using a ladder.
