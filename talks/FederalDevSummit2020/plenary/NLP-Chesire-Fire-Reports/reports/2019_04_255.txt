 Kitchen fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 01/04/2019 - 12:57 Location: Wallworths Bank, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton and one from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to reports of a kitchen fire on Wallworths Bank, Congleton. On arrival crews found a pan had been left on a hob which had caused a significant amount of smoke. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, searched the property and then cleared smoke from the property using a large fan.
