Electrical box fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 17/10/2016 - 11:16 Location: Tulworth Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow An electrical box was on fire in a house in Poynton, meaning crews had to isolate the electrical supply to the building so that the main fuse could be removed. Firefighters wore electrical gloves to remove the fuse and checked the temperature of the box using a thermal imaging camera. They were then able to lower the temperature.
