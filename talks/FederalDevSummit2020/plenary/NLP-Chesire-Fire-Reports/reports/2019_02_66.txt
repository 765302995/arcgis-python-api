 Person trapped in car after collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/02/2019 - 19:08 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes and one from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a two vehicle collision on Dundalk Road, Widnes. One casualty was trapped in a car and crews administered first aid and extracted them from the car using hydraulic cutting equipment before they were taken to hospital. A second casualty was also taken to hospital by paramedics.
