 Chimney fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 02/01/2018 - 14:50 Location: Delamere Drive, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton, one from Lymm and the aerial appliance from Lymm Firefighters were called to tackle a chimney fire on Delamere Drive, Macclesfield. Crews used an aerial appliance and a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
