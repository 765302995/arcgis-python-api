 Fire near railway embankment in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/06/2018 - 12:28 Location: Heath Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 12.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Heath Road in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire near perimeter fencing on the railway embankment. The railway was not affected. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
