 Animal rescue in Runcorn Time of Incident: 22/09/2019 - 16:51 Location: Red Brow Lane, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn along with two animal rescue units from Bollington and Lymm Firefighters with one of the rescued sheepShortly before 5pm firefighters were called to reports of a number of sheep trapped in the Bridgewater canal near Runcorn.  On arrival the crews found that two sheep were stuck in the canal.  The team used specialist equipment to rescue the sheep and they were released into a nearby field.  Firefighters were in attendance for around three hours.
