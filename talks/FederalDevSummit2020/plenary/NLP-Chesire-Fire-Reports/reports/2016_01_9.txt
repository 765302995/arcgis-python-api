Electrical substation fire in Mere Time of Incident: 26/01/2016 - 22:35 Location: Warrington Road, Mere Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Altrincham (GMFRS) Fire in an electrical substation was attended by crews. Live wires were in the area of the fire. The power was isolated by the electricity company and traffic control was set up by police, shutting one line of traffic.
