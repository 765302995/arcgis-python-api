Small vehicle fire on the M56, junction 12 to junction 14. Time of Incident: 27/06/2016 - 09:42 Location: M56 between junctions 12 and 14. Attendance: One fire engines from Warrington and two from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to attend a small vehicle fire on the M56, junction 12 to junction 14. Crews, wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to tackle the fire.
