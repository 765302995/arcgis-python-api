 Smoke at a domestic property in Weston Time of Incident: 10/05/2019 - 04:01 Location: Fairview Avenue, Weston Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At around 4am firefighters were called to a report of smoke at a domestic property in Fairview Avenue, Weston. On arrival the crews discovered that the smoke had been caused by burnt toast. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
