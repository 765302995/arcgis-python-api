Crews help an injured woman Time of Incident: 23/05/2015 - 15:24 Location: St Ann's Street, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Fire crews from Chester helped treat a woman who had fallen outside the fire station.  Crews gave first aid to the woman, which included oxygen therepy, before putting her on a longboard and taking her into the station to await the arrival of the ambulance service.
