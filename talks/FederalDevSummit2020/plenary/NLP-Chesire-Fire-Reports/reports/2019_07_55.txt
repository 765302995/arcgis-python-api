 Fire in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/07/2019 - 19:08 Location: Cotton Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly after 7pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Cotton Lane, Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning that had got out of control. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put the fire out and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
