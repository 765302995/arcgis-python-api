 Small fire in the open in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 19/09/2019 - 21:40 Location: Near to Alnwick Drive, Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At 9.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire near to a tree by Alnwick Drive in the Bunbury Green area of Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire in the open with a hose reel jet. Police attended the incident. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 517648, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
