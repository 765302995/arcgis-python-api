 Small refuse fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/08/2018 - 16:28 Location: Tyrrell Way, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 4.25pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire on grassland in Tyrrell Way, Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire involving rubbish. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 45 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
