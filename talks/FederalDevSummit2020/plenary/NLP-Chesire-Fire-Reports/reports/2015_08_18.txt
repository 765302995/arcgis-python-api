Shed on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/08/2015 - 09:13 Location: Pendine Close, Burtonwood and Westbrook, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a shed which was well alight in Warrington. The crew used a two hose reels to extinguish the fire and wore breathing apparatus at the incident. The shed was totally destroyed in this fire which also affected the wall of the property.
