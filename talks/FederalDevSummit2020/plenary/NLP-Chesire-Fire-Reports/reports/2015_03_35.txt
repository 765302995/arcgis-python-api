Unattended cooking causes fire in Appleton Time of Incident: 23/03/2015 - 00:17 Location: Burfield Drive, Appleton, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Unattended cooking was the cause of a small fire at a property in Appleton.  Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and a high pressure fan to clear the property of smoke.
