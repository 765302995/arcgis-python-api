 Tree leaning over a road in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 10/03/2019 - 13:54 Location: Macclesfield Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Shortly before 2pm firefighters were called to a report of a tree leaning over a road in Macclesfield. On arrival at Macclesfield Road the crew found the tree in a dangerous position. Firefighters removed some of the tree's branches. Police closed the road. Firefighters were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
