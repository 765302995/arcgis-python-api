Public bin deliberately set on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 22/03/2016 - 20:25 Location: Mill street, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish a fire in a public bin.   Police were asked to attend because the fire was started deliberately.
