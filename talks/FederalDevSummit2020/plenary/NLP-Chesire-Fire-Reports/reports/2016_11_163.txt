Vehicle fire on the M62 Time of Incident: 03/11/2016 - 23:11 Location: M62, between junctions 8 and 7 Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel to put out a car which was on fire on the hard shoulder of the motorway. The vehicle was well alight on the M62 westbound, between junctions 8 and 7.
