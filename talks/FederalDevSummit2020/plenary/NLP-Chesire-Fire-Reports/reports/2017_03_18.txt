Hay bales on fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 27/03/2017 - 16:59 Location: Styal Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Three large hay bales were on fire in a field containing horses. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and rakes to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately.  If you have any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
