 Smoke coming from a vehicle's engine in Northwich Time of Incident: 20/08/2018 - 11:23 Location: Manchester Road in Lostock Gralam, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 11.20am firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from a vehicle's engine on Manchester Road in Lostock Gralam, Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that the engine had blown up. Firefighters cooled the engine down and made sure it was safe before leaving. They were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
