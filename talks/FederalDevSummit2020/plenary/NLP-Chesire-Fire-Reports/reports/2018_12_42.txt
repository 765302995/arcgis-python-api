 Four-vehicle collision in Radway Green Time of Incident: 20/12/2018 - 17:35 Location: Butterton Lane, Radway Green Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar Shortly after 5.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Butterton Lane in Radway Green. On arrival the crew discovered that the incident involved four vehicles and that no-one was trapped inside any of them. Firefighters made the scene safe before handing the incident over to the police. The fire crew were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
