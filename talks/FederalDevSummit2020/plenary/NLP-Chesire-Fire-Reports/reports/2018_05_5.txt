 Fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/05/2018 - 22:17 Location: Cromwell Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington used a water back pack to extinguish a mattress that had been deliberately set alight on St Greggs Field, off Cromwell Road.
