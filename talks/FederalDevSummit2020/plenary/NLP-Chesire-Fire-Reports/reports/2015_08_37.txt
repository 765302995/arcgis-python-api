Chip pan fire in Chester Time of Incident: 22/08/2015 - 23:50 Location: Cambridge Road in Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Two fire engines from Chester were sent to a flat on Cambridge Road in Chester to a fire involving a chip pan. The fire was out before fire crews arrived but the flat was full of smoke. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke. One casualty was checked by paramedics for smoke inhalation and was then taken to hospital.  Smoke alarms were sounding at the property.  Chip pan fire safety tips  Kitchen fire safety tips
