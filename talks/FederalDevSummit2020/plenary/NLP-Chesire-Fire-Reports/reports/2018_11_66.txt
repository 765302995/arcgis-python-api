 Burning smell coming from a fuse box in Crewe Time of Incident: 18/11/2018 - 17:49 Location: Bradfield Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At around 5.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a burning smell coming from a fuse box at a domestic property in Bradfield Road in Crewe. On arrival crews used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and isolated the electricity supply to the fuse box, which was located in the living room of the property. After firefighters made the internal fuse box safe the property's occupiers contacted an electrician. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour and 20 minutes.
