 Cooker fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 05/08/2018 - 17:08 Location: Abbey Place, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At around 5.10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a cooker at a domestic property on Abbey Place in Crewe. On arrival the crews discovered a small smouldering fire which they extinguished using water. Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes. For kitchen fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
