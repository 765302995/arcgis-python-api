 Water rescue, Warrington Time of Incident: 06/07/2019 - 04:16 Location: Chester Road, Warrington Attendance: Five fire engines from Warrington, Penketh, Lymm, Stockton Heath and Widnes, a Water Rescue Unit and Ariel Ladder Platform. Fire crews were called to reports of a man in the canal. Police were already at the scene by the time they arrived, and the man had taken hold of a police throwline.  Water rescue specialists from the fire service were deployed, and the man was rescued from the water and brought to the canal bank. He was then passed into the care of paramedics.
