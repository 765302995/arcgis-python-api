Road traffic collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 25/09/2016 - 12:40 Location: Fishpool Road, Delamere, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford, one fire engine from Northwich and one fire engine from Tarporley Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving two vehicles where one casualty had become trapped. Crews released the trapped casualty from the vehicle and handed them into the care of the ambulance. A further two casualties were treated by the ambulance service.
