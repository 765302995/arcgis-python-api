 Large fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 25/06/2018 - 03:47 Location: Middlewich Road, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich. One fire engine from Middlewich. Firefighters attended a large fire off Middlewich Road, Northwich. Crews used two hose reels and one main jet to extinguish the flames. A farmer assisted crews with a digger and a  ground monitor was used while damping down to check for hotspots.
