 Small vehicle fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 12/11/2019 - 22:50 Location: Hewitt Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Crews attended to large smoke issuing from a van on Hewitt Street in Crewe.  Wearing a breathing apparatus, firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish the flames.  Crews continued to dampen down and assess the vehicle using the thermal imaging camera.
