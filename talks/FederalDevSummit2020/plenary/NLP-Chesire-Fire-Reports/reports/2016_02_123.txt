Road traffic collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 31/01/2016 - 10:19 Location: Murdishaw Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine each from Frodsham, Widnes and Runcorn One man was trapped in a car after it collided with a signpost. Specialist cutting equipment was used to release the casualty who was handed into the care of the ambulance service.
