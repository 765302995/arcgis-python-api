Kitchen fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 03/03/2016 - 18:33 Location: Riverside Grove, Wistaston Attendance: Three fire engines from Crewe, Nantwich and Alsager Three fire crews were sent to a property on Riverside Grove in Wistaston where there was a kitchen fire involving a microwave. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. The fire crews used one hose reel jet to deal with the fire.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the property. Firefighters requested the attendance of an ambulance for one casualty who was suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation.
