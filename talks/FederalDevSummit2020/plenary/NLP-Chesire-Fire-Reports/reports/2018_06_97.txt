 Vehicle fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 23/06/2018 - 01:51 Location: Somerville Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters from Widnes used one hose reel jet and one breathing apparatus to tackle a vehicle fire on Somerville Road. The team were in attendance for approximately 25 minutes. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
