 Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 08/11/2018 - 14:53 Location: Princes Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly before 3pm firefighters from Ellemere Port attended reports of a vehicle fire on Princes Road in the town. On arrival the crew found a car well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes.
