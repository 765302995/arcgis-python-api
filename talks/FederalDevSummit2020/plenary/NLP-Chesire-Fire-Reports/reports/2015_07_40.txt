Chimney fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 24/07/2015 - 20:44 Location: Mill Lane, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines from Holmes Chapel Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish a chimney fire in Congleton.  On arrival smoke was issuing from under the stone roof slates of the property. The crews inspected the loft space using a thermal imaging camera and tackled the fire from the hearth as well as the roof using a seven metre ladder.  They were at the scene for over two hours.
