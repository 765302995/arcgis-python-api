 Fire at an Iceland store in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/04/2019 - 01:15 Location: Crosfield Street, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Stockton Heath and one from Widnes At around 1.15am firefighters were called to a report of a fire at the Iceland store in Crosfield Street, Warrington. On arrival crews found that the fire involving a freezer had been extinguished with a dry powder extinguisher and that the store was smoke logged. The electricity supply to the freezer was isolated and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a large fan to clear smoke from the store. The freezer was taken out of the store and cooled down using a hose reel jet by fireighters. The fire crews were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
