 Fire at a recycling plant in Widnes Time of Incident: 27/07/2018 - 12:09 Location: Everite Road, Widnes. Attendance: Three fire engines attended -one from Crewe, one from Lymm and one from Birchwood. Shortly after 12pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at recycling plant in Widnes. The team attended the scene and found two large stacks of paper well alight. Crews initially used a mechanical digger drag out the waste and spread the fire. They then used hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. The team were in attendance for approximately one hour 40 minutes.
