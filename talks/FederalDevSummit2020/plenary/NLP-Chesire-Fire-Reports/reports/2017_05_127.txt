Tumble dryer fire Time of Incident: 10/05/2017 - 17:01 Location: The Crescent, Lymm, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Stockton Heath and Knutsford Firefighters responded to a report of a house fire on The Crescent, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that there was smoke coming from a tumble dryer. This was removed from the property. Special fans were used to clear the smoke.
