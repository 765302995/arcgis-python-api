Road traffic collision in Middlewich Time of Incident: 10/07/2017 - 11:33 Location: King Street, Middlewich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Winsford and Northwich One casualty was left trapped in a car that was involved in a collision with a heavy goods vehicle. Firefighters using specialist cutting equipment removed the roof of the car so that the casualty, a woman, could be released and handed into the care of the ambulance service. She was taken to hospital by air ambulance.
