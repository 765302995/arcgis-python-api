 Vehicle fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 15/05/2018 - 15:45 Location: London Road North, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Firefighters from Poynton attended reports of a vehicle fire on the A523, London Road North near to Poynton Pool. On arrival at the scene crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The team were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes.
