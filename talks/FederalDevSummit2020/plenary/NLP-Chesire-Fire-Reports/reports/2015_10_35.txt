Collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 24/10/2015 - 13:40 Location: Junction 12 and 14 on the westbound carriageway of the M56. Attendance: Fire engines from Runcorn, Widnes and Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a collision between Junction 12 and 14 on the westbound carriageway of the M56.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved three cars. Crews treated three casualties at the scene before they were taken to hospital.  Crews remained on scene for about one hour.
