Driver released from overturned vehicle in Wistaston Time of Incident: 21/10/2015 - 12:37 Location: Wistaston Green Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Nantwich Firefighters have released a casualty from an overturned vehicle. Only one car was involved in the incident which left the lady driver trapped.  The casualty was handed into the care of the ambulance service and firefighters made the scene safe.
