Smoke alarm saves man and dog from house fire Time of Incident: 11/01/2015 - 23:54 Location: Pudding Lane, Chester. Attendance: Fire engines from Tarporley and Chester Firefighters were called to a fire in a semi detached house in Chester. On arrival they discovered a fire in the ground floor living room which was affecting the kitchen of the property. The occupier was alerted to the fire by smoke alarms sounding and got himself and his dog out and dialed 999.  Firefighters used a hose reel to extinguish the fire, which is believed to have started accidentally. A fire investigation is currently underway to determine the cause.  Smoke alarms
