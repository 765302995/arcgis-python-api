 Girl with trapped finger in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/07/2018 - 18:29 Location: Cavendish Farm Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a park in Cavendish Farm Road, Runcorn, after a teenager got their finger stuck in a swing. Crews managed to release the girl's finger from the swing, which was stuck in a chain link. No ambulance was required.
