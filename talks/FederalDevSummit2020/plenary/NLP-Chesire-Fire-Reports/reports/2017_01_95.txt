Extractor fan fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 09/01/2017 - 19:25 Location: Tawny Court, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn fire station and one fire engine from Frodsham fire station There was a fire involving an extractor fan in the kitchen of a property. The fire was already out when the fire crews arrived, but one person was suffering smoke inhalation, so firefighters gave oxygen therapy to the casualty whilst awaiting arrival of paramedics.
