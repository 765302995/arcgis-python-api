Shed fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 11/04/2015 - 23:56 Location: Biddulph Road, Congleton. Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Biddulph Road in Congleton.  When they arrived they found that there was a shed on fire. Crews used hose reel jets to put the fire out. They also carried out a Home Safety Assessment with the home owners.   An investigation has been launched to find the cause of the blaze.
