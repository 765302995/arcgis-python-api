Cable fire in Handforth Time of Incident: 27/02/2016 - 08:39 Location: Epsom Avenue in Handforth Attendance: A fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters responded to a alarm sounding at Lifestyle Fitness on Epsom Avenue in Handforth.  When they arrived crews found that there had been an electrical cable that had overheated. Firefighters turned off the electrics to the building ready for the incident to be passed to an electrician.
