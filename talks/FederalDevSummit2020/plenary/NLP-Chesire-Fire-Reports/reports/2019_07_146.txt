 Fire in the open in Northwich Time of Incident: 07/07/2019 - 10:42 Location: Middlewich Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly before 10.45am firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on Middlewich Road in Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
