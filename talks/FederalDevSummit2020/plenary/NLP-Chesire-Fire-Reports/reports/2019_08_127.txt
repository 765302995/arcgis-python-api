 Motorbike collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 08/08/2019 - 06:00 Location: A533, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters attended a collision involving a motorbike on the A553 in Northwich. Crews used a hose reel to put out a fire involving the motorbike while paramedics assessed the casualty. Police were in attendance and the road was closed while emergency services worked at the scene.
