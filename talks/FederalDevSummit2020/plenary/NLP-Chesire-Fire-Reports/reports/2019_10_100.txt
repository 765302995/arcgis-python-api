 Door fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/10/2019 - 02:24 Location: Sharp Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh. One fire engine attended from Warrington. One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath. Firefighters were called to a fire involving a door at a flat in Sharp Street, Warrington.   Crews rescued a woman from the flat and used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel.  Crews were in attendance for more than two hours.
