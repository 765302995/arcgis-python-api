 Gas leak at Gawsworth house Time of Incident: 27/04/2019 - 23:10 Location: Church Lane, Gawsworth Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters attended to reports of a gas leak coming from a house on Church Lane in Gawsworth.   The incident occurred after a car collided with a gas pipe outside the house.  A hose reel jet and dry powder were used by fire crews.  The Gas Board attended and confirmed the gas supply was safe.  All people were accounted for and there are believed to have been no injuries.
