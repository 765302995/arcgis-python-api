Unattended cooking fills Warrington property with smoke Time of Incident: 20/03/2016 - 18:52 Location: Chiltern Place, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Birchwood One person was taken to hospital after being overcome by smoke when cooking was left unattended.
