Play area fire in Latchford Time of Incident: 23/08/2017 - 20:28 Location: Wilderspool Causeway Park, Latchford Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a fire in a children's play area in Wilderspool Causeway Park, Latchford. Two bins and children's swings were deliberately set on fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. If you have any information about the incident please call Cheshire Police on 101 or call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
