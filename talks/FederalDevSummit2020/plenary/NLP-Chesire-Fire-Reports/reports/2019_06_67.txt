 Scooter fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 16/06/2019 - 17:46 Location: Scott Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 5.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Scott Avenue, Widnes, involving a motorbike. On arrival the crew found a scooter well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
