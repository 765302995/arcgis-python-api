Fire at BMX skate park in Winsford Time of Incident: 02/11/2017 - 20:04 Location: Mount Pleasant Drive, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to reports of a small fire at the BMX skate park on Mount Pleasant Drive, Winsford. Crews attended the scene and used a water back pack to extinguish the fire. An investigation has been launched into the cause of the fire and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 55 111.
