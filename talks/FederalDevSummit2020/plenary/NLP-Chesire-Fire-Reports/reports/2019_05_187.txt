 Second motorbike fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 04/05/2019 - 20:16 Location: A533, Bridgewater Expressway, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly after 8.15pm firefighters from Widnes attended reports of a second motorbike fire in Runcorn.  On arrival the crew discovered a motorbike which was well alight in a subway under Bridgewater Expressway near to Astmoor Industrial Estate.  The crew used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for around 35 minutes.  Anyone with information in relation to either of the fires should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101 quoting IML 392421, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
