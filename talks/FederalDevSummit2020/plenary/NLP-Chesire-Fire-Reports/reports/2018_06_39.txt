 Fire involving a sofa and rubbish in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 20:00 Location: Cotton Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 8pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Cotton Lane in Halton Lodge, Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a sofa and rubbish which was outside. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
