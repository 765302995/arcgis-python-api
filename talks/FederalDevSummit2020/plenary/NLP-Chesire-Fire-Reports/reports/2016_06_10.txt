Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 27/06/2016 - 23:59 Location: Frank Bott Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Alsager A car fire was extinguished by firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and asked for police to attend.  Anyone who may have information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
