 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/11/2019 - 16:31 Location: Evelyn Street, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Penketh and one from Warrington Fire crews attended to a house fire on Evelyn Street in Great Sankey, Warrington.  The fire was located in the kitchen area which was full of smoke.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews used one hose reel to extinguish the flames and a large fan to disperse the smoke.  The fire is believed to have been caused by plastic being left on an electric hob.  Everyone inside the property was accounted for. One woman and a child were handed into the care of paramedics as a precaution due to suffering from smoke inhalation.
