 Rubbish fire at the back of shops in Nantwich extinguished by crews Time of Incident: 17/02/2018 - 19:55 Location: Meeanee Drive, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Fire crews in Nantwich extinguished a rubbish fire at the back of shops on Meeanee Drive in Nantwich. Firefighters used a hose reel to put out the fire which involved bottles exploding.
