 Small vehicle fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/07/2018 - 18:38 Location: St Anselms Place, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At  around 6.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a vehicle fire at St Anselms Place in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involving the engine compartment of the vehicle had been extinguished by its owners. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and make sure the vehicle was safe. They were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
