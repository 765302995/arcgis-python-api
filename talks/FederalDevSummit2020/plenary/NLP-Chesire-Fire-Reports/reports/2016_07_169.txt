Road traffic collision on M53 south, J11 to 12 Time of Incident: 01/07/2016 - 15:51 Location: M53 south, J11 to 12 Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port One car was involved in a road traffic collision on the M53 near Chester. Fortunately nobody was trapped in the vehicle, which had left the carriageway, ending up on its side. Firefighters assisted at the scene and two casualties were treated by paramedics.
