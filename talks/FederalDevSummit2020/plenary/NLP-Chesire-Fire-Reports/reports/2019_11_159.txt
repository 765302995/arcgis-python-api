 Wheelie bin fire, Great Sankey Time of Incident: 02/11/2019 - 06:57 Location: Barrow Hall Lane, Great Sankey Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh Firefighters were called to deal with a wheelie bin on fire at an address in Barrow Hall Lane. They arrived to find the bin well alight, and used a hand appliance to extinguish the flames. Colleagues at Cheshire Police were notified of the incident, due to it being a suspected deliberate ignition.  For advice on preventing your wheelie bin from being at risk of fire, visit our website https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
