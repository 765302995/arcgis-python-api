 Small fire in the open in Frodsham Time of Incident: 23/09/2018 - 21:13 Location: Chester Road, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane At around 9.10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at the back of Helsby Sports and Social Club on Chester Road in Frodsham. The crew extinguished the small fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
