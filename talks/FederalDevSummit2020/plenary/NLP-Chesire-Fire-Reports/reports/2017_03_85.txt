Car burnt out by fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 17/03/2017 - 20:07 Location: Coole Lane, Austerson, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a saloon car on fire on a car park in Nantwich. The car was well alight on arrival and the crew used one hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. A thermal imaging camera was then used to check the tyres for heat before the firefighters left.
