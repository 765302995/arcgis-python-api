Road traffic collision on M6 north junctions 18 to 19 Time of Incident: 08/01/2017 - 21:27 Location: M6 northbound between junctions 18 and 19 Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel, one from Knutsford and one from Middlewich Four vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision on the M6 northbound near Holmes Chapel. Nobody was trapped in any of the vehicles but one car was smoking so a hose reel jet was used to cool it.
