 Fence on fire, Haslington Time of Incident: 13/01/2018 - 22:10 Location: Primrose Avenue, Haslington Attendance: Two appliances from Crewe Crews tackled a fire involving a wooden fence and wheelie bins. A single hose reel was used to extinguish the flames, and police attendance was requested as it was a suspected deliberate ignition. Home safety assessments were completed with the neighbouring properties before they left.
