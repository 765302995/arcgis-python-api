 Fire involving a worktop behind a fixed cooker Time of Incident: 11/07/2019 - 15:42 Location: Rock Road, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 3.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire incident at a domestic property on Rock Road in Latchford, Warrington. The fire involving a worktop behind a fixed cooker was out on arrival. Firefighters cut away the parts of the worktop that had been damaged by the fire and removed tiles to check that the fire had not spread. Once they were satisfied that the property was safe they left the scene. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
