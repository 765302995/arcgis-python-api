Road traffic collision on M56 westbound near junction 14 Time of Incident: 02/08/2015 - 17:06 Location: M56 junction 14 westbound Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Frodsham Two vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision on the M56 westbound near Hapsford Services near Elton.  Fortunately nobody was trapped in the incident, although two men crawled from the back of the black car, uninjured, and a female was helped out of the white car with a suspected back injury.   She was taken to hospital by air ambulance.  Crews made the scene safe.
