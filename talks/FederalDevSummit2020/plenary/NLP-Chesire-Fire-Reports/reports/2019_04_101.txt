 Roof fire in Chester Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 01:41 Location: Hollowmoor Heath, chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester. One fire engine from Ellesmere Port. Firefighters attended a roof fire at an address in Hollowmoor Heath, Chester. Crews used a ladder to reach the roofand removed tiles to gain access to the fire. They then used a hosereel and a jet to put out the flames before using a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots. One casualty suffering effects of smoke was treated by ambulance personnel. Crews were detained one hour 40 minutes.
