 Skip on fire, Winsford Time of Incident: 12/01/2018 - 19:26 Location: Lapwing Close, Winsford Attendance: One appliance from Winsford Crews were called to reports of a skip on fire on a residential drive. A single hose reel was used to extinguish the flames, and the adjoining property was unaffected. The occupier was provided safety advice in respect of the skip and its proximity to buildings.
