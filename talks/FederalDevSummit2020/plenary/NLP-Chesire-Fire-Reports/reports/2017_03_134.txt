Flat fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 09/03/2017 - 17:53 Location: Jubilee Gardens, Nantwich. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Nantwich, Alsager, Crewe and Winsford. Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Jubilee Gardens, Nantwich.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire involving a cooker in a ground floor flat. This had gone out but the property was filled with smoke. Crews cleared the smoke with a large fan.  One man was treated for slight smoke inhalation.  The property was fitted with smoke detectors that helped raise the alarm that there was a fire.
