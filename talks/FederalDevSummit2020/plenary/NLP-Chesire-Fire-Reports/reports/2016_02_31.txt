House fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/02/2016 - 19:53 Location: Lime Street, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: A fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Lime Street, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire involving a grill pan but this had gone out.  Firefighters used a special fan to clear the smoke.
