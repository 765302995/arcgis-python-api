 Large grass fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/07/2018 - 19:12 Location: Factory Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes attended At around 7.10pm firefighters were called to reports of a large grass fire on Factory Lane in Widnes. The crew discovered there were six separate seats of fire involving a total of approximately 100 square metres of grass. Firefighters used beaters to put out the flames and were in attendance for around 40 minutes. The seats of fire are believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information should call police on 101, give the details via General Enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
