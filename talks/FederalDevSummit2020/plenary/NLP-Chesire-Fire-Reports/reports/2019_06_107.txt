 Building checked following collision Time of Incident: 08/06/2019 - 11:32 Location: Groby Road, Crewe. CW1 4PE Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe attended Firefighters were called to Groby Road after reports that a lorry had collided with a building. The crew attended, isolated gas and electric and put a gas monitor in place as a precaution. They also used absorbent sheets to deal with spilled diesel and oil on the road.
