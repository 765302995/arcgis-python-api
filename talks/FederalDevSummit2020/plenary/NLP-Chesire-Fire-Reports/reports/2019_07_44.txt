 Air conditioning unit explosion in Chester Time of Incident: 23/07/2019 - 20:33 Location: Watergate Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and the aerial ladder platform, one fire engine from Powey Lane, one from Congleton and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to Watergate Street, Chester, after a report of a generator explosion and that there was smoke coming from it. Crews used ladders and an aerial ladder platform to access the site and discovered that an air conditioning unit was involved and that there was no fire at the scene. Crews isolated the unit and handed the incident over to the council. Crews were at the scene for three hours.
