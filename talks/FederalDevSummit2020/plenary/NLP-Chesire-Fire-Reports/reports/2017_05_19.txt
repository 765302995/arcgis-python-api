Peat conveyor system fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 27/05/2017 - 11:35 Location: South Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engine from Ellesmere Port, one from Powey Lane and the aerial appliance and supporting fire engine from Chester An outdoor conveyor system carrying peat was involved in a large fire. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two each of main jets, covering jets and hose reel jets to bring the blaze under control.  They were at the scene for just under three hours.Firefighter on aerial platform tackling peat fire
