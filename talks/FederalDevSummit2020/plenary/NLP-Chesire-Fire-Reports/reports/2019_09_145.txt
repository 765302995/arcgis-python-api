 Bedroom fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 03/09/2019 - 17:26 Location: Luton Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port. One fire engine attended from Powey Lane. Firefighters were called to a fire in a bedroom at an address in Luton Road, Ellesmere Port.  Crews extinguished the fire using four breathing apparatus and one hose reel.   They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke and a thermal image camera to check for hotspots. Crews were in attendance for five hours.
