 Fire against community centre wall in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/07/2018 - 04:45 Location: Alnwick Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port At around 4.45am firefighters were called to a report of a fire outside a community centre on Alnwick Drive in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered a small quantity of rubbish on fire against a wall of the community centre. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They then conducted a thorough search of the community centre for any signs of fire, including in the roof space with the aid of an extension ladder. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and make sure that the building was safe. They also used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the community centre. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour and 20 minutes. Police also attended. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 133901, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
