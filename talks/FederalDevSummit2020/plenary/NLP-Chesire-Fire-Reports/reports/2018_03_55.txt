 Garden waste fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/03/2018 - 17:09 Location: Blackheath Lane, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a fire involving grass, tree and bush cuttings in Blackheath Lane, Runcorn. Using water from a nearby pond they used two main jets, then reduced to one main jet and a stack drag to extinguish the flames. Crews remained on site while the cuttings were steaming and used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.
