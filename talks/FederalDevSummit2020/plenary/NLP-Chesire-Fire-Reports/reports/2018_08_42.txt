 Car on fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 21/08/2018 - 21:47 Location: M6 southbound, between junctions 17 and 16 Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Sandbach and one from Holmes Chapel At around 9.45pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire just before Junction 16 on the M6 southbound. The fire, which is believed to have been caused by a battery fault, was out on arrival. The crews used a hose reel jet to cool the car's engine to make sure the vehicle was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
