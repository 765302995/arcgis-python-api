Wood and plastic on fire in a Warrington park Time of Incident: 05/10/2016 - 21:28 Location: Bridgewater avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Pallets, plastic road barriers and wood were on fire in a park. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and to dampen the surrounding area.  This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Do you have any information?
