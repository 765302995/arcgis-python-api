 Rubbish fire, Rudheath Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 15:20 Location: Off Middlewich Road, Rudheath Attendance: Two fire engines attended (Northwich and Winsford) Firefighters attended to reports of a fire near a convenience store in Rudheath. They found a large pile of rubbish was well alight and used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames. A second fire engine was called to provide additional water cover, and they had the fire out after about an hour.
