 House fire in Chester Time of Incident: 07/05/2019 - 19:05 Location: Meynell Place, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Three fire engines attended a fire in a bedroom of a terraced house on Meynell Place, Chester. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets, a covering jet, a large fan and a thermal imaging camera to tackle the fire. One male casualty, who was suffering from burns to his hands, was treated by firefighters before the arrival of paramedics before he was later taken to hospital. Crews were at the scene for two hours and 15 minutes.
