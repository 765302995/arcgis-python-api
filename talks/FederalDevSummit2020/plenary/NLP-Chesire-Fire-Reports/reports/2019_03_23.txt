 Outbuilding building fire in Chester Time of Incident: 24/03/2019 - 19:32 Location: Downsfield Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester. One fire engine from Powey Lane. Firefighters were called to a fire in an outbuilding which had spread to the house in Downsfield Road, Chester. On arrival crews used two hose reels, two breathing apparatus and a covering jet to extinguish the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. Crews were in attendance for approximately three hours.
