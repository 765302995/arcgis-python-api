Conifer hedge and log store well alight in Frodsham Time of Incident: 27/08/2017 - 16:14 Location: Towers Lane, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port A conifer hedge was well alight, and it spread to a log store, so firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.
