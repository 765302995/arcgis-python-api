 Faulty light fitting at a commercial building fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 22/09/2019 - 20:33 Location: Delamere Street, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At around 8.30pm firefighters from Winsford were called to reports of a fire at a commercial building on Delamere Street, Winsford.  On arrival at the scene the crew found that the fire had involved a light fitting which had been extinguished by a member of staff.  Firefighters isolated the electrics to the light fitting, they then used a thermal imaging camera to check the temperature of the light.  Crews were detained at the scene for around 40 minutes.
