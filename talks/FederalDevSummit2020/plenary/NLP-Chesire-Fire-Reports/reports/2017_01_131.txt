Wheelie bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 02/01/2017 - 19:05 Location: Esk Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a wheelie bin fire.  This fire was believed to have been started deliberately. Do you have any information? Report it by calling Crimestoppers, which can be done anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
