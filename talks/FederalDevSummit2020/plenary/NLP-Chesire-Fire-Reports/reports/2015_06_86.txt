Skip fire next to school in Mow Cop Time of Incident: 15/06/2015 - 22:18 Location: Church Street, Mow Cop Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton One hose reel was used by firefighters to extinguish a skip fire next to a school in Mow Cop.  Police were informed due to the crew suspecting the cause of the blaze was deliberate. Heat damage had been caused to some of the guttering and three window panes of the school.  Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101 or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
