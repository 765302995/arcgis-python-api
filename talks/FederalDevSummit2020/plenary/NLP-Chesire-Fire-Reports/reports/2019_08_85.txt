 Collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 19/08/2019 - 16:00 Location: M56, Eastbound between junction 10 to junction nine Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Warrington, one from Stockton Heath and one from Winsford Fire crews attended to a road traffic collision on the M56 eastbound carriageway between junction 10 to junction nine.  One man  was physically trapped inside the bus and extricated by firefighters.  He was then handed into the care of paramedics and crews made up their equipment.
