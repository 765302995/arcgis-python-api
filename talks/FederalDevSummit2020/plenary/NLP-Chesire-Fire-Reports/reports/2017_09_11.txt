Refuse on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/09/2017 - 20:01 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A large amount of refuse was on fire in a wooded area off Thornton Road. A wheelie bin was also involved in the fire which was put out using a hose reel jet.  Always follow our wheelie bin safety advice.
