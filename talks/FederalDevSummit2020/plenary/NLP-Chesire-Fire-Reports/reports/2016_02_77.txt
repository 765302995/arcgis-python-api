Small fire inside a domestic garage in Chester Time of Incident: 13/02/2016 - 10:58 Location: The Crescent, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Two fire crews from Chester were sent to a property where there was a report of a fire in a domestic garage.  On arrival the fire crews found a fire involving an electric socket in a small detached garage.  The electrics were isolated.  Firefighters used one hose reel to bring the fire under control.
