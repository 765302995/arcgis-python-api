 Jetty fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/02/2019 - 14:39 Location: Moore Lane, Warrington Attendance: Four fire engines attended - one from Stockton Heath, one from Runcorn, one from Warrington and one from Chester Firefighters were called to reports of a jetty being on fire on the Manchester Ship Canal on Moore Lane in Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet to fight the fire and a hand pump to collect water. They also used a smaller jet to tackle the fire. Once the fire was under control firefighters used tools to cut away part of the jetty. They also damped the area down to make it safe.
