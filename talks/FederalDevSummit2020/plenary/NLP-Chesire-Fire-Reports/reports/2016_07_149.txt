Flat on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 04/07/2016 - 23:44 Location: Liverpool Road, Widnes Attendance: Fire engines from Widnes and Runcorn Reports of persons reported in a flat which was on fire in Widnes required firefighters to attend. A partial evacuation took place and all persons were accounted for. The crews used a hose reel to extinguish the fire. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check that the fire was out and a large fan was used to remove smoke from the property. One person was assessed by paramedics. Police also attended and a fire investigation into the cause will take place.
