Van fire on M62 East to M6 South near Winwick Road in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/08/2016 - 14:17 Location: One fire engine from Birchwood Attendance: M62 link road to M6S near Winwick Road, Winwick Quay, Warrington Firefighters are currently tackling a van fire on the link road between the M62 East to M6 South. Crews used special cutting equipment to access the seat of the fire. If you are travelling in the area this may cause delays to your journey.
