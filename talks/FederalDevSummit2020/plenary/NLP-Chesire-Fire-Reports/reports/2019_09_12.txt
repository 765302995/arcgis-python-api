 Cooker fire in warrington Time of Incident: 27/09/2019 - 20:55 Location: Oxmead Close, Warrington. WA2 0QA Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Penketh Just after 9pm fire crews from Warrington and Penketh attended a cooker fire at Oxmead Close.  Firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put the fire out and then used a large fan to get rid of smoke from the kitchen.
