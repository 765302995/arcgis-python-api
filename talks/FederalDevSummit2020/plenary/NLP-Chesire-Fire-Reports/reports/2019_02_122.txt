 Person trapped after car crash in Chester Time of Incident: 05/02/2019 - 19:48 Location: Liverpool Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision involving two cars on Liverpool Road, Chester. On arrival crews found that one person was trapped in their vehicle and they used stabilisation equipment and hydraulic spreading equipment to remove a door in order to release the casualty who was then handed into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for 30 minutes.
