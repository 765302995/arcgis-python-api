Cooker fire in Willaston Time of Incident: 04/09/2015 - 22:14 Location: Wistaston Road, Willaston Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe A cooker fire in Willaston was fortunately out on arrival of firefighters.  Smoke alarms were fitted and did activate. The crews carried out a home safety assessment and were at the scene for around 20 minutes. Kitchen fire safety advice
