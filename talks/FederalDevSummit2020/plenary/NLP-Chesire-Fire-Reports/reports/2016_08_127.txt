Bin on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 10/08/2016 - 00:36 Location: Winfield Way, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A large bin which was on fire needed firefighters to attend and use one hose reel to extinguish it. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
