 Grill pan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/04/2018 - 22:40 Location: Norreys Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and Penketh Firefighters attended a kitchen fire at a house on Norreys Avenue, Warrington. Crews used two breathing apparatus, a hose reel and a positive pressure ventilation fan to extinguish the fire. The fire is believed to have been caused by a grill pan.
