 Farm vehicle fire in Scholar Green Time of Incident: 26/07/2019 - 18:31 Location: Townsend Lane, Scholar Green Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar Crews attended to a farm vehicle well alight on Townsend Lane in Scholar Green.  Wearing one breathing apparatus, one jet was used to put out the fire.  Firefighters continued to check for hot spots and dampen down the area.  The fire is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault.
