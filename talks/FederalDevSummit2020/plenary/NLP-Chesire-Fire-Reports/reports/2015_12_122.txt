Car fire on M56 west junctions 15 to 16 Time of Incident: 01/12/2015 - 11:15 Location: M56 west between junctions 15 and 16 Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Firefighters have been tackling a car fire on the M56 west between junctions 15 and 16. A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire, with one crew member wearing breathing apparatus.
