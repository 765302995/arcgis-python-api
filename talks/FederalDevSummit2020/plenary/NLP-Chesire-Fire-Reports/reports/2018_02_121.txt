 Shed fire in Chester Time of Incident: 02/02/2018 - 16:13 Location: Station Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a fire in an outbuilding on Station Road, Chester. A shed, some conifer trees and two motorbikes were on fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and were at the scene for an hour.
