 Bonfire in Chester garden Time of Incident: 03/08/2019 - 21:28 Location: Willow Crescent, Chester. CH2 3LT Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters from Chester were called to a bonfire in a back garden at Willow Crescent.  They used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and were at the scene for 15 minutes.
