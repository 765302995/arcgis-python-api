 Small fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 12/01/2019 - 20:06 Location: Chester Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a fire on scrubland involving building materials off Chester Road, Ellesmere Port. Crews used a small ladder to access the site and then used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
