 Chimney fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 05/01/2018 - 04:33 Location: Whirley Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and the aerial appliance and supporting fire engine also from Macclesfield A chimney fire was tackled by crews at a property on Whirley Road. Firefighters used chimney rods and water from the aerial appliance to put out the fire.   Always follow our chimney safety advice
