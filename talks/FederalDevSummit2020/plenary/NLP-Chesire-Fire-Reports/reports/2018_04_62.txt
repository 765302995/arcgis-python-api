 Skip on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/04/2018 - 19:49 Location: Bold Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine in attendance in Widnes Crews were called to reports of black smoke coming from a skip on fire on Bold Street in the West Bank area of Widnes.  One main jet was used by firefighters as well as two tanks of water to reduce the amount of smoke and it remained in a secure compound while it was fully extinguished.
