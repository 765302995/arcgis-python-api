Water rescue in Chester Time of Incident: 06/06/2016 - 21:29 Location: Castle Drive, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines and the boat from Chester, one fire engine from Ellesmere Port and the rope rescue unit and supporting fire engine from Knutsford The swift water rescue team and boat were called to a report of a person in the River Dee. They, along with firefighters and the rope rescue team, recovered the casualty, a female, from the river and handed her into the care of paramedics.
