Warehouse building fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 08/12/2017 - 17:53 Location: Wincham Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich,Lymm, Winsford and Middlewich On Friday, 8 December at 5.50pm fire engines from Northwich, Lymm, Winsford and Middlewich fire station attended a fire on Wincham Lane in Northwich. The fire involved an electrical powered fork lift truck in a warehouse and crews used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel, one covering jet and one positive pressure ventilation fan to extinguish the fire.  One casualty was suffering from smoke inhalation. The fire service were at the scene for one hour and fifty minutes.
