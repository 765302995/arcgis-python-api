Cooker fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/01/2017 - 18:09 Location: Moorfield Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Widnes Firefighters attended a cooker fire on moorfield road, Widnes. The fire involved a plastic container on a cooker ring, which caused a great deal of smoke inside the property. One person sustained minor burns but did not require medical attention.  Smoke alarms were fitted within the property and did alert the occupier to the fire. Make sure you have a working smoke alarm and check it regularly.  Want to save a life? Practise careful cooking.
