 Fire in the open in Tarvin Time of Incident: 23/04/2019 - 19:19 Location: A54, Tarvin, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley Shortly after 7.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on the A54 in Tarvin, Chester. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a hedgerow and tree stumps. The fire affected an area measuring approximately eight metres by three metres. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They then damped the area down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour.
