 Vehicle fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/03/2018 - 05:43 Location: Wellington Street, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn attended At around 5.50am firefighters were called to a vehicle fire in Wellington Street in Runcorn. The crew found the car in the middle of a car park. They used a hose reel jet and breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire.
