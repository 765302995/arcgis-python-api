Fire at a park in Elworth Time of Incident: 10/11/2017 - 19:26 Location: Elworth Park, London Road, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich A child's play swing was well alight upon arrival. Firefighters used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames and remained behind to damp down the hot metal structure. The ignition is believed to have been deliberate - anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting incident 903 of 10 November 2017.
