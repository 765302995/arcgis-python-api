 Play equipment on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/10/2019 - 18:32 Location: St Elphins Park, Manchester Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to play equipment on fire in a park in Warrington. They used a hose reel to extinguish the fire, which was well alight on their arrival. It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone knows anything about this fire they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111
