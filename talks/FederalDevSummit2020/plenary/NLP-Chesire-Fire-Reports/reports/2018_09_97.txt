 Fire in the open in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 10/09/2018 - 15:43 Location: King Street, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a fire involving a fridge and a mattress on land off King Street, Ellesmere Port. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
