 Machinery fire at a mill in Congleton Time of Incident: 18/06/2019 - 23:09 Location: Brown Street, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Holmes Chapel and one from Congleton Shortly after 11pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Jubilee Mill in Brown Street, Congleton. On arrival crews found a small fire involving machinery that is believed to have been caused by a faulty gas pipe. The electricity supply to the building were isolated and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters then damped the machinery down to make it safe and monitored temperatures using a thermal imaging camera. They were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
