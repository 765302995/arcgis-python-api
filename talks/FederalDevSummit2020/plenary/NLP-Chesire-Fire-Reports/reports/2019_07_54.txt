 Bin fire in Warrington park Time of Incident: 20/07/2019 - 21:43 Location: Troutbeck Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 9.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a bin being on fire in a park in Troutbeck Avenue, Warrington. The crew extinguished the fire with water. Firefighters were in attendance for around 15 minutes. The bin fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 462194, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
