Fuel spillage in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 30/07/2015 - 12:29 Location: Chester Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Fuel was seen seeping from underneath the door of a Macclesfield business. Firefighters used specialist environment packs to prevent the fuel, confirmed to be diesel, from entering the drainage system. The incident occurred when rain water lifted diesel residue which then ran under the doorway and onto the footpath.
