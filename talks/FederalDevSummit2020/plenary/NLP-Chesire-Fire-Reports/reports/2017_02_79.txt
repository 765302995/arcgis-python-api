Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/02/2017 - 20:32 Location: Grizedale, Widnes. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a car fire on Grizedale, Widnes.  This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have information to help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
