 Garden fire in Swettenham Time of Incident: 29/05/2018 - 21:10 Location: Swettenham Lane, Swettenham. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel Crews from Holmes Chapel tackled a garden fire at a residential property in Swettenham, the team used water to extinguish the fire, before damping down and turning over the grass to prevent it from reoccurring. The team  were in attendance at the scene for approximately 90 minutes.
