 Two vehicle collision in Prestbury Time of Incident: 23/12/2018 - 16:37 Location: Bbonis Hall Lane, Prestbury. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a two vehicle collision in Bonis Hall Lane, Prestbury. A female casualty was extricated from one of the vehicles by crews and paramedics after firefighters removed the roof of the car.
