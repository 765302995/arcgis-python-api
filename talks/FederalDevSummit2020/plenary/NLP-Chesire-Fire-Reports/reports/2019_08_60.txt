 Fire in a farmer's field in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 22/08/2019 - 15:23 Location: Weston Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Shortly before 3.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Weston Road in Wilmslow. On arrival the crew discovered a fire measuring approximately nine metres by 10 metres in a farmer's field. It was a case of controlled burning. After consulting with the farmer in attendance firefighters decided to allow the fire to burn. They left a water bowser in place as a precautionary safety measure. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
