 Small rubbish fire in Cuddington Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 20:14 Location: Mill Lane, Cuddington, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters from Northwich used a hose reel jet to extinguish a small refuse fire at an address on Mill Lane in Cuddington. The team were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
