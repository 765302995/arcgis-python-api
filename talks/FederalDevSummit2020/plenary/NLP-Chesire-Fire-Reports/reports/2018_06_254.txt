 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/06/2018 - 11:54 Location: Sankey Way, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to a car which was well alight on a grass verge in Sankey Way, Great Sankey. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames. Crews then continued to damp down the vehicle to ensure it did not reignite. The road was closed while the firefighters dealt with the fire which is believed to have been caused by a fault.
