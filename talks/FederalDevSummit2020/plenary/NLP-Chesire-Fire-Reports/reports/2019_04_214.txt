 Mattress fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/04/2019 - 23:56 Location: Rock Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before midnight firefighters were called to a report of a mattress being on fire in the open on Rock Road in Warrington. On arrival the crew found the mattress well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for more than 10 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
