Unattended cooking causes small fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 19/01/2016 - 14:59 Location: Tytherington Green, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a house in Tytherington Green. They found there had been a small fire caused by cooking left unattended.  Although the fire was out there was smoke logging in the property.  A high pressure fan was used to clear the smoke.   Please don't leave cooking unattended - follow our safety advice - Kitchen fire safety
