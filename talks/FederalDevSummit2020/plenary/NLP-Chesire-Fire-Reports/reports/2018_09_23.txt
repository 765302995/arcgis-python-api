 Smoke in Neston Town Hall Time of Incident: 24/09/2018 - 15:22 Location: Brook Street, Neston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 3.20pm firefighters were called to reports of a smoke alarm sounding at Neston Town Hall in Brook Street. The crew isolated the gas supply to the building and discovered that the source of the smoke that had triggered the alarm was a small cardboard box in a store cupboard. Firefighters removed the box and cleared smoke from the area.
