Vehicle fire on M6 north between junctions 16 and 17 Time of Incident: 09/05/2016 - 10:30 Location: M6 north between junctions 16 and 17 Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Sandbach Firefighters made the scene safe after a hatchback had been on fire on the M6 northbound between junctions 16 and 17.
