House fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 29/08/2016 - 02:48 Location: Hillside Drive, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port and Chester Fire crews responded to a report of a house fire on Hillside Drive, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived they found that there had been a fire involving a cooker. This had gone out but the property was filled with smoke. A woman was suffering from the effects of inhaling the smoke. She was cared for by the ambulance service while crews used a large fan to clear the smoke. Kitchen fire safety
