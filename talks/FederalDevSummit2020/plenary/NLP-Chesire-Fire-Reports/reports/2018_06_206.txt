 Small vehicle fire, Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 09/06/2018 - 02:51 Location: Wharfe Lane, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Crews responded to reports of a car on fire in Wharfe Lane, Ellesmere Port. They used breathing apparatus and a single hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which is thought to have been a malicious ignition. Anyone with information is urged call the police on 101, quoting incident 88313.
