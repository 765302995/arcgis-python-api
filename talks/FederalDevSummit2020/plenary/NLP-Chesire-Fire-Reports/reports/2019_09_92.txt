 Two-vehicle-collison in Widnes Time of Incident: 13/09/2019 - 07:58 Location: Leigh Avenue, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Penketh and one from Widnes Crews attended to a road traffic collision on Leigh Avenue in Widnes involving two cars.  No one was trapped inside the vehicle and one woman was handed into the care of paramedics.  The vehicles were then made safe by firefighters.
