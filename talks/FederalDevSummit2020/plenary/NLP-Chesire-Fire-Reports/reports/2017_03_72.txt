Car on fire on car park in Chester Time of Incident: 20/03/2017 - 23:15 Location: Station Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane A car which was well alight on a car park was extinguished with a hose reel. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and police were requested to attend. If anyone has any information about who may have started this fire they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
