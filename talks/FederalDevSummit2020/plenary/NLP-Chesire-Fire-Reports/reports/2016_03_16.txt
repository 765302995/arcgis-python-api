Fire in doorway of shopping centre in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/03/2016 - 15:12 Location: Old Market Place, Bewsey and Whitecross, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A fire involving a doorway was extinguished by a sprinkler system. Firefighters liaised with centre staff and following a fire investigation this fire is believed to have been started by a discarded cigarette
