House fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 23/03/2015 - 19:41 Location: Sunningdale Close, Winsford. Attendance: Fire engines from Winsford, Northwich and Middlewich Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Sunningdale Close, Winsford.  When they arrived they found that the blaze involved a dishwasher. Crews wearing breathing apparatus used hose reel and main water jets to tackle the fire.  There were no reports of any injuries but crews carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving the scene.
