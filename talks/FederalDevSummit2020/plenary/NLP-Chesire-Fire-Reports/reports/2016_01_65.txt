Two car collision Time of Incident: 15/01/2016 - 17:14 Location: High Street, Runcorn. Attendance: Fire engines from Widnes and Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on High Street, Runcorn.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two cars. There was a man and a woman who required first aid at the scene. The woman was then taken to hospital.
