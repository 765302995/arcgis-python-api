 Horse box fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 23/05/2019 - 14:42 Location: Cross O Th Hill Road, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Fire crews were called to a horse box fire on Cross O Th Hill Road, Malpas. On arrival the crew found the horse box well alight and that the grass around the box had also set on fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.
