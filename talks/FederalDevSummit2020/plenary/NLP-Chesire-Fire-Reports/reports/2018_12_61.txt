 Vehicle fire in Great Sutton Time of Incident: 16/12/2018 - 16:29 Location: Mill Lane, Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 4.30pm firefighters from Ellesmere Port were called to reports of a vehicle fire on Mill Lane in Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew found a saloon car well alight. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
