Rubbish on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 08/08/2017 - 16:13 Location: Linkway, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Approximately five metres of rubbish which was on fire under a tree required firefighters to attend and extinguish the flames with a hose reel. It is believed that this fire may have been started deliberately. Please call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 if you know anything about who may have started it.
