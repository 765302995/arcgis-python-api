Cardboard compactor fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/02/2017 - 06:54 Location: Skyline Drive, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh A fire in a 10m x 2m cardboard compactor was put out by firefighters. A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire. The crew is still on scene waiting for the waste management company to arrive.
