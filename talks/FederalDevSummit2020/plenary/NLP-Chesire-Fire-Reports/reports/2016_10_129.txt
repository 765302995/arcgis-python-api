Bin on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/10/2016 - 16:13 Location: Brick Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A bin which had been on fire inside a room but extinguished prior to the arrival of firefighters required the crew to use a large fan to remove smoke from the area. Police were requested to attended as it was believed that the fire may have been started deliberately. If anyone knows anything about this fire they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
