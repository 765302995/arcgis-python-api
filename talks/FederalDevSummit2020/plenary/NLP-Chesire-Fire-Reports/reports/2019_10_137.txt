 Smoke alarms sounding in Chester Time of Incident: 04/10/2019 - 13:56 Location: The Waltons, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly before 2pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke alarms sounding at a domestic property in The Waltons, Chester. On arrival the crew discovered that the alarms had been activated by cooking fumes. A man was assessed by paramedics and firefighters ventilated the property. Firefighters were in attendance for nearly an hour. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
