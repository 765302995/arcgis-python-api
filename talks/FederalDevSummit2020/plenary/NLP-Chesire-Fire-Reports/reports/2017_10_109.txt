Calf in canal in Northwich Time of Incident: 06/10/2017 - 01:03 Location: Marbury Lane, Anderton Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and the rope rescue unit and a supporting fire engine from Lymm and the animal rescue unit from Bollington A calf that had fallen into a canal and couldn't get out needed the help of the animal rescue unit team from Bollington. The calf which was approximately 12 months old, was rescued after two firefighters got into the water wearing swift water gear and got the animal out.
