People rescued from lift in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/01/2016 - 12:51 Location: Chester University Warrington Campus, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters rescued 10 people from a lift after it became stuck between the ground and the first floor. Equipment was used to open the doors and release the passengers. First aid was given to two of the people and paramedics attended.
