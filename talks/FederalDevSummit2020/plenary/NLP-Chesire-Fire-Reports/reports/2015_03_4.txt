Grill pan fire in Sutton Time of Incident: 29/03/2015 - 18:22 Location: Morton Drive, Sutton Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish a fire involving a grill pan in Sutton. A large fan was used to clear the smoke. Smoke alarms were fitted in the property and activated alerting the occupants to the fire.  Safety advice for grills
