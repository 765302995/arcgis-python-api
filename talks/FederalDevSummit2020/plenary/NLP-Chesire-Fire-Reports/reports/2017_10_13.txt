Tumble dryer fire in Chester Time of Incident: 27/10/2017 - 13:15 Location: Thirlmere Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port A tumble dryer fire on Thirlmere Road, Chester, was put out by firefighters. The crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They were on the scene for approximately 45 minutes.  Tumble dryer safety information
