Tractor fire in Cuddington Time of Incident: 05/09/2015 - 17:08 Location: Dutton Lane, Cuddington, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish a tractor fire.  The tractor also leaked diesel onto the road which was closed while the incident was dealt with.
