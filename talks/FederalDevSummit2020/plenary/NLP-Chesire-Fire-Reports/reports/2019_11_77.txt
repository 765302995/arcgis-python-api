 Fire involving dishwasher in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 14/11/2019 - 21:13 Location: Middlewich Road, Holmes Chapel Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Middlewich and one from Sandbach Crews attended to a fire involving a dishwasher at an address on Middlewich Road in Holmes Chapel.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews extinguished the flames using one hose reel and removed the dishwasher from the property. A covering jet was also in place.   A large fan was used to disperse the smoke.  Crews were in attendance for approximately one hour.
