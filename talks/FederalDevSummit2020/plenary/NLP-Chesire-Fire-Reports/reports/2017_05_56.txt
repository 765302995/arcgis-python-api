Swan rescued in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/05/2017 - 18:19 Location: Park Lane, Warrington Attendance: The water rescue boat from Warrington and the animal rescue team from Knutsford. Swan rescued in Warrington Firefighters were called to rescue a swan which had got trapped in fishing wire on Appleton Reservoir. The swift water team from Warrington and the animal rescue team from Knutsford worked together and used a swan bag to secure the bird and get it on to dry land and to the care of the RSPCA.
