 Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 14/07/2018 - 14:22 Location: Keswick Avenue, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one fire engine from Congleton attended. At around 2.20pm firefighters were called to a kitchen fire at Keswick Avenue. A fire engine from Congleton and a fire engine from Macclesfield attended and found a grill pan alight. Fire crews used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel and positive pressure ventilation fan to extinguish the fire  Keep safe at home.
