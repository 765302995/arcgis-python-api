 People trapped in a room at a school in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 16/05/2018 - 12:47 Location: Parkett Heyes Road, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At around 12.45pm firefighters were called to a report of people being locked in a room at a school on Parkett Heyes Road in Macclesfield. They gained access to the room via a window and then removed a lock. Firefighters cut the lock off to free those trapped inside the room. The crew were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
