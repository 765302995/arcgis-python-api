Car fire in Stockport Time of Incident: 13/05/2015 - 02:31 Location: Mudhurst Lane/Higher Lane, Disley, Stockport. Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton and two from Greater Manchester Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus extinguished a car fire using two hose reels in Stockport.  One saloon car was involved and well alight on arrival of the crews.
