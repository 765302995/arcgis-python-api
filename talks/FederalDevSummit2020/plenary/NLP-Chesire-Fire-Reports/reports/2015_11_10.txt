Wheelie bin on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/11/2015 - 06:08 Location: St Elphins Close, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath A man had to be rescued by firefighters after a wheelie bin was set on fire outside the front door of a house in Warrington.  The crews treated the man for smoke inhalation and handed him into the care of paramedics. They extinguished the fire wearing breathing apparatus, which involved a wheelie bin and the front door. A large fan was used to clear the smoke.  Police also attended the scene and a fire investigator was requested to attend.
