 Areas of grass smouldering at golf course in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 10:47 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 10.45am firefighters were called to reports of a fire alongside a footpath at St Michaels Jubilee golf course on Dundalk Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered a number of small areas of grass smouldering. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fires and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
