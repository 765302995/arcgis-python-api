 Vehicle collides with house in Chelford Time of Incident: 03/02/2018 - 16:28 Location: Wheat Moss, Chelford Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to Chelford following reports of a vehicle having collided with the front of a house on Wheat Moss. Firefighters made thorough checks of the building to ensure it was safe and assisted in the removal of the vehicle. There were no injuries and crews were on scene for just under one hour.
