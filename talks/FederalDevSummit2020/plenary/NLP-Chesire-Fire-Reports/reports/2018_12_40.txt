 Fire in the open in Middlewich Time of Incident: 20/12/2018 - 22:40 Location: Queen Street, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich At around 10.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Queen Street in Middlewich. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
