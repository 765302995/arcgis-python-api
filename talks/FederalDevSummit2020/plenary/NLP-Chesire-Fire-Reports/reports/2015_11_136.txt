Skip fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/11/2015 - 21:35 Location: Eccleston Avenue, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of fire off Eccleston Avenue, Ellesmere Port.  When crews arrived they found that the fire involved a skip. Fire crews wearing breathing apparatus tackled the fire with water jets.
