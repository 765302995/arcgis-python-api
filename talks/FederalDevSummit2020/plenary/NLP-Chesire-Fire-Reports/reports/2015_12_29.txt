Collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/12/2015 - 15:25 Location: Golborne Road, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Golborne Road, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found a two vehicle road traffic collision involving three people with one person trapped.  Crews freed the casualty and they were passed to the care of the ambulance service.   Firefighters were at the scene for just under one hour.
