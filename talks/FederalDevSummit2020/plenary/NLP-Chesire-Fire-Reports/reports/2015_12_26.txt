Collision in Frodsham Time of Incident: 24/12/2015 - 18:28 Location: Lower Rake Lane, Frodsham. Attendance: Fire engines from Frodsham and Runcorn Firefighters responded to a collision on Lower Rake Lane, Frodsham.  When they arrived they found a car on its side. Crews helped a woman from the car and passed her to the care of the ambulance service.
