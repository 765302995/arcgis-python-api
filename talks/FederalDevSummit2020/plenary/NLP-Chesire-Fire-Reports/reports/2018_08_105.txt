 Tree and bush fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/08/2018 - 13:21 Location: Red Lane, Appleton in Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters attended to reports of a fire on Red Lane in Appleton involving trees and bushes.  One main jet, one hose reel jet, beaters and small tools were used to help crews put out the fire.
