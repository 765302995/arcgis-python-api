Fire at recycling plant in Widnes Time of Incident: 14/01/2015 - 18:27 Location: Pickerings Road, Halebank, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes and two from Runcorn Firefighters extinguished a fire in a recycling plant in Widnes. The building was evacuated and firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to put out the blaze. A ground monitor was put in place and thermal imaging camera used to check for hotspots.  The waste was removed outside using a digger and the crews damped down at the scene before handing the incident over to the company.
