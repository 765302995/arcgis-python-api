 Fire in a garden in Poynton Time of Incident: 02/08/2018 - 16:27 Location: Oakfield Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton At around 4.25pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Poynton. On arrival at Oakfield Road the crew discovered a quantity of garden waste on fire in a back garden. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
