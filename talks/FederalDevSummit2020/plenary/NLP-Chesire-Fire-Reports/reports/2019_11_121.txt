 Road traffic collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 07/11/2019 - 15:30 Location: Ditton Road (West), Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Widnes and one from Runcorn Crews attended to a road traffic collision involving two cars (Ford Focus and a Peugeot) on Ditton Road in Widnes.  Three people were extricated from the cars by firefighters who forced open the doors.  One man was handed into the care of paramedics with an injury to his shoulder but was not taken to hospital.  Crews remained at the scene for 50 minutes.
