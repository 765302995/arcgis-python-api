Small fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/03/2015 - 16:50 Location: Regent Street, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters tackled a fire in the garden of a property on Regent Street, Ellesmere Port.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
