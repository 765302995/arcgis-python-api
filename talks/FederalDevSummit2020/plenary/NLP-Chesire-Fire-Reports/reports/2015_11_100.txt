Road traffic collision on M6 Southbound exit slip of Junction 21 Time of Incident: 10/11/2015 - 10:07 Location: M6 southbound between junctions 21 and 20, near Lymm Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Fire and rescue crews were called to a road traffic collision on the M6 southbound, between junctions 21 and 20. The incident involved a car and a van. One casualty was trapped in the car and was released by firefighters using specialist equipment.
