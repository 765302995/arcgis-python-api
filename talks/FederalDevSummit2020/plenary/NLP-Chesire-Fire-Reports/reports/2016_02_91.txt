Motorbike on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/02/2016 - 00:33 Location: Cherry Sutton,Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A motorcycle on a grass verge was well alight when firefighters were called. The crew used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and alerted police to the incident which is believed to be suspicious.  Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
