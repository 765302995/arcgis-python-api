Grill pan fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 23/12/2015 - 11:58 Location: Leigh Green Close, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes A fire involving a grill pan caused crews to be called to a property in Borrowdale Road. Firefighters used a fire blanket to put out the fire and a high pressure fan to clear smoke from the kitchen.  Always follow our safety advice - Grills
