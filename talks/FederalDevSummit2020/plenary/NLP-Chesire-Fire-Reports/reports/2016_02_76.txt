Two derelict caravans set on fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 13/02/2016 - 14:08 Location: Lostock Hall Road, Poynton Attendance: A fire engine from Poynton and a fire engine from Offerton (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service)  Two derelict caravans were well alight on Lostock Hall Road in Poynton. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to bring the fire under control. Two firefighters wore two breathing apparatus.  Firefighters checked two gas cylinders, which were close to the caravans, using a thermal imaging camera to see if the cylinders had been affected by the fire.   The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
