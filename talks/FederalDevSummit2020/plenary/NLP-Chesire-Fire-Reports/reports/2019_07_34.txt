 Collision in Bridgemere Time of Incident: 24/07/2019 - 16:07 Location: Bridgemere Lane, Bridgemere. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe. Firefighters were called to a two vehicle collision in Bridgemere Lane in Bridgemere. A female casualty was trapped in the vehicle. Crews used cutting equipment to take the roof off and the casualty was taken to hospital by air ambulance.
