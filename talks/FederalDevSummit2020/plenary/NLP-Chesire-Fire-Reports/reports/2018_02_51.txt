 Unused building fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 18/02/2018 - 16:07 Location: Hazelbadge Road, Poynton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton and one fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters from Poynton and Macclesfield were called to reports of a fire at a unused school in Poynton. On arrival at the scene crews found a small fire in a shed in the grounds of the school which was extinguished using water.
