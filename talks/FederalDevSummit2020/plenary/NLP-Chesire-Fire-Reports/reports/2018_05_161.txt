 Road traffic collision on M6 northbound between junctions 18 and 19 Time of Incident: 11/05/2018 - 07:16 Location: M6 northbound at Knutsford Service Station Attendance: Four fire engines from Crewe, Holmes Chapel and Winsford (2) One casualty was trapped in a car following a road traffic collision on the slip road at Knutsford Service Station. Firefighters removed the roof using specialist cutting tools to release the casualty.Two other people in the car were able to leave the vehicle themselves. All three were left in the care of paramedics. The slip road was closed while firefighters dealt with the incident. They were at the scene for approximately one hour.
