 Grass on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 18:15 Location: Old Coach Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn attended At around 6.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a grass fire on Old Coach Road in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved around 20 square metres of grass. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and then damped the area down to make it safe. They were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
