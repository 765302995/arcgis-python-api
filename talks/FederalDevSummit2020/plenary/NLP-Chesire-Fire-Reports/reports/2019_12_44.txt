 Fire involving a gas cylinder at a domestic property in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/12/2019 - 15:25 Location: Dale Close, Ditton, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly before 3.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a domestic property in Dale Close, Ditton, Widnes. The small fire involving a gas cylinder was out on arrival. Firefighters administered first aid to someone who had been injured as a result of the fire. They later handed them over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for more than 15 minutes.
