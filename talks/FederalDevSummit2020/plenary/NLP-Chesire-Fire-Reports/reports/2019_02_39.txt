 Flat fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/02/2019 - 08:53 Location: Redshank Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Lymm Fire crews were called to a fire in the living room area of a flat on Redshank Lane in Warrington.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, crews used one hose reel to extinguish the fire and a positive pressure ventilation fan to clear the smoke.  The occupants inside the flat were checked over by paramedics and attended hospital as a precautionary measure for smoke inhalation.  Crews were on scene for one hour and twenty minutes.
