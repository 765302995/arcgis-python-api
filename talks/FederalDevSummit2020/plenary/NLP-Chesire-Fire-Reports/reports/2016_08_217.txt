Outbuilding fire in Rode Heath Time of Incident: 03/08/2016 - 01:25 Location: Sandbach Road, Rode Heath Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and one from Staffordshire Fire and Rescue Service Two fire engines were called to a report of an outbuilding on fire in a field off Sandbach Road. There were two caravans inside the building. Firefighters used hose reel jets and a main jet to bring the blaze under control. LPG cylinders inside the building were cooled. Police were also asked to attend.
