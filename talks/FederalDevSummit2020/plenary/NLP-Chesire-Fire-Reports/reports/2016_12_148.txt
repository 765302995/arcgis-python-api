Small vehicle fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/12/2016 - 14:02 Location: Winwick Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and Birchwood Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving a vehicle, which was leaking fuel and smoke from under the bonnet. One person was taken to hospital before crews used environmental grab packs to mop up fuel left on the road.
