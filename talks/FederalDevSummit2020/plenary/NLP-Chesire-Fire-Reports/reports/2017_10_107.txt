Barn conversion on fire, Allostock Time of Incident: 06/10/2017 - 18:33 Location: Holmes Chapel Road, Allostock, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford Fire crews were mobilised to a barn under conversion on fire on Holmes Chapel Road, Allostock, Northwich. Firefighters used one hose reel, breathing apparatus and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire. One casualty was treated at the scene by paramedics for smoke inhalation.
