House fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 11/07/2015 - 16:01 Location: Audley Road, Alsager. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Audley Road, Alsager.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving a gas barbecue cylinder in a downstairs bathroom.  Crews wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel and main water jet to tackle the fire. They also used a large fan to clear the smoke.   Smoke alarms were fitted and activated but crews also carried out an Home Fire Safety Assessment.
