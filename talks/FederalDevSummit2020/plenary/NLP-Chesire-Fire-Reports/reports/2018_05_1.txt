Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 30/05/2018 - 12:24 Location: Bishops Gardens, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port and one fire engine attended from Chester. Firefighters from Chester and Ellesmere Port attended reports of a Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port. On arrival at the scene there was no response from the occupant so the team forced entry into the address. On entering there was no sign of a fire, but the property was smoke logged due to unattended cooking. The occupant was found asleep in the living room, she was passed to the case of the ambulance service as a precautionary measure. The team were in attendance for approximately 40 minutes.
