Chimney fire in bickerton Time of Incident: 01/01/2016 - 17:09 Location: Coppermine Lane, Bickerton Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas A chimney fire was well alight at a farm in Bickerton.  Firefighters removed the log burner to gain full access to the flue. They used chimney rods to extinguish the fire.  Chimney fire safety tips
