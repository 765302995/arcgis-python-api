 Washing machine fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/03/2019 - 14:41 Location: Church Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Fire crews attended to a fire at a house on Church Lane in Warrington involving a washing machine.  Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, used one hose reel to extinguish the fire.  A positive pressure ventilation fan was used to clear the smoke.  Firefighters dampened down the area and check for hot spots before removing the washing machine and tumble dryer from the utility room.
