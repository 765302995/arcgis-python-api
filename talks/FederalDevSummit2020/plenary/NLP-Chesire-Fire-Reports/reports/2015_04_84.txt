Apartment fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 19/04/2015 - 16:08 Location: Canal Street, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes A fire at an apartment in Runcorn was caused by an e-cigarette charger.  Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish the blaze and a large fan to clear the smoke. Smoke alarms were fitted at the property and did activate.
