 Bungalow fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 04/10/2018 - 10:40 Location: Cheyne Walk, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich and two from Crewe Bungalow fire in NantwichAt 10.40am firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a bungalow on Cheyne Walk in Nantwich.  Crews attended the scene and found that the fire was well alight and had spread to the roof area of the property.  Two people were in the home at the time but managed to evacuate themselves from the property.  Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish out the fire.  They then stripped the tiles from the roof and used a thermal imaging camera to check for ant hot spots, while continuing to use one hose reel to extinguish the areas which are still hot and could reignite.  Crews were in attendance at the scene for approximately three hours. The fire is believed to have been accidental and caused by an electrical fire in the loft.
