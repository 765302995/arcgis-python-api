Outbuilding fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 07/04/2017 - 03:14 Location: Tarporley Road, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine each from Winsford, Northwich and Warrington A single storey outbuilding was involved in fire at a property on Tarporley Road. Firefighters, two in breathing apparatus, tackled the blaze in the building, which measured approximately eight metres by four metres, using two hose reel jets and a covering jet.    Once the fire was out they continued to damp down and check the temperature with a thermal imaging camera.
