Bin compound fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/11/2015 - 22:55 Location: Rosam Court, Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Rosam Court, Runcorn.  When they arrived they found that there was a fire in a bin compound. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.
