 Smell of smoke at a house in Crewe Time of Incident: 18/12/2018 - 20:52 Location: Timbrell Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At around 8pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from an airing cupboard at a house on Timbrell Avenue in Crewe. Crews attended, isolated the central heating and carried out a thorough inspection of the cupboard under the stairs and of the loft space. Firefighters used a short extension ladder to inspect the loft and a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. They could find no signs of fire and were satisfied that the property was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
