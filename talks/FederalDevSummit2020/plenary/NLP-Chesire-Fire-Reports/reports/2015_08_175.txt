Woman rescued from Congleton embankment fall Time of Incident: 04/08/2015 - 14:59 Location: Brookhouse Lane, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine and the rope rescue unit with supporting fire engine Firefighters assisted police and ambulance with the rescue of a lady who had fallen down an embankment. The casualty had fallen around three metres down the embankment in Brookhouse Lane, Congleton. She was rescued by the emergency services using height safety rescue equipment and taken to hospital by air ambulance.
