 Smoke at a flat in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/04/2019 - 16:27 Location: Arena Gardens, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 4.30pm firefighters were called to reports of smoke at a flat in Arena Gardens, Warrington. On arrival at the flat the crew discovered cooking fumes. There were no signs of fire. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the cooking fumes and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
