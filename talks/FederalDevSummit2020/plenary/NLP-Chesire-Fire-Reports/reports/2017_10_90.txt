Electrical fire in Adlington Time of Incident: 11/10/2017 - 11:23 Location: Sugar Lane, Adlington Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Fire crews were called to a small electrical fire in a cavity wall of a house on Sugar Lane, Adlington. Crews had to cut away part of the wall and used a water fire extinguisher to put out the fire.
