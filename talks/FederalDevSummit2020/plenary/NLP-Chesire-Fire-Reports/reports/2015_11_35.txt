Motorbike on fire in Weaverham Time of Incident: 23/11/2015 - 00:07 Location: Hazel Drive, Weaverham Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich A motorbike was completely destroyed in a fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze and asked police to attend as it is believed the fire was started deliberately. Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
