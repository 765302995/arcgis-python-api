 Fire at an end terraced property in Northwich Time of Incident: 02/05/2018 - 13:25 Location: Poplar Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Northwich and one from Penketh At 1.25pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a utility room at an end terraced property on Poplar Road in Weaverham, Northwich. On arrival crews discovered smoke come from the ground floor and that all persons were accounted for. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus were rapidly deployed to use a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Once the fire was out crews used a large high-pressure fan to clear the smoke from the property. The fire is currently being investigated but it is believed to have been caused by a gas handheld blow torch. Firefighters were in attendance for nearly an hour.
