Road traffic collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 07/02/2017 - 10:20 Location: M6 north between junctions 18 and 19 Attendance: Fire engines from Holmes Chapel, Sandbach and Middlewich Firefighters were called to a man trapped following a road traffic collision involving a heavy goods vehicle. Lanes one and two were closed while the incident was dealt with. The crew used cutting equipment to release the casualty from a vehicle and handed him into the care of paramedics.
