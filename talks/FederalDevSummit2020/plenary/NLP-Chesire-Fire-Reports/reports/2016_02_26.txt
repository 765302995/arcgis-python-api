Kitchen fire in Neston Time of Incident: 24/02/2016 - 17:34 Location: Bridge Court in Neston. Attendance: A fire engine from Neston Firefighters were called to an alarm sounding at a property on Bridge Court in Neston.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire in the kitchen. This was caused by a chopping board left on a cooker. The fire was out but a Home Safety Assessment was carried out before crews left the scene.
