Road traffic collision on the Weston Point Expressway in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/07/2017 - 20:58 Location: Weston Point Expressway, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called out to a road traffic collision on the Weston Point Expressway, Runcorn. On arrival they found that there had been a collision between two cars, but there was no fire and nobody was trapped. Firefighters made the vehicles safe. Police also attended this incident.
