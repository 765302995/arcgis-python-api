 Several small fires at Widnes golf course Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 20:49 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 8.50pm firefighters were called to reports of fires on St Michaels Jubilee golf course on Dundalk Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered several small fires. Firefighters used water and beaters to extinguish the fires, which are believed to have been started deliberately, and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. Anyone with information about the fires should call police on 101.
