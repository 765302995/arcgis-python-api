 Collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/10/2018 - 07:27 Location: Hillock Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Warrington Crews attended to a collision involving two cars on Hillock Lane in Warrington.  One woman was able to self extract herself from the vehicle and nobody else involved was trapped.  Firefighters then stayed at the scene to make sure it was safe.
