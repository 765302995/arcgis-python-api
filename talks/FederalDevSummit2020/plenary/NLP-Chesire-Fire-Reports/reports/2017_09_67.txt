Road traffic collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 11/09/2017 - 21:35 Location: Rivacre Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Two vehicles were involved in a collision on Rivacre Road. There were three casualties, one of whom, a man, was trapped in one of the cars. He was released by firefighters and handed into the care of paramedics. All the casualties were taken by ambulance to the Countess of Chester Hospital. The crews made the two vehicles safe.
