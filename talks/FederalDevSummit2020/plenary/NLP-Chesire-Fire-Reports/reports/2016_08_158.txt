Hay field fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 06/08/2016 - 16:29 Location: Chelford Road, Ollerton, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Macclesfield A field of hay was on fire in Ollerton, near Knutsford. The field measured around 100 metres by 100 metres and was well alight. Firefighters used beaters and a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control. Police were asked to attend to help with traffic management as a lot of smoke was blowing across the road. Crews were at the incident for just under two and a half hours.
