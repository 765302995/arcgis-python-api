Van on fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 14/07/2016 - 15:56 Location: M6 north between junctions 20 and 21 Attendance: Fire engines from Stockton Heath and Birchwood Firefighters were called to a large van on fire on the M6 and used one hose reel to extinguish the flames. Lane one of the motorway was closed while the incident was dealt with.
