Bedroom fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 21/06/2017 - 18:26 Location: Shaw Drive, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a fire in a bedroom on the first floor of a semi detached property. All persons were accounted for and the crews extinguished the fire. Nearby properties were checked by the crews and one man and a woman were treated for smoke inhalation. A large fan was used to remove smoke from the property. Paramedics also attended.
