 Fire in the open in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 29/08/2019 - 18:53 Location: Overpool Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly before 7pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open near to a park on Overpool Road in Ellesmere Port. The crew used water to extinguished the small fire and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
