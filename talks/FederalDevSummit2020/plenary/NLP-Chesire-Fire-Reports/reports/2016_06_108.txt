Flooding in Poynton Time of Incident: 11/06/2016 - 14:59 Location: Poolhouse Road in Poynton. Attendance: A fire engine from Poynton Firefighters were called to a report of flooding at a property on Poolhouse Road in Poynton.  Crews used a boom to divert water from flowing into the property. They also turned off the gas and electrics. A Light portable pump was also used to clear some of the water.
