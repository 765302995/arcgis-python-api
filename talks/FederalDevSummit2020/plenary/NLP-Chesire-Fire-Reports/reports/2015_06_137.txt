Batteries on fire in a garage in High Legh Time of Incident: 08/06/2015 - 17:22 Location: Surridge, High Legh Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Altrincham A fire involving batteries in a garage was out on arrival of the Service.  The crews isolated the gas and electricity supply to the house and checked for fire spread, monitoring the temperatures in the property.  Smoke detectors were fitted and did activate and the firefighters carried out a home safety assessment. They were at the scene for around one hour 30 minutes.
