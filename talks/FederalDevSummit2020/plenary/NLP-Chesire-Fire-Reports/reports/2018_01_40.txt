 Chimney fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 21/01/2018 - 21:30 Location: Middlewich Road, Allostock, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At 9.30pm on Sunday 21 January firefighters were called to reports of a chimney fire in Allostock, near Northwich.  On arrival at the scene crews used a thermal imaging camera to locate the source of the fire before using specialist chimney rods to extinguish the fire.
