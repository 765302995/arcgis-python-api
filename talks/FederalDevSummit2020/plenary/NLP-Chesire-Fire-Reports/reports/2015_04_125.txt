Tyres on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 14/04/2015 - 20:03 Location: Shellway Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters used one hose reel to extinguish a fie involving tyres in Ellesmere Port.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and police were informed and attended the scene.  Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
