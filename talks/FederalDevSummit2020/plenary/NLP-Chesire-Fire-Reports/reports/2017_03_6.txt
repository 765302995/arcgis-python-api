Road traffic collision in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 29/03/2017 - 19:08 Location: Burleyhurst Lane, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one from Wythenshawe (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Two cars were involved in a collision on Burleyhurst Lane. The crews assisted paramedics with two casualties, removing the door from one of the vehicles so a casualty could get out, and made the scene safe. The road was closed in both directions during the incident.
