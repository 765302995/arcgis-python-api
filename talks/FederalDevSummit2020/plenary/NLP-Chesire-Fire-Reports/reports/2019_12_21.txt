 Car fire, Great Warford Time of Incident: 06/12/2019 - 22:30 Location: Merrymans Lane, Great Warford Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to initial reports of a building on fire in Merrymans Lane. They arrived to find it was, in fact, a saloon car that was on fire. It was well alight and they used a single hose reel to put the fire out. A fire investigation established that the car had been deliberately set alight, and a home safety investigation was carried out a the property before the crew left.
