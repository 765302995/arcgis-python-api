 Van fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 18/10/2019 - 17:18 Location: Cloverdale Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield. Firefighters attended a van fire in Cloverdale Road in Macclesfield. Crews used a hose reel jet and breathing apparatus to cool the van down which appeared to have overheated.  Crews then discovered smoke issuing from the footwell and cut the area away to access the source of the smoke while continuing to cool the affected areas down. they continued to check the temperature using a thermal imaging camera.
