 Road traffic collision in Walgherton Time of Incident: 03/10/2018 - 18:26 Location: London Road, Walgherton Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters attended to a road traffic collision on London Road in Walgherton involving two cars.  Nobody was trapped inside the cars but crews made the vehicles and the scene safe and the drivers of both vehicles were handed into the care of paramedics.
