 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/09/2019 - 03:34 Location: Marsh Lane crossroad, Lower Whitley, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn, Firefighters were called to a car on fire in Marsh Lane crossroads, Lower Whitley, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately.
