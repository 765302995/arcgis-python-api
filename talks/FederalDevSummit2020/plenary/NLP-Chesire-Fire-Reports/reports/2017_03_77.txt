Detached house on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 19/03/2017 - 22:13 Location: Woodford Lane West, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford, one from Middlewich and the aerial appliance from Chester Firefighters were called to a large two storey detached house on fire. Two main water jets were used along with a hose reel to extinguish the fire. The hydraulic platform was used to get water to the building and police attended to cordon off the area. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check that the fire was out before crews left.  Update at 13:45 on March 20 This house was in the process of being built when the fire started.  Following a fire investigation it is believed that the fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information about how this fire may have started they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
