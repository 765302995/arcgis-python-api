 River rescue in Congleton Time of Incident: 05/05/2018 - 20:56 Location: Havannah Street, Congleton. Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton. One fire engine from Warrington. One fire engine from Lymm. Firefighters were called to the River Dane near Havannah Street in Congleton following reports of a dog and a teenage girl in the water. The dog, girl and a teenage boy were out of the water on arrival and at the bottom of a 20 metre, steep embankment. Firefighters carried out a line rescue to help all three to safety and handed the teenagers over to paramedics to make sure they were okay.
