 Carpet fire outside domestic property in Crewe Time of Incident: 23/07/2018 - 01:07 Location: West Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe attended At around 1.05am firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving carpet outset a domestic property in West Avenue, Crewe. On arrival crews discovered that the fire had affected a bay window and that smoke had entered the property. No-one was inside the property. A cordon was set up and firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus then searched the property for any signs of fire. Crews then damped the area down to make sure it was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for more than an hour. The carpet was left outside the property following renovation work and the fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiriesor contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
