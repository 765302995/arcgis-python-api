Ellesmere Port shed fire Time of Incident: 16/06/2015 - 15:29 Location: Hope Farm Road, Great Sutton Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Firefighters have been tackling a fire involving two sheds belonging to adjacent properties.  Crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze.  The cause of the fire is being investigated.
