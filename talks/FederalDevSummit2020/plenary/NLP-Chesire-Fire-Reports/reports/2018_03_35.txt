 Outbuilding building fire in Adlington Time of Incident: 25/03/2018 - 18:17 Location: London Road, Adlington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton. One fire engine from Macclesfield. Outbuilding building fire in Adlington  Firefighters attended an outbuilding fire at an address on London Road, Adlington. On arrival crews found the fire to be involving an external log store. An adjacent shipping container had also been affected by the heat.  Crews used one hose reel, one main jet and two breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots.
