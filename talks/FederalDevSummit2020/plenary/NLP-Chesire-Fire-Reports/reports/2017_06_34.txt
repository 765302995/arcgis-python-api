Recycling bin on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/06/2017 - 23:28 Location: Tesco Extra, Winwick Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A fire involving a large recycling bin filled with rubbish which was well alight, required firefighters at attend and extinguish it with a hose reel. It is believed that this fire may have been started deliberately. If anyone knows anything about this fire then they should call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
