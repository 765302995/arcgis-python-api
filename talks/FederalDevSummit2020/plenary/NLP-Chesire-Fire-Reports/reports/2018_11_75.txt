 Bin fire, Crewe Time of Incident: 17/11/2018 - 14:42 Location: Eaton Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters responded to reports of rubbish on fire near a commercial premises in Eaton Street. On arrival they found a rubbish bin which was well alight, so they used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames.
