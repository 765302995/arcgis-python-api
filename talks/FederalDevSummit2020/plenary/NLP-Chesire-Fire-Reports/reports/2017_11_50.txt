Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/11/2017 - 20:24 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters used water to extinguish several wheelie bins which had been deliberately set on fire on Grasmere Avenue, Warrington. Anyone with information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
