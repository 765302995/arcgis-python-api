Van fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 20/07/2017 - 23:52 Location: Victoria Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A fire involving a van was put out by firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, using a hose reel jet. Police were asked to attend as it is suspected that the fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 101 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 19 of 21 July.
