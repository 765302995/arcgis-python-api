 Overturned lorry on the M6 Time of Incident: 25/11/2019 - 12:02 Location: M6 southbound, between junctions 19 and 18 Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Birchwood, one from Middlewich, one from Knutsford and one from Trafford in Greater Manchester At around noon firefighters were called to reports of a one-vehicle collision on the M6.  On arrival they discovered that the vehicle, a lorry, was on its side and that no-one was trapped inside it.  Highways England closed lanes one and two of the motorway whilst emergency services dealt with the incident.  Firefighters made the overturned lorry safe and tended to the driver, before handing them over to the care of paramedics.  Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
