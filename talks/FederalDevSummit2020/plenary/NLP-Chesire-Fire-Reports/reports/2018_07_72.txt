 Conifers and fence fire in Birchwood Time of Incident: 23/07/2018 - 15:58 Location: Kelsall Close, Birchwood Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters extinguished three conifers and fence which had caught fire on Kelsall Close in Birchwood.  Crews used one hose reel to put it out.
