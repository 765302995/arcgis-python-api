Garage on fire at property in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 03/11/2017 - 23:42 Location: Park Crescent, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle On Friday, 3 November at 11:42pm one fire engine from Wilmslow and one fire engine from Cheadle fire stations attended to reports of a fire involving a garage from a domestic property on Park Crescent in Wilmslow. Fire crews used four breathing apparatus, two hose reels and one ultra-high pressure lance. Crews were in attendance for just under two hours.
