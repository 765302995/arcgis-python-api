 Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 01/05/2018 - 00:14 Location: North Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a car well alight on a disused railway line off North Road, Ellesmere Port. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. The incident was reported to police.
