Car and garage fire in Chester Time of Incident: 27/05/2017 - 20:36 Location: Cambridge Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Powey Lane Crews were called to a car fire in a garage, with the fire spreading to the garage doors. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control.  They were at the scene for around ninety minutes. An investigation into the cause of the fire is being carried out.
