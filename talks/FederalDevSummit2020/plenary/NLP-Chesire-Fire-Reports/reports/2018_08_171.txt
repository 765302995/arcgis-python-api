 Small grass fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/08/2018 - 17:12 Location: Halton View Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 5.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a grass fire at The Bongs on Halton View Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered a small grass fire, which firefighters extinguished with water. They were in attendance for around 15 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
