 Cyclists trapped by fallen tree in Smallwood Time of Incident: 11/10/2018 - 06:55 Location: Brookhouse Lane, Smallwood Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters were called to reports of two cyclists trapped under a fallen tree on Brookhouse Lane in Smallwood.  Crews helped remove the tree and foliage off the cyclists and handed them into the care of the ambulance service.   .
