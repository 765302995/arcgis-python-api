 Dangerous structure in Middlewich Time of Incident: 04/06/2019 - 20:06 Location: Rushton Drive, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Shortly after 8pm firefighters were called to reports of a dangerous structure in Middlewich.  On arrival the crew discovered that a garden wall had partially collapsed onto a car at the rear of an address on Rushton Drive.  Firefighters removed the damaged vehicle to assess the damage and the scene was then passed to the occupier.
