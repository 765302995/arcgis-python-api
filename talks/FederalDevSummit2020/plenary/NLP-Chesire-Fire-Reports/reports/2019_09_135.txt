 Loft window hanging precariously in Nantwich Time of Incident: 05/09/2019 - 12:52 Location: Pillory Street, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called an address in Pillory Street in Nantwich after someone spotted a loft window was hanging precariously from a loft window space.  Crews cordoned off the footpath and used a 13.5 metre ladder to remove the window and placed it inside the flat.
