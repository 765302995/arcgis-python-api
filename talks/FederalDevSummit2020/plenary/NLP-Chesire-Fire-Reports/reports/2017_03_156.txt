Road traffic collision on M56 eastbound between junctions 15 and 14 Time of Incident: 03/03/2017 - 19:46 Location: M56 eastbound between junctions 15 and 14 Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane A car and a van were involved in a road traffic collision on the M56 at Thornton-le-Moors. Nobody was trapped and firefighters made the scene safe.
