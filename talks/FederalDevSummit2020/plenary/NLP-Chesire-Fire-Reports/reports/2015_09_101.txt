Wheelie bin on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 11/09/2015 - 11:25 Location: Chesterfield Close, winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford A fire involving a wheelie bin in Winsford was extinguished by firefighters using a hose reel. The fire affected the exterior of a house and a car. The fire is believed to have been started accidentally.  Wheelie Bin - Fire Safety advice
