 Vehicle fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/04/2019 - 20:37 Location: Northdale Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters attended to a car on fire on Northdale Road in Warrington.  Crews established the fire was contained within the engine. They used dry powder to put out the fire then all traces of hot spots were dampened down using a hose reel jet. It was then checked over using a thermal imaging camera.  The fire is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault.
