 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/07/2018 - 17:40 Location: Harrogate Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one from Lymm Fire crews were called to a kitchen fire on Harrogate Close, Warrington. The fire involved a deep fat fryer which had an electrical fault. One hose reel jet was used to put out the fire. Crews were in attendance for 25 minutes.
