Kitchen fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 03/03/2016 - 18:52 Location: Wharton Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters used a large fan to clear smoke from a property following a small fire on the top of the cooker.  The fire involved clothes that were on the top of the cooker.  The fire was already out when the fire crews arrived, but the kitchen was filled with smoke. Firefighters requested an ambulance for one female casualty who was suffering from smoke inhalation.
