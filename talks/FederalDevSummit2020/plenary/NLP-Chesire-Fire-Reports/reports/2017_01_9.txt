Car fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/01/2017 - 23:58 Location: Access road for Daresbury Park Hotel (off Redbrow Lane), Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Stockton Heath A hose reel jet was used to put out a car fire near the Daresbury Park Hotel.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone has any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers 0800 555 111.
