Large quantity of cardboard on fire on car park in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/11/2017 - 19:44 Location: Wilson Patten Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh A large quantity of stacked cardboard was reported well alight on a car park near a retail store on Wilson Patten Street in Bewsey and Whitecross, Warrington. Fire crews attended and used one hose reel and a stack drag to separate the piles of the material.
