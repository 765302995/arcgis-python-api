Electrical fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 12/01/2017 - 00:48 Location: High Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to the scene after reports of loud banging and flashes come from a hole in the road, where road works were. Crews discovered that this had been caused by electrical cables submerged in water. After cordoning off the area, crews stood by with dry powder before making plans to have the cables isolated.
