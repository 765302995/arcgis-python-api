 Toaster fire in Chester Time of Incident: 07/06/2019 - 14:40 Location: Auckland Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At 2.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a toaster at a domestic property on Auckland Road in Chester. On arrival the crew found the toaster well alight and that everyone was accounted for. Firefighters isolated the electricity supply to the toaster. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Once the fire was out the crew used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
