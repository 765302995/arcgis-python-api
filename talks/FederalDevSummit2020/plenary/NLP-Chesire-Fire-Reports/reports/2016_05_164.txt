Cooking fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 02/05/2016 - 15:17 Location: Wheelock Street, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich, one from Winsford and one from Northwich One person was taken to hospital after cooking caused a fire at a property in Middlewich. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire. A high pressure fan was then used to clear the property of smoke. The casualty, a woman, suffered smoke inhalation.  To avoid cooking fires please follow our safety advice.
