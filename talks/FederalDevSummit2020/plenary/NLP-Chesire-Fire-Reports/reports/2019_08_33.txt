 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/08/2019 - 13:23 Location: Warrington Road, Risley, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Shortly before 1.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire in Risley, Warrington. On arrival a firefighters found the saloon car well alight on Warrington Road. A safety cordon was set up and a firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The car was then damped down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for nearly an hour.
