Fire at a Chester pub Time of Incident: 28/03/2015 - 08:38 Location: Warrington Road, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at the Shrewsbury Arms on Warrington Road.  When they arrived crews found the kitchen filled with smoke that was coming from an overheated tumble dryer. Firefighters used large fans to clear the smoke.
