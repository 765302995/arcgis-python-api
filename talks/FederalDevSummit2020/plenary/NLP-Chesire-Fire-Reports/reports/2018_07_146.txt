 Car fire in Chelford Time of Incident: 13/07/2018 - 11:46 Location: Chelford roundabout, Chelford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Car fire in ChelfordFirefighters were called to reports of a Landrover on fire near to the Chelford roundabout.  They used two hose reels and breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire and a thermal imaging camera to check for any hot spots.  They were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
