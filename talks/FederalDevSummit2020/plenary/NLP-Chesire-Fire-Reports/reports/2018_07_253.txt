 Commercial building fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 04/07/2018 - 02:58 Location: West Bank Street, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Widnes and one from Runcorn At around 2.55am firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a commercial building at Queensway Business Centre in West Bank Street, Widnes. On arrival the crews discovered the front door of the single storey building on fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and forced entry into the approximately 10 metres by 20 metres building. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus then searched the building. Crews isolated the building's gas and electricity supply. Smoke from the fire had spread to another unit. Firefighters used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from both units. They also checked for hotspots using a thermal imaging camera and were in attendance for around three hours. The fire is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault.
