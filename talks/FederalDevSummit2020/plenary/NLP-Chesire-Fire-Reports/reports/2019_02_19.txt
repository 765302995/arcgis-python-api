 Small fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 25/02/2019 - 16:34 Location: Macclesfield Road, Alderley Edge. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield. One fire engine from Cheadle. Firefighters were called to a smouldering fire in an empty building in Macclesfield Road, Alderley Edge. The fire was left unattended and  crews used a hose reel jet to ensure it was out and couldn't spread.
