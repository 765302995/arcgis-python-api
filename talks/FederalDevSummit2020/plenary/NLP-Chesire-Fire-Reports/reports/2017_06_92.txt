Car collides with lamp post in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/06/2017 - 21:14 Location: Broseley Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Leigh (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Crews made the scene safe after a car had collided with a lamp post on Broseley Lane. The lamp post was left in a precarious position so the council was asked to attend.
