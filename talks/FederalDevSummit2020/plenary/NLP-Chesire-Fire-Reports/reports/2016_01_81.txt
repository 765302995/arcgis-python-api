Flat fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 08/01/2016 - 21:31 Location: Roe Street in Macclesfield Attendance: A fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters responded to an alarm sounding Roe Street in Macclesfield.  When they arrived crews confirmed that the alarm was coming from a basement flat. Crews and the police were told that there was a man in the flat but there was no answer. Forced entry took place and crews rescued a man who was suffering from smoke inhalation. He was passed to the care of the ambulance service.  The fire has been caused by a plastic toaster that had been left on top of a cooker that was also switched on.
