Grill pan fire at the De Vere Carden Park Hotel in Chester Time of Incident: 26/08/2015 - 18:39 Location: De Vere Carden Park Hotel, Barton Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas The kitchen at the De Vere Carden Park Hotel was full of smoke following a grill pan fire. The fire was out when the fire crew arrived at the hotel.  Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke from the kitchen.  Firefighters gave oxygen therapy to one male casualty who was suffering from smoke inhalation.  The casualty was then taken to hospital by ambulance.
