 Small fire at a park in Crewe Time of Incident: 24/11/2019 - 19:49 Location: Redwood Drive, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe used a water back pack to extinguish a small fire at Leighton Park on Redwood Drive.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.  The team were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
