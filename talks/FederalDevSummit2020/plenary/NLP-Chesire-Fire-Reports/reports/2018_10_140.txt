 Assisting ambulance crews in Chester Time of Incident: 05/10/2018 - 10:26 Location: Duke Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester, the aerial ladder platform and the rope rescue unit from Lymm with a supporting fire engine Firefighters assisted ambulance crews on Duke Street, Chester, after a woman became unwell. Crews helped the woman from the property and then handed her into the care of paramedics.
