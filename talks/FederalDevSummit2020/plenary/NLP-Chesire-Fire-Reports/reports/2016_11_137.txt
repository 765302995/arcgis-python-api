Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/11/2016 - 01:15 Location: Sharp Street, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters tackled a car fire with a hose reel on Sharp Street, Warrington.  This fire is believed to have been started deliberately.
