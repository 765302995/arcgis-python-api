 Collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 27/09/2019 - 15:39 Location: Groby Road,Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters attended a collision on Groby Road, Crewe. Fire crews happened upon the incident while they were in the local area.  The collision involved three vehicles.  Firefighters offered casualty care to one casualty who was then handed over to the care of paramedics.  Crews were on scene for approximately fifty minutes.
