 One car collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 30/08/2018 - 00:11 Location: Broad Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Fire crews attended a one vehicle collision on Broad Street Crewe. Fire crews made the car safe by using a hose reel jet to cool the engine and also gave a casualty first aid before handing them in to the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for 30 minutes.
