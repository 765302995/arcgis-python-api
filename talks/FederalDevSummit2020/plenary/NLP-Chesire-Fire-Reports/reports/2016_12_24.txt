Chimney fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 28/12/2016 - 09:38 Location: green street, macclesfield. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a building fire on Green Street in Macclesfield.  When they arrived crews confirmed that there was a fire in the chimney. This was extinguished from the grate.   https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/chimney-fire-safety
