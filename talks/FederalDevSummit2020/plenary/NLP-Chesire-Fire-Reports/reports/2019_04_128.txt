 Rescue of a person in Runcorn Time of Incident: 16/04/2019 - 05:47 Location: Barnfield Avenue, Runcorn. Attendance: Four fire engines attended from Warrington, Widnes, Runcorn and Penketh. At around 5.45am firefighters from Warrington, Widnes, Runcorn and Penketh were mobilised to reports that a woman had fallen into the Bridgewater Canal near to Barnfield Avenue, Runcorn.   On arrival at the scene the woman was already out of the water.   The team provided first aid to the casualty. She was then passed to the care of paramedics and taken to Warrington General Hospital.
