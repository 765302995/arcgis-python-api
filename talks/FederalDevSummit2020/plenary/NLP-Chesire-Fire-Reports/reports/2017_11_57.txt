Grill pan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/11/2017 - 17:08 Location: Clarence Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington A woman was treated by firefighters for smoke inhalation after a fire involving a grill pan on Clarence Street, Warrington. The fire was out on arrival of the crews. The woman did not need hospital treatment. Cooking safety tips
