 Peat bog fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 03/06/2018 - 06:56 Location: Moor Lane, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 6.55am firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Moor Lane in Wilmslow. On arrival the crew discovered two pockets of fire smouldering in a peat bog. Firefighters used water and beaters to extinguish the fire, which measured approximately four metres by two metres. They were in attendance for around an hour.
