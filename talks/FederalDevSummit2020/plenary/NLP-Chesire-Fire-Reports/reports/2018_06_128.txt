 Fire at business park in Northwich Time of Incident: 20/06/2018 - 20:54 Location: Soot Hill, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 8.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in an outbuilding at Cosgrove Business Park on Soot Hill, Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning of a large amount of rubbish that had got out of control. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 25 minutes.
