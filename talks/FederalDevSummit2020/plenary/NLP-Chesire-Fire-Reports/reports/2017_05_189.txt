Refuse fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/05/2017 - 16:46 Location: Loushers Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington A hose reel jet was used by firefighters to put out a small fire involving rubbish. The fire is thought to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
