Rubbish fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 29/06/2017 - 12:11 Location: Appleton Walk, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Fire crews were called to a rubbish fire which had got out of control on Appleton Walk, Wilmslow. Contractors had started a rubbish fire but it had gotten out of control so firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the flames.
