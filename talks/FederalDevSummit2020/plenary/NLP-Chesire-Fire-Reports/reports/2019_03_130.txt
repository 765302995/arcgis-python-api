 Small garden fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 02/03/2019 - 14:23 Location: Cotton Lane, Runcorn. WA7 5ND Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly after 2.20pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a garden on Cotton Lane. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out and completed a health and safety assessment with the owner of the house.
