House fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 14/09/2017 - 13:10 Location: Budworth Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Firefighters were called to an electrical fire at a semi detached house on Budworth Road, Ellesmere Port. Crews found that the fire was out on arrival and ventilated the property. One male casualty was given oxygen therapy at the scene for smoke inhalation. An investigation into the cause of the fire has started.
