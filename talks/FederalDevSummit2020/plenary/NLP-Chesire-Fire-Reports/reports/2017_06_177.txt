Cow rescued from slurry pit Time of Incident: 01/06/2017 - 08:00 Location: Lodmore Lane, Burleydam Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and the specialist animal rescue team from Knutsford Cow rescued from slurry pitFire crews and the specialist animal rescue team from Knutsford rescued a cow from a concrete chamber which is part of a slurry pit, on Lodmore Lane, Burleydam. Crews used lifting equipment, ladders, dry suits and straps to rescue the cow. Crews were on the scene for just under three hours.
