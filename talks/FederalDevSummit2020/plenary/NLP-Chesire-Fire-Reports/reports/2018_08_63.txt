 Tyre fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/08/2018 - 19:48 Location: Bishops Way, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to a tyre which was on fire on Bishops Way, Widnes. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident please contact Cheshire Police.
