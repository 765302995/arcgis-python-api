Collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 12/09/2015 - 11:58 Location: Coliseum Way, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port Fire crews attended a report of a collision on Coliseum Way, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found one car on its side with a woman trapped inside. Crews freed the woman who was then passed to the care of the ambulance service.
