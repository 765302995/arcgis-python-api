Fire on the park in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 07/10/2016 - 23:12 Location: Grangeway, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow A fire had been started using bins and pallets on a Wilmslow park. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control and police attended the scene.  Anyone with any information about this fire, believed to have been started deliberately, is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
