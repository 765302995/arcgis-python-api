 Tiled chimney fire at a primary school in Malpas Time of Incident: 10/05/2019 - 08:30 Location: Green Lane, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At 8.30am firefighters were called to a report of a tiled chimney fire at Shocklach Oviatt C of E Primary School on Green Lane in Malpas.On arrival the crew, with the aid of a short extension ladder, discovered that it was a small fire at the bottom of the chimney stack at the school's boiler room.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and a thermal imaging camera to monitor temperatures. Once the fire was out they cleared debris from the chimney with the aid of an inspection plate and a hose reel jet.  Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
