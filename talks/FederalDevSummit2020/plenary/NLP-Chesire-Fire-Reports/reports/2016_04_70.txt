Chimney fire in Broomhall Time of Incident: 17/04/2016 - 20:19 Location: Heatley Lane, Broomhall, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich A chimney fire was tackled by firefighters at a house in Broomhall. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and chimney rods to attack the fire from the grate. They used a thermal imaging camera to check for further hotspots and needed to get onto the roof to check for fire spread. The occupier was advised not to use the chimney until it had been thoroughly swept and checked for safety.  Always follow our chimney safety advice.
