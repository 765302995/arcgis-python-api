Wheelie bin fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 22/09/2017 - 23:37 Location: Coronation Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Crewe Fire crews were called to reports of three wheelie bins which were on fire on Coronation Street, Crewe. On arrival crews found the bins were well alight and used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. On inspection of the surrounding area crews found that a gas pipe had been damaged by the fire and crews then evacuated the residents in Coronation Crescent until a gas engineer isolated the gas main. Residents have returned to their properties. An investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.
