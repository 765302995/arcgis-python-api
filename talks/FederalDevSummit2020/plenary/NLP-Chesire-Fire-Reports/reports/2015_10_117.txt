Coal on fire at the docks in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 11/10/2015 - 02:47 Location: The docks, Lower Mersey Street, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Ellesmere Port firefighters were sent to an incident involving approximately 100 tonnes of coal and a number of seats of fire. Firefighters used hose reel jets to tackle the fire.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check for hotspots of fire.  Police also attended this incident.
