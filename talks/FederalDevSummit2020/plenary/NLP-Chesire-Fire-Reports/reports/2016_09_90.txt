Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/09/2016 - 17:02 Location: Grappenhall Road, Appleton, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Warrington Firefighters had to remove the roof of a car when a casualty was trapped after a three car collision. The casualty, a woman, was released by firefighters and handed into the care of paramedics. Grappenhall Road was closed by police while the incident was being dealt with.
