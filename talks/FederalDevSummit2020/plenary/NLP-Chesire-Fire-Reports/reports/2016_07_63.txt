Gate fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/07/2016 - 20:58 Location: Marsh House Lane, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Warrington Firefighters used a hose reel jet to tackle a fire involving a garden gate on Marsh House Lane, Warrington.  This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 of Cheshire Police on 101.
