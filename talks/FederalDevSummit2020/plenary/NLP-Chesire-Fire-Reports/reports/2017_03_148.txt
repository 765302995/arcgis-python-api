Horse rescue in Rainow Time of Incident: 05/03/2017 - 17:12 Location: Shrigley Fold Road, Rainow Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and the animal rescue unit from Bollington The animal rescue unit assisted in lifting a horse that had fallen in a in a field and couldn't get back up. The team used strops around the 23-year-old horse called Glen to help it up and get him back into his stable.
