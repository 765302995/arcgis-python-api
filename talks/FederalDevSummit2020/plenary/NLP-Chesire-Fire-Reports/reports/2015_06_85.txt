Chemical spillage at school in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/06/2015 - 14:48 Location: Capenhurst Lane, Whitby, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines A school was evacuated after the small spillage of a chemical in Ellesmere Port.  Firefighters attended the scene and liaised with staff on site. An Ambulance was requested to attend after a woman complained of itchy skin. She was checked over and cleared by paramedics.  The incident was handed over to the school to arrange a specialist company to clean up the area.
