Car deliberately set alight in Plumley, Knutsford Time of Incident: 26/04/2017 - 19:02 Location: Ascol Drive, Plumley, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford A car was on fire in a wooded area opposite Ascol Drive. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.   This fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this fire is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
