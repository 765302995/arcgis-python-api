 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/12/2019 - 23:16 Location: St Elphins Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath At around 11.15pm firefighters from Stockton Heath attended reports of a car fire on St Elphins Close in Warrington.  On arrival the crew found a black hatchback car which was well alight.  One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and a 23-year-old local man has been arrested on suspicion of arson.  Anyone with any information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 579359.
