Suspected deliberate car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 13/05/2016 - 01:32 Location: Lockitt Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters used two hose reel jets to put out a car fire in Crewe. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
