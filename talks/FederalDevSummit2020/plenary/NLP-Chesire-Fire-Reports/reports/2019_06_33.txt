 Small fire in the open in Crewe Time of Incident: 23/06/2019 - 00:15 Location: Chorlton Lane, Chorlton, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At 12.15am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Chorlton, Crewe. On arrival at Chorlton Lane the crew found that it was a small fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
