 Fire in a domestic property in Poynton Time of Incident: 16/03/2019 - 19:54 Location: Lower Park Road, Poynton Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Poynton and one from Stockport in Greater Manchester Shortly before 8pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke at a domestic property in Lower Park Road, Poynton. On arrivals crews crews discovered a fire caused by dry timber being stacked at the side of a wood-burning stove. There was slight smoke logging in the property. Firefighters removed the logs from the heat source using a bucket and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. For tips on avoiding a fire involving a wood-burning stove visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wood-burning-stoves.
