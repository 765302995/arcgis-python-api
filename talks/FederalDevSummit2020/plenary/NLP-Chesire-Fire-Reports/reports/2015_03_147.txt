Bedroom fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 03/03/2015 - 15:39 Location: Princes Place, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish a fire in the bedroom of a mid terraced property in Widnes.  The crews isolated the utilities and contained the fire to the bedroom. They checked the loft space of the adjoining properties to ensure the fire had not spread and a large fan was used to clear the smoke.  The fire had been caused by a candle.
