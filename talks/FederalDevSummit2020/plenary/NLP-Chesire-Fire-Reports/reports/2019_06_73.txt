 Small fire in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 14/06/2019 - 16:47 Location: Mersey Road, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish a small fire at the back of a Scout Hut on Mersey Road.  The crew were detained at the scene for approximately 30 minutes.
