Fire in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 08/10/2016 - 14:26 Location: Boston Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine Firefighters used a water backpack to put out a small fire started outside in Boston Avenue. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
