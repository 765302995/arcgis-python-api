Tumble drier fire in Moore Time of Incident: 18/05/2015 - 22:32 Location: Runcorn Road,Moore Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A tumble drier was the cause of a fire in a flat above a Runcorn pub.   A bedroom became involved in the fire and firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.   A high pressure fan was also used to clear the property of smoke.  Nobody was trapped in the incident.   Crews were at the scene for approximately one hour.
