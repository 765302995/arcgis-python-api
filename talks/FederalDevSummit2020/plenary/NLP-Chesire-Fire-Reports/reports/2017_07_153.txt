Hay bales on fire in Audlem Time of Incident: 02/07/2017 - 04:54 Location: Green Lane, Audlem Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Hay bales on fire in Audlem  Firefighters were called to attend a fire where two large hay bales were on fire on Green Lane, Audlem. Crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to put out the fire which was well alight on arrival. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Crews were at the scene for approximately an hour and a half.  If you have any information about the blaze please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
