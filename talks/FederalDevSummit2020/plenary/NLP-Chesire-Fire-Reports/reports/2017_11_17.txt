HGV overturned in Runcorn Time of Incident: 24/11/2017 - 10:34 Location: A557, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Overturned HGV in RuncornFire crews were called to a HGV which had turned over on the A557 in Runcorn. On arrival they found no one trapped and stopped a small leak from the fuel tank. The driver of the HGV was taken to hospital by paramedics.
