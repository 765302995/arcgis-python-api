 Trapped person rescue, Nantwich Time of Incident: 17/11/2018 - 10:41 Location: Wrexham Road, Burland Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and Nantwich, and the major rescue unit from Winsford Crews were called to assist with the rescue of a farm worker who had become trapped under a tractor. Fire crews worked with colleagues from the ambulance service to extricate him from underneath the vehicle. They used farm machinery, airbags and timber cribbing to assist with the rescue operation.  The man had suffered serious injuries and, once freed, he was passed into the care of the ambulance service. In total, crews were engaged in the operation for around one hour and 20 minutes.
