Wheelie bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/11/2017 - 15:30 Location: Smithy Lane, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port. One fire engine from Powey Lane. Firefighters extinguished a fire involving a wheelie bin and hedge very close to a house in Smithy Lane, Ellesmere Port. Crews used one hose reel to extinguish the fire, and a thermal imaging camera and large fan to check for hotspots and disperse the smoke due to close proximity to the property.
