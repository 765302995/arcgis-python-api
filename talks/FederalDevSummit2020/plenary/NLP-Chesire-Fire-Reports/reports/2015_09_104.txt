Scooter on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 11/09/2015 - 04:11 Location: Lime Avenue, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters were called to a scooter on fire in a field in Northwich. The vehicle was extinguished with a hose reel. The fire is thought to have been started deliberately and police attended. If anyone has any information about who may have started this fire then they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
