 Car fire at Queens Park in Crewe Time of Incident: 31/01/2019 - 21:44 Location: Queens Park Drive, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 9.45pm firefighters from Crewe were dispatched to reports of a fire on the car park near to the golf course at Queens Park.  The team quickly attended the scene and on arrival they discovered a car which was well alight.  One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The team then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML309676.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately 30 minutes.
