Fire at a two storey industrial unit on Berkely Court in Runcorn Time of Incident: 09/10/2015 - 18:26 Location: Berkely Court in Runcorn. Attendance: two fire engines from Widnes Two fire crews from Widnes were sent to a fire at a two storey industrial unit on Berkely Court in Runcorn. Two firefighters wore two breathing apparatus.  The fire crews used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They were on scene for approximately two hours.
