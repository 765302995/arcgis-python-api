 Large scale flooding in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/11/2019 - 00:29 Location: Dansham Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 12.30am firefighters were called to reports of large scale flooding on Dansham Avenue, Warrington. Crews attended the scene and found that around 20 properties had been impacted by flood water. Firefighters visited all of the affected properties to conduct welfare checks and see if any assistance was required. A small number of residents also chose to evacuate their homes as a precautionary measure.
