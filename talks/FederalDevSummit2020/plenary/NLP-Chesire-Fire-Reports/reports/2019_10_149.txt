 Fire at a children's play area in Runcorn Time of Incident: 01/10/2019 - 23:03 Location: Roehampton Drive, Hallwood Park, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters from Runcorn used one hose reel jet to extinguish a small bin fire at a children's play area on Hallwood Park. The team were in attendance at the scene for around 15 minutes.
