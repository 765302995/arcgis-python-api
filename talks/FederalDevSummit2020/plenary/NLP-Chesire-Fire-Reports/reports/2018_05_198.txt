 Disposable barbecue fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 06/05/2018 - 03:30 Location: Princes Park, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Firefighters were called to a fire involving a disposable barbecue on a wooden bench at an address in Princes Park, Northwich. The fire was out on arrival. Crews cut away the burnt wood and removed a fence panel, then damped down the area using a hose reel to prevent the fire reigniting.
