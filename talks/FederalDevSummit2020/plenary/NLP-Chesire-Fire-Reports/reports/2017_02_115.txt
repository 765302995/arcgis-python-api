Three car collision in Davenham Time of Incident: 09/02/2017 - 18:21 Location: A556, Davenham Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich One casualty was given first aid by firefighters and paramedics after a three car collision on the A556 between Davenham and Hartford. The casualty, a man, was then taken to hospital by ambulance as firefighters made the scene safe.
