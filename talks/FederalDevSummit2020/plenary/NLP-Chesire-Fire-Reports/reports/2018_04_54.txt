 Bus fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 20/04/2018 - 09:17 Location: Caldwell Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to reports of a bus on fire in a car park on Caldwell Road in Widnes. Crews used a hose reel and a breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire which is believed to involve the engine compartment.
