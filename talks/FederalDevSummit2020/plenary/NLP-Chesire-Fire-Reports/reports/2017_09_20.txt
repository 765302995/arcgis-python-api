Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/09/2017 - 11:59 Location: Delph Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision on Delph Lane, Warrington. The collision involved one vehicle and fire crews used a winch to release one casualty from a car. The casualty was then handed into the care of paramedics. Crews were at the scene for one hour.
