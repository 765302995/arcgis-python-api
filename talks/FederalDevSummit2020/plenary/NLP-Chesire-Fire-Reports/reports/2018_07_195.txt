 Shed fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 10/07/2018 - 04:59 Location: Dane Street, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Congleton At around 5am firefighters were called to a report of a shed being on fire on Dane Street, off the River Dane walkway, in Congleton. Crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
