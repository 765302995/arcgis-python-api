 Sofa on fire in the open in Winsford Time of Incident: 13/11/2019 - 17:50 Location: Basford Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At 5.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a sofa being on fire in Basford Way, Winsford.  On arrival the crew found the large sofa well alight.  Firefighters extinguished the fire in the open with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for more than 10 minutes.
