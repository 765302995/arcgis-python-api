Large animal rescue in Chester Time of Incident: 29/07/2016 - 09:51 Location: Seahill Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and an animal rescue unit from Bollington Firefighters were called to reports of a horse that had fallen six feet into a shallow water ditch. Crews arrived on the scene alongside a local vet, and discovered that the animal had fallen onto it's side. Using straw, crews stabilised the horse's head, allowing for the vet to sedate the animal.  Crews were then able to attach a harness to the horse and lift it safely out of the enclosure, with the help of a farm vehicle.
