Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 21/05/2016 - 02:20 Location: Stewards Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A third Widnes car fire was tackled by firefighters. Police were asked to attend as it is believed this fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
