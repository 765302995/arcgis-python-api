 Roadside barrier fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/06/2019 - 17:06 Location: Grangeway, Halton Lodge, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly after 5pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in Grangeway, Halton Lodge, Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered a temporary roadside barrier well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
