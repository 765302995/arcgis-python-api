 Car collides with a tree in Poynton Time of Incident: 21/02/2019 - 13:08 Location: Junction of Tern Drive and Chester Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Shortly after 1pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving a car and a tree on the junction of Tern Drive and Chester Road in Poynton. The driver of the car had got out of the vehicle prior to the crew's arrival. She was assessed by paramedics and taken to hospital as a precaution. Smoke was coming from the car following the collision. Firefighters used a hose reel and an environmental grab pack to make the vehicle safe and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
