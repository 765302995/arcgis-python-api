Small fire in sub station in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/03/2015 - 21:41 Location: Tan House Lane/Warrington Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A deliberate fire involving a sub station on open land required firefighters to attend and use dry power extinguishers to put it out. Nearby businesses were affected by loss of power but Scottish Power were able to deal with this issue. Police also attended. If anyone has any information regarding this incident they should call 0800 555 111.
