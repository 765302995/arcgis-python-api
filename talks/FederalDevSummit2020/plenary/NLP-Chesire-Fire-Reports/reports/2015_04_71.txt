Tyres set on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 20/04/2015 - 20:06 Location: Newbridge road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port. A small quantity of disused tyres were on fire causing lots of black smoke.  Firefighters used a high pressure hose reel to extinguish the fire.  The fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with information is urged to call police on 101, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
