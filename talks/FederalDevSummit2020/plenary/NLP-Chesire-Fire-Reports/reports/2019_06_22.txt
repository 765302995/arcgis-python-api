 Garden fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/06/2019 - 19:08 Location: Francis Close, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to a small fire in a garden involving a fence and a propane cylinder, on Francis Close, Widnes. Fire crews assessed the cylinder and discovered it was empty before they extinguished the fire using a hose reel jet.
