Chimney fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 27/11/2016 - 23:41 Location: Dark Lane, Duckington, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas A fire in the flue of a log burner was getting out of control at a house in Duckington. Firefighters got access to the flue to be able to cool it with a high pressure jet. The whole area was then checked using a thermal imaging camera to make sure it was at a suitable temperature before the crew left the incident.
