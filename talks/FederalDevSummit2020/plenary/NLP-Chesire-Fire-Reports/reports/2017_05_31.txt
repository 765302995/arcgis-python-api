Caravan fire on hard shoulder of M56 Time of Incident: 26/05/2017 - 13:04 Location: M56 west between junctions 11 and 12 Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Stockton Heath. Firefighters were called to a caravan on fire on the hard shoulder. The crew used a hose reel to extinguish the flames. Police closed the carriageway in both directions while the incident was dealt with.
