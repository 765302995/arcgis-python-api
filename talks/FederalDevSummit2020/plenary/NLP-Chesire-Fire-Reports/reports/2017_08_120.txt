Kitchen fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 09/08/2017 - 19:13 Location: YMCA Halton Lodge Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Widnes A fire in the kitchen of a YMCA ground floor flat led to a full evacuation of the premises on Halton Lodge Avenue. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet.  The fire is believed to have started in a chip pan. Please follow our safety advice.
