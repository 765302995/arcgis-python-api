Casualty rescue in Crewe Time of Incident: 06/08/2016 - 13:38 Location: Merrills Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and rope rescue technicians from Knutsford A man had fallen a short distance down an embankment in Crewe and was unable to get up. He had fallen approximately one metre from his back garden down onto the railway embankment. Firefighters rescued him and handed him into the care of paramedics.
