Bedroom fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 07/08/2016 - 11:06 Location: Welshmens Lane, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe, one from Nantwich and one from Alsager The bedroom of a house under renovation was involved in fire. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reels and a covering jet to bring the fire under control. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke from the house. Crews damped the area down. Welshmens Lane was closed by police while the fire was being tackled. An investigation into the cause of the fire will be carried out.
