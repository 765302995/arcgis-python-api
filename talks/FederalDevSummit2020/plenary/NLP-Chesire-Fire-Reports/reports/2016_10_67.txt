Garage on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 19/10/2016 - 20:56 Location: Eccleston Avenue, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a garage on fire and used two hose reels to extinguish it. It is believed that this fire may have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information about this incident, they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
