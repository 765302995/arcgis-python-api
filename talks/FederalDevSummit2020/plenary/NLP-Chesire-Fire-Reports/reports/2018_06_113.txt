 Hedgerow fire in Chester Time of Incident: 22/06/2018 - 14:55 Location: Land between Pepper Street and Whitchurch Road in Christleton, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester attended At around 2.55pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open on land between Pepper Street and Whitchurch Road in Christleton, Chester. On arrival the crew discovered approximately 25 metres of hedgerow well alight. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 25 minutes.
