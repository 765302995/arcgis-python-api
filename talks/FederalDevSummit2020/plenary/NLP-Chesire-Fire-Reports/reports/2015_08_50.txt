Car fire in Chester Time of Incident: 19/08/2015 - 21:40 Location: Long Looms, Barrow Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Fire crews have extinguished a two vehicle fires suspected to have been started deliberatley.  One saloon car was completely destroyed and a four-wheel drive was also badly damaged. Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
