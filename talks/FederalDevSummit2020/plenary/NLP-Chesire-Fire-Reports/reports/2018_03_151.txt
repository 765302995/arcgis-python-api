 Rubbish fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 01/03/2018 - 23:22 Location: Brooklyn Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 11.15pm a fire engine from Ellesmere Port Fire Station was called to a rubbish fire on Brooklyn Drive, Ellesmere Port. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. They were at the scene for around 15 minutes.
