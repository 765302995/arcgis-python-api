 Shed fire in Chester Time of Incident: 28/12/2018 - 13:06 Location: The Groves, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port At around 1.05pm firefighters were called to reports of an outbuilding fire at The Groves in Chester. On arrival crews discovered a shed well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with two hose reel jets. They then damped the area affected by the fire down to make it safe. The fire is believed to have been started accidentally. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
