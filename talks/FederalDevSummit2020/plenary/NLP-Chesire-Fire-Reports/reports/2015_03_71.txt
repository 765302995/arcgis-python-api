Road traffic collision in Knutsford Time of Incident: 15/03/2015 - 16:18 Location: Manchester Road, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and two from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Firefighters attended a road traffic collision in Knutsford involving a motorbike and saloon car.  Police and ambulance also attended the incident and roads were closed as the emergency services made the car safe and cared for casualties.  Sadly the death of the motorcyclist was confirmed at the scene.
