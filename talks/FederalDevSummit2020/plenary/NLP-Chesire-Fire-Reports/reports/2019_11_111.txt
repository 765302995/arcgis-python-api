 Fire in open in Malpas Time of Incident: 08/11/2019 - 19:34 Location: A49, Malpas. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas Firefighters were called to reports of a small fire outside a house on the A49 at Croxton Green.  A crew from Malpas attended and used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They also used a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots once the fire had been extinguished.
