 Fire in disused building in Handforth Time of Incident: 09/09/2018 - 17:26 Location: South Acre Drive, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and two from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a fire in a disused building on South Acre Drive, Handforth. Crews, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets and a large fan to put out the fire. Crews were at the scene for two hours. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101.
