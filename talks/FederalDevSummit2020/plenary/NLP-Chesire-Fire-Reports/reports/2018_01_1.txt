Kitchen fire in Moulton Time of Incident: 30/01/2018 - 16:38 Location: Main Road, Moulton Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Northwich At 3.58pm firefighters from Northwich were called to reports of a kitchen fire in Moulton. On arrival at the scene crews found that the fire had been extinguished but the property was heavily smoke logged. Crews used an industrial fan to clear the smoke.
