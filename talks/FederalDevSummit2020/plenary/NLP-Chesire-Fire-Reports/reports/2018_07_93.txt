 Overheated brakes on a vehicle on the M6 Time of Incident: 19/07/2018 - 17:08 Location: M6 south between junctions 21a to 21. Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Warrington Firefighters cooled the brakes of a vehicle which had overheated on the M6. Considerable amounts of smoke were coming from the vehicle on arrival and Highways Patrol were in attendance. One hose reel was used to cool the brakes.
