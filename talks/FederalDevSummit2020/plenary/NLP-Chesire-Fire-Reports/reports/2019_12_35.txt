 Leaking battery in Adlington Time of Incident: 03/12/2019 - 22:44 Location: London Road, Adlington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Fire crews attended to a collision involving a car and a milk float on London Road, Adlington.  Although the vehicles weren't on fire, a battery had ejected from the milk float onto the carriageway and was leaking.  A cordon was put in place and the local authority was contacted.
