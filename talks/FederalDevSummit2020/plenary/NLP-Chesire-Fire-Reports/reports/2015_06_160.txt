Road traffic collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 05/06/2015 - 13:17 Location: Parkgate Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters assisted in rescuing two people involved in a collision in Ellesmere Port.  The incident involved one HGV and a car and a man and a woman were taken to hospital. Firefighters were on the scene for over an hour.
