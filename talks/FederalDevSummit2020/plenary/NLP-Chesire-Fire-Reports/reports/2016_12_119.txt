Candle sets fire to blinds in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 07/12/2016 - 18:55 Location: Priestfield Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A candle set fire to blinds at a house on Priestfield Road. The fire had burnt itself out but left smoke in the house. Firefighters used a high pressure fan to clear the smoke.  Always follow our candle safety advice.
