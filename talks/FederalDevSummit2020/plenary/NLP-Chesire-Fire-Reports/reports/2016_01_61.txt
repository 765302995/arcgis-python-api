Two car collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 16/01/2016 - 08:37 Location: A54 in Winsford. Attendance: A fire engine from Winsford Firefighers were called to a report of a collision on the A54 in Winsford.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two cars. One woman was trapped. Crews used a long board to get her safely from the car. She was then passed to the care of the ambulance service.
