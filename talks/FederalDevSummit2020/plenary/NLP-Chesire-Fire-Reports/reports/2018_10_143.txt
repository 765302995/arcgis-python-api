 Lorry collides with bridge in Beeston Time of Incident: 04/10/2018 - 11:59 Location: Whitchurch Road, Beeston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and Chester Lorry collides with bridge in BeestonFirefighters were called to a road traffic collision on Whitchurch Road in Beeston after a lorry collided with a railway bridge  Other emergency services are also attended the scene along with the Environment Agency. Crews used specialist equipment to stop the diesel from going into a drain and Highways Agency cleared up the spillage.   The driver wasn't trapped and was handed into the care of the ambulance service.
