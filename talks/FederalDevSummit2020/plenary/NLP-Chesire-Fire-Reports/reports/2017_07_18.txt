Metal bin on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 24/07/2017 - 21:11 Location: Terrace Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes A metal container full of rubbish was on fire on Terrace Road. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control and asked police to attend as it is believed the fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
