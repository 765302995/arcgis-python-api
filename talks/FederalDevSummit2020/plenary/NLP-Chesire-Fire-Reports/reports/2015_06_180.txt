House fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 31/05/2015 - 20:15 Location: Connaught Crescent, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes A four bedroom detached house, along with its adjoining garage, were heavily involved in fire.   Firefighters, three wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to tackle the blaze, and a covering jet to protect a neighbouring house.   That house was evacuated as crews continued to damp down the affected property, using a thermal imaging camera to check there were no further hot spots of fire.   The crews were at the scene for over an hour.  The cause of the fire was electrical.
