Refuse fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/02/2015 - 23:18 Location: Mercer Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters used hydraulic cutting equipment to access a storage container involved in a fire in Warrington.  A large amount of rubbish was well alight against the container on arrival of the crew who used one hose reel to extinguish the blaze. They accessed the container to check for fire spread.  Police also attended the scene and the fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
