Building fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 08/05/2015 - 15:24 Location: Brook Street Mill, Goodall Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish a fire in the ground floor of a two storey building.  The fire involved a spray paint booth and ventilation shaft.  The electricity was isolated and the property was ventilated to clear the smoke.
