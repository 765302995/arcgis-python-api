 Barbecue fire, Cuddington Time of Incident: 14/09/2019 - 19:58 Location: Mayfield Drive, Cuddington Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Fire crews were called when a gas valve on a barbecue started leaking. It was reported that the leak could cause the canister to catch fire, but on arrival they found the leak was quite small and no longer on fire, and they were able to isolate it from the canister. The cylinder was removed from the barbecue and checked over with a thermal imaging camera to ensure it had been unaffected by the fire.
