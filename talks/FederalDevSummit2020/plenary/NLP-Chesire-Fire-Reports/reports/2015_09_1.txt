Chip pan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/09/2015 - 19:39 Location: Gables Close, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Gables Close, Warrington.  The fire involved a chip pan and was out on arrival of the crews who then used a large fan to clear the smoke and a thermal imaging camera to make sure there was no fire spread or hotspots.  Two people were taken to hospital by ambulance for smoke inhalation.  Chip pan safety advice
