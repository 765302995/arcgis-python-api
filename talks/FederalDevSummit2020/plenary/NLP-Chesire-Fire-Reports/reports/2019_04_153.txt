 Fire in disused building in Crewe Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 16:19 Location: Electricity Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to a fire in a disused building on Electricity Street, Crewe. The fire was on the first floor of the building and involved a small quantity of rubbish. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
