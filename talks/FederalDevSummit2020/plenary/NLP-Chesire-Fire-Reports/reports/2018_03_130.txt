 Garden fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/03/2018 - 23:35 Location: Moor Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 11.40pm firefighters were called to a garden fire on Moor Lane, Widnes. The fire involved a brasier which had been left unattended. The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
