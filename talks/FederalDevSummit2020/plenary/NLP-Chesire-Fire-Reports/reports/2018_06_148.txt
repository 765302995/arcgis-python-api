 Fire in woodland in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/06/2018 - 23:49 Location: Nortonwood Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 11.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in woodland on Northwood Lane in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved an area of heathland that measured approximately five metres by five metres. Firefighters used water to put out the fire and were in attendance for less than 10 minutes.
