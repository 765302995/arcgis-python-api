Vehicle fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/05/2015 - 05:43 Location: Watkins Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters extinguished a fire involving a saloon car in Warrington.  The vehicle was well alight on arrival of the crew who wore breathing apparatus and used two hose reels to put out the fire.  Police were requested to attend the scene due to suspected deliberate ignition.
