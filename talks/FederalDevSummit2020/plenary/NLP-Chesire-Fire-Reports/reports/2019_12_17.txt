 Small vehicle fire in Little Leigh Time of Incident: 08/12/2019 - 12:01 Location: Cogshall Lane, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 12pm firefighters were called to reports of a car fire on Cogshall Lane, Little Leigh.  On arrival at the scene the crew found that they owner of the car had partially extinguished the fire using a dry powder extinguisher. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to fully extinguish the blaze.  The crew were detained at the scene for around 30 minutes.
