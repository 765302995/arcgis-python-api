Road traffic collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/01/2015 - 10:43 Location: Lythgoes Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to the scene of a road traffic collision in which a casualty was trapped.  The incident involved one car which had hit a lamp post.  The crew stabilised the vehicle and carried out an operation to release the female casualty by removing the roof of the car.  She was handed into the care of the ambulance service.
