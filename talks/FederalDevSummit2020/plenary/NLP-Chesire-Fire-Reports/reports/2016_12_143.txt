Road traffic collison in Arclid, Sandbach Time of Incident: 03/12/2016 - 21:14 Location: Congleton Road in Arclid, Sandbach Attendance: Three fire engines from Sandbach, Alsager and Congleton Three fire engines from Sandbach, Alsager and Congleton were sent to a road traffic collison on Congleton Road in Arclid, Sandbach. On arrival at the incident, the fire crews found that nobody was trapped. The fire crews made the vehicles safe.  Two covering jets were used.  A thermal image camera was used to check fro hotspots of fire.  Two casualties were treated at scene by paramedics and then taken to Leighton Hospital by ambulance.
