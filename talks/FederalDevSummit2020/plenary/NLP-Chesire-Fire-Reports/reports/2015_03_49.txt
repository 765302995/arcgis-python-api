Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 21/03/2015 - 06:44 Location: Stanley Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester One hose reel was used by firefighters wearing breathing apparatus to extinguish a kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port. The property was fitted with smoke alarms which did go off and alerted the occupiers to the fire. Kitchen fire safety advice
