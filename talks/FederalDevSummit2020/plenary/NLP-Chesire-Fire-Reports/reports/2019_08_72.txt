 Skip fire in Thornton-le-Moors, near Chester Time of Incident: 20/08/2019 - 23:12 Location: Thornton Green Lane, Thornton-le-Moors, near Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane At around 11.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Thornton Green Lane, Thornton-le-Moors, near Chester. On arrival the crew discovered a skip on fire on wasteland. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
