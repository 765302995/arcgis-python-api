 Car fire, Warrington Time of Incident: 06/10/2018 - 17:45 Location: Cliff Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Lymm Firefighters responded to reports of a car on fire in the service area of Lymm motorway services. They found a fire in the engine block of the car, and used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to put out the flames. They carried on damping down the engine before handing back to the owner. They were there for around 50 minutes.
