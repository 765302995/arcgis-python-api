 Fire in the open in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/01/2019 - 22:05 Location: Mill Lane, Great Sutton Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port used one hose reel jet to extinguish a large pile of rubbish which had been deliberately set alight on Mill Lane in Great Sutton.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately 15 minutes.  The fire has been reported to Cheshire police and anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
