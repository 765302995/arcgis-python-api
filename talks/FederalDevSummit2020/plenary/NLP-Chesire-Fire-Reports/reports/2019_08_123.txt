 Collision on the M56 involving two HGVs Time of Incident: 08/08/2019 - 17:49 Location: M56 eastbound between junctions 10 and 9 Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one from Runcorn, one from Penketh and one from Widnes Fire crews attended a collision involving two heavy goods vehicles on the M56 eastbound between junctions 10 and 9.  A man was physically trapped inside one of the HGVs.  Firefighters got him out of the vehicle and handed him over to the care of paramedics.  He was taken to hospital via an ambulance.  Firefighters were at the scene for just over half-an-hour. M56 Crash
