Sofa fire in Chester Time of Incident: 16/01/2016 - 14:32 Location: Upper Northgate Street, Chester. Attendance: Fire engines from Chester and Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Upper Northgate Street, Chester.   Upon arrival crews confirmed a small fire involving a sofa. Crews used a hose reel to deal with the fire.  They also removed the sofa outside.  One casualty was treated by paramedics at the scene for slight smoke inhalation.
