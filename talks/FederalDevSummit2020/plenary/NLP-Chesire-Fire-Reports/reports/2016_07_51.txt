Deliberate wheelie bin fire Wilmslow Time of Incident: 21/07/2016 - 03:14 Location: Barlow Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow A wheelie bin had been set on fire under a tree in Barlow Road. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze. Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
