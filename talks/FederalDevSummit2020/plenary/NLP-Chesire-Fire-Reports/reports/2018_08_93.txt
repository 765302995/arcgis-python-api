 Grass fire, Runcorn Time of Incident: 11/08/2018 - 15:54 Location: Runcorn Hill, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Crews were called to reports of a small grass fire on Runcorn Hill. They found an area of grass smouldering, caused by discarded smoking materials. They used a backpack and hand appliance to extinguish the fire, and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
