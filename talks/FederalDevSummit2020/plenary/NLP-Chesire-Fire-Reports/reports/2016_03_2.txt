House fire in Chester Time of Incident: 30/03/2016 - 19:31 Location: Churton Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester A house fire in Chester has caused structural damage to the Churton Road property. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire on the first floor of the mid-terraced house. All people were accounted for and a building inspector attended to assess the structural safety of the house.  The electricity supply to the property was isolated and an investigation into the cause of the blaze is underway.
