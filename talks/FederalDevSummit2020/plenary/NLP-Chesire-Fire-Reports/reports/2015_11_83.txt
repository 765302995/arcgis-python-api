Building fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 13/11/2015 - 15:34 Location: The Fairway, Alsager Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Insulation inside a cavity wall caused a fire at an Alsager property. Firefighters gained access to the cavity to find a smouldering fire in the insulation. Crews worked on top of a single storey flat roofed garage wearing height safety equipment to tackle the incident.
