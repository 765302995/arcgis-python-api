 Property fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 21/01/2019 - 18:12 Location: Wallworths Bank, Congleton Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Congleton and one from Holmes Chapel Crews attended to reports of a fire at a property on Wallworths Bank in Congleton.  Firefighters, wearing two breathing apparatus, used one hose reel to extinguish the fire.  One man was safely removed from the property suffering from smoke inhalation.  A positive pressure ventilation fan was used to clear the smoke.
