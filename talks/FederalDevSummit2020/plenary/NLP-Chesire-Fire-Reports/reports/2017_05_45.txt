Small fire at allotments in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/05/2017 - 14:40 Location: Warrington Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn A controlled burning which then got out of control at Haddocks Wood Allotments, Warrington Road, Runcorn, was put out by firefighters. Crews used one hose reel jet to put out the blaze.
