 Three people rescued from house fire in Chester Time of Incident: 22/04/2018 - 23:30 Location: Bridgewater Drive, Chester Attendance: Three fire engines attended - two from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester At around 11.30pm firefighters were called to a persons reported fire at a house on Bridgewater Drive in Chester.  On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved the downstairs kitchen of the two-storey property.  Firefighters rescued three people from the garage roof using a seven-metre ladder. They were passed to the care of paramedics before being taken to hospital to be assessed.  Four firefigthers wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. They then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the house.  Fire crews were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
