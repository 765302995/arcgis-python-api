Grill pan fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 12/09/2016 - 19:08 Location: Howty Close, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) A fire in a grill pan left a flat heavily smokelogged and neighbours having to evacuate the building. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to put out the fire and check there were no further hotspots. A high pressure fan was used to clear the smoke.  Always follow our kitchen safety and grill pan safety advice.
