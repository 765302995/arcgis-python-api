Commercial bin fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 06/11/2015 - 10:57 Location: Beech Farm Drive, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Bollington. Firefighters were called to a bin on fire in Macclesfield.  Upon arrival crews found a commercial bin well alight in a bin store and using two breathing apparatus, extinguished the fire.
