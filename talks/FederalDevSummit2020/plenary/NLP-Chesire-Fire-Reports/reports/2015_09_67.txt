Hairdryer on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 16/09/2015 - 23:35 Location: Greenall Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich Fire crews were called to a property in Northwich following reports of a hairdryer on fire.  The fire was out on arrival but firefighters completed a home safety assessment after the smoke detectors in the property failed to operate. Smoke alarm information and advice
