Small fire in underpass in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/10/2017 - 16:52 Location: Kingsway South, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Fire crews were called to reports of a small fire in a railway underpass off Kingsway South, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. One fire engine from Wilmslow attended a collision involving a car on Wilmslow Road, Handforth. Firefighters assisted paramedics and assessed one man who was involved in the collision. Crews were on the scene for 25 minutes.
