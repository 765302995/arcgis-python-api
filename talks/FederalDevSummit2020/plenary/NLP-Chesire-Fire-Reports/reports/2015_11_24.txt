Cylinder fire in Rostherne Time of Incident: 24/11/2015 - 11:54 Location: Chester Road, Rostherne Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Greater Manchester FRS Crews from Cheshire and Manchester have been tackling a fire involving an oxi-propane welding cylinder in a farmyard.  There were several cylinders in the area but only one was on fire. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Crews continued to check the cylinder temperature using a thermal imaging camera using a cooling jet. A cordon was put in place for safety.
