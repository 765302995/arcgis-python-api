 Grass and bushes fire at a farm in Chester Time of Incident: 05/08/2018 - 14:02 Location: Village Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 2pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving grass and bushes at a farm on Village Road in Dunham on the Hill, Chester. They used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
