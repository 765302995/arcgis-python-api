 Tractor fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 08/04/2019 - 15:49 Location: Hill Road North, Frodsham. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich. One fire engine attended from Widnes. Firefighters were called to Hill Road North, Helsby following reports of a fire involving a tractor. Firefighters used two breathing apparatus, two hose reels, a small portable pump and one main jet to bring the fire under control.  They were in attendance for approximately30 minutes.
