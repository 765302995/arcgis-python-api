 Bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/12/2018 - 21:07 Location: Carol street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly after 9pm firefighters were called to a report of a bin being on fire on Carol Street in Warrington. The crew extinguished the fire in the open using a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than 10 minutes.
