 Collision at a petrol station in Widnes Time of Incident: 27/11/2019 - 07:02 Location: Green Oaks Way, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly after 7am firefighters were called to reports of a collision at a petrol station in Green Oaks Way, Widnes.  On arrival the crew found that a heavy goods vehicle's exhaust pipe had collided with the petrol station's canopy.  The station was closed as a precautionary measure and an engineer attended the site to assist with the removal of the HGV.  Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
