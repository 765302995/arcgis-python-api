 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/10/2018 - 18:01 Location: Royal Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters extinguished a wheelie bin which was deliberately set on fire on Royal Avenue in Widnes.  Crews used a hose reel t put out the fire and made the area safe.  To help prevent those intent on deliberately setting fire to wheelie bins, please follow our safety advice  https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
