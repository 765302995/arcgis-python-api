Electrical incident in Knutsford Time of Incident: 13/03/2015 - 16:32 Location: Tabley Grove, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Northwich Electric sockets behind a dishwasher were thought to have been involved in fire.  Firefighters were called to the property and found that a static discharge had caused the incident.  They isolated the electricity supply.
