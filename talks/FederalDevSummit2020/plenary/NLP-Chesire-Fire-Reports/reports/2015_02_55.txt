Collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 15/02/2015 - 04:26 Location: Hale Road, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report to a collision on Hale Road in Widnes.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved one car that had gone off the road. One man had been thrown from the vehicle.  Firefighters made sure that the vehicle was safe while the ambulance crews dealt with the casualty.
