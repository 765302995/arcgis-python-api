Ducklings rescued from silt near to Appleton Reservoir in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/06/2015 - 21:29 Location: Park Lane, Warrington Attendance: one fire engine from Warrington Warrington firefighters helped an RSPCA inspector in rescuing a duck and a number of ducklings from silt in an overflow near to Appleton Reservoir on Park Lane in Warrington.
