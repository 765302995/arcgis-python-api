 Smell of smoke coming from a washing machine in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/11/2019 - 13:39 Location: Coronation Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 1.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a smell of smoke at a domestic property in Coronation Street, Crewe.  On arrival the crew discovered that the smell was coming from a washing machine.  Firefighters isolated the electricity supply to the washing machine and gave advice to the occupier.  They were in attendance for around 15 minutes.  For washing machine fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/washing-machine-and-dishwasher-fire-safety.
