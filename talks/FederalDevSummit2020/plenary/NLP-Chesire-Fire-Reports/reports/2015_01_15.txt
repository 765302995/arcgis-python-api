Car fire on M56 west, junctions 9 to 10 Time of Incident: 26/01/2015 - 22:09 Location: M56 west, junctions 9 to 10 Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Greater Manchester FRS A fire in a car was tackled by firefighters on the M56 near Appleton.  The crew, one firefighter using breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
