Smell of burning coming from a lift in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 01/09/2017 - 00:00 Location: Kennedy Ave, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Firefighters were sent to an incident where there was a strong smell of burning on the second floor of a building. The fire crews used a thermal imaging camera. They found that some components had overheated in a motor room of a lift shaft in the building.
