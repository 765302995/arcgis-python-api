Road traffic collision in Chester Time of Incident: 16/12/2016 - 16:29 Location: A55, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters are currently in attendance at a road traffic collision in Chester involving four vehicles and one heavy goods vehicle, where one woman was reported to be trapped. Firefighters gained access to the vehicle and released the woman who was then taken to hospital.  Crews then assessed further casualties and proceeded to make the scene safe. A total of six casualties were taken to hospital.
