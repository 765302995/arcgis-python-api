House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/09/2015 - 11:14 Location: Hucklow Drive, Fairfield and Howley, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Warrington A well developed fire in a property in Warrington was extinguished by firefighters using a hose reel. A large fan was then used to remove smoke from the property. No one was involved in this fire which is believed to have been started accidentally by an electrical fault.
