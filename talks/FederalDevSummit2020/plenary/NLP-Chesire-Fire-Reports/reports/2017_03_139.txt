House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/03/2017 - 18:10 Location: Henry Street, Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Warrington, Penketh and Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Henry Street, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that there was a small fire involving an extractor fan. A hose reel jet and a large fan was used to put the fire out.
