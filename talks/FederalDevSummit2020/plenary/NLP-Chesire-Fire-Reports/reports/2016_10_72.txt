Fire involving an electrical box at a property in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/10/2016 - 19:54 Location: Sunningdale Close, Burtonwood, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Warrington There was a fire inside a semi detached property. The fire was caused by an electrical box. Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  Two hose reel jets and one covering jet were used to extinguish the fire. A large fan was used to clear the smoke. Smoke alarms activated within the property.
