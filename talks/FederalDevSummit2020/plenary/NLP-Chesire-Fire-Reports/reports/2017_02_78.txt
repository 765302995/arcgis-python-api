Tumble dryer fire Time of Incident: 18/02/2017 - 00:39 Location: Tatton Road, Wistaston. Attendance: Two fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Tatton Road, Wistaston.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving a tumble dryer. Firefighters used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for one hour and fifteen minutes.  The house was fitted with smoke alarms that helped alert the homeowner to the fire.
