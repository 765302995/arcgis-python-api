Kitchen fire at Tata Chemicals Europe Ltd in Northwich Time of Incident: 13/08/2016 - 18:24 Location: Tata Chemicals Europe Ltd, Works Lane, Northwich Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich, Winsford and Holmes Chapel A fire inside a kitchen at Tata Chemicals Europe Ltd was well alight.  Four firefighters wore breathing apparatus to tackle the fire using main jets, hose reel jets and covering jets. A cordon was established.  The fire crews extinguished the fire by 19:00pm, but then fire crews had to check the temperature of the wall separating the kitchen and a chemical store room.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the building.
