House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 18/11/2017 - 15:58 Location: Edleston Road, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Crewe Two fire engines from Crewe attended a fire in a first floor bedroom at a house in Crewe. The crews used  four breathing apparatus and two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire, along with a an industrial fan to clear the smoke.
