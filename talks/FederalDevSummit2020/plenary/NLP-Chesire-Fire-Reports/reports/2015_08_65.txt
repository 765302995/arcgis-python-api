Grain seperator on fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 18/08/2015 - 11:42 Location: Pinfold Lane, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Northwich Firefighters dealt with a fire involving a grain seperator in Knutsford.  Crews have gained access to the machine to unload the contents and empty it. One of the crew is wearing breathing apparatus and two hose reels and a covering jet are being used as well as a thermal imaging camera.  The firefighters prevented the fire entering the silos and all power was isolated. One person was given oxygen due to smoke inhalation.
