 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/02/2019 - 18:09 Location: Bancroft Road in Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 6.10pm firefighters were called to a report of a wheelie bin being on fire in the open on Bancroft Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew found the bin well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than 10 minutes. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Police on 101 or give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries. For wheelie bin fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
