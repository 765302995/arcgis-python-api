Bedroom fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/02/2016 - 07:17 Location: Cartwright Street, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a bedroom fire at a property in Cartwright Street. The crews tackled the fire and used a high pressure fan to clear the smoke. All people from the property were accounted for. An investigation into the cause of the fire is ongoing.
