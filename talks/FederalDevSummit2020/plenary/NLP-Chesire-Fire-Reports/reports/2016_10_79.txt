Car transporter fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/10/2016 - 19:43 Location: Winwick Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Two fire crews from Warrington were sent to an incident on Winwick Road in Warrington where a car transporter was on fire.  The fire involved the engine compartment of the vehicle. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus. Firefighters used two hose reels to extinguish the fire and a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
