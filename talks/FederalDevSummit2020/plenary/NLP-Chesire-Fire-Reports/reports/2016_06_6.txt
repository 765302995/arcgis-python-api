Wood fire in domestic garage in Paddock, Handforth Time of Incident: 28/06/2016 - 21:43 Location: Paddock, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle Firefighters were called to a fire involving a small quantity of wood inside a domestic garage at a premises on the Paddock, Handforth. Crews used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and a positive pressure ventilation unit to ventilate the garage.
