Bedroom fire in Chester Time of Incident: 15/06/2016 - 17:45 Location: Cambrian Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port fire station and one from Chester There was a fire involving a bedroom and the loft space of a semi detached house, so firefighters used one hose reel and one covering jet to extinguish the fire.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. A thermal image camera was used to check for hotspots.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke.  An ambulance was requested for the occupant who was suffering from smoke inhalation. Fire crews were one the scene for approximately 90 minutes.
