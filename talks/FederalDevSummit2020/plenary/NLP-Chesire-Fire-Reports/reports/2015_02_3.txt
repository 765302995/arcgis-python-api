Chimney fire reported in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 26/02/2015 - 08:44 Location: Beech Hall Drive, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield  Firefighters were called to a report of a chimney fire in Macclesfield.  The crew inspected the property with a thermal imaging camera and found there was  a fault with the diesel boiler but no sign of fire.  The incident was handed back to the owner.
