Deliberate bin fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 14/05/2016 - 23:32 Location: Wistaston Green Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A rubbish bin was deliberately set on fire in Crewe. Firefighters used water to put out the fire. Police also attended.  Arson can cost lives. If you know anything about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
