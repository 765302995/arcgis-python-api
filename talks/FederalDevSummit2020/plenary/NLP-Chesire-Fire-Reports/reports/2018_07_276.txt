 Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 17:11 Location: Castlefields Avenue East, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach Shortly after 5pm firefighters from Sandbach were called to reports of a grass fire on Castlefields Avenue East in Runcorn. On arrival the team found that the fire involved approximately 50metres by 100metres of scrubland. Crews used two hose reel jets and two beaters to tackle the fire, the then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. The team were in attendance for approximately two hours.
