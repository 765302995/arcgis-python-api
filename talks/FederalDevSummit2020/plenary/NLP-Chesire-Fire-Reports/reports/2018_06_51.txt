 Fires in woodland in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 17:23 Location: Hallwood Park Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 5.20pm firefighters were called to reports of fires in woodland on Hallwood Park Avenue in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered several separate seats of fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fires and then dampened the areas down to make them safe. They were in attendance for around 45 minutes. Police also attended. The fires are believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call police on 101.
