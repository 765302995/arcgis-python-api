Industrial bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 21/06/2017 - 02:11 Location: Crow Wood Lane, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh and one from Widnes Industrial bins in a bin shed were on fire on Crow Wood Lane. A hose reel jet was used to bring the blaze under control and firefighters are urging anyone with any information to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 as it is believed the fire was started deliberately.
