Three car collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 09/02/2015 - 17:19 Location: Pennys Lane, Northwich. Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich and Knutsford Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Pennys Lane in Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved three cars. Nobody was trapped but there was one injured woman who was treated by the ambulance service.
