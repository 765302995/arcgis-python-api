 Wheelie bin fire in Chester Time of Incident: 19/01/2019 - 22:31 Location: Townfield Lane, Chester Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Powey Lane A wheelie bin was reported on fire on Townfield Lane in Chester.  Firefighters arrived at the scene to discover the fire had spread to two nearby fence panels.  Crew used one hose reel jet to put out the fire.
