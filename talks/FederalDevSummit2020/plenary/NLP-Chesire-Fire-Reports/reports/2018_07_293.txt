 Peat fire in Rixton, Warrington Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 22:03 Location: Holly Bush Lane, Rixton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Crews attended to a peat fire on top of a gas pipe on Holly Bush Lane in Roxton, Warrington.  A water backpack and shovels were used by firefighters to help put out the flames.  The gas board were in attendance but no gas was found to be leaking from the pipe.
