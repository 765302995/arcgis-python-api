Collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 16/10/2015 - 22:37 Location: Between Junctions 9 and 10 on the M56. Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on between Junctions 9 and 10 on the M56.  When they arrived crews found that a car had collided with the central reservation. There was nobody trapped and so crews made the vehicle safe before leaving the scene.
