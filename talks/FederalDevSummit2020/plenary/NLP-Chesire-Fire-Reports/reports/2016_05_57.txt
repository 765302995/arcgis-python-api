Bathroom fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 20/05/2016 - 20:58 Location: Postles Place, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich A fire in the bathroom of a Northwich property was tackled by firefighters. The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bringt the fire under control.   After the fire, which involved an electric shower, was put out breathing apparatus wearers were increased to four as the property was ventilated using a high pressure fan and the electricity and gas supplies were isolated.  Everyone from the property was accounted for.
