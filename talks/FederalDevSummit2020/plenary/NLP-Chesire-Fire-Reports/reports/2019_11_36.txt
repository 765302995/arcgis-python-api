 Small fire in the open in Widnes Time of Incident: 21/11/2019 - 22:48 Location: Queensway, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 10.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Queensway, Widnes.  On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire involving metal cabling.  Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for more than 15 minutes.
