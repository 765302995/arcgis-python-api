Caravan well alight in Chester Time of Incident: 22/10/2016 - 11:32 Location: Chester Road, Dunham on the Hill, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Frodsham Two fire crews were sent to a report of a caravan fire at a farm on Chester Road in Dunham on the Hill, Chester. On arrival the fire crews found that the caravan was well alight.  Firefighters extinguished the fire using one hose reel jet. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  The incident has now been handed over to Cheshire Police.
