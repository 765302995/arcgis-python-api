Road traffic collison on Bickley Lane in Malpas Time of Incident: 27/08/2017 - 17:52 Location: Bickley Lane, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Whitchurch (Shropshire Fire and Rescue Service) Firefighters were sent to a road traffic collision involving one car and a tractor, where two people were trapped. Fire crews used cutting gear to release the casualties. They were then placed into the care of paramedics.
