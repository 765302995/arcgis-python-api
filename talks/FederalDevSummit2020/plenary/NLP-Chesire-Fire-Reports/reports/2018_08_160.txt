 Two trapped pigeons rescued in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/08/2018 - 09:54 Location: Union Square, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 9.55am firefighters were called to a report of two pigeons being stuck behind the glass cover of a second floor balcony window of a three-storey property in Union Square, Great Sankey, Warrington. After liaising with an RSPCA inspector, firefighters wearing working at height safety equipment used ladders to access the window from outside the building and free the birds. They were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
