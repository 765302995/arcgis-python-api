 Injured man rescued from scaffolding in Moulton Time of Incident: 02/09/2019 - 11:43 Location: Beehive Lane, Moulton Attendance: One fire engine attended from North. One fire engine attended from Lymm along with the Rope Rescue Unit. Firefighters were called to a building site on Beehive Lane, Moulton following reports of an injured man stranded on the first storey of scaffolding.  Fire crews, along with the rope rescue unit and paramedics brought the man to safety to be treated. Crews were at the scene for 60 minutes.
