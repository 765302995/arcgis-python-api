 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/11/2018 - 22:55 Location: Cromwell Road, Warrington Attendance: Cromwell Road, Warrington Firefighters from Warrington used a hose reel jet to extinguish a wheelie bin which had been deliberately set alight on Cromwell Road. The team were detained for approximately 20 minutes. Anyone with any information about the cause of the fire is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. For more information about wheelie bin security visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
