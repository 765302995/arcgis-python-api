 Flat fire in Chester Time of Incident: 29/03/2018 - 11:29 Location: Albion Street, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine and the aerial ladder platform from Chester and one fire engine from Powey Lane Firefighters attended reports of a kitchen fire in a second floor flat on Albion Street, Chester. On arrival crews found that the fire involved a cooker hood which was well alight.  They used one hose reel jet, two breathing apparatus and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire.  Two casualties were treated at the scene for smoke inhalation, they have since been passed to North West Ambulance Service.
