Garden fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/05/2016 - 02:33 Location: Sandy Lane West, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters responded to a report of a fire at a property off Sandy Lane West, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found a garden building on fire. A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
