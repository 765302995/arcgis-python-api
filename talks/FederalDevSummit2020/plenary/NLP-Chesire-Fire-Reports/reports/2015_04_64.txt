Fire in chip shop in Nantwich Time of Incident: 21/04/2015 - 15:41 Location: Welsh Row, Nantwich Attendance: Two from Crewe and one from Nantwich Firefighters have been called to a fire involving a fryer in a chip shop in Nantwich. All persons were accounted for and a hose reel jet was used to extinguish the fire which involved the fryer and extractor ducting. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property and a thermal imaging camera was required to check the flat above for spread of fire. One woman was taken to hospital suffering from smoke inhalation.
