Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/04/2017 - 19:26 Location: Windermere Avenue,Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters responded to a report of a fire on Windermere Avenue, Warrington.  When they arrived they found that there was a wheelie bin and a mattress on fire. A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out.
