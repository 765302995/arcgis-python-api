 Fridge fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 24/08/2018 - 22:46 Location: Malpas Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Firefighters attended to reports of burning at a house on Malpas Road in Northwich.   On arrival crews found a fridge on fire and, wearing four breathing apparatus, extinguished the fire using two hose reel and a positive pressure ventilation fan. The fridge was then removed from the property and placed in the garden.  Firefighters rescued a dog which was alone inside the property.  They were in attendance for under two hours.
