Flat fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 19/09/2016 - 20:47 Location: Middlewich Road in Sandbach. Attendance: A fire engine from Sandbach and Alsager Firefighters were called to a smell of burning at some flats on Middlewich Road in Sandbach.  The flats were evacuated while firefighters in breathing apparatus went into the building. They found that the fire had involved a broken radiator in an empty room.
