 Small fire at a children's play area in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 18/11/2018 - 17:52 Location: Grange Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 5.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a children's play area near to the Aldi supermarket on Grange Road in Ellesmere Port. The crew extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around half an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
