Road traffic collision in Burland Time of Incident: 25/11/2015 - 13:46 Location: Monks Lane/Ravens Lane, Burland, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Crewe One person was trapped in a vehicle involved in a road traffic collision on Monks Lane/Ravens Lane. Two cars were involved in the collision. Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to release the female casualty. Police, ambulance and the air ambulance were also at the scene.  Ravens Lane was closed by police while the incident was being dealt with.
