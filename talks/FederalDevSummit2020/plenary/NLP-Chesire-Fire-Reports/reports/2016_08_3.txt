Kitchen fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 30/08/2016 - 13:08 Location: Barbank Lane, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Warrington Firefighters have been tackling a fire in the kitchen of a Barbank Lane property. They found a well developed fire and, with two crew members in breathing apparatus, used a hose reel and a covering jet to bring the fire under control and stop it spreading. A high pressure fan was then used to ventilate the property, clearing the smoke.
