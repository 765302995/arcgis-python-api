 Grass fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/07/2018 - 00:22 Location: Gleadmere, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters attended a grass fire off Gleadmere, Widnes. Crews used beaters to extinguish the fire which had affected approximately two metres of grass. The fire is thought to have been started deliberately. Police have been informed.
