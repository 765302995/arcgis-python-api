 Man trapped following collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 08/07/2019 - 13:02 Location: Salters Lane, Lower Withington, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Macclesfield and one from Congleton - as did the rope rescue unit from Holmes Chapel Shortly after 1pm firefighters were called to a report of a man being trapped in a car following a one-vehicle collision in Salters Lane, Lower Withington, Macclesfield. Firefighters managed to get the casualty out of the car. They then administered first aid before handing the casualty over to the care of paramedics. He was taken to hospital via an ambulance. Firefighters were in attendance for around 50 minutes. Police attended to manage traffic.
