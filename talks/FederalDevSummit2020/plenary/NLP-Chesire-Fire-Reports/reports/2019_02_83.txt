 Fire in the open, Warrington Time of Incident: 15/02/2019 - 16:49 Location: Mersey Walk, Latchford Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to reports of a fire on the bank of the River Mersey off Mersey Walk in Latchford. They initially struggled to access the scene due to its location, but proceeded on foot to deal with the fire, which measured approximately 20m x 10m. They extinguished the flames using beaters, and were in attendance for around half an hour.
