 Two-vehicle collision in Hartford, near Northwich Time of Incident: 08/11/2019 - 12:50 Location: A556 in Hartford, near Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At 12.50am firefighters were called to reports of a collision on the A556 in Hartford, near Northwich.  On arrival the crew discovered that the collision involved a heavy goods vehicle and a saloon car.  The car had sustained fire damage.  The fire was extinguished before firefighters arrived at the scene.  The road was closed and firefighters damped the car down to make it safe.  Paramedics assessed a male who had been in the car.  Firefighters were in attendance for nearly an hour.
