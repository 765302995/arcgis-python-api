 Fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/10/2019 - 15:19 Location: Near to Fitzwalter Road and Marshall Road, Woolston, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 3.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open near to Fitzwalter Road and Marshall Road in Woolston, Warrington.  On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning that had got out of control.  Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
