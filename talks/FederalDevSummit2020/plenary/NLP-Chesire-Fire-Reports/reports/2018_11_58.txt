 House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/11/2018 - 19:14 Location: Sunningdale Close, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Warrington and one from Penketh. Firefighters from Penketh and Warrington attended reports of a house fire on Sunningdale Close.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a fire in the kitchen area. Firefighters used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The team then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property. The team were detained at the scene for approximately one hour.
