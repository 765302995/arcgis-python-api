 Hedge fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 17/12/2018 - 15:12 Location: Wallerscote Road, Weaverham, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly before 3.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Wallerscote Road in Weaverham, Northwich. On arrival the crew found a hedge well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and make sure that the area was safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour and 15 minutes.
