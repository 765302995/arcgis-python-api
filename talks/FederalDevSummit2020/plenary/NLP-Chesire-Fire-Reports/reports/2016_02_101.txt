Three car collision on the M53 Time of Incident: 05/02/2016 - 21:16 Location: M53 between Junctions 11 and 10. Attendance: A fire engine frim Chester Fire crews responded to a report of a collision on the M53 between Junctions 11 and 10.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved three cars. There was nobody trapped and so crews helped the Highways Agency remove debris before leaving the scene.
