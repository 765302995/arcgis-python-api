UPDATED - Cooker fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 26/10/2015 - 10:28 Location: Shakerley Avenue, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and one from Macclesfield Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus extinguished a cooker fire in Congleton. A dog was rescued by the crew. A large fan was used to clear smoke from the property.  The fire was caused by Duke, a Labrador, who was trying to get a birthday cake in a container off the hob. He managed to accidentally light the hob, causing the fire. Duke's owner was at work at the time of the incident.  Firefighters were alerted by neighbours who heard smoke alarms sounding and called 999.  Duke is fine following his ordeal.  Please follow our kitchen safety advice to prevent a similar incident.
