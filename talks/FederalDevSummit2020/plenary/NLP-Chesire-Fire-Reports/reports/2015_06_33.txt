Carpet on fire in Runcorn bedroom Time of Incident: 23/06/2015 - 00:29 Location: Chaucer Street, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A bedroom carpet had been on fire at a property in Runcorn.  While the fire was out when firefighters arrived, there was smoke in the room and the occupant, a lady, was suffering slight smoke inhalation.  The crew treated her until the ambulance arrived when the casualty was taken to hospital as a precaution.  Firefighters carried out a home safety assessment.
