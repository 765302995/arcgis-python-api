 Vehicle fire in Chester Time of Incident: 22/06/2019 - 18:12 Location: Whitchurch Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Chester and one from Winsford Shortly before 6.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a two-vehicle collision on Whitchurch Road, Chester. On arrival crews found that there had been a fire involving a vehicle.  The fire was out on arrival - a member of the public had used a fire extinguisher on the vehicle. Firefighters made the vehicle safe.
