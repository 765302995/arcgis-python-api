 Chip pan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/07/2019 - 05:40 Location: Mersey Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a chip pan fire at a house on Mersey Street, Warrington. The crew, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They then cleared smoke from the property using a large fan and checked the building using a thermal imaging camera. Crews were at the scene for 30 minutes. Chip pan safety
