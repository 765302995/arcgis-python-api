Road traffic collision in Chester Time of Incident: 23/09/2015 - 06:00 Location: Whitchurch Road, Christleton, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester, one from Winsford and the rapid response vehicle from Winsford Two vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision, leaving two people trapped in a car.  One man and one woman were released from the car, which had been in a collision with a heavy goods vehicle. Firefighters had to remove the car's roof to be able to get them out.  Both casualties were handed into the care of the ambulance service and crews made the scene safe.
