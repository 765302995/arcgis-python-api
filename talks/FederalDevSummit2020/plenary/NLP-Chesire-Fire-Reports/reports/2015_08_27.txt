Fire in Warrington cafe Time of Incident: 25/08/2015 - 23:34 Location: Annie's Cafe, School Road, Orford, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel and a covering jet to extinguish a fire in a ground floor cafe in Warrington.  A large fan was also used to ventilate the building and the crews were at the scene for over an hour.
