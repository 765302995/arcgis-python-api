 Fire in a kitchen in Moston Time of Incident: 08/10/2019 - 13:19 Location: Martin Close, Moston, near Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port At around 1.20pm firefighters were called to reports a fire in the kitchen of a domestic property in Martin Close, Moston, near Chester.  The fire involving a pan that had been left on a stove was out on arrival.  Firefighters used a large fan to remove smoke from the property and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.  For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
