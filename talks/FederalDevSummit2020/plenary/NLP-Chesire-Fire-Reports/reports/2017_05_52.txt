Hay bale on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 22/05/2017 - 18:52 Location: Access to Allotment Gardens (Factory Lane), Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to a hay bale which was on fire near to the allotments on Factory Lane, Widnes. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
