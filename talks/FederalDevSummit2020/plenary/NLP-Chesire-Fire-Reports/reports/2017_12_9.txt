Two vehicle collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 27/12/2017 - 02:22 Location: M6 southbound between junction 18 and 17 Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Sandbach Fire crews were called to a collision between a car and a HGV on the M6 southbound between junction 18 and 17. The car involved was well alight on arrival of the crews and they used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. There was no injuries as a result of this incident. Crews were in attendance for two hours.
