Kitten rescued after two days in a tree in Nantwich Time of Incident: 26/02/2015 - 12:40 Location: Barony Court, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich The RSPCA asked for the Service's help to rescue a kitten that was stuck in a tree 30 feet above ground.  It had been there for two days.  Firefighter Lee Burgess climbed a seven metre ladder, steadied in the strong winds by the rest of his crew, to return the light grey kitten to its owner.
