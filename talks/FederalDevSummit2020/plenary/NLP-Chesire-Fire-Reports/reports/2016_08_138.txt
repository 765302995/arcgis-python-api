Wheelie bins on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/08/2016 - 00:29 Location: Saxon Terrace, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Runcorn Wheelie bins close to a building had been set on fire at the back of a Saxon Terrace property. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to bring the blaze under control. They are urging anyone with any information about wheelie bin fires in the area to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.  Always follow our wheelie bin safety advice to prevent fires which can damage property.
