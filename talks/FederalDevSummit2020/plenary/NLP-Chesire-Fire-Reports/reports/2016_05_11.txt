Serious collision in Chester Time of Incident: 28/05/2016 - 15:37 Location: Whitchurch Road, Chester. Attendance: Fire engines from Malpas and Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Whitchurch Road, Chester.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved a car and a HGV. Firefighters set about helping paramedics with two casualties in the car. They also used environmental protection equipment to stop a diesel leak.  They remained at the scene for about 2 hours.
