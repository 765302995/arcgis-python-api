Coach wheel on fire on M6 near J16 Time of Incident: 19/02/2016 - 18:25 Location: M6 southbound, Junctions 17 to 16 Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel, one from Crewe and the rapid response vehicle from Sandbach The rear wheel of a coach on the hard shoulder of the M6 was on fire.  Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used dry powder extinguishers to put out the fire. Lanes one and two of the M6 southbound were closed while emergency services dealt with the incident.
