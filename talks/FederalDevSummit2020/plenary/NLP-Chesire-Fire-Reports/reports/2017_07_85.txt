Large fire in a disused building in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/07/2017 - 17:04 Location: Ditton Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes, one from Lymm, one from Penketh, one from Warrington, one from Northwich, one from Birchwood, one from Knutsford and one from Ellesmere Port. Large fire in a disused building in WidnesFire crews were called to a fire in a disused office block on Ditton Road, Widnes. Crews used two main jets to tackle the blaze. Cheshire Police closed the road in both directions whilst firefighters dealt with the fire.  The fire was extinguished by approximately 00:46 and firefighters remained at the scene overnight to monitor the building using thermal imaging cameras.
