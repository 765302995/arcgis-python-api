 Small fire in the open in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/07/2018 - 15:48 Location: Southern Expressway, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish a small fire in a woodland area near to the Southern Expressway in Runcorn. Crews were detained at the scene for approximately 20 minutes. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
