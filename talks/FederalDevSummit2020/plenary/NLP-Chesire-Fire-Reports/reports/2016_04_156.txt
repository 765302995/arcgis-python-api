Kitchen on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 01/04/2016 - 04:23 Location: Weston Grove, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended Firefighters were called to a kitchen on fire in a mid terraced property and used one hose reel and a covering jet to extinguish the fire. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property. All persons were accounted for at this incident which involved an unattended pan. Two people were treated by paramedics.
