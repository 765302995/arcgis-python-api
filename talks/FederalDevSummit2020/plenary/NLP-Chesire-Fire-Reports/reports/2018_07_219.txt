 Tree stump fire in woodland in Northwich Time of Incident: 05/07/2018 - 20:37 Location: Station Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 8.35pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Station Road in Delamere, Northwich. On arrival the crew found a tree stump smouldering in woodland. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
