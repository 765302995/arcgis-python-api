 Collision between a bus and a car in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/03/2019 - 15:58 Location: Southwood Avenue, Runcorn. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Runcorn and Widnes At approximately 4pm firefighters from Runcorn and Widnes were dispatched to reports of a collision between a bus and a car on Southwood Avenue, Runcorn.  On arrival at the incident the team found that one person was medically trapped in the car.  The team isolated the battery of the car to make the vehicle safe. They then used specialist electronic cutting equipment to release the casualty, who was then passed to the care of paramedics.  The team were detained at the scene for around 40 minutes.
