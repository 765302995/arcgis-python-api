Road traffic collision in Disley Time of Incident: 25/12/2016 - 12:12 Location: Mudhurst Lane, Disley Attendance: Fire engines from Poynton, Offerton and New Mills One person was trapped in a car after it had collided with a tree. Cutting equipment was used by the crews to remove the roof of the car and release the casualty, a man, who was then taken to hospital by air ambulance.
