 Wheelie bins fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/04/2018 - 19:02 Location: Brathay Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 7pm firefighters were called to a fire in the open involving wheelie bins in Brathay Close in Warrington. They used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Police on 101.
