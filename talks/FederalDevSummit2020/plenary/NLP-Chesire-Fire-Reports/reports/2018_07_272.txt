 Large grass fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 18:31 Location: St Michaels Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At 6.30pm crews from Widnes attended a large grass fire off St Michaels Road, Widnes. On arrival the team found that there was various seats of fire, totalling approximately 80 square metres. Firefighters used one hose reel jets and beater to extinguish the fires and were in attendance for approximately one hour.
