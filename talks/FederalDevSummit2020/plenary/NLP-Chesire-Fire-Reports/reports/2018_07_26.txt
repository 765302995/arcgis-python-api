 Road traffic collision in Prestbury Time of Incident: 27/07/2018 - 16:19 Location: Mill Lane, Mottram St Andrew, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one fire engine attended from Macclesfield Two fire engines from Macclesfield and Wilmslow attended a road traffic collision on Mill Lane in Mottram St Andrew, near Macclesfield.  On arrival of the fire service there were four casualties who were out of the vehicle and one further casualty trapped.  Crews released the casualty by removing the roof of the vehicle and the casualty was then transported to hospital via air ambulance.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately one hour and 40 minutes.
