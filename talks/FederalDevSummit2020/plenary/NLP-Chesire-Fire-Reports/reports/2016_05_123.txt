Garden fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/05/2016 - 23:08 Location: Mottershead Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes A well-developed fire in the garden of a Widnes house was tackled by firefighters. The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the blaze. There was heavy smoke logging to the ground floor of the house which was cleared using a high pressure fan.
