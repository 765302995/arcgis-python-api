 Smoke alarm triggered at business premises in Crewe Time of Incident: 24/06/2019 - 13:44 Location: Westmere Drive, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to a business premises in Westmere Drive, Crewe, following reports of a smoke alarm sounding. The building was evacuated and the crews discovered that an under counter water heater had triggered the alarm. The electricity supply to the water heater was isolated. Firefighters used a tool kit to ensure that the water heater was cooled down sufficiently and a thermal imaging camera to monitor its temperature. Once they were satisfied that the water heater was safe the staff were allowed to return to the building. Firefighters were in attendance for less than 20 minutes.
