Fire in the open in Northwich Time of Incident: 17/03/2016 - 10:36 Location: Manchester Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Arson is believed to be the cause of a fire in the open in Northwich. A fire involving bushes was put out by a Northwich crew, who asked police to attend for traffic management.  Did you see anyone setting this fire? Is you have any information please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.  Arson is the number one cause of fire in the UK.
