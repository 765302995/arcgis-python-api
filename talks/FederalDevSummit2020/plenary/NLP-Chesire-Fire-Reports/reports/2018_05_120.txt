 Fire in wooded area in Winsford Time of Incident: 15/05/2018 - 19:14 Location: Vauxhall Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich At around 7.15pm firefighters were called to a fire in a wooded area on Vauxhall Way in Winsford. The crew used water to extinguish the fire involving tree branches. Firefighters were in attendance for around 15 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information about the fire should call Cheshire Police on 101.
