Hay loft on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 04/01/2015 - 17:35 Location: Butterton Lane, Crewe Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe, Sandbach and Widnes Firefighters were called to a hay loft which was well alight in Haslington, Crewe. Firefighters used two hose reels and a main water jet to extinguish the fire which was thought to have been started accidentally.The roof of the building was totally destroyed in the fire and the hay was removed from the building using farm machinery. A thermal imaging camera was then used to check that the fire was extinguished and part of the wooden floor was cut away during this process.
