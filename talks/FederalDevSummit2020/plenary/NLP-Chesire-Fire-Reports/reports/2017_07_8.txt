Overturned lorry in Littleton Time of Incident: 28/07/2017 - 13:51 Location: A41/A51 Tarvin Road, Littleton, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Overturned lorry in LittletonFirefighters released the driver of a lorry from the cab after it overturned on the A51 at Littleton. The lorry was carrying concrete which spread over the road at the A55 junction with the M53.
