Chimney fire in Spurstow Time of Incident: 09/10/2016 - 20:01 Location: Badcocks Lane, Spurstow Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley Firefighters were called to a chimney which had been on fire and used chimney rods to extinguish hot spots of fire. A thermal imaging camera was used to the check area and the loft was also checked before the crew left. Chimney safety advice
