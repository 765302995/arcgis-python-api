 Fire in the grounds of a church in Northwich Time of Incident: 06/06/2018 - 10:49 Location: Highfield Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 10.50am police were called to a report of a fire on Highfield Road in Northwich. The crew extinguished the small fire in the grounds of a church with water. Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
