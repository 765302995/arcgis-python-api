 Man trapped following a collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 25/06/2019 - 13:15 Location: Manchester Road in Lostock Gralam, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Northwich and one from Lymm At 1.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a one-vehicle collision on Manchester Road in Lostock Gralam, Northwich. One arrival crews discovered that a saloon car had collided with a wall and that the driver was trapped inside it. The road was closed as emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters got the casualty out of the car and handed him over to the care of paramedics. He was taken to hospital via an air ambulance. Firefighters were in attendance for around 80 minutes.
