One man trapped after collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/10/2017 - 07:06 Location: Long Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one fire engine from Birchwood One fire engine from Warrington and one fire engine from Birchwood attended a collision involving a car and a moped on Long Lane. Crews assisted paramedics in releasing one man who was trapped in the moped. They were at the scene for 50 minutes.
