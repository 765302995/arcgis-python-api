Fire at electrical substation in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/10/2016 - 17:30 Location: Mildenhall Close, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Widnes A suspected deliberate fire was set against the door of an electrical substation in Great Sankey. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to tackle the flames.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
