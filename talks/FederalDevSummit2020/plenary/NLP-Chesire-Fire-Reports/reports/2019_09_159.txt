 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 01/09/2019 - 18:52 Location: Cinnamon Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington attended. One fire engine from Birchwood attended. Firefighters were called to an address in Cinnamon Lane, Warrington following reports of a wheelie bin on fire in an outhouse. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to tackle the flames.
