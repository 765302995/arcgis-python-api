 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/03/2019 - 09:25 Location: Castle Green, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Penketh. Shortly before 9.30am Firefighters from Warrington and Penketh were dispatched to reports of a domestic building fire on Castle Green, Burtonwood and Westbrook.   On arrival at the scene the team discovered a fire in the kitchen area of the building.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The team then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.  One casualty was treated at the scene by paramedics for smoke inhalation and the team were detained at the scene for approximately 50 minutes.
