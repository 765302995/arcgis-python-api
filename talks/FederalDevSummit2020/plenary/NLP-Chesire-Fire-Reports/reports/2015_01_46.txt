Flat fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 19/01/2015 - 03:04 Location: Wood Acres Court, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilsmlow and one from Greater Manchester An elderly man was treated for smoke inhalation after a fire at a flat in Wilmslow.  The fire involved a mattress and firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish it.  A large fan was also used to clear the smoke and the mattress was removed outside to damp down.  The fire was caused by an electric blanket.
