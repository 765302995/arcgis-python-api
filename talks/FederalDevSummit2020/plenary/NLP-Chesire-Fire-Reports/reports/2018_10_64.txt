 Person rescued from river in Northwich Time of Incident: 21/10/2018 - 05:04 Location: Chester Way, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly after 5am firefighters were called to reports of a person stuck in the river near to Chester Way. Crews attended and used a short extension ladder to help the person climb up the embankment to safety. The person was left in the care of North West Ambulance Service.
