Man cut free from car in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/03/2015 - 19:24 Location: Hallfields Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington One man was trapped in a car and had to be cut free by firefighters after a collision in Warrington. The crew removed the roof and the man was left in the care of paramedics along with a woman who was walking wounded.
