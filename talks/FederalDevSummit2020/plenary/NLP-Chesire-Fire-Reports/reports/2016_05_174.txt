Person suffered burns following a small fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 01/05/2016 - 17:39 Location: Nutfield Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe fire station Crewe firefighters were sent to a small fire outside a property on Nutfield Avenue in Crewe. The occupant had used a small can of petrol to ignite a fire, but unfortunately suffered burns to face and arms in the process. The fire was already out when the fire crew arrived.  The casualty was treated by paramedics.
