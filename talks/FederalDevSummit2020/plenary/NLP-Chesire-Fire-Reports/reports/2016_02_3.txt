Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/02/2016 - 22:53 Location: The Bennett Recreation Ground, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Warrington A hose reel jet was used to put out a wheelie bin fire on The Bennett Recreation Ground, Warrington.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have information to find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
