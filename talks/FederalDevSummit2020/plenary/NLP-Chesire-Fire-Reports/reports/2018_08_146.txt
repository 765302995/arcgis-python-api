 Small fire in wooded area in Widnes Time of Incident: 05/08/2018 - 17:06 Location: Moorfield Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 5.05pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire within a wooded area at The Bongs on Moorfield Road in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a small amount of wood. They used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 45 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
