 Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 13/06/2018 - 23:48 Location: Brookdale, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to Brookdale, Widnes, after a reported vehicle fire. On arrival crews found a car well alight and the crew, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The fire was believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire then please contact Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
