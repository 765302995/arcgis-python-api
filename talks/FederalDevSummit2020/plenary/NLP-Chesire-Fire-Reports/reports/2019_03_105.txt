 Fire at a farm in Marbury Time of Incident: 07/03/2019 - 15:28 Location: Wirswall Road, Marbury Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Audlem and one Whitchurch in Shropshire Shortly before 3.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a farm on Wirswall Road in Marbury. On arrival crews discovered a fire involving a pickup truck and a bale. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus each used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which destroyed the pickup and damaged the bale. Firefighters were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.
