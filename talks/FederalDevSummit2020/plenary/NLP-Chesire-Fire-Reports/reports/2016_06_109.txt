Fridge leak in Bollington Time of Incident: 11/06/2016 - 07:32 Location: Hillcrest Road, Bollington. Attendance: A fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Firefighters responded to a report of a strong chemical smell at a property on Hillcrest Road, Bollington.   On arrival fire crews confirmed the smell was in the kitchen.  They used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to investigate. One small fridge  was the cause of the smell. This was removed to the open air.  One woman was taken to hospital for a precautionary check up.
