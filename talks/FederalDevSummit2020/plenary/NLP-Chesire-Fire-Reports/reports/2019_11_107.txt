 Collision in Stockton Heath Time of Incident: 09/11/2019 - 11:43 Location: Nr Fairfield Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Lymm Fire crews from Warrington and Lymm were called to a three vehicle collision near to Fairfield Road.  Firefighters assisted other emergency services and made the vehicles safe. They were at the scene for 40 minutes.
