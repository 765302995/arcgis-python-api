Wheelie bin on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 21/08/2017 - 02:25 Location: West Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A wheelie bin fire, which affected a nearby property by setting light to the curtains of a flat, was extinguished using one hose reel.  The curtain fire was out on arrival of the firefighters, who believe the fire may have been started deliberately. If anyone knows anything about who may have started this fire they should  call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
