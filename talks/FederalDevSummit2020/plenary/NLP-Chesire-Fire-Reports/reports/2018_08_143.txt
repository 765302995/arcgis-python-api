 Small fire in a garden in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/08/2018 - 18:14 Location: Waterside, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath At around 6.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a garden in Waterside, Appleton, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered a small amount of refuse on fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
