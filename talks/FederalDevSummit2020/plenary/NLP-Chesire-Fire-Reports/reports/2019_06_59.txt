 House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/06/2019 - 10:21 Location: Densham Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Crewe and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Firefighters were called to a house in Densham Avenue, Warrington, following a report of a fire. The fire involved a shower on the ground floor of the property. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet and a covering jet to put out the fire. Crews then cleared smoke from the house using a large fan. Firefighters were at the scene for half-an-hour.
