Car fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 15/09/2017 - 04:41 Location: Bridgeway West, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a car fire on Bridgeway West, Runcorn. Crews found a saloon car well alight and used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The fire is a suspected arson attack. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 84 of the 15th September or call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
