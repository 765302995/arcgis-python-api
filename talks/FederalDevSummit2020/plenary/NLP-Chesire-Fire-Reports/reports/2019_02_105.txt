 Fire at a flat in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/02/2019 - 10:12 Location: Wilson Patten Street, Warrington Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Penketh, one from Lymm and one from Birchwood At around 10.10am firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from a flat on Wilson Patten Street in Warrington town centre.  On arrival all persons were accounted for. Two firefighters entered the first floor flat and extinguished a small fire using a hose reel jet. Fire crews were in attendance for around 40 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started by a lit cigarette that had been left unattended.  For safety advice visit Smoking / fire safety tips for smokers
