 Bonfire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 31/03/2019 - 11:14 Location: Blue Bridge Lane, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane and one from Widnes Fire crews were called to a bonfire on Blue Bridge Lane, Frodsham. The smoke from the bonfire was drifting over the M56 so crews used two hose reel jets to put out the fire. A farmer assisted crews by using a excavator to spread the bonfire. Crews were at the scene for two hours.
