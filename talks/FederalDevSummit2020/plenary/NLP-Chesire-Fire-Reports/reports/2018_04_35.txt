 Fire in the open in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 22/04/2018 - 21:07 Location: Palmerston Street, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington Firefighters tackling a fire in the openAt 9.07pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in Palmerston Street in Bollington, Macclesfield.  On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a small quantity of straw and loose material.  Firefigthters used a hose reel jet to put the fire out and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
