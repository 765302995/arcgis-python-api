Woman trapped after collision in Scholar Green Time of Incident: 02/12/2017 - 18:25 Location: Congleton Road North, Scholar Green. Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and two fire engines from Staffordshire and West Midlands Fire Service attended A fire engine from Congleton and two fire engines from Staffordshire and West Midlands Fire Service attended Congleton Road North, Scholar Green following a collision between a car and a van. A woman who was trapped in the car was released by firefighters using a long board. Fire crews remained at the scene for one hour.
