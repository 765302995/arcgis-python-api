 Building fire, Chester Time of Incident: 25/02/2018 - 05:34 Location: Shotwick Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters responded to reports of a building fire, but found the incident involved a smouldering beam caused by a wood burning stove which had caused smoke alarms inside the domestic property to activate.  Crews used a high pressure hose reel, positive pressure ventilation fan, a thermal imaging camera and cutting away tools to deal with the incident, and they were there for approximately 90 minutes.
