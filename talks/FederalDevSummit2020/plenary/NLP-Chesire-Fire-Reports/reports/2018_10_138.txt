 Road traffic collision, M6 near Warrington Time of Incident: 05/10/2018 - 19:26 Location: J21A to J21 southbound, M6 motorway Attendance: One appliance from Lymm Firefighters responded to reports of a collision between a heavy goods vehicle and a saloon car on the M6 motorway, on the southbound carriageway between junctions 21A and 21. On arrival, crews found the car in the central reservation. Neither driver was trapped, though one was passed to the care of paramedics. Lane closures were put in place while the vehicles were moved to the hard shoulder.
