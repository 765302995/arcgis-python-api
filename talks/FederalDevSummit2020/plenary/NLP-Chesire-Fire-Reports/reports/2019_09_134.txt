 Smoke issuing from floorboards in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 06/09/2019 - 00:56 Location: Luton Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended an address in Luton Road, Ellesmere Port following reports of smoke issuing from floorboards on the ground floor.    Crews wearing breathing apparatus lifted the floorboards in the living room and checked the temperature of the electrical wiring. They discovered they were increasing in heat. Electricians attended and the electric was isolated.  They then removed floorboards and cut away parts of joists which had been affected by charring and used water to cool the affected area.
