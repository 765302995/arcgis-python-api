Lift flooded in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/06/2016 - 04:03 Location: Wilderspool Causeway, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Weather conditions caused a lift and electric cupboard to flood at a Wilderspool Causeway flat complex. Firefighters asked for lift engineers to attend and isolated the electricity supply. They cleared all the drains around the complex and drained the water from the lift and electric cupboard.
