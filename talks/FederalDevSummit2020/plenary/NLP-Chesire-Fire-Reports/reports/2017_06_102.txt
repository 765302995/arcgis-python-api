Garden waste on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/06/2017 - 17:56 Location: Thelwall Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington A pile of garden waste was set on fire at the end of Thelwall Lane. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire, which is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
