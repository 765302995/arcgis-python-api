Shed on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 05/12/2016 - 10:38 Location: Timbrell Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a wooden shed which was well alight and used a hose reel to extinguish it. One man suffered from smoke inhalation and needed the assistance of paramedics. Timbrell Avenue was closed by police while the incident was dealt with
