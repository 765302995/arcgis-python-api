 Vehicle fire in Moston Time of Incident: 21/03/2019 - 23:43 Location: Dragons Lane, Moston Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Middlewich and one from Sandbach Fire crews attended to a car well alight on Dragons Lane in Moston.  On arrival the fire had spread to a telecoms pole and a tree. Wearing two breathing apparatus, two hose reels were used to extinguish the fire.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check for hot spots and a hose reel was then used to dampen down the area.
