 Small fire in the open in Knutsford Time of Incident: 22/12/2018 - 16:00 Location: Chester Road, Over Tabley, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 4pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Chester Road in Over Tabley, Knutsford. They extinguished the small fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
