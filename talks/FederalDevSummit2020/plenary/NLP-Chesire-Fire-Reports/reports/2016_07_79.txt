Mattress and fence on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/07/2016 - 15:46 Location: Hood Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Warrington Crews were called to a fire involving a mattress and approximately two metres of fence. On arrival, firefighters discovered there had been some heat damage to the house and used a hose reel jet to dampen the area.
