Suspended ceiling collapses in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 13/09/2016 - 23:59 Location: Hulley Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Approximately ten square metres of a suspended ceiling in a single storey building collapsed and firefighters were called to the scene. Crews isolated the gas and electrics of the building before carrying out a health and safety assessment. Meanwhile, two females, believed to have been in the vicinity when the ceiling collapsed, were assessed by paramedics.
