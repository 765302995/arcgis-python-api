 Grassland fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 13:55 Location: Hooleyhey Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Bollington Firefighters dealt with a grassland fire measuring approximately four metres by four metres on Hooleyhey Lane in Macclesfield. They used water and beaters to fight the fire.  UPDATE at 3pm:  Firefighters made good progress as they try to extinguish the fire.  UPDATE at 4pm:  They damped the area down and checked for any hotspots or flare ups.
