Chip pan fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 19/01/2015 - 17:57 Location: Dunster Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: Fire engines from Macclesfield and Bollington Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Dunster Road, Macclesfield.   When they arrived they found that the incident involved a chip pan.  Crews used a hose reel and a dry powder extinguisher to put the fire out. A woman was treated at the scene after suffering from inhaling smoke.  Chip pan safety advice
