 Mobile crane fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 19/11/2019 - 07:07 Location: A54, Tarporley Attendance: Four fire engines attended - one from Chester, one from Northwich, one from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Mobile crane fire in TarporleyFire crews attended to reports of a mobile crane on fire on Kelsall Hill in Tarporley.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, one hose reel jet and two hose reels have been used to put out the fire. The crane was checked using the thermal imaging camera.  The A54 has been closed in both directions with traffic diverting through Tarvin due to fuel leaking onto the carriageway.  Crews were in attendance for four hours and 20 minutes.
