 Vehicle fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 20/03/2018 - 23:41 Location: Parklands, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 11.40pm firefighters were called to a vehicle fire at Parklands in Little Sutton, Ellesmere Port. Crews found a car well alight on the road and used a hose reel jet and breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames. They were at the scene for nearly an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.
