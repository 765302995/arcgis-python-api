Smokelogged building in Mobberley Time of Incident: 16/02/2017 - 04:21 Location: Alderley Road, Mobberley Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and two from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Firefighters were called to determine the cause of smoke filling the first floor of a building on Knutsford Road. Crews, using a thermal imaging camera, found overheating in a floor void. A heating engineer isolated the system.
