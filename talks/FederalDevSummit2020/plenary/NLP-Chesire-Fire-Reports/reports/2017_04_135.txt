Grass fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/04/2017 - 15:47 Location: Winwick Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Warrington Around a quarter of an acre of grass was well alight in a field off Winwick Lane.  Two fire crews used beaters to put out the fire. The fire was extinguished by 18:30pm.   This fire was believed to have been set alight deliberately.  If you can help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
