Fire in a kennel Time of Incident: 24/02/2016 - 18:06 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Fire crews responded to a report of a fire at a property off Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived firefighters found a fire involving wooden boarding in a kennel. Crews used a hose reel jet and  one breathing apparatus to put the fire out.  There were no reports of any injuries.
