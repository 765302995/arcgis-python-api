Shed fire in Chester Time of Incident: 30/12/2018 - 01:42 Location: Western Approach, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a shed fire on Western Approach, Chester. On arrival the fire had spread to some nearby trees. Crews used a hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to put out the fire. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
