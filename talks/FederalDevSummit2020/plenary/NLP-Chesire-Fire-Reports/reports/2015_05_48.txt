Chimney fire in Newhall Time of Incident: 22/05/2015 - 20:49 Location: Whitchurch Road, Newhall. Attendance: A fire engine from Audlem Firefighters were called to deal with a chimney fire on Whitchurch Road, Newhall.  Crews used chimney rods and hose reel jets to put it out. They also carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving the scene.
