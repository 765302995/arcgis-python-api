Wood burner on fire in Peckforton Time of Incident: 18/03/2015 - 13:46 Location: Peckforton Castle, Stone House Lane, Peckforton Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford, one from Malpas and the aerial appliance from Chester A fire involving a log burning stove was tackled by fire and rescue crews.  The stove was removed by firefighters and embers cleared and extinguished.  The chimney was checked with a thermal imaging camera.
