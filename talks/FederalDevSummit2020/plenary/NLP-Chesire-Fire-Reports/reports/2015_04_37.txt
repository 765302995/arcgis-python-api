Man burnt by bonfire Time of Incident: 25/04/2015 - 11:19 Location: Watery Lane, Astbury. Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton A man was taken to hospital after suffering burns to his hands and face after petrol was thrown on a bonfire.  The incident took place on Watery Lane in Astbury. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the bonfire whole paramedics treated the man.
