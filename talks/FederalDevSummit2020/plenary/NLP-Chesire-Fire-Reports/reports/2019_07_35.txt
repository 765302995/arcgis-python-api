 Tractor fire in Chester Time of Incident: 24/07/2019 - 15:48 Location: Fox Covert Lane, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a tractor on fire in a field off Fox Covert Lane, Chester. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to tackle the fire which was well alight. They then damped down the vehicle and isolated the battery.
