Wheelie bins on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 22/06/2017 - 22:53 Location: Earle Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Two wheelie bins which are believed to have been deliberately set on fire were extinguished with a hose reel. If anyone knows who may have started this fire they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimstopppers on 0800 555 111.
