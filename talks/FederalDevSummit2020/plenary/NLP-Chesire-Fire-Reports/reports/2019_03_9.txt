 Unattended bonfires in Prestbury Time of Incident: 28/03/2019 - 13:07 Location: Dumbah Lane, Prestbury Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to three unattended bonfires on Dumbah Lane, Prestbury. Crews extinguished two using a hose reel jet and allowed the third to burn.
