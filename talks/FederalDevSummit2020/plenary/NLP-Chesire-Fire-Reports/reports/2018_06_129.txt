 Garage fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/06/2018 - 18:28 Location: Cartmell Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 6.25pm firefighters were called to reports of a garage at the back of a house being on fire in Cartmell Close, Runcorn. On arrival crews discovered a small fire in a brick-built garage. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire, which caused damage to facias and soffits of the garage. They then dampened the area down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
