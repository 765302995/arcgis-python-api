 Skip fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/10/2019 - 21:16 Location: Albert Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 9.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Albert Road in Widnes.  On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a skip in an alleyway.  Firefighters extinguished the fire using two hose reel jets.  They then damped the skip down to make it safe.  Firefighters were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
