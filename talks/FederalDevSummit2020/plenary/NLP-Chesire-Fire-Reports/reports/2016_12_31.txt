Tumble dryer fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 26/12/2016 - 22:23 Location: Swan Street, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Cheshire and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Tea towels in a tumble dryer caused a fire at a Swan Street property. Firefighters removed the dryer from the property and used a high pressure fan to clear smoke from inside. They checked the area thoroughly to make sure there was no fire spread.  Always follow our tumble dryer safety advice.
