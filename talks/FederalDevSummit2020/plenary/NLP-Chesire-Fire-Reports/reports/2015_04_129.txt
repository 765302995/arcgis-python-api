Fence fire in Chester Time of Incident: 14/04/2015 - 09:38 Location: Stratford Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port A fence to two properties in Chester was destroyed by a fire believed to have been started deliberately.  One hose reel was used to extinguish the blaze and police informed.  Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101 or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
