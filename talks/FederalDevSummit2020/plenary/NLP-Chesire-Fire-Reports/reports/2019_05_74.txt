 Skip fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/05/2019 - 20:20 Location: Heath Road, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At 8.20pm firefighters from Runcorn were dispatched to reports of a skip fire in  the grounds of the Town Hall.  The fire involved a large industrial skip full of wood cuttings which was well alight.  Crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. They then used a main jet to dampen down at the scene.  Firefighters were detained for around one hour 30 minutes.
