Rubbish fire left unattended in Malpas Time of Incident: 23/06/2017 - 18:03 Location: Wrexham Road, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas Firefighters were called to rubbish which was burning and had been left unattended. The crew used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which involved wood and a thermal imaging camera to check the fire was out.
