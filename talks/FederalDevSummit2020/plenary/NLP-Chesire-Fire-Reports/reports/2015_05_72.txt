Cooker fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 16/05/2015 - 17:52 Location: Grizedale Close in Wistaston Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Two fire engines from Crewe were sent to a property on Grizedale Close in Wistaston o deal with a small fire involving the cooker.  The fire was out when the fire crew arrived.  One casualty was suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation, so was given oxygen therapy by firefighters.  Kitchen fire safety tips
