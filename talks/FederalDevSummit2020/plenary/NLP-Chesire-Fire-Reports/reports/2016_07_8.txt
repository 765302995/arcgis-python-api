Hay fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 28/07/2016 - 22:35 Location: Bell o' the Hill Road, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and one from Shropshire Fire and Rescue Service Firefighters put out a controlled burning because of its location. It was too close to a hay barn which could have caught fire. The crews put out the fire and then damped the area down.
