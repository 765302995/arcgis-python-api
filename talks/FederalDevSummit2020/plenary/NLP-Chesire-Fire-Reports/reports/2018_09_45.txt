 Fire at a house in Hunsterson Time of Incident: 21/09/2018 - 04:23 Location: Pewit Lane, Hunsterson. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich and Audlem. Firefighters attended reports a the smell of smoke at a house in Pewit Lane, Hunsterston. Crews discovered a fire in the cavity and ground floor and first floor. They damped down the area and dug out the affected area. They then used ladders to get to the roof to remove tiles and the affected batons to fully extinguish the fire.
