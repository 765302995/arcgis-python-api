 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/12/2018 - 17:57 Location: Near Oakland Street, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters from Widnes attended a small fire near to Oakland Street where two wheelie bins had been deliberately set alight.   The team used one hose reel was used by crews to extinguish the fire. For information on how to ensure your wheelie bins are kept secure please follow our safety advice https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
