Shrubland on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 15/05/2016 - 20:40 Location: Weston Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A fire crew had to make their way on foot to 50 square metres of shrubland on fire. The firefighters used a hose reel jet and hand extinguishers to put out the blaze.
