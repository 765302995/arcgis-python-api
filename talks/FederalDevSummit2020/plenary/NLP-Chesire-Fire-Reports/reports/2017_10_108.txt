Van on fire, Twemlow Green Time of Incident: 06/10/2017 - 10:35 Location: Chelford Road, Twemlow Green Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and one from Holmes Chapel Fire crews were called following reports of a van on fire in Chelford Road, Twemlow Green. Firefighters used two hose reel jets and breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames. The road was temporarily closed while crews dealt with the incident.
