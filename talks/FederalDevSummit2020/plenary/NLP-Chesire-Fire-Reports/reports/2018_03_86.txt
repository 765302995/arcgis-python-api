 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/03/2018 - 10:31 Location: Powder Mill Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington A fire in a saloon car was tackled by firefighters. A hose reel jet was used to put out the blaze, with one firefighter wearing breathing apparatus. The dashboard was then cut away to check there were no further hotspots.
