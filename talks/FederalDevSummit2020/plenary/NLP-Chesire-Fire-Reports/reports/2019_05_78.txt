 Fuel tank spillage in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 20/05/2019 - 17:21 Location: Oil Sites Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port At 5.20pm Firefighters from Ellesmere port were called to reports of a fuel spillage on Oil Sites Road.  On arrival the crew discovered approximately 100 litres of fuel had leaked from a tanker near to junction 9 of the M53.  Firefighters contained the spillage and a clean up company were called to the site.  Crews were detained at the scene for around five hours.  The Environment Agency have been informed of the incident.
