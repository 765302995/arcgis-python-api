Tree and grass fire in Plumley Time of Incident: 09/07/2015 - 18:08 Location: Pinfold Lane in Plumley. Attendance: Fire engines from Knutsford, Holmes Chapel and Northwich Firefighters responded to a call about a fire off Pinfold Lane in Plumley.  When they arrived crews found that there was a large amount of tree debris and cut grass on fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.
