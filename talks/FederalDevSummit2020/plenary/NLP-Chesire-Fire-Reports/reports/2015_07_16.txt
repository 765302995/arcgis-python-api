House fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 27/07/2015 - 19:16 Location: Chetwood Drive, Widnes. Attendance: A fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Chetwood Drive, Widnes.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire involving candles close to some window blinds. This had already gone out.
