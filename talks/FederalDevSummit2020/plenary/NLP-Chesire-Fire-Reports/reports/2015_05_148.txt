Garage fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 03/05/2015 - 19:51 Location: Tall Ash Avenue, Congleton. Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and one from Holmes Chapel. Firefighters were called to a report of a garage fire on Tall Ash Avenue, Congleton.   The incident involved a double garage with a vehicle inside which was well alight on arrival.  Crews requested the electricity board to isolate the power to the garage and then used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to deal with the fire.  One woman was taken to hospital for a check up.
