 Man on bridge, Chester Time of Incident: 10/10/2019 - 06:17 Location: Northgate Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester. One fire engine attended from Powey Lane. One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port. One fire engine attended from Lymm. Firefighters were called to a bridge on Northgate Street, Chester following reports of concern for safety of a man on the bridge.  Crews were on standby with the rope rescue unit, swift water raft, rescue sled and trauma kit. Police negotiated with the man and he came down from the bridge. He was then taken to hospital.
