 One vehicle collision on Chester High Road, Neston Time of Incident: 22/10/2018 - 18:09 Location: Chester High Road, Neston Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane. Shortly after 6pm firefighters were called to reports of a one vehicle collision on Chester High Road, Neston. Firefighters from Powey Lane and Ellesmere Port were dispatched to the scene and on arrival they found that a car had collided with a lamp post and one person was trapped in the vehicle. The team used specialist cutting equipment to release the casualty from the vehicle. The man was then passed to the care of paramedics.  Crews were in attendance at the scene for approximately one hour.
