House fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 11/07/2015 - 00:05 Location: Belgrave Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: A fire engine from Macclesfield. Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Belgrave Road, Macclesfield.  When they arrived crews found that there was a small fire inside the property but this was out when they arrived.
