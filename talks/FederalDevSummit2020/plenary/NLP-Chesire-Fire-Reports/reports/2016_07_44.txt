Deliberate fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 22/07/2016 - 12:27 Location: Cloud View, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton A fire believed to have been started deliberately damaged a park bench in Congleton. Firefighters put out the fire using a hose reel jet and informed police of the incident. The park area has had to be closed for the next 24 hours.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
