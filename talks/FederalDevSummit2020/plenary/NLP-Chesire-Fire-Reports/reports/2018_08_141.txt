 Off-road motorbike fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/08/2018 - 20:37 Location: Hoylake Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 8.35pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving an off-road motorbike in Hoylake Close, Runcorn. On arrival the crew found the bike to be well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They then moved the motorbike to the side of the road and set up a small road closure. Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes. Police also attended.
