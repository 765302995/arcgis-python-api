 Fire in a silo in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/01/2018 - 07:16 Location: Percival Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Widnes Firefighters extinguished a fire in a silo at an industrial unit on Percival Lane, Runcorn. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which was in a dust unit.
