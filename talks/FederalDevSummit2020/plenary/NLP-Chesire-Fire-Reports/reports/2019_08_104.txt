 Bin fire at a park in Widnes Time of Incident: 13/08/2019 - 21:08 Location: Crow Wood Park, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters used a water backpack to extinguish a bin fire at Crow Wood Park in Widnes.  The crew were in attendance for around 10 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with any information is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 485211. Information can also be reported anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111. .
