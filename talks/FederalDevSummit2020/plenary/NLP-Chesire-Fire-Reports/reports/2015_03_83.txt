Dishwasher on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/03/2015 - 09:08 Location: Malvern Close, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington There had been a fire involving a dishwasher at a property in Great Sankey, Warrington.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. Firefighters removed the dishwasher from the property.  A large fan was used to clear the smoke from the property.  Washing machine and dishwasher fire safety
