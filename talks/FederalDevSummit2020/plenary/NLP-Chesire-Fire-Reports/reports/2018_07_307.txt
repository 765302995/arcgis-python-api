 Heathland fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 15:38 Location: Ivychurch Mews, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 3.35pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in woodland at Ivychurch Mews in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered around 20 square metres of heathland well alight. Firefighters used a main jet, a water backpack and beaters to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
