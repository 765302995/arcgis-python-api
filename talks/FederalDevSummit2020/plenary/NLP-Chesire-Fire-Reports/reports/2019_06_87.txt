 Cellar flooding in Wrenbury Time of Incident: 12/06/2019 - 10:10 Location: Cholmondeley Road, Wrenbury Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Nantwich and one from Sandbach Firefighters were called to a pub on Cholmondeley Road in Wrenbury following reports of a cellar being flooded. The crews used two pumps to remove flood water from the cellar. The depth of the flood water was approximately two feet. Firefighters were in attendance for around six hours.
