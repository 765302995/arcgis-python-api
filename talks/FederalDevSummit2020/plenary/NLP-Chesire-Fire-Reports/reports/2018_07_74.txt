 Fire involving rubbish at derelict building in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 23/07/2018 - 06:51 Location: Rossmore Road East, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two firefighters attended from Ellesmere Port At around 6.50am firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a derelict building on Rossmore Road East in Ellesmere Port. On arrival crews discovered a fire involving a small quantity of rubbish inside the building. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire using water. Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
