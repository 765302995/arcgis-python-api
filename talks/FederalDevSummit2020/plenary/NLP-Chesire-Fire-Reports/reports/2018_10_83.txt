 Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 18/10/2018 - 01:18 Location: Bateman Close, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to a car fire on Bateman Close, Crewe. Crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with information is asked to call Cheshire Police.
