 Minibus fire on the M53 Time of Incident: 17/07/2019 - 10:37 Location: M53 junction 9 to 10 southbound Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Fire crews were called to a minibus fire on the M53 southbound between junction 9 and 10. All the passengers were accounted for on arrival of the crews and the minibus was well alight. Crews closed the motorway and firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The motorway has partially reopened but one lane remains closed whilst arrangements are made to pick up the passengers.
