Fire in wood store in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 13/07/2017 - 09:59 Location: Chester Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield, one from Bollington A fire in the ground floor of a wood store required firefighters to attend. A sprinkler system activated containing the fire to the room of origin and crews damped down with a hose reel and an extinguisher.
