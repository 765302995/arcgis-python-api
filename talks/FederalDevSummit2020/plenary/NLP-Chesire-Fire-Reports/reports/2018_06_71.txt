 Bathroom fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/06/2018 - 03:13 Location: Cambridge Street, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes. One fire engine from Penketh. One fire engine from Runcorn. Firefighters were called to a bathroom fire at an address in Cambridge Street in Widnes. Two firefighters in breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish the fire. All persons were accounted for and one adult casualty was given oxygen therapy.
