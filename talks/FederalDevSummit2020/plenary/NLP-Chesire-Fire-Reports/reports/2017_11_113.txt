Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 05/11/2017 - 16:28 Location: Westmorland Close, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington. One fire engine from Macclesfield. One fire engine from Wilmslow. Firefighters attended the kitchen of a restaurant following reports of heavy smoke. Crews used a large fan to disperse the smoke. Three casualties were treated for smoke inhalation by paramedics.
