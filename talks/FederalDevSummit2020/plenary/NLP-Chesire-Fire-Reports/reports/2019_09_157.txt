 Caravan fire in Burtonwood Time of Incident: 01/09/2019 - 22:49 Location: Alder Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. Firefighters were called to a caravan on fire next to a pub in Alder Lane, Burtonwood. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been deliberate.
