Fire in a garage in Runcorn Time of Incident: 14/06/2017 - 04:46 Location: Woodall Drive, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Widnes Fire crews were called to a fire in a detached garage on Woodall Drive, Runcorn. The fire involved an electricity meter inside the garage. Firefighters, wearing  breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to put out the fire.
