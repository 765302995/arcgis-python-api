Kitchen fire in Mobberley Time of Incident: 12/08/2015 - 17:43 Location: Summerfield Road, Mobberley Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Greater Manchester Two people were given oxygen after a kitchen fire in Mobberley. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke caused by a plastic lid being left on a hob.  Please be careful and don't leave anything on top of your cooker. Kitchen fire safety advice
