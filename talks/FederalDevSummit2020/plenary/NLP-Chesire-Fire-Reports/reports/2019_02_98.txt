 Shed fire in Mobberley Time of Incident: 13/02/2019 - 14:52 Location: Knutsford Road, Mobberley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford and one from Wilmslow Fire crews were called to a shed fire on Knutsford Road, Mobberley. Crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire which was well alight on arrival.
