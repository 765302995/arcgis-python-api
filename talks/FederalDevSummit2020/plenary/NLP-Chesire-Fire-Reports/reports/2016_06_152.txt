House fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 04/06/2016 - 16:35 Location: Beech Close, Knutsford. Attendance: A fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Beech Close, Knutsford.  When they arrived crews found that the fire had involved a lamp fitting but this had gone out. Crews checked the loft of the property and found that it was heavily smoke logged.  Crews used a large fan to clear the smoke. They also sawed away some burnt timbers to prevent further fires.  A Home Safety Assessment was also carried out before they left the scene.
