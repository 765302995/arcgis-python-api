Vehicle fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 08/12/2015 - 08:05 Location: Augusta Drive, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A dry powder extinguisher was used to ensure a vehicle fire was fully extinguished in Macclesfield.  The fire was out on arrival of the crews who also used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
