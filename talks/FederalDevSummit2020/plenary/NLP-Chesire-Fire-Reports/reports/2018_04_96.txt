 Smoke coming from electrical cupboard at care home in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 13/04/2018 - 08:28 Location: Old Chester Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines in attendance from Ellesmere Port At around 8.30am firefighters were called to reports of smoke coming from an electrical cupboard at Whetstone Hey residential care home on Old Chester Road, Ellesmere Port. On arrival crews found smoke coming from the cupboard and used a thermal imaging camera to investigate. The electricity board attended and assessed the electrics as fire crews stood by as a precaution.
