 Outside bin fire damages domestic property in Knutsford Time of Incident: 06/07/2018 - 02:54 Location: Springfields, Knutsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford At around 2.55am firefighters were called to a report of fire involving a bin and a trellis in Springfields, Knutsford. On arrival the crew discovered a bin bag well alight outside a domestic property and that the fire had damaged the building. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and then damped the area down and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. They were in attendance for around an hour and 15 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information about the incident should call police on 101, give the details via General Enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
