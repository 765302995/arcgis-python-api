 Man trapped following a collision in Chester Time of Incident: 03/06/2018 - 14:44 Location: Chester Road, Huntington, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port At around 2.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision near the Rake and Pickle pub on Chester Road in Huntington, Chester. On arrival crews discovered that the incident involved one vehicle and a man was trapped inside it. Firefighters extracted the casualty and handed him over to the care of paramedics. They then made the area safe. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes. Police also attended the scene.
