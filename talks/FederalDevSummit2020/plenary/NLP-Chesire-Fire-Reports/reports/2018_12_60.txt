 One-vehicle collision in Helsby Time of Incident: 17/12/2018 - 00:58 Location: Chester Road, Helsby Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port One vehicle collision in HelsbyShortly before 1am firefighters were called to reports of a one-vehicle collision on Chester Road in Helsby.  Firefighters from Ellesmere Port attended the scene and on arrival they found that a gritter had collided with a domestic property.  The team used specialist equipment to get the driver out of the vehicle. They were then passed over to the care of paramedics as a precautionary measure.  The crew also isolated the gas and electricity supplies to the address and a structural engineer was called to the scene.    Firefighters were in attendance for approximately an hour.
