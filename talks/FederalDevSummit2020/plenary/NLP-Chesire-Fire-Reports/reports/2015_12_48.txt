Sofa fire caused damage to flat in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 18/12/2015 - 01:51 Location: Sutton Way, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A fire involving a sofa which had been left outside a flat was extinguished by firefighters. Two hose reels were used to extinguish the fire which involved the sofa and the front door to flats. Smoke entered the three story block of flats via the damaged door so a large fan was then used to remove the smoke.  Two dogs were taken from a ground floor flat and were unharmed. A thermal imaging camera was then used to check that there was no spread of fire.  Paramedics attended to deal with one casualty.  A fire investigation into the cause of this fire will take place but it is thought that the fire was started deliberately.
