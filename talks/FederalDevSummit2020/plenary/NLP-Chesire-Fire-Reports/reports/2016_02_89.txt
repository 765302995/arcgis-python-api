Chimney fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 09/02/2016 - 21:31 Location: Church Road, Malpas Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas Firefighters were called to a chimney fire in Malpas. The chimney was well alight. Crews shovelled out cinders and coals before tackling the blaze with a chimney hose and a high pressure hose. As the hearth area was being damped down, firefighters used a ladder to reach the chimney top from the outside of the property to use a hose reel jet externally. They then checked both the inside and outside of the chimney with a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no further hot spots.  Please follow our chimney safety advice - Chimneys
