 Chimney fire in Chester Time of Incident: 09/12/2018 - 20:33 Location: Victoria Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 8.30pm firefighters were dispatched to reports of a chimney fire at a residential address on Victoria Road, Chester. Crews attended the scene and used a thermal imaging camera to examine the chimney breast in the fire floor flat. After finding the seat of the fire, crews used specialist chimney rods and a chimney hose to extinguish the fire. The then re-checked the chimney breast with the thermal imaging camera to ensure the fire had been extinguished. Crews were detained at the scene for approximately two hours. For further tips and advice about how to prevent chimney fires visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/chimney-fire-safety.
