 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/10/2019 - 21:15 Location: Alder Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Crews were called to a wheelie bin fire on Orford Park, Alder Lane, Warrington. On arrival the wheelie bin was well alight. firefighters used water to extinguish the flames.
