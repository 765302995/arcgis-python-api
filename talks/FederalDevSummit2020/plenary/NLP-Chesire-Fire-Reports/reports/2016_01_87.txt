Furniture fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 07/01/2016 - 19:41 Location: Barnard Close, Macclesfield Attendance: A fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Barnard Close, Macclesfield.  When they arrived crews found that there was some furniture on fire near some garages. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call 0800 555 111.
