 Wheelie bins on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/04/2019 - 09:45 Location: Gaunts Way, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Runcorn and one from Widnes Firefighters were called to three wheelie bins on fire in Gaunts Way, Runcorn. The bins were close to a domestic property and the fire caused damage to its front windows. Four occupants were safely out of the address when fire crews arrived. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The cause of the fire is being investigated. For advice on preventing wheelie bin fires visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
