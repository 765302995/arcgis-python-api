Tumble dryer fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 05/09/2015 - 09:47 Location: Dickens Lane, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton and one from Greater Manchester Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel to extinguish a tumble dryer fire in Poynton.  The machine was in an outbuilding and a large fan was also used to clear the smoke. Tumble dryer fire safety advice
