Van fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 25/07/2015 - 03:14 Location: Warwick Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A van fire well alight on arrival of firefighters is believed to have been started deliberately.  One of the crew wore breathing apparatus and used one hose reel to put out the fire. Police were requested to attend the scene.  Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101 or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
