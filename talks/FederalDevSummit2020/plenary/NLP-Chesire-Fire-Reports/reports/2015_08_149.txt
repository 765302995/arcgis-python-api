Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 07/08/2015 - 22:59 Location: Queens Park Drive, Crewe. Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a car fire on Queens Park Drive, Crewe.   It is believed that the fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
