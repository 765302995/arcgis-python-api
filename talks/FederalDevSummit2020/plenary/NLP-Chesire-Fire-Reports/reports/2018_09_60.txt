 Man rescued after becoming trapped in machinery in Cholmondeston Time of Incident: 19/09/2018 - 02:48 Location: Winsford Road, Cholmondeston Attendance: Two fire engines from Nantwich, two from Crewe and a fire engine and the major incident rescue unit from Winsford Fire crews were called to Winsford Road, Cholmondeston, after a report of an accident on a railway line. One man, who was working on the line, had become trapped in machinery. Crews and paramedics freed the casualty before handing him into the care of paramedics. He had sustained a serious injury and was taken to hospital. A second casualty with minor injuries had been released prior to the fire service's arrival. Crews were at the scene for two and half hours.
