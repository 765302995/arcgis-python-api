Front door set alight in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/02/2017 - 21:27 Location: Leicester Street, Bewsey and Whitecross, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Penketh Firefightes believe that a front door of a house had been set alight deliberately. Fortunately everybody was safe and nobody needed medical treatment. A fire investigation was carried out.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
