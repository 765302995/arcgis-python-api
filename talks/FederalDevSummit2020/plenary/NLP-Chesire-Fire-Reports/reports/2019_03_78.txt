 Three-car collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 13/03/2019 - 17:14 Location: Pexhill Road, Siddlington, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Macclesfield and one from Congleton At around 5.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a three-car collision at the junction of Pexhill Road and the A34 in Siddlington, Macclesfield. On arrival crews discovered that no-one was trapped inside any of the vehicles. Everyone involved in the collision was assessed by paramedics. Firefighters made the cars safe and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
