 Smouldering bonfire, Runcorn Time of Incident: 02/11/2019 - 23:31 Location: Newquay Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to assist with the extinguishing of a bonfire. There had been a small, organised bonfire earlier that evening, but the organisers were struggling to fully extinguish it. The crew used a single hose reel and a stack drag to spread out the embers, and were able to put it completely out after around 50 minutes.
