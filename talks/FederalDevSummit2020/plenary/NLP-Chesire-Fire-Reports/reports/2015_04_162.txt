House fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 10/04/2015 - 18:50 Location: Harris Street, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Harris Street in Widnes.  When they arrived crews were confronted with smoke coming out of the first floor window. Four fighfighters went into the property to tackle the fire with hose reel jets. They carried out a full search of the property but found nobody at home.  A fire investigation concluded the cause as accidental.
