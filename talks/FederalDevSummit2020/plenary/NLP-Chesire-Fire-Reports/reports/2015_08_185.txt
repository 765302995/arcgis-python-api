Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/08/2015 - 09:47 Location: Mckee Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels to extinguish fire in a Warrington Kitchen.  An ambulance was requested to attend the scene due to three people suffering from smoke inhalation. They were taken to hospital to be checked over.
