Pub fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 24/12/2017 - 09:45 Location: Welsh Row, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe At 9:45am two fire engines from Crewe were deployed to reports of a fire at the Black Lion pub on Welsh Row Nantwich. Firefighters attended the scene and found that the fire was contained in an outbuilding at the back of the pub. Crews used two breathing and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, and a temporary road closure was also put in place. Firefighters also used a large fan to clear the smoke from the premises. The cause of the fire is believed to be accidental.
