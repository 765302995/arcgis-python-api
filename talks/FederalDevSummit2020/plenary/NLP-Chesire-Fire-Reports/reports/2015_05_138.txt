Fire involving hearth in Northwich Time of Incident: 05/05/2015 - 11:57 Location: Forest Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford, one from Northwich and one from Tarporley Firefighters were called to a fire involving a hearth in a semi detached property. The crew used a hose reel to extinguish the fire and a large fan to remove smoke from the property. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the property for spread of fire and floor boards were cut away to help with this search.
