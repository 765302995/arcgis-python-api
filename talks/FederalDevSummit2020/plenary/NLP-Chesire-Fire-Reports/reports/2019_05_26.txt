 Halogen bulb fire in Chester Time of Incident: 26/05/2019 - 19:53 Location: Hough Green, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Fire crews attended to a fire at a house on Hough Green in Chester.  The fire was out on arrival however a large fan was need to disperse heavily logged smoke from the property.  The fire was caused by combustible items being left close to a halogen bulb.  Nobody was injured during the fire.  Firefighters would like to take this opportunity to remind residents that light bulbs give off a lot of heat when in use and should not be placed close to other items as it could result in a fire.
