Quad bike and garage fire in Chester Time of Incident: 25/07/2015 - 17:32 Location: Rake Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters extinguished a fire involving a quad bike and a garage in Chester.  The garage was approximately 5m x 5m and the crews used two hose reels to put out the fire. A large fan was used to clear the smoke and a thermal imaging camera  to check for hotspots and ensure the fire had not spread to the house next door.  Firefighters were on the scene for around 70 minutes.
