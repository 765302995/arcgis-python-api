Road traffic collision on the M56 Time of Incident: 23/11/2016 - 13:43 Location: M56 eastbound, junction 10 Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters have been called to a road traffic collision involving one heavy goods vehicle on the M56, junction 10 at the eastbound entry. The vehicle left the road and came to rest on its side on the embankment. Firefighters handed the driver into the care of the ambulance service.
