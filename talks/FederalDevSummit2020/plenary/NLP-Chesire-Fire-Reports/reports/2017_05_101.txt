Car fire in Rudheath Time of Incident: 14/05/2017 - 18:07 Location: A556/Penny's Lane, Rudheath, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Two hose reel jets were used to put out a fire in a car which was next to a gas pumping station. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus. British Telecom poles and cables were also affected by the fire.
