 Fire in a woodland in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 29/06/2018 - 11:06 Location: Prestbury Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters from Macclesfield used one hose reel jet to extinguish a fire in a wooded area off Prestbury Street in Macclesfield.
