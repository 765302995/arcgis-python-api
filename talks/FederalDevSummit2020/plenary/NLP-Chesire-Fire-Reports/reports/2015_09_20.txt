Road traffic collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 27/09/2015 - 15:23 Location: Hough Green Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Two casualties were treated by firefighters and ambulance crews after a van hit two pedestrians. A land ambulance and air ambulance attended the scene. The driver of the van, a man, was one of the casualties and the other casualty was a lady. Sadly one person lost their life as a result of this incident.
