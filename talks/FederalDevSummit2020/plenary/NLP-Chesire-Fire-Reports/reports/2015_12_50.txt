Skip fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 17/12/2015 - 12:27 Location: Alder Court, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a skip fire in Macclesfield. Upon arrival crews found a well established skip fire and used a hose reel jet to extinguish it. It is believed that this fire was caused by discarded smoking materials.
