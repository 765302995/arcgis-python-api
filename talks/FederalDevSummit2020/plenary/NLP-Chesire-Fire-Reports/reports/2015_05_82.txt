Man's hand released from shed wall Time of Incident: 14/05/2015 - 19:30 Location: Ballater Crescent, Great Boughton Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a man who had his hand stuck to the wall of a shed. One fire engine from Chester went to the scene where a man had got his hand trapped by a nail in the wall of the shed. The firefighters used a saw to release the casualty who was left in the care of paramedics. They were at the scene for 30 minutes.
