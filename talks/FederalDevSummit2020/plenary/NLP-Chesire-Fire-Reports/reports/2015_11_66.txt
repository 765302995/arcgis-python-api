Smoke cleared in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 17/11/2015 - 20:57 Location: Marina Walk, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port A large fan was used to clear security smoke that had activated at a shop in Ellesmere Port.  Firefighters were initially called to a report of smoke coming from a building. Police were in attendance at the scene and it was established that the artificial smoke was from a security activation and not as a result of a fire.
