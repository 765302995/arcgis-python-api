 Road traffic collision in Middlewich Time of Incident: 10/10/2019 - 21:58 Location: Bostock Road,Middlewich Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford and one from Northwich A road traffic collision involving one car on its side required firefighters to work with paramedics to get a woman out of her vehicle. The collision happened just before 10pm and the road was closed in both directions.
