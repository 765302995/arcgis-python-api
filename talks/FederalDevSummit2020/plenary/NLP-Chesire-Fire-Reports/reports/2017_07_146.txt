Fire involving a boiler in Chester Time of Incident: 03/07/2017 - 20:02 Location: Marsh Lane, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port There was a fire involving a biomass boiler located in an out house that had also spread to the fuel stack.  The fire was already out when the fire crew arrived. Firefighters checked for hotspots using a thermal imaging camera.  And then they damped the area down.
