 Fire in the open in Crewe Time of Incident: 15/04/2019 - 18:04 Location: Frank Webb Avenue, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe used one hose reel jet to extinguish a tree which had been deliberately set alight in a field off Frank Webb Avenue.  The team were detained at the scene for around 30 minutes.  Anyone with any information in relation to the fire is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML375097. Information can also be reported anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111.
