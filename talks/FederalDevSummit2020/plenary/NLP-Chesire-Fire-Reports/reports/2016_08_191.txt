Evacuation of shopping centre in Widnes due to smoke from electrics Time of Incident: 03/08/2016 - 15:18 Location: Green Oaks Shopping Centre, Widnes, WA8 Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters were called to a shopping centre in Widnes following a reports of smoke. Crews examined the building with a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots of fire and to investigate the fuse and electric cupboard. Crews then isolated the electrics and an electrician was called to the scene to carry out a further investigation. Following this, the centre was declared safe for the public to re enter.
