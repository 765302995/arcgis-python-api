Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/06/2016 - 23:41 Location: Leatham Close, Birchwood, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Deliberate ignition is suspected in the case of a car fire in Birchwood. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and asked for police to attend the incident.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 1122 of 9 June.
