Car fire on M62 east, junctions 9 to 10 Time of Incident: 09/03/2017 - 14:55 Location: M62 eastbound, junctions 9 to 10 Attendance: Fire engines from Birchwood and Warrington Smoke was coming from the engine compartment of a small vehicle on the M62 eastbound. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to tackle the incident. The crews also fended off traffic around the vehicle until a recovery vehicle arrived.
