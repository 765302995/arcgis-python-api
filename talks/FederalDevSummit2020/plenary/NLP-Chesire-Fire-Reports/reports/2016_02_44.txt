Car on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 19/02/2016 - 22:51 Location: Croft Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Arson is suspected as the cause of a car on fire in Croft Court. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the blaze involving a saloon car. They asked police officers to attend.  Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
