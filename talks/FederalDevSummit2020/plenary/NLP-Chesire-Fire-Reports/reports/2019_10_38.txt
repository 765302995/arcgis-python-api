 Vehicle fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/10/2019 - 01:26 Location: Blackburne Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish a car well alight on Blackburne Avenue in Widnes.  The fire is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault in the engine department.  The fire had caused slight damage to the front of the house.
