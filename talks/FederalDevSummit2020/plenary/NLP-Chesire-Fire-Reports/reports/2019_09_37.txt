 Chimney fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 23/09/2019 - 17:57 Location: The Brow, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Crews attended to a fire involving a chimney on The Brow in Frodsham.  Firefighters used a seven metre ladder and roof ladder to access the top of the chimney. A hose reel was then used to extinguish the embers on top of the chimney stack.  The thermal imaging camera was then used to check the temperature.
