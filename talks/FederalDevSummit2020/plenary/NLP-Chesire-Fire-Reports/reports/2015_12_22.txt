Flat fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 26/12/2015 - 13:00 Location: Castle Street, Northwich. Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich and Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of fire at a flat on Castle Street, Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that a woman's microwave had exploded filling her flat with smoke.  The woman was taken to hospital after suffering from the effects of inhaling smoke.
