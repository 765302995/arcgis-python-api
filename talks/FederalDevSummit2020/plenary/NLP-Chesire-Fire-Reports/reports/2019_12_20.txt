 Road traffic collision, Sandbach Time of Incident: 07/12/2019 - 00:15 Location: Congleton Road, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines from Sandbach and Holmes Chapel Crews turned out to a road traffic collision that had occurred near the Chimney House Hotel. It involved one vehicle, and a female casualty was trapped and suffering with chest pains. She was safely removed from the vehicle by firefighters, before being passed into the care of paramedics.
