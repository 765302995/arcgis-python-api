 One vehicle collision in Congleton Time of Incident: 13/11/2018 - 13:26 Location: Leamington Road, Congleton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters attended reports of a dangerous structure following a collision in Congleton. On arrival at the scene crews found that a car had collided with the front wall of a house on Leamington Road. The crews isolated the gas and electricity supply and made the vehicle safe. The team were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
