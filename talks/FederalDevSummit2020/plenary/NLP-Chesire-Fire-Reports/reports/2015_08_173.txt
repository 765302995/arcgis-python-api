Tractor fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 04/08/2015 - 16:54 Location: Shays Lane in Winsford. Attendance: A fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Shays Lane in Winsford.  When they arrived they found that there was a tractor on fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.
