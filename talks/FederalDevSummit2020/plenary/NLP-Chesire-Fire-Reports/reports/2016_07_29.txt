Car fire at Knutsford Service Station southbound Time of Incident: 24/07/2016 - 11:49 Location: M6 Knutsford Service Station southbound Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a report of a car on fire at Knutsford Service Station on the southbound M6. The engine compartment was cooled using a hose reel jet and checked with a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no further areas of heat.
