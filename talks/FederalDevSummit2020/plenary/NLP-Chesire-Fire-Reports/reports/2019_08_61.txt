 Light fitting fire at commercial premises in Chester Time of Incident: 22/08/2019 - 08:11 Location: Knutsford Way, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Powey Lane and one from Chester Shortly after 8am firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at Camlok Lifting Clamps on Knutsford Way, Chester. On arrival the crew could smell electrical burning and found a fire involving a light fitting. Firefighters thoroughly checked the area using a thermal imaging camera and left once they were satisfied that it was safe. They were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
