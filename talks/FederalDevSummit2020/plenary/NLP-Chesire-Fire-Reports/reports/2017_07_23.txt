Road traffic collision in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 23/07/2017 - 03:43 Location: A34, wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and two from Cheadle in Greater Manchester Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision with one person trapped. Two people were out of the vehicle and the crew released one female who was handed to the care of paramedics. Police were in attendance at this incident.
