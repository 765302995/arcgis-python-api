 Small fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/03/2018 - 20:33 Location: Lunts Heath Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to investigate a smell of smoke in a property on Lunts Heath Road, Widnes. After a thorough search of the property crews found a small smouldering fire in a neighbouring property which they extinguished using a bucket of water.
