Commercial building fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 08/09/2017 - 06:18 Location: Gainsborough Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Fire crews were called to a commercial building fire on Gainsborough Road, Crewe. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a large fan to put out a small fire. Crews then checked the premises using a thermal imaging camera.
