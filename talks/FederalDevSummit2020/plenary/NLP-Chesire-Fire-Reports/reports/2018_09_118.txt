 Smoke coming from a heavy goods vehicle on the M62 Time of Incident: 07/09/2018 - 12:18 Location: M62 eastbound, between Junctions 8 and 9 Attendance: Three fire engines attended - one each from Widnes, Birchwood and Penketh At around 12.15pm firefighters were called to reports of smoke coming from the rear wheels of a heavy goods vehicle on the hard shoulder of the slip road prior to Junction 9 of the M62 eastbound. On arrival crews discovered that the HGV's rear brakes had overheated. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to cool down the brakes. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
