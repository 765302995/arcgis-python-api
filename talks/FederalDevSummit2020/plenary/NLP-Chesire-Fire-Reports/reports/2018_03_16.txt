 Tree on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 27/03/2018 - 17:47 Location: Townfield Lane, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas and one from Sandbach Tree on fire in Chester Firefighters were called to land off Townfield Lane in Farndon following reports of a tree well alight. Due to the tree being 400metres from the road, crews used a tac line extinguisher while other firefighters carried equipment to the scene.  Crews extinguished the fire then began damping down the tree to prevent hotspots reigniting. Police were in attendance. Anyone with information should call police on 101.
