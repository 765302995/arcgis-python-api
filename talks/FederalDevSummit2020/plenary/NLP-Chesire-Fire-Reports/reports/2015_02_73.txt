False alarm in Chester Time of Incident: 11/02/2015 - 12:11 Location: Watergate Street, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester, two from Ellesmere Port and one from Winsford. Firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from a building on Watergate Street in Chester.  When they arrived crews set up a cordon to investigate the incident. They found no sign of a fire and believe that the smoke may have been from a log burner located in one of the shops.
