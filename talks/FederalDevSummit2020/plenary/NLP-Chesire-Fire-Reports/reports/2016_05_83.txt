Building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/05/2016 - 07:24 Location: Wash Lane, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A faulty electrical unit on the ground floor of a shop caused a small fire. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke from the property.
