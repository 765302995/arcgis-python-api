Heron rescue in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 10/03/2016 - 15:36 Location: Bank Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: A fire engine from Macclesfield and the Animal Rescue Unit from Knutsford. Firefighters were called to help rescue a Heron that had got its leg stuck off Bank Street, Macclesfield.  Crews released the bird who was passed to the care of the RSPCA.
