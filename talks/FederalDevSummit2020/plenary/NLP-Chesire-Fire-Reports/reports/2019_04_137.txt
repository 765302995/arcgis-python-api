 Fire in a woodland area in Wharton Time of Incident: 15/04/2019 - 19:10 Location: Weaver Valley Road, Wharton, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters from Winsford used one hose reel jet to extinguish a quantity of logs which had been deliberately set alight in a woodland area off Weaver Valley Road in Wharton.  The team were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.  Anyone with any information in relation to the fire is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101. Information can also be reported anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111.
