 Smoke alarm activated in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/07/2019 - 09:55 Location: Villars Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 10am firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a domestic property in Villars Street, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that cooking fumes had activated the alarm. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the fumes and were in attendance for around 10 minutes. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
