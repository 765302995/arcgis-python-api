 Fire in the open in Winsford Time of Incident: 25/07/2018 - 22:59 Location: Bradford Road, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Shortly before 11am firefighters from Winsford were called to reports of a fire in the open near to the salt mine on Bradford Road, Winsford. The fire was out on arrival, but the team used a water back pack to dampen down at the scene to prevent reignition. The team were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately and anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
