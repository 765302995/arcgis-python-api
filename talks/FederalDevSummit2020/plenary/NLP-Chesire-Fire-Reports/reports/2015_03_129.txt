House fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 06/03/2015 - 08:36 Location: Elworth Road, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Sandbach Three fire engines were called to a report of a fire involving a semi-detached house.  Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, tackled the fire using two hose reel jets.  A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke from the property.   Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for areas of heat and cut away smouldering floorboards, remaining at the scene until the area was declared safe.   The fire started in the kitchen and the cause is believed to have been electrical.
