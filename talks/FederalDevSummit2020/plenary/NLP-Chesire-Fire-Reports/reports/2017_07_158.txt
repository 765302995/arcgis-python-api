Person trapped in car after collision Macclesfield Time of Incident: 01/07/2017 - 07:55 Location: Congleton Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield and one from Congleton Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision at the junction of Moss Lane and Congleton Road, Macclesfield. Crews rescued a male from a car which was on its side and assisted police in making the scene safe.
