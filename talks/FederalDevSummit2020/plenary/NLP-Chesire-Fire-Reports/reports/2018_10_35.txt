 Car fire in Ridley Time of Incident: 26/10/2018 - 14:23 Location: Wrexham Road, Ridley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters tackling a car fire in RidleyFirefighters attended to a car on fire on Wrexham Road in Ridley.  Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, used two hose reels as well as shuttling water to help extinguish it before making the area safe.  They were in attendance for one hour and 40 minutes.
