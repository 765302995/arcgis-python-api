 Car fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 18/03/2019 - 12:41 Location: Chester Road, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters were called to a car on fire in Chester Road, Northwich. Crews used a hose reel on the car which was emitting smoke from the bonnet. The car was in lane one of the A556 near to its junction with Norley Road. The lane was blocked until the car could be recovered.
