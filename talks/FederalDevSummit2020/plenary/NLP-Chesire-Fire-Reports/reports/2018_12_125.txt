 Two-vehicle collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 03/12/2018 - 08:39 Location: Kingsmead Square, off Regency Way, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and one attended from Winsford Two vehicle collision in NorthwichFirefighters from Northwich and Winsford attended reports of a collision on Regency Way, Kingsmead.  On arrival crews found that there had been a collision involving two cars and one person was medically trapped in one of the vehicles.  Firefighters worked closely with paramedics to get the casualty out of the vehicle.  Once out of the car he was handed over to the care of the North West Ambulance Service.  Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately an hour.
