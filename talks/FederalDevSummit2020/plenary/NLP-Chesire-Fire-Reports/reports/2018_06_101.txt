 Garden fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 22/06/2018 - 21:33 Location: Tennyson Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - One from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Firefighters from Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane used one hose reel jet to tackle a fire involving garden decking at a house on Tennyson Road. The team were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes.
