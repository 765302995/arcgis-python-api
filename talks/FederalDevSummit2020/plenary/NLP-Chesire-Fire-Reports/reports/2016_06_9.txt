Bedroom fire in Chester Time of Incident: 28/06/2016 - 00:25 Location: Weston Grove, Upton, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester There was a fire in the bedroom of a semi-detached house in Upton. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control. A high pressure fan was then used to ventilate the house. Bedroom floorboards were cut away to make sure there were no further hotspots.  The cause of the fire is believed to be discarded smoking materials.  If you do smoke please follow our safety advice
