Man trapped after collision in Bickerton Time of Incident: 21/09/2015 - 20:07 Location: Salters Lane/Wrexham Road, Bickerton Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one from Malpas One man was trapped after a collision involving two cars in Bickerton.  Two other people were out of the cars and assessed by paramedics who were at the scene along with the police.  Crews used Holmatro cutting equipment and liaised closely with paramedics to remove the man from the vehicle.  The road was closed in both directions while the incident was dealt with.
