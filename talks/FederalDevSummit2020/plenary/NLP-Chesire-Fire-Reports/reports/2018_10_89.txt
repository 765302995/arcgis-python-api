 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 15/10/2018 - 20:05 Location: Fairfield Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters extinguished a wheelie bin well alight on Fairfield Road in Widnes by using one hose reel.  Follow our safety advice on how to prevent your wheelie bin being set on fire https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
