Van on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 08/09/2016 - 01:33 Location: Garden Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters were called to a van on fire and used one hose reel to extinguish it.  If anyone has any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 as it is possible the fire was started deliberately.
