Refuse on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 21/07/2016 - 20:58 Location: Sycamore Road, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines from Runcorn A small amount of rubbish had been set on fire in Runcorn. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which had been set in bushes near to a shed.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
