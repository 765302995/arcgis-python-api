Macclesfield road traffic collision Time of Incident: 05/10/2016 - 13:29 Location: Moss Lane/Ash Grove, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Two vehicles, a van and a car, were involved in a road traffic collision on Moss Lane, near Ash Grove. Fortunately nobody was trapped in either vehicle but one casualty, a lady, was taken to hospital. Crews made the scene safe.
