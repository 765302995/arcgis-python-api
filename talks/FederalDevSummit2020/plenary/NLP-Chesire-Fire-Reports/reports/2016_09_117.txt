Cooker fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 07/09/2016 - 12:38 Location: Moss Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford Firefighters were called to a small fire involving a cooker. Crews used a large van to ventilate the property whilst one woman was checked by the ambulances services for signs of smoke inhalation.  Remember to always practise careful cooking.
