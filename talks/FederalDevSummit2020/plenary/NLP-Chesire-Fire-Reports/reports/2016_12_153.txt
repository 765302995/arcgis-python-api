Winsford wheelie bin fire Time of Incident: 30/11/2016 - 21:33 Location: Dee Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Fires involving wheelie bins are suspected to have been started deliberately. The fires were put out using a hose reel jet and police were asked to attend.  Anyone with any information about this, or similar incidents in the area, is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
