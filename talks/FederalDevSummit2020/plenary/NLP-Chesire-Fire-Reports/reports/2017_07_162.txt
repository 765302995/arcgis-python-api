Domestic building fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 30/06/2017 - 17:22 Location: Beaconsfield Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a domestic building fire on Beaconsfield Road, Runcorn. The fire was out on arrival but crews treated one person who was suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation. Crews also cleared smoke from the property using a large fan. Crews were on the scene for approximately 30 minutes. The casualty was handed into the care of the paramedics.
