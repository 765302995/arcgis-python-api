Injured swan rescued from river in Nantwich Time of Incident: 01/10/2017 - 11:48 Location: Manor Road North, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Chester with the rescue boat Injured swan rescued from river in NantwichFire crews were called to help rescue an injured swan from the River Weaver on Manor Road North, Nantwich. Crews launched a rescue boat to pick the swan, which had been attacked by a dog, up and then handed into the care of the RSPCA.
