 Moped fire in a Runcorn garden Time of Incident: 27/06/2019 - 22:06 Location: Halton Brook Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly after 10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a moped in a garden in Halton Brook Avenue, Runcorn. On arrival the crew found the moped well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire in the open with a hose reel jet. The moped was destroyed by the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for less than 20 minutes.
