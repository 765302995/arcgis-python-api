Shed fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 08/09/2017 - 21:33 Location: Dale Crescent, Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines from Congleton Firefighters were called to a shed fire in the back garden of a Dale Crescent property. The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control.  They were at the scene for one hour.
