 Woman trapped in a ravine in Barthomley Time of Incident: 18/08/2019 - 12:51 Location: Barthomley, near Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 12.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a woman having fallen off a quad bike and being stuck in a ravine in Barthomley, near to Walnut Tree Farm. The crew assisted paramedics in getting the casualty out of the ravine. Firefighters then left her in the care of paramedics. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
