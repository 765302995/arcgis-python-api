 Small building fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 21/07/2018 - 16:51 Location: Mirion Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe used water to extinguish a small fire in a former youth centre on Mirion Street. The team were at the scene for approximately ten minutes. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
