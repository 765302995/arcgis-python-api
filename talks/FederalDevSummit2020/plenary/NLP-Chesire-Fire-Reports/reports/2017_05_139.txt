House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 09/05/2017 - 19:11 Location: Sandown Road, Crewe. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Crewe  Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Sandown Road, Crewe.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire in the bedroom of the property. Covering and hose reel jets were used to put the fire out.  The gas and electricity was also turned off.  A fire investigation said the probable cause was an unattended incense stick and was accidental. Thankfully there were no reports of any injuries.
