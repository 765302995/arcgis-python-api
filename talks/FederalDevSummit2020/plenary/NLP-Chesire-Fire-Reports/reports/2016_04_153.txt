Tarmac container on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 02/04/2016 - 12:06 Location: Clough Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Middlewich A metal container with a quantity of tarmac inside was involved in fire. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, gained access to the container, which contained approximately two tonnes of burning tarmac, and began to damp it down. A mechanical digger was used to drag out the tarmac as the crews continued to cool it.
