 Fire at skate park, Widnes Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 21:44 Location: Queensbury Way, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Crews were called to a fire at a skate park in Queensbury Way. It involved a wooden pallet stacked against a bin, and had been deliberately set alight. Firefighters used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames and remained for some time further to damp down what was left of the pallet until it was completely out.
