Resident locked out of property in Congleton Time of Incident: 17/12/2015 - 12:22 Location: Borough Road, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton Firefighters were called to a property in Congleton after a female had locked herself out of the house.  Crews gained access to the property using a short extension ladder and were unable to unlock the door. A health and safety assessment of the property was also carried out.
