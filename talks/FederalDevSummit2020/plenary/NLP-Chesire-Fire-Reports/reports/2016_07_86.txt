Smoke-filled kitchen in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 15/07/2016 - 19:25 Location: Macclesfield Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a report of a kitchen fire in Wilmslow. When they arrived at the property there was no sign of fire but the kitchen was filled with smoke from cooking. The crew cleared the smoke.  The flat had smoke alarms fitted which alerted the occupier to the incident. Make sure you have smoke alarms fitted and working in your home.
