Injured man rescued in Runcorn Time of Incident: 17/09/2015 - 14:29 Location: Mersey Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine and the rope rescue unit and supporting fire appliance Firefighters assisted paramedics to rescue a man who had fallen on an embankment. The crew and the Service's rope rescue unit recovered the casualty using lines and then handed him into the care of paramedics.
