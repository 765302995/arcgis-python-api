 Small fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/04/2019 - 18:45 Location: Trent Close, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to a fire on land off Trent Close, Widnes. On arrivals the team discovered two small fires which are beleived to have been started deliberately. Police were in attendance. Crews used a hose reel to put out the fires.
