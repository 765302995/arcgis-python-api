 Brazier fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/02/2019 - 12:07 Location: The Boulevard, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane Firefighters were called to communal gardens in The Boulevard, Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port, following reports of a small brazier being on fire. The crew used a hose reel to put out the fire.
