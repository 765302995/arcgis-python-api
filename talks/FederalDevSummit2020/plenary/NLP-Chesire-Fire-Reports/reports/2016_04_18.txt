House fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 25/04/2016 - 17:05 Location: John Fryer Avenue in Wincham, Northwich. Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on John Fryer Avenue in Wincham, Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire involving a chip pan but it had gone out.  A woman and a child were treated for the effects of inhaling smoke.  Crews used special fans to clear smoke from the property.  Kitchen fire safety advice
