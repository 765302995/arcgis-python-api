Swimming baths flooded in Crewe Time of Incident: 10/07/2016 - 17:25 Location: Flag Lane, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Alsager People attempting to fill the swimming baths in Flag Lane caused flooding to the property. It was initially thought that there were chemicals present but then confirmed by firefighters that there were none on the premises.  Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 680 of 10 July.
