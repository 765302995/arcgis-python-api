 Fire adjacent to a caravan in Sandbach Time of Incident: 26/01/2019 - 17:16 Location: Radcliffe Road, Sandbach. Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Crewe and one from Sandbach At around 5.15pm firefighters from Sandbach and Crewe attended reports of a caravan fire on Radcliffe Road, Sandbach.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a small fire adjacent to a static caravan.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.
