Bonfire in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/07/2015 - 19:46 Location: Foster Street, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a house on Foster Street, Widnes.  When they arrived they found that this was a bonfire that had got out of control. Fire crews used water back packs to put the fire out. They also carrie out a Home Fire Safety Assessment before leaving.
