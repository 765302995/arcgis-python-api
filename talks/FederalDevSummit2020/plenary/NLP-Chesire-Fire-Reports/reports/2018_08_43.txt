 Fire at an underground car park in Neston Time of Incident: 21/08/2018 - 18:26 Location: The Parade, Parkgate, Neston Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port At around 6.25pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a car park underneath a residential building at The Parade on Parkgate, Neston. On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved electrical wiring in a lighting circuit. The building was evacuated and firefighters used dry powder extinguishers to put out the fire. They then used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the underground car park. All electricity to the car park was isolated and it was closed to the public. Once all the smoke was cleared from the car park the building's residents were allowed to return to their homes. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
