Restaurant fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 23/12/2017 - 02:25 Location: McDonalds, Middlewich Road, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and one from Crewe Update - 24/12/17 - Firefighters from Sandbach and Crewe were called to reports of a building fire on Middlewich Road, Sandbach. On arrival crewes found a commercial building was smoke logged due to overheated oil in a commercial fryer. Crews used a large fan, thermal imaging camera, two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to clear the smoke and cool the appliance.
