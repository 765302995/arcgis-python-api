Chimney fire in Mere Time of Incident: 26/09/2016 - 19:40 Location: Warrington Road, Mere. Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford A birds nest had fallen down a flue into the fire place causing a fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check for fire.  Chimney fire safety advice
