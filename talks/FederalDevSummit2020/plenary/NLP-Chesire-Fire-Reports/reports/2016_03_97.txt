Road traffic collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 13/03/2016 - 14:06 Location: Cotswold Way, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision which had a small amount of fuel which had leaked. The collision involved a motorbike and a car. Environmental absorbent mats were used to prevent the fuel from running into drains. Paramedics and the Air Ambulance attended.
