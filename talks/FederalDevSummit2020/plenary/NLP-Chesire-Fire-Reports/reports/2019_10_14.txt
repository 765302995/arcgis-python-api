 Small chimney fire in Disley Time of Incident: 28/10/2019 - 11:58 Location: Redhouse Lane, Disley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Shortly before 12pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a residential property on Redhouse Lane, Disley.  Firefighters from Wilmslow attended the scene and on arrival they a small smouldering fire in the chimney breast.  The crew used specialist chimney rods to extinguish the fire and were detained at the property for around 45 minutes.  For tips and advice on how to avoid chimney fires visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/news-events/latest-news/clean-your-chimney-during-chimney-fire-safety-week
