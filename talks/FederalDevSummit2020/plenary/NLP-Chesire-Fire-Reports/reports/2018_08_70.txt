 Road traffic collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 16/08/2018 - 12:07 Location: Lloyd Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach and Birchwood Firefighters attended to a collision at Cheshire Oaks on Coliseum Way in Ellesmere Port involving a number of vehicles.  Crews removed a man from one car and he was been handed into the care of paramedics.
