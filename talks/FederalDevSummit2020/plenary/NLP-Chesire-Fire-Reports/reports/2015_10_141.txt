Bonfire put out in Crewe Time of Incident: 04/10/2015 - 19:04 Location: Alton Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A bonfire which had got out of control at the rear of a garden was put out by firefighters. A hose reel jet was used to put out the blaze and a stack drag used to spread the embers to make sure the fire was completely out.
