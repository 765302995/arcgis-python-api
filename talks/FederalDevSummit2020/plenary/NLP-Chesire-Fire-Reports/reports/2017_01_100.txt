Oven fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 08/01/2017 - 12:32 Location: Warrington Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes Firefighters put out a fire in an oven and removed it to outside the house. The house filled with smoke so the crews ventilated the property. Smoke alarms were fitted in the house and alerted the residents to the fire.  Please follow our cooking safety advice and always have smoke alarms fitted.
