Bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 28/08/2017 - 20:17 Location: Liverpool Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes A fire in a bin was suspected to have been started deliberately. Firefighters used a water backpack to put out the fire and are appealing to anyone with any information about this suspected arson attack to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
