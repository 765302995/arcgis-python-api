Chimney fire in Bunbury Time of Incident: 15/03/2016 - 16:10 Location: Whitchurch Road, Bunbury Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich A fire in a chimney was attended by firefighters. A crew used chimney rods to put out the fire and a thermal imaging camera to check whether there were any hot spots. They also thoroughly checked the loft space.
