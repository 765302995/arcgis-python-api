 Refus fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 18/07/2018 - 16:07 Location: Alnwick Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port. Firefighters from Ellesmere Port used one hose reel jet to tackle a small refuse fire in an alleyway of Alnwick Drive. the team were detained at the scene for approximately ten minutes.
