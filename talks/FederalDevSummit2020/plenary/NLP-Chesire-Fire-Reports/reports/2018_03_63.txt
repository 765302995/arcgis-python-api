 Fire at recycling centre in Winsford Time of Incident: 22/03/2018 - 15:13 Location: Leslie Road, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Winsford and one from Northwich At 3:13pm firefighters were called to a fire at a recycling centre in Woodford Park Industrial Estate on Leslie Road, Winsford.  On arrival crews discovered a small fire in a baling machine and that fire alarms had been sounded and a full evacuation had been completed.  The electrics were isolated and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames.  Crews then carried out cooling operations and were in attendance for around four-and-a-half hours.
