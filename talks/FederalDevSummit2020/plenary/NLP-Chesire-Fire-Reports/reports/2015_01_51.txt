Warehouse fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/01/2015 - 11:01 Location: Foundry Lane, Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engines from Widnes, Runcorn and Warrington Firefighters from Widnes were called to a report of a building fire on Foundry Lane in Widnes. When they arrived they found that a fire had developed in a large warehouse. A further four fire engines were called to the incident to help deal with it.  Fire crews tackled the blaze with main and hose reel water jets. Five large fans were also used to help bring the fire under control.
