Car on fire at M6 Knutsford Services Time of Incident: 10/07/2016 - 08:08 Location: Car park at Knutsford Service Station M6 southbound Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford A car was on fire on the southbound side of the M6 at Knutsford Service Station. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the blaze under control.
