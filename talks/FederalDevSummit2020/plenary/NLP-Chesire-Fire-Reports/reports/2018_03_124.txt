 Rubbish fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 07/03/2018 - 20:57 Location: Cledford Lane, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich Firefighters were called to an address in Cledford Lane, Middlewich following reports of a fire in the back garden.  Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the fire. They then damped down the rubbish which had been set on fire and turned it over to ensure it didn't reignite.
