Motorbike fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 02/04/2017 - 13:00 Location: Welsh Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane A motorcyclist was injured and a fire from his motorbike spread to a nearby fence. The casualty was taken to hospital by ambulance and the firefighters damped the area down. Welsh Road was closed by police while the incident was dealt with.
