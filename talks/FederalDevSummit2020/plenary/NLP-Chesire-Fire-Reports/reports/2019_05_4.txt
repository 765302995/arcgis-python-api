 Two HGV RTC on M56 Eastbound Time of Incident: 30/05/2019 - 14:17 Location: M56, Eastbound, J10-J9 Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters attended to a road traffic collision on the M56 eastbound between junction 10 and junction 9 involving two lorries.  No one was trapped inside the vehicles and ambulance were not required.  Crews have made the vehicles safe and the incident has been handed to Highways.
