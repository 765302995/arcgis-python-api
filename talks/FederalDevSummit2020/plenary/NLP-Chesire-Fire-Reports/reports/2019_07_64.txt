 Shed fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 18/07/2019 - 01:06 Location: Pensby Drive, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 1.15am firefighters were called to reports of a fire in an outbuilding on Pensby Drive, Great Sutton. On arrival the crews found a wooden garden shed which was well alight. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately one hour.
