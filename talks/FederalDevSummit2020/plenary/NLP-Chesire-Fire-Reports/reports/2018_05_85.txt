 Skip fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 19/05/2018 - 21:42 Location: Vaudrey Crescent, Congleton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters from Congleton used one hose reel jet to extinguish a skip fire which was well alight on a driveway on Vaudrey Crescent, Congleton.
