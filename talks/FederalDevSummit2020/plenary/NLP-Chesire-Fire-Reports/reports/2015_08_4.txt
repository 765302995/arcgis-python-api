Car well alight in Lymm Time of Incident: 29/08/2015 - 01:31 Location: Oughtrington Lane, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a car that was on fire by Lymm High School in Lymm. The car was well alight. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  Police were informed of this incident.
