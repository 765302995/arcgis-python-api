 Gas leak in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 15/04/2019 - 23:31 Location: Chester Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 11.30pm firefighters from Ellesmere Port were called to reports of a possible gas leak at Ellesmere Port Hospital.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered that all patients had been safely evacuated from the affected area.  The team used a gas detector to check the affected area and discovered that the leak had been caused by a faulty refrigeration tank.  The refrigeration tank was removed from the building and the patients were returned to the ward.  The team were in attendance for approximately two hours 30 minutes.
