 Fire in a flat in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 08/12/2019 - 21:39 Location: Richmond Place, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Macclesfield Firefighter with the dog he rescuedAt around 9.50pm firefighters from Macclesfield attended reports of a fire in a first floor flat on Richmond Place.  On arrival the crews found a fire in the kitchen area of the flat. The occupant had evacuated the building and was provided with oxygen therapy.  Firefighters used a covering jet to extinguish the fire and a large fan to clear the smoke from the building.  The crew also rescued a dog which was inside the flat.
