 Woman trapped following two-vehicle collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/11/2018 - 09:49 Location: Delph Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Lymm Just before 9.50am firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving two cars on Delph Lane in Warrington. On arrival crews discovered that a woman was trapped in one of the vehicles. They used specialist equipment to free the casualty from the vehicle and handed her over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters also made sure that the vehicles were safe and were in attendance for around an hour.
