 Commercial building fire in Chester Time of Incident: 07/05/2018 - 12:32 Location: Princess Street, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester, one from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a commercial building on Princess Street, Chester, after reports of a fire. A smouldering fire in the air conditioning system was discovered and crews used an aerial appliance, a high volume pump, a hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to tackle the fire. Crews were at the scene for one hour.
