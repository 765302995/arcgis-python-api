Smoke from cooking left unattended in property in Middlewich Time of Incident: 20/04/2017 - 16:16 Location: Hayhurst Avenue, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich Smoke from cooking which had been left unattended, set of an alarm in a property. No one was home at the time and firefighters used a large fan to remove smoke from the property.  For advice about cooking safely, please visit Kitchen fire safety
