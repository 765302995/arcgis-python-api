Car on fire on M6 Time of Incident: 22/07/2017 - 17:39 Location: Just before junction 21a of the M6, northbound. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Lymm Firefighters used two hose reels to extinguish a car which was well alight just before junction 21a of the M6. Police were in attendance and two lanes were closed while the incident was dealt with.
