 Refuge fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 01/04/2019 - 19:19 Location: Fountain Lane, Frodsham. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters from Runcorn attended reports of a fire on Fountain Lane, Frodsham.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a large amount of loose refuge, adjacent to a brick wall,  had been deliberately set alight.  The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained at the scene for around 20 minutes.  Fountain Lane was closed for a short period of time while firefighters dealt with the incident.
