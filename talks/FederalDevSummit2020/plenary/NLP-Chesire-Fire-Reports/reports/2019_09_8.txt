 Overheated washing machine in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 28/09/2019 - 09:10 Location: Bishopton Drive, Macclesfield. SK11 8TS Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a house on Bishopton Drive following reports of a fire.  The crew discovered smoke coming from a washing machine and isolated the appliance - there was no fire.
