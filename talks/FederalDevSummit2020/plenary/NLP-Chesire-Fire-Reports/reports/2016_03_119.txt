Cooking fumes trigger alarm Time of Incident: 10/03/2016 - 14:03 Location: Rose Terrace, Crewe Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe Firefighters responded to an alarm sounding at a property on Rose Terrace, Crewe.  The alarm had been triggered by cooking fumes. Crews gave first aid to a woman at the scene who was suffering the effects of smoke inhalation.
