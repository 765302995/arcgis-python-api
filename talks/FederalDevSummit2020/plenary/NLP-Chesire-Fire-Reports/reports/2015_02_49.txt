House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/02/2015 - 04:57 Location: Toll Bar Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A fire in the lounge of a property in Warrington left one man needing paramedic attention.  The male casualty was treated for minor smoke inhalation.  Firefighters tackled the blaze, two of the crew wearing breathing apparatus, using a hose reel jet.  The cause of the fire is under investigation.
