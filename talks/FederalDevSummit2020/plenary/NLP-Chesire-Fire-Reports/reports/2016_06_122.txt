House fire in Birchwood Time of Incident: 08/06/2016 - 17:53 Location: Stonecrop Close Birchwood Attendance: Fire engines from Birchwood and Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Stonecrop Close Birchwood.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved a cooker. Crews used hose reel jets a  large fan to deal with the incident. There were no reports of any injuries.
