 Fires in park land in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/08/2018 - 21:08 Location: Woolston Park, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 9.05pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in Woolston Park, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered several fires on the park land. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fires and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. The fires are believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
