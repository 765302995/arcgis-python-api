 Van on fire on a driveway in Poynton Time of Incident: 28/06/2019 - 03:21 Location: Dickens Lane, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton A van which was on fire on a driveway was extinguished with a hose reel and a covering water jet by firefighters. A 25 metre cordon was set up due to a cylinder being involved. The cylinder was removed from the fire and confirmed empty. It was cooled by firefighters.
