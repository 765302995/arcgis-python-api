Mattresses on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/07/2015 - 19:54 Location: Thelwall Lane, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a park off Thelwall Lane, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving four mattresses. Crews used a hose reel to put the fire out.
