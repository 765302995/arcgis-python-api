Pigeon rescued in Chester Time of Incident: 27/08/2017 - 08:04 Location: Frodsham Street, Chester Attendance: The hydraulic platform unit from Chester Fire crews were called to rescue a pigeon which had become stuck in netting above a shop on Frodsham Street, Chester. Crews used a hydraulic platform to reach the netting, above the Pound Bakery, and untangle the bird. The pigeon was then handed into the care of the RSPCA.
