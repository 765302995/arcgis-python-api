Car on fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 22/11/2016 - 08:39 Location: A34, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a car on fire and used a hose reel to extinguish it. It is believed that the fire was caused by a fault and crew made the area safe before leaving. Police attended for traffic management while the incident was dealt with.
