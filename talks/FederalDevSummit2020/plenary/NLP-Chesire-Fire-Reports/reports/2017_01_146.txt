Car fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 01/01/2017 - 02:06 Location: Warwick Place, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford A hose reel jet was used to tackle a fire in a car, which is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
