Vehicle fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/10/2017 - 00:18 Location: Luton Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Fire crews were called to a vehicle fire on Luton Street, Widnes. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 08000 555 111.
