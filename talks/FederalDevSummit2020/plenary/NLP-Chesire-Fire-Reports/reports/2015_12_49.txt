Bungalow on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 17/12/2015 - 21:30 Location: Raglan Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield attended Firefighters were called to a fire in the living room of a bungalow. The crew used a hose reel and covering jet to extinguish the fire and a large fan to remove smoke from the property. Lofts in adjacent properties were checked before the firefighters left.  This fire is believed to have been started by an e-cigarette which had been charging.  Please follow our e-cigarette safety advice - E-cigarettes
