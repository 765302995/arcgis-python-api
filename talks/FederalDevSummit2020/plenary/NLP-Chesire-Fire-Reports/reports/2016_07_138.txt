Fire at Winsford packaging company Time of Incident: 06/07/2016 - 23:06 Location: Road Four, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine each from Winsford, Middlewich and Northwich Fire in ducting at a packaging company on Winsford Industrial Estate was tackled by firefighters. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control. A thermal imaging was used to check there were no further hot spots of fire. The crews were at the scene for just under three hours.
