Car fire on M6 southbound near to J21 Time of Incident: 14/10/2015 - 08:48 Location: M6 southbound near junction 21 Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Overheated brakes on a 4 x 4 were cooled by firefighters using a hose reel jet.
