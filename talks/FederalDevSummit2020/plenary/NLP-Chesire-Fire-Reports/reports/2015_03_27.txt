Fire involving cooker hob in Crewe Time of Incident: 24/03/2015 - 09:19 Location: Frank Webb Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe A fire involving a cooker hob in Crewe was extinguished by firefighters using one hose reel and wearing breathing apparatus. On arrival the crews found the ground floor of the house was full of smoke and a large fan was used to ventilate the property. Smoke alarms were fitted and did go off. Kitchen fire safety advice
