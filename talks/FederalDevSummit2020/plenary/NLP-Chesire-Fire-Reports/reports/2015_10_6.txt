Road traffic collision on M6 (J21A) northbound Time of Incident: 28/10/2015 - 20:01 Location: M6 motorway by Junction 21A northbound. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Two people were trapped in a car following a road traffic collision, involving two cars, on the M6 motorway by Junction 21A northbound.  Firefighters released a female casualty from a vehicle and handed the casualty to the care of the paramedics.
