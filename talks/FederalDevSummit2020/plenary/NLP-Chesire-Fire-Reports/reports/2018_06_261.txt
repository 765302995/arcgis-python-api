 Six vehicle collision in Shotwick Time of Incident: 31/05/2018 - 18:38 Location: Welsh Road, Shotwick Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Six vehicle collision in ShotwickFirefighters attended to a road traffic collision on Welsh Road in Shotwick involving four cars and two vans.  Crews removed the roof of a vehicle to release one casualty who was then handed over to the care of paramedics.  Firefighters were in attendance for one hour and 35 minutes.
