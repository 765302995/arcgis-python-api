Small fire in woodland in Winwick, Warrington Time of Incident: 19/12/2016 - 19:04 Location: Sheridan Place, Winwick, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath Firefighters extinguished a small fire on woodland between Delph Farm and Sheridan Place.  This fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
