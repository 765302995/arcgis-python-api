Vehicle fire in Chester Time of Incident: 30/08/2018 - 12:53 Location: Bumpers Lane, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters from Chester used one hose reel jet to extinguish  a saloon car which was well alight at a garage on Bumpers Lane, Chester. One person was treated at the scene for smoke inhalation. Firefiighters provided oxygen to the casualty and they were then passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service.
