 Small fire in a field in Frodsham Time of Incident: 23/09/2019 - 15:57 Location: Thirlmere Close, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly before 4pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a field off Thirlmere Close in Frodsham. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small smouldering fire. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
