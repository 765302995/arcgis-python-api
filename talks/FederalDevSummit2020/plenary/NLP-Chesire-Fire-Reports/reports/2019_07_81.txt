 Unattended cooking in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/07/2019 - 10:58 Location: North Street, Crewe. Attendance: Three fire engines attended from Crewe, Sandbach and Nantwich At approximately 11am firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a house in Crewe. On arrival the crew discovered that food on a cooker had been left unattended, causing a large amount of smoke in the property. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property and one elderly man was treated for smoke inhalation - he has since been passed to the care of paramedics. The crew were detained at the scene for approximately 35 minutes.
