Car fire on M62 Time of Incident: 08/11/2017 - 07:43 Location: M62 east between junction 8 and 9 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Birchwood A car fire on the M62, between junction 8 and 9, caused a lane to be closed on the carriageway. Firefighters, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The Highway's Agency put in place a road closure.
