 Skip fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 26/03/2018 - 17:14 Location: Carrwood Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to Carrwood Road, Wilmslow, after reports of an uncontrolled burning. Crews found a skip containing plastics on fire and used a hose reel jet to extinguish it.
