Hatchback on fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 23/05/2017 - 14:03 Location: High Street, Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach Fireifghters were called to a vehicle fire on High Street, Sandbach. When crews arrived the small hatchback was well alight and a hose reel jet and two breathing apparatus were used when they tackled the blaze.
