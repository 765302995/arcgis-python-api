 Chimney fire in Goostrey Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 20:23 Location: Station Road, Goostrey Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Fire crews were called to a chimney fire on Station Road, Goostrey. The chimney was well alight on arrival and crews used a 13.5m ladder, chimney rods and a hose reel jet to put out the fire. Crews then checked the property using a thermal imaging camera.
