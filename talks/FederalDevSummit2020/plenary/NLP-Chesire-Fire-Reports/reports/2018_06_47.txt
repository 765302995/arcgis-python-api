 Bushes fire in a garden in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 18:14 Location: Thornton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane attended At around 6.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a bushes fire in a garden on Thornton Road in Ellesmere Port. The crew extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
