Combine harvester on fire in Preston Brook Time of Incident: 28/08/2015 - 16:21 Location: Chester Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn, one from Stockton Heath and one from Warrington Firefighters were called to a fire involving a combine harvester in a field off the A56 in Preston Brook, Runcorn. Firefighters extinguished the fire and have dampened down the vehicle to make the scene safe.
