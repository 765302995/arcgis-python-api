 Vehicle fire on M56 Time of Incident: 22/09/2018 - 17:33 Location: Between junction 14 and junction 12 eastbound Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Ellesmere Port attended Breathing apparatus and two hose reel jets were used to put out a vehicle fire on the M56. Firefighters attended after a VW Golf was reported well alight and also used foam to control the fuel fire. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
