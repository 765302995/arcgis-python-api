 Small bush fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/06/2018 - 21:58 Location: Victoria Park, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At approximately 10pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at Victoria Park in Warrington. On arrival crews found that the fire involved a small bush which had been deliberately set alight. The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes. Anyone with any information into the cause of the fire is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
