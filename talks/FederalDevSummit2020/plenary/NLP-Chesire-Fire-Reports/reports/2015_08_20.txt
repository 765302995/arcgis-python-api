Cooker fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/08/2015 - 19:40 Location: Sidings Court, Howley, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A smoke alarm alerted neighbours to a cooker fire, involving an unattended pan, in the kitchen of a ground floor flat on Sidings Court in Howley, Warrington. Firefighters and Police had to force entry to the property so that they could extinguish the fire.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to deal with the fire. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.
