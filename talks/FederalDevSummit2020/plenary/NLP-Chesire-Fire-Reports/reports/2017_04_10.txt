Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 27/04/2017 - 19:37 Location: Abbey Court in Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Two casualties suffered burns following a small kitchen fire.  The fire was already out when the fire crews arrived. Firefighters gave first aid while they waited for an ambulance to attend.  Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.
