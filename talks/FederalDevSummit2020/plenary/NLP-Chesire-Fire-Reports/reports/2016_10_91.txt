House fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 15/10/2016 - 20:54 Location: Flower Street, Northwich. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Flower Street, Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that the ground floor of the terrace house was filled with smoke and there was smoke coming out of the upstairs window.   Crews used four breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet was used to deal with the incident.  There was nobody in at the time of the incident.
