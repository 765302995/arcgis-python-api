 Chimney on fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 08/03/2018 - 20:38 Location: Radway Green Road, Radway Green, Alsager Attendance: One fire engine from Alsager A chimney on fire at the rear of a log burner, which affected a cavity wall, was extinguished by firefighters using chimney gear. A ladder was also used to gain access to the chimney via the roof of the large detached house.  Chimney safety advice
