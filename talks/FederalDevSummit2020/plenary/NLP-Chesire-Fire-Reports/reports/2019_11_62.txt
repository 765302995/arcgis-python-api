 Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 16/11/2019 - 22:21 Location: Ditton Road (West), Widnes. Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Penketh and Widnes At around 10.20pm firefighters from Widnes and Penketh were dispatched to reports of a fire on Ditton Road, Widnes. On arrival the crews found a car had collided with a fence and was well alight, One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews then used a hose reel jet to check for any hotspots. The crews were in attendance at the incident for around one hour 20 minutes. There were no reports of any injuries.
