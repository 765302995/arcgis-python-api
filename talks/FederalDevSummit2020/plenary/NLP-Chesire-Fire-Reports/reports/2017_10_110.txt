Fire in premises in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/10/2017 - 15:44 Location: The Trawlerman, Old Liverpool Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Lymm Fire at the Trawlerman in WarringtonFire crews were called to fire at a commercial premises which was well alight. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used one hose reel and a water jet to fight the fire.  Police were in attendance to manage traffic and road closures.
