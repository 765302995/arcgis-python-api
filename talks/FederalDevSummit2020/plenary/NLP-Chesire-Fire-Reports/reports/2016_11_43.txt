Casualty from unattended cooking in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 21/11/2016 - 19:38 Location: Wykeham Chase, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine Cooking left unattended left one man needing hospital treatment. Firefighters were called to the flat on Wykeham Chase where they treated the casualty for smoke inhalation and called an ambulance. Paramedics assessed him and took him to hospital. The crew was alerted to the incident by a smoke alarm.  Please always follow our cooking safety advice and never leave cooking unattended.
