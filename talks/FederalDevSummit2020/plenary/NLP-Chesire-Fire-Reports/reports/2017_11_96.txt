Collision on M56 Time of Incident: 08/11/2017 - 06:52 Location: M56 east, junction 10 to 9 Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn, one from Lymm and one from Warrington Fire crews were called to a collision between two cars on the M56 east, between junction 10 and 9. Both cars had left the carriageway and firefighters released one casualty from a car before handing them into the care of paramedics.
