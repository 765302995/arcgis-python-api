 Wheelbarrow fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 03/08/2019 - 07:11 Location: Woodford Lane, Winsford. CW7 2JS Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford A fire crew from Winsford used a hose reel jet to put out a fire in a wheelbarrow filled with rubbish on Woodford Lane.  The fire was reported at 7.11am and firefighters were at the scene for around an hour.
