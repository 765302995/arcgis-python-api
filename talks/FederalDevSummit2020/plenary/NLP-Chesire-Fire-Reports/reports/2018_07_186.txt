 Woodland and peat fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 10/07/2018 - 14:30 Location: Whitebeam Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 2.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Whitbeam Close, Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered approximately three square metres of woodland and peat smoking. Firefighters using beaters to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
