 Wheelie bin on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 28/10/2019 - 23:35 Location: Halton Brook Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A wheelie bin fire, which was affecting a gate at a property, was extinguished with a hose reel. Police were informed about this fire due to the suspected deliberate ignition. Anyone with information should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
