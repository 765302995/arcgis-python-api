 Fire at a school in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 23/04/2018 - 12:25 Location: Manchester Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Macclesfield and one from Bollington At around 12.25pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a senior school on Manchester Road in Macclesfield. On arrival the crews discovered that a full evacuation of Tytherington School had been completed and a small fire caused by roofing work had been extinguished by contractors carrying out the work. They also found that the building was free from smoke. Firefighters conducted an internal and external inspection of the roof space to make sure the building was safe. They were at the scene for more than half-an-hour.
