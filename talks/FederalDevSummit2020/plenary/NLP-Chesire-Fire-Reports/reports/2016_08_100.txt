Playground well alight by Locking Stumps Community Primary School in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/08/2016 - 21:19 Location: Locking Stumps Community Primary School, Glover Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from warrington Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish a fire on a playground (approximately 5 metres by 5 metres) by Locking Stumps Community Primary School.  The playground was well alight.
