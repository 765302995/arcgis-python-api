 Vehicle collision in Prestbury Time of Incident: 11/02/2018 - 11:32 Location: Bonis Hall Lane, Prestbury Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield and Bollington Fire crews from Bollington and Macclesfield attended to an overturned vehicle on Bonis Hall Lane in Prestbury at 11:30am on Sunday, 11 February. One casualty was extricated by firefighters assistance and three people were taken to hospital by north west ambulance service.
