Tractor on fire in Gawsworth Time of Incident: 13/12/2015 - 06:02 Location: Cowbrook Lane, Gawsworth Attendance: Two fire engines from Congleton and one from Poynton A tractor was well alight inside a farmer's building (feeding shed), so firefighters used two hose reels to tackle the fire.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. Animals were evacuated from the building and crews damped down the area. They were on scene for two hours.
