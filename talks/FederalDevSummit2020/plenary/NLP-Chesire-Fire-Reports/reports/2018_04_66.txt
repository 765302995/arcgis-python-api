 Paint booth on fire at a commercial premises in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/04/2018 - 17:33 Location: Newstead Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes and one from Runcorn Firefighters dealt with fire at a train care centre on Newstead Road in Widnes which left one man receiving treatment at Whiston Hospital for minor burns.  The fire involved a paint booth in one of the sheds and the premises was fully evacuated as a precaution.   The sprinklers were activated  and the fire was extinguished.
