Man rescued in Congleton Time of Incident: 24/05/2015 - 20:58 Location: Newcastle Road, Congleton. Attendance: A fire engine from Macclesfield and a fire engine from Congleton Firefighters were called to a report that a man had fallen down an embankment off Newcastle Road, Congleton.  When they arrived crews confirmed that the man had fallen about thirty foot down an embankment. Firefighters stabilised the casualty on a long board and assisted the ambulance crew. The man was then taken to hospital.
