 Caravan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/11/2018 - 17:34 Location: Bradley Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Crews, wearing two breathing apparatus, attended to a caravan well alight on Bradley Lane in Warrington.  Firefighters used one hose reel to tackle the blaze and bring it under control. The area was then made safe.
