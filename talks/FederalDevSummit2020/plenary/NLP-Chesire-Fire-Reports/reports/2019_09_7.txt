 Shed fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 28/09/2019 - 13:54 Location: Middle Lane, Congleton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton and one from Macclesfield Crews, wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out a shed fire at Middle Lane, Congleton.  The firefighters also used a large fan to disperse smoke caused by the fire. They were at the scene for 90 minutes.
