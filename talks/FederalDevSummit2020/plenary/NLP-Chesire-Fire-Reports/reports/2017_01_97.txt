Chimney fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/01/2017 - 09:17 Location: Blackburne Avenue, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines A chimney fire was tackled by firefighters at a house on Blackburne Avenue. The fire was located on a roof truss next to the chimney breast. A roof ladder was used to remove tiles from the base of the chimney and the fire put out. A thermal imaging camera was then used to make sure there was no fire spread.
