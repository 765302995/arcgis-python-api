 Collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 14/05/2018 - 20:05 Location: Chester Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester and one from Tarporley Fire crews attended to a road traffic collision on Chester Road in Northwich. Firefighters stabilised the vehicle which was on it's roof and the casualty was handed into the care of paramedics.  Crews remained at the scene until it was safe.
