Cow rescue in Chester Time of Incident: 15/10/2015 - 16:31 Location: Ridges Lane, Saighton, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and the animal rescue unit from Bollington Firefighters were called to rescue a cow stuck in a Saighton pond. A crew from Chester along with the animal rescue unit from Bollington used a strap system and 4 x 4 vehicle to remove the cow to safety.  Once the straps were removed the cow was able to stand and the farmer arranged for a vet to attend as a precaution.
