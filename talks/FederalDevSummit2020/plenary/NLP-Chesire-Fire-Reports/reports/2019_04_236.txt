 Car fire in Neston Time of Incident: 03/04/2019 - 21:54 Location: Ringway, Neston. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane At around 10pm firefighters from Powey Lane were dispatched to a car fire on Ringway, Neston.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a car in an alleyway which was well alight. The fire had also spread to a nearby fence.  One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the car fire. Meanwhile, a second firefighter used a hose reel to extinguish the fence.  They also used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately one hour 30 minutes.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
