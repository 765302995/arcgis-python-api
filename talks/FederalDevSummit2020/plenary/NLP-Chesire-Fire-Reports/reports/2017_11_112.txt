Fire in a restaurant kitchen in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 05/11/2017 - 16:28 Location: Westmorland Close, Macclesfield Attendance: Three fire engines from Bollington, Macclesfield and Wilslow Three casualties suffered smoke inhalation after a fire in a restaurant kitchen on Westmorland Close. The crews used a high pressure fan to clear smoke from the affected area. The casualties were treated by ambulance personnel at the scene. Firefighters were at the scene for around 75 minutes.
