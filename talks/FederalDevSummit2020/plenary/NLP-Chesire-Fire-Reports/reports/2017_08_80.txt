House fire in Lostock Gralam Time of Incident: 17/08/2017 - 00:08 Location: Harris Road, Lostock Gralan, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich A house which was apparently struck by lightning and a bedroom was set on fire required firefighters to attend and extinguish it with a hose reel and a covering jet. The crew used a large fan to remove smoke from the property. The fire also involved an electrical supply. Ambulance staff attended to deal with one person suffering from smoke inhalation.
