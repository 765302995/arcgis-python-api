 Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/05/2018 - 11:58 Location: Clifton Gardens, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a kitchen fire on Clifton Gardens, Ellesmere Port. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet and a large fan to extinguish the fire and clear smoke from the property. Crews also treated one casualty who was suffering from the effects of smoke inhalation. Crews were at the scene for three hours.
