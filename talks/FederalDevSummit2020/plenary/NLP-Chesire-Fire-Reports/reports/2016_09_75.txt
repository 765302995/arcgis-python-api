Lift rescue from a hotel Time of Incident: 17/09/2016 - 15:38 Location: Craxton Wood Hotel on Parkgate Road in Chester Attendance: A fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to the Craxton Wood Hotel on Parkgate Road in Chester after two people became stuck in a lift.  When they arrived crews worked alongside a lift engineer to rescue the two people. There were no reports of any injuries.
