Flooding in Sandbach Time of Incident: 26/02/2016 - 00:10 Location: Park Lane, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach the Rapid Response Rescue Unit from Sandbach and a further fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to four properties which had suffered from serious flooding. Two portable pumps were used to remove water from the area and an environmental pack to contain some of the water. Firefighters were at the scene for four hours. It is believed that the flooding was caused by a burst water main.
