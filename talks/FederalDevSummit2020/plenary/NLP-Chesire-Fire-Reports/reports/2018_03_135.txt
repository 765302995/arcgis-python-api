 Car fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 05/03/2018 - 18:39 Location: Crewe Road, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Car fire in Nantwich Firefighters attended a car well alight in a field off Crewe Road, Nantwich. The crews used a hose reel jet and breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames.  They then used a thermal imaging camera to ensure there were no hotspots. Police were also in attendance.
