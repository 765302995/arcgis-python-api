 Car on fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 05/12/2019 - 09:53 Location: Macclesfield Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Shortly before 10am firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on Macclesfield Road in Wilmslow.  On arrival the crew found the vehicle well alight and that no-one was unaccounted for.  A quantity of diesel had leaked onto the road.  Firefighters closed the road and extinguished the fire using water.  They then damped the vehicle down to make it safe and used specialist absorbent mats to prevent diesel from entering the drains.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
