 Cooker fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 24/01/2019 - 18:11 Location: Welsh Row, Nantwich. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a cooker fire at an address in Welsh Row, Nantwich. The fire was out on arrival and crews checked the fire hadn't spread using a thermal imaging camera. They the removed the cooker and isolated the electrics.
