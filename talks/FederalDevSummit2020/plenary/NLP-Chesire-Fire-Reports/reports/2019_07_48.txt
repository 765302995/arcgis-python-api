 RTC in Crewe Time of Incident: 23/07/2019 - 13:14 Location: Butterton Lane, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Crewe and one from Alsager Crews attended to a two-vehicle collision on Butterton Lane in Crewe.  The road was closed while firefighters safely extricated one woman from the vehicle.   She was handed into the care of paramedics with injuries to her legs.
