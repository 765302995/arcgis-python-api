Female released from car after a collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/05/2017 - 14:15 Location: Bridge Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington, Penketh and Knutsford attended A female had to be released from a car by firefighters after a road traffic collision on Bridge Street, Warrington. The collision involved two cars and crews, and a nearby construction crew, moved a vehicle to allow the female to be released from the other car.
