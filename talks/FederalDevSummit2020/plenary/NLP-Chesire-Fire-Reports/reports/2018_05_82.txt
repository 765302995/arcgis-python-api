 Rubbish fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 20/05/2018 - 10:36 Location: Llandovery Close, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Fire crews were called to a large quantity of rubbish on fire in a back garden on Llandovery Close, Winsford. Crews extinguished the fire, which mainly consisted of plastic, using a hose reel jet.
