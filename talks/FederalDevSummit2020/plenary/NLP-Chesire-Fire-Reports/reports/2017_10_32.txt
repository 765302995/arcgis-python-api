Hedge on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/10/2017 - 14:43 Location: Whittle Hall Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to a hedge fire on Whittle Hall Lane, Warrington. Approximately 20ft of hedgerow was well alight on arrival of the crews and they used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
