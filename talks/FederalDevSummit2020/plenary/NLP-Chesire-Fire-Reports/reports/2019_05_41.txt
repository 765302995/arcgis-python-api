 Vehicle fire M53 Time of Incident: 25/05/2019 - 05:01 Location: M53, junction 8. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Ellesmere Port and one from Stockton Heath Fire crews attended to a vehicle well alight in undergrowth close to the M53 at junction 8.  Wearing one breathing apparatus, a hose reel jet was used to put out the fire and the area was made safe.
