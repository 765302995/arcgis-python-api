 Lorry fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 25/05/2018 - 05:36 Location: M6 southbound between junction 22 and 21a. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Leigh (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Fire crews were called to a lorry fire on the M6 Southbound between junction 22 and 21a. On arrival crews found that the cab of the lorry was well alight. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for 60 minutes.
