Shed and greenhouse on fire in Weaverham Time of Incident: 06/04/2017 - 00:31 Location: Walnut Avenue, Weaverham, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Winsford A shed and a greenhouse were on fire at the back of a Walnut Avenue bungalow. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the blaze under control and then damped the area down.
