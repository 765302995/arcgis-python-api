 Cow rescued from canal in Little Bollington Time of Incident: 12/09/2018 - 05:36 Location: Park Lane, Little Bollington Attendance: The animal rescue units from Bollington and Lymm attended. One fire engine from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service also attended. Fire crews were called to rescue a cow which had become stuck in a canal on Park Lane, Little Bollington. Crews managed to rescue the cow from the water and then corralled a further seven cows off the towpath and back into a farmer's field.
