Car fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 11/03/2017 - 23:11 Location: Saville Court, Winsford. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Winsford A hose reel jet was used to put out a car fire on Saville Court, Winsford.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone has information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
