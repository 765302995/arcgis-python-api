Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 05/06/2016 - 04:23 Location: Deneside Avenue, Crewe. Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe A hose reel jet was used to put out a car fire on Deneside Avenue, Crewe.  This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Constabulary on 101.
