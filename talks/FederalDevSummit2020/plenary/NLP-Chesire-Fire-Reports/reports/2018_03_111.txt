 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/03/2018 - 22:32 Location: Bowoood Court, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Fire crews were called to Bowood Court, Warrington, after a car caught on fire after striking a lamppost. Crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. No one was injured during the incident.
