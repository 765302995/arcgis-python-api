Settee and refuse fire on Dart Walk in Winsford Time of Incident: 22/10/2016 - 21:14 Location: Dart Walk, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford A quantity of refuse and a settee was on fire on Dart Walk in Winsfords, so firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  This fire may have been started deliberately. If you have any information call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
