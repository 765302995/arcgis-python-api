Shed fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 06/02/2016 - 16:28 Location: Aldersey Road, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Aldersey Road, Crewe.  When they arrived crews found that the fire involved a shed and a fence. Crews used hose reel jets to tackle the fire. A man was also treated at the scene for burns to his foot.
