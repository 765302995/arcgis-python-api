Road traffic collision near Kettleshulme Time of Incident: 15/09/2016 - 16:59 Location: Macclesfield Road, Gnathole near Kettleshulme Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington Three vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision in Gnathole. The vehicles were a car, a van and a horse box. Nobody was injured in the collision and there were no animals in the horse box. Firefighters made the scene safe.
