Assisting police in search of building in Runcorn Time of Incident: 27/05/2016 - 10:48 Location: East Lane, Runcorn. Attendance: Four fire engines and the hazardous materials unit Firefighters were called to assist police in the search of a large disused building in Runcorn. East Lane south was partially closed by police. A search of the building took place after it was understood that it may contain asbestos. No one was found by the crews.
