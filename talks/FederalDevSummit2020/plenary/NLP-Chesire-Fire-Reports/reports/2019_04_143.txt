 Refuse fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 15/04/2019 - 15:07 Location: Hatton Lane, Greenbank, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters from Northwich used one hose reel jet to extinguish a large refuse fire at the rear of a residential address in Greenbank.  The team were detained at the address for around 20 minutes.
