 Front door fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 06/11/2018 - 00:49 Location: Eton Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Firefighters attended to the front door of a house on fire on Eton Road in Ellesmere Port. The fire had been extinguished by police prior to crews arriving.
