Scooter deliberately set on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/02/2015 - 21:55 Location: Murdishaw Avenue, Murdishaw, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A scooter which had been deliberately set on fire on a public walkway in Runcorn, required firefighters to attend and use one hose reel to extinguish it. The crew then removed the scooter to the side of the walkway and out of pedestrians way before leaving. This incident is being treated as arson so if anyone has any information they should call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
