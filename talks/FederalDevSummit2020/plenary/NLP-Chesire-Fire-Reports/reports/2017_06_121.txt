Car on fire on road in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/06/2017 - 10:14 Location: Cromwell Avenue, Callands, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington A car that was well alight on the road was extinguished by firefighters using a hose reel. The fire is believed to have been started due to an electrical problem and police attended to deal with the traffic management.
