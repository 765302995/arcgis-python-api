 Kitchen fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 06/05/2019 - 18:21 Location: Folkestone Way, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Widnes Two fire engines were called to a fire involving an electric cooker on Folkestone Way, Runcorn. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews then cleared smoke from the property using a large fan. Crews were at the scene for 30 minutes.
