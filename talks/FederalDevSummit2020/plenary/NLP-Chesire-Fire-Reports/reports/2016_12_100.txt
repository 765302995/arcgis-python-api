House fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 12/12/2016 - 17:18 Location: Thorn Lane, Hartford, Northwich. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Northwich and Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Thorn Lane in Hartford, Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire in the kitchen of a semi-detached bungalow. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus tackled the blaze with main water and hose reel jets as well as a large fan.   Crews remained at the scene for about an hour.   The property had been fitted with smoke alarms that were activated during the incident. The homeowners and their dogs were thankfully unhurt.
