Casualty care after Elton fire Time of Incident: 24/05/2017 - 19:54 Location: Quinn Glass, Ash Road, Elton Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters gave casualty care to three men who had suffered smoke inhalation after putting out a small fire in bottling machinery. The casualties were handed into the care of the ambulance service.
