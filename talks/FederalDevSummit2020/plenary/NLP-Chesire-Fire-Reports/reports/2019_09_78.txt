 Vehicle fire in Willaston Time of Incident: 16/09/2019 - 16:03 Location: Cheerbrook Road, Willaston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Fire crews attended to a car well alight on Cheerbrook Road in Willaston.  Firefighters, wearing two breathing apparatus, used two hose reels to extinguish the fire.  The thermal imaging camera was then used to check the fire had been fully put out.
