House fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 27/06/2016 - 21:17 Location: Somerton Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield and one from Bollington  Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire in Macclesfield caused by unattended cooking. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel and a covering jet to tackle the fire. A high pressure fan was used to ventilate the property of smoke.  Fortunately nobody was trapped or injured in the incident.  The fire was caused by cooking left unattended. Always follow our kitchen safety advice.
