Collision in Frodsham Time of Incident: 01/08/2015 - 15:49 Location: Primrose Lane, Frodsham. Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Primrose Lane, Frodsham.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved a car that had gone onto an embankment.  Two casaualties were inside the vehicle but not trapped.  Crews stabilised the vehicle and the casualties were assisted from the vehicle and passed to the care of the ambulance. Fire crews were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
