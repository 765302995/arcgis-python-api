 Scooter fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 10/04/2018 - 20:39 Location: Valley Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 8.40pm firefighters were called to motorised scooter fire on Valley Drive in Ellesmere Port. The crew used water to extinguish the fire and were at the scene for around 20 minutes
