 Sheep rescued from embankment in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/09/2019 - 11:10 Location: Walsingham Drive, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Knutsford and one from Bollington Crews attended to reports of sheep on a narrow embankment on Walsingham Drive in Runcorn.  Once located, the sheep were escorted to a place of safety in a farmer's field.
