House fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/07/2017 - 21:03 Location: Viva Way, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port The ground floor of a detached house on Viva Way was heavily involved in fire. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to bring the fire under control. Crews were at the scene for one hour and twenty minutes.
