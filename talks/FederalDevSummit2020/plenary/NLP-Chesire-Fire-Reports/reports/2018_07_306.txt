 Small bush fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 16:51 Location: Church Lane/Regal Close, Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 4.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Church Lane/Regal Close in Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire involving bushes. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
