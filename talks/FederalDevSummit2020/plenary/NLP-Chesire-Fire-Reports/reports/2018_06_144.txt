 Car fire in Chester Time of Incident: 19/06/2018 - 09:21 Location: Moston Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 9.20am firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on Moston Road in Chester. On arrival the crew found that the vehicle was well alight.  The road was closed in both directions as firefighters worked at the scene. They used breathing apparatus and two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  The crew were in attendance for around 40 minutes.  Police also attended.
