 Unknown substance released in solicitors building in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/08/2018 - 15:53 Location: Mandarin Court, Centre Park, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Greater Manchester At around 3.50pm firefighters were called to reports of an unknown substance being released in a first floor locker room of Restons Solicitors in Mandarin Court, Centre Park, Warrington. The building was evacuated and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet on the substance. The crews could find no signs of gas or odours. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for heat sources and ventilated the property before declaring it safe. They were in attendance for more than an hour.
