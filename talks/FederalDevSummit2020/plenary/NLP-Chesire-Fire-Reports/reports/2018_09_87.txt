 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/09/2018 - 21:33 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 9.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a wooded area near to a community centre on Grasmere Avenue, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered a wheelie bin well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire using water and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For advice on how to prevent your wheelie bins from becoming a target for arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
