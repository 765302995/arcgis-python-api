 Small washing machine fire at Leighton Hospital Time of Incident: 10/09/2018 - 08:31 Location: Leighton Hospital, Middlewich Road, Leighton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe, one from Winsford and one from Middlewich Fire crews were called to a fire in a launderette washing machine at Leighton Hospital. Crews extinguished the small fire, which is believed to have been caused by an overheated engine in the machine, using a fire extinguisher.
