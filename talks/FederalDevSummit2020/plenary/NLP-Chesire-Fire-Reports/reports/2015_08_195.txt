Building fire in Handforth Time of Incident: 02/08/2015 - 07:35 Location: Wilmslow Road, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Smoke was seen coming from the rear doors of a property in Handforth and firefighters were called to investigate. Crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, gained entry to the building which measure approximately 15 metres by 10 metres and found a fire on the ground floor. A hose reel and a main jet were used to put out the fire and a high pressure fan used to clear the area of smoke. A search of the first floor was also made and found to be clear.  Following a fire investigation, it was found the fire was caused by an electrical fault.
