 House fire in Bickerton Time of Incident: 22/06/2018 - 18:53 Location: Long Lane, Bickerton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Malpas Firefighters from Malpas were called to reports of a house fire on Long Lane in Bickerton. On arrival crews found that the fire had been extinguished. The team used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots and provided first aid to two adults and two children, who were all treated for smoke inhalation. One of the adults was then passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service and taken to hospital as a precautionary measure.
