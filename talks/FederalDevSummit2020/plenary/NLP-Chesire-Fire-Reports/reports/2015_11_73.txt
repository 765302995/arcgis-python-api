Chimney on fire in Rainow Time of Incident: 15/11/2015 - 21:07 Location: Johns Close, Rainow Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a chimney on fire in Rainow. The fire involved a wood burning stove and the crew removed fire boarding from the fireplace to gain access. They then used water from a back pack to extinguish the fire. Chimney safety advice
