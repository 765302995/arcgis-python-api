 Shed fire in Tabley Time of Incident: 14/03/2018 - 13:47 Location: Heyrose Lane, Tabley Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Holmes Chapel and one from Lymm At 1.54pm firefighters were called to a shed fire on Heyrose Lane in Tabley. They found the shed well alight and used two hose reel jets to extinguish the flames. Crews were in attendance for approximately half-an-hour. No-one is believed to have been injured by the fire.
