Bird rescued from netting in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 03/12/2015 - 13:42 Location: Waters Green, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield The RSPCA called firefighters to assist with rescuing a bird that had been stuck in netting for several days. The crew used a short ladder and small gear to create an opening in the netting for the bird to get out.
