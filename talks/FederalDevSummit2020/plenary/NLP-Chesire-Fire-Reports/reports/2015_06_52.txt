Outbuilding fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 20/06/2015 - 14:56 Location: Stokesay Court, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Stokesay Court, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived they found an outbuilding full of rubbish on fire. Crews used hose reel jets to put the fire out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
