 Outhouse building fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 29/11/2018 - 06:19 Location: Wood Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to a fire at an outhouse on Wood Street in Crewe.  When fire crews arrived smoke was seen issuing from the ground floor extension. Wearing two breathing apparatus, firefighters used one hose reel and had a covering jet in place to help bring the fire under control.  No one was in the property at the time.  Once the fire was tackled, crews ventilated the area and checked the ceiling to see if the fire had spread.  Firefighters will continue to monitor the temperature until it has been made safe.
