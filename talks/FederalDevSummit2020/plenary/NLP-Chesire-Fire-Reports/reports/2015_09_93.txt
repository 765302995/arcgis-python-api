Collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 12/09/2015 - 10:02 Location: Junctions 16 and 17 on the M6. Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe and Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called to a report of a collision between Junctions 16 and 17 on the M6.  When they arrived they found that the incident involved five cars. There was nobody trappped and so crews helped make the cars safe and assisted the ambulance service.
