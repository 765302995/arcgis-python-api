Chimney fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 07/09/2015 - 10:33 Location: Barnabas Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters used a ladder and height safety equipment to investigate a chimney fire in Crewe.  Smoke was issuing from the chimney stack when the crew arrived and a thermal imaging camera was used to check for hotspots. One hose reel was also used to damp down the area and neighbouring properties were checked to ensure the fire had not spread. Chimney safety advice
