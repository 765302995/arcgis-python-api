 Small vehicle collision in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/12/2018 - 14:37 Location: Newbridge Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 2.30pm two fire engines from Ellesmere Port were mobilised to reports of a collision at a motocross event on Newbridge Road, Little Stanney, Ellesmere Port. Crews attended the scene and found that the collision involved a motocross bike and that the rider's arm was trapped under the bike. Firefighters were able to free the casualty from the bike before passing them over to the care of paramedics. Firefighters were in attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
