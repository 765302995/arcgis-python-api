Oil spill in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/09/2017 - 12:07 Location: Central Expressway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn, one from Widnes, one from Warrington and one from Penketh along with the command unit and supporting pump from Northwich Fire crews were called to a large spill of cooking oil on the Central Expressway, Runcorn. The spill occurred at 9am but fire crews were not called until later. Halton Council had closed the roads around the spillage, which was next to the Mersey Gateway site. The incident involved the spillage of several hundred litres of cooking oil. Firefighters assisted the council and Mersey Gateway staff with the clean up operation using absorbent granules. Crews were at the scene for two hours and 30 minutes.
