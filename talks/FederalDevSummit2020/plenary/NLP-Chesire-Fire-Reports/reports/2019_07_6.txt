 House fire in Chester Time of Incident: 28/07/2019 - 12:06 Location: Albion Street, Chester. Attendance: Three fire engines attended from Chester, Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane Shortly after 12pm three fire engines from Chester, Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane attended reports of a building fire on Albion Street Chester.  On arrival crews found a house which was on fire and  heavily smoke logged.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, entered the building and used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.   They then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.  Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately 45 minutes.
