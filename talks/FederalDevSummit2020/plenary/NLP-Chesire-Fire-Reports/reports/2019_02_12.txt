 Small fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/02/2019 - 06:59 Location: Bewsey Farm Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews attended to a small fire inside a blue metal container at the top of a farm on Bewsey Farm Close in Warrington.  Crews wore a breathing apparatus for comfort and then put out the fire using a hose reel.
