 Reported house fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 25/12/2018 - 11:35 Location: Cornwall Close, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield At 11:35am on Christmas Day firefighters from Macclesfield were called to reports of a house fire at an address on Cornwall Close. On arrival at the scene the team found that there had been a small fire which had been extinguished by the homeowner. One person was provided with oxygen therapy at the scene as a precautionary measure. Crews were at the scene for approximately one hour.
