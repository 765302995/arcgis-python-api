 Skip fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 15/08/2018 - 18:34 Location: Park Road, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a skip fire at a yard in Park Road, Nantwich. The crews used a hose reel jet and a stack drag to put out the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
