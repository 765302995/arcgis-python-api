 Fire in the open, Shavington Time of Incident: 07/12/2019 - 16:24 Location: Crewe Road, Shavington Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to deal with a fire in the open that was billowing black smoke. They found quite a large fire involving tyres and bits of scrap including fibre glass and plastic. The crew used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames, and the occupier of the property agreed to damp down with his garden hose so that they could be on their way.
