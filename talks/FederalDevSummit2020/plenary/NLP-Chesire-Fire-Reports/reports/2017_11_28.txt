House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 20/11/2017 - 15:37 Location: Bramhall Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe and one from Nantwich Fire crews were called to a fire in a first floor flat on Bramhall Road, Crewe. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which was in the kitchen. They then cleared smoke from the property using a large fan.
