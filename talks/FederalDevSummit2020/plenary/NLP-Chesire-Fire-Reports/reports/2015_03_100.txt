Chimney fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/03/2015 - 18:19 Location: Pillmoss Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington and Stockton Heath A chimney fire spread to the soffit and facia board of a detached two storey house. Firefighters used one hose reel jet and a seven metre ladder to tackle the fire.  Canvas buckets were used inside the property to deal with the fire.  The roof space was inspected.
