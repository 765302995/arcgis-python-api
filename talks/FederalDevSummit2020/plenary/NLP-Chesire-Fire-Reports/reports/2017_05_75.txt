Motorbike on fire in a field in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/05/2017 - 19:06 Location: Shepherds Row, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn A motorbike was on fire in the middle of a field, so firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.   The fire is believed to have been started deliberately so anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
