Road traffic collision on the A556 in Rudheath, Northwich Time of Incident: 06/05/2016 - 20:40 Location: A556, Rudheath, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Middlewich At 20:40 pm fire crews were sent to a road traffic collision involving one vehicle on the A556 in Rudheath, Northwich.  The vehicle was on its side and one male casualty is trapped inside.  Firefighters used cutting gear and a longboard to release the casualty, who was then handed to the care of paramedics.  Police closed the A556 near to Roberts Bakery (towards Chester).
