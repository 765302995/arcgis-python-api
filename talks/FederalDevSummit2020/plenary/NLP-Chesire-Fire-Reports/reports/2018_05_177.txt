 Trapped person freed following collision in Sandbach Time of Incident: 08/05/2018 - 18:52 Location: Hall Lane, Warmingham, Sandbach Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Sandbach and one from Crewe At around 6.50pm firefighters were called to a collision involving two vehicles on Hall Lane in Warmingham, Sandbach. On arrival crews discovered that one person was trapped inside one of the vehicles. Firefighters removed the casualty from the vehicle and assisted in getting another person out of it. The crews provided casualty care before an ambulance arrived. Once the two casualties were removed from the vehicle they were handed over to the care of paramedics. The road was closed while emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters were at the scene for around half-an-hour.
