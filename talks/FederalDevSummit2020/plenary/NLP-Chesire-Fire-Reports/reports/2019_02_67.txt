 Fire in a bush in Crewe Time of Incident: 18/02/2019 - 17:58 Location: Wistaston Green Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Fire crews were called to a fire in a bush on Wistaston Green Road, Crewe. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which had been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please contact Cheshire Police.
