 Car on fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 20/10/2019 - 10:20 Location: M6 northbound, between junctions 18 and 19 Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Sandbach, one from Crewe and one from Trafford At 10.20am firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on the M6 northbound, between junctions 18 and 19.  On arrival crews found the saloon car well alight.  The first two lanes of the motorbike were cordoned off as firefighters, including one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a dry powder extinguisher to put out the fire.  Firefighters were in attendance for more than 40 minutes.
