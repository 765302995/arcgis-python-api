 Smoke coming from washing machine in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/09/2018 - 12:20 Location: Cromdale Way, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 12.20pm firefighters were called to reports of smoke coming from a washing machine at a domestic property in Cromdale Way, Great Sankey, Warrington. The crew used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots on the washing machine and make sure that it was safe. Firefighters also ventilated the property. The smoke is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
