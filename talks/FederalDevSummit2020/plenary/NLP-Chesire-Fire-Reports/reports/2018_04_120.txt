 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 08/04/2018 - 02:59 Location: Valiant Close, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one fire engine attended from Birchwood. At approximately 3am firefighters from Warrington and Birchwood were called to reports of a kitchen fire. The fire was out on arrival but a 20-year old man was found to have sustained minor burns. He was initially treated by firefighters at the scene before being passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service.
