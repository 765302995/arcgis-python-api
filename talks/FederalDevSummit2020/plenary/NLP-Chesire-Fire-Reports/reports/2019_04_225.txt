 Faulty gas fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 06/04/2019 - 05:40 Location: Hope Farm Road, Ellesmere Port. CH66 2NR Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port At about 5.40am, firefighters were called to reports of a faulty gas fire at a house in Ellesmere Port.  They attended and isolated the gas supply to the fire to make it safe.
