Roof on fire in Wrenbury Time of Incident: 23/11/2015 - 13:53 Location: New Road, Wrenbury Attendance: Two fire engine from Crewe and one from Nantwich. Firefighters were called to a fire in a roof space in Wrenbury. One hose reel and a covering jet was used and timbers were cut away to expose the fire. Salvage operations also took place and police attended for traffic management.  Updated at 15:45  New Road to the junction of Nantwich Road is currently closed due to structural issued with the building.
