Railway embankment fire Time of Incident: 17/10/2015 - 13:05 Location: Lord Street, Warrington Attendance: A fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire near a railway embankment off Lord Street, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that a bonfire had spread to the embankment. A hose reel jet was used to put the fire out.
