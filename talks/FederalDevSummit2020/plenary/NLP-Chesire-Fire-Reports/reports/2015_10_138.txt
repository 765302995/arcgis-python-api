Boat washed up in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/10/2015 - 20:04 Location: Knutsford Road,Warrington Attendance: The line rescue unit from Knutsford and a fire engine and one from Warrington attended Firefighters were called to a dinghy which appears to have been washed up in the River Mersey, just up from Howley Weir.  The crews found an upturned vessel and launched the rescue boat and searched the area on foot and with a thermal imaging camera.  No persons were located and the incident was handed over to the police.
