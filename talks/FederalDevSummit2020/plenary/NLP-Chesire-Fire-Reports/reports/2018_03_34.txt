 Car fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 25/03/2018 - 18:40 Location: Manchester Road, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters attended vehicle well alight in Manchester Road, Knutsford. Crews used one breathing apparatus and hose reel  to extinguish the flames. They then used the hose reel to cool the vehicle down.
