Bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 22/10/2017 - 20:56 Location: Albert Square, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Fire crews were called to Albert Square, Widnes, after reports of a large wheelie bin on fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
