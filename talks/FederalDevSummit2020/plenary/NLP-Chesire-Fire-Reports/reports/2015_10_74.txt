Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/10/2015 - 02:23 Location: Sharp Street, Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Sharp Street, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that there was a wheelie bin on fire against the door. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
