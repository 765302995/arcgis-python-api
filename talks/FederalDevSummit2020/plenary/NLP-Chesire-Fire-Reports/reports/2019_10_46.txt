 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/10/2019 - 23:25 Location: Withers Avenue, Orford, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 11.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Withers Avenue, Orford, Warrington.  On arrival the crew found a wheelie bin on fire.  Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around five minutes.  For advice on how to prevent wheelie bin fires visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
