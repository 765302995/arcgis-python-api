Collision in Birchwood Time of Incident: 01/04/2017 - 12:27 Location: Dewhurst Road, Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Birchwood and Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Dewhurst Road, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two cars. A woman was trapped inside. Firefighters stabilised the vehicle before removing the roof to remove the casualty. The woman was then handed over to paramedics and taken to hospital. Crews were on scene for one hour.
