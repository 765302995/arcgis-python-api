 Animal rescue in Calveley Time of Incident: 19/02/2018 - 03:58 Location: Nantwich Road, Calveley. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley, one fire engine from Lymm and the animal rescue unit from Bollington. Shortly before 4am firefighters from Tarporley, the animal rescue unit from Bollington and the rope rescue unit from Lymm attended a reports of a horse trapped in a stable on Nantwich Road, Calveley. The crews,  in conjunction with the vet on scene, righted to horse to a standing position. The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately two hours 15 minutes.
