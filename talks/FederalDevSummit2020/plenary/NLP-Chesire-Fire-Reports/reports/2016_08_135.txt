Wheelie bins on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/08/2016 - 02:45 Location: Poplars Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Two wheelie bins were on fire at the side of Poplars Avenue. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.  Always follow our wheelie bin safety advice to help prevent this kind of crime.
