Fire involving a door in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/03/2017 - 16:14 Location: Harbord Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a fire involving the front door of a property and used a hose reel to extinguish it. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property. A fire investigation into the cause of this fire is currently underway.
