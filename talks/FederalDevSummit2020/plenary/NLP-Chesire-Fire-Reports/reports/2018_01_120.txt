 Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 31/12/2017 - 21:44 Location: Abbey Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a property on Abbey Court, Ellesmere Port, after reports of a fire in a kitchen. The fire, which was caused by a tea towel being left on the hob, was out on arrival and crews used a large fan to remove smoke from the property.
