 Small fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 12/01/2019 - 16:35 Location: Delamere Street, Winsford. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to a fire involving wood, metal and canisters in land off Delamere Street in Winsford. Crews used a hose reel and breathing apparatus to put out the flames.
