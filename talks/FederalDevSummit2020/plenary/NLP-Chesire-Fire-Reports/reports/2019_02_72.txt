 Car fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 18/02/2019 - 00:26 Location: The Underway, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a car well alight at The Underway in Runcorn. Crews used breathing apparatus and a hose reel to put out the fire which had badly damaged the car and caused slight damage to another.
