Car well alight in Knutsford Time of Incident: 10/06/2017 - 05:20 Location: Booths Park Estate Road, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford Firefighter extinguishing a car on fireFirefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish a fire involving a car. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.  Police were requested to attend this incident.   It is believed that this fire may have been started deliberately. If anyone knows anything about how this fire may of started then they should call Cheshire Police on 101. Alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
