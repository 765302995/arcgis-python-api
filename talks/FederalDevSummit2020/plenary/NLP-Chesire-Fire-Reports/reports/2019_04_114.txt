 Two vehicle collision at Hapsford roundabout Time of Incident: 18/04/2019 - 15:28 Location: A5117, Hapsford roundabout Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane Shortly before 3.30pm firefighters from Ellesmere Port and Powey Lane were dispatched to reports of a collision on the A5117 in Hapsford.  On arrival the team discovered one person was medically trapped in a car following a two vehicle collision at Hapsford roundabout.  The team used electronic cutting equipment to release the casualty and they have since been passed to the care of paramedics.
