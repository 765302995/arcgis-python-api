Chemical leak from HGV in Runcorn Time of Incident: 15/12/2017 - 20:56 Location: Bankes Lane, Weston Point, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn. One fire engine from Widnes. Firefighters were called to Bankes Lane, Weston Point in Runcorn following reports of hydrochloric acid leaking from an HGV tanker. Crews neutralised the acid and contained the leak.
