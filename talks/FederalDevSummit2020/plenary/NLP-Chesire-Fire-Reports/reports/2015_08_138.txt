Decking fire in Disley Time of Incident: 09/08/2015 - 04:26 Location: Storey Road, Disley. Attendance: A fire engine from Poynton Firefighters were called to a repirt of a fire at an address on Storey Road, Disley.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving some wooden decking, which had spread from a fire pit that had been left unattended. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out. They also had to cut away the decking to ensure that there was no further risk of fire.
