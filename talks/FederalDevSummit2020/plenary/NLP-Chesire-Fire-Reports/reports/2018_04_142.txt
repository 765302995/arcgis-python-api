 Vehicle fire in Wimboldsley Time of Incident: 04/04/2018 - 14:14 Location: Nantwich Road, Wimboldsley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Firefighters from Middlewich used one hose reel jet and breathing apparatus to extinguish a car hire at a residential property on Nantwich Road, Wimboldsley. The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately one hour. The fire is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault.
