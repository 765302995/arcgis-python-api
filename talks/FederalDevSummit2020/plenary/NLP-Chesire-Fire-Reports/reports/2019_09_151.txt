 Garden fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 02/09/2019 - 13:57 Location: Byron Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield. Firefighters attended a fire in the back garden of an address in Byron Street, Macclesfield. Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the fire.
