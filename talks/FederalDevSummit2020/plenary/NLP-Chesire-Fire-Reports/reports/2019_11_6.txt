 Possible gas leak in Chester Time of Incident: 26/11/2019 - 17:53 Location: Brook Street, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly before 6pm firefighters from Chester were called to reports of a possible gas leak at a flat on Brook Street.  Firefighters attended the address and used a gas detector to monitor the air quality in the building. The crew were unable to find any trace of a leak.  The crew were in attendance at the address for around 20 minutes.
