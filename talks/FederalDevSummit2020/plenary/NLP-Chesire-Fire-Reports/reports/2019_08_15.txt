 Woman trapped following a collision near Tarporley Time of Incident: 27/08/2019 - 15:18 Location: A49, Tarporley Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Tarporley and one from Winsford Shortly after 3.15pm firefighters from Winsford and Tarporley attended reports of a collision on the A49 near Tarporley.  On arrival the crews found that a woman was medically trapped in her car.  Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to get the casualty out of the vehicle.  She was then handed over to the care of paramedics.  Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
