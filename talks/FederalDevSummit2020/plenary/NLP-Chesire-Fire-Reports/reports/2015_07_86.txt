Kitchen fire in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 18/07/2015 - 20:37 Location: Rees Crescent in Holmes Chapel Attendance: Three fire engines from Crewe and Winsford Three fire crews were sent to a report of a building fire on Rees Crescent in Holmes Chapel. On arrival the fire crews found a fire to be in the kitchen of the property. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus. The fire crews used one hose reel to extinguish the fire. A large fan was then used to ventilate the property.
