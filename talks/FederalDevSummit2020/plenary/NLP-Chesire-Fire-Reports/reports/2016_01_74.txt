Car collides with building in Crewe Time of Incident: 12/01/2016 - 07:03 Location: Flag Lane, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving a car and a building, with one person trapped. The crew released the casualty from the vehicle who was passed to the care of paramedics. The building sustained damage from the collision so a structural engineer was called and the building evacuated as a precaution.
