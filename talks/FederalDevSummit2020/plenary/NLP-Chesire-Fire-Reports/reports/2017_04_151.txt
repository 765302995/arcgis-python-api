Pallet fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 01/04/2017 - 20:34 Location: Woodford Lane, Winsford. Attendance: Fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Woodford Lane in Winsford.  When they arrived crews found that there the fire involved some wooden pallets. Hose reel jets were used to put the fire out.
