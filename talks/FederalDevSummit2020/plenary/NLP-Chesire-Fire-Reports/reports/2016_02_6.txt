House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/02/2016 - 15:15 Location: Grisedale Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Grisedale Avenue, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire in the kitchen of an end terrace property. Fire crews used four breathing appraratus and two hose reels to extinguish the fire. The houses next door were also checked to ensure that there was no fire spread.  There were no reports of any injuries at this incident.
