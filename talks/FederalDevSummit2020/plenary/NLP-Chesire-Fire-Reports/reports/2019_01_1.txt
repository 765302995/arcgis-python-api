 Kitchen fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 13/01/2019 - 06:26 Location: Church Road, Northwich. Attendance:  Two fire engine attended from Northwich, one from Winsford, one from Middlewich, one from Lymm, one from Crewe and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service. Seven fire engines attended a fire at an address in Church Road, Northwich. Crews discovered the fire in the kitchen and basement of a terraced property. As a precaution they checked neighbouring properties to ensure the fire hadn't spread.  During the course of the fire crews used ten breathing apparatus, two hose reels, and safety jets to put out the fire. Crews checked for hotspots and also cut away affected areas.
