 Fire in the open, Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 19/04/2019 - 18:15 Location: Rivacre Valley, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Crews were called following reports of a fire developing near the Rivacre Valley nature reserve. They arrived to find an area of grassland measuring around 15m sq smouldering. They used a backpack to put out the fire, and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
