Grass fires in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/04/2015 - 17:37 Location: Peel House Lane, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters used beaters to extinguish numerous grass fires on old school playing fields in Widnes. The fires are believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
