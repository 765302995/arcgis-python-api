Peat and pallets on fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 18/07/2017 - 13:16 Location: Yeld Lane, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine from Tarporley Firefighters were called to extinguish peat and wooden pallets which were on fire and used two hose reels. A water shuttle was set up to provide water to the fire and a fire break was also set up around plant machinery to protect it.
