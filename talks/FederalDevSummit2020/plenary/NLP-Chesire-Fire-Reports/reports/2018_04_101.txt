 Small fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 12/04/2018 - 15:48 Location: Bailey Crescent, Sandbach. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Sandbach Firefighters attended reports that the smoke alarm had activated at a house in Sandbach. On arrival at the scene crews found that the alarms had activated due to a small fire in a waste paper basket. The crew extinguished the fire and ventilated the property.
