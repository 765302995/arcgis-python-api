 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/10/2019 - 21:54 Location: Scott Avenue, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Shortly before 10pm firefighters were called to reports of a wheelie bin being on fire in Scott Avenue, Widnes, near to Stewards Avenue.  On arrival the crew found the wheelie bin well alight in a wooded area.  Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.  Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.  For advice on how to prevent wheelie bin fires visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
