 Barn fire in Peckforton Time of Incident: 07/11/2018 - 05:44 Location: Hill Lane, Peckforton Attendance: Four fire engines attended, from Tarporley, Crewe, Nantwich and Malpas Shortly before 5.45am firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a farm on Hill Lane, Peckforton. On arrival crews found a barn which was well alight. The roof of the building is believed to contain asbestos, so all crews who dealt with the incident wore respirators. The team used two breathing apparatus, two hose reel jets and a main jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for approximately two hours and 15 minutes.
