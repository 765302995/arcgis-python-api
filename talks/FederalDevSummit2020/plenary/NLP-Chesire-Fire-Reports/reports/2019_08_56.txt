 House fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 22/08/2019 - 21:43 Location: Edwin Street, Widnes Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Widnes and two from Penketh At around 9.45pm firefighters were called to reports of a house fire in Edwin Street, Widnes. A cordon was set up and four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to fight the fire in the terraced property. A covering jet was also used. Once the fire was out the area affected by it was damped down and a large fan was used to clear smoke from the house. Firefighters were in attendance for nearly three hours. The police also attended. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 493212, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
