Boat on fire by Acton Bridge in Northwich Time of Incident: 08/04/2017 - 02:55 Location: by Warrington Road, Acton Bridge, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Firefighters dealt with a fire involving a fibreglass boat (approximately 20 foot by five foot) located by the jetty at Acton Bridge in Northwich. There was an LPG canister on the boat, and 50 litres of diesel.   Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters remained on scene for approximately one and a half hours.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately.  If you can help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, or call Cheshire Police on 101.
