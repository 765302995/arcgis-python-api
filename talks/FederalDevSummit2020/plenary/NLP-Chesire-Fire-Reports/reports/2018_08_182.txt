 Small fire in wooded area in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 01/08/2018 - 11:04 Location: Rotherwood Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 11.05am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a wooded area on Rotherwood Road in Wilmslow. On arrival the crew discovered a small campfire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
