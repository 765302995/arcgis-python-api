Road traffic collision in Frodsham Time of Incident: 04/12/2016 - 02:06 Location: Manley Lane, Frodsham. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Chester Firefighters administered first aid to a casualty, who sustained serious injuries, following a road traffic collision.    Police closed the road.   The casualty was then handed to the care of paramedics and was taken to hospital by ambulance.
