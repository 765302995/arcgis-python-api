Outbuilding fire in Lower Withington Time of Incident: 24/10/2017 - 03:27 Location: Salters Lane, Lower Withington. Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel, one from Macclesfield, two from Congleton and one from Birchwood Firefighters were called to an outbuilding fire on Salters Lane in Lower Withington, Macclesfield. Five fire engines were in attendance with firefighters using two hose reel jets and three main jets to extinguish a large fire. Crews then used a thermal imaging camera to check the building. This incident will be updated when more information becomes available.
