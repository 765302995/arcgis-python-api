 Fire at a residential property in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/01/2019 - 20:08 Location: Kendal Avenue, Warrington. Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Birchwood Shortly after 8pm firefighters from Warrington and Birchwood were called to reports of a fire at a house on Kendal Avenue.  On arrival at the scene the team discovered a fire within a bedroom at the address.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The team then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.  One casualty was treated by paramedics for smoke inhalation; they did not require hospital treatment.  Firefighters were in attendance for approximately four hours.
