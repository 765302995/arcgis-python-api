Flooding in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/12/2015 - 14:44 Location: Densham Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: Fire engines from Birchwood and Widnes Firefighters responded to flooding on Densham Avenue, Warrington.  Crews worked throughout the night to deal with the rising flood water. They used special pumps to get rid of some of the water that was impacting on about 20 homes.  Some people were evacuated to a safe place as the water continued to rise.  The situation had improved this morning and there was signs that the water levels were dropping.
