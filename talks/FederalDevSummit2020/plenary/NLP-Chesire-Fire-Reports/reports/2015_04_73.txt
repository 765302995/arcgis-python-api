Fire in the stair well of an old derelict building in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/04/2015 - 19:36 Location: Crown Gate, Runcorn. Attendance: Four fire engines from Runcorn and Wides and an aerial appliance from Stockton Health Firefighters were called to an old derelict four-storey building on Crown Gate Runcorn, where there was a fire in the stairwell of the building. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.
