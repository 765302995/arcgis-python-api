Two vehicle collision in Helsby Time of Incident: 23/11/2017 - 14:41 Location: Chester Road, Helsby. Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one fire engine from Powey Lane. Firefighters were called to reports of a collision between a car and a HGV on Chester Road in Helsby. Initial indications suggested that one of the occupants was trapped in their vehicle, but on arrival all of occupants were out of the vehicles. Firefighters isolated the batteries on both vehicles to make them safe.
