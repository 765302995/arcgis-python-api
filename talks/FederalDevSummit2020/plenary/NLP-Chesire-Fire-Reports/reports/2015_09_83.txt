Chip pan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/09/2015 - 12:54 Location: Alder Crescent, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A chip pan was the cause of a fire at a house in Warrington. Firefighters assisted a lady from the property and then the crew, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. A high pressure fan was used to clear the property of smoke and the casualty was attended to by ambulance personnel.  Please avoid fires such as this by reading our safety advice - Chip pans
