 Cow rescue in Runcorn Time of Incident: 02/07/2018 - 20:50 Location: Marina Village, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine and an animal rescue team attended from Lymm, one fire engine from Runcorn and one from Bollington The animal rescue team from Lymm were called to reports that a cow had fallen into the canal in Runcorn. On arrival crews found that two cows were both in the waterway. The team used an inflatable raft to get to the animals, they then guided them to a nearby slipway and assisted them out of the canal. The team were in attendance for approximately two hours.
