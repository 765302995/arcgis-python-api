Derelict storage building fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/06/2015 - 17:33 Location: Dallam Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Firefighters extinguished a fire involving a derelict storage building in Warrington.  The crews wore breathing apparatus and used one hose reel to put out the fire and a large fan to ventilate the building. They were at the scene for around half an hour.
