 Multi-vehicle collision in Stockton Heath Time of Incident: 26/07/2019 - 14:45 Location: Fairfield Road, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Penketh and Powey Lane At 2.45pm firefighters from Powey Lane and Penketh attended reports of a multi-vehicle collision on Fairfield Road, Stockton Heath.  On arrival at the scene crews discovered that the collision involved five vehicles.  Four of the vehicles were parked cars and the fifth car was on its side and one person was trapped in the vehicle.  Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to release the casualty and they were passed to the care of paramedics
