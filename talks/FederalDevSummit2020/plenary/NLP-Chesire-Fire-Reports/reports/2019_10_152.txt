 Kitchen fire at a flat in Chester Time of Incident: 30/09/2019 - 21:07 Location: Steam Mill Street, Chester. Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Chester and Tarporley At approximately 9.10pm firefighters were called to reports of a kitchen fire at a third floor flat on Granary Wharf, Chester. On arrival at the address crews found that the fire involved an electric oven. One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus,  used a dry powder extinguisher to put out the fire. Crews then isolated the electrics to the oven and a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots. The also used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property. Crews were in attendance at the incident for around 50 minutes.
