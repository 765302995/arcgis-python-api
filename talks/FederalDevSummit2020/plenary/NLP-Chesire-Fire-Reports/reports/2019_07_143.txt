 Overheated washing machine in Middlewich Time of Incident: 07/07/2019 - 14:17 Location: Seddon Street, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Shortly after 2.15pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke coming from a washing machine at a house in Seddon Street, Middlewich. The crew moved the overheated washing machine from the kitchen to the back garden. Firefighters then cooled the washing machine down with water and monitored its temperature with a thermal imaging camera. Once they were satisfied that the washing machine was safe they left the scene. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes. For washing machine fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/washing-machine-and-dishwasher-fire-safety.
