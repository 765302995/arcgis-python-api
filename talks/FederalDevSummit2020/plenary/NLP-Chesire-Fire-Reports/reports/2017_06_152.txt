Large refuse fire in Barrow, Chester Time of Incident: 05/06/2017 - 16:54 Location: Barrow Lane, Barrow, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Fire crews were called to a refuse fire on Barrow Lane, Barrow, Chester. Crews used two main jets to tackle the fire which was approximately 5m by 3m and was well alight on arrival of the firefighters.
