 Fire in the open in Nantwich Time of Incident: 10/07/2019 - 14:43 Location: Wybunbury Lane, Stapeley, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich At around 2.45pm firefighters were called top reports of a fire in the open in Wybunbury Lane, Stapeley, Nantwich. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning that had got out of control. Firefighters tackled the fire with a hose reel jet. Once the fire was under control they turned over affected materials and damped the area down to make it safe. Firefighters also monitored temperatures with a thermal imaging camera. They were in attendance for an hour.
