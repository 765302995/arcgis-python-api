 Burst water pipe in Lostock Gralam, Northwich Time of Incident: 03/07/2018 - 15:00 Location: Griffiths Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Northwich and one from Middlewich At around 3pm firefighters were called to reports of a burst water pipe on Griffiths Road in Lostock Gralam, Northwich. On arrival crews isolated the high pressure water main and began draining the liquid, which was confirmed to be fresh water. Firefighters liaised with engineers at the location and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
