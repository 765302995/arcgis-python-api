Chimney fire in Adlington Time of Incident: 21/01/2015 - 21:22 Location: Mill Lane, Adlington Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton Firefighters were called to a chimney fire which involved both the chimney and the fireplace.  The flames were put out using a chimney hose and buckets of water.  The crews then dismantled the stove so they could tackle the chimney breast fire using hoses.  A thermal imaging camera was used to check there were no further hot spots.  The loft space was also checked to make sure there was no fire spread.  Please see our chimney safety advice - Chimneys
