Road traffic collision in Lymm Time of Incident: 10/05/2016 - 08:37 Location: Stockport Road, Lymm Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath Two cars were involved in a road traffic collision on the A56 near Thelwall. Firefighters were at the scene. Nobody was trapped in either vehicle but two casualties, a man and a woman, were handed into the care of paramedics. Firefighters made the vehicles safe and fended off traffic. .
