 Skip fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 28/02/2019 - 20:18 Location: Stanneylands Road, Wilmslow Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Wilmslow and one from Poynton At around 8.20pm firefighters were called to a fire in the open on Stanneylands Road in Wilmslow. On arrival crews discovered a fire involving a large skip. Firefighters set up a water shuttle to get enough water to the area. They extinguished the fire with an extended hose reel jet. They then damped the area down to make it safe. Firefighters were in attendance for less than two hours.
