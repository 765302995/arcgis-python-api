Lorry fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/08/2016 - 19:28 Location: North Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the engine compartment of a vehicle transporter. The fire was out on arrival; the crew used a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no further areas of heat. The fire is believed to have been the result of an electrical fault.
