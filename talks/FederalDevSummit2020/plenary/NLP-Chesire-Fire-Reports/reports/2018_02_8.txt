 Skip fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 26/02/2018 - 14:37 Location: Dalton Way, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford. One fire engine from Northwich. Firefighters attended a skip fire on Dalton Way, Middlewich.  The had fire also affected an adjoining building and crews used one breathing apparatus and two hose reels to bring the fire under control.
