Chip pan fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 05/02/2016 - 17:34 Location: Alder Lane, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Alder Lane, Warrington.  When they arrived crews found that the fire had involved a chip pan but this was out when they arrived. Crews cleared smoke from the property.
