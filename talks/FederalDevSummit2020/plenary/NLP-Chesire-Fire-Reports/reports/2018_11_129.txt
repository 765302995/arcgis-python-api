 Wheelie bin fires in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/11/2018 - 21:42 Location: Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 9.40pm firefighters were called to reports of two wheelie bins being on fire at separate locations on Grasmere Avenue in Warrington. One of the fires was out on arrival but the other wheelie bin was well alight. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for less than 15 minutes. The fires are believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fires should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For advice on preventing your wheelie bins from becoming a target for arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
