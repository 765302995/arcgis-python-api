Tumble dryer smoking in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 21/03/2017 - 08:59 Location: Sutton Way, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to smoke coming from a tumble dryer in Ellesmere Port. The tumble dryer was moved to outside the property and a hose reel was then used to extinguish the fire.    Tumble dryer fire safety
