 Fire at a retail park in Runcorn Time of Incident: 20/10/2019 - 16:24 Location: Halton Lea, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly before 4.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open at Trident Retail and Leisure Park, Halton Lea, Runcorn.  On arrival the crew discovered a small fire involving wood near to two metal containers.  Firefighters extinguished the initial fire with a hose reel jet.  But the fire had spread to a container containing clothing.  Firefighters gained access to the container using a grinder and then used the hose reel to fully extinguish the fire.  They were in attendance for nearly an hour.  Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101 or give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries.
