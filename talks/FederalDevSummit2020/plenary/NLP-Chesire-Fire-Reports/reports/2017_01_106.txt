Collision in Disley Time of Incident: 06/01/2017 - 22:49 Location: Buxton Road West, Disley. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Poynton Firefighters responded to a report of a collision on Buxton Road West, Disley.  When they arrived crews found that a car had hit a telegraph pole. Four people had got out of the car and were thankfully unhurt.  Crews made the scene safe before leaving the scene.
