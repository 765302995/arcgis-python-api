 Electrical fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 03/04/2018 - 17:05 Location: Oxford Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield and one fire engine attended from Bollington Shortly after 5pm firefighters from Macclesfield and Bollington attended reports of a building fire on Oxford Road, Macclesfield, involving electrical intake in the cellar of commercial premises. Crews used a covering jet, two breathing apparatus, one hose reel and co2 extinguisher to extinguish the fire and were in attendance at the scene for two hours.
