Cooker fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 13/10/2016 - 17:58 Location: Grangemoor, Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Widnes and Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Grangemoor, Runcorn.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving a cooker. Firefighters dealt with the incident and carried out a Home Safety Assessment as there was no working smoke alarms.
