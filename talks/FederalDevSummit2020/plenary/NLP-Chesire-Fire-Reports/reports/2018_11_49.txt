 Two-vehicle collision in Moston Time of Incident: 22/11/2018 - 07:19 Location: Clay Lane, Moston Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Crewe At around 7.20am two fire engines from Crewe were mobilised to reports of a collision on Clay Lane, Moston. On arrival the crews discovered the collision involved a car and a van, and that the van was on fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and cool the van. They also provided first aid to the driver of the car at the scene. None of the occupants required hospital treatment. Firefighters were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
