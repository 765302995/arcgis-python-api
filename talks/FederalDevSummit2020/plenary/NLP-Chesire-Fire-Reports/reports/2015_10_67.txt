Electrical fire in Chester Time of Incident: 18/10/2015 - 18:36 Location: Weston Grove, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters put a cordon in place while a fire involving an electrical junction box in a manhole was dealt with in Chester.  A hose reel was used to try and cool the area until an electrical engineer arrived and isolated the supply.  The crew handed over the incident to the electrical company.
