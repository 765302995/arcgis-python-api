Fire in disused building in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/08/2017 - 09:03 Location: Tanhouse, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A fire which is suspected as being started deliberately in Runcorn, and involved a disused building, required firefighters to use a hose reel to extinguish it. The fire involved the external of the building and affected cladding. If anyone knows who may have started it they should call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
