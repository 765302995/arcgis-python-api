Three vehicle road traffic collision in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 21/08/2015 - 10:16 Location: Altrincham Road, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving three cars in Wilmslow. The crew administered first aid to one woman prior to the arrival of the ambulance. Police closed the road while the incident was dealt with.
