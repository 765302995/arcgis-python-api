 Fires in disused school grounds in Woolstanwood Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 19:51 Location: Lodgefields Drive, Woolstanwood Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 7.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in disused school grounds on Lodgefields Drive in Woolstanwood. On arrival the crew discovered multiple seats of fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fires and were in attendance for around half-an-hour. The fires are believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
