 Reports of a fire at a house in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 20/11/2019 - 12:32 Location: Sandwich Drive, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire attended from Macclesfield At around 12.30pm firefighters from Macclesfield were called to reports of a kitchen fire at an address on Sandwich Drive. On arrival at the address the crew found that there was no fire. However, the kitchen was heavily smoke logged as a burnt cooking. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke from the address and were in attendance at the address for around 20 minutes.
