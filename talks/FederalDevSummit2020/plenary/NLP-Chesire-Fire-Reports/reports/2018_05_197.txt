 Cooker fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 06/05/2018 - 04:22 Location: Charlton Close, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn. One fire engine from Widnes. One fire engine from Northwich. Firefighters were called to a kitchen fire at a flat in Charlton Close, Runcorn. On arrival crews discovered a pan of food on a cooker which was on fire. Crews used two hose reels and two breathing apparatus to extinguish the flames. The occupant was accounted for. For information on safety in the kitchen go to https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety
