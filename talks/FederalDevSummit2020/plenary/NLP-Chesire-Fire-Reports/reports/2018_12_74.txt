 Serious road traffic incident A51 Nantwich Time of Incident: 14/12/2018 - 11:16 Location: A51, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Crewe and one form Nantwich Firefighters attended to a serious road traffic incident on the A51 in Nantwich involving a lorry.   Crews attended the scene and assisted the emergency services.  A cordon and a full road closure was in place until 5pm.
