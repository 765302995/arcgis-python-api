Road traffic collision on M56 east near Altrincham Time of Incident: 08/12/2016 - 13:32 Location: M56 eastbound, junction 7 Attendance: Two fire engines from Knutsford and Stockton Heath and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Firefighters were at the scene of a road traffic collision on the M56 near junction 7 eastbound. The incident involved a transit van with one casualty trapped inside. Ambulance crews assessed the casualty before allowing him to release himself from the vehicle, under their close supervision. Firefighters stabilised the vehicle before the casualty freed himself.
