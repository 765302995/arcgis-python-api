Chimney fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/11/2015 - 14:17 Location: Holly Bush Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood Firefighters were called to a chimney fire in Warrington.  Crews extinguished the fire and swept the chimney to remove any debris. The loft space of the property was also checked to ensure that the fire had not spread.  Chimney safety advice
