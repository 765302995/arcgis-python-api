Smoke in property following chip pan fire Time of Incident: 13/10/2016 - 09:23 Location: Chester Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath Firefighters were called to an incident where a chip pan had been on fire, but was out on their arrival. The crew used a large fan to remove smoke from the semi-detached property. One casualty was treated for smoke inhalation and taken to hospital by paramedics. Chip pan safety advice
