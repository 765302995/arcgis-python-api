 Collision involving a car and a van in Knutsford Time of Incident: 21/09/2018 - 17:16 Location: Manchester Road, Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Lymm and one from Greater Manchester At around 5.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision involving a car and a van outside Cottons Hotel on Manchester Road in Knutsford. On arrival crews discovered that a man in the saloon car was injured. He got himself out of the car and was assessed by paramedics. Firefighters made the vehicles safe and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
