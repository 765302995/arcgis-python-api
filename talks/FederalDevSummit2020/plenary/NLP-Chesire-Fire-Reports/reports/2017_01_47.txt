Chimney fire in Church Minshull Time of Incident: 17/01/2017 - 19:21 Location: Weaver View, Church Minshull Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a chimney fire in Weaver View. They were able to put the fire out at the grate, using water, and then used a thermal imaging camera to make there were no hot spots that could reignite.  Always follow our chimney safety advice.
