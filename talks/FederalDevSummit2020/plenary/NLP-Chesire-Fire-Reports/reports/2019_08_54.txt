 Fire in the open in Crewe Time of Incident: 22/08/2019 - 21:59 Location: Westminster Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Westminster Street, Crewe. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire and were in attendance for around five minutes.
