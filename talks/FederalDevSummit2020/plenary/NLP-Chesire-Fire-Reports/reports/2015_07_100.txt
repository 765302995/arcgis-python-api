Cow rescued from ditch in Stoak Time of Incident: 17/07/2015 - 08:34 Location: Little Stanney Lane, Stoak, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and the animal rescue unit and supporting fire engine from Knutsford A cow was rescued from a ditch full of water in Stoak. The line rescue unit from Knutsford was asked to attend and they rescued the cow from the ditch.
