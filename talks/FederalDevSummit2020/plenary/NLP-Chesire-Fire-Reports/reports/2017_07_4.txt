Gas cylinder on fire in Nantwich garden Time of Incident: 29/07/2017 - 16:45 Location: Birchin Lane, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich and one from Crewe An LPG cylinder was on fire in the front garden of a Birchin Lane property. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and cool the cylinder down. The temperature was then monitored using a thermal imaging camera.
