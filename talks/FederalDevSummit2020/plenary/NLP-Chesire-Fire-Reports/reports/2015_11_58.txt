Report of a train on fire at Winsford station Time of Incident: 19/11/2015 - 16:29 Location: Station Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich Firefighters called to a report of a fire on a train in Winsford station. There had been a smell of smoke in the third carriage of the Crewe-bound train. Firefighters investigated and found no sign of fire. The incident has been handed to railway engineers.
