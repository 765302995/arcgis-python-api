Scout hut on fire in Saughall Time of Incident: 30/01/2015 - 13:23 Location: Fiddlers Lane, Saughall, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester Firefighters have been tackling a blaze at a scout hut in Fiddlers Lane.  The building, measuring 30m x 10m, was involved in a well-developed fire.  The crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two main jets to tackle the blaze and high pressure fans to ventilate smoke from the property.  The fire is believed to have started in an electricity consumer unit and the presence of electricity board personnel was requested.
