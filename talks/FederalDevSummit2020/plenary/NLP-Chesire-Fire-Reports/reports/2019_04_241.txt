 Chimney fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 03/04/2019 - 15:55 Location: Cronkinson Avenue, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and the aerial appliance from Chester attended Firefighters attended to a chimney fire at a semi-detached property on Cronkinson Avenue in Nantwich.  Crews extinguished the fire from the grate using one hose reel jet and chimney attachment. Crews then inspected the first floor and the loft space using a thermal imaging camera to check if the fire had spread.  Due to difficulty extinguishing the fire, the aerial appliance was used while crews continued to monitor the loft space with the thermal imaging camera.
