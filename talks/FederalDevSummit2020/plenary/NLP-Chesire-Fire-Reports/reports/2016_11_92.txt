Road traffic collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 13/11/2016 - 04:49 Location: Road One, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Middlewich Two cars were involved in a road traffic collision, leaving a man trapped in one of the cars. Firefighters extricated the casualty from the car and he was handed over to the care of the ambulance service.
