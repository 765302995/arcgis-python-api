 Kitchen fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 23/06/2018 - 17:05 Location: Marigold Close, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Firefighters from Macclesfield and Bollington attended reports of a kitchen fire at a detached bungalow on Marigold Close, Macclesfield. On arrival crews found that the fire had been caused by an electrical fault with the oven. The team used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to bring the fire under control and were in attendance for one hour 30 minutes. There were no reports of any injuries at the scene.
