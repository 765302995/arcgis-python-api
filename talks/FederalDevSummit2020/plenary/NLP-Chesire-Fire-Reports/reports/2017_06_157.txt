Van fire in Huxley Time of Incident: 04/06/2017 - 13:58 Location: Red Lane, Huxley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley A hose reel jet was used to put out a fire in the engine compartment of a van. Firefighters believe the fire was started deliberately and asked police to attend.  Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
