Fire at Winsford packaging company Time of Incident: 02/03/2016 - 09:23 Location: Jiffy Packaging, Road Four, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich A Winsford company was evacuated when fire broke out in a piece of machinery. Firefighters were called to tackle the fire in a machine. The crews, two firefighters using breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire. A sprinkler system fitted at the company activated which helped contain damage. The incident was handed over to the owners, crews were at the scene for almost one and a half hours.
