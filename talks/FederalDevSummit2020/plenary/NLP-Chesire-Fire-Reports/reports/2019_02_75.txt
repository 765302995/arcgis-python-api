 Horse rescued from pond in Warrington Time of Incident: 17/02/2019 - 16:19 Location: Summer Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm. One fire engine attended from Warrington. Horse rescued from pond in WarringtonFirefighters were called to a horse stuck in a pond on land off Summer Lane in Warrington. Crews in dry suits used specialist equipment to rescue the horse. They then handed the horse over to the vet and the owner to be checked over.
