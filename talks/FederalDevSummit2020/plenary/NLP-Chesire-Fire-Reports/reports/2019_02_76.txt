 Fire in a bush, Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 17/02/2019 - 04:16 Location: Whitby Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters tackled a fire in a bush on Whitby Road. The affected area measured around 5 metres and crews remained for a little time after they had extinguished the flames to further dampen down to prevent reignition.
