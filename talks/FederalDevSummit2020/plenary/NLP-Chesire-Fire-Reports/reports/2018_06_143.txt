 Tree stump fire at a park in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/06/2018 - 12:03 Location: Mersey Walk, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port attended At around 12 noon firefighters were called to a report of a tree stump being on fire at Mersey Walk in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered a deep seated fire in a large tree in woodland at Westy Park. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and then damped down the surrounding area to make it safe. They were in attendance for around 50 minutes.
