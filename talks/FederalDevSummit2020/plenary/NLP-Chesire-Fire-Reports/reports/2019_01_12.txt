 Vehicle fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 10/01/2019 - 07:03 Location: Nutfield Avenue, Crewe Attendance:  Two fire engines attended from Crewe Firefighters dealt with a Peugeot car on fire parked outside a house on Nutfield Avenue in Crewe.  Wearing two breathing apparatus, one hose reel was used to extinguish the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone who may have any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 290862 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
