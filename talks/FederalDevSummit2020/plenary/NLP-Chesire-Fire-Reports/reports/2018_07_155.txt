 Rubbish fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/07/2018 - 16:40 Location: Wimborne Close, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Firefighters from Penketh used one hose reel jet to extinguish a small rubbish fire on Wimborne Close, Warrington. The team were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
