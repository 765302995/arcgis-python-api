 Fire in the grounds of a disused property in Widnes Time of Incident: 11/10/2018 - 19:32 Location: Milton Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters from Widnes used one hose reel jet to extinguish a large quantity of rubbish which was well alight in the grounds of a disused property on Milton Road. The team were detained at the scene for approximately one hour.
