 RTC in Rode Heath Time of Incident: 16/06/2018 - 22:51 Location: Street Lane, Rode Heath Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar and sandbach Firefighters attended to a road traffic collision on Street Lane in Rode Heath.  One casualty was extricated from a vehicle and handed over to the care of paramedics.  Crews made the vehicle safe before leaving the scene.
