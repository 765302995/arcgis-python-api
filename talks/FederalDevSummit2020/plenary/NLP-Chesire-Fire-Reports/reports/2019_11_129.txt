 One vehicle collision in Tarporley Time of Incident: 06/11/2019 - 21:57 Location: Beech Lane, Cotebrook. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford. One fire engine attended from Lymm. One fire engine from Chester. Firefighters were called to a car which had come off the road and into Mill Pond which is off Beech Lane, Cotebrook. The driver had exited the vehicle himself. Two swift water technicians with a raft checked there was no one else in the car and the driver was checked over by paramedics.
