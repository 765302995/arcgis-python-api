 Casualty trapped following one-vehicle collision in Marton Time of Incident: 27/08/2019 - 14:10 Location: Cinder Hill, Marton Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Winsford and one from Middlewich Shortly after 2pm firefighters from Winsford and Middlewich attended reports of a one-vehicle collision on Cinder Hill, Marton.  On arrival the crews discovered that the vehicle was on its side and the driver was trapped inside.  The crews used specialist cutting equipment to get the casualty out of the vehicle.  She was then handed over to the care of paramedics.
