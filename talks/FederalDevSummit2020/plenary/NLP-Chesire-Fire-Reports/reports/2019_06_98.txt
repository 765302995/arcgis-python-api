 Car fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/06/2019 - 08:46 Location: Tavlin Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 8.45am firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a car in Tavlin Avenue, Warrington. On arrival the crew found the vehicle well alight. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire, which is not believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters were in attendance for less than an hour.
