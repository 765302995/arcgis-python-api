Woman released from car in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/05/2015 - 16:52 Location: Farmers Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters removed the roof of a car in order to release a woman with suspected spine injuries.  The Service was requested to attend the incident to assist in removing the woman safely, who had been injured after being kicked by a horse. She had got into her car but then was unable to get out.  The woman was handed into the care of paramedics and taken to hospital.
