 Flat fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/03/2018 - 18:10 Location: North View, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Penketh Firefighters were called to a fire in the bedroom of a ground floor flat on North View, Warrington. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet, a thermal imaging camera and a large fan to extinguish the fire and then clear smoke from the property. Crews were at the scene for two hours. An investigation into how the fire started is underway.
