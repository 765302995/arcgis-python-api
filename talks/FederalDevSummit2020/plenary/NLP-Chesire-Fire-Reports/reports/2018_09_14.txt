 Wheelie bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/09/2018 - 21:02 Location: Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a wheelie bin fire on Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please contact Cheshire Police.
