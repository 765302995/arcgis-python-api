Chimney fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/02/2017 - 17:13 Location: St Mary Street, Latchford, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh A crew was called to a report of a chimney fire on St Mary Street. Flames and embers were seen coming from the top of the chimney. Firefighters cooled the chimney and log burner using water and a thermal imaging camera was used to check the temperature and make sure there was no fire spread.  Please follow our chimney safety advice.
