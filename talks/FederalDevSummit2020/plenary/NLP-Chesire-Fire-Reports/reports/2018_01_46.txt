 Garage fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 20/01/2018 - 02:08 Location: Gresty Road, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Two fire engines from Crewe attended a garage fire on Gresty Road, Crewe.  On arrival fire crews found the garage to be well alight and used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Crews remained at scene just over one and a half hours. An investigation is currently underway into the cause of the fire and anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
