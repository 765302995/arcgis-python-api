Small bonfire in Delamere Time of Incident: 16/05/2017 - 20:09 Location: Station Road, Delamere Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley Firefighters attended a small uncontrolled bonfire which was located outside Go Ape on Station Road in Delamere, which is believed to have been started intentionally.  One casualty suffered slight burns to one arm. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
