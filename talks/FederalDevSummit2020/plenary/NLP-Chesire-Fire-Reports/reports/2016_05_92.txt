Building fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 14/05/2016 - 15:36 Location: Weaver Street, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Northwich Crews were at the scene of a Winsford building fire for almost two hours. The fire involved the ground floor of the property. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire. A high pressure fan was then used to clear smoke. An investigation into the cause of the fire is to be carried out.
