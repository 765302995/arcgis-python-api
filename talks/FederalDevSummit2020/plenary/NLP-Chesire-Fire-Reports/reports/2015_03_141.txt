House fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 04/03/2015 - 11:28 Location: Green Avenue, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel and one from Middlewich A house in Northwich was well alight on arrival of the Service. Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reels and put covering jets in place to extinguish the blaze. All people were accounted for and a large fan was used to clear the smoke. The gas and electrics were also isolated.
