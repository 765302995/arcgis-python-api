 Uncontrolled burning in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/02/2019 - 15:14 Location: Allcard Street, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Birchwood Fire crews were called to uncontrolled burning in the back garden of a property on Allcard Street in Warrington.  The fire had spread to windows and a neighbouring property.   A hose reel was used by firefighters to put out the fire who were in attendance for approximately 40 minutes.
