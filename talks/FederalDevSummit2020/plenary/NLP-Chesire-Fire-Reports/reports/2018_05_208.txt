 Refuse fire in a layby at a holiday park in Frodsham Time of Incident: 02/05/2018 - 00:02 Location: Ridgeway, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Frodsham Fire in FrodshamAt around midnight firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open at a holiday park in Ridgeway, Frodsham.  On arrival at The Ridgeway park the crew found a small amount of refuse on fire in a layby.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
