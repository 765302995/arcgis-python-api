 One-vehicle collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 01:45 Location: Warrington Road/Shaw Street, Culcheth, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Birchwood and one from Warrington At 1.45am firefighters were called to a report of a collision on the junction of Warrington Road and Shaw Street in Culcheth, Warrington. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved one car and that no-one was trapped inside it. Firefighters made the vehicle safe and then left the incident in the hands of the police. The crew were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
