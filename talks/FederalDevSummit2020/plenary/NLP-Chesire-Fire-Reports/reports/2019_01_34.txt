 Car fire, Crewe Time of Incident: 05/01/2019 - 02:30 Location: Mount Pleasant, Crewe Attendance:  One fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to reports of a Range Rover on fire on the driveway of a house in Mount Pleasant. They used breathing apparatus and two hose reel to put out the fire, and a home safety assessment was carried out with the occupiers.  Crews remained for some time to damped down the engine compartment of the vehicle. The incident is thought to have been a deliberate ignition.
