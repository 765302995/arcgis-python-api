 Domestic building fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 02/03/2018 - 22:47 Location: Margaret Court, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one fire engine attended from Lymm. Firefighters from Runcorn and Lymm attended reports of a building fire on Margaret Court in Widnes. On arrival the crew found that the fire was located in the kitchen. The team used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire before using a large fan to clear all of the smoke from the property. There were no reports of any injuries and the team were in attendance for  approximately 35 minutes.
