 Two vehicle collision Warrington Time of Incident: 22/06/2018 - 17:53 Location: Woolston Grange Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters from Birchwood attended a two vehicle collision on Woolston Grange Avenue, Warrington. On arrival at the crews found that there were no persons trapped. The team assisted by making both of the vehicles safe and provided first aid to two casualties at the scene, both were then passed to the care of North West Ambulance Service. Crews were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes.
