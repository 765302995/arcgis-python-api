Bungalow fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 11/01/2017 - 16:47 Location: Victoria Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Three fire engines from Congleton, Macclesfield and Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a fire in a large detached bungalow with one male believed to be inside. On arrival, the fire appeared to have been extinguished so crews investigated for any further signs of fire and isolated the electricity supply. The ambulance were in attendance for one male who was suspected to have suffered smoke inhalation.
