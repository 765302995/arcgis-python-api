 Motorbike fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 19/03/2019 - 16:56 Location: Castlefields Avenue East, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters attended a small motorbike on fire in a wooded field off Castlefields Avenue East, Runcorn. Crews used a water back pack to put out the fire.
