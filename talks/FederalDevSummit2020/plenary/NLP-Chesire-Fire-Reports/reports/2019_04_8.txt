 Small fire at Birchwood shopping centre Time of Incident: 28/04/2019 - 04:50 Location: Dewhurst Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Birchwood and one from Warrington Fire crews attended to a shopping centre in Birchwood following reports of a small fire inside a supermarket.  The fire, believed to have been caused by light fitting, was out when crews arrived.  Firefighters used water to cool the surrounding areas and dampen down.
