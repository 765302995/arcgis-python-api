 Small fire near the railway in Crewe Time of Incident: 01/09/2019 - 10:32 Location: Middlewich Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Shortly after 10.30am firefighters from Crewe were called to reports of a small fire near to the railway line off Middlewich Road.  On arrival the crews discovered the fire involved a number of trees and bushes which were well alight.  The used water to put out the fire, they then used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  Firefighters were in attendance for around one hour 15 minutes.
