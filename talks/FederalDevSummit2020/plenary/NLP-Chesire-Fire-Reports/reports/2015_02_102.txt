Road traffic collision with a man trapped on the M56 Time of Incident: 05/02/2015 - 01:11 Location: M56 East between junctions 15 to 14 Attendance: No attendance data recorded A road traffic collision with a man trapped in a vehicle required firefighters to attend. Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester attended the collision which involved two heavy goods vehicles.  The man was removed from the vehicle by firefighters who used cutting gear to get him out and onto a long board. He was then handed to the care of paramedics.
