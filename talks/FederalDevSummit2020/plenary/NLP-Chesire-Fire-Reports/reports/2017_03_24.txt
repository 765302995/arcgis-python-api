Fire in the open in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/03/2017 - 20:43 Location: Farrell Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh A large fire which involved around 40 metres by 20 metres of grass was extinguished with hand appliances. It is believed that the fire was started deliberately. Police attended this incident.  If anyone has any information about this fire they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
