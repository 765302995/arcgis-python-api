Road traffic collision in Birchwood, Warrington Time of Incident: 02/01/2016 - 23:08 Location: Admirals road, Warrington. Attendance: one fire engine from Birchwood and one from Warrington There was a road traffic collision involving one heavy goods vehicle and one car. Fire crews released one female casualty from the car, by removing the roof of the car. The casualty was then handed over to the care of paramedics.  Police closed the road.
