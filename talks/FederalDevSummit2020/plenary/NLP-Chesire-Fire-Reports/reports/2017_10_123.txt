Two people trapped after road traffic collision in Poynton Time of Incident: 01/10/2017 - 16:03 Location: Dickens Lane, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Poynton Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision between two cars on Dickens Lane, Poynton. Firefighters extracted two casualties from the cars before handing them over to the care of the paramedics. Crews were at the scene for one hour and 25 minutes.
