Road traffic collision on M56 east Time of Incident: 10/05/2015 - 16:06 Location: M56 east between junctions 14 and 12 Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Runcorn A four-wheel drive vehicle towing a horse trailer overturned on the M56 east between junctions 14-12.  Fortunately there were no people trapped in the vehicle and the crews were able to relelease two horses from the trailer.  A vet was asked to attend as a precaution and police attended.  The Highways Agency closed the motorway while the incident was being dealt with.
