 Garden fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 16/05/2019 - 10:15 Location: Borrowdale Road, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Penketh and Congleton At 10.15am firefighters were dispatched to reports of a garden fire at an address on Borrowdale Road, Widnes. On arrival at the scene the team discovered a wheelie bin which was well alight. The fire had also spread to a garden shed  garden panel fence. A nearby UPC door had also been damaged. Crews used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were detained for around 45 minutes. The fire is believed to have been caused by a discarded cigarette.
