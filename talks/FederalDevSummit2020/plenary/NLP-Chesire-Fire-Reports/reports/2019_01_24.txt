 Two vehicle collision in Arclid Time of Incident: 07/01/2019 - 06:23 Location: Congleton Road, Arclid. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Sandbach Firefighters attended a two vehicle collision on Congleton Road in Arclid. One of the cars was reported to be on fire.
