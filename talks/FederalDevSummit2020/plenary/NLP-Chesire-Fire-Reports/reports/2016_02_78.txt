Smoke filled kitchen in Widnes Time of Incident: 12/02/2016 - 23:27 Location: Millfield Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A pan on a stove was left unattended, which caused a kitchen to be filled with smoke.  The smoke alarm inside the property was sounding, which was heard by a neighbour, who called the fire and rescue service.  Firefighters gained access to the property and removed the pan from the heat.  Firefighters then used a large fan to clear the smoke from the property.  Kitchen fire safety tips.
