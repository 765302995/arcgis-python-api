Swan rescued after getting stuck in fishing line in Northwich Time of Incident: 20/08/2017 - 18:53 Location: Davenham Road, Whatcroft, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one from Lymm and the animal rescue unit from Lymm. Swan needed rescuingA swan that had got tangled in fishing line and was about 30 feet out in water needed the help of firefighters.  RSPCA attended and were handed the swan once the animal rescue crew had rescued it using the a raft.
