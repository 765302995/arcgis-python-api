House fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 03/10/2015 - 13:24 Location: Trafford Road, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Cheadle A large fan was used to clear smoke in the kitchen of a house in Wilmslow.   A thermal imaging camera was also used to ensure the fire was completely out and the crew then carried out a home safety assessment at the property. They were at the scene for 30 minutes..
