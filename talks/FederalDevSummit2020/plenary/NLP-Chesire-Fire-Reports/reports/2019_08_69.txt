 Shed fire in Latchford Time of Incident: 21/08/2019 - 12:26 Location: Parkfield Avenue, Latchford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews attended to a shed and fencing well alight on fire on Parkfield Avenue in Latchford.  A hose reel was used to put out the fire which had started from a bin and spread to the shed and fencing.
