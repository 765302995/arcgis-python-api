Small rubbish fire in Handforth Time of Incident: 22/05/2017 - 21:56 Location: Delamere Road, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a small quantity of wood which had been set on fire in the car park of a disused building on Delamere Road, Handforth. Crews which attended the incident, at the Mermaid Hotel, put out the fire using a hose reel jet. Police also attended the incident.
