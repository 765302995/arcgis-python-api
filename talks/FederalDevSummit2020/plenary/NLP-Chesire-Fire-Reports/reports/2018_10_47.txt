 Fire in brazier in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/10/2018 - 21:25 Location: Cholmondeley Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Fire crews were called to Cholmondeley Road, Runcorn, after a fire in a brazier was left unattended. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the blaze.
