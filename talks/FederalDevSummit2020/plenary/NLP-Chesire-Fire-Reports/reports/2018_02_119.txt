 Campervan fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 03/02/2018 - 15:41 Location: St Anne's Avenue, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich and one from Holmes Chapel Firefighters were called to St Anne's Avenue, Middlewich following reports of a campervan on fire. Crews removed a cylinder and used two hose reels, two breathing apparatus and thermal imaging camera to put the fire out. They were at the scene for around two hours.
