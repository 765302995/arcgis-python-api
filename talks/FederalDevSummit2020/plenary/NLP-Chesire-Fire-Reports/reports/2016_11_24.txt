Four car collison on the M6 Time of Incident: 25/11/2016 - 19:23 Location: Northbound carriageway of the M6 between Junction 21 and 21A. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on northbound carriageway of the M6 between Junction 21 and 21A.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved four vehicles. One woman was trapped in her car. Fire crews used cutting equipment to free her. She was then passed to the care of the ambulance service.
