Caravan involved in fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 18/07/2017 - 21:58 Location: Bradbury Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford A caravan which was well alight and involved three fence panels of a nearby property was extinguished by firefighters. Two hose reels were used to extinguish the flames and a thermal imaging camera to check the area.
