Electrical switchroom fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 13/09/2016 - 11:16 Location: Cledford Primary School, George VI Avenue, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Winsford There was a small fire in the electrical switchroom of a Middlewich school. The school was evacuated as a precaution as the electricity supply was isolated. The fire was out on arrival and crews dispersed smoke using a high pressure fan. An investigation into the cause of the fire is being carried out.
