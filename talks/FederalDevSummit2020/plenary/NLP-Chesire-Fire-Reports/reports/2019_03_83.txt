 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/03/2019 - 03:58 Location: Clay Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to a wheelie bin fire on Clay Lane, Warrington. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which had set fire to some bushes nearby. The fire was started deliberately. If you have any information then please contact Cheshire Police.
