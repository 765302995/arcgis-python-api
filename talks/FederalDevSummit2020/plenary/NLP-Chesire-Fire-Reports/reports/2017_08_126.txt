Kitchen fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 08/08/2017 - 19:54 Location: Stewards Avenue, Widnes Attendance: Three fire engines from Widnes, Penketh and Runcorn A kitchen fire at a property on Stewards Avenue was tackled by firefighters. The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was caused by burnt food. A high pressure fan cleared smoke from the property and firefighters were at the scene for around one hour.
