Skip fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 22/06/2017 - 02:03 Location: Booth Lane, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Fire crews were called to a fire in a skip on Booth Lane, Middlewich. Crews found the skip well alight on arrival and used a main jet and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire. Police also attended the incident which is suspected to be arson. If you have any information about the incident please call 101 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For more information about arson prevention please visit - https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/arson-prevention
