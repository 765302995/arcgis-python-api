Building fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 21/10/2017 - 03:05 Location: Rood Hill, Congleton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton and One fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel At 3:04am on 21 October, firefighters were called to reports of a building fire in Congleton. A fire engine from Holmes Chapel, and a fire engine from Congleton attended the fire at Rood Hill, Congleton. The fire involved the front door of the property and firefighters used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel and positive pressure ventilation to extinguish the fire.  Firefighters were on scene for approximately one hour and thirty minutes.
