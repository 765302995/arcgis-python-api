Matress fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 17/09/2016 - 22:16 Location: Boston Avenue in Runcorn Attendance: A fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Boston Avenue in Runcorn.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire involving a mattress at the back of a house. Crews used a hose reel jet to put the fire out.
