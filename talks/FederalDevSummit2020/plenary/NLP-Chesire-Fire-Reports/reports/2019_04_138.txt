 Fire in a Woodland area near Lymm Time of Incident: 15/04/2019 - 18:21 Location: Sutch Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington used water to extinguish a small fire in a woodland area off Sutch Lane in Lymm.  The team were detained at the scene for around 20 minutes.  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101. Information can also be reported anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111.
