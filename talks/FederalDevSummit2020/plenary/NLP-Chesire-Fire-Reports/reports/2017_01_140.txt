Wheelie bin fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 01/01/2017 - 19:57 Location: dart walk, winsford. cw73je Attendance: A fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Dart Walk in Winsford.  When they arrived crews found that there was a mattress and a wheelie bin on fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to put it out.  It is believed that this fire was stared deliberately. If you can help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
