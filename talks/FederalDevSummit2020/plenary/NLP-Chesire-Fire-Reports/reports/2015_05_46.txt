Persons trapped on the M6 Time of Incident: 23/05/2015 - 06:33 Location: Northbound carriageway of the M6 between Junctions 20 and 21. Attendance: Fire engines from Stockton Heath and Warrington. Firefighters were called to a collision on the northbound carriageway of the M6 between Junctions 20 and 21.  When they arrived they found that the incident involved two vehicles. There was a casualty trapped in one of the vehicles. Crews cut the person free and passed them to the care of the ambulance service.
