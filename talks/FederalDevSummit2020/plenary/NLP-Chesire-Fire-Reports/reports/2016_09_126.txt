Cylinder on road in Sandbach Time of Incident: 05/09/2016 - 16:24 Location: Newcastle road, Sandbach Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton Firefighters were called to reports of a cylinder on the A50, Newcastle Road in Sandbach. On arrival, crews found the cylinder isolated on a grass verge and so examined it using a high pressure hose reel jet. The cylinder was found to be safe with no sign of high temperature it and so crews have arranged for it to be collected.
