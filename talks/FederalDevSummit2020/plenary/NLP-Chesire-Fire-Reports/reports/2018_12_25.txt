 Vehicle fire in Buerton Time of Incident: 24/12/2018 - 13:35 Location: Hankins Heys Lane, Buerton. Attendance: Two fire engine attended from Crewe and Audlem. At around 1.30pm firefighters from Crewe and Audlem were called to reports of a vehicle fire in Buerton, near Audlem. Firefighters  scene and on arrival they discovered a large agricultural vehicle which was well alight. The team are used two hose reel jets and two breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately one hour 15 minutes.
