 Petrol spillage at Wistaston garage Time of Incident: 25/02/2019 - 18:37 Location: Crewe Road, Wistaston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters attended to a spillage of petrol at a garage on Crewe Road in Wistaston.  Approximately 10 litres of fuel had spilt requiring fire crews to assist the garage manager with the clean up.   Firefighters used absorbent mats and a hose reel to wash away the petrol to make sure the area was safe.  They were attendance for approximately 30 minutes.
