 Fire at a hotel construction site in Beeston Time of Incident: 11/02/2019 - 20:19 Location: Whitchurch Road, Beeston, near Tarporley Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Tarporley and Chester At around 8.20pm firefighters from Tarporley and Chester were dispatched to reports of a fire at a hotel construction site in Beeston.  Crews quickly attended the scene and on arrival they discovered a fire on the ground floor of the building.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  The team remained in attendance at the scene for approximately one hour forty minutes.
