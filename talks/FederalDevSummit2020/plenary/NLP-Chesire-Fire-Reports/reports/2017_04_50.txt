Overheated axel on HGV in Runcorn Time of Incident: 21/04/2017 - 09:19 Location: Weston Point Expressway, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine A heavy good vehicle needed its axel cooling with water from a hose reel, after it overheated. A thermal imaging camera was used to check the axel had cooled before crews handed the vehicle over to a recovery team.
