Small fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 18/11/2017 - 21:58 Location: Canal Terrace, Middlewich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Firefighters were contacted by a concerned member of the public who reported a small bonfire which was in close proximity to a diesel tank. One crew from Middlewich attended and a decision was taken to use one hose reel jet extinguish the fire to ensure the safety of people in the surrounding area.
