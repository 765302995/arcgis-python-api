 Small fire in play area, Winsford Time of Incident: 15/02/2019 - 20:28 Location: Station Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters tackled a small fire in a children's play area. They used a single hose reel to put out the flames, and carried out an inspection of the play area to ensure there were no further potential seats of fire.
