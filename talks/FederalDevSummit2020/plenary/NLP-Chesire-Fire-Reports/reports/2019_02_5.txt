 Assisting paramedics in Chester Time of Incident: 27/02/2019 - 09:05 Location: Saighton Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters were called to assist paramedics with a casualty on Saighton Lane in Chester. A woman had been injured by hydraulic equipment and was on the back of a flatbed wagon. Firefighters helped paramedics remove her from the wagon and she was then taken to hospital via an ambulance to be treated.
