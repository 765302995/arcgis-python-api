Car fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/02/2017 - 00:56 Location: Eastwood, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A fire in the engine compartment of a car was put out by firefighters using a hose reel jet.  Police were asked to attend as it is believed the fire may have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is asked to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
