 Boy rescued after falling down an embankment in Styal Time of Incident: 13/05/2018 - 14:20 Location: Farm Fold, Styal Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Wilmslow and one from Lymm - as well as a rope rescue unit from Lymm At around 2.15pm firefighters were called to reports that a boy had fallen down a steep embankment in woods at Farm Fold in Styal. They used line rescue equipment to lift the casualty out of the embankment. He was then handed over to the care of paramedics and was air lifted to hospital by an air ambulance. Firefighters were in attendance for approximately three hours.
