 Quad bike fire in Higher Whitley. Time of Incident: 05/06/2019 - 01:36 Location: Raddel Lane, Higher Whitley. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm Firefighters from Lymm used a hose reel jet to extinguish a quad bike which was well alight on Raddel Lane, Higher Whitley.  The team were detained at the scene for around one hour.
