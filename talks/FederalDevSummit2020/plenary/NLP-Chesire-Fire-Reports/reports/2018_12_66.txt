 Flat fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 15/12/2018 - 21:39 Location: Barony Court, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsagar and one from Crewe Firefighters were called to a fire in a ground floor flat on Barony Court, Nantwich. The fire was extinguished by crews, wearing breathing apparatus, using a hose reel jet. No one was injured as a result of the fire. Crews were at the scene for three and a half hours.
