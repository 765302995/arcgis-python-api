 Small fire in woodland in Wistaston Time of Incident: 10/05/2018 - 17:18 Location: Willow Crescent, Wistaston Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 5.20pm firefighters were called to a small fire in woodland at Willow Crescent in Wistaston. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a hollow tree log. Firefighters used water and small axes in extinguishing the fire. They were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
