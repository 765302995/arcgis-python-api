Chimney fire in Chester Time of Incident: 25/01/2017 - 17:12 Location: Thirlmere Road, Chester. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Thirlmere Road, Chester.  When they arrived crews found that there was a fire in the chimney. They put the fire out using special tools from the hearth.
