 Fire in the open in Crewe Time of Incident: 02/11/2018 - 15:53 Location: Parkers Road, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and one fire engine from Crewe Shortly before 4pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open off Parkers Road in Crewe. Firefighters from Crewe and Nantwich were dispatched to the scene and on arrival they discovered a large fire involving large amount of grass, wood and tyres, approximately 20 metres by 5 metres. The team used one a hose reel jet and two short extension ladders to extinguish the fire. Crews also  a digger to help spread the waste. Firefighters were at the scene for approximately one hour 30 minutes.
