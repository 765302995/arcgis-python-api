 Hedge fire at golf course in Chester Time of Incident: 08/09/2019 - 18:08 Location: Curzon Park North, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Shortly after 6pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire near the fourth green of Chester Golf Club. On arrival at the club at Curzon Park North in Chester the crew found approximately one-metre of hedge on fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for nearly an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 508209, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
