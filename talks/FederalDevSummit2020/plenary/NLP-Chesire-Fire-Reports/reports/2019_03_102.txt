 Caravan fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 08/03/2019 - 00:19 Location: Victoria Avenue, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Fire crews were called to a caravan fire on Victoria Avenue, Crewe. The caravan was totally destroyed by fire and crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. Crews also checked whether the fire had spread to a nearby property but it hadn't. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire then please contact Cheshire Police.
