 Flat fire in Alsager Time of Incident: 23/12/2018 - 19:53 Location: Centre Court, Alsager. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsager and two from Crewe. Fire engines from Alsager and Crewe  attended a fire in a first floor flat in Centre Court, Alsager.  Crews used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to put out the fire. They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke.  One casualty was treated by ambulance on scene for smoke inhalation and transported to hospital.
