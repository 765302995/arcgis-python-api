 Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/03/2018 - 16:14 Location: Meteor Crescent, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington. One fire engine from Birchwood. Firefighters were called to a cooker on fire at an address in Meteor Crescent, Warrington. Crews used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel to extinguish the flames. A woman was treated by firefighters for smoke inhalation.
