 Fire in a garden in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/11/2018 - 18:56 Location: Grappenhall Road, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath At around 6.55pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a garden on Grappenhall Road in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning. The owners were in attendance but a decision was made to reduce the size of the fire for safety reasons. Firefighters made the fire smaller and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
