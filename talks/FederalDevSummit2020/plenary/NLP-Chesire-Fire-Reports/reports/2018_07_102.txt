 Rubbish fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 18/07/2018 - 17:05 Location: Kenilworth Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Fire crews were called to a rubbish fire involving pallets and plastics on Kenilworth Court, Ellesmere Port. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
