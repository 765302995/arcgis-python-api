 Tree fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 15/07/2019 - 19:41 Location: Castle Rise, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 7.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a tree fire at an address in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered a number of conifer trees which were smouldering at the rear of an address on Castle Rise. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to cool the trees and were detained at the scene for approximately 15 minutes.
