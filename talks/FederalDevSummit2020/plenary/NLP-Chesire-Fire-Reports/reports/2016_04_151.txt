Bedroom fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 02/04/2016 - 16:58 Location: Chester Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton and one from Offerton (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) An electric fire is believed to have caused a fire in the bedroom of a Poynton property. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and a high pressure fan to clear the smoke. An ambulance was asked to attend due to one casualty, a man, suffering slight smoke inhalation. Smoke alarms were not working in the house.  Always have working smoke alarms fitted - follow our safety advice.
