 Rescue of a woman who fell down an embankment in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 24/07/2019 - 14:02 Location: West Park, Macclesfield. Attendance: 1 fire engine attended from Macclesfield and the rope rescue unit from Macclesfield and Knutsford. Firefighters were called to West Park in Macclesfield following reports of a woman having fallen down an embankment.  Fire crews rescued the casualty using height safety equipment and passed her to the care of paramedics.  They were in attendance for approximately 40 minutes.
