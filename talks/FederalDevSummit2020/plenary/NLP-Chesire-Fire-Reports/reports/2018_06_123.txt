 Garden fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 21/06/2018 - 16:54 Location: Riverside Grove, Wistaston. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe attended reports of a garden fire in Wistaston. The fire was out on arrival, although the team used one hose reel jet to dampen down hotspots to help prevent reignition.
