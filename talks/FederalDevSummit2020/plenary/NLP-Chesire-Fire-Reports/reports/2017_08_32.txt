Flat fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 26/08/2017 - 04:45 Location: James Hall Street, Nantwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe and one from Nantwich Fire crews were called to a fire in a ground floor flat on James Hall Street, Nantwich. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a large fan to put out the fire. Four people inside the flat evacuated before the arrival of the emergency services and they were taken to hospital suffering from smoke inhalation. Crews were at the scene for two hours. Cheshire Police were also in attendance and the cause of the fire is being investigated.
