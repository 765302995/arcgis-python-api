 Small fire, Winsford Time of Incident: 21/10/2019 - 18:38 Location: Crook Lane, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called following reports of a fire at the rear of properties on Crook Lane in Winsford. They initially struggled to locate the fire, but when they did found that it was already out. They used a hose reel to damp down and ensure there was no chance of reignition.
