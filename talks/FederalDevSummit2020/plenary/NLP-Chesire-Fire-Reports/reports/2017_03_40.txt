Hedgerow on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/03/2017 - 17:21 Location: Coronation Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A fire in a hedgerow is believed to have been started deliberately. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and are urging anyone with any information about this or similar firesetting incidents to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
