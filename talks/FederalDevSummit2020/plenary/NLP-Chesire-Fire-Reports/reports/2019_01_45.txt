 Small fire at a playground in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/01/2019 - 20:09 Location: Manchester Road, Warrington Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly after 8pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at St Elphins Park on Manchester Road in Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small fire in a playground. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 10 minutes.
