Caravan on fire in Betchton Time of Incident: 14/06/2017 - 16:57 Location: The Caravan Park, Newcastle Road, Betchton Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Sandbach A caravan which was well alight on the arrival of firefighters was extinguished with two hose reels. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the area before the crews left.
