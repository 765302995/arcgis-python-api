Road traffic collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 09/05/2016 - 12:50 Location: M6 northbound, between junctions 19 and 20. Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Northwich Firefighters attended a road traffic collision, involving two heavy goods vehicles, on the M6 northbound between junctions 19 and 20. The northbound carriageway was closed while the incident is dealt with.
