Chimney fire in Knutsford Time of Incident: 21/04/2017 - 20:46 Location: Plumley Moor Road, Knutsford. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Knutsford Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property off Plumley Moor Road, Knutsford.  When they arrived crews found a chimney fire. Crews used specialist equipment to tackle the fire.
