Bushes on fire on Little Stanney canal footpath Time of Incident: 07/06/2015 - 17:42 Location: Slip from A5117, Little Stanney Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters had a difficult access to a fire on the footpath of a Little Stanney canal.  Both the canal and industrial fencing made it hard for them to gain access but they went on foot and used hand extinguishers to put out the fire in bushes along the path.
