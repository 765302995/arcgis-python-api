 Kitchen fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 20/09/2019 - 11:49 Location: Dinglebrook Grove, Wilmslow Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Wilmslow, one from Poynton and one from Stockport At around 11.50am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the kitchen of a flat in Dinglebrook Grove, Wilmslow. The crews forced open the front door and discovered that the fire involved an electrical cooker in the first floor flat. The gas and electricity supplies were isolated and two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire, which was contained to the kitchen, with a hose reel jet. A large fan was then used to clear smoke from the property. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour.
