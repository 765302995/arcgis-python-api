 Caravan fire in Burtonwood Time of Incident: 09/09/2018 - 23:16 Location: Clay Lane, Burtonwood Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to Clay Lane, Burtonwood, after a caravan caught fire. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for an hour.
