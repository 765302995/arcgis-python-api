Arson investigation after fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 28/01/2015 - 23:09 Location: Charlesworth Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe An investigation is underway after a fire involving the front door of a property in Crewe. The fire was out when the crews attended but three people had suffered smoke inhalation and were given oxygen by firefighters at the scene. Police were also in attendance and an arson investigation has been launched. Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
