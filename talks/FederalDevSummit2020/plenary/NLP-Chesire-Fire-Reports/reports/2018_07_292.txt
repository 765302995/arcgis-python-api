 Small fires on towpath in Middlewich Time of Incident: 01/07/2018 - 22:40 Location: Leadmsithy Street, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Firefighters dealt with two seats of fire on a towpath on Leadsmithy Street in Middlewich. Crews used buckets of water to put out the fire, which is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information should call police on 101.
