 Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/07/2018 - 07:04 Location: Nortonwood Lane, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters from Runcorn used a hose reel jet to tackle a number of grass fires on Nortonwood Lane in Runcorn. Crews were at the scene for approximately 90 minutes.
