 Fire in children's play area in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 23/04/2019 - 19:52 Location: Tame Walk, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 7.50pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in a children's play area in Wilmslow. On arrival at Tame Walk the crew discovered that the fire involved a seesaw and rubber matting in the play area. Firefighters used a hose reel jet and buckets of water to extinguish the fire. They were in attendance for around 20 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 382702, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
