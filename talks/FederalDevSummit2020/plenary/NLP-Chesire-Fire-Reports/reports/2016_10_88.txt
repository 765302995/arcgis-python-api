Collision in Disley Time of Incident: 16/10/2016 - 05:11 Location: Buxton Road in Disley. Attendance: A fire engine from Poynton and one from Marple (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service). Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Buxton Road in Disley.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved three cars. There was nobody trapped and there was no fire so crews made the scene safe before leaving.
