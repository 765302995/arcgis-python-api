Fire involving log burner in Chester Time of Incident: 15/02/2016 - 17:26 Location: Pennsylvania Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and one from Chester Firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a log burner at a property in Chester. The crews removed ashes and soot and put out the fire before checking the area with a thermal imaging camera to make sure there were no further hot spots.
