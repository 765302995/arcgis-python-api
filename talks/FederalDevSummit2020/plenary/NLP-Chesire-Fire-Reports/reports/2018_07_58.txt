 Horse rescued from a ditch in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 25/07/2018 - 11:46 Location: Strawberry Lane, Wilmslow Attendance: One from Wilmslow Fire Station and the animal rescue units from Bollington and Lymm. FHorse stuck in a ditchire crews rescued a horse which became stuck in a dry ditch on Strawberry Lane in Wilmslow.  The 17 year old horse named Basil, was sedated by the vet, then firefighters along with the animal rescue unit used farming machinery and animal rescue equipment to dig around the horse to help release it from the mud.
