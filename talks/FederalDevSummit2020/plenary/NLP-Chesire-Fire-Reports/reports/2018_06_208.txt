 Car fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 08/06/2018 - 17:05 Location: Coole Lane, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Audlem Car fire on Coole Lane, NantwichFire crews were called to Coole Lane, Nantwich, after reports of a car fire. Crews found the car well alight and the crew, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.
