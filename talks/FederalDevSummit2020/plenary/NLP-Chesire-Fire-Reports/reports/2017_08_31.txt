Out of control bonfire in Lostock Green Time of Incident: 26/08/2017 - 11:39 Location: Birches Lane, Lostock Green Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Fire crews were called to a bonfire in a garden which had gone out of control on Birches Lane, Lostock Green, Northwich. The fire had caused some damage to the cladding of a house. Crews used a hose reel jet to tackle the blaze which was approximately 30 square metres. Crews were detained for two hours.
