House fire in Styal Time of Incident: 29/08/2016 - 10:36 Location: Hollin Lane, Styal Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and one from Wythenshawe (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Smoke was seen issuing from the ground floor of a detached house in Hollin Lane. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out a fire involving a laptop. Everyone from the property was accounted for and crews were at the incident for one hour and 15 minutes. Police were also at the scene to manage traffic.  A man was in the house when the fire started. Smoke alarms alerted him to black smoke on the landing of the property. He escaped through a first floor window and called out the fire and rescue service.  Always follow our electrical safety advice
