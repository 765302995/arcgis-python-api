 House fire in Cuddington Time of Incident: 26/05/2018 - 23:46 Location: Sandown Crescent, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich, one from Nantwich, one from Winsford and the hydraulic platform from Lymm Firefighters were called to a house fire on Sandown Crescent, Cuddington. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets and a hydraulic platform to extinguish the fire which was in the roof space of the property. Crews were at the scene for six hours.
