Cooking fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 22/08/2017 - 14:23 Location: Middlewich Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford and one from Middlewich Fire crews were called to a cooking fire on Middlewich Road, Winsford.  The fire, which was inside an oven, was out on arrival of the fire crews and they used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.
