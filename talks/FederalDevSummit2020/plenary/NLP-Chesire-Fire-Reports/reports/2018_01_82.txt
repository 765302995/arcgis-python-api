 Builder's waste on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 11/01/2018 - 20:28 Location: Churchill Way, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to a small amount of builder's waste which had been left alight and was unattended on Churchill Way, Macclesfield. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
