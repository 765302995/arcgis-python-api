Fire involving scaffolding plank in Warrington Time of Incident: 21/09/2015 - 14:16 Location: Liverpool Road, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A fire involving a scaffolding plank on an industrial unit, which was approximately 20 metres up, was extinguished by firefighters who used a ladder to reach the fire, and a hose reel to extinguish it.
