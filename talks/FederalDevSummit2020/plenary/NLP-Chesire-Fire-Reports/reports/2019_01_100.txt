 Washing machine fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 23/01/2019 - 01:27 Location: Westenra Avenue, Ellesmere Port. Attendance:  Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended a fire involving a washing machine in the ground floor of a house on Westenra Avenue, Ellesmere Port.  Crews used two breathing apparatus and one hose reel to extinguish the fire. They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke. One casualty was treated for smoke inhalation.
