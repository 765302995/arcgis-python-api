Car fire in Cotebrook Time of Incident: 13/09/2016 - 08:01 Location: Tarporley Road, Cotebrook, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Traffic delays are being experienced following a car fire on Tarporley Road at Cotebrook. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to put out the fire in an estate car, and then damped the area down. Police have closed the road while the incident is being cleared.
