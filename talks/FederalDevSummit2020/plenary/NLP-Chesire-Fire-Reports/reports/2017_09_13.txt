House fire in Capenhurst Time of Incident: 26/09/2017 - 09:34 Location: Capenhurst Lane, Capenhurst Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a house fire on Capenhurst Lane, Capenhurst. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put the fire out and then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. The cause of the fire is currently being investigated. Cheshire Police also attended the incident.
