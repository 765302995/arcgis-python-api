 One vehicle collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 02/10/2019 - 05:29 Location: Broad Street, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe Shortly before 5.30am firefighters from Crewe were dispatched to reports of a collision on Broad Street. On arrival at the scene the crews discovered that a car had collided with a parked vehicle and the driver was medically trapped. Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to remove the roof of the vehicle and release the casualty. They were then passed to the care of paramedics. The crews were detained at the incident for approximately 40 minutes.
