 Car fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 26/11/2019 - 14:04 Location: Near High Street, Winsford. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Winsford At 2.04pm firefighters from Winsford were dispatched to reports of a car fire on a car part at the rear to the Dingle recreation centre.  On arrival at the scene the crews found that the vehicle was well alight.  Firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and dampen down the vehicle.  The crews were detained at the scene for approximately 40 minutes.
