 Building fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 13/07/2018 - 14:05 Location: Parkers Court, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Shortly after 2pm firefighters from Runcorn were mobilised to reports of a building fire in Runcorn. On arrival crews found that a domestic smoke alarm had activated as a result of smoke caused by an electric grill. The team used a large fan to clear the smoke from the kitchen and were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes.
