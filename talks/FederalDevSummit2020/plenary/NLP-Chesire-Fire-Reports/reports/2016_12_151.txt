Fire in a kitchen in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 01/12/2016 - 21:54 Location: Sutton Way, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port and one fire engine attended from Chester A fire broke out in the kitchen of a ground floor flat. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to put out the fire. Following this, they used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.  No fire alarms were installed in the property. Buying and fitting a smoke alarm could help you save the lives of your family.
