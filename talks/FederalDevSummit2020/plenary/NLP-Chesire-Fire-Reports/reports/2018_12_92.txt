 Small unattended fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 10/12/2018 - 09:09 Location: Hancock Road, Congleton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Shortly after 9am firefighters from Congleton were called to reports of a small fire on Hancock Road. On arrival at the scene crews found an unattended banzier which was unattended. The team were unable to locate the owner of the fire so they used one hose reel jet to extinguish it. Crews were detained at the scene for approximately twenty minutes.
