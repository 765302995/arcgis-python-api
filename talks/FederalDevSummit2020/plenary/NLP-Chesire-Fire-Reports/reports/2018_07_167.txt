 grass fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 19:57 Location: Glazebrook Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters were called to land off Glazebrook Lane in Warrington following reports of smoke issuing from the grass. Crews identified a number of hotspots and used a hose reel to damp down and cool the area. They then checked there were no more hotspots using a thermal imaging camera.
