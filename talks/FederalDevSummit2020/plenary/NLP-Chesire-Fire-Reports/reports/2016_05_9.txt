Fire on the railway line in Widnes Time of Incident: 28/05/2016 - 19:25 Location: Hale Road, Widnes. Attendance: Fire engines from Widnes Firefighters responded to a report of an electrical cable fire on the railway line off Hale Road, Widnes.  When they arrived crews set up a cordon around the impacted area and liaised with Network Rail while they dealt with the fire.
