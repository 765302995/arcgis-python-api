Road traffic collision on M56 west between junctions 9 and 10 Time of Incident: 18/10/2016 - 18:41 Location: M56 west between junctions 9 and 10 Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath Two vehicles were involved in a collision on the M56 westbound near Stretton. Nobody was trapped in either of the vehicles, which were a lorry and a car. Firefighters kept the scene safe by fending off vehicles.
