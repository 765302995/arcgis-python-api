Chimney fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/11/2015 - 17:12 Location: Secker Avenue, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington, an aerial appliance and supporting fire engine from Stockton Heath. Firefighters used an aerial appliance to extinguish a chimney fire from the roof of a property in Warrington.  The crews used ladders and a hose reel as well and were at the scene for around two and a half hours.  The neighbouring property loft was also checked for smoke. Chimney safety advice
