Refuse fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/03/2015 - 15:44 Location: The Wharf, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Stockton Heath Two hose reels were used to extinguish a fire involving refuse at the base of several large Conifer trees in Runcorn. Network Rail was informed while the incident was ongoing due to the smoke blowing onto the railway line and M56.
