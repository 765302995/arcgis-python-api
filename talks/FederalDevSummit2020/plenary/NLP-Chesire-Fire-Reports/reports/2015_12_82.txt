Small fire in Saughall, Chester Time of Incident: 09/12/2015 - 23:43 Location: Long Lane, Saughall, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters were called to an incident at the back of a property in Saughall where embers from an open fire had set fire to wooden pallets. Crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish the flames.
