House fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 14/12/2017 - 16:02 Location: Eadie Grove, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one fire engine from Nantwich Firefighters from Crewe and Nantwich attended reports of a house fire on Eadie Grove In Crewe. On arrival at the scene the fire was found to be out, but a resident at the address had suffered burns to their upper body. The resident was taken to Whiston Hospital for treatment.
