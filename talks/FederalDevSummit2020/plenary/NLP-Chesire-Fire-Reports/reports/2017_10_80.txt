Kitchen fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 13/10/2017 - 23:17 Location: Higham Avenue, Bewsey and Whitecross Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath. Firefighters were called to a kitchen fire in Higham Avenue.  They wore breathing apparatus and used one hose reel and a positive pressure ventilation unit to bring the fire under control.   All occupants were accounted for and crews were in attendance for 50 minutes.
