Outbuilding fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 23/08/2017 - 17:04 Location: Corbet Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to an outbuilding fire on Corbet Avenue, Warrington. On arrival the building was well alight and crews, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the flames. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information please call Cheshire Police on 101 or call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
