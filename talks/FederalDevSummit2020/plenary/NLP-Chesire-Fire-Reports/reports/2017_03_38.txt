Deliberate grass fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 25/03/2017 - 18:54 Location: Stringer Crescent, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Grass was believed to have been set on fire deliberately in Stringer Crescent. A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire and police were informed.  Anyone with any information about this or similar incidents is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.  Arson costs lives.
