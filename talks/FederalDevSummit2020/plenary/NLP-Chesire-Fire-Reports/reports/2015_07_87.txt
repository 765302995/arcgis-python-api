Hay on fire at a farm in Hale Village, Liverpool Time of Incident: 18/07/2015 - 17:50 Location: Baileys Lane in Hale Village, Liverpool. Attendance: One fire engine from Rucorn Approximately 20 square metres of hay was well alight at a farm on Baileys Lane in Hale Village, Liverpool.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to tackle the fire.
