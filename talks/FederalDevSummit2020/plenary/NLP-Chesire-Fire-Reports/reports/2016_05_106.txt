Road traffic collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 12/05/2016 - 22:26 Location: Butterton Lane, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe, the rapid response vehicle and fire engine from Sandbach One man was trapped in a car involved in a road traffic collision. Two cars were involved, leaving the one casualty trapped. Firefighters assessed his condition and then removed him from the vehicle and handed him into the care of the ambulance service.
