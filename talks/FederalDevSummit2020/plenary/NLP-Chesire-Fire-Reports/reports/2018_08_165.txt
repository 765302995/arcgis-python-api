 Small wood fire in the open in Winsford Time of Incident: 03/08/2018 - 17:04 Location: Wharton Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At around 5pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open near the Red Lion pub on Wharton Road in Winsford. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire involving wood. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
