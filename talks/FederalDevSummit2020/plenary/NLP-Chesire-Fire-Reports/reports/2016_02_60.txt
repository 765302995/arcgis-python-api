Fire in pub annexe in Sandbach Time of Incident: 17/02/2016 - 00:37 Location: Penda Way, Sandbach Attendance: Fire engines from Sandbach, Crewe, Holmes Chapel and Middlewich Four fire engines attended a fire in an annexe at the back of a public house. Firefighters, six wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to tackle the blaze and damp down. A thermal imaging camera was then used to make sure there was no fire spread to adjacent properties. Gas and electricity supplies to the pub were isolated. Crews were at the scene for approximately four and a half hours.
