Tumble dryer fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 04/06/2016 - 11:45 Location: Hulme Square, Middlewich. Attendance: Fire engines from Middlewich, Sandbach and Alsager Firefighters responded to a fire involving a tumble dryer at Hulme Square, Middlewich.  When they arrived crews found that the fire had gone out. Firefighters carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving the scene.   Tumble dryer fire safety
