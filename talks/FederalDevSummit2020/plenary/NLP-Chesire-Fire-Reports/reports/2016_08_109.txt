Serious road traffic collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 12/08/2016 - 22:25 Location: A534 Haslington Bypass, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe, One from Sandbach and one from Alsager There was a serious road traffic collision involving three cars.   Paramedics attended this incident.  Fire crews released one casualty from a vehicle. Police closed the Haslington Bypass and completed a full investigation. The incident was handed over to the Police.
