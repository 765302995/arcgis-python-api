Tree on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/04/2016 - 20:22 Location: Bewsey Farm Close, Burtonwood and Westbrook, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Arson is believed to be the cause of a tree fire in Bewsey Farm Close. Firefighters used beaters to put out the blaze and are urging anyone with any information to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
