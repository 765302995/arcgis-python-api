 Three-vehicle-collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 04/02/2019 - 21:37 Location: Runcorn Road, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Lymm and one from Runcorn Fire crews attended to a road traffic collision on Runcorn Road in Northwich involving three vehicles.  Although no one was trapped in their cars, crews assisted with clearing up the carriageway and disconnecting the vehicles batteries.  A 41-year-old woman was handed into the care of paramedics.
