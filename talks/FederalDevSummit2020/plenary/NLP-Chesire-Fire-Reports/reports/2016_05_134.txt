Car on fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 08/05/2016 - 18:49 Location: M56 East between junctions 16 to 15 Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a car on fire on the hard shoulder of the M56. One hose reel was used to extinguish the fire. Smoke affected both carriageways and the Highways Agency attended.
