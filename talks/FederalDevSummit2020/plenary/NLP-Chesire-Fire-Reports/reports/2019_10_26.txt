 Road traffic collision in Somerford Time of Incident: 26/10/2019 - 15:28 Location: Holmes Chapel Road, Somerford Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Congleton and one from Sandbach Fire crews attended to a collision involving one vehicle on Holmes Chapel Road in Somerford.  Two men inside the car were trapped inside and released by crews before being handed into the care of paramedics along with two other men who were inside the vehicle.  Fire crews were in attendance for approximately one hour.
