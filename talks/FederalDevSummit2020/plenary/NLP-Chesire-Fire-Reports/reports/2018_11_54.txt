 Woman trapped in a lift in Warrington Time of Incident: 20/11/2018 - 09:51 Location: Golden Square Shopping Centre, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 10am firefighters were called to reports of a person being trapped in a lift at a shopping centre in Warrington town centre. The crew attended Golden Square Shopping Centre and used specialist equipment to release the woman from the lift. Firefighters were in attendance for approximately 20 minutes.
