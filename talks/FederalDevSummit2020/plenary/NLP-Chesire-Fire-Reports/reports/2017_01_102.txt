Collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 07/01/2017 - 14:44 Location: Farm Lane, Macclesfield. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Holmes Chapel and Macclesfield Fire crews responded to a report of a collision on Farm Lane, Macclesfield.  When they arrived they found that one car had gone into a ditch. Firefighters helped a woman from the car and she was passed to the care of the ambulance service.
