Bin fire in Nantwich Time of Incident: 25/11/2017 - 20:44 Location: Beechwood Close, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Audlem Firefighters were called to a small bin fire outside Stapeley Community Centre, Nantwich.  Crews found small charring in the bin outside the community hall. They smothered the fire with water to prevent further ignition.
