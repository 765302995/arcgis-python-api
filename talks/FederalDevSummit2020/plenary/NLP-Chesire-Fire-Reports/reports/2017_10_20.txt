Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/10/2017 - 19:39 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to a wheelie bin fire on Dundalk Road, Widnes. The bin was well alight on arrival and crews used a fire extinguisher to put out the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police on 101 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
