Flat fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 16/10/2016 - 02:31 Location: Abbey Road, Macclesfield. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Macclesfield and Poynton. Fire crews were called to a report of a flat fire on Abbey Road, Macclesfield.  When they arrived they went into the flat wearing breathing apparatus. One man was helped to safety while the crews tackled the fire which had been caused by cooking left unattended.  The man was treated by paramedics for the effects of inhaling smoke.
