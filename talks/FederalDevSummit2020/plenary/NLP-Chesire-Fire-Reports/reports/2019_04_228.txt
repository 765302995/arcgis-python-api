 Fire at canal bank in Stockton Heath Time of Incident: 05/04/2019 - 19:27 Location: Fairfield Road, Warrington. WA4 2UU Attendance: One fire engine attended from Stockton Heath Canvas buckets were used to help put out a fire on the canal bank near to Fairfield Road.  The fire, which was in bushes, was difficult to access and the Stockton Heath crew was at the scene for about 30 minutes.
