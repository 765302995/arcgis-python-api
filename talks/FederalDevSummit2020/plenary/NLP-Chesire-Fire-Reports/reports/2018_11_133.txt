 Bonfire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 06/11/2018 - 18:28 Location: Warwick Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port At around 6.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a bonfire at Warwick Court in Ellesmere Port. The crew extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for nearly an hour.
