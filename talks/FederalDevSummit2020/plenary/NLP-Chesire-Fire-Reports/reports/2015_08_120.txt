Cows rescued from derelict swimming pool in Tarvin Time of Incident: 11/08/2015 - 09:55 Location: Holme Street, Tarvin, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and the Animal Rescue Unit with supporting fire engine from Knutsford. Firefighters rescued three cows from a derelict swimming pool in Tarvin.  The animals were stuck in the outdoor pool measuring approximately 3m wide by 10m long by 1.5m in depth and the crews used specialist animal rescue equipment to get them out.
