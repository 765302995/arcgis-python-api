Post box on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 16/10/2016 - 19:11 Location: Kilncroft, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a post box which is believed to have been deliberately set on fire and used dry power to extinguish it. If anyone knows who may have started this fire they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
