Recycling bins on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 07/06/2017 - 22:17 Location: Victoria Crescent, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters were called to three separate incidents where recycling bins were set on fire in Victoria Crescent. Police were requested to attend as it is believed that these fires were started deliberately. Call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
