 Mattress deliberately set alight in Crewe Time of Incident: 15/11/2019 - 16:29 Location: Bramhall Road, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a mattress which had been deliberately set alight by youths on Bramhall Road in Crewe.  The crew were in attendance at the scene for around 15 minutes.
