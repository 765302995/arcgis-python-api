Large skip and vehicle fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 25/06/2017 - 21:21 Location: Finlan Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Large skip and vehicle fire in WidnesFire crews were called to a large skip fire and a car fire on Finlan Road, Widnes. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to put out the fire. Police were also in attendance at the incident which is a suspected arson. If you have any information about the incident please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
