 Car on fire following one-vehicle collision in Winsford Time of Incident: 17/10/2019 - 03:38 Location: Road One, Winsford Industrial Estate, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Shortly after 3.30am firefighters were called to reports of a car being on fire on Road One at Winsford Industrial Estate.  On arrival the crew discovered that the car was well alight following a one-vehicle collision.  The four people who had been inside the car had all got out of it.  They were assessed by paramedics and are not believed to have sustained any significant injuries.  A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet.  Once the fire was out the vehicle was damped down to make it safe.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
