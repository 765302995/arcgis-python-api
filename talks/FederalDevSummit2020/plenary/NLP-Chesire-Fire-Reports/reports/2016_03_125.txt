Boiler on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/03/2016 - 18:29 Location: Sheffield Close, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Birchwood Crews were called to a report of a house fire at a property in Great Sankey. When they arrived they found there had been a fire in a gas boiler. Crews isolated gas and electricity to the boiler and thoroughly checked the appliance to make sure there were no further areas of fire.
