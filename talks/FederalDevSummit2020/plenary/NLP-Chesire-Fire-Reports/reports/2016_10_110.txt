Road traffic collision in Runcorn Time of Incident: 12/10/2016 - 11:23 Location: Weston Point Expressway, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Two cars were involved in a road traffic collision on Weston Point Expressway. Nobody was trapped in the vehicles. Police fended off traffic while firefighters made the scene safe and cleared fuel which had leaked from one of the cars using an environmental pack.
