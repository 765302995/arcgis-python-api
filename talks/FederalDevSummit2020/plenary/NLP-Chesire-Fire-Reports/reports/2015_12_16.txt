Chimney fire in Sutton Time of Incident: 27/12/2015 - 11:05 Location: Walker Lane, Sutton Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were sent to an incident where there was a report of a chimney fire, caused by a log burner.  The fire was already out when the firefighters arrived at the property.  Firefighters used a Thermal Imaging Camera to check that the fire was completely out.  Chimney fire safety tips
