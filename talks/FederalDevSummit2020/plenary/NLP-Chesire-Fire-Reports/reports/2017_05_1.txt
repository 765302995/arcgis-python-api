Wheelie bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 30/05/2017 - 19:50 Location: The Clough, Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a wheelie bin fire on The Clough, Runcorn.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire police on 101.
