 Shed fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 26/09/2018 - 08:17 Location: Waterloo Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich and one from Winsford Fire crews tackled a blaze in a shed on Waterloo Road, Northwich. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a covering jet and a hose reel jet to fight the fire. Crews also removed a large amount of wood from the back of the property and, as a precaution, two properties were evacuated.
