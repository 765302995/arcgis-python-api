Hay bales on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/11/2015 - 13:14 Location: Chester Road, Daresbury, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath Crews shuttled water and used a hose reel jet to put out a fire involving hay bales. There was a large quantity of hay on fire and crews were at the scene for around one and a quarter hours.
