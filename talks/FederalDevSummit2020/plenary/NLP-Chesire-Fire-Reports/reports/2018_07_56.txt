 Building fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 25/07/2018 - 14:57 Location: Bradley Lane, Malpas Attendance: A fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters attended a fire involving hat bales at a building in Malpas.  A hose reel was used to help tackle the fire while crews while an agricultural vehicle helped remove the hay bales.  A cordon was in place and the road was closed as a precaution.  The 20 hay bales were extinguished and the incident was resolved by 6.30pm.
