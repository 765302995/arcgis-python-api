 Vehicle fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 02/03/2019 - 08:51 Location: Derby Street, Crewe. CW1 3ER Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to reports of a vehicle fire at Derby Street shortly before 9am. Crews attended and found the engine compartment of a car well alight., They wore breathing apparatus and used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They also used a thermal imaging camera to make sure the fire was fully extinguished.
