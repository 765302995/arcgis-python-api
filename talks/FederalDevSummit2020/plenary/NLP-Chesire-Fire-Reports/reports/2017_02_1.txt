Caravan on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 27/02/2017 - 19:17 Location: Cholmondeley Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port Crews tackled a blaze involving a caravan at the side of a house on Cholmondeley Road. Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fire and then damped the area down.  It is possible the fire was started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
