 Car fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 21/07/2019 - 20:59 Location: Chirk Place, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford. Firefighters were called to a  car on fire in Chirk Place, Winsford. The car was removed from an attached garage and one hose reel used to put out the fire. One casualty suffered slight burns and smoke inhalation. Paramedics were in attendance. Crews were on scene for approximately 35 minutes.
