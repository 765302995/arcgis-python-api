 Hedgehog rescued from a drain Time of Incident: 16/07/2019 - 19:04 Location: Mustard Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm and the rope rescue unit from Lymm Fire crews were called to Mustard Lane in Warrington after a report that a dog was stuck in a drain. Crews opened the drain and discovered that a hedgehog was stuck so removed it from the drain.
