Small fire in stairwell of flats in Chester Time of Incident: 18/12/2015 - 05:11 Location: Heron Place, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and two from Ellesmere Port A small fire in the stairwell of a block of flats was extinguished with a hose reel. All persons were accounted for and the crew ventilated the stairwell before leaving.
