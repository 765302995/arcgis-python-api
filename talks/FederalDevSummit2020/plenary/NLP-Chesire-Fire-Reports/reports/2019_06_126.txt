 Wheelie bin fire in Chester Time of Incident: 04/06/2019 - 11:21 Location: Eversley Court, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Shortly after 11.20am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open at Eversley Court in Chester. On arrival the crew discovered a wheelie bin that had completely destroyed a fire. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and make the area safe. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour. For advice on how to prevent a wheelie bin fire visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
