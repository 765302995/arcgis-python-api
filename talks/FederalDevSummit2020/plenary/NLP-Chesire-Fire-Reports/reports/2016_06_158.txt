Runcorn grass fire Time of Incident: 03/06/2016 - 14:42 Location: Park Road, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Around 50 square metres of grass was on fire near to Runcorn Hill. Firefighters used beaters and a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
