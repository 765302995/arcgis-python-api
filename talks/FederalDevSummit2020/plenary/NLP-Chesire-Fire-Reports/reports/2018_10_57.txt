 Collision at a house in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/10/2018 - 17:56 Location: Worsborough Avenue, Great Sankey, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Warrington and one from Penk.eth Shortly before 6pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision at an address in Great Sankey. On arrival at the scene crews discovered that a car had collided with a garage at a property on Worsborough Avenue and a woman was trapped in the garage. The team forced entry into the garage at the rear of the building, allowing the woman to walk free. She was then passed to the care of paramedics and later taken to hospital as a precautionary measure.  Firefighters then isolated the utilities and conducted checks on the property. The team were in attendance at the scene for approximately one hour 45 minutes.
