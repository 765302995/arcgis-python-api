 Goat rescue in Alsager Time of Incident: 06/08/2018 - 18:57 Location: Sandbach Road North, Alsager. Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Alsager and one from Crewe. Goat stuck in a fenceShortly before 7pm firefighters were called to reports of a distressed goat trapped in a fence on Sandbach Road North, Alsager.  The team attended the scene and found that the animal had trapped its head in a wire fence. Crews used bolt cutters to release the goat and it was then passed to the care of the RSPCA.  The team were detained at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.
