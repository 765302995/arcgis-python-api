 Fire in a garden in Northwich Time of Incident: 17/12/2018 - 08:27 Location: Beach Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly before 8.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a garden on Beach Road in Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire that is believed to have been caused by discarded coal ash. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour.
