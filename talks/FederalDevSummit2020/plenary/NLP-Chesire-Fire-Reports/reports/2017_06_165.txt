Carpets on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 03/06/2017 - 13:45 Location: Whitchurch Way, Runcorn. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters responded to a report of a fire on Whitchurch Way, Runcorn.  When they arrived they found that the fire involved some carpet that had been placed in a shopping trolley.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have information that could help find those responsible please call 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
