House fire in Haslington Time of Incident: 12/12/2016 - 01:34 Location: Crewe Road, Haslington Attendance: Two fire engines from Sandbach, two from Crewe and one from Alsager Firefighters tackled a fire on the ground of a commercial property on Crewe Road. Firefighters, six wearing breathing apparatus, used three hose reel jets to put out the fire and a high pressure fan to clear smoke.  Although the first floor of the property was occupied nobody was trapped or needed rescuing. Crews were at the scene for over three hours.
