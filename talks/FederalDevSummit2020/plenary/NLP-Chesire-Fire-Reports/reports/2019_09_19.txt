 Wheelchair fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 26/09/2019 - 03:02 Location: Crew Avenue, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield Crews attended to reports of smoke in one of the rooms of a residential care home on Crew Avenue in Macclesfield caused by a wheelchair.   Firefighters removed the wheelchair from the property and naturally ventilated the room.  A man was checked over by paramedics as a precaution.
