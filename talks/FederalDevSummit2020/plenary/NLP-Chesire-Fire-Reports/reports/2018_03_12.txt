 Chimney fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 28/03/2018 - 19:08 Location: The Meade, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At 7.07pm firefighters were called to reports of a chimney fire at The Meade, Wilmslow.  The crew extinguished the fire in the fireplace at the house. They were at the scene for nearly an hour.  For tips on avoiding a chimney fire visit - Chimney fire safety advice
