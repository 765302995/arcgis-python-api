Sofa was well alight in garden of a care home in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 03/09/2017 - 01:16 Location: Ivy Lane Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A sofa was well alight in the back garden of a care home. One hose reel jet was used to extinguish the fire.   It is believed that the fire was started deliberately.  If you have any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
