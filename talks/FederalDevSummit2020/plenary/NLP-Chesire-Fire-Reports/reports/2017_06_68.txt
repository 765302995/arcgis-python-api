Fire in commercial building in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 20/06/2017 - 05:52 Location: Rossfield Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port, one from Powey Lane, Mollington, and one from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a fire in a commercial building on Rossfield Road, Ellesmere Port. Crews, four wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the fire. They then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. Cheshire Police were also in attendance at the incident.
