Road traffic collision in Whitley Time of Incident: 26/09/2016 - 15:16 Location: Bentleys Farm Lane, Whitley Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Warrington A 4x4 and a heavy goods vehicle were involved in a road traffic collision on Bentleys Farm Lane. A man was trapped in the 4x4. Firefighters worked to release him and then handed him into the care of the ambulance service.
