Chimney fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 27/11/2016 - 21:36 Location: Cranford Avenue, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield A fire in a chimney was tackled by firefighters in Cranford Avenue. The fire was above a wood burner and in the chimney flue. A hose reel jet was used to put out the main part of the fire in a wooden lintel and front of the chimney. Debris and burning embers were removed outside the property and a thermal imaging camera to monitor the area to make sure there were no more hot spots.  Always follow our chimney safety advice.
