 Vehicle fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/01/2019 - 21:12 Location: Blackheath Lane, Runcorn Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Runcorn Crews attended to a forklift truck smoking on Blackheath Lane in Runcorn.  Firefighters used a hose reel jet to cool down the vehicle and made sure the area was safe.
