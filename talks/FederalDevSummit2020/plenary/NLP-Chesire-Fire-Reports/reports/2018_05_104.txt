 Car fire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 17/05/2018 - 16:52 Location: Martin Road, Frodsham. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a car fire in Martin Road, Frodsham. The fire, which was in the engine compartment, was extinguished using a hose reel. The car was then handed over to the owner for recovery.
