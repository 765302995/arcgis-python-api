 Minor collision in Middlewich Time of Incident: 24/01/2019 - 13:13 Location: Chester Road, Middlewich. Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Winsford Shortly before 1.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a multi-vehicle collision on Chester Road in Middlewich.  Crews from Winsford attended the scene and on arrival they discovered there had been a low impact collision between two cars and a skip.  The team provided basic first aid to the driver of one of the cars and she was then passed to the care of paramedics - she did not require hospital treatment.  Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately 20 minutes.
