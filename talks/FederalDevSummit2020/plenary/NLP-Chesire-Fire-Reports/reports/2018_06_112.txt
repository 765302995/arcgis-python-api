 Hedgerow on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 22/06/2018 - 14:56 Location: Whitchurch Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Fire crews were called to a hedgerow which was on fire on Whitchurch Road, Chester. Crews used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze. Approximately 25 metres of hedgerow was on fire.
