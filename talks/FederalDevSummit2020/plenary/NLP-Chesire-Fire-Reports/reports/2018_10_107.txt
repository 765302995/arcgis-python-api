 Fire at a farm in Grappenhall Time of Incident: 11/10/2018 - 17:34 Location: Cartridge Lane, Grappenhall, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Lymm and one from Stockton Heath Shortly after 5.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a farm in Grappenhall. Crews from Lymm attended the scene and discovered a large fire involving plastics approximately 10 metres by 10 metres. Additional firefighters from Stockton Heath were called to assist. The team used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire and were in attendance at the scene for approximately one hour 30 minutes.
