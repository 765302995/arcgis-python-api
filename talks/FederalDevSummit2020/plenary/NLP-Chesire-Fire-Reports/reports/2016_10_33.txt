False alarm at Runcorn supermarket Time of Incident: 25/10/2016 - 12:24 Location: West Lane, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Frodsham Firefighters were called to Asda Supermarket after it was evacuated when fumes were believed to be coming from power equipment. This incident was a false alarm and the services of firefighters were not required.
