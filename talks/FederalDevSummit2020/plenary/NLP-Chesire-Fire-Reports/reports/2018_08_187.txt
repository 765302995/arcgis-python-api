 Outhouse fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 31/07/2018 - 15:41 Location: Shetland Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Powey Lane At around 3.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in an outhouse on Shetland Drive in Ellesmere Port. On arrival the crew discovered that the small fire had been extinguished with a garden hosepipe by the occupiers. Firefighters damped the area down to make it safe and used a large high pressure fan to clear smoke from the outhouse, which contained a hot tub. The fire is believed to have been started by an immersion heater used to heat the hot tub. Firefighters were in attendance for around 45 minutes.
