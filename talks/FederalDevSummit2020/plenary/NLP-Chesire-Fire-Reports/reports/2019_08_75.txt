 One-vehicle collision in Widnes Time of Incident: 20/08/2019 - 17:30 Location: Wilmere Lane, Widnes Attendance: Two firefighters attended, one from Penketh and one from Widnes At around 5.30pm firefighters were called to a report of a one-vehicle collision in Wilmere Lane, Widnes. On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved a saloon car. The road was closed in both directions as emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters made the vehicle safe and used specialist equipment to remove it from the centre of the roadway. They then handed the incident over to the police. A man was taken to hospital via ambulance.
