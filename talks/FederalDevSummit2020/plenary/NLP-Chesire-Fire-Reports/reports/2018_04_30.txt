 House fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 24/04/2018 - 00:42 Location: Shelagh Avenue, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes and one fire engine from Penketh. At 00:41 firefighters from Widnes and Penketh were called to reports of a house fire on Shelagh Avenue in Widnes. Crews attended the address and found that the fire had initially started in a shed and had spread to a nearby house. The team used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire and were in attendance at the scene for approximately 90 minutes.
