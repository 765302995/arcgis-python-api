Bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 13/11/2017 - 18:47 Location: Bishops Way, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Firefighters from Widnes attended reports of a small fire on Bishops Way. On arrival they used one hose reel jet to extinguish two separate bin fires. The ignition is believed to have been deliberate - anyone with information is asked to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111
