 Two-vehicle collision in Sandbach Time of Incident: 10/01/2019 - 09:19 Location: Middlewich Road, Sandbach Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Sandbach and one from Crewe At around 9.20am firefighters were called to a report of a two-vehicle collision at the junction of Middlewich Road and Platt Avenue in Sandbach. On arrival crews discovered that a female was trapped in one of the vehicles. A road was closed as emergency services worked at the scene. Firefighters got the casualty out of the vehicle and handed her over to the care of paramedics. Fire crews were in attendance for around an hour.
