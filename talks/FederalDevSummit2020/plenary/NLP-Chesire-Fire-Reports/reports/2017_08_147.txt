Chimney pot fallen into road in Crewe Time of Incident: 04/08/2017 - 09:57 Location: Market Street, Crewe Attendance: The aerial ladder platform from Chester Making a chimney safe in CreweFirefighters were called to a chimney pot which had fallen onto the road below and one still on a roof in an unsafe position. The aerial ladder attended and crews removed debris and the chimney pot.  The road was cordoned off by police while the incident was dealt with.
