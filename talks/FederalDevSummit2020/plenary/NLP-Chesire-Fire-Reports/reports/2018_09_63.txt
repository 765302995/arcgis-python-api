 Vehicle collides with electricity pole in Chester Time of Incident: 18/09/2018 - 15:56 Location: Whitchurch Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended - from Powey Lane and Chester At around 3.55pm firefighters were called to reports of an agricultural vehicle having collided with an electricity pole in a field on Whitchurch Road in Chester. On arrival crews discovered that power lines had been severed and were resting across a garden shed but there were no signs of fire. Firefighters set up a safety cordon and left the incident in the hands of Scottish Power.
