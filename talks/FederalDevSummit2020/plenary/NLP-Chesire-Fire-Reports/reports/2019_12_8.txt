 One-car collision in Knutsford Time of Incident: 09/12/2019 - 17:01 Location: Tabley Road, Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Knutsford and one from Lymm Crews attended to a collision on Tabley Road, Knutsford involving one car.  A 90-year-old woman was safely extricated from the car by firefighters.  She was handed into the care of paramedics and taken to hospital.  Crews were detained for approximately one hour.
