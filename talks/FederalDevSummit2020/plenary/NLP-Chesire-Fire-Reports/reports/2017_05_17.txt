Tumble dryer fire in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 27/05/2017 - 14:41 Location: Elm Drive, Holmes Chapel Attendance: Three fire engines Firefighters were called to a report of a building fire on Elm Drive. They found a tumble dryer on fire in a garage and, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.  Please, always follow our tumble dryer safety advice.
