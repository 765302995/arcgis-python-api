Disused building fire in Great Sutton Time of Incident: 14/04/2016 - 00:03 Location: Mill Lane, Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port There was a small fire in the entrance hall of an old school on Mill Lane. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and are urging anyone with any information about this incident to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 17 of 14 April.
