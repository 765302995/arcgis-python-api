 Small fire at a community centre garden in Runcorn Time of Incident: 09/08/2018 - 23:15 Location: Lockgate West, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Widnes At around 11.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a community centre on Lockgate West in Runcorn. On arrival crews discovered a small fire in a garden. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
