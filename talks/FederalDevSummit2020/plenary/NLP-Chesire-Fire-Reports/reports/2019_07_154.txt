 Road traffic collision, Widnes Time of Incident: 06/07/2019 - 15:07 Location: A533 Mersey Gateway Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Fire crews were called to assist with a road traffic collision on the A533 Mersey Gateway bridge on the Runcorn bound carriageway. It involved two saloon cars, one of which had flipped onto its roof.  The occupants of the vehicles had managed to get out of their cars and one woman was passed into the care of paramedics.  The carriageway was closed for around an hour as firefighters worked with police to make the vehicles safe.
