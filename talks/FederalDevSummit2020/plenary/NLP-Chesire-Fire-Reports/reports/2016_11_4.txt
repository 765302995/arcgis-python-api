Car on fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 29/11/2016 - 15:41 Location: Buxton New Road, Macclesfield Forest Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a car on fire in Macclesfield Forest. On arrival they found a car which had overheated but was not on fire and used water to cool the vehicle. A thermal imaging camera was also used to check the car had cooled down before the crews left.
