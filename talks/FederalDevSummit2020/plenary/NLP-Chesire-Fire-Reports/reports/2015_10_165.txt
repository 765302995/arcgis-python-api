Road traffic collision A536 Time of Incident: 01/10/2015 - 07:50 Location: Church Lane, Gawsworth Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Congleton The junction of Congleton Road and Park Lane by Macclesfield College as well as Macclesfield Road in Congleton were closed this morning because of a road traffic collision. Fire crews assisted at the scene of the collision which involved a saloon car and a bus. The road was closed while emergency services dealt with the incident. The roads have since re-opened.
