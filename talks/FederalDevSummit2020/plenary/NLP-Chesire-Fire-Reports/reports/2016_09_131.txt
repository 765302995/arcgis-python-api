Car fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 04/09/2016 - 08:58 Location: Holmes Chapel Road, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel, one from Winsford, one from Crewe and one from Northwich A car was heavily involved in fire on Holmes Chapel Road. Nobody was trapped in the car. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out the blaze. Police attended to manage traffic.
