Furniture fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 23/08/2017 - 19:02 Location: Kingsway, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a furniture fire in an alleyway near to Kingsway Leisure Centre, on Kingsway, Widnes. The furniture was well alight on arrival and crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews also used a thermal imaging camera to check for hot spots.
