Small fire in a tree in Nortwich Time of Incident: 26/08/2017 - 21:39 Location: Chester Road, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Fire crews were called to a small fire in a tree which had started after a firework struck it on Chester Road, Northwich. Crews used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which was approximately 15m up a tree.
