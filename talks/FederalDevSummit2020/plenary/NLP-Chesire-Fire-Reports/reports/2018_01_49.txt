 Flat fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 19/01/2018 - 14:55 Location: Caesars Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn, one from Widnes, one from Penketh and one from Warrington At 14:55 Firefighters were called to reports of a flat fire on Caesars Close, Runcorn. The fire initially started in a first floor flat but spread to the roof and a neighbouring property. Four fire crews attended the scene and used two breathing apparatus, two hose reel jets, a main jet and a large fan to bring the fire under control. Crews were in attendance at the scene for five hours. All persons in the property were accounted for by the time the fire service arrived.
