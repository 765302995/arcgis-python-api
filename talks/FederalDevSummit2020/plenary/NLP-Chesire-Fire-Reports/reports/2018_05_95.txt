 Fire involving rubbish in Warrington Time of Incident: 18/05/2018 - 08:29 Location: Bridge Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 8.30am firefighters were called to a report of fire outside a unit on an industrial park on Bridge Lane in Woolston, Warrington. On arrival firefighters found that the fire in the open involved rubbish, which measured two metres by two metres. They used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
