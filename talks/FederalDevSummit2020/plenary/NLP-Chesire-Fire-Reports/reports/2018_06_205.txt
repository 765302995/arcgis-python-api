 Small vehicle fire, Wilmslow Time of Incident: 09/06/2018 - 11:03 Location: Carnoustie Close, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Van fire in WilmslowCrews attended a van on fire in Carnoustie Close, Wilmslow. The transit van was well alight on the driveway and firefighters used breathing apparatus and a single hose reel to extinguish the fire. The incident is thought to have been caused by an electrical fault, and they were in attendance for around half an hour.
