Smoke from tyres of lorry M56 West J09 to J10 Time of Incident: 27/09/2016 - 16:47 Location: M56 West J09 to J10 Attendance: Two fire engines from Stockton Heath and Birchwood Smoke rose from the tyres of a lorry after it overheated on the hard shoulder, approximately one mile from junction 10. Firefighters cooled the heavy goods vehicle using a hose reel jet and checked for hot spots of fire using a thermal imaging camera.
