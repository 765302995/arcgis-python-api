 Loft fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 04/10/2019 - 15:46 Location: Charminster Close, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. One fire engine from Penketh. Firefighters were called to a fire in the loft of a house in Charminster Close, Warrington. Everyone was accounted for and firefighters extinguished the fire using four breathing apparatus, one covering jet and one hose reel. They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
