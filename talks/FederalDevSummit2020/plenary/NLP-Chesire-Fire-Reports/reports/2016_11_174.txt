Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 01/11/2016 - 20:12 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to deal with a fire on a playground off Dundalk Road, Widnes.  When they arrived crews found that a wheelie bin had been set on fire.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that may help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
