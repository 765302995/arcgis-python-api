 Road traffic collision on M6 Northbound Time of Incident: 09/09/2019 - 15:01 Location: M6 northbound between junction 20-21A Attendance: Four fire engines have attended - one from Lymm, one from Birchwood, one from Penketh and one from Winsford Firefighters were called to a collision on the M6 northbound between junction 20-21a involving three large vehicles.  Crews extricated a casualty from one of the HGVs and handed into the care of paramedics.  Police, ambulance and Highways England were in attendance.
