 Grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 05/07/2018 - 16:58 Location: Brackley Street, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a grass fire on Brackley Street, Runcorn. Crews used  a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information then please call Cheshire Police.
