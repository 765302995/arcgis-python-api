Unattended pan in Winsford Time of Incident: 26/10/2017 - 16:36 Location: Regent Street, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Fire crews were called to a house on Regent Street, Winsford, after reports of a fire. On arrival crews found an unattended pan had caused smoke in the property. One casualty was treated for smoke inhalation by crews and was taken to hospital by paramedics. Kitchen safety information
