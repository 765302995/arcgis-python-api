 Fallen tree resting on telegraph pole in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 25/09/2019 - 10:50 Location: Strawberry Lane, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow TreeAt around 10.50am firefighters were called to a report of a fallen tree resting on a telegraph pole over a public footpath in Strawberry Lane, Wilmslow.  The crew set up a safety cordon before handing the incident over to Cheshire East Council workers in attendance.  The council has since closed the affected footpath.  Firefighters were in attendance for more than half-an-hour.
