Two car collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 17/09/2016 - 15:51 Location: Tarporley Road in Oakmere, Northwich. Attendance: Fire engine from Northwich Firefighters were called to a collision on Tarporley Road in Oakmere, Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that the incident involved two cars. Although there was no one trapped seven people were hurt. Crews assisted by making sure the scene was safe as the ambulance service organised treatment for the injured.
