Electric blanket causes fire in Warrington bungalow Time of Incident: 27/03/2015 - 04:29 Location: Green Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrington A fire in a bedroom was caused by an electric blanket.  Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and a high pressure fan to clear smoke.  There were two people in the property suffering from smoke inhalation and an ambulance was asked to attend.  Smoke detectors had been fitted at the bungalow and alerted the occupants to the fire.
