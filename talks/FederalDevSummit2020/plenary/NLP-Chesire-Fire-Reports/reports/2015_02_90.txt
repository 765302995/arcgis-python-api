Outbuilding fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/02/2015 - 17:25 Location: Brandwood Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: Two fire engines from Warrinton Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus and used one hose reel to extinguish a fire in a an outbuilding in Warrington. A large fan was used to clear the smoke and a fire investigator requested to attend the scene. The cause was deemed to be electrical.
