 Petrol leak at supermarket car park in Handforth Time of Incident: 25/07/2019 - 11:47 Location: Kiln Croft Lane, Handforth Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow At around 11.45am firefighters were called to a report of a car leaking fuel onto the Tesco Extra car park in Kiln Croft Lane, Handforth. On arrival the crew discovered around five litres of petrol had leaked from the car's fuel tank. Firefighters used specialist paste to stop the leak and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
