Car on fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 21/07/2015 - 16:17 Location: Oulton Park Race Circuit, Park Road, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine Firefighters were called to a report of a car fire at the Oulton Park Race Circuit in Little Budworth.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a car on fire but this had been put out by staff at the circuit. Crews used hose reel jets to fully damp down the area.  One person was taken to hospital by air ambulance after suffering burns at the scene.
