 Smoke in a loft in Northwich Time of Incident: 09/08/2018 - 16:34 Location: Royds Close, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 4.35pm firefighters were called to a report of smoke in the loft of a semi-detached house in Royds Close, Hartford, Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that the small amount of smoke was caused by an overheated light fitting. Firefighters used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots and isolated the power supply to the light fitting. They were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
