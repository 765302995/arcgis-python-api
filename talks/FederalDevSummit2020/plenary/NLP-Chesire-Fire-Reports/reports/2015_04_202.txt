Two people trapped inside an overturned vehicle in Chester Time of Incident: 03/04/2015 - 16:16 Location: Chester Road, Aldford, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester fire station Two fire engines from Chester fire station attended a road traffic collision on Chester Road in Aldford involving one overturned vehicle. Two casualties were extricated from the vehicle by firefighters and they were then left with paramedics. Firefighters were at the scene for almost two hours.
