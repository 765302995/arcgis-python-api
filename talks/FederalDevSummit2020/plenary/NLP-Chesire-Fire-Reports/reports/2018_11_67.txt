 Small fire in the open in Winsford Time of Incident: 18/11/2018 - 17:39 Location: Finsbury Walk, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Just before 5.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open in Finsbury Walk, Winsford. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire involving rubbish. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for less than 10 minutes.
