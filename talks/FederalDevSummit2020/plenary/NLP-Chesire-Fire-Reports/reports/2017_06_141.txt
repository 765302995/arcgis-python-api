Road traffic collision in Birchwood Time of Incident: 07/06/2017 - 19:58 Location: Birchwood Way, Birchwood, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Warrington Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving two vehicles on Birchwood Way in Birchwood, Warrington.
