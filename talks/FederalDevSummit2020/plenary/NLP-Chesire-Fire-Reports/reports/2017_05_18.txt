Casualty released from lorry in Hatton Heath Time of Incident: 27/05/2017 - 13:21 Location: Whitchurch Road, Hatton Heath Attendance: Three fire engines A heavy goods vehicle on its side left the driver trapped in the cab on Whitchurch Road. Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to be able to release the man who was handed into the care of paramedics.
