Bin fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 24/01/2015 - 20:37 Location: Marina Walk, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: A fire Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to deal with a bin fire on Marina Walk in Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived they found two bins on fire in separate locations. Hose reel jets were used to put them out.   It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
