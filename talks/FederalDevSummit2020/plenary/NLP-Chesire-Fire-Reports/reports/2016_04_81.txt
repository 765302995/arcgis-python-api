Road traffic collision on the main car park at Chester Zoo Time of Incident: 16/04/2016 - 11:49 Location: Chester Zoo, Caughall Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one fire engine from Ellesmere Port One fire engine from Chester and one fire engine from Ellesmere Port were sent to an incident involving a road traffic collision in the main car parking area at Chester Zoo.  One female casualty suffered minor injuries.  One male casualty was trapped inside a vehicle.  Firefighters used cutting equipment to release the male casualty, who was then handed to the care of the paramedics.  Police also attended this incident.
