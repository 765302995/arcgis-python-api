Woman trapped between vehicles Time of Incident: 13/05/2015 - 13:26 Location: Joy Lane, Burtonwood Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Stockton Heath Emergency Services were called to an incident where a woman was trapped between a tractor and fertiliser unit in Burtonwood.  The casualty was released prior to the arrival of the firefighters and was taken to hospital by paramedics for a precautionary check.
