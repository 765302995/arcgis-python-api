Log burning stove fire in Poynton Time of Incident: 11/03/2016 - 21:09 Location: Shrigley Road, Poynton Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington and one from Offerton (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Crews needed to remove a log burning stove from a house in Poynton as it had caused a fire in the hearth. They then damped down the hearth area using hand appliances.  Please follow our log burner safety advice.
