 Fire on a playing field in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 18:02 Location: Grizedale, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At around 6pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire on a playing field in Grizedale, Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved approximately 20 metres by 10 metres of grass. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and beaters and were in attendance for around 25 minutes.
