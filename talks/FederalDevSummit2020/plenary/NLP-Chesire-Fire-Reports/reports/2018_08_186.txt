 Fire in the open that spread to a hedgerow in Chester Time of Incident: 31/07/2018 - 18:48 Location: Broomheath Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley At around 6.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open that had spread to a hedgerow on Broomheath Lane in Tarvin, Chester. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of intentional burning that was affecting around two metres of hedgerow. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 25 minutes.
