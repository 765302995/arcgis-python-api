Outbuilding fire in Chester Time of Incident: 18/11/2017 - 15:23 Location: Clover Lane, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters from Chester used a hose reel jet to extinguish a fire in a small brick outbuilding at an address on Clover Lane, Chester. The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to  Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 589 of 18/11/2017. Information can also be reported anonymously, via Crimestoppers , on 0800 555 111
