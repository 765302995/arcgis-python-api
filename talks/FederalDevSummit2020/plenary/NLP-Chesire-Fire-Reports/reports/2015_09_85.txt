Wheelie bin fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 13/09/2015 - 03:32 Location: Solvay Road, Northwich. Attendance: Fire engines from Northwich and Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Solvay Road, Northwich.  When they arrived crews found that a wheelie had been set on fire close to the property. They dealt with the fire and carried out a Home Safety Assessment at the property. Other properties in the area were also visited.   It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
