Road traffic collision in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 03/11/2017 - 16:07 Location: Melrose Way, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow, one from Knutsford and one from Cheadle in Greater Manchester Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision involving two cars and a van with four people trapped. The crew used cutting equipment and an electric saw to free the casualties. Paramedics and police also attended.
