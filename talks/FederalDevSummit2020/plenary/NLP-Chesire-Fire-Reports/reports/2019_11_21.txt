 Small fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 24/11/2019 - 15:54 Location: Rossmore Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters from Ellesmere Port used a hose reel jet to extinguish a small fire in the open in Ellesmere Port.  The crew were detained at the scene for around 15 minutes.
