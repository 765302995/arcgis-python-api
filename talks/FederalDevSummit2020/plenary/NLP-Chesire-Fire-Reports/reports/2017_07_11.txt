Trees on fire in Birchwood Time of Incident: 27/07/2017 - 19:28 Location: Admirals Road, Birchwood Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood A hose reel jet was used by firefighters to put out a fire in woodland.  It is believed the fire was started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
