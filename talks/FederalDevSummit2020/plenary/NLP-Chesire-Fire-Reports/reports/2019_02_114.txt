 One person trapped after a crash on the M6 Time of Incident: 08/02/2019 - 23:21 Location: M6 south between junction 18 and 17 Attendance: Four fire engines Fire crews were called to a collision between two lorries on the M6 southbound. One person was trapped in their vehicle and fire crews extracted the casualty using hydraulic cutting equipment and provided first aid before handing them into the care of paramedics.
