 Truck fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/09/2019 - 09:53 Location: Birchwood Lane, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Birchwood Fire crews attended to a small fire involving a truck at Birchwood Lane petrol services in Warrington.  Firefighters isolated the battery, made the vehicle safe and then used one hose reel jet to put out the flames. It was then checked over using the thermal imaging camera.
