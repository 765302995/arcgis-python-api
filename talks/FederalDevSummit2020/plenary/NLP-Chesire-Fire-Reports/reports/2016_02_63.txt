Fuse box on fire in Brereton Heath Time of Incident: 16/02/2016 - 08:28 Location: Brereton Heath Lane, Brereton Heath Attendance: One fire engine from Congleton and one from Holmes Chapel Firefighters attended a report of a fire involving a fuse box at a property on Brereton Heath Lane. The fire was in a garage attached to a house. Two crew members wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to investigate the extent of the incident. They found an electrical fire in a fuse box and isolated gas and electricity supplies. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke and crews checked for hot spots using a thermal imaging camera.
