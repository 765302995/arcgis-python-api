Delibertate grass fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 13/06/2015 - 22:30 Location: Astmoor Industrial Estate, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Firefighters extinguished a scrubland fire believed to have been started deliberately in Runcorn.  One hose reel was used to put out the fire involving approximately 10m of scrubland. Police were requested to attend the scene.  Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101, or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
