 Small fire at a BMX track in Winsford Time of Incident: 22/05/2019 - 20:04 Location: Mount Pleasant Drive, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Winsford firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out a small fire on the BMX track on Mount Pleasant.  The crew were in attendance at the scene for around 20 minutes,  The cause of the fire is believed to be deliberate and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101.
