 Car fire in M6 Time of Incident: 12/07/2019 - 14:52 Location: M6 northbound between junction 19 and 20 Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood, one from Lymm and one from Knutsford Fire crews were called to a car fire on the M6 northbound between junction 19 and 20. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.
