Road traffic collision in Nantwich Time of Incident: 12/03/2016 - 06:07 Location: Wrenbury Road, Aston, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Audlem and one from Nantwich Two cars were involved in a road traffic collision in Aston, Nantwich. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used two hose reel jets to put out a fire in one of the vehicles. Nobody was trapped in either vehicle. One casualty was taken to hospital by land ambulance.
