 Fire in the open in Winsford Time of Incident: 28/03/2018 - 21:29 Location: Weaver Street, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford At 9.27pm firefighters were called to a fire in the open at Weaver Street in Winsford. One arrival the crew discovered an unattended fire and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the flames. Firefighters were at the scene for around 25 minutes.
