Refuse fire in Runcorn field Time of Incident: 12/06/2015 - 19:51 Location: Stonehills Lane, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn A refuse fire in a field in Runcorn is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101, or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
