Animal rescue in Nantwich Time of Incident: 09/11/2017 - 11:33 Location: Coole Lane near Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one fire engine from Nantwich. Goose rescued by firefightersFirefighters were called to reports of a goose trapped on an island at a Fishery on Coole Lane near Nantwich.  Two crews attended and used a sledge to rescue the goose which had its legs entangled in fishing line. The goose has since been taken to Stapeley Grange Wildlife Centre.
