 Man trapped following two-vehicle collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/07/2019 - 01:14 Location: Farrell Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 1.15am firefighters were called to a report of someone being trapped following a two-vehicle collision in Farrell Street, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that the collision involved a milk float and a heavy goods vehicle. They also found that a man was trapped in the milk float and a quantity of milk had spilt onto the road. Firefighters got the man out of the vehicle and handed him over to the care of paramedics. They then handed the incident over to the police. Firefighters were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
