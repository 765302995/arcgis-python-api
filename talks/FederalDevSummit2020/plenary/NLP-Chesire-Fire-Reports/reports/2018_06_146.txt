 Fire outside a house in Widnes Time of Incident: 19/06/2018 - 04:49 Location: Downside, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes attended At around 4.50am firefighters were called to a report of rubbish being on fire outside a house in Downside, Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved household waste. The waste is believed to have been set alight deliberately. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
