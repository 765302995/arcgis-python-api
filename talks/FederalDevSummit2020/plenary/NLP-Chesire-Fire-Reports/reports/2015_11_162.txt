Two vans on fire in Wistaston Time of Incident: 02/11/2015 - 04:07 Location: Handforth Road, Wistaston Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A fire involving two vans in Wistaston is believed to have been started deliberately.  Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used two hose reel jets to extinguish the blaze and an investigation into the cause of the fire is underway.  Police were informed and anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101 or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
