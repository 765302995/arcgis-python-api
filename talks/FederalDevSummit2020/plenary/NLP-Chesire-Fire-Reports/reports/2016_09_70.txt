Collision on the M6 Time of Incident: 17/09/2016 - 22:50 Location: M6 between Junctions 16 and 17. Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe and Sandbach Firefighters were called to report of a collision on the M6 between Junctions 16 and 17.  When they arrived crews found a car in the third lane of the motorway and a lot of debris on the road. There was no one trapped and so crews fended traffic safely away from the vehicle and assisted the police in moving it to a safe area.
