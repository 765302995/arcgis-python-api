 Fire in woodland, Lostock Gralam Time of Incident: 30/06/2018 - 20:40 Location: Wincham Brook, Lostock Gralam Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich Firefighters were called to tackle a fire in woodland at Wincham Brook. The area affected measured around 4m x 4m. Crews initially used water backpacks to tackle the fire, but then used a hose reel which had to be extended due to access difficulties. They were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
