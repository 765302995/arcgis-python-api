Leaking carbon dioxide cylinder in Nantwich Time of Incident: 03/08/2016 - 11:09 Location: Welsh Row, Nantwich Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich and two from Crewe Crews were called to a report of a cylinder giving off carbon dioxide in the basement of a premises in Welsh Row. Staff had evacuated the premises prior to the arrival of firefighters. Four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus went into the building to isolate the supply, with a covering jet set up as a precaution. The building was ventilated and gas levels checked using a CO2 meter, which showed normal levels.
