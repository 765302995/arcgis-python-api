Dog rescued from the River Mersey in Paddington, Warrington Time of Incident: 29/08/2015 - 14:08 Location: River Mersey, Paddington, Warrington Attendance: A fire engine from Stockton Heath, the Animal Rescue Unit from Knutsford and the boat from Warrington A fire engine from Stockton Heath, the Animal Rescue Unit from Knutsford and the boat from Warrington were sent to an incident where a dog needed rescuing from the River Mersey in Paddington, Warrington.  Firefighters used the boat to rescue the dog from the water.
