Cooker fire in Whitegate Time of Incident: 05/08/2016 - 20:05 Location: Dalefords Lane, Whitegate, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford One fire engine was called to a report of a fire in a kitchen on Dalefords Lane. The fire involved a cooker which was out on arrival. Firefighters removed the cooker to outside the house and used a high pressure fan to clear smoke from the kitchen.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice.
