 Shower fire in Chester Time of Incident: 09/01/2019 - 04:20 Location: Pool Lane, Chester. Attendance:  Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended a fire involving an electric shower in changing rooms at Thornton Science Park in Pool Lane, Chester.  Crews used one hose reel and two breathing apparatus to put out the fire. They then used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
