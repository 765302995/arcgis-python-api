 Large vehicle fire near Knutsford Time of Incident: 14/05/2019 - 16:09 Location: M6 junction 19 Attendance: Three fire engines attended from Powey Lane, Warrington and Greater Manchester. Large vehicle fire near KnutsfordAt around 4.10pm firefighters were called to reports of a vehicle fire on the exit slip road of the M6 junction 19, near Knutsford.  The fire involved a HGV trailer containing cereal which was well alight and causing a large plume of thick black smoke across the motorway.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire. They then assisted with the removal of the cereal from the vehicle.  A full closure was put in place on the slip road while the crews dealt with the incident.
