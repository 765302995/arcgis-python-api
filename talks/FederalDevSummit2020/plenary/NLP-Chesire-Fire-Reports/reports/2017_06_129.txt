Fuel spillage incident on M6 by Junction 21 Time of Incident: 10/06/2017 - 13:59 Location: M6 motorway (southbound) between junction 21 and junction 20 Attendance: Two fire engines from Stockton Heath and from Birchwood Firefighters attended an incident on the M6 motorway (southbound) between junction 21 and junction 20, where a HGV had a damaged fuel tank which was leaking fuel onto the carriageway.  The fuel tank contained 200 litres of diesel.  Highways Agency coned off the hard shoulder and lane one. Firefighters sealed the tank. Motorway Police attended this incident.
