House fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 25/06/2017 - 18:40 Location: Heyes Lane, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one from Macclesfield House fire in Alderley EdgeFire crews were called to a house fire on Heyes Lane, Alderley Edge, which was on the ground floor of a mid terrace property. Firefighters, four wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire which was in the kitchen of the property.  The occupiers of the property were not in at the time of the incident and the fire is believed to have been started accidentally.  Fire service personnel were in attendance at the property for approximately three hours and ten minutes.
