 Fire outside a pub in Plumley Time of Incident: 10/08/2018 - 00:05 Location: Chester Road, Plumley Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich At around 12.05am firefighters were called to a report of a fire at the Smoker Inn on Chester Road in Plumley. On arrival crews discovered a fire outside the pub. Firefighters extinguished the fire using a hose reel jet. They were in attendance for around an hour.
