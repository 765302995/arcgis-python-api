Unoccupied house on fire in Lymm Time of Incident: 10/12/2016 - 19:48 Location: Rushgreen Road, Lymm Attendance: Two fire engines from Stockton Heath A fire in the basement of a disused property on Rushgreen Road was attended by firefighters. The fire was in the basement of the house which was under refurbishment. Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus and used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and damp the area down. All floors of the house were closely inspected using thermal imaging cameras to make sure there were no further hot spots, and the neighbouring house was also checked to ensure there had been no fire spread. An investigation into the cause of the fire is being carried out.
