Smoke logging in Holmes Chapel house Time of Incident: 06/12/2016 - 12:46 Location: Elm Drive, Holmes Chapel Attendance: One fire engine from Holmes Chapel and one from Middlewich There was smoke logging in the utility room of a house in Elm Drive. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, went into the property with a hose reel jet and isolated the gas and electricity supplies. They used a high pressure fan to clear the smoke. Everyone in the property was accounted for.
