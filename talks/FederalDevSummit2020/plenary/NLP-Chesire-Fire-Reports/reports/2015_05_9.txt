Collision on the M53 Time of Incident: 28/05/2015 - 16:18 Location: Junctions 8 and 10 on the M53. Attendance: A fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a collision between junctions 8 and 10 on the M53.  When they arrived crews found a car on its roof but with nobody trapped. They helped make the scene safe.
