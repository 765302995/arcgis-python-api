Building evacuated in Northwich Time of Incident: 20/12/2016 - 14:15 Location: Wincham Lane, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Knutsford and Northwich A small amount of resin in a laboratory reacted with heat inside an oven, leading to a full evacuation of a building in Northwich. Firefighters consulted with an onsite specialist and hazmat officer before putting breathing apparatus on to investigate the laboratories. They then allowed the premises to ventilate naturally.
