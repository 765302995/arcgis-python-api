Outbuilding building fire in Bollington Time of Incident: 11/08/2015 - 11:40 Location: Green Lane, Bollington Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington and one from Macclesfield Firefighters attended a report of a fire in an outbuilding. They found the fire involved a window frame and used a hose reel jet to put it out. A small refuse fire had been lit close to the window, causing the incident.
