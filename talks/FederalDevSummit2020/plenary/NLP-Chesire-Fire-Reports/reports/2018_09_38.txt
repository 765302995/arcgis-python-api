 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 22/09/2018 - 19:50 Location: Cromwell Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Just before 8pm firefighters were called to reports of a wheelie bin on fire in an underpass near to Cromwell Avenue. Crews used a water back pack to put the fire out. See the website for more information on how to keep your wheelie bin safe.
