Caravan on fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 04/08/2017 - 18:12 Location: Wingfield Place, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and one from Northwich A caravan on a driveway was well alight.  The fire affected the side door of a semi detached property. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.  Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus.  A large fan was used to clear smoke. Police attended this incident.  An ambulance was requested for two male casualties who were suffering from smoke inhalation.
