 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 06/03/2019 - 12:39 Location: Fairclough Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 12.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a bin being on fire in the open on Fairclough Street in Warrington. On arrival the crew found a wheelie bin well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for less than half-an-hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. For advice on preventing your wheelie bins from becoming a target for arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
