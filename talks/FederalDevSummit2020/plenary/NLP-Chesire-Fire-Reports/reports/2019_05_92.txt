 Fence on fire in Chester Time of Incident: 19/05/2019 - 12:26 Location: Village Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Chester and one from Powey Lane Crews attended to a fence and small hedgerow on fire on Village Road in Chester.  An electrical provider was also called as the fire had spread to an electricity pole.  One hose reel jet was used to put out the fire and then dampened down the area.  The fire is believed to have been caused accidentally.   Firefighters were in attendance for approximately two hours and ten minutes carrying out fire investigation.
