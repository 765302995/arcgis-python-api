 Smoke alarms activated by burnt food in Crewe Time of Incident: 04/10/2019 - 13:50 Location: Nelson Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe At 1.50pm firefighters were called to a property in Nelson Street, Crewe. On arrival the crews discovered that smoke alarms had been activated by burnt food. A man was assessed by paramedics and firefighters used a large fan to remove cooking fumes from the property. Firefighters were in attendance for nearly an hour. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
