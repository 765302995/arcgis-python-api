 Reports of a fire involving an intercom system in Chester Time of Incident: 11/08/2019 - 15:31 Location: Foregate Street, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 3.30pm firefighters were called to reports that an intercom box outside a public house in Chester was smoking.  Firefighters attended the scene and found that a cigarette had been extinguished in the intercom, causing a small amount of burning.  The crew checked the box with a thermal imaging camera and there were no signs of fire.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
