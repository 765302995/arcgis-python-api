 Fire in woodland in Warrington Time of Incident: 27/05/2019 - 14:09 Location: Dingle Lane, Appleton, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 2.10pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in woodland on Dingle Lane in Appleton, Warrington. The crew extinguished the campfire with a hose reel jet. Firefighters were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
