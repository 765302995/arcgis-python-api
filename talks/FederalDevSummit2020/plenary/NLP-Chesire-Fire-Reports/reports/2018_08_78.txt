 Car fire on the M6 Time of Incident: 15/08/2018 - 07:16 Location: M6 southbound between junction 21 and 20 Attendance: One fire engine from Penketh, one from Warrington and one from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service Fire crews were called to a car fire on the M6 southbound between junction 21 and 20. Crews, one wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
