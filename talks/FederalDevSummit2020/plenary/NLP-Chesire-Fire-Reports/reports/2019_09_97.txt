 Fire in a field in Crewe Time of Incident: 11/09/2019 - 19:53 Location: Stoneley Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly before 8pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open on Stoneley Road, near Groby Road, in Crewe. On arrival the crew found a small unattended fire in a field. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes.
