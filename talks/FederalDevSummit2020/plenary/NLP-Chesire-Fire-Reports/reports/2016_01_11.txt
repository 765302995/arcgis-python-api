Bungalow on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/01/2016 - 08:16 Location: Wellswood Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and two from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a bungalow on fire in Ellesmere Port.  The crews used one hose reel and a covering water jet to extinguish the fire and a large fan to remove smoke from the property. Paramedics also attended.  Updated at 11am  It has been confirmed that sadly one man passed away as a result of this fire.
