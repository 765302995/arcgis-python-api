 House fire in Moulton Time of Incident: 20/08/2019 - 11:06 Location: Hillside Lane, Moulton Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Winsford, one from Northwich and one from Middlewich Shortly after 11am firefighters were called to reports of a house fire in Hillside Lane, Moulton.  On arrival the crews discovered a fire in a first floor bedroom.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus entered the building and used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  A covering jet was also used.  An adult and two children were treated at the scene for smoke inhalation.  After extinguishing the fire the crews stopped using breathing apparatus and used a hose reel jet to damp down the areas affected by the fire.  They also used a large fan to clear smoke from the property.  Firefighters were in attendance for around three hours.
