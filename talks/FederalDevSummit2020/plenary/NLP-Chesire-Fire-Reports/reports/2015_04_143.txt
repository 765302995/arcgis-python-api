Fire involving tyres and hay in Wettenhall Time of Incident: 12/04/2015 - 13:35 Location: Winsford Road, Wettenhall Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford and one from Tarporley A fire involving approximately 100 tyres and a 40 tonne bale of hay was extinguished by firefighters using two main jets and a hose reel.  The unaffected hay bales were removed by the farmer to avoid the fire escalating.
