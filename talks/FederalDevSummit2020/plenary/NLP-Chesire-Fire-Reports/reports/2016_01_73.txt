Road traffic collision in Plumley Time of Incident: 12/01/2016 - 11:52 Location: Plumley Moor Road, Knutsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Knutsford and one from Northwich Three vehicles were involved in a road traffic collision on Plumley Moor Road. Two cars and a delivery van were involved in the incident, leaving one person, a man, trapped in one of the vehicles. Firefighters removed the windscreen from the vehicle to enable the casualty to be released.  He was then handed into the care of paramedics and crews made the scene safe.
