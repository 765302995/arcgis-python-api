 Fire in the open, Grappenhall Time of Incident: 06/07/2019 - 15:17 Location: Knutsford Road, Grappenhall Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Firefighters were called to deal with a fire in woodland off Knutsford Road, near the riverbank. They found two moped tyres had been set alight and used a backpack to extinguish the flames. They also notified the police due to the suspected deliberate ignition.
