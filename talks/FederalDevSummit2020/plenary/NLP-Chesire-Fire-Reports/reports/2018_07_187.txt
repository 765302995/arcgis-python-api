 Cornfield fire in Antrobus Time of Incident: 10/07/2018 - 13:57 Location: Pole Lane, Antrobus Attendance: Three fire engines are in attendance - two from Northwich and one from Lymm Cornfield fire in AntrobusFirefighters tackled a fire in a cornfield on Pole Lane in Antrobus. On arrival crews discovered that the fire involved an area measuring approximately 40 square metres. Firefighters used three hose reel jets and beaters to fight the fire. They also set up an open water supply from a nearby lake. Crews were at the scene for two hours.
