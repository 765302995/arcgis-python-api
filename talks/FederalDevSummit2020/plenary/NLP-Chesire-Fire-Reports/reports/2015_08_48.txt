Fire involving underground cables on the High Street in Nantwich Time of Incident: 20/08/2015 - 07:50 Location: High Street, Nantwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe and one from Nantwich Firefighters were called to deal with a small fire involving underground cables on the High Street outside the Crown Hotel in Nantwich. Police attended and set up a small cordon around the affected area of the High Street. An evacuation of nearby shops took place. National Grid also attended the incident.
