 Fire in the open at a primary school in Congleton Time of Incident: 09/07/2018 - 19:42 Location: Ullswater Road, Congleton Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton At around 7.40pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open at The Quinta Primary School on Ullswater Road in Congleton. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involved a hedgerow and fencing. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around an hour. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information should call police on 101, give the details via General Enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
