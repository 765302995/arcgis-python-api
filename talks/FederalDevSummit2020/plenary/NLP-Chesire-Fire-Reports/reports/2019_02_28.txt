 Tumble dryer fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 23/02/2019 - 22:14 Location: Hurdsfield Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Macclesfield and one from Bollington Fire crews attended to reports of a fire at a house on Hurdsfield Road in Macclesfield.  The fire, believed to have been caused by a tumble dryer, was extinguished using one hose reel with firefighters wearing two breathing apparatus.  The tumble dryer was then removed from the property.  A positive pressure ventilation fan was used to clear away the smoke.  Fire crews were detained at the scene for approximately 40 minutes.
