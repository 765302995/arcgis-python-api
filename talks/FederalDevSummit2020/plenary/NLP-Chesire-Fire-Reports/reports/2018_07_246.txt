 Kitchen fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/07/2018 - 16:06 Location: Dolphin Crescent, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a kitchen fire on Dolphin Crescent, Ellesmere Port. The crews found that the dishwasher was on fire and the crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet and a large fan to put out the fire. Crews then removed the dishwasher from the property. They were in attendance for approximately one hour.
