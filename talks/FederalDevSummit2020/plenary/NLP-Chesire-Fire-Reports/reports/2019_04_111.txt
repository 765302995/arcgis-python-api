 Mini bus engine fire in Chester Time of Incident: 18/04/2019 - 17:40 Location: Grosvenor Road, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester Firefighters were called to a minibus fire in Grosvenor Road in Chester. The fire was in the engine compartment and was out on arrival but was continuing to smoulder. Crews used a hose reel to cool the engine and used a thermal imaging camera to monitor the temrerature.
