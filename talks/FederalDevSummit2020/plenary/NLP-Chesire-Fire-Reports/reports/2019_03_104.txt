 Kitchen fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 07/03/2019 - 17:41 Location: The Knoll, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a kitchen fire on The Knoll, Runcorn. On arrival crews found that the fire was already extinguished so crews cleared smoke from the property using a large fan and also treated two casualties who were suffering from smoke inhalation.  Crews were at the scene for an hour and 20 minutes.
