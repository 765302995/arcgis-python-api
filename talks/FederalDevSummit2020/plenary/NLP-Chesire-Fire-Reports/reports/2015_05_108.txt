Car fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 10/05/2015 - 06:28 Location: Croft Court, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: A fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighters used a hose reel jet to tackle a car fire on Croft Court, Ellesmere Port.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
