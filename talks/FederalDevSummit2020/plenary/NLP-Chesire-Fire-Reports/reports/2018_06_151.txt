 Wooden hut fire in Appleton Time of Incident: 18/06/2018 - 16:21 Location: Barleycastle Lane, Appleton, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Lymm and one from Penketh At around 4.20pm firefighters from Lymm and Penketh were called to a fire involving a wooden hut on Barleycastle Lane in Appleton, Warrington. On arrival the crews found that the large hut was well alight and that it was a case of controlled burning that had got out of control. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to extinguish the blaze. Crews were assisted by a mechanical digger, which was used to spread some of the debris to make it easier for firefighters to put out the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for around 55 minutes.
