 Wheelie bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 06/11/2018 - 21:08 Location: Near to Bishops Way, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh At around 9.05pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the open near to Bishops Way in Widnes. The crew parked by the entrance to Bishops Way, which is known locally as The Bongs, and located the fire on foot. It was a wheelie bin fire. The bin was well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.  For advice on preventing your wheelie bins from becoming a target for arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
