Kitchen fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 23/12/2015 - 21:15 Location: Brookfield Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and two from Widnes A fire in the ground floor kitchen of a property in Runcorn was attended by firefighters. The crews, two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and a high pressure fan to clear the smoke. All occupants were accounted for at the incident.  Firefighters remained at the scene for six hours as they waited for the electricity company to isolate the power to the property.
