 Vehicle fire on M62 Time of Incident: 04/08/2018 - 23:04 Location: Junction 9, M62 Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters attended to a vehicle well alight on the hard shoulder of junction 9 on the M62.  A hose reel and one breathing apparatus were used by crews to put out the fire and then dampened down the area.  Lane one was closed by Highways Agency while firefighters put out the fire.
