 Large rubbish fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 10/03/2018 - 23:22 Location: Chapel Lane, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe and one from Nantwich Fire crews were called to Chapel Lane, Crewe, after reports of a large fire. Crews found a large amount of rubbish, including tyres, on fire. They used foam to tackle the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information then please call Cheshire Police on 101.
