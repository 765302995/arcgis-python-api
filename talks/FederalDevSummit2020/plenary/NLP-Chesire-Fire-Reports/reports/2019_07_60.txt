 Flood water in a property in Winsford Time of Incident: 19/07/2019 - 17:11 Location: Whitegate Lane, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Winsford and one from Northwich At around 5.10pm firefighters were called to a report of flooding at a property in Whitegate Lane, Winsford. On arrival the crews found that there was flood water both inside and outside the property and that everyone was accounted for. Firefighters isolated the gas and electricity supplies to the address. They then used pumps and mops to remove the flood water from the property. Firefighters were in attendance for nearly an hour-and-a-half.
