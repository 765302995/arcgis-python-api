Controlled burning put out in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 12/06/2017 - 15:08 Location: Princes Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port A small fire in a back garden on Princes Road, Ellesmere Port, was put out by fire crews as the smoke from it was affecting a nearby railway line. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was a controlled burning in a back garden.
