Cooking fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 01/12/2015 - 01:46 Location: Moorsbrook Grove, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow and three from Greater Manchester FRS Crews from Cheshire and Greater Manchester FRS attended a kitchen fire in Wilmslow. Firefighters led one casualty, a woman, to safety. She was treated at the scene by ambulance personnel. The fire involved a pan of food left cooking unattended in a flat.  Please, never leave cooking unattended.  Follow our safety advice - Kitchen fire safety
