 Field on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 24/06/2018 - 13:25 Location: Smithy Lane, Northwich. Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich. One fire engine from Birchwood. Firefighters were called to a field on fire in Smithy Lane, Northwich. The field which is approximately 150 metre square was well alight. The crews used two hose reels and beaters to extinguish the fire. They then damped the field down to prevent the fire reigniting.
