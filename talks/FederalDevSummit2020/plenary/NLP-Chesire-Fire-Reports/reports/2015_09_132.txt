Deliberate fire in Chester Time of Incident: 06/09/2015 - 18:23 Location: Sussex Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester One hose reel was used to extinguish a fire involving a large amount of rubbish on fire in a garden in Chester.  The incident is believed to be deliberate and police were requested to attend the scene.  Anyone with any information is urged to contact police on 101, or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
