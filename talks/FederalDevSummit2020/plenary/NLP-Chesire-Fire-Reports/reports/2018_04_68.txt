 Fire at a convenience shop in Runcorn Time of Incident: 17/04/2018 - 22:45 Location: Village Square, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Runcorn and one from Widnes At around 10.45pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire at a convenience shop in Village Square in Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered that the fire involving an industrial freezer had been extinguished by the shop's staff. Firefighters used a large fan to clear the smoke in the shop that was caused by the fire. They were at the scene for around an hour.
