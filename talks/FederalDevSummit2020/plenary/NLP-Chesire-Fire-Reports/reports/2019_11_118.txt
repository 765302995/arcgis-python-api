 Flooding at a house in Clotton Time of Incident: 07/11/2019 - 21:39 Location: High Street, Clotton, near Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Tarporley At approximately 9.40pm firefighters from Tarporley were dispatched to reports of flooding at a residential property in Clotton. On arrival the crew found flood water was coming through the front door of the building. Firefighters used a large pump to clear the water from the property and were detained at the scene for around one hour 45 minutes.
