Female rescued from embankment in Stockton Heath Time of Incident: 08/10/2015 - 23:49 Location: Ellesmere Road, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath, two fire engines and a rescue boat from Warrington, and one fire engine plus a rope rescue unit from Knutsford.  Firefighters were called to a report of someone being stuck on a canal embankment off Ellesmere Road in Stockton Heath.   Fire crews rescued one female using height safety equipment and she was then passed into the care of paramedics. Firefighters were at the scene for approximatley twenty five minutes.
