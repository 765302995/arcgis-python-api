Chimney fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 02/04/2017 - 14:50 Location: Chapel Close, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Firefighters tackled a chimney fire, believed to have been caused by a bird's nest, on Chapel Close. They inspected the chimney using a ladder and height safety equipment, then tackled the fire from the  grate.  Always follow our chimney safety advice.
