 Camper van on fire in Sandbach Time of Incident: 07/01/2019 - 01:29 Location: High Street, Sandbach. Attendance:  1 fire engine attended from Sandbach Firefighters were called to a fire involving a camper van on the car park of the Old Hall Hotel in High Street, Sandbach. Crews used  two hose reels and breathing apparatus. They then damped down the vehicle using one hose reel. A nearby car suffered minor damage from the fire. The fire is thought to have been deliberate and police have been informed.
