Chimney fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 24/10/2015 - 21:28 Location: Bostock Road, Winsford. Attendance: A fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to a house fire on Bostock Road, Winsford.  When they arrived crews found that incident involved a fire in a chimney. Crews tackled the fire from the grate. They also carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving.
