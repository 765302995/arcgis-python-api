 Skip fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 26/06/2018 - 00:01 Location: French Street, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh and one from Widnes Fire crews were called to a skip fire on French Street, Widnes. The skip was well alight on arrival of the crews and they used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the fire then please call Cheshire Police on 101.
