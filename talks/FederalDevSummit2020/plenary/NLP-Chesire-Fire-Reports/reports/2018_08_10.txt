 Wagon collision in Bickerton Time of Incident: 29/08/2018 - 13:14 Location: Sandy Lane, Bickerton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe and one from Ellesmere Port Wagon collision in Bickerton Firefighters were called to Sandy Lane in Bickerton following reports of a wagon having collided with a tree.  Crews set up polybooms to contain diesel which had leaked from a ruptured tank on the vehicle.  They also diluted and washed away further diesel from the road surface and made the scene safe.
