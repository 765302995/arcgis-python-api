Fire in toilet in Frodsham Time of Incident: 08/04/2016 - 01:05 Location: The Ridgeway, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Frodsham Firefighters were called to a fire involving an extractor fan in a single storey toilet cubicle building of wooden construction. One hose reel was used to extinguish the fire.
