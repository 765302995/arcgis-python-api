 Vehicle fire on the M62 near Warrington Time of Incident: 27/02/2018 - 14:18 Location: M62 Eastbound near to junction 10 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington, one fire engine from Birchwood and one from Leigh attended. Vehicle fire on the M62 near WarringtonFirefighters from Warrington, Birchwood and Leigh attended reports of a vehicle fire on the M62 eastbound near to junction 10.  The fire involved a transit van which was well alight. The team used two hose reel jets and two breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire.  Two lanes of the eastbound carriageway were closed while firefighters worked at the scene. The carriageway is now clear..
