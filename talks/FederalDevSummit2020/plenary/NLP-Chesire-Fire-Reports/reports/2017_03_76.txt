Bonfire getting out of control Time of Incident: 20/03/2017 - 17:23 Location: Brancepeth Court, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on Brancepeth Court, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that this was a bonfire. However, as it was getting close to a neighbouring property it was put out with a hose reel jet.
