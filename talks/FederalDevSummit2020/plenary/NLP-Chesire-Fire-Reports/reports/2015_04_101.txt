Shed fire in Middlewich Time of Incident: 17/04/2015 - 14:19 Location: The Green, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich and one from Winsford A fire involving a garden shed was extinguished using one hose reel. The outbuilding was well alight on arrival of the crew. The cause of the fire was accidental as a result of waste paper being burnt in the garden. Burning rubbish / bonfires and the law
