Bonfire in Frodsham Time of Incident: 05/11/2015 - 21:36 Location: Overton Close, Frodsham. Attendance: A fire engine from Frodsham Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on Overton Close, Frodsham.  Crews used a hose reel jet to put out a bonfire in the garden of the property.
