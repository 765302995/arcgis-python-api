Transit van on fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 21/05/2016 - 01:02 Location: Brunner Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes A transit van was on fire in Brunner Road. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which had started inside the vehicle.  If you saw anything suspicious in this area please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
