 Four wheelie bin fires in Warrington Time of Incident: 15/01/2019 - 02:42 Location: Greenwood Crescent, Warrington Attendance:  One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews extinguished four wheelie bins on Greenwood Crescent in Warrington.  Crews used one hose reel before dampening down the area.  The fires are believed to have been deliberate. Anyone with information should contact police on 101.
