Fire in the open in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 31/01/2016 - 18:26 Location: Festival Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port A high pressure jet was used by firefighters to put out a fire in the open in Ellesmere Port. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
