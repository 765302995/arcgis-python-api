 Tree roots fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 22/07/2018 - 21:30 Location: Queens Park Drive, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 9.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire involving a tree on Queens Park Drive in Crewe. On arrival the crew discovered the roots of the tree on fire. Firefighters used an extension ladder to access the fire over a fence and extinguished it with a hose reel jet. They were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
