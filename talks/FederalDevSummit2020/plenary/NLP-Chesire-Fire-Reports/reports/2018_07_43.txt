 Grass and bushes fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/07/2018 - 17:30 Location: High Gates Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington At around 5.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open in High Gates Close, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered approximately 10 square metres of grass and bushes alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire using water and were in attendance for around 40 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
