 Tumble dryer fire at a nursery in Davenham, near Northwich Time of Incident: 11/10/2019 - 17:41 Location: Regent Way, Davenham, near Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich At around 5.40pm firefighters were called to a report of a smell of smoke at a nursery in Regent Way, Davenham, near Northwich.  On arrival a smoke alarm was sounding and a fire was discovered in a laundry room at the nursery.  The nursery was evacuated and the electricity supply to the laundry room was isolated.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire involving a tumble dryer with a hose reel jet.  The tumble dryer was then moved outside of the building.  A large fan was used to clear smoke from the nursery.  The fire is believed to have been caused by an overheated drum.  Firefighters were in attendance for around 50 minutes.  For tumble dryer fire safety advice visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/tumble-dryer-fire-safety.
