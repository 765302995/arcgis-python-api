Road traffic collision in Stockton Heath Time of Incident: 15/05/2016 - 01:02 Location: Ackers Road, Stockton Heath Attendance: One fire engine from Stockton Heath and one from Warrington Two vehicles were involved in a Stockton Heath road traffic collision leaving one casualty trapped. A van and a car were involved in the incident, leaving a casualty trapped in the car. Firefighters had to use a winch to move the car to enable them to extricate the casualty by removing part of the vehicle's side column. The casualty was then handed into the care of paramedics.
