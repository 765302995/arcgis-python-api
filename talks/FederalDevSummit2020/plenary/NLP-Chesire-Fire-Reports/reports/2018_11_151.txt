 Electrical fire in Chester Time of Incident: 05/11/2018 - 20:14 Location: Liverpool Road, Chester. Attendance: Three fire engines attended from Chester and Powey Lane. Firefighters were called to reports of a fir in the grounds of the Countess of Chester Hospital. On arrival the team found the had been caused by an underground electrical fault causing the cables to smoulder.  Crews isolated the site and a cordon has been set up as a precautionary measure.
