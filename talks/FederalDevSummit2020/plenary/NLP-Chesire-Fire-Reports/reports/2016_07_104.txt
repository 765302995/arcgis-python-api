Cylinder exploded outside farm building in Chester Time of Incident: 13/07/2016 - 14:44 Location: Badgers Rake Lane, Puddington, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester A cylinder exploded outside a farm building in Puddington, near Chester. It caused a fire to a nearby shed. Firefighters used two hose reel jets to tackle the fire and cool the cylinder.
