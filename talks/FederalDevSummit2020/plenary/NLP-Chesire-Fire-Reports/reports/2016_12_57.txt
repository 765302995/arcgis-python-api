Sheds on fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 21/12/2016 - 12:15 Location: Warrington Road, Runcorn Attendance: Three fire engines from Widnes, Runcorn and Penketh A number of sheds on an allotment were well alight with fire in Runcorn with cylinders suspected to be inside. Firefighters on the scene turned a hose reel jet on the blaze whilst crews established a water supply from a nearby hydrant. After establishing a steady supply of water, crews set up a second hose reel jet which they used to help tackle the blaze and dampen down the area before cooling the cylinders.
