 Wheelie bin fire in Croft Time of Incident: 19/08/2019 - 20:53 Location: Lady Lane, Croft, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood Firefighters from Birchwood used a  hose reel jet to extinguish two wheelie bins which had been deliberately set alight at an unused address on Lady Lane, Croft.  Crews were at the scene for around 15 minutes.  Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 490440.  For more information about how to prevent wheelie bin fires visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety
