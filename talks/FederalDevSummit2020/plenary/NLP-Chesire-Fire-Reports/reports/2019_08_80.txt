 Motorcycle fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 19/08/2019 - 23:29 Location: Dundalk Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes Widnes firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish a motorcycle which had been abandoned and set on fire on Dundalk Road.  Anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML490451.
