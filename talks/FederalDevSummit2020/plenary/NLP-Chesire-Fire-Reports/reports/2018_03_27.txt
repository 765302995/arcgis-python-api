 Two small fires in Warrington Time of Incident: 26/03/2018 - 18:07 Location: Radley Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to extinguish two small fires on Radley Lane, Warrington. Crews put out the fires using a fire extinguisher. Two people, who had tried to put out the fires themselves, were treated at the scene by paramedics for burns.
