 Firefighters extract woman from car following collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 01/02/2018 - 11:22 Location: Woodhouse Lane, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield and one from Congleton Firefighters extract woman from car following collision in Macclesfield Fire crews extracted a woman from a car following a road traffic collision on Woodhouse Lane in Macclesfield.  Two fire engines from Macclesfield and Congleton attended the collision to find the car had turned on its side.  The woman was then handed over to paramedics.
