 Tree fire open in Poynton Time of Incident: 18/05/2018 - 11:09 Location: London Road South, Poynton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Poynton Shortly after 10am firefighters from Poynton attended reports of a fire on London Road South. Crews attended the scene and found that the fire involved a row of conifers which were close to a nearby building. The team used a hose reel jet and berating apparatus to extinguish the fire; they then used a thermal imaging camera to examine the building. The team were in attendance for approximately two hours.
