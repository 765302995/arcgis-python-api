 Skip fire, Widnes Time of Incident: 06/10/2018 - 16:46 Location: Kershaw Street, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters tackled a skip on fire near a business unit in Kershaw Street. It was partially alight and they used a single hose reel to extinguish the flames, and notified the police as they suspected a deliberate ignition. They were in attendance for around half an hour.
