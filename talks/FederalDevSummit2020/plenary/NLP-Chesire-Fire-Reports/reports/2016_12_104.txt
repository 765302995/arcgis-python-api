Chimney fire in Chester Time of Incident: 11/12/2016 - 16:18 Location: Smithy Lane, Brown Knoll, Broxton, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Malpas and one from Chester A chimney fire on Smithy Lane, Brown Knowl was tackled by firefighters. A high pressure hose and chimney rods were used to gain access to the chimney breast and ladders were used to work at height from the roof. The fire was put out and bricks removed from the rear of the chimney to check there was no secondary chimney access. The chimney breast was monitored with a thermal imaging camera until it reached a safe temperature.  Always follow our chimney safety advice.
