 Fire in a garden in Frodsham Time of Incident: 03/07/2018 - 18:48 Location: Aston Lane, Frodsham Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn At around 6.45pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a garden on Aston Lane in Frodsham. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning that had got out of control. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and a rake to turn over woodchip. They were in attendance for around an hour.
