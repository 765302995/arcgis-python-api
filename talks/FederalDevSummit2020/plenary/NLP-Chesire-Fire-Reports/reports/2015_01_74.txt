Lorry overturned on A483 in Chester Time of Incident: 12/01/2015 - 12:28 Location: A55/A483, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from chester Firefighters, along with police and ambulance, were called to a report of a heavy goods vehicle overturned on Wrexham Road at the Posthouse Roundabout.  Nobody was trapped in the vehicle.  Firefighters cleared diesel spillage from the road before leaving the incident.   Access to the A483 from the roundabout is currently closed, and will be closed until approximately 4pm or 5pm today, due to the diesel spillage.
