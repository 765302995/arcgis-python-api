Fire in Macclesfield restaurant Time of Incident: 21/06/2015 - 14:18 Location: Commercial Road, Macclesfield Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Crews were called to a report of a fire at Temptation in Macclesfield.  A wok had been left unattended on a cooker and was involved in fire.  The fire was put out using a fire blanked and firefighters used a high pressure fan to clear the building of smoke.
