Car fire on M56 near to junction 14 Time of Incident: 02/09/2017 - 12:10 Location: M56 westbound (near to junction 14) Attendance: One fire engine from Powey Lane and one from Ellesmere Port A car was well alight on the hard shoulder of the M56 westbound (near to junction 14) and there was lots of smoke in the area, so the westbound carriageway was closed so that firefighters could tackle the fire.   Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. One firefighter wore breathing apparatus.
