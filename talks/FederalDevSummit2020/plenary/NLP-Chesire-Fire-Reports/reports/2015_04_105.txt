Furniture on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 16/04/2015 - 21:09 Location: Hardy Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one fire engine from Stockton Heath. One hose reel was used to extinguish a fire involving a large pile of furniture at the side of a property in Warrington.  No damage was caused to the property and the crews used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots.
