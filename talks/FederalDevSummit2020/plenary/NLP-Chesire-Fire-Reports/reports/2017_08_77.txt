Road traffic collision in Middlewich Time of Incident: 17/08/2017 - 09:47 Location: Brereton Lane, Middlewich Attendance: Vehicles from Middlewich, Winsford and Holmes Chapel attended Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision with two people trapped in a vehicle. On arrival the crew found the collision involving one car and three people. One person had self rescued themselves and two casualties were release from the car by firefighters. Paramedics attended to deal with the casualties and police closed the road while the incident was dealt with.
