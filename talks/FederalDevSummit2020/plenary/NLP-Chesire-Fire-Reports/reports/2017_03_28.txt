Chimney fire in Adlington Time of Incident: 26/03/2017 - 19:20 Location: Cawley Lane, Adlington Attendance: One fire engine from Poynton Firefighters were called to a fire involving a chimney in Adlington. On arrival the crews found smoke issuing from the chimney pot at roof level. Using specialist chimney rods the firefighters attacked the fire and extinguished it. A check of the roof space was also completed using a thermal imaging camera before the crews left. It is believed that the fire may have been caused by a blockage in the chimney.
