 Fire at a commercial property in Widnes Time of Incident: 16/07/2019 - 22:44 Location: Queensbury Way, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes and one from Penketh Fire crews were called to a fire at a commercial property on Queensbury Way, Widnes. The fire was at the back of the building but had broken through the ventilation ducting and spread to the inside of the premises. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They then cleared a large amount of smoke from the building using a large fan. Crews were at the scene for two hours and 40 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information about the incident please contact Cheshire Police.
