 Skip fire at recycling centre in Chester Time of Incident: 12/12/2018 - 13:50 Location: Bumpers Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester At around 1.50pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a recycling centre on Bumpers Lane in Chester. On arrival at Chester Household Waste Recycling Centre the crew discovered that a large skip was well alight. Firefighters extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
