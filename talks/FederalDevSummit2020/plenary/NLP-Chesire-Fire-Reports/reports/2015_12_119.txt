Bin compound fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 02/12/2015 - 15:54 Location: Beech Farm Drive, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters responded to reports of a bin compound on fire in Macclesfield.  Fire crews used two hose reel jets to tackle the blaze and then ventilated the compound.  This fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with any information is urged to call police on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
