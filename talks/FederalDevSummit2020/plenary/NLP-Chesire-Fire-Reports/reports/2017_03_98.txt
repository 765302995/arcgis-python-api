Road traffic collision in on the M6 between junctions 17 and 16 Time of Incident: 15/03/2017 - 22:53 Location: M6 south between junctions 17 and 16 Attendance: One fire engine from Sandbach and one from Crewe Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision with one person trapped. The collision involved a van and a heavy goods vehicle and one casualty was extricated from a vehicle and handed over to the ambulance service.
