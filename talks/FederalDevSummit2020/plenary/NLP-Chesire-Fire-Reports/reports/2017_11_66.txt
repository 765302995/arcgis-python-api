Road traffi collision in Winwick Time of Incident: 12/11/2017 - 21:45 Location: Hollins Lane, Winwick Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Greater Manchester Fire Service Fire crews were called to a road traffic collision between three cars on Hollins Lane, Winwick. One person was trapped in a vehicle and crews used cutting equipment to remove a roof of a vehicle to extract the casualty who was then handed into the care of paramedics.
