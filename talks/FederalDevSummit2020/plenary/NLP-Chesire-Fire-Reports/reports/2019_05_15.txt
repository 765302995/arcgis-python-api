 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/05/2019 - 18:12 Location: Maple Grove, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly after 6pm firefighters from Warrington were called to reports of a fire in the open on Maple Grove.  On arrival crews discovered that eight wheelie bins had been put together and deliberately set alight.  Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around twenty minutes  Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 413615, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.   For advice on preventing your wheelie bins from being targeted by arsonists visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/wheelie-bin-fire-safety.
