 Swans rescued from overflow pipe in Widnes Time of Incident: 14/10/2019 - 12:01 Location: Canal off Johnsons Lane, Widnes Attendance: Three fire engines attended, one from Widnes, one from Lymm and one from Knutsford Swans rescued from overflow pipe in WidnesFirefighters were called to help the RSPCA rescue two swans in the canal off Johnsons Lane in Widnes after they became stranded in an overflow pipe.  Two firefighters in swift water kit entered the water, which was about a foot deep, alongside an RSPCA worker to look for the swans.  They used a gas monitor to ensure that the area was safe and managed to locate and rescued one of the swans.  They then used a raft to rescue the second swan.  Both swans were handed over to the care of the RSPCA.
