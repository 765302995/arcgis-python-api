Cooker fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 14/02/2015 - 19:29 Location: London Road, Alderley Edge. Attendance: A fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a property on London Road, Alderley Edge.   When they arrived crews found that they fire had involved the chimney of a commercial cooker. The fire was out when they arrived.
