 Building fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 17/07/2018 - 04:56 Location: Deepdale, Widnes Attendance: Two fire engines attended - one from Widnes and one from Penketh. Shortly before 5am firefighters were called to reports of a fire at an address on Deepdale in Widnes. Crews from Widnes and Penketh attended and on arrival they discovered an outbuilding at the rear of the property was well alight, and there was risk that the fire may spread to the house. The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately one hour. The cause of the fire is currently under investigation and anyone with any information is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML126270. Information can also be passed anonymously, via Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111.
