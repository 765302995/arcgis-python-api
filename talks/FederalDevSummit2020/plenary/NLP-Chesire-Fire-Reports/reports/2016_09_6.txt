Bins and sofa on fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/09/2016 - 23:54 Location: Quebec Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington Three bins and a sofa were set on fire in Quebec Road. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the blaze.  The fire is believed to have been started deliberately.  Anyone with any information is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101. Arson costs lives.
