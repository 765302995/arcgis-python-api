Two people trapped after road traffic collision in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 11/10/2017 - 13:09 Location: Melrose Way, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow, one fire engine from Poynton and one fire engine from Congleton. Firefighters attended a road traffic collision between two vehicles. One man was trapped in an overturned vehicle, a second man was trapped in a separate vehicle. The road was closed in both directions by police while the crews removed the casualties. Both men were taken to hospital by North West Ambulance Service. Crews were at the scene for approximately 50 minutes.
