 Kitchen fire in Mobberley Time of Incident: 14/04/2019 - 15:10 Location: Town Lane, Mobberley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow and one from Lymm Fire crews were called to a kitchen fire on Town Lane, Mobberley. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. They then used a large fan to clear smoke from the property. The cause of the fire was a faulty toaster. Crews were at the scene for an hour and 20 minutes.
