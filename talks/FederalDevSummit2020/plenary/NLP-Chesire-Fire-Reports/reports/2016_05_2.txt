Fridge fire in Sainsburys Time of Incident: 29/05/2016 - 21:41 Location: Sainsburys, Venables Road, Northwich. Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich and one fire engine from Winsford Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at Sainsburys on Venables Road, Northwich.  When they arrived they found smoke issuing from a refrigeration unit.  Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel and a covering jet to deal with the incident. A cordon was also established while  awaiting arrival of a refrigeration engineer. Crews were engaged on scene over two hours.
