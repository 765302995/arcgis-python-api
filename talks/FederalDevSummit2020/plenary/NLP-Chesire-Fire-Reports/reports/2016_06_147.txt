Car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 04/06/2016 - 22:44 Location: Gleadmere, Widnes. Attendance: A fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to a report of a fire off Gleadmere, Widnes.  When they arrived crews found a car on fire in a field. Hose reel jets were used to put the fire out.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately.. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Constabulary on 101.
