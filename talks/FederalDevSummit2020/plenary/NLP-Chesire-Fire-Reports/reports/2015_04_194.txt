Building fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 06/04/2015 - 00:45 Location: Warmingham Road in Crewe Attendance: Fire engines from Crewe, Nantwich, Sandbach and Middlewich. Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a commercial building off Warmingham Road in Crewe.  Crews fought the fire with main water and hose reel jets to bring the blaze under control.  Following a fire investigation, the cause of the blaze was found to have been deliberate.  Combustible materials within the building had been set alight.
