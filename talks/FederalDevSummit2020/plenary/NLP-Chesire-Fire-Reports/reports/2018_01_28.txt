 Woman rescued in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/01/2018 - 11:56 Location: Groby Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe and one from Chester Firefighters were called to rescue a woman who had fallen and broken her ankle whilst out walking off Groby Road, Crewe.  The casualty was half a mile down a muddy path and firefighters carried with a stretcher and then handed her into the care of paramedics. Crews were detained for 50 minutes.
