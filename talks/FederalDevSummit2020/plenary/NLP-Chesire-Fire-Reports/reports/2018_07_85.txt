 Building fire in Disley Time of Incident: 21/07/2018 - 13:41 Location: Mudhirst Lane, Disley Attendance: Three fire engines attended - One from Poynton, on from Derbyshire and one from Greater Manchester. Firefighters from Poynton attended reports of an electrical fire at a building on Mudhirst Lane in Disley. The fire, which is believed to have been caused by an electrical fault, was out on arrival but crews used a large fan to ventilate the property. The team were detained at the scene for approximately 40 minutes.
