Firework thrown into a shop Time of Incident: 24/10/2016 - 19:29 Location: Ince Lane, Chester. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a pharmacy in Elton Shopping Centre on Ince Lane, Chester.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire caused by a firework that had been thrown into the shop. Dis you see anything - if so call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
