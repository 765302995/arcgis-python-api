Skip fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/05/2015 - 15:26 Location: Green Lane, Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine from Warrington Firefighters used a main water jet to put out a skip fire on Green Lane, Warrington.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If anyone has information that could help find those responsible they should call Crimstoppers on 0800 555 111.
