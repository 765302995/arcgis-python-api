Firework incident Time of Incident: 05/11/2015 - 22:28 Location: Percival Road, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: A fire engine from Ellesmere Port Firefighers were called to a report of an incident on Percival Road, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that a firework had gone through the window of a ground floor flat.  There was no fire but crews had to use a large fan to clear smoke from the property.  The incident was handed over to Cheshire Police.
