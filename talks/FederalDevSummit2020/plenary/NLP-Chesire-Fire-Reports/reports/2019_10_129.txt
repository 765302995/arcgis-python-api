 One vehicle collision in Chowley Time of Incident: 06/10/2019 - 15:20 Location: Whitchurch Road, Chowley, Near Chester Attendance: Three fire engines attended from Malpas, Chester and Ellesmere Port At around 3.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a collision on Whitchurch Road, Chowley.  On arrival the crews found one person was medically trapped in a convertible car following a one vehicle collision.  Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to release the casualty, they were then passed to the care of paramedics.  Crews were in attendance for approximately 90 minutes.
