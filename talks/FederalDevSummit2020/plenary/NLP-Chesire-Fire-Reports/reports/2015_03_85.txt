Car on its roof in Runcorn Time of Incident: 13/03/2015 - 19:36 Location: A56 / A533, Preston Brook, Runcorn. Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn and one from Frodsham A car was on its roof down an embankment. Nobody was trapped, but one casualty was wounded. The casualty was helped by paramedics. Firefighters made the vehicle safe.
