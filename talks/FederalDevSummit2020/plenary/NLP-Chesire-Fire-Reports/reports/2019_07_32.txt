 Trapped deer in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 24/07/2019 - 18:21 Location: Byrons Lane, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield. One fire engine attended from Bollinton.One fire engine from Lymm attended. Firefighters were called a canal banking off Byrons Lane in Macclesfield following reports of a deer trapped in fencing. Crews liaised with the RSPCA and a vet to work out how to free the animal. Crews deployed their raft in the canal to take the vet to the deer so it could be assessed and sedated so the animal could be rescued safely. As they approached, the deer managed to free itself.
