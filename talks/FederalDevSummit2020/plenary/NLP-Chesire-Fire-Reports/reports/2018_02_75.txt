 Deep fat fryer fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 14/02/2018 - 15:13 Location: Loxdale Drive, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port Firefighters attended a fire in a chip shop in Loxdale Drive, Great Sutton, Ellesmere Port. Crews used four breathing apparatus and two hose reels to extinguish the fire which was in a deep fat fryer. They also used a large fan to disperse the smoke.
