 Collision in Chester Time of Incident: 06/09/2019 - 21:34 Location: Gorse Stacks, Chester. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Chester. Firefighters were called to a one vehicle collision in Gorse Stacks, Chester. One female casualty was being treated by paramedics while crews stemmed a petrol leak using foam.  As a precaution they had firefighters standing by with a hose reel and foam line. Crews removed the petrol tank to stop the leaking and isolated the battery.  They then righted the car which had been on its roof.
