Van fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 15/07/2017 - 23:47 Location: The Tithings, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn Firefighters were called to a transit van which was on fire on The Tithings, Runcorn. The van, which was adjacent to a garage door which was damaged by the fire, was well alight when crews arrived. Firefighters, one wearing a breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The crews then checked the adjacent garage for any signs of fire using a thermal imaging camera. The fire was believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
