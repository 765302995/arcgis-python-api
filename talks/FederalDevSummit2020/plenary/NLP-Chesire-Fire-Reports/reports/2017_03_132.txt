Rubbish fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 09/03/2017 - 19:10 Location: The Bennett Recreation Ground in Warrington. Attendance: A fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a report of a fire on The Bennett Recreation Ground in Warrington.  When they arrived crews found a large amount of rubbish on fire. They used a hose reel to put the fire out.  It is believed that the fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Cheshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
