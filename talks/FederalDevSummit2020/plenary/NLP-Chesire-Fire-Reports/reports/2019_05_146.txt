 Car fire, Wrenbury Time of Incident: 12/05/2019 - 00:19 Location: Pinsley Green Road, Wrenbury Attendance: One fire engine from Nantwich Car fire, WrenburyFirefighters turned out to deal with a Land Rover that had been set on fire in the gateway of a field near Wrenbury. They used breathing apparatus and a single hose reel to put the fire out, and a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots on the vehicle and surrounding area.  They notified Cheshire police due to the deliberate ignition, anyone with information is asked to call 101 quoting IML 398589.
