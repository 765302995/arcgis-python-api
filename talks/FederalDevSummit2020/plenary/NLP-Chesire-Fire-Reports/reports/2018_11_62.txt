 Fire in the men's toilets at a kitchen manufacturing company in Winsford Time of Incident: 19/11/2018 - 11:33 Location: Road One, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Northwich and one from Winsford Just after 11.30am firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the men's toilets at the Howdens Joinery kitchen manufacturing company on Road One in Winsford. A full evacuation of the premises took place and the electrics were isolated. Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Fire crews then used two large high pressure fans to clear smoke from the toilets and the warehouse area of the premises. Firefighters were in attendance for just over an hour. The cause of the fire is believed to have been an electrical fault on a hand dryer.
