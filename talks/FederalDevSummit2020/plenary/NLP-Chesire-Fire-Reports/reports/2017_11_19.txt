Dog rescued from river ledge in Northwich Time of Incident: 23/11/2017 - 19:04 Location: Yarwood Close, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich, the rope rescue unit from Lymm and animal rescue team from Bollington The animal rescue unit rescued a dog which had fallen onto a ledge above the river at Hunts Lock in Northwich. Two rescue technicians went into the water on a rescue sled and were able to remove the dog from the ledge. They took it back to land and handed it over to its owner.
