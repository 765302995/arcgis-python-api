Door on fire inside a disused building in Winsford Time of Incident: 05/07/2016 - 18:29 Location: Smokehall Lane, Winsford. Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford fire station and one fire engine from Frodsham fire station A wooden sliding door was on fire inside a disused commercial premises on Smokehall Lane in Winsford.  Firefighters extinguished the fire using one hose reel jet.
