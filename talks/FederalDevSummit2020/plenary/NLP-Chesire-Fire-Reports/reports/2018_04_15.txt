 Kitchen fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 26/04/2018 - 19:03 Location: Lawton Street, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 7.05pm firefighters were called to a domestic building fire on Lawton Street in Crewe.  On arrival the crew found that the fire involved a kitchen in a ground floor flat that was caused by food being left unattended on a hob.  Two firefighters wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.The crew then used a large high pressure fan to clear the smoke.  Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour.  For cooking safety advice visit Kitchen fire safety
