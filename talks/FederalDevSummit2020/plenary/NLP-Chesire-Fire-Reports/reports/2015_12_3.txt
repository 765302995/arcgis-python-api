Tanker overturned just off A41 Time of Incident: 30/12/2015 - 11:13 Location: Just off A41, Tushingham, Whitchurch (near to Bluebell Inn) Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford, one from Malpas and the Environmental Protection Unit. Firefighters are currently assisting at the scene of road traffic collision just off the A41 in Tushingham, Whitchurch, where a tanker has overturned.   The tanker was carrying molasses (a treacle like substance) which has leaked onto the carriageway. The spillage has been contained, however the incident is affecting the A41 so please avoid the area if possible.  This incident will be updated when more information is available.
