Kitchen fire in Chester Time of Incident: 02/10/2015 - 16:53 Location: Springfields, Mickle Trafford, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester and one from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in the kitchen of a Mickle Trafford property. When they arrived they found the fire had been put out by a neighbour using a fire blanket. The kitchen was left filled with smoke and the crews, one firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a high pressure fan to ventilate the room. Everyone was accounted for.
