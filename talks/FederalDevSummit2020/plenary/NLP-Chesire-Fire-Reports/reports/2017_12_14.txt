House fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 26/12/2017 - 04:16 Location: Ruthin Court, Ellesmere Port Attendance: Two fire engines from Ellesmere Port and one from Powey Lane Fire crews were called to a fire in a bedroom on Ruthin Court, Ellesmere Port. The fire was located on the first floor of a two storey house. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and then a large fan to clear smoke from the property. Three people self evacuated from the property and one person was rescued by firefighters. Two casualties were treated for smoke inhalation before being handed into the care of paramedics. The fire was accidental and caused by smoking materials. Crews were in attendance for three hours. Safety tips for smokers
