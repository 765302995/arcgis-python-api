 Garage fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 07/05/2018 - 22:42 Location: Shipton Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Fire crews were called to a garage fire on Shipton Close, Warrington. The fire had started on a fence and spread to the garage. Crews used a hose reel jet and a thermal imaging camera to put out the fire.
