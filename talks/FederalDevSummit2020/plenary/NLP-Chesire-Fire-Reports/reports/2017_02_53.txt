Garage fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 21/02/2017 - 23:15 Location: Gladstone Street, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines from Winsford A garage attached to a house was heavily involved in a fire on Gladstone Street.  Firefighters, one wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel and a covering jet to tackle the blaze. A high pressure fan was used to clear smoke. Everyone from the property was accounted for and a fire investigation is being carried out to determine the cause of the blaze. Crews were at the scene for around an hour and a half.
