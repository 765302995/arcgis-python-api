 Small fire in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 25/07/2018 - 16:57 Location: Battery Lane, Wilmslow. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Shortly before 5pm firefighters from Wilmslow were called to reports of a of a small fire on Battery Lane. On arrival crews found that  a small rubbish fire had spread to a nearby fence. The team used a water back pack to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately 25 minutes.
