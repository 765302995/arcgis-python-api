Collision in Crewe Time of Incident: 23/12/2015 - 20:48 Location: Victoria Centre, Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of a collision near the Victoria Centre, Crewe.  When they arrived they found that the incident involved two cars. One woman was trapped.  Crews used special cutting equipment to free her. She was then passed to the care of the ambulance service.  The road was closed while the incident was dealt with.
