 Chimney fire in pub in Faddiley Time of Incident: 27/04/2018 - 18:06 Location: Wrexham Road, Faddiley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich and one from Crewe Fire crews were called to a chimney fire in a pub on Wrexham Road, Faddiley. On arrival crews found the first floor of the pub was smoke logged and then carried out an evacuation of the pub. Crews then used two thermal imaging cameras, chimney rods, ladders and water to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for three hours.
