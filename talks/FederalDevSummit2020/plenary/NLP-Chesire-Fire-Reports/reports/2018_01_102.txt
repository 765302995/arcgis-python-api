 Cows rescued from canal in Marston Time of Incident: 06/01/2018 - 17:18 Location: Hall Drive, Marston Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich, the animal rescue unit from Bollington and the rope rescue unit from Lymm Firefighters were called to rescue three cows which were in the canal on Hall Drive, Marston. The fire service were in attendance for approximately one hour and 40 minutes, ushering cows out of the water and back into a field.
