 Shed fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 28/06/2018 - 15:40 Location: Haley Road North, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and Widnes Fire crews dampened down a shed which was alight at a property on Haley Road North in Warrington.  By dampening it down it meant the shed was fully extinguished and the area was safe.
