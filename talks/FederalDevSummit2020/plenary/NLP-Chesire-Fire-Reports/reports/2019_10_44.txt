 Chimney fire in Audlem Time of Incident: 24/10/2019 - 14:15 Location: Longhill Lane, Audlem Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Fire crews attended to reports of a chimney fire at an address in Audlem.  Firefighters initially checked the loft space using the thermal imaging camera.  Chimney rods were then used to extinguish the fire before making a thorough check of the attic using the thermal imaging camera.
