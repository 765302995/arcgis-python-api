 Fire in the open in Northwich Time of Incident: 22/06/2018 - 09:29 Location: Winnington Street, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At around 9.30am firefighters were called to a report of a fire in Winnington Street, Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a case of controlled burning. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire in the open and then damped the area down to make it safe. Police were in attendance to deal with traffic control on a congested road as firefighters worked at the scene. Firefighters were in attendance for around an hour-and-a-half.
