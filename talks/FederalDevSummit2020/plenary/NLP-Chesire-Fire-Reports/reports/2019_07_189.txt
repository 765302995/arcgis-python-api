 Hedge fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 30/06/2019 - 18:05 Location: Sandown Road, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Shortly after 6pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire involving a hedge near to a domestic property on Sandown Road in Crewe. The fire was out on arrival. Firefighters damped down the hedge with a hose reel jet to make it safe and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
