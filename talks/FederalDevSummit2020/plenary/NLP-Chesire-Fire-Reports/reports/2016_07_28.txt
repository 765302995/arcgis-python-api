Motorcycle incident near Chester Time of Incident: 24/07/2016 - 15:14 Location: Little Rake Lane,Chorlton-by-Backford, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester A motorcycle had left the road and gone into a field, leaving the rider trapped underneath the machine. Firefighters stabilised the rider and gave him first aid while they waited for the air ambulance to arrive. Liaising with medical staff they assisted with the air ambulance evacuation.
