 Small fire in Plumley Time of Incident: 23/08/2018 - 21:07 Location: Ascol Drive, Plumley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Knutsford Firefighters were called to Ascol Drive, Plumley, to a small fire in a field. Crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which is believed to have been started deliberately. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police.
