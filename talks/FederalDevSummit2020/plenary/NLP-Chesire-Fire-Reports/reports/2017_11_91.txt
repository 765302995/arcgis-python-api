Bin fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 08/11/2017 - 18:44 Location: Coppenhall Lane, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine from Crewe A number of pizza boxes in a bin were deliberately set on fire in Coppenhall Lane in Crewe. Firefighters used a water back pack to damp down the boxes to makes sure they didn't reignite. Police have been informed.
