Caravan fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 21/11/2015 - 09:46 Location: Mow Lane, Astbury, Congleton. Attendance: A fire engine from Congleton and a fire engine from Staffordshire. Firefighers were called to a report of a fire on Mow Lane, Congleton.  When they arrived they found a blaze involving a caravan.  Crews wearing breathing apparatus used hose reel jets to tackle the fire, which also involved two small LPG cylinders.   Two casualties were treated at the scene after inhaling smoke.
