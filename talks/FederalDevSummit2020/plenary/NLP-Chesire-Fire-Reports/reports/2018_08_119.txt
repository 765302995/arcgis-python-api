 Small fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 07/08/2018 - 21:22 Location: Moss Bank, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine from Winsford Fire crews were called to Moss Bank, Winsford, after a report of a fire. Crews found a controlled fire in a back garden was giving off a large amount of smoke so they used a hose reel jet to extinguish it.
