Firefighters called to machinery on fire in Holmes Chapel Time of Incident: 05/01/2015 - 06:25 Location: Eastgate Road, Holmes Chapel Attendance: No attendance data recorded Firefighters were called to a process oven on fire in Holmes Chapel and a full evacuation of the premises was completed. Firefighters inspected ducting wearing breathing apparatus and with a hose reel. They then used a thermal imaging camera at the incident.
