 Chimney fire in Mow Cop Time of Incident: 26/12/2018 - 09:34 Location: The Hollow, Mow Cop. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Alsager At around 9.30am firefighters from Alsager were called to reports of a chimney fire in Mow Cop.  On arrival at the address the team conducted an inspection of the residential address and discovered light smoke on the first and ground floors. The team specialist chimney rods to extinguish the fire and then used a thermal imaging cameral to examine the chimney breast and loft space to check for any hotspots. Firefighters were detained at the scene for approximately one hour 40 minutes. For more information about chimney fire safety visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/campaigns/awareness-campaigns/chimney-fire-safety
