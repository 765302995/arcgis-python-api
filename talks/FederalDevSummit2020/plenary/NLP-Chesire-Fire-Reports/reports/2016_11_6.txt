Two fires on a playground in Winsford Time of Incident: 28/11/2016 - 19:07 Location: Bradbury Road, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish a small refuse fire on a playground at 19:05 pm.   This fire was started deliberately.  Police attended this incident.  Firefighters also had to deal with another refuse fire on the same playground at 20:05pm. Police were requested to attend this incident too.  Anyone with any information about these incident is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
