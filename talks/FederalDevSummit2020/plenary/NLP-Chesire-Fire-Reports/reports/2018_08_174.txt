 Carelessly discarded cigarette believed to have caused balcony fire Time of Incident: 02/08/2018 - 01:19 Location: Leadworks Lane, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester, one from Powey Lane, two from Ellesmere Port and the aerial ladder platform from Chester Firefighters put out a small fire believed to have been caused by a carelessly discarded cigarette on the balcony of a fourth floor flat.
