 Cow rescue in Runcorn Time of Incident: 06/07/2018 - 20:15 Location: Seaford Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Runcorn and one rope rescue unit form Lymm Firefighters were called to Seaford Close, Runcorn, to a cow stuck in a canal. Crews used the fire service boat to herd the cow to safety. The crews were at the scene for two hours and 10 minutes.
