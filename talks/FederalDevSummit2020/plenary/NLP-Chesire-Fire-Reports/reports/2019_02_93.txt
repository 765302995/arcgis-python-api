 Small bin fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 14/02/2019 - 18:22 Location: Tan House Lane roundabout, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh Shortly after 6.20pm firefighters were call to a report of a fire in the open at Tan House Lane roundabout in Widnes. On arrival the crew discovered that it was a small bin fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire with water and were in attendance for around five minutes.
