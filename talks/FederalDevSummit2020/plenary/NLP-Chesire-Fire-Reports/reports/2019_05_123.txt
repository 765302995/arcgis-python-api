 Small fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 14/05/2019 - 18:50 Location: Parsonage Street, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Macclesfield. One fire engine attended from Bollington. Firefighters attended a small fire outside an empty building in Parsonage Street, Macclesfield. The fire involved a waste paper basket at the door entrance of a mill. Crews used one hose reel and a large fan to extinguish the fire and remained in attendance for 30 minutes.
