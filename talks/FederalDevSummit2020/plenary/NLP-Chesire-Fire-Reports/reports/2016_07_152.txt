Cooker on fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 04/07/2016 - 16:31 Location: Robinson Road, Ellesmere Port Attendance: One fire engine from Frodsham and one fire engine from Chester Firefighters were called to a cooker on fire. Crews, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. A large fan was also used to remove smoke from the property. Kitchen fire safety advice
