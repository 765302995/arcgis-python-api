 Small fire in woodland in Runcorn Time of Incident: 26/07/2018 - 20:05 Location: Murdishaw Avenue, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine attended from Lymm At around 8.05pm firefighters were called to a report of a fire in woodland behind Halton Sports Playing Fields on Murdishaw Avenue, Runcorn. On arrival the crew discovered a small fire involving branches and a tree. Firefighters used water to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for around 20 minutes. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the incident should call Cheshire Police on 101, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
