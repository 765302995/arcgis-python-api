 Generator fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/07/2019 - 16:30 Location: Forrest Way, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh One fire engine from Widnes. One fire engine from Warrington. Firefighters have been called to a generator on fire at a landfill and recycling site in Forrest Way in Warrington. On arrival crews discovered generator measuring 10 by 10 metres which was well alight. Two hose reel jets were in use to protect a nearby generator. Two breathing apparatus and two hose reel jests remained in use while a medium foam branch was used to tackle the fire. They then damped down the area using two hose reels.
