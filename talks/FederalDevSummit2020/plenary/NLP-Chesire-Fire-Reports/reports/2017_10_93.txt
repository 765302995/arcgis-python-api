Bin fire in Runcorn Time of Incident: 09/10/2017 - 21:31 Location: Sanders Hey Close, Runcorn Attendance: One fire engine from Runcorn Fire crews were called to a bin fire on Sanders Hey Close, Runcorn. The bin was well alight on arrival and crews used a bucket of water to put out the fire. The incident is believed to have been arson. If you have any information about the incident then please call Cheshire Police 101 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
