Collision in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 12/09/2015 - 09:35 Location: Priory Lane, Macclesfield. Attendance: Two fire engines from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a report of a collision on Priory Lane, Macclesfield.  When they arrived they found that the incident involved two cars. A man was trapped in one of the vehicles. Crews quickly cut him free and passed him to the care of paramedics. He was then taken to the hospital by air ambulance.
