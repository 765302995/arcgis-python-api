Boiler fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 06/09/2017 - 14:34 Location: Prestwich Close, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington and one from Macclesfield Fire crews were called to a boiler fire in a garage of a property on Prestwich Close, Macclesfield. Crews, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire. The crew then cleared smoke from the garage using a large fan. Crews were at the scene for approximately 40 minutes.
