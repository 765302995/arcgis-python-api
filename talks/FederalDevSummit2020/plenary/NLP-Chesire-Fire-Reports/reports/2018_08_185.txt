 Fire in woodland in Warrington Time of Incident: 31/07/2018 - 19:10 Location: Vulcan Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood At around 7.10pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in woodland in Vulcan Close, Warrington. On arrival the crew discovered that an aerosol in the fire had exploded. One casualty was treated by paramedics and taken to hospital. Firefighters cordoned the area off and extinguished the small fire using water. They were in attendance for around three-and-a-half hours. Police also attended.
