Postbox fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 17/03/2016 - 17:58 Location: Badger Avenue in Crewe. Attendance: A fire engine from Crewe Firefighters were called to a report of some smoke coming out of a postbox on Badger Avenue in Crewe.  Crews waited for Royal Mail to come and open the box before putting a small amount of water to dampen down the fire.
