 House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/07/2018 - 01:35 Location: Watkin Street, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington and one from Penketh Firefighters were called to a house fire on Watkin Street, Warrington. Crews found the fire was under the stairs of a the property and they used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Crews were at the scene for one hour.
