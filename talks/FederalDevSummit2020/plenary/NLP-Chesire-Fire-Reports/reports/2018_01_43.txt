 Chimney fire in Chester Time of Incident: 21/01/2018 - 13:37 Location: Saughall Road, Chester Attendance: One fire engine from Chester Firefighters from Chester were called to reports of a chimney fire on Saughall Road, Chester. On arrival crews found that the fire was located behind a gas fire. The crew isolated the gas and removed the fire, before using one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.
