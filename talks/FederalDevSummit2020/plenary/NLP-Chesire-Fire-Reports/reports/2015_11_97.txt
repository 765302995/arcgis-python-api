Grill pan fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 10/11/2015 - 18:26 Location: Lundy Drive, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: Fire engines from Ellesmere Port Firefighters were called to a report of a house fire on Lundy Drive, Ellesmere Port.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a fire in a grill pan but this had gone out. A large fan was used to clear smoke from the property. Firefighters also carried out a Home Safety Assessment before leaving.
