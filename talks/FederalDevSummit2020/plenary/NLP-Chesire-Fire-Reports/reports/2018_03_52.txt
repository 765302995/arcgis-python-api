 Chimney fire in Congleton Time of Incident: 23/03/2018 - 18:44 Location: Redfern Avenue, Congleton. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton Firefighters were called to a chimney fire in Redfern Avenue in Congleton. Crews used chimney rods to extinguish the flames and used a thermal imaging camera to check for hotspots in the loft space and upstairs chimney breast.
