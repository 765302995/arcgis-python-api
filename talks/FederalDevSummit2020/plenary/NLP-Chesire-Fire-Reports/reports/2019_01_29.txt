 Road traffic collision, Glazebury Time of Incident: 05/01/2019 - 21:19 Location: Warrington Road, Glazebury Attendance:  One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Leigh Crews turned out to a road traffic collision in Glazebury, near Warrington. It involved two vehicles and two women and a child were passed to the care of paramedics as the scene was made safe by fire service personnel.
