 Bin fires in Widnes Time of Incident: 11/01/2018 - 21:59 Location: Hough Green Road, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine from Widnes Firefighters were called to several bin fires on Hough Green Road, Widnes. On arrival crews found several bins alight on playing fields. A hose reel jet was used to put out the fires which are believed to have been started deliberately. If anyone has any information please call Cheshire Police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
