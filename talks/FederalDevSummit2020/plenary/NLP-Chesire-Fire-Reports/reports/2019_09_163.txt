 Reports of people feeling dizzy and lightheaded in Knutsford Time of Incident: 01/09/2019 - 02:06 Location: Princess Street, Knutsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Knutsford and Lymm Shortly after 2am firefighters were called to reports that a number of members of staff at a premises in Knutsford were feeling dizzy and lightheaded.  On arrival crews found that the premises had been evacuated and all occupants were safe and well.  Two firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, searched the premises using a gas monitor to see if there were any gas leaks.  As they moved around the building they also opened all of the windows and doors to help ventilate the property.  Following a thorough search of the premises they were unable to find any trace of a gas leak.
