 Small car fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 09/12/2019 - 07:31 Location: St Michaels Road, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At 7.30am firefighters from Widnes were called to reports of a car fire on St Michael's Road.  The fire was out on arrival, but the crew used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots and made the vehicle safe.  The cause of the fire is believed to be accidental. The crew were detained at the scene for around 30 minutes.
