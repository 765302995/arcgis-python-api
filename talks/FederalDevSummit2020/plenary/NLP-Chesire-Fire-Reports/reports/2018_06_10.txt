 Wheelie bin fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 29/06/2018 - 08:41 Location: Ripley Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters from Warrington used a hose reel jet to extinguish a Wheelie bin which was well alight on Ripley Street, Warrington. The team were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes.
