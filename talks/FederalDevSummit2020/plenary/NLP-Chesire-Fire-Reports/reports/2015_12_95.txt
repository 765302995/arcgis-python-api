Road traffic collision in Northwich Time of Incident: 07/12/2015 - 19:06 Location: Ash House Lane / Runcorn Road, Little Leigh, Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich and one from Warrington A man was trapped inside a vehicle following a road traffic collision involving two vehicles.  Firefighters cut the man out of the vehicle before handing him to the care of paramedics. The road was fully closed by police.
