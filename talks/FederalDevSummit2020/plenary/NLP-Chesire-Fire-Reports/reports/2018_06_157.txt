 Rubbish fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 17/06/2018 - 19:57 Location: Tytherington Drive, Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Firefighters were called to a rubbish fire in a bin in a children's play area on Tytherington Drive, Macclesfield. Crews used a hose reel jet to put it out.
