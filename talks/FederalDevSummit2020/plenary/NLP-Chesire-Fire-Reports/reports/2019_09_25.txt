 Smoke alarm activated in Runcorn Time of Incident: 25/09/2019 - 12:21 Location: Gosforth Court, Runcorn Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Runcorn and one from Widnes At around 12.20pm firefighters were called to a report of a smoke alarm sounding at a domestic property in Gosforth Court, Runcorn. On arrival crews found that cooking fumes had activated the alarm. Firefighters administered first aid to an occupier and requested that paramedics attend to assess her condition. The crews also isolated an electrical cooker and used a large fan to remove the cooking fumes from the property. Firefighters were in attendance for more than half-an-hour. For cooking safety tips visit https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/home-safety/kitchen-fire-safety.
