Road traffic collision in Tarporley Time of Incident: 27/10/2017 - 12:20 Location: Nantwich Road, Tarporley Attendance: One fire engine attended from Ellesmere Port and one from Chester Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision between a car and a lorry on Nantwich Road, Tarporley. Firefighters released a casualty from a car. Crews were at the scene for 45 minutes.
