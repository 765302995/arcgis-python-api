 Bedroom fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 19/12/2018 - 12:50 Location: St Stephens Avenue, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington. One fire engine from Penketh. Bedroom fire in WarringtonFirefighters were called to a bedroom fire at an address in St Stephen's Avenue in Warrington. Crews used a hose reel and two breathing apparatus to put out the fire. They then used a large fan to disperse the smoke. All persons were accounted for.
