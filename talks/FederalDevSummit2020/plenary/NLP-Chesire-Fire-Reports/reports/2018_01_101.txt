 Chimney fire extinguished in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 06/01/2018 - 19:18 Location: Cedar Grive in Macclesfield Attendance: One fire engine from Macclesfield Chimney fire extinguished in MacclesfieldAt 7.18pm on Saturday, 6 January a fire engine and a hydraulic platform from Macclesfield attended a chimney fire on Cedar Grive in Macclesfield. Crews used chimney rods, a seven metre ladder and a roof ladder at the incident where they were detained for approximately two hours and thirty minutes.  Always follow our chimney safety advice
