 Small fire, Marton Time of Incident: 10/10/2019 - 18:11 Location: Cassia Green Lane, Marton, Winsford Attendance: One appliance from Winsford Firefighters were called following reports of a small fire in the open on Whitegate Way in Marton. A walker made the call as they were concerned about the embers and the wind picking up. They used a water backpack to extinguish the flames, and were in attendance for around 40 minutes.
