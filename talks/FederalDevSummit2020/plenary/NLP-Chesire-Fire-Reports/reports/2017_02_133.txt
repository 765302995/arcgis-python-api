Road traffic collision in Congleton Time of Incident: 04/02/2017 - 00:25 Location: Mill Street in Congleton Attendance: Two fire engines from Congleton Two fire engines from Congleton were sent to Mill Street in Congleton, to a road traffic collision involving one car which had smashed into the front of a commercial premises resulting in a burst to the gas main. There were no casualties involved. Firefighters used one covering jet and remained at scene until the gas leak had been isolated by National Grid. The incident was left with Cheshire Police. The fire crews remained on the scene for just over two hours.
