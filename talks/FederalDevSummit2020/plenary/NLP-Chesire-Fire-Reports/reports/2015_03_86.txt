Electrical box fire under a roadway in Neston Time of Incident: 13/03/2015 - 17:21 Location: Shakespeare Road, Neston Attendance: One fire engine from Ellesmere Port Smoke was coming from a grid on the road.  Scottish Power attended.  Police attended.   Police closed the road in both directions.  The power was isolated.  The road was excavated by the electricity board, so that they could locate the fault, and isolate the junction box.
