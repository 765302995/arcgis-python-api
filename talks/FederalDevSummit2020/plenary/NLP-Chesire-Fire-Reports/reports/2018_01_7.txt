 Crews extinguish shed fire at a property in Penketh, Warrington Time of Incident: 29/01/2018 - 20:40 Location: Chapel Road, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Penketh At 8.40am on Monday, 29 January two fire engines from Penketh fire station attended to reports of a shed on fire on Chapel Road in Penketh, Warrington. Crews spent one hour extinguishing the fire and ventilating the garden by using one hose reel and a positive pressure ventilation fan.
