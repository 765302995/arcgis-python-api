Firefighters assist power companies in Crewe Time of Incident: 09/05/2016 - 18:02 Location: Wistaston Road, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe and one from Nantwich Three fire engines were called to an incident in Crewe following an explosion and power outage.  Firefighters set up a cordon and stood by as gas and electrical engineers dealt with the problem.
