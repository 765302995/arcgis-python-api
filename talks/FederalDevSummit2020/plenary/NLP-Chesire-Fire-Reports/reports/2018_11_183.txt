 Bonfire in Warrington Time of Incident: 03/11/2018 - 13:40 Location: Folly Lane, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Firefighters were called to a bonfire out of control which was affecting nearby fences. Crews used a hose reel to extinguish the flames. Please see our safety advice on bonfires. https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/public-safety/outdoor-safety/bonfire-fire-safety-advice
