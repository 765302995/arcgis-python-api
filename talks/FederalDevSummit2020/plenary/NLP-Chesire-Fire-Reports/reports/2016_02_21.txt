Road traffic collision in Acton Bridge Time of Incident: 25/02/2016 - 11:42 Location: A49 Warrington Road, Acton Bridge Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich, one from Winsford, one from Runcorn and the heavy rescue unit from Winsford Firefighters have cut a casualty out of a lorry after a road traffic collision involving a heavy goods vehicle and a van on the A49 Warrington Road. The casualty, a man, was taken to hospital by air ambulance. The A49 is closed between Weaverham roundabout and the A533/A49 junction traffic lights. Please avoid the area.
