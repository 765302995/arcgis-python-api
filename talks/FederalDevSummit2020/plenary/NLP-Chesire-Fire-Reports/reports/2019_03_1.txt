Small fire at a pub in Warrington Time of Incident: 30/03/2019 - 05:57 Location: Fearnhead Lane, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Birchwood and one from Lymm Fire crews attended a small fire at a pub on Fearnhead Lane, Warrington. The fire involved a window box and crews used a hose reel jet to put out the fire.
