Fire outside Neston Community Youth Centre Time of Incident: 28/10/2016 - 23:17 Location: Burton Road in Little Neston, Neston Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Ellesmere Port A large quantity of pallets and wood were well alight outside Neston Community Youth Centre. Firefighters gained access to the yard of and then used two hose reels to extinguish the fire. Police also attended this incident.
