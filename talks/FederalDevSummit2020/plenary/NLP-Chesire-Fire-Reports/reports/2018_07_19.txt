 Peat fire in Macclesfield Time of Incident: 28/07/2018 - 08:11 Location: Marton Lane, Macclesfield. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Congleton. One fire engine from Macclesfield. Firefighters were called to peat on fire in a field off Marton Lane, Macclesfield. Crews used two hose reels to tackle the fire, then continued to damp down the peat to ensure there were no hotspots.
