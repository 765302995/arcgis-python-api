 Minor chemical incident, Wilmslow Time of Incident: 06/07/2019 - 08:52 Location: Hawthorn Street, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow A fire crew was called out following reports of a strong chemical smell coming from a hairdressers. Occupants in the first floor flat above were evacuated as a precaution, while a firefighter wearing breathing apparatus investigated the strong ammonia-type smell.  It was found to be coming from a small refrigerator, and was removed to be dealt with outside. A large van was used to ventilate the premises.  Paramedics were on hand to check for any ill effects to the evacuees - of which there were none - and the premises was handed back over to the property owner.
