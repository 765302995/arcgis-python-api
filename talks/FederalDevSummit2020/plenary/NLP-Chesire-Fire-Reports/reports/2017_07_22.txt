Barn fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 23/07/2017 - 16:31 Location: Swanlow Lane, Winsford Attendance: Four fire engines from Northwich, Winsford, Holmes Chapel and Crewe Firefighters tackled a barn fire on Swanlow Lane. Hay inside a barn was on fire. The crews, four firefighters wearing breathing apparatus, used a main jet and two hose reel jets to bring the blaze under control. Firefighters also moved livestock from a barn next door.
