Wheelie bin fire by Radley Common Community Centre in Warrington Time of Incident: 24/04/2017 - 19:29 Location: Radley Common Community Centre, Grasmere Avenue, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington A wheelie bin was deliberately set alight by Radley Common Community Centre.  The bin was well alight.  Firefighters used handheld fire extinguishers to deal with the fire.  Anyone with any information about this fire is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
