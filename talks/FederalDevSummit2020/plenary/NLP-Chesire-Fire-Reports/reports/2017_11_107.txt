Tractor fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 06/11/2017 - 09:17 Location: Mastiff Lane, Malpas. Attendance: One pump from Whitchurch attended Firefighters were called to reports of a vehicle fire on Mastiff Lane in Malpas. On arrival the fire was found to involve a large tractor, located in a field off Mastiff Lane, which was well alight. Firefighters, wearing breathing apparatus, used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. The fire is not being treated as suspicious.
