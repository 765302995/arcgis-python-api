Cafe fire in Alderley Edge Time of Incident: 01/02/2015 - 07:14 Location: London Road, Alderley Edge Attendance: One fire engine from Wilmslow Firefighters wearing breathing apparatus forced entry into a cafe to extinguish a fire inside the building. One hose reel was used to put out the blaze and a large fan to clear the smoke. The cause of the fire is believed to be unextinguished tea lights.
