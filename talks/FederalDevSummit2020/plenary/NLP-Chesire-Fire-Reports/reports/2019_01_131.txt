 Dog rescued from river in Frodsham Time of Incident: 15/01/2019 - 10:02 Location: Bradley Lane, Frodsham Attendance:  Two fire engines attended - one from Frodsham and one from Lymm. An animal rescue unit from Lymm also attended. Firefighters were called to rescue a dog which had fallen into the River Weaver from a steep bank off Bradley Lane in Frodsham. An animal rescue unit from Lymm used an inflatable raft to rescue the dog and return the animal to their owner.
