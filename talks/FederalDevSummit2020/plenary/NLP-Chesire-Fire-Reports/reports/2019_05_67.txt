 Fire involving conifers in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 21/05/2019 - 19:07 Location: Cross Lane, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine attended from Wilmslow Crews attended to 15ft of conifers alight at a property on Cross Lane in Wilmslow.  One hose reel was used by firefighters to put out the fire.  They then dampened down the area and used a thermal imaging camera to check the fire was fully extinguished.
