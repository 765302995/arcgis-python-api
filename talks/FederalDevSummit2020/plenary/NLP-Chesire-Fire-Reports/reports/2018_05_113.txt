 Collision on A54 in Winsford Time of Incident: 16/05/2018 - 17:20 Location: A54, Winsford Attendance: One fire engine attended from Winsford and Northwich Firefighters attended to a three car road traffic collision on the A54 in Winsford.  Crews assisted Cheshire Police in removing vehicles from the road in order to make the area safe.
