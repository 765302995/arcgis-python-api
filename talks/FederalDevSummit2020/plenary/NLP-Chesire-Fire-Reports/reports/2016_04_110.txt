Small fire in Daresbury Time of Incident: 10/04/2016 - 17:45 Location: Daresbury Lane, Daresbury Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington There was a small fire in a derelict building in the grounds of Daresbury Hall. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire which was in an unlisted part of the property.  It is believed the fire was started deliberately.  If you have any information about this incident please call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
