Car on fire on the M56 Time of Incident: 26/05/2017 - 14:03 Location: M56 between junctions 9 and 10 Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Runcorn A small car which was on fire on the M56 required firefighters to attend and use a hose reel to extinguish it. Police attended and closed the motorway while the incident was being dealt with.
