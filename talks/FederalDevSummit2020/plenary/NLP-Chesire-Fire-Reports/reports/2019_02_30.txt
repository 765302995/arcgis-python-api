 Vehicle fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 23/02/2019 - 21:16 Location: Eardswick Lane, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Middlewich Fire crews attended to a saloon car well alight on Eardswick Lane in Crewe.  Wearing one breathing apparatus, crews used two hose reels to extinguish the fire.  A hose reel was then used to dampen down the area.  The road was partially closed until the area was deemed safe.
