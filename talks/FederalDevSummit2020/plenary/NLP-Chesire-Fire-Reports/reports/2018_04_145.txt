 Fire in a disused building fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 03/04/2018 - 20:12 Location: Greystone Park, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters from Crewe were called to reports of a fire in an unused building on Greystone Park, Crewe. on arrival the team found that the fire involved a small quantity of rubbish which had been deliberately set on fire. The team used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and were in attendance for approximately 15 minutes. Anyone with any information in relation to the fire is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting incident number IML26917.
