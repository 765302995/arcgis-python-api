Strong smell inside house in Wilmslow Time of Incident: 27/04/2016 - 01:31 Location: Chamberlain Drive, Wilmslow Attendance: One fire engine from Greater Manchester and one from Wilmslow Firefighters were called to investigate a strong smell in an end terraced house in Wilmslow. Crews from Wilmslow and Greater Manchester checked the property using a gas detector. No abnormal readings were detected. Two females were assessed by paramedics and a decontamination area was established, National Grid also attended.
