Road traffic collision on the M6 between junctions 16 and 17 Time of Incident: 02/10/2015 - 12:10 Location: M6 between junctions 16 and 17 Attendance: Two fire engines Firefighters were called to a road traffic collision on the M6. The incident involved two vehicles and the crew assisted paramedics in releasing one casualty who was trapped. Two lanes were closed while the incident was dealt with.
