 Fire involving a car and trees in Widnes Time of Incident: 16/06/2019 - 19:14 Location: Arley Drive, Widnes Attendance: One fire engine attended from Widnes At 7.15pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the open involving a car and trees in Arley Drive, Widnes. On arrival the crew found the saloon car and tree branches well alight. A firefighter wearing breathing apparatus used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. Firefighters were in attendance for around half-an-hour. Police also attended, organising the recovery of the car. The fire is believed to have been started deliberately. Anyone with information about the fire should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 430008, give the details via https://cheshire.police.uk/contact/general-enquiries or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
