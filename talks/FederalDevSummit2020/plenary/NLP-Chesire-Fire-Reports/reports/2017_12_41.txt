Large bonfire in Crewe Time of Incident: 16/12/2017 - 19:25 Location: Chapel Lane,Crewe. Attendance: Two fire engines from Crewe Firefighters attended a large bonfire which was well alight in Chapel Lane, Crewe. Crews used a hose reel and respirators to extinguish the fire. They then damped down the rubbish and raked it over before using foam to stop further ignition.
