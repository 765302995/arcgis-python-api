 Fire in garden in Plumley Time of Incident: 27/02/2019 - 13:38 Location: Plumley Moor Road, Plumley Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Northwich Fire crews attended to a small fire in the garden of a house on Plumley Moor Road in Plumley. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to put out the fire, which involved paper. The area was then made safe by crews.
