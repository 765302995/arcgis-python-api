Wheelie bins deliberately set on fire in Fearnhead, Warrington Time of Incident: 25/02/2017 - 00:09 Location: Lysander Drive, Fearnhead, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington A quantity of wheelie bins were deliberately set on fire on Lysander Drive in Fearnhead, Warrington. Firefighters used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.  Police attended this incident.  Anyone with any information about this incident, and how the fire started, is urged to call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
