Shed on fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 09/10/2016 - 13:42 Location: Claughton Avenue, Crewe Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe Firefighters were called to a shed which was well alight and used two hose reels to extinguish it. Police were requested to attend as it was believed that the fire could have been started deliberately. If anyone knows anything about this fire they should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
