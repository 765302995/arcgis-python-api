 Two-vehicle collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 02/12/2019 - 10:13 Location: Kerfoot Street, Warrington Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Birchwood and one from Warrington At around 10.15pm firefighters were called to a report of a collision in Kerfoot Street, Warrington.  On arrival crews discovered that the collision involved two vehicles.  Everyone involved in the collision managed to get themselves out of the vehicles.  Firefighters assessed the casualties before handing them over to the care of paramedics.  They also made the vehicles safe and were in attendance for around half-an-hour.
