 Bin lorry fire in Tarporley Time of Incident: 07/08/2019 - 08:14 Location: Nantwich Road, Tarporley. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Nantwich Firefighters were called to a bin lorry on fire in Nantwich Road, Tarporley. The fire was out on arrival and crews used a hose reel jet to cool and damp down the engine bay. They then used a thermal imaging camera to make sure it wasn't going to reignite.
