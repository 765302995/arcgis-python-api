Two vehicle collision in Warrington Time of Incident: 11/08/2016 - 12:12 Location: Chester Road, Daresbury, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Warrington and one from Runcorn Two vehicles were involved in a collision on Chester Road in Daresbury. A minibus had collided with a traffic light and bollard. The driver was assessed by ambulance staff and assisted from the vehicle by firefighters. A light goods vehicle was also involved. Crews made the scene safe.
