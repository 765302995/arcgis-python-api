 Dog's leg impaled on metal spike in Hartford, near Northwich Time of Incident: 10/11/2019 - 15:26 Location: Chester Road, Hartford, near Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich Shortly before 3.30pm firefighters were called to a veterinary hospital on Chester Road, Hartford, near Northwich, to assist with the treatment of an injured dog.  One of the boxer's legs was impaled on a metal spike.  Firefighters used specialist cutting equipment to remove the spike from the sedated dog's leg.  They were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
