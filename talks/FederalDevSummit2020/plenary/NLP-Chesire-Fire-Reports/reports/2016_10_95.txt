Chemical spill at a dry cleaners Time of Incident: 15/10/2016 - 15:17 Location: Congleton Road, Sandbach. Attendance: Fie engines attended from Sandbach and Crewe Firefighters responded to a report of a chemical spill at Johnsons The Cleaners on Congleton Road, Sandbach.  When they arrived crews found that there had been a leak of cleaning solvent from one of the machines. Firefighters wearing protective clothing cleared the spill and liaised with the Environment Agency before deposing of it.   Three casualties were treated as a precautionary measure by ambulance. Crews the scene for about 2hours 30 minutes
