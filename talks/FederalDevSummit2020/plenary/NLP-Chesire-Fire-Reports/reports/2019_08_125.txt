 Back garden fire in Winsford Time of Incident: 08/08/2019 - 12:28 Location: Ludlow Close, Winsford Attendance: Two fire engines attended, one from Winsford and one from Northwich Shortly before 12.30pm firefighters were called to reports of a fire in the back garden of a domestic property in Ludlow Close, Winsford. On arrival crews found that the fire involved several fence panels and had affected a gas cylinder on a patio. The electricity supply to the conservatory at the address was isolated and a firefighter wearing breathing apparatus extinguished the fire with a hose reel jet. The fence panels were then moved away from the property and the gas cylinder was cooled down and the areas affected by the fire were damped down to make them safe. Firefighters monitored the temperature of the gas cylinder with a hose reel jet. They were in attendance for more than an hour-and-a-half. An investigation into the cause of the fire is being carried out.
