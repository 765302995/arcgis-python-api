 Car fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 22/08/2019 - 00:30 Location: Ashmuir Close, Crewe. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Crewe At around 12.30am firefighters from Crewe were called to reports of a vehicle fire on Ashmuir Close.  On arrival they found a saloon car which was well alight.  One firefighter, wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire. They also used a thermal imaging camera to check for any hotspots.  Firefighters were detained at the scene for around one hour.
