 Rescue of a horse in Smallwood Time of Incident: 28/05/2019 - 20:42 Location: Brookhouse Green, Smallwood. Attendance: Three fire engines attended from Congleton, Bollington and Knutsford along with the rope rescue unit and animal rescue unit At 8.40pm on the 28th May 2019, three fire engines, the rope rescue unit and animal rescue unit attended reports of a horse stuck in a horsebox at Brookhouse Green, Smallwood  On arrival the team discovered that the horse had become trapped on a metal bar within the horsebox.  Crews worked together to remove the bar and the horse was released from the vehicle and checked over by a vet as a precautionary measure.   Firefighters were in attendance for approximately twenty-five minutes.
