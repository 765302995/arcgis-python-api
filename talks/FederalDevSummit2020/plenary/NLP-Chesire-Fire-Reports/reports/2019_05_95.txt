 Small rubbish fire in Widnes Time of Incident: 18/05/2019 - 21:04 Location: Crow Wood Lane, Widnes. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Penketh. One fire engine from Widnes. Firefighters attended a small rubbish fire in a disused outbuilding off Crow Wood Lane, Widnes. Crews used a hose reel to put out the fire.
