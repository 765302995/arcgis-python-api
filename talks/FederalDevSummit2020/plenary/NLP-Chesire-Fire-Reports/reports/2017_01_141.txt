Chimney fire in Malpas Time of Incident: 01/01/2017 - 17:49 Location: Old Hall Street, Malpas. Attendance: Fire engines attended from Malpas and Chester. The Hydraulic Platform was also called to the scene. Firefighters were called to a report of a fire in a property on Old Hall Street, Malpas.  When they arrived crews found that there was a blaze in the chimney of the property. Crews used specialist tools and hose reel jet to battle the fire from both above and below.  Crews remained at the scene fighting the fire until around 23:30.  Chimney fire safety advice
