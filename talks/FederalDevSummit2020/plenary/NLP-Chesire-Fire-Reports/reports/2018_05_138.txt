 Back garden bonfire in Northwich Time of Incident: 13/05/2018 - 20:06 Location: Hatton Lane, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine attended from Northwich At 8.05pm firefighters were called to reports of the burning of rubbish in the back garden of a house in Hatton Lane, Northwich. On arrival the crew discovered that it was controlled burning, but after separating the fire to see what was underneath it they found a large amount of plastic. Firefighters used a hose reel jet to extinguish the fire and cool the plastic and were in attendance for around 15 minutes.
