Small fire in Ellesmere Port Time of Incident: 02/12/2015 - 18:58 Location: Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port. Attendance: A fire engine from Ellesmere Port Fire crews used a hose reel jet to put out a small fire off Bunbury Green, Ellesmere Port.  It is believed that this fire was started deliberately. If you have any information that could help find those responsible please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
