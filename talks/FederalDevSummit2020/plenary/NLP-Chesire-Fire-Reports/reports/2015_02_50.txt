Bathroom on fire in Bollington Time of Incident: 15/02/2015 - 20:25 Location: Wellington Road, Bollington Attendance: One fire engine from Bollington and one from Macclesfield A bathroom fire in Bollington was tackled by firefighters.  The crew, two wearing breathing apparatus with a hose reel jet, investigated smoke issuing from the first floor bathroom.  A covering jet was also used and the gas and electricity supplies were isolated.  Once the fire was put out and hot spots were checked for using a thermal imaging camera, firefighters cut away smouldering materials where needed.
