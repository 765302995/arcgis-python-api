House fire in Warrington Time of Incident: 12/02/2016 - 09:49 Location: Millbrook Close, Warrington Attendance: One fire engine from Birchwood and one from Wigan (Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service) Firefighters were called to a report of a fire at a house on Millbrook Close. There was a small fire next to a wood burner in the living room. The fire was put out, a high pressure fan used to clear smoke and the property ventilated.  Fire alarms in the house were activated which raised the alarm.  Wood-burning stoves safety information.
