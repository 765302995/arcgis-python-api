Hedge on fire in Northwich Time of Incident: 23/06/2015 - 17:00 Location: Melchett Crescent, Rudheath, Northwich Attendance: One fire engine from Northwich Approximately 20 metres of hedgerow was involved in fire in Rudheath, Northwich. A hose reel jet was used to put out the fire and police were asked to attend as it is believed the fire was started deliberately.  Anyone with any information about this or similar incidents is urged to call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Cheshire Police on 101.
