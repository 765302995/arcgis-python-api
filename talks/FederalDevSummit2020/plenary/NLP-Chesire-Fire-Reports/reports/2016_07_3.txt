Fire at a commercial premises on Chester Road in Northwich Time of Incident: 30/07/2016 - 00:58 Location: Caspian, Chester Road in Northwich Attendance: Two fire engines from Northwich fire station Two fire engines from Northwich fire station were sent to reports of a fire at The Caspian on Chester Road in Northwich. Fire crews found the fire to be out on arrival. An investigation with a thermal imaging camera identified hot spots and fire spread in an adjacent property, G & B Motor Spares. Two firefighters wore breathing apparatus. One hose reel was used to extinguish the fire. Firefighters also used a large fan to clear the some from the premises.
