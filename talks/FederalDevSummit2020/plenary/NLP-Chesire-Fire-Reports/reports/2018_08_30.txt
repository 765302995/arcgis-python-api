 Kitchen fire in Crewe Time of Incident: 25/08/2018 - 04:23 Location: South Street, Crewe Attendance: Two fire engines attended from Crewe and one from Nantwich Firefighters were called to reports of a kitchen on fire at a house on South Street in Crewe.  A cordon was put in place while crews, wearing four breathing apparatus, went into the property and put out the fire using one covering jet and a positive pressure ventilation fan. Firefighters then checked for hot spots and dampened down the property to make sure it was safe.  All people inside the property have been accounted for.
