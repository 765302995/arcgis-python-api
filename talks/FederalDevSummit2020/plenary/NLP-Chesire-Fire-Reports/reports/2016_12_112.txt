Chip pan fire in Chester Time of Incident: 09/12/2016 - 18:49 Location: Melbourne Road, Chester Attendance: Two fire engines from Chester A chip pan caused a fire in a flat on Melbourne Road. Firefighters, two wearing breathing apparatus, used a hose reel jet to put out the fire and then ventilated the flat to clear smoke. Everybody was accounted for.  Always follow our kitchen safety advice and never leave chip pans or other cooking unattended
