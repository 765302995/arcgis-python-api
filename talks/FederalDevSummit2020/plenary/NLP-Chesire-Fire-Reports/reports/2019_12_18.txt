 Fire in garden, Middlewich Time of Incident: 07/12/2019 - 23:54 Location: Holmes Chapel Road, Middlewich Attendance: One fire engine from Middlewich Firefighters turned out to reports of smoke drifting across the Shell petrol station in Middlewich. The smoke was coming from a pallet being burned in a nearby front garden, with the wind blowing embers towards the forecourt. They used a hose reel to put the fire out.
