 Flooding at a block of flats in Warrington Time of Incident: 10/11/2018 - 02:55 Location: Wilson Patten Street, Warrington. Attendance: One fire engine attended from Warrington Shortly before 3am firefighters from Warrington were called to reports of flooding at a block of flats on Wilson Patten Street. Crews attended the scene and found that the leak had affected a number of flats and the water was approximately 6' deep in the basement area. The team isolated the gas and electric at the scene and attempted to isolate the water - but were unable to stop the leak. United Utilities were called to the scene and the water was isolated at the mains. Crews were detained for approximately two hours
